Shadow Match Halloween

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of learning with Shadow Match Halloween, a captivating game designed for kids to engage their minds while having fun. This educational game invites players to sharpen their observation skills as they match pictures to their corresponding shadows. On the left side of the screen, you will find a vibrant photo that you need to replicate by selecting from various options displayed on the right panels. With each successful selection, you earn a rewarding 200 points, making the gameplay not only enjoyable but also competitive.

However, beware of the whimsical shadows! Choosing the incorrect shadow will cost you 40 points, so be sure to think carefully and rely on your sharp eyes. Shadow Match Halloween not only entertains but also fosters critical thinking and visual acuity. As your score increases, you can see your progress, motivating you to keep matching and learning as the spooky Halloween theme keeps you engaged. Gather your friends or family and challenge each other to see who can achieve the highest score!

How to play free Shadow Match Halloween game online

To start playing the Shadow Match Halloween game, simply select the photo on the left side. Carefully observe the shadows on the right panels and click on the one that matches your image. Your score will increase with each correct match, while incorrect selections will deduct points. Enjoy and good luck!


Instructions

Tap the correct shadow using mouse or touch pad
