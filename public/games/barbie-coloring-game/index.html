How to play free Barbie Coloring Game online

To play the Barbie Coloring Game, simply select a picture from the gallery. After you ve chosen an image, click on different colors to fill specific areas. You can adjust the size of the brush for detailed work and save your creations once you're happy with your masterpiece.
