Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating underwater adventure in "Search for Treasure 2." After hearing the tale of a recent shipwreck that released countless gold coins into the ocean, you ve decided to dive into the deep blue. With your scuba gear on, you will explore the depths of the sea, searching for hidden treasures while navigating through vibrant underwater life.

Your main goal is to collect as many gold coins as possible scattered across the ocean floor. However, your journey won t be without its challenges. You ll need to steer clear of lurking sharks and stinging jellyfish, which can hinder your treasure hunting efforts. Fortunately, other fish you encounter along the way are harmless, providing a colorful backdrop in your quest for riches. Each dive presents an opportunity for excitement and potential danger, making your treasure hunt both thrilling and nerve-wracking.

As you dive deeper, you ll discover various routes and hidden spots that may harbor even more coins. Explore every nook and cranny, but always keep an eye out for the lurking threats in the water. Can you gather enough coins to become the ultimate treasure hunter before returning to the surface?

How to play free Search for Treasure 2 game online

In "Search for Treasure 2," control your character using the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to navigate through the underwater terrain. Collect coins by swimming into them while avoiding sharks and jellyfish. Keep track of your collected coins to gauge your success in this exciting underwater treasure hunt!
