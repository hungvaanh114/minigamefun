Twisted Form Rope Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Twisted Form, you will embody various characters to undertake a challenging task: connecting threads on leather pieces within a limited number of steps to form predefined shapes. Each level takes you on a creative journey, requiring precision and skill to craft intricate patterns. With exquisite graphics and addictive gameplay, Twisted Form not only tests your intellect but also offers unique and captivating entertainment. Do you have the talent to complete all the shapes?
