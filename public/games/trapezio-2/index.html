Trapezio 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Trapezio 2 is an adorable 2D platformer that invites players to embark on an exciting adventure filled with challenge and fun. In this delightful sequel to the original "Trapezio," you will navigate through intricately designed levels, each packed with unique obstacles and foes. Your primary goal is to collect silver coins scattered throughout the terrain while maneuvering around various hazards such as hostile flying robots, sharp spikes, and rotating saw blades.

The game features a total of 8 meticulously crafted levels, where the difficulty ramps up as you progress. With each new level, players will encounter tougher enemies and more complex traps, requiring quick reflexes and clever strategies to succeed. Successfully reaching the red flag at the end of each level signifies your triumph, allowing you to advance to the next stage and uncover more captivating environments and challenges.

With its charming graphics, engaging gameplay, and steady increase in difficulty, Trapezio 2 offers countless hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you re a seasoned gamer or new to platformers, Trapezio 2 promises a delightful experience as you leap through the air, collect coins, and avoid perilous foes. Dive into the colorful world of Trapezio and see how far your skills can take you!

How to play free Trapezio 2 game online

To play Trapezio 2, use the arrow keys or the 'W', 'A', 'S', 'D' keys for movement. Jump using the spacebar, and aim to collect all the silver coins while dodging enemies and obstacles. Reach the red flag at the end of each level to progress to the next challenge.
