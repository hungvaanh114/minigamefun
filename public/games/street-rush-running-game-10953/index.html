Street Rush - Running Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of Street Rush, players are tasked with saving the future streets and subways from the powerful grip of the Big Boss. As a member of an elite runner squad, you will embark on a thrilling journey filled with unique running controls that allow you to rush, jump, and conquer an array of challenges. Created by the minds behind Subway Princess Runner, this game promises an engaging experience for all running game enthusiasts.

Navigate through bustling urban landscapes, dodge approaching trains, buses, and various obstacles by utilizing advanced moves such as turning, jumping, and sliding. Fly over the busy subway tracks while aiming to collect coins and treasure chests. With multiple environments to explore including the vibrant Future City, a Mini Desktop, and an Abandoned Factory you'll need to remain vigilant as you encounter electricity hazards, laser beams, and hostile bots. Your objective is not just survival but also finding the switch to safely navigate through screen doors!

Street Rush boasts a plethora of features designed to enhance your gaming experience. Immerse yourself in fun level designs set against a backdrop of chaotic subway streets. Test your mettle in a Jet Ski, glide through a skatepark with hoverboard acrobatics, and earn gems by completing daily random special levels. Experience various modes, including Survival mode, where you must run with all your might to survive to the end! Compete globally, upgrade your boosters, and unlock new runners to assemble your perfect team to save the city!

How to play free Street Rush - Running Game online

To play Street Rush, start by dodging dangers on your lane using swift movements. Upgrade your abilities to break records, collect medals for gems, and challenge special levels to refine your running skills. Team up with your runners and navigate your way to victory!
