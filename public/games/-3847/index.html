Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Crazy Miner, an engaging 3D idle game that puts you in control of your own mining business. Set in a vibrant cubic world, your journey begins as a novice miner armed only with a basic pickaxe. Your goal is simple yet exciting: collect various resources from your mines and turn them into profit.

As you dig deeper into the mines, you will discover an abundance of resources such as precious gems, metals, and other valuable materials. The more you mine, the more resources you collect and the richer you become. With the earnings from your mining activities, you ll have the opportunity to upgrade your tools and expand your operations.

Craze for mining doesn t stop there! As you progress, you can invest in advanced mining equipment. These upgrades not only enhance your mining speed but also increase the efficiency of gathering resources. This means more profit and greater chances to unlock new levels and uncover rare gems. Challenge yourself to maximize your output and establish your mining empire!

With new challenges and rewards around every corner, Crazy Miner promises endless fun and strategic gameplay. Whether you re a casual player or a dedicated gamer, you ll find joy in expanding your mining business and competing against global players. Experience the thrill of discovery and wealth in this exciting mining adventure!

How to play free Crazy Miner game online

To start playing Crazy Miner, simply begin by tapping on the screen to break through the blocks with your pickaxe. Collect the resources and watch your earnings grow. As you gather enough funds, access the shop to purchase upgrades and advanced tools to enhance your mining efficiency. Keep mining and upgrading to expand your empire!
