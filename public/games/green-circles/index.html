Green Circles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the captivating adventure of Green Circles, players embark on an exhilarating journey with a ball that finds itself ensnared in a treacherous trap. This thrilling game features thirty challenging levels, each presenting unique obstacles that must be navigated to escape. Central to every level are two rotating circles that feature an empty space, forming the perfect backdrop for a test of agility and precision.

As the circles spin continuously, players must skillfully maneuver the ball to leap through the gaps while avoiding sharp thorns lurking within. The stakes are high; a single touch against a spike spells instant defeat. Timing is crucial in Green Circles. Players need to press the jump button at the right moment, ideally twice, to reach the desired height and clear the hazardous spikes. Each jump's success or failure hinges on precision, making this game a true test of reflexes and strategy.

With thirty levels of increasing difficulty, players will find themselves challenged at every turn. As you progress, the rotating speed and spike placement become increasingly complex, ensuring that the excitement and difficulty scale with your skills. The thrill of dodging dangers and overcoming each level keeps players engaged and eager for the next challenge!

How to play free Green Circles game online

To play Green Circles, simply click to jump and time your presses to reach the gaps created by the rotating circles. Each level requires careful observation to avoid the spikes that can quickly end your game. Make sure to jump twice to achieve greater heights when necessary!
