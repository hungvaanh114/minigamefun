Hitty Axe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hitty Axe is an engaging casual game that invites players to unleash their inner warrior with a basic yet addictive mechanic. In this fun experience, you simply need to tap your screen to hurl your axe towards the target. The catch? Not all targets are stationary; some dart around, providing a thrilling challenge as you strive to hit them accurately.

As you play, you'll encounter a variety of targets, each presenting its own set of challenges. Keep a watchful eye on the bombs scattered throughout the game; hitting these will cause you to lose instantly! The stakes get higher with each level as you must think strategically and manage your limited supply of axes wisely. Make every swing count to maximize your score and progress through the game.

Hitty Axe's simplicity is matched only by its captivating gameplay, making it suitable for players of all ages. Whether you re looking for a quick distraction or a more extended gaming session, Hitty Axe offers hours of entertainment where you aim, throw, and smash your way to victory. Challenge yourself to beat your high score and unlock new levels in this exciting axe-throwing adventure!

How to play free Hitty Axe game online

To play Hitty Axe, tap the screen to launch your axe at the target. Be sure to aim carefully, especially when targets are moving. Avoid the bomb targets, as contacting them will end your game. Strategically manage your limited axes to accomplish each level and keep playing!
