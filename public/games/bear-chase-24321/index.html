Bear Chase

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Bear Chase, an exhilarating platform game where agility and strategy are essential for survival. Your primary objective is to jump across various platforms to collect presents scattered throughout the game landscape. Each present you gather adds points to your score, rewarding your skills and quick thinking as you navigate the challenges ahead.

However, the excitement doesn't stop there! As you leap from platform to platform, you ll soon realize that you're not alone in this adventure. From behind you, a pack of black bears emerges, eager to chase you down. These relentless creatures are fast and determined, adding an element of suspense to your game. You must keep moving, stay agile, and outsmart these bears while continuing to collect presents to maximize your score. The combination of platform jumping and evading these ferocious enemies creates an addictive gameplay experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat!

Bear Chase offers not just a test of your jumping skills, but also your ability to think on your feet, strategizing your route while keeping one eye behind you. So gear up for a wild ride where every second counts, and don t let those bears catch you!

How to play free Bear Chase game online

To play Bear Chase, use the arrow keys or your keyboard's spacebar to jump between platforms. Collect as many presents as you can while avoiding the black bears that follow you. Stay alert, and remember that quick reflexes will be your best ally in this thrilling chase!
