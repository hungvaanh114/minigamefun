How to play free Halloween Burst game online

To play Halloween Burst, simply swipe the screen to align three or more matching Halloween elements vertically or horizontally. Eliminating them will complete your mission and earn points. Keep an eye on the objectives for each level, and don t forget to use power-ups to clear challenging spots!
