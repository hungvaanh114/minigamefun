Zombie Candies Classic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Zombie Candies Classic, an enchanting game that combines charming graphics with delightful music, creating an immersive experience for players of all ages. In this whimsical world, your goal is to collect an array of candies to satisfy the demands of the mummy. As you navigate through vibrant levels, be wary of the mischievous pumpkins that can explode and thwart your candy-collecting efforts.

This captivating game offers a total of 20 levels, each designed with increasing complexity to keep you on your toes. As you progress, you will encounter various challenges that require not only skill but also strategy to master. Don't forget to visit the in-game store to enhance your performance and unlock exciting power-ups that will aid you in your quest for sugary treats.

Zombie Candies Classic is perfectly optimized for mobile devices, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable gameplay experience whether you're at home or on the go. Immerse yourself in this delightful adventure and help the mummy gather as many candies as possible while avoiding pesky hazards.

How to play free Zombie Candies classic game online

To play Zombie Candies Classic, simply use your finger to navigate the screen and collect candies while avoiding explosive pumpkins. Tap on the candies to gather them, and make strategic moves to outsmart hazards. Keep an eye on your score as you progress through the whimsical levels!
