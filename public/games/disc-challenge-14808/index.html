Disc Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to dive into the action-packed world of Disc Challenge, where your reflexes and strategy are put to the ultimate test! In this thrilling mobile game, you'll be throwing discs with precision to smash through your opponents' defenses. However, keep your wits about you if your opponent catches the disc, they ll retaliate by tossing it back, adding an exciting twist to each round.

This is the first mobile rendition of a sling puck game that promises to captivate you right from the start. With its engaging gameplay and competitive spirit, Disc Challenge rises above all other sling puck games on mobile platforms. Your journey will take you through a multitude of levels, each presenting unique challenges and diverse opponents who are fiercely determined to disrupt your trophy collection and claiming the top rank for themselves.

The excitement doesn t just stop there! As you progress through the game, you will encounter an array of players, each with their strategies aimed at toppling your hard-earned achievements. With each successful round, you ll unlock even more stages and opponents, heightening the thrill of competition and ensuring that you remain hooked for hours on end. Are you ready to prove your disc-throwing prowess and rise to glory?

How to play free Disc Challenge game online

To play the Disc Challenge, launch the game and aim to throw your disc toward your opponent s defenses. Use swipes to add power and direction to your throw. If your disc gets caught, prepare to dodge the counterattack! Keep eliminating defenses until you secure victory and collect trophies!
