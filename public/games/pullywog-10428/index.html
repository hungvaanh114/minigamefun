PullyWog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a whimsical and exhilarating adventure in PullyWog, where you literally sling yourself through the air in pursuit of delectable flies! Utilize your long, sticky tongue to catch flies and other tasty treats while soaring above the ground. The thrill lies in chaining your catches together devour multiple items in a single aerial maneuver to rack up remarkable combos! Challenge yourself to stay airborne for as long as possible, as the ever-ticking timer adds a layer of excitement to each flight.

But be warned! The skies are fraught with perilous ghosts that can bring your fun to a swift end if you touch them, so navigate cautiously. As you dart between the clouds, keep an eye out for bombs; they can spell disaster if they hit the water below! However, you have the unique ability to prevent explosions by snatching them up before they land. Consuming anything you can find not only boosts your score but also extends the timer slightly allowing you to keep the fun going even longer. The combination of skillful maneuvering, strategic eating, and quick reflexes makes every moment in PullyWog a burst of thrilling fun!

How to play free PullyWog game online

To start playing PullyWog, simply use your mouse or touch controls to aim and launch yourself into the air. Catch flies with your tongue by hovering over them try to chain your catches for bonus points! Avoid ghosts and eat bombs to prevent explosions. Keep an eye on the timer and stay airborne for as long as possible!
