Dont Hit the Sharp

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of challenge and excitement with Don't Hit The Sharp! In this engaging game, your primary objective is to keep your ball bouncing high while dodging the perilous sharp edges that threaten your ascent. With each tap on the screen, you propel your ball upward, but timing is crucial. As you navigate the unpredictable obstacle course, you must stay alert and make swift decisions to avoid a painful pop when your ball collides with the jagged edges.

As you progress through the game, the stakes get higher your survival skills will be put to the test as you aim for greater heights and larger scores. With every successful bounce, you'll feel the adrenaline rush as you climb higher and higher, adding to your ever-growing score. The game's simple yet addictive mechanics will keep you coming back for more, challenging yourself to beat your previous records and those of your friends.

Are you ready to prove your skills? Engage in a battle of reflexes against the sharp dangers lurking in every corner, and relish the satisfaction of outmaneuvering them. The action is fast-paced, and the excitement is relentless can you become the ultimate champion in Don't Hit The Sharp?

How to play free Dont Hit The Sharp game online

To play Don't Hit The Sharp, simply tap the screen to make your ball bounce. Keep an eye on the sharp edges and time your taps precisely to avoid hitting them. Your goal is to survive as long as possible while climbing to greater heights and achieving a higher score. Enjoy the challenge!
