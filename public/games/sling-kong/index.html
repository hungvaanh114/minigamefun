Sling Kong

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sling Kong is an exciting arcade game that puts your skills to the test as you strive to reach new heights. In this engaging adventure, your main objective is to propel your character upwards as far as you can. You will need to pull your character back and let go at just the right moment to launch them towards round ledges. However, caution is paramount as you navigate through a world filled with treacherous obstacles and menacing traps.

As you ascend, you'll encounter various deadly impediments such as flamethrowers, menacing saws, and crushing walls that can end your journey in an instant. To succeed, you must time your moves flawlessly and avoid these perilous challenges while collecting rewards and power-ups along the way. The further you climb, the more insane characters you can unlock, each with their unique abilities that could aid in your quest to reach the top!

Get ready for an exhilarating experience as you unlock various characters that bring a vibrant twist to your gameplay. With each character, you'll carve your own path to success and discover new strategies to navigate the dangerous terrain. Challenge your friends, track your progress, and become a master of Sling Kong!

How to play free Sling Kong game online

To play Sling Kong, simply tap or click to pull back your character, and release to sling them upwards. Aim for the ledges to latch on, while avoiding hazardous traps. Keep going as high as possible, and unlock new characters to help you along the way!
