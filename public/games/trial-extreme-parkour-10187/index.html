Trial Extreme Parkour

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Trial Extreme Parkour, the ultimate challenge for adrenaline junkies and racing enthusiasts! Dive into the thrilling world of high-speed stunts and physics-based gameplay as you navigate through over 40 meticulously crafted levels. Your goal? To achieve the fastest times possible while executing insane tricks on your motocross bike.

Take on the role of a daredevil racer as you perform jaw-dropping stunts and collect stars scattered throughout the dirt bike courses. Each level is designed to test your skills and agility, pushing you to the limit as you tackle challenging obstacles and unpredictable terrains. With every challenge you conquer, you ll earn in-game gold that you can use to upgrade your bike and enhance your racing experience.

In this adrenaline-pumping game, the key to success lies in your ability to master somersaults and other daring tricks to earn points. The more skilled you become, the more options you have to unlock new motocross and BMX bikes. Keep track of your performance and strive to break your own records, as you stay motivated to climb the leaderboard. Remember, there are no limits to what you can achieve in Trial Extreme Parkour it's all about outrageous stunts and blazing speed!

How to play free Trial Extreme Parkour game online

To play Trial Extreme Parkour, use the arrow keys to control your bike's speed and direction. Press the spacebar to jump and execute stunts. Collect stars while racing through levels, and remember to perform somersaults for bonus points and unlocking new bikes. Good luck on your thrilling ride!
