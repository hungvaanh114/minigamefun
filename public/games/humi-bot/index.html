Humi Bot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Humi Bot, a captivating 2D Sci-Fi platformer that challenges your skills and reflexes. In this exhilarating adventure, players must navigate through a series of intricately designed levels filled with perilous obstacles and relentless enemy robots. The main objective is to collect orbs scattered throughout each stage while carefully avoiding a variety of hazards including sharp saws and deadly spikes.

As you progress through the game, you ll encounter a total of eight unique levels, each presenting a greater challenge than the last. The gameplay intensifies as new enemies and obstacles emerge, testing your agility and strategy. Success in Humi Bot requires not only quick thinking but also precise timing as you leap, dodge, and slide through each level. Collecting orbs not only increases your score but also enhances your ability to confront the escalating dangers ahead.

The vibrant graphics and engaging soundtrack elevate the gameplay experience, immersing players in a delightful Sci-Fi universe. With each completed level, you are rewarded with a sense of achievement that keeps you coming back for more as you aim to clear every stage. Do you have what it takes to overcome the challenges and reach the Exit door to unlock new adventures? Join Humi Bot on this thrilling journey and find out!

How to play free Humi Bot game online

To play Humi Bot, use the arrow keys to navigate and control your character. Jump using the space bar to avoid obstacles and collect orbs. Make sure to stay clear of enemy robots and traps as you race towards the Exit door to complete each level!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
