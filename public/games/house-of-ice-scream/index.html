House of Ice Scream

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chilling world of House Of Ice Scream, an engaging online game that you can enjoy for free at kidsgame.io. In this thrilling adventure, the notorious ice-cream seller has invaded your neighborhood, bringing terror along with his delightful treats. However, beneath his sweet facade lies a dark truth, for he has abducted your neighbor and friend Charlie! Your heart races as you witness this shocking event unfold. Charlie has fallen prey to the ice-cream seller's strange powers, getting frozen and whisked away in his menacing van.

With your best friend missing, your mind races with the terrifying possibility that other children may have suffered the same fate. It s time to rally your courage and embark on a rescue mission. Navigate through eerie locations, solve complex puzzles, and unveil the sinister secrets hiding in the ice-cream seller's world. You'll need your wits about you to explore creepy environments and gather clues that could lead you to Charlie before it s too late. The clock is ticking, and the stakes are high; each moment you hesitate increases the danger for your friend. Will you be able to outsmart the ice-cream seller and restore safety to your neighborhood?

How to play free House Of Ice Scream game online

Once you enter the game, control your character using the arrow keys for movement. Interact with objects using the spacebar to gather clues and solve puzzles. Keep an eye out for hidden passages and avoid dangers lurking in the shadows. Your mission is to rescue Charlie stay alert and think creatively!
