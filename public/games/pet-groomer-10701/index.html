Pet Groomer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Pet Groomer, where you manage your very own pet store focusing on grooming! As the proud owner of this vibrant establishment, your primary task is to pamper adorable pets and ensure they leave your store looking their absolute best. One sunny afternoon, a girl enters your store with her beloved pets a fluffy dog and a playful cat each in dire need of a makeover. It s time to showcase your grooming skills!

In this engaging simulation, you'll provide a variety of grooming services to the dog and cat, starting with a refreshing shower. Use shampoos, conditioners, and various grooming tools to cleanse, detangle, and fluff their fur. After their spa day, you can unleash your creativity by styling their coats with colorful accessories and eye-catching designs. The more beautiful they look, the happier your customers will be, and your reputation as the best pet groomer in town will grow!

Your charming pet store offers a whimsical environment filled with fun activities. You can choose from several grooming options and even decorate your store to attract more furry friends. Keep an eye out for special events and challenges to earn extra rewards while mastering the art of pet grooming. With each satisfied pet and owner, you ll unlock new styles and tools, making the experience even more enjoyable.

How to play free Pet Groomer game online

To play the Pet Groomer game, simply start by selecting the pet you wish to groom. Use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through grooming tools. Follow the on-screen instructions to wash, dry, and style the pets. Don t forget to add accessories to make them stand out! Enjoy the lively graphics and have fun creating the ultimate pet makeovers!
