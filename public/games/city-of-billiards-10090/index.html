City of Billiards

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the intriguing world of City of Billiards, where you step into the shoes of a pool master performing dazzling billiard shows across the vibrant city landscapes. Each venue presents a unique set of challenges, with the arrangement of billiard balls progressively becoming more complex as you progress through the levels. Whether you're an experienced player or a novice, the game will test your skills and strategic thinking.

In City of Billiards, every shot counts. Players have the flexibility to finely tune the direction, speed, and angle of their cue, enabling them to adopt a personalized approach for each challenging layout. The game encourages mastery of your shots by allowing you to adjust the hit point on the ball, which can lead to more precise and powerful strikes. With 20 engaging levels to conquer, you must navigate through a myriad of intricate setups while adhering to the limits on the number of shots permitted, pushing your skills to the limit.

This game combines the thrill of competition with a relaxed, engaging atmosphere, allowing players to enjoy both the strategic aspects of billiards and the satisfying feeling of making that perfect shot. Develop your skills as you rise to each new challenge, making City of Billiards a must-play for enthusiasts of cue sports.

How to play free City of Billiards game online

To play City of Billiards online, simply use your mouse or finger to adjust the angle, speed, and direction of your cue. Click or tap to set your shot, choosing the hit point on the ball for greater accuracy. Aim carefully and try to clear each level using the limited number of hits allowed!
