Kill the Corona

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game "Kill The Corona," players step into the shoes of a heroic doctor fighting to save humanity from a deadly virus outbreak. As the world succumbs to illness and despair, your mission is to battle the relentless spread of viruses infecting people's bodies. Armed with a supply of syringes filled with potent vaccines, it's up to you to target and eliminate these harmful pathogens one by one.

Navigate through vibrant and challenging levels where you ll encounter different types of viruses, each with its own unique behaviors and obstacles. As a skilled doctor, you'll need to swiftly throw syringes to effectively neutralize the threats and help restore health to the afflicted. Timing and precision are crucial, as you aim to save as many lives as possible while dealing with the mounting challenges posed by the spread of the virus.

With every virus you eliminate, you gain points that can be used to upgrade your syringes and unlock special abilities, enhancing your skills in this race against time. As you progress, the game intensifies, requiring quick reflexes and strategic thinking. Your ultimate goal is clear: eradicate the viruses and bring hope back to a world that desperately needs healing.

How to play free Kill The Corona game online

To play "Kill The Corona," use your mouse or finger to aim at the viruses and click or tap to throw syringes. Each successful hit eliminates a virus, and you must manage your resources wisely. Keep an eye on the timer and aim for combos to maximize your score while saving as many patients as you can!
