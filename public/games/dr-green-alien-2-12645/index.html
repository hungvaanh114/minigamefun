Dr Green Alien 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey through the cosmos with Dr. Green Alien 2, a captivating space adventure packed with challenges and excitement! Join our vibrant alien doctor on his mission to collect toxic waste barrels scattered throughout the universe. With its stunning graphics and engaging gameplay, this game promises to keep you entertained as you navigate through treacherous landscapes.

As you guide Dr. Green through various levels, your primary objective is to gather the hazardous barrels while dodging dangers and overcoming obstacles. Remember, these barrels are toxic! So, it s essential to strategize your moves carefully. Each successful collection earns you points, bringing you one step closer to the finish line. The game s thrilling pace and vibrant environment will immerse you in an intergalactic experience like no other!

Be mindful of the hazardous surroundings. Make sure you collect each barrel before proceeding; leaving them behind could cost you the game. Test your reflexes and decision-making skills as you maneuver around pitfalls and traps. Your goal is not just to finish but to gather as many points as possible to prove your prowess as the ultimate collector in the galaxy!

How to play free Dr Green Alien 2 game online

To play Dr. Green Alien 2 online, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character. Navigate through the levels, collect the toxic barrels, and avoid poisonous obstacles. Make sure to aim for high scores by collecting all barrels before reaching the finish line!
