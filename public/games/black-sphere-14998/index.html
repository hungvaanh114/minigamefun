Black Sphere

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the captivating world of Black Sphere, a puzzle game that will challenge your strategic thinking and hand-eye coordination. On your screen, you will find a dynamic playing field filled with several circular cells, one of which harbors a crucial element the white ball. This ball is your main objective, as you will need to navigate it into a specific cell indicated by a unique color highlight.

Each round begins with a black ball positioned at the top of the playing field. Your task is to use your mouse to maneuver this black ball toward one of the cells. The goal is to align it perfectly so that when the black ball makes contact with the white ball, it pushes it into the designated cell. Successfully accomplishing this feat will earn you points and advance you to the next level, where new challenges await.

As you progress, each level introduces a greater complexity with specific configurations and potential obstacles to overcome. The simplicity of the game mechanics combined with the increasing difficulty will keep you engaged and entertained. Can you master the movements and strategy to become a Black Sphere champion? Dive in and find out!

How to play free Black Sphere game online

To play Black Sphere, simply use your mouse to control the black ball at the top of the field. Aim it towards the white ball located in one of the cells and ensure it rolls into the highlighted cell. Complete each level by scoring points and enjoy the fun of progressively challenging puzzles!
