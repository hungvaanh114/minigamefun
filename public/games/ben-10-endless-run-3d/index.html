Step into the thrilling universe of Ben 10 Endless Run 3D, where evildoers threaten the peace of the city, and it s up to you to defend the jungle! Put on your superhero cape and immerse yourself in this action-packed stickman adventure packed with exciting challenges and endless fun. Engage in dynamic gameplay where your skillful taps and strategic movements along the rope line will help you navigate obstacles and perform stunning aerial maneuvers. The more elegantly you swing and soar, the more hidden areas of the jungle you will uncover and protect!

Perfect for your leisure moments, Ben 10 Endless Run 3D offers a casual yet exhilarating adventure with your favorite character!

How to play free Ben 10 Endless Run 3D game online

To play Ben 10 Endless Run 3D, simply swipe right and left to evade obstacles that come your way. Use upward swipes to jump over barriers, and downward swipes to roll beneath them. Tapping the screen twice launches a counterattack against approaching enemies. Make sure to stay on the path and enjoy your adventurous run!
