Princesses BFF Rush to School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The school year has officially kicked off, and the beloved princesses are filled with excitement and a touch of anxiety! With classes starting, they need to ensure their school backpacks are fully stocked and ready for action. However, the challenge lies in locating the scattered textbooks and notebooks that are strewn across their bedrooms. As they gear up for the day, they also want to look their absolute best, which means selecting the trendiest outfits and applying just the right amount of cosmetics and perfume. The quest for the missing lipstick and other essential beauty items is on!

This vibrant and engaging game, Princesses BFF Rush to School , invites players to join these fashionable friends on their thrilling morning adventure. It s a race against time as you assist the princesses in gathering all the items they need to dazzle at school. From finding books to ensuring they have their favorite makeup products, your help is crucial to make their school debut a fabulous one. Get ready to rummage through their colorful rooms and gather an array of essentials while enjoying the fun and exciting atmosphere this game offers!

How to play free Princesses BFF Rush to School game online

To play the game, start by clicking on the items listed on the screen. You ll need to explore the princesses rooms thoroughly to collect textbooks, notebooks, and beauty essentials. Use your mouse to navigate and drag the items into the backpack. Complete the list before time runs out to ensure a stylish start for our princesses!
