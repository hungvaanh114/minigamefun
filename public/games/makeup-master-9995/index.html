Makeup Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Makeup Master invites you to step into the world of fashion and beauty! This exciting makeup game puts you in the role of a renowned stylist tasked with transforming the looks of four distinct clients, each of whom is feeling a bit lackluster. With a night out planned at the mall or playground, it's your job to ensure they radiate confidence and beauty.

Your journey begins as you assess your clients gaunt appearances. Using your expert makeup skills, you ll select the perfect cosmetic products to enhance their natural beauty. From foundation to eyeshadow, blush to lipstick, each choice will help rejuvenate their look. Experiment with various styles to see which one fits their personality best.

But the excitement doesn t stop there! After the makeup is complete, you ll dive into the world of hairstyling and fashion. Choose stunning hairstyles that complement the new makeup, before picking out chic outfits and trendy accessories to complete their transformations. With your creativity and talent, your clients will look entirely different and ready for an evening full of fun and adventure.

Enjoy every moment of the game as you explore the latest trends, and don t forget to prolong the joy by experimenting with different combinations to find the looks that truly shine. Say goodbye to their old looks and welcome their fabulous new styles all thanks to your amazing skills!

How to play free Makeup Master game online

To start playing Makeup Master, simply select a client and click on the makeup options available. Use your mouse to apply cosmetics, style hair, and choose outfits. Drag and drop accessories to complete their looks, and don t forget to save your favorites for a future glam session!
