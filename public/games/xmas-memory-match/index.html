How to play free Xmas Memory Match game online

To play Xmas Memory Match, begin by selecting your preferred difficulty level: Easy or Medium. Once the game starts, you'll see a grid of face-down cards. Click to flip two cards at a time, and try to remember their positions. If the cards match, they're removed from the grid. Continue until all pairs are matched!
