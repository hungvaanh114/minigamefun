How to play free Klondike Solitaire Pro game online

To play Klondike Solitaire Pro, simply start by arranging your cards into a tableau with seven columns, with the top card facing up. Your goal is to build four foundation piles in ascending order, starting from Ace to King, by moving cards from one pile to another. Use the undo and hint features to guide your moves!
