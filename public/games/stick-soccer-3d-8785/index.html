How to play free Stick Soccer 3D game online

To play Stick Soccer 3D, use the arrow keys to navigate your player, and spacebar to shoot the ball. If you are in two-player mode, Player 1 uses W, A, S, D for movement and G to shoot, while Player 2 uses the arrow keys for movement and the L key to shoot. Get ready to score some goals!
