How to play free Recoil Arena 1VS1 game online

To play, simply join the arena and choose your character. Use WASD keys to move around and aim with your mouse. Press left-click to shoot at your opponent. Engage in fast-paced matches, keeping track of your kills and outmaneuvering your rival. Who will claim victory as the top fighter? Enjoy the thrill!
