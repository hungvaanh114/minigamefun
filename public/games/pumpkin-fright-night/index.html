Pumpkin Fright Night

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spine-tingling adventure in "Pumpkin Fright Night"! Join Mr. Jack Lantern on a whimsical quest to gather all the lost pumpkins scattered throughout an enchanted Halloween world. This thrilling puzzle game not only offers engaging gameplay but also immerses players in a delightful autumn atmosphere, packed with spooky surprises and autumnal charm.

In "Pumpkin Fright Night," players must employ their physics skills to maneuver and rotate Mr. Jack Lantern, strategically navigating through challenging levels filled with enigmatic obstacles and clever puzzles. Each pumpkin you collect brings you closer to victory while unleashing a trail of festive fun. The game combines cute graphics with engaging mechanics, making it delightful for players of all ages. Get creative as you think critically and solve various challenges to ensure Mr. Jack Lantern successfully finds his pumpkins.

As you progress through the game, you ll encounter new challenges and puzzles, each designed to test your skills and keep the game fresh and exciting. With each level, you ll need to think outside the box to overcome hurdles and gather all the missing pumpkins before time runs out. Are you ready to embrace the spirit of Halloween and aid Mr. Jack in his quest? Dive into this enchanting puzzle adventure today!

How to play free Pumpkin Fright Night game online

To start playing "Pumpkin Fright Night," simply use your mouse or touch controls to interact with Mr. Jack Lantern. Move him around the screen and rotate him to strategically collect the scattered pumpkins. Use your problem-solving skills to navigate obstacles and successfully complete each level, making sure to gather all pumpkins before time runs out!
