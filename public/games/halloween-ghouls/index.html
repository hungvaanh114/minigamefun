Halloween Ghouls

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spine-chilling adventure in Halloween Ghouls, an exciting tower defense game that transports you into a world of frightful creatures. As Halloween night descends upon your village, ghouls of all kinds, including mischievous ghosts, wicked witches, and bloodthirsty vampires, are making their way toward your home. It's your duty as the defender to prevent these spectral foes from breaching the town s defenses.

Your mission is simple yet exhilarating: strategically place various types of towers along the path to intercept the ghouls before they reach the villagers. You'll need to make tactical decisions on where to position your defenses, as each tower possesses unique abilities and strengths against different types of Halloween ghouls. Upgrade your towers to increase their power and effectiveness, ensuring that they are equipped to handle waves of increasingly challenging opponents.

With captivating graphics and a thrilling soundtrack that sets the mood for this haunting adventure, Halloween Ghouls will keep you on the edge of your seat as you battle to protect the innocent. Utilize your wits and strategy to build a formidable defense, saving the villagers from a Halloween-related fate they would rather avoid!

How to play free Halloween Ghouls game online

To play Halloween Ghouls, begin by selecting your desired tower type and placing it on the game map. As waves of ghouls approach, use your resources wisely to build additional towers and upgrade existing ones. Keep an eye on the ghouls' movement and adjust your strategy to ensure they don t reach the village. Enjoy the challenge and defend your town!


Instructions

Click or touch the available towers at the bottom of the game screen to select them and then click or touch the grassy areas on the game screen to place them Once the towers have been placed click the start wave button
