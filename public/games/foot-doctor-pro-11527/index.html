Step into the shoes of a skilled physician with Foot Doctor Pro, the ultimate leg and hand surgery simulation game! This exciting new addition to doctor simulation games invites players to become a virtual nail foot doctor, where you will treat a diverse array of patients suffering from various ailments affecting their toes, palms, hands, and knees. Immerse yourself in the realistic hospital environment designed to mimic the challenges faced by real-life doctors.

As a budding surgeon, you'll navigate through multiple levels of surgery tasks, each more intense than the last. Use professional-grade medical tools and equipment to perform intricate surgeries on injured nails and feet. From bandaging delicate wounds to conducting advanced surgical procedures, Foot Doctor Pro provides an authentic experience that promotes both learning and fun!

Every patient comes with their unique set of symptoms, and it s your job to diagnose and treat them effectively. Do you have what it takes to become the best nail foot doctor in town? With each successful surgery, you'll gain points and move up the ranks, unlocking even more challenging cases. This game not only tests your medical knowledge but also hones your hand-eye coordination and precision under pressure.

How to play free Foot Doctor Pro game online

To play Foot Doctor Pro, simply select a patient and examine their injuries. Use the toolset provided on the screen to perform necessary operations by following the in-game instructions. Be swift and precise to ensure a successful treatment and watch your patient s recovery unfold!
