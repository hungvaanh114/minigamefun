Stick Merge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stick Merge is an engaging casual action game that combines elements of strategy and merging mechanics. The main objective is to fuse together various weapon types to construct increasingly powerful firearms. As you advance, you'll have the chance to face off against moving stick figures at a shooting range, honing your skills while testing the effectiveness of your custom creations. Get ready to prepare your weaponry, enhance your pistol, and uncover an array of power-ups as you strive to become the ultimate sharpshooter in the game.

The excitement of Stick Merge lies in its unique merging system that allows you to create innovative weapons. How powerful can you build your arsenal? As you blend different guns, watch as your firepower multiplies, making it easier to dispatch opposing stick figures. Each upgrade not only enhances your weapon's efficiency but also provides an exhilarating experience as you witness the dynamic gameplay unfold. Stick with it and see how far you can push your crafting abilities to establish your dominance in Stick Merge!

How to play free Stick Merge game online

To play Stick Merge, simply use your mouse or finger to drag and drop the various weapon icons onto each other to merge them. Keep an eye on the shooting range and target the moving stick figures. Upgrade your weapons by merging higher-level variants and utilize power-ups to maximize your shooting effectiveness!
