Fashion Victoria Secret Show

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the glamorous world of the Fashion Victoria Secret Show, where elegance meets excitement in a dazzling showcase of exquisite lingerie and dazzling performances. This show is a feast for the eyes, featuring stunning wings that captivate the audience, and highlighting bold and sizzling designs by some of the world's leading fashion creators. Among the fans, three best friends Adella, Flora, and Rebecca find themselves mesmerized by the spectacle as they take in every intricate detail from home.

Inspired by the show, these fashionable friends decide to put their own spin on things and create their very own Secret Fashion Show! Their enthusiasm is contagious, and they are eager to collect all the essential elements from chic outfits to a beautifully decorated catwalk that will showcase their individual styles. Adella, Flora, and Rebecca call upon you to help them transform their vision into reality! Will you lend a hand in dressing them up and setting the stage for an unforgettable experience?

Prepare to unleash your creativity as you select dazzling outfits and stunning accessories that reflect the latest trends. Work alongside the girls to perfect the catwalk ambiance and options that will impress their audience. With your assistance, their Secret Fashion Show promises to be an extraordinary event that blends friendship with fashion and flair!

How to play free Fashion Victoria Secret Show game online

To start playing the Fashion Victoria Secret Show game, simply select your favorite characters, then explore the wardrobe to choose chic outfits and accessories. Once you're done dressing them, move on to decorating the catwalk by picking suitable backdrops and stage elements. Enjoy the creative freedom!
