Stair Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Stair Race 3D, where the competition is fierce and only skilled builders will rise to the top! In this adrenaline-pumping arena, your goal is to collect stair planks scattered throughout the environment. With the planks in hand, you must construct staircases that will enable you to ascend to higher platforms. The challenge intensifies as you face off against a diverse cast of adversaries, each eager to claim the title of the ultimate stair builder.

Be vigilant, as your enemies can knock you off your game! If they have collected more planks than you, colliding with them will lead to a rapid loss of your hard-earned materials. However, the rules work both ways if you're strategically collecting planks and build up your collection, you can turn the tables on your opponents. Knock them down, steal their planks, and keep climbing toward victory for a significant edge in the race!

As you progress through multiple rounds, you won t just enjoy the thrill of competition; you'll also unlock unique skins for your character and stylish designs for your stair planks. Dominate the arena, flaunt your achievements, and showcase your stylish upgrades as you climb to the pinnacle of stair-building prowess!

How to play free Stair Race 3D game online

To begin your journey in Stair Race 3D, navigate your character using the arrow keys or on-screen controls. Collect stair planks scattered around the arena, and use them to build stairs leading to higher platforms while dodging enemies. Strategically knock over other players to steal their planks and gain an advantage in the race!
