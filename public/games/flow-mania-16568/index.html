Flow Mania

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the world of Flow Mania, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your problem-solving skills with a delightful twist. In this game, you'll encounter vibrant and colorful dots that are waiting to be connected. Your goal is simple yet intriguing: link each pair of dots with a continuous pipe. However, the challenge lies in covering the entire game board without allowing any pipes to cross or overlap. This engaging task requires a keen eye and strategic thinking as you create a beautiful flow between the dots.

Flow Mania features three sets of 25 unique levels, each designed to progressively test your abilities. As you advance, you'll encounter increasingly difficult challenges that push your logic and creativity to their limits. The vibrant colors and smooth mechanics make every level enjoyable, ensuring that you remain captivated from the first dot you connect to the last. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle player or new to the genre, Flow Mania offers an addictive experience that keeps you coming back for more.

As you navigate through the levels, you'll discover that each solution may require a different approach, adding to the game's replayability. Prepare yourself for hours of fun as you strategize how to fill the board with pipes while adhering to the rules: no crossing and no overlapping. Get ready to put your mind to the test in this charming yet challenging game of Flow Mania!

How to play free Flow Mania game online

To play Flow Mania, simply start by selecting a pair of dots on the game board. Drag your pipe between them to create a connection. Remember, your goal is to connect all dot pairs while ensuring that the pipes do not intersect. Keep experimenting until the entire board is filled!


Instructions

Connect dots of the same colour with pipes creating a flow You also have to fill the whole board with pipe to solve the level No crossing or overlapping allowed
