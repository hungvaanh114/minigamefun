Pacman Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pac-Man Master is an exhilarating addition to the classic franchise that has captured the hearts of gamers since its inception in 1980. Originally developed by Namco, with a wide range of adaptations by renowned game developers such as Midway Games, Atari, and Mass Media, Inc., this iconic game franchise has evolved over the decades while retaining its core maze-chase mechanics.

The gameplay revolves around maneuvering Pac-Man through a maze filled with dots, known as pellets, while avoiding the menacing ghosts that seek to catch him. As players navigate through the winding pathways, they can collect power pellets that temporarily turn the tables on the ghosts, allowing Pac-Man to gobble them up for extra points. While primarily known as a maze-chase game, the Pac-Man series has diversified into various other genres, including platformers, racing games, and even sports, catering to a wider audience and keeping the franchise fresh.

With numerous iterations available on an array of home consoles, along with games featured in Namco compilations, Pac-Man Master offers players both nostalgia and innovation. This accessibility allows both long-time fans and newcomers to enjoy the thrill of navigating through colorful mazes and strategizing against the AI-controlled foes in pursuit of high scores.

How to play free Pac-Man Master game online

To play Pac-Man Master online, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move Pac-Man through the maze. Aim to collect all the pellets while avoiding the ghosts. If you eat a power pellet, you can temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing you to chase and eat them for bonus points. Good luck!
