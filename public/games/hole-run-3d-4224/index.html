Hole Run 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hole Run 3D is an engaging and captivating online puzzle game that promises endless fun for players of all ages, especially kids. In this free-to-play game, you'll encounter the fascinating concept of black holes, which can absorb everything in their vicinity. Your mission is to maneuver the black hole across various platforms, gathering an array of colorful blocks and sparkling diamonds as you go. The more you collect, the higher your score will climb!

As you navigate through different levels, you'll face various challenges that test your timing and strategic thinking. However, beware of the bombs that lie on the platform! Absorbing these hazards will lead to instant failure, so maintaining focus is key as you guide your black hole through increasingly complex puzzles. Each level offers a unique landscape, complete with new obstacles and rewards to keep you engaged. Get ready for a roller coaster of excitement as you dodge the bombs while trying to amass as many treasures as possible!

With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Hole Run 3D is the perfect choice for players looking to enjoy a fun and relaxing gaming experience. Gather your friends and compete for the highest scores, or simply enjoy the satisfaction of perfecting your absorption skills. Whether on your computer or mobile device, this game offers a delightful escape into a world where you can play, collect, and challenge yourself while having a great time!

How to play free Hole Run 3D game online

To play Hole Run 3D, use your mouse or touch controls to move the black hole across the platform. Absorb blocks and diamonds without hitting bombs. Collect as many points as you can before reaching the end of the level. Keep an eye out for strategic opportunities and enjoy the thrill of the game!
