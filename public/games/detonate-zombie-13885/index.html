Detonate Zombie

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into an exhilarating world of chaos and adventure with Detonate Zombie, a top-down shooting game that promises a unique blend of skill, rapid reflexes, and thrilling action. In the game s gripping narrative, a well-meaning scientist is navigating the subway with a vital experimental potion when a mishap leads to disaster. As he fumbles, the potion crashes to the floor, inadvertently releasing a monstrous viral outbreak that swiftly transforms every person in the station into bloodthirsty zombies.

Players take on the role of an unlikely hero, who must act quickly and decisively to eliminate the zombie horde before they burst forth into the city. Your primary objective? Destroy all the entrances to the subway stations to ensure the safety of urban life. With an arsenal of weapons at your disposal, you ll need to employ strategy and precise aiming to blast your way through waves of relentless undead foes. The game's vibrant graphics, fluid mechanics, and engaging gameplay keep the adrenaline pumping as you navigate through intricate subway layouts filled with obstacles and threats.

As you ascend through the challenging levels, you ll encounter various types of zombies, each more menacing than the last. With power-ups and upgrades scattered throughout your journey, players must consistently adapt their playstyle to survive. Detonate Zombie is not just about shooting; it s about survival and ensuring that the chaos remains contained within the subway tunnels.

How to play free Detonate Zombie game online

To play Detonate Zombie, use your mouse to aim and click to shoot. Move your character with the WASD keys to dodge incoming zombie attacks. Keep an eye out for power-ups to replenish health and enhance your weapons as you blast your way through the subway stations and prevent zombies from escaping into the city!
