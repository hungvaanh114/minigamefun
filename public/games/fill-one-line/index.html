Fill One Line

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into the world of fascinating logic and test your mind in an exciting puzzle! Your task is to draw a line across the entire playing field, filling it completely, but keep in mind: there will be obstacles on the way that you need to bypass. The game offers many levels, from simple to incredibly difficult, allowing each player to choose a suitable challenge. Each level is unique: thoughtful routes, various field shapes and unexpectedly located barriers will force you to look for non-standard solutions.

Instructions

Mouse click or Playing our puzzle is simple and fun Your goal is to fill the entire playing field with one continuous line avoiding obstacles How to play Start from the chosen starting point Draw a line connecting the cells of the field to fill them
