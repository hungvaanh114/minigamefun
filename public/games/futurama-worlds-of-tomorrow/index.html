Immerse yourself in the hilariously unpredictable universe of Futurama: Worlds of Tomorrow, where you can create your very own New New York! Experience the wit and charm of the classic TV series as you collect your favorite characters like Fry, Leela, and Bender, embarking on an uproarious adventure that feels straight out of the show. Written by the original creators, this game offers a truly authentic experience that will have you laughing out loud.

In Worlds of Tomorrow, you'll construct and customize your unique city while battling an array of aliens in side-splitting 8-bit combat. Your choices determine the course of the game, allowing you to control the narrative and shape your own destiny within this expansive universe. Prepare for unforgettable space travel where each journey opens the door to new worlds teeming with humorous escapades. Unlock amusing outfits for your characters, leveling them up as you collect ancient artifacts vital to saving the universe.

Get ready for endless laughter with witty dialogue penned by the creators of Futurama, David X. Cohen and Matt Groening. Assemble your perfect crew and send them on intergalactic missions that promise thrill and excitement. Whether you're a longtime fan or new to the series, Futurama: Worlds of Tomorrow invites you to explore strange species and whimsical stories that await in this engrossing game!
