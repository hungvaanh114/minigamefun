Super Pig on Xmas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Super Pig on Xmas is an exciting 2D platformer that immerses players in a delightful holiday adventure. In this festive journey, you take on the role of a cheerful character dedicated to gathering candies to share with a sweet piglet who can't wait to indulge in Christmas treats. Your objective is to navigate through vibrant levels filled with colorful candies while skillfully dodging various traps and enemies that threaten your progress.

With a total of eight engaging levels, each stage presents a unique set of challenges. As you advance, the difficulty ramps up, ensuring that players are continually tested on their reflexes and strategic thinking. The charming graphics and joyful soundtrack create a captivating holiday atmosphere that keeps you engaged from start to finish. Make sure to collect as many candies as possible while overcoming the obstacles in your path.

Super Pig on Xmas offers an entertaining blend of fun and challenge, making it perfect for players of all ages. So put on your adventure hat and get ready to embark on a whimsical quest to make this Christmas special for the adorable little piglet.

How to play free Super Pig on Xmas game online

To play Super Pig on Xmas, use the arrow keys to move your character left and right, and jump to avoid obstacles and collect candies. Keep an eye out for traps and enemies as you progress through the levels. Aim to gather all the candies to maximize your score and make the piglet's Christmas unforgettable!


Instructions

WASD or Arrow keys for player movement Press W or Upper arrow keys twice to double jump
