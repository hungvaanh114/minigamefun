How to play free Moto X Trial Racing game online

To start playing Moto X Trial Racing online, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control your bike's acceleration and balance. Press the up arrow key to move forward and the down arrow key to brake. Lean left or right using the left and right arrow keys to maintain your balance as you tackle challenging hills and obstacles. Good luck!
