Prince and Princess : Kiss Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Prince and Princess: Kiss Quest," embark on a whimsical adventure that flips the traditional fairy tale narrative. Young girls who dream of their Prince Charming will find that true love isn't just about a handsome knight riding in to rescue the damsel in distress. Instead, this princess has her own ideals and is waiting for a suitor with grace and charm.

Your mission begins as the prince, who is in love with his princess, must prove his worth by scaling the towering castle and confronting various challenges. But this isn't just a straightforward quest. To win the heart of the captivating princess, you ll need to gather elegant gifts along your climb. Each present is a stepping stone to her affection, revealing that romance requires effort and creativity.

As you ascend, be wary of the obstacles in your path! Projectiles will hurl toward you, and every hit can lead to a loss of your prince s charm. Keep an eye on your health, as the damage sustained will directly impact your sex appeal, which is crucial to winning the princess's heart. Only the most dedicated and skilled players will conquer this quest and access the sweet kiss at the end of the journey.

How to play free Prince and Princess: Kiss Quest game online

To play "Prince and Princess: Kiss Quest," use the arrow keys to navigate your prince up the tower, collecting gifts while avoiding projectiles. Press the space bar to leap over obstacles and reach higher platforms. Keep track of your health bar and aim to impress the princess with your style and skill!
