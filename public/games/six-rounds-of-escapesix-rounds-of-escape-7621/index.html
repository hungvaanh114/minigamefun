Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of "Six Rounds of Escape," a heart-pounding battle royale that will test your wits and survival skills. In this high-stakes game, players must navigate a series of deadly challenges designed to push them to their limits. The fate of each participant hangs in the balance, as the intense atmosphere heightens the pressure to outsmart opponents and survive. Will you succumb to the dangers, or will you find a way to evade disaster?

The game kicks off with a series of six uniquely designed rounds, each presenting its own perilous test. From the infamous game of "321 Wooden Man," where every step could lead to elimination, to the tug-of-war challenge that requires not only sheer strength but also teamwork and strategy, every moment counts. Players must be quick on their feet and sharp in their thinking as they tackle obstacles that include stepping on glass without getting hurt. These challenges will determine who has the resilience and ingenuity to endure the trials ahead.

As the competition intensifies, alliances may form, but betrayal is always possible. With each round posing a new threat, players must carefully consider their choices, balancing risk with the necessity for survival. Who among you will rise to the occasion and face the ultimate test of endurance? Gather your friends or face the challenge solo, and brace yourself for an exhilarating gaming experience.

How to play free Six Rounds of Escape game online

To embark on your journey in "Six Rounds of Escape," simply enter the game lobby and choose your character. Navigate through each round by following the on-screen instructions. Complete the challenges to progress to the next round, and remember to work collaboratively or strategize against others to secure your survival!
