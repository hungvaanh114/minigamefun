How to play free Popsicle Stack game online

To play Popsicle Stack, use your mouse or finger to drag and stack different popsicles together. Aim for a perfectly balanced tower while picking your favorite flavors and toppings to make your creation stand out. The higher you stack, the more points you earn, so be steady and have fun!
