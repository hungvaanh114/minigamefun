Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting visual quest in Find All Differences, where your keen eye for detail is put to the ultimate test! In this engaging game, your objective is to identify the hidden discrepancies between two seemingly identical pictures before the timer expires. Each level presents you with beautifully crafted images that possess subtle variations just waiting to be discovered.

As you play, remember to examine every part of the images closely. Simply tap on the areas where you see differences. However, exercise caution! Incorrect taps will not only consume precious time but can also hinder your progress in the game. To assist you when the going gets tough, you have three hints at your disposal, allowing you to continue your journey if you happen to get stuck.

Your ultimate goal is to uncover all six differences within the allocated time to advance to the next level. With each new challenge, the images become progressively trickier, testing your concentration and observational skills. This game is not just entertaining but also a fantastic way to sharpen your attention to detail, making it suitable for players of all ages. So, gear up and dive into this captivating puzzle adventure!

How to play free Find All Differences game online

To start playing, simply select a level and concentrate on the two images presented. Use your mouse or finger to tap on the areas where you find differences. If you're having trouble, don t hesitate to use one of your hints. Remember, correct taps earn you points, while wrong taps will cost you time!
