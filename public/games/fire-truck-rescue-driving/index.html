Fire Truck Rescue Driving

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fire Truck Rescue Driving is an exciting driving game that allows you to become a firefighter.Driving the fire engine quickly to the scene of the fire for emergency rescue. The fire scenes and fast driving in the game make you feel a sense of urgency to put out the fire. You must drive the fire engine quickly and carefully to avoid hitting other vehicles.
