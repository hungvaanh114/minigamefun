Sweety Mania

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Sweety Mania, a highly addictive Match-3 game that promises endless fun! In this colorful and engaging puzzle adventure, your mission is to gather all the ingredients displayed at the top of your screen. Simply create lines of three or more matching ingredients to clear them from the board and fulfill your objectives. With vibrant visuals and charming animations, Sweety Mania ensures that players are constantly entertained as they strategize their moves.

However, the pressure is on! Each level is governed by a ticking 2-minute timer, and you must complete your tasks before time runs out. With every tick of the clock, the challenge intensifies, urging you to think quickly and act faster. Will you be able to race against the countdown and secure your title as the ultimate Sweety Mania champion?

As you progress through the levels, you ll encounter increasingly difficult puzzles and a variety of ingredients. Collect special power-ups to help you along the way, and don t forget to devise a clever strategy to maximize your moves. With its simple mechanics and challenging gameplay, Sweety Mania appeals to both casual gamers and hardcore puzzle enthusiasts alike, making it a perfect choice for anyone seeking a sweet, exhilarating escape!

How to play free Sweety Mania game online

To play Sweety Mania, simply swipe or tap to swap adjacent ingredients and create matches of three or more identical items. Clear the required ingredients within the 2-minute time limit, and use power-ups wisely to enhance your gameplay. Keep an eye on the clock and stay focused to conquer each level!
