Super Mario Run 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting realm of Super Mario Run 3D! This is a captivating adventure where darkness threatens the vibrant jungle, and only the legendary hero can save the day. If you aspire to embark on an exciting quest and harness the powerful abilities of Super Mario, you're in for a treat with this exhilarating stickman game brimming with challenges and endless fun.

With just a few skillful taps, you can navigate through the jungle, mastering the rope lines to leap over obstacles and execute amazing stunts. The key to exploration lies in your momentum; the more spectacularly you swing and glide, the deeper you can delve into the jungle's mysteries while rescuing its inhabitants. Remember, each corner holds surprises, and every move counts!

How to play:
- Swipe right or left to dodge incoming obstacles
- Scroll upward to perform a jump
- Swipe downward to roll under barriers
- Tap the screen twice to attack enemies
- Stay alert for foes charging towards you!

Enjoy the game in your leisure time, and let the adventure unfold with your favorite character guiding you through the thrilling challenges ahead. Ready, set, go it's time to run!

How to play free Super Mario Run 3D game online

To dive into the action, launch the game and use intuitive touchscreen controls. Swipe left or right to evade dangers, tap up to jump, and down to roll. Double-tap the screen to strike back at enemies. Stay focused, and aim for a high score while exploring the vivid world of Super Mario!
