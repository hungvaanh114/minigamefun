Free Racing Ayn

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of high-speed action in Free Racing Ayn, where you can perform incredible stunts across a massive open space with a selection of super sports cars. This game invites you to explore a vast free driving area where 115 gems are hidden for you to collect. Showcase your driving prowess as you navigate through this expansive terrain. Each gem you find contributes to your collection of diamonds, which can be used to purchase new super sport vehicles.

The game features a total of 15 super sport cars available in your garage, with 11 of them initially locked and waiting to be unlocked. Can you gather enough gems to buy every car and maximize your racing experience? Dive into the free zone and indulge in the exhilarating feeling of driving at breakneck speeds. Additionally, every three minutes, the prize table becomes available, allowing you to earn in-game currency that can be used for various enhancements and rewards. The open environment is filled with designated areas for stunting and drifting, giving players plenty of opportunities to show off their skills.

How to play Free Racing Ayn game online

To play Free Racing Ayn, drive around the free zone using the on-screen controls to navigate your super sport car. Look for gems scattered throughout the area and collect them to unlock new vehicles. When the prize table opens every three minutes, approach it to earn money that can help you upgrade your garage. Make sure to engage in stunts and drifting to enhance your driving experience!
