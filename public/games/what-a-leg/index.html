What a Leg

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating race in the whimsical world of What a Leg! In this exciting game, you ll meet a group of determined runners who are missing their legs. Your mission? To creatively draw legs for each runner and help them dash towards the finish line as swiftly as possible. This thrilling competition, set against the backdrop of different parkour courses, promises to challenge your quick thinking and artistic skills.

Each parkour is designed with unique obstacles that require different types of legs to successfully navigate. As you advance through the levels, you'll encounter jumps, ramps, and intriguing barriers, and you must adapt your leg designs to ensure your runners can conquer these challenges. The game emphasizes creativity and strategy, as well as the importance of speed. Will your drawing skills be enough to propel your runner ahead of the pack and cross the finish line first?

Experience the excitement solo in single-player mode, or challenge your friends head-on in two-player mode! Compete against online opponents to see who can draw the most effective legs the fastest. The playful graphics and engaging gameplay will keep you entertained for hours on end.

How to play free What a Leg game online

To play the game, simply choose your mode single player or two-player. As the race begins, use your drawing tool to sketch different legs based on the obstacles ahead. Watch the runner sprint and adjust your designs as needed. The faster and more effective your legs are, the quicker your runner will reach the finish line!


Instructions

Single player mode Use MOUSE and LEFT-CLICK to draw legs Two player mode After drawing legs at the beginning of the game use the keys below for switching between the legs Player 1 W A S D Player 2 ARROW KEYS
