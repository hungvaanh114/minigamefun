Zombie Parade Defense 5

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Zombie Parade Defense 5, where your survival skills are put to the ultimate test against relentless waves of zombies in a desert landscape! As a brave defender of your castle, you will face the dual threats of fierce desert storms and a horde of menacing zombies eager to breach your defenses. Prepare yourself for an exciting challenge as you utilize an array of new weapons and traps designed specifically to tackle the ever-growing strength and organization of the undead!

This installment introduces an automatic volley gun positioned at the castle's apex, designed to unleash chaos upon the zombie hordes. As you progress, you will have the chance to upgrade this weapon to inflict even greater damage. However, don t forget that a variety of other formidable weapons are at your disposal, allowing you to customize your defense strategy to fit your unique playstyle. New players should opt for the "Normal" difficulty mode to ease into the challenges that await.

Whether you prefer to play solo or gather your friends for a cooperative experience, your objective remains the same: fend off the zombies and protect the building at all costs. You must endure through a total of 12 grueling waves of undead foes to achieve victory and claim your triumph over the zombie menace!

How to play free Zombie Parade Defense 5 game online

To play Zombie Parade Defense 5, simply navigate to the game site and select your desired difficulty. Choose between single-player or multiplayer mode for a cooperative experience. As waves of zombies approach, strategically position your weapons and traps, and upgrade them to optimize your defensive capabilities. Survive the 12 waves to win!
