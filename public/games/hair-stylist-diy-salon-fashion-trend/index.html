Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Hair Stylist DIY Salon - Fashion & Trend" is an engaging and imaginative educational game designed to inspire creativity in young children. In this immersive experience, players will take on the exciting role of a beauty salon manager, catering exclusively to a youthful clientele eager for stylish transformations.

Within this virtual salon, a wide array of grooming tools awaits, ensuring that each young stylist has everything they need to create stunning hairstyles. Players can curl, wash, and comb hair, experimenting with endless possibilities to achieve the perfect look. From haircuts to vibrant color changes, the options are vast, allowing each player to unleash their inner stylist and craft unique hairstyles that turn heads.

The game encourages players to dress up their chosen models, mixing and matching styles to concoct the most eye-catching and trendy appearances. As budding hairstylists, kids will hone their skills while creating fashion-forward styles that even professional stylists would envy. With each finished hairstyle, players will watch as their model transforms into a dazzling star, ready to shine on the runway of a virtual fashion show.

How to play free Hair Stylist DIY Salon - Fashion & Trend game online

To dive into the fun, simply select your model and navigate through the various styling tools available in the salon. Use the curling iron, scissors, or hair dye to create the hairstyle of your dreams. Once satisfied, dress up your model and prepare them for their spotlight moment in the fashion show!
