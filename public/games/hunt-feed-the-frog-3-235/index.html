Hunt Feed the Frog 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Hunt Feed the Frog 3, where curiosity meets adventure. Join a charming little frog as he explores his vibrant surroundings filled with peculiar sights and delicious insects. This game invites players of all ages to assist the frog in its quest for food, ensuring that he remains well-fed and content.

As you guide our amphibious companion through various landscapes, you'll encounter a diverse array of insects. Each level presents unique challenges and engaging gameplay that will keep you on your toes. Whether it's hopping over obstacles or using your tongue to snag tasty treats, every moment in this game is filled with fun and excitement. The more you help the frog feast on these delectable critters, the happier and more grateful he becomes!

With colorful graphics and intuitive controls, Hunt Feed the Frog 3 offers an immersive experience that captivates players. Navigate through lush environments teeming with vibrant flora and fauna, and witness as your little frog grows stronger with each successful hunt. It s a delightful journey that fosters a sense of accomplishment as you master the art of amphibian hunting.

How to play free Hunt Feed the Frog 3 game online

To play Hunt Feed the Frog 3, use your mouse or touch controls to direct the frog. Click or tap to make the frog jump and to extend its tongue to catch insects. Keep an eye on your surroundings, as some insects may require precise timing. Happy hunting!


Instructions

To catch a tasty insect click the mouse pointer on it If the little frog reaches it with his tongue he will eat it If your device has a touch screen press the insects with your finger
