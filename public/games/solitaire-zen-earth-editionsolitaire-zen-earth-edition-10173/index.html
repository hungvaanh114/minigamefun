How to play free Solitaire: Zen Earth Edition game online

To play SOLITAIRE: ZEN EARTH EDITION online, simply drag and drop cards to build sequences in ascending order while matching suits. Use the stockpile to draw additional cards if needed. Aim to arrange all cards into foundation piles corresponding to each suit. Enjoy the calm as you play!
