Sugar Flow

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a sweet adventure in Sugar Flow, an engaging puzzle drawing game that challenges your creativity and strategic thinking. In this delightful game, your task is to draw lines that lead various colored sugars into their designated cups. It may sound simple, but with 22 levels of increasing difficulty, it will test your puzzle-solving skills! Each level introduces new challenges, requiring you to consider the placement and color coordination of the sugars as you guide them to their correct destinations.

The visuals are vibrant, with charming animations that create a captivating atmosphere. Each cup corresponds to a specific color of sugar, and misplacing them could lead to colorful chaos! With numerous creative ways to solve each puzzle, there s always room for experimentation. Pay close attention to avoid making mistakes while you work your way through the delightful conundrums presented by Sugar Flow.

As you progress, you will encounter a variety of obstacles and puzzles that will require keen thinking and planning to overcome. The game begins with simpler levels to help you grasp the mechanics, gradually escalating the complexity to keep you entertained and challenged. It's the perfect blend of fun and mental exercise, making it enjoyable for players of all ages.

How to play free Sugar Flow game online

To play Sugar Flow, start by selecting a level from the main menu. Use your mouse or finger to draw paths for the sugar to flow into the cups. Ensure that each color matches its corresponding cup. Click Play to activate the flow, and watch as your drawn lines guide the sugars to their targets. Enjoy the puzzle challenge!


Instructions

Draw lines using your mouse for the computer and for touch devices use your finger
