Magic School Story

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Magic School Story invites you into a fantastical world where you can fulfill your dreams of building and managing your very own magic school. Dive into an enchanting experience as you design and expand your campus, creating classrooms specially tailored for various magical disciplines. As the headmaster, it s your responsibility to attract a diverse group of students eager to learn spells and brew potions, guiding them through their magical education until they proudly graduate.

But the adventure doesn t stop at managing the school. Engage in thrilling quests where you'll fight fierce dragons, unearth mystical treasures while mining, and enjoy exciting match-3 puzzle games to collect valuable materials needed for upgrading your institution. These activities not only enhance the educational experience but also add layers of fun and excitement to the gameplay. Collaborate with friends to overcome challenges and assist each other in creating the ultimate magical academy.

With captivating visuals and immersive gameplay, Magic School Story allows players to unleash their creativity while fostering a vibrant and interactive community. Are you ready to embark on this magical journey and prove yourself as a legendary wizard school master? The realm of spells and adventure awaits!

How to play free Magic School Story game online

To play Magic School Story, start by creating your magical campus and managing resources to build classrooms and facilities. Enroll students, assign them to classes, and embark on quests. Participate in match-3 puzzles for materials, and team up with friends to share resources and boost your school s growth!
