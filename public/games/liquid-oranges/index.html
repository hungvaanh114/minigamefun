Liquid Oranges

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the vibrant world of Liquid Oranges, you have the opportunity to create your own refreshing orange juice right from the comfort of your screen! Get ready to squeeze the freshest oranges and quench your thirst as you master the art of juice preparation. The game challenges you to fill a glass at the bottom of the screen with delicious orange juice by cleverly interacting with the orange slices displayed above.

To play, you simply click on the orange slice with your mouse. Hold down the click to squeeze the juice directly into the empty glass below. However, be careful not to overflow the glass! If you pour too much, you'll need to restart the level and perfect your technique. The game is designed to test your hand-eye coordination and timing, making it an exciting and engaging experience for players of all ages. Enjoy the colorful graphics and satisfying sounds as you create the perfect batch of cold juice!

How to play free Liquid Oranges game online

To play Liquid Oranges, simply click on the orange slice you wish to squeeze. Maintain your click to pour juice into the glass below. Monitor the glass level closely to avoid spilling over; if it overflows, restart the level to try again. Have fun and unleash your juicing skills!
