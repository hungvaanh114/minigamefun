City Truck Driver

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an incredible adventure in the bustling metropolis with the thrilling City Truck Driver game! As you navigate through the expansive cityscape, you'll experience the ultimate truck simulation that offers an array of exciting features. Whether you're customizing your trucks, tackling mission challenges, racing against opponents, or just cruising through the city in free drive mode, there's always something to keep you engaged.

This game boasts 13 unique truck models, each with distinct handling, speed, and acceleration attributes. You can customize your vehicles to suit your style or even purchase new trucks through gameplay. Engage in mission mode, where you'll transport various goods from one location to another while ensuring they arrive safely. Keep an eye on the guiding arrows that lead you to your destination, and don t forget to reach the finish line before time runs out!

Alternatively, challenge yourself in race mode, where you will compete against five other drivers on city tracks. Finish among the top three to reap rewards that can be used for upgrades and enhancements. Both the race mode and free drive mode can be enjoyed in single-player or two-player formats, allowing for exciting head-to-head competition. Get ready to hit the streets and let your trucking adventure unfold in City Truck Driver!

How to play free City Truck Driver game online

To begin playing City Truck Driver, simply access the game on your preferred platform. Choose your truck model, and decide between mission or race modes. Utilize the arrow guides for missions, and in race mode, focus on speed to outpace your opponents. Customize your experience for optimal fun!
