Equal Alphabets

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Equal Alphabets is an engaging and interactive game designed to enhance children s picture recognition skills and familiarize them with the alphabet. This delightful game encourages young learners to match images of various objects whose names begin with the same letter. This approach not only makes learning enjoyable but also reinforces vocabulary.

In Equal Alphabets, players can click or touch an image and effortlessly drag it to its matching counterpart. This intuitive mechanic keeps the gameplay fast-paced and dynamic. The challenge intensifies as players strive to complete each level within a limited time of just 2 minutes. Achieving this feat rewards them with bonus points, adding an extra layer of excitement to the experience.

Players earn 500 points for each correct match they make, which serves as great motivation to stay focused. However, a wrong match incurs a 100-point penalty, teaching players the importance of careful decisions. With 12 unique levels to conquer, players will find themselves immersed in a diverse range of challenges that require quick thinking and sharp observation skills.

Are you ready to embark on this alphabet adventure? Complete all twelve levels to emerge victorious in Equal Alphabets, and enjoy the satisfaction of mastering picture recognition and the alphabet!

How to play free Equal Alphabets game online

To play Equal Alphabets, simply select an image and drag it to its matching picture that shares the same starting letter. Keep an eye on the timer, as you only have 2 minutes to finish each level. Aim for correct matches to score points and avoid penalties!
