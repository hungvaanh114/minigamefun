Flapy Fish

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Flapy Fish is an exciting and challenging game where you control a small fish that must navigate through dangerous underwater obstacles. Tap the screen to make the fish flap its fins and avoid hitting the barriers. The further you go, the higher your score! With increasing difficulty and addictive gameplay, Flapy Fish will test your reflexes and keep you entertained for hours!
