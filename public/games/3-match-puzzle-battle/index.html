3 Match Puzzle Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Match three puzzles to attack enemies and advance through the stages. There are four types of puzzles: recovery, attack, defense, and coins, so think about your strategy depending on your stamina and enemy strength to eliminate the puzzles. If a chain occurs, you can cause great damage to the enemy. Once you get coins, upgrade your player level and advance through the stages.

Instructions
