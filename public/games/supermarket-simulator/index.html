Supermarket Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Supermarket Simulator, where you step into the exciting role of a market owner! In this engaging simulation game, you'll take charge of running your very own grocery store. Your primary task is to manage the shelves effectively; ensure products are always stocked and available for your customers. As you navigate this challenge, beware of pesky thieves who might sneak in to pilfer items from your shelves. It's your job to keep a vigilant eye on them and prevent losses!

Cleanliness is a cornerstone of running a successful supermarket. You'll need to keep an eye on the floor as it can become dirty due to customer traffic. Equip yourself with a brush to tidy up and maintain a pleasant shopping environment for your patrons. Remember, a clean market not only looks inviting but can also lead to increased sales and customer satisfaction.

Low stock? No problem! Jump into your truck and head out to replenish your inventory. Timely restocking ensures that your customers find everything they need when they visit your market. The combination of managing theft, maintaining cleanliness, and refreshing the stock makes for an exhilarating gameplay experience in Supermarket Simulator.

How to play free Supermarket Simulator game online

To play Supermarket Simulator, simply navigate around the store using your mouse or keyboard controls. Use the shopping list to keep track of low stock items and press the designated key to clean the floor when it gets dirty. Keep an eye out for thieves and ensure you restock as needed by heading to your truck.
