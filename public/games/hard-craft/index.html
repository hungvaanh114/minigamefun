How to play free Hard Craft game online

To play Hard Craft, simply use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move your character and navigate through the environment. Press the spacebar to jump and use the Ctrl key for special parkour moves. Complete each level by reaching the finish line, overcoming obstacles, and enhancing your skills along the way!
