Angry Bird Jungle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant realm of Bird Island in Angry Birds Evolution, an impressive RPG that allows players to collect an array of new Angry Birds. Your mission is clear: to form an unbeatable squad of evolved super birds and reclaim Bird Island from the pesky pigs. With over 100 new characters joining fan favorites like Red, Bomb, Chuck, Matilda, and Terrence, this adventure has never been more thrilling!

Prepare to engage in fierce battles against other players in exciting PVP tournaments featuring the popular Bird Island pastime, Pigball. Dominate the leagues, showcase your skills, and earn incredible rewards while you rise through the ranks. Epic adventures await as you venture deeper into the game, unraveling mysteries such as the true identity behind the Bacon Corp and the enigma of the Eagle Force. Can you recover the lost dungeon keys? Find out through exhilarating battles!

Remarkably stunning graphics take Angry Birds Evolution to the next level, optimizing mobile technology to deliver console-quality visuals right to your device. Participate in weekly events to earn fantastic rewards and gain opportunities to add rare birds to your roster. Whether you choose to go solo or team up with friends, you can build a formidable clan that aspires to dominate Bird Island!

How to play free Angry Bird Jungle game online

To play Angry Bird Jungle, simply download the game and create or log into your account. Navigate through different missions, collect and evolve your birds, and compete in battles. Utilize strategies and team formations to outsmart your opponents and climb the leaderboards!
