Granny House Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the terrifying realm of Granny House Escape, a 3D horror adventure that will put your nerves to the ultimate test. Your objective is straightforward yet daunting: escape from a sinister house that is under the watchful eye of a menacing Granny. As you navigate through the eerie hallways, you are tasked with finding keys to unlock various rooms. Each door you open reveals further challenges and additional keys, amplifying the peril of your journey.

The game immerses you in a chilling ambiance with its striking visuals and heart-pounding sound effects that heighten the sense of dread. You'll need to outsmart the relentless Granny, who won't hesitate to hunt you down if she catches a glimpse of you. Use stealthy tactics to hide and remain one step ahead of her as you roam the haunted confines of the house.

Can you muster enough courage to locate all the keys and make your daring escape, or will you succumb to the dark fate that awaits? Download Granny House Escape today and confront your deepest fears as you attempt to break free from this nightmarish setting!

How to play free Granny House Escape game online

Once in the game, use the on-screen controls to move around the house, searching for keys hidden in various rooms. Be alert for Granny's movements; if you hear her approaching, hide to avoid detection. Collect keys and unlock doors to progressively reveal new challenges, all while trying to plan your escape.
