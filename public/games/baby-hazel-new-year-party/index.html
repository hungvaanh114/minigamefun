Baby Hazel New Year Party

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel and her mom as they prepare for a fabulous New Year s Eve party! As the clock approaches midnight, Baby Hazel is excited to host her friends for an unforgettable celebration. It's your chance to help her look stunning by choosing the perfect outfit and accessories. Be creative and make sure she shines as the perfect hostess at this fun gathering!

Once Baby Hazel is dressed up, lend a hand in setting the dinner table. From choosing colorful plates to arranging delightful decorations, every detail counts in making the party special. As her mom whips up delicious treats and snacks, you ll need to ensure that the dining experience is perfect for all of Baby Hazel s friends.

The little ones will have various requests, and it s your job to attend to their needs promptly! Keep an eye on the hint notifications to know what Baby Hazel and her friends require. The goal is to make sure everyone is happy and entertained throughout the party, ensuring that Baby Hazel's New Year celebration is a huge success. Can you manage the festivities and help Baby Hazel enjoy a memorable New Year's Eve with her friends?

How to play free Baby Hazel New Year Party game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse to click on the items needed for Baby Hazel and her friends. Follow the hints to understand what they need next and take action quickly to keep everyone satisfied. Enjoy the fun-filled party and help create lasting memories!
