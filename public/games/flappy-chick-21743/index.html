FLAPPY CHICK

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing FLAPPY CHICK, an exciting and whimsical adventure where you get to control an adorable, chubby chicken on its quest to soar across vibrant landscapes! Equipped with a jet pack, this charming little chick takes to the skies, but beware of the numerous obstacles lurking on its path. Your goal is simple yet challenging: flap your way past barrels, branches, and other impediments that stand in your way. The further you go, the higher your score, making every attempt a thrilling race against the odds!

As you navigate the air with finesse, the game tests your timing and coordination. With each flap of the wings, you can feel the delightful thrill of flying. The vibrant graphics and engaging sound effects help create an immersive experience, encouraging you to keep trying despite the occasional tumble. Unleash your competitive spirit by challenging friends and family to see who can achieve the highest score!

FLAPPY CHICK offers endless fun for all ages, making it a perfect game for casual gamers or those looking for a quick, entertaining distraction. The simple controls mean you can easily pick it up and start playing right away, while the progressively challenging gameplay keeps you coming back for more adventures in the sky.

How to play free FLAPPY CHICK game online

Once you enter the game, you will use either the Left Mouse Button or the Space Key to control your chicken. Each click or press will initiate a flap, lifting your chicken into the air. Maintain a steady rhythm to navigate through the obstacles, and aim for the highest distance possible!
