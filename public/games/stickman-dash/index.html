Stickman Dash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stickman Dash is an engaging and thrilling assassin game that invites players into a world of stealth, strategy, and action. You take on the role of a fearless ninja tasked with infiltrating the lair of notorious criminals. The objective is clear: eliminate the enemies before they spot you. Utilize your agility and reflexes to navigate the treacherous environment, bouncing off walls to evade attacks while you plot your next move.

Equipped with the sharpest sword, you'll face various obstacles and adversaries as you progress through the game. But don't worry; you have the opportunity to upgrade your weaponry, making it even more lethal against the criminal underworld. As you master each level, your skills will be put to the ultimate test. Can you outsmart and outmaneuver your foes while escaping their grasp?

Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled journey that will challenge your gameplay tactics and reflexes. With each successful mission, you are one step closer to reshaping the balance of power among criminals. So sharpen your blade, practice your jumps, and prepare for a wild ride in Stickman Dash!

How to play free Stickman Dash game online

To play Stickman Dash, use your keyboard or mouse to control your ninja's movements. Navigate through levels by jumping off walls to dodge enemy attacks. Click to strike your enemies with your sword and complete each stage by defeating all foes. Upgrade your weaponry for better efficiency and take down the criminals swiftly!
