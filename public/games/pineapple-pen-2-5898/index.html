PINEAPPLE PEN 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of PINEAPPLE PEN 2, where precision and strategy are the keys to success! In this exciting game, your main objective is to knock golden coins out of a variety of rotating fruits. Each fruit poses its own challenge, spinning at different speeds and angles, making your task of targeting the metal locks a test of skill and timing. As you aim and throw, you'll need to be precise; otherwise, you risk the chance of hitting your own pen, resulting in a loss!

The gameplay is engaging and dynamic, ensuring that players remain on their toes as they attempt to claim rewards by opening treasure chests. With each throw, the stakes get higher and the challenge becomes more intense. With colorful graphics and a lively soundtrack, PINEAPPLE PEN 2 offers a vibrant atmosphere that will keep you entertained for hours.

Prepare yourself for a whirlwind of fun and strategical thinking as you navigate through this vibrant fruit-themed adventure. Every round is a new chance to outsmart the game and showcase your skills. So gather your focus, aim well, and embark on a quest for treasure as you play!

How to play free PINEAPPLE PEN 2 game online

To play PINEAPPLE PEN 2, simply aim your throw using the on-screen controls. Calculate the perfect angle and power needed to hit the rotating fruits and unlock the treasure chests. Be careful not to hit your pen, as this will lead to a loss. Enjoy the challenge!
