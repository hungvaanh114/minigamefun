Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the eerie world of Undead Corps - Dead Village, where you take on the role of a skilled agent working for the intertemporal organization known as "Undead Corps." Your latest mission plunges you into a forsaken village overrun by the undead. The atmosphere is dense with dread, and every corner teems with danger as you seek to hunt down the notorious "Lord of Undead," a nefarious sorcerer guilty of practicing the dark art of Necromancy. This malevolent figure has stirred the spirits of the dead, leaving the villagers abandoned and their remnants trapped in a spectral nightmare.

In your quest, you will encounter twisted foes, each driven by the wicked magic of the Lord. As you navigate through the village, unravel secrets hidden in its haunted past, and uncover clues that will lead you to the dark heart of this magical criminal. The gripping graphics and chilling soundscapes immerse you in a narrative that combines action with strategic gameplay, challenging your skills as you fight to restore peace to the village.

Arm yourself with a variety of weapons and spells, each designed to combat the undead hordes. You ll need quick reflexes and sharp decision-making to survive. Engage with NPCs, gather intelligence, and unlock new abilities as you progress. The clock is ticking, and the undead lurk at every turn. Can you ultimately face the Lord of Undead and bring an end to his reign of terror?

How to play free Undead Corps - Dead Village game online

To play Undead Corps - Dead Village, use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate through the village. Interact with characters and objects using the spacebar or mouse. Fight enemies with your weapons while managing your spells strategically. Complete quests to gather vital clues and prepare for an epic confrontation with the Lord of Undead!
