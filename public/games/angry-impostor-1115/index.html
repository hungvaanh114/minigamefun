Instructions:

Step into the exciting world of Angry Impostor, where a fierce battle unfolds between a team of humanoids and cunning Impostors. Your mission is simple yet challenging: launch yourself from a slingshot with the objective of exposing the hidden Impostors sheltered behind imposing columns. The thrill lies in the strategy required to clear a path to your foes, as the structures they hide behind can be deceptively strong.

As you progress through Angry Impostor, you'll face a series of increasingly complex levels designed to test your skills. Each level presents unique obstacles that will demand both precision and insight. Your aim will be to dismantle the structures surrounding the Impostors, carefully calculating your shots to ensure they land in a way that makes those sneaky characters vulnerable. With every successful level, you'll find yourself immersed deeper in this captivating battle of wits and timing.

Good luck, and remember to keep your spirits high while navigating the challenges that await you. The key to victory is not just in your aim, but in your ability to think strategically about how to use the environment to your advantage. For more thrilling gameplay, visit the developer s official website at ulyagames.com.

How to play free Angry Impostor game online

To play Angry Impostor, start by aiming the slingshot at the structures that conceal the Impostors. Adjust your angle and power to launch your character effectively. Your goal is to create a clear path by breaking the surrounding structures. Use your shots wisely to expose the Impostors and conquer each level.
