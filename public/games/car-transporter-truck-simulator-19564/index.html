Car Transporter Truck Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the ultimate car transporter truck simulator, the premier vehicle transportation game of 2019! In this thrilling simulation, you will experience the excitement of driving a heavy-duty trailer loaded with elite luxury cars. Put your driving skills to the test as you navigate through bustling cities while delivering modern vehicles from one location to another.

As an expert car transporter, you will start your journey by maneuvering a cargo trailer truck. Your adventure begins at a multi-story car parking facility where an array of luxurious cars awaits. Carefully load these stunning vehicles onto your heavy vehicle transporter truck, ensuring they are secure for the journey ahead. Once loaded, you will steer the truck from city streets to challenging off-road terrain, where skillful driving is essential.

This car transporter truck simulator goes beyond simple transportation tasks; it challenges your expertise in vehicle parking as well. After reaching your destination, you will unload the luxury cars, giving you the chance to experience the sheer joy of driving them around. Test your driving proficiency and enjoy every minute of this immersive vehicle transporting experience.

How to play free Car Transporter Truck Simulator game online

To play the Car Transporter Truck Simulator, simply start the game and follow the on-screen instructions. Use the arrow keys or joystick to navigate the truck, and engage the loading mechanism at the parking zone. Drive carefully to transport the cars safely, and follow the route displayed to complete your deliveries. Enjoy the ride!
