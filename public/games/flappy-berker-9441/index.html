Flappy Berker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Flappy Berker, where precision and skill are your closest allies! In this captivating arcade-style game, your primary objective is to collect as many bananas as possible. Each 15 bananas you gather will reward you with a speed level up, increasing the challenge and excitement as you progress. The ultimate goal? To reach the coveted level 10! As you flap your way through the increasingly difficult obstacles, your ability to navigate with finesse will determine your success.

Flappy Berker offers a vibrant, engaging environment filled with dynamic obstacles that will keep you on your toes. The delightful graphics and smooth gameplay create an immersive experience that is both enjoyable and challenging. As you advance, be prepared to face faster-paced gameplay that will test your reflexes and coordination. Will you be able to master the art of flapping and conquer the leaderboard? Dive into the action and see how far you can go!

With every banana collected, you ll not only earn speed boosts but also the satisfaction of outsmarting the increasingly tricky challenges ahead. Keep your eyes on the prize, and remember that timing is everything. So grab your virtual bird and prepare for the ultimate flap-off experience in Flappy Berker!

How to play free Flappy Berker game online

To play Flappy Berker, simply use your mouse or tap the screen to make your character flap and navigate through the obstacles. Aim to collect bananas to level up your speed every 15 bananas, and try to reach level 10. Avoid touching the obstacles to survive and progress!
