Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting adventure in Meera Quest, a vibrant 2D anime-themed platformer that showcases the charming journey of Meera, a delightful anime girl. Players must navigate through a perilous Monster world filled with perilous obstacles such as treacherous lava, ferocious fire, and dangerous spikes while collecting essential keys. Each key you gather brings you one step closer to escaping this menacing realm and unlocking the Exit door that leads you to the next thrilling level.

With a total of 8 captivating levels, Meera Quest promises a progressive challenge, ensuring that the gameplay becomes increasingly difficult as you venture deeper into the game. The sweet visuals and entertaining soundtrack create an enchanting atmosphere, pulling you into Meera's captivating journey as you strive to outwit the monsters lurking around every corner.

As you explore, players will need to master Meera's jumping and dodging skills to maneuver past obstacles and avoid encounters with menacing creatures. The game caters to both casual players and seasoned gamers, providing a delightful experience for all who wish to join Meera on her quest to escape the Monster world.

How to play free Meera Quest game online

To play Meera Quest, use the arrow keys to move Meera left and right. Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and dodge monsters. Collect keys as you maneuver through the levels, and navigate to the Exit door to complete each stage. Good luck escaping!
