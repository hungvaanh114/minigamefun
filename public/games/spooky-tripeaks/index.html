Spooky Tripeaks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a haunted world with Spooky Tripeaks, a thrilling Halloween-themed variation of the classic Solitaire game. In this eerie yet captivating adventure, you will navigate through 100 challenging levels, each filled with ghostly motifs and challenging card arrangements. Your mission is to clear the board by strategically pairing cards that either match the value or are one higher or lower than the card shown on the tableau.

To aid you in your spooky mission, you'll have access to several bonus cards and jokers. These powerful tools can simplify your gameplay and help you overcome even the trickiest of levels. But how do you earn these delightful bonuses? It's simple! Just click on the ghosts and other haunting characters that appear throughout the game to gain these special cards that can turn the tide in your favor.

As you progress, keep an eye out for tricky setups and plan your moves carefully. With enchanting graphics and a mysterious soundtrack that immerses you in the Halloween spirit, Spooky Tripeaks offers not just a game, but a delightful Halloween experience. Get ready to face increasingly difficult challenges as you work to clear all the levels and achieve a perfect score!

How to play free Spooky Tripeaks game online

To play Spooky Tripeaks, start by familiarizing yourself with the basic Solitaire mechanics. Click on the cards to reveal lower or higher matches, and use your jokers and bonus cards wisely. Aim to clear all cards from the tableau to advance through the spooky levels. Have fun and enjoy the Halloween-themed surprises!
