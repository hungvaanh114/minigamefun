Type Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to accelerate your typing skills in the exhilarating world of Type Run! In this thrilling race, your hand speed is your greatest asset. As you face a fierce opponent, the pressure is on to type out the sentences provided with lightning speed. Each keystroke can mean the difference between victory and defeat, as your villain will sprint ahead based on your typing speed. The challenge intensifies with every level, making your opponent progressively stronger!

Every second counts in Type Run! To stay ahead, hone your reflexes and practice your typing accuracy. With each stage bringing new sentences to conquer, you ll need to remain focused and agile. How far can you push yourself? Test your limits and see how many levels you can withstand against a relentless foe. Become the typing champion while racing against the clock and ensure you don t let your adversary gain ground!

How to play free Type Run game online

To play Type Run, simply launch the game and follow the on-screen instructions. You'll be presented with sentences to type. Input the text as quickly and accurately as possible to make your character run faster. Keep your focus, avoid typos, and strive to outpace your opponent!
