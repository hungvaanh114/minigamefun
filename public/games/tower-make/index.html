Tower Make

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Tower Make, a challenging game that tests your skill and precision! In this engaging experience, you will start with 3 health points (hp), and every time you drop a Tower block, you lose 1 hp. The objective is to stack as many blocks as possible without losing all your health, as the game concludes when your hp reaches zero. The stakes are high, and each decision counts!

For every successful Tower block you manage to stack, you earn 25 points. However, if you achieve a perfect placement on the previous block, your reward increases to 50 points! The excitement doesn t stop there; if you continue making consecutive perfect placements, you'll earn an additional 25 points for each subsequent perfect block. This mechanic encourages precise timing and strategic thinking as you strive to build the tallest tower possible while maximizing your score.

Tower Make offers a unique blend of skill-based gameplay and competitive scoring that keeps players coming back for more. Challenge yourself to beat your high score and improve your stacking abilities. With simple mechanics and addictive gameplay, this game is perfect for players of all skill levels, making it an ideal way to pass the time while pushing your limits!

How to play free Tower Make game online

To play Tower Make, simply drop the blocks onto one another by clicking or tapping the screen. Aim for precise placements to rack up points. Keep an eye on your health points (hp), and try to achieve perfect stacks for higher rewards. The goal is to build as high as possible before running out of hp!
