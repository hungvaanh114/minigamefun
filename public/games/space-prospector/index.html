Space Prospector

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Space Prospector, a captivating game that invites you to soar through the cosmos on a futuristic jet device. Your mission? To search for and collect valuable gems and glittering gold coins scattered throughout the space landscape. Each gem you transport back to your base rewards you with a certain number of points, which vary based on the gem's color, adding a strategic twist to your collection efforts.

In addition to gems, collecting gold coins is critical. When you successfully deliver a gold coin to your base, all points you've previously accumulated are instantly doubled, allowing you to rack up an impressive score. These points aren't just for bragging rights; they can be utilized to refuel your ship and implement repairs, ensuring you can continue your quest for wealth among the stars.

Every level presents new challenges and opportunities to boost your score, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging. As you progress, you'll find yourself enchanted by the vibrant graphics and the thrilling sense of exploration that Space Prospector has to offer. Will you become the ultimate space treasure hunter? The galaxy is waiting for you!

How to play free Space Prospector game online

To play Space Prospector, use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D to navigate your jet device through the space environment. Collect gems by flying through them and guide them back to your base to earn points. Don't forget to grab gold coins to double your points before refueling and repairing your ship!
