Spa Salon Makeup Artist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to unleash your creativity in the exciting world of the Spa Salon Makeup Artist game? If you're young and passionate about beauty, this game will provide you with the ultimate platform to explore your skills. Whether you re looking for a trendy hairstyle or a complete makeover, this game has everything you need to make your imagination come to life.

Step into the role of a talented stylist in this engaging hair salon experience. Beautiful girls are in need of your expert touch. Begin your journey by selecting your model and start pampering her with a relaxing facial spa treatment. Cleanse her face, shape her eyebrows, and tackle those pesky blemishes. With a soothing environment, your model will feel refreshed and rejuvenated!

Next, dive into the world of hair transformations. Use real hair spa tools to create a nourishing DIY hair mask. Makeover your models by trimming, dyeing, and styling their hair to perfection. Don't forget to add that final touch with fabulous fashion accessories! With six different services available, you ll have endless opportunities to express your unique style and create stunning looks that will shine on the runway.

How to play free Spa Salon Makeup Artist game online

To play the Spa Salon Makeup Artist game, start by selecting your favorite model. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete each beauty treatment step. Use the touch controls to wash, trim, and style hair, as well as apply makeup and accessories. Enjoy the creative freedom to design the perfect look for your model!
