Extra Hot Chili 3D?Extra Hot Chili 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the fiery world of Extra Hot Chili 3D, where the heat is on and only the bold survive! This dynamic and vibrant game challenges players to absorb an array of hot peppers rolling down conveyor belts. Your primary focus is to collect these spicy delights, enhancing your score while avoiding other distractions along the way. As you navigate through this sizzling adventure, be mindful: consuming anything that isn't a hot pepper will trigger a setback, causing your progress bar to dwindle and potentially leading to failure.

The thrill of Extra Hot Chili 3D is not just in speed and reflexes but also in strategy. Players must swiftly maneuver through a maze of obstacles, making calculated decisions on which peppers to grab and when to dodge. With each successful collection, the challenge intensifies, as the conveyor speeds up and the peppers become more plentiful. The vibrant 3D graphics and immersive sound effects make every moment in the game feel alive, ensuring that players are completely engrossed in their pursuit of the ultimate chili pepper experience.

How far can you go in this exhilarating journey? Can you master your cravings and maintain your focus solely on the peppers? Test your skills and see if you can beat your own record in this heated race against time!

How to play free Extra Hot Chili 3D game online

To play Extra Hot Chili 3D, simply use your mouse or finger to guide your character along the conveyor. Collect the hot peppers as they pass by while avoiding all other foods. Keep an eye on your progress bar; make sure it doesn t drop by eating anything that isn t a pepper!
