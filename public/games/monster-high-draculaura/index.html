Monster High Draculaura

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Meet Draculaura, a fabulous vampire and the beloved daughter of Dracula, although she was adopted. As a student at the famed Monster High, she embodies a perfect blend of charm and spookiness. Known for her iconic style, Draculaura is always on the lookout for the latest trends and fabulous outfits, seamlessly merging her gothic roots with contemporary fashion. With a penchant for makeup and a flair for fashion, she expresses her unique personality through her clothing choices, making every day at school an exciting adventure in style.

In this game, you have the unique opportunity to join Draculaura in her quest for a stunning new look. Help her select the perfect makeup that highlights her vampiric beauty while ensuring she remains the trendsetter at Monster High. With a variety of cosmetics, you can mix and match colors to create an eye-catching appearance that fits her personality. But that s not all! You ll also dive into her wardrobe, full of spooky yet stylish outfits, and choose the ideal ensemble that showcases her unique blend of elegance and edge.

Are you ready to unleash your inner stylist? Let's help Draculaura experiment with different hairstyles, clothing designs, and accessories to create the ultimate Monster High look. Dress her up in outfits that make a statement, whether it s cute, fierce, or fabulously frightful. The possibilities are endless as you transform her into the star of the school!

How to play free Monster High Draculaura game online

Once you enter the game, use your mouse to click on various makeup items and clothing options. Mix and match her cosmetics and outfits to create a chic new look. Experiment with hairstyles and accessories to ensure that Draculaura shines at Monster High!
