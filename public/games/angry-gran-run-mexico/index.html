Angry Gran Run: Mexico

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Angry Gran Run: Mexico, join the spirited Granny as she embarks on a wild escape from the confines of the Durkee asylum in the vibrant streets of Mexico. With her wits as sharp as ever, this cheeky grandmother races through bustling cityscapes, determined to avoid capture by relentless orderlies who want to drag her back for more 'therapy'.

The thrill of the chase becomes even more exhilarating as Granny navigates a series of tricky paths filled with unexpected challenges. She'll encounter sharp turns, gaping holes, and fences that would make anyone think twice. But Granny is not one to back down! With each leap and dodge, she pushes the limits of her agility while trying to gather sparkling gold coins along the way. These coins not only enhance the excitement of her escape but also unlock special upgrades and new characters to enrich the gaming experience.

As you run for freedom, the vibrant graphics and playful animations bring the lively streets of Mexico to life. Will Granny outsmart her pursuers and make her way to safety? The answer lies in your hands as you help her master the art of evasive maneuvers and strategy!

How to play free Angry Gran Run: Mexico game online

To play Angry Gran Run: Mexico, simply use your arrow keys or swipe the screen to navigate Granny through the city. Jump over obstacles by tapping the jump button and collect as many coins as you can along the way. Keep an eye out for power-ups, but be careful of the orderlies chasing you!
