Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the glamorous world of the Fashion Super Idol Project, where the quest to become the next style sensation unfolds! Today marks a monumental day as our fashionista emerges as a celebrated idol, captivating audiences far and wide. You are invited to join her journey, guiding her to flawless fashion choices that will make her the talk of the town.

In this exciting game, your role is crucial! Help our vibrant protagonist to curate fashionable daily outfits that reflect her personality and set her on the path to stardom. Whether it s a casual look for a day out or a stunning ensemble for an electrifying performance, every outfit matters. Use your creativity to mix and match chic garments, stylish accessories, and trendy footwear to ensure she always dazzles her fans.

But that's not all! Fashion idols live in the spotlight, and they must leave a lasting impression on stage as well. Design spectacular stage outfits that will enhance her performances and showcase her unique flair. Remember, in the world of fashion, confidence is key. Each choice you make will affect her popularity, so make sure she s always looking her best!

Can you rise to the challenge and help her maintain her image as a top fashion idol? Prepare for an immersive experience filled with creativity and style as you build your very own fashion legacy in Fashion Super Idol Project.

How to play free Fashion Super Idol Project game online

Once you enter the game, you'll find an easy-to-navigate interface. Select outfits for your fashion girl from an extensive wardrobe by clicking on different clothing items, accessories, and shoes. Experiment with combinations to achieve the perfect look for both day and stage performances.
