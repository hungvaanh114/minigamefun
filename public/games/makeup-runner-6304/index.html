Makeup Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Makeup Runner, the ultimate beauty adventure where you can unleash your creativity and style! Dive into a world filled with dazzling colors and trendy cosmetics as you embark on an exciting journey to give your friends the most fabulous makeovers imaginable. With our fabulous Makeup Run Kit, there's no limit to the enchanting transformations you can create.

Gather your favorite makeup brushes and get ready to experiment with vibrant palettes, dazzling eyeshadows, and luscious lipsticks. Choose from an array of beautiful princess characters, each looking to shine with a stunning makeup look. Whether your style is classic elegance or modern glam, you ll have a plethora of options to make your characters sparkle. The joy of applying makeup and admiring the delightful results will captivate both novice and seasoned makeup artists alike!

As you dash through various levels, collect beauty items and complete fun challenges that will test your skills and creativity. Dress your princess in fashionable outfits and select the perfect hairstyles to match her stunning makeup. The more characters you transform, the more levels you unlock, revealing exciting tools and styles that elevate your game to the next level. So grab your palette and join the fun it's time to become the ultimate makeup artist in Makeup Runner!

How to play free Makeup Runner game online

To play Makeup Runner, simply control your character using arrow keys or swipe gestures. Collect makeup items scattered throughout the course while dodging obstacles. Once you reach the salon, choose the makeup products and tools, then apply them to create eye-catching looks for your princess!
