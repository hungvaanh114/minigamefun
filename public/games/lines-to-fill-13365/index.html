Lines to Fill

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Lines to Fill is an engaging puzzle game that invites players to think creatively while drawing lines to complete a predefined shape. The objective is straightforward: using your finger or the left mouse button, you can drag colorful blocks to fill the outlines, ensuring that no empty spaces are left behind. The game is designed to be a leisurely experience, free from time constraints and penalties, allowing players to immerse themselves in its vibrant and satisfying gameplay.

With hundreds of unique levels packed into Lines to Fill, players will find a variety of challenges that will keep them entertained for hours. Each level introduces new shapes and designs, encouraging critical thinking and strategic planning as you figure out the best way to complete each puzzle. Whether you are playing on a computer or a mobile device, the smooth interface and intuitive controls make it easy to pick up and play anytime, anywhere. So, can you dedicate some time to conquer all the levels and fully complete the game?

How to play free Lines to Fill game online

To play Lines to Fill, simply select a level and start dragging the colorful blocks into place. Make sure to cover the entire shape without leaving any gaps. Use your finger on touch devices or your mouse on a computer to adjust the position of the blocks. Continue until the shape is completely filled!


Instructions

Fill shapes - Drag one of the colorful blocks using your finger or the left mouse button
