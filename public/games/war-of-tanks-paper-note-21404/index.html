War of Tanks : Paper Note

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of strategy and competition in War Of Tanks: Paper Note, an engaging game that allows you to face off against friends or challenge yourself in solo mode. With options for "1 PLAYER" and "2 PLAYER," this game promises hours of fun and excitement.

In the "1 PLAYER" mode, you will dive into three distinct levels that appear at random. Your mission is to destroy five targets in each level before you can progress. Each time you achieve a target destruction, lasers will activate, allowing you to move on to the next target. However, beware! If you make contact with the lasers without having destroyed the target, your tank will explode, forcing you to start the level again. A helpful map shows your journey's destinations and endpoints to keep you on track.

The "2 PLAYER" mode amps up the competition with a total of 20 levels. You and a friend can choose how many games to play and you will face random levels along the way. The excitement is further enhanced by the bonuses scattered throughout the levels, which can provide strategic advantages in your tank battles. Get ready to unleash your competitive spirit!

How to play free War Of Tanks: Paper Note game online

To play War Of Tanks: Paper Note, select either the "1 PLAYER" or "2 PLAYER" mode. Use the controls to navigate your tank and aim for the targets to destroy them. Be cautious of the lasers that activate after each target is hit; make sure to avoid them. Collect bonuses and strategize your moves for optimal gameplay!
