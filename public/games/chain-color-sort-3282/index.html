Chain Color Sort

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to challenge your sorting and coordination abilities in the exciting game, Chain Color Sort! Your mission is to organize a variety of colorful chains, and you ll need to rely on your physics skills to achieve this. As you encounter different colored chains, your task will be to manipulate them effectively, pulling, pushing, and spinning each one until they re arranged correctly by color.

As you dive deeper into the game, you ll discover that the levels become increasingly complex, presenting you with a greater number of chains and colors to sort. Each level is designed to test your skills, making strategic movement and timing essential. With consistent practice, you will soon become a pro, mastering the various levels and achieving high scores. So don t hesitate; embark on your journey of sorting color chains today!

How to play free chain color sort game online

To play Chain Color Sort, start by selecting a chain to manipulate. Use your mouse or touch controls to pull, push, or spin the chains as needed. Align the colors by carefully adjusting their positions, aiming for the correct order. Complete each level before time runs out for bonus points!
