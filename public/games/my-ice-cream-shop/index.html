My Ice Cream Shop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to My Ice Cream Shop, a delightful game that takes you on a scrumptious journey around the globe, where everyone adores a delicious scoop of ice cream! Kick off your cool culinary adventure in Europe and get ready to travel the world in your trusty ice cream truck. With the clock ticking away, you will need to whip up the most delectable ice cream cones at lightning speed, ensuring your customers leave with a smile on their faces.

In My Ice Cream Shop, a variety of flavors and unique ingredients come together to create an endless array of icy treats. Each level challenges you to create customized cones that satisfy the desires of your diverse clientele. The more flavors you master, the more popular your shop becomes. As you progress, you will be able to expand your business, attract more customers, and unlock exciting new recipes. The art of ice cream making has never been so exhilarating!

Prepare to be captivated by the vivid graphics, enchanting melodies, and engaging gameplay that will keep you glued to your screen. With its charming atmosphere and easy-to-learn mechanics, My Ice Cream Shop is perfect for players of all ages. So, gather your ingredients, turn on your ice cream machine, and set off on this delightful culinary quest!

How to play free My Ice Cream Shop game online

To play My Ice Cream Shop, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to select flavors and ingredients for each order. Follow the customer's requests to create the perfect cone. Keep an eye on the timer and work efficiently to serve as many customers as possible before time runs out!
