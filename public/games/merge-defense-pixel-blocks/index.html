Merge Defense: Pixel Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Merge Defense: Pixel Blocks is an engaging puzzle game designed for boys, offering hours of free fun. Playable online, this game challenges you to merge blocks effectively to create more powerful units. Each block features a unique number, and when you combine two blocks of the same value, they evolve into a larger block that possesses greater power.

The core objective is to strategically place these blocks in designated fort grids to fend off waves of invaders. As you progress, the numbers on your blocks will increase, resulting in higher damage output against the enemy forces. This combination of merging and defense tactics makes the game not only exciting but also compelling as you seek to outsmart your opponents. So, gather your skills and prepare to merge your way to victory!

Don t forget each block you merge increases not just its number but your potential defense capabilities as well. Whether you re a seasoned gamer or just looking for a fun way to pass the time, Merge Defense: Pixel Blocks is sure to entertain and challenge you. So dive in and start merging to enhance your fortifications!

How to play free Merge Defense: Pixel Blocks game online

To play Merge Defense: Pixel Blocks, begin by dragging two blocks of the same number into each other to merge them into a stronger block. Continue merging to build your arsenal and place the newly created blocks into the fort grid for attacking. Keep an eye on enemy waves and strategically choose where to position your strongest blocks for maximum damage!
