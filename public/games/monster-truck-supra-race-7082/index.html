Get ready to dominate your rivals in thrilling head-to-head MONSTER TRUCK races featuring fire-breathing beasts! The much-anticipated launch of the new monster truck racing simulator has finally arrived. Step into the driver's seat of these colossal vehicles, let the flames roar, and speed through adrenaline-pumping racecourses across the stunning American landscape, home to rolling hills and deep valleys. Ever wondered what it's like to drive a monster truck? Now's your chance to showcase your driving prowess by selecting the vehicle that best meets your racing ambitions!

Choose from an exciting lineup of game modes including Boss Battle Races, Ranking Races, Limited Races, Extreme Races, Single Contest, and Daily Race. Compete against players from around the globe in intense online competitions as you strive to climb the leaderboards. Can you claim the title of the most spectacular monster truck driver? Your primary objective is to rack up victories and solidify your reputation as the ultimate racing champion. With fierce competitions set against the dramatic rural backdrop of the United States, the encounters between these fire-breathing giants is nothing short of exhilarating.

Immerse yourself in an exquisitely crafted world filled with scenic hills, picturesque valleys, shimmering lakes, and winding railways. While you revel in the enchanting scenery, don't lose sight of the road, your opponents, and the next ramp that could launch you to victory! The race is about to start, so accelerate, engage in drag racing, tackle the terrain, and conquer every uphill challenge you face. Whether you're a fan of truck racing or radio-controlled car rallies, this game will quickly become your favorite. Balance your quest for the fastest monster truck against your desire for the most striking design. Get ready to customize and enhance your flame-spewing vehicle as you prepare for the ultimate racing challenge!

How to play free Monster Truck Supra Race game online

To play Monster Truck Supra Race, simply select your favorite vehicle from the garage. Once the race begins, use the arrow keys or your controller to accelerate, steer, and navigate through the terrain. Aim for ramps to boost your jump distance and don't forget to keep an eye on your rivals as you strive for that top position!
