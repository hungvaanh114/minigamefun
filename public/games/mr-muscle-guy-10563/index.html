Mr Muscle Guy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Iron Muscle makes a triumphant comeback with its latest installment, Mr Muscle Guy! Dive into a vibrant, cartoon-styled world where you can sculpt your dream physique. The game allows players to create their own muscle-bound characters from scratch. At the start, you ll choose your skin tone, eye shape, hair style, and hair color, giving you the freedom to design a builder that reflects your personal style.

Once you ve customized your character, it's time to hit the gym and embark on your journey to becoming the most muscular person on the beach! Mr Muscle Guy features a new generation gym with a variety of bodybuilding and fitness routines that you can tailor to your liking. Engage in weightlifting and fitness exercises, each designed to help you grow and tone different muscle groups. The mix of nine diverse fitness programs ensures that players will always find something new and exciting to challenge themselves with.

The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay make each training session feel exhilarating, keeping players motivated to reach their ideal muscle mass. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the Iron Muscle series, Mr Muscle Guy promises hours of fun and fitness challenges as you work hard to build your perfect physique.

How to play free Mr Muscle Guy game online

To play Mr Muscle Guy, navigate through the prompts to customize your character. Once in the gym, select an exercise program and follow the on-screen instructions to complete workouts. Engage in multiple routines to build muscle, increase strength, and unlock new skills!
