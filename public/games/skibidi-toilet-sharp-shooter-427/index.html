Skibidi Toilet Sharp Shooter

Description:

"Skibidi Toilet Sharp Shooter" is a high-stakes, precision-focused shooter designed to rigorously evaluate strategic execution and tactical accuracy. In this challenging title, the player commands the notable Skibidi Toilet entity, engaging in the necessary neutralization of successive stickman adversarial waves. Distinguishing itself from conventional shoot-'em-up offerings, each stage is engineered to optimize user decision-making under resource constraints, specifically a limited ammunition supply required to clear all targets.
