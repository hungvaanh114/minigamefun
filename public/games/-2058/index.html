Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Santa Blast is an exhilarating and festive adventure that puts you in the role of Santa Claus, who is trapped in a perilous and shadowy realm. The festive jolly old fellow needs your assistance to navigate through a series of challenges and ultimately reach the safe zone. Your primary objective is to guide Santa to safety using a limited number of explosive charges. Each level presents a unique puzzle where strategic placement of explosives will launch Santa in the right direction but beware of hidden dangers like sharp spikes that could spoil the fun.

Each level is cleverly designed to test your problem-solving skills and timing. As you progress, the challenges get tougher, demanding more creative thinking and precision to achieve that coveted three-star rating. Can you master the art of launching Santa with just the right amount of force to avoid obstacles and land him safely? Competing against the clock adds an extra layer of excitement, making every attempt a thrilling experience!

Santa Blast combines intuitive gameplay with colorful graphics and an engaging storyline. It promises hours of entertainment for players of all ages. As you dive deeper into the game, you ll discover various environments and obstacles, keeping the gameplay fresh and challenging. With a merry soundtrack and charming visuals, this game is fully equipped to put you in the holiday spirit while providing engaging fun.

How to play free Santa Blast game online

To play Santa Blast, simply click on the explosives to set them in the desired location and ensure Santa is launched safely toward the landing area. Be strategic to avoid spikes and reach the target in the fewest possible attempts. Collect stars by completing levels with precision!
