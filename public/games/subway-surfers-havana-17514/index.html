Subway Surfers Havana

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Subway Surfers World Tour: Havana marks the fifty-sixth installment in the popular World Tour series and the sixty-first update featuring Subway Surfers. In this vibrant edition, players are invited to explore the colorful streets of Havana, Cuba, where the lively culture and rich history come alive in a thrilling endless runner experience.

As you dash through the sun-soaked streets, you ll encounter iconic landmarks, vibrant murals, and the rhythm of Havana, all while avoiding oncoming trains and obstacles. The engaging visuals are complemented by Cuban-inspired music, adding an electrifying backdrop to your adrenaline-pumping adventure. Players can unlock new characters such as the charming graffiti artist and collect special rewards like limited-time hoverboards, enhancing the gameplay and providing a fresh challenge with each run.

Join forces with friends or compete against players worldwide in this exciting journey through one of the Caribbean's most enchanting cities. Dive into unique challenges and quests while collecting coins and power-ups to boost your speed and agility. Subway Surfers World Tour: Havana is not just about outrunning the inspector and his dog, but also absorbing the captivating sights and sounds that make Havana a must-visit destination.

How to play free Subway Surfers Havana game online

To play Subway Surfers Havana online, simply start the game and swipe left, right, up, or down to navigate your character. Collect coins and power-ups while avoiding trains and obstacles, and try to complete daily challenges for bonus rewards. Keep running and have fun exploring the vibrant world of Havana!
