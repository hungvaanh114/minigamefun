Batman Ghost Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the haunting world of Gotham City, a dark and eerie presence has emerged as ghosts have begun to overrun the once-thriving metropolis. As panic spreads through the streets, it is up to the Dark Knight himself, Batman, to confront these spectral foes and restore peace to the city. With his unparalleled skills and trusty gadgets, Batman embarks on a thrilling adventure to defeat the restless spirits and ensure the safety of his beloved Gotham.

Players will join Batman on this exhilarating mission, where strategy and precision are key. Utilizing the iconic batarang, players must target and throw it at the ghosts that haunt the different corners of Gotham. It's not just about defeating the ghosts; it s also about mastering the art of timing and accuracy. Each successful hit brings Batman one step closer to reclaiming the city and sends the ghosts retreating into the shadows.

Additionally, the Batmobile plays a crucial role in this adrenaline-pumping escapade. Players can hop into this legendary vehicle to navigate through the city with speed and agility. Players will face various challenges and confront powerful ghost bosses, all while exploring the richly detailed world of Gotham. Can you assist Batman in making Gotham a safe place once again? The city's fate rests in your hands.

How to play free Batman Ghost Hunter game online

To play the Batman Ghost Hunter game online, simply use the arrow keys to navigate Batman through Gotham. Aim your batarang with your mouse or touch controls, and throw it by clicking or tapping the screen. Keep an eye out for power-ups and avoid obstacles to achieve the highest score!
