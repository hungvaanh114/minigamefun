The New Year has just begun, but a frightening green monster has emerged to steal the joy of the season! Meet the Grench, a sneaky creature intent on making off with the precious gift boxes meant for celebration. As the festive atmosphere fills the air, it's up to Santa Claus to save the day!

In this exciting adventure, players will help Santa urgently collect all the scattered gift boxes before the Grench can snatch them away. Time is of the essence, and the mission won t be easy. With each passing moment, the Grench grows more determined, so Santa must act quickly to secure the gifts in his safe chest.

This game offers a fantastic opportunity for competition, allowing friends to challenge each other and see who can collect the most gift boxes. With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, Grench vs Santa is an ideal way to celebrate the New Year while experiencing an adrenaline rush. Gear up for this thrilling race against time, and may the best player win!

How to play free Grench vs Santa game online

To play the game, simply use your arrow keys to move Santa around the screen. Collect the gift boxes while avoiding the Grench. To put the gifts into the chest, just walk over it when you have a box. Keep an eye on the timer and strive to gather the most boxes possible!
