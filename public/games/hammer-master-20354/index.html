Hammer Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the world of Hammer Master, where your skills as a handyman will be put to the ultimate test! Are you ready to unleash your inner craftsman and become a master at fixing anything? Your journey begins with perfecting your nail-driving technique. Each level will challenge your precision and coordination as you strive to hit the target.

The objective of Hammer Master is simple yet addictive: Slide your finger or mouse to maneuver the hammer and target nails that share the same color. With each successful strike, you'll feel the satisfaction of bringing order to chaos. But be cautious your path isn t free of obstacles. Avoid hitting rocks, as this will lead to instant failure! Keep your eyes on the prize as you work through increasingly tricky scenarios.

As you play, your energy meter will gradually fill up. Once it's full, the time has come to activate your magical boosting ability! This power-up will help you drive nails more effectively, allowing you to progress further in your quest. The end of each round culminates in a thrilling tally of the nails you've successfully driven. Can you achieve a personal best and show off your hammer skills?

How to play free Hammer Master game online

To start playing Hammer Master, use your mouse or finger to slide the hammer towards the nails of the same color. Aim carefully and avoid any rocks as you hit the nails. Keep an eye on your energy bar and activate the magic ability when it's full for a big boost. Happy hammering!
