Wedding Makeover Salon - Wedding Artist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to transform into the most stunning bride on the planet? In the Wedding Makeover Salon - Wedding Artist, you can indulge your wedding fantasies by helping three lovely brides prepare for their big day. These brides are anxious as they face the daunting task of achieving their dream look, and they need your expert guidance!

Your journey begins with a makeover session where you'll cleanse and pamper the brides' faces. Get creative as you craft the perfect bridal bun that matches each bride's unique style. After the makeup is complete, you will have the incredible opportunity to select breathtaking wedding dresses for them! Will you choose a classic gown, a modern design, or something entirely unique? The choice is yours!

This game not only allows you to express your artistic flair but also lets you experience the joy of making someone's special day even more magical. With every brush stroke and dress selection, you inch closer to making these brides the happiest women alive. It's a fantastic free game perfect for girls and kids who dream of being wedding artists!

How to play free Wedding Makeover Salon - Wedding Artist game online

To start playing, select one of the three brides waiting for your assistance. Cleanse and prepare her face using the beauty tools offered. Next, apply makeup using a variety of colors and styles. Finally, browse through an array of wedding dresses and accessories to dress her to perfection. Enjoy the process of creating a delightful bridal look!
