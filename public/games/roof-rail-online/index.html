Roof Rail Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Roof Rails Online is one of the most captivating arcade games that has made waves this year, offering a unique blend of engaging gameplay and stunning 3D graphics. If you enjoy action-packed games that challenge your reflexes and coordination, this is a must-try. Your primary objective is to navigate along rooftops while using a wooden pole to maintain your balance.

As you progress through the levels, you will collect wooden blocks to extend your pole's length. This added length is essential for maintaining balance as you maneuver across the rooftops. Be wary of the gears scattered throughout the game, as coming into contact with them will shorten your pole and create challenges in maintaining your balance. The ultimate goal is to reach the end of each level while collecting all the gems you can along the way. With each stage presenting its own set of obstacles and challenges, Roof Rails Online keeps players engaged and eager to improve. Are you prepared to take on this exhilarating roof-top adventure?

How to play free Roof Rail Online game online

To play Roof Rails Online, use the mouse or tap on the screen to control the wooden pole's angle and direction. As you traverse the rooftops, collect wooden blocks to lengthen the pole while avoiding gears that can shorten it. Your mission is to reach the end of the platform and collect as many gems as possible!
