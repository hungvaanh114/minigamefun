Kitty Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Kitty Drop, an engaging and whimsical physics-based puzzle game where your mission is to rescue a little fallen kitty cat! The adorable feline friend needs your help to safely land on a grassy platform below. As you navigate through the charming levels, you'll encounter various boxes and obstacles that must be dealt with strategically.

Your primary objective is straightforward: click on the boxes, blocks, and other items on the screen to eliminate them. However, be mindful that all boxes, except for the grassy areas, need to be removed to successfully progress to the next level. You'll be faced with an exciting challenge as you figure out the best way to use the set blocks and props to create a safe landing area for the kitty. Your problem-solving skills will be put to the test as each level introduces new twists and turns.

Worried about making a mistake? Don t fret! You can reset any level at any time if you find that your moves aren t working out. With 28 unique levels to conquer, each more challenging than the last, Kitty Drop promises hours of fun as you strive to help the spirited little cat reach its grassy haven.

How to play free Kitty Drop game online

To play Kitty Drop, simply click on the various boxes and blocks to remove them from the screen. The goal is to ensure that the kitty lands safely on the grass. Use your surroundings wisely, and don't hesitate to reset the level if you make a wrong decision. Good luck!
