Roller Magnet

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Roller Magnet is an exciting and addictive hyper-casual game designed to test your reflexes and strategic thinking. In this engaging experience, players maneuver a powerful magnet across the screen to attract various blocks and cubes, all while skillfully avoiding the dangerous bombs that can derail your progress. The game's vibrant graphics and smooth mechanics make it a delightful choice for players of all ages, providing endless fun with each session.

The gameplay in Roller Magnet is straightforward yet challenging. As you control the magnet, you'll encounter different shapes and colors of blocks that award points when drawn towards you. Each successful collection increases your score and propels you further into the game. However, be cautious! The bombs are scattered throughout the playing area, ready to explode upon contact. One wrong move and you could lose everything you've worked for. The thrill of trying to collect as many blocks as possible while evading bombs adds an exhilarating element to the game.

With its simple mechanics and charming design, Roller Magnet is perfect for quick gaming sessions or longer playtimes. Whether you're looking to improve your high score or just enjoy a relaxing game, Roller Magnet promises to keep you entertained for hours. Dive into this captivating world of colors and challenges, and see how far your magnet can go!

How to play free Roller Magnet game online

To play Roller Magnet, simply use your finger or mouse to control the magnet's movement. Guide it through the play area to attract blocks and cubes while steering clear of bombs. The more blocks you collect, the higher your score; avoid the bombs to keep your game going!
