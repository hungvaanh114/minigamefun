Welcome to Tetriz Match 3, a delightful puzzle adventure where you can indulge your love for candies! In this exciting game, your objective is to skillfully shuffle and shift the falling groups of colorful candies to create matches. Form a line of three or more identical candies they can be arranged horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally anywhere on the board. The more combinations you make, the higher your score!

Utilize the left and right arrow keys to expertly shift the falling candies. When you want to change their position in a new formation, simply press the up arrow key to shuffle them. The key to mastering Tetriz Match 3 is quick thinking and strategic moves, so you can prevent the board from filling up to the upper row. Keep an eye on the falling candies and think ahead to make the best possible matches!

With each successful match, you not only earn points but also clear space on the board for new candies to fall. The game continues until the board is completely filled, so stay sharp and play smart to achieve the highest score possible!

How to play free Tetriz Match 3 game online

To play Tetriz Match 3, use the left and right arrow keys to shift candies along the rows and the up arrow key to shuffle the falling group. Create lines of three or more identical candies to score points while ensuring that the board doesn t reach the top. Have fun and strategize!
