Garden Rush. Vegetables Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the vibrant world of Garden Rush: Vegetables Escape, you awaken to a wardrobe crisis your clothes have mysteriously vanished! It s an emergency: you need to secure a new outfit to impress your date. Dive into the gardener's adventure where your mission is to cultivate vegetables in your garden, gathering funds to purchase new attire.

To succeed, you'll need to nurture your plants diligently. Watering your vegetables is essential for their growth, and you'll find a well or a nearby river stocked with precious water. However, keep your wits about you these vegetables are not just passive bystanders; they are fast and nimble! To earn money, you'll have to catch them as they escape, adding an exhilarating twist to your gardening endeavors.

Your ultimate goal is to amass enough funds by efficiently planting and harvesting these speedy vegetables. It's a race against time and the clock as you strive to buy all the clothes needed for your date night. Get ready for a gripping combination of strategy, speed, and fun in this unique gardening challenge!

How to play free Garden Rush: Vegetables Escape game online

To play, simply begin by planting seeds in your garden. Once your vegetables have sprouted, ensure to water them using the well or river to help them grow. After that, chase down your fast-moving vegetables to collect them and earn money. Use the funds to purchase new clothes for your upcoming date!
