Squad Goals: Soccer 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step onto the pitch in Squad Goals: Soccer 3D, an engaging and straightforward soccer experience that promises endless action and excitement! This lively game elevates the traditional soccer experience by removing penalties, allowing for a fast-paced match where the only goal is to score first. With just a single finger, you can effortlessly slide, tackle, dribble, kick, and shoot your way to victory. It s not just about the competition; it's about enjoying the chaos of an exhilarating ball fight!

Elevate your gaming experience as you climb the ranks in this competitive arena. The game's one-finger on-screen joystick controls, combined with support for mouse and keyboard, make it accessible for all players. Enhance your character s abilities by upgrading traits to unlock their full potential, while you shop for various cosmetics to customize everything from your character to the ball and stadium. Daily quests and generous hourly rewards keep the gameplay fresh and rewarding, encouraging players to return for more challenges.

In Squad Goals, you can choose to play in two thrilling modes: the fast-paced ARENA or the exhilarating TEAM vs TEAM. No matter which mode you select, every match is a chance to prove your skills and dominate opponents in this vibrant soccer brawl!

How to play free Squad Goals: Soccer 3D game online

To play, simply tap on the screen to move your character, using your finger to control the joystick. Slide and tackle opponents to steal the ball, and tap to kick and shoot towards the goal! Embark on daily quests and enjoy various rewards while customizing your character and aiming for the top.
