How to play free FNF: Red Light, Green Light game online

To play the game, simply follow the beat of the music and hit the corresponding keys as prompted on the screen. When "Red Light" is called, stop moving; when "Green Light" is activated, you can advance. Your goal is to reach the finish line without getting caught!
