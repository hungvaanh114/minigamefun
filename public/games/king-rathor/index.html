King Rathor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in King Rathor, a captivating 2D platformer where you step into the royal shoes of a determined King. Your noble mission is to rescue the beloved Queen, who has been captured by treacherous enemy soldiers. As you navigate through beautifully designed levels, you must skillfully avoid perilous obstacles such as spinning saws and deadly spikes that threaten your quest.

Throughout your journey, gather precious ornaments that not only enhance your score but can also aid you on your mission. Each of the eight levels presents a unique challenge, with increasing difficulties that test your agility and strategic thinking. As you progress, you'll encounter various enemy soldiers determined to thwart your rescue efforts, requiring you to dodge, jump, and time your moves meticulously. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics ensure a thrilling experience as you strive to bring peace back to your kingdom.

The fate of the kingdom lies in your hands, and every successful rescue mission will strengthen your resolve as the heroic King Rathor. Will you rise to the challenge and bring the Queen back safely? Dive into the adventure, collect ornaments, and conquer obstacles in this enchanting platformer!

How to play free King Rathor game online

In King Rathor, use the arrow keys to move and jump across platforms. Collect ornaments for points while avoiding enemies and hazardous spikes. Your goal is to reach the end of each level and rescue the Queen, using your skills to navigate through increasingly difficult challenges. Enjoy the adventure!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
