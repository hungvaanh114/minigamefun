Princesses Beauty Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of the Princesses Beauty Salon, where you get to join Tiana, Aurora, and Mulan on their glamorous journey before an exciting party. These beloved princesses are ready to indulge in a delightful beauty makeover, each with their own unique wishes and styles. Tiana is eager to pamper her hands, Mulan wants to revamp her legs, and Aurora yearns for smooth and radiant back skin. This is your chance to showcase your creativity and bring out the best in these princesses!

Your mission is to help each princess achieve her desired look. Dive into the beauty salon and utilize various tools and products to assist Tiana with her manicure, giving her hands a mesmerizing touch. For Mulan, you ll use special creams and scrubs to transform her legs into a work of art. And don t forget about Aurora the perfect skin treatment will ensure her back glows with beauty. Each princess needs your expertise to look stunning for the grand celebration!

As you work your magic, you can also select breathtaking outfits for the princesses that enhance their charm. Mix and match different styles, colors, and accessories to create enchanting ensembles. With your keen eye for fashion, make sure they are dressed to impress and ready to steal the show at the party. Let your imagination run wild as you help these princesses shine!

How to play free Princesses Beauty Salon game online

To play the Princesses Beauty Salon game, simply select your favorite princess at the start. Use your mouse to navigate through the salon options, applying beauty treatments and choosing outfits for each princess. Follow the on-screen instructions to ensure they look fabulous for the party!
