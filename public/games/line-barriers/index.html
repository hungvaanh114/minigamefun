Line Barriers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Line Barriers is an engaging and fast-paced game that challenges players to navigate a ball through a series of barriers. The objective is simple yet captivating: guide your ball safely to the other side while avoiding solid obstacles. As you play, you'll notice that some barriers are transparent, allowing your ball to pass through effortlessly, while others are solid and will cause you to crash if you try to cross them.

Your reflexes will be put to the test as you tap the screen to slow down the ball s speed, giving you the precision needed to maneuver through the gaps in the barriers. Timing is everything in this game; you must decide when to speed up and when to slow down to ensure a safe passage. Each successful crossing earns you valuable points, and the goal is to maximize your score by finding the right rhythm and avoiding collisions with the indestructible barriers.

The pressure mounts as you attempt to push your score higher with each attempt. But be careful! If you crash into a barrier, the game ends, and you ll have to start over with only one chance to earn a high score. This simple yet addictive gameplay keeps players coming back for more as they strive to beat their personal best.

How to play free Line Barriers game online

To play Line Barriers, simply tap the screen to slow down the ball as it approaches the barriers. Monitor the barriers carefully; when they are transparent, glide through safely, but avoid the solid ones. Your goal is to navigate through as many barriers as possible to achieve the highest score!
