Birds vs Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing a delightful new game that challenges your intellect while providing endless fun: Birds VS Blocks. In this charming and addictive casual game, you ll find yourself guiding a group of adorable little birds as they embark on a daring adventure. The objective is simple but engaging you must lead your feathered friends through a series of blocks while aiming for the lowest number possible.

As you navigate these colorful blocks, you'll encounter various power-ups that add an extra layer of excitement to the gameplay. The introduction of shields and bullets not only enhances the challenge but also makes it easier to protect your birds while clearing the way. The vibrant sound effects elevate your gaming experience, making every moment spent in Birds VS Blocks enjoyable and captivating. It s a race against time and obstacles as you attempt to rack up the highest scores. So gear up and get ready to smash some blocks!

How to play free Birds VS Blocks game online

To play Birds VS Blocks, simply tap the screen to make your flock of birds fly upward. As you navigate through the blocks, aim to collide with them while utilizing power-ups wisely. Navigate carefully to avoid the higher-numbered blocks and collect boosts for maximum score. Let's soar through the challenge!
