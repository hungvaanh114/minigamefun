My Tiny Land

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

An ultra-quiet and super stress-relieving fruit and vegetable matching game with hundreds of types of fruits waiting for you to collect.You can drag identical fruits into a basket, and eliminating them requires only a match of three. To win, you need to eliminate all the fruits and vegetables within the specified time limit. The game boasts simple controls and engaging game play.

Instructions

Once the game begins drag the fruits or vegetables in the baskets to form groups of three which will then be eliminated To win the game and advance to the next level you must eliminate all the fruits or vegetables in all the baskets
