Drunken Boxing: Ultimate

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for the most exhilarating boxing experience with the release of Drunken Boxing: Ultimate. This latest installment in the Drunken Boxing series brings you a fully immersive 3D arena where you can display your skills while navigating the complexities of drunken motion. The game features enhanced physics and spectacular special hits that make each duel a thrilling spectacle.

Your goal in Drunken Boxing: Ultimate is to skillfully approach your opponent while maintaining balance. You ll have the opportunity to unleash a series of punches, where swift left and right jabs are just the beginning. Strategy is key; time your moves carefully to ensure you dodge incoming attacks while delivering powerful blows. Moreover, when your Special Hit bar is filled, prepare to unleash an unexpected trick on your opponent a projectile of vomit! Utilize this move wisely by waiting for the right moment and distance, then simply hit the special hit button for maximum impact.

However, be mindful of your Energy Bar. Depleting it means a lengthy recovery period, leaving you vulnerable in the heat of the battle. This unique blend of strategy and hilarity makes Drunken Boxing: Ultimate not only a test of skill but also a source of endless entertainment.

How to play free Drunken Boxing: Ultimate game online

To play Drunken Boxing: Ultimate online, use the arrow keys to navigate your character and move close to your opponent. Tap your designated punch buttons to deliver left and right punches. Watch your Energy Bar, and when your Special Hit bar fills, press the special hit button at the right moment to unleash a surprise attack!
