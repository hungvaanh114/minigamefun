Makeup Games: Wedding Artist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Makeup Games: Wedding Artist where you can unleash your creativity and show off your makeup skills! Join Jennifer and her fiance as they prepare for their dream wedding, just one month away. With a long to-do list ahead, they need your artistic talents to bring their vision to life.

In this delightful game designed for girls, you get to choose a doll who will embody the beautiful bride. Start the experience with a tranquil girls' spa session, the perfect first step in preparing for the big day! Indulge in hand spa treatments and select stunning nail decorations that add a personal touch to the bride's overall look.

Once the spa is complete, it's time to create the perfect wedding hairstyle that complements the bride's unique style. Don t forget to give her a fabulous makeover, making her feel like the stunning princess she truly is! To ensure everything is flawless, meticulously pick out an exquisite wedding dress that makes Jennifer's dreams a reality.

As you bring the wedding details together, take a moment to design a unique wedding invitation card that reflects the couple's love story. Capture all the cherished moments of their special day, ensuring they will last a lifetime. Are you ready to prove that you're the ultimate wedding makeup artist?

How to play free Makeup Games: Wedding Artist game online

To play Makeup Games: Wedding Artist, begin by selecting your doll and dive into the sapphire spa. Follow the on-screen instructions to apply makeup, style hair, and decorate nails. Progress through each task, picking the dress and creating invitations. Watch as you help make Jennifer's wedding day unforgettable!
