Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of 3D Drive to Point, a high-stakes racing game that will test your skills and instincts! In this thrilling adventure, you re not just racing against the clock; you're competing for survival. With every press of the gas pedal, you're not only speeding towards victory but also diving headfirst into a realm where every point counts.

As a fearless racer, your mission is clear: collect all the shiny points scattered throughout the track before time runs out. With your legendary car under your command, you ll zoom through challenging landscapes, navigating twists and turns while maintaining your speed. It s not just a race; it s a race for your life!

Be mindful that each time you reach a milestone of 10 points, you unlock a new level. However, don't let the thrill of leveling up distract you each subsequent stage presents even greater challenges. Obstacles become more cunning, and the competition more fierce as you strive to gather all the points. Remember, in this race, you are the owner of the road, and nothing should stand between you and your glorious victory.

How to play free 3D Drive to Point game online

Once in the game, accelerate your car using the arrow keys or your preferred control scheme. Navigate through the environment by steering left and right while focusing on collecting all available points. Keep an eye on the timer, and make sure to push through each level as they become progressively harder with every 10 points you earn!
