Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting adventure with Mahjong Around The World: Africa. This captivating game transports you to the heart of the African continent, where you will uncover a world filled with vibrant wildlife and thrilling challenges. Your mission is to find matching mahjong tile pairs, clearing the game board as you go. As you progress through the various levels, you will have the opportunity to liberate and reveal all 10 unique African animals that call this enchanting landscape home.

As your tiles are cleared, you'll also encounter an exhilarating race against the clock. Each level comes with its own time limit, making your quest to free the animals even more thrilling. But fear not! You'll have the option to use helpful prompts and swap tiles to create matches. These added features not only enhance your gameplay experience but also ensure that you stay engaged as you navigate through intricate puzzles and unlock the wonders of Africa.

With its stunning visuals and immersive gameplay, Mahjong Around The World: Africa offers players the perfect blend of relaxation and challenge. Whether you're a seasoned mahjong player or a newcomer, this game promises to keep you entertained as you explore the diverse ecosystems of Africa while honing your tile-matching skills. Gather your wits, channel your focus, and get ready for a mahjong adventure like no other!

How to play free Mahjong Around The World Africa game online

To play Mahjong Around The World: Africa, simply start by selecting pairs of matching tiles on the game board to clear them. Keep an eye on the clock as you work against the time limit for each level. You can use prompts to help find pairs or swap tiles if you're stuck. Enjoy the challenge!
