Floppy Bird

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Floppy Bird, where players guide a charming little Red Bird through a vibrant sky filled with challenging obstacles. Your mission is to help the bird soar through the air, evading perilous pipes that threaten to bring your flight to an abrupt halt. It's a simple yet engaging concept that's easy to grasp but incredibly tough to master.

In Floppy Bird, tapping on the screen gives your feathered friend the ability to flap its wings and navigate the treacherous environment. As the bird free-falls, your timely taps will determine its altitude and path through the sky. The game requires quick reflexes and concentration to avoid crashing into the ominous pipes, each of which is strategically placed to test your skills. The more you play, the more addictive it becomes, making it perfect for players of all ages, especially kids who enjoy a good challenge.

With its straightforward gameplay mechanics and captivating graphics, Floppy Bird promises hours of entertainment. Each level presents new challenges to overcome, ensuring that players remain engaged while trying to achieve the highest score possible. Flap your wings to fly above the hurdles and revel in the joy of this delightful bird game!

How to play free Floppy Bird game online

To play Floppy Bird, simply tap the screen to make the Red Bird flap its wings. Focus on avoiding the pipes scattered throughout the sky. Keep your timing precise; tap too quickly and you may ascend too high, or tap too slowly and risk crashing. Aim to navigate as far as possible while accumulating points!
