Steve and Alex Dragon Egg

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating journey with Steve and Alex in "Steve and Alex Dragon Egg." This is their grand finale adventure, where you will join forces to secure a dragon egg and make a daring escape. The stakes are high, as a fearsome dragon looms over the horizon, eager to reclaim its prized possession. Your mission is clear: seize the egg, confront the dragon, and make your getaway before it's too late.

As you traverse this treacherous terrain, beware of the green exploding zombies that are lurking everywhere! These creatures are determined to thwart your plans, so you'll need to stay alert and work together to overcome the obstacles in your path. Use your skills wisely as you dig through the earth and break down walls to clear the way forward. The key to success lies in teamwork, with one player wielding a sword for combat and the other equipped with a pickaxe for excavation.

Every move you make counts, and your choices could mean the difference between victory and defeat. Do you have what it takes to secure the dragon egg and escape the clutches of the dragon? Gather your courage and set forth on this thrilling adventure with Steve and Alex your dragon egg awaits!

How to play free Steve and Alex Dragon Egg game online

To play the game, begin by selecting your character, either Steve or Alex. Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate the terrain. The player with the sword can attack zombies by clicking or tapping, while the player with the pickaxe can dig and break down walls. Work together to find the dragon egg and prepare for the final showdown!
