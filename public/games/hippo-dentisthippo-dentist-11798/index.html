Welcome to the bustling world of Hippo Dentist, where you'll find a line of adorable animal patients, all in need of urgent dental care. In this charming dental clinic, a little pig, a dog, and a little lion have all come together for one shared reason: they are suffering from toothaches! Usually, this eclectic group wouldn t be seen together, but there s no time to waste when their dental health is at stake.

Little Hippo, the resident dentist, is overwhelmed with patients. That s where you come in! As a skilled and sophisticated dentist, it's your job to step in and assist little Hippo with her busy schedule. Choose your first patient who needs your expertise the most? The little lion, for instance, can hardly wait! He has been indulging in too much meat and neglecting his dental hygiene, leading to infections and missing teeth. Your task is to clean his teeth thoroughly and apply appropriate dentures to restore his smile. Don't forget to remind him about the importance of maintaining good dental habits!

Then there s little pig, who has his own set of dental issues waiting to be resolved. With each patient requiring different treatments, you will need to use your tools creatively and efficiently to ensure every animal leaves the clinic with a dazzling smile. Join this delightful adventure filled with fun and educational moments about dental care!

How to play free Hippo Dentist game online

To play Hippo Dentist, simply select your patient from the waiting room. Use the dental tools provided to clean their teeth, fill cavities, and give oral hygiene advice. Follow on-screen instructions to navigate the game, and complete each dental procedure to help your furry friends achieve a healthy smile!
