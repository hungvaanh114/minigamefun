Prepare for an exhilarating new battle in the FNF universe with FNF TAILS.EXE. In this thrilling mod, you face off against an unlikely and treacherous opponent Tails, the well-known fox from the Sonic series. However, this isn t your average Tails; under the influence of a malicious virus, he has transformed into a sinister EXE version of himself. Get ready for a musical duel that will test your skills and reflexes!

Your mission is to assist Boyfriend in overcoming this dangerous foe. As the funky beats kick in, you must ensure that every one of Boyfriend's movements aligns perfectly with the rhythm of the music. With the iconic tunes and voices of various characters integrated into the gameplay, you'll have an opportunity to create your own unique melodies while staying engaged in this intense confrontation. The stakes are high, and the rhythm is relentless; can you keep pace with Tails.EXE, who is determined to outsmart you?

Each level presents new challenges, and sharp reflexes alongside a keen sense of rhythm are crucial for success. If you can master the beats and outshine your opponent, victory will be within your grasp. Let's see if you have what it takes to help Boyfriend prevail against this twisted version of a beloved character.

How to play free FNF TAILS.EXE game online

To start playing the FNF TAILS.EXE game, use the arrow keys to hit the corresponding directional notes as they come down the screen. Follow the rhythm and match the beats to progress through the levels. The more accurately you perform, the higher your score will be. Aim to outshine Tails.EXE and come out on top!
