Rabbit Samurai

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the captivating world of Rabbit Samurai, a thrilling skill game designed by Mini Duck Games. In this action-packed adventure, you take on the role of a courageous rabbit equipped with exceptional ninja abilities. Your mission? To swing your way through lush landscapes, gather delicious carrots, and save your adorable little mouse friends from the clutches of a cunning wolf!

The vibrant green land is filled with danger at every turn, as you navigate treacherous terrains and evade perilous spikes. With a trusty grappling hook, you can perform remarkable feats of agility, propelling yourself through the air and moving swiftly to reach your goals. Your journey also includes launching from a phenomenal rocket ninja cannon, which adds an exhilarating twist to your escapades!

As you dash through this enchanting environment, keep your eyes peeled for shimmering crystals and abundant carrots that are scattered throughout the levels. Collecting these rewards not only boosts your score but also enhances your skills as a ninja rabbit. The more you collect, the closer you get to freeing all the trapped mice held within the wolf's lair. Are you ready to showcase your extraordinary dexterity and strategy to save your friends and triumph over the wolves? Grab your gear and let the adventure begin!

How to play free Rabbit Samurai game online

To play Rabbit Samurai, use your mouse or touch controls to swing and leap through the game. Aim for carrots and crystals as you navigate levels, avoiding spikes. Utilize the grappling hook to reach difficult areas and launch from the rocket cannon to soar through the air, rescuing mice trapped in traps!


Instructions

Use grappling hook - Tap or hold down the left mouse button
