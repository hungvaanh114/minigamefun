Airport Security

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to dive into the challenging world of Airport Security? This engaging game provides you with an insightful experience of the security screening process that all passengers must go through before boarding an airplane. With a keen focus on safety and efficiency, your role is to meticulously assess individuals at three crucial checkpoints: the passport check, body check, and backpack check.

At the passport check, you'll need to ensure that each traveler's name, age, and occupation correspond with their identification. It s essential to be vigilant; discrepancies could lead to serious safety issues. During the body check, be on the lookout for any unauthorized items. Remember, items such as knives, firearms, and even animals are strictly prohibited on board and must be identified and addressed immediately.

The final stage involves scrutinizing the contents of each traveler's backpack. Your objective is not just to identify dangerous items but also to spot anyone exhibiting suspicious behavior. Your keen observation skills will be your greatest asset as you navigate through this bustling airport environment. The stakes are high, so it s crucial that you remain focused and alert at all times. Can you prove that you have what it takes to be an effective security checker?

How to play free Airport Security game online

To play Airport Security, start by selecting your role and then proceed to the various checkpoints. Carefully examine passports for accuracy, scan bodies for prohibited items, and check backpacks meticulously. Use your mouse to interact with items and keep track of each passenger s details. Stay alert to spot any suspicious behavior!
