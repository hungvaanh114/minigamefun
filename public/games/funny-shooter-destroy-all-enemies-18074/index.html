Funny Shooter - Destroy All Enemies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chaotic world of Funny Shooter - Destroy All Enemies, a zany action-packed adventure where your primary goal is to eliminate hordes of quirky enemies with your chosen arsenal. With an impressive selection of 18 distinct weapons at your disposal, you ll find yourself engaged in a frenetic battle against foes of all shapes and sizes. Each enemy type presents unique characteristics and attacking styles, which makes the gameplay both exhilarating and challenging.

As you face the onslaught of adversaries, you'll need to stay nimble on your feet. Watch out for grenades, pitchforks, and other lethal weapons that come flying your way. The comedic yet dangerous environment demands quick reflexes as you dodge devastating blows from clubs, axes, and chainsaws. It's a glorious mess of action, where destruction is not only your aim but your joy! Unleash chaos as you dismantle the enemy ranks using powerful tools like grenade launchers, bazookas, machine guns, and pistols. The goal is simple destroy them all!

Featuring stunning graphics, a diverse range of weaponry, and addictive gameplay, Funny Shooter keeps you entertained for hours on end. The dynamic sound effects accompanied by driving music enhance the action, making each victory feel grand. If you re ready for some over-the-top fun, jump into the action and put your skills to the test!

How to play free Funny Shooter - Destroy All Enemies game online

To play Funny Shooter, simply use your mouse or keyboard controls to aim and shoot at incoming enemies. Switch between weapons using the designated keys and stay mobile to dodge attacks. Collect power-ups, and enjoy blasting through levels filled with chaos and laughter as you strive to eliminate every enemy in sight!


Instructions

Mouse - look around WASD - movement W Shift - Run Space - jump Left Mouse Button - Shoot Right Mouse Button Hold - Aim Mouse wheel - next leading weapon 1-7 - weapon hotkeys R - reload
