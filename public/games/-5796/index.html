Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Happy Filled Glass 3 is an engaging and interactive puzzle game that invites players to unleash their creativity and problem-solving skills. In this captivating adventure, your objective is to draw lines on the screen to guide water into a glass, ensuring it is filled to the brim. As you navigate through the levels, you'll encounter a range of exciting obstacles that add complexity to your task. From burning platforms that pose a risk to your water flow, to water-accelerating platforms that can help you reach your goal faster, each level presents a unique challenge.

The game also features innovative elements such as rotating platforms that require you to think strategically and platforms designed to tame the unpredictable nature of water. Additionally, there's a beam with zero gravity that adds a twist to how you approach your drawing. Happy Filled Glass 3 not only tests your artistic skills but also your ability to strategize as you contend with various physics-based obstacles to achieve the ultimate goal: filling the glass with water.

Each level is crafted to provide a satisfying experience as you see your drawn lines come to life and successfully guide the water. The intuitive controls and colorful graphics make it accessible and enjoyable for players of all ages, making Happy Filled Glass 3 a perfect choice for fans of puzzle and strategy games.

How to play free Happy Filled Glass 3 game online

To begin playing Happy Filled Glass 3, draw lines on the screen to direct water into the glass. Use the pencil tool to create paths, keeping in mind the various obstacles in each level. Adjust your strategy based on the unique challenges presented, and watch as the water flows to fill the glass!
