Connect the Pipes: Water Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dozens of unique levels await you in this exciting puzzle game, where you need to show your logic and ingenuity! There are pipes, water sources and drains on the playing field. Your task is to turn the pipes so that the water from the source reaches the drain. But thats not all! Water comes in different colors, and to complete some levels you will have to mix the streams, creating new colors. Each drain may require a certain color of water, and the difficulty of the levels will increase, offering new mechanics and challenges.

Instructions

Rotate pipes Tap a pipe on the board to rotate it Connect the water source to the desired drain Mix colors Water comes in different colors If the drain requires a certain color direct the streams from different sources into one pipe to mix them Fil
