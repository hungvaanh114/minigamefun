Gift Glide

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to soar through the holiday season with Gift Glide, the exhilarating adventure where you join Santa on a mission to deliver joy! Navigate through enchanting snowy villages, and let your aim shine as you skillfully drop presents down chimneys, bringing smiles to every home along the way.

As Santa s dedicated helper, your mission requires precision and quick reflexes. Each successful gift drop awards you points, and as you progress through the game, you ll unlock delightful upgrades that enhance your delivery abilities. However, the challenge intensifies as you race against time. The speed picks up, and you ll encounter tricky obstacles designed to test your focus and agility.

The magic of Gift Glide lies in its captivating gameplay. You must perfect your throw to ensure every gift reaches its destination without a hitch. Will you be able to deliver all the presents before dawn breaks? Get ready to experience the joy of spreading holiday cheer while mastering the art of gift delivery!

How to play free Gift Glide game online

To play Gift Glide, use your mouse to aim at the rooftops and click to release the gifts. Adjust your angle and timing carefully to land presents accurately within the chimneys. Collect bonus points and power-ups by making successful deliveries. Keep an eye on the clock to complete all deliveries before sunrise!
