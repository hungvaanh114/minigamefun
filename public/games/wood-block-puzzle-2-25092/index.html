Wood Block Puzzle 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Wood Block Puzzle 2 is a captivating puzzle game that tasks players with arranging wooden blocks to fill complete rows and columns on a grid. With simple drag-and-drop mechanics, players strategically place blocks to create solid lines and clear the board. The game features various modes, including Classic and Challenge, each offering unique gameplay experiences. With its minimalist design and relaxing gameplay, Wood Block Puzzle 2 provides hours of entertainment for puzzle enthusiasts of all skill levels.
