Sweet Candy Collection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Indulge in a delightful experience with the Sweet Candy Collection game, where your objective is to gather sugary treats and create matches for points. Start your candy adventure by simply pressing on any candy to kick off the fun. The gameplay is intuitive and engaging, encouraging you to use your 'mouse' or 'finger tip' to navigate through clusters of identical candies that lie side by side.

Your mission is to connect at least three matching candies, whether arranged horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally. The thrill intensifies as you aim to collect candies efficiently and swiftly. For every six matching candies you gather, a bonus will be awarded, amplifying your score. Moreover, if you manage to connect more than seven candies in one swipe, you ll unlock an exciting time bonus to enhance your gameplay experience.

However, keep an eye on the clock! To keep progressing, you must collect the requested candies within the allotted time frame. Failing to do so means you ll lose your chance to advance in this confectionery challenge. The Sweet Candy Collection game is not just about quick reflexes but also strategizing your moves to make larger matches and earn higher rewards.

How to play free Sweet Candy Collection game online

To play the Sweet Candy Collection game, click on any candy to begin. Use your mouse or finger to draw a line connecting at least three adjacent candies of the same type. Once you've created a match, release the button to collect them. Don't forget to keep an eye on the timer and the candy objectives!
