Tricky Master is a captivating online puzzle game designed specifically for children and available to play for free. In this intriguing game, you step into the shoes of a clever student who must gather a potion and make a stealthy escape from a classroom filled with potential danger. Your goal is to navigate through the room without drawing the attention of the overseeing master.

The game challenges players to utilize their strategic thinking skills and plan every move carefully. A crucial element is the small map located in the upper right corner of the screen. This map provides valuable information about your surroundings, allowing you to pinpoint the positions of both the master and yourself. Mastering this aspect of the game is essential, as it will significantly enhance your chances of a successful escape.

As you immerse yourself in Tricky Master, you'll encounter various obstacles and hidden paths that require keen observation and quick reflexes. The atmosphere is thrilling, especially when you realize that the master can appear unexpectedly right behind you. Staying alert and thinking quickly will be your best defense against getting caught!
