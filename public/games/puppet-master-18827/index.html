Puppet Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chaotic world of Puppet Master, where your aim is to unleash destruction in thrilling ways. This action-packed 3D Clicker game invites players to hurl knives, fire machine guns, and launch rockets at an unsuspecting ragdoll target. As you wreak havoc, each successful hit increases your score and unlocks a wide array of powerful weapons, ready for your use.

Experience the satisfaction of seeing your ragdoll react in exaggerated and humorous fashions. With its vibrant graphics and dynamic physics, Puppet Master captivates players and keeps them engaged through exciting gameplay. Every level offers new challenges as you strive to inflict more damage and increase your arsenal. The thrill of trying out different weapons adds to the fun, pushing you to experiment with various strategies to maximize the destruction.

Challenge yourself to reach higher score thresholds, allowing you to access even deadlier weapons. Customize your gameplay experience by choosing your preferred weapons and refining your technique as you learn how to deal the most damage efficiently. The combination of simple controls and intense visuals makes Puppet Master a captivating game that will have you coming back for more, eager to improve your scores and unlock everything.

How to play free Puppet Master game online

To play Puppet Master, simply click on the ragdoll to initiate the action. As you play, keep damaging the ragdoll to earn points and unlock new weapons. Use the on-screen buttons to switch between the available weapons, experimenting with each one to see which delivers the most satisfying results. Enjoy the chaos!
