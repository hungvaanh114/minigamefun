PJ Masks - Sticky Splat Soccer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of PJ Masks - Sticky Splat Soccer, the perfect addition to your collection of Disney Junior Games! Get ready for an adventure that blends the thrilling action of soccer with the beloved characters from the PJ Masks universe. In this engaging game, you ll step into the shoes of one of our three heroic protagonists and test your soccer skills like never before!

As you begin your journey on the soccer field, your primary goal will be to act as the goalkeeper. Your mission is to catch as many balls as possible that are thrown your way. Each time you successfully snag a ball, you ll earn points that can boost your score. The gameplay demands quick reflexes and perfect timing, so stay alert!

The mechanics are simple yet fun. Watch closely as the points indicator starts to extend along the right and left lines of the goal. Pay attention and click when the indicator is at its maximum length to make the best attempt at catching the ball. With each successful catch, you ll feel the rush of victory, and the excitement will keep you engaged for hours. Don't forget, there are more challenges and PJ Masks games waiting for you, so keep playing and enjoy the adventure ahead!

How to play free PJ Masks - Sticky Splat Soccer game online

To play the game, start by selecting your favorite PJ Masks hero. Once the game begins, watch the balls come towards you as the goalkeeper. Click the points indicator at the right moment to maximize your chances of catching the balls and scoring points. Good luck, hero!
