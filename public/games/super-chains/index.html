Super Chains

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Super Chains, an engaging and entertaining puzzle game that will put your strategic thinking to the test! In this captivating game, your main objective is to create long chains of blocks by linking together adjoining blocks that have values either one number higher, one number lower, or the same as the block you touch. As you progress through challenging levels, you'll discover powerful mythical creatures hidden within this numerical adventure, adding an exciting twist to your gameplay!

Your journey in Super Chains won t be a solo endeavor. You can summon formidable Heroes to assist you in your quest. These heroes possess unique abilities that can help you conquer tough puzzles and uncover even more powerful rewards. The thrill of strategizing your moves, unlocking hidden gems, and collecting creatures will keep you coming back for more, testing your skills and wits at every turn. Can you emerge as the EPIC champion in this whimsical world of numbers?

How to play free Super Chains game online

To play Super Chains, start by touching a block to create a chain. You can link to any adjoining block that is one number higher, one number lower, or the same value. Keep creating longer chains to unlock heroes and mythical creatures, all while aiming for the highest score!
