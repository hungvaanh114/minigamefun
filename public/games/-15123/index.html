Math Driving Test?Math Driving Test

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thrilling adventure in the Math Driving Test! In this exciting game, you'll drive around a vibrant landscape, collecting objects to earn the required amount of unit cubes in a race against time. Your task is to navigate through various levels, each offering unique challenges that will put your driving skills to the test.

As you zoom through the environment, aim to collect the necessary items quickly to earn a shiny medal, which is essential to progress to the next level. However, be cautious! Your driving precision is crucial, as colliding with trees, buildings, or fences three times will result in an instant game over. The challenge lies not just in speed but in your ability to maneuver skillfully without crashing.

Control your vehicle using either the WASD keys or the arrow keys on your keyboard for desktop play. If you're on mobile, simply tap the arrow buttons to steer and accelerate. The more adept you become at gathering the unit cubes and avoiding obstacles, the better your chances of achieving high scores and unlocking new levels. Put on your seatbelt and prepare for a fascinating blend of mathematics and driving in this fun and educational game Math Driving Test!

How to play free Math Driving Test game online

To play Math Driving Test, use the arrow keys or the WASD keys to control your vehicle's speed and direction. Drive around the map to collect the required unit cubes while avoiding collisions with obstacles. Collect enough items within the time limit to earn your medal and advance to the next level!
