Bumper Car Demolition Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Want to experience the thrill of arcade-style bumper car games in Bumper Car Demolition Race? You will engage in tense collision actions in pixelated arenas. Choose Arena Mode or Custom Mode for a challenge. Use your car to crash into opponents and knock them out. Collect treasure chests to get fire-breathing items to increase your damage freeze opponents, etc. Defeat them and compete for the championship.
