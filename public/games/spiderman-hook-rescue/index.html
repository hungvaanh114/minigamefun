How to play free Spiderman Hook Rescue game online

To play Spiderman Hook Rescue, use your mouse or touchscreen to click and hold, allowing your stickman to latch onto hooks scattered throughout the level. Release the hold to swing and fly through the air, aiming to land on the next hook without losing balance. Keep repeating this process to complete levels and achieve high scores!
