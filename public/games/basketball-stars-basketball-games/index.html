Immerse yourself in the exciting world of Basketball Stars, a dynamic 2-player basketball game developed by MadPuffers. Choose your desired team and step onto the court to face off in an exhilarating tournament. The game is designed to test your skills as you dribble, shoot, and dunk your way to victory.

As you control your player, execute impressive dunks and accurate three-pointers to outscore your opponent. With intuitive controls and immersive graphics, Basketball Stars provides an authentic basketball experience that challenges players to become the top scorer. Whether you're playing against a friend or honing your skills solo, Basketball Stars will keep you entertained and engaged with its action-packed gameplay.
