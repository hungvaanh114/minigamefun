Run Ninja

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to embark on an exhilarating journey with Run Ninja, where stealth and speed form the perfect combination! In this thrilling side-scrolling adventure, players take on the role of a formidable ninja tasked with overcoming a series of daunting challenges. The goal is simple: navigate through treacherous environments while facing off against the sinister demon samurais that lurk at every corner.

As the Ninja, you'll utilize your incredible agility and combat skills to leap over obstacles, dodge attacks, and engage in fierce battles. Your arsenal includes throwing stars, which you can strategically hurl at enemies to vanquish them before they can hinder your progress. But remember, even in a world full of danger, the Ninja is invincible so embrace the challenge with confidence!

With vibrant graphics, engaging gameplay, and an array of obstacles to overcome, Run Ninja offers an immersive experience for gamers of all ages. Each level introduces new threats and environments, ensuring that the excitement never fades. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to ninja adventures, this game promises endless fun as you race against the clock to complete your tasks and defeat the foes standing in your way.

How to play free Run Ninja game online

To start playing Run Ninja, simply use your keyboard's arrow keys to move and jump. Press the spacebar to throw stars at the demon samurais. Focus on timing your jumps to avoid obstacles and defeat your enemies, all while striving to complete your missions as swiftly as possible!


Instructions

Tap on the left of the screen to jump tap on the right to throw a star
