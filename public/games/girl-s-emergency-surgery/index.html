Girl #039;s Emergency Surgery

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Girl's Emergency Surgery," players are thrust into a gripping scenario where quick thinking and medical skills become essential. The story begins with Barbie driving home late at night, but her journey takes a dangerous turn when she gets into a serious car accident. In a dire situation, Barbie s heartbeat suddenly stops, putting her life at stake.

As an emergency doctor, you are responsible for Barbie s survival. The game kicks off in the ambulance, where you must act fast to bring her back to life. Your skills will be put to the test as you perform vital procedures to restore her heartbeat. Once stabilized, Barbie will be rushed to the operating room for further treatment.

Inside the surgical area, you will need to carefully assess her injuries, which include significant trauma to her head and leg. The stakes are high, and every decision counts. Use the tools at your disposal to perform life-saving surgeries and make crucial medical choices that could determine whether Barbie survives this horrific accident. Prepare yourself for an immersive experience filled with both excitement and the responsibility of saving a life.

How to play free Girl's Emergency Surgery game online

To play "Girl's Emergency Surgery," use your mouse to navigate the game's interface and select medical tools. Follow on-screen instructions to conduct procedures, stabilize Barbie's condition, and carry out surgical operations. Complete each task carefully to ensure her recovery and ultimately save her life!
