Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Squid Masks Pop, our latest exciting and free casual game that invites players into a vibrant world where strategic thinking meets fun! In this engaging match-3 game, your primary objective is to connect three or more unique masks to clear them from the board. These masks, which include soldiers, shiny gold animal masks, and the iconic frontman mask, must be expertly matched to maximize your score and progress to the next exciting challenge.

As you dive into the game, each mask you match is shot with a crossbow, offering a unique spin on traditional match-3 gameplay. The exciting twist is that new masks will continuously be introduced, adding layers of complexity to your strategy. Remain vigilant! If the cascading wave of masks reaches the bottom of the screen and touches your crossbow, your game will end, bringing your colorful journey to a halt. So keep your wits about you, and embark on an adventure filled with delightful animations and addictive gameplay.

Whether you're a seasoned match-3 player or a casual gamer looking for something new, Squid Masks Pop is easy to pick up, yet challenging to master. Enjoy the thrill of clearing masks, achieving high scores, and experiencing endless fun in this unique game where every match takes you closer to becoming a champion.

How to play free Squid Masks Pop game online

To play Squid Masks Pop, simply aim your crossbow and select three or more matching masks to clear them from the grid. Keep an eye on the incoming masks and create combos for higher scores. Stay alert and act quickly to prevent the masks from reaching the bottom!
