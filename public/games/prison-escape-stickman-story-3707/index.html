Step into the world of Prison Escape: Stickman Story, where you join Lupin 19th, an ardent admirer of the notorious gentleman thief Arsene Lupin. This intriguing character relishes the thrill of escaping the most secure prisons around the globe. No walls, guards, or traps can withstand his cunning and agility as he navigates through perilous scenarios. Each prison presents a distinctive set of challenges and characteristics, demanding that Lupin 19th utilizes his array of special skills to outsmart the system and evade capture.

Throughout your adventure, you'll encounter myriad obstacles that require quick thinking and tactical planning. The game's design ensures that no two escapes are the same; every prison introduces its own unique features and puzzles, testing your wits at every turn. As you guide Lupin 19th through these exciting environments, you will experience the adrenaline rush of a master escape artist at work, where every escape feels like a thrilling heist crafted with finesse.

Prepare to embark on a journey filled with suspense, intrigue, and exhilarating challenges as you help Lupin 19th break out from different prisons scattered worldwide. The game presents an engaging narrative that will hook you from the start and keep you strategizing to overcome each formidable challenge. Are you ready to put your skills to the test and experience the ultimate prison escape adventure?
