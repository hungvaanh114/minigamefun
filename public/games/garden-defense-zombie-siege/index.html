Garden Defense Zombie Siege

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Garden Defense - Zombie Siege is an exciting limited-shooting defense game that offers players a delightful yet challenging experience through its charming 2D cartoon graphics. In this game, you are tasked with protecting your garden from an onslaught of relentless zombies. With just one button to control the crosshair, you ll engage in fast-paced action to fend off these undead invaders.

Your objective is to utilize a cannon effectively to eliminate zombies that emerge from both sides of a gate. You must be strategic in your shooting; focusing on the nearest zombies first can help prevent them from overwhelming your defenses. While you begin with three hearts, it's vital to note that you can only sustain two hits from the zombies, making every shot count.

As you progress through different levels, the intensity and number of zombies will increase, testing your reflexes and aiming skills. The whimsical design combined with thrilling gameplay keeps players engaged as they attempt to clear each level while preserving their hearts. Mastering the art of shooting quickly and accurately will be crucial for success in Garden Defense - Zombie Siege.

How to play free Garden Defense Zombie Siege game online

To play Garden Defense - Zombie Siege, simply click or tap to move the crosshair and aim at the zombies approaching from the gates. Shoot the closest targets first to maximize your chances of survival. Watch your hearts closely and aim accurately to eliminate all zombies before they can take any hits from you.
