Squid Poopy Sniper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling and perilous world of Squid Poopy Sniper, where you will experience the deadly stakes of one of the most popular Korean TV shows from the eyes of a seasoned assassin. As a sniper, your mission is crucial: eliminate the players who fall short in challenging games like red light, green light, tug of war, marble, and the infamous glass tile game. Those who fail to impress must face the grim fate of being shot and killed, as survival in this playground hinges upon your sharpshooting skills.

In this heart-pounding first-person shooter, players must remain alert, as time is against them. With every ticking second, contestants are vying for a chance to win significant cash prizes, but the question remains is risking their lives worth the reward? It s a treacherous game of deception and skill, where the ability to spot a cookie thief can mean the difference between life and death. Use your sniper rifle with precision to protect the cookie from being stolen, and eliminate any threats swiftly and efficiently.

To ascend the ranks of assassination, players will need to hone their shooting talents by pulling the trigger and dispatching fellow competitors. As you progress, you'll unlock powerful guns and rifles, allowing for customization of not only your weaponry but also the uniforms of the guards. Gear up and prepare for an intense competition to become the best sniper among those unable to complete the tasks set before them.

How to play free Squid Poopy Sniper game online

To play Squid Poopy Sniper, start by using your mouse or game controller to navigate your vantage point. During competitions, carefully observe the players, and maintain your focus. When you spot someone failing, pull the trigger to eliminate them swiftly. Keep an eye on the timer and your ammo!
