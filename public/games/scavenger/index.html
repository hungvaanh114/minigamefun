Scavenger

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of exploration in Scavenger, a captivating game designed for those with a sharp eye and a knack for discovery. Players will immerse themselves in beautifully crafted maps filled with intricately hidden objects that must be found before the timer runs out. Each game begins with a list of objects displayed at the bottom of the screen, complete with photo hints to aid you on your quest.

As you navigate through the diverse environments, from lush forests to bustling city streets, you'll need to pay close attention to the details around you. The challenge lies not only in locating the hidden items but also in doing so against the clock. Scavenger encourages players to think critically and observe their surroundings carefully, making it ideal for adventurers who enjoy a test of patience and precision. With each successful find, you'll feel a rush of accomplishment, motivating you to seek even more hidden treasures.

Certain objects may be more challenging to spot than others, pushing your observational skills to the limit. Scavenger is perfect for anyone who enjoys treasure hunts or puzzle-solving games, providing hours of engaging gameplay. So gather your wits, and get ready to dive into this dynamic scavenger hunt!

How to play free Scavenger game online

To play Scavenger, simply enter the game and familiarize yourself with the map. Your task is to find all the items listed at the bottom of the screen. Use the photo hints for guidance and keep an eye on the timer. Click on the items as you locate them to collect points and advance through levels!
