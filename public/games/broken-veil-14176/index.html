Broken Veil

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Broken Veil is a captivating new horror experience that plunges players into a haunting narrative centered around a little boy desperately searching for his mother amidst a chaotic crowd. The atmosphere is thick with tension as the young protagonist traverses through a series of grotesque locations, each filled with unsettling sights and eerie sounds that disturb the very fabric of sanity.

Throughout his perilous journey, players will encounter a variety of horrifying characters, each presenting their own unique challenges that threaten to derail the boy's quest. The game is enriched with intricate puzzles that require sharp wit and keen observation, alongside intense pursuits that elevate the adrenaline factor. Combat encounters are skillfully woven into the gameplay, demanding quick reflexes and strategic thinking to emerge victorious against formidable foes.

A standout element of Broken Veil is the innovative mechanic involving cats, which serve as invaluable allies in the boy's adventure. These clever felines can assist in navigating obstacles, providing solutions to puzzles, and outsmarting malevolent entities that lurk in the shadows. This blend of companionship and utility not only enhances the gameplay but also deepens the emotional connection players forge with the protagonist.

With its gripping narrative and chilling visuals, Broken Veil is set to leave players both haunted and enthralled, making it a must-play for any horror enthusiast eager for a soul-stirring experience.
