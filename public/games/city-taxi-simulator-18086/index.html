City Taxi Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to City Taxi Simulator, an exhilarating taxi driving simulation that immerses you in a vibrant 3D world. With stunning modern graphics, this game allows players to experience the thrill of being a taxi driver as they navigate through bustling city streets. Your mission is simple yet exciting: pick up passengers and transport them to their desired locations while ensuring their journey is comfortable and enjoyable.

In City Taxi Simulator, you are the captain of your own cab, navigating through traffic and dodging obstacles to provide fast and efficient service. As you drive, you ll need to remain vigilant, obeying traffic rules and managing your time effectively to earn tips and bonuses. The game boasts various environments, from crowded downtown areas to quiet suburban neighborhoods, each with its own unique challenges and scenic views.

With realistic driving mechanics, players must master the art of steering, accelerating, and braking, all while keeping an eye on their in-game map to find the best routes. Keep your cool under pressure, as passengers expect timely arrivals. The more proficient you become at driving, the higher your rating, leading to exciting upgrades for your taxi and new challenges to conquer.

How to play free City Taxi Simulator game online

To play City Taxi Simulator, start by choosing your taxi. Once in the game, follow the on-screen prompts to pick up passengers. Use the controls to navigate the streets, following the highlighted routes on the map to reach your destinations quickly and safely. Remember to keep an eye on the time to maximize your rewards!

Arrow keys - move Space - brake
