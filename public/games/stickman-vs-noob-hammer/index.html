Stickman vs Noob Hammer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Stickman vs Noob Hammer! Get ready to embark on an exciting journey where you and your friend will navigate through a series of engaging levels using nothing but a trusty hammer. This unique gameplay mechanic requires you to leverage the hammer effectively, allowing for dynamic movement as you swing back and forth. The objective is to skillfully maneuver your stickman character to reach the coveted lucky box at the end of each level.

As you and your companion journey through the game, be prepared to face various challenging obstacles along the way. Each level is designed to test your coordination and timing, making it essential to be cautious and strategic in your movements. Whether you re trying to avoid pitfalls or navigate tricky traps, teamwork is key to overcoming the hurdles that lie between you and victory. Make sure to work together and execute perfectly timed swings to reach new heights in the game!

Will you and your friend succeed in grabbing the lucky box? With every successful reach, a new level opens up, leading to even greater challenges and rewards. So gather your skills and gear up for a hilariously fun adventure filled with unexpected surprises!

How to play free Stickman vs Noob Hammer game online

To play the Stickman vs Noob Hammer game, simply use your mouse or keyboard to swing your hammer back and forth. Click or press to release the hammer at the right moment to propel yourself and your friend forward. Collect the lucky boxes while avoiding obstacles to progress to the next level!


Instructions

Use WASD Keys to play player 1 Use Arrow keys to play player 2 The game can be played on mobile
