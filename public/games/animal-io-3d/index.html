Animal.io 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Animal.io 3D, an exciting multiplayer online experience where you can unleash your competitive side in the wild! Engage in an exhilarating battle of wits and reflexes as you control adorable animals navigating a perilous platform. The objective is simple: use your character's tail to push opponents off the edge while trying to maintain your own footing.

Throughout the game, you'll encounter various food items that enhance your chances of victory. Consuming meat will increase your size, allowing you to push smaller opponents off with ease. Meanwhile, hamburgers will give you an extra boost by lengthening your tail, making it easier to strike from a distance. If you're feeling bold, pick up a mushroom, which will shrink your size but increase your speed, letting you dart around swiftly and evade enemies. With strategy and skill, eliminate your foes to climb the leaderboard and unlock more delightful animals to play with!

Featuring colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, Animal.io 3D is designed for both individual skill and social fun, making it a perfect choice for friends and family. The thrill of the game lies in outsmarting your opponents while flaunting your unique animal character. So gear up, gather your courage, and dive into the colorful world of Animal.io 3D!

How to play free Animal.io 3D game online

To play Animal.io 3D, start by choosing your animal and entering the arena. Use arrow keys or swipe on your device to navigate your character. Swing your tail to push other animals off the platform while collecting power-ups like meat, hamburgers, and mushrooms to enhance your abilities and size. Eliminate opponents to secure your win!
