Halloween Mania

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the spooky world of Halloween Mania, where the Castle is alive with terrified monsters and whimsical garden creatures! This classic Match 3 casual game offers an engaging experience with 60 thrilling levels, each presenting unique objectives that will challenge your puzzle-solving skills. Your mission is to create combinations by matching at least three monsters of the same color, clearing them from the game board.

As you progress, discover the thrill of combining four or more monsters to generate powerful tiles that can cause explosive reactions across the field, clearing vast areas and unlocking score bonuses. Each level is designed not just to test your matching abilities, but to keep you on your toes with varying challenges and intricate layouts. Strive to clear all levels while aiming for a perfect three-star rating, which will reward you with maximum points. Plan your moves strategically and encounter increasingly complex levels as you continue to advance in Halloween Mania!

How to play free Halloween Mania game online

To dive into the game, simply drag and match at least three monsters of the same color on the game board. Aim to create larger combinations for more powerful explosions. Keep an eye on your objectives for each level, and strive to earn a three-star rating by maximizing your points with clever matches!
