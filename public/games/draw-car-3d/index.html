Draw Car 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Draw Car 3D, a captivating blend of drawing puzzles and thrilling car racing challenges. In this engaging arcade game, your artistic skills will determine the outcome of thrilling races against AI opponents. Each level presents a unique drawing puzzle, where you must sketch a car body atop the wheels provided. The ingenuity of your design is key to outsmarting your AI challenger.

As you progress through the levels, you'll collect coins that allow you to unlock a variety of stylish skins in the shop. The more creative and aerodynamic your car design, the greater your chances of speeding past your rivals. With each race, you'll face an increasing level of difficulty, testing not only your creativity but also your ability to execute your designs on the go. Draw Car 3D promises endless fun as you strive to master the art of drawing and racing.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to drawing-based challenges, Draw Car 3D offers an enjoyable experience filled with vibrant graphics and exciting gameplay. The ability to customize your vehicles with new skins keeps the game fresh, ensuring that players will want to continue improving their skills and racing to victory.

How to play free Draw Car 3D game online

To play Draw Car 3D, start by selecting a level to begin your race. Use your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to draw the shape of your car on the wheels provided. Once finished, watch your creation zoom forward in the race. Accumulate coins during each race to purchase new skins in the shop for enhanced customization.
