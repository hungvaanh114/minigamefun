How to play free Direct Water Puzzle game online

To play Direct Water Puzzle, simply use your mouse or finger to create pathways that allow water to flow toward the cup. Click or tap on various items to manipulate them and clear the way. Your goal is to fill the cup in each level, so plan your moves wisely!
