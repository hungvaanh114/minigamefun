Step into the chaotic and hilarious world of Funny Battle - War Simulator, where you take charge of a massive army consisting of a variety of hilarious and unique units. Unleash your tactical prowess as you command infantry and archers to battle, all while deploying Cyclops and fierce Spartans on the field. Experience the thrill of watching your grenadiers unleash a flurry of grenades toward the enemy, and take control of the mighty Zeus to strike foes with divine lightning!

Your goal is to obliterate all opponents, showcasing your skills as the ultimate military strategist. The game features large-scale battles that are not only spectacular but also infused with humor, making for an entertaining experience. Each encounter is powered by advanced ragdoll physics, adding to the absurdity as enemies tumble and fall in delightfully comical ways.

The stunning graphics will immerse you in a beautifully crafted battlefield, where the animation of every character and unit brings life to this epic war simulator. Prove your might and strategy as you crush your enemies and rise to the title of the best general worldwide. Are you ready to lead your troops to victory in this quirky yet intense showdown?

How to play free Funny Battle - War Simulator game online

To start playing Funny Battle - War Simulator, simply choose your units and arrange them on the battlefield. Click to deploy your army and watch the chaos unfold as you strategically command your troops to engage the enemy. Utilize special abilities like Zeus's lightning strikes to gain the upper hand in each battle!
