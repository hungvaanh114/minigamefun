How to play free Fashion Doll Dream House Design & Decorating game online

To play the Fashion Doll Dream House Design & Decorating game, simply choose a room you wish to decorate. Use the menu to select wallpaper, furniture, and accessories. Drag and drop items into place to arrange your perfect space. Don't forget to save your designs and share them with friends!
