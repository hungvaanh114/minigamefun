Monster Truck Hidden Stars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Monster Truck Hidden Stars is an exciting free online skill game that challenges players to hone their observation abilities. Dive into a vibrant world filled with thrilling monster truck imagery, where you are tasked with locating hidden stars scattered across various scenes. Each level presents a unique challenge, containing a total of 10 stars waiting to be discovered.

This game features six engaging levels, each increasing in complexity as you progress. With a limited time to complete each stage, players must be quick and efficient in their search for these elusive objects. The pressure mounts as the clock ticks down, demanding your sharp focus and speed. However, be cautious! A wrong click in the scene not only wastes valuable time but also penalizes you with an additional 5 seconds deducted from your timer. The goal is clear - find all hidden stars before the clock runs out!

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating challenge that tests both your observational skills and your ability to work under pressure. If you re ready to put your skills to the test, jump right into the game, enjoy each level s unique challenges, and experience the fun excitement Monster Truck Hidden Stars has to offer!

How to play free Monster Truck Hidden Stars game online

To play Monster Truck Hidden Stars, start by selecting a level from the main menu. Once you re in, use your mouse or touchpad to click on the images and locate the hidden stars. Remember to keep an eye on the timer and avoid incorrect clicks, as they will penalize you. Good luck!
