How to play free Cube Sort: Paper Note game online

To play Cube Sort: Paper Note, simply drag and drop the cubes onto the platforms to match them with their corresponding colors. Carefully plan your moves to ensure each cube is placed correctly; once all cubes are sorted, you can move on to the next, more challenging level. Enjoy!
