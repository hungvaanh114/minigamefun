Jack in the Tower

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Jack In The Tower is a thrilling spin on the endless runner and endless flyer genres, inviting players to join Jack, the beloved pumpkin character, on an exhilarating ascent to the heavens. Your objective is clear: guide Jack as he leaps higher and higher, navigating a myriad of treacherous obstacles that stand between him and his lofty goals.

As you soar upward, beware of rolling mines, sharp spikes, and pesky bats that threaten to take Jack down. Your reflexes and quick decision-making skills will be put to the test as you sharply maneuver Jack through these hazards. Along the way, keep an eye out for shimmering coins that you can collect to boost your score, providing an extra layer of challenge and excitement to the gameplay.

But that's not all Jack In The Tower has a competitive edge as well! Players can engage with friends through local leaderboards, competing to see who can achieve the highest score. This adds a social element that makes the game even more captivating and encourages replayability. Whether you're a casual player or a seasoned gamer, Jack In The Tower promises a mix of addictive fun, skillful maneuvers, and challenging gameplay.

How to play free Jack In The Tower game online

To play Jack In The Tower, simply tap or click to make Jack jump and steer him away from obstacles. Use precise timing to collect coins while dodging dangers. The higher Jack climbs, the more challenging it gets, so stay alert and aim for the best score!
