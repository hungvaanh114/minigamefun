Fight Bros

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Fight Bros, where strategy and skill come together for an action-packed showdown! Have you ever aimed and released a stone using a slingshot? Now s your chance to put your abilities to the test against a friend! This engaging two-player game invites you to challenge your buddy in a frenzied battle of wits and aim. With each player having 3 lives, the objective is to outsmart and outshoot your opponent until they ve lost all their lives.

Fight Bros features three unique maps that add variety and excitement to each match. As you both take aim and launch projectiles, keep an eye out for falling superpowers from the sky. Grab the gift boxes to obtain powerful boosts that can turn the tide of battle in your favor! Utilize the quirky arsenal at your disposal, including fish, oranges, and stars, to secure victory over your rival. Every match is a test of agility, precision, and quick thinking can you come out on top in this whimsical brawl?

How to play free Fight Bros game online

Jump into the action of Fight Bros by using the on-screen controls to aim and launch your slingshot at your opponent. Move around to dodge incoming projectiles while collecting power-ups that appear on the battlefield. Remember to keep an eye on your lives, as once you lose all three, it s game over!


Instructions

Player 1 can move with the WASD keys You can shoot with the S key Player 2 can move with the Arrow Keys keys You can shoot with the Down Arrow key Mobile play is available
