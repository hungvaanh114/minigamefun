Witch Dash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spellbinding adventure in Witch Dash this Halloween! You take on the role of a daring witch who must brave the rising sun. As daylight approaches, she must use her magical skills to thwart its arrival. But how can she succeed? The answer lies in collecting candies and drinking elixirs scattered throughout the challenging landscape.

As you navigate through the enchanting yet perilous terrain, each candy you collect adds a precious two seconds to your countdown timer, giving you more time to work your magic. To keep the darkness alive, reach for those elusive elixirs! These magical potions will reset your timer back to the maximum, allowing you to continue your spellbinding quest for a longer time.

The game s vibrant graphics and whimsical sound effects will draw you into a world brimming with excitement and challenges. Each moment counts as you race against time to gather as many treats as possible while avoiding obstacles that could slow you down. The urgency builds as the sun climbs higher, making every decision crucial. Will you be quick enough to preserve the night and dance in the shadows?

How to play free Witch Dash game online

To play Witch Dash, simply guide your witch character using your keyboard or touchscreen controls. Move swiftly to collect candies and elixirs while avoiding any obstacles in your path. Utilize the candies to extend your playtime and the elixirs to reset your timer. Stay alert and act quickly to keep the sun at bay!
