Candy Switch

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing "Candy Switch", an exhilarating game that tests your agility and reflexes! In this colorful adventure, you control a small candy ball that can change its color based on the situation. Your primary objective is to keep the ball soaring high, navigating through a variety of challenging obstacles that come in different shapes and sizes.

The gameplay is simple yet addictive. As you tap the screen, your candy ball jumps, and you must ensure that it passes through obstacles that match its current color. Mastering the timing and rhythm of your jumps is crucial to achieve high scores and maintain your progress. The game is designed to keep you on your toes as it becomes increasingly challenging with each level. Are you up for the sweet challenge? Test your skills and see how long you can keep your candy ball afloat!

How to play free Candy Switch game online

To play Candy Switch, simply tap the screen to make your candy ball jump. Keep an eye on the obstacles ahead and steer your ball toward those that match its color. If you land on an obstacle of a different color, it s game over! Stay focused and aim for a high score!
