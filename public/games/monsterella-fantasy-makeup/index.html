Monsterella Fantasy Makeup

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the monstrous world of beauty with Monsterella Fantasy Makeup. This spookily engaging game lets you work your makeover magic on three iconic Monster High characters - Frankie Stein, Clawdeen Wolf, and Draculaura. In Frankies unit, electrify her look with eerie eyeshadows, neon lipstick, shockingly cool hairstyles, and otherworldly outfits. Next, embrace Clawdeens earthy mystique with jewel-toned makeup and howl-inducing wardrobe choices. Finally, express Draculauras vamp chic vibe with dramatic makeup and goth-glam ensembles. Immerse yourself in monstrous glamour and let your creativity run wild in this spooktacular makeup adve
