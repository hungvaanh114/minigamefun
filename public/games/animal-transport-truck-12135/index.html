Animal Transport Truck

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to take on an exhilarating adventure with the Animal Transport Truck? This exciting game invites players to experience the thrill of driving a truck designed for transporting farm animals. Set against a picturesque rural backdrop, players will navigate through a vibrant village where diverse farm animals, such as Horses, Bulls, and Ibex, await their journey.

Your primary mission in this immersive game is to efficiently pick up these farm animals from one location and transport them safely to their designated farms. The unique aspect of this game is the ability to not only drive but also fly your transport truck. As you race across the skies, you ll encounter challenges that will test your skills as a proficient flying truck driver.

In Animal Transport Truck, each level is teeming with realistic environments that create an authentic feel of farm life. With advanced features, including a well-developed flying truck and stunning 3D graphics, players are in for a true treat. You'll have the opportunity to experience manual loading and unloading processes, ensuring that you treat every animal with care throughout their journey.

With a time limit in place, your efficiency plays a crucial role in succeeding. As players take on this responsibility, they'll earn cash rewards that allow them to unlock new levels and enhancements. Whether you re a fan of farming or simply adore animals, this game is the perfect fit for you as it combines the joy of driving with the thrill of animal transportation.

How to play free Animal Transport Truck game online

To play the Animal Transport Truck game, simply load your farm animals onto the truck by maneuvering the vehicle close to them. Use the controls to drive the truck to the destination, ensuring you stick to the time limit. Upon arrival, unload the animals manually and earn rewards!
