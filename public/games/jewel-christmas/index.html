How to play free Jewel Christmas game online

To play Jewel Christmas, simply swap adjacent items to create a line of three or more identical items. This will cause them to explode, earning you points. Strategically plan your moves in each festive level, aiming to reach the target score or complete the objectives within the allotted moves or time. Enjoy the festive fun!
