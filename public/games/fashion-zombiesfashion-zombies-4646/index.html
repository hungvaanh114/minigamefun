Join the wild ride in Fashion Zombies, where a horde of fashionable zombies is on the rampage! Set in a vibrant cityscape, this game challenges you to lead your army of undead fashionistas as they wreak havoc and turn unsuspecting pedestrians into fellow zombies. Your mission? To grow your zombie horde to monstrous proportions while outrunning obstacles and enemies.

In Fashion Zombies, players are encouraged to munch on friends and rivals alike, launching yourself into insane races filled with chaos and destruction. Navigate through an expansive world, unleashing a variety of unique power-ups and bonuses that enhance your gameplay. From ninja gear and dragon rides to UFO encounters, every game session promises an exhilarating experience.

With over 200 million players globally, this game has made a name for itself, with accolades praising its addictiveness and sheer fun factor. The one-touch gameplay allows players of all skill levels to jump straight into the action, while the countless upgrades and rewards ensure that boredom is never an option. Take on over 300 missions across 11 different environments, discover the powers of zombie birds, and personalize your horde for maximum effect. It's time to gear up and prepare for an unforgettable zombie takeover!

How to play free Fashion Zombies game online

To start playing Fashion Zombies, simply tap the screen to control your horde of zombies. Collect coins and power-ups while dodging obstacles. Use your earned bonuses for powerful boosts and complete missions to enhance your zombie army. Enjoy the chaotic and thrilling race towards ultimate domination!
