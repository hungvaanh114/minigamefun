Hoho #039;s Burger Stacko

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Hoho's Burger Stacko, where you can unleash your inner chef and burger architect! In this exhilarating game, you'll have the chance to create and stack delicious burgers to serve to a diverse array of customers. The aim is simple yet addictive: assemble the perfect burger stack that keeps your customers coming back for more. The higher and more impressive your burger creation, the more money you can earn!

Test your skills as you juggle various ingredients, including patties, cheese, fresh veggies, and mouth-watering sauces. With each successful order, take your cooking game to new heights as you expand your menu and cater to the unique tastes of your customers. The more visually appealing your burger, the happier your customers will be! Enjoy the challenges and beat your records as you aim to become the best burger stacker in town.

Dive into exciting challenges as different customers come in with special requests and time limits. Plan your moves wisely and strategize on how to stack your burgers perfectly while managing your time efficiently. With Hoho's Burger Stacko, every level offers fresh opportunities to impress and earn coins which can be used for upgrades and special items. Start stacking, and let your burger-building adventure begin!

How to play free Hoho's Burger Stacko game online

To play Hoho's Burger Stacko, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to drag and drop burger ingredients onto the stack. Aim to balance the ingredients perfectly to prevent your stack from toppling over. Serve the completed burgers to your customers while keeping an eye on the time! Enjoy stacking!
