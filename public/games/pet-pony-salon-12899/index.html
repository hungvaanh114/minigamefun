Pet Pony Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Do you have a passion for horse care? If so, then the "Pet Pony Salon" game is the perfect digital escape for you! This delightful salon simulation allows you to unleash your creativity by styling the hair of your very own pet pony. With endless possibilities, you can experiment with a variety of fun and unique hairstyles, giving your pony a fabulous transformation. Not only can you change their look at any time, but you can also explore styles you may have never considered before!

Your journey begins by pampering your horse. Use lots of soapy water to wash away dirt and prepare your pony for the ultimate makeover. Once your pet is clean and fresh, dive into the exciting world of styling! Choose from an array of accessories, ranging from colorful bows to shiny tiaras, to decorate your horse to perfection. Spend countless hours crafting a stylish look that showcases your personality and flair.

As you progress through the levels, completing tasks and beautifying your pony unlocks new accessories and styling options. With each completed makeover, a whole new world of creativity unfolds, allowing you to keep your horse looking trendy and chic. Join in on the fun and make your pony the talk of the town with stylish hairstyles that everyone will adore!

How to play free pet pony salon game online

To play the game, start by washing your pony to remove any dirt. Once clean, navigate through the styling options using the menu. Select hairstyles and accessories, drag them onto your pony, and get creative! Experiment with different looks, and complete each level for more exciting options!
