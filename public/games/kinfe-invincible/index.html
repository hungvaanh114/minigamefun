Kinfe Invincible

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Knife Invincible" is a captivating and straightforward relaxation game that immerses players in the role of a professional chef. Your mission? To skillfully slice various vegetables while navigating through a series of levels. By simply holding the screen and swiping the kitchen knife, you'll transform an array of ingredients into perfectly thin slices.

The game draws inspiration from classics like Fruit Ninja, offering an easy-to-learn yet challenging experience. While it may not pose a significant challenge initially, players will quickly find that honing their hand speed is essential for success. As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter a diverse range of vegetables, each with its own characteristics and required techniques for slicing.

As vegetables glide along a conveyor belt, your primary goal is to slice them efficiently while achieving the level's clearance objectives. This engaging gameplay not only serves as a delightful distraction but also acts as a fantastic stress reliever. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day or simply want to enjoy a fun and simple game, Knife Invincible delivers a satisfying experience that keeps players coming back for more. Get ready to showcase your culinary prowess and slice your way to victory!

How to play free Knife Invincible game online

To play Knife Invincible, simply tap and hold your screen to control the kitchen knife. Move it left and right as vegetables appear on the conveyor belt. Your goal is to slice them into thin pieces before they drop off the edge! Complete each level's objectives to advance in the game. Enjoy!
