Instructions:

In the delightful world of Girls Tea Party Cooking, you'll join Sofia as she plans a charming garden tea party to reconnect with her friends. With her heart set on creating a magical experience, Sofia is determined to whip up an array of scrumptious treats, including mouthwatering cupcakes and a colorful fruit platter. Your culinary skills will be essential in helping her prepare the perfect delicacies to impress her guests.

As you venture into the kitchen, gather the fresh ingredients and follow Sofia's recipes to bake fluffy cupcakes adorned with icing and sprinkles. Each cupcake is a canvas for your creativity, so let your imagination run wild! Meanwhile, arrange a tantalizing fruit platter that bursts with vibrant colors, showcasing a variety of juicy fruits that will surely excite her friends' taste buds.

But a tea party is not just about food; it s also about fashion! Once the culinary creations are complete, it s time to assist Sofia in selecting the perfect outfit. From stylish dresses to cute accessories, help her find that dazzling look that will make her the star of the tea party. Coordinate colors and styles to reflect the joyous atmosphere Sofia hopes to create. Get ready for a fun-filled adventure as you help Sofia host the perfect gathering!

How to play free Girls Tea Party Cooking game online

To play the Girls Tea Party Cooking game online, simply click on the interactive icons within the game interface. Follow the step-by-step cooking instructions to prepare the delicious cupcakes and fruit platter. After cooking, browse through Sofia's wardrobe to select the most beautiful outfits and accessories for her to wear at the tea party.
