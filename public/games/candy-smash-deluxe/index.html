Candy Smash Deluxe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Candy Smash Deluxe, an exciting puzzle game that will put your strategic thinking to the test! In this vibrant, candy-themed universe, your goal is to slide blocks of the same color together in order to eliminate them and score points. The rules are simple yet captivating: match identical blocks by sliding them into one another, but be mindful of your moves, as each additional slide reduces your score! With a focus on efficiency, you'll need to seek out the optimal pathways to clear all blocks with the least number of moves.

Each successful block elimination awards you with 100 points, but if you move a block more than once before achieving a match, you incur a penalty of 10 points for each extra move. This unique mechanic encourages players to plan their strategies carefully and think several steps ahead. The game features a total of 24 challenging levels that gradually increase in difficulty, ensuring that players remain engaged as they navigate through colorful, candy-filled landscapes. Take on the challenge as you work your way through each level, placing your skills and wits against delightful yet tricky puzzles!

How to play free Candy Smash Deluxe game online

To start playing Candy Smash Deluxe, simply select the block you wish to slide. Drag it towards the identical block to make a match. Remember to aim for the fewest moves possible to maximize your score. Complete each level by clearing all blocks, and enjoy the journey through the sweet challenges that await!
