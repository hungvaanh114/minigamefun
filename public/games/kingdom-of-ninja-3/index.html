How to play free Kingdom of Ninja 3 game online

To play the Kingdom of Ninja 3 online, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move and jump. Press the spacebar to attack your enemies. Watch out for falling obstacles and monsters as you advance through each level. Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities and complete each level to face the boss!
