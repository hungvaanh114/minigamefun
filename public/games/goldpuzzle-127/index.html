GoldPuzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to GoldPuzzle, a captivating and glimmering puzzle game that invites you to immerse yourself in a world of dazzling gold. Your mission is to collect as much gold as possible by strategically eliminating gold nuggets that appear on your screen. With each move, you have the chance to drag gold pieces to fill either a line or a column, leading to exhilarating eliminations that help you gather this precious resource.

GoldPuzzle is not just about the ordinary gold; you can also collect premium gold and even rare purple gold. Each type of gold nugget carries a different value, adding an exciting layer of strategy to the gameplay. When you reach a point where you can't make any more moves, fear not! You have the option to consume your collected gold to replace a nugget, or even watch an advertisement to receive a new gold nugget, keeping the game moving. Additionally, every time you achieve a significant elimination, you will unlock extra rewards, making your gold-collecting mission even more thrilling.

As you progress through the levels, watch as your collection of gold swells, while challenging your puzzle-solving skills with an array of intricate puzzles. The breathtaking graphics and captivating sound effects create an immersive experience, ensuring that you stay engaged and entertained throughout your journey in GoldPuzzle.

How to play free GoldPuzzle game online

To play GoldPuzzle, simply drag the gold nuggets onto the board to form lines or columns of matching pieces. Eliminate them to collect gold and earn points. If you find yourself stuck, use your gold to replace a nugget or watch an advertisement for a new chance. Aim for large combinations to reap extra rewards!
