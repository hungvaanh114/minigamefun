5 Night at Green Head Monster

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Five Nights at Green Head Monster 2 is a chilling 3D horror game where players must endure five terrifying nights in a small, dimly lit hotel operated by the mysterious and ominous Green Head Monster. What initially seems like a simple overnight stay quickly turns into a nerve-wracking battle for survival as strange occurrences begin to unfold.
