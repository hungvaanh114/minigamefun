Type Sprint

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to put your typing skills to the test? Welcome to Type Sprint, an engaging blend of a racing game and a typing challenge that will both entertain and improve your keyboard skills. In this exhilarating 3D adventure, you control stickman players racing through vibrant and dynamic environments.

In Type Sprint, the faster you type, the further your character will run. Every correctly typed word generates platforms that propel your stickman towards victory. As you aim to be among the top three racers, speed and accuracy become your best friends. Whether you prefer typing on a PC or a mobile device, Type Sprint caters to both preferences, although many players find PC typing to be more accessible.

The game combines fun and relaxation, making it an ideal way to unwind while enhancing your typing abilities. As you progress, you'll encounter various challenges that will keep you engaged and eager to improve. Compete against friends or solo, and watch as your typing proficiency translates into racing success. Good luck, and most importantly, have fun on this unique typing journey!

How to play free Type Sprint game online

To start playing Type Sprint, simply select your game mode and choose your stickman character. When the race begins, type the words displayed on your screen quickly and accurately to generate platforms for your character. Aim to type faster than your competitors to secure your lead and cross the finish line first!
