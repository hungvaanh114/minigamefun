Welcome to Bunnyville, a whimsical realm where our hero, Jack the pumpkin, aspires to make his mark as the ultimate egg collector. Each year, Bunnyville ignites with excitement as the renowned Egg-stravaganza takes center stage. This vibrant festival challenges Jack to showcase his agility and determination in a dazzling quest to collect magical colored eggs that float through the air.

Throughout his journey, Jack faces an array of exhilarating obstacles. As he hops and leaps, he must skillfully dodge shifting elastics that spring into action at every turn. It s a mesmerizing yet tricky environment, where each moment could lead to treasure or trouble. Hidden traps lie in wait to hinder his progress, making it essential for Jack to stay sharp and quick-witted as he navigates the bustling festivities.

The vibrant scenery of Bunnyville adds to the allure of the Egg-stravaganza, with colorful landscapes and enchanting music accompanying Jack s thrilling escapades. The stakes are high, and with each leap, Jack not only chases his dreams but also experiences the laughter and joy that comes with competing in such a spirited event. Will Jack be able to collect enough magical eggs to claim the title of the greatest egg collector in Bunnyville?

How to play free Funny Jack game online

To start playing Funny Jack, simply use the arrow keys to control Jack's movements. Jump over the moving elastics by pressing the spacebar. Collect as many colored eggs as you can while avoiding the obstacles and traps. Keep an eye on your score as you strive to beat your own record!
