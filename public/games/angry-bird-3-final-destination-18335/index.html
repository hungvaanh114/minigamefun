Angry Bird 3 Final Destination

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant universe of Angry Bird 3: Final Destination, where Bubble Shooters unite for an exciting adventure! Immerse yourself in this captivating bubble-blasting game, where your objective is to match three or more bubbles of the same color to release adorable birds trapped within. With over 1000 intricately designed levels to conquer, fresh challenges arrive every week to keep the thrill alive!

Explore a charming bubble-themed world filled with delightful dogs waiting to be outfitted in cute costumes. Command powerful effects and enjoy breathtaking graphics as you mix and match boosters for an explosive experience. Grab the fantastic daily bonuses, spin the lucky wheel, and utilize special power-ups to tackle the more difficult puzzles while you work to save the baby birds.

Connect with friends on Facebook to sync your progress, challenge their scores, and see who the ultimate bubble shooter is! As you crush, burst, and pop through varied levels, hone your skills in this wholly free bubble shooter saga and join a burgeoning community of over 1 million players. Dive into this thrilling adventure as you embark on a journey to rescue the birds and embellish your gaming experience!

How to play free Angry Bird 3 Final Destination game online

To play Angry Bird 3, simply aim your bubble shooter, match three or more bubbles of the same color, and tap to shoot! Clear levels by rescuing birds and utilizing power-ups strategically. Keep an eye on the required objectives and enjoy fabulous visual effects as you progress through each level!
