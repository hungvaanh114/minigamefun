How to play free Minesweeper Deluxe game online

To play Minesweeper Deluxe online, simply click on any square to start revealing tiles. Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that square. Use this information to deduce safe spots while avoiding the mines. Right-click to flag suspected mines and clear the board without detonating any explosives!
