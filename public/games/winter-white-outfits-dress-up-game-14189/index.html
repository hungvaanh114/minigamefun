Just because summer has come to an end doesn t mean you have to pack away your fabulous white outfits! Embrace your stylish side this winter and show off your timeless white wardrobe. With the right accessories, including chic leather boots and a snuggly teddy coat, you can make a white dress perfect for the chilly season. Join our three best friends Elsa, Harley Quinn, and Ariana Grande in this free fashion lesson, where you ll learn to style all your favorite white pieces to fit winter s vibe.

Your adventure begins with Queen Elsa, who is eager to explore feminine styles this winter. From mini dresses to midi skirts, shirts, knitted blouses, and stylish coats, there s plenty to choose from. Delve into her wardrobe, mix and match her fashion items, and curate a dazzling white outfit. Don't forget to add a designer handbag, a chic hairstyle, and a bold makeup look!

Next up is the stylish Harley Quinn. Known as the most fashionable villain, she loves knitted sets and cropped jackets. Select the perfect combination for her and finalize her ensemble with a trendy makeup look in your preferred colors.

Lastly, it s time to style Ariana Grande. Head to the final page of the game to discover her fabulous wardrobe and create an unforgettable winter outfit that will turn heads. Once you finish dressing all three fashionistas, save your creations and share them in the comments below! Enjoy playing the Winter White Outfits dress-up game for girls online at Dop5.com!
