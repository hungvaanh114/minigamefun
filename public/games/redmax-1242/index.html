Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating journey in the world of Redmax, where your reflexes and speed will be put to the ultimate test! In this thrilling game, your primary mission is to evade a colossal cube monster that relentlessly pursues you. As you dash through various obstacles, you must stay focused on the goal of escaping the gigantic threat. The adrenaline rush of the chase will keep you on your toes!

As you speed away from the ominous red cube, your path will be dotted with sparkling coins and essential keys. Collecting these items will not only enrich your gameplay experience but will also help you reunite with your friends who eagerly await your safe arrival at the end of each level. Make sure to grab the keys, as they are crucial for unlocking the doors that lead to the next thrilling stage of your adventure.

The game is designed to keep you engaged with its vibrant graphics and fluid mechanics. Your mission is clear: run fast, collect items, and most importantly, stay ahead of the pursuing cube monster. Teamwork is essential, as you will need to hold the hands of your friends at the finish line to successfully complete each level and progress to the next challenge. Will you rise to the occasion and conquer your fears?

How to play free Redmax game online

To play the Redmax game, simply use your keyboard or touchscreen to navigate your character through the levels. Tap or press the arrow keys to run and jump over obstacles while collecting keys and coins. Keep an eye on the giant cube monster behind you, and make sure to reach the end of the level safely!
