Be a Pirate

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in the engaging world of "Be a Pirate" where your mission is straightforward: dash from door to door. However, this journey is fraught with challenges. As you race against the clock, you must gather coins scattered throughout each level some cleverly concealed, requiring you to solve intricate riddles and complete various tasks.

But beware! Dangerous foes lurk around every corner. You'll encounter fierce rival pirates, treacherous cannons, man-eating whales that explode with bombs, and even mischievous live cucumbers. Each enemy poses a unique threat, intensifying the urgency of your quest. For those looking to increase their treasure trove of stars, you can take on additional challenges defeating all enemies on the map using your bombs grants you bonus points and higher rewards per level.

This pixel art platformer, featuring a charming side-scrolling design, enhances the whimsical yet perilous aspects of your pirate escapade. The vibrant retro graphics transport you to a world brimming with adventure, encouraging you to think strategically as you navigate the diverse levels filled with hidden surprises and exhilarating action.

How to play free Be a Pirate game online

To play "Be a Pirate," simply use the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Jump to reach higher platforms, and press the designated button to throw bombs at enemies. Collect coins and solve puzzles within the time limit to complete each level. Enjoy your pirate journey filled with excitement!


Instructions

A D - moving right left Space - Jump E - drop bomb F - Open door
