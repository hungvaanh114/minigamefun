Sponge Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Sponge Challenge, you command a bouncy, yellow sponge through a captivating 2D puzzle platform adventure. Your mission is to guide the flexible sponge in collecting precious purple diamonds while maneuvering through levels packed with obstacles and devious traps. Each stage presents a unique puzzle that requires you to leverage the sponges sticky abilities to climb walls, jump between surfaces, and discover the perfect path to the magical portal that leads to subsequent levels. The complexity of each level increases as you progress, requiring both precision and quick thinking.
