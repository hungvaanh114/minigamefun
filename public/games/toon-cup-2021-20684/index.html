Toon Cup 2021

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Football is back and better than ever with Toon Cup 2021, the exciting and completely free football game that allows players to dive into the colorful world of their favorite Cartoon Network characters! Choose from a vast lineup that includes Darwin from *The Amazing World of Gumball*, Grizz from *We Bare Bears*, and Apple from *Apple & Onion*. Hone your skills, work together with your teammates, and aim for the top of the leaderboard in the most thrilling Toon Cup Tournament yet!

Create a powerful team by strategically selecting players based on their unique stats and powers. This year, new characters like Jessica from *Craig of the Creek*, Elliott and Mo from *Elliott From Earth*, and Beast Boy from *Teen Titans Go!* join the roster. You can also pick from beloved characters including Batgirl, Wonder Woman, Craig, Kelsey, Finn, Jake, and many more from iconic shows. The possibilities for team combinations are endless!

In this exhilarating football championship, you can choose your country and compete against others worldwide, striving to become the ultimate football champion. Score goals while effectively defending your net against a fierce opponent and their cunning goalkeeper! Utilize tackles, dribbles, passes, and shots while keeping an eye out for the epic power-ups, such as Banana Slip and Super Speed, that can turn the tide of the game.

Explore the new Toon Leagues gameplay mode by creating a team to battle against Super Squads. Earn points through winning matches and strive to unlock fantastic trophies from each League. With tons of unlockables including character upgrades, themed stadiums, and special footballs your journey to excellence awaits!

How to play free Toon Cup 2021 game online

To play Toon Cup 2021, simply start by selecting your favorite Cartoon Network characters to form your team. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate, tackle, dribble, and shoot the ball. Aim to score against your opponents while collecting power-ups to enhance your gameplay. Good luck!
