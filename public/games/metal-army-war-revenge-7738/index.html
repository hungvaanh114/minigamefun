Metal Army War: Revenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Metal Army War: Revenge," the relentless battle for the planet's security escalates as the Metal Army unveils its sinister plan deep within the heart of the jungle. Players are called upon to assist brave heroes in a fierce showdown against these menacing robots. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, it s time to gear up and prepare for intense warfare.

This thrilling episode challenges players to confront a new wave of enemies equipped with advanced weaponry and menacing traps. Utilize upgraded offensive tools and strategize your moves to thwart the robot invasions. As you advance through the perilous levels, you will encounter various obstacles that will test your combat skills and resourcefulness.

Collect valuable metallic materials scattered throughout the game to enhance your arsenal and health. These resources are essential for building a well-equipped fighter capable of overcoming any challenge. Remember to visit the Market at the end of each level to spend your hard-earned materials on weapon upgrades and health boosts, ensuring a stronger stance against future foes.

The immersive graphics and pulse-pounding gameplay create an exhilarating experience as you fight to protect humanity from the clutches of the Metal Army. Will you have what it takes to stop their plans and bring peace back to the world? Step into the action and unleash your potential in "Metal Army War: Revenge."

How to play free Metal Army War: Revenge game online

To play "Metal Army War: Revenge," use the arrow keys for movement and the spacebar to shoot. Navigate through levels, defeating enemies and avoiding traps. Collect metallic materials to upgrade your weapons and health by visiting the Market after each level. Stay agile and strategize your attacks to rescue hostages and eliminate threats.
