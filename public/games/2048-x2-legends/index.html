How to play free 2048 X2 Legends game online

To play 2048 X2 Legends, use your keyboard's arrow keys to slide the blocks in the desired direction. When two tiles with the same number touch, they will merge into one, forming a new number. Your goal is to keep combining blocks until you reach the 2048 tile and achieve the highest score possible!
