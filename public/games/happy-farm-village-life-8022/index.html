Happy Farm - Village Life

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Happy Farm - Village Life, where your dream of running a lively and charming animal farm comes to life! Immerse yourself in a vibrant world filled with adorable animals that require your tender care and attention. From playful puppies to cuddly kittens, each creature has its unique personality and needs. Are you ready to step into the role of a loving farm caretaker?

In this delightful game, you ll manage a variety of farm animals, ensuring they are well-fed, clean, and happy. Your daily tasks will include feeding your beloved pets, cleaning their living spaces, and engaging them in playful activities. The more time you spend with these delightful animals, the stronger your bond will become. They thrive on your affection and care, and in return, they will fill your farm with joy and laughter!

The stunning graphics and heartwarming sounds enhance your experience, making every interaction with your whimsical friends even more enjoyable. As you progress through the game, you ll unlock new animals and farming activities, broadening the scope of your farm life. Are you up for a sweet challenge? You ll learn valuable skills as you balance your time and resources while watching your farm flourish and grow. Join in the fun and embrace the wonderful world of animal farming!

How to play free Happy Farm - Village Life game online

To play Happy Farm - Village Life, simply select an animal to care for and follow the on-screen prompts. Feed them by dragging food to their bowls, clean their areas by tapping on dirt spots, and interact with them to play. Watch their happiness levels rise as you nurture them!
