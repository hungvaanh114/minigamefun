Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Zombie Base is an exhilarating design game that plunges players into a thrilling world filled with the undead. As you embark on this adventure, you will face multiple challenging levels, each one presenting unique obstacles and relentless hordes of zombies. Your mission is to fortify your base, survive the onslaught, and upgrade your arsenal. Collecting gold coins is crucial as these resources empower you to enhance your weapons and stock up on ammunition, making it imperative to scavenge wisely and strategically to progress through each level.

In Zombie Base, players are encouraged to explore and innovate. The design elements allow for creativity, as you customize your base to withstand waves of zombie attacks. Balancing resource management with tactical gameplay is key will you focus on upgrading your defense or boosting your offense? Each decision you make can mean the difference between survival and defeat.

As the levels ascend in difficulty, so do the rewards. Keep an eye out for hidden coins and power-ups scattered throughout the game, as they can drastically improve your chances of success. Team up with friends or other players online to exchange strategies and bolster your team's strength. The captivating graphics and immersive sound effects will keep you on the edge of your seat as you navigate this high-stakes environment.

How to play free Zombie Base game online

To play Zombie Base, simply enter the game and start by gathering gold coins scattered around the map. Use these coins to upgrade your weapons and secure your base. As you progress through the levels, remember to defend against approaching zombies while continuing to collect resources for upgrades.
