Car Wash Workshop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare to dive into the exciting world of the Car Wash Workshop, a thrilling blend of smart car parking and car wash gameplay. If you've always wanted to master the art of auto detailing within a dynamic digital workshop environment, your chance has arrived. In this mobile car wash game, you will learn all the ins and outs of keeping your vehicle pristine without the need to search for a car wash service. Experience a wide range of car care tasks, from o-ring servicing to comprehensive garage cleanups, bringing you the full car wash experience.

This game transcends conventional car wash simulations you'll find elsewhere. It combines the best elements of a smart car wash service with a mechanic simulator, allowing you to refine your car parking and driving skills. More than just another parking game, this modern car parking simulator teaches you the nuances of both parking and cleaning luxury vehicles. Welcome to the advanced Car Driving Academy, where you can experience thrilling gameplay that includes real-life driving tests and challenges.

Engage in hands-on learning to become proficient in various tasks, including car washing and vacuuming. Experience the satisfaction of washing and detailing a car correctly as you tackle the mission of keeping your vehicles looking brand new. Indulge in learning the functions of different car services, such as tyre repair, painting, and other mechanic tasks, making you an expert in the art of auto detailing and modern car wash services.

How to play free Car Wash Workshop game online

Once you enter the Car Wash Workshop game, navigate through various levels where you will receive tasks related to washing and parking cars. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete each task, utilizing your car wash skills. Be sure to pay attention to the details and master your parking techniques to earn rewards and progress through the game!
