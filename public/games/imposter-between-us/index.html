IMPOSTER BETWEEN US

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to IMPOSTER BETWEEN US, the thrilling game of deception and deduction set in the far reaches of space! Here, players team up to complete tasks and locate the hidden imposters among them. Your mission is straightforward: identify the traitors and eliminate them before it s too late!

Engage with friends or connect with players from around the globe in this vibrant multiplayer experience. Utilize the in-game chat feature to strategize and share suspicions with your team. As you complete various tasks and mini-games scattered throughout the spaceship, remember that your choices could unearth the imposter lurking among your ranks.

What makes IMPOSTER BETWEEN US uniquely entertaining is the ability to customize your character's appearance. Choose from an array of stylish outfits to showcase your persona within the group. But amidst the fun, maintain vigilance one of your teammates could very well be the imposter waiting to sabotage the mission!

In every game, engage in meetings where you can discuss your findings and vote on who to evict. Your collective decision could lead to victory if you successfully identify the impostor. Yet, there s a twist; if you find yourself as the double-crosser, your goal is to thwart your teammates efforts. Become a cunning saboteur and watch your fellow players fail in their tasks while you quietly plot their downfall.

How to play free IMPOSTER BETWEEN US game online

To begin playing IMPOSTER BETWEEN US, simply join a game room or create your own with friends. During gameplay, navigate the spaceship, complete tasks, and gather clues. Participate in discussions during emergency meetings to vote out suspected imposters, or if you're the imposter, cleverly sabotage the team while avoiding detection!
