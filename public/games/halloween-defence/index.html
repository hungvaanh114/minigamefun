Halloween Defence

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spooky adventure in "Halloween Defence," where the chilling thrill of Halloween takes center stage! In this action-packed game, you must defend yourself against an onslaught of pumpkin-headed foes intent on reaching you. The aim of the game is to eliminate these mischievous pumpkin heads before they can make contact. Each time you successfully shoot down a pumpkin head, you earn valuable score points, adding to your total tally. But be cautious! You only have two lives, and each time a pumpkin head manages to get past you, one of those lives is lost. Lose both lives, and it's game over!

However, not all hope is lost! At the end of your lives, you are granted a second chance. You will be presented with four different pumpkin heads, and your task is to select one. If fortune smiles upon you, you may uncover an extra life, enabling you to continue your fight against the relentless pumpkin invasion. Stay vigilant and aim well, as each pumpkin eradicated gets you one step closer to survival!

How to play free Halloween Defence game online

To play Halloween Defence, use your mouse or touch controls to aim and shoot at the incoming pumpkin heads. Keep an eye on your lives displayed on the screen. When your lives run out, choose one pumpkin head from the options to potentially gain an extra life. Stay focused and have fun!
