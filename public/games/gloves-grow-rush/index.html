Gloves Grow Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Gloves Grow Rush, an exhilarating 3D arcade game where you take control of a nimble stickman boxer. Your mission is to conquer a vertical running gauntlet, collecting an array of boxing gloves as you dash upward. Along the way, face off against a variety of rival boxers owe it to your reflexes and strategy to defeat the weaker foes while cleverly dodging the stronger ones. A direct hit from an opponent s punch spells instant defeat, so stay alert!

As you progress through the levels, you will encounter formidable bosses that require your utmost skill and quick thinking to defeat. Collect precious gems scattered throughout the course to enhance your performance, allowing you to boost your starting level and attack power (ATK) when the challenge becomes tough. The in-game shop offers six unique boxing glove designs, adding a personal touch to your character, although they do not increase your ATK or levels.

The thrill of Gloves Grow Rush is not just in the battles but also in the progression. Can you master the art of evasion, upgrade effectively, and emerge victorious against the final bosses? Prepare yourself for an action-packed adventure filled with excitement and challenges!

How to play free Gloves Grow Rush game online

In Gloves Grow Rush, use the arrow keys or touch controls to navigate your stickman boxer. Jump over obstacles, collect boxing gloves, and dodge powerful opponents. If you come across a stronger boxer, simply evade their punches while trying to reach the end of each level. Good luck!
