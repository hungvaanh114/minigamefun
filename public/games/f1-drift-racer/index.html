How to play free F1 Drift Racer game online

To play F1 Drift Racer, use the arrow keys for steering and accelerating your car. Press and hold the drift key as you approach corners to execute perfect drifts. Aim to complete each level within the time limit while collecting bonuses for improved scores. Enjoy the race!
