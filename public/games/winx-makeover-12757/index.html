Winx Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Winx, where Bloom, Stella, Tecna, and Musa are ready to embrace their love for makeup and fashion! In the vibrant universe of the Winx girls, beauty is not just a routine; it s a way of life. Each girl has her own unique style and personality, making it even more exciting to experiment with various looks. From vibrant hairstyles to dazzling outfits, you have the opportunity to unleash your creativity and transform these magical fairies into the most fabulous versions of themselves.

In Winx Makeover, players are encouraged to explore a wide array of cosmetics and fashion accessories. Choose from an extensive selection of makeup palettes, lipsticks, eyeshadows, and more to create stunning looks that showcase each girl s individuality. Additionally, you can mix and match different hairstyles that range from elegant waves to trendy updos, ensuring that every hairstyle complements their outfits perfectly.

The fun doesn t stop at makeup and hair! Adorn the Winx girls with a selection of enchanting jewelry and fashionable clothing that will truly encapsulate their sparkling personalities. Experiment with various combinations and styles to generate endless possibilities for your fairy friends. The only limit is your imagination, as you delve into the whimsical world of Winx to create unmatched beauty and charm.

How to play free Winx Makeover game online

To play the Winx Makeover game online, start by selecting one of the Winx girls you want to style. Use the mouse to drag and drop different makeup items, hairstyles, and outfits onto her character. Mix and match to find the perfect combination, and don t forget to accessorize with stunning jewelry for that ultimate fairy look!
