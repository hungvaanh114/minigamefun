Embark on a colorful adventure with Save Rainbow: Blue Monster, an engaging puzzle game designed for players who crave excitement, fun, and a touch of challenge, all wrapped in straightforward mechanics. This game invites you to solve puzzles by drawing lines on your screen, creating countless paths to rescue our Rainbow friends from pesky, angry bees!

To play, simply tap on the screen to draw one line and protect the beloved dog while ensuring that your creative solutions do not inadvertently harm it. With each level, you'll find that there's not just one solution; every puzzle can be approached in multiple ways. Prepare yourself for surprising, quirky, and often amusing results as you navigate through the challenges. Each moment promises to keep you entertained while sharpening your problem-solving skills!

Save Rainbow: Blue Monster features a multitude of benefits such as its addictive nature and the relaxing experience it offers. As you progress, you ll encounter diverse puzzles that will test your creativity and ingenuity. Whether you're a casual gamer or a puzzle aficionado, this game is sure to captivate your attention with its unique approach and delightful gameplay.

How to play free Save Rainbow: Blue Monster game online

To play Save Rainbow: Blue Monster, tap on the screen to begin drawing lines that guide the Rainbow friends to safety. Lift your finger once your drawing is complete, and ensure that your lines shield the dog from harm. Experiment with different drawing styles to discover various creative solutions to each puzzling challenge!
