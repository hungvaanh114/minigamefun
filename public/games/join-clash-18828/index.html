Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the exhilarating world of Join & Clash, where two factions, clad in bold red and blue, are constantly at odds on the battlefield. With no weapons in sight, these stickmen soldiers engage in fierce hand-to-hand combat, relying on their wrestling skills to overpower one another. As the action unfolds, it's your moment to shine as the most courageous knight on the field. Rally your allies and lead them into battle, dodging challenges that come your way. With skill and strategy, you can overcome any adversary and capture the ultimate prize your beloved princess.

In this captivating game, every encounter is a test of strength and teamwork. Assemble a formidable force by rescuing your followers, who will faithfully fight alongside you. Navigate through a series of intense skirmishes, confront foes with cunning maneuvers, and prove that you have what it takes to emerge victorious. Each level presents unique obstacles that will put your bravery and tactical abilities to the test. Whether you re wrestling foes or coordinating your team, the thrill of battle is just around the corner.

Are you ready to claim glory and save your princess? The fate of the kingdom lies in your hands as you engage in epic showdowns. Harness your inner hero and join the ranks of those who dare to clash!

How to play free Join & Clash game online

To play Join & Clash, simply navigate your stickman warrior towards the challenges ahead. Use your mouse or keyboard to initiate wrestling moves against the enemy stickmen. Collect followers by defeating them, and guide your growing team to the ultimate boss. Stay vigilant and fight bravely to save your princess and win the game!
