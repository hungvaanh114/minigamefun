Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the captivating world of "Exit the Maze," where a mesmerizing three-dimensional maze awaits your exploration. Your mission is to navigate a ball through this intricate maze and reach the designated exit point. With an engaging visual experience on your screen, you ll find yourself enthralled as you manipulate the environment around you.

As you begin, your character will be positioned at a specific point within the maze. The exit will be clearly visible, but getting there requires skill and strategy. To maneuver the ball to the exit, you'll use the keyboard keys to rotate the entire maze in various directions. This unique mechanic transforms the conventional idea of maze navigation and challenges you to think creatively about every move you make.

Every time you successfully guide the ball to the exit, you'll earn points, adding excitement and motivation to progress to the next level. Each stage introduces new challenges, offering a progressive difficulty curve that keeps the gameplay fresh and engaging. With each level, you ll encounter innovative maze designs and obstacles that will test your problem-solving skills and dexterity.

How to play free Exit the Maze game online

In the game, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to rotate the maze. Guide the ball along the pathways towards the visible exit. Ensure you explore the various paths and avoid any traps or dead ends. Successfully reaching the exit unlocks new levels, where you can earn more points and face greater challenges.
