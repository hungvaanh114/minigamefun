Badger Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Badger Runner, where your cunning and speed will be tested! In this engaging game, you take on the role of Mr. Badger, who finds himself in a rather sticky situation with Mrs. Hen. The story unfolds with a fierce argument sparked by Mr. Badger s insatiable hunger. The cheeky badger has set his sights on Mrs. Hen's precious chicks, who he believes could serve as his next meal. However, Mrs. Hen, feeling furious and protective, is determined to keep her chicks safe.

As the player, you must navigate through the challenging landscape, snatching up those little chicks while avoiding the relentless pursuit of Mrs. Hen. The gameplay revolves around agility and strategy as you maneuver through various obstacles and levels. It s not just about feeding Mr. Badger; it s about outsmarting the wise and powerful Mrs. Hen, who will chase you at every turn. The stakes are high, and only the cleverest players will succeed in accumulating enough points to advance to the next level.

What sets Badger Runner apart are its stunning high-quality graphics, minimal game size, and user-friendly controls that make it accessible for players of all ages. With interesting quests and a variety of challenges, you will find plenty of excitement as you assist Mr. Badger in his quest for food!

How to play free Badger Runner game online

To play Badger Runner, control Mr. Badger using simple on-screen controls, guiding him to collect chicks while dodging Mrs. Hen. Use quick reflexes to evade her grasp and tackle various quests to score points. Progress through the levels while enjoying thrilling graphics and engaging gameplay!
