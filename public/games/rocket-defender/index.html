Rocket Defender

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare to take on an exhilarating challenge in Rocket Defender, a classic arcade game that pits you against an onslaught of meteors threatening Earth's safety. Your mission is clear: operate your powerful cannon to obliterate the meteors before they crash into the ground. Each level presents a new wave of challenges, testing your reflexes and precision as you strive to protect the houses beneath your watchful eye.

As the meteors rain down, keep your focus sharp and your cannon ready to fire. The more meteors you destroy, the longer you can hold off the inevitable doom. This endless gameplay will encourage you to push your limits, setting personal records as you strive to survive against increasingly difficult levels. Rocket Defender immerses you in a retro gaming experience reminiscent of the iconic 80s classic, Missile Command, adding modern twists that elevate the excitement.

Challenge yourself as you battle through relentless waves of falling meteors, all while trying to keep the homes on Earth safe. The vibrant graphics, combined with the frantic action, create a heart-pounding experience that keeps players returning for more. How long can you survive? Gather your skills and show your determination to become the ultimate defender of Earth!

How to play free Rocket Defender game online

To play Rocket Defender, use your mouse or arrow keys to aim your cannon. Click or tap to fire at the meteors falling from the sky. As you advance, pay attention to the increasing speed and number of meteors. Keep score and challenge yourself to beat your high score!
