Saturday Night Linker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant and lively world of Saturday Night Linker, where the fun of the '70s and the excitement of disco await you! This engaging puzzle game transports you to a magnificent nightclub filled with pulsing beats and outlandish parties. As you navigate through colorful levels, you ll encounter a series of challenging puzzles that will test your logical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

With a total of 100 levels ranging from 6x6 to 9x9, Saturday Night Linker offers an adaptive difficulty that keeps you on your toes. Each puzzle presents a unique challenge, ensuring that the fun never stops. As you move through the game, you will be immersed in a lively atmosphere, complete with a groovy backdrop that might just make you want to dance!

Your task is simple yet intriguing: use your finger to link the squares on the dance floor, forming elegant arabesques. The vibrant visuals and upbeat soundtrack will pull you into the experience, making each successful connection even more fulfilling. With each completed puzzle, you ll feel a sense of achievement while enjoying the retro vibes of the disco era.

How to play free Saturday Night Linker game online

To play Saturday Night Linker, simply swipe your finger across the squares to create connections and form patterns. Start by linking adjacent squares and aim to fill the board while solving each puzzle. Keep an eye on the time and think strategically to progress through all 100 challenging levels!
