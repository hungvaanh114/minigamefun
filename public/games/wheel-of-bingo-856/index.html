Wheel of Bingo

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Wheel of Bingo is an exciting two-part game that combines elements of chance and strategy to create a unique gaming experience. In Part 1, players are invited to spin a vibrant wheel designed to match bingo numbers. You can select a game mode that allows for either 15 or 25 spins, each offering a thrilling chance to rack up points. To assist in matching your bingo numbers, brightly colored marks are placed on the wheel, clearly indicating which numbers you should aim for. Every time you match a bingo number, you earn points along with a colored ball, which will be essential in the next phase of the game. For those experienced players, achieving a complete bingo line rewards you with numerous extra balls, increasing your chances of scoring even more points.

In Part 2, the game transitions into an engaging plinko-style challenge. Here, players drop the collected colored balls to match them with respective colors displayed at the bottom of the screen. The point values indicated represent base points, but any successful color matches will multiply these values, enhancing your score significantly. The multiplication factors for each color are boldly displayed at the top of the board when you launch your ball, adding a layer of strategy as you decide your next move.

How to play free Wheel of Bingo game online

To play Wheel of Bingo online, first, select your preferred game mode and start spinning the wheel to match your bingo numbers. After collecting colored balls, proceed to the plinko stage by dropping your balls to match colors and maximize your points based on the multiplication values shown. Enjoy the thrill of each spin and drop!


Instructions

Part 1 Use the buttons labelled SPIN LEFT and SPIN RIGHT located below the wheel Hold the button to power up the spin which is indicated by the spin gauge above the button Part 2 Click and hold to position the ball at the top of the the plinko board
