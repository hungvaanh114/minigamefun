Cooking Place

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the engaging world of Cooking Place, you step into the bustling kitchen of a talented chef, where your mission is to delight hungry customers by expertly preparing their favorite dishes. The game immerses you in a vibrant atmosphere, with an organized rack in front of you where your culinary hero stands ready to take on orders.

As customers approach the chef, they will place their requests, which appear as mouth-watering images next to them. It s crucial for you to observe these pictures closely and understand what each customer desires. You will have access to a variety of ingredients necessary to recreate the dishes accurately. Following the on-screen instructions, you must skillfully prepare the ordered meals. The game offers a delightful challenge as you must manage your time efficiently while maintaining high quality in your cooking process.

Once you complete a dish, it s time to serve it to the waiting customer. If the food meets their expectations, the customer will be thrilled and reward you with payment. This rewarding feedback propels your cooking journey in Cooking Place, adding layers of excitement and urgency as you strive to keep up with the inflow of hungry patrons.

How to play free Cooking Place game online

To play Cooking Place, simply log in and start the game. As customers arrive, watch for the food pictures they display. Use the available ingredients and follow the on-screen prompts to cook the requested dish. Once ready, serve it to the customer and watch their satisfaction grow!
