Super Mario Halloween Wheelie

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Super Mario Halloween Wheelie is an exhilarating free online game designed to put your wheelie skills to the test. Take control of the iconic Super Mario as he embarks on a thrilling adventure riding his motorcycle through spooky, Halloween-themed courses. With your help, he aims to master various stunts and conquer challenging levels, all while collecting pumpkins along the way. Are you ready to assist Mario in becoming the ultimate wheelie master?

In this exciting game, your main objective is simple yet challenging: execute a continuous wheelie from start to finish. As you dive into the heart of the action, you will find that Mario automatically moves forward on his bike. However, it s up to you to keep the wheelie going and prevent the front wheel from touching the ground, which would sadly end your game. The more skillful you are at executing and maintaining the stunts, the more pumpkins you can gather and the higher your score will be.

Get ready for a fun-filled gaming experience with Super Mario Halloween Wheelie, where quick reflexes and expert timing are essential. With each level ramping up the difficulty, you'll need to master your skills to compete against the clock. So gear up and join Mario on this wild ride through the Halloween landscape!

How to play free Super Mario Halloween Wheelie game online

To play Super Mario Halloween Wheelie, simply click on the screen to start the game. Mario moves forward automatically, and your task is to continuously click the screen to maintain a wheelie. Keep the front wheel elevated to avoid crashing; collecting pumpkins boosts your score along the way!
