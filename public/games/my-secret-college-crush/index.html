My Secret College Crush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of My Secret College Crush! In this delightful game, you will join Ellie as she embarks on her college journey filled with excitement and the thrill of first love. Ellie has her sights set on Ben, a charming classmate who seems completely oblivious to her feelings. Determined to capture his attention, Ellie sets out to transform her style, believing that a fresh look might just be the key to winning his heart.

Your mission is to help Ellie revamp her wardrobe. Dive into a myriad of fashionable clothing options and accessories as you assist her in creating stunning outfits that reflect her personality. From chic casual wear for her classes to elegant ensembles for potential dates, every choice you make will impact Ellie's chances of impressing Ben.

As Ellie navigates her college life filled with classes, social events, and unexpected encounters, you also play a crucial role in choosing the perfect outing outfits for when she finally gets that coveted date with Ben. Will it be a casual cafe meet-up or a romantic evening stroll? Your styling decisions will set the mood for their time together!

How to play free My Secret College Crush game online

To play My Secret College Crush, simply start the game and guide Ellie through her wardrobe choices. Click on the various clothing items to mix and match styles, ensuring each outfit suits the occasion. Use your creativity to help her impress Ben and navigate her college adventures with confidence!
