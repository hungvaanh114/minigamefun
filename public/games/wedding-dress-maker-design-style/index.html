Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of 'Wedding Dress Maker - Design & Style', where creativity and passion intertwine to create stunning wedding gowns. As a skilled tailor, your mission is to craft exquisite wedding dresses that will make any bride feel like a princess on her special day. In this engaging game, you'll receive a visit from a lovely couple who have come to seek your expertise.

Your artistic journey begins by measuring the couple's sizes to ensure a perfect fit for their dream wedding attire. Once you have the measurements, it s time to unleash your artistic flair by selecting your favorite colors that reflect the couple's unique style. With a variety of beautiful fabrics to choose from, you'll cut the cloth into elegant shapes, bringing your designs to life.

As you sew the pieces together, pay attention to every detail to ensure that the final product is nothing short of perfection. Customizing the dresses with stylish accessories will elevate the outfits, making them even more charming for the big day. You ll create stunning looks not just for the bride but also for the bridegroom, ensuring that both shine at the wedding.

How to play free Wedding Dress Maker - Design & Style game online

To play the game, simply navigate through the different stages of wedding dress creation. Start by measuring the couple, then pick the perfect colors and fabrics. Cut and sew the cloth pieces together, finishing with beautiful accessories. Don t forget to personalize each dress to match the couple s vision!
