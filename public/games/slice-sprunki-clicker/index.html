Slice Sprunki Clicker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Slice Sprunki Clicker is a fast-paced slicing survival game. Slash Sprunki characters as they fly across the screen. Avoid bombs and time your cuts for maximum points. The challenge gets harder with each wave, testing your reflexes. Smooth animations and dynamic effects make every slice satisfying. Play endless mode and see how long you can survive. Compete for high scores and master precision cutting. Perfect for fans of arcade and reaction-based games. Jump into the slicing madness and chop Sprunki to pieces!
