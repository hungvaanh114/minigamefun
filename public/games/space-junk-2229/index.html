Space Junk

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game titled Space Junk, you step into the shoes of an astronaut who finds themselves trapped in the chaotic confines of a space junk room. Floating debris from various eras of space exploration surrounds you, creating both an obstacle and an opportunity. As you navigate this treacherous environment, your goal is to survive for as long as possible by using your joystick or arrow keys to maneuver. With every moment counting, you must skillfully dodge the floating junk while keeping the oxygen supply intact.

As you traverse through the debris-filled space, you'll come across blue lights scattered throughout the room. Collecting these lights is crucial as they boost your air supply, buying you more time to explore and evade the hazardous wreckage. However, be prepared for unpredictable challenges and debris that may hinder your progress. The vastness of space presents both beauty and peril, creating an exhilarating experience that keeps you on your toes.

Prepare yourself for an exciting journey where your reflexes will be put to the test! The struggle for survival amidst the clutter of space junk will immerse you in a unique gaming adventure. Are you ready to defy the odds and keep the astronaut breathing in the great beyond? Good luck!

How to play free Space Junk game online

To play Space Junk, use the joystick or arrow keys to navigate your astronaut through the room filled with space debris. Avoid obstacles while collecting blue lights to increase your oxygen supply. Stay agile and be quick on your feet to survive as long as possible!


Instructions

Arrows to move and Space to jump
