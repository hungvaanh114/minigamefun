Super Mario Bros 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Mario on an exciting adventure in Super Mario Bros 2, where you explore a vibrant island brimming with ghosts and classic Mario foes. This installment of the beloved franchise offers a fresh take on platforming action, set against a backdrop of colorful and spooky locales. As you traverse through each level, you'll encounter iconic enemies that Mario fans know and love, providing a nostalgic yet thrilling gaming experience.

Your primary objective is to collect three stars in each level while keeping Mario in top form with full health. Each star brings you closer to completing your mission and advancing through the game. Navigate through lush landscapes and spooky environments filled with challenges and surprises. From jumping on Goombas to dodging the mischievous antics of the Boo ghosts, your reflexes and strategic thinking will be put to the test.

As you journey through the island, you'll uncover hidden pathways, power-ups, and other secrets that enhance your gameplay. With expertly designed levels that offer varying degrees of difficulty, Super Mario Bros 2 guarantees hours of fun as you uncover each unique area of the island. Prepare to hop your way to victory while enjoying the rich graphics and immersive sound design that characterize this classic Mario adventure.
