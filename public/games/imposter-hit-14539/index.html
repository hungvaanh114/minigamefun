Imposter Hit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Imposter Hit, the ultimate challenge that will test your precision and timing! In this engaging game, your objective is to throw knives into imposters while also slashing through tantalizing apples that will aid you in your quest. With each successful hit, you unlock a variety of unique custom knives that not only enhance your gameplay but also add an exciting visual twist to your collection.

Your journey consists of numerous stages, each escalating in difficulty. Every fifth stage features a formidable boss that stands in your way. Defeating these bosses is crucial, as they yield extra apples that can be incredibly beneficial for your progression. However, be cautious aimlessly throwing your knives can lead to disastrous consequences if you accidentally hit your own projectiles. Precision and careful targeting are key to mastering this challenge.

As you navigate through the levels of Imposter Hit, you ll need to time your actions perfectly. Focus on your aim, plan your throws meticulously, and soon you ll be on your way to claiming the title of crew mate master. Can you conquer all the bosses and prove your skills? Dive into this thrilling experience and find out!

How to play free Imposter Hit game online

To play Imposter Hit, simply aim at the imposters and swipe to throw your knives. Strike the apples to collect them while avoiding hitting previously thrown knives. Keep progressing through the stages and remember to focus on defeating each boss at every fifth stage! Good luck!
