Racing in City: In Car Driving

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to immerse yourself in the thrill of street racing with Racing in City: In Car Driving, a top-tier 3D car racing game available for free! This endless car driving experience sets itself apart from typical racing games by offering extensive customization and upgrade options for your vehicles. As you navigate through bustling city traffic, rack up points by either hitting the gas or skillfully drifting past other cars in close quarters. Grab the steering wheel and dive into an exhilarating world of city driving.

Your adventure includes a selection of 13 unique racing cars, featuring 2 hatchbacks, 9 sedans, and 2 offroad vehicles, all primed for an epic driving experience. Complete various missions to demonstrate your prowess as a professional driver. Choose between two different control options: tilt control for a more dynamic feel or the traditional steering wheel for precise maneuvering. You can even toggle the auto speed feature to eliminate the need for a gas pedal, enhancing your focus on the road.

Compete against other traffic racers by climbing the world score leaderboard, showcasing your skills while racing through vibrant city streets. With the customization options available, personalize your cars by altering their color, rims, and even adding a spoiler. Explore the extensive engine tuning capabilities where you can adjust speed, braking distance, acceleration, and steering settings to optimize your ride.

Experience diverse driving scenarios across five distinct maps and enjoy various game modes including one-way, two-way traffic, free mode, and time attack. Each mode offers a unique challenge to test your racing skills.

How to play free Racing in City: In Car Driving game online

To play Racing in City: In Car Driving, choose your vehicle and driving mode. Use the on-screen controls or tilt your device to steer. Accelerate and navigate through traffic while completing missions for points. Collect bonuses and drive with skill to climb the leaderboard!
