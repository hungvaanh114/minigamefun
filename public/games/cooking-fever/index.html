Cooking Fever

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a culinary adventure with Cooking Fever, the thrilling time-management game that invites you to prepare delightful meals and desserts from around the globe all for free! Discover an array of unique restaurants, ranging from cozy dessert shops to bustling fast-food joints, and even indulge in the exotic flavors of Indian and Chinese cuisines.

Enhance your cooking skills as you navigate through various settings and master diverse cooking techniques. Gather hundreds of mouthwatering ingredients as you strive to create top-quality dishes. Experiment with a wide range of kitchen appliances, including coffee makers, rice cookers, pizza ovens, and popcorn makers, giving you the tools needed to serve up delicious meals efficiently.

Not only can you whip up fantastic dishes, but you can also personalize your restaurant with enchanting decor to draw in more customers. Elevate your clients' dining experience by offering homemade treats like cookies or cupcakes, making their visit as memorable and enjoyable as possible. Plus, you can scale up your kitchen capabilities as you upgrade equipment, broadening your menu to feature an even greater variety of culinary delights.

How to play free Cooking Fever game online

To dive into the world of Cooking Fever, simply select a restaurant to start cooking. Click on the ingredients and appliances to prepare meals as customers arrive. Serve them promptly and keep an eye on their satisfaction levels to earn tips and unlock new recipes! Enjoy each hectic kitchen shift!
