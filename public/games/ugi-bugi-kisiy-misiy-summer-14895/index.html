Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Ugi Bugi and Kisiy Misiy as they embark on an exciting summer adventure filled with challenges and teamwork. In this delightful journey, you are tasked with guiding them through various obstacles to ensure they reach the shade of a large tree, where they can escape the scorching summer heat. Yet, the path is fraught with peril, as thorns lurk along the way, ready to harm anyone who strays off the safe path.

As you traverse through the vibrant levels of this game, every decision carries weight. You must act quickly and skillfully to avoid the menacing thorns while ensuring both Ugi Bugi and Kisiy Misiy make it under the protective tree. The game tests your reflexes and ability to strategize as you navigate each challenging level. With every successful maneuver, you draw closer to advancing to the final part of their adventure. Collect special items along the way that can assist you in overcoming the obstacles.

As you progress, the levels become increasingly complex, ensuring that you remain engaged and excited. With the companionship of Ugi Bugi and Kisiy Misiy, the joy of completing each stage is made even sweeter. Can you help them reach the finish line together and enjoy their summer adventure to the fullest?

How to play free Ugi Bugi & Kisiy Misiy Summer game online

To play, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to guide Ugi Bugi and Kisiy Misiy towards the tree while avoiding the thorns. Collaborate with your friend to synchronize your movements and collect items. Time your jumps and actions carefully to successfully navigate through each challenging level.
