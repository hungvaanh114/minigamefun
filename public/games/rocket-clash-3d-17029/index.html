Rocket Clash 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the heart of danger in Rocket Clash 3D, where you command a dedicated resistance squad of elite special forces to thwart the insidious plans of enemy intelligence agents. Their objective? To seize control of a crucial missile base. This high-stakes battle requires your skills, strategy, and ability to adapt, as each moment counts and the stakes are higher than ever.

You ll be equipped with a uniquely compact version of the iconic AK, empowering you to engage in exhilarating firefights. But that's not all hidden deep within the level lies a mysterious secret weapon, waiting for you to uncover its potential. As you navigate this treacherous terrain, be cautious of hazardous acid pools formed from toxic rocket fuel, adding an extra layer of danger to your mission.

Your exploration will lead you through a labyrinthine network of underground tunnels, each corner presenting both opportunities and threats. Use your tactical awareness to flank enemies, gather intel, and uncover secrets that may aid your resistance cause. The dynamic gameplay encourages teamwork and strategic planning, compelling you and your squad to work together to achieve victory and protect the missile base at all costs.

How to play free Rocket Clash 3D game online

To play Rocket Clash 3D online, simply navigate the game's interface using your mouse and keyboard. Move your character using the WASD keys, aim with your mouse, and shoot using the left-click button. Utilize the environment to your advantage, stay aware of your surroundings, and make the most of power-ups as you engage in fierce battles against enemy forces.


Instructions

WASD - movement C - crouch SPACE - jump
