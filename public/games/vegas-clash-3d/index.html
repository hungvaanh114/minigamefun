Vegas Clash 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the electrifying world of Vegas Clash 3D, where the glitz and glamour of Las Vegas mask a life of crime and chaos! It's the perfect time for your audacious Sassy Elvis gang to lace up their boots and make a daring infiltration into the underground vault of a lavish casino brimming with cash. Your getaway van is parked strategically at the edge, so channel your inner bandit and start collecting the loot!

However, be prepared for a showdown! The casino's owners have outsmarted you by summoning the cops, who are ready to defend their turf. With the law breathing down your neck, it s essential to bring your A-game and outmaneuver those determined officers. You'll need to think fast and act faster if you want to make it out with the cash.

While exploring the backstreets surrounding the casino, keep an eye out for hidden treasures! You might stumble upon a dusty yet potent revolver that can turn the tide of battle. As you engage in intense skirmishes, don t forget to utilize the character leveling system, earning stars with every victory. With enough experience, you can enhance your weapons to gold skins, giving you the edge against tougher opponents. Are you ready to prove that you re the ultimate kingpin in Vegas Clash 3D? Only the bold will succeed!

How to play free Vegas Clash 3D game online

In Vegas Clash 3D, navigate your character using the on-screen controls to move and grab bags of cash. Engage in combat with the police to defend your loot, aiming for headshots for maximum damage. Collect stars during battles to level up your character and unlock weapon upgrades!
