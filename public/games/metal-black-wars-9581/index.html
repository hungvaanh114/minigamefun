Metal Black Wars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an intense and thrilling confrontation in the world of Metal Black Wars. As a formidable metal warrior, you will delve into the heart of conflict, facing an entire army of adversaries all on your own. Choose your level of challenge at the outset from options like "NORMAL," "HARD," and "CRAZY," each providing a unique combat experience tailored to your skills.

You'll begin your adventure aboard a sleek helicopter, touching down on the battlefield where danger lurks at every corner. Courage is essential do not hesitate! As you step onto the field, set your sights on the enemy and launch your assault without delay. Constant shooting is crucial; victory hinges on your ability to keep the pressure on the foes. And remember, don t let your Lifeline gauge run low, or your survival chances diminish.

When facing overwhelming odds, be strategic: unleash bombs to shift the tide when enemies swarm. For every foe you eliminate, you'll gain gold, which can be used to acquire new and powerful weaponry from the in-game store. These upgrades will enhance your combat prowess, allowing you to embrace the chaos of battle with greater strength and skill. The Metal Black Wars is a captivating mix of action and adventure, promising hours of entertainment as you navigate through this relentless war.

How to play free Metal Black Wars game online

To play Metal Black Wars, simply choose your difficulty level, then drop into the battlefield from your helicopter. Use your controls to aim and shoot at enemies while evading their attacks. Collect gold from defeated foes to purchase upgrades that will help you in your fight. Survive and conquer!
