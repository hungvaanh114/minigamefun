Candy Xmas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for a sweet holiday adventure in Candy Xmas, where the festive spirit comes alive! With Christmas just around the corner, Santa is in a bit of a pickle he's desperately in need of candies to delight the children around the world. As his trusty helper, it s up to you to join Santa on this delightful mission and gather as many candies as possible before the big day arrives.

But beware! The mischievous snow Yeti is lurking about, determined to hoard those sugary treats for himself. You will need to stay vigilant and agile as you navigate through a winter wonderland filled with challenges and obstacles. Your goal is to collect the candies while avoiding the Yeti, making this a thrilling race against time. With each candy you collect, you ll help spread joy to children everywhere just what Santa needs to make this Christmas special!

Immerse yourself in the magical world of Candy Xmas and experience the exhilaration of saving the holidays one candy at a time. Get ready for colorful graphics, engaging gameplay, and the joy of assisting Santa in his quest. Gather as many candies as you can, outsmart the snow Yeti, and complete your mission. Good luck, and may your holiday season be filled with candy and cheer!

How to play free Candy Xmas game online

To start playing the Candy Xmas game, use the arrow keys or swipe to navigate your character. Move around to collect candies while avoiding the snow Yeti. Be quick, and keep an eye out for obstacles that may slow you down. Aim for high scores as you gather as many candies as you can before time runs out!
