Halloween Night

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to add a little thrill to your Halloween festivities? Look no further! Dive into the enchanting world of Halloween Night, a delightful game tailored for this spooky season. Featuring a whimsical yet eerie theme, this game promises both fun and excitement for players of all ages.

At the center of this magical adventure is an adorable girl dressed as a witch, soaring through the night sky on her powerful broomstick. This broom is not just any ordinary ride; it possesses incredible abilities that help guide her as she navigates through a landscape filled with mischievous and scary pumpkins. Your challenge is to assist her in overcoming obstacles while collecting points along the way.

The controls are simple: tap to ascend and release to descend, allowing the witch to maneuver through various challenges. However, beware of the dangers lurking around! Avoid crashing into the roof, picking up bombs, or hitting the ground to keep the fun going. The game's engaging mechanics and charming visuals make it an ideal choice for Halloween entertainment, whether you're with friends or enjoying a cozy night in.

How to play free Halloween Night game online

To play Halloween Night, simply start the game and use your mouse or finger to control the witch's broom. Tap to ascend and release to descend, ensuring you dodge obstacles like rooftops and bombs. Stay alert and guide your witch safely through the spooky pumpkins to achieve a high score!
