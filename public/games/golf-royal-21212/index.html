How to play free Golf Royal game online

To start playing Golf Royale, simply select your desired course from the main menu. Use your mouse or touchscreen to aim, adjust your swing power, and let go to take your shot. Navigate through the course, eventuating in challenging holes, and complete each round to improve your rank. Enjoy the game!
