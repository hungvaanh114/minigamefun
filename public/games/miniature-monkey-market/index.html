Miniature Monkey Market

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the bustling world of the Miniature Monkey Market, where quirky monkeys crave their favorite snacks and treats! Have you ever dreamed of running a grocery store for these charming primates? Now s your chance to make that dream a reality! Design your own mini market and cater to the diverse tastes of your monkey customers.

Start your entrepreneurial journey by planting and nurturing banana trees after all, bananas are the monkeys' all-time favorite delicacy. Once your bananas are ripe and ready, collect them and display them prominently on your market counter. But don t stop there; keep your shelves stocked with other delightful items! Feed your chickens with those delicious bananas to produce fresh eggs, which you can also sell at your store.

As you accumulate more bananas and eggs, you can venture into the world of canned food products like banana puree, and staple items such as flour, bread, and milk. With each sale, you'll earn money, allowing you to expand your market's offerings and draw in more monkey customers. The vibrant atmosphere and lively interactions guarantee a fun and engaging shopping experience for all!

How to play free Miniature Monkey Market game online

To play the Miniature Monkey Market, simply start by planting banana trees. Once they mature, gather the bananas and other products like eggs, and place them on your counter. Use the money earned from sales to purchase more items and upgrade your store to attract more monkeys!
