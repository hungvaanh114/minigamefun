Candy House Cleaning

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Have you ever imagined what it would be like to tidy up a house crafted entirely from sweets? Welcome to the whimsical world of Candy House Cleaning, where your cleaning duties become a delightful adventure! Step inside this sugary abode, where vibrant colors and delicious treats surround you. Your mission is to restore order within this colorful chaos.

Begin your journey by identifying and picking up the litter scattered across the candy-laden floors. Toys and other playful items are strewn about gather them up and return each one to its rightful place. With the initial clutter cleared, it s time to give the walls some sparkle! Armed with a cleaning solution, scrub away any sticky fingerprints or candy residue that have accumulated over time.

But don t forget about the floor! Use your trusty vacuum cleaner to suck up any crumbs or remnants left behind. With every task you complete, you'll witness the transformation of this candy wonderland into a tidy and inviting space. Enjoy the satisfying visual rewards as you enhance the splendor of this sugary home, making it shine like new!

How to play free Candy House Cleaning game online

To play Candy House Cleaning, simply navigate through the game using your mouse or touchscreen. Click and drag to pick up trash and toys, and utilize the cleaning solution on the walls. Once everything is tidy, use the vacuum to finish the job! Enjoy this fun, interactive cleaning experience!
