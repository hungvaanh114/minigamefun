How to play free Tic Tac Toe Pro game online

To play Tic Tac Toe Pro online, simply select the game mode you prefer: single-player against the AI or multiplayer with friends. Place your symbol on the grid by clicking on a square. Aim to get three of your symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The first player to achieve this wins the game!
