Building New House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to your dream handyman workshop, where creativity meets construction in "Building New House"! In this exciting game, you take on the role of a master builder, ready to fulfill fun and imaginative requests from your young clients. Customers eagerly await your unique products and services, which means it s time to roll up your sleeves and dive into hands-on building activities.

Experience the thrill of digging soil to lay a strong foundation, or switch it up by demolishing old structures to make way for new creations. In addition to building houses and towers, you ll have the chance to construct a variety of wooden products, perfect for sparking the imagination of your customers. Engage in various tasks like cutting wood to precision, welding materials together, and utilizing a range of tools and heavy machinery to bring your visions to life. With an array of building materials at your disposal, there are endless possibilities to create stunning and unique designs!

Your workshop is filled with opportunities for fun; utilize your skills and work ethic to transform your clients' dreams into solid realities. As you level up, you'll be able to unlock advanced tools and machines, enhancing your building capabilities and allowing you to tackle even bigger projects. Get ready for a construction adventure that encourages creativity, teamwork, and a sense of accomplishment!

How to play free Building New House game online

To play "Building New House," simply use your mouse or touchscreen to select the tools and materials needed for each project. Follow the prompts to complete tasks like digging, cutting, and assembling. Keep an eye on customer orders to ensure you create exactly what they want within the given time frame!
