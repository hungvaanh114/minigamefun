C-Virus Game: Outbreak

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of C-Virus Game: Outbreak, a devastating new virus known as the C-virus has swept across the globe, infecting over 90% of the human population. As chaos reigns and the infected roam the streets, the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. You play as a former marine who grapples with PTSD from previous battles, but this time, the fight is against a new kind of enemy one that poses a threat to your very survival. Utilize your combat skills and survival instincts to navigate through desolate urban landscapes, tackle previously unimaginable challenges, and confront the horrors that lurk around every corner.

Throughout your journey, you must gather resources, craft weapons, and find safe havens to withstand the relentless wave of infected foes. Your military training will be put to the test as you devise strategic plans to outsmart both the infected and rival survivors. Experience moments of heart-pounding action intertwined with the psychological battle against your own past demons, making every decision crucial. The atmospheric graphics and immersive sound design will captivate you as you delve deep into this post-apocalyptic scenario where every step could be your last.

Are you ready to embrace your inner warrior, evade the clutches of the infected, and emerge victorious against all odds? The challenge awaits in C-Virus Game: Outbreak!

How to play free C-Virus Game: Outbreak game online

To play C-Virus Game: Outbreak online, use the WASD keys to move your character and the mouse to aim your weapon. Gather supplies by exploring the environment, and build or upgrade your gear using the resources you collect. Stay vigilant, strategize your actions, and keep an eye on your health bar for survival!
