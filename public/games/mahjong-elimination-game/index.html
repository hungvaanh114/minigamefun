How to play free Mahjong Elimination Game game online

To play the Mahjong Elimination Game, start by selecting a pair of matching tiles on the board. Click on the tiles using your mouse or touch screen to connect them, ensuring there s a clear path between them. Once matched, the tiles will disappear, making room for new combinations! Your goal is to eliminate all tiles to complete each level and advance to the next challenge.
