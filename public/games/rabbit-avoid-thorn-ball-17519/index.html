Rabbit Avoid Thorn Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the adventurous game "Rabbit Avoid Thorn Ball," players are thrust into an exciting challenge where agility and quick reflexes are put to the test. Set in the territory of the formidable grey wolf, these courageous rabbits strive to prove their skills. The wolves, known for their fierce nature, drop spiky balls that players must skillfully dodge. Each rabbit must navigate the treacherous landscape while avoiding these menacing obstacles.

As players control their bunny, they must remain alert and agile, sidestepping the thorn-laden balls that the wolf hurls down. The stakes are high, but the rewards are plenty! Throughout their journey, players can collect shining gold coins scattered across the terrain. These coins are not just for show; they can be exchanged at the in-game mall for powerful shields that provide protection during the challenging gameplay.

With each successful evasion and collection of coins, players can upgrade their bunnies and improve their chances against the persistent wolf. The adrenaline of outrunning the spiky threats adds a thrilling edge to the game while encouraging players to improve their reflexes and strategic thinking. Will you help the rabbits conquer their fears and master their agility in the face of danger? Join the fun in "Rabbit Avoid Thorn Ball today!

How to play free Rabbit Avoid Thorn Ball game online

To play "Rabbit Avoid Thorn Ball," use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver your bunny left and right. Avoid the spiky balls dropped by the wolf while collecting gold coins to gather points. After collecting enough coins, visit the game s mall to exchange them for protective shields to aid your journey!
