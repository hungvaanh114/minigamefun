Exit Puzzle : Colors Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the vibrant world of Exit Puzzle: Colors Game, a captivating and easy-to-learn action puzzle experience. In this game, your objective is to maneuver one or multiple green dots across a challenging board filled with various geometric shapes. Your aim is to guide these dots to the designated goal areas while deftly avoiding obstacles that may hinder your progress.

The gameplay is both simple and strategic. Each dot moves in a predictable manner; they will always turn right when they encounter a wall, seamlessly transitioning around corners or changing direction at dead ends. This consistent behavior allows players to anticipate the movements of the dots and other shapes, making it easier to plot a successful course. As you navigate through levels, you will discover increasingly complex boards that require more thoughtful planning and timing.

With its engaging mechanics and charming design, Exit Puzzle: Colors Game promises hours of entertainment and brain-teasing fun. The simplicity of the game belies the challenge it provides, as each level demands an effective mix of reflexes and strategy. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a newcomer to the genre, you're sure to find yourself hooked. Test your skills, improve your timing, and achieve those satisfying victories as you guide your greens dots to safety!

How to play free Exit Puzzle: Colors Game online

To begin playing Exit Puzzle: Colors Game, simply use your mouse or touch controls to guide the green dots. Click or tap in the direction you want the dot to move, and watch as it navigates the board automatically, turning at walls and corners. Your goal is to reach all target areas while avoiding obstacles along the way.
