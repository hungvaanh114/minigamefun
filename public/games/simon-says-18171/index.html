Simon Says

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Simon Says is a captivating electronic memory game that challenges players' cognitive skills and reaction times. Originally conceived by inventors Ralph H. Baer and Howard J. Morrison, this engaging game was brought to life with the programming prowess of Lenny Cope at Marvin Glass and Associates. Featuring a unique combination of sounds and lights, Simon Says invites players to observe and recall increasingly complex sequences, providing an exhilarating test of memory.

As players engage with Simon, they are prompted by the device to watch for a pattern of colored lights that correspond to specific musical tones. The objective is simple yet demanding: players must carefully replicate the sequence as it grows longer and more intricate with each successful round. Any mistake results in an end to the game, making focus and accuracy vital elements for success. The thrill comes from the rising challenge as players strive to outdo themselves and achieve higher scores, all while enjoying a colorful array of flashing lights and catchy tunes.

This interactive game not only entertains but also sharpens mental dexterity, making it suitable for all ages. With its simple premise and stimulating gameplay, Simon Says has created lasting memories for players around the world, cementing its place as a timeless classic in the realm of electronic games.

How to play free Simon says game online

To play the free Simon Says game online, simply click the "Start" button to begin. Observe the sequence of lights and sounds carefully, then use your mouse or keyboard to replicate the pattern. Keep repeating the sequences in the correct order to advance through the levels. Good luck!
