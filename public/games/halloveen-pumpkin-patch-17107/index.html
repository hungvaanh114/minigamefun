Halloveen Pumpkin Patch

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Halloveen Pumpkin Patch, a delightful Halloween-themed game hosted by none other than Vampirina, the charming vampire girl from her popular show. This engaging experience is perfect for fans of various beloved characters, featuring friends like Fancy Nancy, Mickey Mouse, Muppet Babies, and Puppy Dog Pals. With so many familiar faces joining in, you can explore the festive ambiance while enjoying a series of exciting mini-games that embody the spirit of Halloween!

The adventure begins with a memory game represented by the first pumpkin adorned with cards. In this game, you ll uncover hidden characters by clicking on two doors at a time to match pairs. As exciting music plays in the background, you ll feel the thrill of pairing up characters until all are revealed and matched! The second pumpkin showcases the pumpkin carving game, allowing you to choose a show and unleash your creativity by cutting the pumpkin in various fun styles. With the drawing tools provided, you can personalize your spooky masterpiece and save it for later!

The third pumpkin, featuring mouse ears, invites you to engage in a unique decoration activity, where you can create a Halloween scene using characters and items from your chosen show. Arrange, resize, and position objects to craft the perfect festive setting. Lastly, the fourth pumpkin introduces a stacking game challenge, where you ll focus on strategically dropping objects to build the tallest tower of presents without it toppling over.

Join your favorite Disney Junior characters for a spooktacular time filled with laughter and creativity in Halloveen Pumpkin Patch!

How to play free Halloveen Pumpkin Patch game online

To play Halloveen Pumpkin Patch, start by selecting a mini-game from the initial menu. Click on the doors in the memory game to reveal characters, use the tools for pumpkin carving, decorate your Halloween scene, or strategically stack items in the last game by clicking when you want them to drop. Enjoy!
