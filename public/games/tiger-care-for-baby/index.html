Tiger Care for Baby

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Tiger Care For Baby, where you can explore the magic of candy canes in a fun and engaging way! Indulge your sweet tooth by participating in the exciting Fruit Sort Puzzle game. Here, you will have the chance to create your own luscious candy canes, tailored to your preferences. Choose from a variety of fruity flavors and combine them to sweeten your candy cane creation!

The gameplay is simple yet addictive. Move different types of fruits across the board, but remember, you'll need at least three of the same fruits to combine them successfully. As you upgrade the fruits, you re working towards the ultimate goal: crafting delectable hawthorn candy canes. However, you must be cautious - if the number of fruits exceeds the maximum limit of the fork, your mission will end in failure.

Do you have what it takes to make the perfect hawthorn candy cane? With strategic moves and clever planning, you can achieve candy cane glory! Get ready to enjoy a fun-filled experience while building your confidence in the kitchen with some flavorful fruity creations. Dive into the vibrant world of candy making and unleash your creativity, proving you can craft the sweetest treats!

How to play free Tiger Care For Baby game online

To play the Tiger Care For Baby game, simply drag and drop the fruits on the game board. Combine three or more identical fruits by moving them next to each other. As you combine the fruits, keep an eye on the limit of the fork to avoid exceeding it. Aim to collect enough fruits to create the hawthorn candy canes before the time runs out!
