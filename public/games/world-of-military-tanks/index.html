World of Military Tanks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Choose your tank and go on the attack! Become the best tank driver! An exciting tank action game. Choose your tank and go ahead to complete missions and destroy enemies. The game has different missions, base defense missions, survival and 5v5 battles. There is a large selection of tanks in the game, buy and upgrade tanks. Complete the missions and get rewarded! The game will be interesting to everyone who is interested in military topics!

Instructions

The goal of the game is to complete the mission objective destroy enemy tanks To shoot point the scope at the tank and right-click When the target is captured the scope will turn red Shoot when a red circle appears in the scope
