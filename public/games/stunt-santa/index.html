Stunt Santa

Get ready for a festive adventure in Stunt Santa! As Christmas Eve unfolds, chaos ensues when Santa's sleigh loses control, scattering gifts all over the streets. It s up to you to step into Santa s boots and embark on an exhilarating mission to gather as many presents as possible. With each level packed with fun challenges, your quick reflexes and strategic thinking will be put to the test.

As you dash through snow-covered streets, dodge obstacles, and navigate around other holiday mishaps, you ll need to be quick on your feet to collect those flying gifts! The more gifts you gather, the more points you earn, which will ultimately help Santa complete his Christmas deliveries on time. With vibrant graphics and joyful holiday music accompanying your every move, Stunt Santa promises the perfect blend of excitement and festive spirit.

Whether you re a seasoned gamer or just looking to spread some holiday cheer, this game offers an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Each level brings unique challenges and surprises, ensuring that Christmas Eve is anything but ordinary. So, gear up to help Santa restore order in the holiday chaos and make this the best Christmas ever!

How to play free Stunt Santa game online

Jump into the action by controlling Santa s sleigh using your keyboard or mouse. Move left and right to catch falling gifts while avoiding obstacles. Keep an eye on your score, and aim to collect all the gifts to successfully complete each level. Happy gaming!
