Carpenter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

As children, many of us fantasized about crafting unique items with our hands; some realized their dreams, while others did not. The game "Carpenter" revives that childhood imagination, offering a platform to create various products without needing real-world tools or materials the game provides everything you need!

In "Carpenter", you can cut out an array of designs, from delightful animals to speedy cars and soaring planes. After successfully cutting out your desired product, the next step involves assembling the pieces by using self-tapping screws. Once your project is assembled, unleash your creativity by painting it, adding your own personal touch.

The game isn't just about crafting; it also incorporates an exciting reward system. You can earn coins by completing levels, which can then be used to purchase various tools that enhance your crafting experience. Engage in daily challenges to earn additional coins and unlock a range of possibilities for your woodworking adventures. Whether you're a seasoned crafter or just starting, "Carpenter" promises an entertaining experience where creativity knows no bounds.

How to play free Carpenter game online

To play the Carpenter game, begin by selecting your desired product to craft. Follow the on-screen instructions to cut out pieces accurately. Once your items are ready, piece them together using self-tapping screws. Don t forget to paint your creation at the end! Complete levels and daily challenges to earn coins for tools.
