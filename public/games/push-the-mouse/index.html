Push the Mouse

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you're a fan of charming puzzle games, then Push The Mouse is just the right choice for you! This adorably designed game invites players to engage their intellect while guiding an array of cute little mice to safety. Every level presents a new challenge, as you must strategically assist each rodent on their journey.

As you dive into the game, you'll find that interaction is simple yet effective. Simply tap on the mouse you wish to move, and it will start to scurry forward. However, bear in mind that these adorable creatures don't possess much in the way of intelligence they are programmed to continue moving in a singular direction. Therefore, you must carefully plan your moves to navigate obstacles and steer the mice towards their desired destinations.

Each level introduces varying obstacles and intricacies that will test your critical thinking and creativity, ensuring a delightful experience filled with challenges. The vibrant graphics and delightful sound effects add an extra layer of enjoyment, making it an engaging adventure for players of all ages. Will you be able to guide all of the little mice home?

How to play free Push the Mouse game online

To play Push The Mouse, simply click on a mouse to make it move forward. Plan your moves wisely, as the mice will only continue moving in the direction you ve chosen. Your goal is to navigate them safely past obstacles until they reach their destination. Enjoy solving the puzzles while helping the little mice!
