Car Escape Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Car Escape Game is an addictive and challenging puzzle game. It will help you train your brain. The operation of the game is very simple, move the horizontally parked cars to the left and right, move the vertically parked cars up and down, and finally drive the red fire truck to the rightmost exit of the parking lot and the game will be successful. If you are a fan of puzzle games, you will surely enjoy our game. Keep your brain sharp while relaxing, having fun and relieving stress. Come and enjoy playing this puzzle game!

Instructions

Move the horizontally parked cars to the left and right vertically parked cars to the top and bottom and finally drive the red fire truck to the rightmost exit of the parking lot then the game is successful
