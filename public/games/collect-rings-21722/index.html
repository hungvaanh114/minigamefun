Collect Rings

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in the game Collect Rings, where a vibrant world of squares awaits. The playing field is bustling with myriad squares, all set for your adventure. A sleek white ball emerges from the bottom of the screen, launching upward to traverse this colorful landscape. As the ball glides through the squares, you'll notice a transformation the colors of the squares begin to change with each encounter, creating a mesmerizing visual effect.

Your objective is simple yet engaging: as the white ball travels, it will pass over each square up to five times. Once it has completed this task, any square that has been touched by the ball will morph into a shiny ball. Your goal is to click on these newly formed balls to collect charming rings. The more rings you gather, the more rewards you earn, elevating the excitement of your play. However, there s a catch red balls are lurking in the game, and if your white ball collides with any of them, it s game over. Prioritize your strategy to collect as many rings as you can while avoiding those perilous red balls!

How to play free Collect Rings game online

To dive into the action of Collect Rings, simply start the game and watch the white ball emerge from the bottom. Guide it through the squares by using your mouse or touchscreen. Keep an eye out for squares changing color and click on the resulting balls to obtain rings. Stay vigilant to dodge any incoming red balls to prolong your game!
