Welcome to Skaters Impostor, an exhilarating blend of speed and skill! If you're a fan of the popular game Among Us, get ready to dive into a thrilling skating adventure where you ll race down breathtaking hills and perform jaw-dropping tricks. How to play free Skaters Impostor game online

To start playing Skaters Impostor, simply choose your game mode: single or multiplayer. Use the arrow keys or joystick to steer your skater, accelerating downhill to build speed. Perform tricks by combining jumps with specific key combinations. Compete against your friend or challenge yourself to beat your best score!
