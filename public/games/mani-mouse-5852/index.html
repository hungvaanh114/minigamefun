Mani Mouse

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the charming world of Mani Mouse, a delightful 2D platformer that invites players to step into the tiny paws of a brave little mouse. Your mission is simple yet challenging: navigate through whimsical levels filled with delicious slices of cheese while evading cunning enemy cats, treacherous mouse traps, sharp pins, and numerous other dangers. Each level is designed to test your agility and strategy as you race to collect every piece of cheese and ultimately reach the Exit door, which leads you to the next exciting stage of the game.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter eight unique levels, each crafted with increasing difficulty to keep you on your toes. With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, Mani Mouse offers players a fun and captivating experience that s perfect for gamers of all ages. Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick gaming session or a seasoned veteran seeking a new challenge, this game has something for everyone. Test your reflexes and skills as you enjoy the thrill of dodging enemy cats and clever traps while aiming to complete every level with skill and precision.

How to play free Mani Mouse game online

To play Mani Mouse, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your mouse character. Jump with the spacebar to avoid obstacles and collect cheese slices. Keep an eye out for enemy cats and traps as you navigate each level. Your goal is to gather all the cheese and reach the Exit door safely!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
