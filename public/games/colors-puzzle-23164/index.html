Colors Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a vibrant journey with the Colors Puzzle game, where you click on the bucket that matches the name of the color plummeting from the sky! Your quick reflexes are put to the test as you strive to prevent any of the falling colors from landing in the ominous black danger bucket. The clock is ticking, and the challenge heightens with every second!

Your adventure begins with 3 lives and a time limit of 2 minutes, but you can enhance your score by cleverly using periodic power-ups that appear throughout the game. These power-ups not only boost your points but also offer chances to extend your gameplay and increase your lives. Each successful click brings you closer to mastering the vibrant chaos in this captivating puzzle.

Strategically timing your actions is crucial, as the colors descend faster and faster. Stay focused and keep an eye out for bonus opportunities that can aid in achieving a high score. The combination of speed, color recognition, and strategic gameplay will challenge your skills and ensure hours of fun. Get ready to tap into your inner puzzle master with Colors Puzzle!

How to play free Colors Puzzle game online

To play Colors Puzzle, simply start by observing the color name displayed above as it falls. Click on the corresponding colored bucket before it reaches the black bucket. Use the power-ups wisely to gain extra time and lives, enhancing your gaming experience while aiming for the highest score!
