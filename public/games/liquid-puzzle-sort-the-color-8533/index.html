Liquid Puzzle is an engaging and addictive game that challenges players to sort colorful liquids within glasses. The objective is simple: arrange the colored water so that each glass contains only one hue. How to play free Liquid Puzzle: Sort the Color game online

To play Liquid Puzzle, simply tap on one glass to pour its contents into another. Your goal is to ensure that each glass holds only one color by strategically moving the liquids around. Use your problem-solving skills to find the best combinations and complete each level to advance in the game!
