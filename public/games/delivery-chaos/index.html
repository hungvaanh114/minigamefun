Delivery Chaos

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Delivery Chaos is a fast-paced driving game where explosions turn every delivery into a challenge! Navigate through explosive mayhem, collecting waypoints to rack up coins and boost your score. Outmaneuver destruction, and unlock powerful new vehicles and exciting map areas. Can you survive the chaos and become the ultimate delivery driver?

Instructions

Driving - WASD Arrow keys Right trigger Left Trigger Left stick Handbrake - Spacebar Triangle Unlock cursor - V Return to menu - Tab Circle
