Your objective is to keep Skibidi Toilet airborne while deftly maneuvering through the gaps in the pipes. How to play free Flappy Skibidi Toilet game online

Playing Flappy Skibidi Toilet is simple and intuitive. To keep Skibidi Toilet afloat, tap the screen or press the spacebar to make him flap his way upward.
