Subway Rabbit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting adventure with Subway Rabbit, where you ll dash through a vibrant forest full of challenges and surprises. As a speedy bunny, you'll need to run, jump, duck, and swerve to navigate your way through an array of obstacles standing in your path. The enchanting landscape is teeming with gems that you can collect to unlock a variety of upgrades, enhancing your bunny's abilities and boosting your overall score!

Experience the thrill of high-speed action as you hop through dynamic environments, avoiding barriers and outmaneuvering other creatures. Each level is designed to keep you on your toes, ensuring that the excitement never wanes. The colorful graphics and cheerful soundtrack create an immersive gaming atmosphere, making your bunny's journey a delightful experience for players of all ages.

Mastering Subway Rabbit is all about honing your skills. The more gems you collect, the more upgrades you can acquire, allowing your bunny to reach new heights literally! Enhance your jumping skills, gain temporary invincibility, and accelerate your speed to conquer challenging sections of the forest. Can you beat your best score and become the ultimate Subway Rabbit champion?

How to play free Subway Rabbit game online

To play the Subway Rabbit game, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard. Press the up arrow to jump over obstacles, the down arrow to duck under them, and the left/right arrows to swerve in either direction. Collect gems along the way to purchase upgrades once you accumulate enough points, and aim for the highest score possible!
