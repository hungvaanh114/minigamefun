Stretch the Cats

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Stretch The Cats! In this delightful game, your primary goal is to help adorable feline friends navigate through increasingly challenging levels. Your task is simple yet engaging: stretch the cats into their longest forms possible while skillfully maneuvering around obstacles. Collect the various body parts of your cat, which are essential for unlocking subsequent levels. As you progress, your agility and strategy will be tested, as you aim to reach the exit with a required number of stretches displayed at the top of the screen.

In Stretch The Cats, each level presents unique challenges that will keep you on your toes. As you swipe to stretch the cats, remember to keep an eye out for barriers along your path. With a simple swipe, you can clear away these obstacles, allowing your stretchy friends to safely make their way to freedom. However, be cautious! Ensure that you stretch the cat long enough to meet the level's exit requirement, or else you ll find yourself trapped in delightful yet tricky situations. The game combines elements of puzzle-solving, timing, and dexterity to create a thoroughly engaging experience that cat lovers and gamers alike will enjoy.

How to play free Stretch The Cats game online

To play Stretch The Cats, simply swipe the screen to stretch your cat and maneuver through each level. Keep a lookout for the number of stretches needed to reach the exit, and swipe away obstacles that block your path. Aim to collect various cat body parts to advance to the next exciting stage!
