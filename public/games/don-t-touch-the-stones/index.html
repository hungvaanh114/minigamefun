Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of "Don't Touch The Stones," where you take on the role of a nimble owl navigating a treacherous terrain strewn with menacing stones. Your mission is simple yet challenging: navigate through a perilous landscape and avoid touching any stones that come your way. The game tests not only your reflexes but also your strategic thinking as you maneuver your owl through these hazardous obstacles. With every passing moment, the excitement escalates how long can you keep your feathered friend safe? Each game is an exhilarating experience filled with suspense and moments of triumph as you master the art of agility and precision.

The vibrant graphics and captivating sound effects create an immersive atmosphere that draws players into this exciting adventure. The increasing difficulty and random stone placements ensure that no two games are the same, keeping you engaged and on your toes. Compete against your own high scores or challenge friends to see who can last the longest in this test of skill and concentration. Whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive player, "Don't Touch The Stones" offers a delightful escape into a world filled with excitement and adrenaline.

Join hundreds of players who have taken up the challenge. Are you ready to test your limits and see how far you can go? The adventure awaits, and every second counts!

How to play free Don't Touch The Stones game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse or finger to guide your owl. Avoid all the falling stones by moving up, down, left, or right. Stay focused and react quickly to the obstacles. Your goal is to survive as long as possible without touching any stones!
