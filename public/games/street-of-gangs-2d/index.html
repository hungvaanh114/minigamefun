Street of Gangs 2D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Street Of Gangs 2D, a thrilling vintage fighting action game that lets you unleash your combat prowess against skilled street fighters. Engage in a profound battle showcasing your fighting techniques from various martial arts, including karate, Taekwondo, Muay Thai, kickboxing, and boxing. As you fight your way through the streets, impress the gang members with your unique combat style and agility!

In this intense street brawler, you will master hand-to-hand combat, utilizing your kicks and punches to outmaneuver and overpower your foes. With a focus on body control and dexterity, learn to evade and counter your opponents' strikes effectively. But beware the gangs you re up against are formidable adversaries, making it essential to stay sharp and on your toes.

Your journey in Street Of Gangs 2D will teach you an arsenal of offensive and defensive techniques, including striking, grappling, and dodging. Each encounter pushes you to utilize these skills, forcing you to punch, kick, smash, and knock out your enemies to emerge victorious. The streets are ablaze with action, and it's up to you to maintain your composure while battling through chaotic urban warfare.

How to play free Street Of Gangs 2D game online

To begin playing Street Of Gangs 2D, use the arrow keys or WASD for movement. The attack buttons, generally assigned to J, K, and L, will enable you to throw punches, kick, and perform special moves. Strategically combine your movements and attacks to defeat your opponents as you navigate through the gritty streets!
