Cut the Rope 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling continuation of the Om Nom saga in Cut The Rope 2! In this exciting sequel, players join the delightful green monster Om Nom on his quest to munch on delicious candy. As you traverse through vibrant and imaginative environments, you'll encounter new challenges and puzzles that require creativity and quick thinking to solve. Your goal is simple: cut the ropes and feed Om Nom his precious candy, but be prepared for some tricky obstacles along the way!

This installment introduces Om Nom's adorable little brothers, and each level presents fresh surprises and unique mechanics. From swinging ropes to moving platforms, every puzzle in Cut The Rope 2 is crafted to test your logical reasoning and timing. Collect stars while navigating through the levels to unlock different abilities and power-ups for Om Nom and his friends. The stunning graphics and charming animations make this game an enjoyable experience for players of all ages.

Prepare to face an array of engaging and increasingly complex challenges that will keep you entertained for hours. Remember, each level is an adventure waiting to unfold, so keep your wits about you, and don t forget to explore every corner of the vibrant world!

How to play free Cut The Rope 2 game online

To play Cut The Rope 2 online, simply drag your mouse or tap on the ropes to cut them strategically. Ensure that candy falls directly into Om Nom's waiting mouth while collecting stars along the way. Use touch controls on mobile devices for a smooth gameplay experience as you solve each level's delightful puzzles.
