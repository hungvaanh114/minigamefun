Survival on Worm Planet

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Survival On Worm Planet, where your mission is to reclaim the Earth from an overwhelming worm invasion! The environment has been taken over by these relentless creatures, and only you can devise a strategy to eradicate them. Equip yourself with a variety of weapons and take on the challenge of cleaning up the planet. With realistic physics at play, every shot counts, making your survival experience both exciting and engaging.

In this action-packed game, you have the flexibility to choose between single-player mode or team up in two-player mode for cooperative gameplay. Teamwork adds an extra layer of thrill as you strategize with your partner to eliminate waves of worms while ensuring your own survival. The game is designed to keep you on your toes with dynamic worm behavior that tests your skills and reflexes.

As you venture through various levels, you will unlock different weapons, each tailored for maximum impact against the invading worms. The immersive gameplay along with vibrant graphics ensures you are fully engrossed in this fight to save our planet. Remember, every worm you kill brings you one step closer to victory. So gear up, stay alert, and prepare for an unforgettable journey in Survival On Worm Planet!

How to play free Survival On Worm Planet game online

To play the Survival On Worm Planet game, simply use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to navigate your character. Aim your weapon using your mouse and click to fire at the earthworms. In cooperative mode, communicate with your teammate to strategize effectively and combine your firepower for a greater chance of survival!


Instructions

Game controls P1 Fire - Mouse Left click Magazine - Right click mouse Space - Bomb Throw Q - Next bonus E or Mouse Scroll - Change weapon P2 Arrow keys - Aim P - Fire O - Magazine L - Change weapon K - Bomb Throw
