Tonball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exciting world of Tonball, players must test their skills and precision to hit their targets against the clock. The objective is clear: reach the designated target before your time expires. The twist? You have the ability to adjust the weight of the ball simply by clicking your mouse. The heavier the weight you select, the farther the ball will travel upon release.

Strategically determine the ideal weight for your throw by considering the distance to the target. To succeed, players must carefully calculate not only the weight but also the trajectory that will allow the ball to pass through a pot placed strategically along the path. Each throw presents its own set of challenges, and the key to mastering Tonball lies in balancing speed and precision. Tap into your inner strategist as you figure out the adjustments needed to successfully land the ball in the pot.

As you navigate through multiple levels of increasing difficulty, Tonball tests your dexterity and decision-making skills. With dynamic gameplay and engaging mechanics, this game ensures that every throw feels exhilarating. Challenge yourself to better your time with each attempt, and be prepared to adjust your tactics as you take aim at your target. Whether you re aiming for the perfect shot or simply trying to enjoy the challenge, Tonball provides a thrilling gaming experience for players of all ages.

How to play free Tonball game online

To play Tonball, begin by selecting the weight of the ball by clicking your mouse. Aim towards the pot and release the ball to throw it. Remember, the heavier the weight, the farther the ball travels. Adjust accordingly to successfully pass the ball through the pot before time runs out!
