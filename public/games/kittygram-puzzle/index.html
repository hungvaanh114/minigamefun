Kittygram Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Kittygram Puzzle, where the classic logic game "Tangram" converges with irresistibly cute kitties to create the ultimate brain teaser experience! This delightful game challenges you to match blocks of adorable kitty shapes into designated spaces on each level. With 576 unique puzzles to conquer, you'll find yourself immersed in hours of fun while sharpening your spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills.

In this charming world of Kittygram Puzzle, each level presents a fresh challenge. You'll need to think critically and strategically, figuring out how to fit the various kitty-shaped blocks into the empty areas on your game board. The delightful graphics and engaging gameplay will appeal to players of all ages, making it a perfect choice for family-friendly fun or a cozy solo session.

Can you solve all the puzzles and become the ultimate Kittygram Puzzle master? As you progress through the levels, the difficulty will increase, providing an exciting challenge that keeps your mind engaged. Whether you're a seasoned puzzler or a newcomer to the genre, the cute kitties in Kittygram Puzzle will surely capture your heart and keep you coming back for more!

How to play free Kittygram Puzzle game online

To play the Kittygram Puzzle, simply drag and drop the kitty blocks into the empty spaces on the game board. Arrange the pieces to perfectly fit, filling every vacancy. Use the hints available if you get stuck and enjoy the charming visuals as you solve each puzzle.
