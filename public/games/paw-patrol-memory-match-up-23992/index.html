In Paw Patrol Memory Match Up, your mission is straightforward: uncover the matching pairs of cards featuring your favorite Paw Patrol heroes. You'll start with a simple arrangement of four cards, which allows you to familiarize yourself with the gameplay. As you progress and gain confidence, the challenges will become increasingly complicated, introducing additional cards to match.
