Quevi

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Quevi is an exhilarating 2D platformer set in a captivating sci-fi universe, where players embark on an engaging quest to collect shimmering metal balls. As the levels progress, you will navigate through a series of intricately designed obstacles, including relentless enemy robots, menacing flying bots, and perilous projectiles. To succeed, you must skillfully dodge rotating lasers and giant blades while maintaining your focus on the ultimate objective: reaching the elusive Exit door that leads you to the next thrilling level.

The game offers a total of eight progressively challenging levels, each designed to test your reflexes, strategy, and problem-solving abilities. With each stage, the complexity escalates, requiring quick thinking and precise movements to avoid enemy attacks and hazardous traps. The vivid graphics and immersive sound design transport you deeper into the vibrant, sci-fi environment, making every gaming session an unforgettable adventure.

As you traverse through Quevi's exciting landscapes, be sure to gather every metal ball you encounter. Each collection adds to your score and enhances your chances of survival, as you face increasingly cunning adversaries. The platforming mechanics are fluid, allowing you to jump, dash, and evade in a seamless manner that keeps the action fast-paced and exhilarating. Prepare yourself for a journey that challenges both your skills and your wit, as every decision counts in the race towards victory.

How to play free Quevi game online

To play Quevi, use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate your character. Jump to avoid hazards and collect the metal balls while dodging enemy robots and their attacks. Keep an eye on your surroundings to stay safe from traps, and make your way to the Exit door at the end of each level. Enjoy!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the players movement and Use W or up arrow key twice for double jump
