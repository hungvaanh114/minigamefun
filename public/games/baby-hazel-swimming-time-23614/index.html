Baby Hazel Swimming Time

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a fun-filled adventure with Baby Hazel as she embarks on her journey to learn how to swim! In "Baby Hazel Swimming Time," Uncle James has promised Hazel some exciting swimming lessons, and now the moment has finally arrived. It's time for Baby Hazel to dive into the world of aquatic fun!

Join Baby Hazel as she prepares for her swimming sessions. Help her pack her bag with all the essentials, including her favorite swim costumes, goggles, floaties, and towels. Your organizational skills will be put to the test as you ensure that Hazel has everything she needs for her first lesson at the swimming pool.

Once you arrive at the pool, your role shifts to helping Baby Hazel get ready. Assist her in dressing up in her adorable swim gear and ensure she feels comfortable and excited about her lesson. Uncle James will provide instructions on how to swim safely, so pay close attention! You will be cheering for Hazel as she learns different swimming techniques while enjoying the refreshing water.

This engaging game not only entertains but also teaches the importance of water safety and learning to swim. With vibrant graphics and interactive gameplay, you ll find yourself immersed in Baby Hazel's delightful swimming experience.

How to play free Baby Hazel Swimming Time game online

To play "Baby Hazel Swimming Time," start by clicking on the items in Hazel's bag to pack her swimming necessities. Once you reach the swimming pool, assist her in putting on her swim costume and gears. Follow Uncle James s instructions carefully to help Baby Hazel learn how to swim. Enjoy every splash and giggle in this exciting aquatic adventure!
