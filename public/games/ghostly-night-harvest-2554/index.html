Ghostly Night Harvest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Ghostly Night Harvest, an enchanting and thrilling game that seamlessly combines the tranquil experience of nighttime harvesting with the thrilling task of warding off ghostly raiders. In this unique adventure, you take on the role of a devoted farmer, whose main objective is to nurture and cultivate a prosperous pumpkin patch during the daytime hours.

As you sow seeds and care for your crops, the vibrant daylight lets you observe your farm flourish. However, as the sun sets, an eerie twist occurs: malevolent spirits emerge from the shadows, intent on stealing your hard-earned harvest. This is where your strategic thinking kicks in! You must devise effective defense mechanisms and utilize available resources to protect your pumpkins from these spectral foes.

Your skills in farm management will be put to the test as you balance planting and harvesting with safeguarding your crops. Upgrading your defenses, attracting helpful allies, and employing clever tactics will be essential to ensuring that your pumpkins make it through the night unscathed. Will your farming expertise be sufficient to outsmart the ghostly intruders, or will your harvest become just another story in the annals of the haunted fields?

How to play free Ghostly Night Harvest game online

To play Ghostly Night Harvest, start by planting your pumpkin seeds during the daytime phase. Once night falls, switch to defensive mode to thwart the ghostly invaders. Utilize tools and upgrades strategically to protect your crops, all while managing your farming tasks to ensure a successful harvest!
