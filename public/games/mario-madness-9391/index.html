Mario Madness is an exhilarating and blood-soaked top-down shooter that reimagines the beloved Mario universe in ways you've never seen before! Created by the dynamic duo of Noa and Liam Calice for the Weekly Game Jam - Week 50, this game takes players on a wild ride filled with frenetic action and absurdity. You ll navigate through vibrant, yet chaotic environments loaded with perilous enemies and devious obstacles, all while controlling everyone's favorite plumber.

The game integrates classic Mario elements with a twist of carnage, resulting in a uniquely entertaining experience. Armed with an array of outrageous weapons, players must blast through hordes of iconic foes in an effort to survive and achieve high scores. Collect power-ups that enhance your abilities and unlock secret levels that challenge your skills. The art style is a delightful mashup of nostalgia and modern gaming, ensuring players are both amused and engaged during their time in this manic world.

Whether you're a fan of the original Mario franchise or simply looking for a thrilling shooter experience, Mario Madness will keep you on the edge of your seat. It matches the adrenaline of traditional shooters with the quirky charm of a classic platformer, making for a gameplay experience unlike any other!

How to Play Free Mario Madness Game Online

To play Mario Madness, use the WASD keys to move around the map. Aim and shoot with your mouse to take down enemies. Collect power-ups and dodge incoming attacks to survive as long as possible. Conquer each level to progress further in this chaotic adventure!
