Merge to Million

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Merge to Million is an engaging multiplying puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and numerical skills. In this captivating gameplay, players are tasked with merging number-laden blocks on a grid, with the ultimate goal of reaching the impressive milestone of one million. The mechanics are simple yet addictive: drag and drop identical blocks next to each other to combine them and create larger numbers.

As you begin the game, you'll start with humble digits like 2, but the excitement escalates quickly as you climb through thousands and beyond. Each merger opens up new possibilities, and you ll find yourself immersed in the thrill of watching numbers grow. With six types of power-ups available, you can strategize and enhance your gameplay, making challenging levels more manageable and increasing your chances of success.

Combining blocks not only leads to higher values but also offers opportunities to make combos that can propel you to new heights. If you are a fan of games like 2048, Merge to Million promises a similar blend of fun and challenge, enticing you to keep playing to see how high you can go!

How to play free Merge To Million game online

To play Merge to Million, simply drag and drop identical numbered blocks next to each other on the grid. Keep merging blocks to create larger numbers! Use power-ups strategically to boost your progress. Your goal is to combine blocks until you reach the magical number of one million. Enjoy the journey!


Instructions

Click or tap on a tile to select it and click on an empty place on the grid to drop it Make sure the tile you ve selected touches an identical tile
