Reach 100 : Colors Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Reach 100: Colors Game, a captivating puzzle and reflection game that challenges your mathematical skills and quick thinking. In this engaging adventure, your objective is to group numbers in a way that they sum up to 100. With a simple yet stimulating gameplay mechanic, you can slide various shapes and numbers both horizontally and vertically, allowing you to combine them to achieve the required total.

As you successfully create a sum of 100, that particular shape will freeze, making it unusable for the remainder of that level. Your ultimate goal is to gather all shapes and forms to progress to the next stage. With an array of sixty uniquely designed levels, each presenting increasingly complex challenges, Reach 100: Colors Game is sure to put your cognitive abilities to the test. Prepare to engage your brain and strategize your moves as you work towards reaching 100!

Ready to embark on this colorful journey? Each level is crafted to provide a puzzle that requires not only mathematical prowess but also creativity and ingenuity as you explore the best ways to combine different numbers. Get ready to stretch your mind and enjoy the vibrant visuals of the game as you strive to complete every level.

How to play free Reach 100: Colors Game online

To play the game, simply slide the numbered shapes around the grid, aiming to combine them to total 100. You can move the shapes both horizontally and vertically. Once you achieve a sum of 100, the shape will freeze, and you ll need to regroup the remaining shapes to complete the level.
