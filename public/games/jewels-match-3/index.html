Jewels Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Jewels Match 3 is an exciting and engaging match-3 game that takes you on a sparkling adventure through levels filled with stunning jewels. Your primary objective is to clear the dirty cells on each level by aligning three or more jewels of the same type, whether in rows or columns. The game s vibrant graphics and challenging puzzles keep you immersed in its dazzling world.

One of the thrilling aspects of Jewels Match 3 is that each level presents new challenges. As you progress, you'll encounter locked jewels that cannot be swapped, adding an extra layer of strategy to your gameplay. But fear not! By matching jewels of specific shapes, you will unlock special symbols such as bomb, crump, flash, and time symbols that can help you clear obstacles and complete levels more efficiently.

To succeed in Jewels Match 3, you must be quick and strategic. You need to break all the dirty cells within the given time frame to complete each level. As you advance through the game, the puzzles become increasingly complex, requiring keen observation skills and quick reflexes, making for an entertaining and captivating experience.

How to play free Jewels Match 3 game online

To play Jewels Match 3, simply click on the screen to swap adjacent jewels and create a chain of three or more identical jewels. Keep an eye out for special shapes to earn powerful symbols. Aim to break all dirty cells before time runs out to progress to the next level!
