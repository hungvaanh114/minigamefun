Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tap Away is an immersive 3D block movement puzzle arcade game that challenges players to think strategically while having a great time. Your main objective is to launch all the blue blocks off the screen, following the specific directions indicated on each block. As you progress through the game, you will encounter a variety of levels, each designed to test your problem-solving skills and spatial awareness.

What sets Tap Away apart is its unique mechanic of rotating the entire combinations of blocks. By doing so, you can uncover new paths and solutions that weren t immediately visible. This innovative approach adds a fresh dimension to the puzzle genre, encouraging players to experiment and think outside the box. With every completed level, you not only advance through the game but also gain a rewarding sense of achievement.

The game is designed to be both engaging and enjoyable, making it perfect for players of all ages. As you navigate through various challenges, you will find your strategic abilities sharpened, as you ll need to plan moves carefully and utilize the rotation mechanic effectively. With numerous levels to conquer, there's always something new to discover in Tap Away. Get ready to dive into a colorful world of blocks and puzzles, and see if you can master them all!

How to play free Tap Away game online

To play Tap Away, start by selecting a blue block and examining its marked direction. Tap the block to move it, ensuring it aligns with the indicated path. Use the rotation feature to shift the entire block configuration, allowing for more strategic moves. Your goal is to clear all blue blocks, so plan your strategies wisely!
