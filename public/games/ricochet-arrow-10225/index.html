Ricochet Arrow

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure in Ricochet Arrow, where your primary objective is to eliminate menacing skeletons across 30 uniquely designed levels. Each level presents a different challenge, and strategic planning is key. Before you release your arrow, take a moment to assess your surroundings and choose your target carefully. The skeletons are relentless, and they'll never make it easy for you!

You are granted unlimited arrows, which means you can fire as many as you need. However, be cautious! If your arrow ricochets and strikes you, you will lose a life, so clever planning and precision are crucial for your survival. Your skills and reflexes will be tested as you navigate through each level, dodging attacks while trying to eliminate all the skeletons. Can you successfully destroy each skeletal foe and emerge victorious?

With every level completed, the challenge intensifies, providing an exhilarating experience that keeps you on your toes. Are you ready to start your mission to rid the world of the skeletal menace? Gather your focus, and let the arrows fly!

How to play free Ricochet Arrow game online

To play Ricochet Arrow, use your mouse or touch controls to aim your arrow. Click or tap to launch it towards your selected target while avoiding obstacles. Keep an eye on your position to prevent your arrow from bouncing back and hitting you. Clear each level by defeating all skeletons before you run out of lives!
