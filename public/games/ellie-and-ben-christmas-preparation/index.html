Welcome to "Ellie And Ben Christmas Preparation," where festive joy and creativity come together! Ellie and Ben, a delightful new couple, are gearing up to celebrate their very first Christmas together. They are excited to make this holiday unforgettable by transforming their home into a winter wonderland. With each corner of their cozy space waiting for festive cheer, the couple has big plans to decorate with beautiful Christmas ornaments and lights that twinkle with holiday spirit. And of course, they can't forget about the centerpiece of their celebration the Christmas tree, which will be adorned with lovely baubles, garlands, and a shining star on top.

As you join Ellie and Ben on this magical journey, your role is pivotal. Help them select the perfect decorations that reflect their personalities and style. From choosing the color palette to finding the most enchanting ornaments, your creative input is essential. Work alongside the couple as they gather festive decorations from shops and set up their home to embrace the warmth and joy of Christmas. Get involved in fun activities like wrapping gifts, baking delicious holiday treats, and preparing their home for guests. Your choices will help create the most marvelous ambiance, turning their celebration into a treasured memory.

How to play free Ellie And Ben Christmas Preparation game online

To play the Ellie And Ben Christmas Preparation game online, simply navigate through the colorful interface. Use your mouse or touch controls to select decoration items and interact with the surroundings. Follow the prompts to help Ellie and Ben decorate their home and Christmas tree, completing tasks to unlock new festive features, and make their holiday truly special!
