Among Float Connect

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a world of excitement with Among Float Connect, a captivating puzzle game where your goal is to link identical items on the board. The challenge lies in connecting two similar "among" icons with paths that consist of a maximum of two 90-degree angles. Strategy and quick thinking are essential as you work to clear the board by removing all pairs of matching items.

Each level brings a new twist, as some tiles can float in various directions moving downwards, upwards, left, right, to the center, or even splitting apart. This unique mechanic requires players to stay alert and adapt their strategies to navigate the ever-changing layout. With 27 meticulously designed levels, you are bound to be enthralled by the progressive difficulties presented to you as you advance through the game.

To earn extra bonuses, make sure to complete each level before the timer runs out! This adds an extra layer of excitement, pushing you to think and react quickly while still enjoying the gratifying process of connecting pairs. Challenge yourself and see how far you can go in this engaging game, ensuring both fun and mental stimulation.

How to play free Among Float Connect game online

To play Among Float Connect, simply select two matching items on the board and draw a line between them using no more than two right angles. Be mindful of floating tiles and strategically plan your moves to clear the board quickly within the time limit for bonus points!
