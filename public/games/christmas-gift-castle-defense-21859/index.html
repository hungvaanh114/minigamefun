Christmas Gift Castle Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the festive world of Christmas Gift Castle Defense, where the spirit of the season meets thrilling strategic gameplay. As the clock strikes midnight, ushering in the 2021 New Year, it becomes your solemn duty to safeguard the precious gifts housed within the castle walls. However, beware! Malevolent enemy characters have their sights set on your treasured presents and will stop at nothing to pilfer them. Armed with a trusty bow and a quiver of arrows, your reflexes and coordination will be put to the ultimate test as you fend off these persistent attackers.

With each victory, the stakes rise, and you must remain vigilant to defend the Gift Stronghold. Should three gifts fall into the hands of the enemy, it spells disaster and a game restart, providing a stark reminder of the importance of your role as protector. The more you play, the more you ll hone your skills, ensuring that no gift is ever taken without a fight. Gather your courage and sharpen your aim, as you embark on this exhilarating adventure filled with holiday cheer and competitive spirit.

How to play free Christmas Gift Castle Defense game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse or touch controls to aim at the incoming enemies. Release to shoot arrows at them before they reach the gifts. Keep a close eye on the remaining number of gifts, as losing three will cause you to restart. Stay alert and enjoy the festive challenge!
