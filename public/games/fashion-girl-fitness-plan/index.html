Fashion Girl Fitness Plan

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

As summer approaches, Adella's excitement about her beach holiday is palpable. However, she's struggling with some extra pounds after indulging a bit too much in her favorite treats. It's a critical time for Adella, and she knows that shedding those extra pounds is essential if she wants to feel confident in her bikini. The good news is that she has the opportunity to train with you, her expert fitness trainer who will guide her in achieving her goals.

This is where the Fashion Girl Fitness Plan comes into play. Your role is to craft a customized fitness regimen that combines enjoyable workouts and a nutritious diet tailored to Adella's needs. You will help her discover the right balance between fun and effective training sessions, ensuring that every workout is exciting. Choose from a variety of fitness games and activities that keep her engaged and motivated.

The challenge lies in not just improving Adella's physical appearance but also boosting her self-esteem and confidence before her vacation. Your creativity in designing dynamic exercises will play a key role in her transformation. Watch as she progresses through different workouts, builds strength, and adopts healthier eating habits with your guidance. With your hard work and dedication, Adella will be beach-ready in no time!

How to play free Fashion Girl Fitness Plan game online

To start playing Fashion Girl Fitness Plan, simply select your training program and choose fitness exercises from the available options. Follow the on-screen instructions to guide Adella through each workout. Remember to monitor her dietary choices and help her stay motivated throughout the game!
