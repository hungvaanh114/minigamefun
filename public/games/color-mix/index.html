How to play free Color Mix game online

To play the Color Mix game, simply select two colors from the palette available on your screen. Once you've chosen, drag and drop them into the mixing area. After mixing, the resulting color will appear, and your goal is to match it with the target color shown. Keep experimenting as you progress through the challenging levels!
