Break Glass Wine

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the fun and excitement of Break Glass Wine, a thrilling game that challenges players to shatter wine glasses using colorful spheres. The objective is simple yet engaging: throw various vibrant balls to strategically break all the wine glasses presented in each level. As you progress through the game, you ll encounter an array of obstacles that will require skill and precision to overcome.

In Break Glass Wine, players are equipped with a diverse set of physical objects, including giant spheres, sticks, and cubes, each presenting unique ways to achieve victory. The elegance of the game lies in its mechanics, where the interaction between various elements creates a dynamic and entertaining experience that keeps players on their toes. With multiple levels to conquer, you will need to showcase your throwing skills and strategic thinking to effectively utilize the available objects and break the delicate wine glasses.

Prepare for a delightful journey as you navigate through captivating levels, unlocking new challenges and surprises as you progress. The colorful aesthetics and satisfying shattering sound of broken glass make Break Glass Wine an exhilarating game suitable for players of all ages. Gather your friends, compete for high scores, and revel in the joy of smashing those wine glasses!

How to play free Break Glass Wine game online

To play Break Glass Wine, simply enter the game and use your mouse or finger to aim at the wine glasses. Choose the type of object you wish to throw, then release to watch the excitement unfold as you break the glasses. Aim strategically to maximize your score and progress through each level!
