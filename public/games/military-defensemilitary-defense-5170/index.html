Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the gripping world of Military Defense, where you take command of a powerful cannon designed to protect your base from incoming enemy forces. The objective is clear: eliminate your adversaries before they breach your defenses. However, there's a critical twist civilians are present on the battlefield, and any harm caused to them will result in an immediate end to your mission. The stakes are high, and your decisions must be swift and precise.

The gameplay is straightforward yet incredibly engaging. You will need to demonstrate your ability to make quick judgments while firing to ensure that no innocent lives are lost. The game utilizes a dual-screen control system: by tapping on the right side of the screen, you can shoot at enemies to the right, while tapping on the left side targets foes to the left. The simplicity of the controls belies the strategic depths of each encounter, as every shot could mean the difference between victory and failure.

As you progress through multiple levels, the challenges will intensify. Enemies will evolve, presenting increasingly complex patterns of attack, demanding you to sharpen your reflexes and hone your tactical skills. Can you maintain your composure under pressure and keep the civilians safe while defending your territory? Play Military Defense to find out!

How to play free Military Defense game online

To play Military Defense, simply tap on either side of the screen to fire your cannon and take out enemies. Be alert and respond quickly to prevent enemy forces from infiltrating your base while ensuring that civilians are unharmed. Stay vigilant and aim accurately to achieve high scores!
