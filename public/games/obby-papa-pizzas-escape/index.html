Obby Papa Pizzas Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in Obby Papa Pizza's Escape, where you join Obby and Bacon in their desperate bid to flee from a chaotic pizza parlor. Your mission is to help the duo navigate through a series of challenging obstacles while avoiding the wrath of the infuriated Chef Papa Pizza. Team up with your friends as you strategize your way out and keep an eye out for the ever-vigilant pizza maker patrolling the area.

As you progress, be cautious of the various perils scattered throughout the pizzeria, for the pizza maker is determined to capture you. If you re caught, all your efforts to escape will be in vain. Yet, hope is not lost! Bacon wields a sword that can defeat the pizza maker when thrown with precision. Time your moves carefully to ensure his victory in battle.

Your ultimate goal is to gather all the pizzas hidden within the premises while making your way toward the red door that signifies freedom. The clock is ticking, and every second counts as you try to avoid detection. Gather your courage, rally your friends, and dive into the adventure that is Obby Papa Pizza's Escape!

How to play free Obby Papa Pizza's Escape game online

To play Obby Papa Pizza's Escape, simply use your keyboard to control Obby and Bacon's movements. Navigate through the obstacles, collect pizzas, and remain alert to avoid the pizza maker. Time Bacon's sword throws accurately to defeat any approaching threats and race towards the red door to complete your escape!
