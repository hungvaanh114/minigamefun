Amazeballs Estate Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Amazeballs Estate Escape, a captivating point-and-click adventure brought to life by 8B Games/Games2Mad. Picture this: a fun day spent with your family at a beautiful estate, filled with relaxation and entertainment. However, excitement turns to tension when you find yourselves unexpectedly trapped inside the estate. The security guard, oblivious to your playful antics, has locked the gate and left the premises.

Your mission is to team up with your family to solve intriguing puzzles and uncover hidden objects scattered throughout the estate. Each challenge you encounter will test your creativity and problem-solving skills, as you work against the clock to find your way out. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay will keep you entertained as you delve deeper into the estate, unraveling its many secrets along the way. Can you escape before it s too late? Good luck, and have fun!

How to play free Amazeballs Estate Escape game online

To play Amazeballs Estate Escape, simply use your mouse to explore the estate and interact with various objects. Click on items to pick them up or examine them closely. Solve puzzles by finding clues and combining items to unlock new areas. Work together with your family to escape the estate before time runs out!
