Dot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Dot, an incredibly challenging puzzle game that will test your problem-solving skills and strategic thinking. In this captivating game, you are tasked with controlling a square and guiding it to your target position. At each turn, you will be required to choose whether to turn right or left, navigating through an intricate layout filled with colored dots.

The goal is straightforward: align all the dots with their corresponding colored squares. However, don't let the simplicity of the rule fool you the challenge lies in the limited number of moves you can make. With every step you take, the pressure escalates, as you must think ahead and plan your route meticulously to solve the puzzle within the constraints provided. The combination of time pressure and movement limitations creates a thrilling yet frustrating experience that keeps players coming back for more.

As you progress through the levels, the puzzles become increasingly complex, requiring not only logic but also a keen sense of spatial awareness. Dot is perfect for those who enjoy a good mental workout. You'll find yourself engaged for hours as you strive to reach the ultimate goal of aligning every dot on the board.

How to play free Dot game online

To play Dot, use your mouse or touch controls to direct the square toward its target. After reaching the position, select to turn right or left to align the dots with their corresponding squares. Keep an eye on your limited moves and strategize your path wisely to achieve the best results!
