Pumpkin Doodle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Pumpkin Jack on a thrilling adventure in the enchanting world of Pumpkin Doodle! In this festive journey, you'll help our brave hero gather all the precious coins and sparkling rings scattered throughout a vibrant Halloween landscape. Your mission is vital, as these treasures hold the key to reviving the Spirit of Halloween. But beware! The path is fraught with challenges, as mischievous ghosts lurk around every corner, ready to thwart your progress. Your agility and quick thinking will be put to the test as you navigate through this whimsical realm.

As you guide Pumpkin Jack, you'll encounter various obstacles and surprises that make each level uniquely exciting. Use your skills to hop over or dodge spirits while collecting every coin and ring you can find. The more treasures you gather, the closer you get to restoring the spirit of the season. Beautifully designed graphics and delightful sound effects enhance your gaming experience, drawing you deeper into this enchanting Halloween adventure.

With intuitive controls, Pumpkin Doodle is easy to pick up but offers plenty of challenges for seasoned players seeking to refine their skills. Each level presents new ghostly foes and obstacles that require a clever strategy to overcome. Can you help Pumpkin Jack save Halloween before time runs out? Join the fun in this captivating game that combines adventure, strategy, and festive spirit!

How to play free Pumpkin Doodle game online

To play Pumpkin Doodle, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move Pumpkin Jack. Collect coins and rings while avoiding ghosts by jumping or moving aside. Keep an eye on the timer, as you race to gather treasures and complete each level before time runs out. Have fun!
