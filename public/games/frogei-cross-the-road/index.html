Frogei Cross the Road

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the lively world of Frogie Cross the Road, an entertaining adventure game where players embark on a thrilling journey alongside Frogie, the charming frog. Your mission is to guide Frogie across busy roads, rushing rivers, and enchanting countryside landscapes. But beware! The path is filled with challenges, including speeding cars, wild boars, and treacherous water obstacles.

As you navigate each level, you'll need to stay on your toes to avoid being hit by oncoming vehicles and the menacing wild boars lurking about. Timing is crucial as you leap across logs floating in the river, ensuring you don't plunge into the water below. The clock is ticking, so make every jump count!

With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Frogie Cross the Road promises a delightful experience for players of all ages. Challenge your reflexes and strategic skills as you strive to reach the finish line before time runs out. Each level offers unique obstacles that will keep you entertained and coming back for more.

Can you help Frogie successfully cross each challenging path and conquer new heights in this thrilling journey? Have fun and test your skills in this delightful adventure!

How to play free Frogei Cross The Road game online

To play Frogie Cross the Road, use the arrow keys or touchscreen controls to guide Frogie. Navigate him to dodge cars and wild boars while timing your jumps over logs in the river. Collect bonuses and reach the end of each level before the timer expires!
