Jewel Monsters

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Venture into the enchanting world of Jewel Monsters, where an exciting adventure awaits you amidst the mystical forest filled with formidable creatures. As you journey through this magical domain, you ll encounter various monsters that lurk among the trees, ready to challenge your skills. Your objective is straightforward: match 3 or more gems of the same color to launch an attack on the lurking monsters.

Strategically combine gems to unleash powerful moves, eliminating the threatening creatures blocking your path. But beware! The monsters won t wait for you to make your move; they will retaliate if you delay. Stay on your toes and act swiftly to defeat them before they get the chance to strike first. Luckily, there s a lifeline when the battle gets tough: by matching 4 or more gems, you can create a heart bonus that appears within one of the gems. Collect this bonus to restore a portion of your health, ensuring you stay in the fight as long as possible!

Experience the thrill of battling in Jewel Monsters while engaging your puzzle-solving skills. Each level brings unique challenges, captivating visuals, and an immersive gameplay experience. Can you conquer the forest and emerge victorious against the jewel-infused adversaries that stand in your way? It s time to match your way to success!

How to play free Jewel Monsters game online

To play Jewel Monsters, start by selecting gems on the game board. Create matches of 3 or more gems of the same color to attack monsters. If you match 4 or more gems, collect the heart bonus that appears to restore health. Keep matching quickly to avoid monster attacks!


Instructions

Drag a gem to swap it with one of the adjacent gems to make a line of 3 or more gems of the same color Make the matches of the same color with the monster to attack
