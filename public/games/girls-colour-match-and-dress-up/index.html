Girls Colour Match and Dress Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a vibrant world of fashion and fun with "Girls Colour Match and Dress Up"! This engaging game invites you to join your favorite characters, where the ultimate objective is to perfectly match colors and outfits. By selecting one of the girls, you're tasked with picking your favorite color, which will dictate the dresses you can choose from. Only the dresses that align with the selected color will earn you points.

The challenge intensifies as you race against a speed meter. Choosing the right dress will boost your speed meter, enhancing your score and excitement. However, make a misstep by selecting a dress in the wrong color, and you ll see the speed meter decline, putting your progress in jeopardy. It's not just about aesthetics; it's a thrilling blend of color coordination and strategic dress selection. The fun really ramps up as you unlock various dresses and colors as you progress through the game!

How to play free Girls Colour Match and Dress up game online

To start playing, choose your favorite girl and pick a color from the options provided. As you select, keep an eye on the speed meter; it will react to your choices. Match the dresses to the selected color to increase your speed. Avoid selecting mismatched colors, or you won't be able to progress!
