Instructions:

Step into an enchanting underwater world with the "Magic Mermaid Salon," a delightful make-up game designed especially for girls who adore mermaids and magical transformations. In this whimsical aquatic realm, young mermaid girls from all corners of the kingdom gather to celebrate the grand birthday ball of the Mermaid King. It s the most awaited event of the year, filled with splendor, joy, and a touch of magic!

Your mission is to prepare a little mermaid princess for this spectacular occasion. With a myriad of stunning hair colors, shimmering scales, and mesmerizing outfits at your disposal, you have the power to unleash your creativity. Choose from an array of make-up options that reflect the dazzling beauty of the ocean. From sparkling eyeshadow to glistening lip gloss, help the mermaid princess shine like never before.

Once the make-up is complete, explore a breathtaking selection of enchanting dresses and accessories that will truly make her stand out at the ball. Choose flowing gowns adorned with shells and pearls, or funky outfits that reflect her playful spirit. Every choice you make will contribute to her overall look, ensuring that she dazzles everyone at the celebration.

Get ready to showcase your fashion skills in this magical setting. With each stroke of your brush and each elegant outfit selected, you will make the mermaid princess ready to captivate hearts and make memories at the grand ball. Your careful dressing will undoubtedly leave everyone in awe as the princess makes her entrance!

How to play free Magic Mermaid Salon game online

To play the "Magic Mermaid Salon," simply start the game and follow the on-screen instructions. Choose and customize the mermaid's hairstyle, make-up, and outfit. Use your mouse to select options and drag them onto the mermaid, ensuring she looks fabulous. Once you've finished, you can view her stunning transformation for the ball!
