Pocket League 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pocket League 3D is an engaging and exhilarating car soccer game that delivers endless fun for both solo players and those seeking a competitive duo experience. With the option to include AI bots, players have additional allies on the virtual field, making for an action-packed gameplay experience. The thrilling mechanics allow players to leap into the air with their cars, leading to more powerful strikes on the ball. A well-timed double jump can elevate your vehicle, allowing it to perform flips that enhance your impact and improve your chances of scoring.

Customize your gameplay by selecting the flag of your favorite nation as you compete to dominate the scoreboard. Will you lead your team to victory by scoring the most goals before the timer runs out? The simpler controls combined with the vibrant visuals create an atmosphere that is both accessible and exciting for players of all skill levels. With Pocket League 3D in your arsenal, each match becomes a unique combination of strategy, skill, and fun.

How to play free Pocket League 3D game online

To play Pocket League 3D, simply choose between single-player mode or multiplayer mode to compete against friends or AI bots. Control your vehicle using arrow keys, navigate the field, and aim to kick the ball into the opposing goal. Utilize your jumping and double-jump abilities strategically to enhance your shots and outmaneuver your opponents!

If playing single Move ARROW KEYS Jump SPACE Flip Double SPACE If playing two player PLAYER 1 Move ARROW KEYS Jump L Flip Double L PLAYER 2 Move W A S D Jump G Flip Double G
