Granny House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the eerie world of Granny House, a chilling escape game that will test your nerves and stealth. Set within the confines of an abandoned asylum, you must navigate the dimly lit hallways and hidden rooms while evading the terrifying Granny, who lurks within. The atmosphere is thick with tension, and every creak of the floorboards and whisper of the wind adds to your unease. To survive, you must move with utmost caution and avoid making any sound that could alert Granny to your presence.

Every corner could hide danger, and each room may contain clues or tools that can aid your escape. As you explore, gather items that can help you unlock the escape route. But beware; every decision you make could lead you closer to freedom or right into Granny s clutches. Keep your wits about you, listening carefully for any sign that she is near, and remember that the key to survival is silence.

Granny House offers a thrilling mix of strategy and suspense, where your ability to think on your feet and remain undetected will ultimately determine your fate. Will you be the one to break free from this haunted place, or will you fall victim to the horrors that trap you within? The clock is ticking, and the terror is real; prepare for a heart-pounding experience!

How to play free Granny House game online

To play the Granny House game online, use the directional keys on your keyboard to move around the asylum. Be sure to interact with objects by clicking or pressing the designated keys to pick them up. Always listen for sounds that may indicate Granny's presence and practice stealth as you search for an escape route.
