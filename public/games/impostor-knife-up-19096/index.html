Impostor Knife Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating experience with Impostor Knife Up, a knife throwing game that promises to keep you on the edge of your seat! In this action-packed challenge, you'll be aiming at the recognizable Amongus characters, testing your precision and skill with each throw. The more accurate your throws, the more points you rack up!

What sets this game apart is the additional scoring opportunity: hitting the white spaces. These areas are your golden ticket to achieving the highest scores, adding an extra layer of strategy to your gameplay. So, are you up for the challenge? Perfect your aim, earn those points, and see if you can claim the title of the top player!

As you immerse yourself in this thrilling adventure, unlock new levels and benefits with every successful throw. Compete against your friends or players around the world to establish yourself as the ultimate knife-throwing champion. With each game, you ll improve your timing, learn to strategize better, and enjoy the fast-paced action that Impostor Knife Up has to offer. Are you ready to take your skills to the next level and dominate the leaderboards? Get started now!

How to play free Impostor Knife Up game online

To play Impostor Knife Up, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the Amongus character. Tap or click to throw your knife. Focus on hitting the white spaces for bonus points! Keep an eye on the target, improve your aim, and compete for the best score!
