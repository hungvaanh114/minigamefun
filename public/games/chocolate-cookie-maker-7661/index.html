Chocolate Cookie Maker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Chocolate Cookie Maker, a charming cooking game that will satisfy your sweet tooth! Meet Zoe, a chocolate enthusiast whose ultimate treat is the mouthwatering chocolate cookie. When her best friend, Sofia, decides to surprise her with a batch of homemade cookies, it s a journey full of laughter and learning.

However, there s a twist Sofia has never baked cookies before! Determined to impress Zoe, she rushes to the store to buy a recipe that promises scrumptious results. With the instructions in hand, Sofia is ready to embark on her baking adventure. This is where you come in! Can you assist Sofia in creating the perfect chocolate cookies that will leave Zoe delighted?

Gather your ingredients, mix them together, and follow the steps outlined in the recipe. From preparing the dough to savoring the sweet aroma of freshly baked cookies, every moment is filled with excitement and fun. Along the way, you can experiment with various toppings and chocolate types to make each cookie unique. Experience the joy of baking and the satisfaction of surprising a friend with a heartfelt gift!

How to play free Chocolate Cookie Maker game online

To start playing the Chocolate Cookie Maker game, simply follow the on-screen instructions. First, gather the necessary ingredients by clicking on them. Once you have everything, mix the ingredients in the correct order according to the recipe. Bake the cookies in the oven and finally, decorate them to your liking before sending them off to Zoe!
