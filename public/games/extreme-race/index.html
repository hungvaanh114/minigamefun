Extreme Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the adrenaline-pumping world of Extreme Race, a thrilling game that takes racing to a whole new level! In this intense experience, you will take the wheel of a car that is designed for speed but lacks any brakes. Your primary objective is to navigate through a bustling racecourse while avoiding collisions with other vehicles. With every second that passes, your car accelerates, testing your reflexes and decision-making skills.

Your journey involves not just speed, but also strategy. As you race through the chaotic streets, your goal is to collect as many coins as possible. These coins serve as your currency to unlock and purchase new, high-performance vehicles that will help you tackle even more daring challenges. Each new car has its unique attributes, providing you with a chance to enhance your gameplay. Will you opt for the sleek sports car, or choose an off-road beast? The choice is yours!

Extreme Race is not just about raw speed; it s a test of your ability to maneuver your vehicle skillfully while keeping an eye out for obstacles. The tension builds as you strive to gather coins, constantly battling against the urge to crash. Every successful run gives you the chance to climb higher on the leaderboard, showcasing your skills to other players. So buckle up for an exhilarating ride where speed, skill, and strategy collide!

How to play free Extreme Race game online

To play Extreme Race, simply use the arrow keys to steer your car around the track. Collect coins as you drive to increase your score and unlock new vehicles. Avoid crashing into other cars while racing at high speeds for an exciting experience. Enjoy the thrill!
