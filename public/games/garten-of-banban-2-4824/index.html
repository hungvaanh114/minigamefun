Garten of Banban 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the official mobile game, Garten of Banban 2! Step into Banban s Kindergarten, a whimsical yet ominous place where adventure awaits at every turn. As you navigate this intriguing establishment, you ll have the chance to form friendships with the lovable characters of the Banban And Friends Gang. However, be on guard, as darker secrets lurk beneath the surface, and losing your life and sanity is a real possibility!

Once a favored destination for parents seeking a reputable kindergarten for their little ones, Banban s Kindergarten has now become a site of mystery. On an ordinary day, everything changed, and the inhabitants simply vanished. It s your mission to uncover the chilling truth behind this disappearance, and to do so, you'll need to explore every nook and cranny of the eerie institution.

To help you on your quest, you ll be accompanied by a trusty flying drone. This handy device will assist you in navigating the challenging environments while providing companionship when the shadows begin to close in. You might feel alone and frightened, but your drone buddy will always be by your side, ready to support you. Celebrate the thrill of exploration and let your imagination run wild in this unique experience!

This holiday season, why not add an element of fun to your adventures? Step into the festive world of Garten of Banban, where you can decorate for Halloween or Christmas with playful pranks. From witches and vampires to clever sound effects and spooky surprises, you ll have everything you need to become the ultimate prank master and create memorable moments for your friends.

How to play free Garten of Banban 2 game online

To play Garten of Banban 2 online, simply launch the game, navigate through the various rooms using your virtual joystick, and collect items to unlock secrets. Utilize your drone's abilities to reveal hidden paths and complete puzzles to progress through the game. Stay alert, as dangers may lurk at every corner!
