Golden Scarabeaus 2022

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Golden Scarabeaus 2022, a captivating puzzle adventure that challenges your strategic thinking and quick reflexes! In this game, your primary mission is to collect all the sparkling golden scarabs scattered throughout each level. To achieve this, you will have some adorable little square minions at your disposal. Each minion is unique and brings its own set of abilities to the game, making it essential for you to utilize them effectively.

The fun begins as you click on the minions to activate their special powers. Some minions will gracefully float above obstacles, while others can roll across surfaces or make impressive jumps to navigate tricky areas. Additionally, certain minions are smarter than the rest; they ll interact with different environmental objects in specific ways. For example, some can smash through blocks, giving you access to otherwise unreachable areas. To make the gameplay even more exciting, you can manipulate lasers that either attract or repel these little creatures. A well-timed click can change the direction of your minions and help them avoid hazardous traps.

Planning is key in navigating through each level! You ll need to devise proper sequences for activating switches, maneuvering blocks, and leading your minions safely past obstacles to ensure they reach the golden scarabs. The challenge lies in figuring out the optimal order to use the minions abilities to enhance your chances of success on each stage.

How to play free Golden Scarabeaus 2022 game online

To play Golden Scarabeaus 2022, click on the minions to utilize their abilities strategically. Use the lasers to either attract or repel them, ensuring they avoid hazards. Activate switches and move blocks in a planned sequence to guide each minion safely to the golden scarabs!
