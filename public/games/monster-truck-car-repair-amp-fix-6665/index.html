How to play free Monster Truck: Car Repair & Fix game online

To start playing Monster Truck: Car Repair & Fix, select a vehicle that needs repairs. Use the on-screen tools to fix various parts such as the tires, engine, and body. Once the repairs are complete, choose your paint color and decorative patterns to personalize your truck. Share your unique designs with friends and enjoy the thrill of being a master car designer!
