Bus Simulator 2015 is the ultimate simulation game that allows you to step into the shoes of a professional bus driver! With stunningly realistic maps and an array of incredible vehicles, you will truly feel like you're behind the wheel of an authentic bus. It's time to take your seat and navigate through various routes, completing your missions with style!

Featuring cutting-edge graphics, including lifelike animations of passengers, articulated buses, and even double-decker school buses, this game sets a high standard for bus simulations on the market. Whether you're navigating the bustling streets of Los Angeles, Paris, or Rome, or traversing unique landscapes such as the serene countryside, mountainous terrains, or snowy depths, every drive is a new adventure.

Bus Simulator 2015 boasts an impressive selection of 15 diverse bus types, including articulated and double-decker models, along with school buses. The game also includes interactive elements such as open/close buttons for doors, letting you accommodate animated passengers as they board and disembark from the bus. Prepare to face dynamic weather conditions in Free Ride mode, ensuring that no two rides are ever the same. You can also enjoy detailed interiors, intelligent traffic systems, and customizable controls, creating an immersive driving experience!
