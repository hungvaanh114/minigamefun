To play MOTO MANIACS 3, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate your bike. The up arrow accelerates, the down arrow brakes, and left/right arrows help you tilt your bike during jumps. Aim for speed while maintaining balance to conquer each breathtaking level!
