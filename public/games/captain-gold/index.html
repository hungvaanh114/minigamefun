Captain Gold

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey with Captain Gold, a hyper-casual game that immerses players in an adventurous quest for treasure. In this engaging experience, you are tasked with aiding Captain, the main character, as he explores remote areas far from the bustling city to uncover valuable resources hidden deep within the mines.

As Captain diligently extracts gold, diamonds, rubies, and other precious stones, your role is to assist him in smashing these treasures. Equipped with a trusty hammer, you must throw it accurately at the stones to break them apart. The more effectively you shatter these gems, the richer Captain becomes!

The game mechanic is designed for ease of use, making it accessible to players of all skill levels. You'll find yourself caught up in the thrill of collecting an array of shining treasures and transporting them to Captain s cart, where they can be safely stored. Each successful smash brings you closer to achieving a new high score, encouraging you to keep playing and improving your skills. Whether you're a casual gamer or an experienced player, Captain Gold promises an addictive and enjoyable experience.

How to play free Captain Gold game online

To start playing Captain Gold, simply launch the game and use your mouse or touch controls to aim and throw the hammer at the stones. Time your throws carefully to maximize damage and collect the treasures. As you progress, keep an eye out for bonus items to enhance your treasure-hunting experience!
