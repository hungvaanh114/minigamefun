Not My Neighbor Hidden Stars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thrilling experience with the captivating game, Not My Neighbor Hidden Stars. This engaging object-search game is the perfect source of entertainment, combining challenging gameplay with a quest to uncover hidden stars scattered across various vibrant scenes. With a total of 10 exciting levels, each featuring a unique layout and a varying number of stars to discover, players will experience a gradual increase in difficulty that keeps the game fresh and stimulating.

The game is designed to challenge your observation skills, as you will be racing against the clock to find each hidden star before time runs out. A timer adds a layer of excitement, urging you to be as quick as possible. However, exercise caution! Every incorrect click will cost you an extra 5 seconds, intensifying the challenge as you strive to complete each level efficiently. Will you manage to beat the clock and reveal all the hidden stars? This game promises hours of fun and excitement for players of all ages!

To get started, simply visit ulyagames.com and dive into the mesmerizing world of hidden objects and star searches. The adventure awaits you!

How to play free Not My Neighbor Hidden Stars game online

To play Not My Neighbor Hidden Stars, begin by selecting a level from the main menu. Once in a level, examine the scene carefully and look for the stars outlined for you. Use your mouse or touch screen to click on the stars as you find them. Remember, act quickly to beat the timer and avoid mistakes that will deduct time!
