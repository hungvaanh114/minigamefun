Angry Heroes Birds

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant realm of Angry Heroes Birds, where fury and chaos reign supreme! Join the feisty, colorful birds in their relentless quest to vanquish their arch-nemeses, the green piggies. These crafty foes have built impenetrable fortresses that must be dismantled by the determined avian warriors. Your mission is clear: hunt down every last piggie, obliterate their castles, and ensure they never escape your wrath. With each level you conquer, the challenges increase, demanding strategic thinking and pinpoint accuracy.

Armed with a limited number of brave bird soldiers, you must use your wits and agility to ensure their success before you run out of lives. Each bird possesses unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle, so choose your soldiers wisely for maximum effectiveness. Guiding your heroes through explosive slingshot arcs, you ll want to aim strategically to cause maximum damage and defeat the piggies hidden within their fortress walls. The satisfaction of watching your enemy crumble under the fury of your birds is an exhilarating experience!

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter various types of piggies, each with its own defenses and weaknesses. Unlock new characters and power-ups along the way to keep the game fresh and exciting. Join the ranks of these angry heroes, and let your journey of destruction and victory begin!

How to play free Angry Heroes Birds game online

To play Angry Heroes Birds, simply use your mouse to aim and pull back on the slingshot. Release to launch your bird toward the piggies castles. Try to knock down the structures while defeating the piggies within, ensuring you do so before running out of birds. Enjoy the chaos!
