Easter Link

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the delightful world of Easter Link, a captivating Mahjong linking game that adds a twist to the classic gameplay. The objective is simple yet challenging: match two identical blocks to clear the board. However, there's a catch! The connecting line between the pairing tiles can contain no more than two turns, adding an intriguing layer of strategy to each move.

As you navigate through this engaging version of the beloved board game, you'll find yourself racing against the clock. Each level presents a colorful array of tiles adorned with Easter-themed designs, enticing you to make connections while pressure mounts. Will you be able to clear the board before time runs out? With your sharpest wits and a keen eye, you can find and connect the matching tiles, but you must act quickly to conquer the ticking timer.

Easter Link is not just about matching tiles; it's a test of your ability to think strategically and act swiftly. Players of all ages can enjoy this variation of Mahjong, making it perfect for family play or a solo adventure. Dive into the world of Easter Link and challenge yourself to achieve high scores while enjoying the festive theme. It s time to put your matching skills to the test and see if you can clear the board!

How to play free Easter Link game online

To start playing Easter Link, simply select a tile, then click on another identical tile to make a match. Remember, you can only connect the tiles with a maximum of two turns in the connecting line. Keep an eye on the timer, and strive to clear the board before time runs out!
