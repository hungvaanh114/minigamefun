Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to dive into the festive fun with Xmas Float Connect, a delightful puzzle game where your holiday spirits will soar. The objective is simple yet challenging: connect two identical Christmas items using a path consisting of no more than two 90-degree angles. Strategically clear the board by eliminating all the matching pairs of seasonal icons before time runs out. Each level presents unique obstacles, including floating item tiles that can shift in various directions downwards, upwards, to the left, to the right, and even to the center or split! This adds an extra layer of complexity and excitement to your gaming experience.

With 27 captivating levels to conquer, Xmas Float Connect will keep you entertained throughout the holiday season. As you progress, the challenges will increase, requiring both speed and logic to successfully make your connections. Don t forget to keep an eye on the clock a timely completion of each level rewards you with additional bonuses. This holiday-themed game is perfect for players of all ages, offering a harmonious blend of fun, strategy, and creativity.

How to play free Xmas Float Connect game online

To play Xmas Float Connect, simply select two identical Christmas items on the board to create a connecting path. Remember, your path may not have more than two 90-degree angles. Aside from strategic connections, be aware of the floating items that can change position, which may impact your gameplay. Aim to clear the board before time runs out to earn bonuses!
