Fury Bike Rider

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fury Bike Rider is an exhilarating bike racing and simulation game that offers players stunning graphics and immersive gameplay, featuring realistic physics to enhance the riding experience. With a comprehensive motorcycle purchase and upgrade system, you have the freedom to customize your vehicles according to your racing preferences. You can challenge yourself against AI opponents or explore the vast open world freely.

In the Racing mode, you ll encounter 24 unique racetracks where you compete against five AI racers. Aim for a top-three finish to progress to the next challenging circuit. Your performance not only earns you in-game currency based on your race completion time but also rewards you for drifts and airborne stunts. Explore two engaging racing maps, Sky City and Rally Road, to test your skills.

The Challenge mode pushes your limits, requiring you to reach the finish line while overcoming various obstacles. With 24 tracks available, you ll race against the clock to achieve the best possible time and earn star ratings. Two distinct challenge maps, Up The Sea and Deep Forest, provide thrilling environments for your competitive spirit.

For those who prefer a more relaxed experience, the Free Driving mode allows you to roam an expansive world with diverse landscapes. Explore cities, majestic mountains, serene plains, and bustling ports. Collect 80 diamonds scattered across the map, which can be used to unlock new motorcycles. Additionally, stay in the free-drive area for three continuous minutes to earn bonus rewards while a countdown timer ticks down.

How to play free Fury Bike Rider game online

To play Fury Bike Rider online, select your desired game mode - Racing, Challenge, or Free Driving. Use the arrow keys or a game controller to navigate your motorcycle. Complete the races or challenges while collecting diamonds in Free Driving mode. Enjoy the thrill of the ride!
