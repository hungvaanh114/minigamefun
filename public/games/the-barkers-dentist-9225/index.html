The Barkers Dentist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the beloved Barkers family as they embark on an amusing trip to visit their new dentist, Rosa, who is just starting her journey into the world of pediatric dentistry. Excited yet nervous, the Barkers are ready to tackle their dental adventures at the children's hospital, where Rosa is eager to learn the ins and outs of her new profession.

Rosa's enthusiasm for her internship is palpable, but the thought of treating the lively Barboskin family brings a mix of joy and trepidation. As each character approaches the dental chair, their reactions vary from delight to apprehension. Will Rosa's inexperience lead to hilarious mishaps, or will she prove herself as a capable and caring dentist? This light-hearted tale explores the challenges and triumphs that arise when the Barkers trust their smiles to an ambitious intern who wants to learn everything about dental care.

Throughout the game, players will experience the fun and excitement of helping Rosa treat the Barkers' teeth, learning about dental hygiene, and navigating various dental procedures. With each character's unique personality and dental needs, Rosa will need to be both gentle and skilled, making for an engaging and educational experience for children.

How to play free The Barkers Dentist game online

To play The Barkers Dentist, simply click on your favorite character to start their dental check-up. Follow the on-screen instructions to clean teeth, remove cavities, and perform various dental tasks using the tools provided. Complete each step carefully to ensure the Barkers leave with bright, healthy smiles!
