Super Mario Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Mario and his companions in an exciting adventure in Super Mario Run, where your mission is to rescue the princess from the clutches of danger. This fast-paced game allows you to tap the screen to make Mario jump and perform impressive tricks as you dash through colorful worlds. Navigate through various levels, confronting a myriad of enemies that you'll need to stomp on to defeat. Collect coins along the way to enhance your gameplay experience and even purchase collectible Mario friend masks in the shop.

Each level presents its own challenges, featuring spikes and saws that you must avoid at all costs. Your speed is crucial in this platformer; the quicker you run, the higher your chances of achieving the best scores. Dive into the vibrant graphics and catchy music that make Super Mario Run not just a game, but a delightful experience, perfect for players of all ages. Test your skills, conquer levels, and assist Mario in his quest, all while enjoying the exciting gameplay on Kiz10.

How to play free Super Mario Run game online

To play Super Mario Run, simply tap on the screen to make Mario jump and navigate through levels. Timing is key to avoiding obstacles and enemies. Collect coins and power-ups as you progress and try to reach the highest score possible. Have fun maneuvering through the beautifully designed worlds!
