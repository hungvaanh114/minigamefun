This picturesque setting not only sparked a romance but also ignited the excitement of what to wear for such a perfect day. Imagine stepping into Alice's shoes as you prepare for this unforgettable outing. What ensemble would you choose to captivate your boyfriend's heart while enjoying the beautiful seaside? Would you opt for a breezy sundress, comfy sandals, or a chic hat to shield you from the sun? How about the perfect accessories to complement your look?

With endless possibilities, this game invites you to unleash your creativity and help Alice make an unforgettable impression on her beach date.
