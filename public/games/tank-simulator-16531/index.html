Tank Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you re exhausted from the usual driving experiences of cars and motorbikes, then it s time to switch things up with Tank Simulator! Step into the enormous world of armored warfare as you seize control of a formidable tank, navigating through bustling city streets or vast desert landscapes. This game offers two expansive maps, each boasting stunningly realistic graphics that immerse you in the environment. Whether you're rolling past skyscrapers or traversing sandy dunes, the feeling of power and control inside the tank is unparalleled.

The gameplay is designed for both thrill-seekers and strategic thinkers. Engage in intense driving action by selecting any vehicle you desire just by pressing the F key. This feature allows you to experiment with various tanks, enhancing your driving experience and keeping the gameplay fresh. But that's not all your tank isn't just for show! You can also unleash chaos by shooting at targets or obstacles in your path, adding a layer of excitement and tactical planning as you maneuver through the maps.

Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping adventure filled with challenges that require skillful driving and quick reflexes. Whether you prefer a more destructive approach or enjoy simply cruising through the terrains, Tank Simulator is the perfect game to satisfy your craving for something different.

How to play free Tank Simulator game online

To start playing Tank Simulator, simply launch the game and familiarize yourself with the controls. Use the arrow keys or WASD to drive your tank, and press F to switch vehicles. Aim your cannon with the mouse and click to shoot. Navigate the maps and conquer the bustling city or expansive desert!


Instructions

Arrows keys or WASD keys to drive the vehicle Left mouse button to shoot C key to change tank view camera F key to get on off vehicle K key to teleport in tank Tab to toggle pause
