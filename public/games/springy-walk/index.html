Springy Walk

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a whimsical adventure with Springy Walk, a delightful game that challenges players to navigate a colorful world while helping a slinky toy traverse a series of stairs. Your primary mission is to guide the slinky downwards, all while completing various levels filled with obstacles and treasures. Each descent presents a unique challenge, requiring keen reflexes and strategic thinking to reach the finish line unscathed.

As you navigate the slinky down the stairs, be on the lookout for spikes that can impede your progress. These obstacles are designed to test your timing and precision, so avoid them at all costs. Along the way, you will encounter sparkling gems just waiting to be collected. Not only do these gems add to your score, but if you manage to land directly on the center of a gem, you ll earn a bonus for executing a perfect step. Strategic gem-collecting can significantly boost your score and unlock new features in the game.
