Trace Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Trace Run, where your goal is to guide a pen through a chaotic desk filled with a variety of obstacles. The mission is simple yet thrilling: maneuver the pen skillfully to reach the finish line before it runs out of ink! Each level presents unique challenges and exciting twists that will keep you engaged and eager for more. With a total of 30 increasingly difficult levels, players can anticipate a fun and addictive experience that pushes their skills to the limit.

The game boasts stunning 3D graphics that enhance the immersive nature of the gameplay, visually captivating you as you navigate through the cluttered environment. Easy and swift controls make it accessible to players of all ages, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the challenge without frustration. The diversity of objects scattered across the desk adds an extra layer of complexity players must stay alert to avoid hitting anything that could slow them down or cause them to lose ink.

Despite its straightforward premise, Trace Run offers a delightful gaming experience that combines simplicity with engaging gameplay. Players will find themselves eagerly replaying levels to beat their scores and refine their skills. Whether you're looking for a quick five-minute session or a longer gaming spree, this game is sure to entertain.

How to play free Trace Run game online

To start playing Trace Run, use your mouse or touch screen to steer the pen by swiping left, right, up, or down. The objective is to avoid various obstacles while collecting ink reserves and reaching the endpoint of each level. Stay alert, keep your reflexes sharp, and enjoy the challenges!
