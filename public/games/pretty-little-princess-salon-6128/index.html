Pretty Little Princess Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Pretty Little Princess Salon, where fashion meets fantasy! Today is a special day, as it's the night of the prince's prom, and our beloved Chloe finds herself in a bit of a predicament. Her stepmother is making her complete a daunting cleaning task before she can even think about attending the grand event. Chloe is determined to go to the ball, but she needs your help to make that dream a reality!

Your mission begins by assisting Chloe in tidying up the various rooms of the house. You'll have to tackle chores like dusting, vacuuming, and organizing to ensure everything is spotless. The quicker and more efficiently you complete the cleaning tasks, the sooner Chloe can start getting ready for the prom. Once the chores are done, it's time for the exciting part: choosing the perfect prom dress! Dive into a fabulous wardrobe filled with stunning gowns, sparkling accessories, and fabulous hairstyles that will transform Chloe into the belle of the ball.

With your creativity and keen fashion sense, you can help Chloe create a stunning look that will impress everyone at the prom. Show off your styling skills as you mix and match outfits and generate an unforgettable ensemble, allowing Chloe to steal the spotlight at the royal ball. Will you help Chloe make her prom dreams come true? Get ready for a fun-filled adventure in the Pretty Little Princess Salon!

How to play free Pretty Little Princess Salon game online

Start the game by clicking on the cleaning tasks to help Chloe tidy up each room. Use your mouse to interact with objects and complete tasks efficiently. Once cleaning is finished, navigate to the wardrobe section where you can choose from a variety of prom dresses and accessories to style Chloe for the ball!
