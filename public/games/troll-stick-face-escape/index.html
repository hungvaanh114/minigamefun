Troll Stick Face Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Troll Stick brothers are on a thrilling mission to amass wealth and transform their fortunes in the exhilarating game, "Troll Stick Face Escape." In this adventurous quest, players must assist the brothers in their gold-gathering journey while navigating through a perilous environment filled with a myriad of obstacles and challenges. Your primary objective is to help the Troll Stick brothers collect all the gold coins scattered throughout the extreme course.

Time is of the essence, as the brothers must race against the clock to reach the portal that leads to their treasure-laden destiny. Each stage is meticulously crafted to test your skills, ensuring that players remain engaged and challenged. Mastering the controls and timing your movements will be crucial in overcoming the various hurdles that lie in the brothers' path.

As you guide the Troll Stick brothers, keep a keen eye on the surrounding landscape for opportunities to gather coins and avoid traps. Every collected coin brings the brothers one step closer to their goal of becoming immensely rich. Not only do they need to gather the coins, but both brothers must also make it to the portal together to complete their adventure successfully. Will you be able to help them achieve their dreams and escape the clutches of their treacherous course?

How to play free Troll Stick Face Escape game online

To play "Troll Stick Face Escape," use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate the brothers through the obstacles. Collect all gold coins while staying clear of traps and enemies. Ensure both brothers reach the portal before time runs out for a successful escape!
