Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

He Likes the Darkness is an enchanting platform jumping game that brings a delightful challenge to players of all ages. You take on the role of an adorable little black monster, whose fondness for the shadows sets the tone for a captivating adventure. Navigate through beautifully designed levels using the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character left and right, as well as to execute graceful jumps from one platform to another.

In each level, your goal is to collect all the gold stars and coins before moving on to the next stage. Initially, the gameplay offers a straightforward jumping mechanic, allowing you to comfortably familiarize yourself with the controls. However, as you delve deeper into the game, you'll encounter increasingly intricate challenges. The later levels introduce moving platforms that require precise timing and skillful jumps to avoid falling into the abyss.

Spanning seven unique worlds, each consisting of eight engaging levels, He Likes the Darkness guarantees hours of entertainment. With each world presenting its own thematic elements and obstacles, you'll find yourself immersed in a delightful and visually appealing environment. The game's charming graphics combined with its simple yet addictive gameplay mechanics make it a must-play for any platforming enthusiast.

How to play free He Likes The Darkness 2021 game online

To play He Likes the Darkness, simply use the keyboard arrow keys. Press the left or right arrow keys to move your character and the up arrow key to jump. Keep an eye out for gold stars and coins as you navigate through the levels, and aim to collect them all before advancing!
