Blob Giant 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Blob Giant 3D, an exhilarating journey where you morph into a colossal figure exploring vibrant seaside trails. As you traverse these scenic paths, your goal is to absorb smaller jelly figures to grow even larger. Each section of the trail features a unique color scheme, and the color of the jelly men you absorb is crucial for your expansion.

Make sure to stay alert! Absorbing jelly men that differ in color from your own will cause you to shrink in size, so quick reactions are essential to maintain your giant stature. Additionally, the path is lined with various obstacles that you must avoid; hitting these will also lead to a decrease in your size!

As you make your way closer to the finish line, you'll have the chance to bounce for a final boost in bonus points. The larger you grow throughout the game, the quicker you tap and collect, ultimately resulting in a high score. The excitement of exploring the vibrant blob world while managing your growth makes for an engaging gameplay experience. Get ready to run, absorb, and bounce your way to victory!

How to play free Blob Giant 3D game online

To play Blob Giant 3D, use your mouse or touch controls to navigate your giant blob through the seaside path. Absorb jelly men of the same color to grow, while avoiding obstacles and color mismatches that will shrink your size. Tap quickly for a strong bounce at the end for bonus points!
