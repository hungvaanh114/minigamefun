Colour Chain

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Colour Chain, where your puzzle-solving skills will be put to the test in an exciting and colorful format! If you're a fan of knot puzzles, this game is designed just for you. Your task is to strategically sort a series of colorful chains and untangle them from the knots that bind them. The puzzles become more challenging as you progress, allowing you to showcase your problem-solving abilities while enjoying the visual delight of the bright colors. How far can you go in unraveling the complexities of these chains? The game promises hours of engaging challenges that will keep you on your toes!

As you dive into the world of Colour Chain, you'll encounter a variety of levels that increase in complexity, introducing new chains and more intricate knots to untie. With each successful untangling, you'll unlock new areas and gain access to higher levels of difficulty. The gameplay mechanics are simple yet addictive, making it easy for players of all ages to join in the fun. So get ready to immerse yourself in a world full of colors and chains, where every turn leads to new puzzles waiting to be solved!

How to play free Colour Chain game online

To play Colour Chain, simply select a chain segment and drag it to a pole of your choice. Your objective is to move the chains and untangle them so that no knots remain. Keep an eye on the colors and try to organize them in a way that enhances your efficiency. Have fun sorting and solving!
