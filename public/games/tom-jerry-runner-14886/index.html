Tom Amp;amp; Jerry:Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Winter has arrived, and Jerry the mouse is hungrier than ever! In the exciting world of Tom & Jerry: Runner, join Jerry on a thrilling quest to gather all the cheese he can find. But beware! Tom the cat is always on the lookout, making this adventure both exciting and challenging.

Experience three engaging game modes: the classic mode that brings nostalgia, an exhilarating runner mode, and a new color matching challenge that tests your quick thinking. With over 100 levels filled with cheese-filled fun and strategic obstacles, you'll find endless amusement. Navigate through each level while utilizing various objects at your disposal to evade traps, rockets, and other nefarious obstacles that Tom may set in your path.

Explore multiple vivid environments while racing through the living room, garden, attic, and more! Each setting presents distinct challenges and obstacles that will keep you on your toes. Plus, don't forget to collect bonus cards scattered throughout the levels. These cards provide special advantages, helping you stay one step ahead of Tom in this exhilarating chase for cheese!

How to play free Tom & Jerry: Runner game online

To play Tom & Jerry: Runner, simply choose your game mode and use the arrow keys or swipe gestures to control Jerry's movement. Collect cheese while dodging obstacles and traps set by Tom. Make sure to grab bonus cards for power-ups that assist you in your quest. Happy cheese hunting!
