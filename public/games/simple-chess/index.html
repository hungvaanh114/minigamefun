Simple Chess

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Engage in the classic battle of wits with Simple Chess, a 3D Chess experience that brings the timeless board game right to your Android device! Immerse yourself in this strategic contest where every move could lead to glory or defeat as you defend your King s honour.

Simple Chess offers a visually engaging environment that allows two players to face off on an 8x8 checkered board filled with 64 squares. Each player commands an army of 16 pieces, including one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. The objective of the game is clear: checkmate your opponent's king by placing it in a position of unavoidable peril, ensuring it cannot escape capture.

As you navigate through strategic possibilities and anticipate your opponent's moves, you'll need to think several steps ahead. Whether you're a seasoned chessmaster or a newcomer to the game, Simple Chess provides a stimulating atmosphere where you can hone your tactical skills and enjoy friendly competition. Unite with a friend or challenge an AI opponent, and experience the thrill of outsmarting your adversary on this beautifully crafted 3D chessboard.

How to play free Simple Chess game online

To play Simple Chess online, simply select your side, white or black, and make your initial move by tapping on the piece you wish to move, followed by tapping on the target square. Strategize your moves carefully and aim to corner your opponent's king while protecting your own. Good luck!
