Get ready to unleash your creativity with our exciting Easter Nails Design game! As the Easter festival approaches, the Fashion Nail Salon is buzzing with activity, where customers eagerly await stunning, festive nail art. Join Sofia, the talented nail artist, as she prepares to bring joy and beauty through fabulous nail designs that capture the essence of Easter.

Your role as Sofia's assistant is crucial. You will assist her in selecting vibrant colors, playful patterns, and delightful Easter-themed decorations to create eye-catching nail art. The excitement in the salon is palpable, creating a fun atmosphere as you work together to make each client's nail dreams come true. So grab your tools, and get ready for a vibrant, creative adventure that will make this Easter memorable!

To play the game, simply choose the nail style and decorations your clients desire. Use the tools provided to paint, draw, and embellish their nails with Easter-themed designs.
