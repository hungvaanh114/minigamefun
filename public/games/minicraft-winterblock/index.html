Minicraft Winterblock

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Winter has arrived, and everything is covered in snow. In the snow-covered forest, you must be very careful to survive. You need to find two chests and collect all the snowballs. But be cautious, there are monsters everywhere waiting to eat you. You must defeat the monsters and reach the hidden portal at the end of the level. You must leave the house safely and pass through the portal.

Instructions

Played with WASD Arrow Keys Mobile touch control is available Jump on monsters and crush them to kill them
