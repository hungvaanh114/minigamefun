Backrooms Survival Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the eerie and relentless environment of Backrooms Survival Shooter, where you find yourself trapped in a maze of monotonous rooms and endless corridors. This unique game thrusts you into a survival scenario against waves of blocky, menacing zombies. With only your wits and an arsenal of diverse weapons at your disposal, you must fend off these terrifying foes to stay alive.

The game's mechanics are simple yet exhilarating. You'll need to strategize as you navigate the dimly lit backrooms, keeping a keen eye for incoming enemies. The blocky zombies come in different shapes and sizes, each with unique movements and attacks, ramping up the challenge as you progress. As you eliminate these grotesque creatures, you earn points that can be spent on upgrading existing weapons or unlocking more powerful ones. However, beware! After every round, a formidable zombie boss will emerge, escalating the stakes and testing your combat skills to the limit.

Time your shots, manage your ammo wisely, and utilize the environment to cover yourself from the advancing horde. The survival of your character depends on your quick reflexes and precise shooting. Get ready to face apocalyptic chaos in the backrooms, where every moment counts and each decision can mean life or death.

How to play free Backrooms Survival Shooter game online

To begin your survival journey in Backrooms Survival Shooter, simply enter the game and familiarize yourself with the controls. Use the WASD keys to move, the mouse to aim and shoot. Gather weapons and ammunition as you fight through rounds of zombies, and adapt your strategy for each new boss encounter!
