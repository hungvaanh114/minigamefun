Extreme Skate 5000

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the adrenaline-pumping rush of Extreme Skate 5000, where time is of the essence for the Ninja Turtles! After a sudden ambush that leaves them dazed, Mike, Mondo, and Casey awaken in the murky depths of New York's infamous sewers. With their energy depleted and their armor in tatters, these brave heroes must navigate through the labyrinthine tunnels to find their way back home.

As they race against the clock, players will assist Mike and his comrades in overcoming a series of dastardly traps and obstacles that lurk in the darkness. The stakes are high; the villains have cunningly hijacked the tunnels, setting deadly snares to thwart the turtles' escape. It's a test of skill and speed, and without your guidance, they may not make it back to their sanctuary.

Join the adventure and help our beloved heroes as they dash through echoing tunnels, overcoming challenges while keeping an eye out for the villains who want nothing more than to see their plans fail. Each move counts in this thrilling race against time, where every second could mean the difference between victory and defeat!

How to play free Extreme Skate 5000 game online

To play Extreme Skate 5000, use your arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer the characters through the sewers. Jump over traps, slide under obstacles, and collect power-ups to boost your energy. Work quickly and strategically to help Mike and the team reach the Lair before time runs out!
