Air War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Air War, where the expansive skies transform into a dynamic battlefield! In this exhilarating scrolling shooter game, you take on the role of a skilled fighter pilot, maneuvering your aircraft to engage in intense aerial dogfights. Your primary objective is to take down as many enemy planes as possible while navigating through beautifully designed levels filled with explosive action and challenging opponents.

As you soar through the sky, collecting coins becomes crucial to your success. Each coin you gather contributes towards your overall score, allowing you to climb to the top of the leaderboard and demonstrate your dominance in the air. The game features diverse terrains and enemy formations, testing your quick reflexes and strategic skills as you dodge incoming bullets and retaliate against foes.

The adrenaline rush from each encounter, combined with the thrill of aerial combat, will keep you engaged and eager to improve your piloting skills. Will you rise to the challenge and emerge victorious in the battle for air supremacy? Join the fight in Air War and prove you have what it takes to be the ultimate pilot!

How to play free Air War game online

To play Air War, use your keyboard or mouse to control your fighter plane. Move around to avoid enemy fire while firing back at opposing aircraft. Collect coins as you progress through levels to maximize your score. Stay alert and be ready for intense aerial battles!
