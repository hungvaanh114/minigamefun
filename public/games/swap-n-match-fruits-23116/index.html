Swap N Match Fruits

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to indulge in a fruity adventure with Swap N Match Fruits, an exciting puzzle game where your objective is to swap adjacent fruit blocks to create a row of three or more similar fruits. With vibrant colors and delightful graphics, every level offers a feast for the eyes as you work your way through various challenges.

The gameplay is simple to grasp yet engaging enough to keep you coming back for more. Each level presents a grid filled with different types of fruit blocks. To achieve victory, your mission is to manipulate the blocks by swapping them in such a way that you form a horizontal or vertical line of three or more matching fruits. When you successfully align the blocks, they will disappear, allowing new ones to cascade down from above.

What sets Swap N Match Fruits apart is the requirement to turn all the blocks on the board to GREEN. You'll need to think strategically about your moves, as every swap counts towards your ultimate goal. As the levels progress, the game introduces delightful power-ups and obstacles that will challenge your skills and keep the gameplay fresh and exciting. Are you ready to turn the board green?

How to play free Swap N Match Fruits game online

To begin playing Swap N Match Fruits, simply click to swap adjacent fruit blocks on the board. Create lines of three or more matching fruits to clear them and change the color of the blocks to GREEN. Plan your moves carefully to complete each level successfully!
