Fruit Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a juicy adventure in Fruit Master, where your primary mission is to hurl an array of weapons at colorful fruits that await your attention. As you engage in this thrilling game, your objective is to juice each piece of fruit to progress through the challenging levels. With a total of 100 unique levels to conquer, every stage presents an opportunity to showcase your precision and skill while trying to avoid any missed targets.

As you successfully juice the fruits, you'll collect coins that can be used to unlock exciting new knives. These upgrades not only enhance your throwing experience but also add a layer of strategy to your gameplay. Each knife possesses unique attributes, allowing players to experiment and choose the best weapon for their style. The excitement intensifies as you aim to achieve perfect scores while juggling the juicy chaos surrounding you.

Fruit Master offers vibrant graphics and intuitive controls, making it suitable for players of all ages. Every level challenges your reflexes and aiming ability, ensuring a dynamic gaming experience that keeps you coming back for more. Embrace the fun, master your throws, and uncover the variety of fruits hidden within each stage as you strive to be the ultimate juicing champion!

How to play free Fruit Master game online

To play the Fruit Master game, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim and throw your weapons at the fruits. Time your throws accurately to maximize the number of fruits juiced and avoid any misses. Collect coins to unlock new knives and enhance your gameplay experience!
