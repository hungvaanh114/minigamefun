Step into an exhilarating reimagining of the Marvel Universe with Super Heroes Combat & Flying. Assemble your champions and immerse yourself in an epic real-time Action-RPG battle! Customize your Marvel champions with unique weapons and gear tailored to your playstyle. Team up with friends for high-stakes battles against enemies, forming a strategy to dominate the battlefield.

Choose your hero wisely: unleash swift, devastating damage at close range with Black Panther, or opt for high-powered ranged assaults with Web Warrior and Iron Legionnaire. Support your allies as Supreme Storm and Sorcerer, delivering massive damage to overwhelmed foes. The story takes you through Battleworld, once ruled by the powerful Maestro, a twisted version of Hulk from an alternate future. Following the mysterious assassination of Battleworld's leader, chaos reigns, and the barons strive for control, engaging in secret wars. As a rookie, it s now your chance to evolve into a legendary hero of Battleworld!

  • Dominate with Your Alliance: Join forces to conquer challenges and earn substantial rewards while strategizing with your teammates.

Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes of Battleworld, exploring its rich history filled with fierce battles and mysteries. Experience breathtaking graphics and animation inspired by your favorite Marvel characters!

How to play free Super Heroes Combat & Flying game online

To play the game, simply choose your character, customize their gear, and enter the battlefield. Use the joystick controls to move and unleash attacks by tapping the action buttons. Team up with friends to strategize and dominate your foes in various game modes!
