Pen Run Online is an engaging 3D arcade game designed to provide a relaxing yet exciting experience for players. Dive into a vibrant world where you control a colorful pencil as it glides effortlessly across a table. The game's stunning visuals create an immersive environment, making it hard to look away. Your main objective is to collect pencils of various colors scattered throughout the tabletop, enhancing your score and unlocking new levels.

To navigate your pencil, simply slide the mouse or swipe your finger across the screen, guiding it along the surface. However, beware of the obstacles that lie in your path! Books and other stationary items can impede your progress, so you'll need to skillfully maneuver your pencil to avoid them. The thrill of gathering more colored pencils while dodging hazards adds an exciting twist to each round. Do you have the reflexes and precision necessary to conquer the challenges ahead?
