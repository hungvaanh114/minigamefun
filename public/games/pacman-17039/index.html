How to play free PACMAN game online

To play our free PACMAN game, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate Pacman through the maze. Your main objective is to collect all the dots while avoiding the ghosts that chase you. If you eat a power pellet, you can turn the tables and chase the ghosts for bonus points!
