Instructions:

Experience the exhilarating challenge of Flappy Bird 2D, where you take control of the charming bird named "Faby". The game involves skillfully navigating Faby through an endless series of obstacles designed like Mario-style pipes. As Faby continually flies to the right, the player's primary goal is to guide him through these narrow gaps without coming into contact with the pipes. A single tap on the screen will make Faby flap his wings and ascend momentarily, while failure to tap will cause him to plummet due to the force of gravity.

Scoring in Flappy Bird 2D is straightforward: every successful pass through a set of pipes earns you a point. Be wary, though hitting any pipes results in an immediate game over. The excitement ramps up as players aim for higher scores, and the game rewards persistence with a medal system. Players who score below ten don't receive any medals, while those who hit the range of ten to twenty are honored with a bronze medal. Achieving 20 points grants a silver medal, while scoring over thirty points earns a coveted gold medal. If you reach a remarkable score of forty or above, you will be awarded a platinum medal, showcasing your prowess and dedication.

How to play free flappy bird 2D game online

To play the game, simply start by tapping the screen to make Faby flap and rise. Guide him through the gaps between the pipes by tapping rhythmically. Keep an eye on how high or low he flies, as gravity will pull him down when you don t tap. Aim to improve your score with each attempt!
