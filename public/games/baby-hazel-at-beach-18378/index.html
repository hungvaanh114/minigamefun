Get ready for some sun-kissed adventures with Baby Hazel at the beach! This delightful vacation is a special treat from her loving mom and dad, and Hazel couldn t be more thrilled. Join Baby Hazel in creating wonderful memories at the beach while ensuring all her needs are met. This game promises not just fun, but also the opportunity to nurture and take care of your favorite character, making for a delightful beach escapade!
