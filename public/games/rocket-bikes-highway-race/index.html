Rocket Bikes Highway Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to rev your engines and tear down the asphalt in the thrilling world of Rocket Bikes Highway Race. This exhilarating racing experience brings together a diverse range of motorbikes, each tailored for unique racing styles including speed bikes, cross motos, and supersport machines. Whether you re a fan of speed or tackling tough terrains, there s a bike just for you!

The game features two dynamic modes: One Way Traffic and Two Way Traffic. In One Way Traffic, you ll have to maneuver through a continuous stream of vehicles, while Two Way Traffic challenges you to zip past traffic coming at you from both directions. Each mode provides its own unique level of intensity and excitement, pushing your reflexes to the limit.

Environment enthusiasts will love the vibrant graphics found in the game s three distinct maps: the lush greenery of the Forest Map, the arid vastness of the Desert Map, and the frosty terrain of the Winter Map. Choose your battleground wisely and let the race begin! With multiple maps and modes, every race offers a new adventure and a chance to show off your skills as a top-tier traffic rider.

How to play free Rocket Bikes Highway Race game online

To play Rocket Bikes Highway Race, select your desired mode and map. Use the arrow keys or virtual controls to navigate your bike, avoiding traffic and obstacles while aiming for the finish line. Accumulate points by narrowly avoiding crashes and completing the race ahead of your competitors!
