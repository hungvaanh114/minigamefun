Rexo 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rexo 2 is an exciting sequel to the beloved original game, Rexo. This enchanting 2D platformer invites players back into a vibrant world where agility and strategy are key. In this installment, you assume the role of a charming cube tasked with collecting coins scattered throughout the levels. As you navigate through beautifully designed environments, you must be vigilant to avoid hazardous spikes and cunning enemies that threaten your progress.

Throughout your journey in Rexo 2, you will encounter eight progressively challenging levels. Each one tests not only your timing and precision but also your problem-solving skills as you strategize the best approach to reach the coveted red flag at the end of each stage. The increasing difficulty ensures that players remain engaged and motivated to improve their skills as they advance through the game.

The visuals are captivating, with a color palette that enhances the excitement of the gameplay. The controls are straightforward, making Rexo 2 accessible to players of all ages, while also rewarding those who master the intricacies of each level. As you collect coins, you may also discover various bonuses that will aid you in your quest, creating an engaging balance of challenge and delight. Rexo 2 is perfect for both newcomers and fans of the original, promising hours of fun as you strive to complete every level and collect all the coins.
