Rolling Cheese

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rolling Cheese is an engaging puzzle game that follows the whimsical journey of a starving little mouse on a quest for cheese. Your mission is to help this adorable rodent by guiding the cheese to him through a series of cleverly designed levels. Each level presents unique challenges that require strategic thinking and careful planning. With 24 intriguing levels to conquer, players will find themselves immersed in delightful gameplay that combines fun and skill.

As you navigate through the puzzles, you will encounter various breakable objects that may obstruct the path to the cheese. Your task is to click on these items to remove them, clearing the way for the cheese to roll towards the hungry mouse. Additionally, you'll have the chance to collect strawberries scattered throughout the levels. Gathering up to three strawberries in each puzzle not only adds an extra layer of challenge but also boosts your score significantly. Aim high and try to achieve the coveted three-star rating on every level!

With its charming graphics and engaging mechanics, Rolling Cheese invites players of all ages to experience the joy of puzzle-solving while enjoying the delightful antics of our little cheese-loving friend. Whether you're a casual gamer or a seasoned puzzle enthusiast, this game offers hours of entertainment as you strategize and experiment with different solutions to reach your goal.

How to play free Rolling Cheese game online

To play Rolling Cheese, simply click on breakable objects to clear the path for the cheese. Guide it to the mouse by making clever moves and avoiding obstacles. Collect strawberries for extra points and try to achieve a three-star rating in all 24 levels available. Enjoy the challenge!
