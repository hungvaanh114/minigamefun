Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Choose My Summer Style, where the thrill of summer fashion awaits! With the sunny season fast approaching, our stylish girls are eager for sunshine, warmth, and memorable moments to share with friends during their summer vacations. It's the perfect opportunity for them to rejuvenate their wardrobes and embrace the vibrant trends of the season.

In this fabulous fashion game, you'll dive into a kaleidoscope of colors, patterns, and styles. From breezy dresses to chic beachwear, the possibilities are endless! The girls can't contain their excitement as they look forward to long days spent soaking up the sun, enjoying fun-filled outings, and, of course, flaunting their stunning summer looks. Your role is vital, as you will be the one to curate their summer styles. Mix and match different clothing items, accessories, and hairstyles to create the perfect vacation look that reflects their personalities and individual flair.

Join in on the fun by exploring the wardrobes piled high with trendy summer pieces. As you help these girls choose their outfits, you'll unlock your inner fashion designer and discover the latest summer trends as well. Get ready for a wardrobe overhaul that leads to an unforgettable summer filled with cherished memories!

How to play free Choose My Summer Style game online

To play the Choose My Summer Style game, simply select a girl and start by browsing her closet. Click on various clothing items, accessories, and hairstyles to create fashionable summer looks. Experiment with colors and styles, then dress her up for fun summer outings. Enjoy the creative process and unleash your fashion imagination!
