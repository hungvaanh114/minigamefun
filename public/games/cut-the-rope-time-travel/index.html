Cut the Rope Time Travel.

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey through the ages in Cut the Rope: Time Travel, where the adorable creature Om Nom takes center stage! This fresh installment in the beloved Cut the Rope series invites you to aid Om Nom in feeding his ancestors delightful lollipops. Get ready to solve engaging puzzles as you manipulate ropes, navigate tricky obstacles, and collect stars across different historical settings.

As you progress through various levels, you will encounter unique challenges that require clever tactics and strategic thinking. The game beautifully blends humor and creativity, allowing players to explore diverse time periods filled with vibrant graphics and captivating soundtracks. Whether you're in ancient Rome, the medieval era, or the Renaissance, each level presents its distinct array of candies, tools, and mechanics, ensuring that the gameplay remains fresh and exciting.

Time Travel is more than just about feeding Om Nom; it's about learning and adapting to the nuances of each historical era's challenges. Utilize your problem-solving skills to devise the best methods to cut ropes and guide candies to Om Nom, all while keeping those pesky obstacles at bay. The joy of seeing your little green friend satisfied after overcoming intricate puzzles is unparalleled, making every level a rewarding experience. Experience the eclectic charm of history through the lens of this delightful puzzle game!

How to play free Cut the Rope Time Travel game online

To play Cut the Rope: Time Travel online, simply click on the play button on the game page. Use your mouse or finger to cut the ropes at the right moment to drop the lollipops into Om Nom's eager mouth. Ensure you collect all the stars before completing each level for bonus points!
