How to play free FZ Water Crisis game online

To play the FZ Water Crisis game, simply start by tapping on the icons to manipulate the water paths. Use your finger or mouse to adjust the barriers and channels leading to the trees. Strategize your moves carefully to ensure that all trees receive water without wasting any resources!
