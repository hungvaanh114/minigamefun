Bridge Race Wedding Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bridge Race Wedding Master is a lively, romance-filled parkour game where you run to build the wedding bridge! In this unique adventure, you can play as a bride or groom. Collect flowers,cakes, red envelopes, etc., and build a platform leading to the wedding hall. Keep running and collect suits, skirts, keys, rings, etc.
