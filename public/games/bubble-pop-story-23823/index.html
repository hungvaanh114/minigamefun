How to play free Bubble Pop Story game online

To play Bubble Pop Story, aim your shooter at the cluster of bubbles and click to fire a bubble of the same color. Try to match three or more bubbles to pop them! Continue to clear levels by strategically planning your shots and utilizing special bubbles for an added advantage.
