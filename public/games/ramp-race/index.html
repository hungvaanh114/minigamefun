Ramp Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In this exciting race, you will have a unique opportunity to become a master of high-speed tracks! Buy and customize cars to suit your style: choose spoilers, change the color, improve each element. Every day, get gifts and new bonuses that will help you achieve success faster. Test your strength on many different levels, full of unexpected obstacles that will make each race unpredictable and exciting. Compete with other players, climb the leaderboard and prove that you are the best racer! Ramp jumps, sharp turns and competitive spirit await you on every level. Are you ready to become number one? Your rivals are already at the start

Instructions

Controls on the computer WASD - control the car forward backward left right R - restart the level H - exit to the main menu Space - stop the car For mobile devices the game interface is used
