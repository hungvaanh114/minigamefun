Makeup Kit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Makeup Kit, an engaging casual parkour game that combines the thrill of movement with the artistry of makeup. In this colorful and vibrant world, you will discover the empowering philosophy that every girl has the potential to shine; there are no ugly girls, only girls who need a little encouragement and creativity to express their beauty. Your journey through the game is all about collecting an array of cosmetics, including makeup brushes, lipsticks, and eyeshadow palettes, and using them to rejuvenate and beautify the characters you encounter.

As you traverse various levels, your mission is to clean and organize the disheveled girls, transforming them from sloppy and unkempt to stunning and ready to take on the world. With each completed transformation, you'll accumulate points and progress toward your goal of becoming the ultimate makeup queen. The more you play, the more creative combinations of makeup you can discover, allowing for endless fun and experimentation.

Makeup Kit is not just about beauty; it's also about the joy of creating and executing flawless looks. Each level presents unique challenges and obstacles that require quick thinking and sharp reflexes, ensuring that the gameplay is as dynamic as it is delightful. So, grab your tools and let your imagination run wild as you embark on this colorful adventure to prove that beauty is just a brush stroke away!

How to play free Makeup Kit game online

To play the game, navigate through various parkour levels using your arrow keys or touchscreen controls. Collect cosmetics scattered throughout the levels while avoiding obstacles. Once you reach a girl in need of a makeover, use the collected items to clean, organize, and apply makeup, transforming her appearance. Keep an eye out for bonus items to enhance your score!
