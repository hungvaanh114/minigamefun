Gunwars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an action-packed showdown in Gunwars, where the thrill of the battle awaits! With your guns drawn and adrenaline pumping, it s time to team up with a friend for an exhilarating duel. Choose your side red or blue and prepare to fire away as you compete to become the ultimate champion.

In this fast-paced arena, your primary objective is to outgun your opponent. Show off your shooting skills as you take aim and unleash a barrage of fire. The player who lands the most hits emerges victorious, but the competition doesn t stop there! Watch the skies for drop balloons floating above, which contain powerful new weapons to diversify your arsenal. Each weapon boasts unique features that can turn the tide of battle, so be strategic in your choices as you fight to the finish.

With easy-to-learn controls and exciting gameplay, Gunwars promises endless fun. Will you rise above your friend and take the crown, or will you be outmatched in this fierce contest of skill? Ready your weapon, focus your aim, and let the game begin!

How to play free Gunwars game online

To play Gunwars, move your player using the arrow keys or WASD, and aim with your mouse. Click to shoot at your opponent while avoiding their fire. Keep an eye on the drop balloons; shoot them to change and upgrade your weapon. The player with the highest score wins!


Instructions

The red player moves using the WASD keys The blue player moves using the arrow keys The one who gets hit 20 times loses
