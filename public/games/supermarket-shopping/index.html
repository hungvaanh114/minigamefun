Supermarket: Shopping

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Supermarket: Shopping, where you can indulge in the ultimate shopping experience! This delightful game lets you explore an extensive supermarket filled with a plethora of items, including fresh groceries, stylish clothes, fun toys, and much more. Navigate through the colorful aisles, discovering treasures as you go, all while encountering quirky customers whose clumsiness can lead to amusing challenges!

As you delve into the gameplay, your objectives expand beyond mere shopping. You get to clean up spills and messes left by distracted shoppers, ensuring that the store remains tidy and appealing. Feel the excitement as you collect various food items, and hone your culinary skills by slicing fruits to prepare scrumptious dishes. Additionally, unleash your creativity in the bakery section, where you can embark on a delightful journey of baking cakes and pastries.

Are you ready for a fun-filled shopping spree? Grab a trolley, arm yourself with a shopping list, and step into the aisles stocked with a world of possibilities! The lively atmosphere and engaging tasks will keep you entertained as you tackle each shopping mission. Whether you're filling your cart with everyday essentials or rare items, Supermarket: Shopping promises an engaging adventure for players of all ages.

How to play free Supermarket: Shopping game online

To start playing Supermarket: Shopping, simply click on the Play button. Once in-game, you can navigate through the supermarket using your mouse or arrow keys. Follow your shopping list to pick up items, complete fun mini-games like slicing fruits and baking cakes, and manage the store's cleanliness by clearing up messes left by customers.
