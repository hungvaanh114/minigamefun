Invasion Crush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Invasion Crush, a captivating twist on the beloved classic breakout genre! Engage yourself in a vibrant world filled with uniquely designed bricks and formidable challenges across various levels. As you progress through the game, you'll encounter different types of bricks that require distinct strategies for elimination. For instance, the '2x Brick' must be struck twice before it disappears, adding an extra layer of complexity to your mission.

Additionally, beware of the 'Shoot Brick,' which can only be destroyed by shooting it directly; if the ball collides with this brick, it will perish, turning the game into a tactical showdown. On a more positive note, smashing the 'Extra Ball Brick' rewards you with an additional ball, giving you more chances to clear the screen of bricks. Your ultimate goal? Dismantle all the bricks within each level's five-minute limit!

To maximize your success, you'll need to adeptly maneuver the paddle using either your mouse or fingertip, ensuring that you keep the ball in play while aiming for those tricky bricks. With colorful graphics and exhilarating gameplay, Invasion Crush promises to deliver hours of entertainment and challenge.

How to play free Invasion Crush game online

When you start playing Invasion Crush, use your mouse or finger to control the paddle's movement. Aim to bounce the ball against the bricks while avoiding the 'Shoot Bricks.' Click to shoot at the appropriate targets, and strive to clear all bricks before the time runs out. Good luck!
