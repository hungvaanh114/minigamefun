Welcome to the whimsical world of Sheep Sheep Duck, a fresh and engaging casual multiplayer party game that caters to players of all ages. Each scenario is accompanied by a variety of captivating scenes that elevate the gaming experience.

Your journey in Sheep Sheep Duck is all about pushing your limits. Embrace the thrill of competition and strive to unlock new game maps, providing further excitement and opportunities for exploration. Get ready to defeat foes and unlock doors to exhilarating new adventures!

To play Sheep Sheep Duck online, simply tap on the screen to control your character's movements.
