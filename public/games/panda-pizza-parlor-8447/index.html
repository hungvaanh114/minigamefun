Panda Pizza Parlor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Panda Pizza Parlor! In this charming restaurant, the focus is all on pizzas, offering an array of delicious toppings for your culinary creations. Join an adorable panda as he embarks on a mission to run his very own pizza business, crafting scrumptious pizzas to delight his hungry customers!

In Panda Pizza Parlor, you ll engage in a flurry of activity as you prepare pizza dough, whip up savory sauces, and cook pizzas to perfection. Your task is to serve as many patrons as possible throughout the day while keeping up with the increasing demands of your growing pizza menu. As you advance through the game, new exciting offers become available, allowing you to expand your culinary repertoire and tantalize your customers taste buds.

With 35 engaging missions to complete, your goal is to ensure that each customer leaves satisfied. Whether it s mixing fresh ingredients or timing the bake just right, every task contributes to making your pizzeria the most renowned establishment in the world. Released in July 2022, Panda Pizza Parlor has quickly garnered a loyal player base, achieving an impressive average rating of 4.00 from 17 votes. You can easily immerse yourself in this fun pizza management game for free on various platforms including web browsers for PC, Android, and iOS.

How to play free Panda Pizza Parlor game online

To play Panda Pizza Parlor, start by choosing an order from the customers. Gather the required ingredients and prepare the pizza by following the on-screen prompts. Bake it to the perfect level, and lastly, serve the pizza hot before your customers lose patience. Make sure to complete each task efficiently to keep the customers happy!
