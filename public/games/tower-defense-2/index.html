Tower Defense 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Tower Defense, you will direct a powerful army of defense towers to face enemy attacks. Arrange your defense towers carefully and develop the strategy to repel the enemy. Each tower has different attack powers and abilities. Unlock the right type and placement. Update your soldiers to help you battle. Use your crossbow to take them out.
