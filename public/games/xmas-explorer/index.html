Xmas Explorer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a festive adventure with Xmas Explorer, a captivating game that invites you to dive into a world filled with delightful Christmas items. To start your journey, simply click on any item on the board. Your objective is to match identical adjacent Xmas items by moving your mouse or finger tip in horizontal, vertical, or diagonal directions. You'll need to select at least three items for a successful match.

Once you've highlighted the items, release the mouse button to merge them into a new, unique Christmas item. The fun doesn't stop there keep matching and merging to discover even more exciting Xmas items as you explore. Your ultimate goal is to find the supreme Xmas item, a rare treasure that will bring you great joy!

As you progress through the game, be mindful of your board. You can eliminate any item once you find a new one, helping you clear space and optimize your moves. Below the board, you will find essential information about the items you've eliminated, keeping your exploration organized and strategic. With each merge, the excitement builds, and the journey becomes more intriguing as you unveil a plethora of holiday treasures!

How to play free Xmas Explorer game online

To play the Xmas Explorer game, simply click on any item to begin. Then, drag your mouse or finger to select at least three adjacent identical items. Release the button to merge them into a new item. Keep matching items to explore and discover new ones while striving to find the supreme Xmas item!
