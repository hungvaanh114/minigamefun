Cannon Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Cannon Shooter, a thrilling platform game that turns the conventional gameplay on its head. Instead of merely navigating the platforms, you will find the opportunity to unleash your frustrations by using a powerful cannon that fires vibrant balls, providing an exciting twist to your gaming experience. As you play, you will notice that your objective is to target and obliterate the platforms standing in your way.

The gameplay is structured around a vertical ascent, where platforms continuously rise. Your mission is to destroy them before they reach your cannon, and your chances of success depend on your strategic planning and quick reflexes. Every shot counts, as you are afforded a limited number of cannonballs. Be wary of the dangers lurking on the platforms, as any ball hitting spikes will cost you a precious life, and you only have three to spare, represented by red lines in the lower right corner of the screen.

Cannon Shooter features a variety of challenging levels, each designed to test your coordination and skill, ensuring that players remain engaged and eager to progress. With each stage introducing new obstacles and fast-paced elements, players must think quickly and act decisively to clear the levels. Prepare yourself for an action-packed adventure that will keep you on your toes!

How to play free Cannon Shooter game online

To play Cannon Shooter, simply use your mouse or finger to aim the cannon. Tap or click to shoot the colorful balls at the platforms below. Make sure to time your shots carefully to avoid the spikes, and aim for the spots where you can break the platforms effectively. Enjoy!
