My Sweet Strawberry Outfits

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of My Sweet Strawberry Outfits! In this charming fashion game, you ll join the fabulous Rainbow High girls: Skyler, Sunny, Ruby, and Violet, as they embark on a creative adventure over the weekend. With a passion for style and a love for strawberries, these fashionistas have decided to design their very own strawberry-themed outfits, and they need your help!

Your mission is to assist Skyler, Sunny, Ruby, and Violet in mixing and matching a variety of trendy clothing items to create the perfect cute outfit inspired by their favorite fruit. Browse through an extensive wardrobe filled with playful patterns, vibrant colors, and stylish accessories. Don t forget to add a pop of fun with some strawberry motifs!

Once the outfits are ready, it s time for you to unleash your creativity even further with stunning makeup options. From fruity lip colors to sparkly eyeshadows, you can complete each girl's look, ensuring they are dazzling and ready for their weekend adventure. The game offers a wonderful opportunity to explore your fashion sense while bringing the characters' strawberry dreams to life!

How to play free My Sweet Strawberry Outfits game online

To start playing the game, simply click on the start button and select one of the Rainbow High girls. Use your mouse to navigate through the clothing and makeup options available in the game. Mix and match different pieces, and once you re satisfied with the outfit, you can apply the makeup to finalize the look. Have fun being creative!
