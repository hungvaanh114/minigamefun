The game "Regal Academy School Mysteries" immerses players in an enchanting world inspired by the Italian animated series co-created by Iginio Straffi and Joanne Lee. It follows the adventurous journey of Rose, an ordinary teenage girl from Earth, who stumbles upon a mysterious key. This key unlocks the entrance to FairyTale Land, a vibrant realm where beloved fairy tales spring to life.

Upon arriving in FairyTale Land, Rose finds herself in the prestigious Regal Academy, an enchanting school devoted to nurturing young magicians and princesses. To her surprise, Rose learns that she is the granddaughter of the legendary headmistress, Cinderella. This revelation fuels her excitement and curiosity, prompting her to enroll at the academy. Throughout her studies, Rose embarks on a series of magical adventures, forming lasting friendships with her fellow students and uncovering the hidden secrets of the academy.

As players explore Regal Academy, they will engage in various quests and challenges that test their magic skills and problem-solving abilities. The game beautifully weaves together elements of friendship, adventure, and the importance of believing in oneself, making it an ideal experience for fans of fairy tales and magical realms. Whether you are learning spellbinding new abilities or unraveling the mysteries of the academy, every moment promises excitement and discovery.

Once inside, you ll find an interactive menu guiding you through various episodes and challenges.
