How to play free Solitaire game online

To play the free Solitaire game online, simply start by clicking on the deck to shuffle the cards. Your goal is to move all cards to the foundation piles, following the suit and in ascending order. You can place cards from the tableau onto the foundation or other tableau piles, ensuring they are in descending order and alternating colors. Use the stock pile for additional cards as needed!
