Rolling Hand Signal

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the engaging game of Rolling Hand Signal, players are tasked with aiming and rolling a ball that features specific hand signals towards the appropriate basket. The challenge lies in accurately identifying the right basket for each signal while strategically removing unused blocks that may obstruct your path. Utilize your precision and timing to ensure your ball reaches its destination, creating a fun yet demanding experience that enhances your focus and hand-eye coordination.

As you navigate through the levels, you may encounter obstacles and distractions that could impede your progress. Keep your wits about you and use the click or tap feature to eliminate any unused blocks that are blocking the way. This clever mechanic allows for a smoother gameplay experience, letting you concentrate on establishing the perfect shot. Should you find yourself in a tricky predicament, don t hesitate to utilize the Restart button, which lets you reset the game and strategize your next move.

How to play free Rolling Hand Signal game online

To play the Rolling Hand Signal game, use your mouse or finger to aim at the correct basket for each hand signal. Tap to roll the ball towards the basket while clicking on any extraneous blocks to remove them. If you're unable to proceed, simply hit the Restart button for a fresh start!
