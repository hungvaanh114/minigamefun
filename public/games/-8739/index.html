Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Captain Barrel Cat, an engaging template project designed specifically for Construct 3 that brings your game development ideas to life! This vibrant game offers a delightful mix of adventure and excitement as players take on the role of Captain Barrel Cat. With this template, you will have access to a suite of features that elevate gameplay and enhance the user experience.

One standout feature of Captain Barrel Cat is its high score-saving capability, which allows players to track their achievements and display them on the start screen. This adds an extra layer of competition and motivation, encouraging players to improve their skills and aim for higher scores. Furthermore, players can seamlessly switch between different levels without losing their current score, making for a fluid gaming experience.

As you navigate through various levels, you'll have the opportunity to collect points that contribute to your overall score, ensuring that every action counts. Additionally, players can activate a powerful invincibility mode, allowing them to go all out without the fear of losing lives, making it perfect for intense situations!

How to play free Captain Barrel Cat game online

To start playing Captain Barrel Cat, simply use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the screen. Collect points by interacting with items and enemies. Keep an eye out for power-ups to become invincible! Jump to new levels by crossing designated areas, and remember to keep track of your score for a shot at the leaderboard!
