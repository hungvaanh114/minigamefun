Break the Candies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a sweet adventure in Break The Candies, a captivating game that will put your skills to the test! Your mission is simple yet exciting: navigate your candy through a labyrinth of obstacles and challenges. Using the Arrow keys, you will maneuver your candy piece with precision, ensuring that you avoid all hazards along the way. But it s not just about avoiding obstacles; your goal is to reach the target and break all the candies in each level.

With a total of 40 challenging levels, Break The Candies offers hours of engaging gameplay. Each level introduces new obstacles and requires strategic thinking to progress. Can you outsmart the challenges and conquer all 40 levels? The vibrant graphics and delightful sound effects will keep you entertained as you snack your way through this sugary journey.

Feel the thrill of breaking candies and achieving high scores as you become a master candy breaker! The game is perfect for players of all ages, making it a fantastic way to relax and enjoy a little gaming fun.

How to play free Break The Candies game online

To play Break The Candies, simply use the Arrow keys on your keyboard to guide your candy. Avoid any obstacles in your path and make your way to the target to break the candies. Be strategic and quick as you advance through the exciting levels!
