Mien and Bugr Skate

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the delightful adventure of Mien and Bugr Skate, where two adorable friends embark on an exciting journey on their skateboards! As you guide them through vibrant streets, your mission is to locate their lost pink and brown pets that have ventured into the busy city. Time is of the essence, as the duo races to return home safely.

While navigating through the colorful environment, make sure to collect glittering gold coins scattered throughout the levels. These coins not only enhance your score but can also unlock special abilities, making your skateboarding journey even more thrilling. However, don't let your guard down! The streets are filled with lurking monsters eager to impede your progress. Stay sharp and avoid their grasp as you speed towards the finish line.

Mien and Bugr Skate is a chase filled with fun and challenges that will put your reflexes to the test. Get ready to jump, dodge, and weave your way through various obstacles while keeping an eye out for your furry friends. Every moment counts as you skate along, combining excitement with a heartwarming quest of friendship and loyalty. Can you lead Mien and Bugr to reunite with their beloved pets and conquer the thrilling streets together?

How to play free Mien and Bugr Skate game online

To play the Mien and Bugr Skate game, simply use the arrow keys or swipe your finger on mobile to control your skateboarders. Navigate through the streets, collecting coins while avoiding monsters. Press the jump button to leap over obstacles and keep an eye on your pets to guide them back home safely. Happy skating!
