Welcome to the delightful world of Hello Kitty Nail Salon - Fashion Star, where creativity meets cuteness! Join Hello Kitty on an exciting journey as you design and craft the most adorable manicures for her and her friends. Transform your passion for nail art into a fab career as you work your way up to become a superstar nail designer!

In this charming game, you can showcase your skills by taking on various nail art challenges that will test your creativity and precision. Each level offers unique tasks that allow you to refine your techniques and unlock special designs. Whether you are an aspiring artist or a seasoned pro, the game provides ample opportunities to hone your craft and impress your clients with your nail art masterpieces.

If you're in the mood to express your creativity freely, switch to the Free Style mode! This area allows you to experiment without constraints, using a wide range of colors, patterns, and decorations. Unleash your imagination and create stunning nail designs that are as unique as Hello Kitty herself! There s no limit to what you can do in this whimsical salon.

How to play free Hello Kitty Nail Salon - Fashion Star game online

To start playing the Hello Kitty Nail Salon game, simply click on the "Play" button on the homepage. Choose your mode: Challenge or Free Style. In Challenge mode, follow the instructions to complete specific looks within a time limit. In Free Style, select your favorite colors and designs to create manicures at your leisure. Have fun designing!
