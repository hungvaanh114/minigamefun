How to play free Pong Cricket game online

To play Pong Cricket, use your mouse or touchscreen to control the bat. Aim at the incoming balls to hit them and score runs. Keep your reflexes sharp as the speed increases, and strategically time your swings to achieve high scores. Enjoy competing against yourself or friends!
