Angry Birds 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Angry Birds 2, where the competition reaches new heights! Every week, you can dive into 24 fresh levels filled with unique challenges and obstacles. Compete globally as you pit your skills against opponents in the thrilling Star Cup mode. Test your mettle against individual players, and climb the leaderboards to claim your title as the best Angry Birds player around.

The tournament system is designed to keep you on your toes, with new competitive tournaments launching every Monday, Thursday, and Saturday. Each tournament introduces exciting new levels, offering you the chance to challenge other players and earn victories that move you up the leagues. The thrill is in not just winning but holding onto the top position to unlock amazing rewards that will aid you in your quest.

Every other week, enjoy special themed tournaments that present unique challenges and rewards, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging. When you engage in the Star Cup, you'll face off against other players in one-on-one battles, where free power-ups, level effects, and special slingshots are at your disposal. Don t forget to collect feathers to level up your birds, boosting their scoring capabilities, and try to maintain a win streak to maximize your rewards!

How to play free Angry Birds 2 game online

To play Angry Birds 2, simply launch the game and select your desired tournament or level. Use your slingshot to aim at the structures and unleash your birds strategically. Tap on power-ups when necessary to enhance your chances. Collect feathers and complete challenges to upgrade your birds and increase your scores!
