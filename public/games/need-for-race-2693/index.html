Need for Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating journey with Need for Race! This adrenaline-fueled racing game challenges players to embrace their inner speedster. Are you ready to conquer the track and demonstrate your fearless driving skills? Get set at the starting line, rev your engine, and experience the thrill of high-speed racing while maneuvering through a world of obstacles.

Your objective is clear: navigate your vehicle skillfully, avoiding collisions and keeping your car firmly on the track. As you race against time, you'll need to expertly shift between lanes and make daring detours to maintain your velocity. The key to success lies in your ability to stay in motion without crashing, as each second spent on the road accelerates your speed and heightens your racing record.

Take your driving to the limit and challenge the boundaries of speed. As you push through the race, the longer you can maintain your momentum without any setbacks, the higher your speedometer will climb. Aim for the ultimate goal: setting a new maximum speed record that will leave other racers in awe.

How to play free Need for Race game online

Once you're in the game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car. Accelerate to gain speed while deftly switching lanes to avoid obstacles. Keep your focus on the road ahead to dodge collisions and maintain your trajectory. The key to winning is keeping your car moving as smoothly as possible!


Instructions

The car moves automatically Clicking on the right side of the screen allows you to turn it to the right clicking on the left side of the screen makes a left turn You can also turn using the A D keys or the right and left arrows on the keyboard
