Wizard of Symbols

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Wizard of Symbols, where you become part of the grand Wizard Duels, contested in real-time against fellow witches and wizards from every corner of the globe. Embark on a thrilling journey after entering the duels, as you explore a mysterious castle, the site of all magical contests. Here, you can roam freely, encountering other players, and uncover a host of exciting spells that allow for whimsical interactions, like lifting entire bookshelves or shattering them into pieces.

The more time you spend honing your magical abilities, the more formidable spells you will unlock. Among these spells are the awe-inspiring Werewolf, mystical Runes, and the powerful Dragon spell. Imagine the astonishment of your rivals when you unexpectedly transform into a fearsome werewolf, either within the realm of the castle or amidst the intensity of a duel. Wizard of Symbols offers an innovative, magical twist to the classic game of rock-paper-scissors, played among a community of witches and wizards.

Engage in intense PvP action as you challenge players worldwide. The gameplay is straightforward; utilize your spells wisely to overcome your opponent's magic. Each round culminates in a battle of wits, where casting a spell stronger than that of your adversary earns you the victory. Aim to rise to the top of the leaderboard and establish your dominance in this spellbinding arena!

How to play free Wizard of Symbols game online

To play Wizard of Symbols, join a duel by selecting your spell from the available options. Pay attention to your opponent's spell choice and strategize accordingly; your spell must outperform theirs to win. After each duel, explore the castle to discover new spells and meet other players. Enjoy the magic!
