Piggy Bank

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of the Piggy Bank game, where your dexterity and timing will be put to the test! On your screen, you will encounter an adorable piggy bank positioned at the lower section of the playing field, ready to collect your coins. A unique button resides above the piggy bank, precisely placed at an optimal height.

Your goal is to click this button as quickly as possible. With each click, coins will drop from the button, embarking on a wild descent towards the piggy bank. However, the path isn't straightforward! Various objects will be scattered between the button and the piggy bank, creating opportunities for ricochets and deflections. As the coins bounce off these obstacles, they will hopefully find their way into the piggy bank waiting below.

Keep an eye on your target: a specific amount of coins needs to be collected within the playing field. Once you manage to gather the required number of coins, you will be rewarded with points that add to your overall score in the Piggy Bank game. With its vibrant graphics and engaging mechanics, this game promises hours of fun as you attempt to refine your skills and beat your previous scores. So get ready to enjoy a whimsical experience while aiming to fill that piggy bank!

How to play free Piggy Bank game online

To begin playing Piggy Bank, simply use your mouse to click the button above the piggy bank rapidly. Watch as the coins drop and bounce off the objects on your screen. Your objective is to guide the coins into the piggy bank until you reach the required quantity and earn points!
