Christmas: Night of Horror

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This Christmas, an ominous threat looms over the festive season in Christmas: Night of Horror. What should be a joyful celebration has taken a dark turn, as malevolent creatures rise from the shadows, threatening to spoil the holiday cheer. Armed with only your wits and a weapon, it s up to you to drive these horrendous beings back to Hell. Face nightmarish foes such as Slenderman, Momo, and Siren Head, all of whom are determined to invade your Christmas. Can you muster the courage and skill needed to protect the spirit of Christmas?

As you navigate through haunting frozen landscapes, you will encounter various levels filled with spine-chilling terror. Each eerie setting attracts different monsters, each more relentless than the last. It s not just about pulling the trigger; strategy plays a key role in how you tackle each enemy. Use your surroundings to your advantage as you uncover the secrets of the Christmas night, while prioritizing your movements to evade attacks. Will you survive the chaos and restore peace, or will you become another victim in this night of horror? Embrace the challenge, and remember: the fate of Christmas rests in your hands!

How to play free Christmas: Night of Horror game online

To play Christmas: Night of Horror, start by choosing your weapon and customizing your character. Use the WASD keys to move around and the mouse to aim. Click to shoot at the approaching monsters. Keep an eye on your ammo and health, and make sure to explore for power-ups to help you survive the night!
