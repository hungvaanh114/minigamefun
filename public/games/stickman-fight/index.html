How to play free Stickman Fight game online

Once you enter the game, control your stickman using keyboard arrows or on-screen buttons to move around. Utilize the attack buttons to deliver punches and perform special moves. Your goal is to eliminate as many enemies as possible while dodging their attacks and avoiding getting knocked out. Good luck!
