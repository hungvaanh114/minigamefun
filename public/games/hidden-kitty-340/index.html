Hidden Kitty

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hidden Kitty is a captivating and charming hidden object game designed specifically for kids, and best of all, it s completely free! Immerse yourself in an enchanting world where you'll assist a sweet old lady in her quest to find her cherished kitties, who are mischievously hiding throughout the various rooms of the building. Each level offers colorful, vibrant illustrations filled with intricate details, inviting players to search thoroughly for the playful felines.

This delightful adventure promises hours of entertainment as you explore beautifully crafted environments, from cozy living rooms to whimsical gardens. As you progress through the game, you ll encounter cleverly concealed kitties, each requiring keen observation and quick thinking to locate. Whether you re a seasoned detective or a budding adventurer, Hidden Kitty is designed to challenge and excite players of all ages.

With its user-friendly interface and engaging gameplay, Hidden Kitty is perfect for little ones seeking fun and adventure while developing their observational skills. So, gather your friends, embark on this Kitty-Finding Adventure, and have limitless fun as you help the old lady reunite with her furry companions!

How to play free Hidden Kitty game online

To play the Hidden Kitty game, simply open the game interface and observe the colorful scene laid out before you. Look in every nook and cranny for hidden kitties, using the list of items as a guide. Click on the kitties you find to score points, and try to complete each level as quickly as possible for bonus rewards!
