Draw War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Unleash your creativity in Draw War. This is a wonderful strategy game. Here drawing meets battles. Build a hero lineup, draw your legions, then unleash the power of your army. The numbers show the soldiers summoned. Click to send Shielded Soldiers, Soldiers on Horseback, or Super Beasts.
