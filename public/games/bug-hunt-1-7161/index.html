Instructions:

Welcome to Bug Hunt 1, where the relentless invasion of cockroaches has turned your environment into a battleground. These pesky critters are not just lurking in the shadows; they are multiplying rapidly, threatening to take over your space. The usual bug sprays have proven ineffective against this relentless swarm, meaning it's time to escalate your tactics to a whole new level. Your mission? Use bombs to eradicate these annoying intruders!

In Bug Hunt 1, strategy is key. You need to think one step ahead of the cockroaches as they scurry about, searching for cover. With your limited supply of bombs, placement is crucial. You must observe their movement patterns and choose the optimal moments to detonate. Do you place a bomb at their rendezvous point, or do you attempt to trap them in a corner? Each decision can lead to a remarkable explosion or an empty victory.

As you progress through the levels, the cockroaches become more cunning and infuriating in their movements, making your task even more challenging. The escalating difficulty will test your abilities to plan and execute your bug-hunting strategy effectively. Get ready for a thrilling fight against these unwelcome guests and reclaim your territory!

How to play free Bug Hunt 1 game online

To play Bug Hunt 1, use your mouse or touchpad to select and place your bombs across the game field. Monitor the cockroaches movements carefully; time your detonations perfectly to maximize your extermination rate. Aim for clustered groups to increase your score and clear the level efficiently!
