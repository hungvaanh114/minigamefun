Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey through the cosmos with "Conquer the Galaxy." In a universe where chaos reigns supreme, alien races are multiplying rapidly and are locked in a fierce territorial battle. Each species strives to dominate the star-studded expanse, and your territory is their next target. Arm yourself with strategy and cunning as you defend your lands and fight back against these extraterrestrial invaders!

In "Conquer the Galaxy," your main objective is to expand your influence and secure your place among the stars. Prioritize bolstering your population, as a larger force will allow you to invade and capture enemy planets. Use your resources wisely to enhance your power, and strategize your moves to outmaneuver your opponents. The universe is not just about defense; it's a vibrant battlefield where offense can turn the tide of war. Prepare for intense skirmishes as you engage other alien factions and vie for control over valuable territory.

Your strategic decisions will determine the fate of your empire. Will you forge alliances or create enemies? Each gameplay session offers unique challenges requiring quick thinking and adaptability. Show no mercy, and remember: in space, it's survival of the fittest. Will you rise as the ultimate conqueror or fall to the whims of alien forces?

How to play free Conquer the Galaxy game online

To start playing "Conquer the Galaxy," simply launch the game and follow the on-screen instructions to navigate through the menus. Use your mouse or touchscreen to manage resources, build your population, and choose your targets for invasion. Keep an eye on enemy movements to stay one step ahead!
