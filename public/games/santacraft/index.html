SantaCraft

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

SantaCraft is an exhilarating third-person shooter that thrusts you into a vibrant 3D environment filled with challenges and excitement. Set against a backdrop reminiscent of a blocky universe, you'll find yourself as the last survivor in a relentless zombie apocalypse. Your ultimate goal? To construct a mystical portal while combating waves of relentless undead hordes determined to take you down.

As you navigate this expansive world, you'll engage in intense firefights where strategic thinking and quick reflexes are key. Equip yourself with a diverse arsenal of weapons, each designed to help you fend off the grotesque zombies lurking around every corner. Whether it's a trusty pistol or a powerful shotgun, selecting the right weapon will be essential in your fight for survival. Every encounter is a test of skill as you dodge zombie attacks while returning fire in this action-packed adventure.

Your survival hinges on your ability to remain alert and ready for the unexpected. Building the portal that will take you to safety requires resources scattered throughout the blocky landscape, adding an element of exploration and strategy as you plan your next move. Immerse yourself in a world where quick thinking and precision shooting can mean the difference between life and death against the evil undead.

How to play free SantaCraft game online

To play SantaCraft online, simply enter the game and familiarize yourself with the controls. Use your mouse to aim and shoot while navigating with the keyboard. Collect resources to build the portal and prioritize upgrading your weapons whenever possible. Engage the zombies and use strategic combat techniques to survive!


Instructions

WASD to move LMB to shoot RMB to aim Space to jump Left shift to run
