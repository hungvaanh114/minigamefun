Stone Wheel

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the adventure in Stone Wheel, a wildly entertaining game that has captured the hearts of over 1,000,000 players worldwide! This engaging title invites you to navigate a large, rolling stone wheel through 30 uniquely designed levels filled with challenges and fun.

With its vibrant and whimsical graphics, Stone Wheel serves as an ideal distraction for your down time. The gameplay is straightforward yet incredibly addictive, making it suitable for players of all ages. You can choose between two control methods: the classic touch interface or the innovative accelerometer option, allowing you to enjoy the game in the way that suits you best.

To conquer the various levels, you will need to master a number of essential skills, including jumping, accelerating, and braking. Perfect control of your stone wheel is critical, as precision will help you navigate through obstacles and reach your goal. As you progress, you'll encounter increasingly complex scenarios that will test your reflexes and decision-making abilities. Are you ready for the challenge?

How to play free Stone Wheel game online

To begin playing Stone Wheel, simply use your chosen control method. Tap the screen or tilt your device to roll the stone wheel. Use the jump button to leap over obstacles and the brake button for sharp turns. Master each maneuver to successfully complete all levels!
