The Branch Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to The Branch Runner, an exhilarating arcade game that invites you to showcase your skills in endless running while navigating through a vibrant world made entirely of 3D pixel blocks. In this entertaining adventure, you will take control of a character that continuously runs forward, necessitating quick reflexes and strategic thinking.

Your primary task is to manipulate the platform track by rotating it to dodge numerous obstacle blocks littered throughout your path. Precision is key, as timely rotations will ensure your character keeps sprinting without crashing into any barriers. With an array of charming and visually striking skins available in the shop, you can customize your character to reflect your unique style. By skillfully collecting gold coins spread across the track, you can unlock these exciting new appearances and elevate your gaming experience.

As you progress through different levels, the challenges intensify, making The Branch Runner not only a test of speed but also a test of your agility and decision-making. Get ready to immerse yourself in this vibrant landscape, filled with delightful sounds and breathtaking visuals that accompany your thrilling journey. This is more than just a game it's a platform for adventure, creativity, and fun!

How to play free The Branch Runner game online

To play The Branch Runner, use your finger or mouse to rotate the platform track as your character runs forward automatically. Avoid the obstacle blocks by swiftly adjusting your position. Collect gold coins as you dash along to unlock new skins in the shop!


Instructions

Tap the left side of the screen to rotate left tap the right side of the screen to rotate right
