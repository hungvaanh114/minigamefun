Build With Cubes 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into a whole new realm of creativity with Build with Cubes 2, a dynamic voxel sandbox inspired by the beloved Minecraft style. In this immersive 3D environment, you are given the freedom to craft and create anything your imagination can conjure using vibrant cube blocks. Whether you're constructing intricate structures or expansive landscapes, the game provides an extensive array of tools to bring your visions to life.

Start by selecting a simple layout populated with cubes or utilize the game s customizable features to generate stunning 3D terrains. Adjust various settings, including seed values and the height and width of hills, to fine-tune the scenery. Add an array of elements such as trees, flowers, and diverse objects to enhance your building projects. From imaginative dollhouses and charming cottages to majestic castles and bustling towns, the possibilities are endless. Once your structures are built, consider adorning them with stylish furniture and decorative items to give them a personal touch.

Explore the depths of your creativity by integrating portals and using the portal gun to design fun puzzles and teleportation systems in your world. Populate your creations with animated entities and animals, or even compose simple melodies using musical note blocks. Take advantage of the fly mode and mirror functions to expedite your building process. Experiment with physics-based objects such as bouncy dice, trampolines, and TNT to add an extra layer of excitement to your gameplay.

How to play free Build with Cubes 2 game online

To begin playing Build with Cubes 2, simply navigate to the game and use your mouse to place and manipulate cube blocks. Use the toolbar to select various tools and features, adjust terrain settings, and explore the vast building options available. Engage with the environment and unleash your creativity freely!


Instructions

Arrow keys WASD to move around and control flight hold LMB and move mouse to rotate camera Spacebar - to jump E to pick up objects LMB click to select block and place new one RMB to destroy any block and picked object Z and Q - create portals
