How to play free Cut The Buttons game online

To play Cut The Buttons, simply click on the buttons you wish to cut. As you progress through the game, carefully plan your cuts to solve each puzzle. Your goal is to free all the buttons using the fewest moves possible, so think strategically to make the most of each level!


Instructions

1 Use the mouse or finger to control 2 Select a button with the mouse as the starting point 3 Cut same-colored buttons on the same line 4 You cant cut the line with different-colored buttons 5 Dont leave any buttons at the end of the level
