Age of Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the tumultuous world of Age of Quest, where your castle stands at the forefront of a fierce battle against unyielding hordes of monsters and skeletal warriors. Equipped with a mighty sword and extraordinary magical abilities, you are tasked with defending your stronghold from the ceaseless onslaught. Utilize your strategic skills and combat prowess to fend off the relentless invaders that threaten your fortress.

As you engage in combat, every vanquished foe yields valuable rewards in the form of coins, gems, and ammunition. These resources are crucial in upgrading your abilities and acquiring formidable super frames that further enhance your combat skills, allowing you to confront increasingly powerful foes. Your bravery and tactical acumen will determine the survival of your castle and your rise as a legendary hero.

Will you rise to the challenge, defeating waves of rabid beasts and skeletal champions? The fate of your kingdom lies in your hands, and only your valor can ensure the safety of your stronghold. How to play free Age Of Quest game online

To play Age of Quest, simply use the arrow keys or WASD to move around, while the mouse or specific keys perform attacks and cast spells. Defend your castle by targeting the approaching enemies and collecting loot dropped from fallen foes to upgrade your equipment and abilities. Stay vigilant; the battle never ends!
