MultiplArrow

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

MultiplArrow is an engaging and captivating mobile game designed to challenge your mathematical abilities while keeping you entertained. Players take the reins of an arrow adorned with numbers and symbols, venturing through a series of mathematical trials. Your mission is to aim, shoot, and effectively combine arrows to strike the target number.

With a total of 80 levels that progressively ramp up in difficulty, MultiplArrow provides an excellent blend of fun and educational gameplay. As you progress through each stage, you will sharpen your math skills by solving equations, making each round not only a test of aim but also a test of intellect. This stimulating game ensures hours of thrilling arrow-shooting action, urging you to improve your mathematical prowess and become a master marksman in the world of numbers.

Whether you re looking to brush up on your arithmetic or simply enjoy a game that keeps your mind engaged, MultiplArrow delivers on both fronts. Every level is meticulously designed to challenge and entertain, making it an excellent choice for players of all ages. Arrow now and embark on an adventure to become the ultimate math marksman!

How to play free MultiplArrow game online

To play MultiplArrow, use your finger to aim the arrow towards the target number displayed on the screen. Tap to shoot the arrow and strategically combine it with other arrows to form the sum or product needed to hit the target. Solve the equations quickly to advance through the levels!
