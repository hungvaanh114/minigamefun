Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you're on the hunt for an exhilarating car parking experience, look no further than Car Driving In Big City 2! Developed by FROLICS, this game offers a physics-driven Prado parking simulator perfect for enthusiasts of crazy car driving games. Engage in exciting car parking missions and exhilarating multiplayer challenges while enjoying smooth controls that enhance your gameplay. Stunning HD graphics, a variety of racing cars, and daily achievement rewards await you in this offline driving extravaganza.

The game features a beginner level to get you started on your car parking career. With a garage filled with luxurious racing vehicles, you can indulge in time-based parking missions and multiplayer challenges to test your skills against friends. For those seeking a thrill, become part of tournaments and championships that further elevate the fun of parking challenges!

Your driving journey unfolds in several modes:

  • Starter Mode: Perfect for newcomers, this mode allows you to enhance your driving skills and complete easy parking missions.
  • Advance Mode: Level up your skills with tough parking challenges and use various controls to navigate the car parking plaza.
  • Pro Driver Mode: Showcase your legendary driving prowess as you conquer challenging missions and test your expertise with diverse car controls and camera angles.
  • Arcade Mode: Drive freely through the parking plaza, collecting fuel packages and scoring high by parking in multiple spots.

Master your driving skills and secure a Prado driving license by excelling in the car driving school. Compete in thrilling multiplayer tournaments against players worldwide to become the ultimate champion!

How to play free Car Driving In Big City 2 game online

To play Car Driving In Big City 2, simply select your preferred game mode from the main menu. Use the on-screen controls to navigate your car, focusing on parking it within designated zones as instructed in each mission. Collect rewards and fuel along the way to enhance your experience. Enjoy the thrill of the drive!
