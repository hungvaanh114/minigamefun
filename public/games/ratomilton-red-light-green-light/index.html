Step into the world of Ratomilton Red Light Green Light, a thrilling game of action, timing, and quick reflexes! You take on the role of Rato Milton, a brave little character who must reach the finish line while avoiding detection from a squid game enemy. Inspired by the classic childhood game, this version adds a unique twist, requiring players to master precise movement and perfect their sense of timing to succeed. How to Play The rules are simple but the challenge is real! Your objective is to cross the finish line without being caught moving by the enemy.
