Farming Town

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Farming Town, where the agricultural dream comes to life! This immersive farming simulator allows players to engage in a variety of farming activities such as plowing, sowing, and harvesting. With an extensive selection of machines at your disposal, you can tackle your farming tasks more efficiently and boost your productivity.

As you cultivate your fields and reap your harvests, you ll have the opportunity to sell your goods for money. This way, you can reinvest your earnings into acquiring new tractors and combines, upgrading your farming arsenal as you progress. Explore the open-world career mode and build your farming empire, transforming your humble beginnings into a thriving agricultural business.

Experience the thrill of operating realistic farming vehicles and immerse yourself in the delightful day-to-day life of a professional farmer. From smaller chores to significant challenges, Farming Town keeps players engaged with its dynamic gameplay and expansive environment. Whether you re planting seeds, managing livestock, or navigating the market, there s always something exciting to do in this vibrant farming landscape.

How to play free Farming Town game online

To start playing Farming Town, simply load the game in your browser. Use your mouse or touch controls to navigate through the interface. Begin by selecting a plot of land to plow, then choose the appropriate machine to sow your seeds. Once your crops are ready to harvest, collect them and sell your produce to earn money for upgrades!
