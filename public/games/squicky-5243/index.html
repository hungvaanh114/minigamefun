Squicky

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Squicky, the adorable mouse on an exciting adventure to save his friends! Journey through vibrant and challenging levels filled with obstacles and enemies. As you navigate through these colorful worlds, your primary task will be to locate all the hidden keys which will allow you to progress to the next stage.

Throughout your quest, you will encounter various enemies that you must defeat to protect Squicky and his friends. Use your quick reflexes and clever strategies to overcome foes while collecting sparkling coins scattered throughout the levels. Accumulating these coins not only boosts your score but also enhances your overall gameplay experience.

The game is designed to captivate players of all ages with its charming graphics and engaging storyline. Each level introduces new challenges and surprises, making every playthrough unique and exciting. As you dive deeper into Squicky's world, you'll unlock new power-ups and bonuses that add an extra layer of fun to your journey.

How to play free Squicky game online

Playing Squicky is simple and fun. Use the arrow keys to navigate your character, Squicky, through the levels. Jump to collect coins and avoid or defeat enemies by using special abilities. Keep an eye out for the hidden keys to progress to the next level. Stay alert and enjoy the adventure!


Instructions

Use arrow keys to move and the spacebar to jump
