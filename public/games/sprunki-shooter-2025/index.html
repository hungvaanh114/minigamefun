Sprunki Shooter 2025

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sprunki Shooter 2025 is a free to play online game that delivers exciting shooting action in a colorful and challenging world. Players must navigate through each level, clearing obstacles and defeating enemies to reach the exit. The goal is to complete all levels by unlocking the exit doors. To do this, players must shoot and collect all the scattered diamonds while avoiding or eliminating dangerous enemies along the way.

Instructions

Arrow keys to play Mouse to shoot
