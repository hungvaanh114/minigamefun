Instructions:

Embark on a fun and entertaining adventure with Chicken Jump, a delightful casual game where you take on the role of a plucky chicken. With its unique gravity-defying mechanics, players must guide their feathered friend through a whimsical world, filled with various obstacles and challenging platforms.

Your objective is to navigate through 12 exciting levels, each designed to test your reflexes and speed. Timing is key as you run and jump from one platform to another, all while avoiding tricky barriers that could hinder your progress. Gather glittering diamonds scattered along the way, which not only enhance your score but unlock special achievements in the game.

As you advance, the challenges become progressively tougher, requiring precise jumps and quick thinking. The vibrant graphics and engaging sound effects create an immersive environment that hooks players from the beginning. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a casual player, Chicken Jump offers fun gameplay that can be enjoyed at your leisure. Good luck, and may your chicken soar to new heights!

How to play free Chicken Jump game online

To play Chicken Jump, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your chicken. Press the jump button to soar over obstacles and land on platforms. Collect diamonds for bonus points and aim to complete all 12 levels before time runs out. Enjoy the exhilarating ride!
