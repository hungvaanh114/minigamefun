Akihiko vs Cannons

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Akihiko vs Cannons, an exhilarating 2D platformer that puts your reflexes to the test! You take on the role of Akihiko, a brave adventurer on a quest to gather precious Gold Bars while skillfully navigating through a plethora of dangers. Armed with only your wits and agility, you will face relentless cannon bots, menacing bullets, treacherous spikes, and cunning flying cannon bots that are all determined to thwart your progress.

Your challenge lies not only in collecting the Gold Bars scattered across vibrant levels but also in expertly dodging the various hazards that await at every turn. The ultimate goal is to reach the yellow flag at the end of each level, which acts as the gateway to the next thrilling stage of your journey. With a total of eight uniquely designed levels, the game gradually increases in difficulty, ensuring that you stay engaged and challenged as you strive to conquer each new environment.

Starting the game with six lives, every decision counts as you leap, duck, and dash your way through beautifully crafted landscapes while keeping an eye out for hidden treasures. Are you ready to rise to the challenge and become the ultimate Gold Bar collector? Dive into Akihiko vs Cannons today, and put your skills to the test!

How to play free Akihiko vs Cannons game online

To start playing Akihiko vs Cannons, use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to move Akihiko left or right and jump over obstacles. Collect Gold Bars by running into them while avoiding enemy cannon bots and their bullets. Your objective is to reach the yellow flag at the end of each level to progress!
