Burger Stack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Burger Stack, the ultimate challenge for burger enthusiasts! In this exciting game, your mission is to construct the tallest and most extravagant burger imaginable. Get ready to unleash your creativity and stacking skills as you pile up an array of mouthwatering ingredients. From juicy patties and crispy lettuce to delectable cheeses and zesty sauces, the possibilities are endless. But be careful! The higher your burger, the more challenging it becomes to keep it balanced. Use your precision and timing to ensure that each item is placed perfectly, and watch your culinary creation grow before your eyes.

Burger Stack isn't just about height; it's also about style. As you stack your burgers, aim for the highest quality ingredients to earn extra points and unlock special items. Each component you add enhances the overall appeal of your burger masterpiece. Compete against friends or aim for personal records as you strive to reach new heights in this deliciously addictive game. The graphics are colorful and engaging, making it a feast for both the eyes and the stomach. So gather your ingredients, put your chef's hat on, and get ready to create the biggest burger ever!

How to play free Burger Stack game online

To play Burger Stack, simply tap or click to drop ingredients onto the bun. Be steady and precise, as a misplaced item could topple your tower. Keep stacking as high as you can while maintaining balance, and aim for the best score possible. Enjoy the fun!
