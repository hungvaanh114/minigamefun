Fashion Nail Design Day: Art Game for Girls

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fashion Nail Design Day, the ultimate nail art and makeup game designed specifically for girls! Join Sofia and Adela as they prepare for a shopping spree in style. Eager to flaunt stunning manicures, they head to a trendy nail salon for a fabulous makeover. In this vibrant game, you will take charge as the nail salon manager, crafting dazzling nail designs that reflect the personalities of your clients.

With a plethora of nail colors, patterns, and decorative elements at your fingertips, you have the creative freedom to mix and match until you achieve the perfect look. Experiment with vibrant colors, stylish nail shapes, and delightful accents such as gems and stickers to create unique designs that will leave Sofia and Adela feeling fabulous.

As you work your magic, pay attention to the girls' individual styles and preferences to ensure they leave the salon with a smile. With each successful manicure, you'll unlock even more exciting tools and design options, elevating your nail art game to new heights. Gather your friends to share your creations and inspire each other with new designs and trends!

How to play free Fashion Nail Design Day: Art Game for Girls game online

To begin playing, simply choose the nail art option that catches your eye. Use your mouse or touchpad to select colors, patterns, and accessories for each girl's manicure. Follow through with the design steps to complete their looks, and watch them shine as they head out for their shopping adventure!
