Sharpshooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sharpshooter is an engaging casual puzzle game that puts your bullet reflection skills to the test. In a world filled with various captivating settings ranging from arid deserts and bustling bars to majestic castles and fortified bridges you'll need to navigate through obstacles and quickly adapt your strategy to eliminate enemies with deadly precision.

The primary objective is simple yet thrilling: utilize your marksmanship to target foes and ensure each shot counts. With clever puzzles in each level, players must aim and reflect bullets off surfaces to reach their enemies. This mechanic not only challenges your aim but also your problem-solving abilities, making for an exhilarating gameplay experience.

As you progress through Sharpshooter, you'll encounter a variety of scenes, each designed to present unique challenges. The different environments offer layouts that require strategic thinking and careful planning, enhancing the game's replayability. Each level progressively increases in difficulty, ensuring that both novice and seasoned players are continuously engaged. Prepare to unleash your inner gun master as you tackle the intricacies of each scene!

How to play free Sharpshooter game online

To play Sharpshooter online, start by selecting a level that interests you. Use your mouse to aim and adjust your shot's angle. Release to fire, attempting to bounce bullets off walls to hit your target. Keep an eye on your limited shots and try to complete each level with the fewest possible! Enjoy the challenge!
