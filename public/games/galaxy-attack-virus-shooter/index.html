Galaxy Attack Virus Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Galaxy Attack Virus Shooter is an exhilarating retro-style flight shooting game that pits your skills against a relentless wave of harmful viruses. As a daring pilot, you'll take control of your advanced warcraft and battle against an overwhelming horde of these menacing foes. The viruses have multiplied in great numbers, and your mission is clear: wipe them out before they can cause more chaos.

To succeed in your quest, you'll need to enhance your weaponry as you progress through the game. With various upgrades available, you can transform your warcraft into an indomitable force, capable of countering the viruses aggressive attacks. The enemies are not just numerous; they are also cunning and dangerous. Quick reflexes and strategic positioning are essential to evade their relentless onslaught.

As you navigate through vividly designed levels, expect to encounter different types of viruses, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns. Mastering the art of dodging and shooting simultaneously will be crucial for your survival. Arrayed against you are not just tiny threats, but also massive virus bosses that will test your skills to the limit.

Embark on an action-packed journey filled with colorful graphics and retro charm. Galaxy Attack Virus Shooter isn't just about sheer firepower; it's also about devising smart tactics to outsmart the viruses lurking in every corner of space. Only by honing your shooting skills and upgrading effectively will you stand a chance against this viral invader!

How to play free Galaxy Attack Virus Shooter game online

To play Galaxy Attack Virus Shooter, use your mouse or touch controls to steer your warcraft. Aim your shots at the incoming viruses while avoiding their attacks. Collect power-ups and currency to upgrade your weapons and enhance your firepower. Survive each wave and defeat challenging bosses to progress through the game!
