How to play free Super Mario Card Matching Puzzle game online

To play the Super Mario Card Matching Puzzle game, simply start by clicking on any two cards to reveal them. Your goal is to find and match pairs of identical cards. If you successfully match a pair, those cards are removed from the board, and you score points. Keep matching pairs until all cards are paired!
