Draw Pen Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Draw Pen Rush is an exhilarating parkour-style game that provides players with an engaging experience as they navigate challenging courses. In this entertaining adventure, you take control of a dynamic robot, tasked with competing in adrenaline-pumping parkour races. Your objective is to skilfully maneuver past various obstacles while simultaneously collecting parts that can enhance your robot s abilities. As you gather these components, you have the opportunity to upgrade and transform into larger, more powerful robotic forms, adding a layer of strategy to the fast-paced gameplay.

The game is expertly designed for portrait mode, which enables a broader view of the track ahead. This perspective is crucial, as it allows players to anticipate potential traps and obstacles, ensuring they can react swiftly to avoid setbacks. The immersive design and thrill of racing enhance the overall experience, making every moment feel intense and rewarding. As you sprint forward, your ultimate goal is to outsmart and defeat the sinister mastermind known as the ultimate brain, elevating the stakes and adding a competitive edge to your robotic journey.

With its combined elements of speed, strategy, and innovation, Draw Pen Rush delivers a fantastic gaming experience packed with excitement. Are you ready to draw your path to victory and become the champion of parkour racing in this captivating world?

How to play free Draw Pen Rush game online

To play Draw Pen Rush, begin by tapping to start the race. Move your robot by drawing lines to guide its path while avoiding obstacles. Collect parts along the way to upgrade your abilities, and outpace your opponents to reach the finish line first. Enjoy the thrilling race!
