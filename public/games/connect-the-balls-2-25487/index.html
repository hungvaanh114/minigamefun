Connect the Balls 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the second part of the new online game Connect the Balls 2 you will continue to play through an interesting puzzle. In front of you on the screen you will see a playing field on which there will be round dots of different colors. You will need to inspect everything carefully. Find two identical dots of the same color. Now connect them using the mouse with one line. Having done this, you will see how these points will disappear from the playing field. By making your moves, you will completely clear the field of dots and then move on to the next level of the game.

Instructions
