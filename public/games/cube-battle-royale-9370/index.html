Cube Battle Royale

Cube Battle Royale is an engaging third-person shooting simulation game that immerses players in vibrant 3D pixel art environments. In this exhilarating battle royale setup, players are tasked with collecting various useful items strategically scattered throughout the map. Each match challenges you to outsmart your opponents while maintaining essential survival skills.

Your primary objective is to locate and activate the radio machine found in each level. This crucial step not only enhances your chances of survival but also allows you to call for help during intense combat. Engage in thrilling firefights while utilizing the environment to your advantage, and make sure to stay alert for other players striving for victory. Whether you prefer going solo or teaming up for a more strategic approach, Cube Battle Royale offers a versatile gameplay experience.
