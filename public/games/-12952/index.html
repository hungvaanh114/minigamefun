Ava Mouth Makeover?Ava Mouth Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join our delightful character, Ava, on a journey to rejuvenate her smile in the captivating game, Ava Mouth Makeover. In this entertaining and educational game, you will take on the role of Ava's dental hero, helping her achieve a dazzling mouth transformation. The game combines elements of dental care with fun gameplay, allowing players to engage in various activities designed to improve Ava's dental hygiene.

Your mission is to help Ava by removing stubborn stains from her teeth and eliminating harmful bacteria that can lead to dental issues. As you play, you will utilize a variety of dental tools and treatments to clean, whiten, and restore Ava s stunning smile. But don't stop there! Ava also needs assistance in cleaning her throat to ensure she is in tip-top shape. This interactive experience will not only entertain you but also teach you the importance of mouth hygiene.

After you have worked your magic on Ava's mouth, it's time to show off her new look! Choose stylish outfits and accessories that reflect her vibrant personality, completing the makeover process. This game is perfect for kids and anyone interested in learning about dental health while having an enjoyable time.

Ready for some tooth-tastic fun? Dive into Ava Mouth Makeover and enjoy the rewarding experience of giving Ava the brilliant smile she deserves!

How to play free Ava Mouth Makeover game online

To play the Ava Mouth Makeover game, simply click on the different dental tools available on the screen. Follow the prompts to remove stains, kill bacteria, and clean Ava's throat. Use the mouse or touchscreen to interact, and customize her look by selecting outfits once her mouth is all set. Enjoy!
