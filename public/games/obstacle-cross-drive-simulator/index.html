Obstacle Cross Drive Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Obstacle Cross Drive Simulator, a hardcore truck driving simulation that immerses you in a stunning 3D environment. This game features realistic models that enhance your driving experience and put your skills to the test. Navigate through challenging terrains while mastering the art of truck control.

You can choose from four diverse game modes, each designed with straightforward rules to ensure you have an enjoyable time. With the help of real-time hints and guiding arrows, your mission is to complete each task efficiently. The stakes are high, and only by adhering to the game's instructions can you succeed. Your goal is not only to finish the levels but to earn three coveted yellow stars for completing all objectives at every stage!

Each level brings unique challenges that require quick thinking and precision driving. As you progress, the complexity will increase, demanding perfect timing and skillful maneuvering to overcome obstacles. Prove your mettle by navigating through expansive landscapes filled with exciting adventures as you strive for mastery in this exhilarating truck simulator.

How to play free Obstacle Cross Drive Simulator game online

To begin playing, select your preferred mode and start the mission. Use the arrow keys or touchscreen controls to steer your truck, and carefully follow the indicated paths. Keep an eye on the on-screen hints, work on fulfilling each level's requirements, and aim for a perfect score by earning all three stars!
