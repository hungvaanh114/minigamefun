Claritas Dungeon Crawler RPG DEMO

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Claritas is a 2D turn-based dungeon crawler RPG that focuses purely on strategic gameplay. With no story or plot, the game challenges players to experiment with party-building, skill fusion, and tactical combat. Choose from a diverse roster of heroes, each with four unique abilities, and create powerful synergies to dominate every encounter.

Instructions

Desktop Controls - Confirm Z - Cancel X - Move Arrow keys Mobile Controls - Confirm Tap - Cancel Swipe or Two-finger tap - Move Tap
