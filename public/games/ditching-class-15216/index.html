Ditching Class!!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ditching Class!! is an exhilarating escape game that immerses you in the thrilling experience of sneaking out of school. The desire to break free from the confines of the classroom drives you to devise clever strategies that will lead to your successful escape. As you plot your departure, you must navigate a host of challenges, ranging from cleverly hidden traps to the ever-watchful eyes of your teacher.

Your main goal is to slip out of the classroom without being detected. To accomplish this, you will need to explore various props scattered around the room that can aid in your escape. Whether it s tapping on items to interact with them or cleverly using tools at your disposal, the game invites players to think on their feet and act quickly. It's all about dodging the teacher's attention while cleverly solving puzzles and overcoming obstacles that block your path to freedom.

As you play, remember that getting stuck is part of the challenge! If you find yourself at a standstill on a particular puzzle, fear not! You can gain valuable hints by watching quick video ads, allowing you to find the solutions you need without losing your momentum.

How to play free Ditching Class!! game online

To play Ditching Class!!, simply tap on any item that catches your eye to interact with it. Use the collected items strategically to navigate through the classroom. Keep an eye out for traps and the teacher, and if you hit a roadblock, watch a video ad for hints to continue your escape quickly!
