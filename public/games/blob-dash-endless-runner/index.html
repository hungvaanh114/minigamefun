Blob Dash Endless Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Blob Dash - Endless Runner is an exciting adventure game that takes you on a fast-paced journey through a colorful world filled with challenges and obstacles. As the player, you must control a cute blob and guide it through levels of increasing difficulty. In this game, youll have to run, jump, dodge, and slide your way past hazards such as spikes, gaps, and other obstacles.
