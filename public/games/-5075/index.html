Welcome to Sonic Speedway, a thrilling cart racing adventure that combines speed, excitement, and fun! This game features a vibrant world filled with cute characters like Poppy, who will ride in their candy trucks through a variety of challenging tracks. Your objective is to help Poppy navigate the hills and obstacles to reach home while collecting plenty of subway coins along the way.

In Sonic Speedway, you'll engage in high-speed action as you race through beautifully designed environments. The game boasts stunning graphics that immerse you in a realistic racing experience. Cruise along the tracks, jump over ramps, and dodge barriers, all while striving for a new high score. The adrenaline rush is palpable as you dash through over 100 challenging levels, ensuring that both kids and adults will be entertained for hours.

To keep the excitement going, you'll have various characters to select from, each bringing their unique abilities to the race. Collect coins to unlock new levels and purchase power-ups that will help you outrun your enemies and eliminate obstacles in your path. Experience the thrill of racing at ultra speeds while trying to escape the grasp of security guards and other foes, all while zipping through the lively subway. Are you ready for the ultimate racing challenge?

How to play free Sonic Speedway game online

To play Sonic Speedway, simply tap the screen to start running. Use swipe gestures to turn, jump, and slide under obstacles. Collect coins as you dash through the subway and utilize power-ups to gain an advantage. Aim to beat your best score and enjoy endless racing excitement!
