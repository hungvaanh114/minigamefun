Deca vs Rooko

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Deca vs Rooko is an engaging 2D platformer that combines cuteness with challenge. Players embark on an adventurous journey where their primary objective is to collect all the delectable hotdogs scattered throughout the vibrant levels. However, this delightful quest is fraught with danger as players must navigate a variety of obstacles, including menacing enemies, sharp spikes, and massive blades that threaten to impede their progress.

The game features eight uniquely designed levels, each presenting an increasing level of difficulty. As players advance, they ll need to master their agility and timing to overcome the ever-evolving challenges. The colorful graphics and charming characters enhance the delightful experience, making it an enjoyable game for players of all ages. Whether you are a casual gamer or seeking a thrilling platforming adventure, Deca vs Rooko promises hours of fun and excitement.

How to play free Deca vs Rooko game online

To play Deca vs Rooko, use the arrow keys to move your character left and right, and the spacebar to jump. Navigate through each level by dodging enemies and obstacles while collecting all the hotdogs. Reach the Exit door to advance to the next level. Stay sharp and enjoy the challenge!
