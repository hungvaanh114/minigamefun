How to play free Huggy Wuggy vs Zombies game online

To play, use your mouse or touch screen to aim at the zombies and click or tap to shoot. Keep an eye on your remaining bullets, and plan your shots carefully to ensure you don t run out of ammo. Move through the levels, dodging obstacles while strategically eliminating zombies to successfully complete each level.
