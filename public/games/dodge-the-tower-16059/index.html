Dodge the Tower

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Dodge The Tower, an exciting and addictive hyper-casual game that will keep you on your toes! In this thrilling experience, players must leap from tower to tower while racing against others. The objective is simple: dodge obstacles and time your jumps perfectly to land safely on each platform. The faster you finish the race, the better your chances of claiming victory!

As you navigate the heights of this vibrant tower, look out for various perks scattered throughout the course. These power-ups can grant you burst of speed or even freeze your opponents in place, giving you a strategic advantage to clinch that top position. With its intuitive controls and engaging gameplay, Dodge The Tower is the perfect game for those who seek quick and satisfying thrills.

Whether you're playing during a break or looking for a fun way to unwind, this game s charming graphics and smooth mechanics make every jump an exhilarating challenge. Compete against friends or random players around the globe, and prove who can dodge the tower the best!

How to play free Dodge The Tower game online

To play Dodge The Tower, tap the screen to jump from one tower to another while avoiding obstacles. Collect power-ups to increase your speed or freeze your competitors. Keep an eye on your landing to ensure you stay on the towers and maintain your lead throughout the race!
