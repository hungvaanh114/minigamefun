Bubble Shooter Candy Wheel Level Pack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bubble Shooter Candy Wheel Level Pack invites players into a delightful world where candy bubbles whirl on a vibrant, rotating wheel. Immerse yourself in this engaging game where your objective is to aim and shoot candy bubbles to create matches of three or more. As the wheel spins, the challenge intensifies, keeping you on your toes!

In this sugary adventure, you'll have a range of power-ups at your disposal. Use the Bomb power-up to blast away clusters of nearby candies and create larger matches. If you find yourself in a tight spot, activate the Stopwatch power-up to freeze time, granting you unlimited moves for a limited period. Strategically planning your moves while taking advantage of these power-ups can lead to spectacular combos and high scores.

As you progress through the levels, you'll earn coins by successfully clearing candy matches. These coins can be spent to unlock even more exhilarating levels, each more challenging than the last. With varying degrees of difficulty, Bubble Shooter Candy Wheel Level Pack ensures that players remain engaged and entertained, with each level packed with sweet rewards and exhilarating gameplay. Get ready to spin, aim, and conquer the candy wheel!

How to play free Bubble Shooter Candy Wheel Level Pack game online

To play Bubble Shooter Candy Wheel, launch the game and use your mouse to aim at the candy bubbles on the wheel. Click to shoot your candy bubble, aiming to match three or more of the same color. Utilize power-ups strategically and clear levels to earn coins for new challenges!
