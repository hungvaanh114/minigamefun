Sprunki Crosser

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

A simple hop-and-dodge game where you help Oren, Raddy, or Clukr cross endless roads and obstacles! Core Gameplay Tap to move forward Move left and right to dodge obstacles Cross roads while avoiding traffic Collect coins along the way Try to beat your high score! Features Three playable characters: Oren, Raddy, and Clukr Simple shop system to unlock characters using collected coins Classic endless arcade gameplay Basic scoring system: +1 point per successful forward move Shop Spend collected coins to unlock new characters Characters available: Oren (Starting character) Raddy (Unlockable) Clukr (Unlockable)

Instructions

swipe to move
