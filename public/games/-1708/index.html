Save Snowman?Save Snowman

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the heartwarming adventure of Save Snowman, where you ll embark on an exciting journey through a wintry wonderland. Your mission is to navigate your charming snowman across a frosty terrain while skillfully avoiding harmful sun particles that threaten to melt him away. The goal is simple: keep your snowman safe against the relentless rays of the sun!

As you guide your snowman, you'll need to think strategically to protect him, you'll create sturdy snow walls. These barriers are crucial as they defend your snowman against the increasingly harsh sun. As the sun s intensity rises, the challenge heightens, making every move you make critical for survival. Players will soon find themselves fully engaged, competing to achieve the longest survival time while honing their drawing skills to shield their snowman effectively.

Can you outsmart the sun and keep your snowman from succumbing to the heat? The clock is ticking, and the thrill of competition makes this winter escapade more exciting! Challenge yourself and your friends, and become the ultimate savior of your snowman in this enchanting melting puzzle adventure.

How to play free Save Snowman game online

To play Save Snowman, use your mouse or touch screen to draw snow walls around your snowman. Strategically build these barriers to prevent sun particles from reaching him. The objective is to keep your snowman alive for as long as possible while continually adapting to the increasing intensity of the sun's heat.
