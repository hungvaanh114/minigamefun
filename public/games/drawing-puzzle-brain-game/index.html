Drawing Puzzle Brain Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Unleash your creativity and problem-solving skills with the Drawing Puzzle Brain Game. This engaging puzzle experience invites players to draw lines that act as bridges to guide a ball into a glass container. The challenge lies not only in achieving this goal but also in collecting all the stars scattered throughout each level. With distinct designs and progressively challenging stages, each level requires precise thinking and artistic flair to successfully complete the task.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter various obstacles and layouts that encourage you to think outside the box. Employ your artistic abilities to sketch the optimal route for the ball, ensuring it lands precisely in the glass container while simultaneously gathering all the stars along the way. The satisfaction from achieving a perfect score will drive you to improve your skills and tackle even more intricate challenges.

To test your initial sketches, simply hit the Play button and observe how the ball interacts with your drawn lines. If your design doesn t yield the desired results, don t fret! Use the Refresh button to erase your drawing and start anew, exploring different strategies and solutions. The Drawing Puzzle Brain Game is not only a test of logic but also a delightful way to stimulate your brain while having fun.

How to play free Drawing Puzzle Brain Game game online

To play the Drawing Puzzle Brain Game, start by drawing a line that acts as a bridge for the ball to roll into the glass container. Aim to collect all stars on your way. Once you're satisfied with your drawing, press the Play button to see the result. Use the Refresh button to start over if needed.
