Object Hunt

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Feeling overwhelmed by the stress of daily life and looking for a unique way to let off some steam? Dive into the exhilarating world of Object Hunt, where you can unleash your inner chaos! In this captivating game, you ll join forces with iconic characters from the Super Saiyan and Marvel universes, plus the adorable Pikachu, to wreak havoc and destruction.

Equipped with enormous hammers and sickles, you ll embark on thrilling sabotage missions, smashing everything in your path. From furniture to flowers, no object is safe! As you continue your rampage, remember to collect precious gold coins scattered throughout the game. These coins are your ticket to unlocking even more outrageous weapons like lollipops and sharks, allowing you to personalize your destructive experience further.

Get ready to embrace your wild side and transform your worries into fun as you destroy the environment around you. Object Hunt isn t just an outlet for your frustration it s a colorful adventure filled with laughter, creativity, and a hint of mischief. Are you prepared to become the ultimate king of destruction? Fire up the game and let the chaos commence!

How to play free Object Hunt game online

To start playing Object Hunt, simply choose your character and weapon. Use your mouse or keyboard controls to move around the environment and aim your attacks. Destroy as many objects as you can while collecting gold coins to unlock new weapons and upgrades. Embrace the fun and freedom of chaotic gameplay!
