Sandwich Runner 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Sandwich Runner 2, where culinary fun meets a thrilling challenge! In this delightful game, your mission is to gather a variety of tasty ingredients as you dash towards your target. The diner is eagerly awaiting a delicious meal, and it's up to you to prepare the ultimate feast!

On your journey, you will encounter an assortment of mouthwatering items such as juicy steaks, succulent chicken thighs, flavorful fried eggs, and even exotic sushi. However, as you race to collect these delectable foods, you must remain vigilant. There are pitfalls along the way, including undesirable items like moldy food and rotten ingredients that can spoil your hard work and leave the diner feeling unwell.

In addition to the challenge of avoiding bad food, you'll have to watch out for red peppers! These spicy additions can ignite a fiery reaction in the diner, adding an extra layer of excitement to your quest. Keep the diner happy by successfully avoiding the bad food while collecting as many delicious ingredients as possible. With quick reflexes and strategic moves, can you reach the goal and complete each level? Good luck on this ingredient-gathering adventure!

How to play free Sandwich Runner 2 game online

To play Sandwich Runner 2, simply use your arrow keys or touch controls to navigate your character through the level. Collect the good food items while dodging harmful ones. Aim for higher scores by gathering as many ingredients as possible before reaching the finish line!
