Design My Home

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Have you ever fantasized about creating and decorating your own dream home just the way you envision it? With My Home, that dream is now a reality! Dive into a world where your unique decorating skills can shine alongside millions of fellow decorators all for FREE! This game offers the perfect platform for you to unleash your creativity and become a home makeover and interior design expert.

Immerse yourself in a touching storyline as you encounter a variety of realistic characters throughout your journey. From charming living rooms to opulent bedrooms, small condos to extravagant mansions, and even quaint home shops to your personalized fashion showrooms each setting is yours to transform. Start your adventure in home decoration and turn your dreams of designing the perfect home into an exciting reality!

How to play free Design My Home game online

To play My Home online, simply enter the game and select a space you want to decorate. Use the available tools to choose furniture and decor that match your style. Arrange each item to create your ideal layout, and engage with characters to progress through the heartfelt story. Enjoy the design process and showcase your incredible taste!
