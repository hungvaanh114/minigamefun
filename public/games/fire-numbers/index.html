Fire Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fire Numbers is an engaging shooting puzzle game that challenges players to think strategically while aiming and firing. In this exciting adventure, you take control of a shooter and are tasked with annihilating blocks that display varying numbers. Each number denotes the number of shots required to completely destroy that particular block. Therefore, your mission begins by identifying blocks with smaller numbers, as they are easier targets.

As you progress through the levels, creating a clear path for your shooter is crucial. This movement allows you to effectively collect points while strategizing your shots against the blocks. The game offers various power-ups and collectibles which can enhance your shooting capabilities such as upgraded bullets and additional strengths that can significantly increase your performance. The more blocks you destroy, the higher your score climbs, pushing you to achieve the ultimate high score.

With its vibrant graphics and intuitive controls, Fire Numbers promises players hours of addictive fun. Hone your skills and sharpen your aim to tackle tougher blocks and increase your points. Compete against friends or track your progress to see who can obtain the highest total score. It s not just about shooting; it s about smart gameplay and strategic choices! Get ready to experience this gripping shooting puzzle challenge.

How to play free Fire Numbers game online

To play Fire Numbers, simply aim your shooter at the blocks and fire accordingly based on their numbers. Shoot the blocks the required number of times to destroy them and collect points. Use movement wisely to navigate the level and grab power-ups that enhance your shooting abilities!
