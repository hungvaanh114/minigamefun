Fashion Girl Reunion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fashion Girl Reunion is an exciting online game designed for kids, where players join stunning fashionistas Jade and Adella on a special day. As they prepare to celebrate their graduation, the girls venture out to a gorgeous venue within their college campus. This momentous occasion calls for a spectacular outfit, and that's where you come in!

In this engaging game, you will assist Jade and Adella in selecting the perfect outfits that showcase their unique styles. You ll explore a variety of fashionable dresses, trendy shoes, and trendy accessories that will take their looks to the next level. Not only will you help them dress up beautifully, but you will also capture delightful moments by taking fantastic photographs of the duo, preserving their memories forever.

Enjoy a playful atmosphere filled with creativity and style as you dive into this fashion adventure. Your task is to mix and match clothing items and create the ultimate looks that radiate fun and friendship. The vibrant colors and stylish designs will keep you captivated as you unleash your inner fashion designer. Don't miss out on the chance to make their graduation party unforgettable with your styling skills!

How to play free Fashion Girl Reunion game online

To play Fashion Girl Reunion, start by selecting a girl to dress up. Browse through an array of chic outfits, shoes, and accessories. Click on the items to see how they look. Once you are satisfied with your choices, click on the camera button to take pictures of Jade and Adella! Enjoy the creative process!
