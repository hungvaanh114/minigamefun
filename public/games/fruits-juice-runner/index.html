Fruits Juice Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Collecting all fruits and coins while moving glass gives you more excitement of game play. it is very easy level of play and more fun! 1. Please move glass left and right with finger touch on screen 2. Collect same color fruits and avoid different color fruits 3. If the size of the glass as its begin them level is fail 4. If you collect the more number of same color fruits then you can share the juice to people at level end.
