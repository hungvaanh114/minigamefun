Xmas Balls

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to spread some holiday cheer with Xmas Balls! In this festive game, your mission is to carefully aim and shoot at the cascading gift blocks to break them apart. Each successful hit not only clears the blocks but also rewards you with extra balls, enabling you to tackle even more blocks as you strive to save the gifts. However, be prepared for a challenge those blocks will start to tumble down at an ever-increasing speed as the levels progress. You'll need to harness your quick reflexes and impeccable timing to keep up with the accelerating pace!

The game is not just about speed; it s also about precision. As you continue to demolish blocks, your score will rise, reflecting your growing skills and performance. Keep an eye out for special blocks amidst the chaos they contain valuable power-ups that can give you a significant advantage in your gameplay. Each power-up is unique, offering various benefits that can help you clear more blocks faster or gain extra bonus points. The more blocks you eliminate, the better your chances to help Santa maintain the joy and spirit of the holiday season!

How to play free Xmas Balls game online

To play Xmas Balls, simply click or tap to aim your cannon and release to shoot at the falling gift blocks. Focus on hitting as many blocks as possible to earn extra balls. Watch for special blocks to utilize power-ups effectively. Enjoy the game and keep the holiday spirit alive!
