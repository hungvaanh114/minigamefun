Cookie Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing the all-new Cookie Rush game, a delightful and thrilling puzzle experience that will satisfy your sweet tooth! In this innovative game, players are challenged to swap colorful candies and create delectable lines of three or more to trigger sensational candy blasts. The more candies you match, the more points you'll earn, making each move a strategic decision to maximize your score.

As you progress through the levels, you ll discover that matching four candies transforms them into a special candy that possesses unique powers! These special candies can help you tackle tougher challenges and give you an edge in clearing missions. Set out on various stage missions, where you must complete objectives within a limited number of moves, keeping every decision crucial and exciting. Each level introduces a fresh array of obstacles and tasks, ensuring that no two games are the same!

With vibrant graphics, intuitive gameplay, and an array of missions to conquer, Cookie Rush provides an engaging atmosphere that will keep you entertained for hours. Dive into this candy-coated adventure and see how many levels you can conquer while collecting sweet rewards along the way!

How to play free Cookie Rush game online

To play Cookie Rush, start by selecting two adjacent candies to swap them. Your goal is to match three or more candies of the same color in a row or column. As you match and clear candies, keep an eye out for opportunities to form special candies and complete mission objectives within the given number of moves. Have fun strategizing your moves!
