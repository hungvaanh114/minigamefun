How to play free Tic Tac Toe Mania game online

To start playing Tic Tac Toe Mania online, simply select 'Play' on the main menu. Choose between single-player or multiplayer modes, set your marker as 'X' or 'O,' and take turns marking the grid. The first player to line up three symbols in a row wins the game.
