Granny Chapter2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for a spine-chilling adventure in Granny Chapter 2, where dark whispers spread through a quiet town. The terrifying tale of an old woman who kidnaps children at night and locks them in an eerie, abandoned house keeps everyone on edge. More horrifying than a slasher and crueler than evil clowns, Granny is the embodiment of fear. One fateful day, a courageous little girl named Dorothy finds herself kidnapped and trapped within the grasp of this malevolent force. Will you assist her in defeating Granny and escape the terrifying house without being caught? Experience the heart-pounding thrill by playing this chilling game! It s a high-stakes game of hide-and-seek!

The game offers thrilling various modes to enhance the excitement of your escape. In Escape Mode, you can team up with friends as Dorothy, working together to collect hidden hints and free trapped companions from Granny s clutches. If you run into Granny, distract her by throwing stones to slow her down. Alternatively, embrace the role of Granny and hunt down the children before they escape. With Infection Mode, every encounter is spooky when caught, your role switches, creating a tense chase as the game intensifies. For those who prefer cooperative gameplay, Story Mode lets you choose from six unique roles to confront Granny alongside your team. Will you be the Bomber, Thrower, Trapper, Puppeteer, Beater, or Healer to ensure your collective survival?

Granny Chapter 2 features heart-stopping survival horror elements, including creepy sounds and imposing maniacs lurking around every corner. Customize your character and dive into a multiplayer experience where teamwork is essential. Test your skills, rack up rewards, and see if you can outscore your friends!

How to play free Granny Chapter 2 game online

To play Granny Chapter 2, start by selecting your desired game mode from the main menu. Choose your character class, communicate with teammates, and strategize your escape. Follow the objectives on-screen, gather hints, avoid being caught by Granny, and work together to rescue your friends. Stay alert and enjoy the thrill!
