How to play free CHECKERS - Dames game online

To play CHECKERS - Dames, start by selecting your color black or red. Take turns moving your pieces diagonally across the board. Strike your opponent by jumping over their pieces, and remember, if you reach the opponent's back row, you can crown your piece, granting it enhanced abilities. Aim to capture all your opponent's pieces!
