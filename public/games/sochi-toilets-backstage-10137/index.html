Sochi Toilets Backstage

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Sochi Toilets: Backstage, an uproarious parody management game that puts you in charge of one of the most essential but often overlooked services during a sporting event. Set against the backdrop of the bustling Sochi Olympics, your task is to expertly manage a flurry of impatient patrons needing urgent restroom relief. Throughout the game, you ll be racing against time, balancing the needs of your clients while maintaining a clean and organized space.

In each of the sixty levels, you ll encounter an increasing tide of clients who are under pressure. Your responsibilities include assigning them a toilet stall quickly, presenting them with an ample supply of toilet paper, and ensuring they pay before they exit. But that s not all keeping the restrooms spotless is crucial. You ll need to juggle the demands of the customers while managing the aftermath of some rather messy situations. Ignore the unpleasant odor at your own risk, as it could impact your service quality and customer satisfaction.

Strategize your approach to deal with the growing crowds, enhance your abilities as a restroom manager, and unlock upgrades to improve efficiency. Can you handle the challenge and keep your reputation intact as the number of clients skyrockets? Dive in for a hilarious and chaotic gaming experience that will keep you entertained!

How to play free Sochi Toilets Backstage game online

To play Sochi Toilets: Backstage, use your mouse or touchscreen to manage clients coming in. Quickly assign them to a stall, provide toilet paper, collect payment, and clean up after them. Keep an eye on the toilet line and maintain cleanliness to ensure customer satisfaction and progress through the increasingly challenging levels.
