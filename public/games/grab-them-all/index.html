Grab Them All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Grab Them All invites players into an exhilarating world where strategy and excitement collide as you take down robbers by ensnaring them in clever traps! Navigate through various levels filled with unique designs and challenges that are sure to keep you engaged. Your primary goal is to eliminate all the robbers that roam the arena, and every corner hides a new opportunity to showcase your skills.

What sets Grab Them All apart is its simple yet captivating drag mechanics that allow you to control the Hand character with fluid 3D movement. As you play, you will discover an array of traps and destructive tools that can be unlocked with each level, ramping up the challenge and ensuring a fresh experience every time you jump into the game. Prepare yourself for a seamless blend of strategy and action as you devise the perfect plan to capture your enemies and transport them to the designated machines.

This game is suitable for players of all ages, making it the ideal choice for family fun or a competitive match with friends. With a minimum of 30 minutes of gameplay per session, Grab Them All guarantees a fantastic adventure filled with endless entertainment and excitement. So gather your wits and ready your traps; it s time to grab those robbers!

How to play free Grab Them All game online

To play Grab Them All, simply use your mouse or touchpad to drag the Hand character around the arena. Position it strategically to trap the robbers and guide them into the machines. Unlock tools and traps as you progress through various levels, ensuring each capture is thrilling and fun!
