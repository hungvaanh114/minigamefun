Kitty Chase

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Kitty Chase, a captivating HTML5 arcade game that puts you in the paws of an adorable kitty on an exciting adventure! Your mission is simple yet thrilling: guide the kitty through various stages while dodging the playful but pesky dog. As you navigate the colorful landscapes, your agility will be put to the test as you tap the screen to jump over obstacles and avoid capture. Each jump brings you one step closer to freedom!

As you dash through each level, keep your eyes peeled for delightful candy scattered throughout the stage. Collecting these sweet treats not only boosts your score but also adds an element of fun to your escapade. The more candy you gather, the higher your score climbs, challenging you to improve your skills with every round. With its vibrant graphics and simple controls, Kitty Chase provides endless entertainment for players of all ages, ensuring that you will return time and time again to beat your high score!

Get ready to immerse yourself in this enchanting world where you'll face obstacles and friendly challenges. The thrill of the chase is on as you leap through the air, aiming for candy and freedom while evading the dog s playful pursuit. Will you help the kitty escape and collect all the candy? Jump in and find out!

How to play free Kitty Chase game online

Once you re in the game, simply tap the screen to make the kitty jump and avoid the approaching dog. Your objective is to maneuver through the levels while collecting candy scattered around. Pay attention to the timing of your jumps to maximize your score and keep the kitty safe!
