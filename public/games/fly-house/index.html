Fly House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Have you ever envisioned a delightful house soaring through the skies? Welcome to Fly House, a captivating adventure where the sky s the limit! Envision embarking on extraordinary journeys within your very own floating abode, exploring limitless horizons while navigating the clouds. Your mission? To steer clear of various obstacles that threaten your charming residence and keep the journey alive.

In Fly House, your agility and quick thinking will be put to the test as you dodge pitfalls that could bring your airborne adventure to a halt. With every twist and turn in the sky, the excitement escalates! You ll need to channel your inner pilot, maintaining control of your flying house and ensuring it remains safe from impending dangers. Can you keep your home aloft and your adventure ongoing? The thrill of the game lies in your ability to overcome every challenge that comes your way.

Don t forget to check your scores at the end of each round! The scoreboard not only reflects your skill level but also encourages you to beat your previous records. Compete against yourself and challenge friends to see who can maintain a successful journey the longest. Are you ready to take to the skies in Fly House?

How to play free Fly House game online

To play Fly House, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver your flying home. Avoid obstacles by shifting left and right, and keep your house afloat. Remember to stay aware of your surroundings and keep an eye on the scoreboard to track your progress. Enjoy your adventure!
