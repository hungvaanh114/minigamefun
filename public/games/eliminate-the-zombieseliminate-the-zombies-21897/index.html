Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrill of intense action with "Eliminate the Zombies." Your mission is to assist a courageous hunter in exterminating the vile zombie remnants that have taken refuge behind sturdy metal beams and solid walls. Armed with an arsenal of specialized weapons and an abundance of ammunition, the hunter is ready to take on these ghastly foes. Utilize your sharpshooting skills to bounce shots off various obstacles to eliminate the zombies lurking in spots that might seem unreachable.

Strategize and make clever use of your environment; heavy beams hang precariously above, just waiting for the right moment to come crashing down. With a well-aimed shot at the chain attached to these imposing structures, you can send them crashing down onto the unsuspecting monsters below. Remember, in this riveting contest of good versus evil, every weapon in your inventory is a valuable asset in your quest to eradicate the repugnant, stinking zombies.

Whether you re launching a barrage of bullets or leveraging gravity to your advantage, your task is to obliterate every last trace of the zombie menace. Don t underestimate the power of tactics and swift reflexes as you navigate through each level, hunting down the creatures that threaten peace and humanity.

How to play free Eliminate the Zombies game online

In the game, you'll use your mouse to aim and fire at the zombies. Click to shoot your weapons and strategically bounce shots off surfaces to hit targets in hard-to-reach places. Aim to drop heavy beams by shooting their chains, and eliminate all zombies to progress through the levels.
