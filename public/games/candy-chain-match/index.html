Candy Chain Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Candy Chain Match, an exciting puzzle game that challenges your matching skills! In this colorful, sweet-themed world, you will connect similar items by tapping them with your touch device or clicking with your mouse. Your goal is to form groups of three or more adjacent items, whether they are aligned horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. With each successful match, you'll remove the pesky nets that trap the blocks and hinder your progress.

As you dive deeper into the game, you'll encounter a total of 36 levels, each more challenging than the last. To complete each level, focus on clearing all the blocks ensnared within the nets. Challenge yourself to think strategically, as planning your moves will help you achieve more significant combos and earn higher scores. The vibrant graphics and charming animations will keep you engaged as you progress through the challenges.

Can you master each level and free all the blocks from the nets? Gather your friends and family for friendly competition, or enjoy the game solo as you test your skills and concentration. With each solved puzzle, you ll move closer to becoming a Candy Chain Match master!

How to play free Candy Chain Match game online

To play the Candy Chain Match game, simply click or tap on similar candies that are adjacent to each other. Form groups of three or more to clear them from the board and release the trapped blocks. Keep matching until all blocks are freed to complete the level. Enjoy the challenge!
