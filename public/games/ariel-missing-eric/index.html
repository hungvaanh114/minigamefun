ARIEL MISSING ERIC

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the enchanting world of "Ariel Missing Eric," the beloved couple Eric and Ariel find themselves facing a bittersweet moment. As fate would have it, Eric is departing for another country to pursue his studies, leaving Ariel feeling heartbroken and alone. Understanding her sadness, Ariel's loyal friends, Rapunzel and Cinderella, rally to her side with a mission: to lift her spirits and rekindle her joy.

Together, they devise a delightful plan called "Mermaid Cheer Up," which promises a whirlwind of fun and laughter. The trio embarks on a fabulous shopping spree, where they explore charming boutiques filled with fabulous outfits that will make Ariel sparkle once more. They spend hours dressing up, trying on stunning outfits, and finding the perfect ensemble to boost Ariel's confidence.

The evening culminates in a vibrant dance party, where the friends can let loose, move to the rhythm, and celebrate their friendship. With music filling the air and laughter echoing around them, Ariel begins to feel the weight of her sadness lift as she dances the night away. It s a tale of friendship, love, and the magic of camaraderie that helps Ariel realize that distance can t diminish the bond she shares with Eric or with her dear friends.

How to play free ARIEL MISSING ERIC game online

To play the game, start by navigating through the magical scenes where Ariel and her friends are preparing for their fun night out. Use your mouse or finger to help Ariel and her companions select outfits, accessories, and hairstyles. Follow prompts to complete mini-games and earn points as you guide them through their shopping adventure and dance party. Enjoy the colorful adventure and help Ariel feel loved and cheerful!
