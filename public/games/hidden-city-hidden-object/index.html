Hidden City: Hidden Object

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an enthralling quest in Hidden City : Hidden Object, where mystery and adventure await around every corner! This mobile game takes you deep into enchanting and enigmatic locations filled with hidden treasures just waiting to be discovered. Your mission is to search through various beautifully crafted scenes to find the cleverly concealed items that will help you unravel the secrets of the city.

As you navigate through intriguing landscapes, you'll encounter an array of stimulating brain puzzles and riddles designed to challenge your intellect. Each level presents new clues and notes that must be pieced together to advance your detective story. The vibrant characters you meet will share their captivating tales, bringing the hidden city to life as you uncover its mysteries.

In addition to the captivating storylines, you can collaborate with friends to confront the dark forces lurking within the city. Teaming up makes the experience even more enjoyable and allows for shared victories against the challenge ahead. Keep an eye out for regular free updates that continually introduce exciting new content and challenges, ensuring that your adventure never ends!

How to play free Hidden City: Hidden Object game online

To start playing Hidden City: Hidden Object, simply download the game onto your device and create an account. Once inside, navigate each scene using your finger to find hidden objects and complete engaging puzzles. Tap on items as you discover them to collect points and unveil the enchanting story being told!
