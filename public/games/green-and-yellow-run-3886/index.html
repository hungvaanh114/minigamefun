Green and Yellow Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for a thrilling journey in the vibrant world of the Green and Yellow Run! Join the adventure with your charming chubby heroes, who are always on the move. Your mission is to assist these delightful characters in navigating their endless run. With only the power to jump, it s your responsibility to control their leaps as they dash through the landscapes.

As you guide your green and yellow companions, be on high alert! Hidden around every corner are ferocious monsters eager to gobble you up. Your main goal is to jump not just for fun but to crush these enemies that threaten your quest. Use your agility to leap above them and smash down to eliminate the foes in your path. Remember, with every victory over a monster, you'll earn essential rewards that will aid you in your journey!

Gather as much gold as you can and discover chests filled with treasures. But beware, if you miss out on collecting enough gold and treasures, the mysterious door that leads to the next level will remain locked. The stakes are high, and every second counts in this action-packed adventure. Are you ready to jump, crush, and conquer the challenges that await? Let the Green and Yellow Run begin!

How to play free Green and Yellow Run game online

In the game, use your keyboard or touch controls to make your characters jump as they run continuously. Be quick to avoid monsters while collecting gold and treasure chests. Aim for precision to crush the enemies and ensure you collect enough items to unlock the next level!
