Cake Shop Cool Summer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Cake Shop Cool Summer, where creativity and flavor collide! Meet Sofia, your friendly dessert chef, who runs an enchanting cake shop that's bursting with sweet delights. As the summer sun blazes down, demand for refreshing treats has skyrocketed, and Sofia is eager to enhance her menu with exciting, chilled creations.

Join Sofia as she experiments with two sensational ice cream flavors designed to cool off the hottest of days! With a passion for dessert-making and a talent for bringing new ideas to life, she is on a mission to concoct ice cream that will surprise and please her customers. To aid her in this culinary adventure, Sofia invites her adventurous friend Zoe for a taste-testing session. Together, they embark on a fun and creative journey, crafting delicious icy treats that will make anyone's heart flutter.

The cooking process is straightforward and enjoyable. Sofia s kitchen is equipped with everything you need to make ice cream from scratch. Are you ready to don your chef's hat and join Sofia? Dive into the fun, indulge in sugary creativity, and see if Zoe falls in love with these summer delights!

How to play free Cake Shop Cool Summer game online

In the game, you will assist Sofia in preparing her ice cream creations. Follow the on-screen instructions to gather ingredients, mix them together, and freeze your concoctions. Use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through the steps. Make sure to present your ice cream beautifully to impress Zoe and customers!
