Tribal Wars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the intense world of Tribal Wars, a thrilling adventure game that invites players to join forces with powerful dinosaurs for epic battles. In this captivating setting, you ll embark on a journey where teamwork is essential, as you strategically assemble your army of warriors and prehistoric beasts to dominate your opponents.

Victory in Tribal Wars brings rewards in the form of gold coins, which serve as your currency for growth and expansion. With these coins, you can purchase both advanced fighters and fierce dinosaurs, enhancing your combat capabilities. Combine characters of the same level to create more formidable warriors, or synthesize dinosaurs to elevate their strength. The right combination can significantly boost your army's effectiveness, making it crucial to strategize before engaging in battle.

Before each skirmish, players have the opportunity to arrange their lineup, selecting which warriors and dinosaurs to deploy. This pre-battle preparation adds a layer of strategy, ensuring you're ready to tackle any enemy. As you dive deeper into the game, you'll uncover various tactics and formations to outsmart your rivals, leading to greater rewards and recognition.

How to play free Tribal Wars game online

To start playing Tribal Wars, simply create your warrior and select your dinosaur companion. Engage in battles against other players by arranging your units for combat. Use your earned gold coins to recruit and upgrade fighters and dinosaurs, setting the stage for epic confrontations. Have fun devising clever strategies and dominating the battlefield!
