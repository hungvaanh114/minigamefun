Fruit Names

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fruit Names is an engaging educational game designed specifically for children, where they can immerse themselves in the vibrant world of different fruits. Using your cell phone, tablet, or computer, this delightful game provides a fun and interactive way to learn the names and appearances of various fruits. With its colorful graphics and intuitive interface, Fruit Names makes learning enjoyable and accessible for kids of all ages.

As an excellent educational resource, Fruit Names encourages children to develop their vocabulary while enhancing their cognitive skills. The game combines playful elements with learning, turning what could be a mundane task into an exciting adventure. Together with Nau.kids, the developers behind this innovative concept, kids can explore the delicious diversity of fruits in a safe and nurturing digital environment.

In Fruit Names, children can discover everything from common fruits like apples and bananas to exotic ones such as dragon fruit and star fruit. The game features various levels and challenges, ensuring that players remain engaged while enhancing their knowledge about fruits from around the globe. With the opportunity to play at their own pace, each child can maximize their learning experience while having lots of fun. Learning is truly an adventure with Fruit Names!

How to play free Fruit Names game online

To play the Fruit Names game, simply select a fruit on the screen and listen as its name is pronounced. Match each fruit with its corresponding name by tapping or clicking on the correct option. Keep an eye on the timer for added excitement, and try to score high by completing challenges quickly!
