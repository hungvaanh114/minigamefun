Protect My Dog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of safeguarding your adorable pooch in the entertaining puzzle game, Protect My Dog. In this delightful adventure, your mission is to shield your furry friend from relentless bee attacks. As you navigate through various levels, you'll need to draw defensive lines that effectively keep the pesky insects at bay.

But it doesn't stop there! As you progress, you'll encounter an array of challenges, including treacherous lava pits, sharp thorns, and other hazardous elements that threaten your dog's safety. With each of the 50 engaging levels, the difficulty ramps up, providing a stimulating experience that will test your strategic drawing skills and quick thinking.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a puzzle enthusiast, Protect My Dog offers a delightful blend of fun and challenge. Immerse yourself in colorful graphics and charming sound effects as you strive to protect your canine companion from all types of environmental dangers. Will you be able to complete all levels and ensure your dog's safety? Dive into this vibrant world and find out!

How to play free Protect My Dog game online

To play Protect My Dog, simply draw lines on the screen to create barriers between your dog and incoming bees, lava, or thorns. Use your mouse or touchscreen to sketch lines that shield your pet from harm while carefully planning your moves to navigate through each level's unique hazards.
