DoodleCube.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

DoodleCube.io invites players to indulge in their creativity in this engaging building game that draws inspiration from the popular skribbl.io. In this vibrant world, you have the freedom to construct various creations based on a designated theme, encouraging your imagination to run free as you embark on unique building projects. Let the world see your artistic flair!

As you dive into the game, you ll have the opportunity to purchase additional building blocks by simply pressing B or clicking the icon located in the bottom left corner of the screen. This allows you to expand your construction capabilities and take your projects to the next level. Once you feel your masterpiece is ready, it s time to showcase your creations to the other players.

The competitive spirit comes alive as everyone reveals their builds, and you re then invited to cast your votes. Remember to be strategic; voting too harshly with too many Poops could backfire! The goal is to accumulate as many positive votes as possible, as they determine your ranking in the game. Engage with your opponents and appreciate their creativity while aiming to secure your title as the best doodler. May the most inventive builder triumph!

How to play free DoodleCube.io game online

To begin, enter a game lobby and wait for the theme to be announced. Use your mouse to select and place blocks to build according to the theme. Once the building phase ends, vote on other players creations while avoiding excessive negative votes. Work together to nourish a fun and creative community!


Instructions
