Bull Shooting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Bull Shooting, where players are immersed in a high-stakes battle against rampaging bulls in the heart of a city. In this captivating bullfighting game, you take on the role of a sharp-shooting sniper, tasked with protecting the streets from the chaos unleashed by these wild creatures. Your mission is straightforward yet exhilarating: aim precisely and take down each bull to restore peace and safety to the town.

Equipped with cutting-edge sniper rifles, you'll face countless adrenaline-pumping moments as you navigate through urban environments filled with angry bulls. Each bull presents its own unique challenge, making every shot count. Quick thinking and sharp reflexes are vital as the bulls charge towards you, creating an atmosphere of intense action that keeps you on the edge of your seat.

Bull Shooting not only tests your shooting skills but also plunges you into an immersive experience where every encounter demands focus and precision. Work your way through various levels, unlocking upgrades and enhancing your weaponry as you progress. The relentless action, combined with stunning graphics and fluid gameplay, ensures that you'll be hooked for hours.

How to play free Bull Shooting game online

To play the Bull Shooting game online, simply use your mouse to aim and your keyboard to shoot. Focus on the charging bulls and take your time to ensure precision. As you advance through levels, remember to adjust your strategies and upgrade your gear to tackle tougher challenges ahead!


Instructions

WASD - Move Shift - Run Mouse - Shoot
