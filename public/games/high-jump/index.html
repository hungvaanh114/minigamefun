High Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of High Jump, where precision and timing are your best allies! In this engaging game, our protagonist finds themselves on a platform at a unique location, ready to take on the challenge of soaring to new heights. As the game unfolds, colorful plates will materialize from various directions, each gliding towards the platform at distinct speeds.

Your goal is to expertly observe the movements of these plates and wait for the perfect moment when one aligns with our hero's position. At just the right time, tap the screen to make the hero jump off the platform and land on the moving block. The higher and more accurately you land, the more points you ll earn, adding to the excitement of this high-stakes challenge!

However, timing is crucial. If you miscalculate the jump or fail to react in time, our hero will collide with the moving block and plummet off the platform, costing you valuable points. This dynamic gameplay will test your reflexes and strategic thinking as you strive to achieve breathtaking jumps and high scores. With each successful leap, you not only advance in the game but also discover new challenges that keep the thrill alive. Will you become the ultimate high jumper?

How to play free High Jump game online

To play High Jump, simply launch the game and wait for the plates to move towards your character. When a plate gets close enough to your hero, tap the screen to initiate the jump. Aim for perfect timing to land on the plate for maximum points, and avoid falling by jumping at the right moment!
