Princess Castle Cleaning?Princess Castle Cleaning

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting realm of Princess Castle Cleaning, where a magnificent palace is in dire need of a thorough cleanup! Begin your adventure in the spacious living room, where dust bunnies and spider webs have taken residence. Arm yourself with brushes and mops to eliminate every last bit of dirt, re-organizing scattered items to restore the room s elegance. Each nook and cranny of this royal retreat waits for your careful touch.

As you progress through the castle, don t forget about the other rooms. Use your trusty vacuum cleaner to suck up dirt from the floors and toss all unwanted trash into the dustbin. Broken items are an eyesore in this magnificent setting, so take the time to repair and fix everything that s damaged, transforming the castle into a sparkling abode fit for a princess.

Once the rooms are spotless, it s time to tackle the kitchen, where chaos reigns with fire and smoke everywhere! Grab the fire extinguisher and sweep away the flames to ensure the safety of this royal domain. Remove all burnt remnants and dispose of them properly, and clean up the remaining mess to leave the kitchen spotless. Your cleaning skills will turn this castle from a disaster zone into a dazzling palace, ready for royal celebrations!

How to play free Princess Castle Cleaning game online

To play the Princess Castle Cleaning game, use your mouse to navigate through each room, selecting cleaning tools as needed. Click on dirt and clutter to clean them up, and follow prompts to fix broken items and extinguish fires. Complete tasks to unlock new areas and restore the princess's home!
