Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Turn Based is an exciting 3D strategy game that challenges players to master the delicate balance of offense and defense in intense tank battles. In this unique game, players take command of their armored vehicles in a battlefield setting, where strategic planning is crucial to preserve their forces. The main objective is to outmaneuver and outsmart the enemy, minimizing the number of tanks lost while maximizing your impact on the battlefield.

Players must employ tactical thinking as they navigate through various terrains, making crucial decisions about when to attack and when to hold back. Each turn represents a strategic opportunity; do you advance aggressively to take out enemy units, or do you fortify your position to safeguard your remaining tanks? The game also incorporates elements of repair and resource management, requiring players to constantly monitor their tanks' health and deploy repairs to keep them operational for the next round.

As players progress through different levels, they will face increasingly formidable opponents, each presenting new challenges that test their strategic skills. Will you be able to save most of your tanks from destruction while delivering a powerful offensive? Turn Based promises a thrilling experience as you immerse yourself in tank warfare, skillfully managing your units and devising the perfect strategy for victory.

How to play free Turn Based game online

To play Turn Based, start by selecting your tanks and positioning them strategically on the battlefield. During your turn, choose actions like moving, attacking, or repairing your tanks. Plan ahead, anticipate your opponent's moves, and ensure you keep your tanks in good health to dominate the game. Good luck!
