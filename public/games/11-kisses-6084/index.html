In the enchanting world of 11 Kisses, an Angel-Girl and Demon-Boy are bound by love but torn apart by destiny. Your mission is to guide the Angel-Girl through a series of captivating terrains to reunite her with her beloved Demon-Boy. With each kiss, magic unfolds, and your goal is to enchant him and break the barriers that fate has set in place.

As you navigate through beautiful but challenging landscapes, you ll encounter a plethora of puzzles. These puzzles are keys to unlocking the path and for the Angel-Girl to reach her lover. Utilize various tools such as bridges and ladders to traverse obstacles, while strategically blowing up bombs that block your way. Each level presents a unique challenge that tests your problem-solving skills and your ability to think creatively.

Embark on this whimsical journey filled with vibrant visuals and immersive storytelling. With each puzzle solved and each kiss the Angel-Girl delivers, you will inch closer to reuniting this star-crossed couple. The fate of their love hangs in the balance, and only you can help them overcome the forces that attempt to keep them apart. Get ready for an adventure brimming with charm, romance, and brain-teasing puzzles!

How to play free 11 Kisses game online

To play the 11 Kisses game, use your mouse or touch controls to navigate the Angel-Girl through each level. Interact with objects in the environment to solve puzzles, and make strategic choices about using bridges or ladders. Keep an eye out for bombs to detonate, ensuring a clear path to your lover!
