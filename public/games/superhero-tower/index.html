How to play free Superhero Tower game online

To play Superhero Tower online, simply launch the game and use the directional keys or on-screen controls to navigate your superhero. Tap to jump over obstacles, collect power-ups, and defeat enemies. Strategically plan your moves to advance through the levels and unlock new abilities!
