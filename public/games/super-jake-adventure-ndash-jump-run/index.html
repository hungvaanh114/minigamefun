Looking for an exceptional 2D adventure game? Look no further than Super Jake's Adventure Jump & Run, the incredible new game of 2020 that you can enjoy absolutely free! Dive into the vibrant and beautiful Jungle World, which has been overrun by playful yet perilous jumpy monsters!

Join Super Jake, an extraordinary jumping Jack and leprechaun, on his quest to rescue his brothers: Bino, Sboy, Ted, Bob, and Jabber. Each level is a thrilling experience where you'll leap across challenging terrains, collect delicious strawberries, and dash through exciting obstacles. As you run and jump your way through this enchanting world, don t forget to smash any monsters that try to hinder your progress!

Earn coins as you conquer each level, which can help you power up for the ultimate showdown against the boss orchestrating this chaotic invasion. Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping journey filled with fun challenges, vibrant graphics, and addictive gameplay. With its charming characters and immersive environments, Super Jake s Adventure promises hours of enjoyment as you navigate the jungle and restore peace to the land.

How to play free Super Jake Adventure Jump Run game online

To start playing Super Jake Adventure, simply control Jake by jumping with a tap or a click, allowing him to leap between platforms and dodge enemies. Collect strawberries and coins along the way while smashing the monsters to gather more points. Continue your adventure by reaching the end of each level and preparing for the boss fight!
