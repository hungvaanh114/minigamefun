How to play free Rope Puzzle Rescue game online

To play Rope Puzzle Rescue, simply click on the ropes to cut them strategically. Your goal is to free the trapped characters by creating paths and bridges for them to escape. Use your mouse to interact with ropes, and think ahead to avoid obstacles as you progress through each level.
