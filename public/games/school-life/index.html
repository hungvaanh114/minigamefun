School Life

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The journey through high school is anything but ordinary, filled with captivating moments and challenges that shape your future. As a high school student, you constantly encounter decisions that can steer your life in various directions. Will you emerge as the charismatic leader, rallying your peers, or do you prefer the life of a studious honors student, quietly excelling in academics? Each choice you make, whether to hit the disco instead of attending class or to diligently prepare for your next exam, carries significant consequences.

Your high school experience is not merely about attending lessons; it is a mixture of social dynamics, academic pressures, and fleeting moments of fun. A single decision could lead you to joyous parties or could plunge you into regret and self-doubt. Imagine the stakes: do you risk being grounded after getting kicked out, or will you seize the opportunity to transform your destiny and become a millionaire? With this game, if you feel trapped by your choices, you have the incredible chance to turn back time and relive your high school years, making alternative decisions that could lead to a radically different outcome.

Your aspirations can evolve with each playthrough as you explore the nuances of student life. Navigate friendships, academic challenges, and the ever-present temptation of partying while shaping the character and life you desire. It's all about the choices you make, so choose wisely!
