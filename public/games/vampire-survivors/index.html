Vampire Survivors

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Set in the haunting landscapes of rural Italy, Vampire Survivors immerses players in a dark and perilous world. In 2021, the malevolent Bisconte Draculo unleashed a torrent of sinister magic, plunging the region into chaos marked by famine and despair. With his wicked powers, he has tainted the land, and the locals are desperate for salvation.

In this gripping narrative, the courageous members of the Belpaese family rise to the challenge, uniting to confront the sinister forces at play. Their mission is not merely to defeat Draculo but to cleanse the land of his dark magic and restore the joy of good food to their tables. Players take on the role of these brave heroes, navigating through wave after wave of nightmarish creatures summoned by the evil count. Each battle is an exhilarating test of skill and strategy, as players must devise tactics to outsmart the relentless foes committed to maintaining Draculo s reign of terror.

As you venture deeper into the game, you'll gather powerful weapons, unlock unique abilities, and lay waste to your enemies, creating a satisfying blend of chaos and carnage. Get ready to experience an enticing mix of challenging gameplay and rich storytelling that will keep you engaged for hours on end.

How to play free Vampire Survivors game online

To play Vampire Survivors online for free, navigate through the game menu to select your character and choose the level you want to conquer. Use the mouse for movement and aim, while employing keyboard shortcuts to activate special abilities and weapons. Survive against waves of enemies and gather resources to strengthen your heroes!
