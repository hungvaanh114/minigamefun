Rhomb

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rhomb is an engaging puzzle game developed by Kek Games that offers players a tranquil experience through minimalistic design and clever challenges. The game revolves around the task of untangling a complex knot of rhombuses. Your objective is to identify the appropriate sequence in which to release each rhombus without causing any collisions with others. Every level presents its own unique setup, requiring careful observation of each line and its connections.

As you delve deeper into the levels, you ll need to exercise your problem-solving skills to determine which rhombus to tackle first. The serene atmosphere enhances your focus, making the untangling process both relaxing and satisfying. Completing a level comes with the reward of seeing the puzzle come together, as each move brings you one step closer to success.

If you ever find yourself stuck at any point during the game, there's no need for frustration. The hint feature, conveniently accessed by tapping the three dots button located above the puzzle, provides assistance to help you navigate through challenging sections. With a variety of levels to conquer, Rhomb encourages players to think critically and strategically, pushing the boundaries of traditional puzzle-solving while keeping the experience enjoyable. So, are you ready to take on the challenge and see if you can master all the levels in Rhomb? Jump in and give it your best shot!

How to play free Rhomb game online

To play Rhomb, simply tap on the rhombuses to release them in the right order. Analyze the connections between each rhombus carefully, avoiding any overlaps. If you re feeling stuck, don t hesitate to use the hint option by tapping the three dots above the game area. Enjoy the challenge!


Instructions
