How to play free Sweet Land game online

To play Sweet Land, simply connect pairs of matching tiles to clear them from the board. Use the hints provided if you're stuck, and aim to clear all tiles within the given time limit. Keep an eye out for special tiles that can help you boost your score or unlock new levels!
