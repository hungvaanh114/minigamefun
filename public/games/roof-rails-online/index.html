Roof Rails Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare to unleash your inner daredevil in Roof Rails Online, where adrenaline meets skill in an electrifying parkour adventure atop the rooftops. Picture yourself elegantly gliding from one building to another, maintaining your equilibrium on a wooden pole while collecting colorful blocks scattered across the snow-covered platform. The thrill is heightened as you navigate this frostbitten landscape, balancing your ambition to lengthen your pole while avoiding the perilous edges of the rooftop.

Your primary objective is to enhance your pole by gathering blocks, which will help you extend your reach as you traverse the snowy terrain. However, mishaps can happen venture too far and risk losing your balance! With every block you collect, you'll also have the chance to pick up sparkling gems that can be accumulated as you race towards the finish line. Your final score depends heavily on your ability to gather these gems on your journey. So, are you ready to take on the challenge and become the ultimate parkour champion on the rooftops?

How to play free Roof Rails Online game online

To play Roof Rails Online, use the arrow keys or swipe your finger on the screen to steer your character. Balance your wooden pole as you move across the rooftops. Collect blocks to extend your pole and gather gems along the way. Keep your focus and stability to reach the highest score!
