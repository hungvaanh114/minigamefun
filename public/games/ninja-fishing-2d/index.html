How to play free Ninja Fishing 2D game online

To play Ninja Fishing 2D, start by casting your fishing hook into the ocean. Use your device s tilt functions to reel in your catch. Once you've hooked a fish, swipe on the screen to slice it as it jumps out of the water. Collect as many fish as you can while avoiding hazards like dynamite!
