Ear Doctor Pro

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Ear Doctor Pro, the ultimate game where you step into the shoes of an expert otolaryngologist! Players are challenged to diagnose and treat various ear-related ailments. Every case starts with a patient who comes to you with ear pain, and it s your job to alleviate their discomfort using state-of-the-art medical tools. The patients will describe their symptoms, which adds a layer of immersive storytelling to your medical practice. You'll navigate through different fun and engaging mini-games to treat ear infections, remove wax buildup, and even fix more serious issues!

Your medical toolkit includes an otoscope, syringes, tweezers, and healing creams. Each tool has its specific use, and you'll need to apply them correctly to ensure your patients leave pain-free. As you progress, the challenges become more complex with multiple cases requiring your prompt attention. With vibrant graphics and soothing sound effects, Ear Doctor Pro delivers an interactive experience that combines education and entertainment.

Not only will you learn about ear health, but you ll also develop critical thinking skills by deciding the best treatments for each case. Patients will be grateful for your careful attention, and your reputation as the top ear doctor in town will grow!

How to play free Ear Doctor Pro game online

To start playing Ear Doctor Pro, simply select a patient who needs your expertise. Use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the medical tools. Follow on-screen instructions to diagnose the issue and perform the necessary treatments. Keep an eye on the patient s comfort level and complete tasks within the allotted time for maximum points!
