Jumper Rabbit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure with Jumper Rabbit, a delightful platformer that takes you through vibrant landscapes filled with colorful obstacles and tasty carrots. As you control your agile rabbit, your main goal is to hop and leap between platforms, gathering as many carrots as possible while avoiding pesky birds and sharp blades along the way. Each jump you make is crucial, as the correct timing and power are essential for navigating the challenging terrain.

Using simple mouse controls, you can easily adjust the power of your rabbit's jump by pressing the mouse button. The longer you hold down the button, the farther your rabbit will soar. Release it at the right moment to ensure a successful leap! With an array of platforms to jump on, you ll encounter a variety of challenges that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking. As you collect carrots, you ll earn points that add to your overall score, allowing you to compete against friends and other players to see who can grab the most carrots!

With its charming graphics and engaging gameplay, Jumper Rabbit offers a fun and entertaining experience for players of all ages. Get ready to jump into action, navigate through the challenges, and become the ultimate carrot collector!

How to play free Jumper Rabbit game online

To play the Jumper Rabbit game online, simply use your mouse. Click and hold to charge up the jump power, then release to send your rabbit flying towards the platforms. Navigate carefully, collect carrots, and steer clear of birds and blades to maximize your score!
