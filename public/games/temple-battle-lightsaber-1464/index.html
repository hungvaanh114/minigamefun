Temple Battle Lightsaber

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an epic clash in Temple Battle Lightsaber! This thrilling multiplayer experience challenges you and your friend to engage in fast-paced lightsaber combat. Choose your side will you join the noble blue team or the menacing red team? The objective is simple: rack up the most kills against your opponent to emerge victorious.

As you enter the arena, your swords are drawn, and the tension is palpable. The stakes are high with poisonous water steadily rising around you, threatening to engulf you at any moment. Make sure to heed its danger; coming into contact with this toxic liquid means instant death. Not only that, but beware of the TNT raining down from above, as these explosive traps can also end your battle prematurely. Only the most agile and strategic players will be able to navigate these hazards while defending against enemy attacks.

In this thrilling combat, survival is key. Employ your skills to evade attacks, outsmart your opponent, and find opportunities to strike without falling prey to the environmental dangers. Teamwork and quick reflexes will be crucial as you and your friend compete for supremacy within the temple's intense battleground. Are you ready to rise to the challenge and claim the title of champion?

How to play free Temple Battle Lightsaber game online

To dive into Temple Battle Lightsaber, simply select your team and prepare for battle. Use your keyboard controls to move, jump, and swing your lightsaber at the enemy. Keep an eye on the rising poisonous water and falling TNT to avoid being eliminated. Stay alert, strategize your attacks, and aim for the highest kill count to win!


Instructions

The red player moves using the WASD keys The blue player moves using the arrow keys The one who reaches 10 kills wins
