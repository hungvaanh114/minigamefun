MUZY Jigsaw Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

MUZY Jigsaw is an engaging puzzle game that invites you to piece together numerous fragments of a singular image, transforming it into a stunning visual. However, this isn't merely a puzzle; it serves as an entry point into a world brimming with intrigue. As the story unfolds, you find yourself caught in a web of mysterious occurrences that center around this enigmatic toy.

The narrative takes you deep into the heart of an abandoned industrial facility known as "Maybedi." Once thriving under the auspices of "NeuroWorld," a groundbreaking company that uniquely intertwined artificial intelligence with children's toys, this complex has now fallen into disrepair and neglect. Your essential task is not only to fit the jigsaw pieces together but also to unravel the secrets that lie behind the walls of this forsaken building. What hidden truths await your discovery? Dive into the depths of the MUZY Jigsaw world to uncover the story waiting to be revealed.

How to play free MUZY Jigsaw Puzzle game online

To play the MUZY Jigsaw Puzzle game, simply select the puzzle you wish to solve from the menu. Use the mouse or touch controls to drag and drop pieces into their correct positions. Pay attention to the image as you assemble the pieces, and don't forget to explore clues that may help you unveil the mystery behind the abandoned Maybedi complex!
