Noob: End World

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Noob: End World, a thrilling adventure that challenges you to escape from a pixelated apocalypse! In this vibrant yet perilous environment, you'll navigate through a series of intricate levels designed to test your reflexes and parkour skills. With every jump over precarious blocks, you must avoid falling into the void that surrounds you. The game encourages not just individual mastery, but also competition as you can share your high scores and achievements with friends, adding a layer of friendly rivalry to the experience.

As you traverse the treacherous landscape, you'll encounter relentless zombies that pose a significant threat to your journey. Equip your trusty pickaxe and prepare for battle defeating these undead foes will not only keep you safe, but also help you collect essential resources needed to create a portal for your escape. Engage in strategic combat while honing your jumping and dodging skills, as each level becomes progressively challenging. Can you complete all the stages and prove your worth as the ultimate Noob?

Invite your friends for some side-by-side action and challenge them to beat your time or score. Whether it s competing to reach new heights in the game or collaborating to gather materials and fend off zombies, the fun never ends in Noob: End World. Are you ready to embrace the adventure and master your parkour skills in this captivating universe?

How to play free Noob: End World game online

To play Noob: End World, use your keyboard's arrow keys for movement and jumping. Navigate through the levels, avoiding obstacles and enemies while collecting materials. Use the pickaxe to combat zombies, and gather resources needed to craft your escape portal. Complete levels quickly to top the leaderboard!
