Bug Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating and humorous adventure in Bug Run, a delightful free-to-play game where your mission is to stop pesky roaches from escaping! This addictive game challenges you to smash the roaches as they scuttle across the screen, but be careful; you must not touch the harmless ladybugs. If you accidentally crush one, you may have to start over!

Compete with friends by connecting to Facebook and tracking who has the highest score in every level. With a variety of levels to conquer, you ll encounter different types of bugs, including armored roaches and vibrant ladybugs as you progress. It s a fun game that kids of all ages, whether boys or girls, can easily enjoy. Whether you re on a quick break or looking for a longer gaming session, Bug Run offers endless entertainment.

Players have taken to the game with mixed reviews. While Joe exclaims, Great great game! Ralph, the cockroach, has a different perspective, noting, Terrible game, I did not like it! Regardless, there are more exciting levels and new bugs, like ants and rats, on their way to boost the fun factor even further!

How to play free Bug Run game online

To play the Bug Run game, simply click on the roaches to smash them before they escape. Use quick reflexes to avoid touching the ladybugs. As you advance through the levels, aim for a high score and keep an eye on your friends scores through Facebook integration. Enjoy the challenge!
