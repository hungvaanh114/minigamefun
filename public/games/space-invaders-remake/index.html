How to play free Space Invaders Remake game online

To play the Space Invaders Remake online, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your spaceship left and right. Press the spacebar to shoot lasers at the invading aliens above. Aim for the invaders and eliminate them before they reach your ship. Good luck and have fun!
