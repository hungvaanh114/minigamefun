Capture the Chickens

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ariel has a dilemma; his beloved chickens have vanished from the farm, and it's up to you to join him on an exciting quest to retrieve them. In Capture The Chickens, players embark on a thrilling adventure filled with colorful graphics and engaging gameplay. As you guide Ariel through various terrains, you will face diverse enemy traps and numerous obstacles that hinder your progress.

Throughout your journey, you'll spot scattered apples and other helpful items that will aid you in your mission. Each chicken you discover is essential to your overall score, so utilize Ariel s magical wand to collect them into your inventory. The challenge lies not only in rescuing the chickens but also in unraveling the mystery of the formidable monster responsible for their disappearance. The more chickens you capture, the higher your score will soar!

Your adventure is punctuated by fun surprises and challenging elements, ensuring that each level feels fresh and exciting. Explore the vibrant world, overcome tricky traps, and gather all of Ariel's feathered friends. Will you be able to bring them back home safely? It s time to find out!

How to play free Capture The Chickens game online

To play Capture The Chickens, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move Ariel through the farm. As you encounter a chicken, press the action button to use your magic wand and collect it. Dodge traps and gather items to maximize your score. The goal is to capture as many chickens as possible while uncovering the mystery of the monster!
