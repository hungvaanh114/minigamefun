Candy Breaker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Candy Breaker is an engaging twist on the classic brick-breaker genre that will keep players entertained for hours. In this addictive game, you control a paddle located at the bottom of the screen, moving it left and right to bounce the ball upward. Your objective is to destroy all the bricks lining the top of the screen while preventing the ball from plummeting down. Some bricks are tougher than others and may require multiple hits to burst, adding a layer of strategy to your gameplay.

The vibrant candy-themed visuals captivate players right from the start, immersing them in a colorful world where every brick they break releases joyful sounds and visuals. The game increases in difficulty as you progress, presenting new challenges and delightful power-ups that can enhance your paddle or provide extra balls to keep the action going. It s not just about quick reflexes; players also need to plan their shots and anticipate the ball's trajectory to maximize their brick-breaking efficiency.

Whether you are a seasoned gamer or a newcomer to the genre, Candy Breaker offers a perfect balance of challenge and fun. With its intuitive controls and engaging gameplay, it s easy to pick up but hard to put down, making it a must-try for anyone looking to enjoy a modern take on a beloved classic.

How to play free Candy Breaker game online

To play Candy Breaker, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to move the paddle left and right at the bottom of the screen. Aim the ball to hit the bricks above and break them. Keep an eye on the tougher bricks that require multiple hits, and don't let the ball fall below the paddle!
