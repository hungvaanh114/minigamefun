Candy Links

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Candy Links is an engaging Mahjong linking game that takes the classic tile-matching experience to a new level. In this colorful and vibrant world, your objective is to connect two identical blocks to clear them from the board. The challenge lies in the connecting lines; they can only make a maximum of two turns before reaching their destination. This unique mechanic adds a strategic twist, demanding quick thinking and sharp reflexes as you navigate the increasingly complex layouts.

As you delve into the colorful tiles, you'll need to race against the clock, making each decision count. With every match you make, the tension rises as the timer counts down. Will you be able to finish clearing the board before the time runs out? Each level presents newer challenges and distinctive arrangements of tiles, keeping players on their toes. Immerse yourself in the charming aesthetic and delightful sounds of Candy Links, and experience why it has become a beloved variant of the classic Mahjong game.

Prepare to immerse yourself in a delightful world where skill and speed come together. Candy Links is perfect for both seasoned Mahjong enthusiasts and newcomers seeking a fresh puzzle challenge. The delightful visuals and the urgent gameplay create a captivating atmosphere that keeps players coming back for more. Try your hand at Candy Links and see if you can master the art of tile linking!

How to play free Candy Links game online

To play Candy Links, start by identifying two matching tiles on the board. Click on one tile, then click on the identical tile to connect them. Remember, the connecting line can bend at only two turns. Continue matching tiles until the board is cleared, all while keeping an eye on the timer!
