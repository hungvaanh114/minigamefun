Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Doner Simulator immerses players in the mouth-watering world of food preparation, where your primary role is to construct delectable doner wraps. In this fast-paced environment, you'll have to make split-second decisions as you must carefully choose the right ingredients for each order. Be cautious, though! If you mistakenly select the wrong item, you'll have to discard it, resulting in a loss of valuable WrapMoney (WM). The challenge lies in not only assembling the wraps accurately but also maintaining efficiency as customers eagerly await their meals.

The visual appeal of the game is enhanced by vibrant graphics that showcase fresh produce and sizzling meats, creating an authentic culinary experience. With each successfully completed order, players can earn WM, enabling them to unlock exciting upgrades and new recipes. The game encourages players to think critically and develop a strategy that combines speed with accuracy. Can you handle the pressure of the lunch rush while ensuring customer satisfaction? With Doner Simulator, you ll find out!

As you progress, you'll face a variety of challenges, including unique customer preferences and time limits that heighten the excitement. It s not just about making any doner; it s about crafting the perfect wrap that meets each customer's specific tastes. Can you rise to the occasion and become the ultimate doner chef? Test your skills and relish the culinary adventure that awaits!

How to play free Doner Simulator game online

To play Doner Simulator, start by entering the game and familiarizing yourself with the order requests displayed on the screen. As ingredients appear, quickly drag and drop the correct items into the wrap. Remember, any incorrect choice results in lost WrapMoney, so stay sharp and enjoy the delicious challenge!
