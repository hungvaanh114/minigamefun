Dino Huntress

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with the Dino Huntress, where your primary goal is to assist her in collecting all the precious coins and vital eggs scattered throughout her treacherous path. Throughout your journey, you must stay alert to the lurking enemies and utilize your weapons and bonuses effectively to ensure a safe passage to the finish line.

As you navigate through the vibrant landscapes filled with danger and excitement, you can jump on certain enemies, like birds, to eliminate them from your path. However, be wary, as not every foe can be defeated through jumping particularly the formidable dinosaurs that you need to avoid at all costs! The game consists of eight thrilling levels, each designed to challenge your skills, culminating in a showdown with two giant bosses at the end. Are you prepared to join Dino Huntress and become her ultimate ally in this captivating journey?

How to play free Dino Huntress game online

To play the Dino Huntress game online, use the arrow keys to navigate your character through the levels. Press the spacebar to jump and eliminate enemies where possible. Collect coins and eggs as you progress, and don't forget to use your weapons against tougher opponents. Aim to reach the finish area safely!


Instructions

Press LEFT or RIGHT arrow to move UP arrow to jump SPACE bar to shoot On desktop you can also use WAD keys or a gamepad On mobile devices use the on-screen buttons Hold the jump button to jump higher Press jump twice to perform a double jump
