MySolar - Build Your Planets

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the role of a celestial deity in mySolar - Build your Planets and unleash your creativity as you construct your very own solar system. Navigate through the cosmos and gather valuable resources such as God Points (GP) and Mass Points (MP) to expand your universe. Engage in epic battles against rival solar systems, absorbing their resources to fortify your own.

This unique game allows you to design and sculpt a solar system from scratch. Collect asteroids to accumulate MP, which you can then use to create planets and even suns. Meanwhile, GP enables you to establish orbits and enhance them with additional slots. The possibilities are endless as each planet you create can host its own satellites, fostering a diverse and complex solar environment.

Each level immerses you in a sandbox universe where you can explore limitless opportunities. Re-enter each level repeatedly to gather more GP and MP, ensuring that your solar system continues to grow and evolve. With strategic planning and quick thinking, you ll become the most powerful god in the universe, conquering and absorbing enemy planets along the way.

How to play free mySolar - Build your Planets game online

To play mySolar - Build your Planets, use your mouse to navigate through the cosmos. Accumulate GP and MP by collecting resources and defeating enemy planets. Use the points to create new celestial bodies and enhance orbits. Keep revisiting levels to maximize your resource gathering and expand your solar system boldly!
