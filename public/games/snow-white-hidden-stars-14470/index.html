Snow White Hidden Stars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Snow White Hidden Stars, a delightful point-and-click game suitable for players of all ages. Your mission is to find all the hidden stars scattered throughout beautifully crafted scenes inspired by the beloved fairy tale of Snow White. As you embark on this adventure, you have a limited amount of time to discover each star, making your observation skills paramount.

The game poses a challenge; for every incorrect click, you will lose 20 seconds from your remaining time. This aspect adds a layer of tension and excitement as you race against the clock. With a total of 12 engaging levels to conquer, each featuring unique illustrations and hidden stars, you can test your keen eyesight and quick thinking. Upon completing the game, you have the opportunity to submit your score, allowing you to compare your performance with friends and other players, adding a competitive twist to your experience.

To enhance your gaming experience on mobile devices, it's recommended to use a touch pen or a very thin finger. This will enable you to tap accurately on the hidden stars without incident, ensuring that your game play remains smooth and enjoyable. Get ready to dive into Snow White's enchanting universe and seek out the stars that glimmer within!

How to play free Snow White Hidden Stars game online

To play the game, simply click on the stars you spot in each scene. Keep an eye on the timer, as you have a limited duration to find all the stars. Remember, incorrect clicks will cost you precious seconds, so be accurate and swift as you explore every corner of the beautifully designed levels.


Instructions

Mouse for computers and finger or touch pen for touch screen devices
