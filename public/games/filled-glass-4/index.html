Filled Glass 4

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Filled Glass 4 immerses you in a vibrant colorful world where your puzzle-solving skills are put to the test. The game features a unique playing field adorned with three glasses at the bottom, each one sporting a distinct hue. Above these glasses, a variety of colored blocks are positioned, waiting to dispense their contents. As you engage with the game, you ll discover that tapping on any given block releases a cascade of balls that match its color.

Your primary objective in Filled Glass 4 is simple yet captivating: fill each of the glasses with the corresponding colored balls. Precision and strategy are key here; you must carefully choose which block to click to ensure the right balls drop into the respective glasses. Successfully accomplishing this task will earn you points, increasing your score and providing motivation to tackle the subsequent levels.

As you progress through Filled Glass 4, the challenges become increasingly complex, increasing the excitement and keeping you engaged. Each level presents a new puzzle, with the arrangement of blocks and glasses changing. This keeps the gameplay fresh and challenging, making it enticing for both casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts alike. With its colorful graphics and satisfying gameplay, Filled Glass 4 promises an experience that will have you returning for more as you strive to master each level.

How to play free Filled Glass 4 game online

To play Filled Glass 4, simply click on any of the colored blocks above the glasses to release falling balls. Your goal is to match each glass with the corresponding color of balls. Continue to click and fill the glasses until they overflow, completing the level and moving on to the next challenge!
