Granny Halloween House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready for a thrilling and spine-chilling experience? Step into the world of Granny: Halloween House, a captivating 3D survival horror game that immerses you in a Halloween-themed nightmare. As you enter the enigmatic abode of Granny, you'll find yourself surrounded by eerie sights and unsettling sounds that create a truly immersive atmosphere.

Within the walls of Granny's home lies a web of dreadful secrets and daunting obstacles waiting to be uncovered. Your primary objective is to escape this unsettling environment, where every corner you turn could lead to unforeseen horrors. As you delve deeper into the shadowy rooms of the house, you must remain vigilant while searching for crucial clues and helpful items that will aid your escape. Each step could bring about newfound discoveries or terrifying encounters, heightening the suspense and excitement.

The gameplay combines elements of exploration, puzzle-solving, and stealth, demanding both clever thinking and quick reflexes. With each move, be wary of Granny, who is ever-watchful for intruders. Can you navigate through her chilling domain without falling prey to her clutches? The thrill of uncovering hidden passages and solving intricate puzzles makes Granny: Halloween House an unforgettable adventure for horror enthusiasts.

How to play free Granny Halloween House game online

To begin your thrilling escape, use the arrow keys or joystick to navigate Granny's house. Explore your surroundings and interact with objects by tapping the action button. Collect items and solve puzzles to open locked doors or hidden passages. Keep your ears open for Granny's movements, as staying quiet is crucial for survival!
