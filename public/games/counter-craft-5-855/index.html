Counter Craft 5

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Counter Craft 5, where you ll find yourself immersed in a vibrant and pixelated world filled with a vast array of weaponry and relentless waves of blocky zombies. Set against the renowned Dust 2 map, your mission challenges you to employ strategy and skill as you navigate the chaos of undead foes.

In this thrilling game, your primary objective is to methodically eliminate hordes of zombies using an extensive arsenal of powerful weapons. However, you won t just be shooting aimlessly; your tactics will center around strategically targeting TNT boxes that can cause massive destruction and clear the oncoming zombie waves. To succeed, you must maintain a keen focus on your weaponry, optimizing each shot to obliterate zombies efficiently and effectively.

As you progress through various levels, you ll encounter progressively challenging waves of zombies. Completing missions will allow you to unlock new weapons, enhancing your arsenal and increasing your chances of survival. The excitement of Counter Craft 5 lies not only in the mindless shooting but also in the strategic element that tests your problem-solving skills under pressure. Think critically about your surroundings, anticipate the movements of the zombies, and decide when to make your next move for ultimate success.

How to play free Counter Craft 5 game online

To play Counter Craft 5, simply enter the game and familiarize yourself with the controls. Use your mouse to aim and shoot at the zombies while targeting TNT boxes for strategic destruction. Keep an eye on your ammo and reload whenever necessary, as you aim to achieve the kill count required to unlock new weapons.


Instructions

WASD - Move LMB - Shoot RMB - Aim R - Reload G - Grenade Space - Jump
