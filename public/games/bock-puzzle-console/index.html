How to play free Bock Puzzle Console game online

To play Bock Puzzle Console, start by selecting any colored water container. Tap on it to pour its contents into another container, ensuring that only the same colors mix together. Your goal is to sort all the colors effectively into their respective containers, minimizing the number of moves. Enjoy the challenge!
