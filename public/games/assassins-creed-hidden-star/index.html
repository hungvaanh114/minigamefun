Assassins Creed Hidden Star

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Assassin's Creed Valhalla: Hidden Star is an exhilarating action role-playing game developed by Ramser Turizm, released in 2021. The game continues the beloved Assassin s Creed saga while transporting players into the rich tapestry of Viking history. Players assume the role of Eivor, a fierce Norse warrior navigating a world brimming with danger and intrigue.

Set against a backdrop of stunning landscapes and vibrant villages, the game immerses players in a story where they are prompted to unravel the mysteries of Eivor s past. The modern-day storyline centers around Layla Hassan, an Assassin from the 21st century who harnesses the power of advanced technology to experience Eivor s memories. This journey is not only an exploration of personal heritage but also a quest to find solutions to prevent Earth s impending destruction.

With a seamless blend of combat, exploration, and strategic planning, players are encouraged to engage in thrilling battles, establish alliances, and face off against formidable foes. The open-world design invites gamers to embark on epic quests, participate in dynamic encounters, and make decisions that influence the fate of not just Eivor but the world as well.

How to play free Assassins Creed Hidden Star game online

To play Assassin's Creed Hidden Star online, simply access the official game site or a trusted platform hosting the game. Once in the game, navigate through the menus to begin your adventure. Use the keyboard or controller to maneuver Eivor, engage in combat, and complete objectives while exploring the captivating environment.
