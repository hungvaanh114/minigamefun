Box vs Triangles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Box VS Triangles, where your reflexes and strategy will be put to the test! In this simple yet addictive game, your objective is to tap either the right or left side of the screen to effectively hit the falling triangles. The more triangles you break, the higher your score becomes, allowing you to showcase your skills!

As you progress, the challenge intensifies, making it increasingly difficult to shatter your previous records. To assist you in achieving those coveted high scores, you can collect special bonuses for your BOX. These bonuses grant you powerful upgrades that enhance your triangle-breaking capabilities, making each tap more effective. Can you rise to the challenge and prove that breaking an astronomical number of triangles is possible? Overcoming these obstacles might seem daunting, but with practice and quick thinking, you can master the art of triangle destruction!

The game may appear easy at first, but don t be fooled! Only the most dedicated players will be able to reach the top of the leaderboard. Prepare yourself for an exhilarating experience that combines speed, focus, and fun as you aim to break as many triangles as possible. Record your highest score and compete with friends to see who can conquer the challenge!

How to play free Box VS Triangles game online

To play Box VS Triangles, simply tap the left or right side of your screen to hit the triangles as they descend. Aim for quick taps to maximize your score and collect bonuses that will help you break even more triangles. Stay sharp and see how high you can score!
