Back Flipper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of gravity-defying stunts with Back Flipper, the ultimate casual flipping game! Dive into a world where you can showcase your flipper skills as you bounce off rooftops, soar over speeding vehicles, and perform acrobatics on moving elevators. Whether you're dodging airplanes or flipping over satellites, every level presents a new and exhilarating challenge waiting for you to conquer.

The beauty of Back Flipper lies in its simplicity and dynamism. Designed for players of all skill levels, the game allows you to focus solely on executing iconic backflips without the burden of complicated mechanics. Just tap and flip to execute stunts, earning points and unlocking new environments that take your flipping experience to soaring heights. Each successful flip brings you closer to mastering the art of backflipping and claiming the title of the best Backflipper around!

With eye-catching graphics and an engaging soundtrack, Back Flipper creates an immersive atmosphere that keeps players coming back for more. Avoid obstacles, perfect your timing, and impress your friends as you climb the leaderboard. Become a master flipper and showcase your skills as you flip in style, looking for new challenges and exciting experiences at every turn. Join the fun today, and let the flipping begin!

How to play free Back Flipper game online

To play Back Flipper, simply use your mouse or tap the screen at the right moment to execute a backflip. Time your taps to land perfectly on various surfaces, and try to achieve the highest score possible. Enjoy the thrill of flipping while exploring different stages!
