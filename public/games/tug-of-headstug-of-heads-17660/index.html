Tug of Heads is an exciting and engaging wrestling game designed for both solo and two-player experiences. With over 50 uniquely crafted levels, players are in for a delightful surprise at every corner. The primary objective of the game is to safeguard the heads of your characters from various dangers that arise during wrestling matches. As you progress, you'll notice that the game conditions dynamically shift from one level to the next, adding an extra layer of challenge and intrigue.

In Tug of Heads, you have the option to grapple against a friend in local multiplayer mode or take on the computer's AI. The game allows you to select the number of sets for each match, catering to different gaming preferences and ensuring that every session is tailored to your liking. Prepare for some action-packed moments filled with laughter, strategy, and competitive spirit as you dive into this playful wrestling world.

The colorful graphics and playful animations enhance the overall gaming experience, making Tug of Heads not just a challenge, but a fun-filled adventure with every playthrough. Whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast, you will find something to enjoy as you fight to protect your character's head in this unique wrestling showdown.

How to play free Tug of Heads game online

To play Tug of Heads, simply log into the game and choose your preferred game mode: single player or two-player. Use the on-screen controls to maneuver your character and perform moves. Focus on defending your character's head from opponents and aim to knock out your rival to win each set. Have fun!
