Rescue Dog Web is a captivating online puzzle game tailored for children, available to play for free. In this exciting game, players are tasked with helping a cute dog that has found itself trapped within a complex maze. Your goal is to navigate the dog through the maze by utilizing a unique bouncing mechanism.

To maneuver the dog, simply click and drag it in the direction you want it to go. Upon releasing your hold, the dog will bounce off the walls and obstacles in the opposite direction. This clever design encourages players to think strategically, as they must calculate the best angles to achieve a successful exit through the door. As players progress through each level, they will encounter various challenges, including a limited number of hits against the walls, increasing the difficulty and requiring careful planning.

With each level presenting a new puzzle to solve, children can develop their problem-solving skills while enjoying the entertaining concept of bouncing a dog out of confinement. The colorful graphics and engaging gameplay make Rescue Dog Web a delightful experience for kids, fostering a sense of achievement as they lead their furry friend to freedom.

How to play free Rescue Dog Web game online

To play Rescue Dog Web, simply click and hold the dog in the maze and then drag it towards the wall. Release your mouse to make the dog bounce. Aim to use the walls strategically to navigate the dog towards the door while keeping track of your allowed hits against the walls.
