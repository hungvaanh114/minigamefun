Fit Cats

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Fit Cats, where feline fun and strategic gameplay await! In this whimsical adventure, you ll find that cats are irresistibly attracted to boxes. Your challenge is to launch various cats into these boxes, utilizing their unique sizes and capabilities for optimal results. The goal? Create the largest cat possible by merging smaller ones of the same size!

As you play, you will discover that when two cats of identical dimensions touch, they seamlessly merge into a more substantial cat, adding to your collection. But there's a twist! You also have the ability to maneuver any cat out of the box using your mouse, introducing an element of strategy to your gameplay. Periodically, you'll encounter mice that present additional chances to aid your feline creations. Seize these opportunities to unlock all the adorable cats available in the game.

With charming graphics and engaging mechanics, Fit Cats promises hours of entertainment for cat enthusiasts and gamers alike. Dive into this purrfectly playful challenge and see how many cats you can collect and merge!

How to play free Fit Cats game online

To play Fit Cats, simply use your mouse to launch cats of different sizes into the box. Watch for cats of the same size; when they touch, they merge into a larger cat! Don t forget to drive any cat out of the box as needed and catch mice to gain special abilities!
