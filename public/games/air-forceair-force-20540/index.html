Air Force 2018 is an exhilarating HTML5 aircraft game that puts you in control of a powerful F117 stealth fighter. Your main objective is to navigate through enemy territories, engaging in intense aerial combat while ensuring you stay alive as long as possible. As you take to the skies, you'll have the opportunity to collect vital resources such as ammo, fuel, and hearts scattered throughout the game.
