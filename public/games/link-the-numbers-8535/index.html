Link the Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of "Link the Numbers," a captivating wooden board game designed to challenge your mind while providing a relaxing experience. Your journey begins with the number 1, as you strategically connect it to 2, then 3, and so on, forging a pathway through the beautifully crafted wooden board. The objective is simple yet engaging: link all the numbers consecutively, but beware of the twists and turns that may challenge your path.

This delightful game boasts hundreds of levels, ensuring that players of all ages can enjoy an endless array of puzzles. Each level offers just the right amount of difficulty, providing a perfect mix of challenge and relaxation without overwhelming the player. The soothing mechanics of the game allow anyone, from children to adults, to pick up and play, making it a great choice for family game nights or solo playtime.

With every new level you conquer, the joy of connecting the numbers cultivates a sense of accomplishment and tranquility. So why wait? Dive into the world of "Link the Numbers" and see how far you can go! It's as easy as 1, 2, 3 get set, go!

How to play free Link the numbers game online

To begin playing "Link the Numbers," simply start at the number 1 on the board. Click or tap on the number you wish to connect next, proceeding in ascending order by touching each number in sequence. Your goal is to create a continuous path that links all the numbers together. Enjoy the adventure!
