1 2 3 4 Player Tank Game 2D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready for the legendary tank battles? Gather all your friends and start the tank war.All tanks take their positions and the endless battle begins. The tank that destroys the most of your friends tanks wins. In this fun tank battle game, compete with your friends and be the one who destroys the most tanks. Be careful, its not about being fast, but being smart that will lead you to victory.

Instructions

Green Tank Use WASD to move and Q to fire Blue Tank Use Arrow Keys to move and P to fire Black Tank Use UHJK to move and M to fire Red Tank Use FCVB to move and N to fire
