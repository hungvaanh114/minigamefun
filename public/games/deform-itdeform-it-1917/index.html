Deform It is an innovative and engaging game that invites players to unleash their creativity by manipulating and deforming a variety of objects. Set against the cosmic backdrop of space, this unique gameplay experience offers 13 diverse ball options, each with its own distinct characteristics, allowing players to experiment and discover new ways to interact with their environment. With a total of 14 levels to conquer, players will find themselves immersed in captivating challenges that require both skill and strategy.

The game features three distinct modes, catering to different play styles and preferences. In Level Mode, players progress through a series of increasingly difficult levels, each designed to test their deformation skills and problem-solving abilities. Free Deform Mode allows players to explore their creative instincts without constraints, providing a sandbox environment where they can deform objects at will. Meanwhile, Antigravity Mode takes the challenge to new heights, introducing unique physics that will challenge even the most seasoned players.

With its combination of fun mechanics, visually stunning graphics, and a compelling space theme, Deform It is a game that promises hours of entertainment. Whether you're looking to tackle the main levels or simply enjoy the freedom of deforming objects in an open space, this game ensures there's something for everyone.

How to play free Deform It game online

To play Deform It online, simply select your desired game mode once you're in the game. Use the controls to navigate and deform the balls creatively. In Level Mode, focus on achieving the objectives of each level, while in Free Deform Mode, let your imagination run wild without any restrictions!
