Cyber City Driver

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a vibrant future setting with Cyber City Driver, where you'll navigate through a high-tech metropolis. This thrilling game offers an exhilarating experience either racing against the clock or challenging your friends in the engaging "2 PLAYER" mode. With six distinct routes to conquer, the pressure is on to complete your missions before time expires!

As you progress through the levels, new and more powerful cars become available, enhancing your gameplay and adding to the excitement. Choose from an array of sleek super sports cars and showcase your driving skills along the city streets. Need a break from racing? Switch to the "FREE DRIVING" mode, where you can explore the futuristic city at your leisure, taking in the stunning environments.

The game also allows you to demonstrate your acrobatic skills on ramps that enable you to leap onto the rooftops of buildings. Use these ramps to navigate the urban landscape creatively, showing off your driving prowess while unveiling the secrets of the Cyber City.

How to play free Cyber City Driver game online

To begin playing Cyber City Driver, launch the game and select your preferred mode: "Racing" or "FREE DRIVING." For racing, choose a route and compete against the timer or a friend. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver your vehicle, accelerate, and make sharp turns to conquer the tracks!
