Big Team

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Big Team," your mission is to bolster your team and navigate through a colorful world filled with challenges. The objective is to recruit supporters of the same color, forging a robust and cohesive unit. As you progress, your gathered supporters will help you reach the spaceship, where you can embark on a journey to another planet, symbolizing your growth and teamwork.

Your path is not without hurdles, as you will encounter various obstacles along the way. It is crucial to steer your team left and right adeptly, ensuring that you gather more allies while avoiding perils that could cause you to lose valuable members from your team. The more people you collect, the stronger your team becomes, making it essential to strategize your movement and make swift decisions.

This exciting adventure emphasizes cooperation, organization, and precision. With each successful recruitment, you will witness your team flourish, transitioning from a small group to a formidable force. The vibrant visuals and engaging gameplay will keep you entertained as you aim to ascend to new heights literally and figuratively. Will your collective effort be enough to soar into space, or will obstacles prove to be an insurmountable challenge? Join the quest and find out!

How to play free Big Team game online

To play "Big Team," use the arrow keys or swipe to move your character left or right. As you navigate the landscape, gather followers of your color while dodging obstacles. The goal is to amass as many supporters as possible to successfully reach the spaceship waiting to take you to another planet.
