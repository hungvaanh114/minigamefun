Rope Draw

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rope Draw is an engaging puzzle game that challenges your creativity and problem-solving skills. In this game, players manipulate ropes to create specific formations that match a model displayed at the top of the screen. The objective is simple yet captivating: rearrange the ropes by pushing them to recreate the exact arrangement shown above you. With each level, the difficulty increases, requiring strategic thinking and planning to ace each challenge.

There are 120 unique levels to test your abilities, catering to both novices and seasoned players alike. Should you find yourself stuck along the way, don't worry! The game provides a handy hint button that reveals the initial moves, guiding you as you navigate through the intricacies of rope positioning. This thoughtful feature ensures players do not feel frustrated, making it accessible to everyone.

One of the most exciting aspects of Rope Draw is the 'Fun' mode, which unlocks after completing level 40. This mode allows for more creativity as you can insert new ropes and manipulate them freely without the constraints of matching a pre-defined arrangement. This added feature makes the gameplay experience even more enjoyable, allowing you to explore different possibilities and test your rope-drawing skills in a more relaxed setting.

How to play free Rope Draw game online

To play Rope Draw, simply drag and push the ropes to match the arrangement shown at the top of the screen. Use the hint button to get assistance on your moves if you're stuck. Aim to successfully replicate the structure within the least amount of moves for the best score!
