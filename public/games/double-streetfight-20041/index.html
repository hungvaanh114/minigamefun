Double StreetFight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Double StreetFight, an exhilarating fighting game that offers both a multiplayer campaign and a single-player experience. In this electrifying brawler, players can team up with a friend to tackle challenging levels or engage in chaotic free-for-all matches with up to eight competitors. Each campaign features three distinct missions where players battle against enemy bots, racking up coins with every knockout.

As you progress through the game, be sure to collect coins. These coins aren't just for show; they allow you to purchase a variety of costumes, enhancing your character's look and unique abilities. Whether you're working collaboratively with a partner to complete all levels or challenging each other in one-on-one duels, Double StreetFight promises non-stop action and endless fun!

Join your friends and strategize your way through each campaign level, overcoming increasingly powerful enemies. You can also compete in a free-for-all mode where the most skilled player reigns supreme. However, remember, winning is all about using the right tactics and combos, so get ready for fierce battles!

Don't forget to connect with us on Discord! Stay updated with the latest game enhancements and report any bugs you encounter along the way. Join our community and immerse yourself in the world of Double StreetFight!

How to play free Double StreetFight game online

To play Double StreetFight online, simply join a game room with friends or match with other players. Once in the game, use the controls to move, attack, and defend. Follow the on-screen prompts to execute special moves and earn coins by defeating opponents!
