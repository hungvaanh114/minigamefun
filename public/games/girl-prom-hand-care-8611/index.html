Girl Prom Hand Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Chloe has an important day ahead of her she's set to meet her prince charming! However, there's one major issue: her hands are looking far from fabulous. After a long week filled with household chores, Chloe's hands have become dirty and rough. She needs your help to transform them in time for the big event!

In the delightful game of Girl Prom Hand Care, you will step into the shoes of a skilled beauty expert. Your mission is to provide Chloe with a complete hand makeover that will leave her feeling confident and beautiful. Start by giving her hands a thorough cleansing to remove all the dirt and grime. Once her hands are clean, you ll have the opportunity to nourish them. Apply soothing creams and scrubs to restore their soft texture. You'll be amazed at how quickly Chloe's hands go from drab to fab!

But that s not all! The ultimate touch comes with a creative nail art session. Choose from an array of colors, patterns, and designs to make Chloe's nails pop. Whether she prefers something simple or an intricate design, your artistic choices will play a vital role in her transformation. Impress her with your modern nail art skills, and see her smile light up as she admires her beautiful hands!

How to play free Girl Prom Hand Care game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse to interact with the tools provided. Start by cleaning Chloe's hands, then apply moisturizing products. After her hands are rejuvenated, select from various nail art options to create a stunning final look. Help Chloe shine at her royal appointment!
