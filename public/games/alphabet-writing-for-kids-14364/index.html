Alphabet Writing for Kids

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Alphabet Writing For Kids" is an exceptional educational application designed for toddlers, preschoolers, and kindergarten children to master the art of tracing both uppercase and lowercase letters from A to Z. This interactive app not only facilitates the learning of letter formations but also enhances speaking skills as children can listen to the phonetics of words associated with each letter.

As children engage with the app, each letter, along with its corresponding word, is pronounced clearly and audibly, ensuring an easy understanding for young learners. The enjoyable and playful context makes learning to write and read a delightful experience, all for free. With features like ABC tracing games and an extensive collection of over 60 funny cartoon stickers, children will find themselves immersed in a vibrant educational world. The app also includes an assortment of amusing sounds and music to keep the learning process lively.

In addition to focusing on the English alphabet, the app helps children learn about numbers and animals, giving them a well-rounded educational experience. It introduces both uppercase and lowercase letters in a user-friendly environment, making it accessible for all young learners. The super simple game interface ensures that kids can navigate easily, fostering independent learning and exploration.

How to play free Alphabet Writing For Kids game online

To play the game, simply select the letter you want to trace. Follow the on-screen prompts that guide you through the tracing process by tapping and dragging your finger. Listen for the pronunciation of each letter and associated word. Engage with stickers and sounds to make learning fun!
