Embark on a whimsical journey with Funny Bunny Logic, a captivating logic puzzle game designed to tickle your brain and challenge your problem-solving skills. The premise is delightful yet demanding: help our clever bunny navigate through a series of obstacles to reach its beloved carrot. As you progress, you'll encounter an array of intriguing puzzles that are sure to excite puzzle lovers of all ages.

From straightforward beginnings that ease you into the game to brain-bending challenges that will keep you on your toes, Funny Bunny Logic promises hours of engaging gameplay. Will you be able to guide the bunny through every tricky stage and secure the tasty carrot at the end?

To play Funny Bunny Logic, simply navigate through the levels by tapping or clicking on objects to manipulate the environment.
