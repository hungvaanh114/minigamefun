Chess Free - Echecs

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for a captivating duel of wits with Chess Free - Echecs, the ultimate 3D Chess experience tailored for Android devices! Dive into this classic strategy board game that challenges players to think critically and plan their moves meticulously. With a visually appealing 3D interface, this game elevates the traditional Chess experience, allowing you to immerse yourself in strategic gameplay.

Each match involves two players facing off on a checkered 8x8 board, controlling a total of 16 pieces per player. Your arsenal consists of one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. The goal is simple yet challenging: to strategically checkmate your opponent's king, placing it in a position where escape is impossible. Unleash your tactical prowess as you navigate through thrilling matches that require foresight and cunning.

Whether you are a seasoned Chess veteran or a beginner looking to sharpen your skills, Chess Free - Echecs provides a perfect platform for growth and competition. Engage in thrilling matches against friends or AI opponents, enhance your strategic gameplay, and learn from each encounter as you rise through the ranks. With its user-friendly controls and appealing graphics, you'll find yourself falling deeper into the intricate world of Chess.

How to play free Chess Free - Echecs game online

To play Chess Free - Echecs, simply select your opponent and choose a game mode. Move your pieces by tapping on them and then tapping the target square. Ensure you protect your king while aiming to checkmate your opponent. Adapt your strategy as you go, and enjoy the thrill of this classic game!
