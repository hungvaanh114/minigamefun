Instructions:

The city has fallen into chaos as hordes of zombies roam the streets, leaving destruction in their wake. In "Shooting Zombie One," you take on the critical role of a skilled marksman tasked with eliminating the undead threat. Utilize powerful missiles to blast through the relentless waves of zombies that are attempting to take over every corner of the urban landscape. Your mission is clear: protect the remaining survivors and restore peace to the city by eradicating this terrifying menace.

Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-fueled gaming experience where strategy and precision are paramount. You have access to an array of missiles, each designed to wreak havoc on the zombie swarms. With each successful shot, you'll not only preserve the lives of innocent civilians but also earn valuable points to upgrade your arsenal and improve your overall firepower. The more zombies you take down, the more rewards you can unlock, leading to even more explosive and efficient weapons at your disposal.

As the levels progress, the challenge increases, with different types of zombies that possess unique abilities to withstand your attacks. You must think quickly and adapt your strategies if you hope to survive this onslaught. Are you ready to step into the chaos and reclaim the city from the clutches of the undead? "Shooting Zombie One" is a test of your reflexes and tactical acumen can you rise to the occasion and become the city's last line of defense?

How to play free Shooting Zombie One game online

To start playing Shooting Zombie One, use your mouse to aim at the approaching zombies. Click to launch missiles and eliminate threats before they reach you. Keep an eye on your ammo and collect power-ups to enhance your shooting abilities. Aim for headshots to maximize damage and survive the zombie onslaught!
