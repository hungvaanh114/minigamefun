Flower Sorting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Flower Sorting, a delightful puzzle game that challenges you to organize vibrant flowers into separate columns. Your objective is simple yet thrilling: sort all the flowers so that similar ones are grouped together within the same column. This enticing activity not only enhances your problem-solving skills but also provides a colorful visual delight!

In Flower Sorting, you'll encounter a variety of flowers, each with its own unique hue. To achieve the perfect arrangement, you can select a flower from any column and transfer it to either an empty column or to a column that already contains a similar flower at the top. As you progress, the challenge intensifies, requiring thoughtful planning and strategic moves to find the most effective sorting solutions. The game is an excellent way to exercise your brain while enjoying a relaxing and visually appealing experience.

With progressive difficulty levels, Flower Sorting keeps players engaged and entertained for hours. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a newcomer, the game's easy-to-understand mechanics make it accessible to everyone. Dive into the world of Flower Sorting and embark on a colorful journey where logic and creativity bloom!

How to play free Flower Sorting game online

To start playing the Flower Sorting game online, simply click on the flower you wish to move. Next, select either an empty column or a column that has a flower with the same color at the top. Continue this process until all flowers are sorted into their respective columns. Have fun sorting!
