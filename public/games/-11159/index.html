Fruit Juices?Fruit Juices

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the vibrant and entertaining game Fruit Juices, you are invited to immerse yourself in the art of juice-making like never before! The game features a colorful platform teeming with a variety of fresh fruit slices eager to be transformed into delicious drinks. You'll notice sharpening pins lining the walls on either side of the platform, creating an interactive environment filled with fun challenges.

Your goal is to skillfully roll fruit pieces toward the pins with just the right amount of force, ensuring that they strike perfectly. When you hit the pins, juice will flow from the cut fruit and fill the glasses located at the bottom of the screen. Each glass will clearly indicate the type of juice you are expected to create, adding an element of strategy to your gameplay. As you fill the glasses with various juices, you ll earn points which can eventually lead to higher scores and even unlock new fruit options.

The engaging graphics and responsive controls make Fruit Juices an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. With a blend of skill, precision, and fun, this game is sure to keep you entertained as you aim to perfect your fruit-juicing skills!

How to play free Fruit Juices game online

To play Fruit Juices, simply use your mouse to aim and roll a fruit slice towards the sharpening pins. Adjust the force appropriately to ensure the fruit accurately lands on the pin, allowing the juice to flow into the corresponding glass. Make sure to fill each glass according to its designated juice type!
