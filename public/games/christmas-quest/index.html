Christmas Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Christmas Quest is an innovative match-3 puzzle game that invites players into a festive world of colorful holiday items. Your objective is simple yet engaging: shift various items into vacant spaces to form lines of three or more identical pieces, either horizontally or vertically. With each successful match, you ll earn points, but remember, the fewer moves you use, the higher your score will be!

The mechanics are intuitive; just tap on the item you wish to move, then select the spot you want it to occupy. As long as there's an unobstructed path leading to your target position, your item will glide over to its new home. This clever movement adds an extra layer of strategy to your gameplay, as planning your moves wisely can lead to massive score boosts.

But that's not all! Completing levels swiftly rewards you with time bonuses, adding even more excitement and reward to the game. With enchanting graphics and a delightful soundtrack, Christmas Quest promises to immerse you in the spirit of the season as you solve puzzles and collect points. Gather your friends and family, and see who can achieve the highest score in this captivating match-3 adventure!

How to play free Christmas Quest game online

To play Christmas Quest, simply select an item by tapping on it, then choose its destination by tapping an adjacent empty spot. Your goal is to create rows or columns of three or more matching items. Keep an eye on the number of moves for optimal scoring!
