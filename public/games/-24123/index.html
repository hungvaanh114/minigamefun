Join Baby Hazel in an enchanting adventure as she excitedly wakes up early to prepare for a delightful Tea Party with her friends! With the love and support of her mom and dad, Hazel has a myriad of tasks to complete before the guests arrive. From sending out charming invitations to ensuring everything is ready, this adorable little girl needs your help to make her Tea Party a joyful and unforgettable experience.

As you immerse yourself in this delightful game, you'll assist Hazel every step of the way. Your creativity and organizational skills will be put to the test as you help her set the table, choose beautiful decorations, and prepare delicious treats. Participate in fun mini-games that will enhance the festivities, making sure that Hazel's friends have the best time at the party. It's a heartwarming journey filled with laughter, joy, and the sweet spirit of friendship!

Don't miss out on the fun and join Baby Hazel for a celebration that is sure to be filled with delightful memories and the warmth of companionship. Are you ready to dive into this enchanting experience and help create a magical Tea Party for everyone? Get set for a day full of excitement and joy!
