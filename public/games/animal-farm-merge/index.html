How to play free Animal Farm Merge game online

To play Animal Farm Merge, simply tap on the screen to select your animals and start merging. Combine two identical animals to create a new one. Keep merging to unlock new species and eventually reach the giant Horse. Utilize the power-ups and bonuses you earn as you progress!
