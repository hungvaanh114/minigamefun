Mouse Raider is an engaging and entertaining infinite shooting game that plunges players into a chaotic scenario where a horde of hungry mice has invaded your home. These pesky little raiders are on a mission to steal your delicious treats, including a freshly baked pie and scrumptious desserts from a renowned dessert shop. Your objective is clear: you must stop the Mouse Raiders before they devour all your delectable food.

This vibrant game is designed for single-player enjoyment and requires a network connection to play. It's perfect for families and children, featuring simple tap mechanics that make it easy to pick up and enjoy. As you progress through the levels, hit the mice that appear on the screen. With each successful hit, you score points, and the challenge escalates as the mice come faster and in greater numbers. Mouse Raider is compatible with a wide range of Android devices, supporting both 32 and 64-bit systems.
