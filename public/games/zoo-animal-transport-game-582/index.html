Zoo Animal Transport Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exciting world of the Zoo Animal Transport Game, where you ll take on the role of a wild creature transporter driver. In this thrilling adventure, you ll load and transport different animals across various terrains, ensuring their safety as you navigate through bustling streets and rugged paths. This game provides a unique blend of animal care and logistics, challenging players to optimize their routes while adhering to traffic rules to keep the flow of movement uninterrupted.

As a freight transporter guiding your major-load animal truck, you ll encounter all types of wildlife. From fierce lions to playful monkeys, your task is to ensure each animal arrives at its destination safely. The animal transport truck has been designed with all the features you need for an engaging experience, from realistic physics to immersive visuals. In addition, the off-road animal transport mode challenges you to master your driving skills, maneuvering over obstacles while keeping the animals secure in your cargo.

Amidst the excitement of transporting exotic wildlife, you can also explore features reminiscent of farm animal cargo transport, enhancing your experience in this vibrant jungle setting. Whether you're passionate about animals or love driving simulations, the Zoo Animal Transport Game offers a perfect blend of both!

How to play free Zoo Animal Transport Game online

To play the Zoo Animal Transport Game, start by selecting your truck and animal load. Navigate through the levels using your steering controls while following traffic rules, ensuring safe passage for both animals and vehicles. Use the map to plan your route, and complete each mission by delivering the animals to their designated areas. Enjoy the ride!

use keyboard arrows and space button to control the animals and vehicles
