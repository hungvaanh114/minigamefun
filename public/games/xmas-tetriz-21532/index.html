Xmas Tetriz

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Xmas Tetriz, a festive twist on the classic Block Tetriz game! Immerse yourself in a vibrant Christmas-themed atmosphere as you strategically fit colorful blocks together to create complete rows. The excitement is palpable as you navigate through various levels, aiming to achieve the highest score while enjoying the holiday spirit.

To manipulate the falling blocks, simply use the left, right, and down arrow keys on your keyboard. You can also use the up arrow key to rotate the blocks, giving you the ability to fit them into the most effective positions. For those who prefer a more tactile experience, tapping or clicking the respective game buttons will achieve the same result. With the captivating sound effects and visuals complementing your gameplay, reimagine how fun classic block games can be with a Christmas flair.

What makes Xmas Tetriz even more thrilling are the four exciting power-ups scattered throughout the game. These power-ups will elevate your gaming experience, providing unique benefits that can help you clear blocks faster or gain extra points. Embrace the holiday magic as you challenge yourself to get a high score while enjoying a captivating blend of puzzle gameplay and festive cheer!

How to play free Xmas Tetriz game online

In Xmas Tetriz, use the left and right keys to move blocks sideways, while the down key drops them faster. To rotate, press the up key. You can also tap on the on-screen buttons for the same effects. Your mission is to align blocks into complete rows to clear them and score points. Enjoy!
