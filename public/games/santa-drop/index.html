Santa Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting adventure with Santa Drop, where your mission is to help Santa escape from a dark and treacherous forest prison! This engaging physics puzzle game features 60 levels filled with fun challenges that will test your problem-solving skills. The mechanics are simple yet addictive: your task is to eliminate all boxes around Santa, except for the grassy area where he will land safely.

In each level, you'll encounter various blocks that you need to click or tap to destroy. Strategically plan your moves to clear the pathway for Santa, utilizing the remaining blocks to create a safe descent. With clever thinking and a bit of creativity, you'll find ways to manipulate the environment and lead Santa to freedom. The game combines entertaining gameplay with physics-based puzzles, making it a delightful experience for players of all ages.

As you progress through the 60 levels, you'll encounter increasingly difficult scenarios that require you to think outside the box. The challenge will keep you engaged as you refine your strategy and aim to achieve the best results. Join Santa on his quest, and help him overcome the obstacles of the forest! This is more than just a game; it s an adventure that brings joy and excitement while you experience the festive spirit throughout the levels.

How to play free Santa Drop game online

To play Santa Drop, simply click or tap on the blocks to remove them and create a clear path for Santa. Use the remaining blocks strategically to guide his descent into the grassy safe zone. Work through all 60 levels and enjoy the physics-based fun!
