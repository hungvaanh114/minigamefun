Mermaid Baby Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Mermaid Baby Care is an enchanting and widely adored caring game that allows players to step into the shoes of a nurturing caregiver. Princess Mermaid has been swamped with her royal duties and desperately needs assistance with her adorable baby. Are you ready to lend a helping hand?

Your journey begins with giving the mermaid baby a refreshing shower to ensure he is clean and cheerful. Once he s all washed up, it s time to select a stunning outfit that suits his aquatic nature. The mermaid baby has a penchant for colors, and you ll find three delightful animals needing your artistic touch. Show your creativity and surprise the little one by painting vibrant and beautiful artworks just for him.

After a fun-filled session of painting, it s time to tend to the baby s hunger. He hasn t eaten in a while, so be sure to serve him some delicious fruits to replenish his energy. Once all the exciting activities tire him out, your final task will be to soothe the mermaid baby into a peaceful slumber. Your assistance is invaluable to Princess Mermaid thank you for being such a caring helper!

How to play free Mermaid Baby Care game online

To play Mermaid Baby Care, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through different activities. Start by tapping on the baby to give him a shower, then choose his outfit. Select the painting activity to color the animals, and remember to feed the baby fruits before tucking him into bed. Enjoy caring for the mermaid baby!
