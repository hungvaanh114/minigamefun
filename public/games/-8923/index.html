Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Pips Up, where your agility and quick thinking will be put to the test! In this dynamic game, you are tasked with rolling your way to the goal while racing against the clock. Each level presents a unique challenge, and your primary objective is simple: reach the finish line swiftly while ensuring the pips on your dice are facing up!

As you navigate through the vibrant and colorful landscape of Pips Up, you ll encounter various obstacles and pathways that will require strategic maneuvering. Keep your eyes peeled for time limits; every second counts! The thrill of rolling your way through the levels will keep you on your toes, and the satisfaction of completing each stage will fuel your desire to keep going. Can you master the art of rolling the dice just right to ensure maximum pips exposure? Only those who can combine speed with precision will successfully conquer every challenge!

The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay make Pips Up a delightful distraction for players of all ages. Prepare for fun as you embark on this rolling adventure that requires not just speed, but also a keen eye for angles and configurations. Don t just play; immerse yourself in the challenge, and see how far your rolling skills can take you!

How to play free Pips Up game online

To play Pips Up, use your mouse or touchpad to control the direction of your roll. Aim for the goal while ensuring that the pips on the dice are aligned as needed. Keep an eye on the countdown timer and strategize your moves to achieve the highest score possible!
