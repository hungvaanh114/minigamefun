Introducing Free Rider HD, the officially acclaimed web game that has captured the hearts of gamers worldwide with over ONE BILLION PLAYS and countless custom tracks! Now making its way to Android, this thrilling biking experience allows players to race on trails uniquely designed by fellow enthusiasts. In this creative playground, every track is a work of art, crafted by players just like you!

Embark on a journey through endless terrains while aiming to collect all the stars scattered across each track to complete your ride. Whether you re soaring over jumps or navigating tricky turns, every session brings fresh excitement. Discover tracks that resonate with your style, but if you encounter one that doesn t capture your interest, simply skip it and move on to the next adventure. With a platform that offers INFINITE CONTENT, the fun never stops!

Join the vibrant Free Rider HD community and unleash your creativity by crafting your own tracks, sharing them with others, and challenging friends to beat your records. The game's engaging interface combined with the thrill of racing on player-created courses ensures that there s always something new to explore. So gear up and get ready for a ride where imagination meets adrenaline!

How to play Free Rider HD game online

To play Free Rider HD, select a track from the extensive library or create your own. Use the on-screen controls to accelerate, steer, and perform tricks while racing. Collect all stars on the track to secure a complete run and try to achieve the fastest time possible!
