Merge the New Year: New Year Balls!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This game is in the style of the New Year! You have to throw balls along five lines! Your task is to connect the same ones until you get the most beautiful one! Collect points! Compete with friends and colleagues for the title of the best!

Instructions

1 At first it will be easy to connect the balls but over time there will be more and more of them As soon as you connect the most beautiful ball in this game it will disappear and give you the most points
