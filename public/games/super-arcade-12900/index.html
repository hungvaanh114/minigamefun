Arcade games are a classic genre of video games that originated in coin-operated machines found in public spaces like arcades, amusement parks, and entertainment centers. These games are known for their simple yet engaging gameplay, colorful graphics, and catchy sound effects. Popular arcade game genres include platformers, shooters, puzzle games, and racing games. Titles like Pac-Man, Space Invaders, Donkey Kong, and Street Fighter have become iconic, offering players a fun and challenging experience with each new level.

Arcade games typically focus on high scores, encouraging players to compete for the top spot. Many of these games feature fast-paced action, quick reflexes, and memorable characters. Today, arcade games can be found in digital formats on consoles, computers, and mobile devices, allowing players to relive the nostalgia of old-school gaming. Despite the shift to digital platforms, the influence of classic arcade games can still be seen in modern video game design.
