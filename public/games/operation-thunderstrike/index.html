Operation Thunderstrike

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the action-packed world of Operation Thunderstrike, an exhilarating demo that pays homage to beloved classics like Metal Slug, Heavy Weapon, and Silkworm. In this fast-paced shooting shooter (Shmup), you take command of powerful vehicles either a heavily-armored tank or an agile helicopter as you navigate through waves of relentless enemies.

Your mission is clear: fight your way to the end of each level and face off against formidable bosses. Along the way, collect various upgrades that will enhance your firepower, speed, and overall effectiveness in combat. Each level presents new challenges and excitement, providing players with an adrenaline-fueled experience reminiscent of the standout arcade titles of yesteryear.

How to play free Operation Thunderstrike game online

To play Operation Thunderstrike, simply use your keyboard to control the tank or helicopter. Move with the arrow keys and shoot with the spacebar. Collect upgrades as you progress through each level to enhance your abilities and prepare for the final boss showdown. Good luck!
