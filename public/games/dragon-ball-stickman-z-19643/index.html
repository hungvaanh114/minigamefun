Dragon Ball Stickman Z

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the exhilarating world of Dragon Ball Stickman Z, a vibrant fighting game that invites you to step into the shoes of your favorite Dragon Ball Z heroes. Get ready to protect the earth from a myriad of nefarious enemies by entering the arena and battling for survival. This game is not just for Dragon Ball fans; it's designed for anyone who enjoys dynamic gameplay combined with stunning features.

In Dragon Ball Stickman Z, players will face off against iconic bosses such as Hitz, Jirenz, Zamasuz, Black Gokuz, Piccolo, and Frieza. Hone your skills and unlock countless upgrades for your stickman heroes in what is arguably one of the best action RPG fighting experiences out there. The game offers an engaging experience with its fluid controls and spectacular skill designs, ensuring that every match is an exciting struggle for victory.

With 200 levels tailored for Saiyans and an additional 30 Saiyan-jins to explore, players will find themselves immersed in a world full of challenges and powerful transformations. The awe-inspiring transform effects and the introduction of new characters, along with the ultra instinct abilities, make Dragon Ball Stickman Z a must-play for action game aficionados. Prepare to engage in high-paced fights and experience the thrill of being a superhero in a stickman format.

How to play free Dragon Ball Stickman Z game online

To play Dragon Ball Stickman Z, simply select your desired hero and enter the battle arena. Use your controls to attack, defend, and unleash powerful skills against bosses. Collect orbs after defeating enemies to level up and enhance your character's abilities. Stay agile and strategize to conquer all 200 levels!
