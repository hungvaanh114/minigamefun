How to play free Winx Stylish Dress game online

Once you enter the Winx Stylish Dress game, use your mouse to navigate through the wardrobe options. Select a fairy, then browse through the dresses, accessories, and more. Click on the items you like to dress her up in a trendy style. Don t forget to save your favorite looks!
