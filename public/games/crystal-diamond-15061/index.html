Crystal Diamond

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a vibrant world of fun and challenges with Crystal Diamond, an engaging 2D puzzle game where your objective is to match dazzling diamonds. Each level presents a colorful backdrop filled with shiny jewels waiting to be lined up and collected. Your mission is simple: align three or more identical diamonds to clear them from the board and earn points. The more diamonds you clear in a single move, the higher your score!

As you progress through the various levels, expect the puzzles to become increasingly complex and demanding, pushing your strategic thinking skills to the limit. With its eye-catching graphics and delightful sound effects, Crystal Diamond captivates players of all ages. Moreover, special power-ups are strategically placed to add an extra layer of excitement, allowing you to overcome challenging puzzles and vie for higher scores. This game is perfectly tailored for puzzle enthusiasts seeking endless entertainment and a test of their problem-solving abilities.

Are you ready to embark on a diamond-collecting adventure? Challenge yourself to gather all the gems while striving to climb to the top of the leaderboard. Join countless players and immerse yourself in the sparkling world of Crystal Diamond!

How to play free Crystal Diamond game online

To play Crystal Diamond, simply use your mouse or finger to swap adjacent diamonds. Aim to match three or more gems of the same color to clear them from the board. Keep an eye out for special power-ups that can help you tackle tougher puzzles as you progress through the game!


Instructions

Mouse Touch Controls Click Tap on Diamond Select a diamond Drag to Swap Drag the selected diamond to swap it with an adjacent one Release to Match Release the drag to make a match of three or more identical diamonds
