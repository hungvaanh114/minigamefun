Punching Boxes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Punching Boxes, where you step into the shoes of an aspiring boxer with dreams of greatness but limited resources. Unable to afford costly gym fees, you've turned to an unconventional training method smashing boxes! Your goal is simple: tap the sides of the screen to unleash powerful punches that obliterate the cardboard foes that stand in your way.

As you train, keep a watchful eye on the hazardous sharp wood that some boxes conceal. A careless strike could send you sprawling, resulting in an instant knockout! Every second matters in the ring: a ticking clock looms overhead, and by destroying boxes, you can earn precious extra time to sharpen your skills. The faster and more accurately you punch, the higher your score will climb. Are you ready to break records and prove your boxing prowess?

Get ready to unleash your inner champion as you embark on this wild and exciting journey. Can you master the art of punching boxes? Only the quickest and most alert participants will succeed in this thrilling challenge. It s time to channel your energy into punching and become the ultimate box-smashing champion!

How to play free Punching Boxes game online

To play the game, simply tap on the left and right sides of your screen to deliver quick jabs at the boxes. Pay attention to the sharp wood hidden within some boxes and avoid it to prevent getting knocked out. Keep an eye on the timer and punch quickly to gain extra seconds and boost your score!
