Witch to Princess Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the enchanting world of "Witch to Princess Makeover," a captivating game where magic and beauty intertwine. Once a radiant princess, our heroine has fallen victim to a curse that has turned her into a witch. Now, it's up to you to assist her in crafting the perfect potion to reverse the spell and restore her royal charm. Engage in a delightful journey as you gather ingredients, mix potions, and unleash the power of beauty to reclaim her princess status.

As you embark on this whimsical adventure, your tasks will also include preparing for an unforgettable ball where she hopes to meet her true love, Prince Charming. Dive into a world filled with vibrant colors and dazzling outfits as you access a wide variety of skins, enchanting dresses, and stylish accessories to personalize the princess's look. Your creativity will shine as you mix and match different elements to create a stunning appearance for the grand event.

With the right potions and your unique fashion sense, help transform the once-cursed witch back into the beautiful princess she deserves to be. Will you unlock all the magical outfits and ensure she captivates the heart of her prince? The fate of the kingdom rests in your hands, so let the makeover begin!

How to play free Witch to Princess Makeover game online

To play the Witch to Princess Makeover game, simply start by selecting the ingredients needed for the potion. Follow the on-screen prompts to mix the potion correctly. Once transformed, choose from various skins, dresses, and accessories in the makeover salon to prepare the princess for the ball and impress Prince Charming!
