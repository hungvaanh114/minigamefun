Flappy Shark?Flappy Shark

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into the thrilling depths of the ocean with Flappy Shark! It's a world full of vibrant colors and mesmerizing aquatic life where your main objective is to keep the hungry shark fed. Navigate through a myriad of obstacles while guiding your shark to munch on anything in its path. The ocean is not just a haven for food; it s also a place where dangers lurk around every corner. Your skill in maneuvering the shark will determine how long it survives as you tackle these challenges.

As you explore these captivating underwater regions, your shark's hunger becomes more intense. Each fish you devour adds to your score, serving as motivation to keep swimming and avoid the perils that could end your thrilling journey. Flappy Shark promises to bring excitement as you compete against yourself or challenge friends to beat your best high score. The engaging gameplay combined with delightful animations offers an experience you won't want to miss. Can you master the art of swimming like a shark while surviving myriad obstacles? Immerse yourself in the game to find out!

How to play free Flappy Shark game online

To play Flappy Shark, simply tap the screen to make your shark swim upward. Release to let it descend. Aim to weave through obstacles and collect food to raise your score. Avoid crashing into barriers, as they will end your game. The goal is to survive as long as possible while racking up points!
