Prepare yourself for a thrilling escapade with Cargo Truck: Transport & Hunt, an exhilarating simulation game that fuses the excitement of driving with the thrill of hunting. As the sole hero in this vibrant city, you will jump into your cargo truck and embark on an unforgettable journey.

Navigate through the bustling streets as you hunt down a variety of animals ranging from agile leopards to massive elephants. The chase is on, and you must remain sharp and quick on your feet, as these creatures are both fast and fierce! Equip yourself with your sniper gun, ensuring you're ready to take precise shots as you coordinate the task of safely transporting each animal back to the zoo.

The game plays out in a captivating environment, filled with challenges that will test both your driving and hunting skills. Each mission presents unique obstacles that require a blend of strategy and quick reflexes to overcome as you strive to restore order at the zoo. How to play free Cargo Truck: Transport & Hunt game online

In Cargo Truck: Transport & Hunt, navigate your truck using arrow keys or a joystick for steering. Use the sniper aim with your mouse to target animals. Once you ve captured them, drive back to the zoo while avoiding obstacles and ensuring a safe transport. Happy hunting and driving!
