Halloween Merge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to immerse yourself in the spine-tingling world of Halloween Merge, where thrilling adventures await at every turn! This engaging puzzle game invites players to merge a variety of spooky items to unlock an exciting array of haunted treasures and breathe life into their eerie mansion.

In this enchanting game, players will encounter a rich assortment of Halloween-themed puzzles that challenge their merging skills. Combine delightful yet spooky artifacts such as pumpkins, mystical potions, and ghostly decorations to create a mesmerizing atmosphere within your haunted estate. The more you merge, the more chilling treasures you can unlock, bringing each level to life with Halloween magic!

As you navigate through this intriguing game, enjoy the vibrant Halloween vibes that fill the air. Every match and merge leads to unique challenges, offering players the chance to discover hidden secrets while earning fantastic daily rewards. With intuitive gameplay and captivating graphics, Halloween Merge ensures that every session is an entertaining experience filled with delightful surprises!

How to play free Halloween Merge game online

To play Halloween Merge, simply drag and drop items from your inventory onto one another to merge them. Start with basic items like pumpkins and potions, creating new and exciting pieces as you progress. Complete levels by successfully merging enough items, and watch for secret treasures to reveal!


Instructions

Desktop Select and drag items to merge them Click on an item hold down the left mouse button and drag it to the target item to merge Mobile Tap and hold on an item to drag it and drop it onto another to merge
