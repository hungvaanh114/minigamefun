Bloom and Flora Dress Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Bloom and Flora Dress Up, where you get to explore the magical lives of two lovely fairy girls. Bloom and Flora are not just good friends; they are extraordinary fairies who shine bright in their mystical realm. However, like all fashion-forward fairies, they face the delightful challenge of choosing the perfect outfits that highlight their unique styles and vibrant personalities.

In this captivating dress-up game, you take on the role of a fashion stylist, guiding Bloom and Flora through a closet filled with whimsical and beautiful dresses. Whether you prefer ethereal gowns adorned with flowers or sparkling outfits that glimmer under the moonlight, the choice is entirely yours! Experiment with various styles, colors, and accessories to create the most breathtaking looks.

Choose from a plethora of skirts, tops, and accessories that not only transform their appearance but also make a statement about who they are. Don t forget to add in some magical touches, like fairy wings and enchanting hairstyles, to elevate their outfits to the next level. The delightful world of fashion awaits you, and every combination opens the door to endless possibilities! Are you ready to unleash your creativity and help these fairy friends dazzle in style?

How to play free Bloom and Flora Dress Up game online

To play the Bloom and Flora Dress Up game, simply use the mouse to browse through the wardrobe options available. Click on the dresses, accessories, and hairstyles you want to try on Bloom and Flora. Once you are satisfied with their looks, take a screenshot or share it for your friends to admire your fashion skills!


Instructions

You can select the dresses by touching the dress menus on the left and right
