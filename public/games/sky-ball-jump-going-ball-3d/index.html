How to play free Sky Ball Jump - Going Ball 3D game online

To play Sky Ball Jump, simply swipe left or right to control the direction of your rolling ball and tap the screen to jump over obstacles. Keep your ball balanced on the track while avoiding traps, and try to achieve the maximum score by completing all levels!
