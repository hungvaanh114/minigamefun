Crazy Intersection - Car Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the bustling world of Crazy Intersection - Car Game, where you take the reins of traffic lights at busy junctions to prevent vehicular chaos! This engaging city traffic simulator challenges your problem-solving skills as you navigate the complexities of rush hour traffic.

Your primary objective is to avert collisions and minimize traffic delays. Strategically manage the traffic lights, or opt for a roundabout to keep the vehicles flowing smoothly. Additionally, you ll be tasked with drawing the paths that each car should follow to reach its destination, ensuring all vehicles move harmoniously through the city streets.

Experience the thrill of driving various types of cars as they weave through an intricate maze of roads. Every decision impacts the traffic's flow, so stay alert and solve each car's journey efficiently. With multiple levels to conquer, Crazy Intersection - Car Game offers endless challenges for traffic management enthusiasts.

Will you be able to maintain order in the chaos? Put your traffic coordination skills to the test and become the master of city streets!

How to play free Crazy Intersection - Car Game game online

To play the game, simply click on the traffic lights to change their colors, allowing cars to pass through the intersection safely. Draw lines to indicate the routes for each vehicle, guiding them to their desired destinations while avoiding accidents. Stay vigilant during busy traffic hours!
