Burger Super King Sim

Welcome to the thrilling world of Burger Super King Sim, where you can unleash your culinary creativity by constructing towering burgers! In this engaging casual game, your main objective is to gather the necessary ingredients to build your burger and advance through the various levels. As ingredients like juicy tomato slices, creamy cheddar cheese, savory smoked ham, fresh onions, and assorted veggies cascade from above, it s your job to skillfully catch them.

Utilizing both of your hands, you ll maneuver the floating burger across the screen to collect these delectable elements. Feel the excitement as your creation rises higher and higher, reaching astonishing heights that rival even the tallest skyscrapers! The more ingredients you catch, the more impressive your burger becomes. Every level poses its own challenges, so be quick and precise to ensure your burger masterpiece is complete.

How to play free Burger Super King Sim game online

To play Burger Super King Sim, simply use your left and right hands to catch falling ingredients by moving your burger. As you capture more components, watch your burger grow taller!
