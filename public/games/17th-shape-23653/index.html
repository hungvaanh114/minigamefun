17th Shape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the captivating world of 17th Shape, a unique merging puzzle game where your spatial reasoning and strategy skills will be put to the test! In this game, your goal is to combine identical shapes to create new ones. You can maneuver the shapes in any direction thanks to the convenient arrow keys, swipe gestures on mobile devices, or by using your mouse pointer. With each successful merge, you are rewarded with a brand-new shape.

Can you unlock the elusive 17th shape? Challenge yourself and discover how far your abilities can take you in this immersive gaming experience. 17th Shape offers a limited number of shuffles and regroup abilities, allowing you to reshape the board to your advantage. As you progress, the game not only sharpens your cognitive skills but also provides endless fun. Enjoy the thrilling journey of discovery as you blend your way through various intricate levels, strategizing to achieve the highest shapes possible.

Start your adventure by thinking strategically and planning your moves each swipe or key press can lead you closer to unlocking the next level of shapes! So, gather your focus and dive into the colorful universe of 17th Shape, where endless combinations await your creativity.

How to play free 17th Shape game online

To play the 17th Shape game, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard or swipe across the screen on your mobile device. Move matching shapes until they combine to form a new shape. Remember, you have limited shuffles, so think carefully before making your moves!


Instructions
