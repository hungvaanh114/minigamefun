How to play free ColorTurntable game online

To play ColorTurntable, simply tap or click on the colorful segments that appear as the turntable spins. Your objective is to hit the correct colors as quickly as possible. The game tracks your speed and accuracy, so aim for the best timing and compete against friends to see who scores higher!
