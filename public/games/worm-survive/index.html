Worm Survive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Worm Survive, a thrilling adventure that puts you in the shoes or rather, the body of a tiny worm navigating a perilous world filled with obstacles. As you traverse the vibrant landscapes, your primary goal is to dodge giant feet that stomp down from above. These footfalls can come unexpectedly, and quick reflexes are essential to avoid being squashed!

But it's not just about survival; you'll find yourself on a quest to collect glistening yellow cherries scattered throughout the environment. These cherries not only add to your score but also elevate your worm s abilities, making it more resilient and agile. As you munch on these delightful fruits, you'll uncover power-ups that enhance your chances of survival, allowing you to thrive amidst the dangers lurking around every corner.

Experience the world through a worm's eye view, where every moment is a mix of excitement and suspense. Navigate through various terrains, each filled with unique challenges and hazards. The colorful graphics and engaging sound effects draw you into this captivating game, ensuring you stay entertained for hours on end. With each level, the complexity increases more feet, more cherries, and even tougher terrains to conquer, making Worm Survive an addictive game for players of all ages.

How to play free Worm Survive game online

To play Worm Survive, use the arrow keys or swipe on the screen (if on a mobile device) to guide your worm. Move swiftly to dodge the feet while collecting the yellow cherries. Aim for high scores as you progress through increasingly challenging levels. Happy surviving!
