How to play free Basket 3D game online

To play Basket 3D, simply control the ball by dragging your mouse or finger to aim. Release to shoot the ball towards the hoop. Keep an eye on the timer and strive to make as many baskets as possible before time runs out. Enjoy unlocking new balls as you progress!
