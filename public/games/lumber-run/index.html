Lumber Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Lumber Run! In this exhilarating adventure, you take on the role of a lumberjack tasked with chopping down trees to carve out your path. Your journey begins as you seamlessly chop the trees and convert the sturdy logs into wooden planks. With each successful cut, your mission is to run across the river, navigating obstacles and challenges along the way. The further you venture, the more valuable the trees become, adding an exciting twist to your lumberjack escapade!

Each level is a race against time and other competitors. As you axe down trees and gather resources, you'll need to strategize and make quick decisions to ensure you re the first to cross the river. From unique tree types to unexpected difficulties, the game provides a thrilling mix of resource management and speed. Can you outpace your opponents and claim victory? Join the adventure, master your lumberjack skills, and experience the thrill of Lumber Run!

How to play free Lumber Run game online

To play Lumber Run, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to chop down trees as quickly as you can. Collect the logs and convert them into planks, which you ll need to build your bridge across the river. Be sure to keep an eye on the competition and aim to be the first player to complete your crossing!
