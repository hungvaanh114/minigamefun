How to play free Earth Clicker: Idle Clicker game online

To play Earth Clicker, simply start tapping on the Earth to earn coins. Use your collected coins to purchase upgrades from the shop, enhancing your clicker capabilities. Keep an eye out for random asteroid drops that will boost your earnings. Progress through levels and complete quests to unlock even more rewards!
