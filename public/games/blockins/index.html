BLOCKINS

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the captivating world of Blockins, where your puzzle-solving abilities are put to the test as you stack Tetris-inspired blocks to reach a mystical portal. Each level presents a unique challenge, featuring a diverse array of block shapes that require precise maneuvering. Your ultimate goal is to successfully navigate these intriguing puzzles, ensuring that even a single block can lead you to victory.

As you jump between platforms and rotate blocks, you'll encounter obstacles that demand your strategic thinking. The satisfaction of stacking blocks just right to clear a path to the portal is immensely rewarding, making every attempt to solve a puzzle a thrilling experience. With creative designs and engaging gameplay, Blockins invites players to explore a realm filled with enchanting challenges. Should you find yourself stuck at any point, don't hesitate to utilize the hints provided; they can be the key to overcoming particularly tricky puzzles and help keep you progressing through the game.

How to play free BLOCKINS game online

To play Blockins, use your mouse or touchscreen to drag and drop the blocks onto the play area. Rotate the blocks as needed and position them to build a stack that connects to the portal. Keep an eye on the available space and utilize hints if you're unsure of your next move!


Instructions

Move - WASD or Arrow Keys Switch - X or Space bar Back - B or ESC Restart - R
