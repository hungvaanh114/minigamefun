In "School Summer Date," join Hannah as she prepares for a romantic evening with her boyfriend Jack at the beach. With the sun setting and warm breezes in the air, Hannah is eager to impress Jack, and she needs your expertise to transform her into a beauty queen. Team up with her best friend Jessica to dive into the makeover adventure!

Get ready to unleash your inner stylist as you experiment with fabulous makeup looks that highlight Hannah's natural beauty. Choose from a dazzling palette of colors for her eyes, lips, and cheeks to create the perfect summer glow. But that's not all! You can also show off your hairstyling skills by designing colorful, beach-ready hairstyles that will make heads turn. From cute braids to wavy beach locks, the possibilities are endless!

After perfecting her makeup and hair, it s time to select from an array of stunning outfits that capture the essence of summer romance. Mix and match vibrant outfits that reflect Hannah's personality and style, ensuring she feels confident and beautiful on her big date. Engage in this vibrant gameplay where love stories and fashion come together, creating memorable moments to cherish.

Can Hannah and Jack's romantic beach date end with that perfect kiss? It s all in your hands to make it happen! So gather your creativity and styling flair to lead Hannah to a charming evening she will never forget. Download "School Summer Date" now for an amazing makeover experience filled with love and adventure!

How to play free School Summer Date game online

To start playing, simply select Hannah s current look, then use the tools provided to apply makeup, style her hair, and choose her outfit.
