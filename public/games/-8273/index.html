If you're a fan of Poppy Playtime games, then "Kick The Poppy" is just the adventure you've been waiting for! This captivating brain physics puzzle game invites players to engage their minds while enjoying fun, colorful visuals. The main objective is simple yet challenging: tap on various boxes to release a ball that will collide with the poppy and help you collect shimmering stars along the way.

Every level requires a strategic mindset, as players must carefully calculate the perfect moment to release the ball. Timing and precision are key, making each tap exciting and rewarding. As you progress, you'll encounter different obstacles and unique riddles that will test your problem-solving skills, ensuring that no two levels are alike. "Kick The Poppy" blends elements of surprise and strategy, transforming each session into an exhilarating experience.

As you delve deeper into the game, you'll discover new challenges that require quick thinking and adaptability. With each successful level, you ll feel a sense of accomplishment as you clear each stage and unlock new exciting features. Enjoy the addictive gameplay and charming graphics, and immerse yourself in this delightful cosmic adventure that promises countless hours of fun!

How to play free Kick The Poppy game online

To play "Kick The Poppy," simply use your finger or mouse to tap on the boxes surrounding the poppy. When you tap, a ball will be released, aiming towards the poppy. Your goal is to hit the poppy and gather stars while navigating through various obstacles. Strategize your taps for the best results!
