Helle Bot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Helle Bot, a captivating 2D sci-fi platformer that challenges players to navigate a vibrant digital world filled with dangers and rewards. Step into the role of a clever bot tasked with collecting glittering red jewels scattered throughout each level. As you dash through the intricately designed landscapes, you must stay on your toes and evade hostile enemies, including menacing bots and aerial drones that rain projectiles down upon you.

Strategic maneuvering is key as you avoid deadly hazards like flames and sharp spikes, all while keeping your eyes peeled for the elusive Exit door that leads you to the next thrilling challenge. With a total of eight dynamic levels, Helle Bot ramps up the difficulty, ensuring that players remain engaged and entertained. As you progress, you will encounter increasingly complex obstacles and more relentless enemies, testing your skills and reflexes at every turn.

The vibrant graphics and immersive sound design create a rich atmosphere that draws players deeper into the game's universe. Prepare yourself for heart-pounding excitement as you strive to achieve the perfect run by collecting all the jewels and making it to the Exit unscathed. Will you conquer all levels and emerge victorious in the exhilarating world of Helle Bot? Join the quest and find out!

How to play free Helle Bot game online

To play Helle Bot, use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D to move your bot left, right, jump, and crouch. Collect red jewels and avoid enemies and hazards. Make your way to the Exit door to complete each level, while keeping an eye out for the increasing challenges ahead!
