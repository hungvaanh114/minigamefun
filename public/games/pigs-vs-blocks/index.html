Pigs vs Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pigs vs Blocks is an engaging arcade game that brings a unique twist to the classic block-breaking genre. In this vibrant and entertaining game, players assume the role of a pixelated pig, tasked with smashing through a variety of blocks that come their way. Each block is marked with a specific number, indicating the number of hits required for it to shatter. This adds an element of strategy as you calculate your moves and ensure that you don't run out of lives.

The game features a total of 30 captivating levels, each filled with colorful graphics and fun challenges that will keep you hooked. As you progress, the difficulty increases, presenting new block configurations and obstacles that test your reflexes and planning skills. Pigs vs Blocks is not just about mindless tapping; it requires timing, precision, and a little bit of strategy to master the art of block breaking with your trusty pig companion.

With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Pigs vs Blocks is perfect for gamers of all ages. Whether you're looking to kill some time or challenge yourself to achieve higher scores, this arcade experience will bring endless joy. So get ready to immerse yourself in this delightful universe, shatter those blocks, and enjoy countless hours of fun!

How to play free Pigs vs Blocks game online

To play Pigs vs Blocks, use your mouse or finger (on mobile) to control the pig. Click or tap to break the blocks in your path, ensuring you hit each one enough times to shatter it, while keeping an eye on the numbers displayed. Keep moving forward and rack up points!
