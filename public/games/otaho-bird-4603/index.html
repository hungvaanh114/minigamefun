Otaho Bird

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Otaho Bird is an exciting 2D platformer that invites players into a vibrant avian world where quick reflexes and strategic thinking are essential. In this game, you take on the role of a determined little bird on a quest to collect all the slices of bread scattered throughout various challenging landscapes. However, this mission is not without its perils. You must deftly navigate through hazardous obstacles like enemy birds, ominous rotating blades, and raging fires, all while making your way toward the Exit nest that leads you to the next level.

Featuring a total of 8 meticulously designed levels, Otaho Bird escalates in difficulty as you advance, keeping you engaged and on your toes. Each level introduces new challenges and traps, demanding quick reactions and sharp problem-solving skills. The cheerful graphics and delightful animations enhance the immersive experience, making every successful bread collection feel rewarding. Whether it s soaring past danger or cleverly timing your movements to dodge enemies, Otaho Bird offers players a thrilling adventure full of twists and turns at every corner.

How to play free Otaho Bird game online

To play Otaho Bird, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your bird character around the screen. Jump to collect slices of bread while avoiding enemies and hazards. Keep an eye on your surroundings, as timing your moves is key to reaching the Exit nest safely. Enjoy the challenge and strive for higher scores as you progress through each level!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
