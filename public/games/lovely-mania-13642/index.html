Lovely Mania

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the charming world of Lovely Mania, a thrilling and dynamic heart-matching game that promises non-stop fun and excitement! In this engaging challenge, your main objective is to connect hearts of the same color by launching your own heart towards them. With each successful match, you will collect all the hearts in the row, propelling you towards higher scores and achievements.

As you dive deeper into the game, you'll notice that rows of hearts continuously descend towards your position, adding an exhilarating element of urgency. This means you must act quickly and strategically to gather each row before they get too close. The fast-paced nature of the game keeps you on your toes, making every round a race against time!

One unique aspect of Lovely Mania is that your heart frequently shifts in color, requiring you to stay vigilant and adapt your strategy accordingly. Be mindful of your heart's color before aiming for other hearts, as this will dictate your potential matches and score. The game continues until the row of hearts catches up to yours, so prepare for an addictive experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to play free Lovely Mania game online

To play Lovely Mania, simply click or tap to launch your heart toward another heart of the same color. Keep an eye on your heart's changing colors, and aim strategically to collect the hearts in the rows. Stay quick and make decisions fast to avoid being caught by the descending hearts!
