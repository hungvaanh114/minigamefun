Angry Rooster Run Subway

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing the exhilarating adventure of Angry Rooster Run Subway! This captivating animal running game is designed for kids and is filled with excitement and challenges. In this exciting journey, you will assist the brave rooster as he navigates through various obstacles and foes while attempting to reunite with his beloved chicken. With fast-paced gameplay and vibrant graphics, it s an engaging experience for players of all ages.

As you propel the courageous rooster through a bustling subway environment, you must help him dodge deadly traps and ferocious predators lurking along the way. Timing and precision are crucial as you jump over hurdles, slide under barriers, and battle enemies to ensure the rooster's survival. Each level becomes increasingly challenging, ensuring that players remain captivated as they guide the rooster on his quest for love and freedom.

In Angry Rooster Run Subway, survival is not just about evasion; it also requires a proactive approach. Sometimes, you may need to attack foes to clear your path and create a safe route for the rooster. Collect power-ups along the way to boost your speed or gain temporary invincibility. With each successful escape, you ll earn points and unlock new abilities, making the gameplay even more thrilling.

How to play free Angry Rooster Run Subway game online

To play the Angry Rooster Run Subway game, simply use the on-screen controls to jump, duck, and attack enemies. Swipe left or right to navigate the subway lanes. Keep an eye out for traps and collect power-ups while you aim to help the rooster reach his beloved chicken safely!
