Hekov Bot 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hekov Bot 2 is an exciting 2D platformer steeped in a captivating sci-fi atmosphere, where players embark on a thrilling adventure to collect battery cells. As you navigate through the intricately designed levels, you'll need to outmaneuver a variety of enemies ranging from enemy robots to menacing flying bots while dodging numerous deadly obstacles such as bullets and spikes. Your main objective is to reach the Exit door to advance to the next level, making for an exhilarating experience filled with challenges and excitement.

This installment marks the continuation of the original "Hekov Bot" series and features eight progressively challenging levels that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking. Each level introduces new obstacles and tougher enemies, ensuring that players remain engaged as they attempt to complete their mission. As the difficulty ramps up, you'll have to rely on your skills to navigate the treacherous platforming environment and collect all the battery cells to succeed. Immerse yourself in the vibrant graphics and delightful soundtrack as you embark on this thrilling journey!

How to play free Hekov Bot 2 game online

To play Hekov Bot 2, use the arrow keys to move your character left and right, and jump over obstacles. Collect all battery cells throughout the levels while avoiding enemies and hazards. Reach the Exit door at the end of each level to progress to the next challenge and enjoy this engaging platformer!
