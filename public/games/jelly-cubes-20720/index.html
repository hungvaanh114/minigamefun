Jelly Cubes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Jelly Cubes, a vibrant and engaging puzzle game that invites you to immerse yourself in a world of colorful jelly blocks. Your mission is simple: pick and drop sets of jelly cubes onto the board, strategically matching colors to achieve high scores. The game's mechanics are intuitive; just click on a block set to rotate it. This allows for endless possibilities as you work to align three or more jelly cubes of the same hue.

What sets Jelly Cubes apart from other puzzle games is the absence of a time limit. Your only constraint lies in the empty spaces available on the board, enabling you to think critically and plan each move with precision. As you progress, aim to create larger groups of matching jellies, as they not only yield higher points but also grant you a satisfying sense of accomplishment.

Every move counts in Jelly Cubes. The more thoughtful and strategic your choices, the more jellies you'll be able to remove from the board. This addictive gameplay encourages you to think ahead and maximize your scoring potential while enjoying the delightful visuals and fluid gameplay. Dive into the colorful world of Jelly Cubes, where every turn can lead to exciting new challenges and opportunities to rack up points!

How to play free Jelly Cubes game online

To play Jelly Cubes, simply select a set of jelly blocks and place them on the board. Rotate the blocks by clicking on them, then aim to connect three or more jellies of the same color to score points. Make strategic moves to clear your board and create larger matches for bonus points!
