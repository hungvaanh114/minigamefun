Speedy Golf

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of the greens with Speedy Golf, a fast-paced golf game designed for players who crave quick action and incredible scores. With its innovative one-button, three-tap control, you won t need to be a golf pro to enjoy each round. A built-in caddy takes care of the math for you, allowing players to focus solely on the fun of hitting that perfect shot.

In Speedy Golf, you can effortlessly play through an entire 18-hole par 72 course in just a matter of minutes. No more spending hours on the fairway! Whether you prefer to play in portrait or landscape mode, the game accommodates your style, providing a seamless experience tailored to your preferences.

The graphics are smooth and immersive, bringing the vibrant world of golf to life right on your device. With each hole, players are treated to stunning 3D visuals that enhance gameplay while making every swing exciting. Get ready to challenge yourself and your friends as you aim for the best possible scores without the lengthy waits traditionally associated with golf games.

How to play free Speedy Golf game online

To play Speedy Golf, simply select your club and position your shot by tapping the screen. Tap again to control your power, and then one last time to fine-tune your aim. Focus on timing your taps for the best results and let the caddy handle the calculations, allowing you to enjoy the fast-paced nature of this exciting game!
