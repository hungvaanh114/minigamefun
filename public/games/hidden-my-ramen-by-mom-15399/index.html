Hidden My Ramen by Mom

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delightful and humorous journey in the game Hidden My Ramen by Mom, where the quest for a beloved bowl of ramen becomes an exhilarating adventure! In this unique puzzle game, your mother has hidden your precious ramen, and it s your mission to track it down without getting caught. With strict orders from Mom, who firmly believes that ramen is off-limits, you must carefully navigate through your home to find your tasty treasure.

As you search for your hidden ramen, you ll encounter various props and items that can assist you in your quest. You'll need to use your wits and creativity to devise clever strategies to avoid traps set by your vigilant mother. Each level brings new challenges and obstacles, requiring you to think critically and act swiftly. Can you outsmart your mom and enjoy the delicious ramen you've been craving for?

The engaging gameplay mechanics keep you on your toes as you solve puzzles and uncover secret locations. With colorful graphics and playful animations, Hidden My Ramen by Mom offers an immersive experience that will leave you laughing and strategizing in equal measure. Get ready to unlock the ultimate comfort food while dodging your mom s watchful eyes!

How to play free Hidden my ramen by mom game online

To play Hidden My Ramen by Mom, navigate through the home environment using your mouse to interact with various objects. Use props found throughout the levels to distract your mom and uncover ramen hiding spots. Be mindful of your surroundings to avoid traps and stay hidden while you cook and enjoy your ramen!
