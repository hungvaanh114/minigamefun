Zombie Mission X

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chilling world of Zombie Mission X, where our brave heroes, Emily and Silver, find themselves in a frenetic fight for survival during the winter season. As they embark on what was supposed to be a much-needed winter vacation, a shocking turn of events unfolds: the undead have risen and now lay claim to their holiday paradise. This unexpected twist transforms their getaway into a desperate mission to reclaim their vacation and rescue their friends from the clutches of the relentless zombies.

In this action-packed adventure, players will join forces with Emily and Silver as they navigate through a frozen wasteland infested with zombies. Armed with resourcefulness and a variety of weapons, your mission is to battle these hungry creatures while devising strategic plans to save your friends. With each step forward, the stakes get higher, and the challenges become increasingly intense. You'll encounter powerful enemies, trap-filled territories, and complicated puzzles that test your skills and teamwork. Your quick thinking and agile reflexes will be essential in overcoming the undead menace and bringing your friends back to safety.

As you explore frigid landscapes, you'll uncover hidden items and upgrades that can aid you in your quest. It's not just about survival; teamwork will be crucial. Players can join forces with friends or other online players, making it a cooperative experience that brings camaraderie and excitement to each encounter. Gear up and prepare for a heart-pounding adventure like no other as you take on zombie hordes and director your friends' fate in the exhilarating Zombie Mission X!

How to play free Zombie Mission X game online

To play Zombie Mission X online, simply use the arrow keys to navigate through the levels, and employ the 'W', 'A', 'S', and 'D' keys for jumping and crouching. Aim and shoot your weapons using the mouse. Collaborate with friends to defeat zombies and save the captives efficiently!
