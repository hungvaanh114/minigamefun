Light Stream

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to embark on an exciting adventure in Light Stream, where your puzzle-solving skills will be put to the test. In this engaging game, you'll take on the role of a young boy tasked with unlocking a mysterious gate using the power of a laser beam. As you navigate through various levels, you'll encounter challenges that require you to strategically position mirrors to reflect the laser precisely onto the door's sensors.

As you progress, you'll discover that collecting all three stars on each level adds an extra layer of difficulty, encouraging you to think critically and creatively. The game boasts stunning high-quality graphics that enhance the immersive experience, while its minimal size ensures you can enjoy it without taking up much storage space. With super simple controls, every player, regardless of experience, can dive right in and start having fun.

Whether you're a fan of physics-based games or specifically enjoy laser beam puzzles, Light Stream provides an engaging and captivating gameplay experience. So take on exciting quests and master the art of laser manipulation while enjoying every moment of this unique challenge.

How to play free Light Stream game online

To play Light Stream, use your mouse or finger to position the mirrors to direct the laser beam towards the gate. As you manipulate the angles, watch for the best reflections to unlock the door. Collect all three stars to achieve the highest score and advance to the next level!
