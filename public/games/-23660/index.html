Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In a world where dangerous virus strains are rapidly spreading, it's time to take action! Step into the role of a brave defender against the pandemic in the thrilling game, Kill The Coronavirus. This game challenges you to combat various strains of the virus using your quick reflexes and strategic abilities. Your objective is clear: vaccinate as many citizens as possible to halt the spread and protect public health.

With engaging graphics and intense gameplay, you will navigate through different levels, encountering various challenges and enemies. Each round emphasizes the importance of timely vaccinations, making it not only an entertaining experience but also an educational one. As you progress, you ll unlock new tools and upgrades that will enhance your abilities to fight off the virus effectively.

Don't miss out on the excitement! Join the fight to eradicate the virus for good and be part of the solution. Gather your friends and compare scores, making it a competitive experience you can enjoy together. The fate of the world depends on your ability to adapt and strategize as you take on the formidable task of vaccination. So, are you ready to make a difference? PLAY NOW!

How to play free Kill The Coronavirus game online

To play Kill The Coronavirus, simply use your mouse or touch controls to navigate your character around the screen. Click on the citizens to vaccinate them, but be careful to avoid obstacles and ensure you complete each level before time runs out!
