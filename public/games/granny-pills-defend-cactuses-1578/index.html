Granny Pills: Defend Cactuses

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the dystopian world of 2075, where Earth has become a barren wasteland following an alien invasion. With nearly all life and breathable oxygen extinguished, the only remaining plants are the resilient cactuses. As the invaders plot to obliterate these vital plants, it s up to our intrepid heroine, Granny, to defend them at all costs.

Granny, a savvy gardener who has managed to cultivate her beloved cactuses along the roadside for precious sunlight, stands strong against the extraterrestrial menace. Unbeknownst to the aliens, they harbor an intense fear of human pills. Equipped with a vast supply of these pills and a trusty shotgun for unforeseen emergencies, Granny is ready to face whatever challenges lie ahead.

Your mission is clear: assist Granny in safeguarding her cactuses from the predatory aliens intent on destroying them. As you join her quest, be prepared to strategize, plan your defenses, and utilize the unique properties of the pills to repel the alien forces. Together, you can help restore life to Earth and ensure that Granny s cactuses flourish once again!

How to play free Granny Pills: Defend Cactuses game online

To start playing Granny Pills: Defend Cactuses, navigate through the levels using the arrow keys to move Granny around. Collect human pills from the ground to boost your defenses, and use the mouse to aim and fire your shotgun when aliens approach. Protect the cactuses at all cost, and watch for various alien types that may require different strategies to defeat!
