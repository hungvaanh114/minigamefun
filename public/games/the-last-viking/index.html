The Last Viking

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an epic adventure in The Last Viking, a captivating game that puts you in control of a brave Viking riding atop a mighty dragon. Your mission is to navigate through a perilous dark forest, teeming with menacing goblins and mutants lurking at every turn. With your indomitable spirit and the fierce loyalty of your dragon companion, you must fight your way to freedom.

As you glide through the treetops, you will have access to an arsenal of weapons at your disposal, allowing you to launch various attacks against your foes. From throwing axes to hurling spears, each weapon offers a unique strategy for taking down the forest s lurking dangers. Meanwhile, your dragon isn t just a mode of transportation; it breathes a powerful stream of fire, providing an additional layer of offense as you battle against the relentless enemies that threaten your passage.

Your goal is not only to escape the clutches of the dark forest but to collect as many points and ammunition as possible along the way. The game challenges you to master your reactions, strategize your attacks, and utilize your dragon's abilities to overcome obstacles and foes. Will your skills be enough to lead the Last Viking to safety, or will the dark forest claim another brave soul?

How to play free The Last Viking game online

To play The Last Viking, use the keyboard for navigation and controls. Move your Viking and dragon using the arrow keys, and utilize designated buttons to throw weapons or unleash fiery breath. Collect items and points scattered throughout the forest while dodging enemies and obstacles. Stay alert and strategize to survive!
