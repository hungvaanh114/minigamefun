Super Mario Green Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of the Super Mario Green Game, where fans of all ages can embark on an adventuresome journey. This captivating app seamlessly links with your favorite LEGO characters: LEGO Mario , LEGO Luigi , and LEGO Peach . Whether you're a long-time player or just starting, the app allows you to establish a thrilling connection, bringing the LEGO Super Mario universe to life right in your own space.

Start your adventure by building your sets with ease and adding them to your growing digital collection. The app encourages you to expand your personalized LEGO Super Mario universe at your own pace, making every gameplay session a unique experience. Don't forget to begin with the essential Starter Course, as it serves as a foundation for all your building endeavors. Each set unlocks new possibilities and challenges that will test your creativity and problem-solving skills.

One of the standout features of the Super Mario Green Game app is the access to live, easy-to-follow 3D building instructions. These innovative guides allow players to construct their LEGO Super Mario sets without any environmental impact, as there s no need for paper. Everything you need is at your fingertips, making the building process smoother and more eco-friendly.

How to play free Super Mario Green Game game online

To play the Super Mario Green Game online, start by connecting your device to the LEGO Super Mario set you have. Follow the on-screen prompts to select your characters and set up the virtual environment. Use the 3D instructions to build your LEGO sets and enjoy interactive challenges as you navigate through the digital Mario world!
