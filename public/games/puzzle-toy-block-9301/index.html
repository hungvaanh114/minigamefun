How to play free Puzzle Toy Block game online

To play Puzzle Toy Block, simply select your puzzle layout and use drag-and-drop mechanics to position the blocks strategically. Match colors and shapes to clear lines and score points. Keep an eye on the magician's tricks, as they may alter the game's dynamics. Enjoy and challenge your mind!
