Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you're seeking an exhilarating car parking experience set in a vast and bustling city, look no further! FROLICS introduces a captivating physics-based Prado parking simulator tailored for enthusiasts of thrilling driving games. With a plethora of engaging parking challenges and multiplayer driving adventures, this game elevates your passion for car parking to new heights. Experience outstanding HD graphics, diverse racing cars, and daily incentive rewards in this premier offline parking simulator of 2022.

The game features a variety of missions that cater to players of all skill levels. Begin your journey with beginner-level tasks and unlock a luxurious garage filled with impressive racing cars. Push your limits with time-based challenges that require precision and speed. Moreover, gather your friends for multiplayer parking competitions that add an exciting social dimension to your gameplay.

Embark on your parking career through multiple modes:

  • Starter Mode: Ideal for newcomers, polish your parking skills through easy missions and uncover mysterious rewards.
  • Advance Mode: Elevate your expertise by tackling challenging tasks within a realistic parking plaza environment.
  • Pro Driver Mode: Put your advanced driving skills to the test in this mode filled with intricate challenges.
  • Arcade Mode: Enjoy an exhilarating free-driving experience while collecting fuel packages and scoring points.

Want to showcase your abilities further? Take on multiplayer tournaments and championships, where skilled drivers compete in various challenges. Achievements await you in this engaging car parking game, with new missions introduced daily. Utilize the bonus levels and rewards for an extra boost in coins and in-game upgrades.

How to play free Car Driving In Big City game online

To start playing, simply select your car from the garage and choose your desired mode. Use the on-screen controls to navigate through the city and complete parking tasks. Drive carefully, collect fuel packages, and aim for high scores as you progress through challenging missions and tournaments.
