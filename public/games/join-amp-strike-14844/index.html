Join Amp; Strike

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join & Strike is an exhilarating 3D stickman adventure arcade game that promises endless fun on multiple platforms. As you embark on this thrilling journey, you start as a red gunner armed with a pistol, facing a series of challenging levels. The unique aspect of the game is the incorporation of your team-building strategy; by connecting with the white stickman members scattered throughout the levels, you can enhance your squad's strength and effectiveness in combat.

Your main objective is clear: eliminate all yellow-bodied enemies that occupy the platforms. These foes come in two types melee units and gunners creating a diverse range of tactical challenges. The game features an automatic fire mode that simplifies combat, allowing you to focus primarily on movement. As you navigate the environment, your primary goal is to dodge the red bullets fired by your enemies and avoid any absent team members that may hinder your progress. The fast-paced action and vibrant graphics keep players hooked, making it a must-try for arcade enthusiasts.

How to play free Join & Strike game online

To play Join & Strike, simply move your character using the directional controls. Your gunner will automatically fire at enemies as you navigate through levels. Aim to connect with white stickmen to build your team while dodging enemy fire. The objective is to eliminate all yellow enemies while strategically avoiding getting hit. Enjoy the action-packed gameplay and enhance your skills with every level!
