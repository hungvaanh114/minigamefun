Clue Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Clue Hunter is an engaging crime-solving puzzle game that puts your detective skills to the test. If you are a fan of mystery games filled with challenges and logic puzzles, this game is a must-try! With five captivating levels Help Mom, Save the Girl, Laundry Master, Loss Escape, and Strange Neighbors you'll embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind each case.

Though the levels may seem straightforward at a glance, you'll quickly realize that completing them requires sharp analytical skills and keen attention to detail. As you navigate through different scenarios, stay vigilant to spot hidden objects and clues that will aid you in solving each perplexing mystery. Each case is more challenging than the last, providing a great opportunity to sharpen your problem-solving abilities while immersing yourself in intriguing narratives.

Prepare yourself for an array of difficult cases that will test your wits and cunning as you attempt to crack each conundrum. With its captivating gameplay and stimulating challenges, Clue Hunter aims to keep you entertained for hours. Whether you are a seasoned detective or new to the genre, this game will certainly have you engaged as you unravel the mysteries that await! Good luck on your journey to becoming a master clue hunter!

How to play free Clue Hunter game online

To play Clue Hunter, simply start the game and choose a level to begin your investigation. Carefully examine your surroundings for hidden objects, clues, and details that help solve the mystery. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with items, and think critically to put the pieces of each case together!
