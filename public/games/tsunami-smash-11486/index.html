Tsunami Smash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating adventure with Tsunami Smash! In this action-packed game, you will navigate through a world on the brink of destruction. Your mission is simple: run, jump, and avoid plunging into the treacherous waters as a towering tsunami wave chases you down. The stakes are high, and you must remain agile and quick on your feet to survive. This is not just a test of speed but also of strategy as you maneuver through various obstacles standing between you and safety.

Your goal is to reach the ship before the destructive wave catches up to you. Each level is filled with challenges that require precise timing and sharp reflexes. As you sprint forward, be wary of unpredictable terrain and slippery surfaces that can throw you off balance. Collect power-ups along the way to enhance your abilities and improve your chances of outrunning the mighty wave. The thrill of racing against time will keep you on the edge of your seat!

With vibrant visuals and an adrenaline-pumping soundtrack, Tsunami Smash offers an immersive gaming experience that promises hours of fun. Will you be able to reach the ship and escape the chaos? Jump on board and find out!

How to play free Tsunami Smash game online

To play Tsunami Smash, use the arrow keys or tap the screen to control your character's movements. Tap to jump over obstacles and avoid falling into the water. Keep running to escape the tsunami wave behind you, and collect power-ups to aid your progress. Aim for the ship to complete each level!


Instructions

Arrows to move and Space to jump
