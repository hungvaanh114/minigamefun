Step Upper is an exhilarating game that takes you on a thrilling journey from Earth to the far reaches of the Universe! As you step into the shoes of an astronaut, get ready to be inspired as you aim for greater distances. One of the key features of Step Upper is the ability to share your accomplishments. Capture your best score as a screenshot and send it to your friends on social media or through messaging apps. This competitive element allows you to find out who among your friends can reach the highest heights, making every play session exciting and engaging. Step Upper is designed as a hyper-casual arcade mini-game that combines fun with a sense of competition.
