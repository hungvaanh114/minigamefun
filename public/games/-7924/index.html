Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to prove your skills as the ultimate Spinner champion? Dive into the exhilarating world of Fidget Spinner: 4 Players Game, where you'll face off against three opponents right on the same screen! Choose your favorite Fidget Hand Spinner, whether it s made of plastic, steel, gold, titanium, or carbon, and embark on a spinning adventure that s completely free!

The mechanics of this game are designed for pure enjoyment and accessibility. Your fidget spinner will automatically begin to rotate, and by pressing the designated button, you'll unleash a burst of speed that sends your spinner racing in a straight line. The objective is simple yet thrilling: push your competitors out of the designated circle while scoring as many points as possible. Each opponent you manage to shove outside the circle will net you 2 points, but watch out! Being pushed out will cost you 1 point, so stay alert and strategize your spins carefully.

With every match, you ll refine your spinning strategy and aim to climb the leaderboard. Compete in real-time against other players and showcase your reflexes, aiming for that top spot. The excitement escalates as you challenge fellow spinners, each attempting to outmaneuver and outscore the others. So gear up, unleash your best spins, and get ready to dominate the fidget spinner arena!

How to play free Fidget spinner: 4 players game online

To play, use your mouse or touchscreen to control your spinner. Click the spin button to launch it forward at high speed. Navigate your spinner to knock opponents out of the circle while avoiding being pushed out yourself. Score points by eliminating competitors and aim to achieve the highest score!
