Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating game of Fruit Collector, players are tasked with the delightful challenge of gathering vibrant fruits that descend from above. Using a nimble basket, which can be maneuvered effortlessly from side to side, you must catch as many pieces of fruit as possible before they hit the ground. As the fruits cascade down the screen, your reflexes and timing will be put to the test.

However, beware! The game isn't without its perils. Occasionally, bombs will fall alongside the fruits, adding an extra layer of excitement and difficulty. If you collect too many bombs, you'll suffer penalties. Players can afford to lose only three fruits or be hit by bombs three times before facing the end of the game. This feature creates a thrilling atmosphere, requiring quick reactions and strategic movements.

To achieve a high score, you must focus on snatching fruits while skillfully avoiding the bombs. Each fruit collected adds to your score, enhancing the competitive spirit as you attempt to beat your previous records. The colorful graphics and engaging sound effects ensure that players remain captivated throughout their gameplay experience, making Fruit Collector a fun and addictive adventure for all ages.

How to play free Fruit Collector game online

To play Fruit Collector, simply use the left and right arrow keys or swipe across your touchscreen to move the basket. Aim to catch the falling fruits while steering clear of the bombs. Keep an eye on your fruit count to avoid losing the game!
