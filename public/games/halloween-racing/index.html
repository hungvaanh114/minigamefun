How to play free Halloween Racing game online

To play the Halloween Racing game online, simply select your favorite character and vehicle. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your racing speed and direction. Gather power-ups on the track and avoid obstacles to enhance your chances of winning. Finish the race to unlock more spooky levels!
