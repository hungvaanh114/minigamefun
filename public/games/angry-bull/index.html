ANGRY BULL

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The city is in chaos! The bulls have broken free from the arena, and it's up to you to save the day in ANGRY BULL. These aggressive bulls are rampaging through the streets, posing a significant danger to all residents. Armed with your trusty weapon, your primary objective is to take down these ferocious creatures before they can cause any harm. Each bull presents a unique challenge, requiring precision, strategy, and quick reflexes.

As you navigate through the bustling city, you will encounter various levels that ramp up in difficulty. Watch for their movements and anticipate their charges to ensure you remain out of harm's way while you aim to neutralize them. The longer the bulls are allowed to roam free, the more unpredictable they become, so act swiftly! With every bull you successfully shoot, you earn points and unlock additional weapons that will enhance your shooting capabilities.

Don't forget that the safety of the citizens rests in your hands. Your courage and skills will be tested as you strive to rid the city of this bull menace. Are you ready to rise to the challenge and showcase your sharpshooting abilities? Join the fight and restore peace to the city in this exhilarating adventure!

How to play free ANGRY BULL game online

To play the ANGRY BULL game, use your mouse to aim at the bulls and click to shoot. Keep an eye on your ammunition and reload when necessary. As you advance through the levels, stay alert for power-ups that can aid your mission and enhance your shooting skills.


Instructions

Controls WASD to move Left shift to run Left mouse click to shoot Right mouse click to aim
