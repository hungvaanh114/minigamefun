Runner Coaster Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Do you enjoy roller coasters? In Runner Coaster Race, you will experience the fast roller coaster arcade track, combined with parkour elements of free running. In this real simulation, you must collect passengers on the ring road. Collect money, avoid obstacles like bombs, and push your limits to become the king of the track.
