City Bike Stunt

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to showcase your motorcycle skills in the action-packed game, City Bike Stunt! This game challenges you to race through six unique tracks, each designed to test your speed, agility, and stunt capabilities. As you navigate through the vibrant cityscape, you'll encounter thrilling ramps that you must hit flawlessly to earn points and unlock a collection of powerful motorcycles. But beware of the barriers scattered throughout the course their presence requires precision and quick reflexes to avoid crashing and losing valuable time.

One of the standout features of City Bike Stunt is the NITRO power boost. Strategically using this feature can help you soar over long ramps, giving you an edge over your competition. Timing is everything; use it wisely to speed through tricky sections and propel yourself ahead of the pack. If you're up for a challenge, engage the "2 PLAYER" mode and invite a friend to join the fun. Compete head-to-head and put your racing skills to the ultimate test. Who will take home the title of the better motorcycle master?

How to play free City Bike Stunt game online

To play City Bike Stunt, use the arrow keys or the on-screen controls to accelerate, steer, and execute jumps. Aim for the ramps while dodging obstacles to maintain your speed and reach the finish line first. Activate NITRO for an extra speed boost whenever needed to conquer those daunting ramps!
