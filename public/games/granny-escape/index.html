Granny Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Granny Escape, where your survival instincts are put to the ultimate test! In this thrilling game, you find yourself trapped in a house filled with mystery and danger, stalked by the terrifying Granny. The only way to escape is to be swift, smart, and stealthy. As you navigate through the dimly lit corridors and eerie rooms of the house, every sound and shadow can become your worst enemy. Avoid making noise and stay out of Granny s sight if she catches you, it s game over!

Your mission in this heart-pounding escape game is to find your way out before time runs out. Search for hidden objects, solve challenging puzzles, and gather items that will aid in your escape. Be on the lookout for keys and tools that open doors, but be cautious Granny has heightened senses and will react to the slightest disturbance. Can you keep your nerves intact and escape the clutches of this terrifying foe?

Granny Escape offers an interactive experience filled with suspense and strategy. As you piece together clues and devise your plan, you'll need to think quickly and act wisely. The game is designed to test your resolve as you face the ever-present threat of being caught. Can you outsmart Granny and find your way to freedom?

How to play free Granny Escape game online

To play the Granny Escape game online, use the arrow keys or WASD controls to move around. Look for hidden items by clicking on them, and combine items from your inventory to solve puzzles. Keep an ear out for Granny's movements and always be ready to hide to avoid capture!
