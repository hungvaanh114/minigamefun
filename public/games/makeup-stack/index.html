Makeup Stack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to dive into the glamorous world of beauty? Introducing Makeup Stack, the game designed for makeup enthusiasts and fans alike! Have you ever wondered how many cosmetic brands you can name or how skilled you really are at applying makeup? This engaging game provides the perfect platform for you to test and showcase your makeup knowledge while having fun!

In Makeup Stack, you will navigate through a series of doors, each presenting a unique challenge that tests your ability to make up customers. The choices are entirely yours; whether you want to tackle a specific brand or style, the path you take is up to you. The ultimate goal is to serve as many satisfied customers as possible by completing makeup tasks, and remember, the focus is on fun and creativity rather than perfection. With your artistic flair and knowledge of cosmetics, you can help everyone look their best!

So, gather your brushes and palettes, and get ready to play Makeup Stack. It's not just about winning; it's about experimenting, learning, and enjoying the art of makeup while you challenge yourself and see how many brands you can recognize. Are you prepared to become the ultimate makeup artist in this virtual world? Don t wait any longer, let s start making up more clients and show off your talent!

How to play free Makeup Stack game online

To begin playing Makeup Stack, simply choose a door to enter different makeup challenges. Complete each task by selecting the right cosmetics and applying them according to the customers' requests. Enjoy your creative journey and aim for high scores!
