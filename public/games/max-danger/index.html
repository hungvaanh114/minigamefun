Max Danger

Welcome to Max Danger, an exhilarating adventure where not everything is what it seems. As you embark on this challenging journey, you'll quickly realize that the risks lurking in the shadows can catch you off guard. Your initial stickman will likely meet an unfortunate fate, and as you progress, the same fate awaits additional stickmen. The objective? Navigate through a daunting 20 levels while trying to minimize your stickman losses. It s a true test of dexterity and foresight!

To move your stickman, utilize the WAD keys or the arrow keys on your keyboard. If you're engaging with the game on a touch device, simply make use of the on-screen directional arrows available. Each successful completion of a level will save your progress automatically, allowing you to pick up from where you left off at any point. When you click the Play button, you will be presented with an option to either continue from your last saved progress or to reset everything, assuming you haven't cleared your browser's cache.

With dynamic gameplay and intricate levels filled with unseen threats, Max Danger promises players an enjoyable yet perilous adventure. Whether you're aiming to complete the levels with minimal losses or simply trying to gather experience, every decision counts in your quest for survival. So, gear up and prepare yourself for one of the most thrilling stickman adventures you ve ever experienced!
