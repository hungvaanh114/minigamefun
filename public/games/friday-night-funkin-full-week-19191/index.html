With three new tracks, you'll find yourself grooving to fresh beats while trying to outsmart your uniquely skilled opponent.

Challenge your rhythm skills and unleash your inner rap artist as you compete against Chara in a battle for musical supremacy. Get ready to hit the stage, sharpen your reflexes, and enjoy the vibrant new content that Friday Night Funkin FULL WEEK! has to offer.
