Black Thrones

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the dark and thrilling world of Black Thrones, where you embody 'Black', a skilled assassin determined to reclaim his honor. Your journey unfolds on an endless wall, surrounded by relentless hordes of undead creatures that threaten everything you hold dear. As you navigate through this treacherous landscape, your agility will be put to the test as you leap over and slide under an array of daunting obstacles.

In this fast-paced action game, every encounter with the unyielding undead is an opportunity to unleash your combat prowess. Show no mercy as you eliminate enemies in your path, boosting your score with each successful takedown. The more foes you dispatch, the more coins you gather, providing you with the resources needed to upgrade your power-ups. Master these enhancements to bolster your abilities and enhance your fighting style as you face the encroaching darkness.

Your challenges are not just in combat; you must also strategize your movements to overcome various hindrances and maintain your velocity on the wall. As you traverse this dark universe, your determination to protect your loved ones will drive you to battle fiercely against insurmountable odds. Are you ready to take on the challenge and ascend the ranks of the fearless? Dive into Black Thrones now and prove your mettle as the ultimate guardian.

How to play free Black Thrones game online

To play Black Thrones, simply use the designated controls to jump over and slide under obstacles. Engage enemies by tapping your attack button, and collect coins scattered throughout the game. Keep an eye on your surroundings to maximize your score and upgrade your skills effectively!
