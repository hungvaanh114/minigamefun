How to play free Moto SkyRace Mayhem game online

To play Moto SkyRace Mayhem, simply use the arrow keys or WASD to control your bike s movement. Accelerate with the up arrow or W, and use the left and right arrows or A and D to steer. Jump over obstacles with the spacebar, and make sure to collect power-ups along the way for a speed boost!
