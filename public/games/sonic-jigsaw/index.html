How to play free Sonic Jigsaw game online

To play the Sonic Jigsaw game, simply select a puzzle image of your choice from the main menu. Once the image is displayed, you will see the pieces scattered around. Tap and drag the pieces to arrange them into the correct order. Keep an eye on the timer for an added challenge!
