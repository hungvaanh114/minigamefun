Zombie Strike 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Zombie Strike 2, players find themselves ensnared within a perilous farm overrun by relentless zombies. Your primary objective is to withstand five intense waves of undead onslaught, each wave presenting a greater challenge than the last. Equipped with a vast arsenal of weaponry, you must strategically fend off hordes of zombies, ensuring your survival amidst the chaos.

The gameplay is an adrenaline-pumping experience that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. From shotguns to sniper rifles, each weapon provides a unique advantage against the incoming waves of the undead. As you progress, the zombies grow stronger and more aggressive, making every moment a fight for survival. Use the environment to your advantage as you navigate through the farm, finding the perfect vantage points and cover spots while searching for better weapons and supplies.

Master the art of zombie annihilation and unlock powerful upgrades that will help you boost your chances against increasingly difficult foes. With thrilling graphics and immersive sounds, Zombie Strike 2 offers a captivating experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Can you survive the relentless zombie invasion and emerge victorious?

How to play free Zombie Strike 2 game online

To play Zombie Strike 2, use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to move around the farm. Aim with your mouse and left-click to shoot. Keep an eye on your ammunition and switch weapons as needed. Survive all five waves of zombies to complete your mission and secure victory!


Instructions

WASD - Move LMB - Shoot Mouse Wheel - Change weapons R - Reload P - Pause C - Crouch Space - Jump
