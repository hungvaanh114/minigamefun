Instructions:

Embark on a whimsical journey with the troll-faced stick figure in "TrollHead to Face". This thrilling adventure awaits as you maneuver through a challenging sky parkour filled with perilous obstacles and tricky challenges. Your mission is straightforward yet exhilarating: stay calm, keep your sense of humor intact, and race against the clock to survive.

The gameplay revolves around speed and precision. As you guide your troll-faced character through the vibrant sky parkour, your primary objective is to collect all the gold scattered throughout the levels. However, time is not on your side, so you must act swiftly to gather the treasure before it s too late. Each level gets progressively more challenging, testing your agility and reflexes.

To conquer this treacherous world, you ll need to master the art of running and jumping. Avoid pitfalls, outmaneuver obstacles, and ensure that you don't fall as you make your way to the elusive black portal at the end of each stage. Success requires a combination of speed, quick thinking, and a keen eye for the best paths to collect gold without compromising your safety.

Join the adventure today and see if you have what it takes to navigate the treacherous skies while keeping your cool and humor alive. Can you reach the portal and emerge victorious in the perilous parkour challenge? Find out now!

How to play free TrollHead to Face game online

Once you're in the game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your character. Quickly jump over obstacles and collect gold as you race towards the black portal. Stay focused and time your jumps carefully to avoid falling. Good luck! Enjoy playing!
