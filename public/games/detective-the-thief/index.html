Step into the world of intrigue and cunning with "Detective & The Thief," where you will help a diligent detective apprehend elusive thieves attempting to make off with bank treasures. With a team of bad thieves plotting their heist, it's up to you to assist the detective in tracking them down and bringing them to justice.

As the thieves cunningly hide behind walls, your role is to strategically discover the optimal path leading to them. Using your creativity, you'll draw lines that guide the detective on his quest. However, be cautious! The paths are fraught with many traps, demanding your absolute focus and clever decision-making. Remember not to merge paths, or the game will come to an abrupt end. Keep an eye on the color coding of the bags and the detective to stay on the right track.

This engaging game boasts high-quality graphics that ensure a visually appealing experience while maintaining a minimal file size for smooth gameplay. The super simple controls make it accessible to players of all ages, allowing everyone to immerse themselves in the thrilling world of sleuthing and adventure.

How to play free Detective & The Thief game online

To play "Detective & The Thief," use your mouse or finger to draw paths that guide the detective toward the thieves. Ensure you avoid traps and do not let paths collide. Follow the color codes to stay aligned with the game s objective and successfully catch the thieves without making mistakes!
