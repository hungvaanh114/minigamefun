Color Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Color Jump is an exhilarating reflex-based game that challenges your quick thinking and color recognition skills. In this fast-paced adventure, players must tap the screen to rotate a polygon and align it with an incoming colored ball. The objective is simple: match the color of the polygon's section with that of the ball before they collide. If you fail to make the match in time, it s GAME OVER, so stay alert and act swiftly!

The gameplay may seem straightforward, yet it tests your concentration and reaction speed. As you progress, the game accelerates, increasing the difficulty and keeping you on your toes. Every second counts as the anticipation builds for the next color to appear. With each successful match, players can revel in a sense of accomplishment, pushing their limits and striving for a higher score. How long can you endure in this colorful frenzy? Challenge your friends to see who can achieve the longest streak and become the ultimate Color Jump master.

How to play free Color Jump game online

To play Color Jump, simply tap the screen to rotate the polygon and match its color with the approaching ball. Ensure you complete the match before the ball makes contact with the polygon. The game tests your reflexes and color matching skills, so be ready for rapid changes and keep your focus sharp!
