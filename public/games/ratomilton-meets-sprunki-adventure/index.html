Ratomilton Meets Sprunki Adventure

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ratomilton Meets Sprunki is an exciting physics-based puzzle game where players must use their wits and strategic thinking to guide Rato Milton to his friend, Sprunki. Unlike Rato Milton, who depends on the environment to move, Sprunki stands completely still, waiting for Rato Milton to reach him. The challenge lies in manipulating the obstacles and objects in each level to set Rato Milton in motion, relying solely on physics and gravity to complete the journey.
