Christmas Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a festive journey this holiday season with Christmas Defense, an exciting tower defense game where your mission is to safeguard precious Christmas gifts from a relentless horde of marauders. As the holiday spirit hangs in the air, cunning thieves are scheming to steal the joy of Christmas by snatching away the beautifully wrapped presents.

In this engaging adventure, you will need to use your strategic skills to outwit the band of marauders. Set up an array of towers in various configurations to inhibit their advance towards the gifts. Each tower can serve a different purpose, whether it s slowing down enemies, dealing damage, or providing support to other defensive units. The key to success lies in thoughtful placement and clever upgrades that will enhance your defenses throughout the game.

As you progress, the waves of enemies will become increasingly challenging, requiring you to adapt your strategy in real-time. With each successful defense, you can earn points to unlock even more powerful towers and upgrades. Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score while keeping the essence of Christmas intact by protecting the gifts that symbolize joy and giving.

How to play free Christmas Defense game online

To play Christmas Defense, simply drag and drop towers onto the designated spots on the game map to block the marauders. Click on the towers to upgrade them as you collect points, ensuring they effectively fend off waves of enemies. Keep an eye on the path of the marauders and adjust your strategy accordingly!


Instructions

Click or touch towers to select them Once a tower is selected then click or touch the snowy areas of the game screen to place the towers Once the towers are placed click the start wave button to begin the next wave
