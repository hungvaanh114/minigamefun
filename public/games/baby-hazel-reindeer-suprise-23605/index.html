Instructions:

Hurray! Baby Hazel has received an amazing gift from Santa Claus: a baby reindeer to take care of for a few days. This delightful adventure invites players to engage with Baby Hazel as she embarks on a heartwarming journey of nurturing and caring for the little creature. Your primary objective is to ensure that the baby reindeer remains happy and healthy through a variety of enjoyable tasks.

In Baby Hazel Reindeer Surprise, players will assist Hazel in feeding the baby reindeer with scrumptious food that caters to its taste preferences. From carrots to oats, every meal serves to build a bond between Hazel and her new friend. Additionally, engaging in fun-filled games and activities makes this experience even more thrilling. Players must complete several animal care activities to keep the reindeer entertained and satisfied. Whether it's playing interactive games or attending to the reindeer s needs, every action enhances the nurturing journey.

As Hazel completes each task, her efforts will not go unnoticed. Santa Claus will definitely appreciate the care she shows towards his beloved reindeer. Step into this enchanting world and help Hazel create joyful memories while fostering a loving environment for the baby reindeer. It's a perfect opportunity to learn the importance of kindness and responsibility towards animals, all while having loads of fun!

How to play free Baby Hazel Reindeer Surprise game online

To play Baby Hazel Reindeer Surprise, use your mouse to interact with various objects on the screen. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete tasks such as feeding the reindeer, playing games, and ensuring it is happy. Complete all activities within the time limit for a successful game experience!
