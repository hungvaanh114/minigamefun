Duo Skibidi 2Player

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Two red and blue camera men need to break the toilets. The toilets have risen and want to be broken. Break the blue and red toilets, collect all the money. You must be very careful. Enemies are everywhere and each level is filled with challenges. You can lose at any moment. Break the toilets carefully. Break the blue and red toilets, collect the money, and move to the next level.

Instructions

Use the WASD and Arrow keys to move Jump on the toilets to break them Double jump available Collect all the money
