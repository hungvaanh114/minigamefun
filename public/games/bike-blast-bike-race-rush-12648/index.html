How to play free Bike Blast- Bike Race Rush game online

In Bike Blast, control your BMX bike by swiping left or right to dodge obstacles and tap to jump over barriers. Use power-ups wisely to maximize your score and perform tricks off ramps for bonus points. Collect coins and complete missions to unlock new characters and bikes as you dash through the vibrant environments!
