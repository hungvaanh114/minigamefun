Step into the chilling world of Slendrina Must Die: The House, where your courage will be put to the ultimate test. Tasked with uncovering the truth about the infamous Slendrina, players must explore a haunted house replete with dark secrets and frights. The central question looms: can Slendrina be killed, or is she impervious to your attacks? Prepare for a thrilling adventure as you seek out eight scattered pieces of a photograph that may reveal the key to her mortality.

This horror first-person shooter (FPS) game invites fans of the genre, particularly those who enjoyed Slenderman Must Die and Let's Kill Jeff The Killer, to immerse themselves in a terrifying realm filled with suspense and adrenaline. Equip yourself with an arsenal of three deadly weapons: a trusty gun, a powerful shotgun, and the lethal DeathSword for close-quarters combat. Each weapon is designed to elevate the thrill of the chase and ensure you have the means to confront this relentless creature.

With stunning 3D graphics that enhance the atmosphere, you'll find yourself fully engrossed in the dark hallways and eerie rooms of the house. Every creak and shadow heightens the tension as you search for clues, battle enemies, and ultimately uncover the fate of Slendrina. Are you ready to face your fears and confront the truth that lies within the darkness?

How to play free Slendrina Must Die: The House game online

To play the game, simply navigate through the foreboding house using the WASD keys for movement. Use the mouse to aim and left-click to shoot with your selected weapon. Collect the eight pieces of the picture scattered throughout the house to reveal Slendrina s deadly secret, all while staying vigilant for lurking threats.
