Skeleton Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Skeleton Hunter, the city has fallen into chaos as an army of skeletons rampages through the streets. As a courageous warrior, it is your duty to fight back and reclaim the city from these malevolent beings. Equip your trusty bow and prepare for an adrenaline-pumping adventure. Your primary goal is to swiftly defeat the skeletons that lurk in every shadow. The deeper you venture, the more relentless their attacks become.

With each skeleton you eliminate, new foes will emerge, increasing the challenge and excitement of your quest. To help you execute these undead foes with efficiency, you can utilize powerful staggers on the ground. This clever technique allows for a mass execution of skeletons, enabling you to take out multiple adversaries at once and establish control over the battlefield.

As you engage in this sacred mission, your reflexes and strategy will be put to the test. Can you withstand the onslaught and restore peace to the city? Every moment counts in this epic showdown against the skeleton horde, so gather your courage and get ready to unleash your skills. The fate of the city rests in your hands, and only the most fearless warriors will succeed in driving the skeletons back to their dark realm!

How to play free Skeleton Hunter game online

To play Skeleton Hunter, use your mouse to aim and shoot arrows at incoming skeletons. Keep an eye on your surroundings, as they will continue to swarm from all directions. Utilize ground staggers effectively to take out multiple enemies at once. Stay alert and be prepared for new waves of skeletons to appear as you progress!


Instructions

You can direct the character with W A S D and Mouse Use Mouse click to shoot
