4inROW

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

4inRow is an addictive strategy game where you match wits against an intelligent AI opponent in a race to connect four colored discs in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. In this digital version of the classic Connect Four concept, youll take turns dropping your pieces onto a 6x7 grid, while the AI strategically plans its counter-moves.

Instructions

With mouse left or right and drop with click on left button
