DinoZ

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of DinoZ, where intelligent dinosaurs bred for scientific research break free from their lab confines! An unforeseen accident in the laboratory results in a chaotic escape, with the crafty dinosaurs seizing scientists as their unwilling hostages. With the dense forest as their new playground, these cunning creatures have set up a series of traps, challenging anyone brave enough to rescue the captured scientists.

In DinoZ, you can embark on an exhilarating adventure either alone as a fierce solo player or with a companion in exhilarating "2 player" mode. Navigate through the treacherous forest, dodge the cleverly placed traps, and confront the intelligent dinosaurs to save the hostages. Each mission presents new challenges and surprises, requiring strategic thinking and teamwork, especially when playing with friends. Will you outsmart these prehistoric beings and successfully infiltrate their makeshift lair?

As you explore the game, gather resources, and develop your skills to enhance your chances of success. The more time you invest in understanding your surroundings and the behaviors of the dinosaurs, the better prepared you will be to execute a daring rescue! Are you ready to face the danger and save the day?

How to play free DinoZ game online

Once you're in the game, use the arrow keys or joystick to navigate your character. Approach the trapped scientists carefully, avoiding traps set by the dinosaurs. Communicate with your friend in multiplayer mode to coordinate strategies and maximize your chances of a successful rescue. Good luck!


Instructions
