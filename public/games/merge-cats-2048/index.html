Merge Cats: 2048!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

A game with a cute atmosphere! Compete with friends and colleagues! In the game you have a game zone where cat-balls fall and connect if they are the same! Connect as many cat-balls as possible to get the biggest one, collect points! Be the first on the leaderboard! Play and relax!

Instructions

1 At first it will be easy to connect the cat balls but over time there will be more and more of them
