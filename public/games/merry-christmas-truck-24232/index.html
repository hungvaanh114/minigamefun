Gather your family and friends and see who can solve these charming puzzles quickest! How to play free Merry Christmas Truck game online

To play the Merry Christmas Truck game online, simply select the level of difficulty you prefer. Once the puzzle appears, drag and drop the jigsaw pieces into their correct positions. Work at your own pace to complete the puzzle before time runs out, and enjoy the festive images as you progress through all twelve levels!
