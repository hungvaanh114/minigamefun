Fly Ghost

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the whimsical adventure of Fly Ghost, an entertaining game that combines simplicity with engaging challenges. In this unique arcade experience, you'll navigate a friendly ghost who glides through the air, skillfully avoiding a series of pipes without ever touching the ground. The objective is easy to grasp, but mastering the ghost's flight can prove to be quite a test of your reflexes and coordination.

In Fly Ghost, precision is key. You'll need to control the frequency of your screen taps to regulate the ghost s altitude and descent speed, allowing it to soar gracefully through the narrow gaps between pipes. As the ghost approaches the obstacles on the right side of your screen, stay focused and react quickly to ensure that it slips through without a hitch. However, beware! Any contact with the pipes results in a game over, adding an exhilarating layer of tension to every flight.

This captivating game challenges players to perfect their timing, as the ghost floats through increasingly difficult levels. With each successful pass, you'll earn a sense of accomplishment as you guide your ethereal companion along its airborne journey. So, are you ready to take on the challenge and help the ghost navigate its way through the treacherous pipes? Jump in and find out just how far you can go!

How to play free Fly Ghost game online

To play Fly Ghost, simply tap the screen to make the ghost ascend. The more frequently you tap, the higher the ghost rises. Conversely, pausing will cause it to descend. Your goal is to maneuver the ghost smoothly between the pipes that appear on the right side of the screen. Keep an eye on your timing to avoid collisions!
