Rainbow Frontline

Description:

"Rainbow Frontline" is a high-velocity, two-dimensional pixel-art shooter situated in a vividly colored, besieged intergalactic environment. Players assume command of a classic spacecraft, navigating asteroid fields and engaging alien adversaries in structured, wave-based combat scenarios. Successful progression through the thematic levels, ranging across the visual spectrum from red to violet, demands strategic mastery. Resource acquisition permits ship enhancements, including augmented survivability, transient defensive capability, and amplified offensive output.
