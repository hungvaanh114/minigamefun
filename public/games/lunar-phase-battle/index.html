Lunar Phase Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Play Lunar Phase Battle, a simple turn-based strategy game! Connect moon phases to claim tiles and score points. Both players can take each others tiles by creating pairs or cycles. Each captured tile adds to your score. Dive into celestial strategy and compete to outsmart your opponent!

Instructions

Tap or click on a moon phase card and then place it on a blank tile or drag the card directly to the tile Match cards to form pairs or cycles and claim the tiles for yourself
