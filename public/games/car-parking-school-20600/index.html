Car Parking School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Car Parking School, a thrilling car parking simulation game packed with myriad levels that challenge your parking skills. Test your precision as you navigate through various parking scenarios, mastering each challenge along the way. The objective is straightforward: maneuver your vehicle into the designated parking space without hitting obstacles or other cars.

One of the standout features of Car Parking School is the ability to create your own driver s license, adding a personal touch to your gaming experience. In addition, you can customize your vehicle by altering its paint color, adding spoilers, and selecting eye-catching rims. Your car can also be upgraded with enhancements like improved engines, upgraded brakes, and increased speed capabilities, making it not just a visual delight but also a performance machine.

The game offers five exciting modes to enhance your gameplay: Classic, Modern, Mission, Timer, and Snow. Each mode provides a unique experience, from traditional parking challenges to time-sensitive missions in slippery snowy conditions, ensuring that there s never a dull moment in this engaging parking adventure.

How to play free Car Parking School game online

To play Car Parking School, simply select a level, choose your vehicle, and start your parking challenge. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car into the designated parking spot. Be mindful of your surroundings to avoid collisions and complete each level within the given time limits.


Instructions

A W D - Control the car R - Switch gear C - Change camera H - Horn Space - Handbrake Esc - Pause game
