Instructions:

Welcome to MineRaid, an exhilarating shmup/arcade game where you take control of a specialized gold-seeking device as it navigates the depths of a treacherous mine. Your mission is simple but thrilling: uncover as much gold as possible while skillfully dodging numerous obstacles that shoot back at you from the shadows. Different levels of the mine hold various treasures, waiting to be discovered!

As you descend into the depths of the cave, your primary tool is a powerful laser, which can be activated by pressing the 'A' key. This laser not only helps you uncover hidden gold within the rocks but also allows you to defend against aggressive enemies lurking in the dark. Timing and precision are crucial, as you will have to quickly decide whether to focus on mining for gold or shooting down threats to your survival.

Keep an eye on your surroundings and utilize your agility to dodge incoming fire from obstacles and adversaries. The longer you survive, the more significant your potential rewards. However, make sure to manage your resources wisely; running out of health or failing to secure enough gold can end your adventure prematurely. Challenge yourself to see how long you can withstand the barrage of enemies and how rich you can become in the process!

How to play free MineRaid game online

To play MineRaid, simply control your device as it descends deeper into the mine. Use the 'A' key to activate your laser for mining and shooting. Navigate left and right to dodge obstacles while collecting gold. Your goal is to survive as long as possible while gathering as much treasure as you can!
