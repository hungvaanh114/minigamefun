Bubble Shooter: Classic Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Bubble Shooter: Classic Match 3, an addictive puzzle game that challenges your color-matching skills! In this engaging game, your mission is to shoot colored bubbles and create clusters of three or more identical bubbles. The combination of bubbles not only secures your victory but also maximizes your score, making each shot count.

As you embark on your bubble-shooting adventure, be careful with each shot if your bubbles fail to form a combination, you'll accumulate strikes. Accumulating too many strikes triggers the appearance of a new row of bubbles, intensifying the challenge. The goal is to prevent the bubbles from reaching the bottom of the screen, as this will lead to your game ending in defeat. Strategize your shots, plan ahead, and watch as the colorful bubbles pop in cascading delight!

Bubble Shooter: Classic Match 3 provides endless hours of entertainment with its straightforward yet compelling game mechanics. Each level is designed to test your skills and provide a refreshing experience. Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer to the genre, you'll find joy in both the simple mechanics and the thrill of victory as you watch the bubbles vanish!

How to play free Bubble Shooter: Classic Match 3 game online

To play, simply aim your bubble launcher towards the cluster of bubbles, aiming for a group of three or more of the same color. Click or tap to shoot your bubble, and watch it join the fun! Keep an eye on your strikes and act quickly to prevent the bubbles from descending to the bottom!


Instructions

On the computer - Use the pointer and left mouse button to shoot the bubble at the right place On a mobile device - Use your fingers to tap on the screen
