Tank Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tank Attack is an exhilarating action-packed game where your primary objective is to eliminate all rival tanks on the battlefield. Strategically navigate through the combat zone while keeping a close eye on your tank's health to ensure your survival. Engage in intense combat using an array of weaponry, including a standard firearm, a powerful machine gun, and the devastating napalm bomb. Each weapon offers unique advantages, so choose wisely based on your tactical situation.

As you maneuver your tank, you'll encounter various terrains and obstacles, including walls and barriers that can be used for cover. Use these structures to shield your tank from enemy fire while plotting your next move. Staying concealed behind walls can give you the strategic upper hand, allowing you to ambush unsuspecting opponents or evade incoming attacks effectively.

Throughout the game, your skill and agility will be put to the test as you engage in exhilarating battles. Formulate strategies to outsmart and outmaneuver your enemies while ensuring your tank remains intact. With each enemy tank you destroy, you'll feel the rush of victory and satisfaction that comes with being the master of the battlefield.

How to play free Tank Attack game online

To play Tank Attack, start by choosing your tank and weapons. Use the joystick or arrow keys to move your tank around the battlefield. Press the designated buttons to shoot your enemies with your gun or machine gun. Use napalm bombs to clear out multiple tanks at once, and always look for cover behind walls to protect yourself from attacks.
