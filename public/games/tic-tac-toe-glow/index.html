How to play free Tic Tac Toe Glow game online

To play Tic Tac Toe Glow, simply select whether you want to challenge a friend or play against the computer. Once the game starts, take turns placing your glow icon (either X or O) on the grid by tapping the screen. The goal is to align three of your symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before your opponent does!
