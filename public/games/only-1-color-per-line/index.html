Only 1 Color per Line

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing the captivating puzzle game "Only 1 Color Per Line," where your skills will be put to the test! The premise of this game is refreshingly simple yet engaging: your objective is to fill a grid with colors while adhering to a unique rule: you can only use the same color once in each line. This setup offers an exciting twist that is reminiscent of Sudoku, allowing players to think strategically and challenge themselves with each level.

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter various grid sizes, each presenting its own challenges. The grid will be filled with a selection of colors, and your mission is to arrange them without repeating any colors horizontally. The more levels you conquer, the more complex and rewarding the puzzles become. This game requires not only logical reasoning but also an eye for color coordination and problem-solving skills.

Are you ready to prove your prowess? Engage yourself in this colorful brain teaser that is not only entertaining but also a fantastic way to enhance your cognitive abilities. Whether you're looking to kill some time or to train your brain, "Only 1 Color Per Line" promises hours of delightful challenges!

How to play free Only 1 Color Per Line game online

To play the game, simply select a color from the palette and click on the grid to fill a cell. Remember, each color can only appear once per row, so choose wisely! Work your way through the grid until it's completely filled with unique colors in every line. Enjoy the challenge!
