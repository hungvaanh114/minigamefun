Magic Academy: Potion Making

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Magic Academy: Potion Making, where you can establish your very own mystical shop and spend your days creating potions that will amaze your friends and patrons alike. This captivating game immerses you in the life of a budding witch, where your brewing abilities will determine your success. With a variety of ingredients at your disposal, you ll experiment with different combinations to create powerful elixirs, magical brews, and delightful concoctions.

The journey to becoming a great potion-maker isn't without its challenges. In this magical school game, focusing on intricate details and honing your skills is essential. As you advance through different levels, you'll unlock more complex recipes, enabling you to craft an even wider array of potions. The game encourages creativity as you design and upgrade your shop, attracting clientele eager to sample your magical creations. With each potion made, you ll earn experience points that bring you closer to achieving the coveted title of Master Witch.

Engage in exciting quests that will test your potion-making prowess, and earn rewards for your efforts. Whether it s creating healing potions, invisibility drinks, or love elixirs, every successful brew will bring you one step closer to mastering the art of potion making. Join us in Magic Academy: Potion Making and discover the wonders of magical chemistry.

How to play free Magic Academy: Potion Making game online

To play Magic Academy: Potion Making online, select a potion recipe and gather the necessary ingredients from your shop. Follow the specific steps to brew your potion, paying attention to timing and mixing methods. Complete quests to level up and unlock new potions as you progress in your magical journey!
