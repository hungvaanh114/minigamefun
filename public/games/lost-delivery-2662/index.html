Lost Delivery

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you have a passion for driving games, then "Lost Delivery" is sure to captivate you! This thrilling game places you in the driver's seat of an electric van, where your primary objective is to locate and collect lost packages scattered along the winding road. As you navigate the challenging terrain, be mindful of your battery life, as you need to gather packages to recharge your power. With each successful pick-up, you ll not only get closer to your destination but also boost your chances of completing your delivery on time.

However, the journey is fraught with challenges. You must skillfully avoid other vehicles sharing the road, as collisions could jeopardize your mission and drain precious battery resources. Adding to the excitement, unexpected fog can envelop the scene, reducing visibility and testing your driving skills. Will your reflexes be quick enough to maneuver through the obstacles and ensure the timely delivery of packages? The game offers an exhilarating experience filled with fun twists and turns, making every moment a thrilling adventure.

How to play free Lost Delivery game online

To play "Lost Delivery," simply use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your electric van. Collect lost packages to recharge your battery while carefully dodging other vehicles and navigating through fog. Keep an eye on your energy levels to reach your destination successfully!
