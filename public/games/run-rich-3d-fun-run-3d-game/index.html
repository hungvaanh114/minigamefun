How to play free Run Rich 3D - Fun & Run 3D Game online

To play Run Rich 3D, simply tap and hold to navigate your character across the platform. Avoid obstacles to maintain your speed, while swiping to collect cash. The key is to gather as much money as possible before reaching the end of the platform for a chance to enhance your wardrobe!
