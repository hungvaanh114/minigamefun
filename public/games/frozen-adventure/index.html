Frozen Adventure

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delightful journey in the enchanting world of Frozen Adventure, where you step into the role of a charming little dragon. Your mission is urgent: rescue your adorable babies who have been frozen solid in a whimsical refrigerator. With your powerful fire breath, you have the ability to melt their icy prisons, but you ll need to strategize carefully to reach them!

In this engaging slider puzzle game, you will encounter a series of intricately designed puzzles. Each level presents a unique challenge, as you will have to rotate and slide the puzzle pieces to create a pathway for your flames to flow. The objective is to devise the perfect route that allows your fire breath to reach your frozen little ones, unleashing them from their icy confines.

With 50 levels packed full of exciting challenges, Frozen Adventure guarantees hours of addictive gameplay. As you progress, the puzzles become increasingly difficult, testing your problem-solving skills and creativity. The game also includes a fun goal: can you collect all the stars scattered across the levels? Mastering each puzzle not only brings you closer to your babies but also enhances your bragging rights as a puzzle champion!

Get ready to dive into this captivating adventure, unlock your dragon's potential, and save the day in Frozen Adventure!

How to play free Frozen Adventure game online

To begin playing Frozen Adventure, use your mouse or touch controls to rotate and slide the puzzle pieces. Align the pieces in a way that creates a clear path for your fire breath to flow towards your babies. Keep an eye on the star collection as you solve each puzzle!
