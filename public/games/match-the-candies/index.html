Match the Candies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to "Match the Candies," an engaging puzzle game that tests your strategic thinking and quick reflexes! In this delightful challenge, your objective is to move candy blocks from either the left or right, or even slide them up and down to create a cluster of three or more similar candies. The more candies you match, the higher your score!

Each level presents a unique set of candy blocks that you'll need to skillfully eliminate in order to clear the board. With a total of 30 challenging levels, "Match the Candies" offers hours of fun and brain-teasing excitement. Your ultimate goal is to wipe the board clean by removing all varieties of blocks, ensuring that no candy remains. Plan your moves wisely, as you must think ahead to create the perfect combinations that will enable you to progress through each stage.

As you advance, you ll encounter increasingly difficult puzzles that require clever strategies to overcome. Are you ready to embark on this colorful candy adventure? Put your matching skills to the test and see if you can conquer all levels!

How to play free Match the Candies game online

To play "Match the Candies," simply click and drag the candy blocks to form groups of three or more identical candies. Keep swapping and rearranging them until you create matches. Your mission is to clear each board by eliminating all the candy blocks within the limited moves available. Good luck!
