Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Astronaut Run 3D, a thrilling game where players sprint through the infinite expanse of the universe. As you navigate dazzling galaxies, your main objective is to dodge an array of daunting obstacles that include perilous meteor showers and rogue meteorites. To succeed, you must harness your agility and timing, employing nimble jumps and swift slides to steer clear of collisions that could end your cosmic journey.

But the excitement doesn t stop there! As you dash through the stellar landscapes, be on the lookout for unsuspecting passers-by. By bumping into them, you can gain additional speed and accumulate extra points to enhance your score. Your running skills will be put to the test, but with determination and quick reflexes, you can conquer the challenges that lie ahead. Don't forget reaching the finish line is crucial for completing your mission and advancing to the next level. Can you maintain your pace while avoiding danger and collecting points? The universe awaits your swift feet and daring spirit!

How to play free Astronaut Run 3D game online

To play Astronaut Run 3D, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to guide your astronaut. Jump over obstacles by pressing the jump button and slide under challenges by tapping the slide button. Remember to bump into passers-by for a speed boost and keep an eye on the finish line to complete your run!
