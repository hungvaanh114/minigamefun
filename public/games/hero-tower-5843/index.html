Hero Tower

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an epic journey with Hero Tower, where the stickman war beckons! Step into the shoes of a courageous hero and engage in thrilling battles across diverse landscapes. Your mission is to assemble a powerful team of heroes through strategic planning, leading them to victory against formidable foes. The battlefield is rife with challenges and obstacles, and only the strongest and smartest will prevail.

In Hero Tower, your adventure begins as you navigate a treacherous map filled with dangers and excitement. Drag your chosen heroes to explore new territories, unlocking unique powers that will aid you in combating powerful monsters. Your ultimate goal? To rescue the princess and restore peace to the kingdom! Along your journey, collect an array of items, including heroes, gold, and skins, which will help you enhance your tower and fortify your party against the waves of enemies.

Whether you re battling against waves of threats or building your ideal tower, every decision counts. The excitement ramps up as you plan your strategy and witness your heroes in action. With captivating graphics and engaging gameplay, Hero Tower invites players to dive into a world where courage and tactics blend seamlessly to create an unforgettable gaming experience.

How to play free Hero Tower game online

To play Hero Tower, simply drag and drop your heroes onto the battlefield to initiate combat. Gather resources and upgrade your heroes as you progress through various levels. Use strategic placements to maximize their abilities, defeat enemies, and collect treasures that help build your tower.
