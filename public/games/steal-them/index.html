Steal Them

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game "Steal Them," you step into the shoes of a strong and determined protagonist who is fueled by the desire for vengeance. Left to fend for herself after a harsh betrayal, she embarks on a daring mission to sabotage weddings across town. The focus of her wrath? All grooms! With only one goal in mind, she will do whatever it takes to prevent their union with brides, resorting to kidnapping to achieve her objectives.

Your character skillfully navigates through various locations, plotting and executing clever strategies to ambush unsuspecting grooms. Utilizing stealth and cunning tactics, she must evade the eyes of vigilant brides and other obstacles that could thwart her plans. Each successful capture brings her one step closer to retribution, as she seeks to dismantle the expectations tied to her former relationship.

The game is filled with challenges that require you to think on your feet and adapt to fast-paced scenarios. Can you outsmart the brides and ensure none of the grooms escape your clutches? Get ready for an exhilarating journey filled with witty dialogues, surprising twists, and captivating visuals. As you conquer one level after another, you will be drawn deeper into this enticing tale of resilience and revenge as she bravely takes on an entire wedding industry that wronged her.

How to play free Steal Them game online

To play "Steal Them," first, select your character and dive into the immersive levels. Use the arrow keys to move and the spacebar to interact with objects. Stay alert for brides, and strategically plan your moves to successfully grab grooms and escape. Good luck with your mission!
