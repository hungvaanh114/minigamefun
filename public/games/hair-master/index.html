Hair Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This is a hairstyle game for girls. Have you ever wanted to be a fashion hair designer? If you are interested in designing hairstyles, our game is designed for you. Firstly you can choose from a variety of interesting characters who are ready for a new look. Then you need to wash your hair with shampoo and water to get it ready for styling.
