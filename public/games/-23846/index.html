Vector is a thrilling arcade experience that immerses you in the life of a fugitive fighting against a repressive regime. Set in a stark totalitarian society, where freedom and self-expression seem like unattainable ideals, the game challenges you to break free from the chains of oppression. As a determined escapee, you'll need to navigate the urban landscape, utilizing the dynamic and exhilarating moves of Parkour.

The gameplay is fast-paced and adrenaline-pumping, as you run, jump, slide, and climb through intricate environments while evading relentless pursuers. Known as "Big Brother," these formidable adversaries are always on your tail, intent on recapturing you and suppressing your spirit. Your agility and quick reflexes are your greatest assets in this race for freedom.

Each level presents unique challenges that test your skills and push you to improve your Parkour techniques. Whether you are leaping over obstacles or deftly maneuvering through tight spaces, every moment counts. The thrill of the chase keeps you engaged as you strive to outsmart your enemies and uncover the depths of your own capabilities.

How to play free Vector game online

To play Vector, use the arrow keys or swipe gestures on your screen to control your character. Press the jump button to leap over obstacles, slide under barriers, and execute parkour moves by timing your inputs correctly. Focus on maintaining speed and fluidity to evade capture and conquer every level!
