Join Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Numbers is an engaging and addictive puzzle game that challenges your mathematical abilities. In this game, you will embark on a journey where you must navigate various gates marked with different numbers. Your main objective is to ensure that the number you are controlling is always greater than the number of the gate you are approaching. This adds a strategic element, as you need to carefully choose your movements to progress through the levels.

As you start your adventure in Join Numbers, you will be given an initial number, which you must maneuver through numerous gates along the road. Each successful pass through a gate will allow you to upgrade your number, making it larger and enabling you to tackle higher-numbered gates as you advance. The further you go, the more challenging it becomes, with increasingly difficult gates to overcome. The game not only tests your ability to think critically but also encourages you to improve your mental math skills.

Your ultimate goal is to explore and unlock as many levels as possible. Each level brings new challenges and demands better strategies to navigate through the ever-changing numerical landscape. So, put your math skills to the test and see how far you can go!

How to play free Join Numbers game online

To play Join Numbers, use your mouse or touch controls to move your number across the screen. Once you approach a gate, verify that your number is larger than the gate number before passing through. Continue this process to advance through levels, increasing your number and unlocking new challenges along the way.
