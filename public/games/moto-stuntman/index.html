Experience the thrill of motorcycle racing like never before in Moto Stuntman. This exhilarating game challenges your racing skills across a variety of tracks, each offering distinct terrains and dynamic environments. As you navigate these unique landscapes, be prepared to confront natural obstacles that will test your reflexes and precision as you race towards the finish line.

In Moto Stuntman, you have the chance to ride powerful motorcycles that enable you to perform breathtaking stunts. Execute jaw-dropping tricks and maneuvers to earn extra seconds, thereby improving your overall time. The game offers a dizzying adventure that takes you across the surface of the earth and plunges you into intricate depths, making each race an unforgettable experience. However, with great speed comes great responsibility remember to exercise caution as you race to ensure you navigate the challenges successfully and emerge victorious!

Whether you re soaring off ramps or trying to complete challenging courses, Moto Stuntman promises an engaging blend of adrenaline and excitement. Don't just race; become a stuntmaster and show off your skills as you compete against friends or seek to set your own records in this action-packed racing adventure.

How to play free Moto Stuntman game online

To play Moto Stuntman online, use your keyboard to accelerate, brake, and steer your motorcycle. Aim to navigate through the tracks, performing stunts to gain extra time. Keep an eye on the natural obstacles and make precise moves for the best racing experience. Good luck!


Instructions

The W key or the up arrow is gas The S key or the down arrow is the brake Using the A D keys or the right left arrows on the keyboard you can rotate the motorcycle and perform a somersault
