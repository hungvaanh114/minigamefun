How to play free Hula Hoops Rush game online

To start playing Hula Hoops Rush, use the arrow keys or swipe on your screen to move your character. Your goal is to collect the right-colored hula hoops while avoiding the dangerous lava pools. Gather as many hoops as possible to secure your title as the Hula QUEEN!
