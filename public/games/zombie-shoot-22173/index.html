Zombie Shoot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Zombie Shoot, where your aim is to eradicate waves of zombies threatening your survival. Armed with a powerful bazooka, you will strategically target your undead foes scattered across various terrains on the screen. Each zombie presents a unique challenge, often positioned in hard-to-reach spots. Your main objective is to launch explosive shells that will eliminate these grotesque creatures before they overpower you.

As you engage in this adrenaline-pumping action, mastering the art of projectile dynamics is crucial. You'll need to pay close attention to the trajectory of your bazooka shots, considering how they will travel and potentially bounce off surrounding objects. Timing your shots effectively can mean the difference between life and death, as the shells you fire will eventually detonate, delivering precise destruction to your enemies. Prepare for a test of skill as you navigate through the game, aiming to maximize your shots' impact while contending with the relentless advance of the zombie horde.

How to play free Zombie Shoot game online

To play Zombie Shoot, simply use your mouse to aim at the zombies lurking on the screen. Click the shoot button to fire the bazooka, adjusting your aim to account for the shells' bounce. Watch for the explosion to eliminate multiple zombies at once and ensure you stay alive!
