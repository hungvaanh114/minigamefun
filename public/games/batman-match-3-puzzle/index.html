How to play free Batman Match 3 Puzzle game online

To play the Batman Match 3 Puzzle game, simply drag and swap adjacent marbles to create matches of three or more of the same color. Aim for larger combos for extra points and try to complete each level's objective before time runs out. Good luck helping Batman!
