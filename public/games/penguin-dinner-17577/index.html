Penguin Dinner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Penny the Penguin has found herself lost in the icy expanse of Antarctica, and with no money to return home, she has to make her way back by opening her very own diner! In "Penguin Dinner," players embark on a delightful adventure with Penny as she navigates the bustling life of serving delicious meals to her fellow penguins.

Step into the shoes of Penny as you manage her diner, serving up tasty dishes to satisfy the appetites of hungry customers. Dive into the fun across four exciting diner locations, each with unique challenges and demands. As you progress, visit the shop to unlock a plethora of exciting upgrades that will enhance both your diner s appeal and your serving efficiency. Delight in the themed diner party packs that will add an extra layer of excitement to your culinary quest.

Dress Penny in a variety of adorable uniforms! Customize her look to keep up with the chic fashions of the Antarctic, and enhance your gameplay experience further. Along the way, enjoy a heartwarming comic-style storyline that captures Penny s journey, filled with charming interactions with the endearing inhabitants of the frozen world. With captivating visuals and immersive gameplay, "Penguin Dinner" provides a charming blend of fun and adventure, ensuring hours of enjoyment!

How to play the free Penguin Dinner game online

To play "Penguin Dinner," simply click on the start button to enter the diner. Use your mouse or touch controls to take orders from the penguin customers, cook their meals, and serve them quickly. Keep an eye on the money meter to ensure you earn enough tips to upgrade your diner! Enjoy the game!
