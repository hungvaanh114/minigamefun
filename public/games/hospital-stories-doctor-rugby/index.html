HOSPITAL STORIES: DOCTOR RUGBY

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Hospital Stories: Doctor Rugby, step into the shoes of a quirky physician ready to tackle the absurdities that come with treating a rowdy rugby team. Your mission is to address an array of bizarre injuries and ailments that are bound to leave you in stitches both literally and figuratively. Whether it's a player who has taken a tumble and needs their scrapes patched up or an unfortunate soul who has become a human pin cushion, your medical prowess will be put to the test.

The game is a delightful rollercoaster of humor and quick decision-making. As you advance through each level, you'll encounter a range of mini-games that challenge your reflexes and ingenuity. From bandaging up bruises to extracting unwelcome objects lodged in strange places, each task is infused with hilarity that keeps the gameplay fresh and entertaining. Your ability to think on your feet and adapt to the chaos will determine your success. Will you rise to the occasion and earn the title of the ultimate rugby team doctor?

With vibrant graphics and a charmingly whimsical atmosphere, Hospital Stories: Doctor Rugby promises not only laughter but also a twist on conventional medical games, all centered around the unpredictable world of sports injuries.

How to play free HOSPITAL STORIES: DOCTOR RUGBY game online

To play Hospital Stories: Doctor Rugby, simply use your mouse or touch controls to navigate through the various mini-games. Follow the on-screen prompts to perform medical procedures and address the players' injuries quickly. Complete each task within the time limit to unlock the next level, and enjoy the humorous surprises along the way!
