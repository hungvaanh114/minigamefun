Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Machine Gun Squad is a thrilling shooting game that puts you in the boots of a courageous soldier on a high-stakes mission. Your objective? To eliminate the notorious leader of a formidable terrorist group. This treacherous mission is fraught with danger, as you will encounter countless terrorists armed with a variety of weapons. Your skills and strategy will be tested to the limit as you navigate through intense combat situations.

As you progress in the game, you will receive much-needed support in the form of airdrops. These airdrops are vital, as they contain powerful weapons that can significantly enhance your chances of success. Whether it's a devastating machine gun or cutting-edge ammunition, every piece of equipment you obtain will be crucial in your fight against the enemy. Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-packed experience as you aim to take down the terrorist leader and restore peace.

Good luck, soldier! The battlefield awaits you, and only the bravest will emerge victorious against the overwhelming odds.

How to play free Machine Gun Squad game online

To play the Machine Gun Squad game, use your mouse or keyboard to aim and shoot at the terrorists while dodging their attacks. Collect the airdropped weapons and ammo to improve your firepower. Move strategically through the environment to evade enemy fire and stay alive as you complete your mission. Enjoy the action!
