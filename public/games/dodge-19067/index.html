Dodge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dodge is an exhilarating game that invites players to experience a new twist on traditional ball games. In this fast-paced adventure, your focus is crucial as you navigate through a vibrant world filled with challenges. Keep an eye on the screen, as you must respond quickly to avoid getting hit by the relentless wheels that come your way. One moment of distraction, and the ball meets its fate against the spinning gears game over!

As you progress, you have the opportunity to earn stars, which unlock a variety of unique characters that bring additional excitement to the gameplay. Each character carries its own unique traits and abilities, allowing for a more personalized experience that enhances your strategy in the game. You never know what surprises await around the corner, making every session thrilling and unpredictable!

This game is perfect for those looking to challenge their reflexes while enjoying a unique, engaging format that stands apart from typical arcade experiences. Dodge showcases a vibrant and colorful world where attention to detail is paramount. Are you ready to put your skills to the test and see how far you can go without getting hit?

How to play free Dodge game online

To play Dodge, simply use your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to maneuver the ball around the screen. Your goal is to avoid the incoming wheels while collecting stars and unlocking new characters. Keep your reflexes sharp and stay alert to navigate the obstacles successfully!
