Sumo Battle!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the amusing world of Sumo Battle!, where traditional perceptions of athleticism are turned upside down. In this unique game, you're not watching slender, chiseled athletes but rather large, rotund sumo wrestlers who embrace their size as a strategic advantage. Unlike conventional sports, the goal here is to bulk up by collecting sushi, which is a pivotal aspect of your character's journey. As you munch your way through an appetizing selection of sushi, you'll find yourself transforming growing in size and, consequently, in strength.

In Sumo Battle!, sheer size means power, and with enough sushi, you can become an unstoppable force on the ring. Your primary objective is to dominate the competition by throwing your opponents off the circular stage. As you approach your rivals, it's essential to strategically push them while avoiding their attempts to do the same to you. Use your size and superior mass to your advantage, and send your foes tumbling to the ground as they whirl into the targets affixed to their bellies.

This entertaining and challenging game not only tests your reflexes and strategy but also paints a humorous picture of the sumo wrestling sport, allowing for engaging gameplay that balances fun and competition. Will you rise as the champion of the sumo world or be left in the dust? Embark on your adventure of hefty wrestling and sushi gobbling in the whimsical realm of Sumo Battle!.

How to play free Sumo Battle! game online

To play Sumo Battle!, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character around the ring. Collect sushi to increase your size and strength. Once you re big enough, approach your opponents and push them towards the scoring targets on their bellies. Stay alert and avoid being pushed yourself!
