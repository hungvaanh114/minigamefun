In the thrilling world of Flat Out, a devastating epidemic has swept through the globe, transforming ordinary citizens into relentless zombies. As chaos ensues, the inhabitants of your town find themselves trapped, with only one escape route available: the perilous road leading out of town. It s a race against time and the undead, and you must harness your skills to survive this harrowing ordeal.

Your mission is clear become the ultimate zombie hunter by taking out as many of these brain-hungry fiends as you can before reaching safety. Armed with an array of weapons and your wit, you must navigate the treacherous road while fending off hordes of zombies determined to feast on your flesh. The stakes have never been higher, and every moment counts as you race against the clock to clear your path and lead your fellow residents to safety.

As you battle through waves of undead attackers, power-ups and new weapons will aid you in your quest. Timing, precision, and quick reflexes will be your best allies as you make split-second decisions to overcome the zombie menace. Are you ready to face the baying hordes and emerge as the ultimate survivor? It s time to put your skills to the test in a wild ride filled with adrenaline, strategy, and action!

How to play free Flat Out game online

To dive into Flat Out, simply launch the game and use your keyboard or controller to navigate your character. Move quickly to dodge zombie attacks while aiming your weapon to take them down. Remember to collect power-ups and stay vigilant as the zombie waves become increasingly challenging!
