Superhero Violet Summer Excursion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Violet on her thrilling summer adventure in the game, Superhero Violet Summer Excursion. As the sun blazes overhead, our intrepid superhero decides to escape the heat by heading to the beach for a day of relaxation and fun. However, before she can soak up the sun and sip refreshing drinks by the shore, Violet wants to pamper herself with an exciting beauty treatment!

In this engaging game, you ll help Violet prepare for her beach outing. Start by selecting the ideal skincare routine, where you can choose from various facial masks and treatments to give her a radiant glow. Once her face is fresh, dive into the world of face painting and hairstyles. Get creative by trying on different looks that enhance her superhero persona and match her vibrant personality.

After perfecting the beauty details, it s time to explore stylish outfits and chic accessories. Choose from a fabulous selection of summer dresses, swimsuits, and stylish sun hats, ensuring that Violet looks her best under the summer sun. To complete her transformation, you can add unique accessories like sunglasses and beach bags.

But the fun doesn't stop there! Customize Violet s bike with colorful decorations and modifications so she can ride in style to the beach. Once you re done with all the preparations, join her on her journey to the beach where a world of summer fun awaits!

How to play free Superhero Violet Summer Excursion game online

In the game, navigate through various sections by clicking on the available options. Start with the beauty treatment by selecting skincare products, then move to face painting and hairstyles. Finally, choose Violet's beach outfit and bike decorations. Use your creativity to customize her look and accessories before heading to the beach!
