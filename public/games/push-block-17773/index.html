Push Block

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to challenge your strategic thinking and dexterity with Push Block, an engaging puzzle game that will keep you on your toes! In this game, your task is to skillfully guide all the blocks to their designated locations using just your finger. But beware! The path is fraught with obstacles that could hinder your progress and may require you to rethink your moves carefully.

Throughout the game, you'll encounter various types of blocks and different environments, each presenting a unique layout of challenges. The key is to navigate your blocks efficiently while avoiding traps and barriers that can obstruct your route. Each level offers a fresh puzzle to solve, pushing the limits of your problem-solving skills and enhancing your gameplay experience. With intuitive controls and captivating graphics, Push Block immerses you in a world where every decision counts. Are you ready to push your way to victory?

How to play free Push Block game online

To play Push Block, simply swipe your finger in the direction you want to move the blocks. Your goal is to maneuver them to their designated spots without getting stuck. Keep an eye out for obstacles in your path and think ahead to outsmart the challenges. Have fun pushing these blocks and solving puzzles!
