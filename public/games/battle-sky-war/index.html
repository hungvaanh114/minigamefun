Battle Sky War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for explosive action as the legendary mobile blaster, Sky Force 2014, makes its triumphant return to celebrate its tenth anniversary. This scrolling shooter game promises an exhilarating experience, fusing the classic arcade shoot 'em up style with cutting-edge mobile technology. Back in the day, IGN praised its perfection, awarding Sky Force an impressive 9.5 out of 10.

Now, with a fresh wind blowing through its ten-year legacy, Sky Force 2014 is bolder, grander, and more vibrant than ever before. Players are greeted with stunning 3D graphics and intuitive touchscreen controls that make blasting enemy forces a delight. The revamped upgrade system enhances your combat skills, ensuring hours of exhilarating gameplay. However, the most exciting addition is the new social gameplay feature, allowing players to engage in weekly tournaments against fellow pilots, tackling new levels introduced each week.

This anniversary edition not only rewards competitive wins but also challenges players to rescue fellow pilots who have fallen in battle, adding an exhilarating layer of strategy. Join the fight and prove your skills amongst legions of other players while vying for fantastic prizes!

How to play free Battle Sky War game online

To play Battle Sky War, simply navigate through the levels using your touchscreen to aim and fire at enemy aircraft. Collect power-ups and upgrades during missions to enhance your abilities. Participate in weekly tournaments to test your skills against other players, aiming to rescue fallen pilots for bonus rewards!
