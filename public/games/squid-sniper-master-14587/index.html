Squid Sniper Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Squid Sniper Master, where traditional Korean games take on an exhilarating twist! In this action-packed sniper challenge, you are tasked with eliminating players who fail amidst the tension of a green light-red light game. With your sniper rifle in hand, you must remain vigilant identify and strike down those who dare to move when they shouldn t. Precision is key; hitting the correct target earns you points, while taking out innocent players will cost you dearly.

Your objective is to rack up the highest score possible by the end of the game, turning the experience into an intense competition among friends. Each game session not only tests your reflexes and aiming skills but immerses you in visually stunning 3D graphics and impressive sound effects that enhance the overall thrill of the hunt. As you navigate through various locations filled with challenging scenarios, the adrenaline rush is guaranteed to keep you on the edge of your seat!

The Squid Sniper functions as a versatile weapon, known for its long-range capabilities. Although it requires perseverance to accrue enough shark teeth to acquire, its unique design and powerful shots make it a coveted tool in your arsenal. With each fire, you launch a squid projectile, leaving a mesmerizing spinning trail truly a sight to behold. Use the expanded mode to strengthen your shots, and let your skills shine as you master this sniper game.

How to play free Squid Sniper Master game online

To play Squid Sniper Master, focus on the green light-red light game s rules: move only on green light! Use your mouse or touch screen to aim, and click or tap to shoot. Be precise to score points for hitting the correct targets, and practice to improve your skills and achieve high scores!
