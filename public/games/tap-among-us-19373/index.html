Instructions:

Tap Among Us is a captivating educational memory game designed to test and improve your memory skills. Players must watch closely as a series of characters appear on the screen. Among them is a gray traitor holding a knife. For a brief moment, players will see the traitor's location, and it's their task to remember it as the character transforms into an astronaut.

The challenge begins when the timer starts ticking down. Players must quickly identify and tap on the disguised traitor before time runs out. With each new level, the game becomes increasingly complex, introducing more astronauts that must be memorized 4, 5, or even 6 different characters. The escalating difficulty keeps players engaged while providing a fun way to sharpen their memory skills.

Throughout the game, players can select various upgrades to aid them in their mission. Options include extra time for memorization, additional seconds to make a selection, and extra lives. These upgrades can be strategically chosen to enhance gameplay, allowing players to advance through more challenging levels with greater ease. With its combination of fun, strategy, and cognitive training, Tap Among Us is perfect for anyone looking to improve their memory in an enjoyable way.

How to play free Tap Among Us game online

Once you're in the game, simply tap the screen to start. Observe carefully as the traitor's location is revealed for a few seconds. After that, identify the astronaut that corresponds to the traitor and tap on it before the timer expires. Use upgrades strategically as you progress through the levels.
