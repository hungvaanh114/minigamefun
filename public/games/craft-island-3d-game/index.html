Craft Island 3D Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Craft Island 3D, an enchanting new idle game that invites players to immerse themselves in a world of logging, building, and exploration. Set against stunning and diverse landscapes, you will embark on an exciting journey to become a prosperous island craft owner. Your adventure begins by chopping down trees, which serve as the fundamental resource for constructing various buildings.

As you progress, your quick thinking and creative skills will be put to the test as you strive to unlock different structures such as shops, rings, and other essential buildings. Each completed task brings you one step closer to expanding your island and increasing your wealth. The rings you unlock are particularly significant, as they allow you to enter the Breakout mode an exhilarating phase where you can gather more resources and establish your weapon camp.

In Breakout mode, the choice is yours. You can customize your army by selecting from a variety of archers or artillery soldiers. Strategy is key as you navigate through the island and maximize the resources at your disposal. Will you be able to declare victory and become the ultimate craftsman of Craft Island? The adventure awaits, and the potential for riches is boundless!

How to play free Craft Island 3D Game online

To play Craft Island 3D, start by gathering resources by cutting down trees. Use these resources to construct buildings and unlock new features. Once you have enough rings, access Breakout mode to build your weapons camp and recruit soldiers. Strategize effectively to optimize your gameplay and achieve victory!
