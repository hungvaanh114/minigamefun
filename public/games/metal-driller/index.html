Metal Driller

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Metal Driller is an exciting and entertaining casual mining adventure that invites players to immerse themselves in the depths of the earth. With a captivating blend of humor and challenge, this game allows you to take charge of a whimsical ragdoll character wielding a physics-powered drill, making each descent into the mine a unique experience.

As you explore the vast underground caverns, you'll encounter an array of obstacles and treasures waiting to be uncovered. As you advance through the levels, you ll unlock new challenges and upgrades that enhance your drilling capabilities. The game features a dynamic skill tree that resets after each chapter, giving players the chance to experiment with different strategies and build their character's abilities from scratch.

In addition to the strategic features, Metal Driller also lets you express your creativity. As you progress, you will earn cool Drill costumes that allow you to customize your character and their drill companions. Dress them up in various outfits that reflect your style, making your adventure all the more personal and fun.

Whether you are an experience gamer or a casual player, Metal Driller provides endless entertainment with its engaging gameplay, humor, and fun customization options. Dive into the world of mining and discover the treasures that await beneath the surface!

How to play free Metal Driller game online

To play Metal Driller, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to guide your character. Drill into the ground to collect resources, avoid hazards, and complete objectives. Keep an eye on your skill tree to enhance your abilities after each chapter, and enjoy customizing your drill's outfits as you progress through the adventure!
