Santa Racing

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

On Christmas Eve, a thrilling adventure awaits in Santa Racing! When Santa's sleigh unexpectedly loses control, it scatters gifts across the bustling city streets, and it s up to you to save the day. Jump into this fun-filled racing game where you ll guide Santa through a chaotic urban landscape filled with challenges and obstacles.

Your mission is to collect as many misplaced presents as you can while expertly navigating through oncoming traffic. As you race, be prepared to dodge speeding vehicles and avoid heaps of litter that could slow you down. Each level presents a more difficult challenge, with traffic increasing in speed and the placement of obstacles becoming more cunning. Stay sharp and quick on your feet, as the clock is ticking, and Christmas cheer depends on your skill!

With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, Santa Racing guarantees hours of entertainment. As you make your way through the festive streets, the stakes get higher, and only the most nimble players will be able to rise to the occasion. Put your reflexes to the test and help Santa navigate this merry mayhem. Will you collect all the presents and ensure that children everywhere wake up to a delightful Christmas? Grab the reins and dive into the holiday spirit!

How to play free Santa Racing game online

Once you're in the game, control Santa's sleigh using the arrow keys to maneuver left and right. Collect presents by driving over them while avoiding obstacles like cars and trash piles. Keep an eye on your score and aim to gather as many gifts as possible before time runs out!
