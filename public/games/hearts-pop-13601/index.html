Hearts Pop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Hearts Pop, our delightful new Valentine s-themed game designed to provide hours of fun and excitement! This colorful puzzle game invites players to connect and burst clusters of hearts in a vibrant array of colors. Your mission is simple: link three or more identical hearts for a thrilling explosion of joy!

As you dive into the game, you'll find a range of adorable hearts to match. Use your mouse or finger to drag and drop the hearts using a cupid arrow, meticulously placing them within the columns for maximum effect. The more hearts you connect, the more points you score, making every move vital to your success. But beware once the hearts reach the bottom of the columns, the game ends, challenging your skills and strategies with every new level.

Hearts Pop not only caters to your competitive spirit but also immerses you in a charming atmosphere, filled with the spirit of love and joy. Gather your friends and family to enjoy this inviting game where strategic thinking meets delightful fun. Whether you're a casual gamer looking for a quick session or a dedicated player aiming for high scores, Hearts Pop delivers a heartwarming experience for everyone!

How to play free Hearts Pop game online

To play Hearts Pop, simply drag and drop the hearts into the columns using the cupid arrow. Connect three or more hearts of the same color to pop them and earn points. Keep an eye on the descending hearts; if they touch the bottom, your game will end!
