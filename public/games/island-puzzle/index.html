Island Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the ultimate jungle puzzle adventure in "Island Puzzle"! Dive into a delightful world filled with vibrant colors and charming characters. Join a brave pilot and the adorable cat Tom as they navigate the challenges of a deserted island. Your mission is to help them thrive while unraveling the island's hidden secrets.

Embark on a journey where you can restore and beautify various locations on the island. Engage with a cast of whimsical characters and solve exciting matching puzzles. As you progress, you ll discover rare artifacts through your puzzle-solving skills. Combining curious pets and connecting them in long lines will grant you powerful boosters that can aid in your quest to gather precious items.

This game promises not just fun but also a chance to let your creativity shine by building your dream island for free! Every puzzle you solve brings you closer to unlocking new areas and exploring deeper into the mysteries of the jungle. So what are you waiting for? Dive in and become the hero of this enchanting story!

How to play free Island Puzzle game online

To play "Island Puzzle," start by matching colorful tiles in groups of three or more to clear them from the board. Use the available boosters wisely to overcome challenging levels. Collect artifacts and earn rewards for completing tasks. Enjoy the colorful graphics and engaging gameplay as you explore the island.
