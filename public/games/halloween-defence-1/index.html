Halloween Defence 1

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Halloween Defence 1, where the spine-chilling fun of Halloween meets an exciting challenge! As the pumpkin heads invade, your reflexes and aim become your ultimate weapons. The goal is simple: eliminate as many pumpkin heads as possible before they reach you. Each pumpkin head you shoot down earns you a valuable score point, and the action intensifies as you fight for survival.

Players are armed with two lives, meaning that you must be vigilant. Each time a sinister pumpkin head gets past your defenses, you lose a life. Once both lives are gone, the game comes to a heart-stopping end. But don't despair just yet! You have the chance to revive your journey. After losing your lives, you'll be presented with four pumpkin heads. Choose wisely, as one of them may contain an extra life that allows you to continue your defense against the relentless pumpkin heads.

This engaging game invites you to showcase your shooting skills and strategic thinking while confronting these fiendish foes. With each round, the intensity ramps up, providing a unique challenge that keeps you coming back for more. Are you ready to defend your territory and outsmart the encroaching pumpkin menace?

How to play free Halloween Defence 1 game online

To play Halloween Defence 1, use your mouse to aim and shoot at the pumpkin heads. Keep an eye on the incoming enemies; every successful hit adds to your score. If you lose a life, quickly select one of the four pumpkin heads to potentially earn an extra life and keep playing!
