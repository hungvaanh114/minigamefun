Idle Space Tower Defence

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to defend your space base against a relentless onslaught of enemies? In Idle Space Tower Defence, you ll step into the shoes of a commander tasked with protecting your cosmic stronghold. Assemble your defenses and strategically place towers to counter waves of invaders, all while you enhance your arsenal with incredible upgrades.

The game features exhilarating tower defense mechanics that keep you engaged and challenged. Players can choose from a plethora of tower capabilities, adapting their strategies as the waves of enemies become increasingly formidable. As you rack up victories, you will earn valuable coins that can be invested to permanently upgrade your towers, making them more potent against tougher foes.

One of the standout elements of Idle Space Tower Defence is the diversity of upgrades available. You can customize each tower with unique enhancements, allowing for a personalized defense strategy catered to your playstyle. Whether you prefer heavy artillery or rapid-fire defenses, the game offers ample options to refine and elevate your strategy. Prepare to engage in thrilling battles as you fend off countless enemies trying to breach your space base!

How to play free Idle Space Tower Defence game online

To start playing Idle Space Tower Defence, simply select your towers and position them strategically on the battlefield. Once you have your defenses set, monitor the enemy waves and use your earned coins to upgrade your towers between rounds. Adapt your strategy according to the strengths and weaknesses of incoming enemies for the best defense!
