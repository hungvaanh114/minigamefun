Violet My Little Girl

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Violet My Little Girl, an engaging game that offers a delightful experience! Begin your journey by waking up the adorable Violet, setting the stage for a day full of fun. Your first task is to wash her face using soap, ensuring she sparkles with cleanliness. After applying the soap generously, pick up the pink towel to dry her face gently.

Once Violet is freshened up, it's time to head to the bathroom where you ll fill the tub with warm water for a delightful bath. Watch as she enjoys her cleansing routine and emerges feeling rejuvenated. Cleanliness is important, and you'll feel accomplished as you ensure Violet is gleaming! With a bright smile on her face, it s time to journey to the kitchen.

In the kitchen, prepare a delicious meal for Violet. Don t forget her furry friend waiting patiently beside her make sure to feed the cat too! Balancing the needs of both Violet and her feline companion adds an extra layer of fun. If you're eager to explore more, simply click on the phone icon, which opens up exciting new levels and challenges to keep the adventure going!

How to play free Violet My Little Girl game online

To play Violet My Little Girl, follow the on-screen instructions to wake and care for Violet. Use the tools available to wash her face, give her a bath, and prepare meals. Click on items to interact with them and ensure that both Violet and her cat are happy. Discover new levels by pressing the phone icon!
