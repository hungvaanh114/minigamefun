My Puppy Daycare Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to My Puppy Daycare Salon, an adorable and engaging Caring game designed for animal lovers everywhere! In this delightful game, you embark on a heartwarming journey as you rescue an unfortunate stray puppy in need of help. With numerous wounds adorning its small body, your first task is to clean these wounds gently and prepare the puppy for a soothing hot bath.

Once you've given the puppy the care it deserves, it s time to create a cozy little nest for your new furry friend. Use your creative flair to design a unique and comfortable space where the puppy can feel safe and loved. After your puppy's fur is dried off, it s crucial to keep it warm and prevent any chance of a cold. This is where your stylish side comes into play!

You will have access to over 30 trendy accessories that you can choose from to dress your puppy in a way that reflects its personality. Mix and match outfits and accessories to find the perfect look, ensuring that your new companion is not just healed but also fabulously dressed!

How to play free My Puppy Daycare Salon game online

To play My Puppy Daycare Salon, begin by selecting the stray puppy you want to rescue. Follow the on-screen prompts to clean its wounds and give it a hot bath. Once cleaned up, design a nest for your puppy and select stylish outfits from the accessory menu to keep it warm and fashionable!
