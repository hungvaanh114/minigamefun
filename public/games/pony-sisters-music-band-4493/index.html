Join the enchanting world of Pony Sisters Music Band, where creativity and fun come together in an exhilarating experience tailored for boys and girls! To play the game, simply click on the various styling tools available on your screen. Choose the hairstyle, outfit, and makeup options you like best for each pony sister. Once you ve created the perfect look, click on the stage to start the rainbow pop show and enjoy the performances!
