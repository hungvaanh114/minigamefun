Ice Scream Horror Adventure

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chilling world of Ice Scream Horror Adventure, where a seemingly innocuous ice cream vendor wreaks havoc in your neighborhood. The story begins when the local ice cream seller, known as Rod, kidnaps your close friend and neighbor, Bob. You witness the horrifying event as Rod uses his mysterious powers to freeze Bob, and now it's up to you to unearth his sinister motives.

As you embark on your quest, the stakes are high. You must navigate through eerie environments to discover where Rod is taking Bob who knows how many other children might be trapped in his chilling plan? At first glance, Rod appears to be a friendly character, captivating the attention of kids with his sweet treats. However, beneath that cheerful exterior lies a deeply malevolent agenda that you must unveil.

As you explore, you'll gather clues, solve riddles, and potentially rescue other children who may be in Rod's grasp. Each corner you turn could unveil hidden secrets or lead you into a dangerous encounter with Rod the ice cream seller. Your wits will be your only weapon against his nefarious schemes as you strive to save your friend and uncover the truth about Rod's bone-chilling operations.

How to play free Ice Scream Horror Adventure game online

To begin playing Ice Scream Horror Adventure, use the arrow keys or joystick to navigate through the game environment. Interact with objects and characters by clicking or tapping on them. Pay attention to your surroundings to collect items and complete puzzles that will ultimately lead you to uncover Rod s horrific secrets and save Bob.
