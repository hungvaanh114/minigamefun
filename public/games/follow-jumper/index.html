Follow Jumper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Follow Jumper, a delightful and thrilling decompression game that not only provides a mental escape but also engages your reaction skills! Dive into an adventurous journey where you will navigate through daring challenges in order to conquer heights and free your companions. Players are tasked with skillfully generating stairs by pressing and holding the screen, allowing for a smooth ascent and descent through a beautifully designed tower filled with various obstacles.

As you traverse the tower, you'll encounter numerous challenges that test your reflexes. It's a race against time where quick thinking is essential to avoid blockers and ensure the safe rescue of your friends. Your ultimate mission is to reach the very top of the tower, where a fierce flying dragon awaits. Only those brave enough can vanquish this formidable foe and claim victory! Follow Jumper is perfect for anyone seeking a stress-relieving experience bundled with jumping thrills and obstacle evasion. Get ready to scale new heights and create monumental successes!

How to play free Follow Jumper game online

To play Follow Jumper, simply tap and hold the screen to build stairs for your character to ascend or descend. Avoid obstacles and make quick decisions to rescue your friends along the way. Aim to reach the top of the tower and defeat the dragon to complete your adventure!
