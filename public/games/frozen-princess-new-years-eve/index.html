Step into a magical evening with the game "Frozen Princess New Year s Eve." Join the delightful Rainbow High girls Skyler, Sunny, Ruby, and Violet as they prepare for a scintillating New Year s Eve celebration. This interactive experience allows you to browse through their stylish wardrobe, picking the chicest outfits for each character.

With a wide selection of dresses, shoes, and accessories at your fingertips, you can mix and match to create the perfect party looks. Don't forget to add a touch of sparkle with adorable makeup options! Transform the girls into the center of attention at this fabulous event, showing off your fashion sense and creativity.

Make this New Year unforgettable for the Rainbow High gang by utilizing your stylistic skills to surprise them. The more unique and festive their looks, the more fun they will have celebrating together. This engaging game not only tests your fashion expertise but also brings joy as you help your virtual friends get ready for their exciting night. Join the fun and make magical memories this New Year s Eve!

How to play free Frozen Princess New Year s Eve game online

To play the game, simply click on the icons to explore the girls' wardrobe. Use your mouse to drag and drop outfits, accessories, and makeup onto each character. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect New Year s Eve look. Enjoy the creative process and help the girls celebrate in style!
