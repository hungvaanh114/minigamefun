Sort Emoji

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the vibrant world of Sort Emoji, a captivating casual game that combines fun and challenge in a unique puzzle format. The gameplay revolves around sorting various emojis into their respective glass bottles. Your task is simple yet engaging: ensure that each bottle contains only one type of emoji. As you progress, the puzzles become increasingly intricate, providing a delightful break from conventional gaming experiences.

As you dive into the game, you'll encounter an array of colorful emojis, each with its own personality. With every level, you'll discover different sorting challenges that will put your problem-solving skills to the test. The satisfaction of organizing these distinct emojis into separate jars rewards not only patience but also strategy. The game's intuitive mechanics make it accessible for players of all skill levels, allowing anyone to pick it up and enjoy.

The simplicity of sorting emojis belies the complexity that the game brings. You may find yourself caught up in the fun of planning your moves and figuring out the best way to achieve a perfect sort. With charming graphics and delightful sounds, Sort Emoji promises an enjoyable escape from the everyday grind.

How to play free Sort Emoji game online

To play Sort Emoji, simply drag and drop emojis into the glass bottles. Each bottle can only hold a single type of emoji, so be mindful of your placements. Aim to fill each bottle with matching emojis while avoiding any mix-ups. Enjoy hours of fun as you strategize your sorting!


Instructions

You can only move Emoji to empty glass bottles or have Emojis of the same type
