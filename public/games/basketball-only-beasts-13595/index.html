Basketball Only Beasts

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the world of "Basketball Only Beasts," where legends are born, and skills are put to the ultimate test! Ever wondered who is the greatest basketball player of all time? Forget about the debates surrounding Jordan or LeBron; it's your chance to take center stage! This captivating 2D game invites you to showcase your basketball prowess and have a bundle of fun while doing it.

In this exhilarating game, your objective is simple: rack up points by making shots from short, medium, or long distances. Each stage presents a unique challenge, and you ll have 10 basketballs at your disposal to reach the scoreboard target. It s all about precision and timing, as you attempt to score the maximum points and establish yourself as a true beast on the court.

With colorful graphics and fluid gameplay, "Basketball Only Beasts" immerses you in the excitement of one of the world's most celebrated sports. Whether you re casually playing for fun or aiming to become the top scorer, there's something for everyone in this thrilling basketball experience. Are you ready to prove that you have what it takes to dominate the court? Pick up that ball, take aim, and become the finest player in "Basketball Only Beasts!"

How to play free basketball only beasts game online

To begin playing "Basketball Only Beasts," simply select your desired mode and hit play. Use your mouse or arrow keys to aim and determine the power of your shot. Release to shoot the ball and strive to score as many points as possible with the 10 shots provided in each stage. Enjoy the game!
