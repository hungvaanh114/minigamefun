Jewel Magic Xmas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you feeling the festive spirit? Dive into the enchanting world of Jewel Magic Xmas, a delightful turn-based triplet matching arcade game adorned with Christmas-themed decorations! Get ready to immerse yourself in a variety of challenging levels as you match triplets of vibrant jewels, solving engaging puzzles along the way. Each level is designed to test your skills, requiring strategy and quick thinking as you aim for the highest score.

But that's not all! Enhance your gaming experience with the daily spin of the lucky wheel, where you can earn powerful boosters that will aid you in your quest. These boosters can help you make strategic matches more efficiently and overcome those tricky challenges. Whether you're a casual gamer or a puzzle enthusiast, Jewel Magic Xmas promises endless entertainment filled with festive joy. So, gather your friends and family, and embark on this merry matching adventure together! Wishing you a delightful and colorful Christmas filled with jolly jewels!

How to play free Jewel Magic Xmas game online

To play the free Jewel Magic Xmas game online, simply use your mouse or finger to swap adjacent jewels and create sets of three or more matching jewels. Complete the objectives for each level by matching as many jewels as possible before running out of moves. Combine boosters for even greater effects, and enjoy the festive fun!
