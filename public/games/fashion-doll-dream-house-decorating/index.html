How to play free Fashion Doll Dream House Decorating game online

In the game, navigate through a variety of rooms by selecting them from the main menu. Choose from an array of wallpapers, flooring options, and furniture items by clicking on the icons. Drag and drop your selections into place, adjusting their size and orientation as needed. Experiment with different combinations until your dream house comes to life!
