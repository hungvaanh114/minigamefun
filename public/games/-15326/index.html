How to play free Subway Surfers Saint Petersburg game online

To play Subway Surfers Saint Petersburg, simply use the arrow keys or swipe on your touchscreen to control your character. Jump by tapping or swiping up, slide down by swiping down, and move left or right to dodge obstacles. Collect coins and power-ups to maximize your score as you run for your life!
