AURORAS BIRTHDAY BALL

Prepare for a magical celebration in AURORAS BIRTHDAY BALL, where the spotlight shines on Aurora as she celebrates her special day! Belle and Mulan find themselves in a delightful dilemma; they want to surprise Sleeping Beauty with the perfect birthday gift, but they're not quite sure what to choose. Your creativity and expertise are needed to help them brainstorm unique ideas that will truly make this occasion memorable!

As the lead stylist for the grand ball, your primary responsibility is to create a stunning look for the birthday girl. Dive into a world of vibrant colors, elegant gowns, and sparkling accessories. Choose the perfect outfit that reflects Aurora's personality while also ensuring she shines brighter than the stars on her special day. With your keen eye for fashion, you ll have the chance to mix and match various styles, from classic princess gowns to modern fairy-tale ensembles.

But that s not all! Engage in a variety of enchanting mini-games that will unlock new decorations, outfits, and surprise gifts for the birthday celebration. Interact with beloved characters from the Disney universe as they join in the festivities, making this event not just a birthday party, but an unforgettable adventure filled with friendship and laughter.

How to play free AURORAS BIRTHDAY BALL game online

To play AURORAS BIRTHDAY BALL, simply enter the game and follow the prompts to select outfits and accessories for Aurora. Use your mouse to drag and drop items onto her, ensuring that each choice matches your vision for her birthday look. Complete various mini-games to unlock special gifts and finishes to make the ball spectacular!
