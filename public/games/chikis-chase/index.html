Introducing Chikis Chase, a delightful casual game that invites you to join the adventure of a charming little character named Chiki. How to play free Chikis Chase game online

To play Chikis Chase, use your keyboard or mouse to control Chiki's jumps. Simply press the spacebar or click to make Chiki leap over obstacles and avoid enemies.
