Dalgona Squid Game 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling and whimsical world of Dalgona Squid Game 3D, where your dexterity and precision are put to the ultimate test! Inspired by the popular series, this engaging game brings you the challenge of delicately tracing intricate shapes on a piece of honeycomb candy. Each round, you'll be armed with a virtual pin, tasked to carefully carve out shapes without causing any damage. Precision is key here; even the slightest misstep could lead to cracking, and with it, severe consequences!

The clock is ticking, and time is of the essence. As you get immersed in the colorful graphics and immersive sounds, every second counts. Your ability to stay steady under pressure will determine your fate in this intense race against time, as you strive to extract the shape without leaving any cracks. The adrenaline rush intensifies as you progress through various levels, each presenting its own unique shapes and challenges. Are you up to the task of mastering the art of Dalgona candy carving while navigating the perils of the game?

How to play free Dalgona Squid Game 3D game online

To play Dalgona Squid Game 3D, simply use your mouse or finger to guide the pin around the inner edges of the selected shape. Aim to trace smoothly and accurately, avoiding any unnecessary cracks in the candy. Keep an eye on the timer as it counts down, and finish before it runs out to win the round!
