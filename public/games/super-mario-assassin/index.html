Super Mario Assassin

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Super Mario Assassin, step into the shoes of the beloved plumber as you embark on a thrilling mission filled with stealth and strategy. Your objective is to hunt down specific targets while avoiding detection in a world painted with familiar landscapes but shadowed by an unexpected twist. Use the vibrant environment to blend in and hide from sweeping flashlights that could expose your every move.

As you navigate through the levels, be cautious of your surroundings, as enemies are armed with automatic machine guns, lurking ominously around every corner. Timing and precision are key; strategize your strikes and launch surprise attacks to take out your targets swiftly before making your escape. Each successful mission will require sharp reflexes and keen tactical thinking to avoid being spotted. Will you guide Super Mario through a maze of dangers and help him succeed in his covert operations?

How to play free Super Mario Assassin game online

To play Super Mario Assassin, use the arrow keys to navigate Mario through the levels. Press the spacebar to silently eliminate enemies when you are close enough. Stay hidden in the shadows and plan your approach to avoid detection from the watchful guards. Complete your tasks and escape to advance to the next level!
