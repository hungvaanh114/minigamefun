Nu Life

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Nu Life, a delightful idle game where your mission is to engage with the adorable little aliens known as Nus charming, nose-shaped creatures hailing from the frosty planet Nu-2000. In this whimsical universe, the Nus thrive in cold environments and actually shrink in size when they enjoy the chilly temperatures.

Your journey in Nu Life involves utilizing an array of 10 unique cold turrets to shoot and shrink these cheerful creatures, adding an entertaining twist to your idle gameplay. The challenge lies in strategically placing these turrets and managing your resources effectively, all while ensuring the Nus remain joyful and content. The cute and colorful graphics make every interaction with the Nus a visual delight, enhancing your overall gaming experience.

As you progress through the game, you can explore the depth of its mechanics, including an innovative prestige system. This feature allows you to reset your progress after reaching certain milestones, offering rewards that enable you to advance even further upon your next playthrough. With each reset, you will be able to unlock new upgrades, leading to an even more enriching and enjoyable experience.

How to play free Nu Life game online

To play Nu Life, simply start your game and utilize your mouse or touchpad to aim and shoot your cold turrets at the Nus. As you hit them, watch them shrink and collect resources. Keep upgrading your turrets and engage with the prestige system to maximize your progress and create a more vibrant Nus community!
