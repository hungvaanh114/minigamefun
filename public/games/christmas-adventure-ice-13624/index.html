Christmas Adventure ICE

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ho-ho-ho! Welcome to the enchanting world of our Christmas Farm, where holiday magic awaits! Immerse yourself in a snowy paradise filled with beloved characters such as Santa Claus, Rudolph, a cheerful Snowman, a playful penguin, a helpful elf, and the mysterious Bigfoot! Your adventure begins as you evolve your own snow township, creating a magical landscape that thrives with festive spirit.

In this delightful realm, you'll plant and harvest an array of resources including chocolate, wool, paper, wood, and iron, which will enable you to build fantastic factories to craft gifts, toys, and sweets. Tend to delightful farm guests like Unicorns, raccoons, hares, and excited children who visit your farm. Your primary goal is to assist Santa in getting ready for the joyous Christmas holiday while managing your flourishing snowy farm.

Winter is here, and the North Pole is ready for exploration! Mingle with your favorite holiday characters, like the jolly Rudolph and charming Christmas fairies, as you take care of magical animals by feeding, brushing, and dressing them. Transform your magical farm into a charming township filled with festive cheer by crafting Christmas gifts and completing orders delivered via Rudolph s sleigh and the polar car.

As you decorate your snowy farm with vibrant flowers, lanterns, and hay, improve your Christmas tree to fit more presents beneath it. Log in every day to claim daily rewards, which will assist you in becoming the ultimate Christmas elf!

How to play free Christmas Adventure ICE game online

Once you're in the game, start by planting a variety of crops to gather resources. Use these materials to build and upgrade your factories for making gifts and toys. Complete orders from guests arriving by train, care for your animals, and decorate your farm to keep the festive spirit alive. Have fun and collect daily rewards!
