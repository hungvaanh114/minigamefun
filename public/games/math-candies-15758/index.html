Instructions:

Welcome to Math Candies, an engaging math puzzle game that challenges your arithmetic skills in a fun and interactive way. In this delightful game, your mission is to uncover the prices of various candies by tackling a series of equations. You'll need to apply your mathematical prowess as you deal with fundamental operations, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

The unique twist in Math Candies is that once you've successfully deduced the individual prices of the candies through solving the equations, you must then utilize these values to answer a simple yet rewarding Math question. This not only tests your problem-solving abilities but also reinforces your understanding of basic math concepts while providing a sweet twist to your learning experience.

With its colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, Math Candies is perfect for learners of all ages. Whether you're looking to sharpen your math skills or simply enjoy a fun game filled with sugary concepts, you'll find plenty of joy and satisfaction here. Challenge yourself and friends, and see who can solve the equations the quickest!

How to play free Math Candies game online

To play Math Candies, start by selecting a puzzle from the main menu. You'll be presented with equations to solve, focusing on basic math operations. Solve each equation to determine the prices of the candies. Afterward, use these values to answer the final math question and progress through the levels for more challenges!
