Mutant Legs Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the ultimate thrill with Mutant Legs Run, a captivating monster parkour game that will keep you on your toes! In this fast-paced adventure, you will embark on a quest where your mission is to collect legs scattered along the road. However, don't be fooled into thinking the journey is an easy one. The path is fraught with peril, including rolling gears, flying needles, and a barrage of sharp knives that will threaten to take your legs away!

This game is not just about running; it s about strategy and quick reflexes. As you navigate through challenging obstacles, you must time your movements perfectly to avoid the deadly traps lurking around every corner. Little monsters are eagerly waiting for your assistance to help them on their leg-collecting spree. Will you rise to the occasion and be their savior?

As you progress, you ll unlock a variety of skins, enabling you to customize your character with unique patterns and styles. Show off your creativity while tackling relentless challenges in this whimsical yet dangerous world. Each skin adds a fun twist, boosting your gaming experience as you dash toward victory!

How to play free Mutant Legs Run game online

To play Mutant Legs Run, simply use the arrow keys or swipe your screen to control your monster's movements. Collect as many legs as possible while avoiding obstacles like gears and flying knives. Unlock new skins as you advance to keep the gameplay exciting!
