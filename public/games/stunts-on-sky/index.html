Stunts on Sky

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Stunts On Sky, where adrenaline and high-flying excitement converge! This thrilling game brings you an epic car stunts challenge set against the backdrop of a massive cargo plane. Prepare yourself to take off from the city airport, transporting cars in your gigantic airplane while navigating tricky routes to ensure successful arrivals. Get ready to participate in one of the most exhilarating games you've ever experienced!

This game is not just about driving; it's about survival and competition. Cars will be launched from the cargo plane and must skillfully cross an army battleship. As you embark on this daring journey, you ll find yourself in fierce races against opponents who are ready to crash and take you out of the competition. You need to perform unbelievable stunts to stay ahead and reach your destination. This high-octane experience combines car racing, battling, and a unique twist that makes it stand out from other car stunt games.

Each level presents new challenges and risks, as you dodge enemies and aim to land safely on the drop-zone while executing extreme stunts. The battleship roads set the stage for thrilling jumps and heart-pounding action. With each jump, your fate is determined will you crash, or will you rise to the top? Only one car can prevail, and it s up to your skills to ensure it s yours!

How to play free Stunts On Sky game online

To play Stunts On Sky, navigate your car with the on-screen controls as you prepare for launch. Use acceleration to speed up, and master the timing of your jumps to clear obstacles and dodge opponents. Perform stunts in the air for extra points and aim to land safely in the designated drop-zone after each thrilling maneuver!
