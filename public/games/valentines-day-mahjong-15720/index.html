Instructions:

Fall in love with gaming all over again with Valentines Day Mahjong, a delightful twist on the classic Mahjong game! This enchanting version features a romantic theme perfect for celebrating love and connection. Your main objective is to eliminate pairs of matching tiles, revealing the hidden beauty beneath while immersing yourself in the Valentine spirit.

In this simplified yet engaging version of Mahjong, you can only select pairs with at least two adjacent sides open. This adds a strategic layer to the gameplay, requiring you to think ahead and plan your moves. As you race against the clock, every pair you match brings you closer to clearing the board. Hints are available to assist you when you're unsure of your next move, ensuring that you stay in the game even when faced with tricky tile arrangements.

You'll need to stay focused as you make connections between the matching tiles, all while the timer counts down. Can you clear the board before time runs out? If you're clever with your hints and manage your time well, you'll earn bonus points that can boost your overall score. Challenge yourself and see how quickly you can finish the game while enjoying the charming visuals of hearts and love-themed tiles!

How to play free Valentines Day Mahjong game online

To play Valentines Day Mahjong, begin by selecting pairs of identical tiles that are free on at least two adjacent sides. Click on the tiles to create matches and eliminate them. Use hints for difficult pairs and remember to keep an eye on the timer. Clear all tiles before the time runs out for the best score!
