Beehive Yourself 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Beehive Yourself 2, an enchanting and delightful adventure where you guide a charming little bee on her journey to the hive! Using your mouse cursor or touch controls, you'll navigate through challenging mazes filled with obstacles. Exercise caution, as the bee is prone to crash into walls, which can lead to unexpected setbacks!

Your mission doesn t end with merely reaching the hive; it's also about collecting sparkling stars scattered throughout the levels. These stars contribute to your overall score and can help you achieve that coveted perfect finish. Players of all ages will find joy in this charming, colorful world as they engage in a mix of skill and strategy to guide their buzzing buddy safely through each level.

As you progress, the challenges will intensify, with more complex mazes and additional hazards. Your goal is to navigate the bee through each level with finesse while ensuring she collects as many stars as possible. So, hover your cursor and get ready to embark on this sweet adventure full of creativity, enjoyment, and, of course, bees!

How to play free Beehive Yourself 2 game online

To play Beehive Yourself 2, simply control your bee by moving your mouse or using touch gestures. Guide her carefully through the maze, avoiding walls to prevent crashes. Collect stars along the way to boost your score and aim for the perfect finish in each level. Have fun buzzing around!
