Jumping Star

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Jumping Star is an incredibly engaging casual game that will keep you entertained for hours. In this colorful and dynamic world, your primary objective is to build your own unique zombie towers while guiding a cheerful boy as he leaps and bounds atop a delightful bouncy tofu trampoline. It's not just about the jump; it's about timing, strategy, and skill as you navigate through various challenges.

As you guide the lively character upward, you ll encounter an array of obstacles that emerge unexpectedly from both sides of the screen. To ensure a smooth and safer ascent, it s crucial to stay centered and make precise movements. The thrill of stacking zombies adds a dash of excitement, allowing you to create intricate structures that defy the laws of physics. Plus, you can challenge your reflexes and unlock the boy's amazing acrobatic tricks to impress your friends!

Whether you're looking to beat your high score or simply enjoy the vibrant graphics and quirky animations, Jumping Star offers an endless array of fun. Join the adventure, defy gravity, and see how high you can go while battling the endless stream of obstacles. Don t forget to level up your skills and strategies to achieve the ultimate jumping experience. Get ready to make your mark in this whimsical world of jumping fun!

How to play free Jumping Star game online

To play Jumping Star, simply control the boy's jumps using your keyboard or touchscreen. Avoid obstacles that come from the sides by positioning him in the center. Stack your zombie towers carefully and unleash special acrobatic moves to navigate the challenges. Aim for the highest score possible!


Instructions

Use Space and Up Arrow Key or W to Jump and avoid obstacles
