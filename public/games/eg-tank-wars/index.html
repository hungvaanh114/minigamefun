How to play free EG Tank Wars game online

In EG Tank Wars, control your tank using the arrow keys for movement and spacebar to shoot. Navigate the terrain to dodge enemy fire and ensure your survival. Collect power-ups to upgrade your weapons and employ tactical strategies to defeat your foes and claim victory!
