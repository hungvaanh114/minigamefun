Animals Connect

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the delightful world of Animals Connect, a captivating puzzle game reminiscent of the timeless Chinese tile removal game, Mahjong. In this engaging game, your objective is to clear all the tiles on the board. By finding and connecting identical tiles, you can eliminate them from play. However, be mindful that each connection can only take a maximum of two turns, challenging your strategic thinking and spatial awareness.

With 18 unique levels to conquer, Animals Connect offers varying levels of complexity and engaging gameplay that keeps players hooked. As you advance through the game, the difficulty increases, ensuring that you are always on your toes. Complete each level within the allotted time to score extra bonuses, which adds an exciting twist to this classic puzzle format.

The vibrant graphics and charming animal-themed tiles provide a delightful atmosphere, making for a pleasing gaming experience. The combination of fun and brain-teasing challenges makes Animals Connect an excellent choice for puzzle enthusiasts and casual gamers alike. So, grab your device and start eliminating those tiles in this delightful game that promises hours of entertainment!

How to play free Animals Connect game online

To play Animals Connect, simply click on two identical tiles on the board to connect them. Remember, your connections can have no more than two turns. Aim to eliminate all tiles before the timer runs out for bonus rewards. Enjoy strategizing your moves and solving each level!
