Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Bullet And Jump, where agility and quick reflexes are your best allies! In this high-energy adventure, bullets rain down from all directions, and your goal is to survive for a nerve-wracking 100 seconds. Choose to play as either the blue or red character, teaming up with a friend as you navigate this chaotic arena. The clock is ticking, and the bullets won t stop coming, so be ready to move!

Your mission is simple: dodge the endless barrage of bullets and outlast your opponent. Each time you make contact with a bullet, the game lengthens by an additional second, adding to the intensity of the challenge. Quick thinking, sharp movements, and a keen sense of timing are crucial to avoid those deadly shots. Compete fiercely against your friend and aim to come out on top; it s a battle of wits and reflexes!

Prepare for a showdown like no other, where each second counts and survival of the fittest reigns supreme. Keep your eyes peeled, and don t let those bullets catch you off guard, as you strive to be the last player standing. Can you resist the urge to jump into action and emerge victorious in this adrenaline-pumping encounter? Let the games begin!

How to play free Bullet And Jump game online

To play Bullet And Jump, move your character using the arrow keys or your controller. Dodge incoming bullets by jumping, avoiding direct hits, and outmaneuvering your friend. Keep an eye on the timer and enjoy the challenge of lasting the full 100 seconds without getting hit!
