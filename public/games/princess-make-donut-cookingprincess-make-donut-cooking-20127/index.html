Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Princess Mia on a delightful culinary adventure as she prepares to spend her weekend indulging in the art of homemade donuts! With a spark of creativity and a taste for sweetness, Princess Mia is eager to whip up a delectable batch of treats. However, there's a slight hiccup she's run low on essential ingredients for her donut recipe.

Your mission is to assist her in making this weekend special! First, you will embark on a shopping spree at the nearby grocery store to gather everything she needs for her baking project. Explore the aisles, select fresh ingredients such as flour, sugar, eggs, and sprinkles, and make sure to pick up all the necessary items to ensure her donut-making success. Once back home, it s time to transform those ingredients into mouthwatering donuts!

Follow along as you mix the dough, shape the donuts, and fry them to golden perfection. Get creative with various toppings and fillings, from rich chocolate glaze to colorful sprinkles, allowing Princess Mia s personality to shine through her culinary creations. The joy of cooking meets the thrill of decorating as you create scrumptious donuts that are sure to impress.

Help Princess Mia enjoy a sweet weekend filled with fun, friendship, and of course, delicious homemade donuts!

How to play free PRINCESS MAKE DONUT COOKING game online

To play the game, navigate through the grocery store to collect ingredients by clicking on items. After gathering everything, return to the kitchen. Follow the on-screen instructions to mix, shape, and fry donuts. Use your creativity to add fun toppings and showcase your baking skills!
