Welcome to the whimsical world of Running Chicken, a delightful parkour leisure game that promises hours of engaging gameplay. As you glide through various levels, you will encounter an abundance of shiny gold coins scattered along the way, just waiting to be collected. How to play free Running Chicken game online

To play Running Chicken, simply use your keyboard or on-screen controls to navigate your chicks. Jump over obstacles and collect coins by directing your character left or right. Stay focused, as timing is key to avoid falling off the ball and losing precious speed!
