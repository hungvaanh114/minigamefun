Fly Away Monk

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join BaBlah as he faces a winter cliff during a snowstorm! With an umbrella in hand, BaBlah flies off the cliff, gliding over the city. Navigate through icy winds and obstacles and enjoy breathtaking views. Experience the thrill of soaring above the winter landscape, guiding BaBlah. Can you help BaBlah make it through the storm and find his way to safety?

Instructions

Keep tapping or Mouse Click to make BaBlah rise up as he constantly falls down
