Stickman Duel Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating experience in the world of Stickman Duel Battle! This action-packed war game, the sequel to Stick War: Infinity Duel, introduces players to an adrenaline-fueled arena where quick reflexes and strategic thinking are essential. As you dive into the game, you'll need to stay alert for weapons descending from the sky, which can tip the scales in your favor. Your objective? Grab these weapons before your enemy does, and utilize them to eliminate their stickman.

The gameplay is designed to keep you on your toes. Weapons will fall at regular intervals, and each one you collect increases your chances of securing victory against your opponent. However, it's not just a matter of grabbing gear; you'll also need to maintain your balance on the ever-moving tracks. Being cautious not to fall while focusing on disarming your enemy adds an extra layer of challenge that will test your skills.

For those who enjoy a little competition, Stickman Duel Battle offers a 2-player option a perfect way to challenge your friends during a school break or just for fun. With dazzling visuals and smooth animations, Hihoy Games has crafted an engaging experience that you won t want to miss. Dive in and immerse yourself in the vibrant world of stickman warfare!

How to play free Stickman Duel Battle game online

To play Stickman Duel Battle, use your arrow keys to move your character around the battlefield. When weapons drop from above, navigate quickly to collect them. Aim using your mouse or touchscreen, and fire at your enemy to take them down. Utilize the environment to avoid falls and strategize your attacks!


Instructions

Player 1 Move - Arrow Keys Shoot - L Key Player 2 Move - WASD Keys Shoot - F Key
