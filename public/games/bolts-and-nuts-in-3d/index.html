Bolts and Nuts in 3D!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This game is a logic puzzle. You have to unscrew the bolts from the planks! Your task is to choose the right bolt to unscrew so that you can unscrew all the planks ! Compete with friends and colleagues ! Earn points, complete levels . At first, it will be easy to complete the levels, but each new level will be more difficult than the previous one! Prove that you are a pro at logical thinking, complete all the levels! Enjoy the game !

Instructions

The goal of the game is to unscrew the bolts from the planks Unscrew all the bolts from the planks so that they fall down Then you will pass the level
