Rescue the Whale

Rescue the Whale is an engaging point-and-click adventure game brought to you by 8B Games/Games2Mad. In this captivating tale, players find themselves in a quaint village, where a majestic whale has become ensnared. Your mission is to solve intriguing puzzles and uncover hidden objects to free this magnificent creature from its plight.

As you explore the charming village, you will encounter various challenges that require keen observation and clever thinking. Each puzzle you unravel brings you one step closer to rescuing the whale, making every second of gameplay both thrilling and rewarding. Will you rise to the occasion and save the whale? Your adventure awaits, filled with enchanting visuals and thought-provoking tasks.

With its beautifully crafted graphics and immersive gameplay, Rescue the Whale promises an unforgettable experience for fans of puzzle games. As you navigate through this interactive world, you'll discover secret spots, interact with quirky characters, and unlock tools necessary for your rescue mission. Prepare for an adventure where your wits will be put to the test. Enjoy the thrill of the hunt can you find all the hidden objects and use them wisely?

To play Rescue the Whale, simply click on any interactive areas to explore different locations and find clues. Use your mouse to collect hidden objects and solve puzzles.
