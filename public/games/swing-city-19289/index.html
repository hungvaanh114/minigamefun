Swing City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Swing City, an exhilarating physics-based adventure that challenges you to test your swinging abilities through a vibrant city. In this engaging game, you ll find yourself soaring from one skyscraper to another, deftly avoiding obstacles such as traffic signs, vehicles, and other unforeseen challenges. Your mission is to see just how far you can swing while navigating the bustling urban landscape!

As you swing through the city, you'll have the opportunity to collect coins scattered throughout the environment. These coins are not just for show; gather enough, and you will unlock various secrets, characters, and exciting prizes that enhance your gameplay experience. The city is filled with hidden treasures and surprises waiting to be discovered, so keep your eyes peeled for those special items!

Take on the role of a swinging hero as you master the mechanics and physics of the game. Each successful swing gets you closer to your goals and takes you further into the heart of Swing City. Are you ready to test your limits and achieve great heights? Dive into this thrilling experience today and find out just how far you can go!

How to play free Swing City game online

In Swing City, use your mouse or touch controls to launch and swing your character from building to building. Timing is crucial; release at the right moment to gain momentum and avoid obstacles. Collect coins along the way to unlock new characters and upgrades as you swing through the urban landscape!


Instructions

Use mouse click to swing and mouse move to slide from side to side
