Blockapolypse Zombie Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter a world of chaos in Blockapolypse: Zombie Shooter, a gripping defense shooting simulation game featuring 3D pixelated undead foes. As waves of zombies approach from three different directions, your mission is to protect your territory using an arsenal of weaponry. The game's pixel-art graphic style adds a unique charm, immersing you deeply into its challenging environment.

As you eliminate the relentless hordes of zombies, prepare for an even more formidable opponent a massive undead creature that awaits your challenge. Surviving this relentless onslaught requires accurate aim and quick reflexes. Your health points (HP) are your lifeline, and should they reach zero, your adventure ends. Collect gold coins from your victories to unlock superior weapons in the gun shop, enhancing your combat prowess and boosting your chances of survival.

With each successful defense, you will face increasing levels of difficulty, testing your skills and strategy. The addictive gameplay keeps you on the edge of your seat as you strive to improve your techniques while strategizing on how to fend off increasingly difficult zombie waves. Good luck and prepare to survive the Blockapolypse!

How to play free Blockapolypse Zombie Shooter game online

To start playing, control your character using the arrow keys or WASD, and aim using your mouse. Click the left mouse button to shoot and eliminate the incoming zombies. Keep an eye out for gold coins to collect after defeating enemies, which you can use to purchase better weapons when visiting the gun shop. Stay alert and plan your defenses wisely!
