Halloween Defence2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating Halloween experience in Halloween Defence2, where you must defend yourself against a relentless onslaught of mischievous pumpkin heads! As the spooky season approaches, these quirky foes are out for blood or at least trying to reach you! Your primary goal is to shoot down as many pumpkin heads as possible, racking up points with each successful hit. The tension rises with every passing moment as you find yourself cornered by the looming threat of these animated gourds.

In this fast-paced shooting game, you start with two lives. Each time a pumpkin head breaks through your defenses and reaches your character, you lose one life. The stakes are high, with only two chances before your game ends. It's crucial to keep your composure and focus as waves of pumpkin heads swarm towards you, eager to test your reflexes and accuracy. Can you achieve a perfect score by meticulously eliminating your pumpkin-shaped adversaries, or will your Halloween night be marred by failure as you lose both lives? The challenge is set, and only your quick thinking and precise aim can ensure victory!

How to play free Halloween Defence2 game online

To play Halloween Defence2, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the incoming pumpkin heads and click or tap to shoot them down. Keep an eye on your lives in the corner of the screen; if two pumpkin heads reach you, the game will conclude. Stay vigilant and aim for a high score!
