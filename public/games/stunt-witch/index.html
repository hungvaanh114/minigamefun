Stunt Witch

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you poised to become the next legendary Stunt Witch pilot? In this thrilling game, you'll need nerves of steel and remarkable precision to navigate the challenging levels ahead of you. As you progress through the game, the difficulty ramps up, often requiring you to retry certain levels multiple times before achieving success.

Your primary objective is to gather all the stars scattered throughout each level, ensuring that you skillfully fly through the hoops without crashing. However, keep a close eye on your fuel gauge: you may find yourself needing to replenish your fuel by collecting potions during your adventurous flight. Are you ready to take flight, little witch?

How to play free Stunt Witch game online

To play the Stunt Witch game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your witch through the challenges. Remember to expertly navigate through hoops and collect stars while managing your fuel. Gather potions whenever you see them to keep your fuel tank full and maintain your flight. Good luck!
