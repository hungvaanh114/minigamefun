Simulation Racing Car Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of racing with the Simulation Racing Car Simulator, brought to you by Broken Diamond. This sequel to the popular mobile parking simulator takes car driving to a whole new level with its breathtaking graphics and multi-stage challenges. Engage in intense vehicle parking sessions and show off your driving prowess in this completely immersive game.

Unlike any other car parking game, this one is designed for both casual players and serious enthusiasts. The unique features will keep you hooked as you master the art of parking in various environments. It incorporates realistic physics that make maneuvering through obstacles genuinely challenging yet rewarding. With 480 distinctive levels available, you ll find yourself endlessly entertained while refining your skills as a parking ace.

As you progress, you ll encounter diverse missions that not only enhance your parking abilities but also teach you about real-world traffic signs like 'no parking,' 'one-way,' and directional warnings. Remember to dodge obstacles because colliding with them means restarting the level from the beginning, adding an extra layer of excitement to the gameplay.

The Simulation Racing Car Simulator features adjustable camera angles and zoom settings, allowing you to find the perfect view for every parking task. Whether you prefer the steering method or tilt controls, this game offers you multiple options to suit your driving style. Plus, enjoy the freedom of playing offline, making it a perfect time-killer anytime and anywhere!

How to play free Simulation Racing Car Simulator game online

To get started, simply choose your vehicle and select a level. Navigate your car through the parking lots by steering with your device or using on-screen controls. Pay close attention to the traffic signs and avoid obstacles to succeed. Aim to park your vehicle accurately before the time runs out and enjoy unlocking new cars and levels!
