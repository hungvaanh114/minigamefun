Welcome to the enchanting world of Candy Manor! Step into a delightful adventure where you can renovate a charming mansion while solving captivating puzzles. This free match-3 game combines relaxation and excitement, allowing players to create their very own candy manor and restore it to its former elegance.

Join Mary, a passionate designer who returns to her hometown to participate in the annual candy festival. However, she discovers that the old house, rich with sweet memories, holds many hidden secrets. With a vision to revive the grandeur of the festival, embark on a journey to unveil the mysteries that lie within Candy Manor. Together with Mary, you will dive into a world filled with vibrant colors and whimsical decorations.

Experience a plethora of unique match-3 levels packed with delicious candy elements and an array of intriguing props. The game features innovative gameplay that encourages you to swap matching candies to complete levels and unlock various tasks. As you progress, you'll remodel different sections of the estate and uncover hidden areas such as a quaint backyard, a lovely balcony, and even a cozy home theater.

Adopt a charming pet, a cat or dog of your choice, to accompany you on your adventure. Make friendships with a diverse cast of characters, each with their own fascinating stories to tell. Your perfect home design awaits, so why wait any longer? Download now and start on your creative journey in Candy Manor!

How to play free Candy Manor - Home Design game online

To play Candy Manor, simply match three or more candies of the same type to clear levels and earn rewards. Complete tasks by redecorating rooms and uncovering hidden areas. Interact with characters and manage your sweet mansion as you progress through the story. Enjoy the thrill of solving puzzles while creating your dream home!
