Platform Paint 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Platform Paint 3D, where art and adventure intertwine! In this unique game, your mission is to paint over the platforms to reveal striking geometrical forms that are concealed in shadows. With over 100 specially designed platforms to traverse, each level offers a fresh challenge and a chance to express your creativity.

As you progress through the vibrant landscapes, you'll have the opportunity to unlock exciting bonus levels filled with colorful surprises. In addition, you can collect 5 different balls, each adorned with eye-catching skins in delightful shapes. The beautiful hues and stunning aesthetics of the game will immerse you in a playful experience as you unveil the hidden artwork that lies beneath the surface.

Get ready to engage your senses with the delightful visual palette as you splash your way through the fun and interactive levels. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a newcomer, Platform Paint 3D guarantees an exhilarating time packed with both fun and challenge.

How to play free Platform Paint 3D game online

To play Platform Paint 3D, use your mouse or touchpad to control the painting tool. Simply click or swipe across the platform to paint and reveal the hidden shapes beneath. Make sure to explore every corner to uncover all the geometric figures and collect your colorful rewards!
