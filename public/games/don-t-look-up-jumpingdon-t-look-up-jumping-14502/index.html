Prepare for an exhilarating adventure in Don't Look Up: Jumping! A catastrophic meteor is hurtling toward our planet, and the brilliant Dr. Mindy is your only hope for salvation. With his quick thinking, Dr. Mindy devises a daring plan: to escape from the impending doom by leaping onto the fragments of the gigantic meteor that threaten our beautiful world. Your mission is to help Dr. Mindy ascend higher into the sky, minimizing the impact of the meteor as he climbs.

But beware! The journey is fraught with peril and great dangers lurk below. As you guide Dr. Mindy upward, timing and precision are essential. Every leap must be executed perfectly to avoid the hazards that threaten to bring him crashing down. You'll need to gather your wits and be ready for rapid movements; every second counts in this race against time! Are you prepared to take on the challenge and assist Dr. Mindy in reaching safety? The fate of the world lies in your hands!

How to play free Don t Look Up: Jumping game online

In the game, use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to jump between the meteor fragments. Avoid falling by timing your jumps carefully and collect power-ups to enhance your skill. Keep an eye on Dr. Mindy s height, as your goal is to ascend as high as possible and prevent the meteor from impacting the earth!
