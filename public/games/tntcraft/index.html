TNTcraft

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

TNTcraft is an exciting two-player game set in a vibrant Minecraft universe, where teamwork and strategy are key to success. Your primary objective is to utilize TNT to demolish the zombie spawner while dealing with an onslaught of zombies that emerge from it. Collaborate with your friend to devise clever tactics, ensuring that you efficiently coordinate your attacks and explosions to maximize your impact against the relentless undead. However, caution is essential, as some zombies are equipped with explosive traits that can deal significant damage if they come too close.

The game features four unique maps, each offering diverse terrains and challenges that enhance the gameplay experience. Navigate through these environments, using your surroundings to your advantage while you create mayhem with your TNT. The more strategic your planning, the better your chances of obliterating the zombie threat!

TNTcraft combines elements of action and strategy, making each round thrilling as you and your partner work together to outsmart the brain-hungry hordes. With explosive capabilities at your fingertips, you'll need to stay alert, communicate effectively, and think on your feet to survive. So gather your friend, gear up, and get ready to blow some zombies to smithereens in this fast-paced adventure!

How to play free TNTcraft game online

To play TNTcraft, join the game with a friend and select your preferred map. Once the game begins, coordinate with your partner to place TNT strategically near the zombie spawner while fending off zombies. Keep an eye out for the exploding zombies and avoid getting too close while preparing to detonate your TNT!


Instructions

Player 1 WASD - Movement T - Place TNT Player 2 Arrow Keys - Movement P - Place TNT
