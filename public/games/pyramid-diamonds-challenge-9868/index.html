How to play free Pyramid Diamonds Challenge game online

To play the "Pyramid Diamonds Challenge," simply swipe to match three or more diamonds of the same color. Strategically plan your moves within the time limit to maximize your score. Keep an eye on power-ups that can help you clear the board faster and heighten your overall score!
