Fruit Sort

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Fruit Sort, where your sweet tooth meets an exciting puzzle challenge! Have you ever wondered what flavor of candy cane you prefer? Now is your chance to craft the perfect candy cane tailored precisely to your tastes! In Fruit Sort Puzzle, you will embark on an engaging adventure that combines fun and strategy.

Your mission is simple: move and sort various fruits on the board. By combining three or more identical fruits, you can upgrade them and create an assortment of vibrant candies. The ultimate goal is to focus on producing dazzling hawthorn candy canes. But be cautious! If the number of fruits surpasses the maximum limit allowed in the fork, you'll face failure. So, stay sharp and manage your fruits wisely! Can you master the art of candy cane creation? Believe in your skills and enjoy the sweet journey ahead!

As you immerse yourself in this colorful puzzle challenge, let your love for fruits and sweets guide you. Enjoy the satisfaction of merging fruits and watching your candy canes come to life. Get ready to have a blast while honing your sorting abilities and achieving sweet victories along the way!

How to play free Fruit Sort game online

To play the Fruit Sort game, simply drag and drop the fruits on the board to combine three or more identical types. When you successfully match them, they'll upgrade and contribute to making your hawthorn candy canes. Keep an eye on the maximum fruit limit to avoid failure!
