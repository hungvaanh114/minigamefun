Dice Pixel Stealer 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dice Pixel Stealer 3D combines elements of strategy and physics to offer an engaging arcade experience featuring 3D dice and stickman figures on a captivating platform. The core of the game revolves around a pixel pattern situated at the center of this platform, which serves as your target. Your mission is to skillfully launch dice, each throw generating a set number of stickman allies to aid you in your quest.

As you toss the dice, they will roll and bounce, potentially colliding with enemy stickman figures, which will be eliminated upon impact. The mechanics of the game invite players to think strategically about their throws, as the aim is not only to summon enough stickmen but also to maximize destruction and collect over half of the pixel pattern to achieve victory.

The vibrant 3D graphics and dynamic physics create an immersive environment where every throw counts. The challenge escalates as you progress, requiring careful planning and precise timing to ensure a higher chance of success. Like a puzzle to be solved, the game keeps you engaged as you work to outsmart your opponents and capture the colorful pixel pattern.

How to play free Dice Pixel Stealer 3D game online

To play Dice Pixel Stealer 3D, simply click to launch the dice and generate stickman allies. Aim for enemy stickmen as the dice roll, causing destruction. Strategically position your throws to maximize damage and efficiently collect pieces of the pixel pattern. The game continues as you accumulate more stickmen with each launch!
