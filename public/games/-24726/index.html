Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Test your knowledge of geography with the exciting World Flags Trivia! In this engaging quiz game, you will be presented with various flags from around the globe. Your task is to identify the corresponding nation from three possible choices for each flag displayed. With a collection of 100 unique questions, the game becomes progressively challenging, ensuring that you will continue to sharpen your skills with every level you advance through.

As you dive into the world of flags, you'll not only enhance your recognition of national symbols but also expand your understanding of countries and their unique identities. Ideal for both geography enthusiasts and casual players, this trivia game provides an entertaining way to learn about world flags while having fun. Challenge yourself, compete against friends, or simply enjoy the thrill of mastering flag identification. Will you rise to the occasion and achieve a perfect score? There s only one way to find out! Play now and put your geographic knowledge to the test.

How to play free World Flags Trivia game online

To start playing World Flags Trivia, simply choose the correct flag that corresponds to one of the three country options shown. Click on your chosen answer to submit your response. Keep an eye on the timer as you race to complete all 100 questions and aim for the highest score possible!
