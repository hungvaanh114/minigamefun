Bubble Shooter Rainbow

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a vibrant adventure with Bubble Shooter Rainbow, a delightful puzzle game featuring your favorite characters from Disney shows! In this colorful game, you ll find yourself tasked with popping bubbles to rescue beloved characters that are trapped within. The universe of Disney awaits as you embark on a series of engaging levels, each designed to challenge your skills and enhance your puzzle-solving abilities.

As you play through iconic worlds inspired by classics like Mickey Mouse, DuckTales, and Disney's Zombies, you will encounter an array of colored bubbles. The goal is straightforward: find groups of at least two bubbles of the same color and click on them to burst them. Each level sets specific color-related challenges, so make sure to focus on the required colors while keeping an eye on the number of moves you have left.

But that's not all! Throughout the game, you'll come across special bubbles that act as power-ups. Clicking on these can allow you to pop and clear multiple bubbles simultaneously, giving you an edge as you progress. The game is designed to keep you entertained, encouraging multiple playthroughs with its extensive levels and captivating gameplay. Trust us, once you start playing, you won't be able to stop!

How to play free Bubble Shooter Rainbow game online

To play Bubble Shooter Rainbow, aim and shoot bubbles by clicking on the screen. Match at least two bubbles of the same color to pop them and meet the level objectives. Be mindful of your limited moves and look for special power-up bubbles that can help you clear the board faster!
