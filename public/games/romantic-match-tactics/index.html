How to play free Romantic Match Tactics game online

To play Romantic Match Tactics, start by selecting adjacent items on the grid. Your goal is to align three or more like items together, which will clear them from the board. Utilize your limited moves wisely to unlock locked items and successfully complete each level's challenge.
