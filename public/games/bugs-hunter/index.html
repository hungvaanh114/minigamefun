Bugs Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bugs Hunter is an engaging bubble shooter game with a unique twist. In this captivating adventure, players take on the role of a clever spider tasked with capturing pesky bugs heading straight for its web. Your main objective is to strategically drop strands of silk to ensnare these insects before they reach your spider line.

The game features a diverse array of bugs that approach from multiple directions, each displaying varying speeds and behavior patterns. This variety poses a thrilling challenge, requiring quick reflexes and sharp aim as you fire your webbing in all directions. Make sure to stay alert, as the urgency increases with each passing moment if three bugs cross the designated spider line, your game will come to an unfortunate end.

As you progress, you'll encounter increasingly difficult levels that elevate the excitement and demand more precision and skill. Bugs Hunter combines colorful graphics and captivating sound effects, enhancing your gaming experience as you battle against these relentless bugs. Whether you're a casual player or a bubble shooting veteran, Bugs Hunter promises fun and exhilarating moments that will keep you engaged for hours. Engage in this riveting challenge and see how many bugs you can catch!

How to play free Bugs Hunter game online

To play Bugs Hunter, aim your web-shooter towards the approaching bugs and click to launch your silk. Adjust your aim to catch bugs from all directions. Keep an eye on the spider line and act quickly to prevent three bugs from crossing it. Enjoy capturing as many bugs as possible!


Instructions

Tap the game icons on the screen to play the game or use the arrow keys and the space bar to shoot
