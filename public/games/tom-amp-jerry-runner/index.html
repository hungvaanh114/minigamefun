Tom Amp; Jerry:Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Winter has arrived, and Jerry the mouse is hungrier than ever! In Tom & Jerry: Runner, players join Jerry on an exhilarating mission to collect as much cheese as possible while evading the watchful eyes of his nemesis, Tom the cat. With the classic cat-and-mouse chase as the premise, players can immerse themselves in three captivating game modes: the classic mode, an endless runner, and an exciting color-matching challenge.

With over 100 levels filled with cheese-themed escapades, every stage presents new challenges and delightful surprises. Navigate through different environments such as the cozy living room, the sprawling garden, and the shadowy attic. Each locale is meticulously designed to enhance your gaming experience while providing numerous obstacles for Jerry to dodge. Use your quick reflexes to jump over traps, steer clear of rockets, and manage other perilous hazards along the way.

To give players an edge, the game features collectible bonus cards that offer helpful boosts and power-ups. Each card can help Jerry stay one step ahead of Tom and increase the overall score. As you play, strategize your moves and utilize the various objects scattered throughout the levels to ensure Jerry s survival in this thrilling chase.

How to play free Tom & Jerry: Runner game online

To play Tom & Jerry: Runner, simply use the arrow keys or swipe your finger on touch devices to control Jerry. Collect pieces of cheese while avoiding obstacles and traps. Activate bonus cards when necessary to gain temporary advantages, and remember to keep an eye on Tom to avoid getting caught!
