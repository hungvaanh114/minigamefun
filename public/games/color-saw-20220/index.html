COLOR SAW

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of destruction in COLOR SAW, a captivating game where you wield a powerful saw to cut through vibrant 3D cubes. As one of the top color block cutting games of 2020, COLOR SAW invites players to drag and maneuver colorful cubes against the intimidating 3D saw blade. Your mission? Remove all unwanted blocks while skillfully avoiding any contact with your own blocks.

Unleash your creativity as you shape the rough cubes into stunning formations utilizing various cutting tools. With countless levels to master, each stage presents new challenges that will test your precision and strategic thinking. The satisfaction of seeing those blocks fall apart as you slice through them is immensely gratifying, making this an addictive gaming experience for fans of colorful 3D gameplay. If you thrive on vibrant visuals and enjoy an engaging and unique gameplay mechanics, COLOR SAW is the perfect game for you.

So gather your friends and get ready for an exhilarating journey of cutting and smashing through an array of entertaining levels. Let the fun begin and see how far your skills can take you in the colorful world of COLOR SAW!

How to play free COLOR SAW game online

To play COLOR SAW, simply drag your mouse or swipe your finger to move the colored cubes toward the 3D saw. Ensure that you only cut the unwanted blocks while avoiding your own. Use different tools to shape the cubes as you progress through each level, maximizing your score and enjoyment!
