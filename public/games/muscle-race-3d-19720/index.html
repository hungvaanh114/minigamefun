Muscle Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Muscle Race 3D, an arcade game that challenges players to navigate through a thrilling track filled with barriers like walls and swimming pools. Your objective is simple yet engaging: collect as many dumbbells as you can to increase your strength and performance. The more dumbbells you gather, the more capable you become of overcoming the formidable obstacles that lie ahead in each level.

As you race against three AI competitors, speed and strategy are of the essence. You need to devise the best path to collect dumbbells swiftly, ensuring that you maintain your lead against your opponents. Each hurdle presents a unique challenge that tests your reflexes and determination. Will you rise to the occasion and emerge victorious, or will the obstacles thwart your ambitions?

Muscle Race 3D is designed for players of all skill levels, providing an exciting mix of competition and fun. The vibrant graphics and addictive gameplay will keep you coming back for more as you strive to increase your muscle power while competing against AI players. Gear up for an immersive racing experience where strength and speed are the keys to success!

How to play free Muscle Race 3D game online

To play Muscle Race 3D, simply use your keyboard or touchscreen to control your character. Move around to collect dumbbells scattered along the track while avoiding walls and swimming pools. The key to victory is to collect as many dumbbells as possible and reach the finish line ahead of your AI opponents!
