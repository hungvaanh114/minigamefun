Zombie Terror

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Zombie Terror plunges you into a chilling first-person shooting experience where survival is the only objective. A sinister biochemical outbreak has wreaked havoc across your city, transforming it into a nightmarish zombieland. As the deadly virus spreads, hordes of flesh-eating zombies emerge, leaving chaos in their wake. Everyone in the city has succumbed to this horrific fate except for you and a few others who seek refuge.

Faced with the terrifying reality that the undead are now lurking just outside your door, you must act quickly. They devour anyone they can find, turning them into more of their kind, and now they re threatening your sanctuary. It s a fight for survival as your home becomes the last bastion against the undead hordes. Will you be able to protect yourself and the few survivors who have come to you for safety?

Prepare yourself for intense shootouts, strategy, and adrenaline-pumping action in Zombie Terror. Your every decision counts in a world where danger lurks around every corner. Grab your weapons, fortify your home, and take a stand against the seemingly endless waves of zombies that want nothing more than to turn you into one of them.

How to play free Zombie Terror game online

To play Zombie Terror, navigate through the game using the WASD keys for movement and aim your weapon with the mouse. Press the left mouse button to fire and use the spacebar to jump. Collect weapons and supplies as you fend off incoming zombies, and work to keep yourself and your allies safe at all costs.
