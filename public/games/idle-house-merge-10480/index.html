Instructions:

Have you ever dreamed of being the mayor of your very own city? With Idle House Merge, that dream can become a reality! Step into an enchanting world where you can create your own thriving metropolis. Start by merging buildings to expand your city and unlock new lands teeming with potential. Each building you merge generates income, allowing you to reinvest and scale up your city s infrastructure. From quaint houses to towering skyscrapers, every combination brings you closer to creating a vibrant urban landscape.

As you progress, you ll encounter various challenges and opportunities that will make your city unique. Strategically planning your layout and merging effectively will be key to maximizing your earnings. The aesthetic design of the game introduces you to a variety of architectural styles that enhance the charm of your city. With every new land you unlock, new features and buildings become available, enriching your gameplay experience. Build parks, businesses, and residences that will attract citizens and contribute to your city s growth.

Idle House Merge offers accessibility and enjoyment for players of all ages, making it easy to dive into the world of city management. With its addictive mechanics and visually appealing graphics, the game keeps you engaged as you watch your city transform from a small settlement into a bustling metropolis. Can you manage resources wisely and keep the city thriving? Jump into the adventure and let the merging begin!

How to play free Idle House Merge game online

To play Idle House Merge, start by tapping on buildings to merge them. Combine two of the same kind to create upgraded structures, and earn money for each merge. Continue merging, unlocking new areas, and expanding your city to achieve a larger metropolis!
