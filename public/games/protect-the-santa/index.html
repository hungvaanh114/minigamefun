Protect the Santa

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a whimsical winter adventure with Protect the Santa, a hilarious yet challenging Christmas-themed shooting game that will keep you on your toes! Your mission is to follow Santa Claus on his exciting journey while defending him from a horde of mischievous snowmen determined to spoil the festive cheer. Armed with your trusty snowball launcher, you must fend off these frosty foes as Santa collects all the Christmas gifts scattered throughout the snow-covered landscape.

The game ramps up in difficulty as you progress, testing your reflexes and precision shooting skills. Each level presents new obstacles and more snowmen looking to thwart Santa's quest. But fear not! If you successfully help Santa gather every single gift, the magical igloo will unlock the gate to the next level, where even more surprises and challenges await.

With its delightful graphics, catchy holiday soundtrack, and exciting gameplay, Protect the Santa is sure to bring joy and laughter to players of all ages! Whether you're an experienced gamer or someone looking to enjoy a fun Christmas-themed challenge, this game offers the perfect blend of humor and strategy. So gear up, take aim, and make sure to protect Santa at all costs to keep the Christmas spirit alive!

How to play free Protect the Santa game online

To play Protect the Santa, use your mouse or touchscreen to aim and shoot snowballs at the approaching snowmen. Keep an eye on Santa s progress as he collects gifts and defend him from any threats. Work quickly to clear the path and advance through each festive level!


Instructions

WASD keys to move left mouse button to shoot Shift run Space jump R reload
