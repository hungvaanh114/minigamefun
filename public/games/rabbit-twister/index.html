How to play free Rabbit Twister game online

To play Rabbit Twister, use your mouse or keyboard to control the rabbit's hops. Tap or click at the right moment to make the rabbit jump onto the bridges. Collect bonuses along the way while avoiding gaps and obstacles. Aim for high scores and compete with friends to see who can jump the farthest!
