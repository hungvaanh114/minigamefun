How to play free Bubble Pop Shooter game online

To play Bubble Pop Shooter, simply aim your bubble shooter at the desired bubble color on the board. Match three or more bubbles of the same color to clear them from the screen. Use the arrow keys or touch controls to adjust your aim, then release to shoot. Keep clearing bubbles until the board is empty while accumulating points and collecting boosts!
