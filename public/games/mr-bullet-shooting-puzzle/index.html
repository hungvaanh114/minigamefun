How to play free MR Bullet: Shooting Puzzle game online

To play Mr. Bullet: Shooting Puzzle, use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at targets and release to shoot. Carefully calculate the angle and power needed to hit all the targets in each level. Keep an eye on obstacles and avoid getting hit yourself to advance through the game.
