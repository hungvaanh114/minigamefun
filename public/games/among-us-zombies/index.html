Among Us Zombies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Among Us Zombies, an exhilarating game where you take on the role of a protector tasked with defending your city from waves of relentless zombie invaders. Your mission is to safeguard the city s inhabitants while systematically eliminating the monstrous Among Us creatures that threaten your peaceful existence. The gaming arena is displayed prominently on your screen, giving you a strategic view of the chaotic battlefield ahead.

As the undead horde advances towards your city s gates, you have access to an array of special bows strategically placed along the city walls. These powerful bows are your primary tools for defense, and you'll need to skillfully lead them to fend off the incoming zombies. The challenge lies in accurately calculating both the trajectory and power of your shots. The precision of your aim is critical; a well-placed arrow can send a zombie reeling, earning you valuable points towards your overall score.

Accumulating points is essential in this game. As you embark on your mission to eliminate zombies, the more monsters you dispatch, the higher your score will climb. Once you gather enough points, you'll unlock the ability to purchase a variety of new weapons. This feature keeps the gameplay fresh and engaging, as you can adapt your arsenal to tackle more daunting waves of enemies head-on.

How to play free Among Us Zombies game online

To start playing Among Us Zombies, launch the game and use the mouse to aim at the incoming zombies. Adjust the power and angle of your shot, then click to release your arrow. Keep track of your score, and once you've earned enough points, head to the shop to upgrade your weapons for tougher challenges.
