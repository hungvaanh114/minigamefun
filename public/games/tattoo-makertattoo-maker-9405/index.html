Tattoo Maker is an exciting project designed to deliver a simulation experience like never before. Currently featuring a basic game loop, I'm dedicated to expanding the game with a comprehensive shop system and numerous engaging features. One aspect that has caused some confusion is the scoring system. The purple design on the client's skin serves as a reference tattoo stencil, which you must replicate by matching it to the tattoo design shown in the upper left corner of the screen.

Are you ready to unleash your inner rebel? Ever wondered what it feels like to create a tattoo? Now you can dive into that experience without any of the pain! Your mission is to fill in hundreds of different tattoo designs for your eager customers. Aim to make your artwork as close to the reference as possible, but be careful not to make any mistakes! Enjoy the exhilaration of transforming your designs into real-life tattoos!

As you embark on this artistic journey, you'll be tasked with using precision to maneuver the tattoo needle and fill in shapes accurately. The game offers countless satisfying vibrations and easy-to-grasp mechanics, but one slip could ruin your masterpiece. Challenge yourself to be the first to tattoo them all, choosing from hundreds of objects that require careful sizing, outlining, and inking for complete customer satisfaction.

Game Features:

  • Simple yet addictive mechanics: Experience the joy of tattoo artistry without the pain, as you fill in tattoo designs at your own pace.
  • Run your tattoo shop: Attract a wide range of customers with a diverse selection of tattoo designs.
  • Stay within the lines! Tackle challenging shapes and see if you can complete each level flawlessly.
  • Feel the experience: Enjoy incredible haptic feedback with every needle tap for an immersive sensation.

How to play free Tattoo Maker game online

To start playing Tattoo Maker, simply choose a design from the available options. Once in the game, use your mouse or touchscreen to carefully trace the needle along the stencil, filling in the ink precisely. Pay close attention to the reference image to ensure accuracy, and enjoy the satisfaction of completing each tattoo!
