How to play free Chain Cube: 2048 game online

To play Chain Cube: 2048, use your mouse or finger to swipe left, right, up, or down, controlling the direction in which the cubes fall. Aim to align and merge the same-numbered blocks. Watch their chaotic movement closely to maximize your score and keep the game going!
