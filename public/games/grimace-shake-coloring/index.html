How to play free Grimace Shake Coloring game online

To play the Grimace Shake Coloring game online, simply select an image that you would like to color. After that, choose your desired colors from the palette provided. Use your mouse or finger to fill in the areas of the image as you wish. If you want to revert any changes and return to the original look, just select the magic pen tool to reset the image.
