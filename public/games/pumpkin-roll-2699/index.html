Pumpkin Roll

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

As the winter season approaches, Mr. Pumpkin is on a festive quest to recover all the missing Christmas gifts that have mysteriously disappeared! In this captivating game, you'll help Mr. Pumpkin navigate through enchanting realms filled with holiday cheer and Halloween thrills. Immerse yourself in a whimsical blend of Christmas and Halloween as you embark on this delightful journey.

Join Mr. Pumpkini as you spin and dodge various obstacles along the way. The game features three unique worlds, each overflowing with seasonal challenges and surprises. Your mission is to locate and retrieve all the absent gifts while carefully avoiding dangers that lurk in the shadows. Whether you're twirling through a snowy wonderland or tiptoeing through a haunted pumpkin patch, the adventure promises to keep you entertained and engaged.

Puzzle enthusiasts will revel in the clever mechanics of the game, which challenge your problem-solving skills while reinforcing the joy of the holiday spirit. The vibrant graphics and enchanting soundtrack elevate the experience, making every level a treat for both the eyes and ears. So, gear up for some festive fun and get ready to assist Mr. Pumpkin in his delightful pursuit of Christmas cheer!

How to play free Pumpkin Roll game online

To play the Pumpkin Roll game, simply use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to spin Mr. Pumpkini and navigate through each level. Your goal is to avoid obstacles while collecting the missing gifts scattered throughout the three worlds. Enjoy the charming combination of Christmas and Halloween elements as you progress!
