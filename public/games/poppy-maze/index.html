Poppy Maze

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chilling world of Poppy Maze, a spine-tingling first-person adventure crafted with the innovative Construct 3 engine. This thrilling game utilizes the latest 3D technology, offering players a uniquely immersive experience as they navigate a mysterious and ominous maze. Your ultimate goal is clear: escape the labyrinth by locating a hidden key that unlocks the exit gate.

The eerie atmosphere and intricate design of the maze will keep you on the edge of your seat as you race against the clock to break your own time record. Enhanced by dynamic visuals and eerie sound effects, every turn could bring you face-to-face with unexpected challenges or nerve-wracking surprises. Are you prepared to confront your fears and find a way out?

Poppy Maze is not just about speed; it s also a test of your puzzle-solving skills as you decipher clues and navigate through dark corridors filled with twists and turns. With each step you take, the thrill builds, making every moment a captivating experience. Ready your wits, muster your bravery, and dive headfirst into this electrifying quest for escape!

How to play free Poppy Maze game online

To play the Poppy Maze game, begin by exploring the maze through the first-person perspective. Use the W, A, S, D keys for movement, and the mouse to look around. Search for the key while avoiding obstacles and other potential dangers. Aim to unlock the gate and escape as quickly as possible to challenge your time!
