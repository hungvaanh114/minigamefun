Welcome to Bus Simulator 2023, an immersive experience that places you directly in the driver's seat, allowing you to embrace the life of a professional bus driver. Traverse richly detailed maps from around the globe, each designed to bring realism to your driving experience. Featuring modern buses equipped with realistic interiors and cutting-edge 1:1 physics, you can truly feel the thrill of navigating urban and rural landscapes.

Customization is at your fingertips as you choose from an extensive collection of buses including diesel, hybrid, electric, and articulated models. Tailor your bus with options like paint jobs, accessories, and even personalized interiors to make your vehicle unique. Whether you're operating in career mode, enjoying a free ride, or teaming up with friends in online multiplayer, Bus Simulator 2023 promises a vast world of exploration.

Discover various iconic locations, including bustling cities in the United States such as San Francisco and Texas, historical sites in Europe like St. Petersburg and Prague, and exotic locales in Asia such as Shanghai and Dubai. Experience a diverse range of driving conditions across different times of the day, as well as changing weather scenarios that add a layer of challenge and realism to your routes.

Engage in a comprehensive bus company management system, allowing you to hire drivers and create custom route schedules while competing on leaderboards. With an intelligent traffic system and animated passengers entering and exiting your bus, every journey feels alive and dynamic. Join your friends through cooperative gameplay, live chat features, and share your achievements as you rise through the rankings.
