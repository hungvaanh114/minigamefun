Futoshiki

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you enjoy the challenge of Sudoku, then you'll undoubtedly find Futoshiki captivating. This beloved numeric puzzle game hails from Japan and offers a fresh twist on familiar gameplay. In Futoshiki, your primary objective is to correctly place the numbers one through five in every row and column, ensuring that none of the numbers repeat. The twist lies in the relational signs that are strategically positioned between certain cells, which dictate the relationship between the numbers you place. For instance, a greater-than (>) sign means that one number must be larger than the adjacent number, adding an intriguing layer of complexity to each puzzle.

The game caters to a variety of skill levels with its built-in generator, offering three distinct levels of difficulty easy, medium, and hard. This allows players of all experience levels to engage with the puzzle at a comfortable pace. Each generated puzzle features a unique solution, ensuring that players can enjoy a fulfilling experience without the frustration of multiple answers. Whether you're a seasoned puzzler or new to the genre, Futoshiki promises to challenge your logic and critical thinking skills.

As you dive into Futoshiki, you'll appreciate its minimalistic design and engaging gameplay that can easily absorb your attention for hours. The satisfaction of solving a challenging puzzle, marked by the thrill of progression through the difficulty levels, will undoubtedly keep you coming back for more!

How to play free Futoshiki game online

To play the Futoshiki game online, start by selecting your preferred difficulty level. Once you re in the game, fill each cell with numbers from one to five, making sure they do not repeat in any row or column. Use the relational signs to determine the correct placements of numbers in relation to one another. Enjoy the challenge!
