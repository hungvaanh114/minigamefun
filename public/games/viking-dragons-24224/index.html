Viking Dragons

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Viking Dragons is an exhilarating adventure game where you take on the role of a brave Viking soaring through the skies on the back of a fearsome dragon. Your mission? Navigate through a treacherous, dark forest teeming with goblins and lurking mutants. This captivating journey will put your skills to the test as you face numerous challenges along the way.

Your Viking hero comes equipped with an arsenal of different weapons, allowing you to engage in fierce combat against the menacing creatures that dwell in the shadows. Meanwhile, your majestic dragon has the power to unleash fiery flames, providing a spectacular offensive advantage. As you progress through the forest, your primary goal is to survive the onslaught of enemies while collecting as many points and ammunition as possible to enhance your fighting capabilities.

With stunning graphics and immersive sound effects, Viking Dragons creates an engaging gaming experience as you take on waves of enemies in a fast-paced environment. Will you be able to guide the Viking safely out of the woods? The challenging terrain is filled with surprises, so stay alert and use your strategic skills to outsmart your foes. Embrace the power of the Viking and his dragon, and embark on this unforgettable adventure!

How to play free Viking Dragons game online

To play Viking Dragons, use the arrow keys to navigate your Viking and control the dragon's movement. Utilize the space bar to throw various weapons at your enemies and unleash the dragon's fire breath with the 'F' key. Keep an eye on your ammunition, and aim to collect power-ups as you advance to score points and survive the dark forest!
