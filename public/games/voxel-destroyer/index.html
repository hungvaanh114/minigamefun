Voxel Destroyer

Immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of voxel destruction with Voxel Destroyer! Take command of a dynamic robot, equipped with a fierce circular saw, and set out to obliterate various voxel structures. The goal is to demolish each formation swiftly, turning precision and speed into your greatest allies. How to play free Voxel Destroyer game online

To play Voxel Destroyer, simply use your mouse or keyboard controls to navigate your robot around the screen. Aim your circular saw at the voxel structures, and click or press the designated action button to activate the saw. Keep an eye on the timer as you work to demolish the structures before time runs out!
