Princess Fashion Salon 1?Princess Fashion Salon 1

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The kingdom is transformed by the enchanting first snowfalls, setting the perfect scene for a grand gathering. The lovely princess has decided to throw a magnificent dance party at the royal palace, and she dreams of being the epitome of elegance and grace as a snow princess. However, with her royal duties demanding her attention, she s in desperate need of assistance. This is where you come in!

In the luxurious dressing room filled with dazzling outfits, your mission is to help the princess select the most stunning attire that reflects her snow-themed party. Engage your creativity as you explore various beautiful dresses, each more enchanting than the last. Along with choosing the perfect outfit, you ll also help her feel refreshed and radiant by treating her to a facial cleanse, giving her skin that snowy glow.

Once her skin is sparkling, dive into makeup artistry! Apply delicate makeup that will enhance her features and complement her dress, ensuring she looks every bit like the snow princess she longs to be. Finally, finish off the transformation with the perfect accessories that will make her shine at the dance party. The palace will also be in need of some winter-themed decor, so use your skills to adorn the surroundings, turning the venue into a dazzling winter wonderland!

How to play free Princess Fashion Salon 1 game online

To enjoy the game, simply navigate through different sections in the princess's dressing room. Use your mouse to select outfits and makeup options. Follow the on-screen instructions to apply facial treatments and finalize her beautiful look, ensuring the princess is ready to dazzle at her snowy ball!
