DATING PARTY

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an enchanting evening in the DATING PARTY game, where the stunning princesses Mia and Bella are all set to find their perfect dates! They are excited yet a bit overwhelmed by the prospect of choosing from a lineup of charming princes. Your mission is to help these lovely princesses make the right decision for their enchanting night out.

As you dive into the game, immerse yourself in a beautiful world filled with dazzling outfits and exquisite makeup options. You ll play the role of a fashion stylist and matchmaker, giving Mia and Bella the royal makeover they deserve. Choose from an array of stylish dresses, sparkling accessories, and delightful hairstyles that will make the princesses stand out at the party. Will they choose elegant gowns, or will they opt for playful dresses? The decision is yours!

Once their stunning looks are complete, it s time for the ultimate selection: which handsome prince will accompany them to the celebration? Analyze the charming princes traits and make an informed choice to ensure Derek and His Royal Highness make the right impression. Will it be the dashing knight or the charming rogue? Let your instincts guide you as you help them pick the most suitable companion for a memorable evening!

How to play free DATING PARTY game online

To play the DATING PARTY game, simply start by selecting your princess, either Mia or Bella. Follow the game instructions to pick their makeup and outfits by clicking on various options provided on the screen. Once they are all dressed up, proceed to select a prince from the available options, ensuring the best match for their fun-filled evening!
