Blocks vs Pirates

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Blocks Vs Pirates is a thrilling sports game designed for players of all ages. In this engaging experience, you are challenged to harness your aiming skills to successfully score goals. The objective is straightforward yet exhilarating: test your endurance and see how long you can remain in the game!

Your performance hinges on your ability to aim accurately. You must carefully calculate the correct angle and the appropriate amount of force to propel the Ninja character through the air and into the basket. As you progress through Blocks Vs Pirates, you will need to master the art of precision to conquer each level.

Each stage introduces a variety of unique obstacles and challenges, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting. Players can anticipate constant stimulation as they navigate through the dynamic environment filled with new risks and rewards. The combination of strategy, skill, and thrilling action makes Blocks Vs Pirates an addictive choice for anyone looking to sharpen their reflexes while having fun.

How to play free Blocks Vs Pirates game online

To play Blocks Vs Pirates, use your mouse or touch screen to aim at the basket. Adjust the angle and power according to the distance and obstacles in your way. Once ready, click or tap to launch the Ninja. Try to score consistently and survive as long as possible to unlock new levels!
