Witch Flight 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Witch Flight 2, an enchanting adventure that invites players to soar through a whimsical yet dangerous Halloween world! To guide your brave witch through the skies, simply tap the game screen, allowing her to take to the air. As you glide, be on the lookout for shining stars scattered across the horizon. Collecting these stars not only boosts your score but also grants you valuable protection in the form of an 8-second shield. During this duration, your witch is invulnerable to the myriad of threats lurking around.

However, the skies in Witch Flight 2 are fraught with peril! You must cleverly navigate to avoid flying pumpkins, mischievous ghosts, and especially, the formidable purple witch. Each of these foes presents a unique challenge, and staying focused is key to survival. The good news is that your witch has a magical ability to auto-shoot at incoming Halloween ghouls, allowing you to concentrate on dodging enemies while still blasting away at threats. With quick reflexes and strategic star collecting, you ll be able to make your way through this captivating, spooky landscape.

How to play free Witch Flight 2 game online

To play Witch Flight 2, begin by tapping the screen to lift your witch into the air. Gather stars for points and temporary shield protection. Use your auto-shooting feature to eliminate approaching enemies while maneuvering to dodge the flying pumpkins and ghosts. Aim for the best score while enjoying the thrilling Halloween atmosphere!
