Experience the festive thrill with Christmas 2019 Match-3, a delightful match-3 puzzle game featuring 16 exciting levels. Your mission is to cleanse the grid by removing the dirty cells that clutter your path. To achieve this, align three or more Christmas-themed symbols in a row or column, and watch them vanish in a flurry of holiday cheer!

As you progress through each level, you ll encounter locked symbols that cannot be swapped, adding an extra layer of challenge. Strategically plan your moves to create matches and unleash powerful special symbols, including bomb symbols, crump symbols, flash symbols, and time symbols, each designed to assist you in clearing the grid. Your primary objective is to eliminate all the dirty cells within the designated timeframe, ensuring you can advance through the holiday-themed levels.

This game is perfect for those looking to engage in a festive challenge while honing their matching skills. The vibrant visuals and holiday music create an immersive atmosphere, making each level a delightful experience. Will you successfully conquer all the levels and emerge as the ultimate match-3 champion this Christmas season?

How to play free Christmas 2019 Match 3 game online

To play Christmas 2019 Match-3, begin by selecting a level. Simply swap adjacent symbols to create a match of three or more identical Christmas symbols. Combine specific shapes to generate special symbols that have unique abilities, helping you clear dirty cells more effectively. Clear all the dirty cells within the time limit to progress!
