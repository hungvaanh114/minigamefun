Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-pumping adventure in the exhilarating game, Bike Stunts of Roof. In this action-packed experience, a daring biker is eager for your assistance to showcase his incredible skills atop a series of rooftops. Your mission is to control his bike as he performs jaw-dropping stunts while navigating perilous gaps and avoiding various obstacles that threaten to derail his spectacular show.

As you guide the biker through this high-stakes environment, collect shiny coins scattered throughout the rooftops. These coins are vital in unlocking an array of new, powerful bikes that can enhance your performance and trick capabilities. With each successful jump and perfectly executed stunt, you'll not only accumulate points but also get a chance to smash your previous records. The thrill lies in mastering the timing and precision required to pull off challenging moves, all while ensuring the biker lands safely to continue his impressive routine.

With its vibrant graphics and addictive gameplay, Bike Stunts of Roof will keep you on your toes. Whether you are a seasoned stunt performer or a newcomer looking to explore your skills, this game offers endless fun and challenges. Get ready to embark on a wild ride and become the ultimate rooftop stunt master!

How to play free Bike Stunts of Roof game online

To play Bike Stunts of Roof, use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to accelerate, jump, and navigate. Aim for the coins while avoiding obstacles. Speed and timing are crucial, so practice your jumps to enhance your skills and unlock new bikes. Compete to set new records!
