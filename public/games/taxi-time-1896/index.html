Taxi Time

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Taxi Time is an engaging and immersive gaming experience that puts your driving and strategic skills to the test. As you navigate through scenic highways and bustling streets, you'll need to maintain precise control of your taxi to ensure a smooth ride. The main objective is to collect dazzling stars scattered along your route, which serve as both a score multiplier and a measure of your success in the game.

However, don't be lulled into a false sense of security; the journey is rife with challenges that require quick thinking and sharp reflexes. You'll encounter various obstacles and potential hazards that could easily send you veering off course. The key to excelling at Taxi Time lies in your ability to balance speed with caution. Masterful maneuvering will not only lead to a higher score but also keep you firmly on track as you chase those elusive stars.

The game is designed to be both entertaining and challenging, allowing players to engage in a thrilling race against time. With stunning graphics and a captivating soundtrack, Taxi Time offers an immersive environment that draws you deeper into the adventure. Gather your skills, strategize your route, and get ready to become the ultimate taxi driver.

How to play free Taxi Time game online

To play Taxi Time, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your taxi along the highway. Collect stars by driving over them, while avoiding any obstacles that may hinder your journey. Keep an eye on the road to maintain your speed and direction to maximize your score!
