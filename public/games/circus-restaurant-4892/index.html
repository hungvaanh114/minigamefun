Circus Restaurant

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you a culinary enthusiast looking to make your mark in the world of dining? Welcome to Circus Restaurant, where you can step into the shoes of a Masterchef and create a thriving restaurant empire across different countries. In this captivating idle tycoon simulator game, you'll earn money from your delightful dishes and fuel your progression through strategic investments in your business.

Embark on an exhilarating journey of managing a restaurant from the ground up. Collaborate with renowned culinary masters who will elevate your establishment to global fame. Explore diverse world cuisines as you serve an array of dishes to customers from all walks of life. Personalize each restaurant with your unique style by renovating and decorating the dining space to create an inviting atmosphere.

Complete missions to unveil special surprises, and as your restaurant flourishes, watch your earnings continue to grow even when you re offline! Join the competitive landscape by comparing your achievements with chefs worldwide on the leaderboard. Cater to Gourmet and VIP guests to maximize your profits and expand your restaurant empire even further.

Restaurant Tycoon - Idle Game combines strategic thinking, passion, and the joy of cooking delicious meals from various cultures. Develop and implement new strategies for inventing enticing products, selecting top-notch ingredients, and hiring skilled chefs and waitstaff.

How to play free Circus Restaurant game online

To begin playing Circus Restaurant, simply download the game and create your restaurant. Manage resources efficiently, upgrade your menu, and hire staff to serve customers. Complete missions for rewards and keep an eye on your earnings, even when you re not actively playing!
