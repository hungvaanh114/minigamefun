Sushi Supply Co

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delectable adventure with Sushi Supply Co and become a sushi tycoon alongside the charming Kitty Chef! In this engaging game, players will have the opportunity to build their very own sushi empire from the ground up. Your mission is to tap on various chefs, each with their unique skills, to enhance the speed at which sushi rolls are made.

As you progress, strategically upgrading your chefs and expanding your warehouse becomes essential. With each upgrade, you ll increase your sushi production and income, bringing you one step closer to culinary domination. The visual appeal of the sushi kitchen, combined with the intriguing gameplay mechanics, ensures that players remain captivated as they make critical decisions to boost efficiency and profits.

Every tap and upgrade can bring in more profits, allowing you to reinvest in your sushi-making business. Explore diverse sushi recipes while managing resources effectively; the game rewards creativity and strategy as you establish yourself in the bustling world of sushi supply. Compete against friends and fellow players to see whose sushi empire reigns supreme. With Kitty Chef by your side, every moment is deliciously exciting!

How to play free Sushi Supply Co game online

To dive into Sushi Supply Co, begin by tapping on the chefs to speed up sushi production. Focus on upgrading both your chefs and warehouse to maximize earnings. Keep tapping and strategizing for the best combinations to enhance your sushi empire and stay ahead in the game!
