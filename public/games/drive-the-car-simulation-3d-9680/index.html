Drive the Car Simulation - 3D

Experience the ultimate thrill of driving in Drive The Car Simulation - 3D, a remarkable car simulator that elevates your gaming experience with its advanced physics engine. This 2014 gem allows you to immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of sports cars where you can truly feel the power and speed at your fingertips.

Have you ever fantasized about tearing through the streets in a racing sports car? Now is your chance to live that dream for free! Navigate a vast open-world city designed for high-speed chases and intense drifting sessions. No traffic to worry about, no competing rivals in sight just you, the road, and a chance to unleash your inner speed demon. Execute jaw-dropping illegal stunts, accelerate to your heart's content, and enjoy the daring freedom of driving without the fear of police interference!

Feel the adrenaline rush as you drift with finesse, perform spectacular burnouts, and let your tires scream against the asphalt. In this expansive virtual city, every corner presents an opportunity to showcase your exceptional driving skills. Get ready to burn rubber and redefine what it means to be a furious racer!

How to play free Drive The Car Simulation - 3D game online

To start playing Drive The Car Simulation - 3D, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate your vehicle. Press the throttle to accelerate, brake to slow down, and utilize drifting techniques by steering sideways while at high speed. Explore the city and enjoy the freedom to perform stunning tricks without hindrance!
