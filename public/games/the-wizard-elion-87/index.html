The Wizard Elion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of "The Wizard Elion," where the old wizard's enchanting adventure unfolds in an unexpected setting. When Elion attempted to conjure a radiant sphere of light, an unforeseen error whisked him away to the bustling atmosphere of a modern shopping mall, teeming with bizarre monsters. Instead of a serene realm, he finds himself amidst shop windows and peculiar creatures that threaten to break free and create pandemonium.

Now, Elion must navigate through this chaotic environment, using his magical skills to solve puzzles and confront the strange denizens of the mall. His primary objective is to correct the faulty spell that brought him here and make his way back to his enchanted kingdom before the monstrous rampage becomes unstoppable. The stakes are high as Elion faces off against quirky enemies while racing against time. Players will enjoy a colorful blend of action, strategy, and humor as they guide Elion through thrilling challenges.

Available in three languages Portuguese, English, and Italian this game offers a captivating experience that appeals to a diverse audience. Additionally, players can dive into the fun anytime, anywhere, with mobile compatibility, allowing for an engaging adventure at their fingertips. "The Wizard Elion" promises a delightful journey filled with excitement and magical surprises.

How to play free The Wizard Elion game online

To play "The Wizard Elion," simply navigate through the maze of the shopping mall using on-screen controls. Tap to cast spells, interact with objects, and solve puzzles. Collect magical items while avoiding monsters to restore order and ensure Elion's safe return. Have fun and enjoy the mystical adventure!


Instructions

Use WASD to move and MOUSE to interact on the menu title screen If you use the mobile device simply TOUCH the buttons on the SCREEN with your finger and use the ARROW KEYS on the screen to move
