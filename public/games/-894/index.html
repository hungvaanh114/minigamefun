Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Stack Sorting! Dive into a vibrant world of colorful cylinders where your organizational skills will be put to the ultimate test. The objective is simple yet engaging: you need to sort the cylinders by arranging them on different stacks. By clicking on a stack, you can move the topmost cylinder to either an empty stack or one that has a matching color on top. Each move you make counts, so strategy is crucial as you work against the clock.

The game features two distinct modes: Easy Mode and Hard Mode, each designed to challenge your sorting abilities at different levels. In Easy Mode, you'll encounter a generous number of stacks along with plenty of time to strategize your moves. This mode is perfect for beginners and those looking to enjoy a more relaxed gaming experience while honing their skills. On the other hand, for seasoned players, Hard Mode presents a greater challenge with fewer stacks, demanding quick thinking and efficient planning to complete each level. Your goal is to complete all levels before the time runs out, providing a thrilling sense of urgency as you race against the clock!

How to play free Stack Sorting game online

To play Stack Sorting, start by selecting a stack with a visible top cylinder. Click to move this cylinder onto an empty stack or a stack that has the same color on top. Continue this process until all cylinders are sorted correctly before the timer expires. Enjoy and challenge yourself!
