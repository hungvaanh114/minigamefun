Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of soaring through the skies in "Catching Flight." Navigate your aircraft through a breathtaking skyline, filled with towering skyscrapers while skillfully dodging incoming missiles. The challenge intensifies as you strive to evade these deadly threats while maximizing your loot collection!

Your primary objective is to gather as much in-game currency as you can. The more money you collect, the closer you get to purchasing one of the diverse and powerful aircraft available in the store menu. Each plane offers unique abilities, making your aerial adventure even more exciting. But that s not all keep an eye out for the elusive green coins scattered throughout the game; collecting these will grant you temporary power-ups. Activate your shield to protect your aircraft from missiles, or unleash the power to destroy any threats that come your way.

Catching Flight promises exhilarating gameplay as you dodge, weave, and strategize your way through an array of challenges. The vibrant graphics, engaging mechanics, and competitive elements make it a perfect choice for both casual gamers and those lovers of thrilling aviation. Gear up and join in on the fun your adventure awaits in the clouds!

How to play free Catching Flight game online

To dive into "Catching Flight," simply use arrow keys or tap and drag on your screen to maneuver your aircraft. Avoid missiles while collecting coins to increase your score. Don t forget to gather green coins for power-ups, enabling you to shield your plane or destroy approaching missiles!
