Save the Animal Kingdom

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling adventure of Save The Animal Kingdom, a tapping game where you control a courageous animal character striving to escape a perilous, lava-filled environment. Your mission is to navigate through an exotic world brimming with vibrant foliage while cleverly avoiding the deadly lava flows lurking beneath.

Utilizing simple touch mechanics, players can maneuver their character by tapping on either the left or right sides of the screen. This dynamic action allows your character to leap from one leafy platform to another, creating a delicate balance of timing and precision. Each jump brings you closer to safety and ensures the preservation of the animal kingdom.

The game offers an exhilarating experience as you face an escalating series of obstacles and challenges, encouraging quick thinking and sharp reflexes. As you progress, you'll encounter various flora that provide safe havens amid the rocky terrain. Collect bonuses and power-ups along the way to enhance your gameplay and increase your chances of success. Your ultimate goal is to guide your character to safety, outsmart the encroaching lava, and save the animal kingdom from impending doom.

How to play free Save The Animal Kingdom game online

To play Save The Animal Kingdom, simply tap the left or right side of your screen to move and jump. Time your taps carefully to leap between leaves and avoid the molten lava below. Keep your reflexes sharp as you navigate through this engaging and fast-paced adventure.


Instructions

Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys and Click on the Left and Right Screen sides to move the animals
