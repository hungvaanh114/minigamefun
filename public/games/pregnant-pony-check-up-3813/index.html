Pregnant Pony Check Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the delightful game "Pregnant Pony Check Up," you step into the role of a compassionate caretaker for a lovely pregnant pony. As she prepares for the birth of her baby, the tension and excitement blend together. Your first task is to call an ambulance to ensure she safely reaches the hospital. The urgency of the situation sets the tone as you embark on this heartwarming adventure.

Once at the hospital, you ll assist the doctor in performing a thorough check-up on the mommy pony. This involves using various medical instruments and tools to monitor her health and ensure a smooth delivery. After the careful examination, you help the devoted mom during her birthing process, welcoming a cute baby pony into the world. This warm and nurturing game allows you to experience the joys and responsibilities of taking care of both the pony and her newborn.

Fast forward one year, and the little pony has grown into a playful and stylish young lady who adores beautiful dresses. Your next challenge is to dive into a fun fashion segment, where you ll celebrate her style by picking out wonderful outfits for her to wear. Explore a variety of colors and designs to make her feel fabulous and cherish those memorable moments together.

How to play free Pregnant Pony Check Up game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse to interact with the various elements on the screen. Start by calling for an ambulance, then follow the on-screen instructions to assist the doctor during the check-up. Once the baby pony arrives, use your creativity to choose stylish dresses for her. Enjoy the engaging tasks in this delightful pony care adventure!
