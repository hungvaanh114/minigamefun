Friday Night Squid Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Friday Night Squid Game, where the beloved characters from the survival series Squid Game are challenged to a musical showdown akin to Friday Night Funkin. In this captivating crossover, players take on the role of Boyfriend and Girlfriend, who, having lost their fortune to gambling, now find themselves taking part in the notorious Squid games for a chance to regain what was lost.

Your mission is to expertly press the arrow keys in perfect sync with incoming notes that emerge on your screen. Timing is everything; when you hit the notes successfully, you build up your score and unlock powerful combos that can give you the upper hand against your Squid Game opponent. This exciting rhythm game offers a vibrant and challenging experience filled with engaging musical tracks across several levels.

As you journey through the game, you will face 12 distinct levels featuring various songs, all while aiming to recover the money lost. With each successful note hit, you not only advance in the competition but also immerse yourself deeper into this unique blend of rhythm and survival challenges. The game also introduces the charming character Hugie Wugie, adding an element of fun and excitement to your musical adventure!

How to play free Friday Night Squid Game game online

To play Friday Night Squid Game, simply use your keyboard's arrow keys to match the notes as they appear on the screen. Keep up with the rhythm and hit the notes in perfect timing to score points. Maintain your streak and complete all songs across the levels to be declared the champion!
