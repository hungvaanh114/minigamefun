How to play free Nova Solitaire game online

To play Nova Solitaire, begin by shuffling the deck and dealing cards into the appropriate tableau. Your aim is to sort the cards into four foundation piles by suit, starting from Ace to King. You can move cards between the tableau and the stock at your discretion, utilizing strategic thinking to uncover hidden cards and create successful sequences. Enjoy the challenge and have fun!
