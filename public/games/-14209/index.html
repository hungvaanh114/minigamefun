Summer has arrived, and it s the perfect opportunity to refresh your wardrobe! In the exhilarating game Makeover Run, you and your friends will embark on a stylish journey to discover the best summer outfits. Team up with your girlfriends and navigate through a vibrant world as you run towards the finish line, all while enhancing your fashion sense! This exciting wearable parkour game will challenge your agility as you maneuver past tricky obstacles and aim to collect a variety of stylish clothing items along the way.

As you dash across colorful platforms, you ll have the chance to choose from an array of tops and skirts, each available in different hues. What will happen when you mix and match? The excitement lies in the surprises that await you after gathering your chic outfits. Once you've crossed the finish line, it s time to show off your fashionable finds to your friends and impress your boyfriend with your newfound style!

Are you ready to race in the fabulous world of Makeover Run? Bring your friends along for this fun-filled adventure, as everyone strives to collect the most outfits and reach the finish line in the ultimate fashion showdown. Enjoy the thrill of running, jumping, and obstacle-dodging, all while curating the perfect summer look!

How to play free Makeover Run game online

To play Makeover Run, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to guide your character across the platform. Dodge obstacles and collect clothing items by tapping or swiping in the right direction. The more outfits you gather, the more fashionable you become! Aim for a high score and showcase your style at the end!
