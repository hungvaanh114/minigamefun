Welcome to the thrilling world of Wars Z Zombie Apocalypse 2020, where the undead masses have taken over and the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. A virulent outbreak has transformed nearly all humans into ravenous zombies, creating a terrifying landscape for survival. Players are tasked with navigating various levels filled with diverse and menacing zombie models including agile crawlers, vicious zombie dogs, and even fiery foes that will stop at nothing to destroy you.

Your mission is straightforward yet challenging: eliminate all enemies lurking in your path while progressing through each level. Enjoy a variety of offline missions that offer different game modes such as "Run and Destroy" and "Run and Survive." In these modes, you ll need not just to fend off zombies but also to collect vital cures scattered throughout the map in order to complete each level and ultimately emerge victorious. The visceral gameplay and diverse levels are designed to keep you engaged and on your toes as you outsmart and outmaneuver the undead.

As you dive deeper into the game, upgrading your arsenal and learning new strategies will be crucial to your survival. Each encounter with the undead will test your reflexes and decision-making skills as you adapt to the relentless onslaught. Experience heart-pounding action and ensure the survival of the last remnants of humanity in this captivating zombie apocalypse.
