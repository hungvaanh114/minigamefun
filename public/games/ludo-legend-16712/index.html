How to play free Ludo Legend game online

To play Ludo Legend online, start by selecting the number of players and choosing your colors. Once the game begins, take turns rolling the dice, moving your pawns according to the roll. The goal is to get all your pawns to the home area before your opponents. Use your strategy to block others and enjoy the fun!
