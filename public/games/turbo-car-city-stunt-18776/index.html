Turbo Car City Stunt

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Turbo Car City Stunt is an exhilarating 3D car driving experience that places you behind the wheel of sleek supercars within a vibrant modern city setting. With a selection of 15 high-performance sports cars and a thrilling array of 36 meticulously designed racetracks, this game invites players to unleash their inner stunt driver.

The gameplay offers an exciting vehicle acquisition and enhancement system. After executing impressive stunts, players are rewarded with opportunities to upgrade and customize their cars or even purchase entirely new vehicles. The customization options add a personal touch to your driving experience, allowing you to tailor your car's performance and aesthetic to match your style.

As you race through the city and tackle daring aerial maneuvers, don't forget to strategically deploy nitro boosts during challenging moments, elevating your stunts to new levels. Mastering the art of timing and precision will ensure you become a legendary stunt driver in the Turbo Car City universe.

How to play free Turbo Car City Stunt game online

To play Turbo Car City Stunt, simply select your desired car and racetrack, then use the on-screen controls to steer, accelerate, and perform stunts. Activate nitro boosts in critical moments to gain an edge over challenges, and remember to collect points for upgrades and enhancements after each stunt.
