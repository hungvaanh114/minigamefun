Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the whimsical world of Mini Monkey Mart, it's time for some monkey business as you set up a delightful grocery store tailored just for your playful primate friends. Upon entering this vibrant mini market, you ll discover that monkeys have unique tastes, and the journey begins with the essential staple: bananas. Your first task is to cultivate these beloved fruits. Watch them grow and collect the ripe bananas, carefully placing them on the counter to attract hungry customers.

As the monkeys arrive, their enthusiasm for shopping becomes evident, and your mini mart quickly turns bustling. In addition to bananas, you'll also be raising chickens, providing them with a hearty banana diet which will yield fresh eggs. These eggs, along with your delicious bananas, are to be strategically arranged on the counter, inviting customers to indulge in their appetites for tasty treats.

Your entrepreneurial spirit will shine as you begin selling various products like banana puree canned food, flour, bread, and milk, using the profits from the sales to expand your offerings. Each item sold adds to your fun-filled shopping experience, engaging you in a whirlwind of activity as you cater to the unique preferences of your monkey clientele. Immerse yourself in the chaos and joy of managing a grocery store, where your success hinges on fulfilling the shopping needs of these adorable monkeys.

How to play free Mini Monkey Mart game online

To dive into the world of Mini Monkey Mart, simply start by planting and harvesting bananas. Feed chickens, collect their eggs, and place everything on your counter. Use your earnings wisely to expand your inventory and ensure all your monkey customers leave happy and satisfied!
