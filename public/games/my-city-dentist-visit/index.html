My City : Dentist Visit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of My City: Dentist Visit, where a new chapter in your city s adventure begins with the arrival of the local dentist and his family! Dive into a richly interactive environment where you have the power to shape the narrative. What fascinating tales will you create?

In this game, your city now boasts exciting new locations just waiting to be explored. Visit the trendy local Hair Salon to give your characters a stylish makeover, or stock up on essentials at the Supermarket. Of course, the highlight is the dentist's office, where you can engage in toothy fun and learn about dental care through in-game experiences!

As you navigate through your city, you ll meet a variety of new characters, each with their own unique styles and stories. This opens up endless possibilities for imagination and creativity. Dress your characters in new clothing options and unlock new adventures as you play. With My City: Dentist Visit, every decision is yours, and the fun never stops!

How to play free My City: Dentist Visit game online

To play My City: Dentist Visit, simply explore the different locations by tapping on them to interact with objects and characters. Choose various activities, dress up your characters, and create stories. You can switch between characters and locations freely, allowing for limitless creativity and fun within your own city!
