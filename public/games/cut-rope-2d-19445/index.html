Cut Rope 2D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delightful adventure with Om Nom in the captivating world of the original "Cut the Rope" logic puzzle series. Available for free, this game allows you to join millions of players globally as you help the lovable little monster satisfy his sweet tooth. Om Nom s journey is filled with challenges that test your problem-solving skills and creativity while providing endless entertainment.

The excitement begins when a mysterious package arrives, revealing the adorable Om Nom who craves candy more than anything else. As players, your mission is to cut the ropes strategically to drop the candy into Om Nom's eager mouth. Along the way, you ll have the chance to collect gold stars, uncover hidden rewards, and unlock thrilling new levels that will keep you engaged for hours. This award-winning game is praised for its physics-based puzzles that combine fun with mental challenges, making it a favorite among puzzle enthusiasts.

If you're curious about Om Nom's colorful antics beyond the game, you can explore the enchanting tales by watching Om Nom Stories and other entertaining videos on our YouTube channel at www.zep.tl/youtube. Dive into the sweet world of Om Nom today and discover why players around the globe adore this charming little monster!

How to play free Cut Rope 2D game online

To play Cut Rope 2D, simply use your mouse or touch screen to slice the ropes holding the candy. Make sure to navigate obstacles and collect stars before the candy lands in Om Nom's mouth. Plan your cuts carefully to achieve the highest score!
