How to play free MC Flappy Steve game online

To play MC Flappy Steve, simply click or tap on the screen to make Steve flap his wings and rise. Your goal is to maneuver through the gaps between pipes without hitting them. The game becomes progressively challenging as you advance, requiring sharp reflexes and precise timing to achieve high scores. Enjoy!
