Zombie Street Trigger

Get ready to immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Zombie Street Trigger, where your survival skills are put to the ultimate test. This exciting title has been completely revamped to ensure seamless compatibility with the latest Android systems, all while being absolutely free! Forget about intrusive advertisements and in-app purchases, as this game focuses solely on the pure adrenaline of surviving in a zombie-infested landscape.

When the undead rise and take over your town, the only option left is to scavenge their pockets for any hidden treasures. In this nail-biting survival adventure, you will encounter relentless hordes of zombies, and you ll need to fight tooth and nail to stay alive. Your mission? To gather cash and ammunition to equip yourself with a variety of powerful weapons that will help you fend off the oncoming undead. But beware time is not on your side, as danger lurks around every corner. How long can you endure the relentless waves of zombies and manage to survive?

Don't forget to explore the remarkable sequel, Zombieville USA 2, which is now also available for your Android device! Dive into this action-packed saga and experience the evolution of the franchise, complete with enhanced graphics and more engaging gameplay.

How to play free Zombie Street Trigger game online

To play Zombie Street Trigger, launch the game and use the on-screen controls to navigate your character through the zombie-infested streets. Aim your weapon using the virtual joystick, and tap the fire button to shoot zombies. Collect coins and ammo while avoiding getting cornered by the undead as you strategize your next move!
