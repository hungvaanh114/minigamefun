The Sea Beast Card Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing "The Sea Beast Card Match," a captivating game inspired by the stunning Netflix animated film, "The Sea Beast," created by Netflix Animation. This film, set for a theatrical release on June 24, 2022, and coming to Netflix on July 8, 2022, is directed by Chris Williams, who also penned the story. Originally announced as "Jacob and the Sea Beast" in 2018, the movie underwent a title change to "The Sea Beast" on November 7, 2020, marking a new chapter in animated storytelling.

The Sea Beast follows the adventures of a young girl who stumbles upon a legendary sea creature and embarks on an extraordinary journey filled with danger and discovery. This blend of captivating storytelling and vivid animation comes to life in the accompanying card matching game, where fans can immerse themselves deeper into the world of the film by matching pairs of thematic cards.

The Sea Beast Card Match game is designed to offer players an engaging experience, allowing them to challenge their memory while enjoying the delightful visuals inspired by the film. As players attempt to match cards depicting fantastic beasts and maritime themes, they will recall moments from the animated feature, seamlessly connecting the gameplay with its narrative essence.

How to play free The Sea Beast Card Match game online

To play The Sea Beast Card Match game online, simply select a card to flip it over, revealing an image. Then, choose another card to find a match. If the cards match, they will remain face-up. If not, flip them back over. Continue until all pairs are matched!
