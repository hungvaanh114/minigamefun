How to play free Ball Sort game online

To play the Ball Sort Puzzle, simply tap on a tube to select it, then tap on another tube to pour the balls from one to the other. You can only move a ball on top of a tube, and it can only be poured into another tube if that tube is either empty or contains a ball of the same color. Your objective is to sort all the colored balls into their matching tubes.
