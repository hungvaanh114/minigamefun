How to play free The Word Search game online

Playing The Word Search is simple and intuitive. Begin by selecting a puzzle from the menu. Once the grid appears, look for the list of words you need to find. Slide your finger or mouse to circle the words when they are found horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Keep searching until you uncover them all!
