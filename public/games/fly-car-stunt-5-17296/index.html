Fly Car Stunt 5

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fly Car Stunt 5 has launched with exciting new features that elevate the game to new heights! Venture into the futuristic Space City, where breathtaking views and innovative challenges await. This version introduces six original flying cars, each designed to enhance your driving experience in the air. With upgraded physics, players will find improved vehicle control, making those hair-raising aerial maneuvers more realistic and enjoyable than ever before.

In Fly Car Stunt 5, players can engage in ten electrifying levels that test their racing skills against the clock. The clock is ticking, so you'll need to race against time to unlock new and advanced flying cars. To conquer obstacles and gain that extra edge, make sure to use your nitro wisely. Mastering its use can propel you ahead of rivals and help you navigate the complex aerial challenges that await in the Space City.

Want to share the excitement? The game features a "2 PLAYER" mode, allowing you to compete directly with your friends. Challenge them to see who can navigate the futuristic skies and dominate in their flying cars. It s a fun way to compare skills and engage in some friendly rivalry!

How to play free Fly Car Stunt 5 game online

To begin playing Fly Car Stunt 5, select your car and choose a level. Use the arrow keys for steering and acceleration. Press the nitro button to gain a speed boost, but remember to manage it wisely. Aim to complete each level within the allotted time while avoiding obstacles and collecting bonuses. Enjoy the thrilling aerial experience!
