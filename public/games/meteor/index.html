Meteor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Meteor, players are thrust into a battle against cosmic forces as they take on the role of Earth's defender. The planet hangs in the balance, threatened by a relentless barrage of meteors hurtling through space. Your task is clear: shoot down each incoming meteor before they reach the ground and cause catastrophic destruction. With fast-paced gameplay and an array of exciting features, players will be on the edge of their seats as they strive to protect their home.

The game s visuals are designed to immerse users deeply, with stunning graphics depicting a vivid cosmos scattered with meteors. As you progress, the difficulty increases, introducing faster meteors and varying patterns, putting your reflexes and strategic thinking to the test. Players can earn points for every meteor they successfully destroy and may even unlock power-ups to enhance their shooting capabilities or slow down the incoming meteors, adding another layer of excitement to the challenge.

Play Meteor now and experience the adrenaline rush that comes with saving the Earth! Whether you're a casual gamer or a seasoned pro, this engaging shooting game offers plenty of fun for everyone. Gather your friends for a friendly competition and see who can achieve the highest score in this battle against the meteor menace.

How to play free Meteor game online

To play the Meteor game, navigate using your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the incoming meteors. Tap or click to shoot them down as quickly as possible. Keep an eye on the score and try to survive for as long as you can while facing increasingly challenging waves of meteors!
