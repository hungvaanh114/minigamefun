Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling journey in Helix Ball, an exhilarating game that invites you to guide a bouncing ball through an intricate helix tower. This hyper-casual ball-hopping experience features simple one-tap controls that make it easy to pick up and play, while lush visual effects and highly addictive mechanics keep you coming back for more.

In Helix Ball, your primary objective is to navigate the ball down a series of captivating circular paths held up by a central pole. As you bounce your way down, you must skillfully avoid various obstacles that will try to impede your progress. The game offers a splendid range of vibrant background colors and unique bouncing ball designs at every new level, ensuring a fresh and visually appealing experience with each play.

As you advance, you're challenged with increasingly complex mazes where you need to jump, dodge, and swiftly complete the propeller maze. Be on the lookout for oddly colored propellers; they can get in your way! If you manage to pass through three or more propellers, you'll have the chance to land and destroy platforms for extra points. Collect coins along the way to unlock a variety of fascinating characters. Your ultimate goal is to reach the bottom of the tower, facing even tougher challenges as you progress!

How to play free Helix Ball game online

To play Helix Ball, simply tap the screen to bounce the ball downwards. Your goal is to guide it through the colorful paths while avoiding obstacles. Keep an eye out for propellers that need to be dodged and gather coins to unlock new characters. A steady hand and good timing are key to mastering this addictive game!
