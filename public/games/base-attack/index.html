Base Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Base Attack, you step into the boots of Tom, a dedicated member of a special forces unit tasked with an urgent and dangerous mission. Your objective is clear: infiltrate enemies' territories, where nefarious criminal groups operate unchallenged. Each level presents a new opportunity to display your sharpshooting skills and strategic mindset. With a keen eye and steady aim, your job is to eliminate guards patrolling these hostile zones, taking them out one by one without raising the alarm.

As you embark on your mission, the pressure is on. The guards are vigilant, and a moment's hesitation could lead to your downfall. You must be swift and precise in your actions; failure to act quickly could result in enemy fire directed at you. Utilize the terrain, hide behind objects, and strike when the time is right, all while keeping your mission objectives in mind. The opponent's base is filled with adversaries, each more challenging than the last, requiring you to adapt your strategy to succeed. Your tactical decisions will determine if you can achieve victory, proving your worth as a member of the special forces.

With each successful mission, you ll advance further into enemy territory and uncover the deeper machinations of the criminal world you have set out to dismantle. Your skills will be put to the test, making every moment in Base Attack a heart-pounding adventure. Engage with the experience, hone your abilities, and remember the enemy won't wait for you to catch your breath.

How to play free Base Attack game online

To play the Base Attack game, use your mouse to aim and shoot at the guards. Move around the map using the keyboard controls to find strategic positions. Complete your objectives to advance to the next levels, all while avoiding being spotted by the enemies. Stay alert and react quickly!
