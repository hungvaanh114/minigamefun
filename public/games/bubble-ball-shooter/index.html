Bubble Ball Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bubble Ball is an untimed bubble-popping strategy game with a classic interface and arcade special effects noises. The player uses an arrow shooter to pop clusters of three or more matching bubbles to earn points and special gifts. The player achieves their highest score by consecutively popping large matching clusters. They receive ten times the standard allotment of points when they pop avalanches of non-matched bubbles with a cluster and double their score when they clear the field entirely.

Instructions

Mouse click or tap to play
