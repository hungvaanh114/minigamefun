CluckNRun

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling journey through the countryside in ClucknRun, where your mission is to return a lost chickens to their farm home! Navigate through vibrant farm landscapes, collecting bountiful chickens and essential gasoline along the way. But be cautious&amp;mdash;barrels are scattered across the path, ready to derail your ride!

Instructions

Use the A and D keys for smooth left and right movement or simply control the flow with your mouse for an effortless experience
