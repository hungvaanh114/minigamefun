Apple Worm

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Apple Worm is an adorable yet challenging brainteaser game that invites players to guide a hungry little snake as it navigates through a maze filled with tantalizing apples. This charming gluttonous serpent is on a quest to satisfy its hunger, but the journey is laden with clever puzzles and strategic traps that will test your problem-solving skills.

As you maneuver through each level, you'll need to plan your moves carefully. The game's crawling mechanics add an extra layer of complexity, as the worm must collect apples while avoiding various obstacles. Each stage presents unique challenges, requiring players to think ahead and devise a strategy to reach the portal. The colorful graphics and cute character design make the gameplay even more enjoyable, creating a delightful atmosphere as you twist and turn through the maze.

With each apple collected, players must continue to adapt their strategies and think critically to progress. The game may appear simple at first, but the intricate puzzles will keep you engaged and entertained as you strive to help the apple snake escape unharmed. Get ready for a fun-filled adventure full of mind-bending challenges!

How to play free Apple Worm game online

To play Apple Worm, use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to navigate the snake through the maze. Collect apples while avoiding traps and obstacles. Strategically plan your movements to reach the portal at the end of each level. Good luck and enjoy the challenge!
