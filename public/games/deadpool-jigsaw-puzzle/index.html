The gameplay begins with you selecting the difficulty level easy, medium, or hard. This choice allows you to tailor the challenge to your liking, making it accessible for everyone, from beginners to seasoned players. Once you've made your selection, the jigsaw puzzle pieces will be randomly scattered across the game screen. Your mission is to match these pieces to recreate the original image of Deadpool.

Using your mouse, simply drag the pieces over the transparent outline where they belong until each one is locked into place. The satisfaction of connecting the pieces seamlessly will have you hooked as you work toward completing the puzzle. With each attempt, you'll refine your skills and become quicker at recognizing where each piece fits. How to play free Deadpool Jigsaw Puzzle game online

To start playing the Deadpool Jigsaw Puzzle, first, select your preferred difficulty level. Once the pieces are displayed, use your mouse to click and drag each puzzle piece to their corresponding positions on the outline. Keep connecting the pieces until the puzzle is complete, revealing a stunning Deadpool image.
