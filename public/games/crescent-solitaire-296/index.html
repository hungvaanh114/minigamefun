Crescent Solitaire

Description:

Crescent Solitaire is a compelling card-management application focused on achieving full centralization. The core mandate requires users to transfer all peripheral tableau assets into the eight central foundation stacks. Successful execution involves ascending sequencing from Ace to King within the designated suit, while simultaneously enabling descending sequencing from King to Ace. Movement within the peripheral tableaus is governed by strict suit-matching protocols and immediate incremental or decremental rank criteria.
