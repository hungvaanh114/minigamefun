Stickman City Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for adrenaline-pumping action in Stickman City Shooter, where your survival instincts are put to the ultimate test! In this immersive 3D shooter game, you'll encounter waves of enemy stickmen that are hellbent on taking you down. It's a battle for dominance in the cityscape, and only the strongest will prevail. Armed with various weapons and equipped with a range of firearms, you must navigate through the chaotic urban environment and eliminate your foes with precision and strategy.

As you embark on this shooting spree, anticipate thrilling encounters with a diverse range of enemies, including stickman soldiers, SWAT units, and even formidable boss stickmen. Use your skills to gun them down before they can outmaneuver you. Whether you're sniping from a distance or engaging in close-quarter combat, you'll find that the game provides ample opportunities to unleash your pent-up aggression while enjoying hours of engaging gameplay.

Experience the excitement of the battlefield as you utilize vehicles like cars and tanks, and set up turrets to increase your firepower. The dynamic environments will keep you on your toes, and the stunning graphics bring the stickman world to life as blood splatters across the screen a warning for the faint of heart! Get ready for the ultimate stickman-killing experience!

How to play free Stickman City Shooter game online

To dominate in Stickman City Shooter, start by choosing your weapon of preference from the arsenal available. Use the WASD keys to move your character around the city while aiming with your mouse. Left-click to shoot and eliminate enemy stickmen, and make sure to utilize cover effectively. Keep an eye out for health packs and ammo to extend your gameplay. Happy shooting!
