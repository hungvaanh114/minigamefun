Clash of Armour

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the adrenaline-pumping world of Clash Of Armour, where precision, strategy, and rapid decision-making are vital for triumph. Tanks serve as formidable weapons in any battlefield, and in this captivating game, you ll engage in thrilling tank battles against fierce opponents. The excitement increases as you place your tanks in optimal positions on your side of the battlefield, ready to launch an assault. With each tank type demanding a specific amount of time to recharge, managing your power bar becomes essential for deploying new units effectively.

As you navigate through the game, your primary goal is to outsmart and outgun your enemies. Successfully eliminate their tank fleet while strategically upgrading your own. Earn cash as you demolish opposing tanks and use it to purchase impressive upgrades that enhance the durability and firepower of your machines of war. Your tactical skills will determine the battlefield outcome, so be prepared to think several steps ahead!

In Clash Of Armour, victory isn t just about brute force; timing and clever map control will lead your tanks to glory. Dominating the battlefield means understanding when to deploy, when to upgrade, and when to unleash a relentless attack on your vulnerable opponent. Dare to engage in persistent warfare and prove that you have what it takes to conquer the arena!

How to play free Clash Of Armour game online

To start playing Clash Of Armour, simply choose your desired tank type from the menu and strategically place it on your battlefield. Monitor the power bar to deploy new tanks effectively and watch for enemy movements to counter their attacks. Use cash earned from victories for upgrades to enhance your tanks performance.
