Slime Arcade Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Want to do a crazy parkour challenge with bouncy Slime? In Slime Arcade Run, you control a lively slime on a crazy running platform full of obstacles. Enter a colorful world that challenges your running abilities. Try to collect more Slime. Choosing to pass through a door with a positive number will also increase your number.

Instructions
