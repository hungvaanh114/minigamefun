Ever After High Makeover Party

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Ever After High Makeover Party, where magic and creativity collide! This captivating animation has taken the hearts of many, making it one of the most talked-about shows around. Amidst the excitement, four princesses are eager to don the delightful costumes inspired by their favorite characters from the series as they get ready for the ultimate Halloween bash. Will you help them shine in their chosen outfits?

As you embark on this makeover journey, you have the opportunity to mix and match from a delightful wardrobe of costumes, accessories, and hairstyles. Whether you want to transform these princesses into the spunky Apple White, the whimsical Raven Queen, the fierce Cupid, or the charming Princess Ashlynn Ella, the choice is yours! Express your unique fashion sense while blending the fairy-tale elements that make each character special. Get ready to unleash your inner stylist and create jaw-dropping looks that will leave everyone at the party in awe!

The Ever After High universe is bursting with colorful designs and enchanting details that will inspire every aspect of the makeover. Layer fabrics, select vibrant colors, and add finishing touches to create a look that is both magical and memorable. The creativity doesn t stop there; you can also share your stylish creations with friends and fellow fans of the show, sparking endless inspiration and fun.

How to play free Ever After High Makeover Party game online

To play the Ever After High Makeover Party game, simply choose your princess and begin customizing her look by selecting their outfits, hairstyles, and accessories. Navigate through the options using mouse clicks, and when you're satisfied with your creations, showcase them at the Halloween party for a festive celebration full of fun!
