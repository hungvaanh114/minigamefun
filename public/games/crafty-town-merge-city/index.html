How to play free Crafty Town Merge City game online

To play Crafty Town Merge City, simply gather resources by tapping on various items around your town. Merge similar items to create upgraded structures that yield more resources. Strategically place your buildings to optimize space and unlock new features. Enjoy the community aspect by visiting other players' towns for inspiration!
