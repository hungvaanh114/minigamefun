I Love Color Hue

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of I Love Color Hue, a captivating logical hypercasual puzzle game that will challenge your problem-solving skills. Your objective is to organize a spectrum of colors, arranging the hues from light to dark or dark to light based on the established fixed colors represented by squares adorned with white dots.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter fifty cleverly designed levels that introduce shadows, adding an extra layer of complexity to your task. Each colorful square can be tapped and dragged, allowing you to swap their positions with adjacent squares to achieve the correct order. Your keen eye for color and logical reasoning will be your best allies as you navigate through the delightful chaos of hues.

As you successfully complete each level, a sense of accomplishment awaits, and you'll unlock the next challenge that tests your skills even further. With an engaging gameplay experience and visually appealing design, I Love Color Hue promises hours of entertainment. Prepare to lose yourself in the joy of color organization and puzzle-solving as you conquer each level and explore the delightful spectrum of hues!

How to play free I Love Color Hue game online

To play I Love Color Hue, simply tap on the colored squares to select them, then drag them to an adjacent square to swap their positions. Keep arranging the colors until you align each hue correctly according to the fixed squares. Complete the level to advance to exciting new challenges!
