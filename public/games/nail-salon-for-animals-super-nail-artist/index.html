Nail Salon for Animals - Super Nail Artist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the delightful world of the "Nail Salon For Animals - Super Nail Artist," where fashion meets furry friends! In this whimsical game, you will take on the role of a stylish nail salon manager dedicated to pampering your adorable animal clients. Just like humans, many animals love to express themselves through their appearance, and it s your job to ensure their nails steal the show.

Your first furry client of the day is an adorable little turtle who desires a vibrant and colorful manicure. Begin by cleaning her tiny nails to prepare them for some fabulous pampering. Once her nails are spotless, it s time to apply some trendy fake nail pieces that will serve as a dazzling canvas for your creative flair. Unleash your artistic talents by designing stunning patterns on these nail pieces.

Next, bring your designs to life by applying beautiful paints that reflect the turtle s personality and style. The finishing touch involves selecting charming accessories such as dainty bracelets to really make her nails pop. With your expert touch, your clients will leave your salon looking fabulous and feeling confident!

How to play free Nail Salon For Animals - Super Nail Artist game online

To play "Nail Salon For Animals - Super Nail Artist," simply choose your animal client and follow the on-screen instructions. Start by cleaning their nails, then apply fake nail pieces, paint, and decorate with patterns and jewelry. Use your mouse or touch controls to navigate through the fun tasks!
