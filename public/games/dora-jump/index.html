Dora Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Dora and Boots on an exciting adventure in Dora and Friends: Back to the Rainforest! After Backpack and Map are unexpectedly taken away by the wind, it s up to your child to help our brave duo race back into the rainforest to save them. Together, they will run and jump through the vibrant Treetops of the Rainforest, scale new heights in the beautiful Flowery Garden, glide down the Tallest Mountain, and quickly return to Playa Verde to mend Backpack after his wild ride.

Your child will have a blast collecting Backpack s lost items along the way and reconnecting with familiar faces like Tico and Big Red Chicken. In each unique location, they will also gather charms for Dora's special bracelet, all while engaging in educational fun practicing counting in Spanish, enhancing color recognition, and learning enumeration skills. It s an adventure filled with laughter, learning, and fast-paced fun!

As they navigate through enchanting landscapes, players will be encouraged to explore, learn, and assist Dora and Boots in their mission. This exciting journey not only supports cognitive skills but also adds a sprinkle of interactive education into gameplay. Get your young adventurer ready to embark on this colorful quest, where engagement and excitement await!

How to play free Dora Jump game online

Once in the game, your child can control Dora and Boots by using the jump button to leap through the colorful landscapes and avoid obstacles. Collect Backpack's lost items by running through them, and guide the duo to gather charms for the bracelet. Help them master the jumps and slides to complete their mission!
