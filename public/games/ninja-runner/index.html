Ninja Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ninja Runner is an exhilarating side-scrolling auto-runner game that will put your reflexes to the test! Players take on the role of a swift and agile ninja, dashing through vibrant streets filled with challenges and obstacles. As you navigate through the game, your main objective is to avoid bombs that can disrupt your journey. Keep your eyes sharp and reactions quicker as you tap the screen to make your ninja jump over these hazardous items.

As you run, coins line the streets, waiting to be collected. Gather as many as you can to increase your score and unlock exciting new features. The thrill of the chase is amplified as you aim to reach the finish line while dodging dangers that lie in your path. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Ninja Runner challenges players to improve their timing and precision with every session.

If you find yourself needing to take a break, simply pause the game using the space bar or the designated pause button. This enables you to strategize your next move without losing your progress. Whether you re a casual gamer or a seasoned pro, Ninja Runner offers an exciting experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat!

How to play free Ninja Runner game online

To play Ninja Runner, simply tap the screen or use the jump button to make your ninja leap over bombs and obstacles. As you progress, collect coins scattered along the path. To pause the game, press the space bar or the pause button. Dive into the action and enjoy the thrill of being a ninja!
