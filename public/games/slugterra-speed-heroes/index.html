Slugterra: Speed Heroes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Strap on your pilot helmet and get ready to dive into the exhilarating world of Slugterra: Speed Heroes! This exciting racing game takes you on a wild ride where the vehicles are not your typical racers. You'll need some time to adjust and master their unique controls. Your mission? To cross the finish line in first place! If you enjoy the Slugterra series, don t miss out on Battle for Slugterra, which promises just as much action!

In this game, your role is to navigate through various races, aiming to outpace your opponents and secure your position at the top. However, don't just focus on the finish line; you also have the option to disrupt your rivals speed. Can you outsmart and outdrive them to achieve victory? Gear up, hop into your state-of-the-art vehicle, and let the racing begin!

To start your racing journey, it's essential to familiarize yourself with the controls of your unique car. Simply use the left mouse click to activate the cursor, and then drag it to launch your vehicle in your desired direction. Remember, your initial boost will not take you very far, so prepare to repeat this action several times until you reach the finish line!

As you race, you will encounter three distinct challenges that you need to complete. Make sure to engage in all of them for a complete gaming experience! Depending on your performance, you can earn trophies for your placements; aim for at least third place to secure a reward!

How to play free Slugterra: Speed Heroes game online

Once in the game, familiarize yourself with your vehicle's controls. Use the left mouse button to click and drag to launch your car in the desired direction. Engage in different challenges to accumulate points and trophies, aiming to finish in the top three in each race for the best rewards!
