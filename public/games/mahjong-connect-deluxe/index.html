Mahjong Connect Deluxe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the timeless joy of Mahjong Connect Deluxe, a captivating puzzle game that draws inspiration from the beloved Chinese tile-removal classic, Mahjong. Your objective is to clear the board by eliminating all the tiles present. However, there's a twist: you can only connect identical tiles with a path consisting of no more than two turns, introducing a strategic element to your gameplay.

This game features a total of 18 challenging levels, each designed to test your matching skills and puzzle-solving abilities. To earn extra bonuses, make sure to complete each level before the time runs out. The carefully crafted tile arrangements will keep you engaged and provide a perfect blend of challenge and fun. Whether you re a seasoned player or new to Mahjong, Mahjong Connect Deluxe promises an exhilarating experience as you immerse yourself in a world of colors, shapes, and brain-teasing challenges.

With its intuitive mechanics and engaging gameplay, Mahjong Connect Deluxe caters to players of all ages. Embrace the satisfying feeling of clearing tiles and strategizing your moves as you progress through the increasingly difficult levels. Prepare to enjoy hours of entertainment as you master the art of tile connection!

How to play free Mahjong Connect Deluxe game online

Once you're in the game, start by identifying pairs of identical tiles on the board. Click on two matching tiles to connect them. Remember, the connection can only have a maximum of two turns. Aim to clear the board before the timer runs out for bonus rewards!
