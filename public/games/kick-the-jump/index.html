Kick the Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating game of Kick The Jump, your primary objective is to motivate a sluggish character to move from one area of his home to another. Utilizing a light-hearted poke mechanic, it's your job to encourage him to overcome obstacles and ultimately reach his destination. As you guide him, keep an eye out for various dangers lurking throughout the house. Watch for traps, perilous gaps, and other hazardous items that can impede his journey.

The game combines playful elements with strategic planning, encouraging players to think critically about the best ways to navigate the lazy man without putting him in harm s way. Every successful poke brings a sense of accomplishment, making it satisfying to see him progressively advance towards his goal. Your mission is not just about movement; it's about ensuring the lazy man's safety while making him happy as he overcomes the challenges that lie ahead.

Rich in vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics, Kick The Jump immerses players in a world where every poke has a purpose and fun reigns supreme. What will it take to motivate this laid-back character? Only your strategic instincts can determine his fate. Stay alert, as the house has many surprises, and ensure you guide him safely to his objective!

How to play free Kick The Jump game online

To play Kick The Jump, simply use your mouse or finger to poke the lazy character. Watch out for traps and hazardous gaps as you navigate through different rooms. Your goal is to keep him safe while pushing him along to reach his target. The game is easy to pick up but offers plenty of challenges!
