Dot Frame

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Dot Frame, an exhilarating game that challenges your reflexes and color recognition skills! In this fast-paced game, your objective is to keep the colorful dots securely within the frame while ensuring that they match the frame's color. The more dots you can successfully contain, the higher your score will climb!

As you dive into the vibrant world of Dot Frame, you'll notice that the primary challenge comes in the form of rotating the frame using simple taps on the screen. This strategic rotation is crucial, as it allows you to align the frame correctly with the dots. However, be careful! You must avoid touching the yellow border, as doing so will cost you valuable points and potentially end your game. Timing and precision are everything in Dot Frame, making it an engaging experience for players of all ages.

The visual appeal of Dot Frame is heightened by a dynamic color palette that keeps the game exciting. With each level, the speed increases, and the number of dots you need to manage grows, putting your skills to the ultimate test. Are you ready to take on the challenge and achieve the highest score? Start rotating your frame and get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure!

How to play free Dot Frame game online

To play Dot Frame, simply tap on the screen to rotate the colored frame. Your goal is to contain the dots that match the frame's color while avoiding contact with the yellow border. Stay focused and quick to maximize your score as more dots appear!
