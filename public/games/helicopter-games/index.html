How to play free Helicopter Games game online

Upon launching the game, select your desired level. Use the joystick located at the bottom right to maneuver your helicopter up and down, while the left joystick allows for forward, backward, and lateral movement. Avoid collisions, and successfully airlift civilians or drop supplies to complete levels and progress through the game.
