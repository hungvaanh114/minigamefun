Push My Chair

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an action-packed experience in the quirky world of Push My Chair! In this vibrant office battleground, players take on the roles of determined office workers armed with an eclectic array of tools and weapons. Your mission? To outmaneuver and outbattle your colleagues by pushing them out of the arena using your unique fighting gear!

Push My Chair offers an array of chairs and weaponry, ensuring that no two battles are alike. Whether you're utilizing a sturdy swivel chair or a classic rolling desk seat, each type brings its own flair to the fight. Weapons range from basic office supplies turned combat tools to creative, whimsical items that add a twist to your strategy. Challenge the conventional office environment and dive into an exhilarating showdown where skill and quick thinking are key to your victory.

The game supports both single-player and two-player modes, making it the perfect choice for competing against friends or honing your skills solo. Invite a buddy over for a hilarious couch co-op session, or take on the AI in thrilling solo challenges. With fast-paced gameplay, engaging graphics, and lighthearted yet intense competition, Push My Chair guarantees endless fun!

How to play free Push My Chair game online

To play Push My Chair, simply select your character and choose your weapon. Use the arrow keys or joystick to navigate and aim your attacks. Push your opponents off the edge of the arena to score points. Keep an eye on your surroundings and make quick decisions to stay in the game!
