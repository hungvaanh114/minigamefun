Zombie Shooter 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chaotic world of Zombie Shooter 3D, where survival is the only goal amidst a zombie apocalypse that emerged in the year 2037. A catastrophic outbreak of the deadly Ares Virus, unleashed by a covert organization known as the Doom Haters, has transformed cities into lifeless wastelands. With most of the population turned into the walking dead, it s up to you to rise as a seasoned SAS zombie shooter, battling through the pandemic-ridden streets.

In this thrilling online multiplayer PvP mode, brace yourself for intense combat as you encounter hordes of the undead. Your mission? Eliminate zombies with precision headshots while uncovering the mysteries shrouding the plague. The world, now a decayed dead zone, is fraught with danger at every corner, demanding your utmost skill and bravery.

Equip yourself with an arsenal of deadly weapons, and remember hold down that trigger and never release! Feel the adrenaline surge as you blast through unrelenting waves of zombies, chasing down every last undead creature in your path. But be vigilant; the threat is ever-present, and your objective is to save the remaining SAS operatives scattered throughout the chaos. Don t hesitate grab your guns and stand your ground as the dead rise around you!

How to play free Zombie Shooter 3D game online

To start playing Zombie Shooter 3D, simply join an online match and select your preferred character. Use your mouse to aim and shoot, and don t forget to navigate through the eerie landscapes to locate surviving SAS members. Coordinate with your team and unleash your firepower against the advancing hordes in this electrifying zombie shooter experience!
