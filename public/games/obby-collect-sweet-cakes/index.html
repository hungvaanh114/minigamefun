Obby Collect Sweet Cakes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Collect all the sweet cakes in this exciting game called Obby Collect Sweet Cakes In this exciting game you have to find 122 sweet cakes that are scattered around the cute town! Explore the city in search of sweet cakes. Can you collect them all?

Instructions

Computer management Mouse - look around WASD - movement Space bar - jump Control on the phone On-screen joystick for moving Use the on-screen buttons to run and jump
