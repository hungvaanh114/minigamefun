Reef Color Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting journey into the splendid underwater realm of Reef Color Challenge! This exhilarating logic game immerses you in a colorful environment where you become the master of a single block, skillfully maneuvering it horizontally above an ever-growing stack of vibrant blocks. With every turn, your block changes color, and your primary objective is to strategically match it with the corresponding colored block below. Achieving the perfect alignment will cause the entire row to vanish, sending the surrounding blocks soaring upwards and introducing a new challenge.

Your swift reflexes and clever planning will be put to the ultimate test as you race against the clock to clear as many rows as possible within the stipulated time. The game demands quick thinking and sharp instincts, making every second count as you strive to conquer the depths of this colorful aquatic adventure. Do you have what it takes to rise through the ranks and claim the title of the ultimate Reef Color Champion? Dive in and discover your underwater prowess!

How to play free Reef Color Challenge game online

Playing the Reef Color Challenge is simple! Use your mouse or arrow keys to move your block left or right. When your block aligns with a block of the same color below, click to match and clear the row. Keep an eye on the timer and stay sharp to maximize your score!

Touch in the columns to move the tile and match them
