Instructions:

Are you a fan of challenging physics-based puzzle games? If so, then Pop The Bear is tailor-made for you! In this engaging game, your main objective is to use your wits and critical thinking skills to solve each level. The goal is simple yet challenging: drop a watermelon to hit a bear while trying to collect three stars along the way. The fun lies in figuring out the best angle and timing to achieve your mission, making every drop count.

As you progress through the various levels, you'll encounter unique obstacles and puzzles that will test your problem-solving abilities. Timing, precision, and an understanding of physics will be your best friends as you navigate through each stage. The satisfaction of successfully splatting the bear and collecting all three stars is rewarding, encouraging you to think strategically. Do you have what it takes to pop the bear and prove your proficiency in physics puzzles? Give it a try, and enjoy the challenge!

How to play free Pop The Bear game online

To play Pop The Bear, start by selecting a watermelon from the available options. Aim carefully using your mouse or finger to adjust the drop point. Release to let the watermelon fall and hopefully hit the bear! Keep an eye on the stars and aim to collect all three in each level for a perfect score.
