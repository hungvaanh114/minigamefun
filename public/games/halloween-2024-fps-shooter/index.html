How to play free HALLOWEEN 2024 FPS SHOOTER game online

To play the game, simply move your character using the WASD keys, aim with your mouse, and shoot using the left mouse button. Keep an eye out for ammo pickups on the ground to reload your weapon and continue shooting the relentless waves of zombies. Enjoy the adrenaline rush!
