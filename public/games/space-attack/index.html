Space Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in the thrilling game of Space Attack. As Earth faces an onslaught of asteroids and small celestial bodies, it is your mission to defend our planet for as long as you can! Utilizing your mouse, you will maneuver your spaceship to dodge incoming threats while simultaneously shooting them down. Protect Earth by maximizing your firepower and strategic movements.

Throughout the game, you will encounter multiple levels, each presenting its own unique challenges. You will have a limited amount of ammunition at your disposal, but successfully eliminating threats will not only clear your path but also increase your ammo count. At the end of each level, gather extra points and ammunition to increase your overall score, enhancing your capability to conquer even tougher levels.

Space Attack offers an array of thrilling bonuses, including three different types of spaceships, each equipped with distinct features to suit your playstyle. Choose from three varieties of lasers to maximize your offensive capabilities, and don't forget to upgrade your spaceship's life protection to withstand tougher opposition. The more you play, the more you can enhance your firepower and protective capabilities, making each session more exciting than the last.

How to play free Space Attack game online

To play Space Attack, simply use your mouse to control the spaceship's movement. Click to shoot down the incoming asteroids and small planets. Pay attention to your ammo count and collect bonuses at the end of each level to score extra points and gain more firing potential. Keep defending Earth!
