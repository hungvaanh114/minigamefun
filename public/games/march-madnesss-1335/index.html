How to play free March Madness game online

To play the March Madness game, simply choose your favorite team and navigate through the tournament bracket. Use the arrow keys to move your player, and the spacebar to shoot. Make strategic plays, exploit your opponent s weaknesses, and aim for victory. Enjoy the competitive spirit!
