Chinu Neko

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Chinu Neko, a delightful 2D platformer that will charm cat lovers and gamers alike! In this whimsical adventure, you control an adorable neko, a cat-like character on a quest to collect luscious cat food bowls scattered throughout each vibrant level. As you navigate through the beautifully crafted environments, you must stay vigilant and avoid a variety of hazards, including enemy cats that seek to disrupt your journey, ground saws that slice through the air, and treacherous spikes lurking beneath your paws.

Chinu Neko features 8 meticulously designed levels with increasing difficulty, ensuring that each stage presents new challenges and obstacles that test your skills and reflexes. As you progress through the game, the pace quickens, and the foes become more cunning, pushing you to perfect your timing and strategies. With its charming graphics, catchy soundtracks, and engaging gameplay mechanics, Chinu Neko offers an experience that's perfect for players of all ages looking for a fun and challenging adventure.

Avoiding enemies and maneuvering through dangerous traps will require both skill and quick thinking. The vibrant worlds you'll explore in Chinu Neko not only reward your perseverance but also invite you to unravel the puzzles hidden within. Collect as many cat food bowls as you can and dash toward the Exit door to unlock new levels and continue your delightful escapade!

How to play free Chinu Neko game online

To play Chinu Neko online, simply use the arrow keys or joystick to move your neko character. Jump with the spacebar or designated key to leap over obstacles, avoiding enemies and hazards as you collect cat food bowls. Reach the Exit door at the end of each level to advance. Enjoy your feline adventure!
