My Quarantine Glam Look

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of My Quarantine Glam Look, a fun-filled game featuring the trendy Rainbow Girls: Skyler, Sunny, Ruby, and Violet. Tired of the same daily routine while stuck at home during quarantine, these stylish friends decided to engage in some delightful indoor fashion activities. Join them on this chic adventure and unleash your creativity!

In My Quarantine Glam Look, you will have the chance to experiment with vibrant colors and trendy makeup styles. Choose from an array of adorable pink shades for blushes, eyeshadows, and lip colors. Mix and match various eye colors to create stunning looks that reflect your unique style. The game not only encourages creativity but also adds a dash of humor as you dress up the girls in eye-catching outfits and makeup.

Your expertise will shine as you navigate through different levels of fashion challenges, helping each girl emerge with a glamorous look. Don t forget to capture those playful moments and take part in some interactive activities that the girls have planned. It's the perfect way to bond with friends while having loads of fun; so gear up and get ready to give these Rainbow Girls a makeover they won t forget!

How to play free My Quarantine Glam Look game online

To play My Quarantine Glam Look, start by selecting one of the four Rainbow Girls. Use your mouse to pick various makeup items including blush, eyeshadow, and lip colors. Experiment with different combinations until you create the perfect look. Once done, showcase your stylized girl in a fabulous fashion show!
