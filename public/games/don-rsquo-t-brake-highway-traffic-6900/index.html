Don t Brake - Highway Traffic is an exhilarating driving simulation game that immerses players in realistic urban traffic scenarios. Navigate through bustling city streets and busy highways, avoiding collisions with other vehicles to prevent accidents. This game emphasizes the importance of obeying traffic rules while providing an adrenaline-filled experience as you maneuver your car through endless lanes of traffic.

In this game, players have the chance to test their driving skills by piloting their car across various intersections without hitting oncoming vehicles. You will earn points for safely crossing busy roads, which can be used to unlock a variety of new, colorful cars. Enjoy the exhilarating challenge of driving through obstacle-laden roads in one of the best obstacle road racing games available.

To play Don t Brake - Highway Traffic, simply tap on the screen to accelerate your car, allowing it to speed up and navigate through the heavy flow of traffic. Releasing your finger will engage the brakes, enabling you to slow down and avoid potential hazards on the road. The objective is to safely cross as many intersections as possible while adhering to traffic rules, all while striving for a higher score. Invite your friends to compete for the top score in this exciting free driving simulator!
