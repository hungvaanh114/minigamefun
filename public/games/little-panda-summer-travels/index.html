Little Panda Summer Travels

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an enchanting adventure with Little Panda Summer Travels, a delightful travel game that offers an array of exciting activities. The game is divided into four vibrant sections: Hula Dance Party, Thanksgiving Party, Pyramid Archaeology, and Ancient Egyptian Masquerade Ball. Each part promises a unique blend of crafting, culinary experiences, dress-up fun, and thrilling exploration.

In the Hula Dance Party, join Little Panda as you create your own grass skirts and dance to infectious tropical tunes. Experience the joy of community and creativity as you showcase your hula dancing skills. Move on to the Thanksgiving Party, where you ll have the chance to decorate a cozy living room and cook a mouthwatering turkey, ensuring that the festive spirit shines brightly.

Your journey continues in Pyramid Archaeology, where you ll delve into ancient ruins in search of shattered pieces of a cat statue. Use your problem-solving skills to carefully reassemble the fragments, uncovering fascinating pieces of history along the way. Finally, don your most elaborate attire for the Ancient Egyptian Masquerade Ball, where you can dress up and dance the night away amid friends and fantastic decorations.

How to play free Little Panda Summer Travels game online

To play Little Panda Summer Travels, simply navigate through the various sections of the game using your mouse or touch controls. Follow on-screen instructions to complete tasks, craft items, and participate in mini-games. Explore each party s activities to unlock new experiences and achieve high scores!
