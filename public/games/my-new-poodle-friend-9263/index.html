My New Poodle Friend

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

As Sofia s birthday approaches, her friend Jack has the perfect surprise in mind: a poodle! Knowing how much Sofia adores animals and her desire to raise a dog in her yard, Jack is determined to make this birthday unforgettable. With the excitement building, they embark on a journey to prepare for the arrival of Sofia s new furry friend.

Before the poodle arrives, Sofia has some essential tasks to tackle. First on her list is the old pet house situated in her yard. It's a bit worn down, but with a little effort and creativity, Sofia plans to restore it to its former glory. As players join Sofia in this delightful adventure, they can help her repair the pet house, ensuring it s cozy and welcoming for her new companion.

Once the repairs are done, the next step is all about decoration! Players can help Sofia choose vibrant colors, charming accessories, and comfy bedding to make the pet house the perfect sanctuary for her poodle. This creative aspect allows players to personalize the space, making it a true reflection of Sofia s love for animals.

Each part of this journey is filled with joy and anticipation as the big day approaches. Join Sofia in her mission to get ready for her pet poodle and share in the excitement of welcoming a new friend into her life!

How to play free My New Poodle Friend game online

To play My New Poodle Friend, begin by navigating through different tasks that Sofia must complete in preparation for her poodle's arrival. Use your mouse or tap the screen to interact with items, repair the pet house, and select decorations. Complete each challenge to unlock new features and get Sofia ready for her special gift!
