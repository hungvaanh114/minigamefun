Baby Taylor Fashion New Look

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Baby Taylor Fashion New Look! As the new year begins, Taylor's mother has a fantastic idea to refresh their style by going on a shopping spree at the mall. This fun adventure is all about creativity and fashion as they explore new hairstyles, stunning nail designs, and trendy outfits. With various options available, Taylor is eager to find the perfect combination that reflects her vibrant personality.

In this delightful game, you will help Taylor choose from an array of hairstyles, colors, and accessories to achieve her dream look. From braids to bouncy curls, every hairstyle adds a unique touch. Once her hair is styled, you ll have a blast picking out fabulous nail designs. Choose from a variety of colors and patterns to make her nails pop!

After completing the hair and nail transformations, it s time to shop for stylish clothes. Browse through racks full of fashionable outfits and fabulous accessories. You ll need to mix and match various pieces to create the perfect ensemble for Taylor. With your help, she will look stunning and ready to take on the new year in style!

How to play free Baby Taylor Fashion New Look game online

To play the Baby Taylor Fashion New Look game, simply use your mouse or touchscreen. Start by selecting the hairstyle you want for Taylor, then proceed to choose the nails, and finally pick out the perfect clothes. Explore all the fashionable options to create a unique look for Taylor and enjoy this stylish makeover adventure!
