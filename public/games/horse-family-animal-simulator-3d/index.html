Horse Family Animal Simulator 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling journey with the Horse Family Animal Simulator 3D, where your equine companion is poised for an extraordinary adventure! Navigate through a vast and vibrant island while completing an array of exciting tasks. Engage in family life as you breed adorable baby-foals, enhancing your horse family dynamics.

Compete in exhilarating contests to showcase your prowess and develop your horse s strengths. Make your steed even more stunning by customizing its appearance with unique decorations and upgrades. Additionally, beautify your cozy home to create a delightful retreat for your horse and its family.

The world is brimming with challenges, including defending yourself against lurking forest predators. Lend a helping hoof to other horses in need and assist the villagers in their quests as well. With an open-world experience, explore diverse environments filled with different creatures, and immerse yourself in engaging mini-games that promise to keep you entertained.

Achieve milestones and unlock rewards that elevate your gameplay experience, adding depth and excitement to your adventure. Whether you're leading your horse through enchanting landscapes or participating in competitive events, the Horse Family Animal Simulator 3D offers endless fun for players of all ages!

How to play free Horse Family Animal Simulator 3D game online

To play the game, simply navigate through the island using your control keys to guide your horse. Engage in missions displayed on your screen, interact with other characters, and complete challenges to progress. Customize your horse and home from the menu options and strive to achieve various in-game rewards!


Instructions

- WASD or arrow keys to control the horse - left mouse button to attack - space to jump - shift to run
