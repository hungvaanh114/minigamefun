Giant Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Giant Race 3D is an exciting and casual gaming experience that fuses elements of parkour with action-packed fighting mechanics. As you dash through each thrilling level, you will encounter a vibrant cast of characters, each represented by different colors. To empower yourself and enhance your abilities, you need to collect characters of the same color as you race through the course.

However, it's not just about collecting characters; danger lurks at every corner. As you navigate the obstacles, including menacing spikes and rolling gears, you must stay alert and agile to avoid losing your hard-earned followers. Every hit can diminish the strength of your crew, making it crucial to stay focused and dodge these treacherous threats.

While journeying through the game, you'll also want to gather diamonds. These precious gems are not only satisfying to collect but can also be used to unlock a variety of fascinating accessories in the shop. These upgrades will not only add flair to your character but may also provide strategic advantages in your races.

As you progress, you'll face off against formidable bosses that will put your skills to the test. Defeating these giants is essential to advancing to the next level, adding a layer of challenge and excitement to Giant Race 3D. Join this lively race, prove your abilities, and become the ultimate giant!

How to play free Giant Race 3D game online

To play Giant Race 3D, simply use the arrow keys or swipe your screen to control your character s movements. Collect as many characters of the same color as possible while dodging obstacles and gathering diamonds. Keep an eye out for the big boss at the end of the level defeat them to move forward!
