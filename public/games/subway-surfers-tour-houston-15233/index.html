Subway Surfers Tour Houston

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Subway Surfers World Tour: Houston marks the one hundred eleventh update in the iconic Subway Surfers series and represents the one hundred sixth installment in the highly popular World Tour theme. This exciting new chapter invites players to explore the vibrant streets of Houston, adding a fresh and exhilarating dimension to the gameplay.

In this riveting update, players can enjoy the thrill of collecting coins, dodging trains, and evading the pesky Inspector and his dog while taking in the stunning backdrop that Houston offers. Among the highlights of this update are two new characters joining the fun. Firstly, players can unlock Alba, available for 95,000 Coins, bringing a unique flair to the game. Additionally, the Starcruiser character can be obtained for 50,000 Coins, adding more diversity to the players' roster. The introduction of these characters not only enhances gameplay but also adds a charming twist to the Subway Surfers universe.

The new Houston environment is packed with iconic locations, vibrant graffiti art, and exhilarating routes that challenge players' reflexes and skills. As you dash through the city, keep an eye out for collectible items to maximize your score and progress through the game. This update promises to captivate both new players and veterans alike with its colorful graphics, engaging gameplay, and the thrill of exploration.

How to play free Subway Surfers Tour Houston game online

To play Subway Surfers Tour Houston, simply swipe left, right, up, or down to navigate your character through the subway tracks. Avoid oncoming trains and obstacles while collecting coins and power-ups. The goal is to run as far as you can without getting caught by the Inspector! Enjoy the adrenaline rush!
