Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Happy Doctor Mania - Doctor Game, an engaging and immersive experience that lets you step into the role of a beloved doctor! As one of the most prestigious professions, being a doctor comes with immense responsibilities and rewards. In this exciting game, you'll assist our charming young doctor, Dr. Mania, as she prepares to operate on three critically injured patients.

Your journey begins by assessing the patients to gather essential information. You'll conduct various examinations, such as checking their heart rate and performing MRI scans. This initial step is crucial for understanding the severity of their injuries, ensuring that you're well-prepared for the surgery ahead.

Once the assessments are complete, the next important task involves taking care of the wounds. You'll need to carefully clean the injuries to prevent any infections or complications that could arise from harmful viruses. It s essential to act quickly and efficiently! Following that, you will also be responsible for bandaging the surgical wounds, ensuring the patients are comfortable and secure during their healing process.

The game not only tests your medical skills but also puts your patience and attention to detail to the test. Are you ready to dive into the world of medicine and help these patients recover? Step into the shoes of Dr. Mania and embrace the challenge of being a fantastic doctor!

How to play free Happy Doctor Mania - Doctor Game online

To play Happy Doctor Mania - Doctor Game, use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the patient on screen. Follow the prompts to conduct examinations, clean wounds, and bandage injuries. Complete all tasks carefully to ensure a successful surgery and help your patients recover!
