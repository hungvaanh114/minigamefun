Monster Hands

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Monster Hands, where playful monsters are just waiting to be arranged in the perfect order! In this engaging puzzle game, your goal is to press on these delightful creatures, maneuvering them to connect through their hands. Each connection signifies a new opportunity to earn points and achieve victory!

As you navigate through various levels, you ll need to strategically rotate the monsters until all the stars align perfectly where their hands intertwine. Each star represents progress in the game, and a successful connection yields higher rewards. With vibrant graphics and charming sound effects, Monster Hands keeps you entertained while testing your logic and spatial awareness.

With multiple challenging stages, be prepared to encounter increasingly intricate configurations that require sharp timing and meticulous planning. As you unlock new levels, you ll be greeted by a variety of amusing monsters, each with unique colors and designs. Get ready to think creatively and devise clever solutions to the puzzles in front of you. Can you earn all the stars and become a Monster Hands master?

How to play free Monster Hands game online

To play Monster Hands, simply tap on the monsters to rotate them and create hand connections. Aim to line up all the stars at the intersections of the hands. Keep an eye on your moves, strategize effectively, and try to complete each level for the best score!


Instructions

Press on the monsters to turn them in the way they connect with each other by the hands
