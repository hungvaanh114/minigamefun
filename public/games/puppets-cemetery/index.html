Puppets Cemetery

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a world of spooky thrills with the captivating game, Puppets Cemetery. This unique Halloween-themed arena shooter invites players to explore a hauntingly beautiful environment filled with eerie puppets and cunning adversaries. Set against the backdrop of a chilling cemetery, the game combines traditional top-down shooting mechanics with a stylish gothic aesthetic. Players must navigate through intricately designed arenas, battling waves of sinister puppets that spring to life under the light of a full moon.

As you immerse yourself in Puppets Cemetery, you'll encounter a variety of weapons and power-ups, each offering unique advantages against your foes. The fast-paced gameplay challenges players to be quick on their feet, dodging attacks while delivering precision shots. With multiple arenas to conquer, each level presents a new challenge that will test your strategy and reflexes. The thrill of surviving intense waves of puppets not only keeps you on your toes but also promises a rewarding experience as you progress further into the game.

This spine-chilling shooter is perfect for Halloween season, ensuring hours of fun and excitement. Gather your friends or brave the cemetery solo, either way, you re bound to be entertained by the imaginative design and engaging gameplay that Puppets Cemetery has to offer. Are you ready to unveil the secrets of the cemetery and become the ultimate puppet master?

How to play free Puppets Cemetery game online

To play Puppets Cemetery online, start by selecting your character and weapon preference. Use the arrow keys or WASD for movement while aiming and shooting with your mouse. Collect power-ups and defeat the puppets to clear each wave, strategically using the landscape to your advantage to survive the haunting challenges!

WASD or ARROW KEYS to move the player MOUSE to aim and shoot RMB E SPACEBAR to pick up weapons
