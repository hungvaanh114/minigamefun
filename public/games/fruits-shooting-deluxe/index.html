Fruits Shooting Deluxe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fruits Shooting Deluxe, an exhilarating adventure where precision and strategy collide! Embark on a journey across vibrant levels filled with tantalizing fruits just waiting to be collected. Your main weapon? A trusty cannon! Aim carefully and shoot meticulously to gather every piece of fruit in sight, but proceed with caution each cannonball comes at a cost. Manage your ammunition wisely, as money is limited, and you'll need to get every last bit of fruit before your funds run dry.

Throughout your quest, you'll encounter mystifying bonus items that can amplify your earnings or grant you powerful shots to enhance your gameplay. This clever addition not only enriches your experience but also adds layers of excitement as you strategize your moves. Can you master the physics of your cannon and overcome the challenges of each level? Step into the captivating world of Fruits Shooting Deluxe and discover the joy of fruit-collecting in this addictive shooting game!

How to play free Fruits Shooting Deluxe game online

To play Fruits Shooting Deluxe, simply use your mouse to aim the cannon towards the fruits on the screen. Click to shoot and collect as many fruits as you can without running out of ammo. Make sure to target bonus items for extra rewards! Enjoy your journey!
