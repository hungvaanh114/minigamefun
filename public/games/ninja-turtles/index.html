Ninja Turtles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, often known by their acronym TMNT or simply Ninja Turtles, are a quartet of legendary, anthropomorphic turtles, each named after distinguished Italian artists from the Renaissance era. These unique characters have been trained in the ancient and secretive art of ninjutsu by their wise mentor, an anthropomorphic rat. Living in the hidden sewers of New York City, the Ninja Turtles engage in thrilling battles against petty criminals, nefarious overlords, mutated foes, and extraterrestrial threats, all while striving to keep their existence a secret from the outside world.

Created collaboratively by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird, the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles initially emerged in the form of comic books released by Mirage Studios. Their popularity exploded in the late 1980s and early 1990s, leading to the development of successful animated television series, blockbuster films, action-packed video games, a vast array of toys, and a wide range of merchandise. The franchise has since become a beloved icon in popular culture, captivating generations of fans with its compelling storylines, memorable characters, and action-filled adventures.
