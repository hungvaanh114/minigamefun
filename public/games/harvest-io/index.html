Harvest.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of agricultural adventure with Harvest.io, an exciting new farming simulator that brings the addictive .io gameplay style to the fields! In this captivating game, you take on the role of a skilled farmer, navigating your trusty tractor across vast fields to reap the bountiful harvest. As you drive, you'll collect crops to fill up your fodder trailer, which grows larger with every successful scoop. However, this increasing size presents its own challenges, making it increasingly difficult to maneuver your tractor without colliding into obstacles!

The vibrant graphics and intuitive controls enhance your experience as you race against time to harvest as much as possible. The competition heats up as other players also aim to expand their trailers, leading to chaotic and exhilarating gameplay moments. Watch out for rogue tractors and avoid crashes! Timing and strategy become crucial as you work to balance speed and caution. Can you outsmart the competition and become the top farmer in this lively world?

Whether you're a casual gamer or a seasoned pro, Harvest.io offers something for everyone. Use your skills to strategically navigate the fields and gather the largest yield while dodging other players in this fun and engaging farming simulator. Join the excitement, and see how much you can harvest before time runs out!

How to play free Harvest.io game online

To start playing Harvest.io, simply control your tractor using your keyboard or touchscreen. Navigate the fields to collect crops, avoiding collisions with other players and obstacles. Keep an eye on your growing trailer and aim for the highest score by harvesting as quickly as possible!
