Super Mario Run 21

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Super Mario Run 21, an exhilarating endless runner game that puts your reflexes to the test! Join Mario on an exciting adventure where your goal is to keep him running while dodging treacherous obstacles and pesky enemies. Navigate through a variety of beautifully designed environments, each more challenging than the last, as you collect power-ups and coins to enhance your journey.

What sets Super Mario Run 21 apart is its customization feature, allowing you to personalize your version of Mario with different outfits and accessories. Want him to sport a classic look or try something entirely new? The choice is yours! This versatility not only adds a fun twist to the gameplay but also lets you express your style as you race against the clock and compete for high scores.

As you speed through thrilling levels, remember to keep an eye out for power-ups that can give you an edge over your foes. From speed boosts to invincibility stars, these special items will help you tackle even the most daunting challenges. Whether you're a longtime fan of the franchise or a newcomer, Super Mario Run 21 promises hours of engaging gameplay that is both nostalgic and fresh.

How to play free Super Mario Run 21 game online

To play Super Mario Run 21 online, simply click on the 'Play' button after loading the game. Use the spacebar or touch screen to make Mario jump and navigate through the world. Avoid obstacles, defeat enemies, and collect coins to increase your score. Have fun running!
