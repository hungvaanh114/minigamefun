Cube Stack 2048

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Cube Stack 2048 is a highly addictive casual game inspired by the classic puzzle game 2048, but with exciting new gameplay! Merge numbered cubes to double their value, reach 2048, or even higher! Try to make it to the end. Explore tons of amazing and challenging levels by merging cubes, avoiding obstacles, and collecting money!
