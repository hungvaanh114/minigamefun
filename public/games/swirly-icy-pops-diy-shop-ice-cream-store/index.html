Swirly Icy Pops DIY Shop - Ice Cream Store

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the Swirly Icy Pops DIY Shop - Ice Cream Store, where summer's heat meets icy bliss! Join Blush the Bunny on an exciting adventure through the vibrant pet city, where the sun is shining bright, and there's no better way to cool down than with delectable ice pops and frozen treats. This fun-filled ice cream truck is not just any ordinary vehicle; it's a magic bus where your creativity knows no bounds!

As you hop on board, you will have access to a wide variety of flavors, from classic vanilla to exotic tropical fruits. Choose your favorite ingredient and mix them into delightful ice pops. But that s not all! Add a twist to your creations by selecting from an array of shapes and sizes, turning every treat into a work of art. With countless toppings and sprinkles to choose from, you can design the most outrageous and colorful icy pops that will delight all of Blush's fluffy friends.

Your mission is to bring joy and excitement to the pets in the city by creating custom ice pops that cater to their unique tastes. Experiment with different combinations and watch the smiles on their faces as they indulge in your fantastic frozen creations. It's not just about making treats; it's about making memories in the sweetest way possible!

How to play free Swirly Icy Pops DIY Shop - Ice Cream Store game online

To play the game, start by clicking on your desired flavor from the menu. Then choose the shape and size of your icy pops. Add toppings and sprinkles by dragging them onto the treat. Once you're satisfied with your creation, serve it to Blush's furry friends and earn points for each happy customer!
