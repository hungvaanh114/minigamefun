Where Is the Ball ?

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting game of Where Is The Ball?! In this engaging challenge, players are tested on their observation skills and quick thinking. At the start of each round, the ball is hidden beneath one of the cups, and players may choose to play with either 3 or 6 cups, adding to the fun and strategy of the game. Once the game begins, the cups will swap positions rapidly and randomly, creating a delightful puzzle for your mind.

As the cups move, it's essential to keep your eyes on the one that holds the ball. The quicker you focus and remember the cup's location, the greater your chances of guessing correctly when the movement ceases. If you successfully identify the cup that conceals the ball, you'll proceed to the next round, where the movements increase in speed, elevating the challenge even further. This game is perfect for players of all ages and can be enjoyed solo or with friends. Get ready to test your reflexes and memory in this captivating game of skill!

How to play free Where Is The Ball ? game online

Playing Where Is The Ball? is simple! To begin, select the number of cups you wish to use, either 3 or 6. Once you start, watch closely as the cups shuffle. When they stop moving, click on the cup you believe contains the ball. The faster you guesscorrectly, the more challenging the game becomes!
