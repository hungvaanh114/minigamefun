Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Explore the world of delicious fruits with the engaging educational game, Find Fruits Names. In this interactive word puzzle, players are invited to identify different fruits based on images displayed in the right panel. This game not only sharpens your vocabulary but also enhances your cognitive skills as you work to solve the puzzles presented. With each level, you are challenged to click on the correct letters to spell out the name of the fruit accurately.

The format of the game is both simple and exciting. As you progress through the levels, you will find a variety of fruit images, each requiring your keen observation and spelling prowess. However, there s a twist: you have a limited number of lives, specifically five, and making a wrong selection will deduct one from your total. This adds an element of strategy and urgency to the game, motivating players to think carefully before making a choice.

Whether you are a seasoned word game enthusiast or a newcomer eager to learn, Find Fruits Names provides a fun way to expand your knowledge while enjoying the thrill of word puzzles. So, prepare yourself for an adventure filled with vibrant fruit imagery and challenging spelling tasks!

How to play free Find Fruits Names game online

To play the game, simply observe the fruit image displayed on the right panel. Select letters from the given options by clicking on them to form the correct spelling. Keep an eye on your lives and avoid making wrong choices to maintain your gameplay! Good luck!
