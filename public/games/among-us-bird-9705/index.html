Among Us Bird is a delightful twist on the popular social deduction game, Among Us, integrating avian characters into its gameplay. Developed by Innersloth, Among Us Bird takes inspiration from the original 2018 game known for its thrilling blend of cooperation and betrayal, where players must navigate the complexities of teamwork while uncovering hidden dangers among them. This fun-filled adaptation introduces colorful bird avatars, adding a whimsical touch to the gaming experience.

As you embark on your interstellar journey, you'll play online with 4 to 15 other players, either globally or through local WiFi connections. Your main objective is to maintain the spaceship's functionality and ensure the crew's survival. However, lurking within your ranks are one or more Impostors sly deceivers whose goal is to eliminate the rest of the crew without being detected. Engage in various tasks around the ship, like fixing wires or fueling engines, while staying vigilant for any suspicious behavior from your fellow players.

This entertaining game not only relies on teamwork but also sharpens social skills as players must communicate, collaborate, and make difficult decisions about whom to trust and whom to accuse. In Among Us Bird, every game is unique, filled with unexpected twists and turns that keep players on their toes.

How to play free Among Us Bird game online

To play Among Us Bird, join a lobby by selecting either online or local play. Choose a game room or create your own, then customize your bird character. Once in the game, complete assigned tasks while staying alert for the Impostor. Use emergency meetings to discuss suspicions and vote out players you believe are the Impostor!
