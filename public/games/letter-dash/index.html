Letter Dash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Letter Dash is an exhilarating typing game that challenges players' reflexes and accuracy as they race against time. In this fast-paced game, participants are confronted with a cascade of letters falling from above. Players must swiftly type the matching characters on their keyboard to obliterate them before they hit the ground. The main goal is to type the letters correctly and at a rapid pace, ensuring they don t accumulate and ultimately reach the bottom of the screen.

The game offers an engaging way to enhance typing skills while under pressure. As players advance through the levels, they'll find that the falling letters not only come faster but may also include tricky combinations, adding a layer of complexity. This escalating challenge keeps players on their toes and encourages them to improve their typing speed and precision. With every second counting, players can feel the adrenaline rush as they battle to maintain control over the incoming letters.

Letter Dash is perfect for individuals looking to practice their typing or simply enjoy a fun, competitive game. It s easy to pick up but hard to master, making it suitable for all skill levels. Whether you are a beginner working on your keyboard skills or an experienced typist seeking to refine your abilities, Letter Dash is an exciting way to test your typing prowess.

How to play free Letter Dash game online

To play Letter Dash, simply start the game and watch as letters fall from the top of the screen. Quickly type the letters on your keyboard to eliminate them before they reach the bottom. Focus on accuracy and speed to keep the pile from building up!


Instructions

Tap on the letters on the keyboard or press the key on your keyboard if you play on a PC
