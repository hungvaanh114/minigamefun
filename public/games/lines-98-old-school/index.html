Lines 98 Old School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Classic puzzle Balls of Lines 98, made in old school style. Move the balls around the field so as to line up 5 balls of the same color in a row. Score points and take the first place in the leaderboard.

Instructions

Control - touch the ball you want to move and with the second touch choose where to move this ball When playing from a computer select a ball with a mouse click and the next click indicate where to move this ball
