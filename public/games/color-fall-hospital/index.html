Color Fall Hospital

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Color Fall Hospital, an interactive puzzle game where you step into a vibrant world filled with colors and challenges! Your mission is to manage the flow of colorful substances by skillfully maneuvering sliding pins that can move up, down, left, or right. Each truck is designated for a specific color, and it s your job to ensure that each hue reaches its destination safely.

As you navigate the pins, you'll need to think strategically about how to direct the colors while avoiding black hazards that can ruin your efforts. The black obstacles are dangerous and anything they touch will be destroyed, leading to a frustrating restart of the level. Take your time and observe the layout carefully to execute a flawless transfer of colors to their respective trucks.

Color Fall Hospital not only tests your planning and problem-solving skills but also entertains with its engaging graphics and soothing sound effects. Each level presents a unique challenge, and as you progress, expect an increase in difficulty that will require more complex maneuvers and quicker thinking. Get ready to dive into a world of color and overcome obstacles with precision to succeed!

How to play free Color Fall Hospital game online

To play Color Fall Hospital, use your mouse or finger on the touchscreen to slide the pins in the direction you want. Move the pins meticulously to guide the colors into their designated trucks. Avoid the black hazards and strategize your moves to ensure successful delivery of all the colors. If you touch black, restart the level!
