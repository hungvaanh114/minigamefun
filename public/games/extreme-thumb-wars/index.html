Extreme Thumb Wars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thumb-wrestling extravaganza with Extreme Thumb Wars! This exciting game brings the classic thumb war challenge to your devices, capturing the fun and competitiveness of this beloved pastime. Players can choose from over twenty unique thumb characters, each with distinct traits and styles, adding an extra layer of excitement to every match.

In Extreme Thumb Wars, you can challenge your friends in intense thumb battles or take on the CPU in single-player mode. The game features two essential indicators: the health bar and the power bar. Your goal is to deplete your opponent's power bar by smashing their thumb, all while protecting your own health. As you gain control over your thumb character, unleash powerful moves and tactics to outsmart and outmaneuver your opponents!

As you engage in these thrilling thumb duels, you ll find that strategy becomes just as important as speed. Timing your attacks and anticipating your opponent's moves can make all the difference in the match. So gear up, pick your favorite thumb character, and prepare for a contest filled with action, laughter, and rivalry!

How to play free Extreme Thumb Wars game online

To play Extreme Thumb Wars, select your thumb character and choose to battle against either a friend or the computer. Use the on-screen buttons to perform attacks and block your opponent s moves. Keep an eye on the health and power bars, and aim to smash your opponent s thumb before they can defeat yours!


Instructions

You can control the game by touch controls or by W and UP ARROW KEY in keyboarded devices
