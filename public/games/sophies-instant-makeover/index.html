Sophies Instant Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the delightful game "Sophie's Instant Makeover," players are invited to join Sophie on her journey to rejuvenation and self-care. Feeling exhausted and a little down, Sophie is in desperate need of a pampering session that will lift her spirits and restore her inner glow. As the player, you will take on the role of Sophie s personal stylist and spa therapist, guiding her through a transformative experience.

Your first task is to give Sophie a soothing facial treatment that will refresh her tired skin. Choose from a variety of masks, cleansers, and serums, applying each product skillfully to bring out her natural beauty. Once her face is glowing, it's time to move on to the next step: a relaxing spa treatment for her legs, back, and hands. You ll be in charge of selecting fragrant oils and lotions that will soothe her muscles and leave her feeling utterly relaxed.

After Sophie has enjoyed her rejuvenating treatments, it s time for the ultimate makeover! Explore an extensive wardrobe selection, complete with trendy outfits, stylish accessories, and charming hairstyles. Mix and match these elements to create the perfect look that reflects Sophie s newfound confidence and beauty. Share the results with friends or admire your own handiwork as you revel in the satisfaction of transforming Sophie into her best self.

How to play free Sophie's Instant Makeover game online

To play "Sophie's Instant Makeover," simply use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through various beauty treatments and outfit selections. Follow on-screen prompts to perform facial treatments, spa massages, and stylish make-up applications. Choose clothing and accessories to complete Sophie s new look, ensuring she feels fabulous!
