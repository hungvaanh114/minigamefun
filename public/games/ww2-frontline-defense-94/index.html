WW2 Frontline Defense

Description:

"WW2 Frontline Defense" is a strategic idle gaming application centered on historical World War II defense scenarios. Players are tasked with deploying and managing frontline machine gun emplacements to resist continuous, escalating enemy incursions. Sustained operational success is achieved through strategic enhancements, including fortifying defensive structures, accelerating combat tempo, leveraging premium tactical assets like artillery support and financial multipliers, and maximizing revenue streams. The core mandate is to establish a resilient, long-term defensive posture.
