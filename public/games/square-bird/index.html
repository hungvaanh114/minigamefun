Square Bird

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Square Bird, an exciting new adventure game that puts you in control of a charming square-shaped bird on a mission! Navigate through vibrant landscapes filled with a variety of obstacles and tricky traps that challenge your skills and reflexes. As you embark on this thrilling journey, your goal is to reach the destination without succumbing to the perils that lie in your path.

Throughout the game, don t forget to gather coins scattered across each level. These coins are essential for unlocking colorful new skins for your square bird, allowing you to customize your character and enhance your gaming experience. While some levels present a formidable challenge, persistence is key. Keep trying and refine your skills; victory is just around the corner. With each attempt, you ll learn more about the mechanics of the game, making it easier to conquer those tough levels.

Square Bird is not just about jumping and dodging; it is a test of your determination and strategy. Players can look forward to a delightful mix of fun graphics and engaging gameplay that will keep you coming back for more. So put your skills to the test and enjoy the thrill of overages in this playful yet challenging adventure!

How to play free Square Bird game online

To play Square Bird, use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move your bird. Tap to jump, avoiding lava, spikes, and other hazards. Collect coins and unlock new skins while progressing through levels. Stay focused and practice repeatedly to master each obstacle!
