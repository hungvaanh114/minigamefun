Rainbow Girls Hollywood Story

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of the Rainbow Girls Hollywood Story game, where the thrill of an exciting Hollywood-themed dress-up party awaits! Join your favorite Rainbow high school girls Skyler, Sunny, Ruby, and Violet as they embark on this glamorous adventure. With their vibrant personalities and unique styles, these girls are ready to shine and make a statement at the party.

In this game, you'll guide the girls in selecting the perfect outfits that reflect their colorful styles. From stunning dresses and chic accessories to fabulous hairstyles, the right choices will help them stand out on the red carpet. The excitement builds as the girls plan their looks, taking inspiration from the glamorous world of Hollywood. You can mix and match designs to create outfits that dazzle and impress all their friends.

As you get immersed in the Rainbow Girls Hollywood Story, there are various challenges and mini-games that enhance the overall experience. Help the girls navigate through fun activities, such as makeup and hairstyling contests. Each challenge will earn you points, unlocking new outfits and accessories that can elevate the girls' looks to Hollywood heights.

Gather your friends and embark on this vibrant journey filled with creativity and fun. Let your imagination run wild while dressing the girls to perfection for the star-studded event. Will you help them become the talk of the town? Dive into the Rainbow Girls Hollywood Story and show off your fashion flair!

How to play free Rainbow Girls Hollywood story game online

To play the Rainbow Girls Hollywood Story, use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through the menu. Select your favorite character and choose various outfits and accessories from the inventory. Dress up the girls and complete challenges to unlock new items while enjoying the glamorous aesthetic of Hollywood!
