Airplane Simulator Island Travel

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Airplane Simulator: Island Travel is an engaging flight simulation game crafted by the talented team at CoolMathGamesKids.com. This immersive experience allows players to take control of their very own aircraft and soar through the skies, discovering a variety of vibrant and picturesque islands along the way. With realistic flying mechanics and stunning visuals, you ll find yourself captivated by the thrill of piloting an airplane in this delightful adventure.

In this game, you can unlock up to three unique airplanes by gathering coins scattered throughout the levels. Each airplane offers its own distinct features and capabilities, giving you the freedom to choose your preferred mode of travel as you navigate through the lush landscapes and serene waters of the islands. With a total of 10 exciting levels to conquer, players will be challenged to complete various tasks while enjoying the breathtaking scenery that each island has to offer.

The game is designed to not only entertain but also to enhance your skills as a pilot. Whether you are new to simulation games or an experienced flyer, Airplane Simulator: Island Travel offers a fun and educational adventure that you won t want to miss. As you progress through the levels, you ll encounter increasingly complex challenges and obstacles, making each flight a thrilling experience.

How to play free Airplane Simulator Island Travel game online

To play Airplane Simulator: Island Travel, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your airplane. Collect coins to unlock new aircraft and complete missions to progress through the levels. Enjoy the stunning island landscapes as you improve your flying skills and become a master pilot!


Instructions

Use Arrow Keys or ASWD to take off landing left turn and right turn the airplane Use Mouse Wheel to tilt the the aircraft down and up
