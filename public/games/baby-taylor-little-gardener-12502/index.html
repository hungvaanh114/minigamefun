Baby Taylor Little Gardener

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

On a beautiful sunny day, Baby Taylor is feeling extra generous and wants to surprise her mother with a delightful gift. She decides that a stunning fruit basket would be the perfect gesture to show her appreciation. However, this is no easy task, and Taylor needs your assistance to make it happen! Join her on this adorable adventure as you learn the ins and outs of gardening and fruit cultivation.

Throughout the game, you and Baby Taylor will embark on a journey to grow various fruits from seeds to ripe produce. You'll have to plant seeds, water them, and help them flourish into delicious fruits that can be used in the basket. With each new plant, you ll gain experience in the fascinating world of gardening while enjoying playful and engaging graphics that captivate young players.

Once the fruits are ready to be harvested, you and Taylor will select the finest ones to arrange into a beautiful fruit basket. This hands-on experience not only teaches kids about the joy of gardening but also fosters creativity as they customize their basket arrangement. By helping Taylor, you ll create a special surprise that Mom is sure to cherish!

How to play free Baby Taylor Little Gardener game online

In the game, you'll start by choosing seeds to plant. Use the mouse to dig holes in the soil and place the seeds inside. Remember to keep the plants watered and free from pests. After harvesting the fruit, drag and drop them into the basket to create a lovely arrangement. Enjoy the gardening experience with Baby Taylor and have fun!
