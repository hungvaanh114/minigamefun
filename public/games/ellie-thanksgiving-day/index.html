Ellie Thanksgiving Day

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Ellie Thanksgiving Day! It s that special time of the year when families and friends gather together to celebrate gratitude and joy. Young Ellie is taking the lead this Thanksgiving, and she needs your assistance to ensure that everything is perfect for her dinner guests. As the clock ticks down, Ellie has a few last-minute details to attend to, and that s where you come in!

Help Ellie navigate her bustling kitchen as she prepares a delightful Thanksgiving feast. From cooking the turkey to setting the table, every detail matters. You ll find yourself engaged in various tasks, such as chopping vegetables, stirring sauces, and decorating the dining area to create an inviting atmosphere. The goal is to transform Ellie s home into the ultimate Thanksgiving destination, where her friends will feel welcomed and cherished.

As you join Ellie in her culinary adventure, be prepared for a delightful experience filled with fun challenges and exciting mini-games. The satisfaction of seeing Ellie s hard work come to life as she hosts a memorable Thanksgiving dinner will surely warm your heart. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and let s create a holiday celebration that will be remembered for years to come!

How to play free Ellie Thanksgiving Day game online

To play the Ellie Thanksgiving Day game online, use your mouse or trackpad to interact with various items in the kitchen. Click on ingredients to collect them and drag them to the appropriate cooking stations. Follow the prompts to complete tasks quickly and efficiently, ensuring Ellie s dinner is ready before her guests arrive!
