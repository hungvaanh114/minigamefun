Chicken Jumper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in the vibrant universe of Chicken Jumper. This captivating game invites you to navigate a whimsical world constructed from eccentric blocks, presenting players with an exciting challenge across 30 unique levels. Each level is designed to test your reflexes and precision as you guide your chicken character through a colorful landscape.

Your goal is simple: jump on the blocks to break them and progress to the next level. As you leap across various platforms, be prepared to face different obstacles and surprises that will challenge your skills. With intuitive controls, all you need is your mouse or finger to dictate your chicken's jumps. To leap in the desired direction, simply tap the right or left side of the screen, navigating your way through the delightful chaos.

As you hone your jumping skills, you ll discover new block types and mechanics that add depth and excitement to your gameplay. Each of the 30 levels offers its own set of challenges, ensuring that you'll stay engaged and entertained as you strive to complete them all. Will you rise to the occasion and conquer every level? Jump in and find out!

How to play free Chicken Jumper game online

To play Chicken Jumper, simply enter the game and use your mouse or finger to control the chicken's jumps. Tap the right side of the screen to jump right, and the left side to jump left. Aim for the blocks to break them and keep progressing through the increasingly challenging levels. Happy jumping!
