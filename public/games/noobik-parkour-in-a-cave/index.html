Noobik Parkour in a Cave!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The game has 10 hardcore levels! To complete the level, you need to get to the finish line, overcoming various obstacles, dodging various traps on your way!

Instructions

Mobile devices Touch joystick on the left side of the screen - Move Right side of the screen - Rotate the camera Button in the lower right corner - Jump Computers WASD - Move Space - Jump Escape - Pause menu Mouse movement - Rotate the camera
