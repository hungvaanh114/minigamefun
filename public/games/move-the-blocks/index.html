Move the Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting adventure with Move the Blocks, an engaging puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning and quick thinking. In this captivating world, you will join a quirky cube on its quest to traverse various landscapes that are full of surprises. Right from the start, you re greeted with your adorable character poised at the beginning of the journey, ready for you to take charge.

Your main objective is to navigate your cube through a series of obstacles and challenges, reaching the endpoint as swiftly as possible. As you progress, you'll encounter numerous collectible points scattered throughout the path. These points not only enhance your score but also add an extra layer of excitement to the gameplay. With intuitive control keys, the power lies in your hands guide your cube in the desired direction to avoid obstacles and capture those all-important points.

As your cube moves along the road, it will occasionally change colors, signaling moments of success and rewarding your efforts. Each level brings new tasks and ever-evolving challenges, ensuring that your journey remains fresh and captivating. Successfully reaching the end point signifies your level completion, unlocking even more thrilling adventures that await in subsequent levels.

How to play free Move the Blocks game online

To play Move the Blocks, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to direct your cube along the path. Collect points while avoiding obstacles to improve your score. Keep an eye on color changes for bonus points, and aim to reach the endpoint as fast as you can to progress to the next level!
