Blocky Chains

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Blocky Chains invites you into a vibrant world full of colorful candies waiting to be connected! In this captivating puzzle game, your objective is to group together similar candies by simply using your touchpad or mouse. Scan the game board and identify clusters of three or more adjacent candies that share the same color. Whether they are lined up horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, bring them together to turn their blocks blue.

Your challenge involves transforming all the blocks on the board to blue to successfully complete each level. With 24 engaging levels full of sweet challenges, Blocky Chains promises a delightful gaming experience that sharpens your strategic thinking while providing hours of entertainment. As you progress, you'll encounter increasingly complex arrangements that will test your skills and require you to think ahead about your moves. Test your reflexes and puzzle-solving abilities as you strive to achieve a perfect completion rate across all levels!

How to play free Blocky Chains game online

To start playing Blocky Chains, simply use your touchpad or mouse to click on candies of the same color that are connected. When you select a group of three or more adjacent candies, they will change to blue. Aim to turn all the blocks on the board blue to clear the level. Good luck!
