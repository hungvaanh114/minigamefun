Horror Highway

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a chilling adventure with the Horror Highway Online Santa Claus Game, where the beloved figure of Christmas takes a terrifying turn. In this free online game, players are thrust into the role of Santa Claus, but this time, he must traverse treacherous highways fraught with peril. As you guide Santa on his journey, you'll face a myriad of deadly obstacles and sinister foes that threaten to derail your mission of spreading holiday cheer.

The game immerses you in an eerie atmosphere as you navigate through dark, winding roads and dodge dangerous traps that lurk around every corner. As Santa, your agility and quick reflexes will be put to the test. Work your way past haunted tree lines and avoid monstrous creatures that seem to leap from the shadows, all while keeping an eye on your dwindling health. The objective? Reach the end of these horrific highways in one piece, delivering gifts as you fight to survive the gruesome challenges ahead.

With each level, the difficulty ramps up, demanding skillful maneuvers and strategic thinking. Will you rise to the occasion and conquer the nightmarish terrains, or will you meet a ghastly fate before the holiday season even begins? Gather your courage, download the game, and see if you can help Santa make it through this spooky escapade!

How to play the free Horror Highway game online

To play the Horror Highway game, use the arrow keys or onboard controls to steer Santa through the treacherous roads. Avoid incoming obstacles by jumping or dodging, and collect power-ups to boost your survival chances. Keep an eye on your health bar and make it to the end of the highway before it runs out!
