Sliding Hearts Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Sliding Hearts Match 3, an engaging and heartwarming puzzle game that will challenge your matching skills and keep you entertained for hours! Your mission in this delightful game is to slide a row of charming hearts horizontally to create horizontal or vertical lines of three or more matching hearts. The more you match, the more points you score!

In Sliding Hearts Match 3, simply swipe or drag any row of hearts to the right or left to rearrange them and make matches. Keep your eyes peeled for combinations and strategize your moves to maximize your score. The clock is ticking, so be quick and clever with your selections! Each successful match will not only grant you points but also opens up possibilities for even more matches, making every move count.

The game is visually appealing, featuring vibrant colors and beautifully designed hearts that will captivate players of all ages. As you progress, the challenge increases, providing a delightful blend of strategy and fun. Can you keep your cool while racing against the clock and create the highest score possible? Jump into this thrilling adventure and find out!

How to play free Sliding Hearts Match 3 game online

To play Sliding Hearts Match 3, simply click or tap on the row of hearts you wish to move and drag it left or right. Aim to line up three or more hearts of the same color either vertically or horizontally. Keep an eye on the timer, and make as many matches as you can before it runs out!
