Count and Bounce

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Count and Bounce is an engaging ball puzzle game that cleverly combines strategy with fun! In this exciting adventure, players guide their vibrant ball along a path, where the objective is to ensure it keeps moving forward without falling off the edges. The intuitive controls make it easy to immerse yourself in the gameplay simply release your hand, and watch as the ball continues its forward journey, bouncing along the way.

As you navigate through the various levels, you'll encounter colorful tiles that need to be turned strategically. Your main task is to prevent the ball from dropping off; this requires keen observation and quick decision-making. Adding an intriguing twist to the game, some tiles present you with calculation questions. Here, you must assess the number of balls you possess and choose the most favorable tile to give your ball the optimal bounce. This aspect not only enhances the puzzle-solving experience but also adds a layer of educational value, making it perfect for players of all ages.

The excitement of Count and Bounce lies not just in keeping the ball afloat, but also in mastering the art of calculation and tile selection. Each level presents new challenges that will keep you engaged and entertained for hours. So gather your wits, sharpen your maths skills, and dive into this bouncy, colorful world of puzzles. Have fun bouncing, counting, and strategizing your way to the top!

How to play free Count and Bounce game online

To play Count and Bounce, simply use your finger or mouse to control the ball's movement. Watch for the tiles on your path; when you encounter a calculation question, select the tile that best suits your total number of balls. Keep the ball bouncing and enjoy solving puzzles!
