Infinity Golf offers a delightful golfing experience where the competition is solely against yourself. This straightforward game allows you to harness your skills and precision as you sink shots into a variety of intricately designed holes. Using your mouse, controlling the power and direction of your shots becomes second nature. With practice, you will find yourself effortlessly mastering the art of the drive and getting the ball into the hole with just one stroke!

The game features a range of unique tracks that promise both challenges and engaging scenery. From rolling hills to intricate obstacles, each course presents a different set of conditions that will put your golfing prowess to the test. Your aim is to navigate through these courses, making every shot count as you attempt to complete the holes in as few strokes as possible. So, grab your virtual golf club, focus your aim, and show off your skills in this captivating golfing adventure!

How to play free Infinity Golf game online

To start playing Infinity Golf, simply adjust the angle and power of your shot by moving your mouse. Click once to set the direction and click again to determine the strength of your shot. Aim to complete each hole in as few strokes as possible while enjoying the vibrant courses!
