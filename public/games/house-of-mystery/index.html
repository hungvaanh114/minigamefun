House of Mystery

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enigmatic world of the House of Mystery, where each room presents a new challenge waiting to be unraveled! As you enter the eerie corridors of this haunted mansion, your objective is clear: locate all the hidden objects cleverly tucked away within the shadows. Equipped only with your keen observational skills, you'll embark on an intriguing quest to uncover the secrets within.

On the left side of the screen, you'll find a list of the objects you need to find. These items are hidden amidst intricate details, so careful scrutiny is essential. Every corner of the room promises surprises and mysteries, making every search an exhilarating experience. Stay alert, as time is not on your side; once the clock ticks down to zero, it's game over, and you'll have to start anew. Your goal is not just to find the items but to do so with speed and precision to achieve maximum points and earn a coveted three-star rating in each room.

Sharpen your focus and ready yourself for an adventure that combines thrill with nostalgia revisit the joyous days of scavenger hunts and mystery-solving in the House of Mystery!

How to play free House of Mystery game online

To play the House of Mystery game online, simply start each level and scan the room for hidden objects listed on the left. Click on the items as you find them to score points and race against the clock to complete each room before time runs out. Aim for three stars by achieving the highest score possible!
