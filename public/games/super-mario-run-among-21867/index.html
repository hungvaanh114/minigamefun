Experience the exciting world of Super Mario Run, a unique Mario game designed for one-handed play! In this game, Mario runs continuously, and all you need to do is tap the screen to guide his movements. Time your taps perfectly to execute impressive jumps, spin maneuvers, and wall climbs, while collecting coins and striving to reach your ultimate goal!

The game can be downloaded for free, with the option to purchase for full access to all modes, including World Tour, Toad Rally, Remix 10, and Kingdom Builder. You can test out courses before making a purchase, allowing players to discover their favorites without any upfront commitment.

In the World Tour, your mission is to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser's lair. Journey through various environments such as lush plains, spooky caverns, and intricate castles as you navigate through 24 challenging courses. Collect colored coins and aim for high scores while competing against friends. The adventure doesn t end there; after saving Princess Peach, you unlock an additional nine-course special world known as World Star.

The Remix 10 mode offers quick, bite-sized gameplay, where you'll encounter 10 mini-courses, with each playthrough presenting a new set of challenges. Your objective is to locate Daisy, who is lost among these fast-paced courses!

If you re looking for a competitive edge, dive into Toad Rally, where you can showcase your Mario skills against friends and players worldwide. Gather coins, perform stylish moves, and gain support from enthusiastic Toads as you compete for high scores. Victorious players can grow their kingdoms by winning rallies.

Finally, unleash your creativity in the Kingdom Builder mode! Accumulate coins and Toads to construct a personalized kingdom filled with over 100 unique items. The more Toads you collect, the more options you have to furnish your kingdom.

How to play free super mario run among game online

To play Super Mario Run online, simply tap your device's screen to control Mario's jumps and actions. Start in World Tour mode, navigate courses, and collect coins while defeating obstacles. Use your taps wisely to perform wall jumps and midair spins, and enjoy the vibrant levels along the way!
