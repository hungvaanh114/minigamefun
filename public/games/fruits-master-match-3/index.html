Fruits Master Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting journey in the colorful world of Fruits Master Match 3! This engaging HTML5 game invites players to collect vibrant fruit items by making strategic matches. Simply click or tap on any Easter-themed item to begin your fruit-collecting adventure. To create matches, skillfully move your mouse or finger to adjacent items that are the same, whether they are aligned horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally.

For a successful match, ensure that you select at least three identical fruits. Once connected, release the mouse button to see them combine into delicious combinations. The game rewards your skill; every sixth item matched provides a bonus, and if you manage to connect over seven fruits, you'll earn a precious time bonus that can help you advance through the levels more efficiently. However, keep an eye on the clock! You must collect the target fruit items within the allotted time limit, or it s game over. Challenge yourself to see just how many levels you can conquer in this fruit-filled frenzy!

How to play free Fruits Master Match 3 game online

To play Fruits Master Match 3, begin by clicking any Easter item. Then, use your mouse or finger to connect at least three adjacent fruits of the same type. Release the button to match them and aim to collect the target fruits before time runs out. Enjoy the strategic fun and aim for high scores!
