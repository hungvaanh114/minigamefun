Honey Trouble

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Honey Trouble is an exciting and addictive free HTML5 online game that draws inspiration from the beloved Zuma genre. In this delightful version of the classic game, players are tasked with assisting a cute bear on a quest for honey. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay are sure to capture your attention as you embark on this sweet adventure.

As you progress through the levels, your objective is to match groups of like-colored marbles. By doing so, you ll help the bear collect an abundance of honey, ensuring he has enough to get through the winter season. With each successful match, the thrill of the game increases, presenting you with new challenges and obstacles to overcome. The colorful marbles and cheerful characters make for a visually appealing experience, making Honey Trouble a perfect choice for players of all ages.

Players will navigate through various levels filled with cascading marbles, where quick thinking and strategic planning are key. Each level introduces unique elements that keep the gameplay fresh and exciting. As you advance, you can unlock bonuses and power-ups, enhancing your ability to create massive marble combinations and ultimately support the bear in his honey-gathering endeavor. Get ready for hours of fun with Honey Trouble as you indulge in this charming online game!

How to play free Honey Trouble game online

To play Honey Trouble, use your mouse to aim and click to shoot marbles into the chain. Match three or more marbles of the same color to make them disappear. Keep an eye on the approaching marbles, as you must clear them before they reach the end of the path!


Instructions

To shoot the marbles down aim and press the left mouse button Press Spacebar to swap between the 3 marbles held by the bear
