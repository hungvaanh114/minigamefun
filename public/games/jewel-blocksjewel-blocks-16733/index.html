How to play free Jewel Blocks game online

To play Jewel Blocks, simply drag and drop block shapes onto the grid. Your goal is to create complete horizontal or vertical lines to clear them and earn points. As you progress, new block shapes will appear, so think ahead to arrange them smartly and maintain a clear board.
