Angry Hero

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Angry Heroes is an exhilarating, fast-paced shooter physics game that invites players to step into the shoes of valiant little heroes on a mission to thwart alien invaders and rescue the planet from impending doom. With an arsenal of slingshots and an array of invaluable power-ups at your disposal, you will embark on a thrilling adventure that spans multiple levels filled with challenges and excitement. Your objective is simple yet engaging: demolish alien shelters while navigating through an imaginative world teeming with unique obstacles and clever designs.

As you progress through the game's vibrant levels, you will encounter increasingly difficult tasks that test your skills and strategy. Utilize the slingshot mechanics to unleash a barrage of projectiles against your foes, taking full advantage of the power-ups to maximize your destructive capabilities. Each level brims with intricate designs that challenge your aim and timing, making every shot count.

Furthermore, the experience is heightened by epic boss battles that stand as formidable gatekeepers to your progress. Face off against these colossal foes, utilizing your quick reflexes and strategic thinking to take them down. With stunning graphics and engaging gameplay, Angry Heroes provides a captivating escape into a fantasy realm where teamwork, skill, and determination lead the heroes to victory.

How to play free Angry Hero game online

To play Angry Hero, simply use your mouse or finger to aim your slingshot. Adjust the angle and strength of your shot to hit the targets effectively. Release to launch your projectile and eliminate the aliens. Collect power-ups along the way to enhance your abilities and tackle boss encounters.
