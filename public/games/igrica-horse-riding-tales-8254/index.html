Igrica Horse Riding Tales

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Igrica Horse Riding Tales, where your journey begins as a humble stable hand and evolves into an ultimate dressage and horse racing superstar! Join a vibrant online community of horse enthusiasts where you have the opportunity to customize your character to reflect your unique style.

In this captivating game, you will be able to tame and race stunning wild horses, bringing them back to your stable to train. Your goal is to rise through the ranks to become a celebrated equestrian show-jumping champion. You can also collect adorable wild animal pets, including playful foxes, wolf pups, and mystical tigers. Show off your delightful companions to your fellow cowgirls; your pets can even join you on your exciting horse racing quests across the ranch!

Gather your friends and embark on incredible adventures as you ride your horse in glorious 3D. The multiplayer horse racing mode enables you to dash at top speed, competing against rivals in exhilarating online races. Whether you're exploring the outskirts of your farm or challenging friends to a race, there s always a new thrill lurking around the corner!

How to play free Igrica Horse Riding Tales game online

To begin playing Igrica Horse Riding Tales, visit iyigames.com and select the game. Create and customize your character, then start taming wild horses by completing quests. Participate in races with friends and gather cute animal pets as you work towards becoming a horse riding legend!
