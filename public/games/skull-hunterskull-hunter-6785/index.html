Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the chilling world of Skull Hunter, a flappy horror game that takes you on a spine-tingling adventure through dark and eerie caves. In this hauntingly captivating game, you will guide a flying skull ghost as it navigates a maze of treacherous obstacles and fierce enemies. Your primary objective is to destroy all enemy skulls lurking in the shadows. Strategically hit the enemy skulls or cause them to collide with each other to maximize damage and clear your path.

As you navigate the cave, you'll encounter various stone obstacles that may block your way. But fear not! You can use enemies to your advantage, making them crash into these barriers to obliterate them. Keep your wits about you colliding with the cave walls or falling into the abyss will end your game. To amplify your power, aim to hit a total of 10 enemies; doing so will grant you the coveted "fire power," allowing you to decimate everything in your path.

Each level brings new challenges and increasing difficulty, testing your reflexes and strategic thinking. Collect points and aim for high scores as you progress through the exhilarating stages of Skull Hunter. Are you prepared to conquer the cave and emerge as the ultimate Skull Hunter?

How to play free Skull Hunter game online

To play Skull Hunter, use your device's touchscreen or keyboard to navigate the flying skull through the cave. Tap to ascend and strategically collide with enemy skulls to destroy them or cause them to hit obstacles. Collect fire power after hitting ten enemies to enhance your capabilities and progress through each level!
