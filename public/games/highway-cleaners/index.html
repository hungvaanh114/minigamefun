Highway Cleaners

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Highway Cleaners, players are tasked with navigating their upgraded vehicles through bustling roads while working to secure funds for further enhancements. The ultimate goal is to reach the helicopter waiting on the horizon, making each second on the road matter. The farther you drive, the more money you amass, which can then be invested back into your vehicle for improved capabilities and faster speeds.

Every upgrade you implement allows for more thrilling escapades and the potential to conquer new distances. The faster you zoom past various obstacles and challenges, the higher your chances of winning. Skillful driving is essential as you weave through traffic while avoiding obstacles that could slow you down or halt your progress entirely. Every game played strengthens your car, enhancing its speed and functionality, ultimately steering you closer to your goal.

Embrace the challenge of Highway Cleaners and experience the adrenaline rush as you strive to be the best on the road. With its engaging gameplay, ample opportunities for upgrades, and the thrill of competition, this game promises to keep players on the edge of their seats as they aim for that distant helicopter and the rewards that await.

How to play free Highway Cleaners game online

To play Highway Cleaners, start by choosing your car and upgrading it with your initial funds. As you drive, avoid obstacles and aim to cover as much distance as possible. Collect coins along the way to use for future upgrades, focusing on increasing speed for a better chance of reaching the helicopter.

WASD- Vehicle controls Left Shift - Turbo Space- Handbrake
