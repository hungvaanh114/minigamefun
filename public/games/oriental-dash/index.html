How to play free Oriental Dash game online

To play Oriental Dash, match three or more diamonds of the same color by swapping adjacent gems. Aim for combos and chain reactions to earn bonus points. As you progress through levels, keep an eye on time limits and score requirements to unlock new challenges. Have fun and compete for the best score!
