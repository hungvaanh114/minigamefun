How to play free Stickman Merge 2 game online

To play Stickman Merge 2, simply combine different guns by dragging and dropping them into the merging area. As you create powerful combinations, equip your stickman and prepare for battles against enemy stickmen. Look for bonuses throughout the game to further enhance your shooting capabilities. Good luck!
