Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Magic Academy, where you can establish your very own magic shop and brew potions day in and day out until you ascend to the status of a true witch! Immerse yourself in the captivating experience of potion-making within a magical school setting. This enchanting endeavor requires focus, creativity, and a set of skills that will be honed with every successful concoction. As you take on the role of an aspiring sorceress, you ll learn to gather ingredients, follow intricate recipes, and master the art of potion-making.

In Magic Academy, the joy of creating unique potions lies in the exhilarating challenges and delightful surprises that await you. Each potion recipe demands a precise combination of mystical elements, and the outcome hinges on your ability to blend these ingredients with care. As you progress, you ll discover new recipes and unlock rare ingredients, further expanding your magical repertoire. The vibrant, whimsical graphics and engaging gameplay create a spellbinding atmosphere that draws you deeper into the realm of magic.

But be warned success in potion-making isn t solely about following instructions; it requires a keen sense of observation and creativity. Experimenting with different combinations may lead to unexpected outcomes, giving rise to enchanting surprises that add to the excitement. As you continue to refine your craft, you ll transform your humble magic shop into a bustling hub of potion activity, attracting customers from all corners of the enchanted realm.

How to play free Magic Academy game online

To play Magic Academy online, simply start by creating an account or logging in. Follow the tutorials provided to gather ingredients. Select a potion recipe from your spellbook, and drag the necessary items into your cauldron. Once you ve mixed the ingredients, watch as your potion comes to life while earning points and unlocking new challenges!
