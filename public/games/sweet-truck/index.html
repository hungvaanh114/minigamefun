Sweet Truck

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Sweet Truck, an exciting and engaging collecting game that promises sweet fun! Embark on a delicious adventure where your goal is to gather as many cookies as possible in a single level. Navigate your cute and colorful truck through a fantastical landscape filled with delightful treats, all while racking up points and avoiding any crashes. The more cookies you collect, the higher your score will climb!

With intuitive controls, managing your truck is a breeze. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard for seamless steering or tap the on-screen buttons if you re enjoying the game on your mobile device. This means anyone can jump right in and start collecting cookies, making it a fun pastime for players of all ages. Each level is designed to challenge you, offering an exciting mix of obstacles and opportunities that keep the gameplay fresh and enjoyable.

As you progress, the levels will become more challenging, requiring swift reflexes and strategic moves. But don't worry Sweet Truck is designed to be both easy to learn and fun to master! So, buckle up and get ready to drive your truck through various stages filled with yummy cookies. Can you collect them all and achieve the highest score? Only one way to find out!

How to play free Sweet Truck game online

To play Sweet Truck, simply use the arrow keys to control your truck's movement. If on a mobile device, tap the buttons provided on the screen for steering. Collect cookies while avoiding obstacles to earn points and advance through the fun-filled levels. Happy collecting!
