Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of precision and accuracy in Bottle Shooter. This exciting 3D game challenges you to put your shooting skills to the test as you aim to shatter bottles with your chosen weapon. The vibrant graphics and realistic physics create an immersive environment that keeps you coming back for more.

Begin by selecting your weapon from a wide array of choices, each offering a unique style of play. Whether you prefer a classic handgun or a powerful rifle, the choice is yours. As you progress, you'll find various levels that challenge you to hit bottles placed at different distances and angles, ensuring a dynamic shooting experience. The satisfaction of breaking these glass targets offers a rewarding sense of accomplishment and sharpens your shooting abilities.

As you advance through the game, you will encounter different challenges and scenarios, adding variety to your gameplay. Aim carefully and calculate your shots, because the further away the bottles are, the more skill it takes to hit them. Explore multiple environments, from sunny outdoors to dimly lit indoor ranges, making each session feel fresh and exhilarating.

How to play free Bottle Shooter game online

To play the Bottle Shooter game online, simply use your mouse to aim at the bottles. Click to fire your weapon, adjusting your aim for distance and wind. Stay focused and time your shots to maximize your score as you move through various levels and challenges!
