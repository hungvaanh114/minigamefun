Meet Simon, the exuberant and charming little rabbit who s always ready for adventure! Known for his mischievous antics, Simon embodies the essence of joy, curiosity, and the beauty of nature. As SUPER RABBIT, he captivates everyone around him with his spirited personality and values that include positive education, friendship, and a deep respect for the environment.

In Simon's world, life is a delightful journey filled with sunny days, but there are moments where clouds appear. Despite these challenges, Simon's infectious optimism reminds us that "there s always sunshine after the rain!" Alongside his brother Gaspard, their mum, dad, and a vibrant cast of friends and family, Simon discovers the joys of modern family life and the importance of brotherhood and fair play.

From playdates in the park to engaging in friendly competitions, every venture showcases Simon s zest for life and an unwavering sense of wonder. Curiosity leads Simon into new experiences where he learns valuable lessons about nature and camaraderie. Join Simon and his delightful entourage on their exhilarating escapades where positivity reigns supreme, and every day brings a new adventure!

How to play free Simon Puzzle game online

To play the Simon Puzzle game, simply click on the puzzle pieces to move them around and form a complete picture of Simon and his friends. Use the hints available if you get stuck, and race against the timer to complete the puzzle as quickly as possible. Enjoy the fun and challenge!
