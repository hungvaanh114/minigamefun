Xmasjong

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Xmasjong, a festive and engaging tile-matching game that will get you into the holiday spirit! In this delightful game, your objective is to locate and pair identical tiles to clear them from the playing field. The challenge lies in finding the right combinations as you navigate through beautifully decorated tiles featuring classic Christmas imagery. As you eliminate pairs, you'll uncover hidden items that will be magically placed on or beneath the majestic Christmas tree, adding to your festive decorations.

Xmasjong is perfect for players of all ages. The cheerful graphics and soothing sounds will transport you to a winter wonderland, making each level an enjoyable experience. As you play, you'll enhance your memory and develop your matching skills, all while spreading the joy of the Christmas season. The deeper you go, the more intricate the tile layouts become, providing new layers of difficulty that will keep you challenged and entertained. Collecting all the tiles not only brings you closer to completing the game but also fills your Christmas tree with delightful surprises!

How to play free Xmasjong game online

To play Xmasjong, simply click on any two identical tiles to remove them from the board. Keep an eye out for special items that can be unlocked by clearing the tiles below them. Your goal is to eliminate all tiles and help decorate the Christmas tree by collecting all the hidden items!
