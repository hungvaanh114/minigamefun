Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Handless Millionaire 3, a game that tests your reflexes and timing as you aim to grab as much cash as possible! The objective is simple yet challenging: click or tap on the hand icon located on the right side of your screen. This area serves as the safe zone for your hand, a crucial resting spot before you make your daring move.

When you're ready to snatch some cash, you'll need to assess the rhythm of the money movements carefully. Timing is everything! Carefully drag your hand towards the money, ensuring you don t rush the retraction back into your safe zone. If you pull back too quickly, your attempt will be in vain, and you ll end up with nothing. On the flip side, if you take too long, you ll know the bitter disappointment of missing out on your well-deserved loot.

The thrill of Handless Millionaire 3 lies not just in grabbing money but in maximizing your score. With each round you play, the challenge escalates how much cash can you collect in a single attempt? Test your skills and compete against others by submitting your score on the Highscore Board, which showcases top players, or aim for the ultimate accomplishment of conquering all the rounds to become a champion!

How to play free Handless Millionaire 3 game online

To play Handless Millionaire 3, begin by observing the pattern of when the money appears. Tap on your hand to keep it in the safe zone, and when you see the opportunity, drag your hand towards the cash swiftly. Remember to retreat back quickly but cautiously into the safe zone to secure your earnings!
