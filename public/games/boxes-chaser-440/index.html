Boxes Chaser

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant universe of Boxes Chaser, where you must assist the adorable color Boxes in their daring escape from the relentless Chaser! Navigate through a series of intricate obstacles and ensure that your little Boxes stay safe for as long as possible. The challenge increases as you traverse through 20 unique space-themed levels, each presenting its own set of dangers and hurdles.

With engaging graphics and fun animations, Boxes Chaser captivates players with its simplicity and excitement. The more you play, the more you'll uncover the different dynamics involved in each level. Collect bonuses, dodge moving obstacles, and cleverly outmaneuver the Chaser, all while racing against time. Your mission? To successfully guide each Box back to Earth by avoiding the myriad of threats lurking in the cosmic landscape. Hone your skills and aim for the highest score in this exhilarating race for survival!

This challenging adventure not only tests your reflexes but also requires strategic thinking to decide the best route for each Box. The colorful aesthetics, combined with thrilling gameplay, makes Boxes Chaser an irresistible choice for players of all ages looking for a delightful, fast-paced gaming experience. Are you prepared to embark on this whimsical journey? Join the chase and see how long you can last!

How to play free Boxes Chaser game online

To play Boxes Chaser, use the arrow keys or swipe in the desired direction to move the Boxes. Your objective is to avoid obstacles and the Chaser while navigating through each level. Keep an eye on your surroundings and collect bonuses to enhance your escape! Aim to survive as long as possible to complete all 20 levels!
