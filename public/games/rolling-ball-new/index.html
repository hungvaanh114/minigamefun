Rolling Ball New

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rolling Ball is a captivating running game that offers endless excitement as you guide your ball through a vibrant landscape. Your main objective is to evade various obstacles, including trees and rocks, while collecting mushrooms that will help you upgrade your power-up items. The more mushrooms you gather, the better your chances are of unlocking additional balls that can enhance your gaming experience.

As you roll along the dynamic terrains, you'll encounter power-up items like shields, magnets, and magical wings. These items provide distinct advantages, giving you the edge needed to reach impressive distances. The key to victory lies in your ability to keep rolling and navigating through the challenges ahead. Can you set a new high score and prove yourself as a master of Rolling Ball? Get ready to embark on this exhilarating journey!

Features of Rolling Ball include three unique ball skins, with two that can be unlocked as you progress through the game. Plus, there s an upgrade shop where players can enhance their power-up items for maximum effectiveness. Whether you are a casual gamer or a seasoned player, Rolling Ball promises an engaging experience filled with fun and challenges! Don t wait any longer; dive into the action now!

How to play free Rolling Ball New game online

To play Rolling Ball, use the arrow keys or swipe your finger to control the direction of your ball. Navigate through the environment, avoid obstacles, and collect mushrooms. Activate power-ups as you gather them to boost your abilities! Aim for the highest score by rolling further each time you play!


Instructions

Mobile or mouse on desktop - Swipe left or right to move - Swipe up to jump Desktop - Press left or right arrow keys to move - Press up arrow key to jump - Press Space bar to replay the game
