Hole Fire

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the captivating realm of Hole Fire, where strategy meets cosmic chaos! In this exhilarating game, you harness the power of black holes to gather an arsenal of formidable weapons. As you plunge into the action, you'll discover that black holes are not just mysterious cosmic phenomena but also powerful tools to acquire an extensive collection of weaponry. From heavy artillery to explosive bombs, you are tasked with amassing a wide array of guns and bullets to thwart your adversaries.

To dominate in Hole Fire, you'll need to think strategically. The bigger your black hole, the more significant the weapons you can collect. Each enemy presents a unique challenge, and by utilizing the black hole's gravitational pull, you can gather impressive firepower to unleash upon your opponents. Are you ready to face off against fierce enemies while spinning and swirling through the cosmos? Your skills will be put to the test as you maneuver through obstacles, collect weapons, and engage in intense battles!

Only those with the ability to master the black hole will emerge victorious. Can you show strength and cunning in collecting weapons and overpowering your foes? Prepare for an adventure that will push you to your limits!

How to play free Hole Fire game online

To play Hole Fire, navigate your black hole around the battlefield to absorb various weapons scattered throughout. Use your mouse or arrow keys to direct your black hole, collecting as many weapons as possible. Once you've gathered enough firepower, unleash your arsenal on the enemies and dominate the game to secure your win!
