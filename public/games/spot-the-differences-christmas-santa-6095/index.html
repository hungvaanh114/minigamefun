Spot the Differences - Christmas Santa

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Spot The Differences - Christmas Santa, a delightful game perfect for the holiday season! This engaging puzzle game is categorized among hidden object games and invites players to immerse themselves in a festive challenge with a total of 12 levels. Each level presents two seemingly identical images where your goal is to uncover five differences within a specified time limit.

As you progress through the game, each level becomes increasingly challenging. In the second level, you'll need to find six differences, and by the time you reach the final level, you ll be on the lookout for ten distinct variations. The twist? Despite the rising difficulty and number of differences to spot, the time allocated remains consistent, making it a thrilling yet demanding experience.

Featuring charming visuals filled with Christmas-themed elements and an array of Santa Claus images, this game is a visual treat for players of all ages. Be vigilant and click on any differences you notice to score points. However, beware! Clicking on areas without differences will deduct 5 seconds from your time, adding an extra layer of challenge to the gameplay. Get ready to sharpen your observation skills and spread some holiday cheer!

How to play free Spot The Differences - Christmas Santa game online

To play, simply open the game and select a level. You'll see two images side by side. Carefully examine both images and click on the areas that show differences. Keep an eye on the timer, as you'll need to find all the differences before time runs out. Enjoy the festive spirit and happy spotting!
