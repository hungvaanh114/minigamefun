Stickman vs Aliens?Stickman vs Aliens

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Stickman vs Aliens, an exhilarating action war game that is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat! Immerse yourself in this fast-paced adventure where you control your stickman hero with simple finger gestures. Navigate the battlefield by sliding your finger left, right, up, and down to dodge enemy fire and maneuver through an intense environment. Your primary objective? Take down hostile aliens, obliterate their tanks, and shoot down their planes!

As you traverse through various levels, you will encounter an array of enemies bent on your destruction. Use your shooting skills to eliminate them and collect valuable weapons and coins along the way. These rewards not only boost your score but also enhance your combat capabilities, helping you to advance further in the game. With each enemy you defeat, your chances of achieving a high score increase, making it essential to hone your skills and stay sharp. So gear up, aim true, and get ready for a battle that will test your reflexes like never before. Good luck, and may your victory be sweet!

How to play free Stickman vs Aliens game online

To play Stickman vs Aliens, use your finger to control the stickman and navigate the landscape. Slide left or right to dodge gunfire, while moving upward and downward to find the best positions for attacking. Tap or hold to shoot at enemies, collect weapons and coins as you progress, and aim for a high score!
