Rotating Disks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rotating Disks is an engaging puzzle game that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. In this captivating game, players are tasked with maneuvering two yellow rotating disks that spin in unison from a central point. Your mission is to adeptly navigate through the colorful cascade of balls that emerge from the center of the screen.

The balls come in two distinct colors: purple and yellow. As you play, your primary objective is to collect as many yellow balls as you can while skillfully avoiding the purple ones. Hitting a purple ball results in an instant game over, making it essential to maintain your focus and precision. The more yellow balls you gather, the higher your score will be, adding a layer of challenge and competitiveness to the gameplay.

While the mechanics are simple and easy to grasp, the fast-paced nature of Rotating Disks ensures that each round is both challenging and exhilarating. This addictive puzzle game is perfect for players looking for a quick gaming fix or those who enjoy honing their skills over time. With its vibrant visuals and straightforward controls, Rotating Disks keeps you engaged and eager to outperform your previous scores.

How to play free Rotating Disks game online

To play Rotating Disks, use your mouse or finger to control the movement of the yellow disks. Keep them spinning as you aim to catch the yellow balls while avoiding the purple ones. The game ends if you catch a purple ball, so stay alert and focus on collecting yellow balls to maximize your score!
