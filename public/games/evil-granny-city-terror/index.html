Evil Granny: City Terror

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chilling world of Evil Granny: City Terror, where danger lurks around every corner! Trapped in a desolate city overrun by the malevolent Evil Granny and her eerie puppets, you must muster all your courage to escape. The city isn't merely a backdrop; it becomes a labyrinth of terror that tests your wits and stealth. Your primary objective? To locate and gather all 8 keys scattered throughout the sinister environment, each one granting you a step closer to liberation.

Navigating this haunted city won't be as straightforward as it may seem. With the Evil Granny prowling the streets and her sinister puppets lurking in the shadows, you ll need to tread cautiously. Stealth is your best friend; one wrong move, and you ll find yourself face-to-face with the dread-inducing Granny. Utilize the environment to your advantage by hiding and exploring various locations as you search for those vital keys. Each collection brings you a step closer to breaking free, but keep your ears peeled for the telltale sounds of her approach. Your survival instincts are your most potent weapon in this nightmare!

Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-pumping adventure filled with suspense and fear as you attempt to escape from the clutches of Evil Granny. Will you conquer your fears and make it out alive, or will you become just another victim in her terrifying dominion? Good luck!

How to play free Evil Granny: City Terror game online

To play Evil Granny: City Terror, simply navigate through the city using your keyboard or touchscreen controls. Search for keys while avoiding detection by Evil Granny and her puppets. Collect all 8 keys to unlock your path to freedom. Stay alert and hide when necessary to survive!
