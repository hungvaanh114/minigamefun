Black Friday: Shopping Mania?Black Friday: Shopping Mania

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embrace the thrill of retail therapy in Black Friday: Shopping Mania! Join supermodel Kendall Jenner as you embark on an exhilarating shopping spree filled with incredible bargains and jaw-dropping discounts. It s a day dedicated to splurging, so pull on your comfiest shoes and prepare for a fun-filled adventure at the mall!

Your mission is to browse through various stores, collecting fashionable outfits and stylish accessories while racing against the clock. Every shop is bursting with deals that you won t want to miss out on. Navigate through the vibrant mall environment, grabbing the hottest items and filling your shopping bags to the brim. Skills like speed and strategy will prove vital as you strive to maximize your savings.

As you shop until you drop, make sure to keep an eye on limited-time offers and flash sales to snag the best items before they re gone. Interact with different retailers, compare prices, and decide which stylish pieces are worth your budget. Customizing your character and creating your unique shopping look adds an extra layer of fun!

Get ready to immerse yourself in the ultimate shopping experience this Black Friday. Whether you're a fashion lover or just in it for the deals, Black Friday: Shopping Mania will keep you entertained and eager to upgrade your wardrobe.

How to play free Black Friday: Shopping Mania game online

To play Black Friday: Shopping Mania, simply select your character and start your journey at the mall. Explore each store, drag items to your cart, and check out quickly to maximize your savings. Keep an eye on the timer, and make sure to grab as many discounts as you can before time runs out!
