Flower Defense - Zombie Siege

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the vibrant world of Flower Defense - Zombie Siege, players are thrust into a captivating battle against relentless zombies that have ravaged everything in sight, leaving behind only a beautiful garden. Unlike traditional tower defense games such as Plants vs. Zombies, this title introduces an innovative mechanic where players can merge identical plants to evolve them into stronger varieties. This unique merging system not only enhances attack power but also transforms the appearance of the plants, making them more visually stunning as they progress.

Your mission is clear: cultivate and synthesize a diverse array of powerful plants to stand against the impending zombie onslaught. As you continue to merge plants, you unlock new varieties, each providing unique abilities and bolstering your defenses. The challenge escalates as waves of zombies approach, making strategic plant placement and timely merging essential for survival. With beautifully designed gardens serving as your battleground, it s not just about defense but also about creating a flourishing ecosystem amidst chaos.

Take charge and defend the last remnants of untainted land by ensuring your botanical allies are prepared for the perpetual siege. The stakes are high; every plant counts, and their synergy is crucial in overcoming the zombie horde. Your strategic decisions will determine the fate of your garden, making every merge and placement a pivotal moment in the battle for survival.

How to play free Flower Defense - Zombie Siege game online

To play Flower Defense - Zombie Siege, start by selecting two identical plants and merge them by dragging one onto the other. This creates a new plant with enhanced abilities. Continue merging plants to access stronger varieties and strategically place them in your garden to fend off incoming zombies. Use your resources wisely!
