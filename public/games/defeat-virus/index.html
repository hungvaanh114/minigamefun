Defeat Virus

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the engaging world of Defeat Virus, where strategy meets fun in a thrilling battle against glass obstacles. You begin with a limited number of balls and your objective is clear: strategically click to drop the balls in such a way that they shatter all the glass pieces standing in your way. Each level presents unique challenges, demanding focus and precision as you navigate through the glass barricades. Your goal is to break all the glasses and move forward to the next stage, but be careful if you run out of balls before achieving that, it s game over, and you ll need to restart the level.

The addictive gameplay of Defeat Virus keeps you on your toes. With each level, the complexity increases, testing your skills and enhancing your strategic thinking. Players must analyze the layout of the glasses, choosing the best angles for their shots in order to maximize impact. With a variety of glass arrangements and obstacles, every level offers a fresh and exciting challenge. Mastering the game not only provides satisfaction but also sharpens your problem-solving abilities.

How to play free Defeat Virus game online

To play Defeat Virus, simply click on the screen to drop your balls. Aim wisely, considering the angle and the placement of the glasses. Your goal is to break all the glasses with the balls you have before running out. If all your balls are used up without breaking the glasses, you ll need to restart the level. Enjoy the challenges ahead!
