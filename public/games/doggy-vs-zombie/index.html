Doggy vs Zombie

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Doggy Vs Zombie, an action-packed endless running game that will keep you on your toes! In this thrilling adventure, players take on the role of a brave little doggy tasked with navigating through a chaotic landscape filled with relentless zombies. Your mission is simple yet challenging: run as fast as you can, avoid various obstacles, and outsmart the cunning zombie ambushes that lie in wait.

As you dash through the colorful, cartoonish world, you'll have the opportunity to jump over barriers and gracefully overlap your zombie foes, giving you a chance to eliminate them and rack up bonus points. Along the way, be sure to collect delicious candy scattered throughout the level. Not only does candy serve as a delightful treat for your pooch, but it also boosts your score, paving the way to unlocking new levels and exciting bonuses.

The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay will keep you hooked for hours on end. Think you have what it takes to outrun the zombies and become the ultimate canine hero? Lace up your virtual sneakers and get ready for an adventure like no other in Doggy Vs Zombie!

How to play free Doggy Vs Zombie game online

To play Doggy Vs Zombie, simply use your keyboard to navigate your character. Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and enemy zombies. Collect as much candy as possible to increase your score. Be quick and strategic to avoid being caught by the zombies as you race through the endless terrain!
