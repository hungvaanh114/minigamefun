Levis Face Plastic Surgery 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Levi's Face Plastic Surgery 2," players take on the role of a skilled plastic surgeon tasked with restoring the handsome features of Levi, who has suffered a significant facial injury due to an accident. This immersive game allows you to engage in medical tasks that require both precision and care, as you'll perform a detailed cosmetic procedure aimed at grafting healthy skin onto Levi's scarred face.

The game not only provides an engaging storyline but also challenges you with various surgical techniques and tools typical of a real-life clinic. From sterilizing the surgical area to selecting the appropriate donor skin, each step of the process is crucial to ensure the best possible outcome. You must pay close attention to the instructions given throughout the game to perform every task flawlessly, leading to a successful surgery and Levi's return to his former self.

As you progress, you'll encounter sequential challenges that test your skills and knowledge about plastic surgery. The satisfaction of seeing tangible results as you expertly stitch and heal Levi's face will make each moment in the operating room rewarding. Demonstrate your surgical prowess and artistic abilities to transform Levi's scarred visage back to its original beauty, ensuring he regains his confidence and charm. Good luck on this exciting journey in cosmetic surgery!

How to play free Levis Face Plastic Surgery 2 game online

To play "Levi's Face Plastic Surgery 2," simply click on the start button. Follow the on-screen instructions to carry out various surgical procedures. Use your mouse to select tools, apply them correctly on Levi's face, and adhere to the prompt to complete each step of the surgery efficiently for the best results.
