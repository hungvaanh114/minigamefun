Super Mario Stacks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the super plumber on an exciting adventure with Super Mario Stacks! In this thrilling endless runner game, your reflexes will be put to the ultimate test. As you tap left and right, your goal is to jump over an ever-changing array of stacks that appear before you. But be quick these stacks will disappear if you take too long to make your move.

As you navigate through the perilous platforms, it s crucial to avoid the edges to prevent a tumble to your doom. Collect special boxes scattered throughout the levels to earn valuable points, which can help boost your score and showcase your skills. Additionally, keep an eye out for magical mushrooms, as these little power-ups can bring you back into the game after a fall, giving you another shot at reaching new heights.

The vibrant graphics and cheerful sound effects will keep you engaged as you help Mario leap over stacks and overcome obstacles. The faster you tap, the more exhilarating your journey will be! Every jump adds to your adrenaline rush as you aim to beat your previous high score. So gear up, stay focused, and get ready to embark on an unforgettable journey in the world of Super Mario.

How to play free Super Mario Stacks game online

To play Super Mario Stacks, simply tap the left or right side of your screen to make Mario jump over the stacks. Aim for the boxes to score points and grab mushrooms to revive yourself if you fall. Keep moving quickly to avoid the disappearing stacks and prevent falling from the edges!
