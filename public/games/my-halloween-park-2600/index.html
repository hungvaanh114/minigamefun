My Halloween Park

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you prepared to take charge of your very own Halloween-themed amusement park? My Halloween Park is an engaging 3D simulation game that allows you to design a thrilling and spooky park for excited visitors. Collect mischievous ghosts and load them into your haunted house, where guests will eagerly pay to experience spine-chilling attractions. The more visitors you attract, the more money you'll earn to further expand your park.

As you collect revenue, you'll have the opportunity to unlock and upgrade a variety of entertainment options and play areas. Create vibrant pumpkin patches, delightful candy shops, and exhilarating ghost trains complete with twists and turns that will leave your guests shrieking with joy. You can also enhance your ghost-catching abilities and increase your gardening capacity to ensure your park remains irresistible to visitors. With each upgrade, your Halloween park will become even more entertaining and enchanting, capturing the true essence of the spooky season.

My Halloween Park immerses you in the festive spirit of Halloween, making it the perfect game for those who like a mix of fun and fright. As you manage your park and keep visitors entertained, you ll find endless opportunities to create hauntingly delightful experiences. So, gear up and prepare for a hauntingly good time!

How to play free My Halloween Park game online

Once you're in the game, start by collecting ghosts and directing them to your haunted house. Use the money earned to improve and unlock new attractions. Manage your ghost-carrying and gardening capacities to make your park the ultimate Halloween destination. Explore, experiment, and expand your spooky paradise!
