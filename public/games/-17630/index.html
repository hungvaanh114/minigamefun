Knife Hit Challenge?Knife Hit Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of the Knife Hit Challenge, where precision and skill are your best allies! In this thrilling game, your objective is to throw blades at a rotating target, ensuring that you hit the bullseye while avoiding any obstacles that may hinder your progress. As you embark on each level, you'll need to carefully strategize your throws since you have a limited number of knives at your disposal. Make every throw count!

Collect sparkling diamonds as you successfully hit the target. These precious gems are not just for show; they allow you to unlock and purchase new and exciting knives from the in-game store. The more diamonds you gather, the more options you have for customizing your weaponry and enhancing your gameplay experience. Challenge yourself to climb the level ladder, proving your knife-throwing prowess while experiencing the satisfaction of each successful hit.

As you advance through the levels, be mindful of not only the rotating targets but also various obstacles that may obstruct your path. Each level presents a unique challenge, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging. Remember, wasting knives on passing obstacles will diminish your chances of collecting diamonds and progressing further. So take aim, focus, and enjoy the thrill of the knife-throwing challenge!

How to play free Knife Hit Challenge game online

To play the Knife Hit Challenge, simply aim your knife at the target and tap the screen to throw. Time your throws carefully to ensure you hit the center without colliding with any obstacles. Collect the diamonds from each successful hit to unlock new knives and climb to higher levels!
