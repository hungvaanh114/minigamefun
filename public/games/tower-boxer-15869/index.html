Tower Boxer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Tower Boxer, where boxing prowess meets a daring demolition challenge! In this game, a group of audacious boxing champions has taken it upon themselves to obliterate an entire skyscraper using nothing but their fists. Sounds like a wild idea? Join them if you dare!

Your mission is straightforward: punch the towering structure from either the left or the right side to gradually bring it crashing down. Every punch counts, and your ability to strategize your strikes will determine how quickly you can chop through the levels of this colossal tower. But be cautious! As you execute your punches, you ll need to remain vigilant of the pesky balconies that approach you. These balconies can obstruct your punches, so timing and precision are key. If you re not careful, they might hinder your progress!

As you engage in this thrilling demolition adventure, you will find yourself immersed in dynamic environments and delightful graphics that keep the excitement flowing. With each strike you deliver, the building quakes, and the thrill amplifies, inviting you to keep going until the very last brick falls. Are you ready to unleash your boxing skills and tackle this seemingly impossible feat?

How to play free Tower Boxer game online

To enjoy Tower Boxer, simply click on the left or right side of your screen to punch the tower. Aim carefully to avoid incoming balconies while delivering powerful blows to dismantle the skyscraper piece by piece. Keep your rhythm and reaction sharp to conquer each level!


Instructions

Punch the skyscraper to chop down as much of it Switch sides to avoid balconies Choose your character in the menu and play by tapping the left or right side of the screen to punch respectively
