Skibidi Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Skibidi Drop is an engaging and entertaining physics-based puzzle game featuring a total of 60 exciting levels! Your primary objective is to eliminate all boxes that stand in the way of the Skibidi Toilet, ensuring its safe passage to the grass. The game offers a delightful mix of challenge and creativity as you interact with various blocks throughout your adventure. All you need to do is click or tap on the boxes or blocks to remove them. Strategy is key, as you'll have to think carefully about how to utilize the blocks provided to achieve your goal.

Each level poses distinct challenges, providing ample opportunities for you to test your problem-solving skills. Should you make a mistake, don t worry! The game allows you to reset each level, giving you a chance to rethink your strategy and approach the puzzle anew. Dive into the world of Skibidi Drop where fun meets physics, and enjoy solving these entertaining obstacles!

How to play free Skibidi Drop game online

To play Skibidi Drop, simply select a level and begin by observing the arrangement of blocks and the position of the Skibidi Toilet. Click or tap on the blocks you want to remove, keeping in mind the goal of guiding the Skibidi Toilet safely to the grass. You can reset the level at any point if you need to adjust your tactics.
