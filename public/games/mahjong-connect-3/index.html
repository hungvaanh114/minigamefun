How to play free Mahjong Connect-3 game online

To begin playing Mahjong Connect-3, simply match two identical tiles that are connected with a maximum of three straight lines. Keep an eye on the timer to clear the board as efficiently as possible. As you progress, utilize strategic thinking to navigate through the increasing complexity of the levels.
