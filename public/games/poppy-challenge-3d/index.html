Poppy Challenge 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Poppy Challenge 3D, where exciting new characters from the Poppy universe collide with the eerie squid survival dolls in an exhilarating twist on the classic hide-and-seek game. If you're familiar with the infamous Poppy Horror character, then it s time to immerse yourself in this captivating adventure.

In Poppy Challenge 3D, you must outsmart Huggy Wuggy and other creepy dolls as they hunt for you. The game is an adrenaline rush, filled with suspense and strategy. Choose to be either a seeker or a hider, and navigate through various environments to hide or seek your opponents. To survive, find the perfect shelter where you can remain undetected, and strategize your moves carefully to stay just a step ahead of the terrifying creatures lurking in the shadows.

As a hider, you ll need to think on your feet and choose your hiding spots wisely to avoid detection. Will you blend in with the surroundings or find a clever nook to remain concealed? As a seeker, it s your job to uncover the hiding spots of others before they escape your grasp. Quick reflexes and keen observation skills will serve you well in this fast-paced survival game.

How to play free Poppy Challenge 3D game online

To play Poppy Challenge 3D, simply choose your character and game mode. If you re a hider, navigate the map to find clever hiding spots. As a seeker, explore the environment to find and tag hiders before time runs out. Use your surroundings to your advantage and outsmart your opponents!
