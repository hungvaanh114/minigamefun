Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for another wild adventure with the feisty grandmother in ANGRY GRAN RUN: LONDON. This time, the beloved crazy granny is on a mission to rid the streets of London from troublemakers while transforming it into an increasingly chaotic playground. Packed with humor and action, players will find themselves sprinting through the iconic landmarks of the British capital, dodging obstacles, and outsmarting pesky hooligans. You'll need to unleash your inner run-and-dodge master to help this spirited elder navigate through the hustle and bustle.

As you dash through the vibrant streets, you will encounter a plethora of challenges that will test your reflexes and skills. Complete daily tasks and monthly missions to unlock a treasure trove of bonuses and fresh content that adds even more excitement to the game. Engage with your surroundings as you collect coins, perform spectacular stunts, and unveil unique characters that add flavor to your running escapade. The more you play, the greater the adventure becomes!

The lively animations, colorful graphics, and quirky sound effects will immerse players in a whimsical world where the antics of a grandma bring chaos and joy in equal measure. Whether you're a seasoned runner or a newbie, ANGRY GRAN RUN: LONDON promises to provide endless fun and new surprises in each run.

How to play free ANGRY GRAN RUN: LONDON game online

To play ANGRY GRAN RUN: LONDON, use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to navigate. Press the up arrow to jump over obstacles, the down arrow to slide under them, and the left/right arrows or swipes to change lanes. Collect coins and avoid challenges to complete your missions!
