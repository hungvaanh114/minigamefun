Ocean Kids Back to School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Ocean Kids Back to School, an exciting virtual adventure where preparation for the new school year begins! Dive into the world of fun and creativity as you assist these vibrant kids in getting everything ready for their first day back in class. The emphasis is on fashion, style, and perfecting every tiny detail!

In this delightful online dress-up and design game tailored for girls, you'll help the kids select trendy outfits that match their unique personalities. From chic dresses to cool casuals, there are endless options to explore! Don't forget, hairstyles play a crucial role too. Style their hair in trendy looks that will make them stand out.

As you go through the shopping spree for outfits and accessories, make sure to gather all the necessary school supplies they will need for study sessions ahead. From notebooks to backpacks, each item reflects the kids' lively spirit, ensuring they are prepared and excited for a successful year.

Once their outfits are ready, you'll then shift gears to fix up their trusty Vespa scooter. A little bit of tinkering and creativity will ensure the kids can ride in style. Enjoy the thrill of blending fashion with function as you navigate through this engaging game.

How to play free Ocean Kids Back To School game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse to navigate the different sections. Select outfits and accessories by clicking on the options available. For hairstyles, choose your favorites and just drag them onto the characters. Finally, follow the on-screen instructions to repair the Vespa to complete the adventure.
