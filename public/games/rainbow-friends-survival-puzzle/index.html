Rainbow Friends Survival Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the colorful yet perilous world of Rainbow Friends Survival Puzzle! In this exhilarating action game, you must dash and dart around the room, evading a monstrous foe that aims to hunt you down. You take on the role of an Among Us crewmate, renowned for agility and strategy, as you navigate through each level filled with excitement and danger. Your primary objective is to collect shimmering coins scattered throughout the room. These coins are not just for show; they are essential for unlocking the exit that leads to your next adventure.

As you traverse the maze of obstacles, you must remain vigilant and think quickly. The lurking monster can appear unexpectedly, and your survival hinges on your ability to outmaneuver it. Each completed level brings you closer to mastering the art of survival in this visually captivating and adrenaline-pumping environment. Gather all the coins and leap into the exit to advance, testing your reflexes and cunning with every challenge. Do you have what it takes to survive the relentless pursuit and successfully escape each thrilling encounter? There's only one way to find out!

How to play free Rainbow Friends Survival Puzzle game online

To start playing, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your crewmate around the room. Collect all the coins while avoiding the monster. Once you've gathered every coin, jump into the exit to complete the level and progress to the next challenge. Stay sharp and good luck!
