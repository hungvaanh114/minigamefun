Dirty Them All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating and hilarious experience with Dirty Them All, where your main objective is to make a splash literally! In this quirky game, you ll take on the role of a mischievous driver who navigates through muddy terrains, all while splattering unsuspecting pedestrians with mud. The more dirt you fling their way, the more fun you ll have!

As you rev up your engine and tear through the muck, watch out for the characters on the road. They ll jump and scamper after you, trying to catch you as you zoom by. Your aim is to maneuver your car expertly, dodging their efforts while maximizing your splash. Each splash of mud not only adds to the visual hilarity but also boosts your score! Challenge yourself to splash as many people as possible before crossing the finish line. With each level, the excitement grows as the crowd gets thicker, and the obstacles become trickier. So buckle up and prepare for a side-splitting adventure that promises to keep you entertained for hours!

Join in on the fun with Dirty Them All, where the only goal is to get dirty and make everyone else dirty too! This lighthearted game combines racing, adventure, and a whole lot of laughs that s perfect for casual gaming sessions with friends or solo play during downtime.

How to play free Dirty Them All game online

To play Dirty Them All, use your keyboard to control the car's movement. Drive strategically to splash mud on as many people as possible while avoiding obstacles. Keep an eye on the chasing crowd and make swift turns to stay ahead. Collect points for each successful splash and race towards the finish line!
