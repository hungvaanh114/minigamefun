Unravel Egg

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an intriguing journey with Unravel Egg, a captivating HTML5 logic puzzle game that features a sleek and simplistic design. This exciting puzzle experience is all about unraveling eggs intricately connected by fate lines. With a total of 50 challenging levels, your objective is to skillfully manipulate the eggs to turn them green, signifying that you have successfully unraveled them.

As you dive into the game, you will encounter a variety of puzzles that require you to think critically and strategically. To complete each level, you must grab and drag the eggs in a manner that ensures the connecting lines do not intersect. Each successful move brings you closer to that satisfying green glow, indicating that you have effectively solved the puzzle. The minimalistic design not only enhances the visual appeal of the game but also allows players to focus on unraveling the mysteries of each level without distractions.

Whether you are a casual gamer or a puzzle enthusiast, Unravel Egg presents a delightful challenge that will keep you engaged and entertained. With every stage becoming progressively more intricate, you'll find yourself sharpening your problem-solving skills while enjoying the meditative experience that this game offers.

How to play free Unravel Egg game online

To play Unravel Egg, simply grab an egg with your mouse or touchpad and drag it along the screen. Your aim is to position the eggs so that all of them turn green, remembering to avoid intersecting lines during the process. Complete each level by achieving the green state for both the eggs and the connecting lines!
