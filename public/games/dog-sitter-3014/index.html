Instructions:

Step into the paws of a dedicated dog sitter in this exciting and dynamic game, where quick reflexes and keen observation are essential. The dogs from your neighborhood have escaped, roaming freely and causing a delightful ruckus. Your mission is to track them down in their favorite hiding spots, particularly in the lush garden of your home.

As you venture through the garden, be prepared to chase after these energetic pups. Each dog you catch earns you points as you return them safely to their owners. The more dogs you rescue, the higher your score climbs! This game not only tests your agility but also your ability to strategize and prioritize which dogs to catch first. With various breeds to find, from playful puppies to clever terriers, every chase becomes a thrilling challenge.

The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay make "Dog Sitter" an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Dive into the adventurous world where floppy ears and wagging tails reign, and establish yourself as the ultimate dog sitter in your neighborhood. The rush of excitement will leave you wanting more, as each level presents new challenges and adventurous scenarios!

How to play free Dog Sitter game online

To play the Dog Sitter game, control your character using the arrow keys or on-screen joystick. Move around the garden to spot the roaming dogs, quickly approach them, and catch them by clicking or tapping the designated button. Once you ve caught a dog, return it to its owner to score points. Keep chasing more dogs to maximize your score!
