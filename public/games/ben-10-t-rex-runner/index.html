How to play free Ben 10 T-Rex Runner game online

To play Ben 10 T-Rex Runner, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to guide Ben s movements. The up arrow allows him to jump over obstacles while the down key helps him slide under them. Collect power-ups as you run to boost your score and increase your speed. Enjoy the race!
