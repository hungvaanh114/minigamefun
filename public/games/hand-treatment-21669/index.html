Hand Treatment

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Hand Treatment, young players step into the shoes of a little doctor tasked with treating children who have had unfortunate falls on ice. The game kicks off with a series of adorable patients boys and girls alike arriving at your hospital with various hand injuries. It's your job to examine, diagnose, and treat their ailments, providing an immersive experience infused with both fun and education.

As you embark on your medical journey, you ll encounter multiple scenarios where each child presents a unique situation regarding their hand injuries. From simple scrapes to more complex fractures, the game offers a variety of realistic medical procedures to perform. This not only teaches young players about empathy and care but also engages them in the basics of first-aid treatment. The vibrant graphics and interactive tools make each healing experience engaging and enjoyable, enticing players to come back for more. By effectively treating these hand injuries, you'll help the children feel better and transform them back into happy, playful kids!

Engage in your very own hospital adventure and see how your medical skills shine while developing a love for helping others. This online medical simulation game provides an excellent platform for kids to learn about healthcare while having loads of fun providing treatment and watching their patients heal!

How to play free Hand Treatment game online

To begin playing the Hand Treatment game, select your patient and carefully examine their hand injuries. Use the provided medical tools to treat each wound, cleaning, bandaging, and applying any necessary remedies. Follow the on-screen instructions to successfully complete each task and ensure that your patients leave your hospital happy and healed!
