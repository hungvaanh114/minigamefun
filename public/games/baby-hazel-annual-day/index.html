Baby Hazel Annual Day

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a day of laughter and celebration with Baby Hazel in "Baby Hazel Annual Day"! This exciting preschool event is an opportunity for Hazel to showcase her creativity and performance skills. The theme this year features a fun clown act, and Baby Hazel is eager to impress everyone with her colorful costuming and delightful antics.

Your journey begins with helping Baby Hazel and her mom select the perfect clown outfit. Dive into a world of vibrant colors and playful accessories as you choose from a range of costumes and playful items that will transform Hazel into a cheerful clown. Once you've picked out the ideal costume, it's time to get festive! Dress her up in her new attire and apply some fun and attractive makeup shades that will bring her character to life.

But the fun doesn t stop there! As the Annual Day unfolds, help Hazel and her friends overcome their stage fright and perform onstage. This is where your role as a supportive friend comes into play encourage them to shine and enjoy the spotlight. With your support, they can put on an unforgettable show filled with joy, laughter, and fun antics. Join in the celebration and make the Annual Day a memorable experience for Baby Hazel and her friends!

How to play free Baby Hazel Annual Day game online

To play Baby Hazel Annual Day, start by navigating through the game menu. Select to assist Hazel in choosing the costume and accessories by clicking on the items that catch your interest. Once dressed, engage in putting on makeup and finally guide Hazel and her friends through their performance onstage by clicking various prompts to reduce their nervousness and enjoy their time!
