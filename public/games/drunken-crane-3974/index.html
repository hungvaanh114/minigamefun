Step into the whimsical world of Drunken Crane, an exhilarating action-puzzle game where the main objective revolves around transporting a tipsy uncle to a cozy bench. Players utilize intuitive controls located on the right side of the screen to maneuver the crane, allowing for movements to the side, downward, and for releasing your inebriated passenger. The gameplay experience is reminiscent of traditional crane machines, offering a unique twist that promises endless amusement.

This game features a robust collection of 38 distinct stages, ensuring players remain captivated and challenged throughout their journey. Each phase brings fresh and inventive obstacles, including the quirky scenario of guiding your uncle atop an elephant. Additionally, players will encounter thrilling levels that incorporate trampoline mechanics, requiring skillful timing and strategic thinking to master the bouncy environment.

As you progress through the various stages, aim to achieve the coveted three-star rating in each level. Successfully completing all 38 stages with top scores unlocks secret stages waiting to be discovered, adding an extra layer of excitement and challenge to your gameplay. It s a fun-filled adventure that encourages creativity and precision with every lift and drop.

How to play free Drunken Crane game online

To play Drunken Crane, simply use the on-screen buttons to control the crane's movement. Navigate it horizontally to position above your drunk uncle, lower the crane to lift him, and then release him over the designated bench or other playful locations. Timing and accuracy are key to mastering each level!
