Pick Me Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Have you ever dreamed of being behind the wheel and earning money while exploring the city? In Pick Me Up, you can transform that dream into reality! Step into the shoes of a ride-share driver, where your main objective is to pick up and drop off customers as efficiently as possible. As you navigate through the bustling streets, you'll have the opportunity to not only earn cash, but also gain experience and advance to higher levels.

The vibrant city is full of intriguing spots, bustling traffic, and exciting challenges waiting for you! With every fare you complete, you'll uncover new locations and better routes, enhancing your gameplay experience. Upgrade your vehicle, unlock new rides, and optimize your strategy to become the ultimate ride-share driver in town. Will you be able to navigate the maze of city streets and keep your customers satisfied?

In Pick Me Up, every ride is a new adventure filled with surprises. The more customers you serve, the more opportunities you'll have to explore. Keep an eye out for special events and unique passengers who might provide bonus rewards. Put your driving skills to the test and see if you have what it takes to build a successful ride-sharing business!

How to play free Pick Me Up game online

To play Pick Me Up, start by selecting your vehicle and then navigate through the city to find customers waiting for a ride. Simply approach the customer to pick them up, and then drive them to their desired destination. Be attentive to traffic rules, minimize delays, and aim for a smooth drop-off to earn rewards!


Instructions

- Tap and hold to drive - Release to brake - Avoid crashing - Collect cash and unlock cool cars
