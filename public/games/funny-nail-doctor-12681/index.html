Funny Nail Doctor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Funny Nail Doctor, an adorable mouse-themed skill game that puts you in the role of a caring doctor. Here, you'll encounter a young lady who has come to your clinic seeking relief from her nail troubles. Both her hands and feet are affected, causing her considerable discomfort. It's your responsibility to ensure she feels better as swiftly as possible!

In this engaging game, you'll utilize your medical expertise to treat her ailing nails. Begin by cleaning her nails thoroughly to remove any dirt and debris. Once you ve cleaned them, carefully examine the wounds that require healing. Your task is to apply the right medicines and undertake various procedures designed to promote healing. Your diligence and attention to detail will make all the difference for your patient, so be sure to follow each step with care.

After successfully nursing her nails back to health, it s time to unleash your creativity! You can now embark on a delightful nail makeover journey where you can decorate and style her nails to her liking. Choose from an array of colors, patterns, and accessories to help her feel fabulous and confident once again. This vibrant transformation process brings a fun twist to the gaming experience, blending medical skills with an artistic touch.

How to play free Funny Nail Doctor game online

To play Funny Nail Doctor, simply follow the on-screen instructions. Use your mouse to navigate through the clinic, select the necessary medical tools, and perform treatments on the young lady s nails. Once her nails are healed, access the decoration menu to begin the nail makeover process!
