Santa Revenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exciting holiday adventure in Santa Revenge, where it's time to defend your cherished gifts from a horde of cunning thieves. As Santa, you'll need to think quickly and act even faster to thwart the plans of these mischievous bandits who are trying to spoil the festivities. Your mission is simple: protect your gifts at all costs!

In this thrilling game, danger lurks from every direction, so be on high alert! Not only must you fend off the relentless thieves, but you also need to steer clear of unexpected bombs that could ruin your night. With a blend of strategy and dexterity, you'll navigate through various challenges while trying to save every last present in your bag. Are you up for the task?

Play solo in survival mode to put your skills to the test, or gather your friends for an exciting two-player mode that doubles the fun and chaos. Team up or compete against each other to see who can score the best protection for Santa s treasured gifts. Will you emerge as the ultimate defender in this festive showdown? Enter the world of Santa Revenge and find out!

How to play free Santa Revenge game online

To play Santa Revenge, use the directional keys to move Santa around the screen while avoiding thieves and bombs. Click the mouse button to interact with gifts and fend off enemies. In two-player mode, use separate controls for both players to strategize and protect the gifts together. Enjoy the festive chaos!


Instructions

Single Player Move WASD Shoot G Two Player Move WASD and Arrows Fire G and P
