Spooky Forest Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Venture into the eerie depths of the Spooky Forest Run, where an innocent stroll turns into a heart-pounding race for survival. As you meander through the shadowy trees, an ominous skeleton emerges, setting off an adrenaline-fueled chase. With every echo of its bones rattling behind you, the urgency to escape escalates. Your only chance at freedom is to tap the screen and leap over scattered obstacles, including gnarled branches and lurking creatures that pepper your path.

As the skeletal figure relentlessly pursues you, the thrill intensifies. Each successful jump propels you further into the labyrinthine forest, but one misstep could spell disaster. Don't let the eerie atmosphere distract you darkness lurks, and only your quick reflexes will keep you out of the grim grasp of the skeleton. The night is alive with danger, and each moment counts in this high-stakes challenge of agility and speed. Will you outpace the relentless pursuer or become another lost soul in the Spooky Forest?

How to play free Spooky Forest Run game online

To navigate your way through Spooky Forest Run, simply tap the screen to make your character jump over obstacles and evade other menacing figures. Keep an eye on your surroundings and time your jumps well to maintain speed and distance from the chasing skeleton. A single slip could lead to game over, so stay alert!
