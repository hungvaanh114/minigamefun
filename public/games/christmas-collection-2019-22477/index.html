Christmas Collection 2019

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a joyful adventure in the Christmas Collection 2019, where your mission is to assist Santa in delivering presents to well-behaved children around the world! In this delightful game, you'll need to collect as many Christmas gifts as possible so that Santa can pack and send them out in time for the holidays. Your journey begins by simply pressing any gift to ignite the search for presents.

Once you're in the game, maneuver your mouse or fingertip across identical adjacent Christmas gifts, whether they're lined up horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The key is to select a minimum of three gifts to create a match and when you do so, simply release the mouse button to see the magic happen!

Keep an eye out for special bonuses: every sixth gift you collect will reward you with an extra surprise, while gathering more than seven gifts in a single match grants you a time bonus, helping you to further accelerate your gift-gathering efforts. But remember, timing is everything! You must collect the requested gifts before the allotted time runs out, or you will be faced with the disappointment of losing the game.

How to play free Christmas Collection 2019 game online

To play the Christmas Collection 2019 game, start by clicking on any gift. Use your mouse or finger to connect at least three gifts that are adjacent to each other. Once selected, release the button to finalize your match. Keep collecting gifts within the time limit to score points and earn bonuses!
