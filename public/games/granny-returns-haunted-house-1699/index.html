Granny Returns Haunted House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a nightmarish realm in Granny Returns Haunted House, the spine-chilling sequel to the wildly popular Granny game. This 3D horror adventure transports players into the gloomy confines of an abandoned asylum, where every corner holds a new terror. With a blend of escape mechanics, item collection, and stealth gameplay, this game promises an adrenaline-packed experience. You must explore the eerie environment to uncover clues that will aid in your survival.

As you venture deeper into the asylum, it becomes crucial to keep moving; stagnation could lead to your demise, as the terrifying residents are always on the hunt. Be wary of the sounds you make; every creak and shuffle could attract unwanted attention. If the fear of being caught becomes too great, utilize hiding spots like closets to evade danger. But remember, silence is your ally any noise could signal your location to the lurking horrors.

The challenge intensifies as you gather critical items and decipher clues scattered throughout the asylum. Each successful collection brings you one step closer to escaping this nightmarish setting. Will you have what it takes to survive the horrors that await? Dive into the chilling atmosphere of Granny Returns Haunted House and test your skills in this thrilling escape horror game.

How to play free Granny Returns Haunted House game online

To play the game, navigate your character using the on-screen controls to explore the asylum. Collect items and clues while avoiding detection by enemies. If you hear footsteps, quickly find a hiding spot and remain silent. Use your collected items to solve puzzles and unlock your path to freedom.


Instructions

Controls W A S D to walk around Mouse to Look around Left Mouse Button to Fire Right Mouse Button to Aim Mouse Wheel To Change Weapons G for Grenades R to Reload F to Pickup Items Left Shift to Run Left CTRL to Crouch X to Prone V to Melee Space to Jump
