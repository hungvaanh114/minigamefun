Blocky Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Blocky Race invites players into a vibrant, pixelated world where exhilarating racing action and chaos collide. Available to play on iyigames.com, this exciting new racing game lets you take command of a powerful muscle car as you navigate through bustling traffic and colorful landscapes. Your main objective is to race against time, maneuvering at top speeds while evading collisions in the exhilarating race mode. Are you ready for the challenge?

For those who crave a bit more destruction, the demolition mode allows players to unleash their inner wrecking ball. Smash through traffic and leave a trail of chaos behind you as you blow up cars and revel in the destruction. Alternatively, for a more laid-back experience, take a leisurely cruise through the extensive city brimming with hidden secrets in the exploration mode.

Super Blocky Race is designed to test your driving mettle across various race tracks, each featuring unique lengths and lap counts. With six different characters to unlock, which can enhance your gameplay experience, you ll find plenty of opportunities to showcase your amazing racing skills. Master the art of speed and precision to complete each track without crashing. So gear up, secure your helmet, and get ready to put the pedal to the metal in this thrilling blocky racing adventure!

How to play free Blocky Race game online

To play Blocky Race, simply select your character and choose a game mode. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle, and accelerate with the spacebar. Focus on completing the track as quickly as possible while avoiding accidents and traffic. Unlock new characters as you progress through the game!
