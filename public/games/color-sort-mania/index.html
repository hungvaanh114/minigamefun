How to play free Color Sort Mania game online

To play Color Sort Mania, simply drag and drop the colored bubbles into the appropriate flasks. Your goal is to sort all the bubbles by color, filling each flask without mixing colors. Strategically move bubbles between flasks to achieve the perfect arrangement and enjoy the satisfying moments as the colors align.
