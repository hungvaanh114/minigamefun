Feed the Ape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Feed The Ape, an exciting puzzle adventure that challenges your problem-solving skills! Your mission is simple yet engaging: deliver the Num Num to the hungry ape by cutting the ropes in clever ways. At first glance, it might seem straightforward; however, as you dive deeper into the levels, you'll encounter various obstacles that will require creative solutions and strategic thinking.

The initial stages serve as an effective tutorial, gradually introducing you to the game's mechanics while also allowing you to familiarize yourself with the controls. As you progress, the challenges will become increasingly difficult, adding to the excitement and intrigue. Remember, if you fail to feed the Num Num to the ape in time, it will become displeased, making it essential to think quickly and act decisively.

This game is designed to be enjoyable for players of all ages. Kids will love the vibrant graphics and playful theme, while adults can appreciate the clever puzzles that provide a refreshing mental workout. Whether you're seeking a fun way to pass the time or a challenge that tests your critical thinking, Feed The Ape offers an engaging experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to play free Feed The Ape game online

To play the Feed The Ape game, use your mouse or touch controls to cut the ropes strategically. Aim to release the Num Num so that it can roll, bounce, or drop directly into the ape's waiting mouth. Keep an eye out for stars and special items to collect along the way for bonus points!
