Blocky Fun Roads

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing "Blocky Fun Roads," a thrilling running game developed by Running Nose Studio that takes you on an exciting adventure through a charming, voxel-art world. In this game, players navigate through an array of obstacles such as TNT, boxes, cars, and ambulances while striving to earn stars and unlock various cartoon characters. The gameplay is reminiscent of the beloved "Crossy Road," yet it brings its own unique twist to the genre.

The objective is straightforward: run confidently and dodge as many hazards as possible to accumulate points and climb the leaderboard. Each star collected propels you closer to unlocking a diverse cast of over ten characters, including the likes of Bob, Droopy, Cheeky, Peppa Pig, Chuck, and Pororo, among others. With a total of 14 achievements to conquer, every run feels rewarding and engaging.

Blocky Fun Roads is not just about gameplay; it boasts stunning voxel art visuals that enhance the overall gaming experience. Additionally, the game is designed for offline play, allowing you to enjoy your runs anytime, anywhere. Features like the convenient "Save Me" option ensure that you can recover from setbacks and continue your adventures. Aim to place in the world's top 10 to prove your skills and dedication!

How to play free Blocky Fun Roads game online

To play "Blocky Fun Roads" online, simply use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to navigate and jump over obstacles. Collect stars to unlock new characters and aim for a high score by confidently maneuvering through the hazards. Remember to utilize the "Save Me" feature to continue your adventure!
