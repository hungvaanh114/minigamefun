Penguin Diner - Restaurant Dash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Penguin Diner is, without a doubt, the most captivating business simulation game of the year, featuring engaging gameplay and a charming 2D art style. In this whimsical adventure, you will help Penny, a penguin who got lost in a treacherous snowstorm, as she sets out on a journey to establish a lively restaurant.

Penny is determined to serve her customers a delightful array of food and beverages. However, she can't manage the bustling restaurant all by herself, which is where you come in! Your mission is to prepare delicious meals and ensure that each guest is served promptly to keep them happy. As you complete orders and serve food quickly, you'll earn money that can be reinvested into the restaurant.

The income generated from your hard work and excellent service can be utilized to enhance the ambiance of the restaurant or to upgrade the quality of the food you offer. With each level you complete and each satisfied customer, you ll discover exciting new ways to develop Penny s diner and make it the talk of the town. Are you up for the challenge?

How to play free Penguin Diner - Restaurant Dash game online

To play Penguin Diner, simply start the game and use your mouse to navigate. Click on customers to seat them, take their orders, and then prepare the requested dishes. Once the food is ready, serve it to the customers. Keep an eye on the clock to ensure timely service and maximize your earnings!
