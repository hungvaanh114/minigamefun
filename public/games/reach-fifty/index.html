Reach Fifty

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Reach Fifty is an engaging and thought-provoking puzzle game that challenges players to think strategically while honing their reflexes. In this captivating game, your primary objective is to rearrange and combine numbers strategically to create sums that equal fifty. As you navigate through various levels, you'll encounter different forms and numbers that need to be meticulously organized.

Players have the ability to slide numerals both horizontally and vertically, allowing for dynamic interactions and creative number combinations. The mechanics encourage logical thinking, as you'll need to evaluate which numbers to group together effectively. Successfully reaching the target number of fifty causes that specific form to freeze, making it unavailable for further use. This aspect adds an additional layer of complexity to the game, as managing your available numbers becomes crucial to progressing through each level.

With increasingly challenging layouts, Reach Fifty pushes your problem-solving skills to the limit while providing a rewarding experience. As you regroup all forms to achieve the end of each level, the satisfaction of solving each puzzle will keep you engaged. Dive into this number-fueled journey and see how many levels you can conquer!

How to play free Reach Fifty game online

To play Reach Fifty, simply use your mouse or touchpad to slide the number forms into position. Combine the numbers by aligning them to create sums of fifty. Once a form reaches that total, it will freeze. Continue rearranging until you've grouped all forms, completing the level.
