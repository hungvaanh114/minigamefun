A House of 6 Doors is an engaging point-and-click adventure game crafted by the talented team at 8B Games/Games2Mad. As you explore the intricacies of this captivating environment, you'll encounter a variety of intriguing puzzles and hidden objects that must be discovered to unlock the doors and ultimately escape.

The game challenges your problem-solving skills, encouraging you to think critically and creatively as you search for clues and uncover secrets hidden throughout the house. Each door represents a different puzzle, each designed to test your wits and provide a delightful challenge. The beautifully crafted graphics and immersive gameplay create an entertaining atmosphere where every corner of the house holds the potential for discovery.
