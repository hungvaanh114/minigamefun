Octopus Hugs

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the captivating underwater realm of Octopus Hugs, where vibrant octopuses await your command! Dive into this colorful adventure as you match the octopuses and strive to complete each unique stage. The game's enchanting graphics and engaging gameplay will surely capture your attention while providing challenges that keep you coming back for more.

As you progress through different levels, you may encounter tricky obstacles and challenging stage designs. But don't fret help is at hand! Utilize special boosters to aid your gameplay when you find yourself at an impasse. One of the most versatile tools is the Rainbow Booster, which allows you to eliminate all octopuses of the same color as the one you just matched. This can significantly turn the tide in your favor, facilitating more moves and clearing the board.

Moreover, the powerful Bomb Booster can also be a game-changer. When strategically placed, it will obliterate all adjacent octopuses in a bang, allowing you to make swift progress through challenging stages. As you navigate through the varying colorful challenges, remember that smart booster usage can help you conquer even the most difficult levels!

How to play free Octopus Hugs game online

To play the Octopus Hugs game online, simply use your mouse or finger to swap adjacent octopuses and create lines of three or more of the same color. Aim to clear objectives and use boosters wisely to overcome obstacles. Keep an eye on the level requirements and enjoy your playful underwater adventure!


Instructions

Removes all octopuses of the color with which you swap them
