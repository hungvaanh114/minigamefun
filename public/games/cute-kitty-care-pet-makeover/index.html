Cute Kitty Care - Pet Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you adore kittens and have a passion for caring for animals, then "Cute Kitty Care - Pet Makeover" is the perfect game for you! Step into the shoes of a dedicated pet caregiver as you nurture and restore health to a selection of injured kittens. Experience the joys of pet care as you select your favorite little feline and start the healing process.

Your journey begins by assessing the needs of your chosen kitten. With a gentle touch, clean its wounds and apply the necessary medicine to help it heal. After tending to the injuries, it's time to give your furry friend a refreshing bath, washing away any grime and helping it feel comfortable again. Thanks to your attentive care, the kitten will soon look and feel much better!

Once your adorable companion is healthy, it's time to unleash your creativity! Feed your kitten to ensure it regains its strength and energy completely. When the little fluffball is feeling vibrant and happy, dive into the fun world of fashion design. Use your artistic talents to create stunning costumes and accessories, making your kitty the most stylish pet on the block!

How to play free Cute Kitty Care - Pet Makeover game online

To play the game, simply select a kitten and follow the on-screen prompts to treat its wounds, administer medicine, and give it a bath. Use your mouse to navigate through various tools and options available. Once your kitten is healthy, explore the costume design section to dress up your furry friend in stylish outfits!
