Huggy Wuggy Doll

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Meet Huggy Wuggy, the enormous blue bear with a smile that might send shivers down your spine! As delightful as he may look, there's more lurking beneath the surface. This cuddly creature with gigantic, razor-sharp teeth is determined to give you the biggest hug of your life but be careful, you might not want that kind of embrace!

Your goal is clear: gather food and eat it to gain strength while devising your escape from Huggy Wuggy. As you dash through the game, watch out for the colorful balls scattered throughout the environment. Colliding with them will slow you down, increasing the chances of Huggy catching up to you. Time your movements wisely and navigate through various challenges that come your way!

The shop is your ally in this frantic race. With collected resources, you can purchase powerful bombs to blast away those pesky balls that impede your progress and also help to temporarily stun Huggy Wuggy, giving you a significant advantage. For those moments when you really need to cut through the chaos, the knife of rage is your ultimate weapon. Equip it to become invulnerable for a short period, allowing you to slice through any obstacles, including the intimidating blue bear!

How to play free Huggy Wuggy Doll game online

To play the Huggy Wuggy Doll game, use your arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your character. Collect food items to boost your health and outrun Huggy Wuggy. Avoid the balls to maintain speed, and visit the shop to buy upgrades such as bombs and the knife of rage. Stay agile and keep moving!


Instructions

Collect as much food as you can while trying not to touch the balls and run away from Huggy Wuggy Control Mouse or motion joystick on mobile devices - movement Z - use a bomb X - use the knife of rage
