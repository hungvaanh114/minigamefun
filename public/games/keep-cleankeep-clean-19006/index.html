Welcome to "Keep Clean," a delightful simulation game that invites you to turn your dream home into a spotless haven. Immerse yourself in the joy of home decoration, where fresh flowers and vibrant plants become essential elements in your living space. With the help of your family and a quirky house helper, engage in a fun-filled journey as you tidy up and beautify your surroundings.

There's no need to wait for spring to give your home a thorough clean! In "Keep Clean," every day can be your own In-House Cleaning Day. Gather your family members and tackle the clutter that has accumulated over time. Get ready to declutter your living spaces, removing items that no longer serve a purpose. Create a serene atmosphere as you fill your home with refreshing blooms and lush greenery, breathing new life into each room.

As you progress in the game, you'll discover unique challenges that encourage teamwork and creative problem-solving. Will you remodel the living room, or perhaps give the kitchen a facelift? The choice is yours! Enjoy the satisfaction of organizing and enhancing each area while unlocking exciting new items to decorate with in the process. "Keep Clean" is not just about cleaning; it s about creating a home that reflects your personal style and warmth.

How to play free Keep Clean game online

Playing "Keep Clean" is simple and engaging! Once you enter the game, navigate through various rooms of your home. Use your mouse or touchscreen to choose cleaning and decorating tools. Complete tasks by dragging unwanted items to the trash, and place beautiful flowers and plants throughout your home to earn points. Enjoy your cleaning adventure!
