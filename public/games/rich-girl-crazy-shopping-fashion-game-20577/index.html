Rich Girl Crazy Shopping - Fashion Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of Rich Girl Crazy Shopping - Fashion Game! This delightful dress-up app invites you to join six stunning girls from diverse backgrounds as they indulge in an extravagant shopping spree. With a selection of around 200 virtual clothing items, hairstyles, and accessories at your fingertips, you can unleash your inner fashionista and create endless unique looks!

Whether you're aiming for a glamorous ensemble, a trendy outfit, a classy appearance, a traditional style, or a casual vibe, you'll find options to suit every occasion. The enjoyment escalates as you mix and match various components, letting your creativity shine. These affluent ladies prioritize style and sophistication, completely unfazed by the constraints of a budget. They know the best shops to procure their fabulous finds and the most luxurious salons to pamper their hair. With this game, you can explore the fascinating realm of fashion without any financial which restrictions!

So gather your styling tools and prepare for a lavish experience where you can dress up these gorgeous dolls to match their lavish lifestyles. From selecting the perfect outfits to choosing exquisite accessories, the world of fashion and endless possibilities awaits you in Rich Girl Crazy Shopping - Fashion Game.

How to play free Rich Girl Crazy Shopping - Fashion Game online

To play the game, simply choose one of the six girls to dress up. Browse through the extensive wardrobe of items and pick outfits, hairstyles, and accessories to create stunning looks. Mix and match until you find the perfect combination, and don t hesitate to experiment with different styles!
