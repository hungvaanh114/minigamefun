Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bricks & Blocks is an engaging and addictive hypercasual strategy puzzle game that tests your spatial reasoning and puzzle-solving skills. In this entertaining game, you ll be challenged to drag brick blocks into available cells on a grid. Your objective is to clear rows and columns by arranging these blocks efficiently, ensuring that every move counts. The thrill of the game lies in its simplicity, yet it offers deep strategic elements that demand careful planning and foresight.

As you immerse yourself in Bricks & Blocks, you ll discover a delightful challenge: how far can you progress without running out of space? Each brick block you move should be positioned with intent; the goal is to find enough spots to accommodate all of them while forming complete rows and columns that disappear. However, watch out! Once you find yourself with no available moves, the game is over, leaving you to ponder your strategies for the next round. Test your skills, improve your tactics, and see if you can beat your highest score, all while enjoying a beautifully crafted puzzle experience.

How to play free Bricks & Blocks game online

To play Bricks & Blocks, simply drag the brick blocks onto the grid s empty cells according to your strategy. Aim to arrange them to form complete rows or columns, making them disappear. Plan your moves wisely to ensure you have enough space for all the blocks, as the game ends when there s no room left!
