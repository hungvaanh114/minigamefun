Embark on an epic adventure with Orco The Dragon Crown, where courage, skill, and strategy are your allies in a dark and enchanted world. The Dragon Crown, a powerful magical artifact, has had its seven precious jewels taken away by a malevolent wizard and his relentless army of undead minions. This theft has left the realm in chaos, and it s up to you to join Orco, the brave hero, on a quest to reclaim the stolen jewels and restore the Dragon Crown to its former brilliance.

As you traverse through ominous forests, treacherous mountains, and haunted castles, prepare to confront fearsome foes that will test your resolve. Employ Orco s extraordinary abilities to smash through waves of undead soldiers and charge through various challenging environments. Along the way, uncover hidden dragon eggs that contain the lost jewels, each one holding the potential to restore balance to the fractured world and enhance Orco's powers.

With beautiful art design and engaging gameplay, Orco The Dragon Crown invites players of all ages to delve deep into a narrative filled with treasure, enemies, and heart-stopping encounters. Will you be able to defeat the dark wizard and reclaim the treasures? The fate of the Dragon Crown lies in your hands!

How to play free Orco The Dragon Crown game online

To play Orco The Dragon Crown, use the arrow keys to navigate through the stunning landscapes. Press the space bar to jump and attack enemies. Collect dragon eggs and jewels by defeating undead foes and explore every corner of the realm for hidden treasures. Enjoy the adventure!
