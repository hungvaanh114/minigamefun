How to play free Innocent Hexa Puzzle game online

To play Innocent Hexa Puzzle, simply drag the hexagonal pieces from the side of your screen and drop them onto the puzzle area. Fit the pieces together until you complete the entire image. Remember, the challenge lies in using spatial reasoning to find the perfect orientation for each piece!
