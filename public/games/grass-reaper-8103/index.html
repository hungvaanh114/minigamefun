Grass Reaper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Grass Reaper is a fun and relaxing mini game that invites players to take on the role of a reaper tasked with harvesting grass. In each level of the game, you will have a specific target amount of grass to collect. However, your reaper starts with limited abilities, making your initial attempts at harvesting quite a challenge.

As you venture through the levels, you will need to gather and sell the grass you collect. The gold you earn from selling the harvested grass can be used strategically to upgrade your reaper. These upgrades are crucial, as they improve your reaper's efficiency, allowing you to gather grass faster and progress through the game at a quicker pace. The gameplay offers a mix of strategy and skill, making it engaging as you strive to meet the harvesting goals set for each level.

With its charming graphics and soothing gameplay, Grass Reaper is perfect for players looking for a casual gaming experience. The satisfaction of seeing your reaper evolve and become more skillful adds a rewarding layer to the game, making each level feel like an accomplishment.

Whether you aim for quick runs or enjoy a leisurely pace, the game caters to all play styles while providing hours of fun and enjoyment. Just hop in, harvest some grass, and see how far you can take your reaper!

How to play free Grass Reaper game online

To play Grass Reaper, use the controls to move your reaper around the level and click to harvest grass. Collect enough grass to meet your target for the level, sell it to earn gold, and then spend the gold on upgrades for your reaper to enhance its abilities. Have fun reaping!
