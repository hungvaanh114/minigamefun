How to play free block puzzle classic plus game online

To play Block Puzzle Classic Plus, simply drag and drop the falling blocks onto the game board. Aim to fill a row completely to clear it and earn points. Be aware of the limited space; once the blocks reach the top, the game is over! Plan your moves wisely!
