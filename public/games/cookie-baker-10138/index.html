Cookie Baker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the delightful world of Cookie Baker, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your matching skills! In this sugary adventure, your objective is to clear out colorful cookie panels by strategically tapping on cookies that form groups of three or more. With each successful match, you'll feel the satisfaction of progressing through increasingly challenging levels.

The rules are refreshingly simple: identify clusters of identical cookies, whether arranged vertically or horizontally, and tap to remove them from the board. Achieving combos by matching more than three cookies not only enhances your score but also brings you one step closer to beating the stage. As you advance, you'll encounter various target numbers that must be surpassed to complete each level, making every move crucial.

Get ready for an exhilarating experience that combines quick thinking with sweet rewards! With addictive gameplay and vibrant visuals, Cookie Baker guarantees hours of entertainment. Whether you're a newbie or a seasoned puzzle master, this game invites all to join in on the fun. Don t miss out on this epic craze; it s time to bake some cookies and have a blast!

How to play free Cookie Baker game online

Playing Cookie Baker is easy! As you enter the game, look for clusters of cookies that match tap on a cookie to register a match. Focus on lining up three or more identical cookies either horizontally or vertically to clear them from the board. Keep an eye on the target number to complete each level successfully!
