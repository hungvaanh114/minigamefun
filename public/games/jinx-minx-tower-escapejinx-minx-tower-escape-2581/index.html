Get ready for a spine-tingling adventure this Halloween with "Jinx & Minx Tower Escape"! Join Jinx, the spirited skeleton, and Minx, the playful bunny, as they find themselves trapped in a mysterious and deserted tower. Shadows flicker and whispers echo within the spooky corridors, and the duo must navigate their way through eerie rooms filled with challenges and puzzles. Your mission is to guide these lovable characters to safety by solving intricate riddles and collecting keys to unlock the tower's mysteries.

As you traverse through each floor of the tower, keep an eye out for delightful candy treats that not only sweeten your adventure but help you score points along the way. With each puzzle you solve and object you collect, you bring Jinx and Minx one step closer to reuniting and escaping the haunted tower. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay make for a captivating experience that will keep you on your toes and your heart racing, perfect for gamers seeking a little spooky thrill this season!

Whether you're a puzzle enthusiast or simply looking for a fun Halloween-themed game, "Jinx & Minx Tower Escape" promises endless entertainment. So gather your wits and prepare for a challenging yet exciting journey as you help your favorite characters break free from their towering prison.
