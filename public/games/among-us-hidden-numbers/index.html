Among Us Hidden Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Discover the exciting challenge of Among Us Hidden Numbers, a captivating free online skill and hidden object game that will put your observation abilities to the test. Your mission? Locate the hidden numbers scattered throughout specified images inspired by the beloved Among Us universe. With a total of six engaging levels to conquer, each level presents you with ten elusive numbers to find.

As you navigate through the levels, keep in mind that your time is limited. That means you must work quickly and efficiently to uncover all the hidden objects before the clock runs out. Each mistake counts, as clicking in the wrong area too many times will cost you valuable seconds 5 seconds deducted for each incorrect click! So, sharpen your focus and get ready for an exhilarating hunt for numbers that will keep you on your toes.

If you're ready for a challenge, jump into Among Us Hidden Numbers and have a blast while honing your skills. This game promises hours of fun and excitement as you immerse yourself in a world where sharp eyes are your greatest asset. Are you able to find all the hidden numbers in record time? Play now and find out!

How to play free Among Us Hidden Numbers game online

To play the game, simply start by selecting a level. Within each level, you will see an image with ten hidden numbers to find. Click on the numbers as you locate them, but be cautious incorrect clicks will cost you time. Complete all levels before the timer runs out!
