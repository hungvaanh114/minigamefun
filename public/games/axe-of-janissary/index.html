Axe of Janissary

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a whimsical world with the Axe of Janissary, where two adorable Janissary characters engage in an epic axe-throwing duel! Set against a vibrant backdrop, each character stands on platforms that oscillate left and right, adding to the excitement and unpredictability of the game. Your aim is to hit your opponent with the axe while dodging the challenging obstacles that come your way.

As you play, you ll encounter moving bricks that rise and fall in the center of the arena. These bricks can obstruct your axe, creating a strategic layer to the gameplay. Timing and precision become essential as you throw your axe, ensuring that you aim accurately while navigating around these barriers. The competition heats up as both characters try to outmaneuver each other, making every throw critical in this fast-paced clash.

The goal is simple yet thrilling: be the first player to reach a score of five. Each successful hit on your opponent adds to your score, bringing you one step closer to victory. Will you master the art of tossing axes and emerge as the champion of the Janissaries? Discover the fun and challenges in this delightful game that blends strategy and skill beautifully.

How to play free Axe of Janissary game online

To play the Axe of Janissary game, use the left and right keys to move your character. Press the space bar to throw your axe at your opponent. Be mindful of the moving bricks as they can block your axe! Score five points before your opponent to win.
