Zombies Crusher

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Zombies Crusher, a catastrophic war has erupted, and it's up to you to protect humanity from the relentless undead! As zombies make their way toward their destination, your objective is to tap and eliminate them before they reach the bottom. With an endless wave of these brain-hungry fiends, you'll need to be quick and precise in your actions.

However, it's not so simple. Amidst the chaos, beware of the jumans these impostors can sabotage your mission. Every time a zombie manages to escape your clutches and reach the edge of the screen, you lose one precious life. With only three lives at your disposal, each decision becomes crucial. Can you keep the horde at bay and ensure the survival of the world?

As the levels progress, the zombies will grow more numerous and relentless, challenging your reflexes and accuracy. The exhilarating gameplay along with a variety of backgrounds and zombie types ensures that you ll experience an action-packed adventure every time you play. Are you ready to become the ultimate defender against the zombie apocalypse? It's time to squash those zombies and save the day!

How to play free Zombies Crusher game online

To play Zombies Crusher, simply tap on the zombies making their way down the screen to eliminate them. Be cautious not to tap on any jumans, as this will impact your score and lives. Aim to clear as many zombies as possible before they reach the bottom, utilizing your three lives wisely!
