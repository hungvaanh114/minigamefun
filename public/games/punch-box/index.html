Punch Box

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Punch Box, the exhilarating boxing game that puts your reflexes and skills to the ultimate test! In a world where money is scarce, you find yourself unable to afford traditional training in gyms. Instead, you ve taken the unconventional route of honing your boxing skills by smashing boxes!

In this fast-paced game, your objective is simple: tap on the sides of the screen to unleash a series of powerful punches, destroying the boxes that come your way. But be warned! Not all boxes are created equal; some contain hidden dangers, with sharp wood that can lead to an early knock-out if you re not careful. As you punch through boxes, keep an eye on the clock time is of the essence! The only way to extend your limited time is by accumulating more punches and scoring points.

Each successful strike not only increases your score but also adds precious seconds to your countdown. The more you punch, the higher your score climbs, and the longer you get to continue the thrilling challenge. Train your eyes and reflexes, and aim for the highest score possible in this addictive fighter s game!

How to play free Punch Box game online

To play Punch Box, simply tap on the left and right sides of your screen to throw punches at approaching boxes. Keep a lookout for the dangerous sharp wood, and make sure to punch quickly to maximize your score and extend your time limit. Get ready to unleash your inner boxer!
