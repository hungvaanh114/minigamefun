World of Alice Animal Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

World of Alice - Animal Numbers is an engaging educational game crafted specifically for children, designed to make learning numbers an enjoyable experience. With the help of Alice, players embark on a whimsical adventure where they will uncover numbers cleverly disguised as various animal shapes. This unique blend of learning and play helps children not only recognize numbers but also understand their forms in a fun and interactive manner. The game is developed by Nau.Kids, ensuring a seamless learning experience for young minds.

As players navigate through the enchanting world of Alice, they will encounter an array of colorful creatures, each harboring a hidden number within their form. The exploration takes children through vibrant landscapes filled with delightful animations and engaging sound effects that keep their attention and maintain their enthusiasm for learning. By seeing and interacting with numbers in the context of animals, children are more likely to remember and understand them. This method of teaching also fosters an appreciation for nature and creativity while bolstering foundational numerical skills.

World of Alice - Animal Numbers promotes cognitive development and visual recognition by challenging children to identify numbers through play. The intuitive gameplay encourages curiosity and experimentation, allowing kids to engage at their own pace. With Alice as a charming guide, learning numbers has never been more captivating or enjoyable.

How to play free World of Alice - Animal Numbers game online

Playing World of Alice - Animal Numbers is simple and fun. Begin by exploring different scenes where animals are present. Tap on the animals to reveal hidden numbers. Engage with interactive elements by tracing the shapes of the numbers with your finger, helping reinforce both recognition and understanding. Enjoy the captivating journey of learning!


Instructions

Click on the number that corresponds to the image of the animal corresponding to its shape
