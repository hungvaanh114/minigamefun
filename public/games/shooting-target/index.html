Shooting Target

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Shooting Target, where precision and skill are put to the test! In this action-packed game, you have access to an impressive arsenal of four distinct weapons: a colt, a pistol, an assault rifle, and a machine gun. Each weapon provides a unique shooting experience, allowing players to tailor their gameplay according to their preferences. You can find the visual representation of these weapons in the lower left corner of the screen, making it easy to select your favorite right from the start.

The objective of the game is straightforward yet challenging. As you progress, you will be presented with various targets to aim at, and your performance is tracked closely. The scoring system, located in the upper left corner, records both your successful hits and any missed shots. Keep a close eye on these counts, as reaching a specific limit on your misses will lead to the end of your gaming session. This adds an exciting level of tension and encourages players to improve their accuracy with every shot.

Challenge yourself to achieve the highest possible score while mastering each weapon type in your collection. Whether you prefer the quick shots of the pistol or the rapid-fire power of the machine gun, Shooting Target promises an engaging experience filled with fast-paced action and fun.

How to play free Shooting Target game online

To play the Shooting Target game, start by selecting your weapon from the options available at the bottom left corner of the screen. Use your mouse to aim at the targets and click to shoot. Remember to keep track of your scoring and misses displayed in the upper left corner, and strive for accuracy to avoid reaching the miss limit!
