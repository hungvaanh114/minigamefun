Four Colors Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Four Colors is an engaging and challenging puzzle game that tests your reflexes and color-matching skills. At the heart of the game lies a large square, vibrant and full of potential. Around it, smaller colored squares approach from all directions, creating an exciting dynamic that requires quick thinking and strategic decision-making.

Your objective is simple yet captivating: match the color of the big square with that of the approaching smaller squares to score points. With each successful match, you gain points and continue your journey through this colorful challenge. However, you must stay alert; if a smaller square collides with the big one when their colors do not match, the game will come to an abrupt end, adding an extra layer of tension to your gameplay.

To control the game, players can effortlessly click on the screen to change the color of the large square. There are four distinct colors available, offering a variety of combinations to match with the incoming smaller squares. As the game progresses, the speed and frequency of the approaching squares increase, making it a test of both speed and accuracy. Each round offers a fresh challenge, keeping the gameplay exciting and competitive.

How to play free Four Colors Game online

To play the Four Colors game, simply enter the game interface. Watch the small colored squares approach the big square, and click on the screen to cycle through the four available colors. Keep an eye on the colors of the small squares and change the big square s color accordingly. Match them to gain points while avoiding mismatches that end your game!
