Open Factory

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Open Factory, a captivating simulation business game known as Factory King. In this engaging experience, you will have the opportunity to grow your own shoe store from the ground up. Immerse yourself in the dynamic gameplay as you slide your screen to control your character's movements, navigating through a bustling retail environment. Your primary goal is to stock the store with stylish shoes, placing them strategically on racks for eager customers to purchase.

As customers flock to your store, your ability to sell shoes becomes crucial. With each sale, you earn gold coins, the lifeblood of your business. These coins can be reinvested to upgrade various functions within your store, enhancing your capabilities and strengthening your overall operation. The more money you make, the more opportunities open up for you to expand and refine your shoe store. Whether it's improving your inventory, increasing the store's size, or boosting marketing efforts, every decision you make can drive your success in the game.

With its intuitive controls and rewarding progression system, Open Factory combines fun gameplay with strategic planning, making it a must-play for simulation and business enthusiasts alike. Get ready to experience what it takes to become the king of your very own shoe factory!

How to play free Open Factory game online

To start playing Open Factory online, simply create or log into your account. Use the slide control to move your character around the store. Stock up on shoes by placing them on the racks and keep an eye on incoming customers to sell effectively. Collect gold coins to upgrade your store and enhance your selling strategies!
