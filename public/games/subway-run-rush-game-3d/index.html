How to play free Subway Run Rush Game 3D game online

To play Subway Run Rush Game 3D, use the touchscreen or keyboard to move your character. Swipe up to jump over obstacles, swipe down to slide under barriers, and swipe left or right to dodge trains and buses. Collect coins as you run to boost your score and power-ups!
