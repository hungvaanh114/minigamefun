Lost Yeti

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Lost Yeti is an engaging 2D puzzle game brought to you by Neutronized, where adventure awaits as you assist a charming yeti on his quest to return home. Players will find themselves sliding and maneuvering blocks of ice in order to create a clear pathway. Dive into the nostalgic retro puzzles located in the picturesque Snowy Hills, where you ll experience the thrill of shifting frozen rows and columns to help the yeti navigate his journey.

As you progress, you'll unlock new and vibrant worlds filled with exciting challenges and obstacles. The game encourages you to not only focus on finding the fastest route home for the yeti but also to gather delicious snacks scattered throughout each level. With 60 uniquely designed levels spread across three enchanting worlds, players will encounter a variety of captivating puzzles and intriguing enemies that will keep them on their toes.

The blend of puzzle-solving mechanics and charming graphics makes Lost Yeti an irresistible choice for fans of the genre. Each level poses a new challenge, requiring strategic thinking and quick reflexes as you guide the yeti through increasingly complex scenarios. With the perfect balance of difficulty and fun, Lost Yeti promises hours of brain-teasing entertainment. Are you prepared to embark on this exhilarating journey and aid our adorable yeti in reaching home?

How to play free Lost Yeti game online

To play Lost Yeti online, simply use your mouse or keyboard to slide the ice blocks in the puzzle grid. Move columns or rows to create a clear pathway for the yeti while collecting snacks along the way. Aim for the quickest route to help him return home! Enjoy the challenge!


Instructions

Clear the road by dragging ice blocks around using your left mouse button or finger
