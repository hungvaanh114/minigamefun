Endless Siege Online is a thrilling and immersive tower defense game that promises endless excitement with its daily updated maps and stunning graphics. Available on iyigames.com, players are invited to strategically place and upgrade their defense towers to fend off relentless waves of enemies. The objective is simple: defend against hordes of orcs and monstrous foes by cleverly positioning your defensive structures like Cannons, Ballistae, Torches, and Time Warp Cannons.

The gameplay involves identifying the best spots to place your turrets, particularly near the entrance where enemies are most likely to breach your defenses. As you progress, you ll encounter various enemy types and will need to utilize a combination of tactics and tower upgrades to succeed. Each tower has unique strengths, allowing for subtle strategies that can change the course of the game with diligent planning and execution.

To begin your siege, place your turrets wisely, ensuring they cover the most ground against incoming attacks. After preparing your defenses, simply hit the next wave button to unleash the first onslaught of enemies. The game keeps you engaged as you refine your strategy and upgrade your towers to create an impenetrable fortress capable of withstanding endless assaults!

How to play free Endless Siege Online game online

To play Endless Siege Online, start by placing your defense towers in strategic locations on the battlefield. Focus on areas where enemies will approach. Once your defenses are set, click the next wave button to initiate the battle. Use the resources earned from defeating enemies to upgrade your towers and strengthen your defenses.
