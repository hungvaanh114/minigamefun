Zodiac Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the cosmic realm of Zodiac Runner, where astrological adventure meets thrilling arcade action! This vertical-running game invites players to explore a vibrant 3D world brimming with zodiac-themed items. Embark on your journey choosing from two distinct zodiac signs and prepare to navigate through exhilarating levels designed around the twelve celestial symbols of the zodiac.

As you dive into the game, your goal is to collect various zodiac items scattered across the platforms. Each zodiac sign corresponds to unique assets that help you upgrade your character's suit throughout the game. Progressing through the levels requires skill and agility, so stay focused and aim to gather as many items as possible. The better you perform, the more dynamic your transformation to the next zodiac suit becomes!

In Zodiac Runner, you can express your identity by picking your favorite zodiac sign and letting it guide you through a mesmerizing journey filled with challenges and rewards. Keep your cool, as the game's captivating visuals and fast-paced action will have you hooked on mastering all the zodiac signs. Will you be able to conquer the challenges and climb to the heights of zodiac mastery? Jump in and find out!

How to play free Zodiac Runner game online

To start playing, simply select one of the two zodiac signs available. Navigate through the platforms using arrow keys or swipe controls, collecting corresponding zodiac items to upgrade your suit. The goal is to stay on your feet and keep gathering items to reach the end of each level. Enjoy your cosmic run!
