City Taxi

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the bustling metropolis in City Taxi, an immersive 3D game that allows you to experience the life of a taxi driver navigating through a vibrant city. Your mission is simple yet exhilarating: pick up passengers from various locations and drop them off at their desired destinations as indicated on the map.

The game features stunning graphics that truly bring the city to life, showcasing intricate details that make your driving adventures more engaging. As you progress, you'll encounter multiple levels, each with its own unique challenges and obstacles. From navigating busy intersections to avoiding roadblocks and adhering to traffic rules, every ride presents a thrilling opportunity to hone your driving skills.

Whether you're racing against the clock to deliver passengers quickly or cruising at a leisurely pace to enjoy the sights, City Taxi offers endless entertainment. The more efficiently you drive, the higher your scores will be, making it a competitive yet enjoyable experience for all players. Get ready to take on the role of a city taxi driver and explore every corner of the urban landscape.

How to play free City Taxi game online

To play City Taxi online, start by selecting your taxi from the available options. Once you re in the game, follow the map to locate your passengers. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver your taxi and ensure that you drop off each passenger at the correct location. Balance your speed and caution to succeed!
