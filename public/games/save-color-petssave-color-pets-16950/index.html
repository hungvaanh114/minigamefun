Embark on a delightful adventure with Save Color Pets, an exciting match-3 game featuring 16 engaging levels that will challenge your skills and keep you entertained for hours. In this vibrant world, your mission is to rescue adorable color pets trapped in the dirty sand. Each level presents a unique puzzle that requires strategic thinking and quick decision-making.

To save your furry friends, connect three or more color pets of the same type in either a row or a column. When you successfully link these pets, they will vanish, and you ll help clear the dirty sand that surrounds them. The objective is to eliminate all the dirty sand within the given time limit to ensure that your beloved color pets are freed. As you progress, the challenges increase, requiring you to think critically and plan your moves carefully to achieve success.

Every level introduces different layouts and obstacles, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting. With charming graphics and delightful animations, Save Color Pets promises a fun-filled experience for players of all ages. Gather your friends and family to enjoy this captivating game, and see who can rescue the most color pets in record time!

How to play free Save Color Pets game online

To start playing Save Color Pets, simply click on groups of three or more matching color pets to make them disappear. Aim to clear all the dirty sand within the time limit to complete each level. Utilize special power-ups that may help you combine pets effectively for bigger combos and faster rescues!
