Save Juan

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Save Juan" is an exciting and engaging casual puzzle game that challenges players to safeguard the quirky Juan cat from the menacing attacks of Chocho Charles. As you navigate through various levels, your main objective is to create defensive lines to barricade the adorable Juan from an array of threats.

In this vibrant world, players will encounter lava flows, thorny obstacles, and diverse environmental dangers that put Juan's safety at risk. Each of the 40 levels escalates in difficulty, testing your puzzle-solving skills and strategic thinking. You'll need to creatively draw lines of any shape, harnessing your artistic flair to shield Juan and its charming companions from the impending onslaught.

As you progress, you'll also face unique challenges including explosive rainbow bombs, hazardous spikes, and the relentless pursuit of Poppy and Chocho Charles. The game is not only about protecting Juan but also about mastering the art of defense against an ever-evolving array of hazards, ensuring that your skills continuously adapt and improve.

How to play free Save Juan game online

To play "Save Juan," simply click and drag your mouse to draw protective lines around Juan. Strategically shape your defenses to block enemy attacks and environmental threats. Keep an eye on incoming dangers and adjust your lines as necessary to ensure Juan's survival throughout the increasingly challenging levels.
