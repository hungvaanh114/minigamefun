Tiny Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the captivating world of Tiny Blocks, embark on a colorful journey where your goal is to collect blocks of the same color. The mechanics are simple yet addictive; all you need to do is touch or click on these vibrant blocks. As you gather larger groups of the same hue, you'll be rewarded with exhilarating power-ups that not only boost your progress through various levels but also introduce elements of excitement and strategy to your gameplay.

However, be cautious while playing! If you accidentally collapse a single block, a penalty of 200 points will be deducted from your score. This unique balance between risk and reward makes every move critical, compelling you to focus and think strategically. As you advance through the colorful blocks, each level becomes more challenging and engaging, ensuring endless entertainment for players of all ages. With its vibrant graphics and simple yet engaging mechanics, Tiny Blocks is more than just a game; it s an immersive experience filled with fun and surprises.

So gather your wits, hone your skills, and see how far you can progress in this delightful puzzle game. The combination of beautiful graphics and strategic gameplay guarantees hours of enjoyment as you aim to collect blocks efficiently and minimize penalties.

How to play free Tiny Blocks game online

To play Tiny Blocks, simply click on blocks of the same color to collect them. Aim to gather them in larger groups for exciting power-ups that will assist your progress. Be mindful not to collapse single blocks, as this will cost you valuable points. Enjoy strategizing as you advance!
