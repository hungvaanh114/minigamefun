Army The Resistance invites players into an exhilarating world where strategy meets action. Available for free in your web browser on Superkidgames.com, this engaging game places you in command of a skilled duo of stick soldiers tasked with defending their territory from a relentless onslaught of rogue stickmen. With your area under siege and opponents determined to wreak havoc, it s up to you and your team to restore peace.

Your journey begins as you arm yourself with an arsenal of weaponry designed to counter the relentless enemy. Strategically choose your equipment, which ranges from defensive turrets and sandbags to powerful options like shotguns, sniper rifles, and rocket launchers. Each weapon comes with unique abilities, so mastering their use is crucial for your success. Assemble your defenses wisely to prevent the enemy from infiltrating your base; otherwise, failure awaits!

With 40 distinct and challenging levels, Army The Resistance promises thrilling gameplay as you progress. Gather bonuses and tackle unexpected surprises along the way to enhance your gaming experience. Players can invite friends to compete, aiming to achieve high scores and carve out a lasting presence on the leaderboard. Power upgrades for your weapons are essential for building an impregnable fortress, ensuring that you are the ultimate defender!

How to play free Army The Resistance game online

To play Army The Resistance, use your mouse to aim and shoot at incoming enemies. Position your defenses using the available weapons strategically and manage your resources wisely. Upgrade your armaments between levels to enhance your effectiveness. Keep your guard up, as the waves of enemies become increasingly challenging as you progress!
