Locoman 2 is an engaging 2D platformer that challenges players to navigate through a series of vibrant levels filled with obstacles and enemies. However, reaching the exit won t be a walk in the park players must remain alert as they encounter various hazards like flying creatures, spikes, and other adversaries that can thwart their progress.
