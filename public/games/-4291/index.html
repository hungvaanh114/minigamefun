Hospital Stories: Doctor Basketball is a hilarious simulation game that puts you in charge of the infirmary for a quirky basketball team. Ever dreamed of being a wacky doctor? Here s your chance! Dive into a series of outrageous mini-games where you ll tackle the wild medical dilemmas of your eccentric players and their relatives.

Prepare for a laugh as you encounter bizarre medical situations, such as injecting ketchup into patients, extracting axes from unexpected places, and using candles as makeshift medical tools. You'll also find yourself performing strange antics like popping zits and hitting scars with sausages. The medical procedures are as peculiar as the players themselves, making for an uproarious gaming experience that pushes the boundaries of the Hippocratic Oath!

Will you have what it takes to complete all the challenging levels while dealing with the most ridiculous and over-the-top health issues? Get ready to embrace the chaos and humor that come with being a crazy doctor in this light-hearted simulation. The hilarity unfolds with every challenge, and you'll find yourself laughing out loud as you save the day with your unconventional medical skills!

How to play free HOSPITAL STORIES: DOCTOR BASKETBALL game online

To play Hospital Stories: Doctor Basketball, use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with various tools and perform the bizarre medical tasks required. Follow on-screen instructions and complete each mini-game to treat your quirky patients. Each level offers a new challenge, so be creative and ready for some fun!
