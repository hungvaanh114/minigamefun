Subway Surfers Zurich

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Subway Surfers World Tour: Zurich the latest addition to the immensely popular mobile game. Launched in 2020, this marks the one hundred twenty-first update and the one hundred fifteenth episode in the renowned World Tour series. Players are invited to explore the stunning Swiss city of Zurich while dodging trains and collecting coins.

This edition reintroduces the beloved character Hugo, now available as a Special Prize in the thrilling Season Hunt feature. Players can dress Hugo in an exciting new look - the Pirate Outfit, which can be unlocked for 30 keys. This fresh outfit not only enhances Hugo's character design but also adds an extra layer of excitement to your gaming experience. Additionally, the iconic character Clockwork makes a comeback as another coveted prize in the Season Hunt, bringing nostalgia and charm to veteran players.

Subway Surfers Zurich also introduces players to the beautiful landscapes and vibrant urban scenes of Zurich. Experience the unique blend of cultural landmarks alongside the game s fast-paced action as you run through the streets, evade obstacles, and ride the hoverboard. With each level, players can enhance their skills while striving to achieve high scores and complete missions.

How to play free Subway Surfers Zurich game online

To play Subway Surfers Zurich online, simply use the arrow keys or swipe your finger on the screen to navigate your character through the subway tracks. Collect coins and power-ups while avoiding oncoming trains and obstacles. Remember to perform tricks for bonus points and aim for the highest score possible!
