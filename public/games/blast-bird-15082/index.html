Instructions:

In the whimsical world of Blast Bird, a charming little green bird finds itself trapped within a shadowy realm filled with perils. Your role is pivotal as you guide this endearing avian to safety! The objective is simple yet engaging: land the bird in a designated safe zone with as few attempts as possible.

As you embark on this exciting adventure, you will encounter a variety of levels, each offering its own set of challenges. Your strategy revolves around utilizing cleverly positioned explosives to direct the bird in the desired direction. However, caution is imperative; treacherous spikes and other hazardous obstacles lurk along the way, ready to thwart your mission. Precision and timing are crucial as you maneuver through the intricate terrain of each level.

Along the journey, your skills will be put to the test as you strive to achieve the highest score. Can you manage to navigate the perilous landscapes and successfully guide the bird to safety? Moreover, embrace the challenge of collecting three stars for each level. This requires not only skillful play but also a strategic approach to minimize your attempts while optimizing your route to victory.

How to play free Blast Bird game online

To play Blast Bird, simply use your mouse or finger to activate the explosives at the right moment. Aim carefully to steer the bird towards the safe area while avoiding obstacles. Watch out for spikes that can end your game prematurely, and strive for minimal attempts to earn three stars on each level!
