Button Fever

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Button Fever is an engaging idle game designed to capture your attention during your free moments. This delightful game invites players to click a button to accumulate virtual currency, which can then be utilized to unlock an assortment of new buttons. Each button has its own unique design and functionality, making the gameplay experience both enjoyable and rewarding.

One of the key mechanics in Button Fever is merging. When you collect a certain number of the same buttons, you can combine them to create larger and more powerful buttons. These upgraded buttons generate money at a faster rate, offering a satisfying progression as you advance through the game. As you unlock new buttons and merge them, you'll find yourself mesmerized by the quickening pace at which your wealth grows. The simplicity of clicking combined with the thrill of merging offers a perfect balance that will keep you coming back for more.

Whether you're waiting for an appointment or just looking to unwind, Button Fever is a fantastic choice. Its intuitive design makes it accessible for players of all ages, and its charming graphics add to the overall enjoyment. Dive into the world of Button Fever today and see how addictive clicking and merging can be!

How to play free Button Fever game online

To play Button Fever, start by simply clicking on the main button to earn currency. Use the money you earn to unlock new buttons and watch your income grow. When you have enough of the same button type, merge them to form larger buttons that will increase your earnings significantly. Enjoy the process!
