Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of "Turn Based Ship War," where strategic planning and timing are the keys to victory! In this captivating battle of wits, players engage in an intense naval duel, commanding their ships as they take turns firing upon each other. To launch your attack, simply press your mouse or touch the screen to adjust the angle of your shot accurately. Once you've locked in the perfect trajectory, release to unleash your cannon fire onto the enemy vessel.

Throughout the game, each player will relentlessly bombard the other with powerful strikes. However, don't let your guard down! After firing, you'll need to brace yourself for incoming attacks. It's vital to stay alert and prepare for the enemy's turn while you await your next opportunity to strike. The ultimate goal? Outlast your opponent and emerge victorious by strategically planning your shots and positioning your ship wisely.

The tension rises as each turn unfolds, making every decision critical to your survival. Will you choose to go aggressive with your attacks, or play defensively and wait for the perfect moment to retaliate? The choice is yours. Only the player who can withstand enemy assaults and maintain a strategic advantage will claim the title of champion in the "Turn Based Ship War." Are you ready to set sail and face your foes?

How to play free Turn Based Ship War game online

To play Turn Based Ship War, start by adjusting your shot angle using your mouse or touch controls. After setting your aim, release to fire at the opposing ship. You will alternate turns, so prepare for incoming attacks while waiting your turn. Stay sharp and strategize to outlast your enemy!
