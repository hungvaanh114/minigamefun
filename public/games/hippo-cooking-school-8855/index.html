Hippo Cooking School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Hippo Cooking School, a delightful and charming cooking game that's perfect for dessert lovers! If you have a sweet tooth and enjoy creating sugary treats, you re in for a real treat! In this vibrant and colorful game, players can choose from five mouthwatering dessert recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds.

With the guidance of Hippo and Hippo's granny, you will embark on an exciting culinary adventure where you can learn the art of dessert-making. Each level is filled with fun instructions and engaging tasks that make cooking feel like a breeze. Whether you're mixing, baking, or decorating, the intuitive steps will help you whip up scrumptious sweets in no time!

If you're ever feeling bored or just want to pass the time with something enjoyable, Hippo Cooking School is the perfect game for you to dive into. With its endearing characters and easy-to-follow instructions, you'll find yourself not only entertained but also improving your culinary skills. Come join Hippo and let your creativity flourish while making delectable desserts!

How to play free Hippo Cooking School game online

To play Hippo Cooking School, start by selecting your favorite dessert from the menu. Follow the on-screen instructions step by step to gather ingredients and execute the cooking process. Mix, bake, and decorate your treats. Don't forget to check for hints if you get stuck enjoy creating delicious desserts!
