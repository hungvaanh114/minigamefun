City Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to City Drive, the ultimate urban racing experience that lets you explore vibrant cityscapes at your own pace! Prepare to immerse yourself in three distinctive game modes that cater to different player preferences, whether you enjoy high-speed racing, casual cruising, or thrilling challenges. Each mode offers unique gameplay mechanics that will keep you engaged and on your toes.

One of the standout features of City Drive is the ability to customize multiple cars to match your style and performance needs. Tweak everything from paint jobs and decals to engine upgrades and handling adjustments. This level of customization ensures that every player can create a vehicle that's not just visually appealing but also optimized for their driving style. With such a wide variety of options, you can truly make your ride unique.

City Drive encourages players to partake in exhilarating competitions and friendly races against their friends or AI opponents. Navigate through bustling streets, conquer challenging turns, and collect rewards as you aim for the top of the leaderboard. No matter your skill level, there's always a challenge waiting for you in this dynamic city environment.

How to play free City Drive game online

To start playing City Drive, simply select your desired game mode from the main menu. Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to steer your car and the spacebar for acceleration. Keep an eye on your surroundings and use the mini-map to guide your path. Customize your vehicle in the garage and hit the streets to begin your adventure!

Controls WASD to control car Press Space for handbrake Press Escape Tab to toggle pause Press C to change camera
