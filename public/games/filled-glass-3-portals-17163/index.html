Filled Glass 3: Portals

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience a delightful challenge in Filled Glass 3: Portals, where your objective is to fill a glass vessel with vibrant, colorful balls. Each container is designed to bring joy when it's filled to the required level, marked by a blue dotted line. When a glass is empty, it feels neglected and out of place, so your task is to rectify that by strategically using mystical portals to transport these colorful spheres into the glass.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter various puzzles that require keen observation and creative thinking. The portals serve as crucial pathways, allowing you to move balls from one location to another, but the rules dictate the importance of filling the glass precisely to the required level. Missing this target will impede your progress and create a less satisfying experience for your glass!

Filled Glass 3: Portals puts your problem-solving skills to the test as you must navigate through increasingly complex challenges. Each level offers a unique setup, enticing you to devise clever strategies to meet the filling requirements. The blend of colorful graphics, engaging gameplay, and satisfying mechanics will keep you entertained while you aim to complete each stage with precision.

How to play free Filled Glass 3: Portals game online

To play Filled Glass 3: Portals, simply use your mouse or finger to interact with the colorful balls. Drag and drop them into the portals to transport them to the glass. Be mindful of the blue dotted line, ensuring that you fill the glass to the marked level for a successful completion of each puzzle!
