Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Ski Safari! Ski Safari is an exhilarating adventure where animals, avalanches, and action collide to offer a unique casual gaming experience. Our drowsy protagonist, Sven, must outrun a relentless avalanche that threatens the local mountain slopes. As he races against time, Sven can enlist the help of various animals to aid his icy escape. Each mountain creature boasts distinct attributes that can contribute to a swifter getaway.

The penguins allow Sven to glide further, while the sturdy yetis provide resilience against the rushing snow. Eagles help him soar to great heights, and each animal offers a beneficial advantage during his thrilling flight. Occasionally, Sven may come across speedy snowmobiles along the slope, which can leave several animals in the dust, ensuring he maintains a high velocity. Staying ahead of the avalanche not only enhances the thrill of the game but also gives players a strategic edge.

By completing objectives throughout the game, Sven can progress to higher levels and boost his score multiplier. Performing stunts on the animals or executing backflips can further add to his points, and with an elevated score multiplier, Sven will surely climb the ranks on the leaderboard, showcasing his skills!

How to play free SKI SAFARI - Adventure game online

To play Ski Safari, use your keyboard s arrow keys to navigate Sven down the slopes. Tap the spacebar to hop on and ride the animals, while performing tricks to gain extra points. Keep an eye out for snowmobiles and challenge yourself to stay ahead of the avalanche for the ultimate adventure!
