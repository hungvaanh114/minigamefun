Radius Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Radius Ball is an engaging shooter puzzle game that challenges players' precision and timing. The gameplay revolves around a circular arrangement of eight distinct dots, where the primary objective is to hit a red ring with a consistently rotating red ball. At the start of the game, the red ball circles one of these dots, adding a layer of excitement from the outset.

As the game progresses, a red ring will appear around another dot, prompting players to focus their aim. The real challenge lies in hitting the ring at the right moment; successful shots will cause the red ring to relocate, presenting new opportunities and obstacles. However, be wary! If you fail to hit the ring, the game ends, escalating the competition. With the red ball continuously moving around the white dots, you'll need to strategize and shoot accurately to keep up.

This dynamic gameplay, combining elements of speed and strategic thinking, makes Radius Ball an addictive experience. Whether you re playing to pass the time or hone your skills, every shot counts. The thrill of successfully hitting the ring only to watch it move presents an enticing challenge that keeps players engaged while testing their reflexes and aiming abilities.

How to play free Radius Ball game online

To play Radius Ball, simply use your mouse or touch screen to aim the red ball. Press or click at the right moment to shoot the ball towards the red ring. Timing is key, as the ball rotates around the dots continuously. Aim carefully to ensure that you hit the ring before it disappears!
