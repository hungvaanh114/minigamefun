Color Chain Sort Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing the Color Chain Sort Puzzle, an engaging game that will challenge your mind and test your problem-solving abilities. If you enjoy chain puzzles or water sorting games, you're in for a treat! In this captivating puzzle experience, your objective is to sort colorful chains and arrange them on various pillars according to matching colors.

In Color Chain Sort Puzzle, the mechanics are similar to water sorting games, where you must stack and organize items based on their colors. However, there's a twist: to move a chain, the color of the chain at the top of the stack must match the color of the target chain you wish to move it to. This adds an exciting layer of strategy, as you'll need to think ahead and carefully plan your moves. With multiple levels to complete and a variety of puzzles to solve, each challenge will require you to sharpen your logic and critical thinking skills.

Prepare to dive into a world of vibrant colors and intricate puzzles. You're guaranteed hours of fun as you devise the best strategies to solve each level. With levels ranging in difficulty, whether you are a beginner or a skilled puzzle enthusiast, the Color Chain Sort Puzzle has something for everyone. Are you ready to sort your way to success and complete all the levels? Challenge yourself today!

How to play free Color Chain Sort Puzzle game online

To play the Color Chain Sort Puzzle, select a chain that you wish to move. Ensure that the top chain's color matches the target chain on the designated pillar. Click to move it, and continue sorting until all chains are organized by color. Enjoy strategizing your moves to complete each level!
