How to play free Shape Anti Magnet game online

To play Shape Anti Magnet, use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the magnets on the screen. Click or tap to activate the magnetic forces, pushing the geometric shapes away from the magnets and guiding them to their targets. Your goal is to safely direct all shapes without letting them be attracted to any obstacles.
