Escape From Castle Doom

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Escape From Castle Doom, a thrilling adventure where you assist the beloved Blue Peter presenters in their quest for freedom! Set within the ominous walls of Castle Doom, your objective is to guide them towards the exits by strategically placing trampolines and springs throughout the castle's treacherous layout.

Utilizing your mouse, you can easily drag trampolines from the bottom of the screen to desired locations, creating bouncy pathways that help the presenters navigate their escape. Mistakes are part of the fun if you don t like your initial placement, you can reposition the trampolines as needed to find the best escape route.

However, beware of the lurking ghosts! These spectral foes are a menacing threat; if they catch up to the presenters, they will put them to sleep, jeopardizing their chances of escaping Castle Doom. The quicker you help the presenters reach safety, the more bonus points you can earn, making speed a crucial factor in your strategy!

Prepare for the perils of this cursed castle, where many heroes have tried and failed to defeat its dark magic. The castle holds sword shards, the only hope of conquering its sinister owner and breaking through the enchantments that lock its doors. Are you ready to assist the presenters in navigating these dangers and avoiding capture as they seek their freedom?

How to play free Escape From Castle Doom game online

To play, simply drag trampolines and springs onto the castle grounds using your mouse. Place them strategically to help the presenters bounce past obstacles and avoid ghosts. Keep an eye on the timer for bonus points as you guide the presenters to safety!
