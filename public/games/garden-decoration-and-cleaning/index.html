Garden Decoration and Cleaning

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of "Garden Decoration and Cleaning," where your dreams of gardening become a vibrant reality! Embark on an exciting journey as you cultivate your caring skills, transforming neglected spaces into stunning gardens. In this engaging game, you ll don the hat of a dedicated gardener, nurturing every plant and landscape you encounter. Your mission isn t just about planting; it s about revitalizing the environment around you.

Grow an array of your favorite flowers, from delicate daisies to vibrant sunflowers, all while managing your very own flower shop! As you cultivate your blooms, you ll get the chance to assemble delightful bouquets, catering to the wishes of various clients. Each order you fulfill brings in money, allowing you to reinvest in your garden and upgrades for your shop. Watch as your green thumb creates a flourishing business, attracting customers and fellow gardeners alike.

As you explore different levels of the game, challenge yourself to clean up the gardens, all while maintaining a breathtaking aesthetic. The satisfaction of seeing your hard work pay off in the form of beautiful floral displays will keep you coming back for more. With every flourish of your gardening skills, you unlock new possibilities for decoration and expansion.

How to play free Garden Decoration and Cleaning game online

To start your gardening adventure, simply log in to the game and select your garden. Use your mouse to click and drag tools for cleaning and decorating. Grow flowers by planting seeds, watering them, and ensuring they flourish. When your flowers are ready, deliver bouquets to customers to earn money and upgrade your garden!
