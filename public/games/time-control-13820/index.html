Time Control

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling journey with Time Control, an engaging puzzle game that challenges your agility and strategic thinking. You will take charge of a red ball that moves vertically from one point to another, collecting points as it strikes designated spots. However, watch out! The path around the ball is fraught with rotating squares, ready to end your game in a split second.

The key to success in Time Control lies in mastering the ball's speed. You can click or tap on the screen to slow down the ball, allowing you to navigate through the obstacles more easily. Timing your movements is essential, as an errant collision with one of the squares will bring your adventure to a halt. Your goal is to score as many points as possible while skillfully maneuvering the ball to avoid disaster.

As you progress through the game, you will face increasingly complex arrangements of squares that will test your reflexes and decision-making skills. The satisfaction of weaving the ball through layers of obstacles while racking up points will keep you hooked for hours. Will you rise to the challenge and become a master of Time Control?

How to play free Time Control game online

To play the Time Control game online, simply click or tap on the screen to adjust the speed of the red ball. Use quick taps to slow down and navigate around the rotating squares while aiming to hit the scoring points. Stay focused, strategize your movements, and aim for a new high score!
