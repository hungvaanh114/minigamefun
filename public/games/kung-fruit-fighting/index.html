Kung Fruit Fighting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the exhilarating world of Kung Fruit Fighting, where your love for kung fu meets the thrilling art of chopping. In this vibrant game, you take on the role of the legendary leader of an ancient clan of blue vegetable masters, renowned for their exceptional martial arts skills. Your arch-nemesis, the fierce clan of red fruits, has issued another challenge, and this time it's a brutal competition to determine who reigns supreme in the battle of flavor and skill!

Embrace your role as the protector of your clan, and get ready to slice and dice your way through wave after wave of fruit foes. With each chop you make, you must maintain your focus and precision only the swiftest and most accurate warriors will succeed. But be warned! Your vegetables are your allies in this epic confrontation, and you must avoid harming them. Every mistake could cost you the war against the cunning fruit adversaries who seek to topple your reign!

Kung Fruit Fighting is not just about the thrill of combat; it s also a test of strategy and reflexes. Put your skills to the test, and prove that you are the ultimate kung fu champion of the culinary world. Are you ready to chop your way to glory and defend your clan from the relentless assault of the red fruits?

How to play free Kung Fruit Fighting game online

To play Kung Fruit Fighting, use your mouse or touchscreen to chop the incoming fruits while avoiding your blue vegetables. Each successful chop gains you points, but be cautious not to hit your allies! The game speeds up as you progress, so stay alert and keep your kung fu skills sharp to claim victory!


Instructions

Slide your finger in the screen to cut the red fruits and avoid the blue vegetables
