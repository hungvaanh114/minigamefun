Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the eerie world of Halloween Store Sort, where the thrill of the spooky season meets the excitement of a sorting challenge! Your goal is to match creepy toys, spooky decorations, and chilling items by dragging them across shelves filled with festive clutter. The objective is simple: create sets of three identical items to clear them and uncover hidden surprises lurking in the shelves above or adjacent to your current view.

Each level presents a new array of challenges, with increasing difficulty that will keep you on your toes. You'll have a limited time to strategize and make your moves, making it essential to think quickly and act even faster. Don t worry if you find yourself in a tight spot; the shuffle button is there to help mix things up when you're blocked, giving you a chance to find new matches!

As you progress through the game, you ll discover a series of intricately designed levels, each more challenging than the last. Your sorting skills will be put to the test, but with quick thinking and a sharp eye for matching, you can conquer every level. Your ultimate goal is to complete all levels and claim the title of the ultimate Halloween sorting champion. Are you ready to dive into this thrilling holiday adventure?

How to play free Halloween Store Sort game online

To play Halloween Store Sort, simply drag items from one shelf to another to create sets of three. Keep an eye on the timer as you match spooky decorations and toys. Use the shuffle button if you find yourself stuck, and aim to clear all levels to win!
