Point to Merge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Point to Merge is an exciting and addictive puzzle game that challenges players to strategically connect numbered blocks in order to achieve the highest score possible. The game begins with two blocks marked with the number 2, and players must use arrows to direct each block towards its matching counterpart. By merging the numbers in a specific sequence 2, 4, 8, 16, and beyond keen players can unlock even larger values. However, the game demands precision; any miscalculation or misdirected arrow can lead to an immediate restart of the puzzle!

With a total of 50 progressively difficult levels, Point to Merge seamlessly combines mental agility with precise control. Each level presents a unique challenge, pushing players to harness their problem-solving skills to achieve mastery in merging the blocks. The satisfaction of watching numbers exponentially grow as blocks align and combine is immensely rewarding. Are you ready to take on the challenge and discover if you can ultimately reach the highest number possible?

How to play free Point to Merge game online

To play Point to Merge, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to point arrows at the blocks you want to merge. Click or tap on a block and drag the arrow towards its matching block to combine them. Be careful aiming outside the game area restarts your progress!
