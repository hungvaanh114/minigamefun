Squidgame Dalgona Collect

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into the thrilling world of "Squidgame Dalgona Collect," where strategy and stealth collide! As the squid gamer, your mission is to collect delicious candy while avoiding detection from other players. However, there's a twist: you are confined to moving back and forth on a flat surface to evade the other competitors. Every move counts!

The game challenges your agility and foresight as you can tap on blocks to modify the walking environment, facilitating your candy collection efforts. Additionally, by touching the zombie's body, you can influence its movement, steering it away from your path. Remember, timing and quick thinking are your best friends on this adventure!

Be warned, though! If the candy or even the iconic squid figure slips off the screen, it's game over! The stakes are high, and every choice you make will determine your fate. Can you outsmart the competition and emerge victorious in this captivating challenge? Test your skills and strategies to see if you have what it takes to be the ultimate squid gamer!

How to play free Squidgame Dalgona Collect game online

To play "Squidgame Dalgona Collect," simply navigate the flat surface using your controls, ensuring you stay hidden from other players. Tap on the blocks to change your walking environment and help you reach the candy. Also, keep an eye on the zombies; touching them will redirect their movement, but watch out for any hazards that could make you lose!
