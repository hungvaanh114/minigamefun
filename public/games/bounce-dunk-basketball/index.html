Bounce Dunk Basketball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Love basketball? Want to master the art of bouncing and dunking? In Bounce Dunk - Basketball, you must skillfully control the balls bounce to make perfect shots and get high scores. Tap the screen to control the angle and strength of the basketball so that the ball bounces perfectly into the basket. Or destroy enemies, get coins, etc.
