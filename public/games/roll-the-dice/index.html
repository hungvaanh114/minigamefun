Roll the Dice

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Roll The Dice, an engaging isometric puzzle game where strategy meets dexterity! In this captivating adventure, players take control of a vibrant dice, aiming to position it precisely on various tiles, ensuring that the face of the die matches the specific requirements of each tile. Your challenge is to navigate through this colorful world by clicking on the tiles to roll your dice towards the designated spots.

As you immerse yourself in this enchanting experience, you'll discover that the game mechanics revolve around rolling the dice accurately to match tile patterns. Utilize your keyboard's arrow keys or directional buttons to explore the environment from different perspectives, allowing you to gain a better understanding of each level's unique layout. Although the initial release focuses on core gameplay features, there's a promising roadmap for future updates, which may introduce exciting elements like rotating platforms, conveyor belts, and portals to elevate the complexity further.

While Roll The Dice is currently limited in the number of levels due to the constraints of the Gamejam, the existing puzzles offer a delightful mix of challenge and entertainment. Dive into this world of ingenuity and see if you can master the art of dice control while solving intricate puzzles.

How to play free Roll The Dice game online

To play Roll The Dice, simply click on a tile to roll your dice towards it. Use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to shift your viewpoint and better navigate the puzzle landscape. Aim to match the top face of the die with the tile s requirements to progress through the levels!
