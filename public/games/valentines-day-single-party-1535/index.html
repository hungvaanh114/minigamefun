This Valentine's Day, Adella finds herself single and ready to celebrate with her best friends, Rebecca and Jade! Instead of wallowing in loneliness, the trio decides to throw a fabulous Valentine's Day single party. As Adella takes the lead, she coordinates the party by carefully selecting the decorations that will set the mood. The celebration is an ode to friendship, featuring fun games and bubbly drinks as they dance the night away.
