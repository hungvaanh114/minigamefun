Zombie Defence Team

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Zombie Defence Team is an adrenaline-pumping action game that immerses players in a world overrun by the undead. With striking graphics that bring the chaos of a zombie apocalypse to life, players will find themselves in four expansive levels teeming with hordes of zombies. As a member of an elite special forces unit, you are tasked with eliminating these nightmarish foes using a vast array of cutting-edge weapons.

Swinging into action, players must navigate through intricately designed battlegrounds, each filled with challenging obstacles and relentless waves of zombies. It's not just about survival; you need strategic gameplay to utilize your skills and gear to fortify your position and push through each level. From powerful assault rifles to explosive grenades, your arsenal is equipped to decimate the undead with precision and flair.

Throughout your mission, players will have the opportunity to upgrade their weapons and unlock new gear, enhancing their combat capabilities. Teamwork becomes vital, as players can coordinate with other team members to devise strategies for overcoming tough zombie bosses and navigating treacherous environments. As you delve deeper into the game, prepare for epic encounters that will keep the thrill alive and the adrenaline rushing. Are you ready to take on the Zombie Defence Team challenge and reclaim the world from the undead menace?

How to play free Zombie Defence Team game online

To start playing Zombie Defence Team online, simply load the game in your web browser. Use the WASD keys for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot. Ensure to collect ammo and health packs while advancing through each level. Work as a team and strategize to defeat zombies and bosses effectively!
