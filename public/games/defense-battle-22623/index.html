Defense Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Defense Battle is an engaging tower defense game that offers a delightful twist reminiscent of the beloved Plants vs Zombies. In this colorful and vibrant world, players are tasked with assembling an effective battalion to fend off waves of relentless foes, particularly the notorious troop of skull soldiers. With 09 intricate levels designed to challenge your strategic prowess, players must think critically and act quickly to protect their territory.

As you progress through each level, the difficulty intensifies, testing not only your skill but also your ability to adapt and devise new strategies. Be vigilant, especially when facing the formidable Stone Monster, a fierce enemy known for its destructive capabilities. If it breaches your defenses, it can obliterate your base in no time. Your main objective is clear: safeguard the left side of the battlefield, where your base stands firmly at the frontline.

The game features beautifully designed graphics that enhance the overall experience, providing a feast for the eyes while you strategize your defense. Each unit you deploy adds a unique flavor to your strategy, creating endless combinations to experiment with as you fend off the fierce army of skull soldiers. Prepare yourself for a colorful and entertaining journey that will keep you on the edge of your seat!

How to play free Defense Battle game online

To play Defense Battle, simply use the mouse to select and place your units on the battlefield. Choose strategically to counter the incoming enemies and upgrade your defenses as you progress. Keep a watchful eye on the Stone Monster and adapt your tactics accordingly to ensure your base remains secure. Good luck!
