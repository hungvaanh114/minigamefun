Among Chen Bots

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Among Chen Bots is an exciting 2D platformer set in a vibrant Sci-Fi universe. In this engaging adventure, players take on the role of a brave character tasked with collecting Blue Energy boxes scattered throughout intricate levels. However, be careful the path is fraught with danger as you must navigate past enemy bots, treacherous spikes, swinging blades, and menacing flying bots. Your ultimate goal is to reach the Exit door at the end of each level, allowing you to progress to the next challenging stage.

Spanning across 8 levels, Among Chen Bots offers a progressively difficult gameplay experience that keeps players on their toes. Each subsequent level introduces new obstacles and enemies, requiring quick reflexes and strategic thinking to overcome the challenges. The game combines elements of skill and timing as you leap over hazards and navigate the dynamic environment. With pixel-perfect controls and stunning visuals, Among Chen Bots delivers an exhilarating platforming experience.

Dive into a world where precision is crucial and every decision counts. As you immerse yourself in this thrilling adventure, you'll find yourself eager to tackle each new challenge and push your skills to the limit. Will you collect all the Blue Energy boxes and make it through to the last level?

How to play free Among Chen Bots game online

To play Among Chen Bots, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character and jump. Collect all the Blue Energy boxes while avoiding the enemy bots and hazards. Reach the Exit door to advance to the next level. Plan your moves carefully and enjoy the thrilling challenges ahead!
