Step into the thrilling world of Robbie 1 Damage Per Second, where our courageous hero, Robbie, is surrounded by a relentless horde of malevolent enemies! As foes swarm in from every direction, players must strategically guide Robbie to fend off these attackers using an array of formidable swords and enchanting magical abilities. The goal is clear: destroy the evil adversaries before they overpower you!

Upgrade Robbie's skills to enhance his damage output and overall strength, making him a formidable warrior against the incoming waves of enemies. As you progress through the game, you'll have access to over 200 unique swords, each bringing a special touch to your combat strategy. Furthermore, you can summon various pets to assist Robbie in the heat of battle, adding an extra layer of excitement and teamwork!

Explore a multitude of diverse locations, each offering its own challenges and treasures. The immersive music serves to elevate the adrenaline-pumping atmosphere, ensuring players are completely absorbed in their adventure. Compete with others by joining the global player rankings, proving your skills and showcasing your progress in this exhilarating game.

How to play free Robbie 1 Damage Per Second. game online

To play Robbie 1 Damage Per Second, start by using your mouse or keyboard controls to navigate Robbie through the battlefield. Click or tap to attack enemies and activate special abilities when available. Collect upgrades and swords as you defeat foes, and remember to utilize your pets for added support. Keep an eye on your damage per second to see how powerful Robbie can become!
