Scroll and Spot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embrace the festive spirit and indulge in the delightful challenge of Scroll and Spot, a Christmas-themed puzzle game designed to bring joy to your holiday season! Dive into two intricately crafted images that capture the essence of Christmas. Your task is to scroll through these images and find five subtle differences cleverly hidden within them.

The unique scrolling feature ensures that both images remain perfectly aligned, creating a seamless and engaging puzzle experience. With stunning graphics and a cozy holiday vibe, Scroll and Spot allows players to immerse themselves in a charming virtual world filled with festive cheer. As you navigate through the images, each discovery brings a sense of accomplishment and enhances the joy of the season. Perfect for all ages, this game not only sharpens your observation skills but also adds a sprinkle of fun to your holiday festivities.

Gather your family and friends and challenge each other to see who can spot the differences the fastest. With its user-friendly interface and captivating visuals, Scroll and Spot is an ideal game to enjoy during the holidays, whether you are relaxing at home or hosting a festive gathering. Celebrate the season by putting your skills to the test while soaking in the merry atmosphere!

How to play free Scroll and Spot game online

To play Scroll and Spot, simply click on the start button and view the two images side by side. Use your mouse or touch screen to scroll between the images while carefully observing for the five differences. Click on each difference to mark it and complete the puzzle!


Instructions

Drag the screen to scroll tap to spot the differences and find all 5 differences to advance to the next level
