How to play free Tic Tac Toe Puzzle game online

To play Tic Tac Toe Puzzle online, simply choose your mark (X or O) and select an empty square on the grid to make your move. The objective is to connect three of your marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Stay alert and outsmart your opponent while trying to block their moves!
