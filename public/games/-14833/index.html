Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get into the festive spirit with Xmas Mahjong Deluxe, a captivating tile-matching game that brings the joys of Christmas right to your screen. In this delightful version of the classic Mahjong, your objective is simple: match pairs of identical xmas tiles to clear them from the board. However, only free tiles can be used in your matches. A tile is considered 'free' when it s not covered by any other tiles and has at least one open side, either left or right.

With a total of 24 levels to conquer, each representing a different Christmas item, the game promises hours of engaging gameplay. As you progress through the levels, the challenge ramps up, requiring sharper concentration and strategy to succeed. Will you be able to collect all three stars for each level, showcasing your Mahjong mastery? The festive graphics and cheerful music will only add to your enjoyment as you play. It's the perfect game to unwind with during the holiday season!

How to play free Xmas Mahjong Deluxe game online

To start playing Xmas Mahjong Deluxe, simply select a free tile on the board and then click on another matching tile. Remember, both tiles must be free otherwise, you won't be able to make the match. Keep making pairs to clear the board, aiming for three stars in every level to showcase your skills!
