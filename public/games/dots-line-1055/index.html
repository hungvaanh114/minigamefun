Dots Line

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into the captivating world of Dots Line, a challenging puzzle game that tests your brainpower and creativity in the beloved Connect the Dots genre. This game is not just about connecting points; it's a delightful exercise in intelligence and ingenuity, requiring you to think strategically as you navigate your way through the puzzles.

The objective is simple yet engaging: connect all the dots with continuous lines. However, there s a twist you must complete the game without lifting your fingers from the screen and cannot retrace any line you have already drawn. This unique challenge pushes you to carefully plan each move, making it a thrilling experience for puzzle lovers of all ages.

How to play free Dots Line game online

To play Dots Line, simply touch a dot and drag your finger to connect it to another dot. Keep connecting the remaining dots without lifting your finger until all points are linked. Remember, you cannot draw over the same line twice so strategize your moves wisely to complete each level!
