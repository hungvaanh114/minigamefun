Bubble Hunt

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bubble Hunt is an engaging and captivating classical bubble shooter game that will keep you entertained for hours. With a total of 48 unique challenges, players will find themselves immersed in a colorful world of bubbles. The objective is straightforward yet thrilling: aim and shoot your bubble to connect it with other bubbles of the same color. Successfully connecting bubbles allows you to clear them from the board, inching you closer to your ultimate goal of collecting the coveted Golden Heart within the allotted time.

The game introduces an element of urgency, as you must prevent the bubbles from reaching the bottom border of the screen. Each level presents various strategic opportunities and obstacles, making it essential to think quickly and carefully as you plan your shots. Additionally, players can discover a variety of powerful power-ups scattered throughout the game, which can be activated by shooting at them. These power-ups offer special abilities that can help clear bubbles more efficiently, providing an exciting twist to your gameplay experience.

Bubble Hunt combines classic gameplay with modern graphics and challenges, making it suitable for players of all ages. Whether you re a seasoned bubble shooter enthusiast or a newcomer looking for a fun pastime, Bubble Hunt promises to deliver endless enjoyment and satisfaction with every bubble you shoot.

How to play free Bubble Hunt game online

To play Bubble Hunt, use your mouse or finger to aim at the bubbles on the board. Tap or click to release your bubble and try to match it with two or more bubbles of the same color. Keep an eye on the timer and collect the Golden Heart before time runs out while avoiding letting the bubbles reach the bottom of the screen!
