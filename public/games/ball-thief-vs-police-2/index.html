Ball Thief vs Police 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ball Thief vs Police 2 is an exciting 2D platformer that puts you in the shoes of a cunning thief. Your mission is to gather as many bags of money as possible while navigating through various obstacles. Throughout the game, you will encounter relentless police officers, menacing drones, and treacherous spikes that will test your agility and reflexes. As the sequel to the original Ball Thief vs Police, this installment enhances the gameplay with a total of 8 challenging levels that progressively increase in difficulty, ensuring a thrilling experience with each stage you complete.

Each level is meticulously designed to challenge your strategic thinking and quick decision-making skills. You must maneuver through the intricate environments, avoiding detection and traps set to hinder your progress. Utilize your skill set to outsmart the police and efficiently collect the bags of money before time runs out. Mastering the mechanics of the game will lead you to become a legendary thief in the Ball Thief universe.

How to play free Ball Thief vs Police 2 game online

To play Ball Thief vs Police 2, use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move your character left and right, and jump to avoid obstacles. Your goal is to collect all the money bags in each level while evading the police and other hazards. Be quick and strategic!
