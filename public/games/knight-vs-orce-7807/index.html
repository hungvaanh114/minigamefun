How to play free Knight Vs Orce game online

To play Knight Vs Orc, simply drag your knight character to aim and drop him onto the orcs to attack. Calculate your moves wisely to maximize your kills, as doing so will significantly boost your score. Keep an eye on your surroundings for strategic advantage!
