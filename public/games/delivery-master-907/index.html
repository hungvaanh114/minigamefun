Instructions:

Delivery Master is an exciting new game that combines the thrill of transport and delivery with the challenges of navigating busy roads. Step into the shoes of a driver and delivery person as you pick up and drop off passengers while also delivering pizzas and parcels to various locations. Your goal is to earn money while having fun in this engaging gameplay experience.

As you traverse the vibrant streets on your motorbike, you'll need to steer carefully to avoid collisions with oncoming traffic. The game challenges your reflexes and decision-making skills, ensuring that each journey is both rewarding and exhilarating. Completing deliveries not only earns you cash but also levels up your character, allowing you to unlock more features and enhance your gaming experience.

To play Delivery Master, simply tap and hold to ride your bike, making it easy to pick up passengers and drop them at their desired locations. The intuitive controls and captivating visuals will keep you engaged as you work your way to becoming the master of shipping orders. Are you ready to take on the road and build your delivery empire?

How to play free Delivery Master game online

To start playing Delivery Master, use your mouse or touchscreen to tap and hold to accelerate your bike. Navigate through the traffic carefully, pick up passengers, and drop them off at the marked destinations. Complete various levels to enhance your skills and earn more money for additional upgrades and features!
