Kitty Animal Hair Salon - Fashion Hair Stylist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Amy's Animal Hair Salon, where the world of pet fashion comes alive! This revolutionary cat beauty salon game invites both girls and boys to unleash their creativity as they step into the role of a pet stylist. Set in a vibrant and whimsical environment, the game features Amy, a stylish leopard who has dedicated her skills to pampering fluffy feline clients.

In this delightful salon, kids can immerse themselves in the joy of grooming and fashion with the cutest cats imaginable. You'll meet a variety of charming cat clients, each with its unique personality and style preferences. Your mission is to help these adorable cats achieve their dream hair and makeovers. Engage in a wide array of fun grooming activities, such as washing, brushing, curling, straightening, and even coloring their stunning fur.

But the fun doesn t stop there! Once the hair styling is complete, dive into the realm of fashion by dressing up the kitties in an assortment of colorful and trendy outfits. The game allows players to experiment with countless styles, enabling them to create fabulous looks that reflect the individuality of each feline friend.

Overall, Amy's Animal Hair Salon offers a unique opportunity for kids to explore their inner stylist while providing hours of entertainment and creative expression. So get ready to groom, style, and dress some of the most fashionable cats in town!

How to play free Kitty Animal Hair Salon - Fashion Hair Stylist game online

To play the game, start by selecting a cat client, then choose from a variety of grooming tools to wash and style its hair. Use the styling options to curl, straighten, or color the fur as desired. Finally, browse a selection of trendy outfits and accessories to complete the cat s fabulous look before showing off the stunning results!
