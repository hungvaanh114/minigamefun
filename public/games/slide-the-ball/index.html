Slide the Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Slide The Ball, an engaging arcade game that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. In this thrilling adventure, you control a ball that glides along a horizontal track. However, beware of hazards lurking at both ends of the path: sharp spikes ready to end your journey in an instant. Your primary objective is to navigate the ball safely while dodging various shapes that attempt to collide with it.

Throughout the game, you will encounter blue balls which serve as power-ups. These blue balls are not only valuable for scoring but also provide unique benefits. Collecting these will either eliminate all surrounding shapes or slow them down, giving you a much-needed advantage. The more blue balls you gather, the longer you can sustain your score and improve your gameplay experience. Focus on dodging the shapes while tactically seizing the opportunity to collect blue balls, enhancing your chance of survival and success. Aim to gather as many blue balls as possible and avoid hazards to achieve a high score!

How to play free Slide The Ball game online

To play Slide The Ball, use your mouse or finger to guide the ball left and right across the track. Keep an eye out for the shapes that come towards your ball and steer clear of them. Be on the lookout for blue balls and grab them to gain advantages. Stay alert and react quickly to avoid the spikes and other hazards while aiming for the highest score!
