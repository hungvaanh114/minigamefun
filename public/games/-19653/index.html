Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating journey into the world of tower defense with Fortress Defense. In this captivating game, your mission is to safeguard your castle against a myriad of formidable creatures. With a staggering 100 levels to conquer, you'll find plenty of action as you strategize your defenses against 15 unique enemies.

As you progress through the game, you'll have the opportunity to choose from 5 distinct archers, each with their own set of strengths and weaknesses. Make use of the gold earned from completing each mission to enhance your archers and fortify your castle through the Shop menu. This enables you to develop a tailored strategy that fits your play style and counteracts the specific threats you face.

To tip the odds in your favor, you'll also have access to 3 powerful bonuses, each capable of turning the tide of battle. Unleash the chilling effects of the Forzen bonus, release toxic clouds with the Poison bonus, or call down destructive storms using the Lightning bonus. Discover how these features will aid you in overcoming even the toughest of foes in your quest for victory.

Dive into Fortress Defense and unleash your tactical prowess. Each level presents new challenges, demanding quick thinking and precise timing. With engaging gameplay and stunning graphics, this is a tower defense experience you won t want to miss. For more exciting games like this, head over to meyagames.com.

How to play free Fortress Defense game online

To play the game, simply select your archers and place them strategically along the fortress walls. As enemies approach, use your resources to upgrade your defenses and activate special bonuses to deal damage. Monitor the battlefield closely and adapt your strategy based on the enemies you face to ensure your castle remains standing.
