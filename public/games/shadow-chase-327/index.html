Shadow Chase

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Shadow Chase is an exhilarating action-adventure game that brings the thrilling world of ninjas to your fingertips. As a courageous Ninja, you'll traverse through beautifully designed levels, each filled with dazzling stars waiting to be collected. But beware; formidable enemies lurk in every corner, pursuing you relentlessly. Your mission is to navigate these challenges while gathering as many stars as possible, relying on your agility and stealth.

What makes Shadow Chase so captivating is its addictive gameplay, where each level presents a unique blend of strategy and skill. Your Ninja is equipped with a powerful ninjutsu ability that allows you to eliminate enemies in your path. This power-up becomes essential as foes will often mirror your movements, creating a thrilling chase dynamic. The key to success lies not just in collecting stars but also in expertly timing your attacks to clear a path through packs of enemies.

As you progress, you ll encounter increasingly difficult levels designed to test your reflexes and problem-solving abilities. The stars are cleverly hidden, and you must be sharp to uncover them all while staying one step ahead of your pursuers. Are you ready to embrace the challenge and prove your ninja skills in Shadow Chase? Embark on this action-packed quest and unleash your inner warrior!

How to play the free Shadow Chase game online

To play Shadow Chase, use the arrow keys to navigate your Ninja through the levels. Collect stars while avoiding enemies and unleash your ninjutsu ability by pressing the designated key to eliminate foes. Strategically time your moves to collect all the stars and clear each level successfully!


Instructions

Use the W or UP Arrow Key to Jump Use A or D or Left and Right Arrow Key to go Left and Right Use E to Dash and Kill the Shadow when power-up is selected
