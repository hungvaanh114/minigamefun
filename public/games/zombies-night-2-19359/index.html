Zombies Night 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Zombies Night 2 plunges players into a thrilling first-person shooter experience, set against a backdrop of darkness and chaos. As a new dawn approaches, the city is besieged by hordes of relentless zombies emerging under cover of night. In this highly anticipated sequel, your primary objective is to safeguard the city and eliminate the undead threat before it overwhelms the innocent.

With responsive controls and an immersive environment, Zombies Night 2 offers a dynamic gameplay experience. Use your mouse to accurately shoot and aim at the incoming zombies. Keep your weapon ready, as they come in waves, and don't forget to reload your firearm using the 'R' key when your ammunition runs low. Situational awareness is key; you'll need to hustle and think quickly to outmaneuver the undead.

Equipped with a variety of weapons at your disposal, use the number keys 1-6 to switch between your arsenal. Each weapon has its unique advantages, so choose wisely depending on the situation. Additionally, the 'G' key allows you to throw grenades for those moments when you need to clear a large group of zombies in one explosive blow. With skill and strategy, you can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure filled with suspense and action. Zombies Night 2 challenges your reflexes and strategy, pushing you to the limits as you aim for victory against the undead menace!
