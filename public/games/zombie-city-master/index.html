Zombie City Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you weary of typical escape and survival games? Step into the chilling realm of Zombie City Master where the roles have reversed, and the undead are now in charge! A catastrophic outbreak of a zombie virus has devastated an entire city, turning its lively streets into a haunting landscape filled with danger and despair. What was once a thriving community is now a shadow of its former self, inhabited by flesh-hungry zombies who hunt relentlessly.

In this gripping game, you will guide a woman who mysteriously awakens alone in a hospital amidst the chaos. With all the police, medical staff, and friends now transformed into ravenous zombies, she must navigate through this perilous world. Use your strategic skills to devise a plan of escape while avoiding the relentless undead lurking around every corner. Can you outsmart the horrifying monsters and help her survive?

Survival is not the only challenge in Zombie City Master; you'll also encounter various obstacles that test your wits and reflexes. Collect essential items that can aid in her escape and uncover the haunting secrets of the city. Every decision you make can mean the difference between life and death as you face harrowing encounters and complex puzzles in a race against time.

How to play free Zombie City Master game online

To play Zombie City Master, begin by navigating your character through the eerie streets. Use your keyboard for movement and mouse clicks to interact with objects. Stay alert to dodge zombies and gather resources. Explore your surroundings to find escape routes and items that will help you survive the terrifying adventure!
