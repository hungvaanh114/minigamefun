GARTEN of BANBAN?GARTEN of BANBAN

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chilling world of Garten of Banban, an unconventional kindergarten shrouded in mystery. Here, an unsettling silence reigns as you navigate through empty hallways and deserted classrooms, where laughter once echoed. The absence of children and adults alike raises urgent questions about their disappearance, prompting you to uncover the truth lurking within this eerie establishment.

As you venture deeper into the Garten of Banban, you will discover a myriad of rooms each holding secrets and clues to unravel the enigma. Explore cabinets, search through drawers, and piece together fragmented hints. But tread carefully ominous shadows may hide dangerous entities watching your every move. The atmosphere is thick with unease, and the dust-coated toys tell a tale of abandonment; something sinister is at play.

Stay vigilant as you encounter bizarre occurrences and sudden sounds reverberating in the silence. Each corner of this abandoned kindergarten harbors potential dangers, so it's essential to keep your wits about you. Will you be able to discern the truth behind the missing inhabitants? Or will the malevolent forces within the Garten of Banban ensnare you as well? Your journey will test your resolve, encouraging you to think critically and react swiftly as you gather evidence and solve intricate puzzles.

How to play free GARTEN OF BANBAN game online

To play the game, use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate through the kindergarten. Interact with objects using the E key to collect clues and solve puzzles. Always be on the lookout for potential dangers, and remember to think critically to uncover the fate of the missing children and staff.
