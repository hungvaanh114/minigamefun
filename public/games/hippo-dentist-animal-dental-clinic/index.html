Hippo Dentist - Animal Dental Clinic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Hippo Dentist - Animal Dental Clinic, where you ll step into the shoes of a skilled dentist tackling the challenge of a bustling dental office! With Piglet, Puppy, and Lion Cub desperately seeking relief from their toothaches, it s up to you to provide the care they need. Join Little Hippo in this engaging adventure and assist in bringing smiles back to these adorable patients.

At Hippo's dental clinic, the atmosphere is lively and filled with adorable animals in need of some dental attention. As each patient enters with their individual dental woes, you will choose whom to treat. The game offers a delightful mix of fun and education as you perform various dental procedures.

Your mission is to clean their teeth, fill cavities with appropriate dentures, and impart crucial advice on maintaining good oral hygiene. This interactive experience allows you to demonstrate your dentist skills while keeping the colorful and charming environment of the clinic in mind. The more patients you help, the more proficient you become in their care!

Get ready to roll up your sleeves and embrace the responsibilities of being a dentist. With engaging graphics and cheerful characters, each successful treatment brings satisfaction as you witness your patients leaving with bright smiles!

How to play free Hippo Dentist - Animal Dental Clinic game online

To play the game, simply select a patient from the lineup and follow the on-screen instructions. Use special dental tools to clean and repair teeth while keeping your patient comfortable. Remember to provide tips for future dental care after each treatment to ensure their smiles stay bright!
