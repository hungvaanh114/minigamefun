Get ready for a thrilling experience with Extreme Motorbikes Match 3! This engaging three-in-a-row puzzle game challenges players to match blocks of the same color by lining them up in groups of three or more. As you connect these vibrant blocks, you will accumulate points, unlocking new levels of excitement and difficulty along the way. The game is designed to keep you entertained for hours with its dynamic challenges and vivid graphics.

However, be cautious as you play! A scale on the left side of the screen keeps track of your progress. If you fail to make matches quickly enough, the scale will begin to drop. If it falls too low, it means game over! This adds an extra layer of strategy and urgency, as you ll need to think fast and make moves that not only create matches but also keep the game going. Can you push your skills to the limit and score the highest points in this adrenaline-fueled puzzle adventure?

How to play free Extreme Motorbikes Match 3 game online

To play Extreme Motorbikes Match 3, use your mouse to click and swap adjacent blocks to create a line of three or more matching colors. Each successful match will score points and clear the blocks. Keep an eye on the left side scale to avoid falling below the limit!
