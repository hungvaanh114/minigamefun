Adds and Match Christmas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Adds And Match Christmas, a delightful and festive math game designed for players of all ages. The game features a simple and intuitive drag-and-drop mechanic, allowing players to effortlessly match numbers with their corresponding boxes. Whether you're looking to brush up on your math skills or simply enjoy a relaxed gaming experience, this Christmas-themed game is perfect for you!

In Adds And Match Christmas, players are greeted with two levels of difficulty that cater to varying skill sets. The first 20 levels present a gentle challenge, as you only need to match 4 numbers with 4 boxes. As you progress, the difficulty ramps up slightly, requiring you to match 5 numbers with 5 boxes in the subsequent levels. This gradual increase in challenge makes the game suitable for everyone, from young children to adults, all while providing a stress-free environment for learning and fun.

Designed as a kid's math game with a festive twist, Adds And Match Christmas is an enjoyable way to celebrate the holiday season while sharpening your mathematical abilities. Dive into a world of festive fun, enjoy the cheerful graphics, and remember, it's all about having a good time as you match numbers. Embrace the holiday spirit, and make the best of this engaging game. Merry Christmas and Happy New Year!

How to play free Adds And Match Christmas game online

To play Adds And Match Christmas, simply drag the numbers presented on the screen and drop them into the corresponding boxes. Aim to complete each level by making the correct matches. Enjoy the fun and challenge of the game while celebrating the festive holiday spirit!
