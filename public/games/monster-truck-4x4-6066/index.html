Monster Truck 4x4

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure with Monster Truck 4x4! In this exhilarating game, you'll take control of a powerful monster truck, tasked with navigating a challenging and treacherous 4x4 track. This is far from your average off-road experience; the terrain is riddled with obstacles that will test your driving skills to the max.

The course is designed with a variety of difficult challenges, including log jumps that require precise timing, refrigerated trucks that block your path, and metal bridges that add a layer of complexity to your drive. You must also maneuver through concrete rings and other assorted structures that are scattered throughout the hilly landscape. As you navigate this dynamic environment, keep an eye out for hidden hazards such as wooden crates, misplaced traffic cones, and uneven levels on the road.

Your goal is clear: skillfully drive your monster truck through the track without crashing, avoiding all obstacles along the way, and make it to the finish line parked right next to the finish flag. Can you conquer the elements and prove your prowess as a master off-road driver? Each level presents unique challenges that will keep you on the edge of your seat, so buckle up and prepare for a wild ride!

How to play free Monster Truck 4x4 game online

Once in the game, use the arrow keys to steer your monster truck. Press the forward key to accelerate while using the backward key to slow down or reverse. Navigate left and right to avoid obstacles and stay on course. Aim for the finish flag without crashing to complete the level!
