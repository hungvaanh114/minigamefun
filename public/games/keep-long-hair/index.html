Keep Long Hair

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the joy of hairstyling in "Keep Long Hair," a highly entertaining simulation game where creativity knows no bounds. Players are invited to step into a vibrant world where they can craft a multitude of stunning hairstyles. The game encourages participants to express their unique style as they navigate through various levels, unlocking new hairstyles and hair accessories that enhance the gameplay and aesthetic appeal.

As players engage with the game, they will discover that each new hairstyle not only brings individual flair but also contributes to greater in-game earnings. Economy mechanics allow for strategic play, where players can invest their earnings into unlocking even more elaborate hairstyles or upgrading existing ones. The experience is rich and diverse, allowing players to enjoy runway walks, showcasing their hair creations to an appreciative audience.

This simulation game offers players the freedom to explore their creativity. With every successful goal completion whether that s completing a challenging hairstyle or impressing virtual spectators a player earns points and rewards that can be utilized to further their hairstyling adventure.
