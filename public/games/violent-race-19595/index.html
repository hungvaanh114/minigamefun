Violent Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Violent Race, an exhilarating arcade racing game designed to deliver a mix of speed and strategy. Immerse yourself in stunning 3D graphics as you navigate through a variety of challenging tracks. Your primary objective is to reach the finish line before your opponents while skillfully avoiding various obstacles that may impede your progress.

As you race, you'll encounter not just the course itself, but two AI competitors who are relentlessly determined to finish first. These rivals will push you to stay sharp, requiring quick reflexes and a clever approach to overcome the hurdles scattered throughout the circuit. Get ready for an action-packed experience that combines the thrill of competition with the tranquility of racing.

In Violent Race, every moment counts, and every decision can make a difference between victory and defeat. Use your driving skills to outsmart your opponents and find the optimal paths to glide past obstacles. Embrace the challenge and aim to be the first to cross the finish line. Best of luck as you embark on this racing adventure!

How to play free Violent Race game online

To start playing Violent Race, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate your vehicle. Accelerate with the up arrow, steer left and right with the corresponding keys, and brake with the down arrow. Keep an eye on your opponents while skillfully dodging obstacles to ensure a first-place finish!
