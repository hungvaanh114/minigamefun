Mad Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game Mad Defense, players are plunged into an epic battle where diverse creatures are relentlessly advancing toward the castle. The eccentric engineer, equipped with innovative weaponry, is determined to safeguard the fortress from these monstrous foes. Your mission is to assist him by strategizing and utilizing the arsenal at your disposal.

As you face waves of attackers, you will earn gold for each victorious defense. This gold is not just a currency but a vital resource that allows you to upgrade the castle s fortifications and enhance the weapons that the quirky engineer has meticulously crafted. With each successful defense, the stakes rise, demanding that you continuously optimize your strategy and gear to repel stronger waves of enemies.

Moreover, as you progress through the game, you can recruit the engineer's brothers, each bringing unique abilities to bolster your defensive capabilities. This recruitment adds an exciting layer to gameplay, as each brother can contribute differently to your defensive strategy. It's essential to find the right balance between upgrading weapons, fortifying the castle, and assembling a team that can withstand the escalating challenges ahead.

How to play free Mad Defense game online

To play Mad Defense, simply use your mouse to select and deploy weapons strategically against incoming enemies. Earn gold by defeating waves, which you can then spend on upgrades for your castle and weapons. Don't forget to recruit the engineer s brothers to strengthen your defenses and adapt your strategy throughout the game!
