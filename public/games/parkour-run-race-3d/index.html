Parkour Run - Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the exhilarating thrill of city racing in Parkour Run - Race 3D! Propel yourself from one rooftop to another, as you flip, jump, and vault over various obstacles to achieve new personal bests. Compete with other players daily for special rewards as you navigate through the urban landscape, showcasing your incredible parkour skills. Scale towering buildings, perform impressive tricks, and gain extra speed, while leaving your competitors fuming in your wake!

In this dynamic game, you ll embody a stickman character, starting anew alongside other players. Your mission is simple: race across high-rise buildings and be the first to reach the finish line. As you dash forward, strategically avoid obstacles and utilize power-ups to quicken your path to victory. The more tricks you execute such as roof jumps and vaults the more excitement you ll experience, ensuring that every match feels fresh and exhilarating. With each challenging run, you ll develop your skills as a top-notch parkour athlete!

Set in iconic cities like New York, Paris, and Tokyo, every run presents unique challenges and breathtaking scenery. Don't forget to customize your character by unlocking epic outfits and special effects through race victories. Escape the sea of competitors by performing tricks and setting records. The adventure doesn t stop; it only intensifies with more obstacles and thrilling maneuvers waiting for you to conquer!

How to play free Parkour Run - Race 3D game online

To play, simply navigate your stickman through the bustling city by using your screen controls to jump, flip, and vault over obstacles. Make use of power-ups to accelerate your speed and avoid barriers. Compete with other players to reach the finish line first, showcasing your parkour prowess!
