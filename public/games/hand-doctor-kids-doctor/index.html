Hand Doctor : Kids Doctor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exciting world of Hand Doctor: Kids Doctor, where you can care for your patients' hands, nails, and fingers! This engaging medical game is designed for teenagers and is perfect for girls who love interactive gameplay. You'll become a skilled hand doctor, diagnosing and treating a variety of ailments, from broken nails to painful injuries.

In Hand Doctor: Kids Doctor, your patients come in with all sorts of issues. It s your job to perform virtual surgeries, apply ointments, and even give makeovers to manicured nails! The vivid graphics and realistic sound effects bring the experience to life, making you feel like a true medical professional while having fun. You'll learn how to use medical tools, all while ensuring your patients leave with happy, healthy hands.

This game not only entertains but also educates younger audiences about the importance of hand care and health. Get ready to unleash your creativity as you decorate nails and give stylish makeovers! Whether you're treating a patient with a splinter or providing a fabulous nail design, the possibilities are endless. Don t miss out on this fun-filled, educational adventure that's perfect for aspiring doctors and nail artists alike!

How to play free Hand Doctor: Kids Doctor game online

To start playing Hand Doctor: Kids Doctor, simply select a patient and choose the treatment option. Use the medical instruments provided to diagnose the issue and apply the needed treatment. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete each task and help your patients heal while having a blast!
