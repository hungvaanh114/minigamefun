Yuas Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Yuas Quest, a thrilling 2D platformer where you step into the shoes of Yua, an intrepid anime heroine. Your mission is to collect antidote bottles scattered throughout the levels to combat a relentless zombie virus threatening humanity. Each level poses unique challenges, including swarms of zombies and deadly saws, demanding quick reflexes and strategic navigation as you dash and dodge your way through.

With 8 engaging levels designed to test your skills, the game ramps up in difficulty as you progress, ensuring a captivating experience every step of the way. The vibrant graphics and dynamic gameplay draw you into Yua s world, where each successfully completed level brings you closer to saving the day. Are you ready to face the undead and rise above the challenges? The fate of humanity rests in your hands!

In Yuas Quest, not only do you need to collect the antidote, but you also have to maintain your wits to avoid being caught by lurking zombies and deadly saw blades. Will you navigate through the chaos and reach the elusive red flag that signifies your victory? It s time to find out!

How to play free Yuas Quest game online

To play Yuas Quest, use the arrow keys on your keyboard for movement. Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and collect the antidote bottles scattered throughout each level. Stay alert to dodge approaching zombies while making your way to the red flag to advance to the next stage.


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
