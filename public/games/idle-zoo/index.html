Idle Zoo

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the captivating world of Idle Zoo, where you take on the role of a prosperous investor dedicated to rejuvenating zoos across the nation. In this engaging simulation game, your mission is to restore dilapidated habitats and develop new enclosures that will house diverse animal species. Build your dream zoo and witness it transform into a vibrant sanctuary filled with exotic creatures and delighted visitors.

As you progress, you'll uncover a variety of animals to populate your zoo. Extend your collection by discovering new species and ensuring they have comfortable surroundings. Your success hinges on strategic planning and wise investment, as you repair aging habitats and upgrade facilities to create an attractive environment for both animals and guests. With every improvement you make, watch your zoo flourish and bring joy to animal lovers from all walks of life.

Manage your resources efficiently while exploring new opportunities to enhance your zoo's reputation. By attracting more visitors, you'll generate revenue that can be reinvested to expand your wildlife sanctuary even further. The more you engage with your zoo, the more it will thrive, leading to a rewarding gameplay experience packed with creativity and discovery.

How to play free Idle Zoo game online

To play Idle Zoo, simply tap on various habitats to upgrade and expand them. Manage your finances wisely by investing the earnings from visitor tickets and animal exhibits. Explore new animals by unlocking them as you progress, and keep an eye on your resources to ensure smooth operations within your zoo.
