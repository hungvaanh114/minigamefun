Space Supremacy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Space Supremacy invites players to embark on an exhilarating journey through the cosmos, commanding a fleet of three combat ships in an intense battle against formidable foes. As you engage in fierce space warfare, the strategic options available to you are virtually limitless. You can choose to fight alongside your squadron, coordinating attacks and maneuvers to outwit your enemies, or assign specific positions to each of your ships for optimal defense of your base.

Each ship in your arsenal boasts unique capabilities, allowing for a personalized combat experience. With the option to set your ships to automatic or manual targeting, you can tailor your approach to suit your playstyle. Manual aiming provides a hands-on challenge for those looking to hone their skills, while automatic targeting ensures that even new players can engage effectively in the heat of battle. Mastering your fleet s strengths and weaknesses is crucial as you navigate through enemy territory, seeking to establish dominance in the galaxy.

Whether you re defending your base or launching offensive strikes against your adversaries, teamwork and strategy are essential in Space Supremacy. Coordinate with fellow players to devise effective tactics or go solo to showcase your epic battleship skills. The fate of your space empire rests in your hands will you emerge victorious?

How to play free Space Supremacy game online

To play Space Supremacy, simply enter the game and select your ships. Use your mouse to aim and fire, while the keyboard navigates your fleet. Decide on your strategy whether to engage in combat manually or let the ships target automatically. Coordinate with teammates to defend your base and outmaneuver your opponents!
