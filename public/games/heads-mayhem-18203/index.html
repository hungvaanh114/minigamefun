Heads Mayhem

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating adventure with "Heads Mayhem"! This action-packed game presents a delightful yet chaotic experience, featuring eleven quirky characters that are sure to keep you entertained. Players can explore eight unique game maps, each designed with its own challenges and surprises. Alongside a variety of weapons at your disposal, the gameplay offers plenty of room for strategy and creativity.

Whether you prefer to play solo or with friends, "Heads Mayhem" has something for everyone. The game boasts four exciting modes: a Single Player mode and multiplayer modes for 2, 3, or 4 players ideal for a fun-filled gaming session with friends. Players begin their adventure with five lives; the goal is to outlast your opponents by pushing them off the platforms using an array of powerful weapons. The RPG is the strongest weapon in your arsenal, but be cautious! Its explosive power can send you flying if you aren't careful when you fire it.

Gather your friends, jump into the action, and experience the thrills of "Heads Mayhem" together. Will you emerge victorious, or will you be sent tumbling off the platforms? Find out now!

How to play free Heads Mayhem game online

To play "Heads Mayhem" online, choose your character and game mode. Use your keyboard to navigate and select your weapons. Aim carefully with the mouse to shoot your opponents off the platforms while avoiding being knocked out yourself. Stay sharp and use strategic weaponry to dominate the arena!
