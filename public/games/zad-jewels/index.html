How to play free Zad Jewels game online

To play Zad Jewels online, simply align three or more identical jewels in a row to clear them from the board. Use strategic moves to create special gem combinations that trigger powerful effects. Keep an eye on your goals and try to accomplish them within the limited number of moves for maximum points!
