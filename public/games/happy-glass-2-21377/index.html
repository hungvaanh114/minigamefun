Happy Glass 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the colorful world of Happy Glass 2, a delightful puzzle game where your creativity and logic come into play! The mission is simple yet engaging: transform a sad glass into a jubilant one by filling it with delicious fruit juice. You get to draw lines freely to guide the flowing juice into the glass, creating a unique path for each level.

Each level challenges you to think strategically while trying to minimize the number of lines you draw. Can you achieve a perfect score and earn all three stars? Don't worry if you get stuck; the hint option is available to assist you in discovering the most efficient way to fill the glass. Dive into various fruity flavors, including Orange, Mango, Pomegranate, Watermelon, Pineapple, and Chikoo, each adding a refreshing twist to your puzzle-solving experience.

With a multitude of fun puzzles to tackle, Happy Glass 2 offers different levels of challenges, pushing your puzzle-solving skills to the limit. Prepare yourself for obstacles that will make each challenge unique and exciting. As you progress, stay tuned for even more levels coming soon! Are you ready to uplift the glass and enjoy a fruit-filled adventure?

How to play free Happy Glass 2 game online

To enjoy Happy Glass 2, simply use your mouse or touch control to draw lines on the screen. Your goal is to direct the fruit juice into the glass. The less you draw while completing each level, the better your score will be! Utilize the hint option if needed to find the best pathway to success.
