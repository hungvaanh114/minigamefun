Cooking Street

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Cooking Street, a dynamic and engaging free cooking simulator designed especially for boys, available on PuzzleGame. In this culinary adventure, your mission is to whip up gourmet dishes tailored to the specific tastes of your guests.

Your restaurant thrives on customer satisfaction, so it s essential to serve your dishes promptly. Keep your guests delighted and they will reward you with gold coins to help you elevate your culinary establishment. With persistent effort, you will create a renowned cooking street that everyone talks about!

As you progress in Cooking Street, use those hard-earned coins to upgrade your restaurant s facilities and expand your menu. The more satisfied your customers are, the faster your restaurant will grow in popularity. Join in on the fun and experience your journey to becoming a celebrated chef.

This game is easily accessible and can be enjoyed on various platforms including Mobile, Tablet, or Desktop, making it perfect for a quick cooking fix wherever you are!

How to play free Cooking Street game online

To play Cooking Street, simply tap on the dishes your guests order and drag the ingredients into place to cook them. Pay close attention to the time, as waiting too long can frustrate your customers. Once the meals are ready, serve them quickly to earn coins and improve your restaurant!
