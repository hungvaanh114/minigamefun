Join & Strike is an exciting 3D stickman adventure arcade game designed for enthusiasts of action-packed gameplay. The game begins with you controlling a red gunner equipped with a pistol, navigating through various levels. Your main objective is to recruit white stickman allies by approaching and joining them to form a powerful team.

As you progress, you will encounter various yellow-bodied enemies, including melee units and gunners, who are determined to take you down. Your mission is clear: eliminate every yellow enemy on the platform to succeed. The game's automatic fire mode adds to the fluidity of the experience, allowing you to focus on movement and strategy while avoiding enemy attacks, including their red bullets.

The combination of fast-paced action and strategic team-building makes Join & Strike an engaging game that keeps you on your toes. Mastering the art of dodging while effectively defeating your foes is key to advancing through the game. Each level presents new challenges and opportunities to strengthen your army of stickmen, enhancing the overall gameplay experience.

How to play free Join & Strike game online

To play Join & Strike, simply navigate your stickman towards the white allies to join forces. Use your movement keys to dodge incoming attacks and aim for the yellow enemies to defeat them. Rely on the automatic firing feature as you maneuver around the platform, ensuring you avoid enemy bullets while accumulating team members.
