Join Baby Hazel and her friend Jasmine as they embark on a delightful adventure in organizing an exciting Pumpkin Party! This festive event is Jasmine s first time hosting, and she needs all the help she can get. Together, they will navigate through various fun tasks, ensuring that every detail is perfect for their friends.

As the Pumpkin Party unfolds, Baby Hazel and Jasmine will be busy selecting decorations, preparing delicious treats, and planning engaging games. From choosing the coziest pumpkins to creating spooktacular invitations, players will help the duo in making this event memorable. Delight in the variety of activities as you assist them in decorating the party area with vibrant autumn colors and cute ghostly crafts!

The fun doesn t stop there! Players will join in on fun-filled games that bring excitement to the party atmosphere. It's a fantastical celebration filled with friendship, laughter, and joy!
