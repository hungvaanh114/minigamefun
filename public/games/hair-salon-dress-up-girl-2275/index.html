Hair Salon Dress Up Girl

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the captivating world of the Hair Salon Dress Up Girl game, you have the opportunity to pamper a lovely girl from head to toe. Begin your adventure by treating her to a refreshing hair wash, ensuring to clear away any air bubbles before diving into the exciting styling process. Choose the perfect hair color and length that highlights her beauty, setting the stage for a fabulous makeover.

Once her hair is styled to perfection, it's time to move on to the nail salon. Here, you can unleash your creativity by selecting from an array of fun patterns and vibrant colors. Add a sprinkle of glitter and some cool stickers to give her nails that extra flair! The transformation continues as you head to the dressing room, where a wide selection of stylish outfits awaits. Try on multiple clothing items to discover which ones complement her look the best, and remember, the right pair of shoes is essential for completing the ensemble!

How to play free Hair Salon Dress Up Girl game online

To play the Hair Salon Dress Up Girl game, simply use your mouse to interact with the different salon stations. Click on the hair washing area to start, then proceed to customize her hair. Afterward, navigate to the nail salon and select your choice of nail art. Finally, explore the dressing room to find the ultimate outfit that showcases her new style!
