Cubical Jump invites players to dive into a delightful jumping adventure! In the Normal Mode, you can relax while jumping, unlocking a variety of characters along the way. Your score is saved every 50 points, allowing you to focus on the joy of hopping without any pressure. Enjoy the simple thrill of jumping through vibrant landscapes!

Feeling adventurous? Switch to Challenge Mode! If Normal Mode feels too easy for you, Challenge Mode ups the ante. In this more demanding setting, your score will only be saved if you surpass your last best score. It's a classic race against yourself, where only the most skilled jumps will help you achieve glory!

If you're after a laid-back experience, Relax Mode is just the thing. Here, points keep adding up without the threat of losing your game. Embrace excitement seeing combinations and score bonuses grow as you tap quickly! But beware, linger too long, and you might find yourself starting over!

With over 30 unique characters 36 to be precise each bringing their own flair and special abilities, there's plenty to collect. Plus, you can challenge yourself to unlock nine different medals as you play. Feeling social? You can also engage in local multiplayer games! Compete with your friends or meet new players for some extra fun!

How to play free Cubical Jump game online

To play Cubical Jump, simply tap the screen to make your character jump. Time your taps to achieve combos for extra points! Select your game mode from Normal, Challenge, or Relax, and start jumping your way to the highest score. Enjoy the unique characters and strive to collect all the medals!
