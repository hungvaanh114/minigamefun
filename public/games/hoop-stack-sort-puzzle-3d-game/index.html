How to play free Hoop Stack Sort Puzzle 3D Game online

To play the Hoop Stack Sort Puzzle 3D Game, simply tap on a hoop to lift it, then select a stack to drop it onto. You can only place a hoop on an empty stack or on top of a matching color. Your goal is to sort all hoops into their specific stacks, using as few moves as possible.
