Kids Zoo Farm

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Kids Zoo Farm, a delightful adventure that immerses your little ones in the fascinating world of animals! In this interactive experience, kids can say hello to a variety of creatures while embarking on an exciting quiz challenge. Choose your favorite animal and let the fun begin!

As part of the quiz, your child will listen to the distinct sounds that animals make and then guess which of the two animals the sound belongs to. This engaging activity boosts observation skills and helps children learn about the animal kingdom. Once they've completed the quiz, it's time to explore the enchanting zoo or the vibrant farm!

As they wander through the zoo or farm, kids will be greeted by the sounds of their beloved animals and will be able to see their favorite treats. The interactive features allow them to engage with the animals further; by feeding them their preferred food, they can hear the satisfying growl or bleating sounds once again, making for a memorable experience.

How to play free Kids Zoo Farm game online

To play the Kids Zoo Farm game, start by selecting the animal you want to interact with. Listen to the sound and choose the matching animal from the options provided. After completing the quiz, navigate to either the zoo or farm, feed the animals their favorite treats, and explore the sounds they make!
