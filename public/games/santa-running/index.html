Santa Running

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This festive season, our adorable Santa is on a mission to spread joy and gifts, and he needs your help! As the Christmas spirit fills the air, join Santa in his exciting adventure in "Santa Running." You'll embark on a thrilling journey through 21 enchanting levels, each brimming with challenges and surprises. Your goal is to help Santa collect as many gifts as possible while deftly navigating through a myriad of obstacles that stand in your way.

With each jump and dodge, you ll experience the thrill of racing against time while enjoying delightful Christmas-themed graphics and captivating music. Can you help Santa gather enough gifts to complete all the levels? Challenge yourself and see how quickly you can get through each stage, making every moment of gameplay filled with fun and excitement. The holiday cheer awaits you, so it s time to lace up your virtual sneakers and get ready to run!

How to play free Santa Running game online

Playing Santa Running is simple and intuitive. Use the arrow keys to control Santa's movements press the up arrow to jump over obstacles and the down arrow to slide under them. Collect gifts scattered throughout the levels and aim to complete each one before time runs out!
