Gift Express

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Gift Express, where the magic of Christmas comes alive! In this enchanting game, step into Santa Claus's toy factory, bustling all year round to create delightful gifts. As Christmas Eve arrives, it's time to load up the sleigh and deliver these precious toys to children around the world. However, there's a challenge ahead: the toys remain stacked high in the warehouse while time is of the essence.

Prepare for an exhilarating adventure filled with snowy hills and charming towns. You will navigate through different environments during both day and night, encountering various terrains that test your driving skills. The objective is clear: transport as many gifts as possible without losing them or getting involved in accidents. With engaging gameplay, each successful delivery brings you closer to completing your holiday mission!

Explore a variety of train styles as you steer your way through 30 unlockable levels. Each level presents unique challenges, and creativity comes into play as you strategize your delivery routes. You can enhance your train by purchasing keys to unlock new mods, making your journeys even more exciting. The magic lies in how you manage the cheerful chaos of delivering gifts while ensuring Santa s reputation as the jolliest figure of the season remains intact!

How to play free Gift Express game online

To play Gift Express, begin by choosing your train and loading gifts from the toy factory. Navigate your train along the tracks through snowy landscapes, dropping off gifts at designated stations. Maintain control of your speed and direction to avoid losing gifts and ensure a safe delivery. Enjoy the festive journey!


Instructions

Use A or D or Left and Right Arrow Keys to move Use Left and Right Button on Screen to Move
