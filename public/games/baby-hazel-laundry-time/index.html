Baby Hazel Laundry Time

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel on an exciting and educational adventure in "Baby Hazel Laundry Time," where she is about to explore the fascinating world of laundry! Today, Hazel's mom will guide her through the entire laundry process, making this a fun and interactive learning experience. You'll assist Hazel in washing and drying clothes, ensuring she understands the importance of separating white and colored items to prevent any mishaps.

As the game progresses, you'll help Hazel operate the washing machine, teaching her how to load it correctly and select the right washing cycles. Make sure to supervise her closely as she maneuvers through this hands-on activity. Once the clothes are washed and ready, it's time to dry them! Help Hazel hang up the wet clothes or use the dryer, emphasizing the significance of handling everything safely.

But the fun doesn't stop there! After washing and drying, Hazel will need your help to iron her clothes and get them perfectly neat. Pay attention to each detail and ensure she performs these tasks with care. Throughout the game, stay alert to Hazel's needs and wishes, as she often requires your assistance to make her laundry experience delightful. Will you support our darling little angel as she learns these essential life skills? Dive into "Baby Hazel Laundry Time" and enjoy a playful and educational journey together!

How to play free Baby Hazel Laundry Time game online

To play "Baby Hazel Laundry Time," simply use your mouse to interact with the various tasks. Click on the items to pick them up, and follow the prompts to guide Hazel through each step of the laundry process. Help her sort, wash, dry, and iron the clothes while ensuring she stays happy and safe! Enjoy this engaging experience!
