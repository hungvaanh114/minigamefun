Welcome to Snowman's Naughty Trap! In this enchanting winter adventure, it's your mission to save Santa Claus from the clutches of a crafty snowman. Discover a whimsical snowy maze filled with challenges that will test your skills and reflexes. Beware of the slippery icy obstacles lurking throughout the maze they can hinder your progress and slow you down.

Keep your eyes peeled for hidden surprises and bonus items that may help you along your journey. It s crucial to act swiftly to reach Santa before it s too late! Will you be able to outsmart the playful snowman and rescue Santa in time? Gear up for an action-packed adventure filled with thrills and chills!

How to play free Snowman's Naughty Trap game online

To play Snowman's Naughty Trap, use the arrow keys or swipe controls to navigate your character through the snowy maze.
