Squadron Hero : Alien Invasion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game Squadron Hero: Alien Invasion, players step into the shoes of courageous intergalactic warriors engaged in an epic battle against a relentless alien force. As these extra-terrestrial beings unleash chaos and destruction upon Earth, our dedicated guardians embody the spirit of resilience and determination. Their mission is clear: cleanse the planet of hostile invaders and restore peace. However, this daunting task won't be easy, as the formidable Fatboy, their cunning adversary, stands in the way.

As you immerse yourself in this action-packed adventure, prepare for intense combat scenarios that demand quick reflexes and strategic thinking. The fate of Earth rests heavily on your shoulders, and the warriors rely on your skills to guide them through the onslaught. Engage with diverse alien foes and navigate through various stages, all while honing your combat abilities.

To emerge victorious, players must outmaneuver enemies, execute powerful attacks, and devise effective strategies to confront the daunting boss, Fatboy. Will you be able to rally the guardians, rise to the challenge, and protect our planet before it's too late?

How to play free Squadron Hero: Alien Invasion game online

To get started, simply use the on-screen controls to navigate your character through the battlefield while attacking alien foes. Utilize various combos to maximize damage and collect power-ups to enhance your abilities. Be vigilant and adapt your strategy to overcome challenging enemies, especially when facing Fatboy!
