RACE: Rocket Arena Car Extreme takes you on an adrenaline-fueled journey through stunning 3D environments where action meets intensity. Drift, slide, and outmaneuver your rivals as you push the throttle to leave them in your dust. With every race, soaring engines and burning rubber bring a fresh thrill as explosive action unfolds on the track.

Forge your path through intricate arenas filled with fire traps, chainsaws, and other perilous challenges. This isn t just a racing game; it s an immersive action movie experience where survival meets speed!

Choose your weapons wisely! Dealing damage with missiles, bombs, and electric weapons is vital to eliminating competitors. You start with a full gas tank there s no need to wait to race; simply hit the accelerator and dive into the ultimate racing showdown.
