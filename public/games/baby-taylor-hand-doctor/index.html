Baby Taylor Hand Doctor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Taylor in her delightful new adventure with the game "Baby Taylor Hand Doctor"! Living near a beautiful beach, Taylor often enjoys spending holiday time with her friends, having fun collecting colorful shells along the shore. However, the joy of exploration sometimes leads to unfortunate accidents, as Taylor frequently scrapes her hands while gathering these treasures.

In this engaging game, players will step into the role of a hand doctor to assist Baby Taylor s mother in treating her injuries. It's important to make sure Taylor's hands are properly healed after her beach outings. Use various medical tools to clean, bandage, and heal her scratches, ensuring that she is back to her playful self in no time.

Moreover, to prevent future injuries during her fun beach trips, you will also design and create a special pair of gloves for Taylor. This creative aspect allows players to mix and match styles, colors, and patterns, adding a personal touch to her protective gear. Get ready to let your artistic skills shine, while also providing essential care to this cute character!

How to play free Baby Taylor Hand Doctor game online

To play the Baby Taylor Hand Doctor game, simply follow the on-screen instructions to use different medical tools to treat Taylor's hand injuries. Click and drag items to clean her wounds and use band-aids to protect them. After treatment, enjoy crafting custom gloves for her to wear on future beach adventures!
