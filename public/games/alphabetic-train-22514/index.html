Alphabetic Train

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Alphabetic Train is an engaging and educational game designed to blend fun with learning. In this vibrant adventure, you ll accompany a turtle aboard a whimsical train, equipped with a letter board. The mission is straightforward: collect images that correspond to the letter displayed on the turtle s board.

To gather an image, simply tap the screen when it aligns with the turtle, allowing the letter to make contact with the corresponding picture. However, be cautious! If you tap on an incorrect image, you ll lose health, making it vital to focus and think critically about your choices. Along the way, you ll encounter various bonus cards; these give you an advantage by providing extra fuel for your train or restoring health. Green cards will help you boost your score, while red cards should be avoided as they will diminish your points.

The objective is to reach a target of either 25 or 100 alphabetic cards before the train runs out of fuel or your health dwindles to zero. With dynamic gameplay and a focus on the alphabet, Alphabetic Train promises an enjoyable experience that enhances both recognition and vocabulary skills.

How to play free Alphabetic Train game online

To play Alphabetic Train, simply tap the screen when the correct picture aligns with the letter shown on the turtle's board. Aim to collect as many images as possible while avoiding distractions and incorrect pictures to maintain your health. Watch for bonus cards to enhance your gameplay!
