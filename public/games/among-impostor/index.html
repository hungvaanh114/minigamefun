Among Impostor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling universe of Among Impostor, a multiplayer game that blends strategy, deduction, and teamwork. Whether online or via local WiFi, gather a crew of 4 to 15 players and work together to prepare your spaceship for takeoff. However, amongst your team lies an Impostor with a sinister agenda to eliminate all others! As a Crewmate, your mission is to complete various tasks around the spaceship to ensure a successful launch. However, you must remain vigilant, as the Impostor is lurking, ready to strike when you least expect it.

The Crewmates' victory hinges on either completing all assigned tasks or smartly identifying and voting the Impostor out during team discussions. Collaboration, communication, and observational skills are key! As you navigate tasks, keep an eye on suspicious behavior and take note of the crew's movements. If you can successfully deduce who the Impostor is, you can save your spaceship and each other from doom.

On the flip side, the Impostor thrives in chaos. Armed with the ability to sabotage essential systems, they create distractions and opportunities for stealthy kills while crafting their alibi. It s a game of wits, deception, and strategy that keeps players on their toes as they attempt to outsmart one another.

How to play free Among Impostor game online

To play Among Impostor, join or create a game lobby, either online or via local matchmaking. As the game starts, take on the role of either a Crewmate or an Impostor. Your aim is to complete tasks or eliminate players, depending on your role, while engaging in discussions to root out the Impostor or to evade detection.
