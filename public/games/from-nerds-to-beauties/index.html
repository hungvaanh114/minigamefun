From Nerds to Beauties

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Two unusual girlfriends - Little Mermaid and Arlene - embark on an exciting journey into the world of stylish transformation. Their appearance, full of flaws, requires magical intervention. In the first stage of the game, the girls go through a series of fun cosmetic procedures: from cleansing masks to magical elixirs that give them radiant skin and luxurious hair. With each successful step, they become more attractive. In the second stage, the player will have to become a real stylist for girls, choosing unique hairstyles, fashionable clothes and bright accessories for the heroines that will emphasize the individuality of the girlfriends. In this game, friendship, self-expression and fun go hand in hand, and each small victory brings the Little Mermaid and Arlene closer to their dream of becoming real beauties!

Instructions

Help two girl friends to transform their appearance and choose a hairstyle clothes and accessories Depending on the gaming device either a click of a computer mouse or a simple touch on touchscreens is used to control it
