Laser Ray

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Laser Ray! Are you adept at guiding lasers with precision? In this engaging puzzle game, you'll be challenged to position mirrors strategically to direct the powerful beams of light toward all the sparkling diamonds. Your primary goal is to ensure that every diamond is illuminated with the laser rays before time runs out. With only 2 minutes on the clock, every second counts!

With a total of 60 unique levels, Laser Ray will put your critical thinking and spatial reasoning skills to the test. As you advance through the game, you will encounter increasingly complex challenges that require clever manipulation of the laser beams. Each level offers its own set of hurdles, ensuring that no two stages are alike and that the gameplay remains fresh and exciting throughout your journey.

Master the art of reflection as you experiment with different angles and positions of the mirrors to create the perfect setup. Can you figure out the optimal arrangement to hit all the diamonds in record time? With intuitive controls and captivating graphics, Laser Ray promises an exhilarating experience for puzzle enthusiasts and casual gamers alike!

How to play free Laser Ray game online

To play Laser Ray, start by selecting a level. Utilize your mouse or touch controls to drag and position mirrors on the grid. Align the lasers to ensure they hit every diamond within the two-minute limit. Keep an eye on the timer and strategize quickly for the best results!
