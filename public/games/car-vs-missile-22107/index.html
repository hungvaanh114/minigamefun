Car vs Missile

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Car vs Missile, where high-speed action meets strategic evasion! In this electrifying game, you are tasked with navigating your car through a bustling city while relentless homing missiles pursue you. The longer you survive, the greater the challenge becomes, as the missiles adapt to your movements, keeping you on the edge of your seat.

To stay ahead of your pursuers, you must not only rely on your driving skills but also gather power-ups scattered throughout the city. These power-ups can provide you with temporary shields or speed boosts, giving you a fighting chance against the relentless missile onslaught. As you race through the streets, keep an eye out for coins that can be collected. These coins are your key to success, allowing you to purchase new vehicles and essential upgrades to enhance your performance.

Every decision matters in Car vs Missile. Choose the best routes through the city, navigate around obstacles, and outwit your missile foes. The more coins you collect and upgrades you acquire, the more equipped you'll be to face the relentless challenges that lie ahead. Are you ready to test your reflexes and see how long you can outrun the missiles?

How to play free Car vs Missile game online

To play Car vs Missile, use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your car and evade incoming missiles. Collect power-ups for temporary advantages, and gather coins to unlock new vehicles and upgrades. Master the city's layout to find the best escape routes and stay alive as long as possible!
