Space Guardian

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating adventure in the universe with Space Guardian, an action-packed space shooter that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Your mission is simple yet critical: save Earth from an array of relentless alien invaders. As you navigate through the vastness of space, your laser weapon will automatically fire whenever hostile forces are positioned above you, allowing you to focus on dodging and maneuvering your ship.

Throughout your journey, pay close attention to your red health bar located in the upper left corner of the screen. This vital indicator shows how much damage you've taken, as being hit by enemy projectiles will decrease both your health and score. Make strategic moves to outsmart your enemies and minimize damage while racking up points. Carefully manage your health; if you run out, it's game over!

Customization is key in Space Guardian. You can adjust the music and sound effects according to your preferences or mute them entirely for a more immersive experience. Take full control of your space adventure and create the perfect atmosphere as you battle through waves of extraterrestrial foes. Your skills will be put to the test as you aim to defeat each alien invader and protect your home planet. Will you rise to the challenge, or will the aliens conquer Earth? Play now to find out!

How to play free Space Guardian game online

Once in the game, use your mouse or keyboard to navigate your ship through enemy attacks. Your laser fires automatically when enemies come into range. Keep a watchful eye on your health bar, dodge incoming attacks, and remember to strategize your movements to defeat all aliens before they overpower Earth!
