They Are All Zombies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the frantic world of "They Are All Zombies," you find yourself in a perilous situation where the undead are on your tail. To survive, you must use various items scattered along your path to hinder the relentless zombies that are determined to catch you. Your quick thinking and resourcefulness will be your greatest assets in this harrowing escape.

As you dash through the desolate landscape, you'll come across multiple types of objects that can be utilized to slow down the approaching hordes. Whether it's barricades, traps, or distractions, every item can play a crucial role in your survival. However, your journey is not just about escaping; you also have the opportunity to save fellow survivors along the way. Each decision you make could mean the difference between life and death, not only for you but for those who are still holding on to their humanity. The pressure mounts as you must decide quickly who to save and who can be left behind. Will you help others, or will they join the ranks of the undead?

The stakes are high in this adrenaline-filled dash through a world plagued by the zombie apocalypse. You must remain agile, clever, and decisive if you want to outsmart the undead and lead yourself and the survivors to safety. The clock is ticking, and danger lurks at every corner, making every move count in your quest for survival.

How to play free They are all zombies game online

To play "They Are All Zombies," use your keyboard or touch controls to navigate through the environment. Collect items to slow down zombies and make split-second decisions about rescuing survivors. Your goal is to escape while ensuring as many souls remain unturned as possible. The challenge intensifies as you progress, so stay alert!
