Golden Scarabeaus?Golden Scarabeaus

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure with Golden Scarabeaus, where your primary goal is to collect all the golden scarabs scattered across diverse levels. Utilize a team of quirky little square minions, each equipped with unique abilities, to navigate through exciting challenges. These minions are not only charming but also provide different ways to interact with the game environment, making every level a new puzzle to solve.

As you guide your minions, you'll discover that some can float gracefully, while others can roll, jump, or manipulate nearby objects. This diversity means you'll need to choose your minions wisely based on the obstacles they face. Some minions can smash through blocks, providing a direct path through barriers, while others may alter their behavior depending on their surroundings. Additionally, lasers can be deployed to attract or repel your minions simply click to toggle their activation and strategize your approach!

The key to success in Golden Scarabeaus is understanding how to work with the unique abilities of each minion. You'll need to carefully plan your moves to ensure safe passage around hazards and to secure the golden scarabs. This will involve hitting switches in the right order, moving blocks strategically, and leveraging each minion's skills to their fullest potential. Prepare for brain-teasing gameplay that challenges your problem-solving abilities as you work towards completing each level!

How to play free Golden Scarabeaus game online

To play Golden Scarabeaus, simply use your mouse to click on the minions to activate their unique abilities. Navigate through each level by solving puzzles, avoiding hazards, and collecting all the golden scarabs. Adjust the lasers and manipulate objects to help your minions reach their destination safely!
