Experience the enchanting world of Solitaire Story - Tripeaks, a captivating card game that combines the timeless solitaire experience with the excitement of engaging storylines. This game invites players to embark on a journey filled with colorful graphics, unique challenges, and a variety of levels that are sure to keep you entertained for hours.

In Solitaire Story - Tripeaks, you control the game using your mouse on a computer or by tapping on touch screens, making it easily accessible to everyone. The main objective here is to clear as many cards as possible from the board. You achieve this by selecting the face-up cards that are either one rank higher or one rank lower than the last card displayed at the bottom of the screen. Not only will you be working to unravel each level, but you ll also want to form sequences to maximize your score. For instance, if you have a sequence like Ace, 2, 3, and back to Ace, or even a sequence that flows into higher cards like King, Queen, Jack, you ll be setting yourself up for success!

As you progress through the game, new challenges and obstacles will arise, enhancing your gaming experience as you navigate colorful landscapes full of hidden treasures. With every card you flip and level you complete, you'll find yourself drawn deeper into the captivating lore of Solitaire Story - Tripeaks.

How to play free Solitaire Story - Tripeaks game online

To play Solitaire Story - Tripeaks, start by selecting cards that are one rank higher or lower than the card at the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to clear the board by forming long card sequences. Keep an eye on the available cards to maximize your moves and achieve higher scores!
