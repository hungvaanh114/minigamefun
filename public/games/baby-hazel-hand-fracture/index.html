Baby Hazel Hand Fracture

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In this heartwarming and interactive game, Baby Hazel faces an unexpected challenge after slipping while trying to climb onto a table. As she tumbles down, her hand encounters an unfortunate fracture, leaving her in distress. You have the chance to step into the role of a caring friend and help Baby Hazel navigate this tough time.

Accompany Baby Hazel to the doctor s office, where she will receive the medical attention she desperately needs. Your mission is to ensure that she gets the proper treatment while keeping her spirits high. After the doctor s visit, Baby Hazel is left with a plaster on her hand, making it difficult for her to carry out her usual activities, from playing with toys to enjoying her favorite snacks.

Throughout this touching journey, you ll be responsible for attending to Baby Hazel's various requests. This includes helping her complete everyday tasks with limited mobility. Will you be able to provide her the comfort and assistance she needs? Pay careful attention to her expressions and preferences to give her the best care possible, filled with affection and love.

How to play free Baby Hazel Hand Fracture game online

To play the Baby Hazel Hand Fracture game, use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game environment. Follow on-screen prompts to help Baby Hazel with her needs, and complete tasks like feeding, playing, or helping her get dressed. Be attentive to her mood and preferences to ensure she feels loved and cared for.
