To play Temple Run 2: Jungle Fall, simply swipe your finger left or right to navigate through the paths, and swipe up to jump over obstacles. Swipe down to slide under barriers, and collect coins along the way to unlock power-ups and upgrades. Keep running and enjoy the thrill of the chase!
