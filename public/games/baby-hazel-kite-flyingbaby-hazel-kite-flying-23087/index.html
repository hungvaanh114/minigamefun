Baby Hazel is excited to join her father and friends for the much-anticipated Kites Festival. She looks forward to learning the art of kite flying from her dad, but things take an unexpected turn when the mischievous Bunny causes her kite to get stuck in a tree. Baby Hazel feels disheartened, but her dad encourages her to turn this setback into an opportunity by creating a new kite for the festival.

Join Baby Hazel in her quest to design a vibrant new kite! Gather supplies and use your creativity to craft a beautiful kite that s ready to take to the skies. Once you ve created the perfect kite, it s time to head to the festival where her friends are already having a blast flying their own kites. Experience the joy of kite flying together as she reunites with her friends.

This lively adventure is not just about overcoming obstacles; it s also about friendship, creativity, and experiencing the thrill of kite flying. Watch as their kites soar high in the sky, celebrating the excitement and enthusiastic spirit of the festival. Embark on this fun-filled journey with Baby Hazel and make unforgettable memories as you and your friends fly kites together!

How to play free Baby Hazel Kite Flying game online

In the Baby Hazel Kite Flying game, use your mouse to interact with various objects. Follow the on-screen prompts to help Baby Hazel gather materials and create her kite. Once ready, lead her to the festival area where you can control the kite and participate in friendly competitions. Enjoy flying and competing with her friends!
