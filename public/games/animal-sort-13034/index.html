Animal Sort

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exciting adventure with Animal Sort! This engaging game presents you with 30 uniquely challenging levels that will test your matching skills and animal recognition. Each level features a variety of animal faces, and your objective is simple: match the identical animals to progress.

As you navigate through the game, you will encounter different numbers of animal faces, creating a diverse and stimulating gameplay experience. You ll need to use your analytical mind to identify and pair the matching creatures before time runs out. With delightful graphics and enchanting sounds, Animal Sort guarantees hours of fun regardless of your gaming skill level.

As you advance through the levels, the challenges will become increasingly complex, pushing your problem-solving abilities to their limits. Whether you're a casual player or a seasoned gamer, you'll find yourself immersed in the vibrant world of Animal Sort as you aim to complete all levels while having a blast!

How to play free Animal Sort game online

To play the Animal Sort game, simply tap on the animal faces to select them. Match three or more identical animals to clear them from the board. Keep an eye on the remaining faces and try to plan your moves strategically. Use hints if you re stuck to help you along the way!
