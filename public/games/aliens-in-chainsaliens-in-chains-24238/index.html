Aliens in Chains is an exciting new casual game available for free on both desktop and mobile platforms at Superkidgames.com. This thrilling adventure sets you on a mission to track down mischievous aliens who are trying to escape with stolen treasures. Your objective is to cut the chains that connect these evasive creatures, forming powerful combos to effectively capture them. As you navigate through the galaxy, you'll encounter over 700 unique levels, each presenting its own set of challenges.

Throughout your journey, prepare to harness your sharp clicking skills to pop connections and thwart the aliens' escape plans. The gameplay revolves around completing specific objectives that require you to collect a certain number of aliens of a particular type. However, with limited moves at your disposal, strategy is key make every click count! The more points you accumulate while trying to achieve your goals, the better your overall score will be. While it can be tempting to dabble in other alien types, focus on your mission to avoid a premature end to your turns.

Aliens in Chains not only provides a captivating storyline but also challenges players to think critically and act swiftly. Whether you're seeking a relaxing gameplay experience or a test of your quick reflexes, this game promises hours of entertainment. So gear up for an extraordinary cosmic journey, and see how far you can go with just a few clicks!

How to play free Aliens in Chains game online

To begin playing Aliens in Chains, simply click to cut the chains connecting the aliens. Each cut will unleash a combo effect that helps you capture the aliens and complete your objectives while managing your limited moves. Strategically plan your clicks to maximize points and minimize turns!
