How to play free TRZ Pop it game online

To play the TRZ Pop it game online, simply click on each 'button' on the toy displayed on your screen. Each click pops a button, and the goal is to pop all the buttons of the toy to unlock the next one. Enjoy the soothing sound effects and pop your way through all 80 toys!
