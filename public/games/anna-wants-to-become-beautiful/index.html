Anna Wants to Become Beautiful

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the enchanting world of Anna Wants To Become Beautiful, players are invited to join Anna on her heartfelt journey to transform her appearance before an important coronation ceremony for her sister, Elsa. Despite her kind nature and vibrant personality, Anna has always struggled with her looks, a fact that has weighed heavily on her self-esteem. As she matured, her lack of attention to skincare and makeup left her feeling insecure.

With Elsa s upcoming coronation on the horizon, Anna's feelings of inadequacy deepen, as she dreads being the subject of laughter on such a significant day. Witnessing her sister's distress, Elsa takes it upon herself to support Anna and help her achieve the beauty she desires. This intimate sisterly bond makes the experience not only a quest for beauty but also an exploration of love and support between two siblings.

Throughout the game, players will step into the shoes of Elsa as she guides Anna through various beauty treatments and makeup applications. From selecting the perfect products to choosing stylish outfits, players will have the opportunity to explore their creativity and enhance Anna's beauty step by step. It s a lively and engaging adventure that revolves around friendship, self-discovery, and the true meaning of beauty.

How to play free Anna Wants To Become Beautiful game online

To play the game, simply navigate through the different beauty treatments offered. Use your mouse to select skincare products and makeup essentials for Anna. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete each beauty task successfully, transforming Anna into the stunning sister she wants to be for Elsa's coronation!
