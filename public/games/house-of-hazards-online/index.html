House of Hazards Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of House of Hazards Online, a chaotic multiplayer experience that challenges you to tackle everyday tasks while dodging an array of outrageous obstacles. In this absurdly entertaining game, players must navigate the treacherous landscape of a perilous household, where the environment is anything but safe. Prepare yourself for an unpredictable adventure where light fixtures may collapse, sinks might unleash torrents of water, and airborne slices of toast look to take you down!

In the House of Hazards, every seemingly simple chore like brewing coffee, checking the mail, or watering flowers turns into a test of your agility and wit. While you focus on these mundane activities, beware of your fellow players, who are lurking in the shadows, ready to spring their traps. This is a wild competition that blurs the line between friendship and sabotage. The ultimate goal? Successfully complete your tasks and exit the house before becoming a casualty of your roommates' dastardly schemes!

If you're feeling mischievous, you can embrace your inner troublemaker and unleash havoc on your housemates. Fire objects like toasters and lasers, or drop heavy items from above to impede their progress. You even have the option to incapacitate opponents and stow them away in chests. Every moment is an adrenaline-fueled race against yourself and those around you!

How to play free House of Hazards Online game online

To begin playing House of Hazards Online, simply select your character and join a game lobby. Once inside, your goal is to complete various household tasks while avoiding traps set by other players. Use the controls to navigate and interact with objects, while strategically avoiding hazards that might impede your progress.


Instructions

Player 1 Controls A D - Move Change hazard selection W - Jump S - Crouch Grab player Activate hazard Player 2 Controls J L - Move Change hazard selection I - Jump K - Crouch Gr
