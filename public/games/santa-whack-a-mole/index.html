Santa Whack a Mole

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to join the festive fun with Santa Whack a Mole, a thrilling game perfect for friends and family! In this action-packed adventure, your mission is to hit all the mischievous popping monsters that burst forth from their hiding spots. Each successful hit brings you closer to achieving a high score, turning your reflexes into a celebration of points!

Similar to the beloved classic game, Whack A Mole, Santa Whack a Mole offers an engaging twist by incorporating holiday cheer. As you aim to improve your score, you might find Santa Claus himself making a surprise appearance so be on high alert! This unexpected visitor could add a new layer of excitement to your gameplay, encouraging you to stay focused and reactive. Who will emerge as the champion in this festive melee? Challenge your loved ones and see who can dominate the leaderboard in this highly entertaining holiday-themed game.

Gather your family, bring along some friends, and let the whacking commence. With each hit, not only do you rack up points, but you also create unforgettable moments filled with laughter and excitement during the holiday season!

How to play free Santa Whack a Mole game online

To play Santa Whack a Mole, simply click on the popping monsters as they emerge. Use quick reflexes to hit as many as you can within the time limit. Watch for Santa's appearance, as hitting him grants bonus points! Compete against friends or aim for a personal best. Enjoy!
