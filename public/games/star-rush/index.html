Star Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Star Rush, a fast-paced space shooter where daring players take on the Zorkizn, a hostile alien race bent on exploiting Earth's valuable resources. After successfully demolishing a Zorkizn collection center, your mission doesn t end; it s just the beginning. You must swiftly navigate through thrilling environments, evade enemy attacks, and strategize your escape towards the nearest control point.

As you accelerate through the cosmic battlefield, you'll encounter various challenges and formidable foes, each determined to thwart your progress. Master your skills and utilize an array of powerful weaponry to fend off the Zorkizn and protect our planet before they deplete its natural resources. The fate of Earth hangs in the balance, so stay alert, keep your shields up, and don't let your guard down!

In Star Rush, every second counts. Timely decisions and quick reflexes are crucial as you dash through levels filled with obstacles and enemies. Collect power-ups along the way to enhance your abilities and fortify your defenses. Engage in epic battles, unlock new missions, and experience pulse-pounding action as you strive to become the ultimate defender of Earth.

How to play free Star Rush game online

To play Star Rush, use the arrow keys for navigation and the spacebar to fire your weapons. Your primary goal is to dismantle Zorkizn collection centers while avoiding enemy fire. Collect bonuses to enhance your abilities and ensure a smooth escape to the next checkpoint. Good luck!


Instructions

On devices that have keyboards use the left and right keys to move To shoot use the spacebar On mobile devices use the touch on the screen to move and the lower buttons to shoot
