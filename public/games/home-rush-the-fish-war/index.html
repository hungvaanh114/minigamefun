Home Rush the Fish War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Engage in an epic battle between Humans and The Fish! Your goal is simple: drag and hold to move the Human to the perfect spot to defeat The Fish. Watch out for obstacles and tough enemies along the way. Take down The Fish, then return safely to the starting point. But remember, only Humans of the same color can defeat The Fish! Experience exciting levels with increasing challenges, stunning 3D visuals, unique characters, and easy-to-learn controls for all ages. Enjoy the adventure!
