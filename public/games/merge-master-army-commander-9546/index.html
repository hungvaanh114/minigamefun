Merge Master - Army Commander

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the action-packed world of Merge Master - Army Commander, where island wars rage and strategy reigns supreme! Begin your journey with small troop units and merge them to create a more formidable force. Engage in fierce battles, level up your army, and equip your troops for the ultimate fight against powerful adversaries. This game offers a combination of simplicity and excitement, making it a delightful experience filled with thrilling challenges.

Create an unparalleled army featuring massive tanks, lethal airplanes, and a plethora of soldiers as you aim to be the top commander on the battlefield. Merge squads to enhance your strength and obliterate enemy troops along with their bases. Your adventure initiates on a desolate island where you will construct a stunning base to train your soldiers, increase your power, and reclaim the land. Remember, true strength is not solely derived from troops; it's also about crafting a stylish yet resilient island, utilizing a variety of buildings and decorations to showcase your uniqueness.

Become the army commander of your own war island! Grow your troop size by gathering stickmen from the battlefield, integrating them into your commanding team. The larger your stickman army, the greater your advantage in epic wars against Huggy and Wuggy armies, giving you the chance to seize their bases. The game includes features such as building stations, raiding, and looting other islands, along with vibrant graphics and easy controls to enhance your gameplay experience!

How to play free Merge Master - Army Commander game online

To play Merge Master - Army Commander, merge your troops by dragging and combining units in the game interface. Utilize resources to upgrade your base and unlock new battle stations. Engage in battles against other players by deploying combined units strategically, and continue to expand your army by gathering more stickmen from your territory.
