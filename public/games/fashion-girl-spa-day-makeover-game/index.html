Fashion Girl Spa Day - Makeover Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the ultimate pampering experience with the Fashion Girl Spa Day - Makeover Game! In this exciting game, you have the chance to manage your very own beauty salon where fashion meets relaxation. Gather your friends and immerse yourself in the world of spa treatments, where you can cater to a variety of beauty needs for your stylish clients.

Get ready to unleash your creativity as you perform various skincare treatments. From treating pesky acne to meticulously plucking excess eyebrows, this game allows you to take on multiple roles as a beautician. You can also provide shaving services for smooth legs, experiment with vibrant hair dye colors, and help your clients feel their absolute best!

Each girl who visits your salon has her unique preferences and requirements. Ensure you provide customized services to meet everyone's desires. With a broad array of tools and beauty treatments at your disposal, you can easily create a fun and engaging experience for your clients, making them feel relaxed and rejuvenated. The interactive gameplay and delightful graphics will keep you entertained, allowing you to enjoy a day filled with fashion and beauty!

How to play free Fashion Girl Spa Day - Makeover Game online

To start playing the Fashion Girl Spa Day - Makeover Game, simply select a girl to pamper. Use your mouse to click on various beauty tools and treatments to perform the necessary steps. Follow the on-screen instructions to ensure each treatment is completed successfully. Make your clients happy and create their dream makeovers!
