Newborn Unicorn Daycare

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This is a caring unicorn game for girls. We have a lot of fun activities in this educational game.It is tailor-made for those who like to play with unicorns. You have to take care of the health of the pregnant mum, help her with her daily activities, and become her obstetrician. After the baby unicorn is successfully born, you need to bathe it and give it a check-up.
