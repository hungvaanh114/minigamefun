Idle Explorers

**Idle Explorers** is an engaging idle adventure game where you lead a team of explorers on exciting journeys to discover hidden treasures and mysterious artifacts. Upgrade your explorers, unlock new biomes, and collect rewards even while offline. Strategically manage your crew and expand your empire to become the ultimate adventurer!
