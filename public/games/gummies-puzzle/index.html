How to play free Gummies Puzzle game online

To play Gummies Puzzle, simply tap on the gummies to select them, and then connect them by drawing lines between them. Try to form groups of three or more of the same color to clear them from the board. Aim to create green connections for bonus points and discover new challenges at each level!
