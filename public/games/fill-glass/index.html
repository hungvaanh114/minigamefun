Fill Glass

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fill Glass is an engaging and uncomplicated game that tests your precision and pouring skills in an exciting way. Your primary objective is to pour liquid into a glass, achieving the perfect fill level without spilling any over the edges or falling short of your target. The challenge lies in your ability to control the pour accurately, making each attempt a thrilling endeavor.

As you play Fill Glass, you strive to reach the highest highscore possible, which adds an element of competition to your gameplay. The more you play, the more adept you will become at gauging the right amount to pour, turning each could-be mistake into a triumphant correction. With each level, you encounter new challenges that require greater skill and dexterity, keeping you engaged and eager to improve.

To keep the game fresh and exciting, you ll have opportunities to upgrade your pouring machine. These upgrades are designed to improve your precision, allowing you to achieve perfect fills more consistently as you progress through various levels. With every successful pour, you inch closer to mastering the right techniques and obtaining higher scores, creating an addictive cycle of challenge and achievement.

How to play free Fill Glass game online

To enjoy Fill Glass, simply start the game and use your mouse or finger to control the pouring angle and speed. Aim carefully to fill the glass just right, avoiding any spills. As you successfully complete levels, collect points to unlock upgrades that will enhance your pouring abilities!


Instructions

Mobile tap and hold to where you want the liquid to pour to move the dispenser PC Move the dispenser with mouse and click to pour the liquid
