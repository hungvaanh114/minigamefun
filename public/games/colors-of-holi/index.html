Colors of Holi

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

'Colors of Holi' is an exciting themed game that brings to life the vibrant spirit of the Indian festival, Holi. Known as the festival of colors, Holi is a time of joy where people throw colored powder and water at each other while singing cheerful songs and dancing merrily. This game encapsulates that fun-filled atmosphere, allowing players to immerse themselves in the colorful revelry.

In 'Colors of Holi,' you will engage in a colorful challenge where strategy meets excitement. Your mission is simple yet captivating: select a color-pot and place it on a blank tile, aiming to create a visual masterpiece of matching colors. The objective is to align 5 or more similar color-pots in a straight row. As you progress, you ll face overcrowded boards that may hinder your progress. But fear not! The game offers unique power-ups to help you clear the board and make room for new color-pots. The more strategic moves you make, the more fun you will have!

The game continues until you can no longer place any new color-pots on the board, challenging your skills and keeping you engaged with its vibrant gameplay. With each turn, the joyous spirit of Holi infects your screens while you strategize your next move. So get ready to experience the thrill and laughter of this festive game!

How to play free Colors Of Holi game online

To play 'Colors of Holi,' begin by selecting a color-pot and clicking on an empty tile to place it there. Aim to create a line of 5 or more matching color-pots to score points. Use your power-ups wisely to clear the board whenever it gets too crowded. Keep playing until no new color-pots can fit!
