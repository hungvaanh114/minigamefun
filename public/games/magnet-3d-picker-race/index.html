Magnet 3D Picker Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the captivating world of Magnet 3D Picker Race, an exhilarating racing adventure that combines swift vehicle maneuvering with strategic sphere collection. In this thrilling 3D environment, you control a uniquely designed U-shaped Picker vehicle, specifically engineered to gather colorful spheres while weaving through a dynamic racecourse. Your objective is to collect as many spheres as possible, but beware! The track is filled with cunning traps that can hinder your progress and cost you precious time.

As you navigate intricate twists and turns, your reflexes will be put to the test. Use your magnetized Picker to effortlessly attract spheres, demonstrating your skill in maintaining speed while avoiding the perilous obstacles that lie in your path. The more spheres you collect, the higher your score, leading you to the ultimate goal of becoming the master Picker of this vivid world.

With stunning graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics, Magnet 3D Picker Race promises an immersive experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Challenge yourself to beat your previous high scores and unlock new vehicles as you advance. Compete against friends or take on the global leaderboard to see where you rank in the fierce competition of sphere collecting and racing!

How to play free Magnet 3D Picker Race game online

To start playing Magnet 3D Picker Race, simply use your arrow keys or touch controls to steer your U-shaped Picker vehicle. Move it around the track to attract spheres while avoiding traps. Collect as many spheres as you can to boost your score, and enjoy the exhilarating racing experience!
