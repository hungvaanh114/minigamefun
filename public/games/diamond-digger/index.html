Diamond Digger

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Diamond Digger is an exciting online game where your goal is to collect as many sparkling diamonds as possible while avoiding dangerous rocks! Dig through the earth to uncover hidden gems, but be careful one wrong move and you might hit a rock, blocking your path or ending your run. As you progress, the levels become more challenging, with tricky obstacles and special power-ups to help you on your treasure hunt. Do you have what it takes to become the ultimate Diamond Digger? Start digging and find out!

Instructions

Slide on the game screen to play this game or use the arrow keys on your keyboard
