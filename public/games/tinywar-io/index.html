Tinywar.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tinywar.io throws you into a ruthless sword-fighting free-for-all where a single hit means defeat. Fight to unlock all 10 character evolutions and boost your speed with each upgrade. Can you dominate the leaderboard and survive the longest?

Instructions

Move the mouse to guide your character Click left mouse button - Attack Hold right mouse button - Speed Boost
