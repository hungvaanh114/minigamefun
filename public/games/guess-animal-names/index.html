Guess Animal Names

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Discover the delightful world of animals with the engaging game, Guess Animal Names. This educational word puzzle challenges players to identify various animals based on images displayed on the right panel. By honing your spelling skills and animal knowledge, you'll enjoy a fun and interactive experience that promotes learning.

In Guess Animal Names, players must examine the animal's picture and use their knowledge to deduce its name. To complete the spelling, you'll click on the letters provided below the image. But be cautious! Every incorrect letter you select will cost you one of your five lives. Keep your wits about you and try to guess the name before you run out of lives!

As you progress, the game becomes increasingly challenging, introducing a wider variety of animals to guess, and allowing you to expand your vocabulary along the way. It's a perfect blend of fun, education, and excitement, making it suitable for players of all ages. Whether you're playing alone or with friends, Guess Animal Names guarantees to be an entertaining way to learn about the animal kingdom.

How to play free Guess Animal Names game online

To play Guess Animal Names, start by examining the animal image displayed on the right side of the screen. Below the image, you'll see a selection of letters. Click on the ones you believe spell out the animal's name correctly. If you click on the wrong letter, you'll lose one of your five lives, so choose wisely!
