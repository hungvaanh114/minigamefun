Zombie Invasion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chaotic world of Zombie Invasion, an electrifying first-person shooter that plunges players into a relentless battle against hordes of undead. Set in a vibrant yet dangerous environment, you are given the freedom to explore your surroundings whether that s the crumbling streets of The City or the eerie depths of The Forest. Each corner you turn could hold a lurking zombie ready to attack, so stay alert!

Your primary objective is to survive. As you navigate through the environment, you will face relentless zombie assaults. Use quick reflexes to dodge their attacks or take them out with your arsenal of weapons. Every zombie you defeat nets you money, crucial for bolstering your defenses and firepower. Spend your hard-earned cash wisely on new weapons and ammunition; remember, a well-stocked inventory can mean the difference between life and death!

Health management is vital as you fend off the undead. Keep an eye on your health bar, as sustaining injuries can hasten your demise. Ammo conservation is equally important running out of bullets might leave you vulnerable to a zombie swarm! Explore every nook and cranny for supplies and ammunition, but be cautious; danger lurks in every shadow.

With two distinct maps to choose from, The City offers urban challenges, while The Forest immerses players in a more natural yet perilous setting. Your choices will dictate your strategy and survival!

How to play free Zombie Invasion game online

To start playing Zombie Invasion, enter the game and select your preferred map The City or The Forest. Use the arrow keys or WASD for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, making sure to dodge or eliminate zombies quickly for survival!
