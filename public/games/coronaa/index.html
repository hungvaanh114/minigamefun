CoronAA

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In a world grappling with the devastating effects of a relentless virus outbreak, you find yourself on the front lines of a medical battle in CoronAA. You have been infected by the highly dangerous pathogen known as Corona, and it s up to you to neutralize this insidious threat. Armed with your medical spears, you must engage in a high-stakes fight to eradicate the virus before it wreaks havoc in your laboratory.

As you navigate through the game, you ll encounter waves of the virus lurking around every corner, each more vicious than the last. Precision and timing are essential, as missing a shot could lead to catastrophic consequences. If you fail to hit your target, the virus will explode, potentially causing irreversible damage to your lab. With vivid graphics and compelling gameplay, CoronAA offers an adrenaline-pumping experience where every decision counts, and your skills are put to the ultimate test.

Prepare to use your wits and reflexes as you strategize the best approach to eliminate the virus. Collect power-ups along the way to enhance your spears and increase your chances of success. Only the bravest will conquer the challenge, so gear up and take back control in this vital mission against Corona!

How to play free CoronAA game online

To play CoronAA online, start by launching the game interface. Use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the virus targets. Click or tap to throw your medical spears. Remember, accuracy is crucial if you miss, the virus will explode! Stay alert and keep attacking to cleanse the lab!
