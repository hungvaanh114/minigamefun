Welcome to the thrilling world of Supra Crash Shooting Fly Cars 2022, where you embark on an exhilarating journey across diverse landscapes! In this game, not only will you race against intelligent and cunning opponents, but you also have the freedom to explore the globe and accumulate experience points (XP) through various races. Use your hard-earned XP to unlock cosmetic upgrades or new cars ranging in difficulty levels. Investing in specific maps will also be essential for advancing in your chosen career path. Keep in mind that your drifting skills will greatly influence how much experience you gain.

Prepare for a challenging road ahead! Each racing circuit tests your abilities, demanding precision handling and strategic speed management. If you ease up even slightly, your agile enemies whether in cars or on foot will be right on your tail, ready to overtake you. With stunning visuals and brilliantly designed terrains, every race is an immersive experience. Practice drifting to refine your skills, but beware: crashing into barriers may set you back significantly. Unlock a variety of features, including 17 distinct colors, six vehicle types, and seven intricate courses to enhance your gameplay experience.

The gameplay mechanics are centered around drifting and controlling your vehicle on challenging courses. Each racetrack is designed to keep you on your toes with unexpected twists and turns. Nitro boosts are available to give you that extra advantage, and your XP will depend on your race performance and drifting prowess. Expect to face tougher competitors as you progress, pushing you to improve your techniques and strategize your races effectively. Remember, you can enjoy reasonably accurate physics without strict rules perfecting your drift is key!
