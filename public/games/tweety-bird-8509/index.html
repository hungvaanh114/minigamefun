Tweety Bird

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the zany universe of Looney Tunes with the beloved character, Tweety Bird! In this exciting action RPG, you can team up with iconic characters such as Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, and Marvin the Martian to form the ultimate "Toon Team." Immerse yourself in a bright and dynamic world where you can collect a plethora of classic characters like Taz, Road Runner, and Tweety, each boasting unique and funny fighting styles.

Engage in outrageous battles as you harness the distinctive abilities of your favorite Looney Tunes characters. From wacky slapstick attacks to strategic turn-based combat, every battle is a laugh-inducing thrill. Dive into classic rivalries like Sylvester vs. Tweety or Wile E. Coyote vs. Road Runner, gaining bonuses for taking down your long-time foes. The quirky gameplay allows you to unleash outlandish cartoon gags, such as dropping an ACME safe on Daffy or bringing down a giant Anvil on Elmer Fudd, making every encounter a riot!

Challenge players in PvP matches and compete for crates filled with rewards and power-ups that can enhance your gaming experience. The adventure continues as you send your beloved characters on missions to gather vital resources to help you strengthen your Toon Team. Download Looney Tunes World of Mayhem today and collect, battle, and climb the ranks to become the true master of mayhem!

How to play free Tweety Bird game online

To play the Tweety Bird game online, simply download Looney Tunes World of Mayhem from your app store. Once in the game, start by collecting characters and assembling your Toon Team. Engage in turn-based battles, deploy your character s special attacks, and participate in missions to gather resources and power-ups for your teams!
