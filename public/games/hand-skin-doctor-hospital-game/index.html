Hand Skin Doctor - Hospital Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the world of Hand Skin Doctor - Hospital Game, where you step into the shoes of a talented medical professional. The case before you is urgent: a hand in dire condition that requires your expert care. This hand looks dreadful and needs your medical skills to restore it to its former beauty. From injuries to skin infections, every layer and detail of the hand demands your attention. Put on your gloves, gather your tools, and get ready to diagnose and treat this problematic hand!

In this exciting hospital game, you will encounter a range of medical scenarios that will test your abilities. You ll get to use various medical equipment and techniques, like cleaning wounds, applying ointments, and possibly even performing minor surgeries. Your goal is to analyze the condition of the hand, take the appropriate steps to heal it, and watch as it transforms back to a pretty and healthy state. Each level brings new challenges, so your medical expertise and quick decision-making will be essential. Are you ready to accept the challenge and prove you can handle the pressure of a real-life doctor?

As you work to restore this hand, you ll also learn valuable insights into basic hand anatomy and common skin problems, making the game not only fun but educational! So, roll up your sleeves and get started on the path toward healing!

How to play free Hand Skin Doctor - Hospital Game game online

To play Hand Skin Doctor - Hospital Game, simply follow the on-screen instructions to use the tools provided. Begin by diagnosing the problem with the hand, then select the appropriate tools to treat each issue. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game elements and complete each stage of treatment accurately.
