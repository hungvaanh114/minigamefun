Grand City Car Thief

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Grand City Car Thief, where you step into the thrilling life of an intrepid car thief roaming the bustling streets of a sprawling metropolis. In this immersive game, you will navigate through crowded urban environments, evading persistent law enforcement while executing daring heists. Your ultimate objective is to liberate high-end vehicles and chisel them away to your private garage, all while mastering the art of stealth and speed.

As you delve deeper into the game, you'll discover a vibrant city filled with diverse automobiles, each presenting a unique challenge and opportunity. Your skills will be put to the test as you identify your targets, plan your escape routes, and decide on the best methods to steal the cars without attracting too much attention. The city is rife with surprises a high-speed pursuit might ensue at any moment, requiring quick reflexes and strategic thinking on your part.

But beware! The police are always on the lookout for suspicious activity, and they won't hesitate to chase you down the streets. Use your wits and agility to evade capture, and remember to upgrade your driving skills and choose the right vehicles to aid in your daring escapades. The thrill of the chase and the satisfaction of a successful heist await you! Are you ready to become the ultimate car thief in Grand City?

How to play free Grand City Car Thief game online

To play Grand City Car Thief, start by selecting your character and then choose a vehicle from the numerous cars available in the city. Once you're in control, navigate through the streets, approach parked cars, and initiate the stealing process. Be quick to escape and drive the stolen vehicles to your garage while avoiding the police!


Instructions

WASD - Move Space - Jump F - Enter car C - Change camera
