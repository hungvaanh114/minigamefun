Ninja Clash Heroes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Ninja Clash Heroes, where you lead a team of elite heroes on a mission to safeguard the cherished Fortune Cat. Choose from a diverse roster of characters, each with unique skills, including a stealthy Ninja Scout, a heavily-armored Samurai, a wise Buddhist Monk, or a sharpshooting Sniper Geisha. Each hero caters to different playstyles, ensuring that you will find one that suits your preferences.

The game unfolds in a stunning oriental garden, rich in detail and beauty. However, as the enemy is determined to seize the Fortune Cat, your focus must remain sharp, and your reflexes swift. Dive into fast-paced combat and strategic gameplay where timing and coordination with your squad play a crucial role in your success.

As you progress, you'll unlock powerful abilities associated with your chosen class. Collect stars throughout your missions to open crates filled with incredible weapons and exciting boosters, enhancing your combat prowess. Upgrade your character to improve their skills further, earn medals for your triumphs, and strive to achieve high scores. Whether you're playing solo or in multiplayer mode, the excitement is boundless as you experience the action-packed adventure of Ninja Clash Heroes.

How to play free Ninja Clash Heroes game online

To play Ninja Clash Heroes, select your hero and join a match. Use WASD keys for movement, mouse for aiming and shooting, and access your hero's special abilities with designated keys. Communicate with your team to coordinate attacks and protect the Fortune Cat efficiently!


Instructions

WASD - movement SPACE - jump G - throw grenade C - crouch RMB - aim TAB - score table
