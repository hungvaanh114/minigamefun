Wickey Adventure

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Wickey Adventure is a skill game where you control a cute boy called Wickey and eat all the delicious candies in a dangerous obstacle course. Hop on and off platforms while avoiding rapidly increasing deadly traps that appear out of nowhere. Dont forget to share Poor Wickey with your friends! How long can you survive in this cute but deadly high-score-driven game?

Instructions

Move around - WASD or Arrow keys
