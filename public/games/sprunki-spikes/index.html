Sprunki Spikes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sprunki Spikes is a fun adventure game thats exciting to play. Here is our little color box hero, who has been stuck in the middle of a whole lot of traps. Help him dodge them and navigate through the challenging environment. There are really fast-paced obstacles passing by keep away from them and survive as long as you can. This game will boost your reflexes as you deal with the hardest objects moving toward him. The dynamic and engaging gameplay ensures an adrenaline-pumping experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Join the adventure and see how long you can last!
