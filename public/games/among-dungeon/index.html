How to play free Among Dungeon game online

In Among Dungeon, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and avoid enemies. Collect items to boost your score and stay alert for moving hazards as you navigate through challenging mazes. Aim to reach the end of each level safely!
