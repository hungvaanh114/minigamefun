In "My New Poodle Friend - Pet Care Game," you'll dive into an adorable adventure centered around Sofia and her upcoming birthday. Jack has a wonderful surprise planned a fluffy poodle! Sofia has a deep love for animals and has always dreamed of caring for a pet dog in her yard. But before that dream can come true, she must get everything ready for her new furry friend.

Your journey begins with Sofia as she prepares to welcome her poodle. This involves repairing and decorating the old pet house, making it a cozy and inviting home for the playful pup. You'll get to explore various activities, such as collecting tools to fix the pet house and choosing vibrant decorations to create a playful environment. As you engage in these tasks, you'll experience the joy that comes with preparing for a new pet.

Join Sofia in her delightful quest to set up the perfect space for her birthday surprise. With interactive gameplay, beautiful graphics, and fun sound effects, you'll be completely engrossed in the world of pet care. Can you help Sofia get everything ready before her big day? Be a part of this heartwarming story and help make her birthday memorable!

How to play free My New Poodle Friend - Pet Care Game online

To start playing, simply click on the game link and follow the on-screen instructions. Use your mouse or tap the screen to interact with objects, gather necessary items, and repair the pet house. Complete each task by dragging and dropping items where needed to ensure Sofia is ready for her new poodle friend!
