If you dream of becoming a champion on the battlefield, then Tower Squad is the perfect game for you! This exciting puzzle game invites players to engage in epic combat against a myriad of foes and challenging towers. Your journey begins by dragging and dropping your chosen hero into the fray, where strategy and skill will dictate your success.

As you engage in battle, there are opportunities to earn unique skins and weapons that enhance your abilities and add flair to your gameplay. Tower Squad features an impressive array of over one hundred towers and a diverse selection of enemies, ensuring that no two battles are the same. To emerge victorious, you'll need to utilize your arithmetic skills, as well as your tactical prowess, to outsmart your opponents and seize the day.

The game cleverly combines elements of action, strategy, and warfare within its intricate puzzle mechanics, providing a challenging yet rewarding experience for players of all ages. Collect cash as you progress, allowing you to upgrade your heroes and improve your chances of beating the seemingly unstoppable enemies that stand in your way. Are you ready to embrace the challenge and prove your heroism in Tower Squad?

How to play free Tower Squad game online

To play Tower Squad online, simply drag your hero onto the battlefield to engage your enemies. Utilize strategic placements to send waves of attacks while defending your towers. Keep an eye on your resources, collect cash, and upgrade your heroes to enhance your combat abilities as you clear each level!
