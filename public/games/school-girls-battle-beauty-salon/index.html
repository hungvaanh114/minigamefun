School Girls Battle Beauty Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the charm and excitement in School Girls Battle Beauty Salon, a delightful casual simulation game where friendship and fashion collide! Follow the journey of Kristen and Elle, two best friends navigating the ups and downs of school life. Known for their stunning makeup and stylish clothing, these girls have always been the envy of their classmates. However, a recent conflict over a boy has put a strain on their once-unbreakable bond. Can you step in and help mend their friendship?

In this engaging game, you ll immerse yourself in a colorful world where creativity knows no bounds. Customize the girls looks with an array of makeup options, hairstyles, and fashionable outfits. Each decision you make can influence their relationship and impact the way they face challenges that come their way. The vibrant graphics and entertaining gameplay create a captivating experience that captures the essence of youthful friendship and rivalry.

As you progress, encounter fun mini-games and challenges that will test your skills while allowing Kristen and Elle to express their unique personalities. It's all about teaching them the importance of communication and forgiveness, reminding players of the value of friendship. So jump in and see if you can restore their sisterly bond while showcasing your stylistic flair!

How to play free School Girls Battle Beauty Salon game online

To play School Girls Battle Beauty Salon, simply select your favorite character between Kristen and Elle. Use the on-screen tools to apply makeup, choose outfits, and accessorize. Follow prompts to complete various beauty challenges and make decisions that help repair their friendship. Enjoy the journey of self-expression!
