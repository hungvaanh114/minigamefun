Drift City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating drift simulation experience with Drift City! This thrilling game invites players to unleash chaos across the city streets as they engage in intense drifting battles. You'll burn rubber and rack up drift points while navigating through the bustling metropolis. The ultimate goal? To rise to the status of drift king by utilizing the points you earn to purchase a variety of new cars.

As you immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Drift City, you'll have the opportunity to showcase your driving skills and gain fame by unlocking an impressive collection of 30 achievements. Keep your eyes peeled for drift bonuses scattered throughout the city during special timeframes; these limited bonuses can significantly boost your drift score. With the option to switch between four different camera perspectives, you can personalize your gameplay and enjoy the simulation like never before.

With a roster of six unique cars available for purchase, the drifting excitement continues to escalate. Each vehicle offers its own distinctive handling and performance traits, allowing you to choose the perfect ride for your drifting style. Get behind the wheel and transform into a nightmare for nearby vehicles as you skillfully maneuver through traffic, leaving a trail of smoke and excitement in your wake!

How to play free Drift City game online

To play Drift City online, simply select your car and navigate around the city using the arrow keys or controller. Perform drifts to earn points, collect bonuses for extra scores, and complete achievements to unlock new vehicles. Master the art of drifting and enjoy the adrenaline rush!
