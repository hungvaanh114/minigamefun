Pixel Zombie Survival

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the thrilling and action-packed realm of Pixel Zombie Survival, where you ll face relentless waves of blocky zombies in a captivating pixelated universe. As a highly trained special forces soldier, your mission demands precision and quick reflexes as you strategize on how to eliminate every incoming zombie while ensuring your own survival against the clock.

The game offers a rich variety of weapons at your disposal, allowing you to customize your offensive approach. Whether you prefer wielding powerful rifles, shotguns, or even explosives, it's crucial to choose the right weapon to maximize your firepower against these pixelated foes. With vivid graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics, every moment in this blocky world is filled with adrenaline as you fight for survival amidst the relentless chaos.

As time ticks down, you'll need to make swift decisions and effectively use the environment around you to your advantage. Explore vast terrains, seek shelter, and keep an eye out for ammo and health packs to stay in fighting shape. The challenge increases progressively, offering an exhilarating experience as you seek to score the highest and last as long as possible against the near-endless horde of zombies!

How to play free Pixel Zombie Survival game online

To play Pixel Zombie Survival, simply use the WASD keys for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot at the approaching zombies. Make sure to switch your weapons as needed and keep an eye on your ammo supply. The goal is to eliminate as many zombies as possible before the timer runs out!


Instructions

WASD - Move LMB - Shoot RMB - Aim Mouse Wheel - Change weapon R - Reload Space - Jump P - Pause G - Grenade
