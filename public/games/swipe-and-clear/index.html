Swipe and Clear

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Swipe and Clear, an exciting and captivating puzzle game that challenges you to employ your strategic thinking! In this vibrant game, you will encounter a grid filled with an assortment of colorful blocks. Your main objective is straightforward: by dragging and moving these blocks, you aim to create matches of three or more blocks of the same color, causing them to vanish into thin air.

However, tread carefully! If you leave even a single block of any color behind by the time you finish, brace yourself for a level restart. Every decision counts, and careful planning is essential to completely clear all the blocks from the grid and advance through a total of 20 uniquely crafted levels. The thoughtful mechanics paired with challenging puzzles ensure that Swipe and Clear keeps your brain engaged and entertained for hours.

With its eye-catching graphics, intuitive gameplay, and a variety of levels designed to progressively increase in difficulty, this game promises a thrilling experience for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages. Are you ready to put your skills to the test and swipe your way to success?

How to play free Swipe and Clear game online

To play Swipe and Clear, simply click and drag to move blocks in any direction. Your goal is to line up three or more matching blocks of the same color. Make strategic moves to clear the grid completely, but remember, you must not leave any single block behind to avoid restarting the level!
