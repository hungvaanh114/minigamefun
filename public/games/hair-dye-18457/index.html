How to play free Hair Dye game online

To start playing the Hair Dye game, enter the salon and choose your client. Select a hairstyle, then pick your desired hair dye color. Adjust the hair color intensity and add fun decorations to complete the look. Once you're satisfied, save your creation and serve your delighted customers!
