Portal Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Portal Game, an exhilarating and visually captivating puzzle adventure crafted by a dedicated fan of the original Portal series. In this immersive experience, you find yourself trapped within an experimental laboratory teeming with obstacles, laser beams, and a variety of challenges. Your mission is to navigate through these trials using wit and strategy.

Throughout the game, you'll encounter a myriad of puzzles that require you to lift heavy cubes, activate buttons, and skillfully manage the dangers posed by hostile turrets. The key to success lies in your ability to create and manipulate portals, allowing you to transport yourself and objects across the complex environment. Use your surroundings to your advantage by moving cubes to positions where they can assist you, and carefully channel the portals to bypass traps and reach the vital exit door.

In Portal Game, every level presents a unique challenge that tests your problem-solving skills and creative thinking. As you venture deeper into the laboratory, the obstacles become more intricate, making each victory even more satisfying. With stunning graphics and engaging gameplay, this puzzle game promises endless hours of entertainment.

How to play free Portal Game online

To play Portal Game, use your mouse or keyboard to navigate through each level. Lift cubes by clicking and move around to press buttons. Create portals by aiming your cursor and clicking to place them strategically. Dodge turret attacks while figuring out puzzle solutions to reach the exit door!
