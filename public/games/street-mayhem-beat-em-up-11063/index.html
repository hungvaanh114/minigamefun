Street Mayhem: Beat Em Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the adrenaline-fueled world of Street Mayhem: Beat 'Em Up, where danger lurks at every corner. This exciting adventure pits you against a varied cast of villains, including notorious bad guys and menacing mutants, in an array of immersive locations. Band together with your allies, Edward, Jennifer, and Russell, each equipped with their unique abilities and fighting styles, to navigate the treacherous streets.

As you progress through the game, you aim to score points that will allow you to unlock new characters, specifically Russell and Jennifer, enriching your gameplay experience. Your journey takes you through a gritty narrative that unfolds in several unique environments, including the perilous Dark City, the eerie Forest, the bustling City Town, and culminates in an epic showdown at the formidable Castle.

In addition to the story mode, Street Mayhem: Beat 'Em Up also offers a thrilling 2 PLAYER mode. Challenge your friends to classic Street Mayhem duels, where the first to win three rounds is crowned the champion! Each match is a test of skill and strategy, making it an electrifying experience for both players.

How to play free Street Mayhem: Beat Em Up game online

To play Street Mayhem: Beat 'Em Up, use the arrow keys to move your character and spacebar to attack. Combine different keys for powerful combos. Earn points through your fights to unlock additional characters and progress through each level. Team up with a friend for epic 2 PLAYER battles!
