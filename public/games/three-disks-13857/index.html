Three Disks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Three Disks, an engaging and colorful puzzle game that will challenge your reflexes and precision! In this captivating experience, players are tasked with controlling three distinct disks as they traverse through their designated circular orbits. The disks glide gracefully in unison, presenting a unique twist on traditional gameplay.

As if this wasn t intriguing enough, the challenge escalates when colorful balls begin to emerge from the center of the circles, each matching the vibrant hues of the disks. Your objective is straightforward yet demanding: align the corresponding disk with each incoming ball that bears the same color. However, a word of caution: these disks can only navigate within their orbits. Therefore, timing and strategic movement are essential to ensure that you catch the balls accurately.

All it takes is a single mistake catching a ball with a mismatched disk and it's game over! The game requires both focus and quick thinking, as the speed of the incoming balls increases, adding to the level of difficulty. So, are you ready to test your skills and see how many balls you can catch? It s an exhilarating challenge that will keep you entertained for hours!

How to play free Three Disks game online

To play Three Disks, simply tap the screen to control the disks. They will shift synchronously along their circular paths. Watch for the colored balls and position the matching disk to catch them. Stay alert and avoid misalignment, as catching a ball with the wrong disk ends the game!
