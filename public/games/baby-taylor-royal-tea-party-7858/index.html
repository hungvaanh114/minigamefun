Baby Taylor Royal Tea Party

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Taylor as she prepares for an enchanting afternoon in the delightful game, Baby Taylor Royal Tea Party! Taylor, a young girl with a passion for delectable treats, looks forward to her daily afternoon tea. This little affair is her favorite moment of the day, filled with laughter and scrumptious snacks. Today, she has an exciting plan to host her friend Jessica for a fabulous tea party at her home.

As the clock ticks closer to tea time, Taylor needs your assistance to make everything perfect! Help her whip up some mouth-watering delicacies that will wow Jessica. From pastries to finger sandwiches, each dish must be prepared with care and creativity. The kitchen is bustling with activity, so your culinary skills will come in handy to help Taylor bake, mix, and decorate delicious treats that look irresistible.

Once the food is ready, it s time to set the scene! Help Taylor decorate the dining area with delightful tea party essentials, including fancy tablecloths, pretty teacups, and vibrant flowers. Make sure everything looks inviting and charming to create the perfect atmosphere for an unforgettable afternoon. Your attention to detail will ensure that Jessica is impressed as soon as she arrives.

This is more than just cooking; it's about creating memories with friends over food. So get ready for laughter, joy, and the sweet taste of friendship as you enjoy every moment of this lovely tea party with Taylor!
