Game on Cat vs Rats

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating adventure in the game Game On: Cat vs Rats, where courageous cats rise to challenge their most dreaded foe the insidious Rat King! This formidable opponent has wreaked havoc on their peaceful lives, and the time has come for these feline heroes to take action. It's hard to believe how a rat could grow to such an enormous size, but one thing is certain: the cats are fed up with the chaos that the Rat King has unleashed.

In a fierce battle for supremacy, the cats are determined to thwart the Rat King's malevolent schemes. Armed with a trusty slingshot, these daring warriors launch themselves at their towering enemy, aiming for the Rat King's face! Your mission is to navigate through various levels and strategically launch the brave cats toward their nemesis to defeat him. With each successful attempt, you'll gain the satisfaction of seeing the Rat King take damage while enjoying the humorous antics of the heroic cats.

Get ready to strategize and plan as you progress through challenging obstacles and crafty rat minions. The gameplay is not just about brute force, but also requires precision and timing to ensure the cats land right where they need to be to deliver a critical hit. Are you up for the challenge? Join the cats in their mission, and together, let s rid the world of the Rat King for good!

How to play free Game On: Cat vs Rats game online

To play the game, use your mouse or finger to aim and adjust the power of your slingshot. Launch the cats at the Rat King by releasing the slingshot when you're ready. Aim for the king's face and hit him strategically to complete each level and move on to the next challenge!
