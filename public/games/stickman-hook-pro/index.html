Stickman Hook Pro

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stickman Hook Pro is a thrilling and engaging skill-based platformer developed by Mini Duck Games, where players must utilize their grappling hook to navigate through vibrant landscapes filled with exciting challenges. Swing from point to point, gather an array of carrots, and embark on a quest to rescue your adorable mouse companions.

In this captivating adventure, your primary objective is to reach the den of the menacing wolf, where your friends are trapped in a mousetrap. As you traverse the lush green lands, prepare to demonstrate your ninja-like agility and precision. The unique grappling mechanics allow you to launch yourself with great skill, using your trusty rocket ninja cannon to gain soaring heights and dive into perilous situations.

Throughout your journey, you'll encounter various obstacles, including treacherous spikes and tricky traps that test your reflexes. Collect valuable carrots and sparkling crystals that will not only enhance your gameplay experience but will also aid you in the noble mission of rescuing all the little mice from danger. Each level presents new challenges, keeping the gameplay fresh and exhilarating, providing endless entertainment for players of all ages.

How to play free Stickman Hook Pro game online

To play Stickman Hook Pro, simply use your mouse or touch controls to aim and launch your stickman at various points throughout the levels. Swing gracefully by timing your jumps and grappling movements. Collect carrots and evade spikes while racing against time to rescue all the mice trapped in the wolf's lair.


Instructions

Use grappling hook - Tap or hold down the left mouse button
