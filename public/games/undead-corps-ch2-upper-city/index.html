Undead Corps - CH2. Upper City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of "Undead Corps - CH2: Upper City," you step into the shoes of a skilled agent tasked with a perilous mission. As a member of the prestigious intertemporal organization known as the "Undead Corps," your objective is clear: locate and eradicate the infamous self-proclaimed "Lord of Undead." This formidable foe is notorious for dabbling in the forbidden and dark art of Necromancy, raising an army of the undead to do his bidding.

Set against a backdrop of a sprawling urban landscape filled with peril at every turn, the Upper City presents an array of challenges. You ll navigate through menacing alleys while facing various undead creatures, each with its unique abilities. Unravel the mysteries of this haunted metropolis and collect powerful artifacts that will aid you in your quest. Use your intelligence, combat skills, and a variety of weapons to fend off the ghoulish enemies that threaten your mission.

Throughout your journey, align yourself with allies who share your goal of putting an end to the "Lord of Undead." Engage in high-stakes battles, solve intricate puzzles, and explore the rich lore of the city to uncover crucial information that will lead you to your target. Are you up for the challenge? The secrets of the Upper City await, and your skills as an agent will determine the outcome of this epic confrontation between life and the animated dead.

How to play free Undead Corps - CH2. Upper City game online

To play "Undead Corps - CH2: Upper City," simply navigate the game interface to explore the city, interact with NPCs, and engage in combat. Use WASD keys to move, the mouse to aim and shoot, and access your inventory to utilize items and weapons you'll collect along the way.
