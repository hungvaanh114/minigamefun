Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the Zoo Animal Hotel, where you will embark on an exciting cleaning adventure that promises to be unique and engaging! In this game, your mission is to make a fabulous impression on potential guests by ensuring that every corner of the animal hotel sparkles and shines. Dive into the fun by starting with the dress-up activity for our adorable tiger cubs, or choose your own order; the choice is in your hands! What s essential is that you diligently complete all tasks, transforming the hotel into a vibrant and welcoming space.

Unleash your creativity as you design a distinct style for the tigers, utilizing a variety of wacky accessories like colorful hats or oversized sunglasses. The whimsical nature of these outfits adds a delightful twist to the game! Additionally, you will engage in essential cleaning duties at both the guest rooms and the reception area. Work alongside your cute animal companions a dog and a rabbit to provide top-notch service. Your cleaning tasks involve wiping away dust, scrubbing floors, and tidying up other messy spots to create an immaculate environment for your guests.

How to play free Zoo Animal Hotel game online

To play the Zoo Animal Hotel game online, simply use your mouse to navigate through the tasks. Click on the items to clean or dress up the animals. You will move through different levels, completing cleaning chores and ensuring everything is in perfect order. Have fun making the hotel shine!
