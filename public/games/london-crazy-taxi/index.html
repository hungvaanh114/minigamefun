London Crazy Taxi

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to rev your engines and experience the thrilling ride of London Crazy Taxi, a revolutionary open-world cab simulator! In this exhilarating modern taxi game, you'll unleash your inner speedster as you navigate through bustling city streets. Your mission is to pick up and drop off passengers as quickly as possible. The crazier you drive, the more rewards you'll earn, so don't hold back!

In London Crazy Taxi, the rules are yours to bend. Speed through traffic, execute slick drifts, and tackle corners like a pro. With a wide range of both modern and classic cabs available for customization, you can build a fleet that reflects your style. Each vehicle can be fully personalized, ensuring that every ride is as unique as the city you re driving in!

Embark on a wild adventure across a massive and dynamic 3D cityscape where the world is your playground. Not only does this taxi simulator offer endless fun, but it also serves as an educational tool, teaching you essential driving rules and techniques along the way. As a crazy taxi driver, you re not just providing a service; you re transforming the way citizens experience transportation in your vibrant metropolitan area.

How to play free London Crazy Taxi game online

To play London Crazy Taxi, simply use your keyboard or game controller to navigate your cab through the streets. Pick up passengers by driving close to them, and then follow the highlighted routes to drop them off at their destination as quickly as possible. The faster and crazier the drive, the higher your score!
