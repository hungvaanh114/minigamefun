Nutrition School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Nutrition School, an exciting and interactive gaming experience that transforms the way kids learn about nutrition! This engaging game is designed to make understanding healthy eating not only informative but also enjoyable. Through various activities, children will explore the fascinating world of nutrition while developing essential habits for a healthier lifestyle.

Nutrition School features four essential activities that guide players through their nutritional journey. First, kids will dive into the learning module, where they can discover different types of healthy foods and their benefits. They can then put their newfound knowledge into practice by shopping for nutritious items, giving them the chance to make smart food choices for their characters.

Feeding their character is another exciting part of the game, as children have the opportunity to apply what they've learned in a fun and interactive way. Finally, players can test their nutrition knowledge through engaging quizzes that reward them with game coins. These coins can be used for various in-game enhancements, with the option for in-app purchases to obtain additional coins if desired.

With its colorful graphics and captivating gameplay, Nutrition School ensures that kids look forward to learning about healthy eating every day. By integrating education and fun, this game empowers children to take charge of their nutrition while cultivating lifelong healthy habits.

How to play free Nutrition School game online

To play the Nutrition School game, start by selecting your character and navigating through the different activities. Click on the food items to learn about them and then proceed to the shopping section. Purchase nutritious items, feed your character, and answer quiz questions to earn coins!
