Christmas is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate the season than with the delightful Xmas Bubble Shooter? This classic bubble shooter game immerses players in the holiday spirit with its festive theme, offering a delightful twist on a beloved gaming genre. Boasting 36 challenging levels, each presents a unique test of skill and strategy. Your primary objective is to clear the board by shooting colorful lights, ensuring all lights are eliminated from the screen before time runs out.

As you progress through each level, you'll encounter various challenges that require quick thinking and precision. To successfully eliminate lights, group them by color; a minimum of three matching lights ensures their removal. The more efficiently you complete a level, the higher your score will be, rewarding you for your swiftness in gameplay. Can you manage to clear the board in record time while enjoying the jolly atmosphere of this Xmas-themed adventure?

With each level presenting new obstacles and a delightful array of colorful lights, Xmas Bubble Shooter promises hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned bubble shooter or a newcomer to the game, the blend of festive cheer and engaging gameplay makes for an unforgettable holiday experience. Challenge your friends or enjoy solo fun as you attempt to become the ultimate bubble shooter champion this Christmas!

How to play free Xmas Bubble Shooter game online

To start playing, aim your cursor where you want to shoot the lights. Click to fire a light from your launcher towards the cluster on the screen. Aim to connect with at least three lights of the same color to make them disappear. Keep an eye on the time limit and clear all lights to complete the level!
