Experience the thrill of Flipping Bottle, a captivating game inspired by the iconic antics of Tom and Jerry! Get ready to challenge your skills as you aim to flip your bottle into the air and achieve the perfect landing on an array of platforms. Each level brings its own set of obstacles and excitement, including moments when you'll need to flip the bottle twice to progress. The fun doesn t stop there; when your bottle lands on a TV, get ready for a delightful surprise!

As you dive into the game, you'll explore a variety of enchanting scenes, each filled with unique objects like shelves, sofas, chairs, tables, stereos, and those eye-catching TVs. Mastering the distance and timing of your flips is essential as you navigate through these diverse environments, putting your agility and coordination to the test. The ultimate goal? Conquer the challenge and reach the finish line! But beware, as the competition is fierce; few have managed to complete 10 levels in a single run. Will you be the first?
