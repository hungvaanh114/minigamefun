Prankster 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prankster 3D is an exciting and engaging free online puzzle game designed especially for kids. Players will find themselves immersed in a lively environment where they must solve riddles and gather clues to locate the hidden textbook. The objective is to answer various questions accurately and find the key to escape from the room before the prankster catches you off guard.

Strategically using the mini-map located in the upper right corner is crucial for enhancing your gameplay experience. This tool enables you to track the prankster s movements and helps you gauge their proximity. It's essential to remain alert, as the prankster can appear unexpectedly, adding an element of surprise and thrill to the game. As you navigate through different challenges, you'll need to employ your problem-solving skills to outsmart the prankster while ensuring you find the necessary items to succeed.

With its innovative gameplay and playful design, Prankster 3D provides a fun and interactive way for kids to develop critical thinking abilities and spatial awareness. As players progress through various levels, they are met with increasing difficulties that stimulate their minds and encourage teamwork if played in groups. Are you ready to outsmart the prankster and escape the room? Join the fun and test your skills in Prankster 3D!

How to play free Prankster 3D game online

To play Prankster 3D, start by exploring the room to locate the textbook while keeping an eye on the prankster's movements. Use the hints provided to answer questions and unlock the key. Employ the mini-map wisely to track your opponent s location, and remember to stay stealthy to avoid getting caught!

If you play on a PC WASD or click the mouse to move If you play on a mobile phone Click buttons to move
