Liquid Sort

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Liquid Sort is an engaging logical puzzle game that challenges your problem-solving abilities. The premise is straightforward yet captivating: you must sort various liquids housed in test tubes. Once you start, you'll have the option to select your preferred difficulty level easy, medium, or hard. This customization allows you to enjoy the game suited to your skill level, providing a personalized and enjoyable experience.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter multiple levels filled with test tubes brimming with liquids in an array of vibrant colors. Your primary objective is to segregate and organize the liquid, ensuring that each tube contains only one color. To initiate the sorting process, simply pour the liquid from one test tube into another, always aiming to match the colors. Tapping into your strategic thinking will be essential as you navigate through the challenges.

With a total of 100 meticulously designed levels, Liquid Sort promises countless hours of entertainment. Each level gradually increases in complexity, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting. The game not only tests your logic and concentration but also provides a satisfying experience as you successfully achieve each sorting task.

How to play free Liquid Sort game online

To play Liquid Sort, begin by selecting your desired difficulty level. Once in the game, observe the colored liquids in the test tubes. Click on a tube to pour its contents into an empty tube or a tube of the same color. Your aim is to sort all liquids until each tube contains only one color.
