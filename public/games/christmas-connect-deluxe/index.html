Christmas Connect Deluxe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the festive spirit with Christmas Connect Deluxe, a delightful puzzle experience that brings a holiday twist to the classic Chinese game of Mahjong. Your primary objective is to clear the board by eliminating all the tiles. The captivating gameplay engages players as they search for matching tiles hidden among an array of colorful designs.

Each level presents a new challenge, requiring keen observation and strategic thinking. To eliminate two identical tiles, they must be connected with three or fewer straight lines. Simply tap on the tiles to create a link the brighter your connection, the louder the celebration! With 24 stimulating levels, each designed to offer a unique puzzle, players are sure to enjoy hours of entertainment.

Time is of the essence in Christmas Connect Deluxe; completing levels before the clock runs out will earn you extra bonuses. The festive graphics and charming sounds invoke the holiday spirit, making it an ideal game to enjoy with family and friends. Are you ready to challenge your puzzle-solving skills and deck the halls with tiles?

How to play free Christmas Connect Deluxe game online

To play Christmas Connect Deluxe, start by selecting a level from the main menu. Look for two matching tiles that can be connected by up to three straight lines. Tap on the matching tiles to remove them from the board. Keep an eye on the timer and try to complete each level as quickly as possible to score bonus points!
