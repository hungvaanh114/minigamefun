456 Survival

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to 456 Survival, an exhilarating and heart-pounding game that challenges players to survive a series of deadly trials! The experience begins with the infamous Red Light, Green Light, where players must freeze in place when the light turns red and sprint towards the finish line when it s green. Your reflexes will be tested, as even the slightest movement could lead to catastrophic consequences.

Following this intense opener, brace yourself for the Dalgona, Honeycomb challenge, where precision and patience are key. Players will carefully carve shapes out of sweetened honeycomb without breaking them, testing their nerves and skills under pressure. As the stakes rise, participants will also face the treacherous Glass Bridge, where they must choose between tempered glass panels, with each step potentially leading to an unexpected end.

In 456 Survival, the ultimate goal is not just to conquer these challenges, but to win a staggering prize of ?45.6 billion. Would you risk it all for a chance at unimaginable wealth, or do you prefer a safer existence? This game will push your limits with its gripping tasks and thrilling atmosphere, making it an unforgettable adventure.

How to play free 456 Survival game online

To play 456 Survival online, simply join the game lobby and select your challenge. As the game begins, listen carefully to instructions, follow the cues for each task, and remember to stay focused. Use your mouse or keyboard to control your character s movements, and strategize your approach for each deadly game to maximize your chances of survival!
