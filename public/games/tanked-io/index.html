Tanked.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tanked.io is an exhilarating real-time survival game that pits players against each other in an arena filled with action-packed tank battles. Players must gather experience points by eliminating opponents, allowing them to strengthen their tanks and boost their resilience. The primary objective is to survive as long as possible, making strategic decisions to outmaneuver and outlast enemies.

As you progress through the game, you'll earn points that can be allocated to upgrade your tank's characteristics, enhancing its combat capabilities. One of the unique skills available is the smoke ability, which lets you conceal your tank from the enemy, providing a tactical advantage in intense situations. This adds a layer of strategy to the gameplay, as players can choose to fight head-on or use stealth to outsmart their opponents.

In Tanked.io, you re not just alone on the battlefield; AI-controlled tanks will assist you in combat, defending against incoming threats and engaging other players. The combination of AI fighters and your decisions creates a dynamic and engaging game environment. With its vibrant animations and impressive graphical effects, Tanked.io provides an immersive experience, ensuring players stay entertained while longing for victory.

Don't hesitate to jump into the action; join the fight in Tanked.io now and prove your dominance by being the last tank standing in this thrilling multiplayer battlefield!

How to play free Tanked.io game online

Once you enter the game, control your tank using the arrow keys or WASD for movement and aim with your mouse. Engage enemies by firing at them while dodging their attacks. Collect points from defeated foes to upgrade your tank and utilize the smoke skill for stealth maneuvers. Survive and thrive!


Instructions

- Move with WASD ZQSD arrows - Attack and control your weapon with your mouse - Use smoke skill by clicking on the icon or by pressing the X key
