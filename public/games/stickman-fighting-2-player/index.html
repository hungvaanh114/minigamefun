How to play free Stickman Fighting 2 Player game online

To start playing Stickman Fighting 2 Player, select your game mode and choose your character. If you re playing solo, use the arrow keys for movement and attacks. For a two-player experience, player one controls their stickman with the WASD keys. Strategize your moves and be the last stickman standing!
