Kick the Zombie

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

As Halloween approaches, an unexpected terror has emerged in your neighborhood a lurking zombie! Designed for fans of clicker games, Kick The Zombie challenges you to harness an array of weapons to eradicate this menacing foe. Your mission is clear: utilize your arsenal and obliterate the zombie before it can cause any harm.

The game boasts an impressive selection of features. With eight distinct weapons available for upgrade, players have the autonomy to customize and enhance their play style to maximize destruction. The game supports both Russian and English languages, ensuring accessibility for a wider audience. Furthermore, it is fully responsive, allowing for seamless gameplay on any device or screen size, all while delivering stunning high-resolution graphics. Embrace the spirit of Halloween and join the battle against the undead; it's time to kick the zombie into oblivion!

Don't miss out on the fun this season play now and get ready to defend your neighborhood!

How to play free Kick The Zombie game online

To play Kick The Zombie, simply start clicking on the zombie to attack, utilizing the weapons you have at your disposal. You can upgrade your weapons by earning points through each successful hit. Keep clicking to unleash your full potential and unlock more powerful weaponry!


Instructions

- Tap to kick the zombie - Drag to toss the zombie
