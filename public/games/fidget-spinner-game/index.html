Fidget Spinner Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the ultimate relaxation on the go with the Fidget Spinner game, the most soothing fidget spinner simulator available! Designed to fit right in your pocket, this captivating game allows you to unleash your stress by simply swiping your finger. With a limited number of swipes just five you must master the art of spinning to achieve the best results possible. Use your finger to swipe the spinner adeptly to the left or right, and then sit back to watch it whirl. As it comes to a stop, you will be rewarded for your precision and skill.

As you explore the vibrant world of the Fidget Spinner game, you ll gather sweet rewards and unlock new levels. Enhance your gameplay by upgrading your finger spinner with various improvements that help you spin longer and faster. Challenge your friends to compete against your high scores and see if they can outdo you in the quest for the ultimate spin. Every completed turn brings you a step closer to unlocking an exciting array of hand spinner toys. With numerous designs to discover, the question remains: Can you unlock them all?

How to play free Fidget Spinner game online

To play the Fidget Spinner game, simply tap on the spinner with your finger and swipe left or right to give it a spin. After your swipes, wait for the spinner to come to a stop to collect your rewards. Use these rewards to upgrade your spinner and challenge your friends for the highest score!
