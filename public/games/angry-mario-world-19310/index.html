Angry Mario World

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling universe of Angry Mario World, a captivating game that combines the excitement of traditional platformers with the unique mechanics of slingshot gameplay reminiscent of Angry Birds. In this vibrant and challenging environment, you'll enlist the help of familiar characters from the Mario franchise to overcome obstacles, defeat enemies, and collect treasures. Navigate through beautifully crafted levels filled with interactive elements, clever traps, and strategic challenges that require both skill and creativity.

As you journey through Angry Mario World, you'll unleash the full potential of our beloved plumber as he takes on his enemies with a new twist. Utilize special power-ups, solve puzzles, and employ unique abilities specific to your character to help you advance. With finely-tuned physics and engaging gameplay, each level presents an adventure that will keep you on your toes and encourage you to keep pushing forward in your quest for victory.

Whether you are a long-time fan of the franchise or new to the scene, Angry Mario World promises endless hours of entertainment. Challenge your friends for the highest score, compete for the fastest completion times, and enjoy the thrill of discovering hidden secrets throughout the game. Embark on this exciting journey now and experience a world where Mario's anger fuels his quest for triumph!

How to play free Angry Mario World game online

Once you enter the game, you'll use your mouse or touchscreen to aim and launch Mario at the enemies blocking your path. Each level requires you to strategize your shots to maximize damage and clear obstacles. Complete levels by defeating all enemies to progress to the next challenge!
