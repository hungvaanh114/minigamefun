In PJMasks Starlight Sprint, you can join your favorite superheros Catboy, Owlette, or Gekko as they race across rooftops. Your mission is to navigate through each level by avoiding gaps between buildings and dodging various obstacles or enemies that may impede your progress. The heroes will automatically run, and it s up to you to control their jumps and speed.

To jump, simply press the spacebar, and if you want to add a burst of speed, just hit the shift key. As you dash through the levels, collect as many orbs as you can to increase your score and track your progress. Get ready for an adventurous sprint, and may you achieve the highest score possible!

How to play free Pjmasks Starlight Sprint game online

To play, start the game and select your character. As you run automatically, press the spacebar to jump over obstacles or gaps. Use the shift key for a speed boost when needed. Track your score by collecting orbs scattered throughout the levels, aiming for the highest score by the end of the game!
