Frogie Cross the Road Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Frogie Cross the Road, a delightful game that will put your reflexes to the test! In this engaging experience, you will guide our little hero, Frogie, through bustling roads, flowing rivers, and serene countryside landscapes. Your mission is to help Frogie navigate various challenges and reach the end of each level safely.

Throughout your journey, you ll encounter a variety of hazards such as speeding cars and mischievous wild boars. Timing is crucial as you need to dodge these obstacles to keep Frogie safe. Additionally, when you face a river, be prepared to leap over floating logs and other debris falling in is not an option! The clock is always ticking, adding an extra layer of excitement to each stage as you rush to complete levels before time runs out.

Collect power-ups along the way to enhance your abilities and make your journey easier. Every level offers new challenges and visually stunning backdrops, keeping the gameplay fresh and entertaining. Whether you're looking for a casual gaming experience or a way to hone your skills, Frogie Cross the Road is sure to provide hours of fun. So, gather your wits and help Frogie take on each challenge with courage and agility! Enjoy your adventure and keep hopping!

How to play free Frogie Cross The Road Game online

To begin playing Frogie Cross the Road, use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to control Frogie s movements. Navigate Frogie across the road while avoiding cars and boars. For river sections, jump between the floating logs carefully to prevent falling in. Complete each level before time expires!
