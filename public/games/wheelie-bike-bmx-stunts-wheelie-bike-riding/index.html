How to play free Wheelie Bike - BMX stunts wheelie bike riding game online

To play Wheelie Bike, simply use the arrow keys to accelerate and steer your bike. Press the spacebar to perform wheelies, balancing your rider as you navigate the ramps and tracks. Challenge yourself to keep the perfect wheelie while collecting points and unlocking new BMX bikes!
