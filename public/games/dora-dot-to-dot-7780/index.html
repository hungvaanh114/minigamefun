Dora Dot to Dot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delightful adventure with Dora in the enchanting game, Dora Dot to Dot! Join the adventurous young explorer and her lively friends as they prepare for their first-ever slumber party under the twinkling stars. However, they soon discover that they are missing several essential items for their nighttime bash. They will need a cozy sleeping area, huggable plushies for comfort, snacks to satisfy their munchies, and much more!

Your mission is to assist Dora and her companions in gathering all these necessities for their memorable sleepover. Engaging in a series of dot-to-dot challenges, you will connect stars and solve riddles to help Dora identify the crucial items. Boots, Swiper, and the other friends are eagerly waiting to bring everything together for a spectacular slumber fiesta!

To play the game, simply use your mouse to connect the stars, starting from number one. As you hover over the following numbers, you ll gradually form the shape of the desired object. Dora will count along with you in Spanish, allowing you to learn numbers while you play! Each new item is initiated by a riddle, and by deciphering the hints Dora provides, you can guess what she needs next. For instance, to carry all her slumber party supplies, she ll require a familiar friend her trusty Backpack!

How to play free Dora Dot to Dot game online

In the game, just click and drag your mouse to connect the dots in numerical order, revealing each object gradually. Pay attention to Dora's hints to guess the items she needs for the slumber party. With each successful connection, you ll get closer to completing Dora s slumber party preparations!
