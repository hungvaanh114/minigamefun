Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exciting adventure with Olli, the charming round elephant, in this captivating game! Based on beloved European books and toys, Olli needs your help to soar through the sky as he embarks on a quest to collect his favorite rubber ducks. The challenge lies in timing; you must tap twice at just the right moment to launch Olli into the air and keep him airborne for as long as possible. The longer you can keep Olli flying, bouncing, and floating, the greater the distance you will achieve.

As Olli navigates through this whimsical world, collecting as many rubber ducks as possible is key to boosting his speed. Each duck caught not only accelerates Olli's flight but also helps unlock stylish new outfits that make his airborne journey even more fun. As you progress, you will encounter numerous levels filled with delightful surprises and various features that enhance your gameplay experience. Get ready to challenge yourself and improve your skills as you aim for high scores and more rubber ducks!

How to play free Olli Ball game online

To play the Olli Ball game, first launch Olli by tapping the screen twice at the right time. Monitor Olli's ascent and make sure to catch as many rubber ducks as possible during his flight. Use the collected ducks to unlock new outfits and enhance Olli s performance. Keep flying and enjoy the adventure!
