Kitty Animal Hair Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Kitty Animal Hair Salon, where the charm of feline companionship meets the excitement of pet styling! Welcome to Amy's Animal Hair Salon, the ultimate kitty makeover experience designed for both girls and boys. Here, young stylists can unleash their creativity and transform adorable cats into glamorous fashionistas.

At the heart of this delightful game is Amy, a trendy leopard who has turned her passion for pet grooming into a bustling salon. She invites you to meet her first batch of fluffy feline clients, each with unique personalities and styling needs. Your task is to provide them with their dream hair transformations and fashionable looks. Dive into a variety of grooming tools and products to wash, brush, curl, straighten, and even color the cats' beautiful fur. With your expertise, each cat will emerge looking fabulous!

But the fun doesn t stop there! Once you ve styled their hair, it s time to pick out fabulous outfits from a selection of colorful and trendy clothes. Combine your favorite styles and accessories to give each kitty a distinctive and stylish flair. Unlock the potential of every cat and make them the talk of the town with their striking new looks.

How to play free Kitty Animal Hair Salon game online

To get started, simply select a cat client from the salon's lineup. Use the various grooming tools available to wash and style the cat's fur. Once you're satisfied with the hair, navigate to the dressing room to choose adorable outfits and accessories that complement your cat's new look. Enjoy the styling process and let your creativity shine!
