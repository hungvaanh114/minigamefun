Taxi Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Taxi Drive, where the urban landscape transforms into your personal playground! In this exhilarating car simulator, you take the role of a taxi driver navigating the bustling city streets. Unlike the typical mad cab drivers you may have heard about, your character remains composed and professional, ready to tackle the chaos that unfolds every weekend night. As you pick up various passengers, be prepared for their unpredictable behavior, especially after a night of revelry. Your driving skills will definitely be put to the test!

As you cruise through beautifully rendered city environments, your mission is to provide safe, prompt, and entertaining rides to a cast of quirky characters, each with their own stories and demands. From business people in a rush to party-goers ready to tell you their tales, the dynamic interactions create a unique experience every time you play. Navigate through busy streets, dodge obstacles, and manage your time efficiently to earn tips and unlock new features. As one of the top real car taxi games of 2022, Taxi Drive combines fun and challenge, allowing you to experience the life of a cab driver like never before.

Whether you re looking to sharpen your driving skills or simply enjoy a virtual ride through a vibrant city, this game has something for everyone. Buckle up and get ready for a wild ride!

How to play free Taxi Drive game online

To play the Taxi Drive game online, simply use the arrow keys or joystick to control your taxi. Follow the on-screen pointers to locate passengers and drop them off at their destinations. Be mindful of the traffic rules and complete your rides within the time limit to maximize your earnings!
