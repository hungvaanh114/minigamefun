BlackPink Chibi Claw Machine

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of BlackPink Chibi Claw Machine, where excitement and chance intertwine! This engaging game allows players to experience the thrill of a classic claw machine with a delightful twist, as you aim to retrieve adorable prize eggs. Simply use the right and left buttons to maneuver the claw, then press the claw button to scoop up your prize.

Inside each prize egg, you might discover a sweet treat or collectible figurines of the beloved BlackPink band members, making every attempt a delightful surprise. With a total of 20 distinct dolls to collect, each round adds to the anticipation and excitement. Furthermore, look out for the exclusive golden figurines featuring enchanting fairy singers that make the collection even more special!

Keep track of your progress and admire your collection on the achievements screen. There, you can click on any figurine's image to get a closer look and view all the intricate details of your collectibles. This adds an extra layer of enjoyment to your gameplay, allowing you to take pride in your haul. Whether you're a fan of BlackPink or just love claw machines, this online game at dressupwho.com promises hours of fun and nostalgia. So, grab your virtual claw and get ready to test your luck!

How to play free BlackPink Chibi Claw Machine game online

To play the BlackPink Chibi Claw Machine, simply navigate the claw using the left and right buttons to position it over the prize eggs. Once it's in place, press the claw button to attempt to grab an egg. Repeat this process to build your collection and uncover various exciting surprises!
