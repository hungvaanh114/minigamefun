Winter Bubbles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Winter Bubbles invites players into a charming winter wonderland, where the excitement of bubble shooting meets a festive atmosphere. Set against a backdrop of snow-covered landscapes, this classical bubble shooter game introduces a delightful twist, as players aim to release colored candles instead of traditional bubbles. The objective is to strategically combine these candles to create matches of three or more, effectively popping them off the screen and earning valuable points.

As you embark on this frosty adventure, time becomes an essential factor. You must swiftly align your shots, ensuring that the candles don t reach the bottom of the screen, as they dangerously approach the border. Your ultimate goal is to collect the coveted Golden Bell before time runs out. Be on the lookout for special power-ups scattered throughout the levels. These game-enhancing bonuses can be activated by shooting candles onto them, granting you unique advantages like the ability to clear multiple candles at once or to receive additional time for your mission.

The beautifully crafted visuals and cheerful sound effects make Winter Bubbles not only a test of skill but also a joyful experience following the winter theme. As you progress through various levels, each filled with challenges and rewards, you ll find yourself lost in a whimsical world of holiday cheer, making every play session an enchanting escape.

How to play free Winter Bubbles game online

To play Winter Bubbles, simply aim your cursor at the desired candle and click to shoot. Combine three or more candles of the same color to pop them and score points. Activate power-ups by hitting them with your candles to gain advantages. Remember to act quickly, as the candles will gradually move closer to the border!
