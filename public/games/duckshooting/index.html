DuckShooting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Duck Shooting is an exhilarating and dynamic shooting game inspired by the vibrant atmosphere of amusement parks. Step into the lively shooting range where a whimsical duck adorned with a crown floats gracefully, while a sulking red bird adds to the playful chaos. Amidst this colorful scene, you also encounter ducks marked with a cross.

Your mission is to aim carefully and take down the ducks and pesky monsters that appear. However, exercise caution! If you accidentally target the wrong duck, it will result in a point deduction, making it impossible to continue shooting at them. Keep your eyes peeled and your reflexes sharp to maximize your score! Prepare for a fun-filled adventure that tests your accuracy and decision-making skills.

With each successful hit, you'll feel the thrill of victory, but be wary of the penalties for missing your mark. Ducks are not your only targets; monsters lurk around too, adding an extra layer of challenge. Join the fun and see how many ducks you can shoot before the timer runs out! Good luck!

How to play free DuckShooting game online

To play Duck Shooting, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the floating ducks and monsters. Click or tap to fire your shots. Remember, only target the right ducks to accumulate points. Stay alert and enjoy the fast-paced action as you compete for a high score!
