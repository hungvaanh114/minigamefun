Are you a fan of shooting games? If you enjoy thrilling adventures while wielding powerful weapons, then dive into the action-packed world of Super Mario Shooting Zombie right now! This game stands out as one of the newest additions to the iconic Super Mario series, following popular titles like Ben 10, Spiderman, and Power Rangers.

In this exciting game, a zombie apocalypse has taken over the landscape, with the undead infesting various locations throughout the map. These relentless zombies are determined to destroy everything in their path, from lush forests to innocent lives. Not content with mere chaos, they've constructed bases and strategically placed obstacles to hide from any potential threats. However, all hope is not lost! Armed with courage and determination, Impostor is here to turn the tide. With a trusty gun in hand, he bravely confronts the undead, clearing obstacles and taking down dangerous zombies. Your mission is to join Super Mario in this exhilarating quest to eradicate these malevolent creatures and restore peace to the world.

How to play free Super Mario Shooting Zombie game online

To play Super Mario Shooting Zombie, use the arrow keys to navigate Mario around the map and the spacebar to shoot zombies. Keep an eye on your ammo and collect power-ups to aid in your quest. Complete levels by defeating all zombies and overcoming obstacles to save the world!
