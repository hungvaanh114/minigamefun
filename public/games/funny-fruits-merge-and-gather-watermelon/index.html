Funny Fruits: Merge and Gather Watermelon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the delightful world of Funny Fruits: Merge and Gather Watermelon. Tasked with collecting vibrant and cheerful fruits, your adventure begins as you match and merge identical fruits. This exciting mechanic is key to evolving them into even tastier varieties. Witness the transformation as your fruits grow larger and more delectable, all while filling your collection.

But beware! The challenge comes as you race against time to gather fruits before your box becomes overflowing. Your goal is to harness the power of merging to create the biggest watermelon possible! With each successful merge, you ll discover new fruit types, adding a layer of strategy to your delightful fruit adventure.

Can you collect them all and achieve the highest score? Engage with your friends and compete in this vibrant game to see who can gather the most fruits, adding an extra layer of excitement and friendly rivalry. The more fruits you collect, the larger your watermelon grows, paving the way for endless fun and laughter in this whimsical world of fruity fun!

How to play free Funny Fruits: Merge and Gather Watermelon game online

To play the game, simply drag and drop identical fruits next to each other to merge them. As they combine, new fruit types appear, creating opportunities for even more merges. Keep an eye on your box limit, and aim to collect as many fruits as possible to score high!


Instructions

CONTROLS Use the mouse to move fruits Click on the game field to release a fruit GAME RULES When two fruits of the same type touch they merge into a larger fruit of the next level If the fruits overflow the box the game ends
