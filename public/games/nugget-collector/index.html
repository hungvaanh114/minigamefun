Nugget Collector

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Nugget Collector is a fun and engaging puzzle game where players must skillfully collect nuggets that fall from above into a bucket. The game is set against a charming wooden backdrop that gives it a rustic and cozy feel. As nuggets fall, players must draw paths to guide them into the bucket while avoiding obstacles and ensuring none of the nuggets are lost.

Instructions
