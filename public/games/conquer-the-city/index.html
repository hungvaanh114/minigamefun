Conquer the City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Conquer The City, an engaging and straightforward puzzle game that challenges you to dominate cities painted in different colors while simultaneously safeguarding your own vibrant city. The objective is clear: turn every city blue to achieve victory! This delightful game combines strategy and reflexes, making it an entertaining experience for players of all ages.

As you immerse yourself in the gameplay, you'll find yourself sliding your finger across the screen to connect two adjacent cities. Each connection contributes to the transformation of cities as you aim to expand your blue territory. However, the challenge doesn t stop there be prepared to think quickly! If it seems like you re about to lose ground, activating a special skill may provide the edge you need to secure your dominance. With each level presenting fresh puzzles and opportunities to flex your mental muscles, you ll enjoy a satisfying challenge.

So gather your strategic mindset, put your problem-solving skills to the test, and dive into this colorful and lively world of Conquer The City. Join thousands of other players and see if you have what it takes to conquer all cities and emerge as the ultimate champion by exercising not just your fingers, but your brains!

How to play free Conquer The City game online

To play Conquer The City, simply tap on any city and then drag your finger to connect it to another city of a different color. Keep an eye on your moves, as strategic connections are crucial for turning cities blue. When you're close to making a significant change, don t hesitate to use your special skills for an advantage!
