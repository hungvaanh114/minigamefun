Riko vs Tako

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the universe of Riko vs Tako, an exhilarating 2D Sci-fi themed platformer where adventure awaits at every turn. In this colorful, action-packed game, players assume the role of Riko, who embarks on a mission to collect delicious chocolate ball candies scattered across diverse terrains. As you navigate through each carefully designed level, you must stay vigilant and avoid enemy bots, agile flying drones, and their lethal projectiles that pose a constant threat to your success.

The game consists of eight challenging levels, each escalating in difficulty as players progress. With intricate obstacles such as menacing spikes and giant blades lurking in the environment, timing and precision become key elements of your strategy. The vibrant graphics and smooth animations ensure an engaging experience, pulling players deeper into the challenging yet rewarding gameplay.

In Riko vs Tako, mastering your movement is essential. Use your skills to dodge and maneuver around hazards while collecting as many chocolate ball candies as possible. Each level presents unique layouts and challenges, making preparation crucial before embarking on your thrilling quest. Can you overcome the adversities and reach the loop hole that leads you to the next exciting level? Equip your determination and get ready to experience the delicious thrill of Riko vs Tako!

How to play free Riko vs Tako game online

To play Riko vs Tako, use the arrow keys to move and jump. Collect chocolate ball candies while avoiding enemy bots and obstacles. Stay alert as you navigate through spikes and blades. Your goal is to reach the loop hole in each level to progress further in the game!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
