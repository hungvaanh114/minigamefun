Immerse yourself in the delightfully chaotic universe of Hospital Stories: Doctor Soccer. In this uniquely amusing game, you'll assume the role of a quirky doctor assigned to a soccer team's infirmary, where hilarity meets healthcare. Prepare yourself for an array of offbeat challenges!
