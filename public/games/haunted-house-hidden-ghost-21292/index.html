Haunted House Hidden Ghost

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Haunted House Hidden Ghost, a free online skill and hidden object game that will challenge your observational skills in a spooky setting! In this thrilling game, players embark on a quest to uncover hidden stars hidden within beautifully crafted haunted house images. With each level presenting its own unique challenges, you'll need eager eyes and a keen sense of perception to find the elusive stars. There are a total of 10 stars to locate in each level, and with 6 levels to explore, the fun is virtually endless.

The clock is ticking as you race against time to find all the hidden objects before the timer runs out! Keep in mind, making incorrect clicks can cost you precious seconds each wrong click will deduct an additional 5 seconds from your remaining time. So, it s essential to be strategic about your clicks and focus on areas where the stars might be cleverly concealed. Are you ready to take on the challenge? Dive into the mysterious atmosphere of the game and enjoy an exciting adventure!

How to play free Haunted House Hidden Ghost game online

To start playing, simply click on the designated areas of the screen to search for hidden stars. Utilize your mouse or touchpad to navigate through the haunting images. Remember to keep an eye on the timer and avoid unnecessary clicks, as they will decrease your time. Aim to find all hidden stars as quickly as possible!
