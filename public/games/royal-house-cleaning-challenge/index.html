Royal House Cleaning Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of the Royal House Cleaning Challenge! In this captivating game, you get to step into the shoes of Chloe, a kind-hearted girl trapped in an unfair situation. Today is a monumental day; the prince's prom is happening, but Chloe's stepmother has other plans. She insists that Chloe must clean every room in the house before she can make her way to the grand ball.

As the clock ticks down, your mission begins! Explore the rooms, gather cleaning tools, and tackle the mess that stands between Chloe and her chance to shine at the prom. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, you will be immersed in the task of making sure each room sparkles. From dusting to vacuuming, and organizing to decorating, every detail matters. Your efforts will not only help Chloe prepare her home but also transform her into the stunning princess she deserves to be when she finally arrives at the prom.

Once the cleaning is done, it s time for the grand transformation! Help Chloe pick out an elegant dress, stylish accessories, and perfect shoes that will dazzle everyone at the royal event. With your guidance, Chloe will look breathtaking and be ready to dance the night away with her prince charming. Join in this whimsical adventure and make Chloe s dreams come true!

How to play free Royal House Cleaning Challenge game online

To play the Royal House Cleaning Challenge, simply navigate the rooms using your mouse or touch controls. Click on items to clean up or organize, and watch as the room transforms before your eyes. Once all tasks are completed, assist Chloe in choosing her stunning prom outfit and enjoy the celebration!
