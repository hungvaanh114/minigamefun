Subway Squid Game 3D - Subway Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Subway Squid Game 3D - Subway Runner, a thrilling experience where childhood games take on a deadly twist! In this immersive adventure, players are plunged into the heart of the Squid Game challenge known as Red Light, Green Light. The stakes are high, and only the bravest will survive this test of skill and nerve.

In this gripping survival game, participants must navigate a hazardous Glass Bridge, separated into two sides, where danger lurks with every step. The goal is straightforward: reach the finish line while avoiding elimination. Watch your footing as you walk on potentially deadly glass panels, uncertain of which will hold your weight. Not everyone can make it this game demands both caution and speed.

As you advance, heed the warnings of the powerful Squid Doll AI, which will detect the slightest movement during the "Red Light" phase. Move swiftly on "Green Light," but when the call of "Red Light" rings out, all motion must cease! Failure to comply results in instant elimination. In this high-stakes race for survival, players vie for a staggering prize of 46.5 billion won, adding to the tension and thrill.

Engage in fierce competition as you fight to stay alive, knowing that your every move could lead to victory or doom. The atmosphere is charged with both excitement and fear as you navigate through challenges that will test your wits and reflexes. Will you be able to endure the deadly hurdles and emerge as the ultimate champion, or will you succumb to the unforgiving nature of the game?

How to play free Subway Squid Game 3D - Subway Runner game online

To play, position yourself along the starting line. When the signal "Green Light" is given, dash towards the finish. Upon hearing "Red Light," freeze immediately. Any movement during this phase will lead to elimination. Stay focused and strategize to secure your victory in this life-or-death challenge!
