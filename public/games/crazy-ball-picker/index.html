CRAZY BALL PICKER

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you are ready, try to get the balls thrown upwards into the bucket by drawing lines. Dont let the balls fall down. Lets see how many balls you can collect, You only have the right to draw three lines.

Instructions

Mobile Controls Hold your finger on the screen and drag to draw a line PC Controls Hold the left mouse button and drag to draw a line
