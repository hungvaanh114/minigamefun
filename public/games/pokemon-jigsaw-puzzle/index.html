In Pokemon Jigsaw Puzzle, your journey begins with selecting a difficulty level: easy, medium, or hard. Once you've made your choice, the puzzle pieces will be randomly scattered across the game screen. Your objective is to use your mouse to drag and drop each piece, aligning them with their corresponding spots on the transparent image until the entire picture is completed. How to play free Pokemon Jigsaw Puzzle game online

To play the free Pokemon Jigsaw Puzzle, start by selecting your preferred difficulty level. Once the puzzle pieces scatter, use your mouse to pick up a piece and drag it to the corresponding location on the image outline. Keep placing pieces until the entire puzzle is complete and the Pokemon image is revealed!
