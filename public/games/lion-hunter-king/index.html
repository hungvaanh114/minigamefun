Lion Hunter King

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Lion Hunter King, players step into the shoes of a skilled lion hunter, equipped with a variety of high-powered sniper rifles. This hunting adventure offers an exciting experience where the main goal is to track down and eliminate majestic lions and fierce tigers that roam the wilderness in search of prey. Your journey as a sniper hunter will test your precision and strategic planning as you aim to take down these formidable beasts.

As you navigate the game's intricate environments, you'll have access to an extensive arsenal of weapons, including a unique x-ray gun that allows you to scan and identify critical areas on the lions and tigers for a successful shot. Aim for their head, heart, or lungs to bring these animals down efficiently. However, be cautious; missing your target can lead to dire consequences. If your first shot fails, the lion will quickly become aware of your presence and may charge towards you, risking your character's life. Therefore, not only must you have impeccable aim, but you also need to manage your time wisely to avoid imminent danger.

Get ready for an adrenaline-packed adventure where every shot counts and survival depends on your hunting skills. Will you rise to the challenge and become the ultimate lion hunter, or will the wilderness claim you as its next prey?

How to play free Lion Hunter King game online

To play Lion Hunter King, simply launch the game, choose your weapon, and use your mouse to aim at the targets. Take your time to line up your shot and click to fire. Be alert; after each missed shot, reposition yourself quickly to avoid incoming threats from the lions. Manage your resources carefully, and enjoy the hunt!


Instructions

Please use right mouse button to shot and left mouse button for zoom and arrow buttons for move the hunter
