World Cup Penalty Football Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you an avid soccer enthusiast? If so, you're in for a treat! With the excitement surrounding the 2014 World Cup, we've developed our debut mobile game, "World Cup Penalty Football Game." In this thrilling soccer-themed experience, the objective is straightforward: score more penalty shots than your rival.

In World Cup Penalty, you and your opponent will take turns attempting to score goals while also trying to defend against each other's shots. Each team is given five opportunities to send the ball into the net and five chances at goalkeeping. The team that successfully lands five goals first will emerge victorious and claim the title of World Cup champion.

This soccer game allows you to represent your favorite country and showcases their individual playing styles. Compete against a variety of teams, alternate your shots, and focus on your defense to block incoming strikes. Advance to the next round by defeating your opponents, but beware! A single loss could mean elimination from the tournament.

With ten shots per player in a match, strategizing is essential to outsmart your opponent. If you plan your moves effectively, you can secure that all-important goal. World Cup Penalty is not just a game; it's an exhilarating journey through the ups and downs of soccer competition, where only the most skilled teams will triumph!

How to play free World Cup Penalty Football Game online

To play the World Cup Penalty Football Game online, simply select your country and start playing. You will take turns shooting and blocking. Pay close attention to the timing as you shoot and defend! Use quick reflexes to block incoming shots while aiming for a successful goal during your turn.
