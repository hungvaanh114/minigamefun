Car Avoid Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrilling challenge of the Car Avoid Game, where quick reflexes and sharp attention are essential to survive the bustling streets. In this heart-racing adventure, your main objective is to navigate your vehicle while dodging other cars and pedestrians that roam the road. With every second that ticks by, the pressure mounts as you strive to maintain control and steer clear of collisions.

It's a high-stakes game where you have only three chances; hitting three cars results in a definitive game over. Additionally, if you're reckless enough to collide with any pedestrians, you'll find yourself in a bit of a predicament as the police are summoned to capture you. Once the law is on your tail, your focus shifts from avoiding obstacles to eluding the police. Can you outsmart the authorities while evading the chaos of the road?

Every move counts in this fast-paced environment filled with endless obstacles. The addictive gameplay will have you returning time and time again, determined to beat your previous score and refine your skills. Keep your eyes peeled and your fingers nimble as you make your way through the challenging landscape. The more adept you become at dodging, the more satisfying the gameplay grows.

How to play free Car Avoid Game online

To play the Car Avoid Game, use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to maneuver your car through traffic. Your goal is to avoid collisions with other vehicles and pedestrians. Stay alert, as hitting three cars or a pedestrian will end your game, and you ll need to outpace the police if they come after you!


Instructions

Touch or use the mouse to move the car left or right to change lanes so that you avoid the oncoming traffic and pedestrians
