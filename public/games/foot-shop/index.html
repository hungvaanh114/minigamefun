Foot Shop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Foot Shop is an immensely popular makeover game that puts you in charge of your very own foot care salon. As the owner of this unique establishment, you'll interact with a variety of customers, each presenting their own distinct foot-related issues. From managing long and unruly nails to tackling dirty nails and treating wounds, you have the opportunity to solve it all! Remember, being a nail technician doesn t just mean providing basic services; you also have a creative flair as a nail artist, capable of designing stunning nail art that will make every client leave feeling fabulous.

Your fashion potential is boundless in Foot Shop. With a range of tools and colors at your disposal, you can customize nail designs that reflect your customers personalities and styles. Whether they seek something simple and elegant or bold and eye-catching, your skills will be put to the test. Create unique nail art that caters to their specific traits, turning their foot care experience into a fashionable masterpiece. Immerse yourself in this colorful and engaging world, and watch as your salon becomes a go-to destination for foot care enthusiasts!

How to play free Foot Shop game online

To play Foot Shop online, simply select the customer you want to assist, then follow the prompts to address their foot issues. Use various tools to clean, trim, and pamper their feet. Once the basics are done, unleash your creativity by selecting nail designs and colors that match their style. Enjoy the transformation!
