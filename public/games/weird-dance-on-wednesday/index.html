Weird Dance on Wednesday

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an enchanting evening with "Weird Dance on Wednesday," where a goth girl named Rogue is ready to take center stage at her school prom. This isn't just any dance; Rogue has spent a whole month meticulously crafting a unique choreography inspired by her favorite classic films. With her heart set on impressing her peers, the gothic beauty has donned a striking black dress, but she realizes there's one crucial element missing her makeup!

As she stands before her mirror, it s your time to shine! Dive into the world of creativity and help Rogue select the perfect makeup that showcases her true self. Choose bold and dark shades to accentuate her mysterious eyes and alluring lips. Adding a few delicate freckles can give her look a whimsical twist, capturing the romantic essence she desires. Will you guide her to the ultimate goth glam that will leave everyone breathless?

Once you're all set with her stunning makeup, it's time for Rogue to strut her stuff on the dance floor! Encourage her to let loose and enjoy herself as she ventures into the prom night, filled with music, laughter, and of course, her weird dance that no one will soon forget. Are you ready to help her make a lasting impression and truly embrace the night?

How to play free Weird Dance on Wednesday game online

In the game, you ll navigate through various levels where you'll choose Rogue's makeup options by clicking on the items. After her transformation, use on-screen arrow keys to guide her movements during the dance. Collect musical notes for extra points and unlock new dance moves!


Instructions

Depending on the gaming device a computer mouse click or a simple touch on touch screens is used for control
