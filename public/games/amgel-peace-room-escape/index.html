Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the Amgel Peace Room Escape, a captivating point-and-click escape game crafted by Amgel Escape. In this immersive experience, you find yourself trapped inside a room filled with intriguing objects and clever puzzles. Your primary objective is to explore your surroundings, find hidden items, and solve a series of challenging riddles that will help you unlock the door and escape.

As you delve deeper into the game, you ll need to utilize your wit and observation skills to piece together clues scattered throughout the room. Each puzzle you solve will bring you one step closer to freedom. The atmosphere is designed to keep you engaged, with serene visuals and engaging auditory elements that enhance your gaming experience. Can you navigate through the challenges and prove your ability to escape? Your success depends on your keen eye for detail and logical thinking. Good luck on your quest for escape, and most importantly, have fun!

How to play free Amgel Peace Room Escape game online

To play Amgel Peace Room Escape, use your mouse to click on objects and explore the room. Collect items that can assist in puzzle-solving and interact with various elements within the environment. Keep an eye out for clues that can help unlock the next stage of your escape. Enjoy the challenge!
