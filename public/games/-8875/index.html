Get ready to experience the thrill of law enforcement like never before in the exciting world of Police Chase Traffic Car Racer. In this fast-paced highway racing game, you'll speed through traffic jams and expertly navigate between cars and trucks to rack up points. With its endless gameplay and top-notch graphics, this racing game is a must-play for fans of the genre. Drawing over 10 million downloads, Traffic Racer offers a realistic driving simulation that incorporates various elements from street racing to leisurely cruising on free highways.

Choose from a wide array of vehicles and engage in real-time multiplayer modes to challenge players from around the globe. Dive into 100 different online missions to earn rewards or invite friends for exhilarating 1V1 races. With unique gameplay features like a robust car racing system (CRS), the excitement never ends!

Customize your ride with 40 different cars and upgrade their speed, handling, and brakes while encountering a variety of traffic vehicles such as trucks, buses, and SUVs.

How to play free Police Chase Traffic Car Racer game Traffic Racer online
