Fish Frenzy Eat Some Fish

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into Fish Frenzy! Play as a goldfish and eat smaller fish to grow, but beware of bigger fish that can eat you! Progress through 5 unique levels and unlock 2 bonus levels. Enjoy exciting challenges with 3 different feedable fish. Can you become the biggest fish in the sea?

Instructions

Control the goldfish with your mouse Auto-eat nearby smaller fish 5 levels 3 bonus levels Grow to take on bigger fish
