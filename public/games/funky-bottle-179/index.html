Funky Bottle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thrilling adventure with Funky Bottle, where balance and precision are key! This delightful game challenges you to maneuver a fragile glass bottle over a variety of obstacles. Your objective is simple yet engaging: ensure that Funky Bottle clears each hurdle without sustaining any damage. However, beware this whimsical character can easily crack if not handled with care!

As you guide Funky Bottle through each level, you must master the art of jump strength. Too little force may send the bottle tumbling, while too much could lead to a disastrous crack. Each jump requires careful calculation, so be alert and precise! With minimal game size and high-quality graphics, you ll be engrossed in this vibrant universe as you navigate through a series of increasingly challenging quests.

Whether you re a fan of brain teasers or just looking for a fun way to pass the time, Funky Bottle offers a unique experience packed with excitement and skill. The controls are super simple, allowing players of all skill levels to jump right in and enjoy! So gather your concentration and get ready to help Funky Bottle avoid potential disasters while having a blast.

How to play free Funky Bottle game online

To play Funky Bottle, simply use your finger or mouse to adjust the jump strength. Tap or click to launch the bottle over obstacles. Keep an eye on the bottle s position to avoid any crashes. With practice, you ll learn to judge the perfect jump needed to cross each challenge successfully!
