Alice Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting realm of Magical Island in the exhilarating adventure game, Alice Jump. In this captivating journey, you will assist Alice in her quest to locate the fairies who hold the key to saving her friends. Harness magical powers as you traverse this wondrous landscape, filled with challenges and magical surprises. Collect apples along your way to boost your energy levels, enabling you to jump higher and further in your quest.

As you navigate through colorful platforms and soaring heights, keep an eye out for the elusive fairies. The more successful jumps you make, the closer you will get to reuniting Alice with her friends. However, beware of the lurking skulls scattered throughout the island; coming into contact with them will result in losing precious lives. The stakes are high, and only the most skilled players will be able to conquer the heights and achieve victory. With beautiful graphics and engaging gameplay, Alice Jump promises to keep you entertained for hours on end.

Challenge your jumping skills, timing, and reflexes to traverse the Magic Island, and rise to new heights in this thrilling adventure. Are you ready to help Alice save the day? Join her in this magical quest and watch as the adventure unfolds before your eyes!

How to play free Alice Jump game online

To play the Alice Jump game, use your mouse or keyboard to control Alice's jumps. Click or press the spacebar to launch Alice into the air, aiming for floating platforms while collecting apples for energy. Avoid skulls to maintain your health and succeed in rescuing her friends!
