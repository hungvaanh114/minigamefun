Bomb Prank

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thrilling experience in the frantic multiplayer arena game, Bomb Prank! As the countdown ticks down, tension rises as you find yourself with a bomb that must be passed along to other players before it explodes. It's a race against time where strategy and quick reflexes are key. You ll have to be deft and cunning, ensuring that the bomb is handed off to another player just in the nick of time. The excitement escalates as players scramble to offload the hot potato, trying to avoid being the one left holding it when the timer runs out.

In Bomb Prank, players must navigate a vibrant arena filled with obstacles and other competitors, all while managing the deadly device that can go off at any moment! The laughs and adrenaline are sure to ensue as alliances form and break in the quest for survival. Can you successfully deliver the bomb to another player and stay out of harm's way? Or will you fall victim to the explosive fun that awaits? Join in on the chaos and embrace the thrilling challenge of staying alive in this outrageous game.

How to play free Bomb Prank game online

To play Bomb Prank, simply join a multiplayer match and wait for the game to start. Once it begins, you will receive the bomb your mission is to pass it to others by tapping on their characters. Keep an eye on the countdown clock and make quick decisions to avoid holding the bomb when it explodes!
