Draw Defence

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Draw Defence, an engaging and straightforward tower defense game where your primary objective is to protect your castle from waves of relentless enemies! As these foes approach, your homeland hangs in the balance, and it's up to you to ensure its safety. Unleash your creativity by drawing various lines to summon different kinds of soldiers, each possessing unique abilities to fend off your attackers. The more intricate your lines, the more powerful the soldiers you can call upon.

As you progress, you ll notice that the enemies grow stronger and more formidable. It's not just about defense; strategizing and upgrading your equipment becomes essential to overcome the increasingly challenging foes. Constantly adapt your tactics, enhance your soldiers, and devise clever plans to outsmart and defeat the advancing hordes. The blend of creativity, strategy, and quick thinking will keep you engaged as you continue to fortify your defenses and protect your kingdom.

In this captivating game, each level presents new challenges and opportunities to showcase your skills. Gather resources, refine your drawing techniques, and unlock new soldier types to maximize your defense strategy against the enemy onslaught. The satisfaction of seeing your well-planned defenses hold against waves of attackers makes Draw Defence an addictive gameplay experience!

How to play free Draw Defence game online

To play Draw Defence, simply draw lines on the screen to summon soldiers. Use your mouse or touchscreen to create different patterns and summon various units. As the enemies approach, repair and upgrade your defenses by allocating resources wisely to improve your soldiers' strength and abilities.
