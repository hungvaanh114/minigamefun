Angry Fun Zombies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating ride in the realm of the undead with Angry Fun Zombies! Grab your trusty catapult loaded with stones and get ready to embark on a hilarious journey where the primary goal is to obliterate as many zombies as you can! This free arcade game guarantees loads of entertainment as you navigate through varied levels filled with quirky and destructive challenges.

As you progress in ANGRY ZOMBIES, you'll encounter an array of mischievous zombie foes that will test your aim and strategy. Each level is designed with heaps of destructible objects that can be used to your advantage. Whether you re smashing through barrels or causing chaos with hidden traps, the fun never stops. Aim carefully, pull back your catapult, and release to launch your stones at the zombies, ensuring that you wipe them out before they reach you!

The colorful graphics and lively sound effects add to the captivating atmosphere of the game, keeping players engaged and entertained. The unpredictability of the various zombie types keeps each level fresh and exciting, making ANGRY ZOMBIES a must-play for fans of arcade games. So gather your wits and prepare for some exciting zombie-smashing mayhem!

How to play free Angry Fun Zombies game online

To play Angry Fun Zombies, simply use your mouse or touch screen to aim your catapult. Drag to adjust the angle and power of your shot, then release to launch stones at the zombies. Your objective is to eliminate all the zombies present in each level before running out of stones. Enjoy smashing and watch out for bonus items!
