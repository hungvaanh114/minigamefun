World of Alice the Bones

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

World of Alice - The Bones is an engaging educational game designed specifically for children, aiming to introduce them to the fascinating world of human anatomy, focusing on the bones of the body. With its bright visuals and interactive elements, players can explore the skeletal system in an immersive environment using their cell phones, tablets, or computers. Children will embark on exciting adventures that make learning feel like play, allowing them to understand where bones are located and their functions in a way that's both enjoyable and memorable.

This game serves as an excellent resource for parents and educators alike, providing an innovative approach to teaching anatomy. World of Alice - The Bones is developed by Nau.kids, a team dedicated to creating fun and educational experiences for young learners. As kids navigate through various challenges and activities, they will gain a deeper appreciation for the human body and its incredible design. The game combines learning with gameplay, ensuring that children remain engaged and curious as they uncover new information about their own bodies.

Every feature in World of Alice - The Bones has been crafted to promote interaction and retention of knowledge. Players will be motivated to continue their journey through a series of levels, educational quests, and rewards that highlight their progress. This learning tool provides a delightful approach to anatomy that can pique interests in future scientific endeavors.

How to play free World of Alice - The Bones game online

To play World of Alice - The Bones, simply start the game on your device and follow the guided tutorials. Explore different chapters that introduce various bones through interactive quizzes and challenges. Tap on the bones to learn their names and functions, and complete tasks to progress through the game while having fun!
