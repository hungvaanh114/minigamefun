Heli Monsters Giant Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you prepared to confront an enormous challenge? In Heli Monsters - Giant Hunter, you'll encounter wild monsters and embark on a thrilling shooting journey! Armed with your gun, you ll need to aim precisely at these terrifying creatures to fight them. Your primary mission is to eliminate these beasts and safeguard the city from their rampage!

As you progress through the game, you can upgrade your arsenal by purchasing powerful firearms and ammunition using gold collected from your victories. Weapons like the AWM, M200, and M24 are at your disposal, each offering increased damage and effectiveness against the gigantic foes that threaten your city. Get ready to enhance your firepower and dominate your monstrous opponents!

Along the way, utilize strategic thinking and precise aiming to ensure your survival and the safety of the city. With dynamic gameplay and increasingly challenging adversaries, every level presents a new test of skill and determination. Are you the hero this city needs?

How to play free Heli Monsters Giant Hunter game online

To play Heli Monsters - Giant Hunter, use your mouse to aim at the monsters on the screen and click to shoot. As you defeat monsters, collect gold to upgrade your weapons and bullets. Keep shooting accurately to clear the levels and save the city from destruction!
