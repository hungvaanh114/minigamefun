Valto

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Valto is an engaging HTML5 jumping game that immerses players in a colorful and vibrant world where the primary objective is to leap and collect stars. With its simple yet addictive mechanics, Valto invites players of all ages to test their jumping skills as they navigate through a series of exciting platforms. The game's aesthetic features playful graphics and cheerful animations, making it a delightful experience while players strive to gather as many stars as they can.

As you embark on your journey in Valto, you'll encounter various challenges
