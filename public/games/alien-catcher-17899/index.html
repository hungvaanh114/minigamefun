The much-anticipated Alien Catcher game has returned, now in an exciting HTML5 version! How to play free Alien Catcher game online

To play the Alien Catcher game, use the keyboard to control your agents. Navigate through levels by moving left and right, and jump to dodge obstacles. Collaborate with your friend to corner and capture the aliens, utilizing your combined skills to strategically outmaneuver them. Team effort is essential to succeed!
