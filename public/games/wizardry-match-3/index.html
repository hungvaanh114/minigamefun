Wizardry Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an enchanting adventure in Wizardry Match 3, where you take on the role of the clever witch Gueraula. With a treasure trove of potion knowledge at her disposal, Gueraula is tasked with safeguarding magical portals from waves of menacing dark creatures. Your mission is to match potions and unleash powerful magical attacks to fend off these forces of darkness!

As you travel through a variety of captivating environments including spellbinding wizardry labs, shadowy forests, mystical caves, and eerie haunted ruins you will encounter a myriad of challenging creatures and formidable bosses. Each level will test your strategic thinking and matching skills as you create explosive combos to banish the creeping shadows from the realm. Can you rise to the challenge and help Gueraula protect her world? Dive into magical puzzles and discover the thrilling universe of Wizardry Match 3!

How to play free Wizardry Match 3 game online

To play Wizardry Match 3, simply match three or more identical potions in a row, either horizontally or vertically. Click and drag to swap adjacent potions, aiming to create combos that trigger magical attacks against enemies. Keep an eye on your objective for each level and strategize to defeat challenging creatures and bosses!
