Monkey Farm

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Monkey Farm, an engaging idle management game brought to you by TinyDobbins. In this delightful adventure, you step into the shoes of a charming monkey character who has just opened a vibrant supermarket. Your primary objective is to cultivate a variety of fruits and keep your supermarket stocked to attract customers.

As you embark on this exciting journey, you'll need to plant a range of produce, including delicious bananas and sweet corn. Control your monkey to navigate between different workstations, where you can efficiently fill up stands with fresh fruits and vegetables. Watch as eager clients flock to your store, patiently waiting at the cashier as they pick up the delicious bounty you've prepared for them. The more you manage effectively, the more satisfied your customers will be!

Upgrade your monkey character to enhance your efficiency and unlock new, exciting workstations that offer additional goods for sale. As you progress, you can even recruit other adorable workers to assist in the management of your bustling supermarket, ensuring that no customer leaves empty-handed. With a delightful graphic style and engaging mechanics, Monkey Farm is sure to provide hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

How to play free Monkey Farm game online

To play Monkey Farm online, start by planting fruits to grow your stock. Move your character to different stations to fill the stands. Keep an eye on your customers as they shop and wait at the cashier. Use earned resources to upgrade your monkey and hire helpers for better productivity!
