Subway Surfers Halloween Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The adventure of Subway Surfers takes a festive twist in the new Halloween Puzzle edition! Join our trio of intrepid surfers as they embark on a whimsical journey to the enchanting streets of Paris, the heart of romance and style. With its iconic landmarks and vibrant culture, Paris provides the perfect backdrop for this delightful puzzle experience.

Your mission is to piece together stunning photographs captured by our heroes during their visit to the City of Lovers. Although you can t accompany them on their escapade, you can immerse yourself in the charm of Paris by assembling beautiful images of its most famous sights. Each picture is divided into a series of interlocking pieces, and it's your task to connect them based on their color and shape.

Select your preferred difficulty level to tailor the challenge to your skills. Whether you re a novice or a puzzle master, there s an option that s just right for you. The more puzzles you complete, the more stunning images of Paris you will unveil. So, gather your persistence, pay attention to the colors and patterns, and enjoy the fun of assembling fantastic images while celebrating the Halloween season with a unique Subway Surfers twist!

How to play free Subway Surfers Halloween Puzzle game online

Once you're in the game, begin by selecting your preferred difficulty level. You'll see a mixed-up image puzzle on the screen. Drag the puzzle pieces into position, trying to fit them together by matching colors and shapes. Complete each puzzle to reveal beautiful photos from the trio's Parisian adventures!
