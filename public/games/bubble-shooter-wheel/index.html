Bubble Shooter Wheel

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Bubble Shooter Wheel, a unique twist on the classic bubble shooter genre. In this exhilarating game, you will encounter a dynamic rotating wheel populated with colorful bubbles. Your primary objective is to eliminate all the bubbles present on the wheel. To do this, skillfully shoot bubbles from your launcher towards the wheel, aiming to create groups of at least three bubbles of the same color. Each successful combination will clear the bubbles and earn you points!

As you play, keep in mind that time is not on your side. The clock is ticking, and your points decrease as seconds pass. Therefore, it's crucial to make quick decisions and shoot strategically to maximize your score. The thrill of racing against time while trying to clear the wheel adds an exhilarating layer to the gameplay experience that will keep you on your toes.

With its vibrant graphics and engaging mechanics, Bubble Shooter Wheel offers endless fun for players of all ages. Whether you're looking for a challenging solo experience or a quick game to enjoy with friends, this bubble shooter is sure to deliver hours of entertainment. Prepare to put your bubble shooting skills to the test and strive for the highest score before the timer runs out!

How to play free Bubble Shooter Wheel game online

To play Bubble Shooter Wheel, simply aim your bubble shooter at the rotating wheel, and release to shoot. Match three or more bubbles of the same color to clear them. Keep an eye on the timer and aim for quick combinations to save your points!
