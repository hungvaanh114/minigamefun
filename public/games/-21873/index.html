Ninja Pumpkin?Ninja Pumpkin

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Ninja Pumpkin, where you embody an agile pumpkin with a mission. In this colorful platformer, your objective is simple yet challenging: guide your ninja pumpkin through an array of levels filled with obstacles and enemies. Each stage is designed to test your reflexes and strategy, making every move crucial as you navigate towards the end goal.

The journey is fraught with peril, featuring three types of menacing foes. The red enemies are the most prevalent, easily dispatched by leaping onto their heads or executing a swift tackle. However, do not underestimate the yellow enemies; they share the same fate upon contact but pose a significant challenge as they leap every few seconds. Finally, be wary of the black enemies, the ultimate test of your skill. These foes are invincible, meaning your only option is to jump over them and avoid any direct contact. Mastering the interactions with these diverse enemies is vital for progressing through the game.

As you traverse through each animated level, make sure to keep an eye out for the shiny coins scattered throughout. Collecting a total of 30 coins will reward you with an extra life, providing a much-needed safety net during your adventurous escapades. So gear up, embrace your inner ninja, and embark on this pixelated adventure with Ninja Pumpkin!

How to play free Ninja Pumpkin game online

To play Ninja Pumpkin, simply use the arrow keys to move and jump. Avoid enemies and obstacles while collecting coins. Press the jump key to bounce on enemies or leap over the invincible black ones. Keep track of your coin collection to earn extra lives along the way!
