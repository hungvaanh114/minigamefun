Tangram King

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Tangram King is an intriguing dissection puzzle that challenges players to think critically and creatively. Comprised of seven distinct flat shapes known as tans, this puzzle allows for endless possibilities when it comes to assembling and forming new shapes. The primary objective revolves around using all seven pieces to recreate a specific outline or silhouette without any of the pieces overlapping, making each attempt a fresh test of spatial intelligence and problem-solving skills.

Traditionally believed to have originated in China during the Song Dynasty, the Tangram puzzle quickly gained popularity and made its way across the globe with trading ships in the early 19th century. Its timeless appeal continues to captivate puzzle enthusiasts of all ages, allowing for both leisurely enjoyment and stimulating challenges that can enhance cognitive abilities. The beauty of the Tangram King lies in its simple rules paired with complex outcomes, encouraging players to explore creativity while sharpening their analytical skills.

As you delve into the world of Tangram King, you'll discover numerous shapes waiting to be formed, from animals to geometric figures and more. The delight of fitting the tans together sparks a sense of accomplishment when the correct configuration is achieved. Whether you re a novice or a seasoned player, the Tangram King promises a rewarding experience that blends fun with mental exercise.

How to play free Tangram King game online

To play the Tangram King game online, start by selecting a silhouette you wish to recreate. Drag and drop the seven tans from the side onto the outline, adjusting their positions until you achieve the desired shape. Remember, all seven pieces must be used without overlapping!
