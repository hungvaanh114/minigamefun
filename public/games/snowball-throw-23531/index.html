Instructions:

Snowball Throw is an exciting winter-themed game that tests your throwing skills as you compete against the clock to achieve the highest score possible. With an initial supply of six snowballs, players must aim and launch their projectiles at targets to calculate the distance for each successful throw. The greater the distance, the higher the score you accumulate!

As you progress through the game, various power-ups become available to enhance your gameplay experience. The Additional Lives power-up grants you three extra chances to throw, giving you an edge in your pursuit of excellence. Scoring is further incentivized by the Silver Star, which adds an impressive 1000 points to your overall score, making achieving high scores even more attainable. For the ultimate score booster, players can seek out the Golden Star, which adds a whopping 5000 points to their tally, turning a good game into an exceptional one.

With its engaging mechanics and supportive bonuses, Snowball Throw guarantees fun and competitive play for all ages. Whether you're looking to beat your previous record or challenge friends, the thrilling gameplay ensures an action-packed experience every time you throw. Will you be able to master the art of snowball throwing and become the champion of this winter wonderland?

How to play free Snowball Throw game online

To play Snowball Throw, simply aim your cursor to target where you want to throw the snowball. Click to launch, and watch as the distance unfolds and your score increases. Keep an eye out for power-ups and maximize your throws by making strategic choices with your limited snowballs!
