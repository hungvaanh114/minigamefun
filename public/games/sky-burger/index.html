Sky Burger

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sky Burger is an incredibly engaging and addictive hyper-casual game that promises endless fun for players of all ages. In this delightful culinary adventure, you ll find an array of ingredients tumbling from the heavens, ready to be caught. Each level presents unique challenges, as you re tasked with completing specific missions to advance further in the game. The thrill lies in collecting a required quantity of various ingredients, which becomes more challenging with each stage.

As you progress through the game, you will encounter different types of ingredients, each offering a distinctive appeal and contributing to your overall score. You can experience the excitement of strategically moving your base object from left to right, ensuring that you maximize your collection efficiency. The simple yet captivating mechanics of Sky Burger make it easy for anyone to pick up and play, but mastering each level's requirements takes skill and quick reflexes.

With beautifully designed graphics and vibrant colors, Sky Burger immerses players in a delightful world of culinary creativity. Your ultimate goal is to build towering burgers filled with fantastical ingredients, enticing both your vision and taste buds. Join in the fun, and see how high you can stack your delicious creations!

How to play free Sky Burger game online

To play Sky Burger, simply navigate your base object using left and right arrow keys or swipe your touchscreen. Aim to collect the falling ingredients and ensure you gather enough of each for your current level's mission. Stay alert, as the ingredients can fall quickly and require prompt reactions to keep up!
