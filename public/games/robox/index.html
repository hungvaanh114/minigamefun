Robox

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Robox is an exciting puzzle platformer where players take on the role of a resourceful robot navigating through a series of challenging levels. Your main objective is to find the exit door while cleverly avoiding various enemies and hazardous traps. As you progress through each stage, you'll encounter a multitude of obstacles that require both quick thinking and agile movements to overcome. Use your unique abilities to solve puzzles strategically, enabling the robot to move through the level efficiently and safely.

To aid you in your quest, Robox is equipped with a teleportation gun that allows you to manipulate and shoot boxes within the environment. These boxes can become vital tools for creating pathways, blocking incoming threats, or even triggering hidden switches that may lead to your escape. With each level becoming increasingly complex, players must harness their problem-solving skills and reflexes to effectively navigate through the intricacies of the game.

Get ready for an adventure filled with suspense and excitement as you help the robot dodge menacing foes and evade cunning traps. The combination of strategic puzzle-solving and platforming challenges in Robox ensures that every player remains engaged as they strive for victory. Will you be able to guide Robox to freedom?

How to play free Robox game online

To play Robox, use the arrow keys or W/A/S/D to control the robot's movement. Jump with the spacebar to scale platforms, and deploy your teleportation gun by clicking to shoot boxes. Navigate through the level by strategically placing boxes, avoiding enemies, and seeking the exit door to advance to the next stage.
