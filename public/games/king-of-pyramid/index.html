King of Pyramid

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the mysterious and captivating world of King of Pyramid, where ancient treasures lie hidden within the massive stone structure of a pyramid. In this action-packed adventure, the King finds himself trapped inside, waiting for a brave hero to set him free. As you traverse the intricacies of this enchanting pyramid, you'll need to navigate treacherous traps, dodge lurking enemies, and strategize your next move.

Your main objective is to steal precious gems scattered throughout each level. Collect these gems not only to boost your score but also to build formidable defenses that will protect you from rival thieves and aggressive foes. In this unique fusion of arcade thrills and platforming challenges, you'll engage in fierce guild wars in the Arenas, where strategy and timing are essential for survival.

Explore the depths of the pyramid and unlock ancient spells that amplify your capabilities. These powerful spells will enhance your skills, making you the most feared thief of all. Use them wisely to outsmart opponents and claim victory in battles fought for treasure and glory! Team up with other players or go solo on your quests, as your journey through the pyramid will test your wit, agility, and mastery of the arcane arts.

How to play free King of Pyramid game online

To play King of Pyramid, start by selecting your character and entering the pyramid. Use the arrow keys to navigate your way through each level, jump to avoid traps, and collect gems along the way. Activate spells by clicking on their icons, and strategize your moves to outsmart opponents in the Arenas. Good luck!
