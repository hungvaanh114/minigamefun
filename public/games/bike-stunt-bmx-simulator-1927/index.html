Bike Stunt BMX Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to experience the thrill of extreme biking in Bike Stunt BMX Simulator. This exhilarating game allows players to take the reins of their BMX bikes by performing mind-blowing stunts and tricks on massive ramps. Soar through the air with spectacular flips and gravity-defying maneuvers. Alternatively, hone your skills with technical street skating in various urban environments.

Featuring seven diverse skate parks inspired by locations around the globe, Bike Stunt BMX Simulator offers endless possibilities for exploration and creativity. From the hustle and bustle of vibrant cities to serene landscapes, each park presents unique challenges and opportunities for daring stunts. But the fun doesn t stop there you can design and build your own custom skate parks, ensuring that every rider can create their perfect playground.

Customization is at the heart of the game. Players can personalize their riders and BMX bikes in numerous ways, using a wide range of options to stand out. Whether you prefer a sleek, professional look or a wild, eclectic style, you can tailor every detail to reflect your personality on the tracks. Stand out as you chain together incredible combos, including manuals, grinds, and wallrides, all while pushing the limits of what s possible on two wheels.

The excitement of BMX biking awaits in Bike Stunt BMX Simulator, where your creativity and adrenaline can run wild. Are you ready to conquer the ramps and showcase your skills?

How to play free Bike Stunt BMX Simulator game online

Once in the game, use your keyboard or controller to navigate your BMX rider through the skate parks. Press specific buttons to perform tricks, such as flips and grinds. Experiment with different combos to earn points and boost your skills while customizing your character and bike to enhance your gameplay experience.
