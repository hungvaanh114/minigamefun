Rise to Sky

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling universe of Rise To Sky, players are invited to embark on an exhilarating journey through the clouds. Navigate your way through a vibrant landscape filled with obstacles that challenge your reflexes and strategic thinking. With each level, the stakes get higher, pushing you to hone your skills as you soar further into the sky.

As you maneuver your spaceship, your main objective is to collect sparkling gems scattered throughout the air. These precious gems serve as a currency within the game, enabling you to unlock and purchase a variety of advanced spaceships. Each spaceship comes with unique attributes and enhancements that will assist you in overcoming even the toughest barriers. The more gems you collect, the more powerful your fleet can become, allowing for greater distances to be covered in your aerial adventure.

With its engaging gameplay and stunning graphics, Rise To Sky guarantees a captivating experience for gamers of all ages. Avoiding barriers requires skillful precision and quick reflexes, making every play session a thrilling race against the clock. Will you rise to the challenge and dominate the skies? The adventure awaits!

How to play free Rise To Sky game online

To play Rise To Sky, simply use the arrow keys or screen controls to maneuver your spaceship. Avoid obstacles by dodging left and right while collecting gems. Keep an eye on your scores and strategically purchase new ships to enhance your gaming experience. Enjoy the journey!


Instructions

Touch in left screen for left movement and touch right screen for right movement
