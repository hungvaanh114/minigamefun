Supermarket Sort N Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Supermarket Sort N Match, a thrilling puzzle game where your organizational skills will be put to the test! In this engaging gameplay experience, you'll need to rearrange products across various shelves to create groups of three identical items. By aligning these items, you can clear them from the shelf and let the items from higher shelves cascade down. This dynamic movement creates an exciting challenge as you strategize how to effectively manage the incoming rows of products.

But that s not all! As you advance through the levels, the complexity of the game steadily increases, keeping you on your toes. Your primary goal is to clear entire stacks of items, which allows you to shift adjacent shelves and create new opportunities for matching more items. With a ticking clock counting down, the pressure mounts as you race against time to collect all the items required to complete each level.

With vibrant graphics and delightful sound effects, Supermarket Sort N Match provides an immersive and enjoyable experience for players of all ages. If you love puzzles and matching games, this delightful sorting challenge will keep you entertained for hours on end. Are you ready to take on the ultimate item-sorting challenge and become the supermarket sorting champion?

How to play free Supermarket Sort N Match game online

To play Supermarket Sort N Match, simply drag products between shelves to create sets of three or more identical items. Clear them to allow upper items to fall, and keep an eye on the countdown timer. Use quick thinking to collect all items before time runs out!


Instructions

Use your mouse or touchpad to drag items between shelves Match 3 identical items on one shelf to clear it and let upper shelves slide down Clear entire stacks to shift adjacent shelves Collect all items before time runs out
