Make All Equal

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Make All Equal, a captivating puzzle game where your numeric skills are put to the test! Each level presents you with a series of boxes, each containing various numbers. Your objective is to align the values within these boxes to match a specific target number displayed at the top of the screen. The challenge ramps up as you progress through the levels, introducing an array of configurations and numerical combinations to keep your mind engaged.

To succeed in Make All Equal, you must strategically transfer numbers between the boxes. The game mechanics are simple yet engaging: click on the number in one box and then click on another number in a different box to swap them. By doing this, you can gradually adjust the sums within each box until all of them align perfectly with the target number. It s a delightful mix of strategy and spatial reasoning that makes for a stimulating gameplay experience.

As you advance through the game, expect an increase in difficulty with more boxes and diverse numbers to manage. Your logical thinking will be essential as you experiment with different combinations. Take your time, plan your moves wisely, and watch as the boxes transform into perfectly equal sums, creating a satisfying end to each level!

How to play free Make All Equal game online

To play Make All Equal, simply select a number from one box and then choose a number from another box to swap them. Your goal is to adjust the numbers so that each box totals the target number at the top. Keep an eye on your moves while you strategize to match the target!
