Police Obby Prison Save

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the shoes of a daring undercover police officer in Police Obby Prison Save. Your mission? To rescue your companions who have become entangled in a dire situation at the prison. Disguised as one of the guards, you must carefully navigate the prison s layout while avoiding detection. Time is of the essence, and every second counts as you race against the clock to find your friends!

Throughout your journey, you will face a series of challenging obstacles and puzzles. Gather clues, unlock doors, and use your environment to outsmart the prison guards. Your ultimate goal is to locate each friend and launch them into your waiting helicopter. Keep an eye on the prison patrols and be strategic in your movements one wrong step could jeopardize the rescue mission!

Once all your friends are secured, the thrill continues as you must make a daring escape. Reach the designated car while evading the guards capture. Utilize the skills you ve honed throughout your quest, demonstrating agility and quick thinking to safely flee the prison grounds. Remember, teamwork is essential, so be vigilant and save every last friend!

How to play free Police Obby Prison Save game online

To play Police Obby Prison Save, simply navigate your character around the prison using the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Interact with objects and fellow players by clicking on them. Complete missions to save your friends and make your way to the helicopter while avoiding guards! Good luck!
