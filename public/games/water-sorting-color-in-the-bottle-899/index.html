In the captivating game of Water Sorting Color, players are invited to embark on a colorful journey where the goal is to skillfully sort colors within various bottles. How to play free Water Sorting Color in the bottle game online

To play Water Sorting Color, click on a bottle to pour water into another bottle. You need to ensure that only the same colors are poured into one another. If a bottle is full or the colors don t match, you can t transfer them. Strategically plan your moves to achieve a sorted bottle for each color!
