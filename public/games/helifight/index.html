Helifight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the exciting world of Helifight, where strategy and dexterity are put to the ultimate test in a vibrant pixelated battlefield! In this exhilarating multiplayer game, you take control of a tiny blue helicopter, while a friend pilot navigates their red chopper. The goal? Engage in a thrilling aerial duel as you both strive to outmaneuver and outscore each other.

Your primary weapon? Rocket fire! As you pilot your helicopter, you can collect rocket symbols that appear randomly in the skies above. Carefully navigate and time your movements to touch these symbols, enabling you to unleash powerful rockets aimed at your opponent. With each successful hit, the tension escalates as both pilots must utilize their skills to dodge incoming attacks while simultaneously launching their own. The first player to successfully land five hits on the rival helicopter emerges victorious!

Helifight offers an action-packed, competitive experience wrapped in charming pixel art. Whether you re playing with a friend or engaging in a few rounds to hone your skills, the fast-paced gameplay ensures you ll always be on the edge of your seat. Sharpen your reflexes, develop your tactics, and prepare for intense dogfights. Can you master the sky and claim the top spot in Helifight?

How to play free Helifight game online

In Helifight, control your blue helicopter using the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Look out for rocket symbols in the sky to collect and empower your attacks. Your goal is to hit the opposing red helicopter five times. The player who achieves this first wins the game!
