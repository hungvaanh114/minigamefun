How to play free Pop It 3D Fidget Toy Maker game online

To start playing the game, select your desired mini-game from the main menu. Once in the crafting mode, choose a Pop It template and begin customizing it using the spray paint and stickers available. Simply click and drag to apply colors and adorn your toy, then showcase your finished creation!
