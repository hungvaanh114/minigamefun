Car Stunt Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Car Stunt Drive, where the thrill of racing meets the freedom of an open-world city! In this groundbreaking simulator, you can unleash your inner speed demon and embark on incredible stunt adventures without any limitations. With its state-of-the-art physics engine, this 2022 driving simulator offers a dazzlingly realistic experience that brings every drift, jump, and burnout to life.

Have you ever dreamed of being behind the wheel of a high-performance sports car? Now is your chance! Explore a sprawling metropolis designed specifically for avid racers. Forget about traffic lights and rival cars this city is yours to conquer! Feel the adrenaline rush as you perform wild stunts, effortlessly kicking up the asphalt as you drift around corners and execute jaw-dropping tricks.

This immersive game allows you to fully embrace the thrill of speed without the fear of the police imposing restrictions. Race across diverse terrains, from busy streets to abandoned alleys, where the only limit is your imagination. The freedom to drive recklessly and explore every nook and cranny of this bustling city makes every session unforgettable. Whether you re whooshing past towering skyscrapers or navigating through hidden shortcuts, Car Stunt Drive promises heart-pounding excitement and limitless fun!

How to play free Car Stunt Drive game online

To start playing Car Stunt Drive, simply select your favorite sports car from the garage. Use the arrow keys or a joystick to navigate your vehicle through the city. To execute tricks, accelerate using the throttle control and apply the drift button for stunning turns. Enjoy your ride!
