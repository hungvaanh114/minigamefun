Boom Stick Bazooka 2 Puzzles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling universe of Boom Stick Bazooka 2 Puzzles, a free online game that sets you on a mission to obliterate platforms and enemy stickmen using a highly customizable bazooka. In this explosive arena, your objective is to aim, shoot, and demolish the towers where black stickmen reside. However, be cautious; some stickmen are armed and will fire back, jeopardizing your three precious lives. With only three shots allowed per level, strategic planning is essential, but you can replay levels or purchase additional ammunition to enhance your firepower.

To expedite your victory and take out your foes more effectively, aim for barrels which, when shot, will explode and cascade destruction upon the towers nearby. As you conquer each level, you will earn coins, allowing you to unlock new skins and weaponry with improved capabilities. The colorful graphics and engaging gameplay will keep you on the edge of your seat as you strategize your shots and tackle progressively challenging stages. Get ready to experience intense shootouts and improve your bazooka skills in this action-packed puzzler!

How to play free Boom Stick Bazooka 2 Puzzles game online

To play Boom Stick Bazooka 2, simply use your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to aim your bazooka at the enemy towers. Click or tap to shoot and eliminate the stickmen. Keep an eye on your lives, and remember you can restart the level anytime for better timing or strategy!
