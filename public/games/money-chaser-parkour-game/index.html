Money Chaser Parkour Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Chasing/Running endlessly for collecting coins(Money). Try to avoid traps &amp;amp; *kick out all the enemy/competitors on your way. *You can kick out enemy infront of you with slide skill* After you have enough money, you can challenge the Boss fight. The amount of coins(Money Bullets) is the only weapon to defeat Boss, the more the better. Remember, no money no life...just keep running :)

Instructions

For Keyboard Space Key rarr Jump A Key rarr Slide or Shoot In Boss Battle For Touch Screen Mobile Tap Right Part of Screen rarr Jump Tap Left Part of Screen rarr Slide or Shoot In Boss Battle
