Monster High Spectra

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Meet Spectra Vondergeist, the fashionable ghostly student of Monster High, who truly embodies the spirit of creativity and style. As the prominent reporter for the renowned Gory Gazette blog, formerly known as the Ghostly Gossip, she has established herself as a celebrity among her schoolmates. Spectra is not just your average ghost; she s a trendsetter who knows how to express herself through her unique and chic outfits, ensuring she stands out in the vibrant halls of Monster High.

With her ethereal charm and dedication to journalism, Spectra covers all the latest happenings in the monster world. Whether it s a thrilling monster dance or the latest gossip, she s always ready to share the scoop with her friends and followers. Now, you have the chance to join her in an exciting adventure where styling is key! Get ready to dive into a world of fashion and fun as you help Spectra prepare for her next big meeting with her friends.

Put your creativity to the test by picking out stylish outfits and accessories for Spectra. Mix and match different clothing styles, hairstyles, and eye-catching accessories to create the perfect look for our ghostly reporter. Your fashion choices will not only reflect Spectra's personality but also help her shine as she navigates through her bustling school life.

How to play free Monster High Spectra game online

To play the Monster High Spectra game, simply select your desired outfits and accessories from the available options on the screen. Use the mouse to drag and drop items onto Spectra to dress her up. Once you re satisfied with your selections, show off your creation by taking a snapshot or joining Spectra as she goes out to meet her friends!
