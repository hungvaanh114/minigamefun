Elemental Domination

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Elemental Domination, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking as an alchemist tasked with harmonizing the elements. Spanning an impressive 145 levels, this enchanting adventure immerses you in a world teeming with elemental blocks such as fire, water, earth, and air. As you navigate through each level, you'll face a grid filled with these diverse elements, each presenting unique challenges and opportunities.

Your mission in this mystical realm is to transform all the blocks into a singular grid element. To do so, you must tactically touch the blocks to initiate a transformation process that spreads the selected active element to its neighboring blocks. The complexities grow as you progress, demanding sharper intellect and precise execution of your strategies. Only through clever manipulation can you achieve true alchemical mastery and restore balance to the elements.

With each level, Elemental Domination tests your problem-solving skills and pushes you to think critically about your moves. Will you rise to the challenge and prove your prowess as an alchemist? Embark on this mesmerizing journey, and see if you can unify the elements and conquer the intricate puzzles that await you!

How to play free Elemental Domination game online

To play Elemental Domination, click on an elemental block to select it as your active element. Then, tap on adjacent blocks to spread the active element and begin transforming them. Strategically plan your moves to convert all blocks into one type and progress through the increasingly intricate levels.
