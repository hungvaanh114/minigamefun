Pinocchio

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pinocchio is a renowned fictional character and the central figure in the beloved children's novel, The Adventures of Pinocchio, published in 1883 by the Italian author Carlo Collodi. This enchanting tale tells the story of a wooden puppet named Pinocchio, crafted by the skilled woodcarver Geppetto, who resides in a small village near Lucca, Italy. Pinocchio's journey begins with his longing to become a real boy, which sets the stage for a series of adventures filled with valuable lessons.

As Pinocchio navigates his world, he faces temptations and challenges that test his character. One of his most notable traits is his tendency to lie, which often leads to dire consequences. Each lie he tells is met with an elongation of his wooden nose, serving as a clever metaphor for the repercussions of deceit. Readers are drawn into Pinocchio's experiences as he interacts with the colorful characters that populate his story, including the wise talking Cricket and the nurturing Fairy with Turquoise Hair, both of whom guide him on his quest for humanity.

The adventures of Pinocchio resonate with themes of honesty, bravery, and the importance of making virtuous choices. Through his trials, Pinocchio learns that being truthful is essential to achieving his dreams, ultimately leading him to discover the significance of love and the joy of living as a real boy.
