Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of "Wedding Dress Maker - Design & Style," where you, as a skilled tailor, have the unique opportunity to craft stunning wedding dresses for your clients. In this role-playing adventure, a lovely couple visits your shop, excited to bring their dream wedding to life with your exquisite designs.

Your journey begins as you greet the couple, who share their vision for the perfect wedding dress. To create a masterpiece, you first take their measurements, ensuring a flawless fit. Next, it s time to unleash your creativity choose your favorite color from a vibrant palette that captures the essence of romance and joy. Once the color is decided, cut the fabric into elegant shapes, combining style and tradition to reflect the couple's personality.

As you expertly sew the pieces together, watch as the dress comes to life, taking shape in a dazzling display of craftsmanship. But don t stop there! Add the finishing touches by attaching beautiful accessories that will make the bride sparkle and the groom look dapper on their special day. With each dress you create, you weave a story of love, commitment, and celebration, leaving your clients in awe of your talent.

How to play free Wedding Dress Maker - Design & Style game online

In the game, start by selecting the couple and entering the tailoring workshop. Use your mouse to take measurements, choose colors, and cut fabric. Click to sew pieces together and drag accessories to customize the outfits. Follow the prompts to complete each order and achieve stunning results!
