Stone Merge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Stone Merge, an engaging puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and quick decision-making skills. In this game, your objective is to merge rocks to create larger, more powerful stones. Simply drag one rock towards another that shares the same number and color. When you successfully combine two identical rocks, their values and colors will increase, allowing you to create even bigger numbers as you progress.

The beauty of Stone Merge lies in its simplicity yet captivating gameplay. Each level presents a new challenge, and as you merge rocks, you'll need to plan your moves carefully to optimize your score. Different combinations yield various results, providing an exciting layer of strategy as you work to build your rock collection into impressive numerical values. Watch as larger rocks appear with each successful merge, and see how quickly you can advance through the levels!

Accumulate higher numbers to unlock advanced stages and discover what lies beyond the ordinary. The game is designed to keep you entertained and engaged, making it perfect for short bursts of fun or extended gameplay sessions. Experience the satisfaction of watching your rocks transform before your eyes, and become a master of merging in Stone Merge.

How to play free Stone Merge game online

To start playing Stone Merge, simply drag a rock onto an identical rock of the same color and number. Repeat this to create larger rocks. Keep merging until you reach the highest values! Plan your moves wisely to clear the board and achieve high scores.
