How to play free Block Puzzle King game online

To start playing Block Puzzle King, simply select a level and begin by dragging blocks onto the board. Arrange the pieces to form complete rows or columns, which will then be cleared. Keep an eye on the available space as you strategically place your blocks to maximize your score!
