Meteor Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating experience with Meteor Attack, a thrilling action-packed game that challenges your reflexes and precision under pressure. In this game, you are tasked with protecting your colonies from a relentless barrage of meteors flying towards your base. Your mission is straightforward, yet the stakes are high: you must click or tap to shoot missiles at these incoming threats. However, it's not as easy as it sounds! Each flashing meteor is resilient and requires a double hit to be completely obliterated.

As you engage in this frenetic battle, your agility and timing will be put to the test. The meteors come at varying speeds and angles, increasing the challenge as the game progresses. Should any meteor manage to smash into your colonies, the consequences could be dire, leading to a loss of resources and potentially devastating your hard-earned progress. You will find yourself on the edge of your seat, fully immersed in the urgency of the moment as you strategize your shots and aim with precision. The visuals are captivating, with vibrant colors and dynamic movements that enhance the gaming experience.

With each round, the difficulty ramps up, ensuring that players remain challenged and engaged. Can you save your colonies from destruction? Take on the meteor threat and show off your shooting skills in this fast-paced, thrilling adventure!

How to play free Meteor Attack game online

To play Meteor Attack, simply use your mouse or tap on the screen to aim and shoot missiles at the incoming meteors. Keep an eye out for the flashing ones, as they require two hits to be destroyed. Stay alert, and don t let any meteor reach your colonies!
