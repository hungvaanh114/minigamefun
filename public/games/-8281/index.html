Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Super Mario Run Tour is a refreshing addition to the Mario franchise that allows players to engage in an exciting platforming experience using just one hand. In this unique title, you guide Mario by simply tapping your screen, which prompts him to run forward continuously. Your timing is crucial as you tap to execute stylish jumps, perform midair spins, and execute wall jumps, all while collecting coins and progressing towards your ultimate goal!

Available for download at no cost, Super Mario Run offers a full range of gameplay modes after an initial purchase, allowing for a one-time investment without further fees. Before you decide to buy, you can sample four different modes: World Tour, Toad Rally, Remix 10, and Kingdom Builder. In the World Tour, put your skills to the test as you traverse diverse environments to save Princess Peach from the villainous Bowser. You'll navigate through plains, caverns, and castles across 24 exhilarating courses, aiming to collect unique colored coins and achieving high scores against your friends.

Remix 10 presents a rapid-fire approach, featuring ten short and dynamic courses that change every time you play. Here, your goal is to locate Daisy as you race through these bite-sized challenges. For those who thrive on competition, Toad Rally allows players to showcase Mario's stylish moves, pitting you against friends and players globally to see who can score the highest!

How to play free Super Mario Run Tour game online

Once you start playing Super Mario Run Tour, simply tap the screen to make Mario jump. Timing is key, as you'll need to navigate obstacles and collect coins effectively. Focus on mastering wall jumps and midair spins to enhance your score and ultimately rescue Princess Peach. Enjoy the thrilling adventures!
