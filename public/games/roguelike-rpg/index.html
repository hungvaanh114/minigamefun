Roguelike RPG

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Roguelike RPG is an immersive adventure game where players explore ever-changing RPG maps filled with dangerous monsters. Strategize, fight, and destroy these creatures to survive, collect loot, and grow stronger with each run. Every decision matters in this thrilling roguelike journey! Use WASD to walk , E to get weapons or anything in map and mouse click left to attack
