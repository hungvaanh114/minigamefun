How to play free Ben 10 Match 3 Puzzle game online

To play Ben 10 Match 3 Puzzle, simply click on two adjacent items to swap their positions. Align three or more identical characters or objects to eliminate them and gain points. Keep an eye out for special power-ups that can help you clear obstacles and complete levels faster. Enjoy the challenge!
