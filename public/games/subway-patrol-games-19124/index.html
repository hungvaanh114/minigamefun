Subway Patrol Games

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Subway Patrol Games, a thrilling adventure that combines endless running with the exciting world of superheroes! In Subway Pups Patrol Superheroes Run, you will join Ryder and the lovable Paw Patrol pups on a daring mission to escape the clutches of evil monsters lurking in the Angry Jungle. This new free-to-play 3D endless runner game launched in 2021 promises nonstop action and fun!

In this engaging storyline, Ryder finds himself trapped in a bustling subway system, while the mischievous pups have managed to escape from the palace. Now, police are hot on Ryder's trail, attempting to capture him and return him back to the palace. Your task is to guide Ryder through various obstacles, ensuring he runs, jumps, dashes, and slides to avoid any capture. With stunning 3D graphics and fast-paced gameplay, each run through the subway turns into an exhilarating experience.

As you navigate the subway tracks, you can utilize a skateboard to increase Ryder's speed, propelling him faster than ever. Compete for the highest score on the leaderboard and become the top princess runner! With daily missions to complete and levels to conquer, you will find countless opportunities to enjoy this endless running experience. Power up your scores by collecting score multipliers and complete missions to earn daily rewards, including unique outfits and upgrades for your superhero runner!

How to Play Free Subway Patrol Games Online

To play Subway Patrol, use the on-screen controls to guide Ryder. Tap to jump over obstacles, swipe down to slide under barriers, and tap twice for a super dash. Maneuver left and right to collect coins and power-ups while completing missions to earn rewards!
