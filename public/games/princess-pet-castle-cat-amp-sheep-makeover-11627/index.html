Princess Pet Castle - Cat Amp; Sheep Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the enchanting world of the Princess Pet Castle - Cat & Sheep Makeover as our beloved princess and her adorable pets prepare for a delightful Christmas party on Christmas Eve! The holiday cheer is in the air, but our charming characters are not quite ready for the festive celebrations. This is where your creative touch comes into play!

In this magical makeover adventure, you ll assist the princess and her fluffy companions in getting glammed up for the occasion. Your first task is to help the kitty achieve a stunning party look! Choose from a selection of cosmetic contact lenses to give the adorable cat a sparkling gaze, and don t forget to apply that lovely Christmas red lipstick to enhance its charm. Watch as the kitty beams with joy from its stylish transformation!

But the fun doesn t stop there! You will also have the opportunity to give the princess a fabulous makeover. Choose from an array of beautiful dresses, accessories, and hairstyles to ensure she looks as splendid as her pets. With your expertise, they will be the stars of the Christmas party, spreading joy and holiday spirit to everyone they meet!

How to play free Princess Pet Castle - Cat & Sheep Makeover game online

To play the game, simply drag and drop the makeup tools and accessories onto the princess and her kitty. Choose from various options to apply cosmetics, outfit changes, and finalize their looks for the party. Have fun experimenting with different styles and creating unique festive appearances!
