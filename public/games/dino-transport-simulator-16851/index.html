Dino Transport Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the prehistoric world of Dino Transport Simulator, a thrilling 3D gaming experience where you take on the role of a skilled truck driver tasked with transporting dinosaurs. Maneuver your truck through various challenging environments, showcasing your driving skills while ensuring the safe delivery of these magnificent creatures.

The game features multiple levels that test your precision and control, as you navigate through rugged terrain. Each level presents unique challenges, including obstacles like steep hills, narrow paths, and even other wildlife. As you progress, you ll unlock new locations and encounter different types of dinosaurs, each with its own requirements for transport.

Dino Transport Simulator also offers three distinct game modes to enhance your experience. Choose from the challenging Career mode where you complete missions and earn rewards, or enjoy the Free Roam mode that allows you to explore the vast landscapes at your own pace. Lastly, the Time Trial mode pushes your skills to the limit as you race against the clock to deliver dinosaurs swiftly.

How to play free Dino Transport Simulator game online

To start playing Dino Transport Simulator, simply select your desired game mode and level. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver your truck. Carefully load the dinosaurs into your vehicle and navigate the course, avoiding obstacles and ensuring a safe delivery. Complete each mission to progress through levels and unlock new challenges!
