Is It Golf?

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the hilarious world of Is It Golf?, a uniquely entertaining arcade game that blends the essence of traditional golf with a whimsical twist. Featuring charming 3D models, this game invites you to embrace the absurdity of its gameplay as you navigate through a series of wacky levels. Instead of the usual golf clubs and balls, you ll be aiming to move a variety of unexpected objects, including a sheep, a car, a chicken, and even a closestool. Each of these distinctive items requires a different strategy, making for unpredictable and side-splitting gameplay.

The objective is simple yet engaging: guide your chosen model to the marked flag while adhering to the humorous rules of golf. Players must skillfully adjust the launching power to propel their chosen object across the course and strategically maneuver around creative obstacles like drums. With every level presenting new challenges, your precision and timing are put to the test as you seek victory in this light-hearted golf adventure.

As you progress through the game, you ll encounter various environments and increasingly complex challenges that will keep you entertained for hours. Whether you re aiming to outsmart the quirky obstacles or simply enjoy the charm of the colorful graphics, Is It Golf? is sure to provide a delightful experience for players of all ages. Get ready to laugh and enjoy this absurd parody of a classic sport!

How to play free Is It Golf? game online

To play Is It Golf?, select one of the quirky models to launch from the starting point. Adjust the power meter to dictate your shot strength and aim carefully to avoid obstacles while heading toward the flag. Click to launch your model and watch the hilarity unfold!
