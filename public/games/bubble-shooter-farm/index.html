BUBBLE SHOOTER FARM

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

BUBBLE SHOOTER FARM is an engaging and cheerful bubble shooter game designed for players of all ages. Set in a vibrant environment, you join a delightful little yellow bird on an exciting quest to clear colorful bubbles from the screen. Your mission is simple: shoot bubbles and create combinations of three or more to pop them, thereby clearing the game board.

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter a charismatic character known as Big Teddy, who urgently needs your help to rescue adorable mini teddy bears trapped within the bubbles. Each level presents a unique challenge that requires careful strategy and timing. To maximize your impact, aim your bubble shooter with precision and employ tactics that will allow you to burst the most bubbles possible with each shot.

This charming game comes packed with features that enhance the bubble-popping experience. An interactive tutorial guides new players through the mechanics of the game to ensure you're ready to tackle the 20 challenging levels that await. The fun theme and colorful graphics keep the gameplay lighthearted and enjoyable. Whether you use clever angle theories or target higher bubbles, you ll need to practice various strategies to conquer each level and attain victory.

How to play free BUBBLE SHOOTER FARM game online

To play BUBBLE SHOOTER FARM, aim your bubble shooter by dragging your finger or mouse to adjust the angle. Tap or click to shoot bubbles toward the cluster, matching three or more of the same color to pop them. Continue popping bubbles until you've cleared all of them to win the level.
