Grimace Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the captivating world of Grimace Blocks, a physics-based puzzle game that promises fun and challenges at every turn! In this unique gaming experience, you'll need to tap or click on the wooden blocks to strategically eliminate them while ensuring that Grimace remains perched atop a block. Your goal is to prevent Grimace from tumbling into the grass below, which adds an extra layer of excitement to each level.

With over 40 diverse levels to unlock, each one presents its own set of challenges that will test your problem-solving skills and ingenuity. As you progress through the game, you ll encounter puzzles that require careful planning and timing, making every level an engaging challenge for players of all ages. Get ready to navigate through obstacles and think critically to keep Grimace safe!

Whether you're a casual gamer looking for a fun distraction or a puzzle aficionado seeking to hone your skills, Grimace Blocks delivers a delightful combination of amusement and mental exercise. So gather your wits, and enjoy unraveling the puzzling challenges that lie ahead as you aim for high scores and ultimate success!

For more fun and games, visit the website of the developer at https://vodogame.com/.

How to play free Grimace Blocks game online

To play Grimace Blocks, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to tap or click on the wooden blocks you wish to remove. Be cautious and plan your moves wisely your objective is to ensure that Grimace stays on a block while eliminating others. If he falls to the ground, it's game over!
