Five Nights at Freddy S

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Five Nights at Freddy's AR: Special Delivery is a spine-chilling addition to the beloved FNAF franchise. In this immersive mobile experience, players are thrust into a world where terrifying animatronics have stepped out of the shadows and are wreaking havoc in the real world. As a player, your mission is to navigate through an atmosphere of dread, facing off against these malfunctioning characters who have taken a sinister turn from their intended purpose.

This latest game introduces the innovative Festive Service by Fazbear Entertainment, a subscription-based offering that enables players to receive animatronics directly to their devices. You can even share the horror by sending them to friends, but beware the animatronics sent are far from harmless. Each one is flawed, and they are programmed to attack their unsuspecting recipients rather than entertain them, adding an extra layer of tension.

To enhance the gameplay, players will experience augmented reality elements that blur the lines between the game and the real world. Using your device's camera, you'll have to be vigilant, as these animatronic foes can strike at any moment. Survival depends on your ability to stay calm under pressure, as you strategically manage your resources and react quickly to the scares that await you. Prepare for a heart-pounding experience that will test your courage and wits like never before.

How to play free Five Nights at Freddy s game online

To play the game, simply download Five Nights at Freddy's AR: Special Delivery from your preferred app store. Upon launching the game, follow the on-screen instructions to set up your character.
