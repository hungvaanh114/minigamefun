Mart Puzzle Bus Jam

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the bustling world of Mart Puzzle - Bus Jam. In this fun and challenging puzzle game,buses are lined up in chaotic traffic jams.Everyone is waiting to get on the bus. You need to sort and match the same people in order according to the character, and when there are ten people, you can click the board to send them on the bus.

Instructions

Mouse click or tap to play
