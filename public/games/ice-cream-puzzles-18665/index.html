Ice Cream Puzzles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to satisfy your sweet tooth with the delightful challenge of Ice Cream Puzzles. In this engaging puzzle game, your objective is to slide the colorful ice cream blocks around the board. The goal is to match identical blocks by sliding them towards one another, causing both to disappear from the grid. The fewer moves you make to eliminate the blocks, the higher your score will be!

Each time you successfully eliminate a block, you earn a rewarding 100 points. However, be mindful of your moves: if you slide a block more than once, the score diminishes by 10 points for each additional move. With strategies in mind, aim to clear each level using the least number of moves possible. Completing a level not only challenges your logic and skills but also brings a sense of accomplishment as you watch those delightful blocks disappear.

There are 24 layers of exciting puzzles for you to navigate, each designed to test your problem-solving abilities and keep you entertained. As you progress through these levels, the puzzles become more challenging, requiring keen observation and thoughtful planning. Can you master every level and achieve the highest score?

How to play free Ice Cream Puzzles game online

To play Ice Cream Puzzles, simply click and drag the ice cream blocks to slide them across the game board. When you position two identical blocks adjacent to each other, they will vanish, earning you points. Keep track of your moves to maximize your score and complete the 24 levels of fun! Enjoy!
