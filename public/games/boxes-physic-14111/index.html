Boxes Physic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Boxes Physic, an engaging puzzle game that challenges your problem-solving skills and quick-thinking abilities. In this game, your main objective is to swiftly eliminate the green boxes from the screen. Utilizing clever strategies, you'll break the boxes down into four smaller segments. This innovative gameplay mechanic not only requires precision but also encourages players to think critically about their actions.

As you progress through the levels, the challenges become more complex, introducing different gadgets and bombs that you can employ to maximize your efficiency. Manage your resources wisely, as the bombs can significantly help in clearing the boxes faster. The thrill lies in completing each level promptly, as achieving a time threshold can earn you up to three stars! However, if you're not quick enough, don't worry you can always retry the level and devise a new strategy to improve your score.

Each stage of Boxes Physic presents unique puzzles that require both strategic planning and rapid execution. Compete against your own best time as well as challenge your friends to see who can clear the boxes the fastest. With each successful level, you ll gain a sense of accomplishment while honing your abilities. So gear up and get ready to think outside the box!

How to play free Boxes Physic game online

To play Boxes Physic, begin by clicking on the green boxes on the screen to break them down. Use your available gadgets and bombs strategically to achieve your goal. Keep an eye on the timer to earn stars based on your completion speed. If needed, retry levels to beat your best time!


Instructions

Just tap or click boxes to split them into 4 smaller parts Click on bombs to have them explode
