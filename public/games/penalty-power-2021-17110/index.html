Penalty Power 2021

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of soccer penalties with Penalty Power 2021, a captivating game you can enjoy online for free at Superkidgames.com. This game offers you a chance to immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of penalty kicks, where your shooting and defensive skills will be put to the test. What makes this game even more exciting is its connection to the beloved Cartoon Network universe, where characters from your favorite shows come alive on the soccer field.

As the Euro Cup evolves, you can partake in this celebration by scoring goals and defending shots alongside characters like DC Superhero Girls, Gumball, and Teen Titans Go. Your objective is clear: become an exceptional penalty kick shooter to lead your team to victory!

In Penalty Power 2021, the focus is solely on penalty kicks, allowing you to hone your skills without the distractions of an entire soccer match. Select your captain and goalkeeper, then prepare for intense action. Using your mouse, swipe in the direction you wish to send the ball when you re shooting. When it s time to defend, tap where you believe the ball will go to make that crucial save. With limited time to outscore your opponent, every second counts!

Prepare for matches against various teams across iconic European locations, making every game unique and thrilling. The beautiful graphics and smooth gameplay transport you right onto the field, bringing the magic of soccer and Cartoon Network together.

How to play free Penalty Power 2021 game online

To play Penalty Power 2021, simply use your mouse to swipe in the direction you want to shoot the ball or tap to defend against incoming shots. Score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. Choose your favorite characters from Cartoon Network for both shooting and goalkeeping to enhance your gameplay experience!
