Prepare for an exhilarating adventure in Angry Gran Run - Running Imposter, where you'll join our feisty grandma as she breaks free from the confines of the Angry Asylum! Urged on by the need for freedom, your mission is to guide her through vibrant city streets while dodging and defeating bothersome bots.

Experience the chaos of an endless running game that packs a punch! As you sprint through the bustling environments of iconic cities like New York and Rome, you'll encounter numerous quirky obstacles. Jump over trash cans, duck under falling signs, and dash past various eccentric hazards. Don't forget to knock out the pesky bots to collect their coins these treasures will help you clean up the streets!

Transform your gran with an array of outrageous costumes such as the funky 70 s hippy gran, the whimsical wonder gran, or even a hilarious penguin costume! Keep an eye out for incredible power-ups, like Bullet-Time and Invincible Shields, to enhance your running experience. But be on guard aliens, dinosaurs, and other unexpected surprises lurk around every corner!

Angry Gran Run is not just another runner; it's a delightful mix of humor and action, making it the ultimate choice for fans of grandma games and endless running challenges. Download today and run free in the most thrilling 3D running game available!

How to play free Angry Gran Run - Running Imposter game online

To play Angry Gran Run, simply tap to jump over obstacles, swipe to slide under them, and dash forward to clear the path. Engage with the on-screen prompts to collect coins and power-ups, and remember to defeat the bots for extra rewards. Enjoy the fun and keep running!
