Star Lines

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Star Lines, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking as you aim to align stars in order to win! Your goal is to shift the stars into vacant spaces on the board, creating rows or columns of five identical stars a thrilling task that will test your problem-solving skills.

To move a star, simply tap on the item you wish to relocate, then tap the desired destination. But be mindful! A star can only travel to a new location if there is a clear path connecting the two. If your move doesn't result in a match, don't worry; three new stars will be added to the board, providing you with continuous opportunities to strategize your next move. However, you must keep an eye on the board s situation; if it becomes overcrowded, your game will come to an end. The pressure mounts as you try to clear the board while eliminating as many duplicates as possible!

Star Lines is not just about moving stars; it's about visualizing potential moves and planning ahead. Each move could create a cascading effect of matches or leave your board in a challenging state. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, this game ensures endless hours of fun. Do you have what it takes to master Star Lines and beat your high score? Jump in and find out!

How to play free Star Lines game online

To play Star Lines, tap on a star to select it, and then choose an empty spot to move it. Create horizontal or vertical lines of five identical stars to score points. Remember, if your move doesn t make a match, three more stars will appear, so strategize wisely!
