Corona Virus.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Corona Virus.io, players step into the shoes of a heroic Saviour Virus, purposefully injected into a Covid-19 patient. Your mission is to combat the relentless Corona Viruses that threaten your host s health. As you navigate through a vibrant and dynamic environment, your main objective is to grow your virus by becoming the one with the largest nose. Every time your nose touches and eliminates a pesky Corona Virus, it will burst, granting you additional points!

The game offers an exhilarating twist where you must also remain vigilant. If you accidentally collide with other competing viruses, it results in your defeat, and the game ends abruptly. This intricate balance between aggression and evasion creates a challenging and compelling gameplay experience, pushing you to strategize your every move. With each virus you defeat, watch as your nose grows longer and more formidable, but be cautious other players have the same goal and will be vying for the top position!

Players will enjoy an exciting blend of action and strategic planning as they aim to dominate the leaderboard and establish themselves as the ultimate Saviour Virus. Are you ready to take on the challenge and prove your worth in the war against the Corona Virus?

How to play free Corona Virus.io game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse or arrow keys to navigate your Saviour Virus through the game area. Aim to touch the Corona Viruses to eliminate them and gain points, while avoiding contact with other viruses. Keep growing your nose longer as you progress to dominate the game!
