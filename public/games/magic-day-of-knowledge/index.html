Magic Day of Knowledge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting realm of the "Magic Day of Knowledge"! This delightful celebration marks the World Day of Knowledge, where young sorceresses rush excitedly to their magical academy. As they prepare for their first day, it's crucial to adhere to the school's dress code, which surprisingly allows for a dash of style and flair.

Join these aspiring witches as they explore an extensive wardrobe filled with trendy school uniforms. From stylish blouses and tailored jackets to adorable skirts and chic pants, the choices are yours to make as you help them curate the perfect outfit. Experiment with various outfit combinations to discover unique styles that express each sorceress s personality and charm.

Accessorizing is key in the world of magic as well! Select cute handbags, sparkling necklaces, and stylish earrings to complement the girls looks. This adds a touch of whimsy and sparkle that every fairy needs to shine. And let s not forget the ultimate accessory wings! After all, what would a fairy be without their beautiful wings to flutter about?

How to play free Magic Day of Knowledge game online

In the game, navigate through the wardrobe by clicking on different clothing items to mix and match outfits for the sorceresses. Use your mouse to select blouses, skirts, jackets, and accessories. Combine different pieces to create a stylish back-to-school look, ensuring each magical girl stands out as they head to their classes!
