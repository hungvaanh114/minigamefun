Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure in Lava And Ninja Skateboard! As the molten lava rushes down the mountainside, it s up to you, the agile ninja, to skate away to safety! Awaken your inner ninja as you glide down treacherous slopes on your skateboard, dodging obstacles and collecting valuable darts along the way. Each dart you gather not only boosts your score but also provides an opportunity to upgrade your ninja's powers and abilities at the in-game shop.

The fast-paced gameplay keeps you on your toes as you navigate through various terrains while avoiding fiery dangers. Your goal is to skate as far as possible, unlocking hidden characters and striving to achieve an impressive high score. With its exciting mechanics and engaging challenges, Lava And Ninja Skateboard offers endless fun for players seeking to test their reflexes and skills. The game supports both English and Russian, making it accessible for a wider audience.

How to play free Lava And Ninja Skateboard game online

Once you're in the game, simply use the left and right arrow keys to steer your skateboard. Press the space bar to jump over obstacles and collect darts. Remember to keep moving forward to outrun the lava while unlocking upgrades and hidden characters. Good luck!
