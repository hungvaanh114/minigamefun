On a seemingly ordinary day, you and your brother decide to attend a captivating puppet show in your town. The vibrant colors and whimsical characters pull you into a world of enchantment and laughter. However, as the curtains draw to a close, and the audience erupts in applause, an unexpected twist awaits. You both eagerly rush to meet the puppet artist, hoping for an autograph to remember this delightful experience.

But in a startling turn of events, you and your brother are suddenly kidnapped by a gang of puppet artists! What felt like a fun-filled day has turned into a terrifying adventure. The puppet artist s home, once a place of creativity and joy, now transforms into a maze filled with mystery and danger. Your only goal is to escape and free yourselves from the clutches of this bizarre gang.

In this thrilling escape game, you must use your wits and problem-solving skills to navigate through the artist s home. Explore each room carefully, gather clues, and solve intricate puzzles to unlock the secrets that hold you captive. Time is of the essence, as you race against the clock to find a way out before the puppet gang returns!

How to play free The Puppet game online

To play The Puppet game online, simply use the mouse or touch controls to explore your environment. Search for hidden objects and interact with various items to solve puzzles. Keep an eye on clues that can help guide your escape, and work quickly to devise a successful plan for getting out!
