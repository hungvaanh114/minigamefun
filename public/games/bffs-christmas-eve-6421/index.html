BFFs Christmas Eve

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Christmas is right around the corner, and best friends Sofia and Barbara are ready to embrace the holiday spirit in their own delightful ways! In this charming game, join the duo as they prepare for a magical Christmas Eve celebration. Barbara has the exciting task of decorating the Christmas tree to perfection. With an array of ornaments, tinsel, and twinkling lights at her disposal, she ll need your keen eye for design to create a festive masterpiece that will light up the room.

Meanwhile, Sofia is focused on selecting the most wonderful Christmas gifts. Help her navigate through a plethora of colorful presents, choosing the perfect ones for their friends and family. From selecting shiny wrapping paper to adding little details like ribbons or tags, you can make this gift-giving experience truly special.

As the finishing touch to their Christmas Eve preparations, Sofia and Barbara want to dress up in beautiful outfits that will make them shine on this special night. Help them pick out stunning dresses, coordinating accessories, and stylish hairstyles that will ensure they look their absolute best. Making memories is what the holiday season is all about, and with your help, Sofia and Barbara will have a Christmas Eve to remember!

How to play free BFFs Christmas Eve game online

To play the BFFs Christmas Eve game, simply start by selecting Barbara to help her decorate the Christmas tree. Use your mouse to drag and drop decorations where you see fit! Afterward, switch to Sofia and assist her in picking the perfect gifts, then finally choose gorgeous outfits for both girls to complete their festive look!
