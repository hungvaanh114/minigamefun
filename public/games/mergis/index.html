Mergis

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Mergis, where strategy meets fun! In this engaging puzzle game, your primary goal is to strategically release Mergis that share the same number. The challenge lies in entering the right lane to release these quirky little characters. Once they are in place, you can merge them vertically to create an even higher number, leading to fantastic combinations!

As you delve deeper into the game, you ll discover various power-ups that can significantly enhance your gameplay. These power-ups are your secret weapons, assisting you in making the perfect moves at just the right moments. Use them wisely to maximize your score and unlock new possibilities in each level of Mergis. The excitement lies not only in merging but also in strategizing your moves for the highest score.

The game's vibrant graphics and intuitive interface make it accessible for players of all ages. Every level offers a new challenge, encouraging you to think critically and quickly. With each successful merge, the satisfaction of watching your Mergis evolve keeps you coming back for more. So, are you ready to embark on a unique merging adventure and see just how high you can score?

How to play free Mergis game online

To play Mergis, begin by selecting a lane to release a Mergi. Once in the game, merge Mergis with the same number by arranging them vertically. Keep an eye on power-ups, as they can help you maximize your merges. Aim for higher scores by creating larger numbers through strategic placements!


Instructions

Touch in the screen lanes to release the Mergis
