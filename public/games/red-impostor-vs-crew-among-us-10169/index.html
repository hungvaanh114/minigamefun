Red Impostor vs Crew - Among Us

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Our adorable little robots have made their return in the thrilling world of Among Us. In the exciting game titled Red Impostor Vs Crew, you will embark on a memorable adventure aboard a spaceship floating in the vastness of space. This time, you ll take on the role of a cunning assassin alongside your Among Us companions.

Your objective in Red Impostor Vs Crew is to locate hidden swords scattered throughout the spaceship. Once you've found these weapons, don t hesitate to put them to use. But beware! Your mission is highly classified, and secrecy is of the utmost importance. If any crew member spots you, your mission could come crashing down. The challenge lies in eliminating each crewmate without drawing attention to yourself.

Every level presents a unique task: eliminate all crew members while maintaining your stealthy persona. The gameplay is incredibly straightforward, making it easy to dive right in and start your stealthy campaign. Are you prepared to take on this exhilarating challenge? Get ready to complete your mission and move on to the next exciting level!

How to play free Red Impostor Vs Crew - Among Us game online

To play, navigate your character through the spaceship using arrow keys or touch controls. Explore different rooms to find swords. Once you find a sword, use it to eliminate crewmates unnoticed. Keep an eye on the surroundings to ensure no one is watching you while hunting your targets!
