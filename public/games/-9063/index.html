Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Cake Shop: Bake Lover! Your very own cake shop has just opened its doors, and it's time to unleash your inner pastry chef! Are you prepared to whip up scrumptious cakes that will satisfy the cravings of your eager customers? Don t fret if you're new to baking, as this game is designed to guide you every step of the way. Here, you can learn the art of cake-making while honing your skills in managing a bustling cake shop.

In Cake Shop: Bake Lover, you'll embark on an exciting journey filled with mouth-watering ingredients and endless creativity. Choose from a variety of recipes that range from classic cakes to innovative designs that will please both the eye and palate. Your customers have unique tastes, and it s your job to cater to their desires. Experiment with different flavors and decorations to create cakes that will keep them coming back for more.

As your skills improve and your cake shop grows, you'll unlock new recipes and vital upgrades that will enhance your baking capabilities. Remember, time is of the essence in the kitchen! Manage your time wisely to ensure each order is completed promptly. Your journey in cake-making is just beginning, and your tasty creations are sure to make a lasting impression on everyone who visits your shop.

How to play free Cake Shop: Bake Lover game online

To play Cake Shop: Bake Lover, start by selecting your ingredients and choosing a cake recipe from the menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to mix, bake, and decorate your cakes. Use your mouse to drag and drop ingredients and tools, ensuring each order is completed to perfection before time runs out!
