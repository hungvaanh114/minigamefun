Immerse yourself in a thrilling aquatic adventure with Squirt Gun War! This exciting game allows players to wield an array of colorful squirt guns while embarking on a treasure-hunting quest. To begin playing Squirt Gun War, simply choose your hero and select a squirt gun. Move around the map using the arrow keys or WASD, and aim your water weapon with your mouse. Splash opponents to gain points and search for treasures be strategic to outsmart others in this epic water fight!
