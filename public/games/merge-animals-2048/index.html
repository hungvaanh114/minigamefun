Merge Animals 2048!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Your goal in this fun game is to connect identical animals and get new ones! Guide the animal straight to its double in the game arena and connect them into one cooler animal! Compete with your friends in the ranking and have fun! Can you find out which animal is the biggest?
