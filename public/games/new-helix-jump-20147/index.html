NEW HELIX JUMP

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing New Helix Jump, an exciting hyper-casual game that combines simple gameplay with challenging mechanics. In this game, players control a bouncing ball that traverses a spiraling helix tower. As you descend, you must carefully navigate through the platform's gaps and avoid the treacherous red sections that will end your game. The excitement comes from the need for precision and timing as the ball bounces at dizzying speeds.

The game boasts vibrant graphics and fast-paced action that keeps players engaged for hours. Perfect for players of all ages, New Helix Jump offers a straightforward yet addictive gameplay experience. As the ball jumps through each layer of the tower, you will find yourself needing to focus intently to anticipate the next move. Can you muster the skill and reflexes needed to conquer the helix tower and achieve a new high score?

With intuitive controls, New Helix Jump allows you to rotate the tower using your mouse button, facilitating the ball's path. The faster and more strategically you rotate, the better your chances of avoiding the red areas, which leads to more extensive gameplay sessions and a vibrant leaderboard experience.

How to play free NEW HELIX JUMP game online

To play New Helix Jump, simply click and hold the left mouse button to rotate the helix tower. Release the button to let the ball bounce downwards. Your objective is to make it through the gaps without hitting any red parts. Trust your instincts, and aim for the lowest possible score as you leap through the tower!
