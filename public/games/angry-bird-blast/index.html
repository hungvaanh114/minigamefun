Angry Bird Blast

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to experience the excitement of slingshot shooting with "Angry Bird Blast," one of the top offline games released by SPARTANS GLOBAL INC. This engaging adventure invites you into a thrilling world of bottle shooting, where you must harness your skills to knock down targets with precision. Featuring a captivating narrative about lost pirate kids, this game seamlessly blends physics-based mechanics with fun gameplay to keep you entertained for hours.

In "Angry Bird Blast," players are challenged to utilize their catapult prowess to take down bottles in creative ways. As you progress, you'll discover that each level presents unique challenges, requiring keen aim and strategic thinking. With updated features and stunning graphics, this shooting game guarantees an immersive arcade experience that stands out among both new and seasoned players.

The game's intuitive controls allow players of all ages to jump right in and start having fun. As you aim to become the ultimate bottle shooter, you can unlock special power-ups that enhance your shooting skills and impact. Compete against friends and other players to see who can achieve the highest score and claim the title of best bottle shooter.

How to play free Angry Bird Blast game online

To play Angry Bird Blast, simply tap and hold to adjust your aim, then release your finger to launch the slingshot at the bottles. Aim for the perfect shot to knock down as many bottles as possible. Keep an eye out for power-ups that appear during the game, as they will enhance your shooting capabilities!
