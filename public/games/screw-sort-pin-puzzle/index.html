Screw Sort Pin Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into the clever world of Screw Sort-Pin Puzzle, where logic and strategy come together! Your task is to sort the colorful screws, click three screws of the same color as the glass, the glass will be shot. You need to pull pins in the right order to avoid chaos!
