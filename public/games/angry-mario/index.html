ANGRY MARIO

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spooktacular adventure with ANGRY MARIO, where our favorite plumber brothers set off on an exciting quest this Halloween! This game is not your typical Mario experience. As you journey through creatively themed environments adorned with Halloween flair, you will encounter an array of spooky adversaries, such as zombies, skeletons, witches, and vampires, each adding to the eerie atmosphere.

If you re looking for a twist, don t be surprised to find the familiar collection of fruits has been replaced by delightfully carved pumpkins! These festive collectibles contribute to your score while immersing you deeper into the Halloween spirit. Explore each level filled with numerous challenges that will test your agility and reflexes to the max. With each jump, dash, and power-up, you aim to conquer the game while setting new personal bests in your playthrough of Super Mario Halloween!

With an array of levels that keep you on your toes, the combination of classic Mario gameplay with Halloween-themed excitement ensures that you have a thrilling experience. As you navigate through each stage, your skills will be put to the ultimate test. So gather your courage, put on your ghostly costume, and join Mario and Luigi on their hair-raising Halloween adventure!

How to play free ANGRY MARIO game online

To play ANGRY MARIO, simply use the arrow keys to navigate through the levels. Jump over obstacles and enemies, collect carved pumpkins for points, and try to complete each level as quickly as possible. Use the spacebar for special power-ups and unleash your skills to beat your high score!
