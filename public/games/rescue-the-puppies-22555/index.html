Rescue the Puppies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rescue The Puppies is an engaging point-and-click adventure game crafted by 8B Games/Games2mad. In this captivating narrative, players find themselves at a farmstead where they discover two adorable puppies trapped in a cage, much to the distress of their mother dog. The scene is set as the mother dog embarks on a quest to locate her precious pups, who have fallen victim to the forester's actions. As she ventures into the area, players must navigate through an intriguing environment filled with puzzles and hidden objects.

Your mission is to assist the mother dog in her desperate search for her lost puppies. You'll need to solve various challenges while looking for clues and items that will help unlock the cage. Each puzzle you complete brings you one step closer to reuniting the puppies with their mother. With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, this game is designed to not only entertain but also to stimulate your problem-solving skills.

As you progress, you'll encounter a series of intriguing scenarios that will test your wit and creativity. Can you uncover all the hidden objects and pieces together the clues needed to free the puppies? With good luck and determination, get ready to have a fantastic time on this heartwarming adventure!

How to play free Rescue The Puppies game online

To start playing Rescue The Puppies, simply click on objects in the environment to interact with them. Gather hidden items and solve puzzles by dragging and dropping your acquired objects where they are needed. Use your observation skills to click on clues that will aid in unlocking the cage, and remember, every detail can lead you closer to the puppies!
