Cannon Shot Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Cannon Shot Online, a thrilling and engaging puzzle game that puts your strategic thinking to the test! Your primary objective is simple but enticing: fill all the buckets with balls to complete each exciting level. With a variety of challenges awaiting you, every stage offers unique obstacles that require more than just skill; they demand clever thinking to ensure the balls land exactly where you want them.

Using your finger, you move different objects scattered across the board to alter the trajectory of the balls you shoot. Adjusting these elements is crucial as they can either propel your balls directly into the buckets or lead them astray. It s all about precision and smart aiming! As you progress through the levels, you ll unlock new cannons, each with its unique firing mechanics and abilities, adding layers of fun and strategy to your gameplay.

The visually vibrant graphics and satisfying ball physics create an enjoyable atmosphere that keeps players engaged. With numerous levels to conquer, each one more challenging than the last, you ll find yourself immersed in hours of ball-shooting excitement. Whether you re a casual gamer or someone who seeks more intense problem-solving experiences, Cannon Shot Online caters to all! So get ready to master the art of cannon shooting and see how many buckets you can fill!

How to play free Cannon Shot Online game online

To play Cannon Shot Online, start by selecting a level. Use your finger to swipe and adjust the movable objects on the screen. Aim carefully to direct the balls towards the buckets. Complete each level by filling all the buckets to unlock new cannons and advance in the game!
