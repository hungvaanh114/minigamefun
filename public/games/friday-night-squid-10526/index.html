Friday Night Squid

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating ride in Friday Night Squid, where your favorite characters, Boyfriend and Girlfriend, embark on a thrilling new journey! After a night of reckless gambling, they've found themselves in a precarious position, only to realize they must compete in the notorious Squid Games. Now, it's up to you to help them navigate through impossible challenges and recover their lost fortunes.

The stakes are high, and the competition is fierce as they fight to complete a series of musical levels in different weeks. Your top objective is to ace each song performance while avoiding the deadly pitfalls that await. With dangerous opponents around, you must stay sharp and keep the rhythm flowing to secure the grand prize of $38 million! But beware many will fall in their pursuit of victory, so every moment counts.

As you guide Boyfriend and Girlfriend through 12 intense levels of music-filled mayhem, focus on hitting all the arrows to keep the beat and fend off failures. The rhythm combines the high-energy gameplay you love from Friday Night Funkin with the dramatic tension of survival games, providing an electrifying experience. Success isn t just a matter of skill; timing is everything in this fight for survival!

How to play free Friday Night Squid game online

To play Friday Night Squid online, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard to match the corresponding arrows that appear on the screen. Keep an eye on the rhythm and ensure you hit every note in sequence. The better your timing, the higher your score will be, propelling Boyfriend and Girlfriend closer to victory!
