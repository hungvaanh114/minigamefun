Physics Knife

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Physics Knife invites players into a stunningly immersive experience that makes you feel as if you re wielding a real knife. With intuitive controls, you can throw, rotate, and interact with your weapon in ways you ve only dreamed of before. Every movement feels organic, thanks to the advanced physics engine. But if a knife isn t quite enough for your adventurous spirit, why not try your hand at other weaponry? Engage with axes or shurikens, adding even more variety to your gameplay. This is the first game ever to feature incredibly realistic knife-throwing mechanics, allowing for a deep and satisfying challenge that keeps you coming back for more.

Imagine standing in a beautifully crafted environment where the nuances of weapon handling come into play. The feel of the knife in your hand, the arc of its flight through the air every throw is driven by physics that react realistically to your movements. Whether you re aiming at a target or trying to reach a specific score, the intricacies of physics make every attempt unique and thrilling. You can test your skills, increase your accuracy, and discover various techniques to master the art of knife throwing. The experience is both fun and challenging, making for hours of engaging gameplay.

How to play free Physics Knife game online

To play Physics Knife, simply load the game on your preferred device. Use your mouse or touch controls to aim your weapon and swipe to throw. You can rotate the knife by flicking your wrist or adjusting your grip. Try to hit the targets to rack up points!
