Instructions:

Flat Jumper is an engaging puzzle game that challenges players to skillfully navigate a bouncing ball across various platforms. In this game, you'll control a colorful jumper ball that leaps from one platform to another. Each platform is designed in a different color, corresponding to three balls located at the bottom of the screen.

Your goal is to ensure that the jumping ball matches the color of the platform it lands on. To achieve this, simply click on one of the colored balls at the bottom. The color of the jumper ball will instantly change to that of the selected ball. As new platforms appear, you need to continuously match your jumper ball to the color of the platform to keep progressing through the game. This mechanic creates a delightful twist on traditional jumping games, emphasizing quick thinking and fast reactions.

The challenge intensifies as you advance, with platforms becoming more complex and varied in color. Your success depends on your ability to swiftly adapt and make decisions in real-time. Strive to hop over as many platforms as you can while maintaining the correct color. Each successful jump will boost your score and enhance your gaming experience. So, get ready to jump, switch colors, and see how far you can go in this captivating Flat Jumper adventure!

How to play free Flat Jumper Game game online

To play Flat Jumper, simply control the jumping ball by clicking on the colored balls at the bottom of the screen. Change the color of the jumper ball to match the platform it's about to land on by tapping on the corresponding color. Keep jumping and matching colors to achieve a higher score as you progress through the game!
