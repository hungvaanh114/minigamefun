"Cannon Ball + Pop It Fidget" is an exciting game that fuses the thrill of a shooting challenge reminiscent of Angry Birds with the delightful and calming experience of a Pop It Fidget game. As you advance through various levels, you'll unlock new Fidget designs, allowing you to enjoy the simple pleasure of popping bubbles while also interacting with vibrant colors and soothing sounds. Your mission is clear: beat every level and collect stars to achieve a perfect score! Aim to conquer the game 100%!

The Cannon Ball segment offers a dynamic gameplay experience where your precision and problem-solving skills are put to the test. With an intuitive Drag and Drop mechanism, you can stretch the rubber band to launch your cannonball and take down various targets. Use strategy and aim for explosive crates that will help you knock down multiple targets with a single shot, maximizing your score and gameplay enjoyment.

On the other hand, the Pop It Fidget game component presents a fun, stress-relieving activity. Here, you must conquer all Cannon Ball levels to unlock an array of delightful figures. Once unlocked, enjoy the satisfaction of popping bubbles while changing colors and sounds, making it a perfect way to unwind. Dive into this engaging combination of skill and relaxation today!

How to play free Cannon Ball + Pop It Fidget game online

To play the game, start by dragging the rubber band backward to aim and adjust your power. Release to launch the cannonball and hit targets like crates and TNT. Once you clear a level, you can pop bubbles in the Pop It section, where every interaction creates unique colors and sounds!
