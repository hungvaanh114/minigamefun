Emoji Color Sort Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the challenging world of Emoji Color Sort Puzzle! This engaging game takes sorting puzzles to the next level with its vibrant 3D emojis and colorful tubes. Your mission is to strategically organize all the same emojis into designated tubes throughout various levels. Whether you are a novice or an expert, this game provides an exhilarating experience that will keep you on your toes.

Begin your journey in the Beginner mode, where you will learn essential sorting skills and familiarize yourself with the mechanics of the game. As you progress, you will discover three additional difficulty levels: Intermediate, Advanced, and Expert. Each level introduces a wider variety of emojis and reduces the number of available tubes, making it increasingly difficult to organize your emojis effectively. As you advance, you will encounter unique challenges that require strategic thinking and decision-making, especially when determining the best starting point for sorting.

The game not only enhances your problem-solving skills but also offers a colorful and delightful aesthetics as you sort through countless emojis. Compete against the clock, race through levels, and enjoy hours of entertainment. Will you rise to the challenge and master Emoji Color Sort Puzzle? Good luck!

How to play free Emoji Color Sort Puzzle game online

To start playing, simply tap on a tube to select it, then tap on another tube to transfer emojis. Your goal is to move the emojis so that each tube contains only one type. Keep an eye on the tubes and plan your moves wisely to avoid getting stuck!
