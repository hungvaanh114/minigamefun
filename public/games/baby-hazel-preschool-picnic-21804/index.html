Baby Hazel Preschool Picnic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel on an exhilarating adventure as her school organizes an exciting picnic to the local water park! With endless enthusiasm, Baby Hazel is preparing for a day filled with splashes and laughter. Your mission is to assist her in packing her picnic bag with all the necessary essentials for a fun-filled day in the sun, including swimwear and floaties.

The adventure begins as Baby Hazel boards the school bus with her friends. Experience the joyful ride filled with laughter and games as they travel towards the water park. The bus journey is not just about getting there; it s an opportunity for the kids to bond and enjoy various fun activities together. Once they arrive, the real excitement kicks off!

At the water park, Baby Hazel, along with her friends, will take on thrilling water slides that promise to provide an adrenaline rush. The charm of the water park lies in its colorful slides and refreshing pools, where the kids can splash around and create joyful memories. Join Hazel in various fun games and activities that await at the water park. Get ready to dive into endless laughter and make unforgettable moments this picnic day!
