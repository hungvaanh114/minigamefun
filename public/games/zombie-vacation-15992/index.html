Zombie Vacation

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Zombie Vacation, where your dream getaway turns into a nightmare! You are no ordinary tourist; you are a special forces soldier whose plane has crash-landed on a remote tropical island. Instead of sandy beaches and coconut drinks, you find yourself besieged by hordes of relentless zombies. This unexpected vacation rapidly transforms into a fight for survival as you are faced with waves of the undead.

Equipped with your modern sub-machine gun, the goal is simple yet exhilarating: shoot as many zombies as possible while evading their hungry grasp. As each wave of undead creatures descends upon you, your reflexes and tactical skills will be put to the test. Utilize the scenic environment to your advantage, taking cover and navigating the landscape while you strategize your attacks. Each eliminated zombie brings you one step closer to survival, but they are relentless and ever-present.

Your mission won't be easy; the island is filled with different types of zombies, each presenting unique challenges that will require quick thinking and precision aiming. Be prepared to face increasingly difficult waves that will test your limits. Upgrade your gear and unlock powerful weapons to fortify your chances against the onslaught. Will you emerge as the island's last hero, or will you become just another victim?

How to play free Zombie Vacation game online

To play Zombie Vacation, simply start the game and use your mouse to aim at the incoming zombies. Click to fire your sub-machine gun and eliminate them before they reach you. Make sure to move around to dodge their attacks, and be ready to reload to continue your fight for survival!
