Danger Floor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Danger Floor, an exhilarating platform game that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking! In this thrilling adventure, players must navigate treacherous terrain where the floor turns into molten lava within seconds. Be quick on your feet and evade the fiery danger, or you'll find yourself engulfed in flames!

Your mission is to dodge various red obstacles and ferocious wolves that roam the increasingly perilous landscape. To gain the upper hand, search for the elusive blue ball, which temporarily grants you immunity to hazards. Not only can you withstand fire and wolves while under its effect, but you also have the power to extinguish flames and freeze floors, turning the tides in your favor.

The blue ball reappears in the same spot after a short duration, so plan your movements carefully! Any wolves that tread on frozen floors will slow down, giving you a chance to outmaneuver them. Moreover, if you touch a wolf on the frozen ground, you'll receive the same abilities granted by the blue ball, opening new opportunities for survival. Want to restart a level? You can, but beware that doing so will cost you a life!

Danger Floor is designed for both mobile devices and computers, ensuring you can enjoy the game anywhere you go. Utilize the controls WASD or arrow keys for movement, Shift to restart, and H or Space to unleash your fire-extinguishing abilities. Can you survive the chaos and make it to the end?

How to play free Danger Floor game online

To play Danger Floor, use the W, A, S, and D keys or the arrow keys to navigate your character. Watch the floor closely as it turns to lava! Collect the blue ball for temporary immunity, and use your fire extinguishing abilities with H or Space to clear paths and freeze floors. Avoid wolves and red obstacles to survive!
