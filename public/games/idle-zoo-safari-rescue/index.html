Idle Zoo: Safari Rescue

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Idle Zoo: Safari Rescue, an exciting idle management game where you can take charge of your very own zoo teeming with exotic Safari animals! Start your journey with a majestic elephant, and as you progress, unlock a captivating array of wildlife including colorful parrots, cunning wolves, and powerful bears, along with numerous surprises from the enchanting African landscape.

Your primary goal is to nurture and enhance the abilities of your animals while ensuring they remain well-fed and healthy. As you invest resources into the care and upgrade of your animals, you'll reap greater rewards, leading to an increasingly prosperous zoo. The more you nurture your beasts, the more you'll earn in return!

What's particularly exciting about Idle Zoo: Safari Rescue is the unique feature that allows you to release animals back into the wild. By doing so, you'll gain special green powers which can be utilized to unlock even more enhancements and upgrades for your zoo. This engaging and dynamic gameplay keeps you motivated as you explore the extensive world of Safari life. Are you ready to embark on this wild adventure and build the most amazing zoo ever?

How to play free Idle Zoo: Safari Rescue game online

To play Idle Zoo: Safari Rescue, simply tap on the animals to collect resources. Use the earned coins to purchase upgrades and unlock new animal species. Manage your zoo by ensuring they are fed and cared for, and remember to release them to gain green powers for further enhancements!
