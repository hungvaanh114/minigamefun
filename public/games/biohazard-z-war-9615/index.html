Biohazard Z War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the gripping world of Biohazard Z War, humanity's struggle for survival against a relentless zombie apocalypse takes center stage. The world as we know it has succumbed to the undead, and the only option available is to endure every day while battling these terrifying foes. Armed with an arsenal of weapons and wits, your mission is straightforward yet harrowing: survive at all costs.

As you navigate through the treacherous landscapes, your objective is to eliminate as many zombies as you can. Collect vital supplies such as ammunition, fuel, food, and water, which are essential for sustaining your fight against the horde. With an intuitive control system, you can quickly engage and dispatch the zombie threat, while the unique body dismemberment system adds a thrilling layer of brutality to the combat. Prepare for pulse-pounding action as you progress through an intricately designed setting filled with varying challenges.

Biohazard Z War consists of a staggering 20 levels, each designed to test your survival skills and strategic thinking. As you advance, you will earn coins that can be used to upgrade and purchase new weapons, ensuring you stay one step ahead in this post-apocalyptic battlefield. Get ready to embark on a heart-pounding journey where every decision counts. Good luck, survivor!

How to play free Biohazard Z War game online

To play Biohazard Z War online, simply access the game through your preferred gaming platform. Once you are in the game, use the on-screen controls to move your character, aim your weapon, and shoot zombies. Collect items to enhance your survival chances while progressing through the levels. Good luck surviving the onslaught!
