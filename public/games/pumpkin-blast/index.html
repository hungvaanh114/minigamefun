Pumpkin Blast

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Pumpkin Blast, where you assist Jack, the brave pumpkin, in breakout from his ominous prison! This engaging puzzle game challenges players to navigate through increasingly complex levels, aiming to guide Jack safely to the designated area with the fewest attempts possible. With clever use of explosives, you must strategize each move carefully while avoiding perilous spikes that threaten to thwart your progress. Every decision counts as you unleash explosive power to propel Jack forward, ensuring he avoids hazards along the way.

With each completed level, players can strive for perfection by collecting up to three stars, making every attempt rewarding and exciting. The game offers a perfect blend of strategy and quick thinking, ensuring players of all ages will find the thrill of experimenting with different tactics to achieve the best score. The vibrant graphics and engaging sound effects further enhance the gaming experience, immersing you deeper into Jack's daring escapade.

Are you ready to join Jack on his mission and prove your problem-solving skills? Dive into Pumpkin Blast today, and see if you have what it takes to beat all the levels and attain three stars on each one!

How to play free Pumpkin Blast game online

To play the game, use your mouse or touch controls to aim and place explosives strategically. Click or tap to detonate the explosives, guiding Jack towards the safe area while dodging spikes. Keep track of your attempts to maximize your star rating at the end of each level!
