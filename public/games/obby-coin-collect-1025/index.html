Embark on a thrilling journey in Obby Coin Collect, where our hero Obby and his buddy Bacon face off against an array of daunting challenges! This exhilarating parkour adventure promises to test your agility and response as you navigate through a vibrant yet hazardous landscape filled with obstacles aiming to trip you up at every turn.

Choose your character wisely; will you guide the courageous Obby or the quirky Bacon? Only the quickest and most attentive players will prevail as they strive to collect coins scattered throughout the course while dodging these perilous traps!

Join Obby and Bacon to embrace the exhilarating thrill of Obby Coin Collect. Are you ready to jump, run, and collect your way to victory? Test your skills today and rise to become the champion of the parkour realm!

How to play free Obby Coin Collect game online

To play Obby Coin Collect, choose your character Bacon or Obby and start navigating through the parkour course. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to jump and run, avoiding obstacles like balloons and rotating bales while collecting coins. Keep an eye out for your flying pet to enhance your adventure!
