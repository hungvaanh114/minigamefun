Vikings : War of Clans

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Vikings: War of Clans is an exhilarating online game that immerses players in the fierce world of Viking warriors. In this action-packed game, you can participate in epic battles by simply touching the screen or by long-pressing the left mouse button to hurl an axe at an adversary. Perfecting your throw requires keen attention to the wind, as it influences the trajectory and strength of your axe. Players must strategize their attacks while navigating the dangers of Viking combat.

This captivating game offers two distinct modes: you can either challenge the AI or engage in multiplayer battles. Whether you're going head-to-head with computer-controlled enemies or competing against friends and family, Vikings: War of Clans guarantees an exciting experience filled with tactical decisions and fierce competition. Form alliances or go solo; the choice is yours as you build your Viking empire and conquer others in your quest for glory.

With its stunning graphics and immersive gameplay, Vikings: War of Clans is not just about throwing axes; it's about strategy, teamwork, and outmanoeuvring your opponents. Gather your friends for a thrilling multiplayer session or test your skills against the AI. Are you ready to embrace your inner Viking?

How to play free Vikings: War of Clans game online

To get started with Vikings: War of Clans, launch the game and select your desired mode: AI or Multiplayer. Use the touch screen or long-press the left mouse button to aim and throw your axes. Adjust your throwing force according to the wind conditions displayed on the screen for maximum impact. Good luck on your Viking journey!
