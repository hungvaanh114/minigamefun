Pop It Antistress

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pop It Antistress is the best casual game to help you relax and relieve stress. If you love the feeling of popping bubbles and want to relax, this game is the best choice. With simple and enjoyable gameplay, youll experience the soothing joy of touching and pressing the bubbles. Choose a model of a tower, duck or fruit, and choose the color you like to start your decompression bubble.
