Mermaid Girl Wedding Cooking Cake

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Adella and Eric in a delightful adventure as they prepare for their romantic wedding in Hawaii! The stunning backdrop of lush tropical landscapes and serene beaches sets the perfect scene for their special day. To make their marriage unforgettable, you have a vital role in crafting the perfect wedding cake for the couple. This exciting game allows you to dive into the world of baking and decorating, showcasing your creativity as you design a cake that embodies love and joy.

As you begin, gather all the necessary ingredients and follow the recipe step by step. From mixing the batter to baking it to golden perfection, you'll be guiding Adella and Eric through each crucial moment of cake preparation. The real fun, however, is in the decoration! You have a wide variety of frosting, toppings, and embellishments at your fingertips. Choose from elegant flowers, shimmering edible glitter, or whimsical sea-themed motifs to illustrate their underwater romance.

Every detail counts as you create a cake that reflects their love story, ensuring it's as beautiful as it is delicious. Make sure to consider the couple's preferences and the wedding's color scheme in your design. With your skills, Adella and Eric's big day will be an enchanting celebration, marked with a stunning cake that everyone will remember!

How to play free Mermaid Girl Wedding Cooking Cake game online

To start playing, choose the ingredients from the shelf and mix them in a bowl following the on-screen instructions. Once the cake is baked, enter the decoration phase where you can drag and drop various toppings onto the cake. Make it beautiful and unique to ensure Adella and Eric's special day is as magical as they envisioned!
