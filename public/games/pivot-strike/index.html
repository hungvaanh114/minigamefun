Pivot Strike

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pivot Strike is an innovative and engaging game that brings a fresh twist to naval combat. With an intuitive control scheme, players can effortlessly drag their ships around the screen with just a single touch. Additionally, tapping the screen allows players to pivot their vessels, creating tactical advantages during heated battles.

The gameplay mechanics emphasize strategy; when a player lines up their ships, the leading vessel experiences a significant increase in firepower, boasting double the fire rate. When ships are positioned side by side, they can still fire but at a diminished rate of half. Each ship has unique strengths and weaknesses; the Blue Ship is designed to take down blue enemies and has the ability to absorb red bullets, while the Red Ship is tasked with destroying red enemies, gaining the capacity to absorb blue bullets. This duality of ship functionality creates dynamic gameplay where positioning can turn the tide of battle.

Players can also engage with a rewarding trophy system, unlocking three distinct trophies based on their performance and scores. This incentive encourages players to perfect their strategies and achieve higher scores. The unique combination of tactical positioning, ship specialization, and an interactive environment ensures that every match in Pivot Strike is thrilling and competitive. Join the fray and see if you can navigate your ships to victory!

How to play free Pivot Strike game online

To play Pivot Strike, simply drag your ships around the screen to maneuver them strategically. Tap to pivot your ships into position for optimal firing. Remember to align your ships for increased fire rate and absorb enemy bullets to protect yourself. Focus on defeating enemies of the corresponding colors for victory!
