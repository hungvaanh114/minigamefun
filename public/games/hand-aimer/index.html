Hand Aimer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Hand Aimer, an exciting arcade game that tests your precision and timing! In this fast-paced challenge, your objective is to hit the target using a virtual mech-hand by throwing a ball. As you navigate through various levels, you have the ability to adjust the speed and direction of your hand to enhance your aim. The dynamic gameplay ensures that no two rounds are alike, providing you with hours of entertainment.

What makes Hand Aimer particularly thrilling is the bouncing mechanic. When you successfully bounce the ball off surfaces within the game, you accumulate extra points, adding a layer of strategy to your aiming techniques. Mastering the bounce can significantly increase your score, rewarding skillful players who take the time to learn the optimal angles. With a user-friendly interface and captivating graphics, players of all ages can enjoy the challenge that Hand Aimer provides. Aim carefully, calculate your moves, and see how high you can score!

How to play free Hand Aimer game online

To play Hand Aimer, use your mouse or touchscreen to control the mech-hand's direction and speed. Click to throw the ball at the target and adjust your angle for optimal bounces. Score points by hitting the aim accurately and performing bounces off surfaces. Keep trying to beat your high score!
