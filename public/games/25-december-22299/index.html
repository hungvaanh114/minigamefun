25 December

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the excitement of the holiday season with the engaging game *25 December*! Challenge yourself to reach the festive date of December 25th by skillfully merging identical dates on the board. You can do this by swiping your finger across the screen or using the arrow keys on your keyboard. Each successful merge will bring you one step closer to Christmas, creating new dates that lead you along your festive journey.

The game presents an enjoyable puzzle that requires both strategy and a bit of festive spirit. As you navigate through the board filled with dates of the month, aim to combine them into higher dates. The further you get, the more complex the board becomes, challenging you to think critically about your moves. Not to worry if you find yourself in a tight spot, you have a limited number of Shuffle and Regroup options at your disposal to rearrange the board and keep the gameplay flowing smoothly.

Are you up for the challenge? Dive into this delightful game that s perfect for anyone looking to embrace the holiday atmosphere while exercising their mind. With each strategic move, your excitement builds toward celebrating the joyful occasion of Christmas on December 25th.

How to play free 25 December game online

To play the *25 December* game online, simply start by swiping on the screen or using the arrow keys to merge identical dates. Keep a keen eye on your moves, and when you find yourself stuck, utilize Shuffle or Regroup options to clear your path. Aim for December 25th!
