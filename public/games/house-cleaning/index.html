House Cleaning

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of House Cleaning! Step into the enchanting realm of a stunning baby doll house, where every corner beckons for a meticulous tidying up. This whimsical abode is adorned with multiple rooms filled with scattered toys, clothes, and various decorations that need a loving hand to restore order.

Prepare yourself with an array of cleaning tools at your disposal! Grab your broom to sweep up the dust, your mop to shine the floors, and a set of cloths to polish the surfaces. As you navigate through the charming rooms, each one presents a different cleaning challenge. From the colorful playroom strewn with plush toys to the cozy kitchen with crumbs scattered about, it s your mission to make this house sparkle and shine once more!

Take your time to organize each room, putting everything back in its rightful place. You can move items around to ensure the doll house is not only clean but also aesthetically pleasing. This game not only fosters creativity but also improves your organizational skills as you transform this cluttered dollhouse into a tidy, inviting space for playtime! Enjoy the satisfying experience of cleaning and watch as your hard work brings joy to this whimsical setting.

How to play free House Cleaning game online

To start playing the House Cleaning game, simply click on the play button when prompted. Use your mouse to select cleaning tools and drag them around the house to tidy up. Follow on-screen instructions to complete various tasks, ensuring that every room is spotless before moving to the next challenge!
