Dream Restaurant

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Dream Restaurant, where you can create and manage the restaurant of your dreams! In this delightful simulation game, your culinary adventure begins as the cook gets ready to serve mouthwatering meals right from the start of January. Your role as a waiter is crucial: invite customers, take their orders, and fulfill their wishes without delay.

As you embark on this flavorful journey, you'll start by serving delicious burgers and sandwiches to hungry patrons. It's all about efficiency gather all the orders in a row and deliver them to the tables promptly to keep your customers satisfied. With each successful order, you'll earn money, which you can reinvest into your restaurant's expansion.

As you progress, you'll have the opportunity to enhance your establishment. Begin with simple tasks, such as adding more tables and hiring additional waitstaff to handle the increasing flow of customers. Soon enough, you ll unlock advanced culinary delights like sushi and donuts, allowing you to diversify your menu and attract a broader clientele. Your goal is to build a flourishing restaurant chain that embodies your dreams while enjoying this engaging 3D clicker game!

How to play free Dream Restaurant game online

To play Dream Restaurant, start by clicking on the available ingredients to prepare meals. Once orders are taken, drag and serve the food to customers. Utilize your earnings to upgrade your restaurant by adding items and hiring staff. Keep the orders flowing to progress and expand your culinary empire!
