Magic Stones Collection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the enchanting world of Magic Stones Collection, you embark on a captivating journey where your skills in strategy and quick thinking will be put to the test. This thrilling HTML5 game invites you to collect an array of magical stones. To begin your adventure, simply press any stone on the screen. Once activated, your task is to skillfully glide your mouse or finger across adjacent stones that share the same design these can be arranged horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

To successfully match stones, make sure to select at least three identical ones before releasing your click or tap. Each time you collect a group of stones, especially on your sixth match, a bonus will be awarded to elevate your score and progress. For those who showcase exceptional skill and gather more than seven stones in one go, a time bonus will also be granted, engaging you further in the game. Remember, the challenge lies in collecting the designated number of magic stones within the time limit; failing to meet this requirement will result in a game over, testing your speed and decision-making abilities.

As you immerse yourself in this enchanting realm, you may find yourself asking, how far can you go? Each level offers new challenges and increasing difficulty, pushing you to reach your maximum potential in this magical stone collection experience.

How to play free Magic Stones Collection game online

To play the Magic Stones Collection game, start by clicking on any magic stone. Move your cursor or finger to highlight adjacent identical stones. Select at least three to create a match and release to score. Aim for quick combinations to earn bonuses and avoid running out of time!
