Baby Taylor Skin Trouble

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Baby Taylor Skin Trouble," you will join Taylor, an adorable little girl with a big love for food, especially spicy dishes. However, her passion for fiery flavors leads to unexpected consequences. After gorging on a large plate of spicy chicken wings and neglecting her nighttime skincare routine, Taylor wakes up to find her face covered in pesky acne.

Your mission is to accompany Taylor to the hospital, where she will receive the necessary treatments for her acne. You'll help her become comfortable during her visit, following the advice of the doctor to ensure her skin heals properly. But the adventure doesn't stop there! Once Taylor returns home, it's your job to instill good habits that will aid in fading those troublesome acne marks.

Throughout the game, engage in fun and educational tasks that promote skincare routines, like proper face washing, using creams, and maintaining a healthy diet. By helping Taylor develop these habits, you ll be on the path to restoring her confidence and beauty. Can you transform Taylor back into the pretty little girl she once was? Join her on this important journey of self-care and health!

How to play free Baby Taylor Skin Trouble game online

To play the "Baby Taylor Skin Trouble" game, use your mouse or touchscreen to complete various tasks. Follow on-screen instructions to help Taylor at the doctor s office, apply treatments, and teach her the proper skincare routine. Pay attention to the time and complete challenges to progress through the game!
