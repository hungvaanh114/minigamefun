Family Picnic of the King

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

After enduring months of relentless rain, the feared typhoon has finally passed, leaving behind a beautiful, sunny day. The queen seizes this rare opportunity to organize a delightful family picnic in the garden, aiming to bask in the warmth of the sun alongside her loved ones. However, the recent downpours have left the garden in disarray. Neglect has taken its toll; the ground is overrun with fallen leaves, scattered branches, wild weeds, and a fence that has seen better days. The once-vibrant garden now appears aged and neglected.

Understanding the urgency of the situation, the queen feels the weight of her responsibilities as she prepares for this important family gathering. She knows that the garden needs a significant cleanup: weeds must be trimmed, leaves cleared away, and the broken fence repaired. In addition, delicious meals must be prepared for the family to enjoy together. The queen stands distressed by the heavy burden of tasks at hand. This is where you come in take on the role of a multi-talented deacon and lend the queen a helping hand!

How to play free Family Picnic Of The King game online

To play the Family Picnic Of The King game online, navigate the vibrant garden scene by using your mouse or touch controls. Your main tasks include trimming weeds, collecting leaves, and repairing the fence. Complete these tasks quickly to prepare the garden for the picnic and ensure the queen and her family can enjoy their well-deserved meal!
