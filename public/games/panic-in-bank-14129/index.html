Panic in Bank

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the shoes of the newly appointed town marshal in "Panic in Bank," where chaos erupts during the highly anticipated payment day! Every month, citizens flock to the local bank to safely deposit their hard-earned money. However, disaster strikes as bandits initiate a daring heist right at that moment, putting everyone in peril. Your mission is to swiftly neutralize the bandits while ensuring the safety of innocent bank depositors.

As the action unfolds, you must utilize your shooting skills to take down the villains wreaking havoc in the bank. Be vigilant, though your accuracy is crucial, as shooting innocent townsfolk is strictly off-limits. Explore the bank's intricate interiors, searching for all 12 doors that hide treasures waiting to be deposited. Only by unlocking each door and collecting the money within can you successfully complete your level and restore peace to your town.

With its charming graphics and engaging gameplay, "Panic in Bank" promises an addictive experience that can be enjoyed by the entire family. The game is designed to be easy to learn, making it accessible to players of all ages. Get ready for an exhilarating challenge as you become the hero that the town needs.

How to play free Panic in Bank game online

To play "Panic in Bank," simply use your mouse to aim and shoot at the bandits while avoiding any innocent civilians. Move through the bank using the arrow keys to navigate and explore all 12 doors. Collect the money behind each door to complete your mission effectively!
