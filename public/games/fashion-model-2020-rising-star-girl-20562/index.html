Fashion Model 2020 - Rising Star Girl

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Calling all fashion enthusiasts! Get ready to dive into the vibrant world of glamour and style with "Fashion Model 2020 - Rising Star Girl," a captivating dress-up game designed for those who adore everything chic. Immerse yourself in the glitzy universe of celebrities, renowned actresses, chart-topping singers, and stunning supermodels as you unleash your creative flair.

This engaging game showcases six diverse dolls, each boasting a beautiful face and a perfect body. You have the freedom to dress each model in unique and glamorous outfits. With eight enchanting backgrounds to choose from, your models can shine in various settings, adding a personal touch to your fashion creations.

Explore over 160 fashion items to curate your ideal looks. Select from an impressive array of hairstyles, dresses, tops, skirts, jewelry, tights, trousers, and accessories to build the ultimate wardrobe. Whether you prefer elegant gowns or trendy casual wear, the possibilities for styling are endless!

Design stunning outfits for each doll and make your mark in the glamorous world of fashion. Showcase your unique sense of style and creativity, and see who can create the most extraordinary looks!

How to play free Fashion Model 2020 - Rising Star Girl game online

To play "Fashion Model 2020 - Rising Star Girl," simply select a doll to start dressing up. Browse through the extensive collection of clothing and accessories, clicking on your favorites to dress the model. Once you re satisfied with the look, choose a stunning background and showcase your fashion expertise!
