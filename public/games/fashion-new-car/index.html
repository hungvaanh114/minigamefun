Fashion New Car

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Fashion New Car, where you're on a mission to assist Barbara in upgrading her old vehicle. Barbara s dream is to drive a stylish new car, but first, she needs your help navigating through the bustling streets. As she drives, your task is to collect coins scattered along the road. These shiny tokens are vital for her to reach her goal of purchasing a brand-new car!

Be vigilant as you guide Barbara; the streets are filled with obstacles that can hinder her progress. From busy pedestrians to unexpected roadblocks, it s crucial to avoid these dangers while collecting as many coins as possible. The more coins you gather, the closer Barbara gets to her dream car.

Once you have accrued enough coins, it's time for excitement! Barbara can head to the auto 4S store, where she has the opportunity to choose from a variety of stylish new cars. But that s not all; you can also customize and decorate her new ride to reflect her unique fashion sense and style. Choose colors, accessories, and designs that will make her car stand out on the road, ensuring it s as fashionable as she is.

How to play free Fashion New Car game online

To play the Fashion New Car game, use your arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer Barbara s car. Collect coins while deftly avoiding various obstacles on the road. Once you have enough coins, head to the dealership to select and customize a new car for Barbara! Enjoy the ride!
