Toilet Rush: Draw to Pee

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the whimsical world of Toilet Rush: Draw To Pee, chaos unfolds as a horde of monsters invades the toilets! Join the adventure to save Wednesday and her eclectic group of friends, including Steve from Minecraft, characters from Rainbow Friends, and Naruto. Together, you'll navigate through a maze of challenges as you draw your way to victory!

As you embark on this hilarious and frantic journey, your primary objective is to rescue Wednesday from the clutches of these mischievous monsters. Each level presents unique puzzles and obstacles that require quick thinking and creativity. Use your artistic skills to draw pathways or defenses, creating safe routes for Wednesday and the gang to escape. Teamwork is key, and each character brings their own special abilities to help overcome the monster threat!

The game's vibrant graphics and playful mechanics immerse you in a fun-filled environment, perfect for players of all ages. The frantic pace, combined with humorous visual gags, ensures that every moment is packed with laughter and excitement. Will you be able to outsmart the monsters and free Wednesday and her eclectic crew from the toilet traps? It s a race against time, so gather your friends and prepare for a hilarious adventure!

How to play free Toilet Rush: Draw To Pee game online

To start playing Toilet Rush: Draw To Pee, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to draw lines that will guide Wednesday and her friends away from the dangers. Ensure to avoid the lurking monsters while making sure they reach the exit safely. Collaborate with the special abilities of each character to solve puzzles and clear the path for a successful escape!
