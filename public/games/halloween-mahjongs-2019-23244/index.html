Halloween Mahjongs 2019

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spooky challenge with Halloween Mahjongs 2019, a thrilling game that blends the classic tile-matching fun of Mahjong with festive Halloween elements. Your objective is to match two identical Halloween-themed tiles to clear them from the board. To make a match, ensure that the tiles you choose are free. A tile is considered free if it is not covered by any other tile and has at least one open side either the left or the right side.

The game comprises 15 exciting levels, each offering its own unique challenges. The first nine levels are designed in the shapes of the letters H A L L O W E E N, adding a special twist to your game experience. As you progress to the last six levels, be prepared to tackle random tile configurations that will keep you on your toes. The fun doesn't stop after one play; you can dive into this Mahjong adventure as many times as you wish, each time facing different layout challenges that test your strategic thinking and tile-matching skills.

How to play free Halloween Mahjongs 2019 game online

To play Halloween Mahjongs 2019, start by selecting two matching tiles that are free from surrounding obstacles. Click on them to remove them from the board. Keep an eye out for the letter shapes in the first nine levels, and aim to clear all the tiles before time runs out. Enjoy the game and uncover the Halloween fun!
