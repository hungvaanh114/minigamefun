How to play free Balls Throw Duel 3D game online

To play Balls Throw Duel 3D, simply position your aim using the on-screen controls, then swipe or click to throw the ball. Aim for the center of each ring to maximize your points. Time your throws carefully to outscore the AI and secure your victory!
