Mission Coronavirus

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for a gripping challenge in Mission Coronavirus, where you take on the crucial role of an ambulance driver during a health crisis that originated in Wuhan, China. Your objective is to transport patients to the hospital swiftly, ensuring they receive the medical attention they desperately need before it s too late. The virus spreads rapidly, and every second counts. Your mission is not only to save lives but also to navigate through a bustling city without blocking the vital airways required for your patients.

In this exhilarating ambulance driving game, you ll encounter a uniquely designed city filled with dynamic traffic patterns. Get ready for a gaming experience like no other, featuring advanced graphics and realistic physics that immerse you in the role of a heroic responder. The fluid gaming mechanics ensure that you ll remain engaged as you strategize and perform the necessary tasks within the high-pressure environment of pandemic response. With over 50 distinct missions to tackle, you will face various challenges as you help stop the virus from spreading any further.

Are you ready to join the battle against the unseen enemy? Start your engines and take the wheel in Mission Coronavirus. Every mission counts, and the fate of many depends on your skills behind the steering wheel!

How to play free Mission Coronavirus game online

To play Mission Coronavirus, use the arrow keys or w, a, s, d to navigate your ambulance through the city. Your goal is to find patients, assess their conditions, and rush them to the hospital without obstructing their breathing. Complete the missions quickly to earn points and unlock new levels.


Instructions

You can direct the ambulance with the arrow keys or W A S D You can change the camera angles with the C key You can turn the lights on and off with the L button
