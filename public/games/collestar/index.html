Collestar

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Collestar, an engaging star collection game that puts your flying skills to the test! Dive into the skies and pilot your Collestar planes, aiming to collect as many stars as possible. With challenging obstacles like asteroids and missiles looming in your path, quick reflexes and strategic navigation are crucial to your success. The thrill of soaring high while trying to gather stars intensifies as you race against the clock, adding layers of excitement to your gameplay.

Your objective in Collestar is simple yet captivating: fly your planes as far apart as possible to maximize the number of stars you collect. The more stars you gather, the higher your score will soar! However, be cautious! Colliding with asteroids or missiles can drastically cut your adventure short and sap precious lives. Focus on dodging these dangers while keeping your eyes peeled for star clusters scattered throughout the game.

As the timer ticks down, every second counts. Strategize your movements, soar over obstacles, and explore vast areas to accumulate stars efficiently. Can you rise to the challenge and collect them all before time runs out? The game captures the thrill of flight while pushing you to constantly improve your speed and agility.

How to play free Collestar game online

To start playing Collestar, use your keyboard or mouse to navigate your planes across the screen. Collect stars by flying near them, and avoid touching asteroids and missiles. Keep an eye on the timer and your lives as you strive to collect as many stars as you can!
