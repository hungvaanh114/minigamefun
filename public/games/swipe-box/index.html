Swipe Box

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Swipe Box is a challenging and engaging puzzle game where players must use their logic and strategic thinking to navigate boxes through various obstacles to reach the goal. With sleek and futuristic visuals, players will find themselves immersed in a world of metallic challenges and brain-teasing levels.

Instructions

Objective The goal is to swipe the boxes to move them through the grid and get them to the target area Controls Swipe in any direction up down left or right to move the box in that direction Avoid Obstacles Be mindful of obstacles that block you
