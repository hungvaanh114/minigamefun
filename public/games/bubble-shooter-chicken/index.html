Bubble Shooter Chicken

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the colorful and delightful world of Bubble Shooter Chicken! This engaging bubble shooting game is perfect for players of all ages, combining fun and strategy in a whimsical format. In this game, your mission is to aim and release bubble fruits, matching three or more identical ones to explode them and earn points. As you progress through various levels, the excitement builds with each challenge you encounter.

Your main objective is to save all the adorable little chicken babies trapped within the clusters of bubbles. By strategically shooting and bursting the colorful orbs, you will free the chicks and unlock the next levels of this captivating adventure. With an array of challenging sweet levels waiting for you, the game keeps you entertained while testing your skills and reflexes.

As you dive deeper into Bubble Shooter Chicken, be on the lookout for boosters! These handy power-ups can be collected and utilized when you face particularly hard levels, providing you with an extra advantage to help you conquer even the trickiest puzzles. Each level brings new challenges and unique layouts, requiring you to devise clever strategies to progress and unlock additional stages.

Join the bubble-popping fun in Bubble Shooter Chicken and embark on an epic adventure filled with exciting challenges, charming graphics, and endless entertainment. This vibrant game is sure to keep you hooked as you aim, shoot, and save the day!

How to play free Bubble Shooter Chicken game online

To play Bubble Shooter Chicken, simply aim your shot using your mouse or touch controls. Release the bubbles to match three or more of the same color to burst them. Strategically use boosters when needed and focus on freeing all the trapped chicken babies to complete each level!
