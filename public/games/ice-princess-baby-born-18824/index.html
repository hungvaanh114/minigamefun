Ice Princess Baby Born

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a frosty world of love and excitement in "Ice Princess Baby Born." Join Elsa, the beloved ice queen, as she embarks on the magical journey of motherhood! Excitement fills the air when Elsa shares the wonderful news of her pregnancy with her loyal husband, Jack Frost. Although Jack is determined to support Elsa, his quirky personality adds a fun twist to their adventure!

As the couple prepares for their little one's arrival, you ll need to assist Jack in taking care of Elsa. From sweet moments at home to important visits to the hospital for check-ups, every moment is filled with joy and laughter. You'll help guide them as they navigate this enchanting time, ensuring that Elsa remains healthy and happy throughout her pregnancy.

After the arrival of their beautiful baby, your adventure continues! Embrace the wonderful challenge of parenting as you help Jack and Elsa take care of their newborn. You ll explore various activities for the baby, from feeding and diaper changes to playing and bonding. Get ready to create precious memories and enjoy the challenges of being new parents in the sparkling land of snow and magic!

How to play free Ice Princess Baby Born game online

To play the "Ice Princess Baby Born" game, use your mouse to navigate through the interactive scenes. Follow the prompts to help Jack Frost take care of Elsa during her pregnancy and accompany them to the hospital. After the baby arrives, engage in fun activities like feeding, changing diapers, and playing with the newborn!
