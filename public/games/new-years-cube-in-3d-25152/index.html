New Years Cube in 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This game is in the style of 2048, but now in 3D! Disassemble a figure consisting of blocks and cubes! To complete a level, you need to disassemble it all, but do not rush! In the game, you will have a limited number of moves, and if they run out, you will have to complete the level again! The game perfectly trains intelligence and intuition! This game is also great for children, pleasant music and measured gameplay will give your child a good mood! Blue, unobtrusive background will immerse you in the spring and summer atmosphere! You can also compete for the title of the best player on the leaderboard! Play and relax!

Instructions

1 At first it will be easy to disassemble the cubes but over time there will be more and more of them Once you have disassembled all the cubes the next level will begin
