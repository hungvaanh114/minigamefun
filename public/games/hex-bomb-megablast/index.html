Hex Bomb - Megablast

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an explosive gaming experience with HEX BOMB - MEGABLAST, a captivating fusion of a classic BUBBLE SHOOTER and an engaging BRICKS BREAKER. In this dynamic title, you'll face a cascade of hexagonal blocks tumbling down from the top of the screen. Your mission? To blast them away before they hit the bottom!

As you dive into the action, you'll notice the intuitive controls that allow for precision shooting. Simply tap the screen to slow down the ball shooter, giving you the time you need to adjust your aim perfectly. Once you release your finger, the shooter fires automatically, making it easy to unleash a barrage of colorful projectiles. Your goal is to match colors, create combos, and obliterate the blocks that threaten to overwhelm you.

Collecting bonuses and upgrades is key to mastering HEX BOMB - MEGABLAST. These enhancements not only improve your shooting capabilities but also boost your overall score, helping you progress through the increasingly challenging levels. Whether you're a casual gamer or a seasoned pro, there's something here for everyone.

How to play free Hex bomb - Megablast game online

To play HEX BOMB - MEGABLAST, simply use your finger to tap the screen, adjusting your shot before releasing to fire. Aim to hit the blocks as they fall and collect bonuses along the way for extra points. Keep your eye on the bottom of the screen it s a race against time!


Instructions

Hexagonals blocks are falling from the top of the screen and you need to shoot at them and blast them before they reach the bottom of the screen
