Stop Them All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stop Them All is a visually stunning casual game designed to captivate players of all ages. The gameplay is incredibly straightforward, making it an easy pick-up-and-play experience. Your primary task revolves around timing; simply tap the Activate button when you wish to trigger a trap. Timing is everything in this game, as your ability to focus and make precise taps ensures success.

As you progress, you'll face more characters attempting to reach the finish line. If you miss the opportunity to activate traps at the perfect moment, those characters will continue on their path, complicating your efforts to stop them. This adds a thrilling layer of challenge as the game evolves, requiring quick reflexes and sharp concentration.
