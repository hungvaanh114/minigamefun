Friends Battle TNT

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to engage in an electrifying competition with your friends? Dive into the exhilarating world of Friends Battle TNT, where speed and strategy determine the ultimate victor! In this fast-paced game, competitors race against each other to collect as many flying TNTs as possible while competing in teams of red and blue. The excitement builds as players scramble through the air, snatching these explosive treasures before their opponents can get to them.

This thrilling challenge requires quick reflexes and sharp decision-making. Players must aim to gather 20 TNTs before the timer runs out while outmaneuvering and outsmarting their teammates. The first team that reaches the target wins, and glory awaits those who demonstrate the most agility and cunning in this race. Will you strategize to help your team dominate, or will you go solo to secure your spot as the champion?

With Friends Battle TNT, every second counts, and each TNT collected brings you closer to victory. As you leap and dodge to capture the airborne explosives, remember that teamwork can lead your team to success. So gather your friends, choose your sides, and may the best team win!

How to play free Friends Battle TNT game online

To play Friends Battle TNT, simply choose your team red or blue. Use your arrow keys to navigate your character and jump to catch the flying TNTs. Aim for speed and be the first to collect 20 TNTs to secure your team's victory. Have fun!


Instructions

The red player moves using the WASD keys The blue player moves using the arrow keys Collect the TnT and bring it to the chest
