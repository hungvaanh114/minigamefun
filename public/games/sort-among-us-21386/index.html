Sort Among Us

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a fresh twist on the beloved classic that took the gaming world by storm: Sort Among Us! Love and nostalgia combine in a brand-new puzzle experience where your favorite Among Us characters are torn apart and mixed together. It s time to put on your thinking cap and dive into an exciting challenge that will test your problem-solving skills.

In Sort Among Us, the familiar crew members are fragmented into pieces, requiring you to utilize your keen eye and sharp intellect to reassemble them. As you interact with the colorful pieces, you ll unlock the unique ability to match the correct segments of different characters. The joy of piecing them back together not only reignites your attachment to these quirky characters but also provides a fun and exhilarating experience that stands apart from previous iterations of the game.

With intuitive controls and vibrant graphics, Sort Among Us offers an engaging puzzle-solving adventure that appeals to both new players and seasoned fans of the franchise. Enjoy the satisfaction of completing each character as you navigate through levels filled with visual splendor, delightful sounds, and plenty of surprises. Gather your friends and see who can complete the characters faster for added competition and excitement!

How to play free Sort Among Us game online

To play Sort Among Us, simply drag and drop the mixed character pieces to their respective positions on the board. Organize the segments strategically to form a complete character and complete the level. Keep an eye on the timer to challenge yourself further!
