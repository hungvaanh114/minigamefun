Fashion Maid Coffee

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a world of charm and elegance with "Fashion Maid Coffee." In this delightful game, you join Sofia as she opens the doors to her very own maid cafe! This weekend, she has called upon her friends Adella, Rebecca, and her sister Zoe to assist her in making the cafe a stunning success. The cafe, filled with delectable treats and adorable uniforms, provides the perfect backdrop for a stylish transformation.

Your task is to dress up Sofia and her friends in beautiful maid outfits that reflect their personalities. Choose from a variety of chic dresses, playful aprons, and charming accessories that will make them the talk of the town. Each character has their own unique style, allowing you to mix and match to create the perfect look for a sunny cafe day. Explore an extensive wardrobe filled with delightful selections to ensure that each maid looks spectacular while serving customers.

But it s not just about fashion! As they serve coffee and delicious pastries, you ll also get to experience the fun of running a bustling cafe. Help Sofia and her friends interact with their customers and create an inviting atmosphere. The more fabulous the outfits and the friendlier the service, the more customers they ll attract!

How to play free Fashion Maid Coffee game online

To play Fashion Maid Coffee, simply select your character and begin customizing their outfits with the various clothing options provided. Use the mouse to drag and drop clothing items onto your characters, ensuring each one looks perfect for their cafe duties. Once dressed, help them serve customers and complete fun tasks throughout the game!
