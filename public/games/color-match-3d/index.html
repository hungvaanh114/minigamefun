Color Match 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to dive into the vibrant world of color blending with Color Match 3D? This captivating puzzle game tests your knowledge of color relationships and challenges your creativity. It allows you to explore the fundamentals of color mixing by using primary colors to create shades that perfectly match various real-life items.

In Color Match 3D, players are presented with an array of colorful objects that require keen observation and an understanding of color theory for successful matching. The game offers a unique twist by combining entertainment with education, making it an excellent choice for all ages. As you navigate through different levels, you will discover a plethora of items that need your expertise. Your goal is simple: mix the provided colors to achieve the main color of the item. However, the challenge lies in ensuring that you achieve over 50% accuracy to advance to the next level.

This game not only enhances your color-matching skills but also encourages strategic thinking and problem-solving. Each level progressively increases in difficulty, ensuring that you remain engaged and entertained while sharpening your artistic acumen. The captivating 3D graphics add an immersive experience, drawing players into a colorful realm where knowledge meets fun.

How to play free Color Match 3D game online

To play Color Match 3D, start by selecting a level. Once the level loads, you'll be presented with a primary color palette and a target item. Use the primary colors to mix and create the desired shade that matches the item. Aim for above 50% accuracy to successfully complete each level and unlock new challenges!
