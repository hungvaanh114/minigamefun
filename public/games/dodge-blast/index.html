Dodge Blast

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dodge Blast is a thrilling and entertaining game from Go Panda Games that lets players step into the shoes of a budding surgeon. The game begins with Leah, a skateboarding enthusiast, who suffers a serious incident after losing focus and colliding with a car. As a result of this unfortunate accident, Leah ends up with a badly broken arm and foot, leaving her in need of urgent medical attention.

In this immersive bone surgery simulator, players will have the chance to take on the role of a skilled doctor tasked with healing Leah's injuries. You will navigate through various surgical procedures that involve diagnosing her conditions, performing operations, and ensuring Leah's recovery is smooth and efficient. Each level is filled with engaging challenges that require precision and quick thinking, all while keeping the gameplay light-hearted and humorous.

As you advance through the levels, you'll unlock new tools and techniques, allowing you to address Leah's needs with increasing efficiency. The charming graphics and entertaining sound effects enhance the overall experience, making the game enjoyable for players of all ages. Will you be able to help Leah get back to skateboarding in no time?

How to play free Dodge Blast game online

To play Dodge Blast, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the surgical tools. Follow on-screen instructions to perform each procedure step-by-step. Make sure to act quickly and accurately to efficiently treat Leah's injuries and succeed in each level.
