Indulge your sweet tooth with the delightful Fruit Sort Puzzle - Color Puzzle game! Have you ever wondered what flavor of candy cane tickles your taste buds? Now, you can create your very own candy cane in this exciting puzzle adventure. In this colorful world, your mission is to move and combine fruits creatively. When you group three or more fruits together, they will merge and evolve into delightful upgrades. What fun awaits as you work towards your ultimate goal: crafting the perfect hawthorn candy cane.

However, be mindful of the challenge that lies ahead. You need to keep an eye on your fruit limits, as exceeding the maximum capacity on the fork will result in failure. This adds an extra layer of excitement, pushing your problem-solving skills to the limit. So gather your determination and get ready to embark on this flavorful journey that guarantees joy and laughter. Can you rise to the challenge and skillfully make hawthorn candy canes? Trust in your abilities and let the fun commence!

How to play free Fruit Sort Puzzle - Color Puzzle game online

To play Fruit Sort Puzzle, simply drag and drop the fruits on the screen. Match three or more of the same type to combine them into a higher-level fruit. Be cautious of the fork s limits, and strategize to avoid overloading it. Work efficiently to reach your goal of creating hawthorn candy canes!
