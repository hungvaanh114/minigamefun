Fit And Squezze is an engaging and challenging puzzle game designed to test your spatial awareness and problem-solving skills. In this captivating game, players are tasked with filling various shaped bottles with differently sized balls. Each level presents a unique set of challenges where the objective is to fit all the balls within the designated bottle without exceeding the dotted line that signifies the maximum fill level. Success in the game requires careful thought and strategic planning, as players must consider the size and placement of each ball to ensure they utilize all available space effectively.

The game's intuitive design allows players to easily drag and drop the balls into their respective bottles, making it accessible for all age groups. As you progress through the levels, the complexity increases, with shapes becoming more intricate and the bottle sizes varying.
