Street Fighter IV Champion Edition brings a thrilling and authentic street fighting experience to your Android device. This classic free arcade game captures the essence of traditional fighting games while fully embracing modern gameplay elements. With stunning 3D graphics and explosive action sequences, it immerses players into a vibrant world of martial arts where only the strongest can emerge victorious.

In this game, you are challenged to defeat top kungfu fighters from around the globe, each equipped with unique moves and fighting styles. Street Fighter IV Champion Edition is not just about brute strength; it requires strategy, timing, and skill to overpower your opponents. Engage in intense one-on-one battles, utilizing character-specific abilities and powerful combos to claim supremacy in the streets.

Additionally, with its innovative terra tag tournament feature, you can organize competitive fights that pit you against other skilled fighters. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, Street Fighter IV Champion Edition delivers an exhilarating arcade experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat. Fear is left at the door as you dive straight into the action, proving your mettle as you strive to become the ultimate champion.

To play Street Fighter IV Champion Edition, select your fighter and choose your game mode. Use virtual buttons on your screen for attacking, blocking, and performing special moves.
