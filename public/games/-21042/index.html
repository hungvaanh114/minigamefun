Planet Defense?Planet Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The universe is a vast and uncharted territory, but now it faces a grave threat. Welcome to Planet Defense, where your home planet is under siege by a colossal asteroid. Powerless against the enormity of this celestial intruder, it falls upon you to safeguard your world. Your mission is to deploy advanced weaponry and strategize your defenses to obliterate the asteroid before it collides with your planet, wreaking havoc on everything you hold dear.

In Planet Defense, your quick thinking and tactical prowess will be your greatest assets. As the asteroid hurtles towards your planet, gather resources, upgrade your defenses, and craft powerful arms to repel the threat. Use your skills to coordinate your responses and harness the full potential of your equipment. Each moment counts in this thrilling race against time, so be prepared to make split-second decisions that can determine the fate of your planet.

The graphics are stunning, immersing you in vivid landscapes and an animated atmosphere that highlights the impending danger. As you progress through levels, the challenge escalates, introducing faster and larger asteroids that will test your reflexes and strategic abilities. Join forces with allies or engage in competitive multi-player modes to see who can best defend against the cosmic assault. Can you rise to the occasion and emerge as the ultimate protector of your planet?

How to play free Planet Defense game online

To begin playing Planet Defense, simply use your mouse or touchscreen controls to aim and shoot at the approaching asteroid. Manage your resources carefully to upgrade your defenses and create powerful weapons. Stay alert and adapt your strategy as the asteroid approaches, ensuring you defend your planet before it's too late!
