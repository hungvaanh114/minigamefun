How to play free Rope Rescue Puzzle game online

To play the Rope Rescue Puzzle game, simply drag the rope with one finger to form the perfect path for your characters to escape from danger. Make sure to avoid fire and other obstacles while rescuing them. Keep an eye out for challenges in higher levels that will test your skills!
