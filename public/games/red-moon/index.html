Red Moon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an epic journey in Red Moon, a thrilling action-adventure game where the moon's crimson glow heralds a time of monumental power for the Red Samurais. Once every ten years, the moon shines red, gifting us with incredible strength and determination. Do you have what it takes to feel this power surge?

Join Eivana, a fierce warrior, on her quest to overthrow a tyrannical king and liberate the kingdom from oppression. Traverse through 20 action-packed levels, each more challenging than the last, filled with formidable enemies and exhilarating obstacles. You'll also face 2 intense boss battles, where strategy and skill will be crucial to your success.

As you progress, unlock 13 exclusive medals by achieving various feats, rewarding your prowess and dedication. Immerse yourself further in the world of Red Moon by collecting diverse concept art and music tracks, enhancing your gaming experience. Will you rise as a legendary Red Samurai and lead the charge against the king? Step into the adventure and unleash your fury!

How to play free Red Moon game online

To play Red Moon, navigate through the game using the arrow keys for movement and the spacebar to attack. Complete each level by defeating enemies and collecting power-ups. Keep an eye out for hidden treasures and complete the objectives to unlock new areas and boss battles. Good luck!
