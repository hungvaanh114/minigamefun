Shoot Your Nightmare: Space Isolation

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chilling world of Shoot Your Nightmare: Space Isolation, where you awaken on the desolate planet ML-397 in the year 2299. Confusion envelops you as you find yourself surrounded by an eerie silence, with no memory of who you are or how you arrived. The feeling of loneliness is profound, but this solitude is soon disrupted by an ominous truth you are not as alone as you thought. Prepare to face unspeakable horrors and uncover the secrets hidden within this abandoned space base.

This game delivers the ultimate sci-fi horror FPS experience. With stunning futuristic 3D graphics that immerse you in a realistic environment, every corner of ML-397 is filled with suspense and terror. As one of the few remaining beings on this forsaken planet, you will need to depend solely on yourself to prevent an impending apocalypse. Engage in relentless sci-fi FPS alien shooter action, armed with an impressive arsenal of sci-fi weapons designed to keep you one step ahead of terrifying enemies.

With seamless gameplay that features easy-to-learn controls, Shoot Your Nightmare: Space Isolation provides an exhilarating yet dark narrative that will keep players on the edge of their seats. Venture deep into the heart of the mystery that surrounds this forsaken space base, where every discovery could lead to either salvation or doom. Discover the truth about the horror that lurks in the shadows and fight for your survival!

How to play free Shoot Your Nightmare: Space Isolation game online

To play, begin by using the W, A, S, D keys for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot. Interact with various objects and uncover clues to piece together the mysteries of the abandoned base. Keep an eye on your ammunition and health, and make strategic decisions to survive against lurking dangers. Enjoy the thrilling experience!


Instructions

W A S D to walk around Mouse to Look around Left Mouse Button to Fire Right Mouse Button to Aim Mouse Wheel To Change Weapons G for Grenades R to Reload F to Pickup Items Left Shift to Run Left CTRL to Crouch X to Prone Space to Jump
