Fashion Nail Design Day

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fashion Nail Design Day, where creativity meets fun in the chic world of nail art! Today, Sofia has a day off and is excited to spend quality time with her friend Adella. With a thirst for style and a desire to express themselves through dazzling nail designs, the duo heads to the local nail salon. As the manager of this vibrant establishment, your job is to cater to Sofia and Adella's nail art dreams!

Upon entering the salon, you'll be greeted by a plethora of colors, designs, and accessories. Both girls are eager to try out trendy patterns and express their personalities with unique nail art. You have access to a wide range of nail polish shades, from classic reds to glittery golds, ensuring that you can create stunning looks for both Sofia and Adella. Don t forget decadent embellishments like rhinestones, stickers, and foils that can add that extra touch of glam to their manicures!

As the manager, you encourage the girls to explore their creativity. Guide them through selecting nail shapes, colors, and designs, all while making sure they re enjoying the experience. The salon's atmosphere buzzes with excitement as designs come to life under your expert supervision, and the laughter and chatter of the two friends fill the air. By the end of their visit, Sofia and Adella will walk out with fabulous nails, ready to show off their stylish new looks to the world!

How to play free Fashion Nail Design Day game online

To play the Fashion Nail Design Day game, simply select your favorite nail shape and color for each girl. Use the tools available to apply polish, decorate with cute stickers, and add sparkling gems. Follow prompts to create stunning designs, ensuring Sofia and Adella leave the salon looking fabulous!
