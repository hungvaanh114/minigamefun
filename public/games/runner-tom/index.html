Runner Tom

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Meet Tom, an unsuspecting hero who finds himself in a race against time as he strolls down the street. Out of nowhere, a menacing bad dog comes charging towards him, leaving no room for hesitation. This is where you come in! Your mission is to guide Tom through this thrilling adventure, ensuring he manages to escape the clutches of the relentless canine.

As Tom makes his way down the road, he will encounter numerous obstacles that impede his journey. It's up to you to help him navigate through these challenges! Whether it's hopping over hurdles or ducking under pesky birds and spiders that pop up unexpectedly, quick reflexes are key. Swipe up on the screen to make Tom jump over hurdles, and swipe down to duck and bend under any looming threats. Timing and precision are essential to keep Tom ahead of the dog that is hot on his heels!

This adventure is as much about speed as it is about strategy. The more obstacles you overcome, the higher your score will be. Keep your eyes peeled for extra points, and don t forget to grab power-ups that can give Tom a much-needed boost. Think you can help Tom stay one step ahead of that dog? Test your skills and see how far you can take him!

How to play free Runner Tom game online

To play Runner Tom, simply swipe up on the screen to leap over obstacles and swipe down to crouch under hazards. Stay alert and react quickly to avoid the dog chasing you. Collect points and power-ups along the way to enhance your gameplay experience!
