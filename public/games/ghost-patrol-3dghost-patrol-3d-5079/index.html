Welcome to the thrilling world of Ghost Patrol 3D, where you step into the shoes of a playful and cunning ghost. Your mission is simple yet exhilarating: sneak up on unsuspecting visitors and give them a good scare! However, your ghostly antics come with a twist; your powers diminish if anyone catches sight of you. Stay hidden from their view, as lingerings in their vision cone could make you vanish without a trace.

Utilize your tactical skills to navigate the eerie halls of the museum. Your environment is filled with obstacles, making your ghostly pursuit both challenging and entertaining. As you weave through the exhibits, it's essential to plan your moves carefully to ensure you can frighten the visitors and guards effectively while avoiding detection. The adrenaline rush of the stealthy gameplay keeps you on your toes!

One of the most exciting features of the game is the ability to "hide in armor." This unique disguise allows you to blend into your surroundings, providing an excellent opportunity to approach your target without arousing suspicion. Timing and strategy are crucial as you pick the right moments to strike, leading to memorable and spine-tingling scare experiences!

How to play free Ghost Patrol 3D game online

To play Ghost Patrol 3D, navigate your ghost through the museum using the arrow keys or on-screen controls. Move stealthily and keep your distance from the visitors while looking for opportunities to scare them. Use the "hide in armor" feature strategically to avoid detection. Happy haunting!
