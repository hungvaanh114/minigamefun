Survivor in Rainbow Monster

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Survivor In Rainbow Monster, a thrilling horror adventure where you find yourself trapped in the terrifying Spooky Park amusement park. Kidnapped during an innocent school trip, you play as an unnamed child striving for survival in an eerie environment devoid of life apart from the looming presence of the Rainbow Monster the guardian and your guide in this heart-pounding journey.

Your mission is to navigate through five terrifying nights. Each night presents its own unique set of challenges and tasks that you must master while skillfully avoiding the mischievous Rainbow Monster. With each passing night, the stakes rise, and the challenges become increasingly difficult. Time is of the essence, and every move you make could either lead you closer to freedom or directly into the clutches of danger.

As you progress through this chilling game, you'll need to use your wits, quick reflexes, and strategic thinking to outsmart the Rainbow Monster. Completing tasks will not only aid in your escape but also unveil the secrets hidden within Spooky Park. Can you endure the fright and unravel the mystery that awaits you? The only way to find out is to experience the adventure yourself!

How to play free Survivor In Rainbow Monster game online

To play Survivor In Rainbow Monster, start by navigating through the park using on-screen controls. Complete the nightly tasks by solving puzzles, collecting items, or interacting with objects. Always keep an eye out for the Rainbow Monster; stealth and quick decision-making will be key to your survival!


Instructions

Use the keyboard mouse or touch to move the character and complete the tasks
