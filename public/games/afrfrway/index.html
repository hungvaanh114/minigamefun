Afrfrway

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Afrfrway is an exhilarating action arcade game that takes car-hopping fun to the next level. As the third installment in this thrilling series, players have the opportunity to unleash their inner speedster while navigating the crazed highways. Experience the rush of driving at high speeds, dodging oncoming traffic, and colliding with other vehicles to amass points. The key to victory lies in your ability to jump from car to car, performing incredible stunts along the way. Chain these stunts to maximize your score and showcase your exceptional skills!

As you zoom through the chaotic concrete jungle, be mindful of the damage you inflict on the cars you inhabit. Over time, excessive damage can lead to losing a life, effectively shortening your journey in this intense game. Thus, frequent car-switching is essential, not only for survival but also for strategically outmaneuvering obstacles and increasing your overall score. With every thrilling jump and heart-stopping twist, you ll learn to master the art of chaos on the roads.

So, buckle up and get ready to dive into an explosive experience. Test your limits and see just how far you can go in Afrfrway, where the thrill of high-speed action awaits!

How to play free Afrfrway game online

To play Afrfrway, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car and accelerate. Jump between cars by pressing the designated key at the right moment, and perform stunts to earn extra points. Keep an eye on the damage meter and switch cars frequently to maintain your life count!


Instructions

Steer - A D or Left Right arrow keys Nitro - W or Up arrow key Get on the roof - S or Down arrow key Hop on another car - A D or Left Right arrow keys While on the roof Get in the new car - S or Down arrow key After jumping on another car
