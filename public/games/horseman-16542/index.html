Horseman

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the boots of a daring cowboy in Horseman, where the Wild West meets a post-apocalyptic world filled with zombies. As the undead rise from their graves and overrun the landscape, it s your mission to take charge and eliminate these menacing foes. Journey through a range of terrains, from dusty plains to eerie graveyards, while riding one of your five unique horses, each with its own strengths and abilities.

Your arsenal consists of three formidable weapons: a bow for precision shots, a sword for close encounters, and a gun for power-packed firepower. The thrill comes from mastering each of these weapons while navigating through hordes of zombies with outstretched hands, intent on dragging you into their undead clutches. Choose your battleground wisely each location presents its own challenges and tactical opportunities as you defend against the relentless onslaught. Let your courage shine and become a true hero in this thrilling cowboy adventure.

With stunning graphics and engaging gameplay, Horseman offers an immersive experience that combines the grit of cowboy life with the thrill of survival horror. So saddle up, draw your weapon, and show those zombies who rules the Wild West!

How to play free Horseman game online

To play the Horseman game online, simply select your favorite horse and weapon from the main menu. Use the arrow keys to navigate and your mouse clicks to aim and shoot at the approaching zombies. Keep moving to avoid their grasp and use the environment to your advantage as you fight for survival!
