Nuts and Bolts: Screw Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Nuts and Bolts: Screw Puzzle, a unique puzzle game designed to challenge your problem-solving skills! In this engaging experience, your objective is to eliminate all bolts from the board by skillfully maneuvering them to designated bolt holes. The thrill lies in strategizing your moves to create a clear path for each bolt, making every level an intriguing brain teaser.

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter various levels of difficulty that will keep you on your toes. Each board presents a new challenge, requiring you to think critically and plan your moves effectively. Touch the selected bolt and drag it to an available bolt hole to successfully remove it from the game screen. Your ultimate goal is to clear all the boards, leading to an exhilarating sense of accomplishment once you have completed each level.

However, beware of getting stuck! If you find yourself in a predicament where you can't make any more moves, it becomes your responsibility to drop the board strategically, setting the stage for new possibilities. The satisfaction of clearing the board is immensely rewarding and will keep you returning for more!

How to play free Nuts and Bolts: Screw Puzzle game online

To play Nuts and Bolts: Screw Puzzle, simply touch a bolt to select it, then drag it to an empty bolt hole to remove it from the board. Continue this process until all bolts have been eliminated. Be careful to avoid getting stuck plan your moves wisely to clear each level effectively!
