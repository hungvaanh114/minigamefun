Breakout Level Pack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Breakout Level Pack, where classic arcade gaming meets innovative challenges! In this game, players must navigate through a series of intricate levels filled with a variety of bricks that require different strategies to conquer. Experience the thrill of breaking through 2x Bricks, which demand two hits to clear, and tackle the unique Shoot Bricks, which can only be destroyed by shooting them. However, be cautious if your ball makes contact with a Shoot Brick, it will be lost!

As you progress, look out for the invaluable Extra Ball Bricks that give you an additional chance to rack up points. The objective is simple but captivating: eliminate all bricks on the screen to successfully complete each level. A timer adds a layer of urgency, allowing only five minutes to accomplish your goal, so stay sharp and quick!

Mastering the paddle control is essential, and you can maneuver it effortlessly using either a mouse or your fingertip. With each level introducing new layouts and obstacles, you ll need to adapt your strategy to enjoy the full break-out excitement!

How to play free Breakout Level Pack game online

To play Breakout Level Pack, use your mouse or finger to move the paddle left or right. Aim to bounce the ball into the bricks, destroying them strategically. Be aware of special bricks like 2x Bricks and Shoot Bricks that require different tactics. Complete each level within five minutes for maximum fun!
