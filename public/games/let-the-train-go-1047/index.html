Let the Train Go

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Let The Train Go is an engaging and mind-bending puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking skills. Your mission is clear: clear the tracks for the metro by maneuvering various vehicles obstructing its path. Each vehicle from cars to trucks to ambulances has a specific orientation that cannot be altered, adding an extra layer of complexity to the gameplay.

The vibrant visuals enhance the gaming experience, as you navigate through beautifully designed scenes filled with intricate vehicles. The diverse assortment of cars not only makes the game visually appealing but also enriches the puzzles with varied challenges. You'll find that each level presents a new obstacle set in a colorful environment, creating a relaxing yet stimulating atmosphere.

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter increasingly difficult configurations that will test your problem-solving ability. The satisfaction of seeing the metro glide effortlessly along the tracks after solving a complex puzzle is truly rewarding. With each successfully unraveled labyrinth of vehicles, you'll be eager to tackle the next challenge. Get ready to experience a delightful blend of fun, logic, and creativity as you embark on this exhilarating journey!

How to play free Let The Train Go game online

To play Let The Train Go, simply drag the vehicles blocking the metro's path. Align each vehicle in such a way that the metro can move forward without any obstructions. Remember, you can only slide the vehicles in the direction they are facing. Keep solving puzzles to progress through the game!
