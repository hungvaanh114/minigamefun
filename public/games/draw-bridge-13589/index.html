Draw Bridge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Draw Bridge," players are tasked with using their creativity and skill to navigate a car through various challenges. The objective is to drag your finger to draw a path that guides the vehicle to its destination. However, players must be cautious, as you can only draw the line once, and breaking the car could lead to failure. This engaging puzzle mechanic combines elements of strategy and timing, making each level both exciting and thought-provoking.

Here, you can freely explore an expansive city, away from traffic and adversarial competitors. The detailed open-world environment is teeming with possibilities, and realistic car damage ensures every crash feels authentic!

How to play free Draw Bridge game online

To play "Draw Bridge," simply start by using your mouse or finger to sketch a continuous line that will form a path for the car. Your goal is to connect the car to the finish line without breaking it. Timing is key, so plan your route carefully to ensure a successful journey!
