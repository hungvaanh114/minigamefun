Scary Granny

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Scary Granny is a thrilling and spine-chilling gaming experience that immerses you in a heart-pounding atmosphere. In this game, players must navigate through a dimly-lit house haunted by a terrifying old woman known as Granny. Your main objective is to escape while avoiding her grasp. You can choose to run, hide, or, if necessary, use a knife to fend off the menacing Granny. This intense gameplay forces you to stay alert and strategize your moves carefully, as you explore the creepy environment in search of a way out.

Each level presents new challenges and obstacles that test your skills and wits. As you progress through the game, you'll encounter various puzzles and hidden secrets that can help you unravel the mystery of the old woman's lair. The adrenaline rush of fleeing from Granny, coupled with the thrill of unlocking each level, keeps the excitement alive as you race against time. Will you manage to find the hidden door and escape, or will you become just another victim of the Scary Granny?

Players can also enjoy the immersive sound design and graphics that enhance the eerie vibes of the game, making each escape attempt feel even more intense. Challenge yourself to unravel the game s secrets and emerge victorious, while enjoying the chilling journey that Scary Granny offers!

How to play free Scary Granny game online

To play Scary Granny, use the WASD keys to move around and navigate your surroundings. Look for items that can help you survive, such as the knife, and listen carefully for Granny's sounds to avoid being caught. Your goal is to find the door to escape while uncovering all hidden secrets.
