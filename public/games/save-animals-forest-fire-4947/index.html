In "Save Animals: Forest Fire," embark on an exhilarating journey to protect wildlife from devastating forest fires. As the flames rage and threaten to engulf their homes, it's up to you to guide various animals like cats, hares, and turtles to safety. Navigate through perilous landscapes, including fragile bridges that span dangerous rivers, treacherous swamps, and even shark-infested oceans. Each escape route presents unique challenges that demand your strategic thinking.

Travel across diverse regions, from the expansive savannahs of Africa to the icy terrains of the North Pole, and ensure that every animal makes it to safety. The game encourages you to rescue a variety of creatures, each contributing to your growing farm. Once a rescue mission is completed, you have the option to settle these adorable animals on your own farm, creating a vibrant world filled with rescued wildlife. As you progress through increasingly difficult levels, you'll encounter even more pets to save, keeping the excitement alive.

The more animals you rescue, the more your farm thrives, allowing players to experience a sense of accomplishment while raising awareness about the importance of saving wildlife. Dive into "Save Animals: Forest Fire," where adventure, strategy, and compassion come together in a heartwarming gaming experience.

How to play free Save Animals: Forest Fire game online

To play "Save Animals: Forest Fire," use your mouse or touch controls to guide the animals across bridges and obstacles. Click on the animals to help them jump or navigate through hazards. Complete each level by rescuing all the animals and unlocking new regions to explore!
