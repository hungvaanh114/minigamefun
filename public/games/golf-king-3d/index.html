Golf King 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of realistic multiplayer golf with Golf King 3D! From the developers of Mini Golf King, this exciting new golf game offers you the chance to participate in real-time 1v1 matches on beautifully designed courses filled with stunning graphics. With intuitive shot control mechanics, you ll feel just like you re on the green, swinging away to claim victory.

Challenge players from all corners of the globe in online battles. As you earn victories, you ll collect trophies that allow you to unlock higher tours and discover a variety of golf clubs to enhance your performance. Engage with daily and weekly leaderboard challenges to compete for amazing prizes and elevate your game. Customize your character with limitless combinations of outfits and accessories, including hats, glasses, tops, pants, and shoes, ensuring you step onto the virtual course in style.

Unleash your competitive spirit as you play against friends and share the excitement of the game. Embrace your victories with expressive emotes, and gather powerful clubhouses and caddies to boost your gameplay experience. Begin your journey to greatness in your quest to be crowned the Golf King!

KEY FEATURES:

  • PvP golf duels
  • Simple swipe and release controls
  • A variety of realistic 3D golf courses
  • Trophy collection for new stage unlocks
  • Powerful golf club upgrades
  • Extensive character and clubhouse customization options
  • Compete in weekly leagues & Coin Rush for special rewards
  • Family and friend multiplayer options

For updates and news, follow Golf King on Facebook: Golf King Facebook Page. Note that an internet connection is required to play.

How to play free Golf King 3D game online

To play Golf King 3D, simply select a match type, choose your character, and swing by swiping and releasing your finger on the screen to hit the ball. Aim carefully to navigate the stunning courses and outplay your opponent. Good luck, and have fun!
