3d Super Tank

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to 3D Super Tank, an exhilarating casual action game where your primary mission is to protect your base from relentless enemy forces. Engage in thrilling battles as you navigate through a variety of landscapes and confront waves of enemy tanks bent on destruction. Your strategic skills will be put to the test as you aim to obliterate all adversarial tanks and demolish their fortified structures.

With over 30 immersive levels, 3D Super Tank provides players with endless excitement and challenges. Each level presents unique obstacles and enemies that grow increasingly difficult to defeat. Utilize your tank's firepower, maneuverability, and tactical prowess to outsmart your opponents and claim victory. Each battle demands precision and quick thinking, ensuring that you always stay on your toes as you devise effective strategies to overcome your foes.

The graphics and sound effects in 3D Super Tank enhance the gaming experience, immersing you in a world of destruction and tactical warfare. Progress through the levels, unlock powerful upgrades, and customize your tanks to create a force that's uniquely yours. Can you stand against the fierce competition and emerge as the ultimate tank commander? Jump into the action today and show your skills!

How to play free 3D Super Tank game online

To dive into the action of 3D Super Tank, use the arrow keys or WASD to control your tank's movement. Aim your cannon with the mouse and click to fire at enemy tanks and structures. Collect power-ups and upgrades throughout the game to enhance your tank's abilities and increase your chances of victory!
