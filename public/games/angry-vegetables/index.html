Angry Vegetables

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to dive into the colorful and chaotic world of Angry Vegetables, a thrilling puzzle adventure that combines strategy and speed! In this engaging game, your mission is to connect and eliminate vegetables by creating chains of at least three similar ones. The more you match, the more powerful your moves will become. Start by selecting a vegetable and then guide your pointer towards adjacent vegetables of the same type. Each successful chain removes those veggies from the board, giving you points and clearing the way for new ones.

As you play, aim for larger combinations! If you create a chain of seven vegetables, you will be rewarded with a special 2x power vegetable, which can help you achieve an even higher score. Striking a chain of ten vegetables grants you an extra bonus minute to enhance your gameplay, offering you additional time to strategize and maximize your score. The game is timed, so every second counts as you race against the clock to clear the board!

With its vibrant graphics, intuitive controls, and addictive mechanics, Angry Vegetables offers endless fun for players of all ages. Gather your friends or challenge yourself and see how many points you can rack up before time runs out. Are you ready to become the ultimate vegetable vaquero?

How to play free Angry Vegetables game online

To play Angry Vegetables, begin by pressing on a vegetable to select it. Next, move your cursor to adjacent vegetables of the same type to form a chain of three or more. Aim for longer chains for power-ups and bonus time. Keep an eye on the clock and score high!
