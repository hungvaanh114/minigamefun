How to play free Line & Dots game online

To play Line & Dots, begin by selecting a level. Your objective is to connect all the dots using one continuous line. Simply click and drag from one dot to adjacent dots to create segments, ensuring that lines don t overlap. Keep experimenting until all dots are connected with a single line!
