Landscape Paradise Hidden

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Landscape Paradise Hidden, an exciting and engaging free online skill game that challenges your observational abilities! Embark on a captivating journey through stunning landscapes where your task is to uncover hidden stars within enchanting images. Each carefully designed level presents you with a unique visual puzzle where you need to locate 10 concealed numbers.

With a total of 6 levels to explore, your adventure will take you deeper into this mesmerizing world, where every click counts. However, be swift, as each level is time-locked, adding an exhilarating layer of urgency to your gameplay. This thrilling race against the clock requires not just speed, but also sharp eyes to spot every hidden object before the time elapses.

Beware of clicking haphazardly; if you touch the wrong area multiple times, you could incur a penalty of 5 seconds deducted from your remaining time. This adds an intriguing twist, encouraging players to hone their focus and precision while enjoying the vibrant visuals of Landscape Paradise Hidden. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a newcomer to hidden object games, this adventure promises fun and excitement!

How to play free Landscape Paradise Hidden game online

To play Landscape Paradise Hidden, simply click on the play button to start the game. Once in a level, examine the scene closely and click on the hidden stars as you find them. Keep an eye on your timer, and be cautious with your clicks to avoid reducing your time. Happy searching!
