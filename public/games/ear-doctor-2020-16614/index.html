Instructions:

Step into the shoes of a skilled physician in the exciting world of Ear Doctor 2020. Your patients are lined up in the hallway, eagerly awaiting your expertise. With a variety of challenges ahead, it's time to prepare your gloves and dive into a day filled with medical mysteries and intriguing cases. From diagnosing ear infections to performing intricate surgeries, every patient presents a unique puzzle for you to solve. As you progress through the game, you will encounter multiple scenarios that require swift decision-making and precise execution. The clock is ticking, and your patients won t wait!

Get ready to utilize an array of medical tools at your disposal. You'll extract earwax, remove foreign objects from ears, and treat more serious complications. Each successful treatment will boost your reputation and pave the way for more advanced challenges. The colorful graphics and engaging sound effects bring the clinic to life, making your tasks all the more immersive. Challenge yourself to complete each level and become the town s top ear doctor!

As you navigate through the game, pay close attention to the symptoms your patients exhibit, as this will guide your diagnosis. Are you ready to handle the pressure? Your skills will be put to the test as you juggle multiple patients and different conditions. The better you perform, the more satisfying your journey as a doctor will be!

How to play free Ear Doctor 2020 game online

To play the Ear Doctor 2020 game, simply click on the patient's icon to start your treatment. Use the mouse to select tools and follow in-game instructions to diagnose and treat various ear conditions. Be attentive and act quickly to ensure your patients receive the best care possible!
