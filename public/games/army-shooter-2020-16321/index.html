Army Shooter 2020

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Army Shooter 2020, an action-packed military shooter that places you in the heart of an intense global conflict. As a brave soldier, your mission is clear: eliminate the enemy bases that harbor dangerous weaponry and thwart the rebel threat aimed at annihilating humanity. With several locations scattered around the globe, each base presents unique challenges and fierce adversaries determined to stop you at all costs.

In Army Shooter 2020, players will experience the adrenaline rush of high-stakes missions, armed with an array of weapons and tactical gear. Your journey will take you through diverse terrains and enemy strongholds, where you'll need to utilize your skills and strategy to outsmart the rebels. Each level provides a chance to upgrade your arsenal, enhance your combat abilities, and push back against the imminent danger.

Prepare for thrilling encounters as you engage with enemy forces in breathtaking scenarios. Utilize cover, take precise aim, and make critical decisions on the battlefield. The fate of humanity rests on your shoulders, and every shot counts. Will you be able to breach the defenses of these enemy outposts and emerge victorious? Load your weapon, soldier, and venture into the fray in Army Shooter 2020.

How to play free Army Shooter 2020 game online

To play Army Shooter 2020, simply control your character using the on-screen joystick for movement while using buttons to aim and shoot. Pay attention to your surroundings, gather ammo and health packs, and complete your missions by eliminating all enemy units before time runs out. Enjoy the explosive action!


Instructions

WASD to move Right mouse click to aim Left mouse click to shoot Space to jump Left shift to run Left ctrl to crouch F to pick ammo
