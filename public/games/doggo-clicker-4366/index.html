Doggo Clicker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Doggo Clicker is a delightful clicker game that provides players with an adorable canine companion to interact with. Each time you click on your lovable pup, you ll notice an uplifting change in his mood. Your goal is to keep him happy while exploring various fun features of the game. With over 50 unique upgrades available, you can enhance your dog's abilities and appearance, ranging from special treats to amusing items that boost his joy and style.

The game offers 21 charming accessories that allow you to customize your furry friend to your liking. Make him look even cooler as you assemble the perfect combination of looks that express his personality. As you progress in the game, you can level up and spend your earned stat points, making it easier for you to advance and unlock new features.

In addition to the clicking fun, Doggo Clicker introduces two layers of prestige that add extra depth to your gameplay. This means that, upon reaching certain milestones, you can reset portions of your progress to achieve greater boosts and unlock additional features. Furthermore, players can build a cozy house for their dog, adding an exciting element to the game as you create a perfect home for your best friend. Dive into Doggo Clicker and enjoy the charming fun it brings along with the excitement of building and upgrading your canine companion!

How to play free Doggo Clicker game online

To play Doggo Clicker, simply click on your dog to increase his happiness and earn in-game currency. Use this currency to purchase upgrades and accessories. Distribute your stat points wisely to maximize your progress. Explore the prestige system and build a comfortable home for your furry friend to enhance your gameplay experience!
