Color Match 3D Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the vibrant world of Color Match 3D, an engaging online puzzle game designed for players of all ages. Dive into a colorful adventure where painting becomes a delightful challenge. In this game, you have the freedom to unleash your creativity by mixing colors from a palette to match a target item.

Your goal is to paint a 3D model that accurately reflects the specified color, making keen observation and color mixing essential skills. Once you ve completed your masterpiece, you can compare your creation to the target color to see how well you performed. The better your colors match, the higher your grade will be.

As an added twist, you can showcase your artistic endeavors in your orchard or put them up for auction based on their quality. The excitement of competition and creativity mingles in this game, making it an excellent choice for anyone looking to test their color-matching skills. So, grab your brush and let the colors flow as you paint stunning 3D models!

How to play free Color Match 3D Game online

To play Color Match 3D, start by selecting a 3D model that needs painting. Open the color palette and mix colors to find the perfect match for the target item displayed. Once you re satisfied with your creation, paint the model and submit it for grading. Remember, practice makes perfect!
