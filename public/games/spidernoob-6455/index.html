SpiderNoob

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to SpiderNoob, where you can unleash your inner acrobat and swing through a perilous world! In this thrilling game, you are equipped with a net that allows you to traverse the vast terrain with agility and finesse. As a novice spider, your mission is to navigate through treacherous landscapes while collecting gold along the way. However, beware! The ground is fraught with danger, and a slip could spell your doom. Falling onto the ground will lead you to an untimely demise, so vigilance is key.

Steer clear of the sharp, rotating blades and menacing wooden thorns that are scattered throughout the levels. These hazards will reduce your health significantly if you touch them. Your primary goal is to gather as much gold as possible while avoiding these deadly traps. The more gold you collect during your swings, the richer you become, pushing you closer to becoming the ultimate SpiderNoob.

Master the art of net throwing and jumping to create the perfect momentum for your flight. Your agility and quick reflexes will be your best assets as you navigate through this dynamic environment filled with obstacles and surprises. So gear up, collect gold, and make your mark in the world of SpiderNoob!

How to play free SpiderNoob game online

To play SpiderNoob, use your mouse or keyboard to aim and throw your net, launching yourself into the air. Control your swings to gather gold while avoiding traps. Use strategic jumps to escape hazards and reach new heights, and remember, staying airborne is your key to survival!


Instructions

Hold and pull then release to jump
