Cooking Cafe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Cooking Cafe, where culinary dreams come to life! In this delightful cooking simulation game, you ll have the opportunity to whip up a variety of mouth-watering dishes that will tantalize the taste buds of your customers. With a well-crafted menu at your fingertips, you can select from a wide array of recipes, each offering a unique culinary challenge.

To bring your delicious ideas to fruition, you'll first need to gather the necessary ingredients. Navigate through the local market, where you can purchase everything from fresh vegetables to exotic spices. Be strategic in your shopping; ensure you don t run out of essential items as you prepare meals to serve in your charming cafe.

As you move through various levels, your skills will be put to the test. Mastering the art of cooking in Cooking Cafe is not just about following recipes; it's also about managing time efficiently. Serve your dishes promptly to keep your customers happy and gain valuable tips that can help upgrade your cafe. The more satisfied your customers are, the more your cafe will thrive!

How to play free Cooking Cafe game online

To play the free Cooking Cafe game, simply choose a dish from the menu and check the ingredients needed. Purchase any missing items from the market, then follow the on-screen instructions to prepare the dish. Keep an eye on the timer to serve your customers quickly and efficiently for the best score!
