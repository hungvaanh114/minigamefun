Late to Go to School 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Oh no! It's that time again you're running late for school! In Late to go to school 2, you wake up to discover that the clock has betrayed you and you're already behind schedule. A sense of urgency fills the air as you scramble to make it to class on time. As you race to school, your heart sinks when you spot your teacher waiting at the entrance. Now, the challenge really begins!

In this engaging game, your objective is to devise a clever plan to sneak into school without being caught. You have a variety of methods at your disposal: will you bravely climb through the dog hole, scale the wall like a ninja, or simply muster the courage to open the door? The choice is yours, but be careful! The teacher is vigilant, and one wrong move can blow your cover. It s a race against time and a test of your stealth skills.

Navigate through the various obstacles while keeping an eye on the teacher s movements. Your success depends on your ability to use your environment creatively and to act swiftly. With adorable graphics and captivating challenges, Late to go to school 2 guarantees hours of fun as you try to outsmart the ever-watchful teacher. Can you make it to class without getting caught?

How to play free Late to go to school 2 game online

To play Late to go to school 2, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your character. Your goal is to avoid detection by the teacher as you look for alternative entrances to the school. Experiment with different routes like climbing, sneaking, or sliding to successfully enter the school without raising alarm!
