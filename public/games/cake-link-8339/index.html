Cake Link

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Cake Link, a charming puzzle game where your mission is to connect vibrant, multi-colored jellybeans across the playing field! Using your mouse, you can easily create paths between these delicious treats, making the gameplay both simple and irresistibly engaging. With every successful connection, you ll be enchanted by the playful visuals and the sweet satisfaction of your creations.

As you dive deeper into Cake Link, you'll discover a variety of modes designed to challenge players of all ages and skill levels. Choose from Novice, General, Simple, Difficult, or go straight to the ultimate test of wits with Nightmare mode! Each level offers unique challenges, and to keep things exciting, many large levels come with sub-levels that need to be completed within a set time limit. Your goal is to combine a specific number of jellybeans in each stage to move on, making strategic thinking and quick reflexes essential to your success.

Need a little help navigating your sweet path? No worries! The game features a handy light bulb button located in the lower right corner of the screen, which provides hints for creating the optimal paths when you find yourself stuck. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, Cake Link is all about having fun and indulging in a colorful adventure filled with delicious challenges.

How to play free Cake Link game online

To play the Cake Link game online, simply use your mouse to click and drag between adjacent jellybeans of the same color. Try to connect as many as possible within the time limit. Use the light bulb button for hints if you're having trouble finding the best path!
