Real City Car Stunts

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to experience the adrenaline-packed world of Real City Car Stunts, where you can showcase your driving skills and perform jaw-dropping stunts in a vibrant city teeming with action! Navigate through bustling streets filled with traffic while executing thrilling jumps off expertly designed ramps. With 15 exhilarating levels to conquer, each completed challenge propels you closer to becoming the ultimate stunt master.

Unleash a new level of excitement by exploring hidden areas sprinkled across the city, where you can collect valuable Coins. These coins can be used to unlock and purchase an incredible selection of 5 unique sports cars, each offering its own exhilarating driving experience. The game's free mode provides you with the freedom to roam and explore the city at your leisure, allowing you to practice your skills or take in the dynamic urban landscape.

Whether it's soaring off ramps or dodging oncoming traffic, Real City Car Stunts guarantees hours of entertainment and challenges your reflexes. So, jump in, ignite the engine, and dive into a world where your only limit is your ambition to complete each level while having an absolute blast!

How to play free Real City Car Stunts game online

To play Real City Car Stunts, use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your car, accelerate, and brake. Aim to hit the ramps at the right angle for maximum altitude and airtime! Collect hidden Coins as you navigate through levels and enjoy the freedom of exploring the city in free mode.
