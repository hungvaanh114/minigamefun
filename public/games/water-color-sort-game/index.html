How to play free Water Color Sort Game online

To play the Water Color Sort Game, start by selecting a beaker with colored water and pour it into another beaker. Your goal is to rearrange the colors until each beaker contains only one shade. Keep in mind that you can only pour water if both beakers have enough space. Enjoy the challenge!
