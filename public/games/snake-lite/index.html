Snake Lite

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Snake Lite is a game in which you need to control a snake and collect cubes that move along different trajectories, also avoiding obstacles and collisions.

Instructions

The arrow keys control the movement of the snake and the space bar restarts
