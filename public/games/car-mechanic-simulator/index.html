Car Mechanic Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the immersive world of Car Mechanic Simulator, where you can indulge your passion for automobiles in a lifelike workshop setting. Here, you ll have the opportunity to become a master car mechanic, engaging in various tasks that include oil changes, painting, and modifying intricate model kits. This thrilling experience is designed for those who wish to explore the in-depth mechanics of car repair and restoration.

As you navigate through the workshop, you ll encounter a variety of vehicles requiring maintenance and upgrades. Each car presents its own unique challenges, from routine service work to more complex repairs that test your skills and knowledge. You'll need to choose the right tools and parts for each job while adhering to the industry standard practices. The game includes detailed simulations of engine work, body repairs, and customizations, providing a comprehensive experience that mirrors real-life car mechanics.

Your journey in this captivating simulator allows for immense creativity as you paint cars and make them uniquely yours. You will also manage customer service, addressing client needs while ensuring high-quality results. Whether it s a classic vintage car or a modern sports vehicle, you ll thrive in crafting the ultimate automotive experience. Get ready to roll up your sleeves and dive into the realistic chaos of a mechanic's workshop.
