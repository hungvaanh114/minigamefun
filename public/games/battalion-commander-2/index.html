Battalion Commander 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Motherland calls upon you once more in Battalion Commander 2! Strange movements have been observed in the northern region, and it's up to you to respond with the utmost speed and determination. Equip yourself with a powerful arsenal of guns, rockets, and grenades as you prepare to secure the territory and protect your homeland. The fate of your nation rests on your shoulders, and it s time to demonstrate your tactical prowess. With an army at your disposal, the mission is clear: push forward, eliminate the threats, and reclaim what is rightfully yours. Ready. Aim. Fire!

Battalion Commander 2 immerses players in an exhilarating world filled with action and strategic combat. As you navigate through various missions, you ll encounter challenging enemies and diverse landscapes. You must adapt your strategy, utilizing the right weapons at the right time while engaging in intense firefights. The graphics and physics engine will keep you on the edge of your seat as you experience each exhilarating battle unfold. So, rally your troops, orchestrate your plan, and make every round count!

How to play free Battalion Commander 2 game online

To play Battalion Commander 2 online, simply move your character using the arrow keys and aim your weapons with the mouse. Press the space bar to shoot and deploy grenades. Scavenge for ammo and upgrades while completing missions to advance through the game. Enjoy the thrill of battle as you protect your territory!
