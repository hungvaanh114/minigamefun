Kimberly Noel Kardashian West, known to the world as Kim Kardashian, was born on October 21, 1980. She is an influential American media figure, entrepreneur, socialite, and model. Kim's rise to fame began as a stylist and close friend of the famous socialite Paris Hilton. However, her notoriety skyrocketed in 2007 after the public revelation of a private video featuring her and former partner Ray J. This incident paved the way for her family's entry into reality television.

Later that same year, Kim and her family starred in the E! hit reality show, Keeping Up with the Kardashians, which not only captivated audiences but also transformed their lives into a global brand. The show's remarkable success resulted in various spin-offs, including Kourtney and Kim Take New York and Kourtney and Khloe Take Miami, further solidifying the Kardashian family's status in pop culture.

Throughout her career, Kim has diversified her endeavors, launching successful business ventures in beauty, fashion, and mobile gaming. Alongside her business acumen, she has also been a powerful voice on social media, utilizing platforms to connect with millions of fans worldwide. Her influence extends beyond entertainment, as she is known for advocating for social justice and criminal reform.
