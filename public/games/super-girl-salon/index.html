Super Girl Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Super Girl Salon, where you can transform into a powerful superhero alongside the fabulous Power Girls and their adorable miraculous pets! Venture through the vibrant streets of Super City, where danger lurks around every corner. When the city cries out for assistance, it s your mission to catch the unruly monsters that threaten its peace. But don t worry your skills will not only subdue them but will also help them evolve into amiable super citizens!

In Super Girl Salon, your journey begins as you customize your own superhero character. Choose from various hairstyles, outfits, and accessories that embody your unique super persona. Once you re dressed to impress, it s time to team up with the Power Girls and their magical pets to tackle the monsters that have invaded the city. With an array of exciting challenges, you'll utilize special powers and clever strategies to capture the rascals and rehabilitate them.

As you successfully turn monsters into allies, feel free to explore new areas of Super City where additional adventures await. Engage in fun mini-games that further develop your superhero skills while collecting special items along the way. Transform your salon into a bustling hub for heroes, where you can beautify your characters and level up. It's a whimsical blend of fashion, fun, and heroism!

How to play free Super Girl Salon game online

To play Super Girl Salon, navigate the game using your mouse or touch screen. Customize your superhero by selecting outfits and accessories, then enter challenges to catch monsters. Follow on-screen prompts to use special powers and complete tasks, transforming your captured monsters into friendly citizens!
