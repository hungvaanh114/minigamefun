Boxes Wizard 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting universe of Boxes Wizard 2, the captivating sequel to the acclaimed 2D pixel art platformer. Step into the shoes of a talented wizard equipped with unique teleportation abilities and box-pushing prowess as you navigate through 40 intricately designed levels filled with engaging puzzles. This game challenges not only your reflexes but also your problem-solving skills, as you swap places with boxes to forge new pathways and tackle formidable obstacles.

The retro-inspired graphics will take you back to the glory days of gaming, while the immersive gameplay promises a delightful blend of nostalgia and excitement. Each level presents tougher challenges that require you to master innovative mechanics, testing your magical skills to their limits. As you progress through the game, you will find that every puzzle solved and every obstacle conquered brings you one step closer to victory.

With its charming aesthetic and well-crafted levels, Boxes Wizard 2 offers an adventure that will keep you engaged and entertained. Gather your wits and prepare yourself for a magical journey filled with excitement and discovery!

How to play free Boxes Wizard 2 game online

To enjoy Boxes Wizard 2, simply navigate through the game using the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Use the spacebar or designated key to teleport and push boxes into position. Solve puzzles by strategically swapping places with boxes to clear your path and reach the exit!
