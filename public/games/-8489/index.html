Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Bumper Cars Attack, an exhilarating car shooting game designed especially for kids! Get ready to dive into an action-packed arena where you control a vibrant bumper car, tasked with taking down enemy soldiers positioned above you. These foes are armed and will shoot back, so you must stay sharp and agile!

Your objective is to eliminate as many enemies as possible while navigating through a frenetic battlefield. With simple touch controls, you can move your bumper car left or right on the screen to dodge incoming bullets that can harm you. The more enemies you defeat, the higher your score will climb, granting you the opportunity to play longer and achieve top scores in every session.

As you engage in each gameplay round, approach the soldiers strategically to maximize your firepower while skillfully avoiding their bullets. The thrill of close encounters adds an exciting layer to the gameplay, ensuring that every match is filled with suspense and adrenaline. Can you master your bumper car and emerge victorious?

How to play free Bumper Cars Attack game online

To play Bumper Cars Attack, simply tap the left or right side of the screen to steer your bumper car in either direction. Use these movements to dodge bullets and line up shots against the enemy soldiers. Aim for precision and speed to dominate the battlefield and achieve an impressive score!
