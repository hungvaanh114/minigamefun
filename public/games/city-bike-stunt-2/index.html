City Bike Stunt 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to rev up your engines with the exhilarating sequel, City Bike Stunt 2! This latest edition of the popular franchise introduces a completely overhauled Free Riding map that promises even more thrilling adventures. Whether you're a casual player or a seasoned stunt enthusiast, this game has something for everyone!

In City Bike Stunt 2, players have the opportunity to customize and upgrade their motorcycles at the in-game "Garage." As you explore the vast Free Riding map, you ll need to collect diamonds that will enable you to enhance your bike's performance and aesthetic appeal. The game encourages exploration and skillful riding to gather the resources needed for upgrades.

To unlock exciting new motorcycles, you'll engage in heart-pounding races against the clock in the "Racing" section. This competitive element adds an adrenaline rush as you navigate through challenging levels, pushing your biking skills to the limit. With options to play in both single-player or two-player modes, you can enjoy the thrill of stunts alone or challenge a friend for added fun!

How to play free City Bike Stunt 2 game online

Begin your thrilling journey by selecting your desired game mode either solo or against a friend. Use the on-screen controls to accelerate, steer, and perform stunts. As you ride around in the Free Riding area, collect diamonds to upgrade your bike. Participate in races to unlock more motorcycles and take your gameplay to new heights!
