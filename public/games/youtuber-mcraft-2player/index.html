Youtuber Mcraft 2Player

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

You need to help the YouTuber girl and her boyfriend escape from prison. In this two-player adventure, they must collect all the YouTube plaques without getting too far apart, obtain the key and the golden plaque, and reach the chest. Open the chest and run to the door. You need to be very fast, you must escape from prison!

Instructions

Use the WASD and Arrow keys to move Double Jump available Collect all YouTube plaques and find the key After finding the key reach the chest
