Kick

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Kick is an engaging single-player game that invites players to step into the shoes of an aspiring soccer player. Set against the backdrop of bustling street alleys, this game provides an unconventional playground for honing your soccer skills. As you navigate through these tightly-packed locales, your agility, speed, and shot accuracy will be put to the ultimate test as you kick the ball around.

In Kick, every action has its consequences. Players will earn points by skillfully striking walls with the soccer ball, but there s a catch! Careless kicks that send the ball crashing into windows will lead to point deductions. Thus, maintaining a strategic approach is key to keeping your score above zero. The challenge is to navigate the environment carefully, avoiding damage while racking up points through clever and accurate shots. The game s intuitive mechanics keep players engaged, as they strive to achieve high scores while testing their precision and control.

With increasing levels of difficulty and the potential for high scores, Kick offers an addictive experience for soccer enthusiasts. Each kick you make holds the chance for triumph or a setback, making every session a thrilling journey of skill development and fun. So lace up your virtual boots and get ready to show off your talent in the streets!

How to play free Kick game online

To play Kick, simply use your mouse to aim where you want to kick the ball. Click to initiate the kick and adjust your power with the slider. Remember to avoid windows to keep your score from falling, while aiming for walls to earn points. Keep playing to better your skills!
