Tiktok Gravity Knife Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

TikTok Gravity Knife Rush is an interesting arcade game that combines parkour and shooting! In this dynamic game, you will experience the fun of extreme parkour while beginning nervous battles with enemies. When selecting the shooting mode, shoot the digital post and collect the knife. Beware of spinning mace, stick, etc. In Parkour mode, collect as many knives as possible, choose the right digital gate, dodge bombs, hammers, etc.

Instructions

Mouse click or tap to play
