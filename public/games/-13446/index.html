Angry Tower?Angry Tower

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the exciting world of Angry Tower, a captivating tower-building game inspired by the iconic birds! In this fun and addictive game, your objective is to construct a towering marvel by stacking blocks, one at a time. With each block you drop, the tower rises higher, but be cautious! If you misjudge your timing, your tower will come crashing down, leading to a dizzying collapse.

How far can you take your construction? Can you surpass all previous heights and reach for the stars, or even the moon? With simple and intuitive controls, Angry Tower offers an engaging experience for players who adore tower-building games. Challenge yourself to see how high you can stack those blocks while testing your precision and timing skills. The thrill of watching your tower grow while keeping it stable is guaranteed to keep you hooked for hours!

As you progress through the game, you will encounter various challenges that will test your abilities to manipulate the blocks efficiently. Do you have what it takes to achieve the tallest tower and prove your skills? Angry Tower is not just a game; it s a test of wits, coordination, and balance that will leave you yearning for more!

How to play free Angry Tower game online

To play Angry Tower, simply click to release the block, aiming to drop it precisely atop the previous one. Keep an eye on your timing, as precise placement is crucial for the tower's stability. The higher you stack, the more challenging it becomes. Stay focused and try to build your tower as high as you can!
