Step into a chilling realm with Bad Dolls, where you can select the most malevolent doll to combat relentless foes. Equipped with a broad array of weapons and tactics, your mission is to obliterate every challenger in your way and assert your dominance. This game is a thrilling rollercoaster of action and excitement, offering four distinct game modes designed to engage you for hours.

In Single mode, you will need to progress through levels by vanquishing each enemy that dares to cross your path. It s a true test of your skills as the enemies become tougher with each level. If you re seeking a relentless challenge, Endless mode allows you to fend off waves of enemies for as long as possible without being overwhelmed. For those who enjoy camaraderie, the 2p CO-OP mode enables you to unite with a friend, tackling adversaries side by side. Meanwhile, the 2p VS mode pits you against your friends in a battle for supremacy, allowing you to demonstrate your prowess in a competitive environment.

Each mode offers a unique experience, ensuring that you're constantly entertained and challenged as you dive deeper into the horrifying universe of Bad Dolls. With its captivating gameplay and heart-pounding action, you ll find yourself clawing through levels and bosses to emerge victorious.

How to play free Bad Dolls game online

To play Bad Dolls online, select your preferred game mode from the main menu. Use the directional arrows or WASD keys to move your doll, while the mouse controls your aiming and shooting. Collect weapons and power-ups to enhance your abilities, and work strategically to defeat your enemies and advance through the levels.
