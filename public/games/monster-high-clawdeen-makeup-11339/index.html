Monster High Clawdeen Makeup

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Monster High with Clawdeen, the fabulous wolf student whose creativity knows no bounds! Clawdeen is not just any student; she is a rising fashion designer aiming to carve her niche in the fashion industry. With her impeccable taste, she spends her time at Monster High honing her skills and building an impressive portfolio. Her ambition shines as she works towards organizing a sensational fashion show in Scaris that carries her name.

In this exciting Monster High Clawdeen Makeup game, you get to take on the role of Clawdeen s personal makeup artist. The goal is to enhance her natural beauty with the latest makeup trends. Choose from a diverse range of colors and styles that reflect her vibrant personality. From bold lipstick shades to elegant eyeshadow palettes, you have the creative freedom to explore countless looks.

To complete Clawdeen s stunning transformation, accessorize her with stylish jewelry that complements her unique outfits. Experiment with different dresses, ensuring she stands out in every occasion. Whether she is attending a class at Monster High or showcasing her designs at a fashion event, Clawdeen deserves to look her best!

How to play free Monster High Clawdeen Makeup game online

To play the Monster High Clawdeen Makeup game, simply follow the on-screen instructions. Begin by selecting various makeup options from the menu, and apply them to Clawdeen's face. Next, choose stunning outfits and jewelry pieces to complete her look. Use your mouse to click on different elements, allowing you to create a unique style for Clawdeen!
