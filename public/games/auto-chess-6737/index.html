Auto Chess

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the strategic realm of Auto Chess, players dive into a captivating blend of turn-based tactics and chess-like gameplay. Every turn, you begin with an income of 5 gold, which forms the foundation of your strategy. Your primary objective is to assemble a powerful team of Champions to outmaneuver and defeat your opponents.

Champions can be purchased for 3 gold each, allowing you to build a diverse roster with unique abilities and traits. The strategic element comes into play as you gather Champions of the same type; doing so unlocks potent bonuses that can give you the upper hand in battles. Each turn, you have the option to refresh your shop for 2 gold to discover new Champion offerings, increasing your chances of acquiring the most effective lineup. Additionally, leveling up your player character costs 4 gold and expands the number of Champions you can have on the board, thus enhancing your tactical options.

To stay alive and claim victory, it s crucial to maintain your gold balance while strategically investing in your Champions. Win rounds by defeating opponents and gradually improve your lineup to become an indomitable force on the board. As you progress, you'll find that adapting your strategy to meet the unique challenges posed by each enemy team is essential for success.

How to play free Auto Chess game online

To play the Auto Chess game online, start by entering the match and accumulating gold each turn. Use your gold wisely to buy Champions, refresh the shop for better options, and level up to unlock more strategic possibilities. Focus on synergy between Champions of similar types to build a powerful team, and adapt your strategies based on your opponents' compositions to secure victories.
