Color Liquid Sorting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience a vibrant challenge like no other with Color Liquid Sorting. This innovative sorting game invites you to immerse yourself in a world where colorful liquids reside in multiple flasks. Each flask contains distinct layers of liquid, each a unique color, ensuring that they do not blend into a chaotic, indistinguishable hue. Your goal is simple yet demanding: rearrange the liquids so that each flask ultimately contains liquid of the same color.

As you engage with the game, you'll notice various flasks filled with different colored liquids. The strategic aspect lies in how you pour these liquids between flasks, utilizing available empty containers to help you in your endeavor. Careful decision-making is essential, as you'll need to analyze the current configuration and determine the most efficient way to separate the colors while avoiding color contamination.

With each level you conquer, the complexity increases; additional flasks and colors are introduced, challenging your sorting skills even further. You ll find yourself making numerous careful moves, all aimed at achieving a perfectly organized and colorful outcome. The satisfaction of watching your flasks transform into individual color groups is incredibly rewarding, making every effort in Color Liquid Sorting worthwhile.

How to play free Color Liquid Sorting game online

To play the game, simply click on a flask to select it, then tap on another flask to pour the liquid. You can only pour liquid into a flask if it s either empty or contains the same color on top. Use the available empty flasks wisely to succeed!
