Prepare yourself for an exhilarating escapade in the depths of space with the thrilling game, Motor Racing in Space! This isn't just any ordinary racing game; it combines the excitement of high-speed motorbike racing with the vast, uncharted territories of the cosmos. As you navigate through a series of challenging courses, your primary objective is to gather sparkling diamonds scattered throughout the galaxy. These precious gems are not only visually appealing but will also empower you to upgrade your engine.

Your journey will not be easy. Each level presents a unique and perilous path that tests your skills and reflexes. From treacherous asteroid fields to gravity-defying loops, every turn may bring new surprises. You must dodge obstacles, outpace rivals, and ensure your survival as you race to the finish line. As you accumulate diamonds, return to the garage to purchase faster and more efficient engines, giving you the edge you need to conquer every difficult track that lies ahead.

The thrill of Motor Racing in Space intensifies as you progress through the 30 adrenaline-pumping levels. Will you be able to master each one and emerge victorious? Grab your space helmet, rev up your engines, and get ready to accelerate into an unforgettable adventure!

How to play free Motor Racing in Space game online

Once you're in the game, use the arrow keys to control your bike's speed and direction. Collect diamonds to earn points and unlock new engines. Keep an eye out for obstacles and carefully navigate through the level to avoid crashing. Complete all 30 levels to become the ultimate space racer!
