How to play free Candy Crush Eggs Blast Game: Eggs Link Puzzle online

To play the game online, simply use your mouse or tap on matching egg colors to link them together. Connect three or more same-colored eggs to blast them and free the baby birds. Keep an eye on the timer and strategize your moves carefully to avoid letting the eggs spoil!
