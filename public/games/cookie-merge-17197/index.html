Cookie Merge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Cookie Merge, the latest captivating match-3 game that promises to whisk you away into a world of delicious fun! In this enchanting game, your primary task is simple yet engaging: match three or more similar candies by placing them next to each other. When you successfully combine these delightful treats, they will merge into a brand-new candy. The more candies you connect, the more exciting transformations you'll witness!

Your ultimate goal in Cookie Merge is to reach the magnificent big cake at the end of your journey. Each successful merge brings you one step closer to victory and the sweet satisfaction of enjoying a glorious cake. With eye-catching graphics and charming sounds, this game is designed to entertain and challenge your mind as you strategize the best moves and combos to achieve high scores.

As you navigate through various levels and challenges, you'll also encounter obstacles that keep the game fresh and invigorating. Test your puzzle-solving skills and enjoy the thrill of rewards as you progress through each level. Whether you're a casual gamer or a seasoned pro, Cookie Merge offers endless fun and excitement. So, gather your friends, put on your thinking cap, and dive into this delightful adventure!

How to play free Cookie Merge game online

To play Cookie Merge, start by selecting candies on the board. Drag and drop the candies to line up three or more identical ones either horizontally or vertically. Once merged, they will transform into a new candy type. Keep merging to reach the big cake and win the game!
