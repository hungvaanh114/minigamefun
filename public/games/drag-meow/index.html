Drag Meow

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Drag Meow, a whimsical drag-and-drop adventure that tests your skills and reflexes. In this delightful game, your mission is to guide a spirited feline to its cherished cushion while navigating a landscape filled with peril. As you embark on this challenging quest, be prepared to dodge a variety of hazards that threaten the life of our adorable kitty.

The game features vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay that will keep you entertained for hours. Your objective is straightforward: drag the cat across the screen towards its cushion. However, as you progress, obstacles will arise, challenging your timing and strategy. These obstacles include perilous pits, menacing creatures, and other unpredictable challenges that could lead to disaster if you're not quick enough. Make every move count as you work your way to safety, ensuring your furry friend makes it to their destination unharmed.

With increasingly complex levels and the whimsical charm of the game, Drag Meow promises a delightful experience for players of all ages. Whether you re a cat lover or a fan of casual gaming, this adventure will surely bring a smile to your face as you plot the safest path home for your beloved pet.

How to play free Drag Meow game online

To play the Drag Meow game, simply click and hold on the cat to drag it towards its cushion while avoiding hazards. Use your mouse or finger (on mobile) to navigate around obstacles and make sure not to let the cat get caught! Aim for a smooth journey and watch out for dangers along the way!
