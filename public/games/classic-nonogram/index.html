Classic Nonogram

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you have a passion for brain-teasing puzzles like Sudoku, then Classic Nonogram is a game that will capture your interest! This captivating puzzle game challenges you to use logic and deduction to fill in the grid. Your main objective is to decide which cells should be painted and which ones should remain blank, leading to the creation of a mesmerizing pixel art image.

As you embark on this intriguing journey, you'll encounter clues provided by the numbers along the rows and columns. These numbers indicate how many consecutive cells in that particular line should be filled. However, tread carefully; you are limited to just three lives throughout your gameplay. Each wrong move could lead to losing one of these precious lives, heightening the thrill of each decision you make!

Classic Nonogram not only hones your strategic thought process but also provides endless hours of entertainment. Whether you're a novice or a puzzle master, you'll find varying levels of difficulty to suit your skill level. This game is perfect for those looking to unwind or challenge their minds while still having fun. So get ready to dive into a world of colorful blocks and intricate patterns, and see how quickly you can complete the puzzles!

How to play free Classic Nonogram game online

To play Classic Nonogram, begin by examining the numbers at the ends of each row and column. These will guide you on which cells to fill. Use logic to decide where to paint and where to leave empty. Keep an eye on your lives, as you can only make a limited number of mistakes!
