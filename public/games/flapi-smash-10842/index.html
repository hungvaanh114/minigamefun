Flapi Smash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the quirky world of Flapi Smash, a thrilling game that is as simple as it is addictive! The mechanics are straightforward: just tap the screen to close the pipes and stop those pesky birds from flying through. Your goal is to eliminate as many avian intruders as possible before they escape!

Unlock an array of powerful boosts as you progress through the game, each designed to help you obliterate the birds with ease! Choose from an exciting selection of potent pipes that provide exceptional advantages, enhancing your gameplay experience. As you advance, you'll also collect bone bucks, a valuable in-game currency that you can use to shop for upgrades and cosmetic items.

Dress the birds in an assortment of hilarious outfits that you can unlock, adding a touch of fun to your gameplay. Keep track of your achievements by saving your progress to the cloud, allowing you to seamlessly switch devices without losing any of the fun you ve had in Flapi Smash!

How to play free Flapi Smash game online

Once you enter the Flapi Smash game, your objective is to tap the screen at the right moment to shut the pipes and capture the birds. Focus on the timing of your taps to maximize your bird catches, and keep an eye out for power-ups to increase your score. Enjoy the game, and aim for the highest score possible!
