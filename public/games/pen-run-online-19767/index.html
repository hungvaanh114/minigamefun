Pen Run Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pen Run Online is an engaging arcade game that offers players a unique and entertaining experience. In this game, players can immerse themselves in the whimsical world of a pen running along a tabletop. With smooth sliding mechanics, you guide the pen through various obstacles while trying to gather other pens along the way. The colorful visuals and dynamic gameplay keep you hooked as you navigate the pathways.

Your primary objective in Pen Run Online is to avoid numerous hazards, including books, pen containers, and other items that may hinder your progress. Each obstacle presents a challenge, requiring quick reflexes and strategic maneuvering to sidestep them. The thrill of dodging these barriers while picking up additional pens makes for an exhilarating experience, adding layers of excitement to the gameplay.

As you successfully collect pens and make your way to the finish line, you'll be rewarded for your efforts upon reaching the end. The extra pens you've amassed throughout your run will be captured in the book at the finish, enhancing your overall score and boasting your collection. This feature encourages players to always strive for more, as they can see their progress and achievements accumulate over time, creating a satisfying loop of excitement and motivation.

How to play free Pen Run Online game online

To play Pen Run Online, simply swipe your finger on the screen to slide the pen forward. Collect additional pens by moving towards them while steering clear of obstacles like books and containers. Reach the end of the path to store the pens you've collected in the book, aiming for the highest score possible!
