Hit Master 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Hit Master 3D, where your skills as a covert operative are put to the ultimate test! In this action-packed adventure, you take on the role of a master spy tasked with eliminating waves of enemies that seek to thwart your mission. As hordes of foes close in on you, the only weapons at your disposal are an arsenal of knives. Your objective? Take down each opponent stealthily and strategically, one blade at a time.

If you re a fan of gripping action spy narratives, this game is tailor-made for you. Engage in intense gameplay where precision and timing are paramount. Whether you opt for a stealthy approach or a more explosive exit, the choice is yours. You can either throw knives directly at your targets or spark chaos by launching your blade into explosive barrels, which can wipe out multiple enemies in one fell swoop! Additionally, keep an eye out for boxes scattered throughout the levels breaking them can provide you with various advantages, slowing down those who threaten your mission.

Your journey is fraught with challenges, as you must navigate through dangerous terrains while rescuing hostages and reaching the extraction point the helicopter. However, be prepared; your adversaries aren't just ordinary foes. They come armed with baseball bats, wrenches, and an aggressive attitude, converging to overpower you in their numbers. But fear not, the ultimate hero within you can handle any threat!

How to play free Hit Master 3D game online

To dive into Hit Master 3D, simply start an online session and use your mouse or finger to aim and throw knives at your enemies. Tap or click to release the knives, aiming for explosive barrels to create chain reactions. Keep an eye on the surroundings for interactive items that will aid your escape and increase your score!
