In "Alien Shoot Zombies," prepare to engage in a thrilling battle where extraterrestrial beings take up arms against the undead! Set in a chaotic world overtaken by zombies, your mission is to strategically eliminate these relentless foes using precise aim and expert timing. How to play free Alien Shoot Zombies game online

To play the game, use your mouse to aim your weapon and click to shoot. Adjust the angle using the on-screen indicators to ensure your shots hit the zombies. Keep an eye on your ammunition and reload when necessary. Complete the level by eliminating all zombies before they reach you!
