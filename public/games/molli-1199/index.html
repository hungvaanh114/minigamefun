Collect coins as you explore, and test your knack for problem-solving by tackling the many puzzles embedded within each level. Get ready to jump into action and discover the magical world of Molli!

How to play free Molli game online

To play Molli, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate Molli through each level. Jump to avoid obstacles and collect diamonds and keys. Pay attention to the platform layout while timing your jumps accurately to reach new areas and successfully complete puzzles along the way!
