Instructions:

Step into the bustling world of PAWN BOSS, where every day brings a new challenge in running your very own pawn shop! As eager customers flock to your establishment, it s up to you to discern the true value of their items and identify the hidden gems among the trash. Hone your bargaining skills to negotiate the best prices to expand your inventory, but tread carefully some items may not be as they seem!

The dynamics of PAWN BOSS are thrilling and engaging, requiring not only sharp instincts but also a keen eye for detail. Each transaction presents an opportunity to earn big, but with that comes the risk of purchasing fakes. Developing your ability to spot inconsistencies and anomalies in objects will be crucial to your success. Create a reputable business that attracts more customers and keeps them coming back for more.

As you progress in the game, upgrade your shop and unlock various features that will help elevate your pawn shop over the rest. Each visit from customers will challenge you to leverage your expertise and intuition to maximize your profits. Are you prepared for the hustle and bustle of daily deals and the excitement of uncovering hidden treasures? Dive into the action of PAWN BOSS and see if you re up for the challenge!

How to play free PAWN BOSS game online

To play PAWN BOSS, start by tapping on incoming customers to view their items. Use your bargaining skills to negotiate the best deals. Pay attention to clues that may indicate whether an item is genuine or a fake. Once you decide to buy, make your purchase and prepare for the next customer! Enjoy managing your shop!
