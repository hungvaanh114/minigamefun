Totem Breaker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Totem Breaker, the exhilarating game that will put your skills and reflexes to the test! Engage in a fun, fast-paced challenge where your main objective is to dismantle the totem pole before the timer hits zero. The bright, colorful graphics and engaging gameplay make it easy to lose track of time as you strive for that coveted high score!

As you dive into the vibrant world of Totem Breaker, you'll quickly find out that there's more to it than just smashing blocks. You must navigate through a series of obstacles represented by wings, which can hinder your progress. Stay alert and avoid these obstacles as you work against the clock, because every second counts! With each successful strike, you'll earn points, pushing you closer to the top of the leaderboard.

The charm of Totem Breaker lies not only in its addictive gameplay but also in its simplicity. It's a game that can be enjoyed by players of all ages, whether you're a casual gamer looking to unwind or a competitive spirit aiming for mastery. Challenge your friends and family to join in the fun and see who can achieve the highest score. Will you rise to the challenge and become the ultimate Totem Breaker?

How to play free Totem Breaker game online

To play Totem Breaker, start by using your mouse or touchscreen to aim your strikes at the totem. Tap or click to break down segments of the totem while dodging the flying wings. Remember to keep an eye on the timer and act quickly to maximize your points before the countdown ends!
