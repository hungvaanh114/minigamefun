Putot 2 is an exhilarating 2D platform game that challenges players to collect treasure-filled cubes while skillfully dodging an array of dangers. As you embark on your adventure, you'll encounter menacing alien foes, various flying creatures, and a barrage of bullets. The path is fraught with spikes and rotating lasers that intensify the challenge as you progress. Your ultimate goal is to reach the Exit door, which will lead you to the next, even more challenging level. With 8 engaging levels to complete, Putot 2 is designed to ramp up the difficulty, keeping you on your toes and ensuring your skills are put to the ultimate test.

This sequel to the acclaimed original "Putot" series enhances the gameplay experience with improved graphics and new obstacles, ensuring that both returning fans and new players will find excitement in every jump and dodge. Strategically time your movements to navigate through the treacherous environments and collect all the treasures waiting to be discovered. Each level is uniquely crafted, presenting players with diverse challenges that require quick reflexes and sharp decision-making to conquer.

As you master the game's mechanics and learn the patterns of your enemies, you'll develop strategies to overcome each level's tricky arrangements. Will you be able to collect all the hidden treasures and escape from the clutches of the alien enemies? Gear up for a thrilling platforming experience in Putot 2!

How to play free Putot 2 game online

To play Putot 2, use the arrow keys or WASD keys to navigate your character. Jump to avoid hazards and collect treasure cubes. Press the space bar to interact with the Exit door at the end of each level. Focus on timing your movements to evade enemies and obstacles effectively!
