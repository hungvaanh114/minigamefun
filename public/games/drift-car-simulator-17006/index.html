How to play free Drift Car Simulator game online

To enjoy the Drift Car Simulator, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard to steer and accelerate your car. Use the spacebar to perform stunts and initiate drifts. Challenge yourself by completing different tracks and unlocking new vehicles as you progress through the game. Enjoy the ride!
