Gravity Switch Cube

Experience the thrill of physics with Gravity Switch Cube, an engaging and free game available for Android devices. Navigate your way through a series of challenging levels by swiping in various directions to maneuver the box. Your primary objective is to guide the box to the designated green block to finish each level successfully.

But beware! The game introduces a twist you can alter gravity with a simple tap of the designated buttons, adding an intriguing layer to gameplay. Be cautious, however, as colliding with any red or orange blocks will result in your immediate demise. How to play free Gravity Switch Cube game online

To play Gravity Switch Cube, simply swipe to move the box in your desired direction. Use gravity change buttons to alter the box's gravitational pull as you navigate through levels. Always aim to reach the green block while avoiding contact with dangerous red and orange blocks. Stay strategic and have fun!
