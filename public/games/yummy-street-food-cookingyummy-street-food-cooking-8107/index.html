Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a flavor-packed adventure in the kitchen with Sofia in Yummy Street Food Cooking! If you have a passion for street food and enjoy cooking, then this game is perfect for you. Today, Sofia is on a mission to recreate her favorite street food delights right at home. Will you lend her a hand in whipping up these delightful treats?

First up is the colorful and whimsical rainbow cotton candy! Experience the thrill of sugary sweetness as you spin fluffy strands of cotton candy in vibrant colors. Mix and match different hues to create an eye-catching masterpiece that is sure to impress. Once you ve mastered the art of cotton candy making, it's time to take on the next tasty challenge.

Next, you'll dive into the world of popcorn. But this isn t just any popcorn; we're talking about rainbow popcorn! Combine popping skills with creativity to make popcorn that s bursting with color and flavor. Drizzle on some delicious toppings to elevate this classic snack into a festive treat!

Finally, no street food day is complete without a scoop of homemade ice cream. Work alongside Sofia to mix flavors, churn, and freeze your way to creamy perfection. Experiment with different ingredients to craft unique concoctions that are both refreshing and satisfying. Get ready to taste the sweet rewards of your culinary efforts!

How to play free Yummy Street Food Cooking game online

Jump into the game by selecting Sofia to start your street food cooking journey. Follow on-screen instructions to gather ingredients and use the cooking tools provided. Click to spin cotton candy, pop popcorn, and mix ice cream, ensuring you satisfy Sofia s street food cravings!
