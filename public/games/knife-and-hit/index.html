Knife and Hit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Master the art of knife throwing in this addictive arcade game! Challenge yourself in this thrilling knife-throwing adventure where precision meets excitement. Aim, throw, and stick your knives into the rotating wooden target with perfect timing. Can you land 10 knives side by side without failing? Level up through increasingly challenging stages, each featuring unique rotating patterns and obstacles. Collect golden apples for bonus points, unlock an impressive arsenal of razor-sharp knives, and face epic boss battles every 5 levels. Perfect your timing, develop lightning-fast reflexes, and become the ultimate knife-throwing master!
