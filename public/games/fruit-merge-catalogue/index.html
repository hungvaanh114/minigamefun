How to play free Fruit Merge Catalogue game online

To play the Fruit Merge Catalogue, simply select two identical fruits to merge them into a larger one. Continue this process, combining fruits strategically to create the next size up. Keep merging until you finally reach the giant watermelon! Use your wits to plan your moves efficiently and help speed your way to victory!
