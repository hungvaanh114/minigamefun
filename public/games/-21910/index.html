Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Black Hole Billiard is an engaging and entertaining billiard game that appeals to players of all ages. This modern twist on the classic pool game takes an arcade-style approach, offering a relaxed single-player experience that lets you unwind while sharpening your skills. If you're passionate about billiards and looking for a laid-back gaming session, Black Hole Billiard is the perfect choice for you!

In this captivating game, your primary objective is to sink all the red balls into the designated black hole to complete each level. With 11 uniquely designed levels, you're guaranteed an enjoyable experience filled with fun and challenges as you progress and hone your billiard abilities. Each level increases in complexity, testing not only your precision and strategy but also your creativity in finding the best angles and shots. The simplistic yet vibrant graphics and smooth gameplay make it easy to get lost in this delightful billiards adventure.

Get ready to explore the field, strategize your moves, and enjoy the satisfaction of clearing each level in Black Hole Billiard. It's time to grab your cue, line up your shots, and immerse yourself in some great billiard entertainment!

How to play free Black Hole Billiard game online

To play Black Hole Billiard, aim your shot using the on-screen controls to guide the cue ball towards the red balls. Adjust the angle and power as needed before striking. Clear all red balls by sinking them into the black hole to advance to the next level. Enjoy the challenges ahead!
