Princess Ella Hand Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Cinderella in an urgent quest to prepare her hands for a special meeting with the prince in the delightful game, Princess Ella Hand Care! In this fun and engaging activity, you'll experience the thrill of transforming Cinderella's hands from tired and dirty to absolutely beautiful and ready for a royal encounter. Every day, Cinderella is loaded with housework, leaving her hands covered in dust and dough. Now, it s your mission to rescue her hands before her big date!

Step into the magical world of hand care as you clean, scrub, and pamper Cinderella's hands. Start by washing away the dirt and grime; use a variety of tools and products to achieve that perfect cleanse. Once her hands are spotless, dive into the exciting makeover stage where you can apply luxurious creams and soothing treatments to rejuvenate her skin.

The grand finale awaits it's time for a fantastic nail art session! Let your creativity shine as you choose from a range of beautiful colors, patterns, and decals to create stunning nail designs that Cinderella will cherish. She s sure to be grateful for your help in turning her hands into the talk of the kingdom, making a lasting impression on the prince!

How to play free Princess Ella Hand Care game online

To play the Princess Ella Hand Care game, simply use your mouse to navigate through the different stages of hand care. Start by cleaning Cinderella s hands, then proceed to apply beauty treatments; finally, indulge in the nail art section where you can select designs and colors that best suit her style. Enjoy the magic of transformation!
