Gravity Frog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Gravity Frog, where your mission is to guide a brave little frog through a myriad of challenging levels! In this unique adventure, you will have the power to manipulate gravity to navigate the landscape filled with daunting obstacles. Your objective is simple yet engaging: steer your froggy friend towards the door in each level while avoiding various hindrances that could impede your progress. With 12 levels of increasing difficulty, every stage presents new challenges that will test your skills and reflexes.

The gameplay is both innovative and captivating, as switching gravity adds a dynamic twist to traditional platforming mechanics. You'll need quick thinking and precise timing to make the most of the gravitational changes. As you conquer each level, you'll discover creative ways to dodge barriers and utilize the environment to your advantage. The inviting graphics and cheerful sounds will keep you entertained as you bounce and leap your way to victory. Ready to put your skills to the test? Play now!

How to play free Gravity Frog game online

To play Gravity Frog, simply use the arrow keys or designated buttons to switch gravity and control the frog's movements. Your goal is to reach the door at the end of each level while avoiding obstacles. Keep an eye on the screen, strategize your jumps, and adjust the gravity as needed to successfully navigate the challenges that lie ahead!
