Step into the enchanting world of the Cotton Candy Style Hair Salon, the ultimate destination for every girl who dreams of a colorful and whimsical makeover! Here, the Candylocks dolls await you, flaunting their delightfully long and ultra-soft cotton candy hair, just ready to be styled to perfection. The sweet charm of this salon goes beyond imagination as you explore endless possibilities for hair play!

Use your creativity to curl, braid, straighten, or even give the dolls a fabulous haircut. The choice is yours! Want to brighten things up? Dye their dreamy locks in every shade of the rainbow, turning each hairstyle into a vibrant masterpiece. But the fun doesn t stop there personalize your creations with delightful DIY hair accessories that add a delicious touch to your styling experience!

Unleash your inner artist by creating cute makeup looks for your dolls, ensuring that they not only have the best hair but also a glamorous flair. The Cotton Candy Style Hair Salon is your canvas, and every detail counts, from the hair to the accessories to the makeup!

As you immerse yourself in this magical salon, don t forget to decorate it according to your fantasy! Choose colors, themes, and designs to create a salon that reflects your style and captures the sugary essence of cotton candy dreams!

How to play free Cotton Candy Style Hair Salon game online

To play the Cotton Candy Style Hair Salon game, simply select your Candylocks doll and choose the styling tools available. Click and drag to curl, braid, or cut the hair, then dye it in your favorite colors. Accessorize with DIY items and apply makeup to complete the look. Enjoy your creative journey!
