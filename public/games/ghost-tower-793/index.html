Ghost Tower

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Ghost Tower, an engaging and addictive tower-building experience where each move counts! The game invites players to drop blocks on top of each other, creating a spectacular tower that reaches for the sky. With each successful placement, your tower grows taller, but stay on your toes! Missing the mark will not only lose a block but may result in a chaotic tumble, forcing you to restart your construction from scratch. Can you conquer the challenge and reach new heights? The sky isn t the limit; aim for the moon!

Ghost Tower Blocks is wonderfully suited for fans of tower-building games who thrive on testing their precision and skill. Every block you stack builds excitement and tension, pushing you to surpass your personal best. It s a game that not only offers fun but also an opportunity to improve your timing and hand-eye coordination. Get ready to build your masterpiece, stack your blocks high, and challenge yourself to become the master builder of the tallest tower in the game!

If you enjoy friendly competition, share your scores and progress with friends or challenge them to beat your record. The game is designed to be simple to understand yet incredibly engaging, ensuring that players of all ages can enjoy building their ghostly towers. So, gather your concentration, overcome your fear of falling blocks, and see how high you can really go in this whimsical world of Ghost Tower!

How to play free Ghost Tower game online

To play Ghost Tower, simply click or tap to drop each block as it moves across the screen. Time your clicks perfectly to stack the blocks precisely on top of one another. The goal is to keep building your tower as high as possible without letting any blocks fall. Enjoy the challenge!
