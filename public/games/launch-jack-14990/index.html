Launch Jack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure with Launch Jack, a captivating physics-based game that immerses you in a world where pumpkins and zombies collide! As the daring pumpkin protagonist, Jack, you will fling yourself into action, launching through eerie, haunted landscapes filled with zombies who threaten to ruin Halloween night. Your mission? To obliterate zombie heads and safeguard the spirit of the holiday!

With each level, you will encounter a progressively challenging environment filled with creative obstacles and tricky targets. The gameplay revolves around strategic shot placement, where precise angles and force play crucial roles in maximizing your pumpkin-launching power. Utilize special power-ups that can significantly boost your abilities, allowing you to crush even the toughest zombie heads and achieve higher scores.

The vibrant graphics and spooky sound effects set the perfect atmosphere for your Halloween endeavor. As you advance through the game, you'll discover diverse locations that add to the eeriness of the experience. The combination of humor and action keeps players engaged while they unleash epic combos to clear each stage. Can you conquer the challenges that await and rescue Halloween from the clutches of these mischievous undead? It's time to find out!

How to play free Launch Jack game online

To play Launch Jack, use your mouse to set the angle and power for your pumpkin launch. Aim carefully at navigating zombies and tap to launch. Keep an eye on the special power-ups and utilize them strategically for maximum damage. Clear the levels by hitting all zombie heads and enjoy the spooky fun!
