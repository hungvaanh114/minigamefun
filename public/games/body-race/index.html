Body Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Body Race is an engaging new arcade game that offers a unique take on the weight loss journey. If the prospect of shedding pounds feels daunting, this game provides a fun and motivating alternative. With its addictive gameplay and appealing 3D graphics, Body Race captivates players while encouraging healthy habits.

Throughout the game, you will take control of a character who is continuously navigating a vibrant platform. Your objective is to manage your character's energy intake by consuming different types of food. Each level presents its own challenges and requires a specific caloric goal to be met. For instance, in the initial level, you must consume a total of 10,000 calories. As you progress, you will discover how many high-protein and energy-rich foods you should gather to satisfy the requirements of each level.

With its unique blend of fun and education, Body Race aims to inspire players to think critically about nutrition while enhancing their gaming skills. The vibrant graphics and engaging mechanics create a gaming experience that keeps you hooked, making each calorie consumed feel like a victory. How far can you go in this exciting adventure?

How to play free Body Race game online

To play Body Race, simply control your character as they walk along the platform using on-screen buttons or swipe gestures. Make strategic choices about what food to collect to meet the level s calorie requirements. Successfully gather the right nutrients and advance through increasingly challenging levels!
