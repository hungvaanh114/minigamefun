Noob vs Pro Chicken

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating showdown in the vibrant world of Noob vs Pro Chicken! This exciting adventure challenges you and your friend to see who can capture the most chickens in the shortest amount of time. With one player taking the role of the NOOB and the other the PRO, the fun begins as you race against each other. Your mission is simple: gather as many chickens as possible and bring them back to the coop before your friend does!

However, don t be fooled these chickens are incredibly fast and crafty! You will need to be quick on your feet and develop clever strategies to outsmart them. The competition heats up as you both scramble through the colorful landscape, dodging obstacles and finding the best routes to snatch those slippery chickens. The thrill of the chase is palpable, and every second counts!

Formulate your tactics wisely; collect all the chickens in your path while keeping an eye on the competition. Will the NOOB s slower pace be enough to outwit the PRO, or will the PRO s experience lead to a swift victory? Gather your friends, test your skills, and experience the laughter, excitement, and friendly rivalry that this game brings!

How to play free Noob vs Pro Chicken game online

To play, simply choose your character type NOOB or PRO and enter the vibrant game world. Use your keyboard or touch controls to navigate and catch chickens scattered throughout the map. Once you have collected your chickens, race back to the coop to score points. Time your moves carefully to outpace your opponent!


Instructions

Move using the WASD keys and the arrow keys The first player to collect 10 chickens wins The player who collects the most chickens before time runs out wins Mobile controls available
