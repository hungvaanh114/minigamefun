Step into the glamorous world of fashion and beauty with the exciting game, Elegant Style Makeover. Join the fabulous trio - Mia, Ava, and Clara - as they prepare for a luxurious party that awaits them. These best friends are in dire need of a stunning makeover to dazzle everyone at the event. Your mission is to assist them on their stylish journey by taking them to the salon for a complete transformation.

At the salon, you will have the opportunity to explore various beauty treatments, including hair styling, spa treatments, and exquisite makeup applications. Choose from an array of glamorous hairstyles and trendy makeup looks that will enhance their natural beauty. Once the beauty treatments are complete, it s time to select elegant gowns that will leave everyone breathless at the party. Mix and match colors, styles, and accessories to ensure that each girl looks their absolute best.

If you ve ever had dreams of becoming a stylist or a fashionista, this game is perfect for you. With its vibrant visuals and engaging gameplay, 7sgames.com offers a fantastic platform to unleash your creativity. Get ready to dive into a world of beauty, fashion, and friendship as you help Mia, Ava, and Clara shine at their special event!

How to play free Elegant Style Makeover game online

To play the game, simply navigate through the various sections of the salon, where you can choose hairstyles, apply makeup, and pick dresses for Mia, Ava, and Clara.
