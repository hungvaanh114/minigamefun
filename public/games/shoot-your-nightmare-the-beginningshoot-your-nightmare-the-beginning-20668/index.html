Experience a harrowing journey in "Shoot Your Nightmare: The Beginning." Step into the shoes of a war hero struggling with the tumultuous aftermath of combat. After returning home in May 2013, the horrors of the battlefield haunt your mind, manifesting as relentless nightmares. These flashbacks are vivid and suffocating, pushing you into a dark realm where you fight for control over your psyche. Your mission? Confront those horrors head-on by collecting 12 clocks that symbolize your fleeting grip on reality. Each clock you gather brings you one step closer to liberation from your tormented dreams.

In this gripping psychological shooter, danger lurks at every corner. As you traverse through fragmented memories, you ll face twisted apparitions and nightmarish foes that embody your deepest fears. Armed and determined, you ll shoot anything that stands between you and your goal. It s a relentless battle against your own demons, as each enemy you dispatch is a piece of your past you re finally able to conquer.

The stakes are high to emerge victorious, you must master your fears and eliminate the nightmarish creatures that haunt you. With each clock collected, you edge closer to waking from this torturous slumber. Can you endure the onslaught and ultimately reclaim your sense of reality? It's not just a fight for survival; it's a desperate attempt to awaken from the nightmare that ensnares your soul.

How to play free Shoot Your Nightmare: The Beginning game online

To play "Shoot Your Nightmare: The Beginning," navigate through the nightmarish environment using the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Use your mouse to aim and shoot at enemies that come your way. Collect the 12 clocks scattered throughout the levels to progress and unlock the path to waking up from your nightmares. Stay vigilant and manage your ammo wisely!
