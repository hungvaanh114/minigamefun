Kick the Buddy Jigsaw is a captivating online puzzle game that invites players to engage in a delightful jigsaw experience. This free-to-play game features a collection of 12 vibrant images related to the beloved character, Buddy. To progress through the game, you must solve the jigsaw puzzles sequentially, starting from the initial image and unlocking new ones as you complete each challenge.

Players can choose from three distinct modes for each image: Easy mode, which consists of 25 pieces, Medium mode with 49 pieces, and Hard mode featuring 100 pieces. This flexibility ensures that both novice players and experienced puzzle enthusiasts can enjoy the game at their preferred difficulty level. The stunning graphics combined with the engaging gameplay make Kick the Buddy Jigsaw a delightful way to unwind and enjoy some puzzling fun.

With its straightforward mechanics and intuitive controls, players can easily drag and drop pieces into place, gradually revealing each picture. As you proceed, you ll find that not only does the game challenge your puzzle-solving skills, but it also offers an entertaining way to pass the time. Whether you're looking to relax or sharpen your concentration, Kick the Buddy Jigsaw provides an enjoyable experience for players of all ages.

How to play free Kick the Buddy Jigsaw game online

To play Kick the Buddy Jigsaw, simply select one of the images from the main menu. Choose your preferred difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard. After that, use your mouse or touch controls to drag and drop the puzzle pieces into their correct positions. Complete the image to unlock the next one!
