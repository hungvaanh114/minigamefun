Obby Barry Prison Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in Obby Barry Prison Run, where your mission is to navigate through a daunting prison obstacle course. In this action-packed adventure, your ultimate goal is to reach the finish line and board the helicopter to flee from captivity. To ensure your escape is successful, you must gather all the gasoline cans scattered throughout the prison.

As you make your way through the complex, be on high alert! A vigilant police officer is on the prowl, searching for you at every turn. Stealth and strategy are crucial as you maneuver through each carefully designed obstacle. Plan your route wisely, avoid detection, and collect those essential gasoline cans to fuel your escape. With each can you collect, you inch closer to freedom.

The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay will keep you on your toes, providing a thrilling experience as you work your way through challenging jumps, tricky platforms, and other hazardous environments. Feel the adrenaline rush as you sprint towards the helicopter, ensuring you secure your escape from the relentless pursuit of the law.

How to play free Obby Barry Prison Run game online

To play Obby Barry Prison Run, simply use your keyboard to control your character's movements. Navigate through the prison by jumping over obstacles and avoiding the police officer. Collect the gasoline cans by running into them, and keep running towards the helicopter for your escape. Good luck!
