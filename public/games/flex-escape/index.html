Flex Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Flex Escape 3D is a fun game where you stretch and flex to avoid obstacles. Instead of running, you stay on a mat and use your flexibility to dodge furniture and appliances thrown at you. By moving left or right with simple controls, you can navigate through the challenges. Collecting gems helps unlock new objects, making the game even more exciting. The goal is to make it to the end without hitting any obstacles while staying focused and flexible.

Instructions
