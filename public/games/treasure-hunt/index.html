Treasure Hunt

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Treasure Hunt is an exhilarating action shooting game that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. In this game, you are faced with an onslaught of gem blocks that are relentlessly charging toward your base. Your mission? To eliminate these blocks before they reach you! The gameplay revolves around shooting blocks to match and destroy them, creating a dynamic and engaging experience.

Your survival hinges on your ability to recognize patterns and react swiftly. You can take down a row of gem blocks by shooting your own block at any identical block in that row. As you successfully eliminate blocks, you not only clear the path toward your base but also accumulate points. The longer you manage to fend off the advancing blocks, the more points you earn, escalating the challenge as the game progresses.

As the intensity of the game escalates, players are encouraged to develop quick thinking and coordination skills. The visual appeal of vibrant gem blocks adds an exciting layer to the gameplay, making each session a colorful adventure. Can you hold off the relentless army of blocks and achieve a high score? The competitive spirit will have you coming back for more every time! Get ready to put your skills to the test in this thrilling block-elimination experience.

How to play free Treasure Hunt game online

To play Treasure Hunt, start by using your mouse or touch controls to aim your block. Shoot at the corresponding block in any row to eliminate it. Keep track of the advancing gem blocks and strategically plan your shots while aiming for longer combos to maximize your score. Enjoy the challenge!
