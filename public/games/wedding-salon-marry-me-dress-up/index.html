Wedding Salon Marry Me Dress Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of "Wedding Salon: Marry Me Dress Up," where you can unleash your creativity as you prepare a bride for her dream wedding day. In this engaging game, you get to dress up a stunning bride, ensuring she looks absolutely breathtaking as she walks down the aisle. Choose from a wide variety of design options for her hair, makeup, and wedding gown to truly capture her unique style and personality.

But the fun doesn t stop there! You can also create the perfect look for the groom. Select from a fashionable array of suits and hairstyles to ensure the groom complements his beautiful bride. With over 400 fashionable items available in the game, there are countless combinations to explore, allowing for endless customization and creativity to reflect that perfect couple vibe.

The charming bride is anxious to flaunt her elegance on her big day. With your expert styling techniques, she will shine both inside and out. Help her realize her dream of being a blissful bride by dressing her up from head to toe. Once the magical looks are complete, capture the picturesque moment by taking a stunning wedding photograph of the couple, preserving their love forever.

How to play free Wedding Salon: Marry Me Dress Up game online

To play the game, use your mouse or touchscreen to pick and drag clothing items and accessories onto the bride and groom. Explore the extensive wardrobe options, mix and match to create the ideal outfits, and customize their hair and makeup. After dressing them up, click on the camera icon to take your beautiful wedding photo!
