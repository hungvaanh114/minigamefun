Link Line

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Link Line, an engaging puzzle game that will test your problem-solving skills and strategic thinking. As you navigate through a vibrant matrix of squares, you will encounter various levels of complexity ranging from 5x5 up to 10x10 grids. Each level poses a unique challenge, allowing players to select a difficulty that best suits their experience.

Your goal in Link Line is straightforward yet captivating: connect pairs of dots of the same color by drawing lines between them. However, the mission becomes more intricate as you progress. To complete each level, you must fulfill three essential conditions: ensure that all same-colored dots are paired, avoid any intersections between drawn lines, and fill every square in the matrix with your lines. As you ascend through the levels, the game introduces new colors, increasing the challenge and keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

With thousands of levels awaiting your skill, Link Line promises endless fun and mental stimulation that will keep you coming back for more. Test your intellect and enjoy the satisfaction of linking colors in a beautifully crafted game that grows in complexity and offers numerous hours of entertainment.

How to play free Link Line game online

To play Link Line, click on any two dots of the same color within the matrix. Draw a line connecting them while making sure it doesn't intersect with other lines you've already drawn. Keep connecting all pairs of same-colored dots until you've filled the entire matrix. Enjoy the challenge!
