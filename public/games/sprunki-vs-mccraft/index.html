Sprunki vs MCCraft

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Sprunki team has fallen into the McCraft world, and they want to escape and return to their own world. Help the Sprunki siblings and make sure they escape this world. Dont forget, the McCraft world is very dangerous, and there are many powerful monsters here that want to eat them. To defeat the monsters, throw music notes at them. Defeat all the monsters and reach the portal at the end of the game.

Instructions

Use the WASD and Arrow keys to move Defeat all the monsters and reach the portal safely Collect all the music notes in the forest Guide the Sprunki siblings to the end of the level safely
