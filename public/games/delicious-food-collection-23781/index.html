Delicious Food Collection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Delicious Food Collection, an engaging HTML5 game where your culinary skills are put to the test! In this delectable adventure, your goal is to gather various mouthwatering food items scattered across the grid. To kick off the game, simply tap or click on any food item you fancy. This will signal the start of your food-collecting quest!

Once you've begun, navigate your mouse or finger tip to connect identical food items that are adjacent to each other. You can link them horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally! To make a successful match, ensure you select at least three items before releasing the mouse button. The more food you collect, the sweeter the rewards! Every sixth food you unite grants a bonus, while connecting more than seven items earns you precious time bonuses that can enhance your gameplay.

As you dive into the game, keep a close eye on the timer. You must collect the requested food items within the allocated time frame; otherwise, you'll face the risk of losing the game. Challenge yourself to see how far you can go: what s the maximum level you can conquer in this deliciously addictive experience? Get ready to savor the fun!

How to play free Delicious Food Collection game online

To play Delicious Food Collection, begin by tapping on any food item. Use your mouse or fingertip to connect at least three identical food items that are adjacent to each other. Release to make the match. Keep collecting within the time limit to avoid losing!
