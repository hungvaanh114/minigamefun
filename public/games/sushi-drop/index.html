Sushi Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Sushi Drop, an engaging puzzle game where you embark on an exciting journey to save sushi! The Sushi Brothers are on a quest to fulfill an age-old rite of passage required for all young sushi. These adorable characters must master the art of landing gracefully on plates attended by delighted customers at the hibachi.

In Sushi Drop, players are challenged to navigate through a series of intricate obstacles and tricky circumstances. As the Sushi Brothers drop, your objective is to ensure they land safely while overcoming the most demanding scenarios. Each level introduces new hurdles, pushing your spatial awareness and critical thinking skills to the limit. The fun is amplified with vibrant graphics and captivating sound effects that add to the immersive experience.

This puzzle game not only tests your reflexes but also encourages strategic planning as you calculate the best angles and timing for your drops. With each successful landing, the satisfaction of saving sushi enhances the joy of the gaming experience. Dive into thrilling gameplay that rises above the ordinary and keeps you coming back for more!

How to play free Sushi Drop game online

To play Sushi Drop, simply launch the game and use your mouse or finger to aim and drop the Sushi Brothers onto the plates below. Adjust your drop timing to overcome obstacles and ensure a safe landing. Collect bonuses and complete each level for higher scores!
