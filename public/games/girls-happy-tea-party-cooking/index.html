Girls Happy Tea Party Cooking

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the delightful world of Girls Happy Tea Party Cooking, Sofia is longing to reconnect with her friends. To bridge the gap between them, she decides to organize a charming garden tea party. This is not just any gathering; it s an opportunity to whip up scrumptious treats and create lasting memories.

Your delightful mission is to assist Sofia in preparing delectable cupcakes and a vibrant fruit platter that will tantalize her friends' taste buds. Roll up your sleeves and get ready to mix batter, bake fluffy cupcakes, and artfully arrange colorful fruits. The kitchen is your playground, and Sofia relies on your culinary expertise!

But a tea party isn t complete without the perfect attire! After the cooking excitement, you will help Sofia in selecting elegant outfits and stylish accessories to ensure she looks fabulous for her special gathering. Browse through a wonderful collection of dresses, hats, and more each choice will add charm and create a festive atmosphere for the tea party.

It's a vibrant experience filled with nostalgia, creativity, and fun! Embrace your inner chef and fashionista as you embark on this enchanting journey with Sofia and her friends.

How to play free Girls Happy Tea Party Cooking game online

To play the game, simply follow the on-screen instructions to mix ingredients and bake treats. Once the food is prepared, explore the wardrobe options to select Sofia's outfit. Click on different items to dress her up in beautiful clothes and accessories, ensuring she is ready for the best tea party ever!
