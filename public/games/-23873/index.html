Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Memory Booster Animals is an engaging and stimulating memory game that will challenge your cognitive skills in a fun way. The game consists of two exciting phases that test both your memory and your ability to strategize. In the initial phase, you start by selecting a set number of random cards. As you flip over each card, you'll unveil a delightful picture of an animal, and your task is to remember not only the animal itself but also the exact sequence in which you selected the cards.

Once all the cards have been revealed and shuffled, it s time to put your memory to the test in the second phase. You ll need to select the cards in the precise order you initially chose them. This engaging gameplay keeps you on your toes and encourages you to hone your memory skills as you progress through various levels. As you advance, hints and lives are available to aid your progress, but be mindful: using fewer hints and making fewer mistakes will earn you bonus points!

With each level presenting new animals and increased complexity, Memory Booster Animals is not just a game it's a delightful way to stimulate your brain while enjoying the charming animal illustrations. Whether you're playing solo or competing with friends, this thrilling memory challenge promises hours of entertainment and mental exercise.

How to play free Memory Booster Animals game online

To play Memory Booster Animals, start by selecting a specific number of cards to flip. After revealing the animals, remember their order. Once the cards are shuffled, select them back in the original order. Use hints wisely to maximize your score and improve your memory skills as you advance through levels!
