Galaxy and Stone

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Galaxy and Stone is an exhilarating arcade game that draws inspiration from classic favorites like Pong and Breakout. The objective is simple yet challenging: keep the stone in play on the screen for as long as you can. In this immersive experience, three sides of the screen are enclosed, while your platform resides on the right side, ready to react!

As the game begins, the stone springs to life and begins to bounce around the screen. Each time it collides with a wall, it will change direction, and it s your responsibility to intercept it with your platform. Your goal is to prevent the stone from escaping the confines of the screen. The longer you keep the stone in play, the higher your score will climb! However, be warned: this game requires focus and quick reflexes, making it a thrilling challenge for players of all skill levels.

Galaxy and Stone offers a unique blend of strategy and skill, as you must anticipate the stone s movements and position your platform accordingly. With an engaging gameplay loop, it will keep you on your toes and coming back for more! Are you ready to test your agility and timing? Dive into this addictive arcade experience where every second counts!

How to play free Galaxy and Stone game online

To play the game, simply move your platform up and down using your mouse or keyboard arrows. Position your platform to intercept the bouncing stone and keep it within the screen's confines. The goal is to hit the stone back towards the walls and rack up points based on how long you can maintain play!
