Skibidi Basket

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Skibidi Basket takes the fun of basketball to the next level with its unique twist. This engaging game revolves around a Skibidi Toilet ball, and it combines addictive gameplay with realistic physics dynamics. Utilize your skills by tapping and sliding the ball, adjusting both the angle and the power of your shot as you aim for the hoop.

The visuals are striking, and the physics effects enhance your shooting experience, allowing you to precisely see your shots in action. Skibidi Basket is designed as an endless arcade game where the challenge intensifies with each level you conquer. Each time you shoot, you'll encounter a hoop with one of four randomized movements, which keeps the gameplay fresh and exhilarating.

However, be mindful that you only get three shots for each level, so precision and strategy are critical if you want to secure your place on the leaderboard. Can you score the highest and prove your skills against players around the world? Test your abilities, improve your aim, and aim to become the top player!

How to play free Skibidi Basket game online

To play Skibidi Basket, simply touch the screen to start. Drag the Skibidi Toilet ball to adjust its power and angle, then release to take your shot at the hoop. Remember to make every shot count, as you only have three attempts to complete each level!
