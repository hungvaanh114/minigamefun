Funny Shortcut Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you tired of the same old running races? If so, then Funny Shortcut Race 3D is the perfect game for you! In this thrilling and hilarious competition, you don t just run you innovate! Gather various items around the track like bricks, wooden planks, and decks, and unleash your creativity by stacking them into impressive piles.

The twist? You can shortcut across the water! By constructing a bridge from the materials you collect, you ll bypass your rivals and zoom ahead on the main racecourse. However, be cautious! If your stack isn t tall enough to bridge the gap, you risk falling into the water, costing you precious time. This engaging gameplay will challenge you to strategize and think on your feet, ensuring that only the most cunning racers will emerge as champions. Remember, the title of champion can only belong to one player, so every decision counts!

As you race to victory, keep an eye on your inventory and make sure you have enough materials to finish the map. Plan your shortcuts wisely and build efficiently to stay ahead of the competition. Will you conquer the hilarious obstacles and claim the title of the ultimate racer? Only time will tell, as the race unfolds!

How to play free Funny Shortcut Race 3D game online

To play Funny Shortcut Race 3D, start by collecting bricks and materials scattered along the track. Use these resources to build a bridge across the water. Stack them strategically to ensure you can connect the racecourse points. Move quickly to outpace your opponents, and make sure to complete the map with enough decking!
