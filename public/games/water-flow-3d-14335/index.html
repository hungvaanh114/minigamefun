How to play free Water Flow 3D game online

To play Water Flow 3D, simply tap on the pins to remove them, allowing the colored liquid to flow into the cups below. Each level presents new challenges and obstacles, so think strategically about the order in which you pull the pins. The goal is to fill the cups accurately and solve the puzzles using your logical reasoning skills!
