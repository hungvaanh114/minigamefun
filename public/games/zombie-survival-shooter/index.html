Zombie Survival Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-pumping experience with Zombie Survival Shooter, where the fate of your city rests on your shoulders! In this thrilling game, you'll face relentless waves of zombies and ferocious zombie dogs in a modern urban environment. Your mission is clear: eliminate all zombies and restore peace to your city!

Challenge yourself with more than five brand new zombie missions that will test your survival skills and shooting prowess. Equip yourself with an arsenal of eight powerful weapons, including a Pistol, 9mm Silencer, M16A4, Shotgun, Machine Gun, A400 Shotgun, MP44, and Hand Grenades. With options for armor and grenades at your disposal, you can strategize your attacks and defend against the undead effectively.

Experience gripping gameplay with unique city maps designed for realistic settings that enhance the intensity of your battles. The game features easy-to-learn gameplay mechanics, making it accessible for players of all skill levels. This means anyone can jump into the action and start fighting the zombie hordes without a steep learning curve!

How to play free Zombie Survival Shooter game online

To start playing Zombie Survival Shooter, simply choose your mission and select your preferred weapons. Move your character around using the joystick, and use your aim controls to target incoming zombies. Tap the shoot button to fire your weapons, and make sure to keep an eye on your health and ammo. Survival depends on your quick reflexes and strategic planning!
