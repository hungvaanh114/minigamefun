Iron Snout - Fighting Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Iron Snout, the electrifying online fighting game where you step into the shoes of a courageous pig fighting against an army of wolves! Strap in for non-stop action as you unleash a barrage of punches, kicks, and flips to take down wave after wave of these cunning foes. Armed with your hooves and unbeatable spirit, you ll have to dodge axes and other projectiles using the arrow keys, showcasing your agility and quick reflexes.

In Iron Snout, it's not just about survival; it's about teaching those wolves a lesson! Dive into the thrilling combat and demonstrate that pigs can be fierce fighters when the stakes are high. Whether you prefer to go solo or team up with a buddy, this game has got you covered. You can easily switch between two exhilarating modes: tackle the wolves alone in Classic mode or amp up the excitement in Sudden Death mode. For those looking for an even split of fun, the 2-player Wolfieball mode allows you to engage in cooperative gameplay and bash wolves side by side with a friend all for free!

How to play free Iron Snout - Fighting Game game online

Getting started with Iron Snout is a breeze. Once you're in the game, use the arrow keys to move your pig around the screen, and unleash your attacks with punch and kick buttons. Keep an eye on incoming projectiles, and remember to dodge them to stay in the fight. In 2-player mode, cooperate with your friend to take down wolves together. Enjoy the action!
