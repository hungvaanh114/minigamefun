Tenno

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Tenno, a captivating 2D platformer that puts you in the shoes of an office worker named Tenno. Your mission? Retrieve crucial documents that have been absconded by your competitors. As you navigate through intricately designed levels, you'll encounter a variety of challenges that test your reflexes and strategy.

Throughout your adventure across 8 thrilling levels, you will need to evade not only your crafty office rivals but also hazardous obstacles like spinning saws and deadly spikes. Each level presents escalating difficulty, ensuring that the gameplay remains exciting and engaging as you progress. Collecting the stolen papers requires skillful maneuvering timing your jumps and dodges just right to avoid your foes and dangers lurking around every corner.

The colorful pixel art and dynamic animations enhance the immersive gameplay experience, pulling you deeper into Tenno's quest. Will you be able to gather all the documents and make it back safely to your office door, or will the competitive atmosphere prove to be too challenging? Prepare yourself for fast-paced action, strategic planning, and a dose of nostalgia in this charming platformer.

How to play free Tenno game online

To start playing Tenno, use the arrow keys or WASD to move and jump. Your goal is to collect all the important documents scattered throughout each level while avoiding enemies and obstacles. Reach the door by the end of the level to advance to the next one. Enjoy the challenge!
