COVER ORANGE: WILD WEST

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the engaging puzzle-platform game Cover Orange: Wild West, players embark on a thrilling adventure in a rustic setting where our orange heroes need your help. Tasked with constructing a safe haven for these citrus characters, you must strategically use various objects to shield them from the impending danger of acid rain. As the storm cloud looms overhead, the challenge will be to appropriately drag and drop items to create an effective cover that keeps our protagonists safe during this wild western escapade.

Set against a backdrop of dusty trails and old-timey saloons, Cover Orange: Wild West invites players to think creatively while solving fun puzzles. With each level designed to test your problem-solving skills, your ultimate goal is to ensure that not a single orange is left out in the elements. Moreover, players are also encouraged to hunt for a hidden star in each stage, which adds an extra layer of excitement and achievement to the gameplay. Gather your family and friends for a delightful time solving these challenges together, as you enjoy the whimsical charm of the wild west theme.

Whether you're a casual player or a seasoned puzzle enthusiast, Cover Orange: Wild West promises an enjoyable experience that can be shared with loved ones. Each level you conquer brings new puzzles and surprises, keeping the fun alive and ensuring that no two games feel the same. Dive into the adventure and let your creativity shine as you protect those adorable orange characters from the wild western weather!

How to play free COVER ORANGE: WILD WEST game online

To play, simply drag the objects available on the screen and drop them above the orange characters to create a protective shelter. Your goal is to ensure that the oranges are safe from the acid rain as the storm cloud passes. Pay attention to each level s specific layout and hidden star for added challenges!
