Misland Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Misland Online is an engaging placement and management game that invites players to transform a deserted island into a vibrant, thriving paradise. Initially, you'll begin your adventure by foraging for apples using nothing but your bare hands. As you progress, you'll have the opportunity to enhance your skills and upgrade your tools to become more efficient in gathering resources.

This island is brimming with untapped resources waiting for you to uncover. By trading these resources with passing ships, you can accumulate wealth that will enable you to expand your island. With enough resources at your disposal, you'll be able to recruit a team of skilled assistants to help you tackle various tasks, including apple harvesting, tree felling, and rock quarrying.

However, you must remain vigilant, as invading monsters lurk nearby, threatening to steal your hard-earned resources. You'll need to wield your sword and defend your beloved island from these foes. Are you prepared to take on the challenge of overseeing this transformation and turn your deserted island into a flourishing community?

How to play free Misland Online game

To play Misland Online, start by gathering apples and exploring your island's resources. Use the menus to upgrade your tools and skills, hire assistants for various tasks, and open trade routes with ships. Keep an eye on invading monsters, and defend your island with your sword when necessary!
