Moto Racer is an exhilarating racing experience that invites players to compete against AI opponents in high-speed motorcycle races. Choose from a diverse selection of bikes, each offering unique performance attributes. Customize and enhance your ride using various upgrades available in the garage accessible from the main menu. Collect coins scattered throughout the levels to boost your score and unlock additional features.

How to play free Moto Racer game online

To play Moto Racer, simply choose your motorcycle from the selection available and upgrade it in the garage. Once the race begins, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate and accelerate. Collect coins during the race and aim to finish first to win and unlock more upgrades!
