Lipuzz-Water Sort Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to challenge your mind and have endless fun with the Lipuzz-Water Sort Puzzle! This engaging game is designed to enhance your memory while keeping you entertained for hours. Your task is to sort and collect liquids of the same color into designated containers. The gameplay revolves around strategic thinking as you transfer liquids between containers.

The rules are simple yet exciting: you can only pour liquid into a container that is either empty or contains the same color liquid as the one you wish to transfer. This requires careful planning and consideration of your moves. If you find yourself stuck on a particular level and unable to proceed, don't worry! You have the option to access additional containers from the top menu to help you on your journey.

This puzzle game is not only stimulating for your brain but also offers a relaxing experience as you work through each level. With each completed challenge, you'll feel a sense of accomplishment, making it a perfect game for those who love to solve puzzles and enjoy a bit of friendly competition.

How to play free Lipuzz-Water Sort Puzzle game online

To play Lipuzz, simply tap on a container to pour its liquid into another container, adhering to the rules of empty or matching colors. Try to sort all liquids into containers with the same color while minimizing the number of moves you make. Enjoy the challenge and have fun sorting!
