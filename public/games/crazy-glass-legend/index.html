Crazy Glass Legend

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Crazy Glass Legend, a brand-new puzzle adventure that invites you to embark on a quest to quench the thirst of a melancholic crow. The crow is feeling down due to its thirst, and it's your mission to restore its happiness. Your challenge is to creatively draw lines to guide water into the glass and, ultimately, bring a smile back to the little bird's face.

In this engaging puzzle game, you are tasked with discovering the most ingenious methods to fill each glass with water. Immerse yourself in the fun of crafting unique solutions, as there are countless ways to approach each level! Don t hesitate to think outside the box; creativity is key to unlocking new challenges and achieving high scores. Celebrate the spirit of the Diwali holiday while playing and enjoy the festive mood as you tackle these captivating puzzles.

Every level is a blend of simplicity and smart design, ideal for players seeking a relaxing yet stimulating experience. While some challenges might appear straightforward, aiming for a perfect score of three stars will truly put your skills to the test. The game boasts numerous levels, with more being added regularly, ensuring endless fun. The delightful theme and engaging gameplay will keep you entertained for hours on end.

How to play free Crazy Glass Legend game online

To play Crazy Glass Legend, open the game and use your finger or mouse to draw lines that guide the water from the tap into the glass. Observe the layout and obstacles in each level carefully, and aim to fill the glass to activate the star rating system. Experiment with different line shapes to find the best solutions!
