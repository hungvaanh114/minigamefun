Rolling Halloween

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rolling Halloween is an engaging and addictive running game that will keep you on your toes. In this spooky adventure, players must navigate through a haunted landscape filled with various obstacles like monster trees and ominous headstones. The goal is to roll as far as possible while avoiding these dangers and collecting magical skulls along the way.

As you progress through the game, you'll have the chance to gather magic skulls that can be used to upgrade your power-up items. Unlock a variety of Halloween-themed balls that not only enhance your experience but also give you unique abilities in this thrilling game. The power-ups such as shields, magnets, and magical wings provide excellent advantages, helping you evade threatening monsters and extend your run. The quicker you react and strategize, the better your chances of survival in this spine-chilling race!

Are you ready to face the challenges and see how far you can roll? Experience the exhilaration and fun by diving into Rolling Halloween today. With vibrant 3D graphics and support for multiple languages, including English and Russian, the game offers an immersive and exciting gaming experience for players from around the world.

How to play free Rolling Halloween game online

To start playing Rolling Halloween, simply navigate your character using the on-screen controls. Tap to jump over obstacles and swipe to dodge them. Collect magical skulls and power-ups to boost your capabilities while keeping a keen eye on your surroundings to avoid being caught by monsters. Happy rolling!


Instructions

Mobile or mouse on desktop - Swipe left or right to move - Swipe up to jump Desktop - Press left or right arrow keys to move - Press up arrow key to jump - Press Space bar to replay the game
