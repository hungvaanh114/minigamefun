Ship Mazes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on thrilling naval adventures with Ship Mazes, where your strategic prowess is put to the test as you become the ultimate guardian of the lighthouse. In a world filled with adversarial ships, your mission is clear: defend the lighthouse at all costs while engaging in electrifying sea battles. Navigate the treacherous waters adorned with sharp rocks and unpredictable currents, honing your skills to outwit and outmaneuver your foes.

As you embark on this maritime quest, combat becomes a tactical endeavor. Utilize the environment to your advantage, expertly avoiding natural obstacles while launching cunning attacks against enemy fleets. The stakes are high, and each battle could be your last unless you sink your adversaries. Gather powerful bonuses that drop during combat to enhance your ship, granting you the upper hand in this naval showdown and allowing you to become an indomitable force on the waves.

You can choose to take on this challenge solo or team up with a friend for cooperative gameplay. Coordinate your tactics, strategize together, and become an unstoppable duo as you work to obliterate any threat that approaches the lighthouse. The excitement is palpable, and the sense of camaraderie makes every victory even sweeter. Are you ready to accept the call of the sea and defend your lighthouse against the tide of enemies?

How to play free Ship Mazes game online

To play Ship Mazes, use your mouse to navigate your ship through the water while avoiding obstacles and engaging in combat with enemy vessels. Collect bonuses by sinking opponents to upgrade your ship s capabilities. Use strategic thinking and quick reflexes to protect the lighthouse and emerge victorious!


Instructions

Player 1 - move using the W A S D keys or the green buttons on the screen Shooting with the Spacebar Player 2 - Move using the arrow keys or blue buttons on the screen Shooting with the Num 0 key
