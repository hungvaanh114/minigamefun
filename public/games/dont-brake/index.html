Dont Brake

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the adrenaline rush of high-octane racing with "Dont Brake," where racers have turned the mundane act of driving into a thrilling and chaotic competition. The thrill of racing championships has evolved from traditional tracks to open-world highways filled with real traffic, but that got boring too. Now, an exciting new format has emerged: Brake to Die.

In this over-the-top road rage game, players take control of a car rigged with a bomb. The principal objective is deceptively simple survive! But there s a catch: if you slow down too much, the bomb will detonate. Excite your inner daredevil as you crash into other vehicles, smash through barriers, and obliterate the environment around you. Every reckless move contributes to your score, allowing you to rack up points and coins that can help you upgrade your vehicular arsenal.

Whether you choose to unleash chaos on the roads or drive with meticulous caution while navigating through obstacles, the gameplay is relentless. Speeding down the highway while keeping an eye on the ticking bomb creates a pulse-pounding experience, making each decision critical. Will you go for maximum destruction or play the long game? The choice is yours, but remember: in Dont Brake, stopping means failure!

How to play free Dont Brake game online

To begin your chaotic journey in "Dont Brake," use the arrow keys or your controller to steer and accelerate. Your bomb will begin a countdown once you reduce your speed; keep moving fast to prevent detonation. Aim for traffic and obstacles to score combos for points and upgrades. Always keep an eye on your speed!
