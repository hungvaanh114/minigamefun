Beenvaders

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the whimsical world of Beenvaders, you embark on a mission to save the beloved bees from the sinister clutches of the evil Floretron. This malevolent villain has unleashed a horde of dreadful flowers, aiming to eradicate the buzzing population of bees. Your task is to eliminate as many of these nefarious blooms as you can before they overpower the bee colony.

As you navigate through the vibrant landscapes filled with colorful yet dangerous flowers, be vigilant of the evil little pink flowers they are particularly nasty and pose the greatest threat to your mission. To enhance your chances of survival, grab capsules and hearts scattered throughout the game. Collecting these items will not only increase your score but also boost your lives, giving you more opportunities to thwart the Floretron s plans.

With each level, the challenge intensifies, presenting you with a variety of flower foes, each requiring different strategies to defeat. The engaging gameplay keeps you on your toes, urging you to think quickly and act decisively. Harness your inner hero and dive into the action-packed arena of Beenvaders, where every flower you eliminate brings you one step closer to saving the bees!

How to play free Beenvaders game online

To start playing Beenvaders, use your mouse or arrow keys to control your character's movement. Click or press the spacebar to shoot at the incoming flowers. Keep an eye out for capsules and hearts to collect and extend your gameplay. Stay alert to maneuver around the evil pink flowers, as they can seriously hinder your progress!
