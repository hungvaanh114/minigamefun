Pull My Tongue

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Pull My Tongue, prepare yourself for an exciting adventure that combines clever gameplay with delightful challenges! You'll embark on a mission to assist an adorable reptile in its quest for tasty gum. However, to succeed, you may need to think creatively and take some shortcuts along the way.

The game features a variety of levels filled with intricate obstacles and cunning traps designed to test your skills. You'll face numerous challenges as you navigate through each stage, requiring you to strategize and come up with innovative solutions. Use your wits to maneuver around sticky situations and ensure your cute reptile gets the treat it desires!

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter different settings and challenges that will keep you on your toes. The vibrant graphics and charming character design add to the game's appeal, making it a delightful experience for players of all ages. So gear up for a playful escapade that combines precision, strategy, and loads of fun!

How to play free Pull My Tongue game online

To play Pull My Tongue, simply click and drag your mouse to manipulate the reptile's tongue. Your goal is to navigate around obstacles and traps while grabbing the gum. Use the shortcuts wisely to help the reptile successfully reach each piece of gum in the level. Enjoy the whimsical gameplay!
