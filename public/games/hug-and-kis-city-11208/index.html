Hug and Kis City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Hug and Kis City, an exhilarating action-adventure game that will have you on your toes! In this engaging series, you and your partner must navigate through a bustling city while collecting gold coins, dodging menacing zombies, and ultimately unlocking the door to safety. Each step you take counts, as you strive to keep both characters safe while embarking on this thrilling journey.

As you explore the lively environments, be prepared to encounter a plethora of challenges. Zombies lurk at every corner, adding an element of suspense to your quest. Your mission is not only to gather as much gold as possible but also to work together intelligently to overcome obstacles in your path. Teamwork is essential; you must guide both characters to the door, ensuring that you arrive unscathed. The more gold you collect, the higher your score will be, so stay alert and strategize your movements wisely!

The adventure doesn t stop at merely reaching the door. There are various levels and surprises that await you in Hug and Kis City. Discover hidden treasures, unlock achievements, and compete with friends to see who can achieve the highest score. With each playthrough, experience a new thrill and expand your skills. Get ready to plunge into an exciting world where teamwork and adventure are at the forefront!

How to play free Hug and Kis City game online

To play Hug and Kis City, use your arrow keys to move the characters. Collect gold coins by walking over them while avoiding zombies. When you gather enough courage, find the key and unlock the door to progress to the next level. Remember, teamwork is crucial for achieving the highest score!
