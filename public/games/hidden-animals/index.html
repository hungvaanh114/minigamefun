Hidden Animals

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hidden Animals is an engaging skill-based puzzle game that invites players to embark on an exciting adventure through the depths of a mysterious forest. In this game, your primary task is to uncover the various hidden animals scattered throughout beautifully crafted environments. Each level presents a unique challenge, requiring keen observation and attention to detail to locate all the elusive creatures. With a total of eight intricately designed levels, players will face increasing difficulty as they progress, making it a true test of skill and persistence.

The enchanting visuals and immersive soundscapes make Hidden Animals not just a game, but a delightful experience that captures the essence of wildlife exploration. As you navigate through each level, you ll encounter various clues and hints that can aid in your search, enhancing the gameplay and making it even more enjoyable. Are you ready to sharpen your observational skills and see how many animals you can find? The clock is ticking, and every second counts!

Hidden Animals is perfect for players of all ages who love a good challenge and have a keen eye for detail. It promotes critical thinking and problem-solving while providing hours of entertainment. Gather your friends and family, and see who can find all the hidden animals first! Once you complete all eight levels, you can proudly declare victory and showcase your skills in this captivating adventure.

How to play free Hidden Animals game online

To play Hidden Animals, simply click on the start button and choose a level from the menu. Use your mouse or touchpad to search the forest scene for hidden animals. Once you spot one, click on it to collect it. Find all animals within the time limit to move on to the next level!
