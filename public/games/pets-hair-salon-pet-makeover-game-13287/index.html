Pets Hair Salon - Pet Makeover Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Pets Hair Salon - Pet Makeover Game, where adorable pets gather to transform their looks and unleash their inner stars! In this delightful game, you will meet a variety of lovable animals including bears, cats, dogs, and rabbits, all eager to receive their fabulous makeovers. Each pet has its own unique personality and style, and it s up to you to bring forth their best looks!

As the stylist in charge, your mission is to create fun, funky hairstyles that will make your pets stand out as movie stars, if only for a day! With an array of tools at your disposal, such as scissors, brushes, and vibrant hair color options, you ll have everything you need to craft stunning styles. Dive into a creative journey where you can combine colors, patterns, and styles to create the perfect hairstyle for each furry client.

Remember, the clock is ticking, and the pets are eagerly awaiting their transformation, so be quick and imaginative! Whether you prefer sleek and glossy, wild and spunky, or cute and cuddly styles, your options are endless. Get ready to pamper these adorable companions and give them looks that will make everyone go wow!

How to play free Pets Hair Salon - Pet Makeover Game online

To start playing the game, choose a pet from the selection available on-screen. Once you have your furry client, use the various grooming tools and accessories to style their hair. Click and drag to cut, color, and accessorize, and remember to check the timer as you work to ensure each pet gets the superstar treatment before time runs out!
