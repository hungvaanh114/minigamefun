Pic Pie Puzzles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pic Pie Puzzles is an engaging photo puzzle game that invites players to challenge their problem-solving skills and creativity. The game presents you with a round photo that has been divided into several pie-shaped segments. Your objective is to piece together the beautiful image by swapping these pie segments until the complete picture is revealed.

Each level offers a unique photo, providing varied landscapes, beautiful portraits, or intriguing still life images, making every puzzle a delightful experience. The game's intuitive mechanics allow players of all ages to enjoy it easily. Simply swipe your mouse or finger across two adjacent pie pieces to swap their positions. This simple action paves the way for strategic planning as you work to align the segments correctly.

As you progress through the levels, the complexity may increase, providing an exciting challenge for even the most seasoned puzzle enthusiasts. Pic Pie Puzzles also develops cognitive skills such as spatial awareness and memory, making it not only entertaining but also educational. Immerse yourself in this captivating game that merges enjoyment with mental stimulation, and enjoy hours of puzzling fun!

How to play free Pic Pie Puzzles game online

To play the free Pic Pie Puzzles game online, simply click on the pie pieces you wish to swap adjacent to one another. Keep swapping until the entire photo is correctly assembled. Use your logic and spatial reasoning to complete each level, and enjoy the satisfaction of revealing stunning images!
