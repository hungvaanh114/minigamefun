Welcome to the delightful world of the Ice Cream Chocolate Doll Cake Maker 2020, where you can indulge in the art of cake making alongside a charming doll bakery boss! This adorable doll has recently launched her vibrant bakery in the heart of the city, offering an exquisite range of cakes perfect for wedding parties and birthday celebrations. With an array of flavors like strawberry, vanilla, pineapple, chocolate, and caramel apple, this bakery shop is sure to satisfy every sweet tooth!

Are you a fan of chocolate cake? You're in for a treat! The cake boss at this bakery offers everything from classic chocolate cakes to unique versions like moist fluffy chocolate, eggless, milkiness, gluten-free, and vegan options. Each cake starts with sifting dry ingredients, then carefully mixing in the wet components before pouring the batter into a greased pan. Bake to perfection, cool, and decorate to create a delightful masterpiece!

In addition to chocolate, explore other mouthwatering creations including a charming vanilla pound cake, perfect for special occasions. Gather your ingredients, cream the butter and sugar, add in the eggs and vanilla, and pour your mix into a pan. Let your culinary skills shine making caramel apple cakes that blend the sweetness of apples with the richness of cake. Special occasions such as anniversaries, weddings, and holidays are made even more festive with these delicious desserts!

How to play free Ice Cream Chocolate Doll Cake Maker 2020 game online

To play, start by selecting your favorite cake recipe from the bakery menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to collect ingredients, mix them, and bake your cake. Once baked, use the game s decorating tools to create a beautiful cake for various celebrations. Enjoy your sweet creation!
