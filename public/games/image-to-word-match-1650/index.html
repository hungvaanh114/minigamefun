Image to Word Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the charming world of Image to Word Match, where fun meets learning! This engaging word matching game invites you to match pictures with their corresponding words. Ideal for players of all ages, it's a fantastic way to enhance vocabulary and cognitive skills while having a blast!

How to play? It couldn t be simpler! Using your mouse, click on any picture and drag it over to the word you think it represents. If your choice is correct, you will be greeted with a cheerful message saying, "Well done!" However, if you miss the mark, an error message will guide you back on track. This feedback keeps the gameplay enjoyable and encourages learning through play.

The game features two thrilling modes to cater to different skill levels. In the Easy mode, you can play at a leisurely pace, free from any pressure. You have the flexibility to navigate back and forth in the game, making it a perfect choice for young learners. On the other hand, the Normal mode introduces an element of challenge. Here, you start with three lives and must match the pictures to the words accurately. A single mistake could lead to a game over, so pay close attention and sharpen your matching skills!

How to play free Image to Word Match game online

Once you enter the game, start by selecting either the Easy or Normal mode. Click on a picture to select it, then drag the image over the corresponding word to make your match. Keep an eye on your lives in Normal mode, and enjoy the feedback as you progress through each challenge!
