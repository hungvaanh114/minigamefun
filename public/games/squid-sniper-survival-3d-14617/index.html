Squid Sniper: Survival 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Squid Sniper: Survival 3D, a unique blend of traditional Korean games and intense sniper action! Your mission is to target and eliminate other players during the iconic green light-red light game. Exhibit your marksmanship as you shoot down players who fail to stop when they should, but exercise caution hitting the wrong target will cost you valuable points!

With each successful shot on the right player, you'll be rewarded with points, allowing you to climb the leaderboard and challenge friends for supremacy. Compete in various challenging locations filled with intense scenarios that will put your reflexes to the ultimate test. As a player, you will need absolute concentration and quick reflexes to secure your victory against an array of opponents.

Equipped with the formidable squid sniper gun, which requires 1,250 shark teeth to obtain, you'll enjoy its remarkable range and unique design, featuring a squid tentacle and an interesting color scheme. Despite its slow firing rate, its high damage output makes it a great choice for long-distance engagements. Press the Z key to enter expanded mode for a tactical advantage!

Featuring visually stunning 3D graphics and impressive sound effects, Squid Sniper: Survival 3D offers an engaging experience for both new and seasoned players alike. Visit the game store to check out a vast array of weapons to enhance your gameplay and take your sniper skills to the next level!

How to play free Squid Sniper: Survival 3D game online

To play Squid Sniper: Survival 3D, click to aim your sniper at players during the green light phase. As soon as the red light is announced, stop moving! Shoot carefully to hit the right players for points. Avoid shooting the wrong players to maintain your score and survive to the end!
