Kuri in Lull the Ghosts

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Kuri in Lull the Ghosts! In this enchanting physics puzzle game, you'll embark on a magical adventure with Kuri, an adorable hedgehog who has one mission: to restore sunlight and bring peace back to the realm. Unfortunately, a mysterious force has snatched the sun away, causing mischievous ghosts to roam freely and disrupt the tranquility of the land. It s up to you to assist Kuri in casting a gentle glow to lull these spirited beings into a peaceful slumber.

As you guide Kuri through various levels filled with mind-bending puzzles, you ll encounter obstacles and challenges that will test your logic and creativity. Use the game's intuitive mechanics to manipulate objects in the environment and find innovative ways to illuminate Kuri's path. Every level presents a fresh riddle, demanding keen observation and clever strategies to guide Kuri as you light up the darkness. Your ultimate goal is to create a comforting ambiance that encourages the ghosts to peacefully drift off to sleep, allowing harmony to be restored.

With vibrant graphics and engaging soundscapes, Kuri in Lull the Ghosts offers an immersive experience that will captivate players of all ages. Dive into this delightful adventure, and witness the unique interactions that unfold as you help Kuri bring the sun back to life!

How to play free Kuri in Lull the Ghosts game online

To play Kuri in Lull the Ghosts, use your mouse or touch screen to interact with the objects in each level. Move Kuri around, solve puzzles by manipulating items, and create light sources to soothe the ghosts. Your goal is to ensure Kuri shines brightly enough to send the ghosts to sleep!
