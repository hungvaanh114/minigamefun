Tunko

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tunko is an engaging 2D platformer where your primary mission is to gather all the grapes scattered throughout the levels while cleverly sidestepping various threats. As you navigate through vibrant environments, you'll encounter cunning enemies, perilous spikes, and mechanized saws that all pose a challenge. To progress to the next level, your ultimate goal is to reach the red flag at each stage.

The game comprises eight meticulously crafted levels, each designed to provide an increasingly challenging experience. As you advance, the complexity escalates, testing your agility and strategic thinking. Players must develop their skills and learn the best routes to collect every single grape without falling victim to the enemies lurking around every corner. Tunko's captivating design and escalating difficulty ensure that players remain engaged and entertained throughout their journey.

The graphics present a charming aesthetic that complements the gameplay, contributing to a delightful gaming experience. With each level, players will appreciate the whimsical nature of the game's world, motivating them to explore every nook and cranny to gather those elusive grapes. Mastering the controls and timing your jumps aptly will be crucial in overcoming the numerous obstacles ahead.

How to play free Tunko game online

To play Tunko, use the arrow keys to move your character left and right, and the spacebar to jump. Make sure to collect all grapes while avoiding obstacles and enemies. As you complete each level, your journey continues until you reach the final challenge!
