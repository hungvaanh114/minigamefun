Poppy Playtime Hidden Ghosts is an engaging online skill game that challenges players to locate concealed ghosts within captivating images. How to play free Poppy Playtime Hidden Ghost game online

To play Poppy Playtime Hidden Ghosts, simply start the game and choose a level. Use your mouse or touchpad to click on the images to identify and locate the hidden ghosts. Be mindful of the timer and try to complete each level before time runs out to maximize your score!
