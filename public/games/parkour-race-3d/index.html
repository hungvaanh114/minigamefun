Parkour Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of urban agility in Parkour Race 3D, an exhilarating 3D running game where you take the reins of your very own stickman. Navigate through a bustling city landscape, competing against other players as you dash through the streets and leap across rooftops. With simple controls and dynamic gameplay, you can easily immerse yourself in this high-energy racing experience.

As you speed through the vibrant environment, look out for glowing speed bumpers that boost your momentum, propelling you forward at lightning speed. Execute impressive vaults and flips to not only clear obstacles but also to show off your unique style on this adrenaline-pumping course. Challenge your reflexes and timing, testing your skills as you maneuver through complex urban settings.

Parkour Race 3D isn't just about speed; it offers a variety of features that keep the excitement alive. Participate in daily challenges that test your prowess and grant you rewards. Discover an assortment of power-ups that enhance your performance, enabling you to leap further and run faster. Personalize your stickman with stylish accessories, making yours a one-of-a-kind character in the competitive world of parkour racing.

This is more than just a game; it's an opportunity to elevate your running skills and experience the exhilarating world of parkour racing. So lace up your virtual shoes and prepare to run like never before in Parkour Race 3D!

How to play free Parkour Race 3D game online

To play Parkour Race 3D, use the on-screen controls to navigate your stickman. Press the jump button to leap across rooftops, and hit the dash button to sprint past opponents. Collect power-ups and speed boosts along the way, while performing flips and tricks to gain extra points and style points!
