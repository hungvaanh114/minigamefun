Cats and Trees

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Cats and Trees is an engaging idle game that invites players to join a group of charming cats on a fruity adventure. Your mission is to assist these cool felines in acquiring fruits that are perched high in the trees. Each cat has its own unique traits and abilities, making them essential for your journey to harvest these tasty rewards. As the cats work their magic, you will need to strategically manage their efforts to ensure a steady stream of fruits and wood.

The game boasts a variety of 14 different types of fruits, each with its own delightful graphics and characteristics. Players will experience a vibrant environment filled with colorful trees and playful cats, enhancing the overall gaming experience. To progress, you will need to knock down trees, successfully gathering fruits and wooden resources. This gameplay mechanic adds an exciting layer of strategy, as you decide how best to deploy your cats to maximize your yield.

As you collect fruits and wood, you ll have the opportunity to unlock upgrades for your cats and improve your tree-knocking capabilities. With a variety of upgrades available, the customization options allow players to develop their own unique strategies. Explore the delightful world of Cats and Trees and help your furry friends thrive in their quest for delicious fruits!

How to play free Cats and Trees game online

To play Cats and Trees, simply start by selecting your cool cats and setting them to work on knocking down trees. Click on the trees to collect fruits and wood. Use the resources you gather to upgrade your cats and enhance their capabilities. Keep tapping and managing to gather all 14 fruits!
