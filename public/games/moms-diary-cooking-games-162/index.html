How to play free Moms Diary : Cooking Games game online

To play the game, start by selecting a level and reading the order that your customers have placed. Use the on-screen controls to prepare the ingredients, cook the dishes, and serve them on time. Pay attention to customer feedback and aim for high scores by completing orders quickly and accurately!
