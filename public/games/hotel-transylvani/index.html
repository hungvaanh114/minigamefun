Hotel Transylvani

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bring your beloved characters from the enchanting world of Hotel Transylvania to life with the thrilling Hotel Transylvania Augmented Reality Experience at Motiongate, Dubai! After downloading the dedicated app, simply point your smartphone at the Augmented Reality (AR) markers strategically placed near and within the Hotel Transylvania attraction. Alternatively, you can print these markers and enjoy the fun at home!

Once you've launched the app, you'll be greeted by iconic figures like Dracula, his daughter Mavis, Jonathan, and a host of their monster friends. They will provide you with an exclusive behind-the-scenes glimpse of all the spooky yet delightful activities occurring inside the Hotel, as they prepare for its first human guest you! Get ready to laugh, dance, interact, and even strike a pose for memorable photos with these beloved characters.

To get started, simply follow these easy instructions: On the main menu, tap on Find Markers. Next, aim your device at a marker. When the app successfully recognizes the marker, it will activate an array of entertaining features, ensuring a delightful and immersive experience for you and your friends!

How to play free hotel transylvani game online

To enjoy the free Hotel Transylvania game online, navigate through the game interface using your mouse or touch controls. Select your character, and follow on-screen instructions to complete various fun-filled tasks and challenges. Collect items, solve puzzles, and interact with other characters to progress through the exciting scenarios inspired by the movie!
