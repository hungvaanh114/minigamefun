Monster Teeth

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Monster Teeth, a delightful twist on a beloved classic video game! Get ready to immerse yourself in a whimsical world where your goal is to dodge the alarming teeth of a not-so-cute monster. Engage in this fun-filled challenge filled with laughter and excitement as you strive to survive for as long as possible. The monster's teeth can come at you from various angles, so staying alert and agile is essential!

As you navigate through this vibrant environment, your reflexes will be put to the test. The longer you can evade the monster's chompers, the higher your score will climb! Compete against friends or aim to beat your personal best. With its simple yet captivating gameplay, Monster Teeth ensures hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Get ready to unleash your skills and see if you can outsmart the monster!

How to play free Monster Teeth game online

To play Monster Teeth, use the keyboard arrows to move your character around the screen. Your objective is to dodge the monster s teeth while navigating the colorful landscape. Avoid getting caught for as long as you can to accumulate points. Have fun breaking your record!
