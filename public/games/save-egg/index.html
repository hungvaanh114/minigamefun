Save Egg

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you're a fan of platform games and enjoy a touch of whimsy, then Save Egg is the perfect game for you! In this colorful adventure, players are tasked with navigating a series of platforms while delicately maintaining the balance of a giant egg that floats above the ground. Your mission is simple: jump from platform to platform and collect as many colored balloons as possible to keep the egg steady. Each balloon you gather contributes to the egg's stability, making your leaps all the more critical.

However, it s not just about collecting balloons. Throughout your journey, you ll also encounter shiny stars that not only offer extra points, enhancing your score, but beware of the bats lurking about; they ll try to snatch away your hard-earned points! The challenge lies in your ability to stay focused while maneuvering your character, as the platforms may shift and change, adding an extra layer of excitement to the game. Can you maintain the fragile balance without breaking the egg or falling into oblivion? Choose your character wisely and embark on this thrilling adventure!

How to play free Save Egg game online

To play Save Egg, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your character. Jump between the platforms, collect colored balloons for balance, and grab stars for bonus points. Avoid bats at all costs to protect your score. Keep the egg safe and see how long you can last!
