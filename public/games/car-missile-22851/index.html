CAR MISSILE

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the adrenaline-pumping world of CAR MISSILE, you find yourself at the helm of a high-speed vehicle, navigating through a bustling cityscape while evading relentless homing missiles that relentlessly pursue you. Your mission is not just to survive but to outsmart these deadly projectiles by skillfully maneuvering through the streets to find the quickest escape routes. Each corner you turn presents a new challenge, making your escape a thrilling race against time.

As you dash through the vibrant city, make sure to collect valuable power-ups that will enhance your defenses and provide additional advantages against the missile onslaught. These power-ups can range from temporary speed boosts to shields that protect your car from damage. Additionally, coin collection plays a vital role in your journey; gather coins scattered throughout the city to unlock new cars and powerful upgrades, enabling you to enhance your performance and increase your chances of outpacing the missiles.

Strategically navigating the city's layout can be the key to your survival. Learn the best routes and shortcuts to evade your pursuers while avoiding obstacles that could slow you down or lead to your demise. With each successful evasion, you are rewarded not only with the thrill of the chase but also with opportunities to improve your vehicle and tackle even tougher challenges ahead.

How to play free CAR MISSILE game online

To start playing CAR MISSILE, simply use your keyboard's arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car through the city. Collect coins along the way and grab power-ups for protection. Stay alert to the missile's movements and plan your escape route strategically to stay alive! Have fun and be quick!
