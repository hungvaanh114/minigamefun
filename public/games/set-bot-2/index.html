Set Bot 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Set Bot 2, a thrilling 2D platformer infused with a sci-fi motif. You will be tasked with navigating treacherous terrains as you control a nimble bot, aiming to collect red tech balls scattered throughout each level. These tech balls are crucial for completing stages and will test your skills as you evade relentless enemy bots, their rapid-fire bullets, and an array of deadly hazards such as flying bots, menacing spikes, and gigantic blades.

This engaging platformer is the sequel to the original "Set Bot" game, and it delivers an exhilarating experience across 8 unique levels. With each level you conquer, the challenges become increasingly difficult, pushing your reflexes and strategic thinking to the limit. Your ultimate objective is to reach the Exit door at the end of each stage. Prepare for fast-paced action and thrilling obstacles as you progress through this captivating game!

Set Bot 2 captivates players not just with its engaging gameplay but also through its beautiful graphics and immersive sound effects. Overcome the exhilarating hurdles, master your movement, and aim for a high score as you embark on this extraordinary adventure. Will you be able to collect all the tech balls and successfully navigate through all 8 levels? Dive into the vibrant universe of Set Bot 2 and discover your potential!

How to play free Set Bot 2 game online

In Set Bot 2, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your bot left, right, and jump. Collect the red tech balls while avoiding obstacles and enemy bots. Time your jumps carefully to dodge bullets and spikes, and make your way to the Exit door to complete each level. Good luck!
