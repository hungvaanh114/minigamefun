Mad Day Special?Mad Day Special

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating ride in Mad Day Special, where the fate of Earth rests on your shoulders! This action-packed racing game plunges you into a chaotic world threatened by an alien invasion. Your mission? Rescue your adorable pet octopus, Fluffy, and escape the clutches of these nefarious extraterrestrial beings!

In Mad Day Special, you get to experience a thrilling blend of high-speed racing and intense shooting gameplay. As you race against time, you'll have the opportunity to upgrade your vehicle, arsenal, and protective gear. Customize your car with powerful upgrades, enhance your weapons, and don armor and clothing that boosts your capabilities against the alien threat. With each upgrade, you ll improve your chances of defeating the alien forces and keeping Fluffy safe.

As you navigate through treacherous terrains and face oncoming alien attacks, prepare to unleash a barrage of firepower using your truck s rocket launcher. Skillfully climb hills, smash through barriers, and dodge incoming laser blasts as you make your way to victory. When things get too intense in the vehicle, don t hesitate to jump out and continue the fight on foot! Armed with a shotgun, you can unleash destruction upon the alien hordes, ensuring you won t back down until Fluffy is free.

How to play free Mad Day Special game online

To play Mad Day Special, use the arrow keys to steer your car forward and backward, while the spacebar activates your rocket launcher. Move your character with the WASD keys when you exit the vehicle. Aim with your mouse and click to shoot at the aliens, collecting power-ups to enhance your weapons and ride!
