Sushi Bar

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Sushi Bar, where your dream of managing your very own sushi restaurant comes to life! If you successfully unlock Chef SonGoku, you ll be recognized as the ultimate sushi manager. This game allows you to showcase your culinary skills and charm customers with delightful dishes that will have them returning for more.

In Sushi Bar, you'll have the opportunity to open restaurants in some of the most iconic cities across the globe. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the romantic canals of Venice, you ll be challenged to perfect your sushi-making techniques and serve delicious meals to satisfied patrons. As you grow your business, you ll encounter unique challenges and exciting events that will test your management abilities and creativity.

Your main goal is to create an enticing menu, design a beautiful restaurant setting, and provide top-notch customer service. Develop your restaurant into a world-renowned food destination by understanding what your customers want and delivering it quickly. With each successful dish you serve, you'll earn points, unlock new recipes, and gain access to even more prestigious locations. Are you ready to become a sushi legend?

How to play free Sushi Bar game online

To dive into the Sushi Bar, simply start by selecting your restaurant location. Follow the in-game tutorials to learn how to prepare various sushi recipes and serve customers efficiently. Manage your resources wisely and aim to satisfy as many patrons as possible to grow your restaurant and progress through the game.
