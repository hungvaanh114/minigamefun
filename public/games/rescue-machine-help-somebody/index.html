Rescue Machine - Help Somebody

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rescue Machine - Help Somebody is an engaging blend of arcade action and puzzle-solving that promises to keep players on their toes. In this unique game, you're not just focused on survival; it's your mission to save trapped survivors beneath heavy stones. The challenge lies in utilizing a variety of 3D mechanical components, including gears and stones, to create effective rescue solutions.

Players must connect different machines strategically to activate power operations that will lift the trapped stones, freeing the survivors. As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter various types of machines inspired by real-world physics, such as gear drives and crank-link mechanisms. This captivating gameplay experience encourages not just quick thinking but also strategic planning as you navigate through increasingly complex scenarios.

With its vibrant graphics and rewarding mechanics, Rescue Machine allows you to feel the satisfaction that comes with every successful rescue. The game seamlessly blends fun and education, making it a fantastic choice for players of all ages. Will you be able to save all the survivors and complete the levels? Dive in and find out!

How to play free Rescue Machine - Help Somebody game online

To play Rescue Machine, start by observing the trapped survivor and the stones on top of them. Use the available machinery components to create connections by clicking and dragging them into place. Once the machines are linked, activate them to lift the stones and rescue the survivor. Complete each level by successfully freeing all trapped individuals!
