Red Ball 4

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Red Ball 4, the thrilling continuation of the beloved jump ball series! In this exciting installment, the evil minions have sinister plans to transform our planet into an unrecognizable square shape. Fortunately, you have the chance to become the heroic Roller Ball, tasked with rescuing the world from this impending doom.

As you navigate through a treacherous factory filled with obstacles, your goal is to roll and leap your way past deadly challenges. Defeat menacing enemies and dodge perilous laser beams that threaten your progress. Do you possess the skills needed to thwart the villains and save the world from becoming square? Use the arrow keys to guide your Roller Ball towards the finish line, while collecting all the stars scattered throughout the levels.

Every encounter with the bad guys requires strategy: leap on them to eliminate them, but be cautious! Contact with sharp corners can lead to failure. The game promises an engaging experience, where you will develop your skills in rolling, bouncing, and conquering mechanical wastelands. Your mission revolves around gathering stars as you valiantly face off against various evil squares designed to hinder your progress. In certain zones, you'll need to navigate through moving lasers precision is key to advancing safely through each area!

How to play free Red Ball 4 game online

To dive into the action of Red Ball 4, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard. The right arrow key propels the ball forward, while the left arrow key rolls it backward. Use the up arrow key to perform jumps. Your objective is to maneuver through levels, collect stars, and avoid hazards to ensure a smooth and victorious journey!
