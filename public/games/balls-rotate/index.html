How to play free Balls Rotate game online

To play Balls Rotate 3D, simply use your mouse or touch controls to rotate the platform. Your goal is to align the platform perfectly to catch the balls as they fall, while avoiding any obstacles that may come your way. Keep rotating to maximize your score and unlock various skins!
