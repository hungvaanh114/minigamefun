Picker 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the excitement of Picker 3D, a thrilling game where your agility and strategic thinking come into play! As you navigate through vibrant environments, you'll be faced with the challenge of using your drag picker to the left or right. This intuitive control mechanism allows you to gather a wide array of objects scattered throughout the levels.

In Picker 3D, every item you collect brings you one step closer to unlocking the numerous gates that stand in your way. Whether it's classic treasures or more unique finds, it's your mission to pick everything in sight! The more you collect, the higher your scores will soar, pushing you to compete against friends or challenge your own best records. The game s simplistic yet engaging design ensures players of all ages can immerse themselves in this captivating experience.

With each level presenting new challenges and a plethora of items to gather, Picker 3D keeps the excitement alive. The visually appealing graphics create an inviting atmosphere that enhances gameplay, while dynamic sound effects immerse you even further into this addictive world. Gear up to reach astounding high scores and discover all the hidden gems the game has to offer!

How to play free Picker 3D game online

To play Picker 3D, simply drag your picker left or right to collect items as they appear on the screen. Focus on picking up everything in sight to unlock the gates ahead. Keep an eye on your score as you progress, aiming to achieve the highest score possible by collecting more items!
