Cooking Mania

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a culinary adventure with Cooking Mania, where you can indulge your passion for cooking and restaurant management. Step into the shoes of a chef and create mouthwatering dishes in your very own restaurant. With five diverse dining establishments to choose from, each offering a new challenge as you progress, the game keeps your culinary skills sharp as the levels intensify.

As you attract a growing number of customers eager to savor your delicious offerings, upgrading your kitchen equipment becomes essential. Enhance your cooking tools and speed up service to meet the demands of your loyal patrons. Cooking Mania brilliantly combines time management with culinary creativity, allowing you to experiment with flavors and menus while juggling the fast-paced environment of a bustling restaurant.

The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay capture the essence of running a restaurant, making every cooking session a delightful experience. With each successful level, unlock new recipes and dishes to keep the menu fresh and exciting, ensuring that your customers remain hungry for more. Will you rise to the challenge and build your restaurant empire?

How to play free Cooking Mania game online

To play Cooking Mania, simply select your restaurant and begin cooking delicious dishes. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the kitchen items, prepare meals, and serve them to your customers. Speed and accuracy are key to keeping your patrons satisfied and to leveling up quickly!
