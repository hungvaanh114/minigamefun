Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Ball 8 Pool, a thrilling billiards game that puts your skills to the test! In the main menu, you can select your difficulty level: easy, medium, or hard. This choice allows you to tailor the game to your previous experience and enhance your playing prowess. Once you and your opponent are at the table, the colored balls are racked, and it s time to showcase your billiards acumen.

To start, you will strike the white cue ball, aiming to hit the colored balls in a strategic manner. Your goal is to pocket as many of these balls as possible into the designated pockets around the table. Each successful shot allows you to continue your turn, giving you the chance to devise a plan for your next move. However, be cautious, as failing to pocket a ball will result in the turn passing over to your opponent. Remember, it s critical to avoid pocketing the white cue ball, as doing so can lead to an immediate disadvantage.

The game features an indicator located at the bottom left of the screen, which shows the force of your shot. This tool is essential for crafting precise and powerful hits. As you improve your skills, you'll learn to select which ball to target next strategically, allowing you to outmaneuver your rival and claim victory. Dive into the game and experience the joy of mastering billiards like never before!

How to play free Ball 8 Pool game online

To play Ball 8 Pool, use your mouse to aim and adjust the shot's power with the indicator on the screen. Click to strike the white ball, aiming for the colored balls to pocket them strategically. Keep playing until all your balls are pocketed, and ultimately sink the 8-ball to win!
