Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of Crab & Fish, a riveting skill-based game that challenges your quick thinking and dexterity. As you navigate the dynamic platform, your primary mission is to rescue trapped fish by strategically popping blocks that stand in your way. Each block you clear opens the path for you to reach the fish cage, where your aquatic friends await salvation.

But beware! Maintaining your balance is essential as the ground beneath you shifts with each move. Carefully consider your strategy with every block you pop to avoid tumbling off the platform. The more fish you free, the higher you can climb in the game's leaderboard, allowing you to compete with friends and strive for the highest scores.

With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Crab & Fish provides endless enjoyment as you set out on this underwater adventure. The thrill of releasing the fish and the challenge of staying on the platform promise to keep players of all ages hooked. They are all counting on you to help them escape their cages and return to the open sea! So grab your virtual tools and get ready to dive into this fun-filled escapade.

How to play free Crab & Fish game online

To play Crab & Fish, simply click or tap the blocks surrounding the fish cage to pop them. This will open up a way to reach the cage and free the fish. Be careful with each move you make, as you need to maintain your balance on the platform while continuing your descent!
