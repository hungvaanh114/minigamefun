Two Rows Colors

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Two Rows Colors is an engaging puzzle game designed specifically for kids, where the primary objective is to align the central ball with other balls of matching colors. At the heart of the game, the ball at the center of the screen moves vertically and frequently changes its hue, adding a dynamic twist to the gameplay.

On either side of the central ball, there are two rows containing three balls each, positioned at the top and bottom of the game screen. Players can interact with the game by tapping the screen, which prompts the balls in the rows to move horizontally. Your task is to rearrange these horizontal rows so that they align with the color of the ball in the middle. Successfully matching the colors will allow you to score points, making the game exciting and competitive.

However, precision is key in Two Rows Colors; making an incorrect move will result in the game ending. Thus, vigilance and quick thinking are important as you watch the central ball s color change and strategize your moves. Dive into the colorful puzzle world, enjoy the challenge, and have a blast while aiming for a high score!

How to play free Two Rows Colors game online

To play Two Rows Colors, start by tapping on the screen to move the rows of balls horizontally. Your goal is to match one of the side balls with the central ball s color. Keep an eye on the moving colors and make quick decisions to score points without making mistakes that could end the game!
