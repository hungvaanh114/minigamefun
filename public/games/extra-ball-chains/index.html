Extra Ball Chains

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Extra Ball Chains, a thrilling game where you step into the role of a brave little frog dedicated to defending her cozy home from a barrage of encroaching balls. Your mission is to expertly blast away these relentless spheres before they reach your domicile, ensuring the safety of your beloved space.

The game's mechanics challenge you to strategize your shots, as each ball travels along a preset path toward your frog's home. Precision and quick decision-making are crucial in this dynamic environment if even a single ball manages to break through, it could lead to loss. As you progress through multiple levels, there are abundant opportunities to increase your score by destroying balls and chain reactions. This not only keeps your score climbing but also aids in honing your logical thinking and rapid reaction skills.

Extra Ball Chains promises endless entertainment and is perfect for players of all ages. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay will ensure you re hooked from the first level. It is not just about protecting the frog s home; it s about the fun you will have while outsmarting those pesky balls. With each successful shot, you will feel a rush of satisfaction as you clear the path for your little hero!

How to play free Extra Ball Chains game online

To play Extra Ball Chains, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim your shots at the approaching balls. Tap or click to release a new ball from the frog's position. Focus on matching colors to create combos and destroy groups of balls for higher scores. Keep an eye on the countdown and act quickly to stop the balls from reaching your home!
