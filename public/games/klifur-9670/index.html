Welcome to Klifur, a delightful and engaging squishy physics puzzle game that challenges your problem-solving skills and dexterity. In this unique game, you control a character by clicking and dragging various body parts to navigate through a series of intricate levels. The goal is to reach the designated target hold at the top of each level, all while overcoming the delightful obstacles in your path.

Embark on an exciting journey across 12 carefully designed levels, each offering varying degrees of difficulty, encouraging players to test their skills and creativity. The squishy physics bring an organic feel to your movements, making every attempt to conquer the heights an entertaining venture. You are tasked with finding the most efficient way to reach the top, which often requires a combination of skillful hand-eye coordination and clever maneuvering.

Once you master each level, challenge yourself further by trying to beat the developer's best routes. Doing so will reward you with stars for your achievements, enhancing your sense of accomplishment as you climb the leaderboards. With colorful visuals and engaging gameplay mechanics, Klifur provides a whimsical experience that keeps players coming back for more.

How to play free Klifur game online

To play Klifur, use your mouse to click and drag individual limbs of your character, adjusting their position to climb each level effectively. Aim to reach the target hold while overcoming obstacles, and once you've completed the level, challenge yourself to beat the developer's best time for a star!
