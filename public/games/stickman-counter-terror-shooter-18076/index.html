Stickman Counter Terror Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of Stickman Counter Terror Shooter, players step into the shoes of elite stickmen tasked with confronting the threat posed by terrorist stick forces. This action-packed shooting game pits the blue team, composed of SWAT (counter-terrorist) stickmen, against the red team of ruthless terrorist stickmen in an intense and engaging clash.

As a skilled stickman shooter, you'll arm yourself with various weapons, including powerful firearms like the M16, shotgun, AK-47, and machine guns, along with reliable pistols. Your mission is to conduct an assault on the enemy stickman team, striking where it hurts most. You ll immerse yourself in this thrilling battle from a first-person perspective, providing an exhilarating feel as you engage in shootouts and strategize your moves.

The game features two distinct stickman teams: the red team represents the terrorists, while the blue team stands for the counter-terrorists. The best part is that you can enjoy these fast-paced battles offline, ensuring you can dive into the action anytime without the need for an internet connection. Prepare yourself for a gripping experience that combines tactical gameplay with stickman-inspired fun!

How to play free Stickman Counter Terror Shooter game online

To play Stickman Counter Terror Shooter, select your preferred stickman team. Once in the game, use the on-screen controls to move your character and aim your weapon. Tap the fire button to shoot at enemy stickmen, and stay alert to dodge their attacks. Utilize your weapons skillfully to win the battle!
