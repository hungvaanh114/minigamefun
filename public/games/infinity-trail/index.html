Infinity Trail

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of Infinity Trail, your mission is to guard the planet from an onslaught of menacing asteroids! As these hazardous celestial bodies rain down from the sky, you must rely on your quick reflexes and strategic thinking to protect your home. Each asteroid that strikes the planet damages its resilience and adds to the urgency of the challenge. Are you fast enough to prevent total destruction?

Players must be on high alert, as the planet can withstand only two hits before succumbing to catastrophe. With each successful dodge, you accumulate points, testing your skills and enhancing your competitive spirit. The excitement builds as you strive to beat your previous scores while keeping the planet safe from impending doom. How long can you hold off the relentless barrage of asteroids, and how many points can you amass before you face defeat?

Your adventure in Infinity Trail is not just a test of your reflexes but also a race against time. As you navigate through this endless challenge, every moment presents an opportunity to improve your score while ensuring the safety of your celestial home. Gather your wits and prepare for an engaging journey filled with heart-pounding action and strategic gameplay!

How to play the free Infinity Trail game online

To play Infinity Trail, simply use your arrow keys or mouse to move your protective shield around the screen. Aim to intercept falling asteroids before they hit the planet. Remember, you have just two lives, so make every movement count. Good luck!


Instructions

Keyboard left and right arrow keys or A and D Touch touch left or right of the full screen to move in that direction
