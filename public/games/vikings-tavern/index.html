Vikings Tavern

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Vikings Tavern, where the clamor of excitement fills the air! In this exceptional game, you find yourself at the center of a lively tavern, besieged by a boisterous horde of Vikings. Their sole mission? To devour every drop of your beer stock! It s not just a game; it s an epic challenge of speed and skill as you rush to satisfy the voracious thirst of these legendary warriors.

The tavern is packed, and the Vikings are roaring for their next round. They won't settle for half measures; they want it all! Your task is to swiftly serve these hearty patrons, handing out frosty mugs of ale as they clamor for more. But take note each Viking has a different level of thirst. Some might drain their mugs in record time while others may take a little longer. It's a thrilling race against time as you manage your resources and anticipate their needs!

As your Viking customers indulge in their drinks, remember to collect the empty glasses to keep the bar running smoothly. The line for the bathroom will inevitably grow, adding another layer of excitement to this raucous tavern adventure. Do you have what it takes to keep the Vikings happy and prevent them from causing a ruckus? Dive into the chaos and embrace the spirit of the Vikings!

How to play free Vikings Tavern game online

To play the Vikings Tavern game, simply use your mouse to click and serve beer to each Viking at the bar. Keep an eye on their thirst levels and collect the empty glasses once they finish their drinks. Aim to satisfy as many customers as you can before they get impatient!


Instructions

Your tavern has 4 large tables Many Vikings are waiting for their beers at the tables You must not deliver beers to an empty table
