Radiation Zone

Description:

This first-person shooter (FPS) title integrates military action and survival elements within a highly regulated containment zone contaminated by radiation. The core objective mandates the complete eradication of all hostile combatants from the designated area. Players are afforded access to a robust inventory system, enabling the acquisition and deployment of diverse weaponry and necessary resources. Crucial survival mechanisms include the mandatory procurement of four cloth units and four branch units for shelter construction, along with the continuous sourcing of sustenance and hydration.
