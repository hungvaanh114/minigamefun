Valentines Love Link

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Valentines Love Link! Dive into a whimsical world where romance meets challenge in this enchanting puzzle game. Your objective is to connect matching Valentine-themed items across the board using straight lines. The twist? You can only make up to two turns while connecting the tiles! So, strategy and quick thinking are your best friends as you navigate through tricky layouts.

With a total of 48 captivating levels to conquer, each presents unique combinations of Valentine items that will test your skills and reflexes. Collecting all tiles within the specified time is essential to progress and emerge victorious as the ultimate love link master. experience the thrill of racing against the clock while immersing yourself in a vibrant atmosphere filled with hearts, roses, chocolates, and other endearing symbols of love.

Valentines Love Link is not just about winning; it's about spreading love and joy through every connection you make. Absorb the delightful visuals and whimsical sounds as you embark on this romantic adventure. As you play, you'll find yourself solving puzzles while basking in the spirit of Valentine's Day. So gather your friends and family, challenge each other, and see who can achieve the highest score while enjoying a fun and heartfelt gaming experience!

How to play free Valentines Love Link game online

To play the game, simply click on a Valentine item to select it, then click on its matching pair to create a link. Ensure the connection does not exceed two turns. Aim to clear all tiles before time runs out to advance to the next level. Enjoy the challenge!
