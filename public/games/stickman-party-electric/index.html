Stickman Party Electric

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Stickman Party Electric, where teamwork is key in a dynamic battle against hordes of pesky monsters! In this action-packed game, players can split into two teams, forming groups of either 2 or 4 participants. Your mission? To defeat the relentless enemies that swarm the stage and guide your Stickman characters safely through the portals to advance to the next challenging level.

As you navigate through vibrant and perilous environments, you ll encounter an array of foes that are determined to put a stop to your progress. It s crucial to stay vigilant; the enemies are numerous and can attack from any direction. Be cautious of the treacherous thorns that can instantly end your adventure and dodge the flying monsters that zip through the air with speed. Success in this game relies on your ability to work together with your teammates, strategize effectively, and remain agile as you maneuver through the battleground.

Stickman Party Electric promises a thrilling cooperative experience, filled with engaging gameplay mechanics and stunning visuals. Every decision counts as you rack up monster kills and guide your Stickman through challenging obstacles. Are you ready to unleash your skills and conquer the enemy? Join forces with your friends and dive into this electrifying adventure!

How to play free Stickman Party Electric game online

To play Stickman Party Electric, gather your friends and choose your team size. Use the controls to move your Stickman character, attack enemies, and dodge hazards. Work together to eliminate all monsters and reach the portal to teleport to the next level. Stay alert and strategize for maximum success!


Instructions

4 players are played in 2 groups Use WASD Arrow keys to play players Use the button in the game to activate group transitions You can only kill the electric colored monsters you own Use the R or P keys to fire
