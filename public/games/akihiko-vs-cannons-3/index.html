Akihiko vs Cannons 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Akihiko vs Cannons 3, a thrilling 2D platformer that challenges your agility and reflexes! In this engaging adventure, players must navigate through eight meticulously designed levels while collecting gold bars and dodging various menacing obstacles. Sharpen your skills as you maneuver past cannon robots, aerial cannon bots, and a plethora of lethal threats including bullets, ground saws, and spikes.

Your primary objective is to reach the yellow flag at the end of each level to unlock the next stage of your daring journey. As you advance through the levels, the challenges become increasingly difficult, pushing your gaming prowess to the limit. With a total of six lives to start with, you'll need to strategize and time your movements carefully to avoid the hazardous traps and enemy fire that can end your quest prematurely.

Akihiko vs Cannons 3 is the latest chapter in the beloved "Akihiko vs Cannons" franchise, building on the exciting gameplay elements that fans have come to love. The vibrant graphics and dynamic mechanics enhance the overall experience, making each level a unique and captivating adventure. Whether you are a seasoned player or new to the series, this game offers an addictive platforming challenge that will keep you coming back for more.

How to play free Akihiko vs Cannons 3 game online

To play Akihiko vs Cannons 3, simply use the arrow keys to move Akihiko left and right, and the space bar to jump. Your objective is to collect gold bars and avoid the cannon robots and other obstacles. Touch the yellow flag to complete the level and progress through this exciting platformer!
