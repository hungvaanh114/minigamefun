Paw Mahjong is an engaging puzzle game that combines the classic tile-matching mechanics with adorable animal icons. In this delightful version of Mahjong, players are tasked with matching identical animal tiles across various challenging levels. The objective is simple yet captivating: find pairs of the same animals and link them to score points. Each successful match not only clears the tiles but also destroys adjacent crates, adding an extra layer of strategy to the gameplay.

The game features a range of exciting levels, each introducing unique challenges and special tiles. These special tiles enhance the gameplay by offering players the ability to move animals in either a straight line or in a circular pattern. This innovative twist on the traditional Mahjong formula makes every level a thrilling experience, as players must carefully plan their moves and think ahead to maximize their points while clearing the board.

Paw Mahjong not only helps sharpen your matching skills but also immerses you in a delightful world filled with charming animal graphics. Whether you re a seasoned Mahjong player or a newcomer to tile-matching games, Paw Mahjong offers something for everyone. Dive in and see how quickly you can conquer all the levels while enjoying the cute and colorful animal tiles along the way.

How to play free Paw Mahjong game online

To play Paw Mahjong, simply select a tile with an animal icon by clicking on it, then click on another tile to swap their places. Make sure to match two identical animals. As they connect, you ll earn points and eliminate adjacent crates. Keep an eye out for special tiles that allow unique movements!
