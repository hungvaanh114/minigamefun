Get ready to sprint and leap with flair in Super Mario Riders as you embark on a thrilling adventure to free Princess Peach from the clutches of the nefarious Bowser! Dive into a vibrant world where your skills are put to the test across a diverse range of captivating environments including verdant plains, intricate caverns, spooky ghost houses, flying airships, and formidable castles. The quest is not just about reaching the end but also about enjoying every moment while navigating through 24 exhilarating courses designed to challenge and excite.

Your primary objective is to rescue Princess Peach, who awaits in Bowser s fortress at the journey's climax. Throughout your gameplay, you will encounter various collectibles, including three uniquely colored coins, adding an extra layer of fun. Engage in friendly competition with your friends as you race against their scores, striving to claim the top spot on the leaderboard.

Additionally, players can get a taste of the action by trying out the initial four courses, from 1-1 to 1-4, free of charge! After successfully liberating Princess Peach, an additional challenge awaits with the unlocking of the special nine-course world, World Star, featuring even more adventures and exciting obstacles.

How to play free super mario riders game online

To enjoy Super Mario Riders online, simply use the arrow keys for movement and jumps. Navigate through the courses, collect coins, and avoid enemies while keeping an eye on your score. Make use of power-ups and try to complete each course as swiftly as possible to score higher and compete with friends!
