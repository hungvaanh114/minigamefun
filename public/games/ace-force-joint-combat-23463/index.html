Ace Force: Joint Combat

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating experience in Ace Force: Joint Combat, where high-octane aerial battles await! Step into the cockpit of some of the world s most advanced military aircraft as you take on the vile forces of the Devil Region. Your skills will be put to the ultimate test as you navigate through enemy fire, executing evasive maneuvers to stay alive while you unleash devastating firepower on enemy jets.

In this thrilling combat simulator, you will have access to an impressive lineup of aircraft, including the iconic F/A-18 Super Hornet, F-14 Super Tomcat, and the cutting-edge F-22 Raptor. Each aircraft comes with its own unique capabilities and arsenal, allowing you to customize your approach to each mission. Engage in high-speed dogfights, expertly dodging enemy attacks while delivering your own barrage of missiles and cannon fire, lighting up the sky with stunning installations of explosions.

Take control of military legends like the F-16 Falcon and the A-10 Thunderbolt II, or indulge in the futuristic design of the F35B Lightning II. The variety of planes ensures every player finds a favorite, making every battle thrillingly unique. As you soar through the virtual skies, your aerial skills and strategic thinking will be essential in establishing aerial supremacy.

How to play free Ace Force: Joint Combat game online

To start your mission in Ace Force: Joint Combat, select your aircraft from the available options and gear up for battle. Use your keyboard or controller to navigate and initiate combat. Engage enemies while dodging incoming fire, and deploy your weapons skillfully to secure victory. Enjoy the adrenaline rush of intense aerial combat!
