Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Stick Defenders, players are tasked with the challenge of defending their base against relentless waves of enemies. The unique gameplay mechanic allows you to merge stickman units of various capacities to create stronger defenders, elevating your strategic options and enhancing your chances of survival. By combining gunmen and other stickman types, you can develop a formidable defense that stands resilient against incoming threats.

As you progress through the levels, enhancing your offensive capabilities becomes essential. Upgrade your units, fortify your walls, and manage your resources wisely to withstand the onslaught of foes that intend to breach your defenses. In addition to the core gameplay, Stick Defenders includes entertaining bonus activities such as the exciting "spin the wheel," where players can win useful items that aid in their defense efforts and uncover new options to explore.

Timed strategy is key in Stick Defenders. Be vigilant and prepared to act swiftly when enemies leave their cooldown period. Quick decision-making can mean the difference between victory and defeat. With each passing wave, the challenge intensifies, prompting you to refine your tactics continuously. How long can you endure in the face of overwhelming opposition and prove your mettle as a top-tier defender?

How to play free STICK DEFENDERS game online

To play Stick Defenders, start by merging stickman units to create stronger defenders. Use your mouse or tap the screen to combine units, allocate resources for upgrades, and fortify your base. Pay attention to enemy cooldowns and react quickly to fend off waves of attackers while collecting bonuses along the way.
