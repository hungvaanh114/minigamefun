Moto Pizza Delivery

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to hit the streets and become the ultimate pizza delivery hero in Moto Pizza Delivery! In this exhilarating simulation game, you will take on the role of a skilled pizza delivery man, tasked with bringing hot and delicious pizzas to hungry customers throughout the bustling city. With every order, you'll feel the exhilarating rush as you ride your heavy-duty motorcycle through busy streets, dodging cars and obstacles while executing thrilling stunts!

As you navigate through this lively urban environment, precision and speed are essential. Your customers are counting on you to deliver their pizzas quickly and efficiently. Master the art of biking while also showcasing your skills with daring jumps, flips, and sharp turns. The more adventurous your ride, the more points you earn! However, be cautious; the city is full of unexpected challenges that could jeopardize your delivery mission.

With each successful delivery, you'll unlock new levels and upgrades for your motorcycle, making it even easier to conquer the busy streets. The vibrant graphics and immersive sound effects create an engaging experience that will keep you entertained for hours. Compete with friends or against the clock to prove you're the best pizza delivery man in town and establish your reputation as the go-to delivery expert!

How to play free Moto Pizza Delivery game online

To start playing Moto Pizza Delivery, simply choose your delivery mission from the menu. Use the arrow keys or your mobile device to control your bike, accelerating to speed through the city. Collect pizza orders and deliver them to the specified locations while avoiding obstacles. Keep an eye on the timer to complete your deliveries efficiently!
