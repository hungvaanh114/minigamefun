To play Bombers.io, simply use your mouse to aim and shoot at opponents within the arena. Click to fire your ranged weapon, and pay attention to the movement of your enemies for precise shots. Unlock upgrades as you gain experience by eliminating players, and enjoy the chaotic gameplay!
