Halloween Cooking - Run a Restaurant

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the festive spirit with "Halloween Cooking - Run A Restaurant." In this exciting culinary adventure, you have the opportunity to prepare, cook, and serve delicious meals that span the globe. From classic coffees to mouth-watering Chinese dishes, and delectable desserts to juicy grilled burgers, you ll dash between various stations, showcasing your culinary skills.

Put your cooking prowess to the test as you navigate through the bustling kitchen during Halloween. Each level offers unique challenges and time management gameplay, pushing you to work quickly and efficiently. Unleash your creativity by baking sweet treats that not only look good but taste amazing! With vibrant graphics and a lively atmosphere, "Halloween Cooking" captivates players of all ages.

As you advance, you'll unlock new recipes and exciting kitchen upgrades, allowing you to expand your gastronomic empire. Compete with your friends or aim for high scores as you serve customers in record time while keeping the eerie festivity alive. This game is perfect for anyone looking to conquer the cooking world with a sprinkle of Halloween magic!

How to play free Halloween Cooking - Run A Restaurant game online

To play "Halloween Cooking - Run A Restaurant," start by selecting a kitchen station. Follow the on-screen instructions to prepare and cook various dishes. Tap on ingredients to select them, and drag and drop items to cook or serve. Keep an eye on time as you fulfill customer orders to earn points!
