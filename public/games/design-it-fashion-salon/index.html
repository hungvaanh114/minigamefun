Design It Fashion Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Design It Fashion Salon, where creativity meets entrepreneurship! Tailor Lucy has finally achieved her dream of owning a tailor shop, but she needs your expertise to manage her growing business. Dive into the world of fashion as you help Lucy set up her stylish salon, creating an inviting space for customers of all kinds.

As you embark on this exciting journey, you'll encounter a variety of clients, each with their own unique style preferences and demands. Some customers may have particular tastes for seasonal trends, while others may request extravagant designs fit for red carpet events. Don t worry, with your keen eye for fashion and attention to detail, you ll be able to cater to their needs!

Your tasks will include combining different fabrics, selecting accessories, and crafting stunning outfits that highlight Lucy's sewing skills. Keep up with the fashion trends, and don t hesitate to showcase your creativity through custom designs. Whether it's creating glamorous gowns for VIP celebrities or chic everyday outfits, the sky's the limit in Design It Fashion Salon. Get ready to unleash your imagination and transform Lucy's tailor shop into a premier fashion destination!

How to play free Design It Fashion Salon game online

To begin playing, simply select your desired fabric and accessories in the shop. Once you've designed your outfit, dress up your clients with the custom creations you ve made. Complete various tasks to earn points, upgrade the salon, and unlock new outfits as you advance through different levels of the game!
