Soft Christmas Cookies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for the festive season with our exciting new game, Soft Christmas Cookies! This free online game is perfect for the holidays, where Santa Claus needs your assistance to bake the most delightful cookies ever. As Christmas approaches, Santa is busy preparing for his big night, and he needs your help to ensure he delivers delicious treats to everyone.

In this fun and engaging cooking game, you will take on the role of Santa's little helper. Follow the step-by-step instructions provided in the game as you mix, bake, and shape mouthwatering cookies. Show off your creativity by crafting beautiful cookie shapes that are sure to please. Once the cookies are baked to perfection, it's time to let your artistic flair shine while decorating them with vibrant colors and designs.

But the fun doesn't stop there! After creating stunning cookies, you can also arrange your table setting, making it look festive and inviting for the holiday celebrations. With every level you complete, you will feel the spirit of Christmas as you help Santa prepare for his joyful journey!

How to play free Soft Christmas Cookies game online

To play the Soft Christmas Cookies game, start by following the on-screen instructions. Use your mouse or touch controls to mix ingredients, shape the cookies, and apply decorations. Be attentive to the time limits and make sure to complete each step to keep Santa on track. Enjoy your cooking adventure!
