TRZ Cannon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

TRZ Cannon is an entertaining casual game that combines strategy and skill as players aim to fire cannonballs into a variety of baskets scattered throughout the levels. With over 100 engaging levels to conquer, players will find themselves immersed in a world of colorful graphics and intriguing challenges. Each level presents a unique combination of distances and angles that must be calculated accurately, testing players' ability to estimate the optimal strength and trajectory of their cannon shots.

The game also features small puzzles that add an additional layer of fun and complexity. These puzzles require players to think critically about how to overcome obstacles or utilize the environment to their advantage, making TRZ Cannon not just a game of aim but also a game of problem-solving. The welcoming design and straightforward mechanics make it accessible for players of all ages, ensuring that it remains an enjoyable experience.

As players progress through the levels, they ll encounter increasingly difficult challenges that will require practice and precision. Each successful shot not only brings a sense of accomplishment but also unlocks new levels and features, keeping players engaged and motivated to continue their adventure.

How to play free TRZ Cannon game online

To play TRZ Cannon, simply aim your cannon at the desired basket using the on-screen controls. Adjust the power of your shot by dragging the slider before releasing to fire the cannonball. Keep an eye on the distance and obstacles to ensure your shot lands successfully in the basket!
