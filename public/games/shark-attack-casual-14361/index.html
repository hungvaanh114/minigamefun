Shark Attack-Casual

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrill of the ocean with Shark Attack-Casual, where you become the apex predator of the seas! As a ravenous shark, your mission is to cause panic among beachgoers and indulge in a feast of terrified humans. The beach is teeming with unwitting swimmers, and it's your job to strike when they least expect it.

Navigate through the sandy shores with ease by swiping your finger across the screen, guiding your killer shark as you hunt for your next meal. But beware you're not the only one with an appetite. Other hungry sharks roam the waters, and you'll need to keep an eye out for them as you feast on the innocents.

Each successful attack will not only fill your belly but will also give you the power to grow stronger and bigger. The more you eat, the more formidable you become in the ocean s hierarchy. Challenge yourself to see how many humans and rival sharks you can consume in this action-packed, casual game. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Shark Attack-Casual is an exhilarating experience that will keep you coming back for more!

How to play free Shark Attack-Casual game online

To play Shark Attack-Casual, simply swipe on your screen to control your shark's movement. Target unsuspecting beachgoers to devour them and grow in size. Avoid other sharks while attempting to feast on them too. Stay alert, act quickly, and enjoy your reign as the ocean's most feared predator!
