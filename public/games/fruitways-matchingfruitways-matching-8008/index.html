Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Fruitways Matching, where strategy meets fun in a vibrant fruity adventure! Your objective is simple yet engaging: shift the fruits into available spaces to align three or more identical pieces either vertically or horizontally. The challenge lies in minimizing your moves to maximize your score. The fewer moves you make, the higher your score will soar!

To move a fruit, begin by tapping on the chosen fruit, then tap on an empty tile where you'd like the fruit to go. As long as there is an unobstructed path connecting the fruit to its new position, it will glide smoothly into place. However, be vigilant each level presents a countdown on your score. You must eliminate all the fruits and complete the objectives before your score dwindles to zero.

To assist you in your quest, the game offers special powers. For instance, the Bomb can clear away multiple fruits at once, while extra score bonuses can help you rack up points quickly. With strategic thinking and a little help from power-ups, you can navigate through increasingly challenging levels in this delightful fruit puzzle."

How to play free Fruitways Matching game online

To enjoy the Fruitways Matching game, simply select a fruit and tap on an adjacent blank space to move it. Always aim to form rows of three identical fruits. Keep an eye on your score, and strategically use special powers to enhance your gameplay and complete levels efficiently!
