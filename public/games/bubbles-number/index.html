Bubbles Number

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the fascinating world of Bubbles Number, an exhilarating bubble shooter game designed to challenge your skills and keep you entertained for hours. Your primary objective is to aim and shoot colorful bubbles to prevent them from descending to the bottom of the screen. Each bubble you pop contributes to your score, and as you progress, you'll encounter a variety of exciting levels, each presenting unique challenges and obstacles.

As you engage in this fun-filled adventure, don t forget to collect fruits while shooting the bubbles! These juicy rewards not only add thrill to your gameplay but also enhance your overall score and help you unlock new levels. With its captivating graphics and soothing soundtracks, Bubbles Number offers a delightful experience for players of all ages. The game combines strategy with action, making it ideal for both those looking to pass time and avid gamers eager for a puzzling challenge.

Unlocking all levels will take skill and precision, but the satisfaction of achieving a high score is truly rewarding. Enjoy every moment in this vibrant bubble shooter as you master the art of bubble shooting and hone your reflexes. Have fun and put your aiming skills to the test!

How to play free Bubbles Number game online

To play Bubbles Number, simply use your mouse or touch screen to aim at clusters of bubbles. Click or tap to shoot and match 3 or more bubbles of the same color for them to pop. Keep an eye on the descending bubbles and try to collect any fruits that appear while shooting for maximum points!
