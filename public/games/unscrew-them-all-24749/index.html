Unscrew Them All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Unscrew Them All invites players into a captivating world of puzzles where every level presents a unique challenge. Your objective is to remove screws that firmly hold large wooden planks to a board. Each screw must be meticulously unscrewed and then correctly placed in corresponding open slots. The excitement escalates with a ticking clock, urging you to complete each level before time elapses.

This game offers an engaging blend of strategy and skill as players must plan their moves wisely. Each level intensifies in difficulty, testing your ability to think ahead and react quickly. With increasingly complex configurations of planks and screws, players will find themselves engrossed in the task of strategically unscrewing and replacing screws in the right locations.

Enjoy the sense of accomplishment that comes from mastering each level. Every time you successfully unscrew all the planks, you're one step closer to victory. Unscrew Them All is not just a game of mechanical dexterity; it challenges your problem-solving skills, pushing you to discover the most efficient ways to navigate through the intricacies of each puzzle. Challenge yourself, and see if you have what it takes to conquer them all!

How to play free Unscrew Them All game online

To play Unscrew Them All, simply click on the screws to unscrew them from the wooden planks. Once a screw is removed, drag and drop it into the corresponding hole on the board. Keep an eye on the timer, as you need to complete all levels within the allotted time to advance in the game!
