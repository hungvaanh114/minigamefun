How to play free Extreme Stunt Car Game game online

To start playing, select your vehicle and choose your desired track. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver your car through the challenging obstacles. Accelerate to gain speed, and time your jumps perfectly to perform stunts. Maintain control to avoid falling off the edges, and complete levels to unlock new vehicles and tracks!
