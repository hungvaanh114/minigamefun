Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Connect Joy, a thrilling and engaging Onet puzzle game that will put your cognitive skills to the test! In this delightful experience, your objective is to connect two identical tiles on the board. By tapping or clicking, you can link matching pairs with precisely three straight turns, making for a fun yet challenging gameplay.

As you navigate through the vibrant and expansive world of Connect Joy, you will discover a multitude of demanding levels, each designed to stretch your problem-solving abilities. Race against the clock as you strive to clear the board before time runs out, adding an exhilarating layer of urgency to your puzzle-solving adventure. Challenge yourself and see how many levels you can conquer!

Each level offers unique designs and obstacles, ensuring that you remain entertained and engaged. As you progress, the game introduces progressively difficult challenges, keeping you on your toes and encouraging you to think critically. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Connect Joy is perfect for puzzle enthusiasts looking to enhance their mental agility while enjoying a casual gaming experience. Good luck!

How to play free Connect Joy game online

To play the Connect Joy game online, tap or click on any tile to select it. Then, connect it to another identical tile by drawing a path with no more than three straight turns. Clear the board by matching tiles within the time limit to progress to the next level!
