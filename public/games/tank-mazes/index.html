Tank Mazes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of Tank Mazes, your command is crucial. The time has come to defend your base against a fierce enemy counteroffensive. Armed with intelligence about the enemy's movements, you, as the elite tank commander, must employ your strategic skills to hold the line against overwhelming odds. The top engineers have equipped you with several modifications of our most advanced tank models, ensuring you are more than prepared for the battle ahead.

Your arsenal includes cutting-edge support technologies that can turn the tide of combat, featuring capabilities such as point bombing to rain destruction upon enemy forces, energy shields to protect your tank from incoming fire, and a time stop ability to freeze your adversaries in their tracks. However, you re not entirely alone; while key reinforcements have been deployed elsewhere, you can choose to take a partner with you to fortify your defenses and tackle the imminent threats as a cohesive unit.

The stakes are high, and the enemies are relentless. Utilize your strategic acumen to navigate through the mazes that serve as both a battleground and a shield for your base. Will you uphold your duty and emerge victorious against the encroaching forces? The fate of the base lies in your hands, and your courage will be tested as you face waves of relentless adversaries.

How to play free Tank Mazes game online

To play Tank Mazes, use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate your tank through the maze. Deploy your special abilities with designated keys for effective defense. Communicate and strategize with your partner to take down enemies, protect the base, and survive the onslaught of enemy forces.


Instructions

The first player is moving with the W A S D keys or the green buttons on the screen shooting with a spacebar The second player is moving with the arrow keys or blue buttons on the screen shooting with the num 0 key
