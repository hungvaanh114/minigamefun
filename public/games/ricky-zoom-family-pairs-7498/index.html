Ricky Zoom - Family Pairs

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enhance your memory and complete the exciting puzzle in the game Ricky Zoom: Family Pairs! Join Ricky, Loop, DJ, and Scootio on a delightful new adventure as they introduce you to their family members. Are you prepared to test your matching skills and quick thinking alongside your adorable friends? Now's your opportunity to reveal the hidden images of your motorcycle buddies!

Your mission is simple yet challenging: find all the matching pairs until every picture is unveiled. Sharpen your concentration and see how quickly you can tackle this engaging challenge! In this game, you'll only need your mouse to flip the circles. Start by clicking on any item to discover which character is concealed beneath. Then, choose another circle to check if the pictures match!

As you progress through the game, expect four distinct stages where the difficulty ramps up. Initially, you'll have to match six items, but by the end, you'll be working with up to twelve objects. Can you keep pace and demonstrate your remarkable memory? Keep in mind that these characters are all family members, so the images may appear quite similar. Concentrate on their unique features to remember the location of each character!

Fortunately, there s no time pressure, which means you can take your time and work at your own pace. Don t worry if you make a few mistakes; you can keep trying to find the pairs as often as necessary. Once you reveal all the pairs, you can advance to the next level and continue honing your matching skills!

How to play free Ricky Zoom - Family Pairs game online

To start playing the game, simply use your mouse to click on any of the circles to reveal a hidden character. After revealing one, click on another circle to see if it matches. Your goal is to find all matching pairs, moving through increasing levels of difficulty without a time limit!
