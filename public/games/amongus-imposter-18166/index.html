Amongus Imposter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Amongus Imposter, step into the shoes of a compassionate crewmate aboard the Aurora space station. This engaging twist on the classic social deduction game invites you to dive into a world filled with intrigue and deception. Your mission, however, is far from conventional: execute your devious plan to eliminate as many of your fellow crew members as possible, all while ensuring that your motives remain hidden. Your ultimate goal? To impress Moma by showcasing your skills in stealth and strategy.

As the game unfolds, you will find yourself navigating the bustling corridors of the space station, where each crewmate is a potential threat or ally. Forge temporary friendships but be ready to betray them at any moment. Take advantage of opportunities as you lurk in the shadows, plotting your next move. Use every tool at your disposal to sow chaos and confusion among your crewmates, all while advancing your agenda. Tension builds as accusations fly, and your clever ruses will keep your fellow players on their toes.

The challenge lies in balancing your murderous intentions with the need to blend in. Will you be the last one standing? Can you outsmart your friends and create the perfect alibi? Amongus Imposter sets the stage for an exhilarating experience where trust is scarce, and survival is paramount. Are you ready to embrace the role of the cunning imposter and elevate your status in the eyes of Moma?

How to play free Amongus Imposter game online

To play Amongus Imposter online, simply join a game lobby with friends or other players. Once in, your task is to blend in with the crew while secretly eliminating them one by one. Complete tasks to maintain your cover, find strategic moments to attack, and use cunning tactics to avoid suspicion.
