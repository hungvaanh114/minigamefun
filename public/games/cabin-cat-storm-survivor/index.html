Cabin Cat Storm Survivor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Cabin Cat Storm Survivor," you step into the paws of a small but determined cat as it navigates through a world fraught with threatening weather. The game, developed in just five days for the 2022 Sleeping Giant Game Jam, immerses you in a whimsical yet perilous environment where you must prepare for an impending storm. As the storm clouds gather, your goal is to gather supplies, fortify your cabin, and ensure your safety before the storm strikes.

The creative theme of "Advancing Wall of Doom!" adds an exciting twist to the gameplay. Players must think strategically to outmaneuver the relentless wall of chaos that approaches. As the storm looms closer, it progressively intensifies, challenging players to act quickly and efficiently. Collect resources scattered throughout the environment, all while dodging obstacles and other hazards as the countdown to chaos begins.

With charming graphics and engaging mechanics, "Cabin Cat Storm Survivor" captures the essence of adventure and survival. Whether you re stocking up on food or crafting makeshift defenses, every decision counts as you prepare for the ultimate showdown with nature's fury. Experience the thrill of the impending disaster and see if you can keep your cabin and your kitty safe from the storm!

How to play free Cabin Cat Storm Survivor game online

To play "Cabin Cat Storm Survivor," navigate your tiny cat around the cabin using arrow keys or WASD. Interact with objects to collect supplies, build shelters, and stay safe as the storm approaches. Keep an eye on your resources and manage your time wisely to survive the intense challenges ahead!
