Temple Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of Temple Quest, your adventurous spirit has landed you in a heap of trouble! After stealing a precious artifact from a hidden temple, you're now chased through a sprawling labyrinth of ancient ruins, where each twist and turn could either lead to salvation or peril. As you sprint through the maze, gather an array of golden idols and ancient coins to boost your score and unlock amazing power-ups along the way.

To navigate the treacherous terrain, you'll need to master a diverse set of obstacles that stand in your way. From crumbling stone walls to swinging traps, quick reflexes and strategic thinking are crucial. Enhance your gameplay experience by upgrading your power-ups, which will help you tackle more challenging sections of the maze and exponentially increase your score multiplier.

Customization is key in Temple Quest. Experience the thrill of trying out various outfits, each equipped with unique special abilities that grant you an edge over the competition. Explore different combinations and find the one that best suits your playstyle, allowing you to delve further into the depths of the temple. How far can you run before the shadows catch up to you? The challenge awaits!

How to play free Temple Quest game online

Start the game by using the arrow keys or swipe gestures to navigate your character through the temple's maze. Collect items and avoid obstacles to keep your score climbing. Use power-ups strategically and switch outfits when necessary to maximize your progress. Aim for the highest score and see how far you can go!
