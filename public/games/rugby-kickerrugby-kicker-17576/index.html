Step onto the pitch with Rugby Kicker, an exhilarating game that lets you engage in intense rugby matches right from your device. Challenge your friends in a head-to-head showdown on the same device, or take your skills global by competing against some of the best teams in a thrilling world championship. The gameplay is simple yet captivating, making it suitable for rugby enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

In Rugby Kicker, players aim to score points by mastering their kicking techniques. With each successful kick, you inch closer to victory while unlocking a variety of trophies and achievements. There s even an exciting 60-second kickout mini-game where you can prove just how far your skills will take you in a limited time frame. It s all about precision, timing, and your ability to perform under pressure. The more points you rack up, the higher your score will climb set new records and challenge your friends to beat them!

Pushing the limits of your kicking prowess, Rugby Kicker offers a dynamic sports experience that promises hours of fun. Whether you re playing competitively against a friend or striving to conquer the leaderboard against global players, every kick counts. Join the exciting world of rugby, where every match is a chance to test your skills and dominate the competition!

How to play free Rugby Kicker game online

To start playing Rugby Kicker, load the game and choose your mode: either single-player or multiplayer. Follow the on-screen prompts to adjust your aim and power using the joystick controls. Tap the kick button to unleash your kick and score points. Good luck!
