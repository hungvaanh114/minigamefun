Slippery Descent by Car

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pass obstacles on cars from the cube game! A hardcore game that takes you into an exciting world of gambling challenges. You have to conquer dangerous descents on various cars, overcoming the most difficult obstacles on the way to victory. Each level is unique, full of surprises and challenges!
