Express Delivery Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of solving logistics challenges in Express Delivery Puzzle, a captivating slide puzzle game that pushes your strategic thinking to the limit. In this game, you are tasked with maneuvering road blocks on a grid to create a clear route for a delivery truck. Your objective is to connect the truck with various homes, ensuring that every delivery is made seamlessly. Each carefully designed level presents a unique puzzle, guaranteeing that there's only one correct way to arrange the blocks and achieve success.

With a total of 100 levels, players will encounter a range of difficulties that will require both patience and intelligence. From simple layouts to intricate paths that demand critical problem-solving, Express Delivery Puzzle caters to puzzle enthusiasts of all skill levels. The satisfaction of finding the perfect solution and seeing your truck roll smoothly through the roads makes every completed level rewarding. Engage your mind as you strategize your moves, ensuring that each slide brings you closer to building the optimal route for your delivery truck.

How to play free Express Delivery Puzzle game online

To play Express Delivery Puzzle, simply click on the road blocks to slide them into empty spaces and form a continuous path for the delivery truck. Analyze the grid carefully to determine your moves, as each level has a unique solution. Keep an eye on the goal and make strategic decisions to complete the puzzle!


Instructions

Slide the road blocks to make a path connecting the delivery truck to all the homes
