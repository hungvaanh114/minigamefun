Donut Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing the ultimate sweet showdown: the Donut Challenge! If you're a donut enthusiast who just can't share, this thrilling game is tailor-made for you. Get ready to dive into an exciting battle to snatch the tastiest donuts before your friends do. The challenge is on as you compete to grab as many donuts as possible from a tantalizing spinning tray.

Your mission is simple: snag the delightful donuts by seizing them right at their center while they whirl around. The first player to successfully grab 5 donuts wins the game and claims the title of the ultimate donut lover. But be vigilant! As you reach for those sugary treats, keep an eye on the rotating + and - points on the plate. These numbers can either boost your score or lead you to defeat, adding an extra layer of strategy to the fun.

Gameplay is intuitive and interactive. You can control your moves easily by tapping the screen, allowing you to execute quick grabs and dodge any penalties. With each spin, excitement builds, and the race for donuts intensifies. Test your reflexes, enjoy the colorful graphics, and immerse yourself in the playful atmosphere of the Donut Challenge because sharing is overrated when it comes to donuts!

How to play free Donut Challenge game online

Once you're in the game, tap the screen to grab the spinning donuts by their middle part. Aim to collect 5 donuts before your opponents do to win. Watch out for the rotating point values on the plate, as these can affect your score. Good luck and enjoy the sweet competition!
