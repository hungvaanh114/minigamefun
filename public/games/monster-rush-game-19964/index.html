Monster Rush Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for a thrilling ride in the world of Monster Rush Game, where destruction becomes your playground! Select your favorite Kaiju monster and get ready to smash through cities with just a tap. Each level introduces new buildings to demolish and offers opportunities to unlock more powerful Kaiju, including iconic figures such as Godzilla, Mothra, King Kong, and Ghidorah, along with a variety of dinosaurs and gorillas.

As you traverse through different cities around the globe, don't miss the chance to upgrade your monsters. Collect rare materials to evolve your creatures, turning them into colossal titans that can wreak even more havoc. With every evolution, your monsters gain new abilities and increased strength, making them even more formidable. Build the ultimate monster army by strategizing which monsters to upgrade and unleash their destructive powers in intense battles.

Monster Rush is not only about smashing; it's about building a legacy. Experience innovative core gameplay that combines tapping, swiping, and slinging mechanics. Your monsters' unique abilities will help you demolish buildings and gather epic loot, leading to an exhilarating adventure filled with action and entertainment. Rise to the occasion and claim the title of the ultimate monster king!

How to play free Monster Rush Game game online

To play Monster Rush, simply tap on your chosen Kaiju to launch it into action. Use the swipe and sling mechanics to target buildings and unleash your monster's special abilities. Keep smashing for resources that allow you to upgrade and evolve your monsters, enhancing their skills and power!
