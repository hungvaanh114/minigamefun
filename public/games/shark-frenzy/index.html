Shark Frenzy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling underwater world of Shark Frenzy, where ferocious sharks await your assistance in their hunt for seafood! In this vibrant match-three puzzle game, your objective is to swipe and match three identical seafood items, clearing the blocks that stand in the way of these hungry predators. With each move, you ll experience a burst of exciting visuals and addictive gameplay, making it impossible to resist returning for more.

As you progress through various challenging levels, keep an eye out for golden opportunities! When you successfully align four or more seafood pieces, you trigger the creation of a special water balloon block. These magical balloons come with powers that can erase excess time from the clock and clear entire lines on the game board, giving you an extra edge against the ticking timer. Use them strategically to rack up points, outsmart the levels, and help those sharks feast on their favorite meals!

From colorful graphics to whimsical sounds, Shark Frenzy promises an entertaining experience for players of all ages. The game combines a classic match-three format with dynamic features, ensuring that every session is unique and engaging. Dive deep into the oceanic fun and discover how satisfying it is to help these hungry sharks while honing your puzzle-solving skills.

How to play free Shark Frenzy game online

To play Shark Frenzy, simply swipe the seafood pieces on the game board to match three identical items. Keep forming matches to clear blocks and make the sharks happy! Collect four or more in a row to create special water balloon blocks that will help you clear larger sections of the board and save time!
