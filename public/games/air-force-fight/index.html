Air Force Fight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for a thrilling aerial combat experience in Air Force Fight, where you can engage in battle with nostalgic air fighters! This action-packed game presents you with multiple modes to test your skills and strategies. Your primary objective is to eliminate enemy aircraft while ensuring your survival in both single-player and two-player modes.

Dive into the Campaign mode, a challenging journey consisting of 12 exhilarating levels. As you progress, you'll face various enemy air fighters and can earn medals for your achievements. This mode not only requires sharp reflexes and strategic thinking but also the ability to collect necessary upgrades and power-ups to enhance your combat capabilities.

If you're seeking some friendly competition, the Battle vs Player mode allows you to challenge a friend in head-to-head aerial confrontations. Show off your skills by strengthening your defenses and arsenal through the collection of weapons and bonuses scattered throughout the game. The adrenaline rush from outsmarting opponents while navigating through enemy fire will keep you on the edge of your seat!

Whether you're a nostalgic fighter pilot or new to aerial combat games, Air Force Fight promises endless excitement and engagement with your friends and the AI.

How to play free Air Force Fight game online

To play Air Force Fight, simply use the arrow keys to navigate your aircraft up, down, left, and right to dodge enemy fire. Press the designated attack key to shoot down opposing air fighters. Collect power-ups and weapons throughout your journey to increase your firepower and defense. Enjoy the exhilarating experience of aerial battles!
