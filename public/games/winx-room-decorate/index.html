Winx Room Decorate

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Winx as you join beautiful fairy girls Bloom, Flora, Stella, and Tecna in their quest to decorate their rooms for the New Year! With a vibrant palette of pink and light blue, each fairy is eager to create a magical atmosphere that reflects their unique personalities.

As you embark on this enchanting decorating adventure, you ll have plenty of creative choices at your fingertips. Choose from a variety of stylish furniture, chic accessories, and playful decorations that capture the essence of each fairy. Will you opt for Bloom's fiery charm, Flora's natural elegance, Stella's glamorous flair, or Tecna's modern sophistication? The possibilities are endless as you mix and match items to create the perfect fairy sanctuary!

Unlock your inner designer and help the Winx girls transform their rooms into dreamlike spaces, where harmony and beauty flourish side by side. The charming graphics and delightful ambiance of the game will immerse you in the magical realm of the Winx fairies. So, gather your creativity and bring your vision to life in this delightful room decorating challenge!

How to play free Winx Room Decorate game online

To play the Winx Room Decorate game, simply choose your favorite fairy from the character selection menu. Use the items displayed on the right to pick furniture, decorations, and accessories that you wish to use. Drag and drop each item into the room until you achieve your desired look!
