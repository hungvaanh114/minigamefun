Sonic Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Sonic the Hedgehog with "Sonic Run," an exhilarating endless runner game where speed is your ultimate weapon! Embark on an adrenaline-fueled journey as you swipe your way through breathtaking 3D landscapes, showcasing Sonic's iconic agility in a race against time. With each run, you'll face an array of challenging obstacles that test your reflexes and skills. Navigate through thrilling loops and avoid hazards while dashing at lightning speed!

Unleash Sonic s signature dash ability to obliterate everything in your path while soaring over barriers and dodging obstacles. Whether you're performing amazing jumps or executing the homing attack on foes, Sonic s powers keep you engaged and on the edge of your seat. Each level is crafted with stunning graphics that highlight Sonic's charismatic world, making the game a visual treat on both phones and tablets.

Expand the excitement by choosing between Sonic and his beloved friends like Tails, Shadow, and Knuckles, each possessing unique abilities. Engage in epic boss battles against notorious adversaries, including Dr. Eggman and Zazz from Sonic Lost World, who will challenge your speed and tactics. Collect various power-ups including head starts, shields, and ring magnets that enhance your gaming experience and help you run even further!

Continue leveling up and earning rewards as you race against the clock! Embrace the thrill of daily challenges and spins that provide incredible prizes, including character unlocks and Red Star Rings. The endless fun awaits as you dash your way to victory with Sonic!

How to play free sonic run game online

To play Sonic Run, simply tap and swipe your screen to guide Sonic through the course. Swipe up to jump over obstacles, swipe down to slide under barriers, and tap to activate his dash move for extra speed. Collect rings and power-ups while avoiding enemies to extend your run!
