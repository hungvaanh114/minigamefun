How to play free Tower Defense: Fish Attack game online

In Tower Defense: Fish Attack, you will be tasked with building various defensive structures to fend off waves of invading fish and other marine creatures. Click to select your towers, place them strategically along the path, and upgrade them as you gather resources. Keep an eye on the incoming waves and adjust your tactics accordingly to defend your territory!
