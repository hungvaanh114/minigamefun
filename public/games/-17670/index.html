Embark on an exhilarating adventure in the fifth installment of the acclaimed Zombie Mission series! In Zombie Mission 5, players are back in action, navigating through treacherous terrains infested with relentless zombies. Your primary objective is to rescue hostages while collecting valuable coins scattered throughout each level. These coins can be utilized in the "UPGRADES" section, where you can purchase powerful new weapons and additional lives to bolster your chances of survival.

A standout feature of this version is the introduction of multiple difficulty levels, allowing you to tailor your gaming experience. Choose from Normal, Hard, or Extreme difficulty settings to challenge yourself and test your skills against increasingly formidable foes. As you progress through the game, prepare for the ultimate showdown with the dreaded "BOSS" in the final level, where strategy and combat prowess will be put to the test.

The stakes are high, and the world depends on you to reclaim peace and save all the hostages! Whether you re new to the series or a seasoned veteran, Zombie Mission 5 offers thrilling gameplay, captivating graphics, and an immersive storyline that will keep you hooked for hours on end. Dive into this action-packed experience and show those zombies who's boss!

How to play free Zombie Mission 5 game online

To play Zombie Mission 5, use the arrow keys to navigate your character and the space bar to jump. Engage zombies and complete missions by utilizing weapons you've purchased in the "UPGRADES" section. Work strategically to save hostages and progress through levels until you face the boss in the final challenge.
