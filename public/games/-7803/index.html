Cute Forest Tavern?Cute Forest Tavern

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Cute Forest Tavern, where a whimsical invasion of adorable animals is turning your cozy establishment upside down! The peaceful ambiance is disrupted as these furry visitors don't seem to care about using your facilities. Instead, they're on a mission to devour all your ice cream! It s a race against time as you must keep these cute creatures happy before they lose their patience.

Your objective is clear: quench their thirst with delicious ice cream and make sure to retrieve the empty glasses afterward. Be mindful that not all animals are created equal; some will guzzle down the tasty treats faster than others, so you'll need to strategize your ice cream distribution effectively. The game combines elements of skill, speed, and reflexes that will keep you on your toes.

Master the art of ice cream serving as you manage multiple counters, ensuring the right treat is sent to the right animal at the right time. With each level, the challenge increases, and you must stay sharp to outpace the cuteness overload. So roll up your sleeves and dive into this adorable chaos in Cute Forest Tavern and embark on a delightful journey filled with laughter and fun!

How to play free Cute Forest Tavern game online

In Cute Forest Tavern, use your mouse or finger to tap on the ice cream counters and serve your furry guests quickly. Keep track of each animal s ice cream preference and refill the glasses when they're empty. The goal is to please them fast to avoid any tantrums!
