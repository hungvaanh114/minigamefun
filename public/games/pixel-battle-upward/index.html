Pixel Battle Upward

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pixel Battle Upward, an exhilarating adventure where quick reflexes and strategic thinking are your best allies! Engage in a thrilling contest with your friend as you navigate a fiery battlefield filled with hazardous lava pits. Your objective is simple yet exciting: dodge the lava while attempting to send your opponent tumbling into its molten depths. Are you prepared for epic showdowns?

In this game, you must remain agile and outsmart your opponent. Maintain your distance or use clever tactics to push them into the lava. However, the landscape is filled with dangers, including rockets that rain down from above, eager to disrupt your battle! These exploding rockets can catch you off guard, leading to elimination if you're not cautious. Remember, the player who succumbs to the lava or dies the most is declared the ultimate loser. So gather your wits, embrace the challenge, and see who can emerge victorious in the fiery arena!

How to play free Pixel Battle Upward game online

To dive into the exhilarating action of Pixel Battle Upward, start by selecting your character and entering the arena with a friend. Use the directional keys to move swiftly and avoid falling into the lava. Strategically employ your skills to push your friend into the lava while dodging incoming rockets. The player with the best survival skills wins!


Instructions

Press the S key or the Down Arrow key to push or hit The player with the most points in 120 seconds wins Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys to move
