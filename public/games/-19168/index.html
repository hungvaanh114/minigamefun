Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Rivadis Empire, once a beacon of power and opulence, has succumbed to the dark influence of necromancers, leaving its legacy in question. As the empire crumbles, the call for dominance reverberates across the continent. Now is the moment for a bold resurgence. Dive into the world of Takeover, where you take on the role of a leader of one of three distinct nations, each vying for control over the remnants of the empire. In this engaging real-time strategy game, your tactical prowess will determine the fate of the realm.

Strategize your way through a series of challenging battles as you gather resources, build your army, and conquer your foes. Select your nation and harness its unique strengths to gain the upper hand in combat. You will face waves of enemies and the treacherous environments that now populate the land once ruled by the Rivadis Empire. The game's mechanics allow for deep strategic planning as you decide when to advance, defend, or retreat.

Each victory not only brings you closer to reclaiming the empire but also deepens your knowledge of the world around you. Build alliances or forge enmities as you navigate through this treacherous landscape, where every decision could lead to glory or downfall. Embrace the opportunity to revive the legacy of Rivadis or let it fade into obscurity. The time for Takeover has arrived, and the destiny of the empire rests on your shoulders!

How to play free Takeover game online

To play the game, start by selecting one of the three nations available. Then, strategize your moves in real-time as you gather resources, build military units, and engage in combat against rival factions. Use your mouse to select units, issue commands, and manage your forces on the battlefield.
