Taxi Driver Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Taxi Driver Simulator immerses players in the exhilarating world of cab driving, where every journey counts. Set out on a mission to master the art of taxi driving through 80 challenging levels in Work Mode. Your goal is to earn three yellow stars in each level, showcasing your skills and logic in an urban environment.

As you navigate the bustling city, follow blue direction indicators to locate passengers swiftly and drop them off at their desired destinations. It s essential to strategize your movements slow down before making turns to save precious time while ensuring a smooth ride for your customers. As you progress and accumulate wealth, don t forget to visit your garage where you can purchase up to nine additional cars, each offering unique advantages and enhancing your gameplay experience.

In Free Mode, the adventure expands as you take on the open road. Here, you can drive as far as you want, refueling at gas stations to keep your taxi operational. Continue to serve customers and enjoy the experience, all without the pressure of advancing levels. This non-linear approach allows you to play at your own pace while appreciating the small details in your virtual city. Remember to adhere to traffic regulations even the red lights to maintain a good reputation as a responsible taxi driver!

How to play free Taxi Driver Simulator game online

To begin playing Taxi Driver Simulator, simply select your desired game mode: Work Mode or Free Mode. Use the on-screen controls to navigate your taxi through the city, following the blue indicators to pick up and drop off passengers. Keep an eye on fuel levels and traffic rules for a smooth driving experience.
