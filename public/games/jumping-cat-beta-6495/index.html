Step into the whimsical world of Jumping Cat (Beta), where you embody an agile feline destined to outsmart pesky rats that threaten your peaceful existence! This thrilling adventure invites you to tap into your reflexes as you navigate a vibrant landscape filled with challenges. With intuitive touch screen controls, you can make your cat leap into the air, perform double jumps to dodge obstacles, and slide under barriers to keep moving forward.

Your mission is straightforward: survive and rack up points while evading those inevitable rat encounters. Each jump brings you closer to earning a new personal record, encouraging players to push their limits with every session. Jumping Cat is more than just a game; it's an exhilarating test of skill and strategy that keeps track of your best scores, fostering a competitive spirit among players. Plus, being a beta version means improvements and new features are on the horizon! Updates are planned every two weeks to enhance gameplay and introduce exciting new content.

Join the adventure today, and become one with your agile feline character. Whether you're looking for a quick replay or an extended gaming session, Jumping Cat (Beta) offers endless fun with its engaging mechanics and charming visuals. Ready to unleash your inner cat hero? Dive in and start your journey!

How to play free Jumping Cat (Beta) game online

To play Jumping Cat (Beta), simply tap the screen to make your cat jump over obstacles and rats. For a higher leap, perform a double jump by tapping twice in quick succession. If you encounter low barriers, slide by swiping down on the screen. Keep an eye on your score and try to beat your best record!
