Ghostly Spikes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This Halloween, prepare for a thrilling experience with Jhurr Games' unique offering: Ghostly Spikes. This whimsical yet challenging game invites players to guide an adorable ghost through a spooky realm filled with menacing spikes. Your mission is to help this charming specter soar through the air while deftly avoiding perilous obstacles that could spell doom at any moment.

As you navigate this bizarre and treacherous environment, the goal is simple: survive for as long as you can! The longer you keep your ghost afloat, the higher your score climbs. Challenge yourself to reach impressive heights, setting the bar high enough to entice your friends to take on the challenge of outdoing your scores. Ghostly Spikes guarantees a delightfully eerie adventure while you revel in the Halloween spirit.

With its silly humor and addictive gameplay, Ghostly Spikes is a fantastic way to enjoy the Halloween season. Whether you re a seasoned gamer or someone just looking to have some fun, this game offers an entertaining respite from the familiar scares of Halloween. So grab your friends, share your scores, and see who can master the flight of the ghostly hero!

For more information about Jhurr Games, visit their website at https://jhurr.com/.

How to play free Ghostly Spikes game online

To play Ghostly Spikes, simply use your mouse or keyboard to control the ghost's flying direction. Tap or click to ascend, and release to descend. Your goal is to navigate through the spikes without crashing, collecting points as you go. The longer you fly, the higher your score stay focused and have fun!
