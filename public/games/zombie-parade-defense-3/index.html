Zombie Parade Defense 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the latest installment of the Zombie Parade Defense series Zombie Parade Defense 3! This exciting chapter elevates the challenge as you face increasingly formidable waves of zombies. It s crucial this time around to cooperate with friends, so we highly recommend tackling the game in 2 or 3 player modes. Solo players should be cautious: avoid attacking the newly introduced medic characters, as they provide essential support to help your base endure the zombie onslaught.

As the tension builds with each wave, you ll need to strategize effectively. The new spider web defense system is at your disposal, allowing you to trap and immobilize your enemies perfect for buying some time. Work together to strengthen your defenses, coordinate attacks, and make sure to utilize the medic characters wisely. Successfully surviving all 10 waves will ensure your victory in this thrilling battle against the undead!

How to play free Zombie Parade Defense 3 game online

To play Zombie Parade Defense 3, start by selecting your player mode. Choose to play solo or invite friends for cooperative gameplay. Use your weapons strategically to fend off incoming waves of zombies while supporting and protecting your base. Remember to utilize the medic characters and spider web defenses for maximum survival chances.
