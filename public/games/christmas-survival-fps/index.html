How to play free Christmas Survival FPS game online

To play Christmas Survival FPS, simply join an online match or create your own. Once in-game, navigate using the WASD keys and aim with your mouse. Use your weapon to eliminate opponents while dodging incoming fire. Don't forget to visit the shop to upgrade your gear as you collect in-game currency!
