How to play free Balls Sorted game online

To play Balls Sorted, start by selecting a level. Tap on a ball to pick it up and then select the container where you wish to place it. Your goal is to group the balls by color into the designated containers. Keep sorting until all balls are in their correct places to successfully complete each level!
