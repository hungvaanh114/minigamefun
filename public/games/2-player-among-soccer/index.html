How to play free 2 Player Among Soccer game online

To play 2 Player Among Soccer, simply select the 2 Player option on the main menu. Choose your teams and set the match duration. Control your player using the keyboard or game controller to move, shoot, and pass the ball. Score more goals than your opponent before time runs out to win!
