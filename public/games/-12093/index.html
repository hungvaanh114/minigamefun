Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Slimoban V2 is an exciting sequel to the beloved Sokoban-inspired game, Slimoban. Embark on a thrilling journey with a courageous little girl as she navigates through a dungeon teeming with enormous and intimidating slimes. Each level presents unique challenges where your primary objective is to collect golden coins while avoiding the clutches of these sticky foes.

Throughout your adventure, you will come across magical potions that grant the girl the ability to lift heavy blocks and slimes. Mastering the art of lifting and strategically placing these objects is key to progressing through each level. Not only can you move blocks around, but you also have the option to drop them into water, providing an array of creative solutions to tackle each puzzle.

The game allows you to manipulate your environment by luring slimes into traps or using wooden boxes to fend them off. If you re clever, you can push slimes into water, effectively taking them out of the equation. Yet, while these tactics can make navigating the dungeon easier, the ultimate goal remains: collect those shiny coins to complete each level. Prepare to strategize and think ahead in Slimoban V2 as you face increasingly complex puzzles!

How to play free Slimoban V2 game online

To play Slimoban V2 online, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the girl around the dungeon. Collect magic potions to lift blocks and slimes, and maneuver strategically to avoid or trap the slimes. Focus on gathering the golden coins to clear each level.
