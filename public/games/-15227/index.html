Step into the thrilling world of Assassins Creed Freerunners, where you can take command of your most trusted Assassin and challenge other players in an exhilarating race across stunning landscapes. Join forces with 7 fellow Assassins as you dive into the animus, navigating obstacles and outmaneuvering your competition to reach the extraction point before anyone else.

This adrenaline-pumping multiplayer experience encourages you to showcase your parkour skills by climbing rooftops, leaping across walls, and utilizing the environment to gain an edge. Execute an extraordinary leap of faith to enhance your synchronization rates, unlocking the ability to summon iconic characters from the entire Assassin's Creed series. Each character brings unique abilities, allowing for a diverse range of strategies and gameplay styles.

With intricate level designs that challenge both your agility and quick thinking, every match promises to be a unique adventure as you battle it out to be the fastest Assassin. Engage with friends or join random players worldwide, forging alliances and rivalries in this fast-paced virtual competition. Explore various historical landscapes as you race, dodge, and leap your way to victory!

How to play free Assassins Creed Freerunners game online

To play Assassins Creed Freerunners, start by selecting your preferred Assassin character. Once the game begins, navigate the environment using your controls to run, climb, and jump. Keep an eye out for shortcuts and use your surroundings effectively to reach the extraction point faster than your opponents. Good luck!
