Castle Wars: Cell Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into an exciting strategy game! You will find a map divided into segments, full of obstacles and enemies of different levels. Use coins to place your heroes: warriors, mages, archers, dragons or catapults. Each character has unique abilities that will help in the fight against opponents. Earn coins by destroying enemies and barriers to buy new heroes and strengthen your army. The games special feature is the ability to combine characters of the same level, creating stronger fighters to defeat powerful opponents.

Instructions

Start by choosing a hero use coins to place warriors mages archers dragons or catapults on the map Drag characters to empty segments to block enemies and destroy obstacles
