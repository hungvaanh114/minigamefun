Corona Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Corona Defense, players are thrust into a world where the deadly COVID-19 virus threatens humanity. Armed with a powerful gun, your mission is straightforward yet crucial: defend the world from this unseen enemy. As an elite protector, you will face waves of viral foes that come at you relentlessly. The game features exhilarating levels packed with varying challenges, each designed to test your reflexes and shooting skills.

Throughout your journey, you will encounter different types of viruses, each with unique patterns and behavior. As you progress, your weapon can be upgraded, allowing you to increase your firepower and effectiveness against the relentless onslaught. Additionally, you can earn bonuses and unlock special abilities that will help you in your quest to safeguard the planet.

The graphics in Corona Defense are vibrant and engaging, immersing you in a gripping atmosphere as you fight for survival. The sound effects amplify the experience, providing the adrenaline rush you need to stay focused. With every successful wave defeated, players will feel a sense of accomplishment and motivation to push further.

Join the fight against COVID-19 and experience the endless fun of Corona Defense. Are you ready to step up and become a true hero in this battle for global health? Play now and prove your mettle!

How to play free Corona Defense game online

To play Corona Defense, use your mouse or touchpad to aim and fire your gun at the incoming viruses. Click to shoot while ensuring you dodge the enemies. Utilize power-ups collected throughout the game to enhance your abilities and survive longer against the viral waves. Good luck!
