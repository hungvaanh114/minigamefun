How to play free Ice Cream Maker game online

To play Ice Cream Maker online, simply select your ingredients from the menu and drag them into the mixing bowl. Once you've created your custom flavor, decorate your ice cream with toppings and serve it to your virtual customers. Complete orders quickly to earn points and unlock new recipes!
