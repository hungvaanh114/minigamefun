Xmas Presents Mahjong

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the festive spirit with Xmas Presents Mahjong, a delightful puzzle game that combines traditional Mahjong mechanics with a holiday-themed twist. In this engaging match-three game, your objective is to match three identical tiles to clear them from the board. With bright, colorful graphics and charming holiday icons, this game offers an entertaining way to celebrate the Christmas season. Gather your friends and family, and enjoy hours of fun as you immerse yourself in the joy of matching tiles and clearing the board.

As you play, you will find various festive-themed tiles featuring Christmas presents, ornaments, and more. The rules are simple yet addictive: just click on a tile to move it into the designated collection spots at the bottom of the screen. When you collect three tiles of the same type in these spots, they will be removed from play, bringing you closer to victory!

With a mix of strategy and quick reflexes, Xmas Presents Mahjong provides an engaging experience for both casual gamers and seasoned players. The game also features a relaxing soundtrack that will keep you in the holiday spirit as you progress through different levels. So, gather your holiday cheer and dive into the world of Mahjong like never before!

How to play free Xmas Presents Mahjong game online

To play the Xmas Presents Mahjong game, simply click on any tile to move it to the collection area below. Aim to gather three of the same tiles in your collection spots to clear them. Keep an eye on the board and strategize your moves wisely to maximize your score!
