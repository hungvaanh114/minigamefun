Soccer Flick the Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Soccer Flick The Ball is an essential game for any soccer lover or free-kick aficionado! This exciting football experience combines easy-to-learn gameplay with engaging visuals, set across five stunning environments. With arcade-style target practice, it offers a fantastic opportunity to test and hone your free kick and soccer skills. Best of all, you can enjoy this game free of charge, without needing to unlock any features!

The game introduces a variety of new designs, players, and beautifully crafted grounds, offering different times of day and weather conditions, including rain and snow. Players can look forward to an array of classic and professional footballs, as well as diverse player uniforms and animations. Your aim is to hit the target to score points, and by striking the bonus targets, you can earn delightful gifts. The fun is limitless, but the question remains: how long can you keep the excitement going?

How to play free Soccer Flick The Ball game online

To play Soccer Flick The Ball, simply aim your shot using your finger or mouse, then flick the ball towards the target. As you progress through the game, focus on hitting the main targets for points and try to reach the bonus targets for extra rewards. Keep practicing to improve your skills and enjoy the game!
