Drop the Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embrace the challenge presented by Drop The Numbers, an engaging puzzle game reminiscent of the highly acclaimed 2048. In this captivating game, your objective is to strategically arrange blocks inscribed with numbers on a grid. When you manage to align two blocks displaying the same digit, they merge to form a new block, the value of which is the result of combining the digits. This clever mechanic not only adds depth to your strategy but also keeps you glued to the screen as you aim for higher scores.

As you navigate through increasingly complex levels, you will have a preview of the upcoming number at the top of the game interface, allowing you to devise plans and tactics in advance. This foresight is crucial as it aids in optimizing your moves and prolonging your game time. The ultimate aim is to keep merging those numbers until you achieve the highest possible value on the board and perhaps even surpass your previous records!

Drop The Numbers is not just about speed; it's about effective planning, strategy, and a touch of luck. Each turn presents a new opportunity for you to refine your approach and increase your score. So, gather your wits and prepare for hours of fun and brain-teasing challenges as you dive into this addictive, number-crunching adventure!

How to play free Drop The Numbers game online

To play Drop The Numbers, simply start by moving the blocks on the grid. Use your mouse or keyboard to slide the blocks left, right, up, or down. Match two blocks with the same number to merge them into one, creating a higher value block. Aim to keep playing and merging to achieve the highest number possible!
