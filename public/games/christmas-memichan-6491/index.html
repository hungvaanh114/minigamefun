Christmas Memichan

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the festive world of Christmas Memichan, a delightful 2D platformer that combines the joy of Christmas with exhilarating gameplay. In this charming adventure, you take on the role of Memichan, a cat dressed as Santa. Your mission? Gather as many chocolate bars as possible while deftly navigating a series of challenging obstacles.

As you journey through each level, you'll encounter various types of enemy demon cats that threaten to thwart your progress. In addition to these pesky foes, you must beware of perilous spikes and menacing saw blades that can jeopardize your mission. Collecting chocolate bars not only boosts your score but also unlocks new levels filled with increased difficulty to keep you on your toes.

Christmas Memichan features eight exciting levels, each carefully crafted to test your skills and reflexes. With each stage you conquer, the challenges become more intricate, demanding precise timing and strategy to avoid dangers while racing against the clock. Can you reach your cozy cat house door at the end of each level? The combination of festive graphics, upbeat music, and engaging gameplay make for an enthralling experience that captures the spirit of the season.

How to play free Christmas Memichan game online

To play Christmas Memichan, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character and jump. Navigate through the levels, avoiding enemies and hazards while collecting chocolate bars. Reach the cat house door to complete each level and advance to the next. Enjoy the festive atmosphere and challenge yourself to gather all the treats!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
