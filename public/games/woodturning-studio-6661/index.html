Woodturning Studio

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the immersive world of Woodturning Studio, where your creativity knows no bounds. This captivating game allows you to turn a simple piece of wood into stunning art through the skilled use of various wood carving knives. Engage in the art of wood crafting as you experiment with shapes and designs that reflect your unique style.

Once you've shaped your wooden masterpiece, it's time to add your personal touch. Choose from a vibrant palette of colors to paint your creation, followed by a variety of varnish options to give it that perfect finish. The possibilities for customization are endless, allowing your imagination to flourish as you create breathtaking wood art.

When your artwork is complete, you have the option to save it directly to your phone or tablet gallery, ensuring you can showcase your talent and creativity wherever you go. Share your creations with friends or simply admire your own handcrafted pieces whenever you re in the mood for inspiration.

The game features intuitive controls that make it easy for anyone to pick up and enjoy. Simply swipe your finger to carve the wood and create remarkable shapes, while endless combinations of tools allow for a personalized crafting experience. With coloring, polishing, and matting tools at your disposal, every detail can be perfected.

How to play free Woodturning Studio game online

To play Woodturning Studio, start by selecting your wood piece. Use swipe gestures to carve it into your desired shape, then explore the color palette to pick your favorite hues. Apply varnish for a finishing touch, and once satisfied, save your artwork by selecting the save option to keep it in your gallery.
