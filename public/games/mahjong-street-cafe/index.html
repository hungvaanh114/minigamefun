Mahjong Street Cafe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the delightful world of Mahjong Street Cafe, a captivating puzzle game that invites you to immerse yourself in a vibrant atmosphere filled with tantalizing food-themed tiles. Your objective is straightforward but engaging: locate and match three identical food tiles to eliminate them from the board. The unique layout accommodates up to seven tiles at once, offering a dynamic gameplay experience that keeps you on your toes.

As you delve deeper into the game, you'll discover a variety of bonuses designed to enhance your gameplay. These three types of bonuses can be strategically employed to simplify challenges, allowing you to clear the board more efficiently or uncover hidden tiles. Utilizing these power-ups at the right moment can mean the difference between victory and defeat, adding an exciting layer of strategy to each session.

The charming visuals and captivating soundtrack of Mahjong Street Cafe transport you to a cozy cafe setting, making each round a delightful escape from the everyday grind. With each level presenting unique challenges, players of all skill levels will find enjoyment and satisfaction as they master the art of tile matching. Gather your friends or go solo, and see who can reach the highest score in this engaging game.

How to play free Mahjong Street Cafe game online

To play Mahjong Street Cafe, start by selecting your desired level. Once the game begins, click on any food tile to reveal its match. Aim to find three identical tiles to clear them from the board. Utilize the bonuses wisely to aid your progress and manage the tiles efficiently. Happy playing!

Match three of the same tiles Click on a tile to move it to the collection spots at the bottom If you have collected three of the same tiles there they will be removed
