Makeup Kit DIY Dress Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the delightful world of cosmetics with the Makeup Kit DIY Dress Up game! This engaging DIY experience offers an opportunity to create an extensive range of cosmetics, including lipsticks, cream eyeshadows, mascaras, and much more. Each new update brings even more exciting cosmetic-making activities, ensuring that you ll never run out of fun!

You'll have access to a variety of realistic cosmetic-making tools to enhance your gameplay. Use bowls, spatulas, spoons, and even a microwave oven to mix and create your personalized beauty products. With the help of plates, digital scales, measuring cups, and an induction cooker, your homemade cosmetics will achieve professional-quality results.

Get ready to experiment with an array of cosmetic materials and decorations. Choose from cosmetic mica powder, glitters, and liquid pigments, alongside nourishing oils like coconut, castor, sweet almond, and avocado. Combine these ingredients with wax to concoct the perfect formulas for your beauty creations! Each session offers endless possibilities to express your creativity.

Once you ve crafted your unique cosmetics, it s time to apply them to your supermodel! Choose the perfect shades and styles to create a standout look, then capture the moment by taking a photo of your masterpiece. Share your creations with friends and fellow beauty enthusiasts, and continue experimenting in this infinite world of DIY glamour!

How to play free Makeup Kit DIY Dress Up game online

In the game, start by selecting the cosmetic you want to create. Gather the necessary tools and ingredients from the virtual kitchen. Follow the simple instructions for mixing and combining the materials. Once your cosmetic is ready, apply it to the supermodel and take stunning photos of the final look!
