Monster of Eyes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chilling world of Monster Of Eyes, a spine-tingling horror game that immerses players in a thrilling adventure through a haunted mansion. In this free-to-play experience, you and a friend find yourselves drawn into an ominous mansion, rumored to be home to a terrifying creature lurking within its dark corridors.

Your journey begins on a stormy night as you and your companion stumble upon the eerie mansion. Ignoring the whispers of caution, you both decide to venture inside, eager to unveil its hidden mysteries. But as you step into the mansion, a pair of sinister red eyes fixates on you, setting off a chase that will have you questioning every shadow and creak.

As you navigate the maze-like interior, the atmosphere thickens with tension. You must solve various puzzles and gather clues hidden throughout the mansion while evading the relentless pursuit of the eye monster. With terrifying jump scares and ghostly whispers echoing around every corner, one wrong move could spell disaster.

Can you muster the courage to uncover the truth behind the terrifying rumors of this haunted place? Monster Of Eyes promises a heart-pounding experience that combines elements of survival horror and mystery-solving. Be sure to tread carefully this game is not for the faint of heart, especially if you choose to play alone in the dark.

How to play free Monster Of Eyes game online

To navigate the game, use your keyboard or controller to move around the mansion while searching for clues and solving puzzles. Stay alert for the red-eyed monster s presence, and utilize stealth to avoid detection. Work together with your friend to escape and uncover the mysteries hidden within the mansion.
