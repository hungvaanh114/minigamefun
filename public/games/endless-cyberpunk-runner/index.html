Endless Cyberpunk Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

An exciting endless runner set in a futuristic cyberpunk world. Player must navigate through neon-lit streets, avoiding robot enemies and dodging their bullets while collecting coins to rack up a higher score. The ultimate goal is to achieve the highest score possible while surviving as long as you can in this fast-paced, action-packed challenge.
