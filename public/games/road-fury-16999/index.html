Get ready to hit the asphalt in Road Fury, the adrenaline-pumping action game that has you tearing down highways at breakneck speeds! Prepare to unleash your inner rage as you shoot down everything in sight, from rival cars to pesky obstacles that dare to get in your way. How to play free Road Fury game online

To play Road Fury, use the arrow keys to steer your car while pressing the spacebar to fire your weapons. Navigate through traffic, collect power-ups, and defeat bosses that appear throughout your journey. The faster you react and shoot, the higher your score will be!
