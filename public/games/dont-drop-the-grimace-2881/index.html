Dont Drop the Grimace!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Celebrate Grimace's special day in the fun-filled game, Don't Drop The Grimace!. In this delightful adventure, your main objective is to prevent Grimace from taking a tumble while he indulges in his favorite treat milkshakes! As Grimace happily bounces, make sure you catch all the milkshakes that come flying his way to ensure he has the best birthday bash ever. With each shake you catch, you re not just aiding Grimace; you're also accumulating points that can help you decorate him with fabulous bling!

Feel the festive spirit as you sing along to the catchy "Happy Birthday Grimace" song, making this experience even more enjoyable. The heartwarming tunes combined with the delightful visuals make it a perfect game for players of all ages. With vibrant graphics and an engaging premise, Don't Drop The Grimace! promises to be both entertaining and uplifting. So gather your friends and family, and enjoy a unique birthday celebration that you can partake in from the comfort of your home!

How to play free Don't Drop The Grimace! game online

To play Don't Drop The Grimace!, use your mouse or touchscreen to catch the falling milkshakes while keeping Grimace safe. Be quick and alert, as the shakes will come down faster as you progress. Remember to collect special items that add bling to Grimace and earn bonus points. Enjoy!


Instructions

Tap Grimace to keep him in the air And tap all the birthday milkshakes that appear
