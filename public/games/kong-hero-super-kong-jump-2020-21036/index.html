Kong Hero Super Kong Jump 2020

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Kong Hero Super Kong Jump 2020, take on the role of Mono, a brave hero on a quest to save his brother and protect the world from giant invaders. Set in the vibrant and lush landscapes of the Banana Forest, this exhilarating platformer game challenges you to navigate through various obstacles while confronting powerful enemies. As you jump and run through numerous thrilling levels, you ll encounter hidden areas teeming with treasures and surprises.

As Mono embarks on his adventure, he quickly learns that giant invaders have come from outer worlds, wreaking havoc and enslaving the earth's animals. As a member of the Monkey Bros clan, Mono is determined to thwart their plans and restore peace to the land. With the help of his brilliant yet quirky sidekick, Doctor, players must strategize and utilize unique abilities to outsmart these formidable foes and drive them away from their home planet.

Discover breathtaking settings filled with colorful environments, from dense forests to ancient ruins, as you leap through the air, ensuring Mono's swift journey towards adventure and victory. Collect bananas and power-ups along the way to enhance Mono's skills and push through challenging levels that test your agility and reflexes. Are you ready to join Mono in his mission to save his brother and bring down the evil invaders? It's time to jump into action!

How to play free Kong Hero Super Kong Jump 2020 game online

To play Kong Hero Super Kong Jump 2020, use the on-screen controls to guide Mono through the levels. Tap to jump over obstacles, and initiate special moves by performing combos. Collect bananas for upgrades and power-ups, ensuring you defeat enemies and locate your brother in your quest to save the world!
