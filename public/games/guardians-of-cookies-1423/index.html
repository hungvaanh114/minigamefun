Guardians of Cookies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Guardians of Cookies", players dive into an engaging battle against an army of relentless ants set on raiding your precious cookie stash. With quick reflexes and strategic upgrades, your mission is to fend off these tiny invaders and safeguard your delectable treats. The gameplay revolves around tapping on these pesky ants to eliminate them before they can reach your cookies.

As you crush ants, you'll collect coins that can be utilized to enhance your character's various attributes. These upgrades are crucial; improving your health ensures you can withstand more damage, while increases in defense help mitigate the attacks from the swarm. Enhancing your attack power will allow you to take down the ants more efficiently, and expanding your resource gathering radius means you can collect more coins at once. Every upgrade is an essential step towards successfully defending your cookie kingdom and prolonging your survival in the game.

Your ultimate goal is to last as long as possible against waves of ant attacks. The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes as the number and speed of the ants increase. Strategize your upgrades wisely and keep your fingers nimble to secure the top score and become the ultimate guardian of cookies.

How to play free Guardians of Cookies game online

To play "Guardians of Cookies", simply click on the ants to destroy them before they can grab your cookies. Use the coins earned from your victories to upgrade health, defense, attack, and resource-gathering radius to enhance your survivability. Keep your focus, strategize your upgrades, and aim for a high score!
