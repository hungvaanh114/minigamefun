Kids Hospital Doctor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Kids Hospital Doctor is an engaging and educational game designed for young players who aspire to be doctors. In this delightful adventure, you join your friend Hippo on a series of fun medical missions. Previously, you've traveled to the airport, visited the dentist, and created wonderful photo albums. Now, it's time to don your scrubs and step into the role of a skilled surgeon!

In today s hospital scenario, you will encounter three charming patients needing your medical expertise. The first patient is a tall giraffe who requires an eye examination. Next, you'll meet a little raccoon suffering from seasonal allergies, and finally, there's a little hippo with a stomach ache that needs your immediate attention. Your objective is to carefully tap through the game and follow interactive prompts to diagnose and treat each patient, ensuring they leave the hospital happy and healthy!

This fun and educational game not only provides an enjoyable experience for kids but also encourages teamwork. Invite your friends to join you in this exciting medical adventure and challenge each other to see who can be the best doctor! The more, the merrier in this vibrant healthcare setting where learning and fun go hand in hand!

How to play free Kids Hospital Doctor game online

Once you enter the Kids Hospital Doctor game, simply follow the on-screen instructions. Use your mouse or finger to interact with various medical tools and equipment. Carefully examine each patient and apply the right treatments as prompted. Keep a lookout for any special mini-games for additional fun!
