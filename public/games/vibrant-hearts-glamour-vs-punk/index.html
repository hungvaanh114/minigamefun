Vibrant Hearts Glamour vs Punk

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Two BFF, Lily and Roxana, are striking opposites. Lily is the personification of tenderness and romance, dressed in airy pink dresses decorated with sequins and floral patterns. Her style radiates light and warmth. On the other hand, Roxanne is the true queen of punk rock, with her black leather dresses, gothic earrings and bold hairstyles. But do not forget about the third girlfriend, Melody, who masterfully combines both styles, creating unique images where glamour meets punk. Players will be able to experiment with outfits, creating their own styles, and choose which one is best suited for Valentines Day celebrations!

Instructions

Depending on the gaming device a computer mouse click or a simple touch on touchscreens is used to control it The most successful images can be saved at the end of the game as PNG images
