Welcome to the exhilarating world of the Car Wash Garage Service Workshop! While it may sound like a typical car washing simulation, this game actually immerses you in a thrilling car parking simulation experience powered by an advanced 3D game engine. Your primary challenge is to navigate the busy streets and ensure your passengers reach their destinations promptly.

As you embark on your journey, keep an eye on the road conditions. Avoiding the muddy roads is crucial, as it could lead to a messy car and delay your service. Your goal is to pick up passengers and transport them efficiently to earn cash rewards. The excitement intensifies when you realize that you can enhance your performance by spending a portion of your earnings to clean your car at the garage service; a thorough wash costs 600 dollars!

This engaging game not only tests your driving skills but also challenges your ability to strategize and manage resources. The more passengers you transport successfully, the more money you accumulate to keep your vehicle looking pristine. So get ready to hit the streets, dodge the mud, and provide an impeccable service experience in the Car Wash Garage Service Workshop!

How to play free Car Wash Garage Service Workshop game online

To play the Car Wash Garage Service Workshop game, simply navigate the streets, pick up passengers, and safely drive them to their destinations. Avoid muddy paths to keep your car clean, and utilize the garage service when needed for a wash. Aim for efficient routes to maximize your earnings!
