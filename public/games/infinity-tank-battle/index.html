Infinity Tank Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Infinity Tank Battle brings a modern twist to the classic tank battle genre that has captivated gamers since the era of 8-bit consoles. This brand new game marries nostalgic features with innovative gameplay elements, creating a thrilling experience for both old fans and new players alike. With an expansive total of 610 meticulously designed maps, players will find themselves immersed in a diverse range of environments, each presenting unique challenges and opportunities.

The core rule of Infinity Tank Battle is simple yet engaging: defend your base while systematically destroying all enemy tanks. As you navigate through different maps, you'll encounter a variety of enemy types, each with their own tactics, forcing you to adapt your strategies swiftly. Whether it's a quick skirmish or an all-out warfare, every match is packed with excitement and strategic depth.

One of the standout features of Infinity Tank Battle is the introduction of special items to enhance gameplay. These items can provide advantages in battles, whether it's a power-up for your tank or a tool to sabotage your foes. Additionally, players can utilize the auto helper tank to support their defenses, making gameplay more dynamic and enjoyable. Another highlight is the inclusion of legendary maps from Super Tank Battle 500, allowing players to relive classic moments while exploring new challenges.

How to play free Infinity Tank Battle game online

To play Infinity Tank Battle, simply select your tank and choose from the myriad of maps available. Control your tank with the on-screen joystick, aiming carefully to destroy enemy tanks while protecting your base. Utilize special items and your auto helper tank effectively to outsmart opponents and achieve victory!
