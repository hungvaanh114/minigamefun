Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a unique shopping experience with the captivating Shopaholic Black Friday game! Join the fabulous Selena Gomez on her thrilling shopping expedition filled with trendy outfits and the latest makeup styles. This online dress-up game takes you to a bustling mall where you can explore an extensive range of chic fashion items.

As you dive into the gameplay, your mission is to shop for as many glamorous pieces as possible before the clock ticks down. Navigate through an impressive selection of makeup options that allows you to flaunt stunning lipstick colors and experiment with alluring eye shadows, whether you prefer a classic smoky eye or vibrant shades. The choices are endless, letting your creativity soar!

In addition to makeup, the game showcases a myriad of fashionable apparel, from elegant dresses and stylish tops to the perfect pair of shoes. Don t forget to accessorize! Complete each look with eye-catching jewelry and designer handbags to elevate your outfit. As you race against time, the thrill of finding the best deals will keep you on your toes!

How to play free Shopaholic Black Friday game online

To play the Shopaholic Black Friday game, simply log into the game and select Selena as your shopping avatar. Explore the mall, choosing items by clicking on them. Once you collect enough clothing and accessories, move to the styling area to mix and match your finds. Enjoy the hunt!
