Sniper Master City Hunter Shooting Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Sniper Master City Hunter, where your skills as a sharpshooter will be put to the ultimate test. As the guardian of the city, it's your duty to eliminate threats lurking in the shadows. The evil forces have employed a cunning group of snipers, and they are ready to ambush you at any moment. Stay vigilant and prepare for intense encounters as you fight to uphold justice and maintain order.

In this action-packed shooting game, you'll engage in tactical gameplay, navigating through various urban landscapes while strategically taking down enemy snipers. Utilize your surroundings to your advantage, master your sniper rifle, and make every shot count. Precision and patience are key as you identify the lurking foes and neutralize them before they can unleash their deadly intentions upon the city.

As you progress through the game, you'll face increasingly difficult challenges, including diverse enemy types and intricate maps. Sharpen your reflexes and display your sharpshooting abilities as you conquer each mission. The immersive graphics and realistic sound effects will draw you deeper into the intense storyline, making every decision critical. Are you ready to uphold justice against those who seek chaos in the city?

How to play free Sniper Master City Hunter shooting game online

To play Sniper Master City Hunter, use your mouse to aim and adjust your sniper scope. Click to shoot and take out enemies while staying hidden. Pay attention to your surroundings, and be quick to adapt your strategy for each level as enemy snipers will be lurking, ready to attack.


Instructions

Use keyboard arrows to control player and left mouse button for shoot and right mouse button for focus
