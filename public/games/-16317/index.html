Driv? Taxi?Driv? Taxi

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the vibrant world of Driv? Taxi, you step into the shoes of a skilled taxi driver, navigating through bustling city streets. Your primary mission is to pick up a variety of passengers and ensure they reach their desired destinations safely and on time. The fast-paced environment keeps you on your toes as you must balance efficiency with caution. As you zip through the traffic, remember that your passengers are counting on you!

The game features an engaging mix of strategy and reflexes. While you dash around the city, you'll encounter other vehicles on the road that pose challenges to your driving skills. It's essential to avoid collisions, as crashing into other cars can slow you down and affect your passengers' satisfaction. Challenge yourself to master the art of maneuvering through tight spots and busy intersections while keeping an eye out for your next fare.

Customize your taxi, enhance its performance, and unlock new areas as you progress. Each level introduces new obstacles and a variety of passenger requests, making every drive a fresh adventure. Will you satisfy your customers and earn a reputation as the best taxi driver in town? With quick reflexes and smart decision-making, you can conquer the streets and make every ride an unforgettable experience!

How to play free Driv? Taxi game online

Once you enter the Driv? Taxi game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to drive your taxi. Navigate the city, pick up passengers highlighted on your map, and safely transport them to their destinations. Keep an eye on the road to dodge other cars and avoid accidents for the best scores!
