Pong Ball Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pong Ball Master, an exhilarating and colorful challenge that tests your reflexes and quick thinking! In this engaging game, you are tasked with maintaining the harmony of bouncing balls. As a vibrant ball continually travels across the screen, your objective is to ensure that it never collides with the stationary balls of a different color. The rules could not be simpler, yet the competition steadily heightens as you strive for the highest possible score.

Your journey in Pong Ball Master encourages focus and precision. As the colored balls roll and bounce at a breathtaking pace, it s your responsibility to toggle the rows of colored balls at the top or bottom of the screen. Each time the moving ball strikes a ball confirming to its color, you score points. However, if it hits a mismatched color ball, your game ends. The dynamic gameplay demands your attention and swift reactions, making it a test of skill and timing.

One of the most exciting features is the ability to track your progress. As you play, your best scores get recorded, allowing you to notice improvements in your gameplay over time. Will you rise among the leaderboard and show off your Pong Ball Master prowess? The fun never stops, and with constant practice, you can achieve remarkable mastery!

How to play free Pong Ball Master game online

To play Pong Ball Master, use your finger or mouse to move the top and bottom rows of balls. Match the moving ball s color to the stationary balls to score points. Avoid mismatches to keep the game going and aim to beat your high scores!
