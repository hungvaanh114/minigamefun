Baby Hazel Learns Shapes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Baby Hazel Learns Shapes! In this charming educational game, your little one can join Baby Hazel on an exciting journey of discovery as she explores different shapes. Home is regarded as the first school for children, and what better way to foster learning than through engaging and fun activities?

As Baby Hazel grows, she needs to grasp essential concepts to become more intelligent. This game offers an interactive experience where kids can help Hazel learn about shapes while indulging in playful tasks. Start by solving a captivating puzzle game with Hazel, where you need to place different shapes in their correct positions. This engaging activity helps develop problem-solving skills and shape recognition.

Once you've tackled the puzzles, get ready to enjoy some baking fun! Join Baby Hazel as she bakes delicious cookies in various shapes. This delightful activity not only emphasizes shape identification but also introduces a dash of creativity and culinary skills. Moving on, assist Hazel in making a beautiful necklace by affixing beads of different shapes. This crafting task will ignite imaginations and encourage fine motor skills.

Lastly, take a stroll in the garden with Baby Hazel and help her identify playground equipment of various shapes. This outdoor adventure reinforces the learning process while promoting active play. With so many fun-filled activities, Baby Hazel Learns Shapes is the perfect game for young learners to enjoy while acquiring valuable knowledge.

How to play free Baby Hazel Learns Shapes game online

To play Baby Hazel Learns Shapes, simply use your mouse or touch screen to interact with the items on the screen. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete tasks such as solving puzzles, baking cookies, and crafting jewelry. Engage with each activity and watch Baby Hazel grow smarter with every shape she learns!
