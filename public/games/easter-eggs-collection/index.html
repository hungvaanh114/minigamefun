Easter Eggs Collection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join in the festive fun with the Easter Eggs Collection game, a vibrant and engaging HTML5 experience where you can hunt for colorful Easter eggs! Simply click on any egg to kickstart the excitement. Your objective is to create matches by connecting at least three identical eggs that are placed in close proximity whether that's horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally.

As you explore this delightful game, you'll discover that collecting each 6th egg rewards you with a special bonus, while gathering more than 7 eggs grants you extra time to keep the festivities going! Keep your eyes on the timer, as you must collect the required eggs within the designated time limit to secure your victory. The challenge escalates as you progress through multiple levels; how high can you climb? Test your skills and strategy as you strive to reach the ultimate level!

The Easter Eggs Collection game is a perfect blend of fun and challenge, ensuring a captivating experience for players of all ages. Ready to embark on this egg-citing adventure? Let the egg hunt begin!

How to play free Easter Eggs Collection game online

To play the Easter Eggs Collection game, simply click on any egg, then drag your mouse or finger to adjacent identical eggs to select them. Aim to connect at least three eggs, and release the button to make them match. Stay alert and collect the required eggs before time runs out!
