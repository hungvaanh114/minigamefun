Sky Corona Evasion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sky Corona Evasion is an engaging mobile game developed by jojolife that thrusts players into a high-stakes aerial adventure. , this game combines the fast-paced elements of an endless runner with the precision and challenge of flight navigation. Players control an airplane, navigating through a sky filled with various obstacles and virus-like enemies, symbolizing the titular Corona. The primary objective is to achieve the highest possible distance without crashing, demanding quick reflexes and strategic maneuvering.
