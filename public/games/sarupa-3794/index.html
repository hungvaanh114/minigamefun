SARUPA

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing SARUPA, an exciting three-match puzzle game that invites players to engage with its charmingly vibrant graphics and addictive gameplay. In SARUPA, your mission is simple yet entertaining: align three monkeys of the same color in a row to make them vanish from the screen. Each time you successfully match a trio, more monkeys cascade down from the tree, setting the stage for further exciting challenges.

As you progress through the various levels, you'll notice an increase in both the variety and the difficulty of the monkeys that fall. The game keeps you on your toes as it introduces new colors and combinations, meaning no two rounds are ever the same. The puzzle mechanics are designed to stimulate your brain while providing endless hours of fun, making SARUPA an excellent choice for casual gamers and dedicated puzzle enthusiasts alike.

For those seeking an even greater challenge, the Difficult play mode offers a thrilling twist: two monkeys drop at once! This feature not only raises the stakes but also enhances your opportunity to create impressive chain reactions, leading to higher scores and an exhilarating gaming experience. With its simple yet captivating premise, SARUPA is sure to become a favorite pastime for players of all ages.

How to play free SARUPA game online

To play SARUPA, simply click on the monkeys to swap their positions and match three or more of the same color. Aim for strategic combinations to clear the screen and create chain reactions. As you advance, keep an eye out for new monkey types and adapt your strategy to tackle the increasing difficulty levels!
