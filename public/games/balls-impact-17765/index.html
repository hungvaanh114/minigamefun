How to play free Balls Impact game online

To play Balls Impact, simply click your mouse to release the balls. Adjust the angle and direction of your shot by dragging your cursor before releasing. Aim for targets to collect stars and points, and keep practicing to enhance your skills throughout the various challenging levels!
