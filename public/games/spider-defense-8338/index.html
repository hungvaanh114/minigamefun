Spider Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating challenge in Spider Defense, a thrilling tower defense game that pits you against an army of giant mutant spiders threatening your town. As the hired exterminator, your mission is clear: protect the innocent villagers at all costs! These monstrous arachnids are not just any pests; they have been mutated, growing to terrifying sizes and wreaking havoc on your community.

Your ultimate goal is to strategically place soldiers along the pathways leading into town, identifying the best positions for maximum effectiveness. Each well-placed soldier will help eliminate the approaching spiders before they can reach the villagers. However, you must act quickly! Each spider you fail to stop threatens to claim innocent lives, and once the count hits 30 villagers lost, it's game over.

As you progress through the levels, expect to encounter increasingly challenging waves of mutant spiders, each more menacing than the last. Utilize your resources wisely, upgrade your soldiers, and employ tactical strategies to outsmart the relentless enemy. The fate of your town lies in your hands, and every decision counts. Are you up to the challenge?

How to play free Spider Defense game online

To play Spider Defense, start by placing soldiers on the clear paths where spiders will approach. Click on a soldier to upgrade or reposition if necessary. Keep an eye on the number of villagers; once you lose 30, the game ends. Strategically plan your moves to protect the town and prevent the invasion!
