Makeup Doll Creator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of the Makeup Doll Creator, where creativity knows no bounds! In this delightful game, you ll embark on a journey to transform a stunning yet dirty-faced doll into a fabulous fashion icon. It s time to unleash your inner beauty guru and fashion designer!

First things first, our adorable doll requires a thorough clean-up to wash away all that dirt and grime. With just a few clicks, you can restore her radiant complexion and prepare her for a glamorous makeover! Employ a variety of cleaning tools and watch as her face transforms, becoming the perfect canvas for your artistic vision.

After her face is primed, dive into the world of makeup! Choose from an array of options including stylish eyebrows, vibrant eye shadows, trendy color contacts, and luscious lipsticks. Mix and match until you create an eye-catching look that highlights your doll s beauty. The power to create a unique makeup style lies entirely in your hands!

But the fun doesn t stop at makeup! Once her face is beautifully adorned, it s time to select the perfect outfit. Will she strut her stuff in a charming skirt or a fashionable dress? Put your fabulous matching skills to the test as you curate the most stylish and adorable outfits that bring your makeup doll s personality to life!

How to play free Makeup Doll Creator game online

To start playing, simply select the cleaning tools to wash your doll's face thoroughly. Once cleaned, navigate through the makeup menu to apply eye shadows, lipsticks, and more. Finally, access the wardrobe to pick the ultimate outfit by combining different pieces until you find the perfect match!
