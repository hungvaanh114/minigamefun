Office Horror Story

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the chilling world of Office Horror Story, where the mundane setting of an office transforms into a nightmarish landscape. Players will embark on a spine-tingling journey through the eerie corridors, treacherous stairs, and dark basement of the office building. As you navigate through 16 chilling levels, uncover the terrifying tale of the infamous Mad Killer that lurks within the shadows.

Equipped with two powerful melee weapons, you will face your fears head-on and defend against the lurking dangers. Each level presents challenging puzzles that require keen observation and critical thinking to solve. As you search for essential objects scattered throughout the environments, your resourcefulness will determine your survival. If you have a passion for horror games, Office Horror Story will grip your imagination and test your nerves.

The game combines an unnerving atmosphere with engaging gameplay mechanics, offering a unique blend of fright and excitement. Prepare yourself for unexpected twists and turns as the story unfolds, leading you deeper into the horrors of the office. Can you unveil the mystery behind the Mad Killer and escape the nightmare? Only time will tell in this heart-pounding adventure.

How to play free Office Horror Story game online

To start playing Office Horror Story online, simply navigate to the game s website and click on the Play button. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character, and interact with objects by pressing the appropriate action key. Solve puzzles, and explore to uncover clues while avoiding the Mad Killer.
