Polywar 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Polywar 2 plunges players into the heart of the chaos that is World War II. As a dedicated member of the Allied Forces, your mission is clear: liberate the territories that have been overrun by Nazi forces. The battlefield is unforgiving, and every moment counts as you gear up to confront the enemy head-on.

In Polywar 2, you will engage in adrenaline-pumping firefights, using a variety of weapons at your disposal to take down as many Nazis as possible. Whether you prefer the accuracy of a sniper rifle or the brute force of a submachine gun, the choice is yours. But the battle doesn t stop there; you also have the opportunity to jump behind the controls of a powerful battle tank. This heavy machinery allows you to unleash devastation on enemy positions, making for a thrilling gameplay experience where strategy and quick reflexes are vital.

Your success hinges on completing the missions assigned by your commander. Each objective is designed to challenge your skills and enhance your tactical approach. Whether it's securing a location, rescuing captives, or laying waste to enemy strongholds, every mission contributes to the ultimate goal of liberation. So, suit up, take aim, and prepare for an epic journey of valor and teamwork in Polywar 2. Good luck, soldier!

How to play free Polywar 2 game online

To play Polywar 2, navigate the battlefield using your keyboard for movement and your mouse to aim and shoot. Select your weapon from the inventory menu, and follow the mission objectives visible on your screen. Teamwork is essential; communicate with allies to strategize and overcome enemy forces effectively.


Instructions

WASD or arrow keys to move Left click or Z to shoot Right click or X to throw a grenade Space bar to jump Left Shift or C to activate hammer time E to enter a vehicle
