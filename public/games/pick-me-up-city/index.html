Pick Me Up City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to put your driving skills to the test in Pick Me Up City! Step into the role of a professional car driver and embark on an exciting journey through the bustling streets of New York City. In this immersive taxi driving experience, your main objective is to pick up passengers and deliver them to their desired destinations. The more clients you successfully transport, the more cash you ll earn, allowing you to unlock new vehicles and upgrade your driving capabilities.

As you navigate through the vibrant landscapes of Pick Me Up City, you'll encounter a variety of challenges that will put your abilities to the test. From rush hour traffic to navigating narrow alleyways, each ride brings new experiences. Enjoy the thrill of the open road as you explore the wonders of the city and meet diverse characters along the way. If you ve played numerous car pick-me-up themed games before, you ll find that this one stands out as a top contender in the genre. The engaging gameplay combined with high-quality graphics makes for an unforgettable taxi driving adventure.

Whether you re a seasoned gamer or a newcomer, Pick Me Up City offers an enjoyable gaming experience packed with fun and excitement. So, start your engine, grab hold of the steering wheel, and get ready to take on the city as you master the art of pick-ups and drop-offs!

How to play free Pick Me Up City game online

To start playing Pick Me Up City, simply choose your car and drive through the city to locate passengers. Once you pick them up, follow the map to their destination and make sure to avoid obstacles and traffic to ensure a smooth ride. Enjoy the thrill of earning cash as you complete each drop-off!


Instructions

- Tap and hold to drive - Release to brake - Avoid crashing - Collect cash and unlock cool cars
