Animal Simulator Truck Transport 2020

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the excitement of heavy vehicle operation in Animal Simulator Truck Transport 2020! This engaging game caters to fans of truck, cargo, and bus driving simulations by offering a unique twist: animal transport. As a skilled driver, your mission is to load your truck with various animals, including cattle, farm animals, and wild creatures like cows, bulls, horses, zebras, lions, foxes, and elephants. Your goal is to safely deliver these animals to the zoo while ensuring their comfort throughout the journey.

Your adventure begins with managing your cargo truck and carefully placing each animal into transport cages. Once everything is securely loaded onto the truck bed, you'll navigate through challenging terrains and realistic environments. Feel the thrill of heavy-duty driving as you encounter obstacles and road conditions that test your skills. Precision and strategy are key as you maneuver the truck to maintain a steady speed while avoiding any bumps that could jeopardize the safety of your precious cargo.

Engaging graphics and immersive gameplay bring the wild to life, making each transport mission an exhilarating experience. Players will enjoy not only the trucking aspect but also the challenge of animal welfare, ensuring that each creature arrives at the zoo healthy and happy. Are you ready for the ultimate transport challenge? Hop into your truck, fasten your seatbelt, and let s hit the road for a wild adventure!

How to play free Animal Simulator Truck Transport 2020 game online

To play Animal Simulator Truck Transport 2020, start by selecting your truck and load it with animals in the farm or wild section. Once loaded, drive carefully through the specified routes, paying attention to traffic rules. Use the steering controls to navigate, accelerate, and brake smoothly as you transport the animals to the zoo. Complete each level by successfully unloading your cargo!
