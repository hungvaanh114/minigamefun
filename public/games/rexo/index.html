Rexo

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rexo is an exciting 2D platformer that immerses players in a vibrant world filled with challenges and treasures. In this game, you take control of a charming cube, embarking on a quest to gather an assortment of dazzling gems scattered across various levels. Each level is meticulously designed, presenting not just gems to collect, but also a range of obstacles that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking.

Your primary objective is to collect every gem in each stage, which will activate a transformation of the red flag at the end of the level into a green one. This color change signifies that you have successfully cleared the level and can advance to the next challenge. However, beware! Throughout your journey, you will encounter spikes and a variety of enemies determined to halt your progress. Navigating through these perilous hurdles requires skill and precision, making every moment in Rexo thrilling.

Rexo is structured across 8 unique levels, each progressively increasing in difficulty. As you dive deeper into the game, expect to face more intricate traps, smarter enemies, and demanding platforming sections that will keep you engaged and coming back for more. The vibrant visuals and fluid gameplay mechanics ensure that every jump and movement feels satisfying, making it a delightful experience for fans of platforming games.

How to play free Rexo game online

To play Rexo, use the arrow keys or the 'WASD' keys on your keyboard for movement. Guide your cube through each level, collecting gems while avoiding spikes and enemy threats. Once all gems are collected, reach the flag to complete the level and unlock the next one!


Instructions

Use Arrow keys to control the movements of the Cube Press Up arrow key twice to Double Jump If you are using a mobile you can use the movement touch buttons on the screen to play the game
