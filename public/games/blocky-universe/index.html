Blocky Universe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the vibrant realm of Blocky Universe, where monstrous foes lurk around every corner! Embark on an exhilarating adventure equipped with your trusty bow and axe, as you face waves of menacing zombies, skeletons, and a variety of other enemies. To rise to the challenge and secure your victory, you must evolve and strengthen your skills!

Your journey begins with cutting down trees to gather vital resources. This not only enhances your abilities as an archer but also helps you log wood at an astonishing speed. The more wood you collect, the more progress you'll make. By investing your hard-earned coins, you can upgrade your woodcutting efficiency, ultimately transforming into a formidable combination of an expert lumberjack and an elite marksman!

As you navigate through this blocky world, you'll encounter powerful bosses that pose a significant challenge. Each confrontation will test your skills and strategy, and only by mastering both your axe and bow will you prevail. Your ultimate goal is to defeat these bosses, repair the mystical portal that links worlds, and make your way back home. Will you rise to the occasion and conquer the chaos of Blocky Universe?

How to play free Blocky Universe game online

To play Blocky Universe, use the arrow keys to navigate your character through the world. Gather wood by clicking on trees, and upgrade your skills with the coins you collect by defeating enemies. Engage enemies using your bow to shoot and your axe for close combat. Strategize your upgrades and aim to defeat bosses to unlock the portal!
