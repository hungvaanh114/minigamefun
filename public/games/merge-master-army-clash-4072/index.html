How to play free Merge Master: Army Clash game online

In the game, use your mouse or touch controls to navigate the battlefield. Collect soldiers by moving them towards each other to merge and create stronger units. Avoid obstacles while strategically forming a powerful army, and lead your troops to defeat the enemy forces efficiently.
