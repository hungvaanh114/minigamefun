Frisbee 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Frisbee 3D! In this engaging game, you will take on the challenge of launching your frisbee into the sky. Simply tap or click on your screen to send your frisbee soaring, and use the swiping motion left or right to steer it as it glides through the air. Your primary objective is to navigate through a series of circles while skillfully avoiding a variety of obstacles, including tricky trees that could impede your progress.

As you progress through each captivating level, you'll find the challenges increasing in difficulty, presenting you with new hurdles and obstacles to conquer. You must stay alert and be precise with your movements to ensure your frisbee reaches the basket at the end of each level. Successfully landing in the basket will allow you to unlock new levels and further challenges, keeping you entertained and engaged for hours. Prepare for a fun aerial adventure, and enjoy the thrill of Frisbee 3D!

How to play free Frisbee 3D game online

To play Frisbee 3D, launch the game and tap or click to send your frisbee flying. Use a swipe motion to maneuver it left or right, guiding it toward the circles while avoiding any obstacles. Make sure to aim for the basket at the end of each level to advance to the next challenge. Good luck!
