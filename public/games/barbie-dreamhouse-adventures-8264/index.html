Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures

Welcome to Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures, where you can create your very own personalized Barbie experience! Dive into a world filled with exciting activities, ranging from baking and cooking to dancing and home design. Whether you're hosting a fabulous pool party or indulging in a luxurious makeover, there's never a dull moment in the DreamHouse!

Are you passionate about interior design? Unleash your creativity by transforming every room in your DreamHouse with stylish wallpapers and jaw-dropping decorations. You can make every corner reflect your unique style! Meet my amazing friends, including Barbie "Brooklyn" Roberts, the sporty Renee, talented DJ Daisy, science enthusiast Teresa, aspiring fashion designer Nikki, and of course, Ken. Together with my fun-loving sisters Skipper, Stacie, and Chelsea, and our supportive parents, Mr. and Mrs. Roberts, the adventures never end!

Join me in our fabulous kitchen where we'll whip up delicious recipes and bake mouth-watering treats with Skipper. Post our culinary creations on BarbieGram and share the fun with friends! Ready for a fashion moment? Dress up in stunning princess dresses or comfy pajamas with fabulous tips from Nikki. Create stylish hairstyles in our DreamHouse salon alongside Teresa, and don t forget about pampering! Visit the Malibu Nail Spa for a girl s day out filled with custom nail designs.

Summer in Malibu means endless fun at the beach! Swim, grill, lounge, or build sandcastles, and even compete in surf competitions through exciting mini-games. You can also chill by the DreamHouse pool where Ken throws unforgettable parties! Plus, get ready for a Royal Ball at the Floravian Castle, where you'll help transform friends into princesses for a night of enchantment.

How to play free Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures game online

To play Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures, simply select your desired activity from the main menu once you're in the game. You can choose to design rooms, prepare meals, or engage in fashion challenges. Follow the prompts for each activity to explore different aspects of the game, unlocking new fun as you go!
