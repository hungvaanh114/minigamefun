Sort Them All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sort Them All is an engaging and addictive hyper-casual game that captivates players of all ages. The objective is simple yet fun: sort a variety of colorful balls into their respective matching bins. This vibrant and entertaining game challenges your reflexes and decision-making skills as you quickly determine which color ball to pick.

As you navigate through the levels, the game presents an ever-increasing challenge that will keep you hooked. With a plethora of colors and increasingly tricky arrangements, you will find yourself striving for higher scores. Each successful sort gives you a satisfying sense of accomplishment, while the addictive gameplay ensures that you return for more. The intuitive controls and bright visuals make it easy for anyone to jump in and start sorting right away.

Whether you're looking to pass the time or engage in competitive play, Sort Them All offers a delightful experience that is hard to put down. Ideal for quick gaming sessions or longer play times, the nonsensical fun of sorting balls makes it a perfect choice for casual gamers. Challenge yourself or compete with friends to see who can achieve the highest score in this vibrant sorting adventure!

How to play free Sort Them All game online

To play Sort Them All, simply select the color of the ball you wish to pick from the screen. Use your finger or mouse to drag the selected ball to its corresponding bin. Keep sorting as quickly as you can to maximize your score, and aim to match all the balls before time runs out!
