Slenderman Must Die: Dead Space

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Slenderman Must Die: Dead Space is an exhilarating addition to the beloved Slenderman Must Die series, where players find themselves entrenched in a spine-chilling adventure. In this haunting episode, the sinister legend of Slenderman looms large, promising relentless terror and intimidation at every turn. Your mission takes you to a desolate space station, where escape from the clutches of this malevolent entity becomes your ultimate goal.

As you navigate the eerie corridors and darkened rooms of the abandoned station, it is crucial to collect various energy cells that are scattered throughout the environment. These vital resources are not just key to survival but also necessary to power your way through the haunting threats that await. Tension steadily builds as you constantly remain on the lookout for the dreadful Slenderman, whose presence instills dread and fear.

Your survival hinges on your ability to find and utilize advanced weapons that may aid you in confronting the deadly antagonist. From hi-tech firearms to makeshift tools, arming yourself is essential in your quest to vanquish the dark figure that stalks you relentlessly. The clock is ticking; will your courage and ingenuity prevail as you strive to survive the horrors of Slenderman Must Die: Dead Space?

How to play free Slenderman Must Die: Dead Space game online

To play Slenderman Must Die: Dead Space, use the WASD keys to maneuver your character around the space station. Explore the environment to locate energy cells, and deploy any weapons you find to combat Slenderman. Stay alert and plan your moves carefully, as he could appear at any moment!


Instructions

W A S D to walk around Mouse to Look around Left Mouse Button to Fire Right Mouse Button to Aim Mouse Wheel To Change Weapons R to Reload F to Pickup Items Left Shift to Run Left CTRL to Crouch X to Prone Space to Jump
