How to play free Drift Fury game online

To play Drift Fury, use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle. Accelerate and drift around the curves by pressing the brake and gas simultaneously for perfect slides. Aim to complete each race under a set time or achieve the highest score through skillful drifting. Enjoy the ride!
