Birds Link

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting world of Birds Link, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and swift decision-making skills. In this thrilling game, your objective is to slide blocks towards identical blocks to eliminate them, clearing your game board efficiently. Each block you eliminate nets you 100 points; however, you must strategize carefully each additional slide reduces your score by 10 points. Mastering the art of minimal moves is vital for attaining the highest possible score!

With a total of 36 engaging levels, Birds Link promises a delightful challenge for players of all ages. As you progress, each level increases in complexity, putting your problem-solving skills to the test. Plan your moves wisely to eliminate all blocks and move on to the next level. Every decision counts, and with every successful elimination, you ll find yourself immersed further in this fun-filled adventure.

The vibrant visuals and charming sound effects enhance your gaming experience, making Birds Link a joy to play. Whether you re looking for a casual pastime or a serious challenge, this game provides it all. Combine your skills with strategy, and see how quickly you can clear each level while accumulating points. Join the ranks of puzzle enthusiasts and discover why Birds Link stands out among puzzle games!

How to play free Birds Link game online

To play Birds Link, simply slide the blocks by tapping or clicking on them to move them towards matching blocks. Aim to eliminate pairs with the fewest moves possible to maximize your score. Keep an eye on the number of slides you make, as excessive moves will cost you points. Enjoy the puzzle-solving challenge!
