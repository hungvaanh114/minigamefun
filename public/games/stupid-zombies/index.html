Stupid-Zombies

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The absurdly entertaining Stupid-Zombies game has returned, and once again, you're humanity's final line of defense against an army of foolish undead. In this thrilling adventure, your mission is to keep these brainless creatures at bay while navigating through an astounding 960 engaging levels. With a limited supply of ammunition at your disposal, clever strategies and resourcefulness will be essential for achieving victory.

Prepare yourself to wield a powerful shotgun and make every shot count. You'll need to master different types of ammunition: from straightforward bullets to grenades, and versatile split- and buckshots. Each type has unique characteristics and can be utilized in various ways to efficiently eliminate multiple zombies in one go. The key lies in calculating the angles and obstacles in the environment, ensuring each shot lands where it's most effective.

As you progress through the game, expect a wide array of challenges and increasingly intelligent foes. Not every level will be straightforward, so be prepared to think outside the box and adapt your tactics on the fly. Along the way, you'll encounter unique zombies, each with their quirks, adding layers of complexity to each encounter. It's a test of skill, timing, and an understanding of your weapons. Will you rise to the challenge and keep the stupid zombies brainless?

How to play free Stupid-Zombies game online

To play Stupid-Zombies, launch the game and use your mouse or touch controls to aim and shoot. Select your ammunition wisely based on the situation, and tap or click to fire. Strategically plan your shots to take out multiple zombies with fewer bullets. Good luck!
