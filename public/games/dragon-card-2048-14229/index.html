How to play free Dragon Card 2048 game online

To play Dragon Card 2048, start by sliding the dinosaurs in any direction using the arrow keys or swipe gestures. When two similar dinosaurs come into contact, they will merge into a larger dinosaur. Keep merging until you reach the highest-level dinosaurs and aim for the best possible score!
