Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in Escape Noob, where you step into the shoes of a hapless noob on the run from his malevolent clone. The clone, filled with nefarious intent, is determined to end the real noob s existence by employing a savage bear as his instrument of chaos. Your mission is to navigate through a treacherous landscape while evading both the clone and the ferocious bear that stands in your way.

As you scurry through various terrains, keep an eye out for shimmering gold coins scattered across your path. Collecting these coins will not only enhance your score but may also unlock essential items that can aid in your escape. However, your primary objective lies in acquiring the key. This vital item will unlock the iron bars situated at the finish line, a crucial barrier that separates you from safety.

Be vigilant every moment counts, and there s no time for hesitation! The evil clone and the bear are in hot pursuit, so you must sprint forward without looking back. With each step, your heart races as you dodge obstacles and foes. It's a race against time and treachery, requiring both cunning reflexes and strategic thinking to survive.

Will you outsmart the wicked clone and the relentless bear? Only you can steer the way towards freedom in Escape Noob!

How to play free Escape Noob game online

To play Escape Noob, use your keyboard s arrow keys to control the noob s movement. Navigate to collect gold coins while keeping a safe distance from your clone and the bear. As you progress, look for the key to unlock the iron bars at the end of the level. Stay sharp and keep running!
