Supermarket Mania Journey

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey with Supermarket Mania Journey! This delightful time management game invites you to dive into the bustling world of grocery retail. Join Nikki and her companions as they travel to Tinsel Town to assist the Mayor in boosting the local economy. As you step into Nikki's shoes, get ready for a whirlwind of activities where every second counts!

Open up new supermarkets, ensuring they are always fully stocked to meet the demands of a diverse array of picky customers. Navigate through hundreds of entertaining levels packed with unique challenges, serving customers with finesse while managing your inventory effectively. Upgrade your shelves and freezers to increase your capacity and revenue, tackling various quests that reward your hard work and dedication. Just be cautious of Mr. Torg's sneaky sabotage; the competition is fierce!

Although Supermarket Mania Journey is free to play, you have the option to purchase additional bonuses within the game. For those who prefer not to make extra purchases, in-app transactions can easily be disabled in your device's settings. With daily visits to the bank for free gifts and the chance to spin for crystals, there are numerous ways to enhance your gaming experience!

How to play free Supermarket Mania Journey game online

To play Supermarket Mania Journey, navigate through the city map and select a level. Once in the game, focus on efficiently serving customers by quickly processing their orders and replenishing stocks. Keep an eye on the timers and manage upgrades wisely to enhance your supermarket's performance!
