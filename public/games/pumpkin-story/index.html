Pumpkin Story

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delightful adventure in Pumpkin Story, an engaging game set in the spirit of Halloween! Join a spirited little pumpkin as it journeys through a mysterious and abandoned landscape filled with challenges. Your primary goal is to collect keys that will help unlock various portals leading to the exit of each level.

As you navigate through this whimsical world, you will encounter numerous obstacles and hurdles that test your agility and problem-solving skills. The environment is constructed with various elements that you can utilize to your advantage. For instance, strategically placed blocks can be used to climb and reach keys that would otherwise be out of reach. Each key you collect brings you one step closer to completing the level and getting to the end-fire. The whimsical graphics and enchanting sounds create a festive atmosphere, making every moment spent playing Pumpkin Story a delightful experience!

Get ready to immerse yourself in this charming adventure, where each level presents new challenges and surprises. As you progress, you will find that the game skillfully balances fun with strategy, ensuring that players of all ages will enjoy the thrill of the chase and the satisfaction of reaching the finish line.

How to play free Pumpkin Story game online

To play Pumpkin Story, use the arrow keys to move the pumpkin around the screen. Collect keys scattered throughout each level while avoiding obstacles. Utilize blocks to climb higher and reach the keys. Your objective is to gather all keys and reach the end-fire to complete the level before time runs out!
