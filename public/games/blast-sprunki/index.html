Blast Sprunki

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hey, the cute Green Sprunki needs your help to escape from this dark world! Your mission is to guide Sprunki safely to the designated area with the least number of attempts. Strategically place explosives to propel Sprunki in the right direction, but be careful - dangerous spikes, obstacles, and tricky terrain can end your journey prematurely. Each level introduces unique challenges requiring precise timing, strategy, and creativity. Test your skills by aiming to earn three stars on every level through smart moves and minimal attempts. Can you lead Sprunki to safety and become a true master of precision?
