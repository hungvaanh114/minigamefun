Instructions:

Prepare for an exhilarating showdown in Knock'em All, where an onslaught of playful yet challenging puppets is coming your way! As you take on this vibrant and fast-paced adventure, your goal is clear: eliminate the puppets before they reach you. Armed with an array of powerful shells, you must engage in rapid-fire action to send the puppets tumbling off the platform and keep danger at bay.

This game combines elements of strategy and reflexes, as you will need to aim carefully and time your shots perfectly to maximize your effectiveness. Each puppet that approaches poses a unique threat, making quick decision-making crucial to success. Your shooting skills will be put to the test as you navigate through various levels, each filled with more fast-paced challenges than the last. The puppets won't give up easily, so stay alert and ready to fire!

As you progress, you'll find exciting opportunities to unlock upgrades and new weapons, allowing you to enhance your capabilities and tackle even the most daunting waves of puppets. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Knock'em All promises hours of entertainment and thrill. Ready your weapon, stay focused, and prepare to knock down those puppets!

How to play free Knock'em All game online

To play Knock'em All, use your mouse or touchscreen to aim your gun and shoot at the approaching puppets. Keep firing shells to prevent them from getting too close. Your objective is to clear each level by knocking down all the puppets and making it to the finish line!
