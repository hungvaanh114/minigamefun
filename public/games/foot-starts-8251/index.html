Experience the thrilling excitement of soccer like never before with Foot Starts. This engaging game allows players to immerse themselves in a vibrant soccer environment, making each match thrilling and enjoyable. All it takes is a simple drag, aim, and release to kick the soccer ball. Your objective? Score three goals before your opponent does. It s that straightforward!

Choose your team from a roster of 32 different nations and feel the intensity of competition as you face off against smart and strategical AI opponents. Each match presents a unique challenge, requiring quick thinking and precise execution. If you re new to the game, don t worry! The included tutorial will guide you through the rules and mechanics, ensuring you re ready to play hard from the get-go.

With a visually stunning soccer stadium setting, Foot Starts is crafted for soccer enthusiasts at heart. This turn-based soccer game boasts simple yet addictive gameplay and impressive physics that make it easy to learn yet hard to master. You don t have to be a soccer pro; just adjust your power and direction before unleashing a kick towards the goal. Whether you re a casual player or a soccer aficionado, Foot Starts promises hours of fun and excitement!

How to play free Foot Starts game online

Once you enter the game, choose your nation and then aim the cursor at the ball. Use your finger or mouse to drag back to set the power and angle of your kick. Release to take your shot! The first player to net three goals emerges victorious!
