Xmas Gifts Chain

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Xmas Gifts Chain is an engaging and delightful puzzle game perfect for the festive season! In this charming game, players embark on a mission to gather all the holiday gifts scattered across the board. Your goal is to connect all the blocks strategically, creating a continuous chain that leads from the starting block to the final one. The challenge lies in planning your path delicately to ensure you capture every single gift while navigating the obstacles and limitations of the board.

As you traverse through various levels, you'll encounter unique configurations and patterns that require thoughtful decision-making and quick reflexes. The visual appeal of the game, combined with a cheerful soundtrack, enhances the overall experience, making it a joyous way to celebrate the Christmas spirit. Whether you're playing solo or competing with friends, Xmas Gifts Chain provides endless hours of fun and mental exercise. Each level increases in difficulty, pushing your strategic thinking to the limits, resulting in a rewarding gaming experience that brings holiday cheer.

How to play free Xmas Gifts Chain game online

To start playing Xmas Gifts Chain, click on the starting block and then connect the subsequent blocks by selecting direct paths. Ensure you collect all gifts along the way before reaching the final block. Plan your chain moves wisely to avoid dead ends and maximize your score!
