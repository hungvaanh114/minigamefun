How to play free Color Hoop Stack - Puzzle game online

To play Color Hoop Stack, simply tap on a hoop to select it, then choose the destination hoop where you want to stack it. Your goal is to sort all the hoops by color, moving them strategically to eliminate any excess or misaligned ones. Make the most of each move and enjoy your sorting journey!
