Bubble Shooter 2020

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exciting adventure of Bubble Shooter 2020, players embark on a thrilling mission to rescue adorable raccoon cubs kidnapped by the nefarious evil monkey. As the mother raccoon, you must harness your skill and strategy by shooting colorful bubbles at the clusters of similarly colored balls scattered across the screen. Your aim is to create matches of three or more balls of the same color, causing them to burst and disappear. This enchanting game challenges your problem-solving abilities as you navigate through a series of dynamic levels filled with vibrant colors and escalating difficulties.

As you progress through Bubble Shooter 2020, you ll encounter various obstacles that require clever planning and precise aim to overcome. The captivating graphics and engaging sound effects immerse you in a charming environment, making each level an exciting experience. Demonstrating quick reflexes and strategic thinking is essential as you work to free each raccoon cub trapped in the bubble prison. Each successful shot brings you closer to saving the little cubs while earning points and bonuses along the way. The more efficiently you clear your shots, the more rewarding the experience becomes, keeping you engaged and wanting more.

Join in the fun as you help the mother raccoon reclaim her babies, tackle challenging puzzles, and ultimately confront the evil monkey to restore peace. Bubble Shooter 2020 is more than just a bubble-popping game; it s a heartwarming story of bravery and love that perfectly combines action and strategy.

How to play free Bubble Shooter 2020 game online

To play Bubble Shooter 2020, aim your cursor to target the bubbles at the top. Click to shoot your colored bubble towards clusters of the same color. Create matches of three or more to clear them from the screen. Keep popping until all bubbles are gone to rescue the raccoon cubs!
