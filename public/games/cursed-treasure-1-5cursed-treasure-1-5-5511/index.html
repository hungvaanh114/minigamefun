In Cursed Treasure 1.5, the battle for your precious gems starts anew! After a peaceful ten years, the heroes of light have returned with the intent to steal the last three remaining gems safeguarded by the diabolical Overlord. As the guardian of this cursed treasure, it is your solemn duty to uphold the legacy of centuries filled with sinister deeds and thwart the forces of good at every turn.

Your adventure begins with a strategic challenge build and upgrade a series of formidable towers that serve as your first line of defense. Each tower can be customized to unleash devastating attacks on waves of relentless invaders including stealthy ninjas, celestial angels, and fierce lizard riders. As you progress, collect resources to enhance your defenses and expand your arsenal of spells.

Utilize powerful magic spells to wreak havoc upon your enemies when they breach your defenses. Mastering these abilities is crucial, as they provide the opportunity to turn the tide of battle in your favor. Additionally, you ll need to learn valuable skills that will aid in managing your resources effectively and boosting your tower s performance.

Pit your wits against advancing waves of foes as you protect the cursed gems a legacy that cannot be allowed to fall into the hands of heroes. Get ready to engage in this thrilling test of strategy, as you defend what is rightfully yours and ensure that the dark era of villainy continues!

How to play free Cursed Treasure 1.5 game online

To play Cursed Treasure 1.5, begin by selecting your tower placements strategically on the game map. Upgrade your towers as enemies approach to maximize defense. Click on spells in your spell menu to cast them at critical moments. Utilize earned resources to enhance your defenses and skills to keep the gems safe!
