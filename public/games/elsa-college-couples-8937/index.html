Elsa College Couples

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The year s ultimate showdown is happening at Fairy Tale High, and three beloved couples are vying for the title of hottest couple on campus! In this exciting dress-up game, titled "Princess College Couples," you have the chance to participate in this electrifying competition. Are you ready to help pick the winner?

Your first fashion challenge features the iconic duo, Beauty and the Beast. Start with Belle, selecting from her fashionable wardrobe. Choose a trendy pair of jeans, a stylish t-shirt, casual sneakers, and a sporty jacket to complete her look. Don t forget to find the perfect outfit for the Beast that complements Belle s chic ensemble, setting the stage for an unforgettable couple.

Next, gear up for the vibrant couple Aladdin and Jasmine. Their playful, colorful style reflects their adventurous spirits. Opt for eye-catching bright hues and denim pieces that embody their charm. Create a statement outfit that highlights their unique personalities, combining elegance with a rebellious twist!

Finally, meet the popular couple Elsa and Jack. With their trendy outfits to browse through, you ll get to create matchy-matchy looks that truly stand out. Experiment with their stylish choices and be the designer of their fabulous outfits. Once you ve dressed up all three couples, save your creations and share your thoughts in the comments section!

How to play free Elsa College Couples game online

To begin playing the Elsa College Couples game, simply log into the game at DressUpWho.com. Follow the on-screen prompts to select a couple, browse their wardrobe, and click on clothing items to dress them up. Combine outfits to showcase their unique styles and don t forget to save your looks!
