How to play free Mr Toro 2 game online

To play Mr Toro 2, use the arrow keys or the W, A, S, D keys for movement. Gather coins without getting hit by enemies or falling into traps. Jump over spikes and dodge saws to reach the red flag at the end of each level and progress to the next one. Good luck!
