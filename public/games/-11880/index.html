Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Poppy Dungeons, a brand-new addition on kiz10.com. Join a courageous little soldier who answered the call of the special forces, only to find himself ensnared in a treacherous maze inhabited by a fearsome monster! In this captivating blend of action, shooting, and puzzle-solving, players must help the lone survivor navigate the perilous dungeons filled with hostile enemies.

The adventure kicks off as a special team responds to panicked citizens claiming to have witnessed a terrifying purple monster wreaking havoc in the alleys. Now, it's up to you to guide the soldier to safety! You ll need to maneuver skillfully through the dungeon while fending off merciless enemy soldiers eager to eliminate you. Your mission is to shoot down everything that threatens your survival and escape the clutches of danger.

Each level presents its own unique challenges, including intense encounters with the ferocious Huggy Wuggy. To clear a stage, you'll need to defeat this monstrous foe and collect the key that unlocks progression to the next level. Enhance your gameplay by upgrading your arsenal, including an array of powerful weapons, sturdy shields, and valuable first aid kits to ensure you stay alive in this nail-biting showdown.

With various difficulty levels and multiple adversaries to contend with, Poppy Dungeons promises hours of heart-pounding excitement and strategic gameplay. Prepare for a fierce clash between your soldier and an army of monsters, and prove your prowess in this action-packed game!

How to play free Poppy Dungeons game online

To play Poppy Dungeons, navigate your soldier using the arrow keys to move around the maze. Use the spacebar or the mouse to shoot at enemies. Collect keys, weapons, and health packs while avoiding monster attacks. Your goal is to defeat enemies and escape each level safely!
