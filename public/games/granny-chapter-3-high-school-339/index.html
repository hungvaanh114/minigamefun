Granny Chapter 3 High School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Granny Chapter 3: High School" immerses players in a thrilling third-person horror experience. In this gripping installment, players find themselves trapped within the eerie corridors of an abandoned school, where they must evade the relentless pursuit of Granny and the haunting specters of Slendrina. Your primary objective is to survive while searching for essential items hidden throughout the school.

As you navigate through the creepy hallways, it's vital to explore the various classrooms and discover keys that unlock crucial areas of the school. Be on the lookout for a revolver stocked with ammunition, as it could be your lifeline against the two terrifying figures that haunt your every move. Each classroom holds secrets, including a singular desk that acts as a clue to Granny's hidden past and the essential keys you will need to escape.

Time is of the essence, as Granny and Slendrina will stop at nothing to track you down. Use stealth and strategy to maneuver through the dark and ominous spaces of the school while keeping a watchful eye on your surroundings. The tension builds as you uncover more about the school s disturbing history, fueling your determination to find a way out while staying alive.

How to play free Granny Chapter 3 High School game online

To play Granny Chapter 3: High School, simply navigate the school s hallways using your keyboard controls to run and look around. Search for keys and ammo hidden in classrooms while staying out of sight. Avoid making noise, as Granny and Slendrina are always listening. Your mission is to gather the keys and find your escape route!
