High School Princess Date Spa

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the glamorous world of royal charm with High School Princess Date Spa, a delightful design game that combines the excitement of high school life with luxurious self-care. As a royal princess, your journey begins at the spa, where you ll indulge in a well-deserved pampering session. Let go of your worries and prepare to impress Prince Charming with a rejuvenating facial using all-natural oils and scrubs.

In this enchanting game, you will cleanse, exfoliate, and tone your skin to achieve that radiant glow. From washing your face to carefully shaping your eyebrows into elegant arches, every detail matters in this royal beauty routine. Don t forget to apply a soothing moisturizer to complete your spa experience! Once you ve achieved the ultimate pampering, it s time to stand out with a fabulous trip to the salon.

At the salon, you have a variety of hairstyles and colors to choose from, allowing you to express your unique royal style. Experiment with different looks until you find the perfect one that complements your princess character. Finally, select an exquisite outfit that reflects your status as a high school princess ready for romance. Will you sweep Prince Charming off his feet? It s up to you to create the perfect style for your memorable date!

How to play free High School Princess Date Spa game online

To play High School Princess Date Spa, simply use your mouse to navigate through the spa and salon options. Click on the different beauty treatments to apply them, and personalize your princess by selecting hairstyles, outfits, and accessories. Enjoy creating your royal look and preparing for your fabulous date!
