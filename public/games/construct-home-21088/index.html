Construct Home

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Construct Home, a thrilling maze puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. In this captivating game, each level presents you with uniquely designed maze shapes displayed on house walls. Your objective is to expertly maneuver a color roller to effectively construct the home by navigating through the intricate pathways of the maze.

You must swipe the roller in the right direction to travel through the maze and successfully complete each level. As you progress, you will encounter increasingly complex mazes that demand precise movements and careful planning. The beauty of Construct Home lies in its simplicity, allowing players of all ages to engage in a delightful, rewarding experience while enhancing their cognitive skills.

The colorful visuals and compelling sound effects keep players enthralled as they swipe their way through delightful challenges. Each completed maze not only advances your progress in the game but also brings you one step closer to constructing your dream home! With a variety of levels to explore, there is always a new challenge around the corner, ensuring that your journey in Construct Home is never dull.

How to play free Construct Home game online

To play the Construct Home game, simply swipe your finger across the screen to guide the color roller through the maze. Aim to navigate the roller in the correct direction to connect all the pathways and complete the level. Use your intuition and analytical skills to clear each challenging maze successfully!
