How to play free Old School Billiard Pool game online

To start playing Old School Billiard Pool, simply select your ball color, either red or yellow. Use your mouse or arrow keys to aim and adjust your shot. Click to strike and watch as the balls roll towards the pockets! Keep track of your score and enjoy the playful atmosphere as you play.
