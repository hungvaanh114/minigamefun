Autumn Endless Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a world of autumnal charm with "Autumn Endless Runner." This exhilarating adventure begins with a peaceful stroll through a stunning wooded area, showcasing the vibrant colors of fall. However, your serene experience quickly takes a turn for the spooky when a menacing pumpkin ghost appears, giving chase! Now, it's a race against time as you dash down the winding path, aiming to outpace the ghostly gourd that haunts your steps.

As you navigate through this enchanting yet eerie landscape, you'll encounter various obstacles ranging from fallen branches to the delightful yet distracting forest animals. To avoid them and maintain your momentum, simply tap the game screen to jump. Timing is crucial as you must perform perfect jumps to dodge not only the obstacles but also the wildlife that fills this autumn wonderland.

As the pumpkin ghost looms ever closer, you'll find yourself immersed in a thrilling atmosphere filled with the sounds of rustling leaves and the distant echoes of the chase. Keep your reflexes sharp, and don t let the ghost catch you! It's an endless race filled with excitement and a touch of autumnal eeriness that will keep you on your toes.

How to play free Autumn Endless Runner game online

To play "Autumn Endless Runner," simply tap the game screen to jump over obstacles and animals in your path. Keep an eye on the approaching pumpkin ghost, and try to stay ahead as long as possible to maximize your score and prolong your autumn adventure!
