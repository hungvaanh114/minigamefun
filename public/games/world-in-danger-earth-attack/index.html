World in Danger Earth Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The world is facing an unprecedented crisis as malicious planets invade our Solar System with the intent to annihilate Earth! It's a battle of epic proportions where Earth stands ready to defend itself. Joined by the Moon, our planet isn't going to go down without a fight. As the player, you'll take control of Planet Earth, rotating it to launch devastating attacks against the hostile invaders. Your mission is clear: shoot down the enemy planets before they can bring destruction to our beloved home.

Equip yourself with an array of formidable weapons designed to increase your combat capabilities. As you progress through the game, you'll have opportunities to evolve your defenses, enhance your speed, and acquire power-ups that will enable you to withstand the onslaught from the invading forces. Surviving each wave of attacks is crucial, so your reflexes and strategy will be put to the ultimate test. Gear up and prepare for a thrilling challenge as you strive to crash all incoming enemy planets and secure Earth's safety!

How to play free World in Danger Earth Attack game online

To play the game, use your mouse or arrow keys to rotate Planet Earth. Position it strategically to aim at incoming enemy planets. Click or press the action button to shoot your weapons at the enemies. Collect power-ups to evolve your abilities and increase your defense while surviving the relentless attacks!
