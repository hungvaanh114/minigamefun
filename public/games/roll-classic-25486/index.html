Roll Classic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Roll Classic is a timeless dice game that combines strategy and luck. Players take turns rolling dice to collect points, aiming to reach a target score before their opponents. With each roll, players must decide whether to risk it all for more points or hold back to secure their lead. The game offers thrilling twists, where the unexpected can change the course of the game. Quick to learn, yet endlessly engaging, Roll Classic is perfect for family and friends.
