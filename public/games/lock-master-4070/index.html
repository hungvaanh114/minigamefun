Lock Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Lock Master is an exhilarating adventure game where players embark on a quest to unravel intricate puzzles within a captivating maze world. Your primary objective is to successfully maneuver a lock towards an orange dot using your mouse. As you progress through the game, each of the 16 levels presents increasingly complex challenges, putting your problem-solving abilities to the test.

In this immersive journey, players will encounter various obstacles that require quick thinking and strategic navigation. The enchanting atmosphere of the maze adds to the thrill as you work to overcome each hurdle. With every successfully unlocked lock, you are rewarded by advancing to the next level, where new challenges await. Lock Master not only challenges your logical reasoning but also demands swift reactions, making it a true test of skill.

Are you ready to put your puzzle-solving abilities to the test? Lock Master is waiting to challenge your intellect and speed! Dive into this mysterious maze world, unleash your inner locksmith, and see if you can conquer all 16 levels.

How to play free Lock Master game online

To play Lock Master, click and drag the lock with your mouse towards the designated orange dot. Navigate around obstacles and employ strategic thinking to solve each level's puzzle. Keep an eye on the timer and react swiftly to emerge victorious in this thrilling adventure!


Instructions

Click and hold the left mouse button to move the lock release to stop You have 3 lives in each room if you run out you go back to level 1 Complete each level by reaching the orange dot with the lock There are a total of 16 levels
