Pest Control

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pest Control is an electrifying game that plunges players into the shoes of brave exterminators who strive to rescue their city from an unyielding onslaught of insects. Armed with an impressive arsenal of potent pest-eliminating weapons, players must skillfully traverse through intricate levels. Your mission is to strategically eliminate waves of pesky creatures that threaten the peace.

As you progress through the game, each successful extermination not only brings satisfaction but also rewards that can be utilized to unlock various upgrades. This allows players to enhance their weaponry and prepare for encounters with tougher and more resilient bug species. The vibrant and immersive graphics further amplify the excitement, ensuring that players are constantly on the edge of their seats as they confront swarms of relentless pests.

The dynamic gameplay mechanics keep the adrenaline flowing, challenging players to think quickly and react decisively in their quest to protect the city from total infestation. With each level bringing new challenges and increasingly difficult foes, the game provides an invigorating experience that keeps players captivated.

How to play free Pest Control game online

To play Pest Control online, begin by choosing your weapon and navigating through the various levels. Aim and shoot at the incoming pests using your mouse or touchscreen, depending on your device. Collect rewards for every bug you eliminate and use them to unlock upgrades that improve your extermination capabilities. Stay sharp time is of the essence!
