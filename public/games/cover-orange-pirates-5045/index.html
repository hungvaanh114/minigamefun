COVER ORANGE: PIRATES

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure in Cover Orange: Pirates, where your mission is to safeguard your orange friends from the perilous downpour of acid rain. In this captivating puzzle platformer, you are tasked with constructing creative shelters using a variety of objects scattered throughout a whimsical pirate-themed island. Each level presents unique challenges that will test your strategic thinking and problem-solving skills.

Your orange companions depend on your ingenuity as you drag and stack various items to create adequate coverage. The colorful graphics, paired with engaging sound effects, immerse you in the world of swashbuckling pirates and daring challenges. Additionally, players can hunt for hidden stars in every level, adding an extra layer of excitement and rewarding those who take the time to explore their surroundings.

Whether you re a casual gamer or a puzzle enthusiast, Cover Orange: Pirates offers a delightful blend of fun and challenge. Gather your friends and family to share in the enjoyment, as you collaborate or compete to see who can master the levels while saving all the oranges. The game s engaging mechanics ensure that you ll keep coming back for more, testing your skills and creativity along the way!

How to play free COVER ORANGE: PIRATES game online

To play Cover Orange: Pirates, simply drag objects from the inventory onto the screen to build a protective shelter for the oranges. Ensure that the shelter is secure to prevent them from getting wet. Use your mouse or finger to interact, and don't forget to search for hidden stars in each level to increase your score!
