Hidden Car Tires

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the intriguing world of Hidden Car Tires, where your observation skills and speed are put to the ultimate test! This exciting game presents you with three sensational automobiles featured in a collection of eight stunning photographs. Your adventure begins with selecting your favorite car image.

The challenge lies in spotting the hidden treasure within that image; specifically, you will need to locate a total of 15 concealed car tires. Use your mouse to click on any tires you uncover, but remember time is of the essence! As the clock ticks down, you must swiftly find all the hidden tires before it runs out.

Each mistake you make might bring you closer to game over, adding an extra layer of challenge to this thrilling experience. Gear up for fun-filled moments as you navigate through the images, test your agility and keen eye, and race against time!

How to play free Hidden Car Tires game online

Once you've selected a car image, focus on spotting the hidden car tires. Use your mouse to click on the tires you see. Keep an eye on the timer as you search. Make sure to act quickly but be wary of making mistakes, as too many errors will end the game! Enjoy the hunt!
