Viking Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Gather around, brave souls, for the Viking saga unfolds in the thrilling game of Viking Puzzle! The fearless Vikings have arrived on distant shores, but their path to freedom is obstructed by a tangled mess of colorful bricks. These bricks stand as a formidable barrier to their ships, and they need your quick thinking and strategic prowess to regain control.

Armed only with your wits and the determination of a warrior, you must eliminate these multicolored bricks by combining the same colors. However, beware! Each swing of your club not only breaks down the barriers but also causes more bricks to materialize, adding to the challenge. You are racing against time, as the relentless arc of electricity draws nearer to you with each passing moment. Should it touch you before the Viking warriors can escape, it will be game over! Your mission isn t merely about brute force; it requires clever combinations and timely decisions to ensure the Vikings regain their ships and sail forth to glorious adventures.

Can you rise to the challenge and help the Vikings navigate through the chaos? The excitement awaits, so muster your courage and dive into this electrifying puzzle adventure where only the swift and strategic can succeed!

How to play free Viking puzzle game online

To play the Viking Puzzle game, simply click on matching bricks of the same color to merge them. Use your mouse to aim and strike, breaking down the obstructive bricks while keeping an eye on the approaching electric arc. Work quickly to ensure the Vikings safe return to their ships!
