Dotted Girl Wedding

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in a whimsical parallel universe with the enchanting game, Dotted Girl Wedding. In this delightful world, you can help the lovestruck Louis, who has long cherished a crush on Marie, arrange the perfect wedding for the two star-crossed lovers. Imagine a celebration filled with romance and beauty as you step into the role of a wedding planner.

Your task begins with selecting the ideal wedding dress for the lovely bride, Marie. You have an array of options in enchanting hues like pink, white, or lilac. Don t forget to accessorize her look with a stunning veil and a bouquet of colorful flowers that capture the essence of love. Next, bring out Marie's natural beauty by applying gentle makeup and styling her hair in a dreamy wedding hairstyle that complements her radiant look.

But the celebrations don t stop at the bride! Shift your focus to the charming groom, Louis. Dress him up in a fashionable hairstyle that reflects his personality and choose a dapper suit in his favorite shade of turquoise-blue. To complete the groom's outfit, add a charming boutonniere that echoes the colors of Marie s dress, tying together this beautiful union.

With both bride and groom ready to embark on their new journey together, you ll be thrilled to witness their magical wedding day unfold. This delightful game not only lets you unleash your creativity but also allows you to contribute to a heartwarming romance.

How to play free Dotted Girl Wedding game online

To play the Dotted Girl Wedding game, simply navigate through various sections in the game interface. Select a dress, hairstyle, makeup, and accessories for both Marie and Louis. Use your mouse to click and drag items onto the characters, then watch as the wedding preparations come to life!
