Feeding Fun

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Feeding Fun is an electrifying puzzle game that challenges players to connect circuits and light up bulbs using limited moves. Set in a futuristic neon world, players must strategically connect energy sources to light up all the bulbs on each level. With increasingly complex puzzles, Feeding Fun offers hours of brain-teasing fun, testing your logic and problem-solving skills.

Instructions

Objective Connect all the kids and foods Controls Tap and drag to connect the kids and foods Ensure all kids are connected before running out of moves
