Cooking My Story: Cooking Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of "Cooking My Story: cooking game," where culinary dreams turn into a delightful reality. Join the journey of a determined aspiring chef, who went from steaming hot dogs at a local contest to being chosen as the apprentice of the renowned Chef Alex. This thrilling cooking game immerses you in a narrative full of charm, challenges, and endless fun.

Your adventure begins with the protagonist's humble beginnings, reminiscing about their role as the town's official "wiener steamer." With humor and warmth, you'll navigate the ups and downs of culinary school, learn magical recipes, and face hilarious kitchen mishaps. As the story unfolds, you'll gather tips and tricks from Chef Alex, hone your skills, and eventually discover your unique cooking style.

As you progress through "Cooking My Story," you will participate in exciting cooking mini-games that will test your speed and creativity. Unlock new recipes, decorate your kitchen, and serve delicious dishes to earn points and impress your mentor. With charming graphics and engaging gameplay, this adventure is perfect for cooking enthusiasts and story lovers alike.

Ready to cook up some fun? Dive into "Cooking My Story: cooking game" and transform your culinary aspirations into mouthwatering triumphs!

How to play free Cooking My Story: cooking game game online

To play "Cooking My Story," simply start by guiding your character through various cooking challenges presented in the game. You will need to tap and swipe on the screen to chop ingredients, stir pots, and serve dishes. Pay attention to Chef Alex's instructions to earn points and progress through the levels. Don't forget to decorate your kitchen and customize your cooking space as you go!
