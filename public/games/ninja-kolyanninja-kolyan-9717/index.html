Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the shadows as you take on the role of Ninja Kolyan, a stealthy night ninja navigating through eerie, abandoned buildings. Your mission is to sprint through the darkness, utilizing your agility and cunning to collect as many points as possible. Each level presents a thrilling challenge, testing your reflexes and timing as you leap over obstacles and evade mysterious traps that await in the semi-darkness.

The haunting atmosphere adds a layer of excitement, pulling you into the world of a ninja where every swift move counts. As you dash through crumbling walls and shadowy corners, you must remain vigilant and strategic in your movements to gather valuable collectibles that boost your score. The more points you accrue, the more thrilling your journey becomes, driving you to push your limits and achieve a new personal best.

Ninja Kolyan invites you to explore a variety of unique environments that contain hidden secrets and power-ups to enhance your gameplay. Embrace the challenge of this exhilarating adventure as you master the art of stealth, speed, and precision to emerge victorious in your quest for points. Are you ready to take on the darkness and prove yourself as the ultimate night ninja?

How to play free Ninja Kolyan game online

To begin playing Ninja Kolyan, simply use the arrow keys to navigate your character. Press the space bar to jump over obstacles and collect points scattered throughout the abandoned buildings. Stay alert for traps, and strive to gather as many collectibles as you can to increase your score.
