Trash Factory

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Trash Factory, where you will assist adorable trash pandas in their mission to clean up the world, one piece of garbage at a time! Your main objective is to sort the trash into the appropriate containers, making sure that each item finds its rightful place. This action-packed game challenges your quick thinking and coordination as you navigate through a fun-filled factory setting.

As you step into the vibrant world of Trash Factory, you will encounter various types of waste, ranging from recyclables to biodegradable materials. Your task is to expertly analyze each piece of trash and sort it accordingly. The game also features creatively designed switchers that change the direction of the trash path your job is to tap them at the right moment to ensure the trash flows seamlessly into the correct bins.

Leveling up is part of the excitement. By successfully filling up trash containers, you earn points and unlock new challenges. The better you sort, the happier the manager becomes, leading to additional goals and rewards! With engaging graphics, upbeat music, and intuitive controls, Trash Factory is not only fun but also a great way to learn about the importance of recycling and waste management.

How to play free Trash Factory game online

To play Trash Factory, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to sort the trash into the designated bins. Watch the trash flow along the paths and tap on the switchers to redirect it as necessary. Keep an eye out for the manager s satisfaction level while you strive to fill the containers correctly!


Instructions

- Sort trash into the correct containers - Tap the switchers to change the direction of the trash path - Level up by filling trash containers - Keep the manager happy by correctly sorting trash
