Vikings Royal Battle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the fierce world of the Vikings with Vikings Royal Battle, a game that lets you unleash your inner warrior. Have you ever wondered how much you know about these legendary Norse seafarers? Here s your chance to engage in epic battles using your trusty circle shield and axe as you navigate the thrilling landscapes filled with fierce enemies. You ll embark on a journey aboard a Viking ship, ready to conquer various levels while facing insurmountable foes.

As you advance through the game, your fury and determination will be put to the test. Each level presents unique challenges requiring strategic thinking and relentless action. Eliminate all adversaries that dare stand in your way as you harness the power of your weapons. The gameplay is designed to keep you on your toes, demanding quick reflexes and sharp aim to effectively launch your axes and defend your territory.

With every successful level completed, you will earn the opportunity to enhance your skills significantly. You can choose from one of three skills to add to your Viking repertoire, ensuring your character becomes more powerful and better suited for the challenges ahead. The game not only offers thrilling combat but also allows players to customize their approach to battle, adding layers of excitement to the Viking experience.

How to play free Vikings Royal Battle game online

To play Vikings Royal Battle, simply navigate your character using arrow keys or WASD. Use your mouse to aim and click to throw axes at incoming enemies. Be vigilant and stay mobile to evade attacks while you eliminate foes. Level up by defeating enemies and choose new skills to enhance your gameplay!
