Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exciting and entertaining experience with Sumo Party, featuring the exhilarating Huge Sumos game! This delightful title brings you into the world of sumo wrestling, allowing you to engage in thrilling bouts that are perfect for both solo players and those who want to challenge friends in a 2 Player mode. With its stunning 3D graphics and immersive sound effects reminiscent of traditional sumo wrestling, Sumo Party delivers a visual and auditory experience that will capture your attention!

In Sumo Party, you take control of your wrestler using the intuitive controls: the "W, A, S, D" keys for one player and the "Arrow" keys for the second player. The gameplay is straightforward press a button once for your wrestler to move in the corresponding direction. To unleash powerful actions, tap the buttons twice quickly to propel your wrestler into specific maneuvers. Whether you're dodging your opponent or executing a forceful push, the game offers various strategies to keep the match engaging.

Compete against AI, or challenge your friends in a showdown to see who can master the art of sumo wrestling. As you progress, you can unlock entertaining features and enhance your skills, all while enjoying the festive atmosphere that Sumo Party creates.

How to play free Sumo Party game online

Once you're in the game, choose your game mode single or 2 Player. Use "W, A, S, D" for player one and the "Arrow" keys for player two. Remember, one tap moves your character, and double taps execute special actions. Get ready to wrestle and enjoy the fun!
