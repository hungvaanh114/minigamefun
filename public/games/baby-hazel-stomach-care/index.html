Baby Hazel Stomach Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the delightful world of Baby Hazel, our little heroine finds herself in distress due to her poor eating choices. Instead of opting for her nutritious mom-made breakfast, she has indulged in too many peanuts, resulting in an unhappy stomachache. Your mission in this engaging game is to nurse Hazel back to health with your tender care.

As you enter the game, you will guide Baby Hazel on a journey to recovery. First, take her to the doctor, where you'll need to help diagnose her discomfort. Once the doctor has provided the appropriate medication, it will be your responsibility to administer it to Hazel. Throughout this experience, be attentive to her needs, ensuring that she remains happy and comfortable.

Keeping a close eye on the happiness bar will help you understand how satisfied Baby Hazel is feeling at any given moment. As you address her demands, strive to keep her entertained and contented, allowing her to relax and slumber peacefully. The more attentive you are, the faster she will recover, and the higher her happiness level will rise.

This charming game not only emphasizes the importance of healthy eating but also teaches players about caring for others when they are in need. Your goal is to provide Baby Hazel with the love and support she needs in her time of discomfort, ensuring she bounces back in no time.

How to play free Baby Hazel Stomach Care game online

To play the Baby Hazel Stomach Care game, simply follow the on-screen instructions to navigate through different scenarios. Help Hazel visit the doctor, administer her medication, and fulfill her various needs. Keep an eye on her happiness bar to ensure she stays content throughout the game!
