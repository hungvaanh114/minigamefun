Duck X Target

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Duck X Target, a captivating shooting gallery game where you step into the shoes of a brave protector of the pond! In this enchanting habitat, friendly ducks once thrived until they were captured by malicious aquatic foes. Your mission is to save these delightful creatures by using your sharp aim and quick reflexes.

As you enter the shooting arena, you'll be armed with a trusty gun, ready to take on moving targets that represent the threats to the pond. The game challenges you to shoot accurately and swiftly; the better your aim, the longer you can maintain your time scale. This feature is crucial as it grants you the chance to accumulate more points, helping you in your quest to restore peace to the serene pond.

The graphics are vibrant, drawing you into the picturesque setting filled with animated ducks, gentle ripples, and plenty of engaging action. Not only will you have fun aiming at the targets, but you'll also feel the thrill of supporting your feathered friends as they return to their home. Join the adventure and see if you can achieve a high score while saving the pond from its perilous captors!

How to play free Duck X Target game online

To play Duck X Target, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the moving targets. Click or tap to shoot; try to hit as many targets as possible within the time limit. The quicker and more precisely you shoot, the higher your score will be. Enjoy the game and help the ducks reclaim their home!


Instructions

Use the mouse cursor to move the sight Left mouse button - shot Also a tap on the device with a touch screen is responsible for moving the sight and the shot
