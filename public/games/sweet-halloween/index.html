Sweet Halloween

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the delightful world of Sweet Halloween, where festive fun meets challenging puzzles! In this enchanting game, you ll need to demonstrate your skills by solving engaging "Three in a row" puzzles. Get ready to swipe the colorful candies, arranging them both horizontally and vertically to create mesmerizing lines of three or more. Whether it s a straight line or shaped like a T or L, your matching prowess will lead to sweet success!

As you immerse yourself in the spooky yet playful atmosphere of this game, you will soon discover that matching four or more candies can unlock exciting power-ups. These power-ups are essential tools that will assist you in clearing the board and achieving your objectives more efficiently. But that s not all! Combine two of these powerful boosters for an even more effective Super Power-up, propelling you further into the game and giving you a competitive edge against challenging levels.

With each level, the excitement intensifies as you navigate through colorful graphics and delightful sound effects. Aim to collect special Halloween-themed candies while racing against the clock and completing objectives that elevate your gaming experience. The fun and addictive nature of Sweet Halloween will keep you engaged and entertained for hours!

How to play free Sweet Halloween game online

To play Sweet Halloween, simply drag your mouse or swipe your fingers to move the candies. Create matches by aligning three or more candies of the same type. Keep an eye out for opportunities to create power-ups and combine them for strength! Enjoy matching away while racking up points in this sweet Halloween challenge!
