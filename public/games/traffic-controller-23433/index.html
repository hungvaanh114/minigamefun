Traffic Controller

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Traffic Controller, a thrilling simulation game that puts you in charge of managing busy intersections in major cities. In urban landscapes where traffic can become chaotic, your role as a dispatcher is vital. Your primary responsibility is to control the flow of vehicles and pedestrians while ensuring safety at dangerously crowded intersections.

As you take on this challenging role, you will wield the power of traffic lights, strategically changing their signals to manage the constant ebb and flow of cars and pedestrians. Timing is crucial, as you must make quick decisions to avert potential mishaps and keep everyone safe. The game offers a realistic portrayal of the pressures faced by traffic controllers, and your ability to multitask and think critically will be tested at every turn.

Each level presents unique urban settings, challenges, and increasing traffic loads. You'll have to balance the needs of vehicles moving through the intersection with those of pedestrians who want to cross safely. Your ultimate goal is to achieve seamless traffic control, minimizing waiting times and preventing accidents to ensure a smooth and efficient flow of transportation throughout the metropolis.

How to play free Traffic Controller game online

To play the Traffic Controller game, simply start the game and observe the traffic lights. Click on the traffic light icons to change their signals, allowing cars to move while safely managing pedestrian crossings. Pay attention to the flow of traffic and adjust your signals quickly to ensure everyone's safety!
