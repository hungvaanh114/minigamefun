Flex Run 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Flex Run 3D is an immersive yoga simulation arcade game designed with vibrant 3D cartoon models and engaging furniture layouts. In this exciting adventure, your main objective is to navigate through a series of challenging obstacles, all while maintaining various yoga poses. The key to success lies in your ability to adapt and change your real-time pose and positions as you move forward.

The game features a selection of characters, including one girl and a duo of girls, each offering unique movement patterns that respond to your drag controls. As you maneuver through the game, your characters will execute stunning poses and actions based on how you guide them. Reaching the Finish wall is just the beginning; you ll need to stay agile and continue adjusting your pose as you encounter purple walls adorned with numbers. The gameplay is both fun and challenging, encouraging players to improve their reflexes and strategic movements.

Flex Run 3D also offers a variety of six distinctive skins to choose from, allowing you to personalize your gameplay experience. These colorful skins add a layer of excitement as you progress, making every moment enjoyable. So, brace yourself for an exhilarating experience where your focus and flexibility will be put to the test!

How to play free Flex Run 3D game online

To start playing Flex Run 3D, simply use your mouse or finger to drag and control your character's movements. Avoid furniture obstacles as you approach the Finish wall. Once you reach it, continue to shift your pose to connect with the numbers on the incoming purple walls for extra points. Enjoy your game!
