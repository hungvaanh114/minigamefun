Mermaid Newborn Baby Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Mermaid Newborn Baby Care, a delightful and engaging game where you get the chance to assist Princess Mermaid in taking care of her adorable baby. The princess has had a busy schedule and really needs a helping hand with her little one. Are you ready to step in?

Your adventure begins with giving the mermaid baby a refreshing bath. Once he is clean, it s time to dress him up! Choose from a variety of stylish outfits and accessories that will make the baby shine. But that s not all this cute little mermaid loves to express himself through art. You can keep him entertained by coloring in three charming animal pictures. Show off your creativity by creating a masterpiece that will leave him beaming with joy!

After all the fun, it s important to attend to the baby s hunger. He s been waiting for a delicious treat, so come and feed him some fresh fruits to keep him healthy and happy. Once all the fun-filled activities are over, it s time to wind down. Help the mermaid baby relax and coax him to sleep. Your gentle care will surely make Princess Mermaid proud, and you ll feel great knowing you were there for her little one!

How to play free Mermaid Newborn Baby Care game online

To play the game, follow the on-screen instructions for each activity. Start by tapping the shower icon to clean the baby, then select clothing from the wardrobe. Use your mouse or finger to color the animal pictures. Finally, drag the fruit to the baby s mouth and gently tap him to help him fall asleep!
