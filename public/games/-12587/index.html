Prepare for an adventurous ride in Jumps Blocks Road, a one-of-a-kind physics racing game that invites you to leap into the driver s seat and explore vibrant green hills, majestic snowy mountains, and expansive desert dunes. After a tornado wreaks havoc on your farm, it s up to you to collect scattered pieces and restore it to its former beauty! Experience 3D racing in a uniquely blocky mining world.

Choose from an array of nine diverse vehicles or leverage the innovative car editor to design a custom car block by block. This feature becomes available once you conquer the first three tracks! The game features 12 dynamic tracks to conquer and four challenging tracks that will truly test your racing skills. Whether you prefer trucks, bikes, monster trucks, or tanks, there's an awesome vehicle perfect for your racing style.

Dive into the incredible world of Jumps Blocks Road, where charming voxel graphics bring your experience to life. Compete against your own best times and earn achievements via GameServices while climbing up the leaderboards. This game is designed to be enjoyable for kids, making it a great choice for family gaming. Stay connected for the latest updates and game news from the creators at Dogbyte Games, known for titles like Offroad Legends and Dead Venture.

How to play free Jumps Blocks Road game online

To play, simply select your vehicle and track, then use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate through the race. Collect missing farm parts along the way while racing against your personal best time. Customize your car in the editor once you unlock it, adding a personal touch to your racing experience!
