Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an unforgettable aquatic adventure in "Dolphin Life," where you take on the role of a lively dolphin navigating through an ocean filled with debris and pollution. Your mission is simple yet exhilarating: survive as long as you can while dodging various items like banana peels, oil barrels, and aluminum cans that invade your underwater habitat.

As you swim through the vibrant blue waters, you ll encounter a mix of hazards that threaten your well-being. With each passing moment, the challenge intensifies, pushing you to hone your reflexes and adapt your strategy. The game not only provides a fun and engaging experience but also raises awareness about the impact of garbage on marine life. Your performance is measured by how long you can keep your dolphin safe from the litter that has found its way into your home. Can you become the ultimate champion of the ocean?

Immerse yourself in this captivating environment, where the serenity of underwater life meets the urgent need to protect it from human negligence. Feel the thrill of dodging obstacles while appreciating the beauty of aquatic ecosystems. Join other players around the world and gauge how your survival skills stack up against theirs. Enjoy the harmonious blend of entertainment and environmental consciousness in "Dolphin Life!"

How to play free Dolphin Life game online

To play "Dolphin Life," simply use your mouse or keyboard to steer your dolphin and navigate through the water. Avoid the floating garbage items by moving swiftly and strategically. Try to collect any items that may boost your survival while staying clear of harmful debris. Compete for the highest score and have fun!
