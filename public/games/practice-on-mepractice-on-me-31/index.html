Brace yourself for a spectacular summer experience with Practice On Me, the ultimate makeup game designed for aspiring artists and beauty enthusiasts alike! How to play free Practice On Me game online

To play Practice On Me, simply select a themed unit from the main menu. Once in a unit, use your mouse or touchscreen to choose makeup products, including eyeshadows, lipsticks, and accessories. Apply your desired styles and finalize your look before showcasing your masterpiece at the virtual summer scene!
