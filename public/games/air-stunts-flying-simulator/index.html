Air Stunts Flying Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of soaring through the skies in the exhilarating game, Air Stunts Flying Simulator. Take control of your aircraft as you embark on an adrenaline-pumping journey to various destinations. The sky is your playground, allowing you to demonstrate your flying skills while enjoying the breathtaking views from above.

Once you reach your destination, it s time to switch gears and embrace the excitement of freefalling. Jump out of your plane and feel the rush of the wind as you dive towards the ground at super speed. Remember to manage your descent carefully and open your parachute at the right moment to ensure a safe landing!

The ultimate challenge awaits as you aim for a giant target below. Use your parachute to navigate through the air with precision, adjusting your position to land perfectly on the target. With each jump, improve your skills and enjoy the satisfaction of mastering your parachuting techniques. Will you hit the bullseye?

How to play free Air Stunts Flying Simulator game online

To play, simply select your aircraft and take off. Once in the air, pilot your plane to the desired destination. When you re ready, jump out to begin your freefall. Use the controls to steer your parachute towards the target as you descend. Remember to open it at the right time for a safe landing!


Instructions

Mouse to speed plane and open parachute WASD or Arrows to controll plane Space - Air Brake
