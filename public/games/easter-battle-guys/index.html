Easter Battle Guys

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to take part in an exhilarating Easter egg hunting competition? In Easter Battle Guys, players will immerse themselves in a colorful and whimsical world where the iconic Jelly Bon characters come to life. Your mission is simple yet challenging: gather as many eggs as you can while navigating precarious platforms.

As you compete against your friends, the stakes are high. Stay alert and be strategic in your egg-collecting efforts. Failing to maintain your balance could lead to unfortunate falls off the platform, costing you valuable time and potentially allowing your friend to snag the lead. Utilize your agility to outsmart opponents while keeping an eye on the prize - those delicious Easter eggs!

The game doesn t just test your reflexes; it also features perilous challenges. Beware of wicked sharp rotating knives that swing dangerously from the sides and above. These threats add an exhilarating layer of excitement, as a single misstep could result in your defeat. Remember, in this competitive arena, the player who gathers the most eggs before time runs out will emerge as the champion!

So, hop into the fun, embrace the spirit of competition, and may the best Jelly Bon prevail in this thrilling Easter egg hunt!

How to play free Easter Battle Guys game online

To play Easter Battle Guys, start by selecting your Jelly Bon character. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate and jump across platforms to collect eggs. Avoid hazards like rotating knives and outmaneuver your friend to gather the most eggs before time expires!
