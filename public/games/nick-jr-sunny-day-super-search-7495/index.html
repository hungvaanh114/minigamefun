How to play free Nick Jr. Sunny Day Super Search game online

To play "Sunny Day Super Search," select your desired level at the start. Explore each room and search for hidden characters. Click on them as you find them; you must click all characters to complete the level. Keep your eyes sharp and enjoy the colorful world of Sunny Day!
