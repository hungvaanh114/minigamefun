Foot Surgery Simulator 2d - Foot Doctor

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the shoes of a foot surgeon in the engaging world of Foot Surgery Simulator 2D - Foot Doctor. In this vibrant hospital setting, you'll encounter a plethora of patients, each suffering from various foot ailments. Your mission is to alleviate their pain and restore their feet to full health. Are you ready to tackle the dirt, bacteria, and rashes troubling these patients?

Equipped with a handy sprayer, you'll start by washing the affected areas thoroughly with water. This crucial first step helps eliminate surface dirt and prepares the feet for further treatment. But it doesn t stop there! Utilize the scrubber to deeply cleanse the feet, ensuring that all grime and infection-causing agents are removed. As you treat each patient, you'll also need your trusty tweezers at hand. These are essential for removing stubborn debris and tackling any unwanted bacteria that may be hiding.

With each successful treatment, you'll gain valuable experience and become more proficient in foot care. Improved skills will unlock new tools and techniques, making the game even more exciting. The colorful graphics and realistic sound effects combine to create an immersive experience for budding foot doctors and surgery enthusiasts alike. Dive into this fun simulator, and show your skills while earning the trust and gratitude of your patients!

How to play free Foot Surgery Simulator 2D - Foot Doctor game online

To play the game, simply navigate through the hospital interface. Select a patient in need of treatment, then use the sprayer, scrubber, and tweezers to clean and heal their feet effectively. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete each procedure and keep track of your progress as a foot doctor.
