Plinker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Plinker is a fast-paced and addictive game where players drop a ball and guide it through a maze of obstacles. The goal is to hit and destroy as many blocks as possible, earning points with each successful hit. After each turn, players are presented with a choice of two powerful upgrades to strategically enhance their gameplay and maximize their score. This strategic element adds depth and replayability, encouraging players to experiment and find the most effective combinations for achieving the highest score.

Instructions

Use your touch mouse to place the ball in the best spot release your finger to drop the ball
