Welcome to Dino Simulator City Attack, where the year is 2024, and the long-extinct dinosaurs have miraculously returned, cohabitating with humans in the bustling cityscape. However, this peaceful cohabitation is anything but tranquil! Get ready to unleash your inner dinosaur in this exhilarating and realistic simulation game.

In Dino Simulator City Attack, players have the thrilling opportunity to choose from a variety of compelling dinosaur species. Each dinosaur comes with unique abilities that allow you to run, smash, jump, and wreak havoc throughout a beautifully crafted 3D world. Whether you want to topple skyscrapers, terrify pedestrians, or simply explore your surroundings, the game gives you the freedom to act as a colossal creature in an urban environment.

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter numerous levels that challenge you to complete various missions. These missions are designed to test your skills and provide a sense of progression, as you unlock new levels and dinosaurs along the way. With each successful mission, you can enhance your dinosaur and discover new abilities, all while immersing yourself in a world filled with vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay.

Select your favorite dinosaur from an impressive roster, and prepare to dominate the city! The game's dynamic atmosphere ensures that your experience remains fresh and full of surprises as you dive into this prehistoric adventure.

How to play free Dino Simulator City Attack game online

To play Dino Simulator City Attack, simply choose your favorite dinosaur and start exploring the city. Use the on-screen controls to move, run, and perform various actions. Complete missions by interacting with objects or fulfilling tasks, and enjoy the chaotic fun of being a dinosaur on the loose!
