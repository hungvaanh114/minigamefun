Newborn Puppy Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the Newborn Puppy Salon, an enchanting world where you get to care for adorable puppies from birth! Your journey begins with a pregnant dog mom, who s on the verge of giving birth to a litter of cuddly puppies. Your role is essential assist in prenatal checkups to ensure the mother and her upcoming puppies are healthy and ready for the big moment. As a caretaker, you will learn the importance of nurturing, making sure every step is done with love and care.

Once the puppies arrive, the fun really begins! Your responsibilities include checking their health after birth, making sure they are in perfect condition. Bathing these little pups is an absolute delight; you ll get to wash away the remnants of birth and make them squeaky clean. But it doesn t stop there! Dress each puppy in adorable outfits to showcase their unique personalities. With a variety of accessories available, the style options are endless.

As the day winds down, it s your job to tuck these tiny furballs into bed for a cozy sleep. Create a safe and warm environment where they can dream sweet puppy dreams. Will you rise to the challenge of being the best puppy caretaker in the Newborn Puppy Salon? Choose your favorite puppy and begin this heartwarming adventure today!

How to play free Newborn Puppy Salon game online

To play the Newborn Puppy Salon game, simply navigate through the easy-to-use interface. Click on different icons to interact with the pregnant mom and the newborn puppies. Follow the prompts for checkups, bathing, dressing, and finally, putting the puppies to sleep. Enjoy managing your adorable puppy salon!
