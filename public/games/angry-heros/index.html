Angry Heros

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Angry Heroes is an exhilarating shooter physics game that invites players to team up with a group of brave little heroes on a mission to eradicate the alien threat threatening our world. As you navigate through a vibrant and fantastical landscape, you ll rely on your skills and strategy to use a slingshot, launching your heroes towards the enemy with precision to ensure maximum damage. Each level presents a unique challenge, where you must destroy alien shelters while employing various power-ups that can enhance your destructive abilities. The goal is not only to clear obstacles but to also gather resources to bolster your heroes strengths.

Throughout this action-packed adventure, players will encounter formidable bosses that require clever tactics to conquer. Whether utilizing the power of teamwork or unlocking special abilities, each encounter will test your reflexes and problem-solving skills. The vibrant graphics and dynamic gameplay keep players engaged, ensuring that every moment spent in the game is filled with excitement and anticipation.

As you progress through innumerable levels, the game rewards your efforts with new slingshot techniques, unique heroes, and an array of upgrades that add depth to the gameplay. With relentless aliens at your heel, it s a race against time to save the world alongside your heroic companions. Battle your way through beautifully designed terrains, each filled with nostalgic elements reminiscent of classic arcade shooters. Are you ready to take on this adventure and restore peace to your planet?

How to play free Angry Heroes game online

To play Angry Heroes, first, select your hero from the available options and familiarize yourself with their abilities. Use the mouse or touch screen to aim and pull back the slingshot. Release to launch your hero at the alien shelters. Collect power-ups along the way to enhance your destruction and defeat the bosses!
