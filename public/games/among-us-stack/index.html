Among Us Stack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Among Us Stack, a multiplayer game that engages you in an exciting battle of wits and deception. Team up with 4 to 10 players, either online or via local WiFi, as you work together to prepare your spaceship for takeoff. But keep your eyes peeled, as one among you is a cunning impostor intent on eliminating fellow crew members.

As a crewmate, your goal is twofold: accomplish all essential tasks scattered across the spaceship or unmask the impostor among your ranks. Each task completed inches your team closer to victory, while careful observation and communication help unveil the deceitful player hidden in plain sight. Engage in strategy-filled discussions during emergency meetings to determine who to vote off the ship and secure your path to safety.

On the flip side, if you take on the role of the impostor, you'll enjoy the thrilling challenge of sabotaging the team's efforts. Utilize clever tricks and strategic sabotage to sow discord and create opportunities for stealthy eliminations. Rely on deception and cunning to maintain your alibi while evading detection until you can claim victory over the unsuspecting crewmates.

How to play free Among Us Stack game online

To play Among Us Stack online, join or create a game lobby and invite friends or other players. Once in the game, work on completing your assigned tasks if you're a crewmate or plot your strategy as the impostor. Communicate with other players, vote during discussions, and use your wits to achieve victory!
