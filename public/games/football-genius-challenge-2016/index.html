How to play free Football Genius Challenge 2016 game online

To play the Football Genius Challenge, simply tap on a football ball to burst it. Each ball you pop may cause others to burst, creating a chain reaction. Your objective is to clear the entire screen of footballs. Plan your moves carefully and enjoy the strategic fun!
