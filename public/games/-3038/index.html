Cleaning the Islands?Cleaning the Islands

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Cleaning the Islands," players step into the shoes of a survivor stranded on a deserted island, where they must muster their wit and resourcefulness to thrive. As they navigate this vibrant, tropical environment, your key objective is to collect various resources scattered across the island. From wood and stones to rare herbs and treasures, each item you gather can be transformed into gold coins, which serves as your currency in the game.

As you accumulate wealth, you have the opportunity to spend your gold coins on enhancing your island paradise. Build stunning structures, create beautiful landscapes, and customize your very own slice of paradise. The game also emphasizes the importance of progress, as players can upgrade their tools for more efficient gathering. With improved tools, tasks can be completed much faster, allowing you to maximize your resource collection.

Additionally, players can expand their backpack capacity, which means you ll be able to store more materials at once. This feature is crucial for streamlining your collection efforts and ensuring that your island continues to grow and flourish. Balancing resource management and building is key to achieving a thriving and visually stunning island sanctuary.

How to play free Cleaning the Islands game online

To play "Cleaning the Islands," simply start by exploring your surroundings to gather resources. Click on items to collect them and navigate back to your home base to sell these resources for gold coins. Use the coins to buy upgrades for tools and enhance your backpack capacity, allowing you to work more efficiently and expand your island's beauty.
