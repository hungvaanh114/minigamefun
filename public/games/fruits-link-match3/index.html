How to play free Fruits Link Match3 game online

To play the Fruits Link Match3 game, simply swipe across the screen to connect three or more identical fruits. Once linked, they will disappear, and new fruits will fall into place. Aim to complete each level's objective as quickly as possible to earn higher scores and unlock subsequent levels!
