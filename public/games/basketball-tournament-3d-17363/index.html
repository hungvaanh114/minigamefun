Basketball Tournament 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting journey with our latest release, Basketball Tournament 3D! This immersive game invites you to chase your basketball dreams while participating in thrilling free throw competitions. With six unique modes, you'll find continuous excitement and challenges that will keep you engaged for hours on end. Gone are the days of monotonous gameplay; our diverse basketball settings ensure that every session feels fresh and invigorating!

In Basketball Tournament 3D, the rules take a fun twist. As you play this free shot game, you'll earn 2 points for each basketball you successfully throw through the hoop. Challenge yourself further by aiming for a clean shot, which grants you 3 points, regardless of the distance from the basket. To elevate your scoring experience, you can achieve a fireball bonus through consistent successful shots, or tap into the backboard bonus by using the backboard to land those crucial baskets. This way, every shot counts, and strategy plays an important role in accumulating points!

Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the world of basketball, this game is designed for everyone. Get ready to develop your shooting skills, show off your precision, and compete against friends or AI opponents in electrifying tournaments. Basketball Tournament 3D is where passion meets competition in the most thrilling manner!

How to play free Basketball Tournament 3D game online

To play Basketball Tournament 3D, simply use your mouse or touch screen to aim and shoot. Adjust your angle and power before releasing the ball toward the hoop. Keep an eye on your score and try to hit streaks for bonuses! Explore different modes and become the ultimate basketball champion!
