Gravity Control

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience an exhilarating journey in Gravity Control, a unique puzzle platformer that challenges your problem-solving skills in a whole new dimension. In this game, you won't be guiding the character directly; instead, you'll manipulate the very force of gravity itself. By changing the direction of gravity, you affect the player's movement, creating a fresh and engaging gameplay experience.

Your ultimate goal is to navigate through 60 meticulously crafted levels, each presenting its own set of puzzles. You'll face various obstacles and brain-teasing challenges that require you to think creatively. As you guide the player toward the elusive wormhole, you ll employ gravity manipulation to traverse platforms, avoid hazards, and solve intricate puzzles that get progressively more challenging.

Every level is an opportunity to experiment and discover innovative solutions as gravity becomes a tool in your hands. Whether you re flipping the world upside down or altering gravitational fields to create safe paths, each decision you make will impact the player's journey. Get ready to unlock your potential and enjoy an engaging puzzle-adventure that combines strategy and physics!

How to play free Gravity Control game online

To play Gravity Control, use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to change the direction of gravity while the player runs automatically. Your objective is to guide them towards the wormhole, using gravity shifts to navigate obstacles efficiently. Keep trying different angles to find the best path!
