Zombie Horde

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Zombie Horde, an exhilarating shooting game where your primary mission is to eliminate relentless waves of zombies that threaten your survival. As you navigate through this intense world, each zombie you take down earns you valuable points, which you can invest in an arsenal of powerful weapons and advanced bullet types. The stakes get higher as the game progresses; not only do the zombies multiply, but they also become increasingly fierce and agile, making your task even more challenging. It s crucial to stay alert and take strategic actions, as just one encounter could lead to your demise.

Throughout the game, players will encounter various types of zombies, each with distinct characteristics and difficulties. The longer you survive, the more formidable your foes become. Your ingenuity and reflexes will be tested as you strive to rack up kills and avoid getting overwhelmed. Will you be able to withstand the Zombie Horde and rise to the top of the leaderboard?

Develop your strategy, upgrade your weapons, and ensure your survival in this thrilling battle against the undead. Engage in the fight, outsmart the advancing horde, and show no mercy as you defend your territory from these monstrous invaders!

How to play free Zombie Horde game online

To play Zombie Horde, use your mouse to aim and click to shoot at the incoming zombies. Keep an eye on your ammo and collect points from each kill to purchase upgrades. Avoid letting the zombies get too close, as they will defeat you if you aren't quick on the draw!


Instructions

Use A S D and W to move and the mouse to aim and shoot Press P to pause and restart the game
