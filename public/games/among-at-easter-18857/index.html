Among at Easter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the excitement of "Among at Easter," a thrilling and engaging online game that can accommodate 4-10 players. Set in a vibrant, colorful Easter-themed spaceship, players unite to prepare for launch, completing various tasks that are crucial for a successful departure. Gather your friends or join players around the world over local WiFi as everyone races against the clock to finish their objectives, all while keeping an eye out for the lurking threat within their ranks.

Among the crewmates, however, lies an impostor whose sole mission is to sabotage their efforts and eliminate them one by one. This sinister character has multiple tools at their disposal, allowing for strategic chaos and the perfect opportunity to execute stealthy kills. As the impostor weaves through the colorful corridors of your spaceship, they can sow confusion and mislead the crew, utilizing sabotage to hinder their progress. This blend of cooperation and deception creates a unique social gameplay experience where trust is continually challenged.

Crewmates must not only focus on completing their tasks but also remain vigilant in identifying the impostor. The game's nail-biting tension escalates with each passing second, culminating in intense discussions and voting rounds where players must decide who they believe the impostor is. Will you successfully expose the traitor among you, or will the impostor outsmart you all and claim victory?

How to play free Among at Easter game online

To join a game of "Among at Easter," simply create or enter a lobby. Once inside, familiarize yourself with your tasks and observe fellow players. As a crewmate, try to complete your tasks while staying alert for suspicious behavior. If you spot an impostor, rally your crewmates to vote them out during emergency meetings!
