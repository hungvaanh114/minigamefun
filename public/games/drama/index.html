DRAMA

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

DRAMA is an intricately designed platformer game that challenges your problem-solving skills through a variety of engaging puzzles. As you dive into the game, you'll need to think creatively to navigate through the world while ensuring the survival of your character. The game encourages you to explore and interact with your environment, uncovering hidden objects and utilizing your skills to progress.

Your ultimate goal in DRAMA is to reach the end of each level, which comprises a series of 96 uniquely crafted challenges. Each level introduces new obstacles and puzzle elements, demanding agility and quick thinking. Be mindful, for every time your character meets an unfortunate end, a body will be left behind, and you will lose a precious life from your initial pool of 1000. The stakes are high, and you ll need to carefully balance your approach to each puzzle.

As you advance, you'll have the option to request hints when you find yourself stuck; however, proceed with caution. Utilizing clues does come at a cost you ll lose one life for each hint you ask for. Additionally, if you choose to restart a level, that will also lead to the loss of a life. Time is of the essence in DRAMA, so remain focused, adapt your strategies, and use your cleverness to help guide Frank to the exit while preserving as many lives as possible.

How to play free DRAMA game online

To play DRAMA online, simply start the game and follow the on-screen instructions to learn the controls. Use the arrow keys or designated buttons to run, jump, and swing your way through obstacles. Keep an eye on your remaining lives and make strategic choices to solve puzzles efficiently.


Instructions

UP or W jump and climb rope or ladder DOWN or S go down rope or ladder RIGHT or D move right LEFT or A move left SPACE jump off the rope or ladder
