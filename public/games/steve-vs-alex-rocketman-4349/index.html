Steve vs Alex Rocketman

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In a thrilling adventure titled Steve vs Alex Rocketman, players step into the shoes of two astronauts Steve and Alex navigating the perilous world of skyblock. As they journey through this dangerous terrain, you must assist them in surviving against all odds. The path is fraught with hazardous TNT rocks and menacing rotating sharp TNTs that can obliterate them in an instant if they stray too far from the safety of their zone.

Your objective is clear: help Steve and Alex collect three gold bars. These precious items are crucial for their escape through the portal. However, time is of the essence and careful strategy is required. Stay alert as you venture into the unknown, working in tandem with your companion to navigate the treacherous landscape. Make calculated moves to avoid the peril lurking around every corner while ensuring that both astronauts make it to safety.

In this intense race against danger, every choice counts. Only one of you will be able to reach the portal once you ve secured the gold bars, so teamwork and precision are vital. Will you manage to evade the deadly traps that await and lead Steve and Alex to victory? The fate of these astronauts rests in your hands. Prepare yourself for an exhilarating experience that promises challenges and excitement at every turn!

How to play free Steve vs Alex Rocketman game online

To play Steve vs Alex Rocketman, use the arrow keys to move your character and the space bar to jump. Team up with your friend to explore the skyblock environment safely. Collect gold bars together while avoiding TNT hazards, and guide one of you to the portal once all the gold is secured. Remember, teamwork is essential for survival!
