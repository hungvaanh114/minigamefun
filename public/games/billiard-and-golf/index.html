Billiard and Golf

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thrilling combination of two classic games with Billiard and Golf, an innovative blend of golf and pool that promises endless fun! This unique game challenges players to navigate a snooker board, where the objective is to strategically calculate your shots to sink the ball into the golf notch. Using your mouse, aim your cue, and send the ball rolling toward its destination.

Each level requires not just skill, but also precise planning, as you attempt to drop the ball into the hole with pinpoint accuracy. As you progress, you'll encounter over 30 different levels, each featuring unique pool tables and setups to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting. The more successful shots you make consecutively, the higher your score a testament to your growing expertise in this captivating mix of billiards and golf.

With vibrant graphics and seamless gameplay, Billiard and Golf provides a fantastic gaming experience for players of all ages. Whether you re looking to unwind after a long day or challenge yourself to beat your own high score, this game has something for everyone. Dive into the fun and see how many levels you can conquer while honing your skills in both billiards and golf!

How to play free Billiard and Golf game online

To start playing Billiard and Golf, use your mouse to aim and set the force of your shot on the snooker board. Click to release the ball in the desired direction, ensuring you calculate the trajectory to sink it into the golf notch. Keep an eye on your consecutive successful shots to maximize your score!
