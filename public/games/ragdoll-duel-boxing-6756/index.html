Ragdoll Duel: Boxing

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of "Ragdoll Duel: Boxing," an action-packed fighting game where fun meets excitement in a closed fighting arena. In this dynamic game, you ll take control of various stickman boxer characters, each designed with unique shapes and animations that bring life to the arena. Engage in intense bouts where your primary objective is to outsmart and overpower your opponent with swift punches and clever moves.

The gameplay is straightforward yet exhilarating, allowing players to unleash their inner fighter. You ll have to think quickly and act even quicker landing swift jabs and dodging incoming hits is essential for victory. Don't forget about your ultimate weapon: the Super Punch! This powerful attack can turn the tides of battle when used at the right moment. Time is of the essence, and mastering your timing will be key to scoring those all-important points.

Play with a friend for a competitive edge, or challenge yourself against an AI opponent designed to test your skills. Whether you re looking for a light-hearted duel or an intense showdown, "Ragdoll Duel: Boxing" has got you covered. Jump into the fun and experience the electrifying moments that only this unique fighting game can deliver. Remember, each match consists of two rounds, so make every punch count and strive for the glory of victory!

How to play free Ragdoll Duel: Boxing game online

To play "Ragdoll Duel: Boxing," select whether you want to face an AI opponent or challenge a friend. Use your keyboard keys to control your stickman boxer one player can use WASD keys while the other uses arrow keys. Attack with quick punches and save your Super Punch for the perfect moment to ensure your triumph!
