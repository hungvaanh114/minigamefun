Jelly Match 4

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the vibrant world of Jelly Match 4, your objective is to create combinations of delightful jelly blocks. The game presents you with sets of blocks located in the left panel, which you can choose and strategically position on the game board. To score points, simply align four or more identical jelly blocks in a row either horizontally or vertically. To enhance your gameplay, you have the option to rotate the block sets by clicking on them, allowing for more tantalizing combinations and higher scores.

Be cautious, as your moves are limited to the three sets of blocks available at any given moment! If you encounter a situation where none of the current block sets can fit onto the board, your game will come to an abrupt end. The challenge escalates as you progress, demanding thoughtful placements and quick strategic thinking to keep the jelly blocks flowing and the game alive. Dive into the colorful realm of Jelly Match 4 and refine your puzzle-solving skills as you compete for the highest score!

For more thrilling games and challenges, visit the developer's website at https://www.lofgames.com/.

How to play free Jelly Match 4 game online

To enjoy Jelly Match 4, start by selecting a block set from the left panel. Drag and drop the blocks onto the board, aiming to align four identical blocks in either a horizontal or vertical line. Rotate the block sets by clicking on them to find the best placement, and remember to keep an eye on your available sets to avoid game over!
