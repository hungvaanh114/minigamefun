Spooky Block Collapse

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Spooky Block Collapse, a captivating puzzle game that invites players to engage with an array of spooky blocks. Your objective is simple yet addictive: collect all the spooky blocks by clicking or tapping on them in clusters. To earn points, make sure to gather groups of two or more. The excitement ramps up when you combine seven or more blocks in a single move, unlocking powerful bonuses like Bombs, Arrows, or the elusive Magic spooky block. However, be cautious! Clicking on a solitary block will result in a deduction of 200 points from your score.

To maximize your score, keep an eye on the projection box, which indicates the value of each collection. As you progress through the levels, aim to reach or surpass the target score to clear all blocks from the board and advance to the next stage. Even if you fall short of the required amount, don t worry you can always take another shot at the level, allowing for endless opportunities to improve your strategy and gameplay.

Get ready for an engaging experience filled with spooky challenges and excitement. Can you master the art of block collection and rise to the top of the leaderboard? Test your skills and embrace the fun of Spooky Block Collapse today!

How to play free Spooky Block Collapse game online

To play Spooky Block Collapse, simply click or touch blocks in groups of two or more to collect them and earn points. Keep an eye on the score projection box to track your progress. Aim for larger groups to unlock power-ups and clear the board for advancement to the next level!
