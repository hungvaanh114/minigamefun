How to play free Monster Truck game online

To play the Monster Truck game, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control your truck's movement. The up arrow accelerates your monster truck, while the down arrow allows for braking and reverse. Collect coins by driving over them and perform stunts with the space bar to achieve extra points. Enjoy the ride!
