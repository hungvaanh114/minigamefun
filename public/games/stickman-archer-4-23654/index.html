Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating experience with Stickman Archer 4, a dynamic stickman game that promises hours of fun across three intriguing game modes. In the first mode, players must test their archery skills by shooting at both moving and stationary targets. With a limited supply of arrows, precision and timing are essential to maximize your score.

The second mode brings a thrilling one-on-one duel with another stickman. Here, you ll need to rely on both strategy and skill to outsmart your opponent. Can you prove yourself as the ultimate archer in this combat showdown? The excitement never ends as you engage in fast-paced battles, dodging arrows while aiming to land the perfect shot.

For those looking for an entirely different challenge, the third mode of the game will have you clearing a multi-storey building but without the trusty bow. Instead, you ll be utilizing a conventional gun to take out targets lurking within. Each level brings its own set of challenges, requiring players to think quickly and act decisively.

In all three modes, players are tasked with collecting emeralds, a form of in-game currency that can be exchanged for brand new weapons and stylish skins. This feature adds an exciting layer of customization, allowing you to tailor your stickman to your liking and enhance your gaming experience.

How to play free Stickman Archer 4 game online

To play Stickman Archer 4, simply choose a game mode from the main menu. Use your mouse or touchscreen to aim your weapon and release to shoot. Collect emeralds as you progress, and remember to switch weapons between modes for the best possible performance!
