Heaven or Hell the Choice Is Yours

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Distribute souls between heaven and hell! The choice is yours! In the game you have to choose where to send the souls of people! Send sinners to HELL, and the righteous to HEAVEN, dont make a mistake! Well, or vice versa, the choice is yours! Angels and demons will tell you. Its just a fun game. Enjoy and have a fun time. Good luck!

Instructions

Management for mobile devices Hold your finger on the screen to move from side to side Control for PC Hold down the cursor on the screen or use the arrows on the keyboard to move from side to side
