Ice Princess Is Preparing for Spring Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Spring has arrived in the enchanting kingdom, and excitement fills the air as all the inhabitants look forward to a grand celebration at the Ice Princess Palace. Elize, the beloved Ice Princess, needs your assistance to make this Spring Ball an unforgettable event. Your mission is to help her prepare the palace to welcome her royal guests.

Begin your adventure by giving the palace a thorough cleaning. You will need to replace any broken glass and sweep the floors to ensure everything sparkles. Once the palace is spotless, it s time to shift your focus to decoration! The throne room will serve as the centerpiece of the celebration, and it requires your creative touch.

Adorn the walls with beautiful garlands of flowers, set up sumptuous treats on the tables for the attendees, and arrange delightful gifts that will impress guests. Don't forget the balloons they will add a cheerful touch and a festive atmosphere to the gathering! With all the preparations completed, Elize will surely have a magical ball that everyone will remember.

How to play free Ice Princess is preparing for spring ball game online

In the game, assist Elize by using your mouse to interact with different objects around the palace. Click to clean and fix broken items, then drag and drop decorations into place to prepare the throne room. Make sure to complete each task to ensure the Spring Ball is a dazzling success!
