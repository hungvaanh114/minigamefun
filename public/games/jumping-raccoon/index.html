How to play free Jumping Raccoon game online

To play the "Jumping Raccoon" game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to guide your raccoon. Press the jump key to evade obstacles and collect items. The key to success is timing your jumps accurately to clear hurdles while maintaining your speed to reach the finish line!
