Endless Cat Climb

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Endless Cat Climb is an exhilarating game that captures the spirit of fun and challenge! If you enjoyed Only Up, you re in for a treat with this delightful adventure. In this game, players guide an agile cat as it climbs a towering tree, navigating its way to the highest branches while eagerly collecting coins. The higher you climb, the faster your feline friend ascends, ramping up the excitement and difficulty as time progresses.

The goal is to achieve a score of 1,000 points while attempting to earn the coveted 3-star rating. But beware! Along your ascent, various obstacles will appear, demanding quick reflexes and strategic maneuvers to avoid them. Each coin collected contributes to your score, and maintaining a steady climb will increase your chances of topping the leaderboard. Are you ready to put your skills to the test? Let s see if you can master the treacherous tree and leave your mark as the ultimate Endless Cat Climb champion!

How to play free Endless Cat Climb game online

To play Endless Cat Climb, simply tap or click to make the cat jump between branches while avoiding obstacles. Collect coins to boost your score and monitor your progress. Aim for the highest score possible while trying to get those 3 stars! Good luck!
