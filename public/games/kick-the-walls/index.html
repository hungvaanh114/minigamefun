How to play free kick the walls game online

To play Kick the Walls, simply use your arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your character around. As you approach barriers, time your kicks carefully to clear them successfully. Collect power-ups along the way to maximize your score and enhance your gameplay. Enjoy the challenge!
