Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with your trio of best friends in the enchanting BFF World Trip Halloween. This time, your journey takes you to the glitzy streets of Hollywood, where dreams come to life! Transform lovely Naomi into a captivating Hawaiian princess by adorning her in the most stunning hula girl attire. Unleash your creativity as you select colors and patterns that highlight her beauty while incorporating vibrant floral accessories.

Meanwhile, sweet Serena longs for a magical transformation into an ice princess. Your task is to craft the most enchanting dress, complete with shimmering fabrics and elegant details. Use your fashion sense to make her look every bit the regal princess she dreams to be.

As for Bella, the fun escalates! Get creative and design a spooky yet stylish vampire princess look for her. Alternatively, you can turn her into a lively zombie cheerleader or even opt for a quirky monster girl makeover. Each choice allows you to explore various fun outfits and accessories that fit the Halloween theme.

To sweeten the adventure, surprise your new best friends with delicious ice cream treats! Channel your inner chef and create mouth-watering frozen desserts that are sure to delight. With so many exciting options, you ll be at the heart of unforgettable Halloween fun with your BFFs!

How to play free BFF World Trip Halloween game online

In the game, you will navigate different levels by selecting characters and customizing their looks using an array of outfits and accessories. Use your mouse or touch controls to drag and drop fashion items onto each friend. Complete the stunning transformations and serve them delightful treats to elevate the experience!
