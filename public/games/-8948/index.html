Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Your adventure awaits in MY Unicorn Pony Pet Salon, where you get the chance to care for the most enchanting creatures unicorn ponies! This delightful game invites you to train your skills in grooming and styling these magical pets. With various fun activities at your fingertips, you're bound to have a wonderful time transforming your unicorn pony into a stylish spectacle.

Your unicorn pony is in need of your expertise to achieve the ultimate chic look. Begin your salon experience by giving your pony a warm bath, using luxurious shampoo to cleanse her shimmering mane. Then, gently massage her skin with soap, ensuring she is clean and ready for her stylish makeover. After pampering, it s time to select a charming outfit that will make your unicorn pony stand out among all others!

This game offers a plethora of dress-up options and stunning hairstyles that let your creativity shine. Take your time to choose the perfect look; whether it s a glamorous gown or a cute accessory, dressing is a crucial part of making her look extraordinary. Experiment with numerous hair designs and select the one that perfectly complements your unicorn pony's personality.

Once your unicorn is all styled, pose for fabulous pictures in the photo booth and apply cute and funny stickers to enhance those photo memories. Enjoy non-stop fun while grooming, dressing, and accessorizing in MY Unicorn Pony Pet Salon. Every step you take leads your unicorn closer to becoming the princess of unicorn land. Embrace your imagination and unforgettable experiences await!

How to play free MY Unicorn Pony Pet Salon game online

To play MY Unicorn Pony Pet Salon, start by selecting your unicorn pony from the available options. Use intuitive touch controls to give your pony a bath, dress her up, and style her hair. Once your pony is ready, capture pictures and share your magical creations!
