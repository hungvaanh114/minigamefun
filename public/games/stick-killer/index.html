Stick Killer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Stick Killer, players are thrust into a chaotic urban environment where survival and wealth accumulation are the primary objectives. As a stick figure navigating the bustling streets of this treacherous city, your mission is to amass as much money as possible while dodging pedestrians and outsmarting the law enforcement officers who patrol the area. The stakes are high, as the police aren't afraid to use live ammunition to maintain order. You must strategize your movements and utilize the city s layout to evade capture and rack up your earnings.

Each encounter with a pedestrian can either yield rewards or consequences, so you must carefully decide which paths to take. The streets are unpredictable, filled with various obstacles and opportunities. Only those who can think on their feet and adapt to the ever-changing landscape will thrive in this perilous pursuit. Will you take risks to maximize your profits, or will caution be your guiding principle? Only time will tell in the frenetic rush for riches within the city of Stick Killer.

With a mix of fast-paced action, clever maneuvers, and the constant threat of violence from both pedestrians and police, Stick Killer offers a unique gaming experience that keeps players engaged and on their toes. It s a game of quick decisions, adrenaline-pumping encounters, and an insatiable craving for money that drives the gameplay forward. Get ready to navigate the dangerous streets and prove your mettle!

How to play free Stick Killer game online

To play the Stick Killer game online, navigate your stick figure through the city by using the arrow keys or on-screen controls. Avoid getting hit by pedestrians and police while collecting money scattered throughout the streets. Use quick reflexes to survive and maximize your earnings!
