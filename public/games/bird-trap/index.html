Bird Trap

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the captivating game Bird Trap, players embark on a thrilling adventure to assist a charming bird in navigating a hazardous environment. Your primary objective is to safeguard the bird from colliding with the deadly walls of the trap. With each passing moment, the challenge intensifies, and your reflexes will be put to the test. The goal is simple yet addictive: survive as long as possible while racking up points. The longer you keep the bird safe, the higher your score will soar!

As you guide the bird, you'll encounter numerous obstacles that demand quick thinking and precise timing. Every successful maneuver boosts your score and contributes to a growing sense of accomplishment. The game's vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay keep players immersed in this delightful yet challenging experience. Can you beat your previous scores and emerge as the ultimate protector of our feathered friend? Get ready for endless fun in Bird Trap!

How to play free Bird Trap game online

To play Bird Trap, use your mouse or keyboard to maneuver the bird around the screen. Keep it away from the walls while avoiding any approaching traps. Your objective is to stay safe for as long as possible, accumulating points with each second you survive. Let the adventure begin!
