Haton 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Haton 2 is an engaging 2D platformer that challenges players to navigate through vibrant levels while collecting oranges. Each level presents unique obstacles, including flying robots, treacherous spikes, and dangerous saws. The ultimate goal is to reach the red flag at the end of each stage, which serves as the gateway to the next thrilling level. Packed with eight distinctive stages, Haton 2 offers increasing difficulty as players progress, ensuring an exhilarating experience that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking.

As you embark on this colorful adventure, you'll encounter various enemy types designed to impede your progress. Timing is crucial as you leap over spikes and dodge the relentless robots that hover in your path. The path to the red flag is not only about speed but also about keen observation and learning enemy patterns to effectively avoid them. Each collected orange adds to your score, motivating players to explore every nook and cranny of the beautifully designed levels.

With vibrant graphics and smooth controls, Haton 2 provides an immersive platforming experience that both casual gamers and seasoned players can enjoy. Whether you are in it for the challenge or just to enjoy the colorful aesthetics, Haton 2 is sure to provide hours of entertainment as you strive to master every level while trying to avoid the menacing traps along the way.

How to play free Haton 2 game online

To play Haton 2, use the arrow keys for movement and jump over obstacles while collecting oranges. Be mindful of your surroundings to dodge enemies and navigate through spikes and saws. Aim for the red flag to finish each level and progress to the next.
