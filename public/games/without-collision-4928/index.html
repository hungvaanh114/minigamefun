Without Collision

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the world of Without Collision, an exhilarating reflex puzzle game that tests your agility and quick-thinking skills. In this game, you will navigate within a confined space where every decision matters. Your objective is simple yet challenging: control the blue egg and catch as many blue dots as possible.

The gameplay mechanics are straightforward. The blue egg can only move vertically, while the tempting blue dots come rushing in from both horizontal sides. However, you must stay alert because red triangles pose a serious threat. These red dangers move in every direction, and a collision with them will result in an immediate loss. With your instincts pushed to the limit, the challenge escalates as you try to gather blue dots while evading the triangles.

Each blue dot you collect prolongs your survival and boosts your score, urging you to think fast and act even faster. How long can you last in this tense environment? The more blue dots you catch, the longer you'll play, making for a compelling experience wherein each attempt to beat your high score becomes more thrilling than the last.

How to play free Without Collision game online

To play Without Collision, use the up and down arrow keys or the W and S keys to move the blue egg vertically. Your goal is to catch the blue dots while steering clear of the red triangles that move unpredictably. Stay sharp, and see how many blue dots you can collect to extend your gameplay!
