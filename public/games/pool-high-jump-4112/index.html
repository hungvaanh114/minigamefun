Pool High Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pool High Jump, an exhilarating game that challenges your diving skills like never before! In this exciting adventure, you take on the role of a diver whose objective is to leap gracefully into a moving pool of water. Sounds simple? Think again! The dynamics of the game will put your precision and timing to the ultimate test.

As you prepare to take the plunge, be aware that the pool is constantly swinging from side to side while shrinking in size. This means that you must not only jump from a significant height but also remain focused to land safely in the ever-decreasing target. Each unsuccessful jump will lead to dramatic consequences: if you miss the pool, your diver will be met with a splash that spells disaster, resulting in a loss of life.

Your objective is clear make sure you land in the pool to keep your diver safe and maximize your score. However, be cautious! You only have three lives to spare. Losing all your lives means game over, so precision and timing are crucial as you aim to become the champion diver. The tension of each jump increases as the pool continues to change, providing an exhilarating experience that keeps you coming back for more!

How to play free Pool High Jump game online

To play Pool High Jump, use the controls to guide the diver's jump. Timing is key, so watch the pool's movements closely. Click or tap to leap at the right moment for a chance to land in the shrinking pool. Aim carefully, and don't forget missing the pool will cost you a life!

Tap at the game screen when you think it is the right time to jump
