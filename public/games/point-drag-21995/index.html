Point Drag

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of high-speed driving in Point Drag, the ultimate sports car drifting game! Take control of four distinct cars, each designed for speed and precision as you navigate challenging tracks. Use your finger to steer and execute flawless drifts to showcase your skills.

In this exciting skill game, your primary goal is to maintain control of your car while pushing it to its limits. Mastering the art of drifting is essential to achieving the best results. As you zoom around bends and straights, stay focused on your trajectory to avoid going off-track. The more fluid your movements, the higher you'll score, making every drift an opportunity to shine.

Point Drag isn t just about speed; it s about honing your driving abilities in a captivating environment. With each successful drift, you'll feel a rush of adrenaline as you prove your prowess behind the wheel. Gather points and unlock new features as you progress through this thrilling game. Are you ready to take on the challenge and become a drifting champion?

How to play free Point Drag game online

To play Point Drag, simply use your finger to control the car's direction on the screen. Swipe left or right to steer, and tap to accelerate. Keep your vehicle on the track while executing perfect drifts. Aim for the highest score by performing well-timed maneuvers and avoiding obstacles. Good luck!
