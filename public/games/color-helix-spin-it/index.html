Color Helix: Spin It

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into a dynamic world of color and speed! In this exciting arcade game, you control a ball that rolls along a rotating cylinder. There are obstacles painted in different colors along the way. Your task is to turn the cylinder so that the ball passes through sections of its color. But be careful! The ball can change its color right during the level, making the task more difficult. Each new stage offers increasingly complex combinations, testing your reaction and ability to make quick decisions. Game features: Exciting gameplay with intuitive controls. Colorful levels with a unique design. Gradually increasing difficulty.

Instructions

Control the rotation of the cylinder so that the ball passes through obstacles of its color Turn the cylinder left or right avoiding sections of other colors The ball will roll automatically
