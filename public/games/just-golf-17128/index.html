Just Golf

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Just Golf is a unique blend of minimalistic design and thought-provoking puzzle mechanics that offers players 210 meticulously crafted levels, each presenting a gradually increasing challenge. Your main objective in this engaging game is to skillfully drop the ball into the hole marked by a red flag. The game allows for unlimited attempts, encouraging you to refine your skills and techniques as you progress through each phase.

The simplicity of the gameplay is complemented by a guided aim feature, which helps you accurately line up your shots, making it easier to navigate each level. This intuitive aiming system adds an extra layer of accessibility for players of all skill levels, allowing both newcomers and seasoned golfers to immerse themselves in the gameplay without feeling overwhelmed. Additionally, as you strive to complete each level, you'll have the opportunity to collect three stars scattered throughout the course, enhancing your challenge and rewarding thorough exploration of the mechanics.

In Just Golf, the combination of straightforward gameplay and vibrant visuals creates an enjoyable gaming experience, making it perfect for those looking to unwind while still engaging their minds. The increased complexity of each level encourages strategic thinking and creativity, ultimately providing a satisfying sense of accomplishment once you sink that final ball into the hole.

How to play free Just Golf game online

To play Just Golf online, simply aim your shot using the on-screen guide and adjust the power of your swing by dragging your finger or cursor. Release to hit the ball and watch it travel toward the flag. Collect all three stars in each level to maximize your score!
