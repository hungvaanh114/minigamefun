Galaxy Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Galaxy Attack, the fate of Earth hangs in the balance as alien swarms threaten humanity's existence. Players are tasked with assuming control of a solitary spaceship, tasked with defending our planet against waves of menacing foes. You must deftly navigate through a series of hazardous environments, showcasing your skills as you face an increasing army of alien adversaries. The stakes are high, and every moment counts as you take on this dangerous mission to save the Universe from total destruction.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter diverse challenges that will put your reflexes and strategic thinking to the test. Each level introduces new enemy types and intensifies the battle, requiring quick reactions and precise maneuvering. Throughout this exhilarating space shooting adventure, players have the opportunity to accumulate points that can be used to upgrade their spacecraft. These enhancements will unlock powerful weapons and formidable defenses, allowing you to blast through the alien horde with increasing ferocity.

With stunning graphics and immersive sound effects, Galaxy Attack creates a captivating experience that draws players into its cosmic battlefield. Are you ready to take command of your spaceship and embark on this epic journey to defend Earth? Prepare yourself for an engaging challenge that will keep you at the edge of your seat!

How to play free Galaxy Attack game online

To play the Galaxy Attack game, simply move your spaceship using the arrow keys or touchscreen controls. Fire your weapons at incoming enemies, dodging their attacks while collecting power-ups along the way. Strategically upgrade your spaceship to maximize your firepower and resilience against the overwhelming alien forces.
