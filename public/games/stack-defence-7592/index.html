Stack Defence

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Stack Defense, a captivating multiplayer game where exhilarating battles await you against real players from around the globe! In this dynamic adventure, your primary objective is to construct the tallest tower possible while tactically outmaneuvering and demolishing your opponents. Each level presents unique challenges that require quick thinking and strategic planning to dominate the leaderboard.

As you embark on your Stack Defense journey, you'll navigate through an array of vibrant and engaging environments, collecting resources and power-ups to bolster your tower-building efforts. The mechanics are simple yet addictive, making each match a blend of strategy and skill. Compete in countless levels where your building prowess and combat tactics will be put to the test. Compete to claim the top spot, earning rewards and unlocking new features as you progress.

Prepare yourself for thrilling encounters and intense showdowns, as you aim to outsmart your rivals and rise to the challenge. Will you be the architect of the tallest tower, or will your opponents knock you down? Dive into Stack Defense, where the excitement never ends, and every match brings new opportunities for glory. Formulate your strategy, execute it flawlessly, and engage in epic battles as you strive for supremacy!

How to play free Stack Defence game online

To play Stack Defense, start by joining a match with other players. Use your mouse to build your tower by stacking blocks while simultaneously defending against attacks. Keep an eye on your opponents and use power-ups effectively to maintain your advantage. Aim to build higher while disrupting others!
