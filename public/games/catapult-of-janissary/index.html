Catapult of Janissary

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Janissary Series proudly presents the thrilling installment, "Catapult of Janissary." In this engaging game, players take on the role of tacticians aiming to outsmart their opponents using a powerful catapult. The objective is to accurately strike your adversary by skillfully adjusting the angle and speed of your launch.

Throughout the intense combat, players can access a variety of catapult bonuses, such as "Smart," "Ghost," "Big," and "Bomb." Each bonus significantly enhances your chances of landing a successful hit, adding an exciting layer of strategy to the gameplay. For every successful strike you land on your opponent, you'll score points two points in particular if you're precise on your first attempt or deliver a powerful shot.

The game follows a simple yet captivating point system: the first player to reach five points emerges victorious. This competitive element fuels the adrenaline, as opponents exchange powerful shots filled with strategy and skill. The combination of angle adjustments, speed configurations, and the clever use of bonuses ensures that each match is unique, filled with excitement and challenge.

How to play free Catapult Of Janissary game online

To start playing "Catapult of Janissary," select your catapult and aim at your opponent. Adjust the angle and speed of your shot by dragging the controls. Once you're satisfied with your settings, release to launch the projectile. Utilize bonus powers for an added edge, aiming to be the first to score five points!
