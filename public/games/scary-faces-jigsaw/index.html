Scary Faces Jigsaw

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Scary Faces Jigsaw, an exhilarating puzzle game that will test your skills and give you a thrill! This is not your typical jigsaw puzzle; here, you'll encounter an array of spooky and chilling faces that add an element of frightful fun to your gameplay. With a total of 12 uniquely crafted jigsaw puzzles, you ll find endless entertainment as you piece together these eerie images.

Each puzzle is designed to unlock progressively as you complete the previous one, allowing you to experience a sense of accomplishment with every creepy face you assemble. You can choose from three difficulty levels for each puzzle. For a relaxing experience, opt for the Easy mode featuring 25 pieces. If you're looking for a moderate challenge, the Medium mode offers 49 pieces to work with. And for those brave enough to take it a step further, the Hard mode presents an intense challenge with 100 pieces!

Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts and horror fans alike, Scary Faces Jigsaw invites you to immerse yourself in a world of spooky visuals and engaging gameplay. Each completed jigsaw will not only reveal a new scary image but will also sharpen your problem-solving skills in a fun and entertaining way. So gather your focus, choose your difficulty, and prepare to dive into the unsettling realm of jigsaw puzzles like never before!

How to play free Scary Faces Jigsaw game online

To begin playing Scary Faces Jigsaw, select a puzzle to start with. Choose your difficulty level Easy, Medium, or Hard. Use your mouse or finger (if on mobile) to drag and drop pieces into the correct positions. Complete the puzzle to unlock the next scary face!
