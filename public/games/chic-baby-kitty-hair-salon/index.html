Chic Baby Kitty Hair Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Chic Baby Kitty Hair Salon, where you can unleash your creativity and pamper some adorable kittens! In this delightful game, you will step into the shoes of a professional hair groomer, tasked with transforming these charming little felines into glamorous supermodels. With your expert styling skills, you ll cut their hair and apply fabulous makeup, ensuring each kitty looks their absolute best.

Mommy Kitty Kate has brought her precious kittens to your salon, and it s your job to treat them like the VIPs they are! Indulge them with soothing spa treatments using both cool and warm options, ensuring they feel relaxed and rejuvenated. Witness the joy as these cuddly furballs play and socialize with one another in a welcoming environment you ve created.

At Chic Baby Kitty Hair Salon, you also have the responsibility of managing a nursery for these delightful animals. This includes providing them with proper care by catering to their needs such as eating, bathing, and grooming. Watch as you turn a simple grooming session into a full pampering experience, where every kitten leaves feeling special and looking fabulous!

How to play free Chic Baby Kitty Hair Salon game online

To play the Chic Baby Kitty Hair Salon game online, simply choose a kitten from the roster. Use your mouse to navigate through various styling options, haircuts, and makeup choices. Follow the on-screen prompts to provide spa treatments and keep the kittens entertained. Make each visit fun and memorable!
