Knife Ninja

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Knife Ninja is an exciting and highly addictive knife-throwing game that truly tests your skills and reaction time. In this thrilling game, players must aim and throw an array of knives at a large target positioned prominently in the center of the screen. The challenge lies not only in hitting the target but also in timing your throws perfectly to achieve the highest possible score.

As you progress through each level, the difficulty ramps up, giving you even more obstacles to overcome. Players can earn bonus points by hitting apples that are cleverly placed on the target, adding an extra layer of challenge and fun. The mechanics are simple: the more accurately you throw your knives, the better your score will be. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Knife Ninja is perfect for gamers looking for a fun way to test their precision and focus.

Compete against yourself or friends, striving to set a new high score that no one has achieved before! The thrill of hitting your mark and the satisfaction of seeing your skills improve keep players coming back for more. Are you ready to become the ultimate Knife Ninja?

How to play free Knife Ninja game online

To play Knife Ninja, simply use your mouse or finger to aim at the large target and click or tap to throw your knives. Try to hit the target accurately and aim for the apples for bonus points. Keep throwing knives until you ve used them all or successfully hit the target! Enjoy the challenge!
