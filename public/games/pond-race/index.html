Pond Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pond Race is an exhilarating skill-based game that invites players to navigate a vibrant pond filled with delightful challenges. Your main objective is to seamlessly guide a spirited frog across the pond by skillfully clicking on floating lotus leaves and strategically moving logs. But beware! The journey is fraught with peril as sneaky water snakes may lurk, ready to disrupt your progress at any moment. With each level designed to test your reflexes and strategic thinking, you will find yourself immersed in a colorful world that offers both excitement and fun.

This captivating game features a multitude of levels, each progressively more challenging than the last. As you advance, you ll encounter various obstacles that demand quick decision-making and precise timing. The frog s leap depends on your clicks, making every move critical as you attempt to cross the pond as swiftly as possible. Remember, the faster you navigate through the lily pads and logs, the higher the score you can achieve! Rally your skills and set out on this joyful adventure to see if you can conquer the pond.

How to play free Pond Race game online

To play Pond Race, simply click on the lotus leaves to leap forward, and click on moving logs to create safe paths. Keep an eye out for water snakes, and be sure to time your jumps accurately to avoid obstacles. Your goal is to reach the other side of the pond as quickly as possible!
