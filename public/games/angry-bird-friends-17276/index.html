Angry Bird Friends

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the exhilarating world of Angry Birds Friends, where strategy meets competition! Take a leap into the thrilling Star Cup, where you can test your skills against individual opponents in fierce one-on-one battles. Compete with friends and rise through the ranks as you strive to become the ultimate Angry Birds champion worldwide!

Every week brings fresh excitement with NEW Competitive Tournaments launching every Monday, Thursday, and Saturday. Dive into 24 brand new levels each week, where you'll face various challenges. Prove your worth by defeating your opponents and advancing through the leagues. Reach the pinnacle of success, and the incredible rewards will be yours!

Don't miss out on the Special Themed Tournaments held every other week, adding unique twists and challenges to keep gameplay exciting. In the Star Cup, you'll have the opportunity to challenge other players directly, boosting your competitive spirit. Every level comes equipped with free Power Ups, unique Level Effects, and special Slingshots to enhance your gameplay experience.

Collect Feathers as you progress to level up your Birds, unlocking greater scoring potential. Can you maintain a win streak? Overcome multiple matches in a row to maximize your rewards and solidify your status among the best players. Get ready to launch those birds and aim for victory!

How to play free Angry Birds Friends game online

To play Angry Birds Friends online, simply launch the game and choose a tournament. Use the slingshot to launch your birds at structures to defeat the pigs. Each level will provide unique challenges and power-ups. Keep an eye on your opponent's moves, strategize your shots, and aim for the highest score!
