Gym Stack 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Gym Stack 3D, an engaging and dynamic game where your mission is to master the art of merging weights. In this exciting 3D puzzle world, you ll need to drag and combine various weights to organize your gym setup effectively. Your ultimate goal is to sort them in a way that allows you to complete each challenging level. Each stage presents unique obstacles and puzzles, making for an exhilarating experience that will keep you on your toes and test your strategic skills. Are you ready to tackle all the puzzles and show off your gym organizing prowess?

In Gym Stack 3D, you will embark on a journey filled with a plethora of intricate and thought-provoking challenges. As you progress through the game, the puzzles become increasingly complex, demanding that you think carefully about how to drag and merge the weights. With every level you complete, you'll unlock more exciting gameplay and satisfying rewards that make the effort worth it. Leverage your problem-solving skills and get ready to embrace the challenge. Let the fun begin as you lift, merge, and sort your way to victory!

How to play free Gym Stack 3D game online

To play Gym Stack 3D, simply drag the weights from the gym floor and merge them by stacking identical weights together. Navigate through the levels by sorting the weights correctly to complete the challenges. Keep an eye on the time and strategize your moves to achieve the best score!
