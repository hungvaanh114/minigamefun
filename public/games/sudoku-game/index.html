How to play free Sudoku Game online

To play the Sudoku Game, simply select your desired difficulty level and start filling in the grid. Use logic to place numbers from 1 to 9 in each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid without repeating any digits. You can click on a cell to enter a number or use the pencil feature to make notes for a more strategic approach. Enjoy the challenge!
