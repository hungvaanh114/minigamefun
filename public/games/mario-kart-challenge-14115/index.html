Mario Kart Challenge is an exciting online game designed for kids that provides hours of free entertainment on both PC and mobile devices. How to play free Mario Kart Challenge game online

To play Mario Kart Challenge, simply use the arrow keys to steer Mario through the racecourse. Press the space bar to activate power-ups when you collect them.
