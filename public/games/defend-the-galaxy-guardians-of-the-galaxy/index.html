Defend the Galaxy - Guardians of the Galaxy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of cosmic adventure with Defend the Galaxy - Guardians Of The Galaxy, a free browser game available at Superkidgames.com! Join the iconic team of the Guardians as they embark on an exhilarating mission to save their friends, Drax and Gamora, who have fallen into the enemy s clutches.

As Star-Lord, Rocket, and Groot rally together, you will encounter fierce battles against the nefarious Ronan. Prepare yourself for intense spaceship combat and one-on-one confrontations as you navigate through enemy territory. Can you handle the rapid challenges in a captivating environment? The game allows players to customize their difficulty level from the start, making it accessible and engaging for everyone.

Teamwork is key in this game; you're not alone in this mission. Help Rocket to assert dominance in the galactic tussle! The Milano, Star-Lord's remarkable blue and orange ship, will be your base of operations. Your task is to assist Rocket in obliterating hostile ships while also ensuring the safety of your teammates. Move the Milano using your mouse, and remember that its primary weaponry fires automatically, streamlining your combat experience.

Stay vigilant and collect health kits to replenish your health bar while keeping an ear out for power-ups during exhilarating skirmishes. Missiles can unleash devastating damage within their radius, while the Scatter Shot provides both extended range and multiple shots to enhance your effectiveness. Are you ready to lead the Guardians to victory?

How to play free Defend the Galaxy - Guardians Of The Galaxy game online

To begin your adventure, move the Milano with your mouse to navigate space. Fire automatically, but hold the right-click for a powerful special attack. Collect health kits to restore health and grab power-ups to enhance your abilities. Collaborate with Rocket and Groot to defeat enemy ships and rescue your allies!
