Save the Galaxy 1?Save the Galaxy 1

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Save The Galaxy 1, embark on an exhilarating adventure across the cosmos as you navigate through stunning planetary orbits. Your mission is to skillfully dodge an array of Planets while collecting shining Stars that appear along your interstellar journey. Each Star collected not only boosts your score but also propels you closer to becoming the ultimate galactic champion.

However, danger lurks around every corner. Rely on your ship's compass to stay on course, or risk descending into darkness and facing fierce Asteroids hurtling toward you. The deeper you delve into a Planet's orbit, the faster the challenges arise, making it essential to maintain your focus and precision as you maneuver through the celestial expanse.

Avoiding crashing into other Planetoids is critical, as colliding with them results in an instant game over. As you progress, you'll experience increasingly complex pathways and faster-moving obstacles, requiring quick reflexes and strategic planning to master the gameplay. Enjoy breathtaking visuals and a captivating soundtrack as you traverse from one Planet to the next, testing your skills in this thrilling online adventure.

How to play free Save The Galaxy 1 game online

To play Save The Galaxy 1, use the keyboard's arrow keys to steer your spacecraft. Collect Stars while avoiding collisions with Planets and Asteroids. Keep an eye on your ship's compass to maintain your path. Stay focused, and good luck navigating through the galaxy!
