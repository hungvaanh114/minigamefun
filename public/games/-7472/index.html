Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Flappy Pumpkin! In this engaging and lighthearted game, you have the chance to soar through the air with a charming Flappy Pumpkin. Your objective is simple: tap the screen to navigate your pumpkin through the challenging obstacles ahead. As you fly, you'll encounter the mischievous hands of robot poppies that pose a threat to your journey. Your skill and reflexes will be tested as you make your way past these quirky obstacles without making contact.

As you venture on this aerial adventure, keep an eye out for medals scattered throughout the game. Collecting these medals not only adds to your score but also brings you one step closer to unlocking various achievements. The thrill of trying to surpass your personal best score adds an addictive quality to the gameplay, encouraging you to take on the challenge time and again. With each tap, you ll experience the joy of flight while aiming for a record that showcases your skills!

Join in on the fun and see just how far you can take your Flappy Pumpkin! Whether you are looking for a casual gaming experience or a competitive challenge, this game promises to deliver hours of enjoyment and excitement. Immerse yourself in this delightful universe and embark on a journey filled with charming visuals, addictive gameplay, and endless fun. So what are you waiting for? It's time to take flight!

How to Play Free Flappy Pumpkin Game Online

To play the Flappy Pumpkin game, simply tap anywhere on the screen to make your pumpkin ascend. Watch out for the robot poppy hands and steer clear of them to avoid getting knocked out of the game. Collect all the medals along the way to boost your score and challenge yourself to beat your highest record!
