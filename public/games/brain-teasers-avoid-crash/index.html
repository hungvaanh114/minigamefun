Brain Teasers : Avoid Crash

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Brain Teasers: Avoid Crash, a captivating puzzle game that places you in the vital role of a traffic controller. Your primary objective is to prevent any vehicular collisions at a hectic intersection. By expertly managing the traffic lights, you can guide cars safely and strategically through the chaotic crossroad.

The gameplay is straightforward yet challenging. You ll be tasked with switching the traffic lights from red to green at the appropriate moments to manage the flow of cars effectively. Each level presents its own unique set of trials, requiring you to think quickly and act decisively to keep the streets safe. With careful observation and strategic timing, your goal is to complete all levels while earning a top score and achieving a three-star rating on each stage.

As you progress, the difficulty ramps up, introducing more vehicles and complex scenarios. You must remain vigilant, as any lapse in concentration could lead to a disastrous collision. Your score reflects not only your ability to manage traffic effectively but also your skill in navigating each level's increasing complexity. Keep your reflexes sharp and your timing impeccable to survive the exhilarating and sometimes chaotic challenges ahead.

How to play free Brain Teasers: Avoid Crash game online

To play the game, use your mouse or touchscreen to click on the traffic lights and change their colors. Switch lights from red to green to control the cars safely. Pay attention to the flow of vehicles and make quick decisions to prevent crashes. Aim to complete levels with a three-star rating!
