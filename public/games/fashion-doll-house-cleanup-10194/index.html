Fashion Doll House Cleanup

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Fashion Doll House Cleanup, where you can indulge your inner designer and organizer! In this captivating game, your mission is to restore every room in the dollhouse to its former glory. The task requires a meticulous approach, making it a test of both patience and care.

Your adventure begins with a detailed cleanup of each space, from the bustling kitchen to the cozy bedroom. As you navigate through the house, you'll encounter various kinds of clutter that need to be meticulously disposed of. Make sure you handle each piece of trash with care, as a pristine environment is your ultimate goal!

Once you've decluttered the rooms, bring in the high-tech sweeping robot to clean all the floors. This handy gadget will help you maintain a spotless surface, allowing you to focus on making the decor shine. However, be cautious! Be sure not to stain any surfaces as you sweep away debris.

With every room cleaned and organized, you'll be amazed at the transformation of the dollhouse. Your attention to detail will not only refresh the space but also set the stage for delightful new adventures. Are you ready to take on the challenge of cleaning and design? Dive into Fashion Doll House Cleanup and showcase your skills!

How to play free Fashion Doll House Cleanup game online

To begin playing Fashion Doll House Cleanup, simply choose the room you want to clean first. Use your mouse or screen gestures to drag and drop items to dispose of clutter. Activate the sweeping robot by clicking on it to clean the floors. Don t forget to keep surfaces clean while organizing!
