Monster Dinosaur Rampage City Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Monster Dinosaur Rampage: City Attack, where you dive deep into the prehistoric era of dinosaurs. This action-packed game invites you to embody ferocious dinosaurs on a mission to wreak havoc in a modern metropolis. Unlike typical rampage games, your journey takes you straight into the heart of the Jurassic world, allowing you to unleash your inner beast.

As a fierce predator of the ancient times, you will engage in a series of bone-shattering rampage missions, seeking vengeance against those who have wronged you. The narrative unfolds with you as one of the dinosaurs subjected to horrific experiments by evil scientists. Fueled by rage, your objective is to destroy the city and take down the mad scientists behind the cruel experimentation. However, your path to chaos will not be easy, as military forces are ready to counter your wrath with a barrage of bullets.

In this thrilling game, players are challenged to navigate through intense gameplay, evading attacks while unleashing devastating damage on both the city and its defenders. Use your agility and strength to dodge military assaults and retaliate with powerful attacks that send tanks and helicopters spiraling into chaos. The robust city environment offers various landmarks and buildings to demolish, each adding to the immersive experience.

How to play free Monster Dinosaur Rampage City Attack game online

To play Monster Dinosaur Rampage: City Attack, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your dinosaur. Attack buildings and military forces by tapping designated action buttons. Keep an eye on your health bar, and use power-ups to enhance your dinosaur's abilities as you progress through the game. Enjoy the thrill of being a prehistoric monster on the rampage!
