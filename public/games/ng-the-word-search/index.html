NG: The Word Search

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Word Search is a riveting game that pits your vocabulary knowledge against a complex puzzle of words and jumbled letters. Dive into the depths of thorny English crosswords, unleashing your linguistic prowess to discover hidden words. This game pushes the boundaries of conventional word-finding games, proving to be a unique and challenging endeavor for even the most seasoned puzzle enthusiasts.

Instructions

A word list is given on one side while a chaotic grid of scrambled letters takes up the rest of the screen Your mission is to find and highlight all the listed words within that grid Words can be oriented up down diagonally and even backwards To hi
