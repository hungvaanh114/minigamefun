Angry Bull Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Angry Bull Shooter, chaos reigns as bulls have bolted from the confines of the arena, posing a significant threat to the local community. These formidable creatures roam the streets, and it's up to you to restore peace and safety. Armed with your precision shooting skills, your mission is clear: neutralize all aggressive bulls before they can cause serious harm to the innocent inhabitants of the city.

Experience the adrenaline rush as you navigate through various challenges in this dynamic shooter game. Each level presents increasingly aggressive bulls that charge at you, testing your reflexes and accuracy. As you progress, be prepared for obstacles and power-ups that can either assist you in your mission or complicate your efforts. Mastering your aim and timing is crucial, as you'll need to act quickly to avert disastrous consequences.

Strategically choose your shooting spots and time your shots wisely to maximize your effectiveness. With a variety of bulls displaying unique characteristics and attack patterns, players must adapt and develop strategies to tackle each situation. Keep an eye on the countdown timer making each shot count is essential for achieving high scores and unlocking new stages of the game.

How to play free Angry Bull Shooter game online

To start playing Angry Bull Shooter, use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the bulls and click or tap to shoot. Focus on hitting the bulls accurately while avoiding collateral damage to civilians. Keep track of your ammo and reload when necessary, and try to complete each level before time runs out!


Instructions

Controls WASD to move Left shift to run Left mouse click to shoot Right mouse click to aim Tab Escape to toggle pause
