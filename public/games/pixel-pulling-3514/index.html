Pixel Pulling

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Pixel Pulling, where you will engage in an electrifying rope-pulling competition! In this exciting multiplayer race, you will test your strength, strategy, and a sprinkle of luck as you aim to outpull your opponents. Compete in five thrilling rounds that will put your skills to the ultimate test. The engaging mechanics and colorful pixel art create a captivating atmosphere as you battle for the title of champion.

With each round presenting unique challenges and escalating excitement, you will need to hone your timing and reflexes. Will you outlast your challengers and emerge as the victor? As you pull against the rope, anticipate your opponents' moves while also managing your own energy. Can you maintain your grip and fortitude as you strive to secure your place at the top?

Don't underestimate the power of luck in your journey! Sometimes, a fortunate turn of events could be just what you need to gain the upper hand. Whether you play alone or challenge friends, Pixel Pulling promises a fun and fast-paced experience that keeps you coming back for more. Get ready to pull, strategize, and claim your rightful spot as the pull champion!

How to play free Pixel Pulling game online

To play the game, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to pull the rope in the direction of your choice. Timing is crucial; pull at the right moment to maximize your strength. Keep an eye on the progress bar and outlast your opponents to win each round!


Instructions

Controls for the computer The W key makes the red character pull the rope the up arrow key makes the blue character pull the rope On touch devices The red and blue buttons on the screen make the character pull the string
