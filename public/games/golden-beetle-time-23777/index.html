Instructions:

Welcome to Golden Beetle Time, a delightful and engaging game that challenges your time-telling skills while guiding a charming little Beetle! The objective of this game is simple yet stimulating: assist the Beetle in making its way towards blocks that contain specific numbers that require rounding. It's not just about speed; you ll need to apply your mathematical knowledge to determine the correct rounded number and navigate accordingly.

As you steer the Beetle, you ll encounter various blocks, each bearing different numbers. Your task is to quickly calculate which number the Beetle should aim for. Can it round up or down? Time is of the essence as you guide it through this vibrant virtual landscape! But be careful if the Beetle stumbles upon a block with the wrong number or falls off the edge, it will lose one of its three lives. The challenge lies in balancing speed with accuracy, ensuring you aim for the right blocks in order to keep the Beetle safe and sound.

Golden Beetle Time is not just a test of your arithmetic abilities; it also enhances your reflexes and quick-thinking skills. With each successful round, the levels become progressively more challenging, providing endless fun and learning opportunities for players of all ages!

How to play free Golden Beetle Time game online

To play Golden Beetle Time, use the arrow keys to direct the Beetle towards the blocks. As numbers appear, calculate the rounded value quickly and navigate to the corresponding block. Stay alert; avoid the wrong blocks and edges to keep your lives intact!
