Draw and Park

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Draw and Park, a thrilling online experience that perfectly blends the challenges of parking with the creativity of drawing. In this engaging game, players are tasked with guiding cars to their designated parking spots, but with a fun twist: you draw the path they will take. Forget about steering; your skill lies in plotting the safest route!

As you begin, the objective is straightforward: draw a clear path that enables the car to navigate obstacles and reach the parking lot without any mishaps. However, don t let the initial simplicity fool you! As you progress through the increasingly challenging levels, you'll face multiple cars that need to park simultaneously. With cars launching at the same time, it s crucial to strategize and plot distinct routes to avoid collisions. Each car is color-coded, and you must ensure they arrive at their respective parking spots without accidents.

The game features a variety of obstacles that add to the excitement, test your problem-solving skills, and require quick thinking. With each level, the challenges become more complex, pushing you to think creatively and adapt your strategies. Can you successfully park all the cars without causing an accident? Join in on the fun and find out!

How to play free Draw and Park game online

To play Draw and Park, simply use your mouse or finger to draw a path from the car to the parking zone. Make sure to navigate around obstacles carefully. Once all the paths are drawn for the cars, they will start moving towards their parking spots simultaneously. Keep an eye on each vehicle to avoid collisions!
