Real Gangster City Crime Vegas 3D 2018

Welcome to Real Gangster City Crime Vegas 3D 2018, where the vibrant streets of Las Vegas transform into a playground for crime and chaos. Dive into a world filled with thrilling missions and intense gangster rivalries. As you navigate the elaborate cityscape of Vegas, you ll engage with a host of characters, all vying for power and respect. Your journey begins as you build your reputation and become one of the most infamous gangsters on the US streets.

In this immersive 3D experience, players can utilize a plethora of vehicles, including cars, bikes, trucks, and even planes, to execute daring heists and escape from the law. Engage in frenetic shootouts using a variety of weapons, ensuring you re always one step ahead of rival gangs and law enforcement. Your journey to becoming a crime lord won t be easy, but with skill and strategy, you can claim your spot at the top of the Vegas underworld.

Master the art of combat and navigation with intuitive controls. Use WASD keys to maneuver through the city, Left Mouse Button for attacking, Shift to sprint, Space to leap over obstacles, and F to commandeer vehicles. Whether you re racing down the iconic Las Vegas Strip or engaging in all-out gang warfare, every moment is filled with adrenaline and excitement.

How to play free Real Gangster City Crime Vegas 3D 2018 game online

To play the game, use the WASD keys to move your character around the city. Deploy the Left Mouse Button to attack enemies. Press Shift to sprint away or escape dangerous situations, and use Space to jump over obstacles. When you need to drive a vehicle, hit F to hop in and start your getaway!
