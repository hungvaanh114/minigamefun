Kingdom Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the world of Kingdom Rush, where your kingdom is in peril and only you can save it! As dark forces threaten to invade your peaceful realm, it's your duty to stand against the oncoming waves of orcs, trolls, wicked wizards, and numerous other vile creatures. Strategically place and upgrade your array of valiant warriors and powerful mages to thwart these intruders and restore peace to your lands.

Utilize a wide range of heroes, each with unique abilities and skills tailored to help you strategize better against challenging enemies. Build your defenses wisely by placing archers, barracks, and magical towers at strategic points along the path to your castle. Each level brings not only increasing enemy complexity but also opportunities to earn valuable resources for upgrading your troops and defenses.

Make use of spells and special abilities at critical moments to turn the tide of battle in your favor. Keep an eye on the enemy waves and adjust your tactics accordingly: some enemies might be resistant to certain attacks while others may have their own special attacks that can disrupt your plans. Progress through challenging levels, unlock new heroes, and face off against villainous bosses to claim victory.

How to play free Kingdom Rush game online

To start playing Kingdom Rush online, simply visit your favorite gaming website or platform that hosts the game. Once you're in, choose your preferred hero and begin deploying your warriors. Click on the path to place towers, upgrade them as you collect resources, and use your spells wisely to defeat the invading hordes.
