Space Aliens Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Space Aliens Match 3! Get ready to embark on an intergalactic adventure with this captivating match-three puzzle game. Your mission is to align blocks of the same color in combinations of three or more. As you successfully merge these colorful blocks, you will rack up points and strive for the highest score possible.

The game presents a thrilling challenge where strategy and quick thinking are your best allies. Keep a close eye on the left side of the screen, as you must manage the scale meter. If this meter drops too low, it will signal the end of your thrilling space quest. So, stay alert and make those matches to keep the game alive!

With brilliant graphics and engaging gameplay, Space Aliens Match 3 offers hours of entertainment as you aim to outsmart the colorful aliens and create spectacular combinations. Explore different levels filled with fun surprises and unlocking new features as you progress through the game. Dive into this vibrant world and enjoy every moment!

How to play free Space Aliens Match 3 game online

To play Space Aliens Match 3, begin by selecting two adjacent blocks of the same color to swap them. Aim to create a line of three or more identical blocks, which will disappear, resulting in points for each match. Keep an eye on the meter and make matches quickly to ensure your adventure continues!
