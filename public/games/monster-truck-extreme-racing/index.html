Monster Truck Extreme Racing

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping experience in Monster Truck Extreme Racing! Step into the driver's seat of a powerful monster truck and prepare to conquer the desert. Prove your skills as you race against other drivers, aiming to secure a spot in the top three. Achieving this feat not only boosts your confidence but also rewards you with exciting prizes!

However, the thrills don t end there! In addition to racing, you can showcase your daring stunts on a massive "Free Drive" map. Perform jaw-dropping tricks and maneuvers to earn additional rewards, which can be used to enhance your trucks or unlock new monsters. Don't forget to check out the "Garage" section to upgrade your vehicles; more powerful trucks will give you the edge in upcoming races!

Monster Truck Extreme Racing features both single-player and two-player modes, making it perfect for a little friendly competition or solo challenges. Whether you're looking to master your driving skills or team up with a friend for some chaotic racing fun, this game has something for everyone. So buckle up, rev your engines, and may the best driver emerge victorious!

How to play free Monster Truck Extreme Racing game online

To play Monster Truck Extreme Racing, select your mode and choose your monster truck. Navigate using the directional keys and accelerate with the designated button. Compete against opponents in races or demonstrate your skills in stunt mode, aiming for top scores to unlock more trucks and upgrades!
