Star Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling adventure of Star Quest, the fate of Earth hangs in the balance as a colossal asteroid hurtles toward our planet. This catastrophic event spells doom for all life, but just in the nick of time, mysterious alien forces intervene. They mine the asteroid for its valuable resources, leading to its explosion into smaller, yet still deadly fragments. While the alien forces scavenge the remnants, a critical situation unfolds; their mothership departs, leaving a significant number of smaller asteroids untouched and on a collision course with Earth. With the planet s survival at stake, it s up to you to ensure that these threats are neutralized.

Your mission is to obliterate the remaining asteroids before they have a chance to strike. However, a formidable challenge lies ahead. The aliens, who initially seemed to be allies, are anything but friendly. They will stop at nothing to defend their new quarry, engaging you in fierce battles as you work to destroy the asteroids. Strategize and utilize your skills to outsmart these hostile extraterrestrial foes. With a mixture of resource management, clever tactics, and relentless shooting, you must race against time to save millions of lives. The fate of Earth rests on your shoulders in this gripping interstellar quest!

How to play free Star Quest game online

To play Star Quest, use your mouse or keyboard controls to navigate your ship and aim at the approaching asteroids. Fire your weapons to destroy them before they reach Earth. Watch out for the hostile aliens and use power-ups strategically to enhance your chances of survival. Good luck!
