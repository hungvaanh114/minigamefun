Step Upper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step Upper is an exhilarating game that takes you on a thrilling journey from Earth to the far reaches of the Universe! As you step into the shoes of an astronaut, get ready to be inspired as you aim for greater distances. The further you travel, the higher your score climbs, giving you the motivation to push your limits.

One of the key features of Step Upper is the ability to share your accomplishments. Capture your best score as a screenshot and send it to your friends on social media or through messaging apps. This competitive element allows you to find out who among your friends can reach the highest heights, making every play session exciting and engaging. The game is perfect for those who thrive on competition, with opportunities to compare scores and find out who reigns supreme in speed and distance.

Step Upper is designed as a hyper-casual arcade mini-game that combines fun with a sense of competition. Enjoy the auto night mode, adding a unique twist to your gaming experience that keeps you hooked. With big, joyful emojis to enhance score sharing, you'll find yourself wanting to beat your previous records just to share that winning moment.

How to play free Step Upper game online

To play Step Upper, simply tap the screen to make your astronaut jump and reach new heights. Keep an eye on the obstacles while timing your jumps for maximum distance. The more you play, the better you ll get, so challenge your friends and see who can outscore the rest!
