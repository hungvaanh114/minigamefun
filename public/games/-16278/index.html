How to play free Fruit Ninja 2 game online

To play Fruit Ninja 2, start by swiping your finger across the screen to slice the fruits that appear. Make sure to avoid slicing any bombs, as they will end your game. Aim for combos to multiply your score and boost your abilities as you progress through different game modes.
