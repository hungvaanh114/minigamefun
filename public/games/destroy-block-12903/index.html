Destroy Block

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Destroy Blocks is an engaging arcade game where your main objective is to obliterate as many blocks as possible. These blocks come in two distinct colors blue and red and drop from the top of the screen. A ball sits at the bottom, which you will use to target these blocks. The key to success is color coordination; hit the blue blocks with the blue ball and the red blocks with the red ball.
As you progress, the speed at which the blocks descend will increase, adding an exciting challenge to your gameplay. You must rapidly switch the color of the ball to match the blocks that are falling down. To change the ball's color, simply tap on the screen, allowing you to respond swiftly to the incoming blocks. Your goal is to maximize the number of blocks you destroy before they pile up at the bottom of the screen. The game rewards quick reflexes and sharp focus, making it an exhilarating experience. The adrenaline rush comes from working against the clock while ensuring each shot counts.
So gear up, steady your aim, and prepare for an action-packed adventure in Destroy Blocks, where the fun is endless and each level presents new challenges to conquer!

How to play free Destroy Block game online

To play the Destroy Block game, start by tapping on the screen to switch the color of the ball to match the falling blocks. Aim carefully and shoot the ball at the corresponding colored blocks to destroy them. Keep an eye on the speed of the blocks and stay alert to change your ball s color as needed. Have fun!
