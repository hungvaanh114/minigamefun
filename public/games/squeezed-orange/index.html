Squeezed Orange

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the refreshing world of Squeezed Orange, where your skills in juice preparation are put to the test! In this exciting new game, your primary objective is to prepare delicious cold juice by squeezing an orange slice right into your glass. The gameplay unfolds on a vibrant screen featuring a playing field, with an empty glass positioned at the center. Above this glass, you'll notice a tantalizing orange slice hovering at an elevated position, waiting for your interaction.

Your task is simple yet engaging. With a quick click of the mouse, you initiate the squeezing action on the orange slice. To fill the glass with juice, hold down the click to control the flow of the liquid. However, exercise caution! Timing is crucial, as you ll need to release the click just before the glass overflows. Filling the glass to perfection will earn you points, and upon succeeding, you'll advance to the next level of the game, where the challenges increase, and the juicing becomes even more fun!

Get ready to test your reflexes and precision in Squeezed Orange, a game that brings fun and excitement to juice-making!

How to play free Squeezed Orange game online

To play Squeezed Orange, simply click on the orange slice above the empty glass to start squeezing. Hold the click to fill the glass with juice, but release it just in time to avoid an overflow. Successfully fill the glass to score points and progress through various levels!
