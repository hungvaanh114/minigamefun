Angry Cats is an exhilarating sports game that promises fun for players of all ages. This action-packed title challenges your precision and aiming skills as you strive to score goals by launching adorable cats into the basket. As you progress through Angry Cats, you'll encounter increasingly tough challenges that will test not only your aim but also your strategic thinking. How to play free AGC game online

To play the Angry Cats game online, use your mouse to aim at the basket. Adjust the angle and the force by dragging the cursor, and release it to shoot the cats towards the hoop. Try to score as many points as possible while keeping an eye on the timer!
