Embark on another thrilling adventure in Zombie Mission 10, where the action intensifies in the highly anticipated Prison Breakout section! In this iteration, the sprawling underground prison is overrun by relentless zombies, and it s up to you to navigate through the chaos. Your mission is twofold: you must rescue hostages who are trapped and provide assistance to those who are injured. The tension is palpable, as time is of the essence.

With this expanded edition of Zombie Mission, prepare yourself for a staggering challenge consisting of 30 unique levels. As you progress, you'll encounter 7 formidable bosses, each more menacing than the last. The ultimate showdown awaits you as the most powerful boss lies in wait at the final level, testing your skills and strategy to the limit. Collect coins as you defeat enemies and complete levels, and don t forget to visit the shop to purchase powerful weapons and upgrades that will aid you in your battle against the undead.

How to play free Zombie Mission 10 game online

To play Zombie Mission 10 online, simply use your keyboard to navigate. Utilize the arrow keys to move your character and the spacebar to jump. Engage enemies with your selected weapon and work collaboratively with your partner if you re playing in co-op mode. Remember to strategize your upgrades wisely for maximum effectiveness against the zombies!
