Bijoy 71: Hearts of Heroes is an immersive war-action shooting game that takes you deep into the heart of Bangladesh's liberation struggle. Set against the backdrop of the Great War for Independence, where countless lives were sacrificed, this game challenges players to honor the legacy of those who fought tirelessly for freedom. Your mission is to defend your homeland from relentless enemy attacks while reliving the fierce battles that shaped the nation.

Embody the bravery of Bangladeshi soldiers as you engage in high-octane, side-scrolling shooting action. You will navigate through three intense stages: the Dacca Sector Commander Arms Raid, Operation Searchlight, and the Jute Mill Battle. Each level is designed to reflect the raw emotions and dire decisions faced by freedom fighters during the turbulent times of 1971. Immerse yourself in a storyline that pays tribute to the martyrs whose sacrifices paved the way for the peace and opportunities we cherish today.

The game presents a realistic experience where strategic shooting and calculated decisions are crucial. Be vigilant against waves of advancing enemies and utilize your resources wisely. Keep track of your ammunition count displayed on the screen to ensure you are always ready to reload at the right moment. Additionally, you can switch positions for better shooting angles while remaining alert to your surroundings. Every bullet could potentially alter the course of history, so precision is key as you fight alongside fellow freedom fighters including unsung heroes from within the community.

Your journey through this action-packed shooter will not only engage you but also remind you of the sacrifices made for the freedom we enjoy today.
