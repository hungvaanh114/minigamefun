Island Princess Magic Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Princess Kalani on an enchanting adventure in Island Princess Magic Quest. The island villagers are in desperate need of her assistance to save their stunning territories. Out of all the girls in her village, Kalani has been specially selected for this crucial mission, but she cannot succeed without your help!

In this magical journey, Kalani must navigate through the breathtaking Hawaiian islands while overcoming various challenges. The vibrant landscapes are alive with the spirit of the islands, and the people are counting on you to guide Kalani through her paradise adventure. With valuable support from the shaman, the island's spiritual guide, and a set of unique tools, you and Kalani form an unbeatable team!

However, time is of the essence. You must race across the ocean before Lukela gets there first to claim leadership of the islands. If you succeed, you ll not only save the islands but also help Kalani fulfill her destiny as the true leader of her people. Are you ready to take on this exciting quest for magic and adventure?

How to play free Island Princess Magic Quest game online

To play Island Princess Magic Quest, use the arrow keys to guide Princess Kalani through various landscapes, collecting magical items and completing quests. Interact with villagers for hints and tools to help in your journey, and keep an eye on the timer to ensure you reach your destination before Lukela!
