Welcome to the eerie world of Boxy Horror House, where ominous thrills await you! In Project Playtime Boxy Boo, you encounter stunning visuals along with an exciting new survivor character and the menacing Boxy Boo monster. This multiplayer online horror game challenges players to choose their roles wisely, defend themselves, and ultimately escape the clutches of death lurking around every corner.

As a survivor, your mission is to complete various tasks assigned within the game and evade the relentless pursuit of the Boxy Boo monster. Every choice you make could be the difference between life and death as you navigate the treacherous environment. Teamwork is crucial; six players will assume the role of a Survivor, collaborating to solve intricate puzzles while crafting an enormous toy all while trying to stay alive in the heart of the terrifying Boxy factory. The game brilliantly combines the aspects of horror with unique gameplay mechanics, creating an unforgettable experience.

Boxy Project includes several compelling features, such as surviving in an imaginative yet threatening toy factory. You ll have the opportunity to control the toy project Boxy Boo as you devise strategies to outwit the monster. The heart-pounding excitement and nail-biting tension make this multiplayer horror adventure a must-try for anyone who loves a good scare.
