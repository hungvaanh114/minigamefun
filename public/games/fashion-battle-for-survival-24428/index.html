Fashion Battle for Survival

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the captivating realm of Fashion Battle for Survival, a dynamic puzzle game that invites you to challenge your memory skills. In this engaging experience, your objective is to uncover hidden cards by matching them in pairs. With each successful match, you inch closer to victory and earn the opportunity to explore the glamorous world of fashion.

The game centers around an Asian beauty endowed with a bold and fearless personality. As you progress through the rounds, you will unlock an exquisite array of hairstyles and outfits that reflect her unique character. The excitement builds as you collect items to create a stunning wardrobe, showcasing your fashion prowess. However, be mindful of the ticking timer that adds an extra layer of challenge to each round, pushing you to think quickly and strategically.

Do you have what it takes to complete your fashion collection? With a limited time to showcase your skills, the pressure is on to remember and react swiftly. It's not only a battle of memory but a fashionable adventure that accentuates your creativity and style!

How to play free Fashion Battle for Survival game online

To play Fashion Battle for Survival, start by clicking on cards to reveal their images. Your goal is to find and match pairs of identical cards. Continue matching until all pairs are discovered. Keep an eye on the countdown timer to ensure you finish before time runs out!
