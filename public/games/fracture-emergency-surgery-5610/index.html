Fracture Emergency Surgery

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Fracture Emergency Surgery," players are thrust into an intense and dramatic scenario where a young woman named Sofia has suffered a severe accident after falling from her balcony. With a serious leg injury, she requires urgent surgical intervention to mend her broken limb. Your skills will be put to the test as you step into the role of the surgeon, preparing for an emergency operation to ensure her well-being. This fast-paced and engaging game challenges you to perform a series of medical procedures, from administering anesthesia to repairing fractures, using a variety of surgical tools and techniques.

Once Sofia has made a full recovery, she decides to celebrate her newfound health with a grand party, and the preparations begin! Players will assist her as she organizes the event, which includes decorating the hall, preparing delicious food, and selecting the perfect outfit. The game blends medical simulation and time management, requiring quick thinking and resourcefulness to ensure everything is ready before the guests arrive. Your role is crucial, as each detail matters in making Sofia s party a memorable one.

Are you ready to experience the excitement and responsibility that comes with emergency surgery and party planning? "Fracture Emergency Surgery" offers a unique gaming experience packed with challenges and opportunities for creativity. Join the adventure, help Sofia recover, and make her celebration successful!

How to play free Fracture Emergency Surgery game online

To play the game, start by following on-screen instructions to complete each surgical step. Use your mouse to select tools and interact with items. After the surgery, switch to party mode where you will drag and drop decorations, prepare food, and choose outfits to get everything ready for Sofia s guests!
