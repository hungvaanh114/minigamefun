Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fight Match is an engaging casual puzzle game that brings you back to the roots of classic elimination gameplay. Dive into a colorful world where your goal is to eliminate balls of the same color. Each successful match empowers Ultraman to strike his adversaries, making the game not only entertaining but also strategic. Your mission is to knock out your opponents by completing the elimination targets at each level. But that's not all there are special props available to assist you in navigating through the challenges and level requirements, ensuring that every match is unique!

Gather your friends and embark on this exciting adventure together! Fight Match is designed to bring friends closer with its competitive edge; challenge them to see who can clear levels the fastest. The thrill of competition coupled with the joy of puzzle-solving creates an exhilarating experience that will keep you glued to your screen for hours. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to puzzles, Fight Match offers a delightful way to unwind and have fun!

So why wait? Join Ultraman and engage in the mesmerizing world of elimination fun. See how far you can go by matching colors and outsmarting your foes! The satisfaction of completing levels and the joy of friendly rivalry make Fight Match a must-try for puzzle enthusiasts. Let the elimination games begin!

How to play free Fight Match game online

To play Fight Match, simply tap on two or more adjacent balls of the same color to eliminate them. Keep an eye on your opponent and aim to reduce their health by successfully clearing colors before they do. Utilize special props strategically and aim for the targets set for each level to progress!
