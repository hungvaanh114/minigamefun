How to play free Gravity Gnome game online

To play Gravity Gnome, start by using the arrow keys to navigate your gnome through levels. Press the spacebar to activate gravity switches that alter the direction of gravity, allowing your character to walk on walls and ceilings. Collect coins and power-ups to enhance your gameplay as you solve puzzles and defeat obstacles!
