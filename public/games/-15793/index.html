Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the festive world of Findergarten Christmas, a delightful photo puzzle game that will surely bring joy to your holiday season! Immerse yourself in a captivating gameplay experience where you ll be tasked with finding and tapping on the specific locations of picture pieces displayed on the right side of your screen. Each level presents its own unique challenges, filled with beautiful Christmas-themed images that will keep you entertained for hours.

As you navigate through the game, you ll encounter a variety of scenes adorned with twinkling lights, jolly Santa clauses, and joyful snowmen. The objective is simple yet exciting: locate all designated picture pieces within the time limit to progress to the next level. The more accurately you pinpoint the pieces, the more satisfaction you will gain, making it a perfect game for holiday gatherings or some personal downtime.

What makes Findergarten Christmas truly special is the mesmerizing art and cheerful music that accompany your gameplay. This captivating game fosters your observation skills while painting a warm, festive atmosphere. With each completed level, you ll unlock new layers of intriguing images depicting the spirit of Christmas, enticing players of all ages to join in on the fun!

How to play free Findergarten Christmas game online

To play the game, simply start by selecting a level. Look at the picture pieces on the right side of your screen, then tap on the matching locations in the main image to uncover them. Aim to find all pieces quickly to complete the level and move on to the next one!
