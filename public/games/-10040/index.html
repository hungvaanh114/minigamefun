RESOLVE : A Math Game?RESOLVE : A Math Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you have a passion for numbers or even if math isn't your strong suit, you will absolutely enjoy RESOLVE, the ultimate math game experience. This engaging and accessible game caters to players of all skill levels, providing an entertaining mix of fun, speed, and challenging puzzles.

As you dive into RESOLVE, you will find a series of incomplete equations displayed at the bottom of your screen, which will put your arithmetic skills to the test. At the top, the correct solution awaits your discovery. Your task is to connect the right numbers together in a way that completes the equations accurately. Sounds simple, right? But don't be fooled this game is designed to push your mathematical abilities and help you discover whether you possess true math genius.

Every level offers a new set of challenges, ensuring that you will never run out of exciting gameplay. Plus, the intuitive design makes it easy for anyone to jump in and start playing right away. So, whether you're looking to sharpen your math skills or simply want a delightful, quick game to pass the time, RESOLVE is the perfect choice for you.

How to play free RESOLVE: a math game online

To play RESOLVE, simply start by selecting your level of difficulty. Once the game begins, observe the incomplete equations at the bottom of your screen. Your goal is to determine which numbers from the provided options need to be connected to achieve the correct solutions displayed at the top. Use your mouse or touch screen to link the numbers accordingly and complete the equations before the timer runs out!
