Attack of the Robots

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure in Ant-Man and the WASP: Attack of the Robots! If you're a fan of Marvel superheroes, this game offers a thrilling opportunity to join Ant-Man and the WASP on a high-stakes mission. Will you rise to the challenge and help them defeat menacing villains that threaten their world?

This time, the notorious Yellowjacket has taken control of the Pym Technologies laboratory, implementing a virus that overrides all robots and security systems. To make matters worse, he has locked down the facility, creating an intense environment filled with danger. While Hank Pym safeguards the most sensitive technology, it falls on you to assist Ant-Man and the WASP in regaining control of the situation. Are you ready to face the intensity and pressure that comes with this hostile takeover?

Prepare yourself for exhilarating gameplay where quick thinking and strategic planning are essential. Navigate through the laboratory, dismantle the robotic threats, and avoid traps set by Yellowjacket. Your skills will be put to the test as you aim to save the day alongside your superhero allies!

How to play free Attack of the Robots game online

To play the game, simply use the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Click the mouse button to attack or interact with objects. Pay attention to on-screen prompts to complete objectives and defeat enemies. Remember to collect power-ups to enhance your abilities as Ant-Man and the WASP join forces in this action-packed adventure!
