Enter the thrilling world of the Squid Game Dalgona Candy 3D, an exhilarating experience that challenges your precision and nerves. In this game, your objective is to delicately extract shapes from the fragile Dalgona candy molds using a needle. Success hinges on your attention to detail and steady hand. The candy, while tempting, is brittle, so one wrong move can lead to immediate elimination.

As you embark on this sugary quest, keep a close eye on the Crack Meter. This essential tool helps you gauge the integrity of the candy as you proceed. Completing the shape extraction within the allotted time is crucial, or you'll face disqualification from this high-stakes game.
