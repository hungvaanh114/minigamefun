How to play free MiawDoku game online

To play MiawDoku, simply choose your preferred difficulty level, then click on the grid to begin. You will see a mix of cat characters already placed on the board. Your goal is to fill in the empty spots with different cats, ensuring no character is repeated in any row, column, or box. Have fun solving!
