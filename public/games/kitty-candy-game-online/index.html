How to play free Kitty Candy Game Online

To start playing the Kitty Candy Game, simply tap on groups of three or more matching kitties on the screen. Remove them to earn points and create combos for even higher scores. Keep an eye on the timer and aim to clear the board before time runs out for maximum rewards!
