Flying Santa Gifts

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the magic of the holiday season with Flying Santa Gifts, an exciting single-touch game that brings festive fun to your fingertips! In this enchanting adventure, you will take on the role of Santa Claus as he soars through the skies, collecting a flurry of gifts. The objective is simple: drag Santa across the screen to gather as many presents as you can while navigating through a whirlwind of obstacles.

As you glide through the skies, be cautious of mischievous enemies lurking about, eager to spoil your gift-gathering spree. Avoiding these foes is essential to keep your score climbing! But fear not, for along your journey, you will also unveil special powers like magnets, which draw gifts closer to you, and shields that protect you from threats. Mastering the use of these power-ups is key to achieving a record high score and spreading holiday cheer.

With its vibrant graphics and captivating gameplay, Flying Santa Gifts promises endless fun for players of all ages. Compete with friends or challenge yourself as you strive to reach new heights of score and gift collection. Dive into this festive escapade and let the spirit of giving guide you through a whimsical world filled with joy!

How to play free Flying Santa Gifts game online

To play the game, simply drag your finger across the screen to guide Santa and collect gifts. Tap on power-ups like the magnet and shield to enhance your abilities. Avoid enemies as you fly, and aim for the highest score by gathering as many gifts as possible!
