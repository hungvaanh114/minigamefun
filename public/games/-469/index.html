Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to City Run.io, an exhilarating multiplayer game that will have you racing through vibrant urban landscapes! In this action-packed experience, you'll dash through densely populated streets, weaving between pedestrians and various obstacles that stand in your way. Your main objective? To collect and merge grey pedestrians into a colossal figure that enhances your abilities and strength.

As you navigate the hustle and bustle of the city's avenues, you ll need precision and quick reflexes. The streets become progressively more congested, demanding sharp maneuvering to avoid collisions with both obstacles and rival players. Your oversized entity will allow you to dominate the competition, but be wary other players are aiming for the same goal to become the top contender!

Only the fastest and most strategic players will survive in the chaotic environment of City Run.io. Utilize your growing size to push your opponents off the path while simultaneously collecting more grey pedestrians to amplify your power. With each hurdle you overcome, you ll become closer to claiming the crown as the ultimate city runner in this action-oriented showdown. Are you ready to sharpen your skills, outpace your rivals, and be crowned the champion?

How to play free City Run.io game online

To play City Run.io, start by moving your character with the arrow keys or touch controls. Collect grey pedestrians by running into them, and watch as they merge into your growing entity. Use your superior size to outmaneuver and push away opponents while avoiding obstacles. Aim to dominate each level and claim your victory!
