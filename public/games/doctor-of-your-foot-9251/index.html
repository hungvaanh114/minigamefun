Doctor of Your Foot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the world of foot care with Doctor of your Foot, the ultimate ASMR nail salon experience. Welcome to Foot Clinic, the top-rated game designed to immerse you in the soothing art of foot therapy. With a deliciously satisfying range of tasks, you will scrub, scrape, pluck, and pamper various feet in dire need of treatment.

Your journey as a foot doctor begins by diagnosing common foot ailments such as calluses, corns, and ingrown toenails. In this interactive ASMR clinic, you ll engage in intricate surgical procedures that promote healthy and beautiful feet. Use realistic tools to perform delicate operations, ensuring your patients walk away pain-free and looking fabulous.

Beyond surgical finesse, embrace the relaxing side of foot care with luxurious spa treatments. Indulge your clients with soothing foot massages and intricate pedicures, providing them not just with medical attention but also a delightful dose of relaxation. Doctor of your Foot elevates your doctoring skills while delivering the pampering treatment that every foot deserves. Are you prepared to embark on this engaging adventure? Whether you're conducting surgery or offering a tranquil spa experience, your skills will shine in this unique nail salon game.

How to play free Doctor of your Foot game online

To play the game, simply navigate through various stations where you can treat feet with cutting-edge tools. Follow on-screen instructions to perform procedures, using your mouse or touchscreen to interact with different items. Complete each task effectively to earn a satisfied client, and unlock fun new features as you progress!
