Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the vibrant world of Ben 10: Super Slime Ben, the exciting sequel to the popular free-to-play game, Super Slime Blitz! In this thrilling adventure, players will engage with bigger challenges that are not just better, but SLIMIER than before! You can choose from ten of Ben's incredible alien heroes, each boasting unique abilities that will aid you in your victorious ascent. Your mission is to climb higher, dodge countless obstacles, and collect as many coins as possible.

As you navigate through the colorful landscapes, watch out for the gooey slime creeping up behind you! You ll encounter an array of hazards, including flying slime balls, fearsome creatures, and tricky disappearing blocks. The excitement doesn t stop there! Discover special Omni-enhancements and playful costumes that will make your adventure even more enjoyable. Keep an eye out for unlockable hats and adorable critters that grant bonus abilities, all of which can give you the edge needed to achieve a new high score!

Prepare yourself for the ultimate test of skill as you attempt to outrun the advancing slime and prove that you re the best slimefighter in the universe! How high can your aim take you before the slime engulfs your path? Join Ben in this exhilarating and challenging game to find out!

How to play free BEN 10 PUZZLE game online

To play Ben 10: Super Slime Ben, simply use the on-screen controls to run, jump, and climb your way through the levels. Avoid obstacles and defeat enemies while collecting coins and enhancements. Keep your skills sharp, and don t let the slime catch you!
