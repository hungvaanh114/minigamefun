Mario Dash JetPack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you a fan of Super Mario? Dive into the thrilling world of Mario Dash JetPack, an exciting flying adventure featuring everyone's favorite plumber. In this game, jump into action as you guide Mario through a vibrant sky filled with colorful coins. Your objective is not only to fly but also to collect as many coins as you can to increase your score.

As you soar through the clouds, be on the lookout for the letters D, A, S, and H. Collecting these letters activates the powerful green Mario, giving you a temporary boost to glide further and earn extra points. However, navigating this high-flying world is not without its challenges. You must avoid the bottom of the screen and pesky birds that will try to thwart your progress. Damage to Mario means losing life, so stay alert and keep your reflexes sharp!

The bright visuals and engaging gameplay make Mario Dash JetPack not only fun but also visually delightful. With each tap, Mario flies higher, and each coin collected increases the thrill of your journey. Will you be able to help Mario achieve a new high score? Jump into this game for an exhilarating experience that pays homage to the beloved franchise.

How to play free Mario Dash JetPack game online

To play Mario Dash JetPack, simply tap your screen to make Mario fly higher. Collect the colorful coins for points while looking out for the letters D, A, S, and H to activate the super mode. Steer clear of the bottom and avoid birds to preserve your lives and enhance your gameplay.


Instructions

Tap and hold to fly higher and release to get down
