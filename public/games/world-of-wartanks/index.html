World of WarTanks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Fascinating tank action. Choose your tank and go ahead to complete missions and destroy enemies. The game has different missions, survival and 5 vs 5 battles. The game will be interesting to everyone who is interested in military topics! New tanks have been added to the game, which can be obtained for Stars. Upgrade tanks and complete missions. Complete missions and get Stars! 1st mission = 1 star 12th mission = 12 stars 5 vs 5 battle = 10 stars

Instructions

The goal of the game is to complete the mission objective destroy enemy tanks To shoot point the scope at the tank and right-click When the target is captured the sight will turn red Shoot when a red circle appears in the sight
