This thrilling ball sorting color puzzle game is designed for those who seek an entertaining experience that also challenges their mental acuity. ColorCandy Sort Puzzle is not just a game; it is a delightful journey that will keep you engaged for hours on end. You will never find yourself feeling bored here. How to play free ColorCandy Sort Puzzle game online

To play the ColorCandy Sort Puzzle game, simply click on a ball to pick it up and then drag it to a bottle of the same color. If a bottle is full or the colors do not match, you can try another bottle. Your goal is to fill each bottle with balls of the same color. Enjoy!
