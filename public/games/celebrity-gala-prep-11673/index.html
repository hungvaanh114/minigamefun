Celebrity Gala Prep

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the glamorous world of Celebrity Gala Prep, where you can join some of the biggest stars in pop culture! In this captivating game, the likes of Ariana Grande, Selena Gomez, Rihanna, Kendall Jenner, and Taylor Swift are throwing an extravagant gala party, and they need your help to make it a night to remember.

The theme of the event is nothing less than a dazzling gala, and your fashion expertise is crucial. Encourage the celebrities to shine by assisting them in selecting the most stunning outfits and accessories from their vast wardrobes. With a variety of options at your disposal, you ll have a blast trying out different combinations to ensure each star looks their best for the grand occasion. From elegant gowns to striking jewelry, each choice impacts the vibe of the gala, making your decisions all the more significant.

Explore the eclectic collection of clothing and accessories available, mix and match styles, and showcase your creativity. As you dive into the colorful world of fashion, the girls will rely on you to not only help them look fabulous but also to instill confidence as they step into the spotlight. Celebrity Gala Prep guarantees fun and excitement as you navigate through stylish possibilities and help your favorite stars shine brightly on their special night!

How to play free Celebrity Gala Prep game online

To play the Celebrity Gala Prep game, start by choosing one of the featured stars. Then, navigate through their wardrobe using your mouse. Click on the outfits and accessories you want to try on the celebrities. Mix and match until you find the perfect look for the gala. Enjoy the creative process!
