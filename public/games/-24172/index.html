Looney Tunes Jigsaw Puzzle Collection?Looney Tunes Jigsaw Puzzle Collection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

If you're a fan of classic cartoons, the Looney Tunes Jigsaw Puzzle Collection is an absolute must-try! This captivating game features an extensive array of jigsaw puzzles showcasing your favorite Looney Tunes characters. Whether you're a longtime admirer or a newcomer to the cartoon series, you're bound to have a blast piecing together these vibrant images.

As you dive into the action, you'll be tasked with dragging puzzle pieces from the lower section of the screen and placing them onto the puzzle field. But beware, as each level is timed! You must complete the puzzle before the countdown reaches zero; otherwise, you ll have to restart. The fun begins with the introduction of three beloved characters: Bugs Bunny, the iconic prankster rabbit known for his clever antics; Lola Bunny, the charming pink bunny who delivers pizzas and shares a friendship with Bugs; and the eccentric Marvin the Martian, an alien dressed in Roman attire with a penchant for mischief.

Each successful puzzle completion unlocks new characters and challenges, ensuring an exciting journey through the Looney Tunes universe. Get ready for delightful surprises and a nostalgia-filled experience as you engage with the vibrant world of Looney Tunes puzzles. So gather your wits and get ready for a joyful jigsaw adventure!

How to play free Looney Tunes Jigsaw Puzzle Collection game online

To begin playing the Looney Tunes Jigsaw Puzzle Collection, simply select a puzzle from the main menu. Once the puzzle is displayed, drag the puzzle pieces from the bottom area and place them on the designated space of the puzzle field. Remember to work quickly, as you have a limited amount of time to complete each challenge!
