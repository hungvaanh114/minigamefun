Tanuki Sunset

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tanuki Sunset is an exhilarating third-person longboard-skating adventure that invites players to take on the role of an agile raccoon gliding down a breathtaking procedurally generated seaside route. Set against a vibrant synthwave-inspired backdrop, players engage in thrilling downhill rides filled with twists, turns, and daring maneuvers.

As you navigate the narrow corners of this captivating course, the key to maximizing your score lies in deftly drifting your way around obstacles. Collect Tanuki Bits to power up your Bonus Roulette Meter, allowing you to unlock additional points and rewards. The more you drift, the better your chances of showcasing your skills and accumulating higher scores.

Take to the slopes with a fearless spirit as you attempt to gather air on jumps, skillfully dodge oncoming cars, and cleverly maneuver around various obstacles. Risking close calls against walls and edges not only heightens the excitement but also sets you up for incredible near-miss moments that can significantly boost your score.

Explore a diverse world as you refine your longboarding techniques. Dash through breathtaking mountain ranges, glide past vibrant city landscapes, and cruise along the sun-kissed beaches of Sunset Island. With plenty of tricks to master, from launching off ramps to executing impressive backwards longboarding, there s no shortage of thrilling gameplay to enjoy. Grab your longboard and embark on an unforgettable journey through Tanuki Sunset, developed by Squid Squad Games!

How to play free Tanuki Sunset game online

To play Tanuki Sunset online, use the arrow keys or a controller to steer and accelerate your raccoon down the hill. Initiate drifts by pressing the designated button to navigate sharp turns while collecting Tanuki Bits. Remember to execute jumps and tricks to enhance your score as you enjoy the ride!
