Welcome to the ultimate off-road tractor pulling simulator, crafted by a leading simulation studio. Experience the excitement of commanding a powerful tractor across diverse terrains in this immersive adventure. This 2020 simulator is perfect for enthusiasts who thrive on driving high-torque vehicles. Dive into the tractor driving craze and enjoy every moment!

The heart of this tractor pull game lies in aiding those in distress. Utilize your hefty tractor to tow stranded vehicles back to garage parking. Navigate with precision to prevent any damage while completing challenging levels. The game offers three distinct modes: hills, off-road, and city, enabling you to enjoy tractor driving amidst scenic hill landscapes, rugged zig-zag paths, or orderly urban streets.

Your mission is to rescue damaged vehicles and transport them to the garage, earning coins to acquire new tractors along the way. A fleet of tractors awaits you in the garage, ready to be used to conquer upcoming levels within the time limit.

To get started, choose a tractor, hop in, and fire up the engine. Follow the on-screen map to locate and rescue the stranded vehicle, then pull it to safety. Completing tasks involves a careful approach, using the chain pull to bring cars, trolleys, or other vehicles to the specified location swiftly. Precision is critical even the smallest mistake can result in accidents that could end your game.

With intuitive controls featuring forward, neutral, and reverse options, you can easily navigate. Leverage various camera angles and navigation buttons to master the art of tractor driving. The game boasts stunning 3D HD graphics, engaging animations, and lifelike sounds, making each moment feel real.

How to play free Tractor 3D no game online

To begin playing, select your preferred tractor and climb aboard. Start the engine and refer to the map for directions to the stuck vehicle. Once located, utilize the control buttons to carefully pull it towards the garage, ensuring you adhere to the time limits and avoid any accidents.
