Princess Wedding Cleaning - Washing Amp; Fixing

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "Princess Wedding Cleaning - Washing & Fixing," you embark on a delightful adventure as you lend a helping hand to the royal preparations for the princess s wedding day. The atmosphere in the castle is filled with excitement, but before the joyful celebrations can commence, the castle needs to be perfectly spotless! Your mission is to ensure that each area is shimmering and immaculate for the big event.

You will find yourself in three key locations: the dressing room, the wedding hall, and the banquet hall. Each space presents its own challenges, filled with decorations, items, and remnants of previous celebrations that need sorting and cleaning. As you tackle the mess, you'll uncover all sorts of interesting objects that have been misplaced, and it's your job to put everything back in its right place.

The dressing room must be tidy and organized, as the princess will need a calm and serene space to prepare for her special day. The wedding hall should shine with elegance and beauty, as it will serve as the backdrop for the couple s vows. Finally, the banquet hall needs to be ready for all the guests, filled with delicious food and decorated splendidly. With your help, the princess's wedding will be a dream come true!

How to play free Princess Wedding Cleaning - Washing & Fixing game online

To play "Princess Wedding Cleaning," simply click on the rooms to start your cleaning adventure. Drag and drop items to their proper places, wipe surfaces clean, and make quick decisions to clear away the clutter. Use the tools available to you and race against the clock to make the castle ready for the princess s perfect day!
