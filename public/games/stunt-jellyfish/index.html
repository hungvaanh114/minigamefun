Stunt Jellyfish

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical underwater world of Stunt Jellyfish and put your jellyfish piloting skills to the test! Are you ready to embark on an aquatic adventure that will challenge your nerves and your precision? As you glide through vibrant ocean levels, your primary objective is to collect all the sea stars scattered throughout each stage. But beware, the currents of difficulty will rise as you progress. You may find yourself navigating the same level multiple times before mastering the task at hand!

Your journey isn't just about gathering sea stars; you'll also need to swim through hoops without crashing. Keeping an eye on your life meter is crucial, as you may need to replenish it. Be on the lookout for gold jellyfish that act as lifelines to keep your jellyfish thriving and fit for the challenging levels ahead. The environment is teeming with obstacles that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking, so a steady hand and quick decision-making will be your best allies.

With each level, the excitement builds, and your skills will be put to the test. Each mission completed not only brings satisfaction but also nudges you closer to becoming the ultimate jellyfish pilot. Are you up for the challenge? Dive in and let the hunting begin there's a whole ocean of adventure awaiting you!

How to play free Stunt Jellyfish game online

In the game, navigate your jellyfish using the arrow keys or on-screen controls. Your objective is to collect all the sea stars while swimming through hoops. Watch your life meter and gather gold jellyfish to replenish your health. Complete each level by avoiding obstacles, and stay focused as the difficulty escalates!
