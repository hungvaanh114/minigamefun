Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the chaotic world of Trap Craft, where a mischievous hacker has unleashed a zombie apocalypse within a monster school. This time, he challenges you to engage in an exhilarating game alongside familiar characters such as a Noob, a Pro, a Princess, and others. Each character possesses individual aspirations; for instance, the Noob yearns for the Princess's affection. The hacker promises to fulfill these wishes, but only if players can conquer a series of daunting challenges.

Your primary objective is to defend the portal from relentless zombie assaults by employing creative and strategic defenses. Build unique towers and devise traps on each level to thwart the enemy. The game provides you with an array of weapons and resources to enhance your tactics. As you progress, you ll mine valuable coins that can be used to fortify your defenses or purchase pets that augment your hero's abilities. Each level introduces fresh traps and challenges that will test your wits and adaptability.

As you navigate this vibrant world, prepare for surprising twists orchestrated by the cunning hacker. No character is exempt from his meddling, even the skilled Pro or the naive Noob. Players will discover amusing endings tailored to each hero, ensuring that every playthrough offers something new and entertaining. Will you be clever enough to survive the hacker s tests and fulfill the characters' desires? If you re ready for an adventure filled with humor, strategy, and thrilling gameplay, Trap Craft is the game for you!

How to play free Trap Craft game online

To play Trap Craft online, simply launch the game and select your character. Use your mouse or touchscreen to build defenses around the portal, placing traps and towers strategically. Keep an eye on the relentless zombie waves and adapt your strategy as necessary. Mine coins to upgrade your defenses and purchase pets that enhance your character's abilities. Good luck!
