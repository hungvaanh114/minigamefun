Whack a Mole

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the whimsical Adventure of Whack A Mole, players find themselves in a vibrant field teeming with mischievous moles. These pesky critters pop up unexpectedly from their burrows, challenging you to test your reflexes and precision. Your primary mission is to drive these cheeky moles away before they disappear back into their underground hideouts. Armed with a virtual mallet, you must strike quickly and accurately, for every mole you successfully hit counts towards your score.

The thrill of the game lies in its fast-paced action, as the moles emerge at varying speeds and from unpredictable locations. With each passing moment, the challenge increases, demanding sharp focus and swift reactions. As you progress, you may encounter special moles that offer bonuses or time challenges, adding another layer of excitement to the classic gameplay.
