AnimalCraft Friends

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting realm of AnimalCraft Friends, where adventure and survival go hand in hand! Join two adorable companions a pig and a sheep as they navigate through a perilous forest in search of safety. Your mission is to guide these lovable characters to their sanctuary: the cozy barn.

As they embark on their journey, challenges lurk at every corner. The forest is filled with hidden dangers, and every moment counts in this race against time. The pig and the sheep are quite hungry, so it is essential to gather food along the way. Collect carrots and hay to satisfy their appetites and provide them the energy they need to make it through the treacherous terrain.

Be on high alert! The farmer, always on the lookout for his wandering livestock, poses a significant threat. If he spots your friends, he will not hesitate to capture them, leading them to a perilous fate. Make strategic decisions as you maneuver through the dense foliage, ensuring that your adorable companions avoid detection.

With nimble fingers and a quick wit, you can help the pig and the sheep find their way out of danger and into the safety of the barn. Your choices and timing will determine the outcome of their adventure, so stay vigilant and work together to reach their goal!

How to play free AnimalCraft Friends game online

To play AnimalCraft Friends, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate the pig and sheep through the forest. Collect carrots and hay to feed your animals while avoiding the farmer. Utilize strategy to find safe routes and guide your friends to the barn without getting caught!
