Airport Manager 2022

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the bustling world of Airport Manager 2022! It s time for you to take control of your very own city airport. As you embark on this adventure, you will be responsible for creating and managing your own airline, overseeing every aircraft and crew member, ensuring that your airport ranks as the best in the business. The plane simulation experience is incredibly immersive!

Your duties as an airport manager are diverse and exciting. You will serve as the airport scanner, inspecting passengers and their luggage with an X-Ray scanner to ensure that no prohibited items make it through. Additionally, you'll check visas for all travelers, discovering surprising stamps along the way!

Some aircraft and helicopters may require maintenance, so you ll need to fix and clean them in the hangar. As a pilot, get ready to take to the skies and navigate your plane safely. In-flight services are vital, so make sure every passenger is treated with care and comfort. You will also act as the commander of planes and helicopters, ensuring their smooth operation.

Lost luggage? No problem! You ll need to track down customers' missing belongings. And when inspiration strikes, feel free to unleash your creativity by drawing your ideas. The possibilities are endless in Airport Manager 2022!
