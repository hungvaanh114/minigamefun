Connect and Merge?Connect and Merge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Connect and Merge is an engaging puzzle game that challenges players to draw lines between various points on the grid. The objective is simple yet captivating: create as long a line as possible that connects identical numbers, which not only enhances your score but also helps you unlock new levels of difficulty. The clever mechanics of the game allow you to strategize your moves to maximize your points. Each time you merge identical numbers, they add together to form multiples of 2, elevating the excitement as you combine smaller numbers into larger ones.

As you progress through the game, the excitement builds with each successful connection. The more points you connect, the higher your score will soar. The satisfaction of watching those numbers multiply is exhilarating, making every line drawn feel rewarding. With a visually appealing interface and smooth gameplay, Connect and Merge offers an addictive experience that keeps players coming back for more. Whether playing casually or aiming for a high score, this game promises hours of fun and mental stimulation. So, gather your wits, practice your strategy, and see how many points you can connect!

How to play free Connect and Merge game online

To play Connect and Merge, simply start by tapping on a point, and then drag your finger to connect it with other identical points. The longer the line, the more points you accumulate. Focus on creating longer connections for higher scores and merging numbers to reach larger multiples of 2. Enjoy!
