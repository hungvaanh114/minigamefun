Swing Grimace

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Grimace in an exhilarating adventure with "Swing Grimace!" Your mission is to guide Grimace as he leaps left and right, navigating a treacherous landscape filled with menacing spikes. The aim is simple but challenging: avoid these obstacles for as long as possible. The longer you keep Grimace safe, the higher you ll climb on the leaderboard!

As you jump from side to side, each second counts. With swift reflexes and precise movements, try to achieve your personal best and outshine your friends. The thrill of competition awaits as you vie for the top spot. The vibrant visuals and playful character will keep you engaged, making it a perfect way to celebrate Grimace s special day. Happy birthday, Grimace! Embrace the challenge and see how long you can sustain the fun!

How to play free Swing Grimace game online

To play, use the arrow keys or swipe left and right on your device to move Grimace. Jump side to side to dodge the spikes while collecting bonuses along the way. Keep an eye on your score and aim to beat your previous records! Have fun!
