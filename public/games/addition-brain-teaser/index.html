Addition Brain Teaser

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing the Addition Brain Teaser, a captivating and challenging math puzzle game that will put your problem-solving skills to the test. The primary objective is simple: to sum up all the numbers displayed on the board. However, this game introduces an exciting twist that makes it more complex and engaging. You can only combine numbers that are either equal or sum up to a value that already exists on the board.

For instance, if you see the number 3, you can effortlessly add two 3s together. On the other hand, if the number 7 is present, you have the option to combine the numbers 5 and 2, as their total equals 7, which is already on the board. This unique rule adds a layer of strategy, compelling players to think critically about their moves and consider the relationships between the numbers they can add.

The Addition Brain Teaser not only sharpens your math skills but also enhances your cognitive abilities with its engaging gameplay. Whether you are a math enthusiast or simply looking for a brain exercise, this game offers fun and challenges. Test your limits and see how quickly you can clear the board by adding numbers wisely while adhering to the game's specific rules!

How to play free Addition Brain Teaser game online

To play the Addition Brain Teaser online, simply click on the numbers on the board that you wish to combine. Make sure they adhere to the combining rules: either equal pairs or numbers that total an existing number. Keep adding until all numbers are cleared from the board!
