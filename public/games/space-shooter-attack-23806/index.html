Space Shooter Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Defend the cosmos in the thrilling game of Space Shooter Attack, where you take command in an intense battle against alien invaders! Our galaxy is under siege, and the stakes have never been higher. As captain of the last remaining defense squadron, you must navigate the perilous expanse of space to save our galaxy from complete annihilation.

The alien forces have ravaged our fleets, leaving destruction in their wake. Now it's up to you to rally what's left of the space team and launch a counterattack. Jump into your starship and prepare for an epic showdown. Use sharp strategy and quick reflexes to dodge enemy fire and obliterate the alien ships as they swarm through the stars.

Throughout your journey, you ll encounter an array of challenges, including navigating through treacherous asteroid fields and engaging in epic dogfights with formidable foes. Collect power-ups to enhance your ship's abilities, upgrade your arsenal, and unleash devastating attacks that will turn the tide of battle. Will you rise to the occasion, reclaim the galactic territories, and establish a new era of peace? The fate of the universe rests on your shoulders!

How to play free Space Shooter Attack game online

To play the game, use your keyboard or mouse to control your ship's movement. Dodge incoming fire while targeting alien ships. Tap the fire button to launch missiles and eliminate enemies. Keep an eye on power-ups and collect them to boost your ship's performance. Survive waves of invaders to score points and advance through the levels!
