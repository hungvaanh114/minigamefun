Protect Draw It

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Protect Draw It is an engaging and entertaining casual cartoon game available for free on Puzzlegame, designed especially for boys and puzzle enthusiasts alike. In this delightful online adventure, players are tasked with safeguarding adorable sheep from hungry wolves eager to feast on them. Each level presents a unique challenge as you strategically draw fences to create barriers between the sheep and their fierce predators.

As you progress through the game, you'll face various levels of difficulty, requiring clever thinking and creativity to outsmart the wolves. Players must carefully consider their moves since the number of fences available to draw is limited, adding an extra layer of intrigue to each puzzle. This captivating gameplay encourages players to develop problem-solving skills while enjoying a fun, colorful environment filled with charming cartoon graphics.

Protect Draw It is designed for play on multiple devices, including mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers, ensuring that you can enjoy the game wherever you are. Whether you're looking for a quick distraction or a longer gaming session, Protect Draw It promises to deliver simple yet fascinating puzzle challenges that keep you coming back for more.

How to play free Protect Draw It game online

To play Protect Draw It, simply draw fences on the screen using your mouse or finger to segment the sheep from the wolves. Make sure to position the fences wisely within your limited number of lines, ensuring no wolf can reach the sheep. Complete each level by successfully protecting all the sheep!
