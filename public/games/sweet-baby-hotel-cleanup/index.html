Sweet Baby Hotel Cleanup

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical universe of Sweet Baby Hotel Cleanup, where excitement and chaos intertwine! Dive into the lively atmosphere of the Crazy Holiday Hotel, where every corner is filled with fun yet needs a little tender loving care. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to maintain the hotel's sparkling charm amidst all the delightful disorder.

In this vibrant game, you'll encounter sticky slime monsters that need to be vanquished while you embark on a cleaning adventure. Clean various areas such as messy hotel rooms, bustling cafes, untidy bathrooms, energetic gyms, and refreshing swimming pools. No spot is too challenging as you roll up your sleeves and tackle the muck and mayhem that has taken over!

Sweet Baby Hotel Cleanup is not just about tidying up; it's also an opportunity to express your creativity! Design each area according to your unique style choosing colors, decorations, and furnishings that reflect your personal taste. Invite your friends to join in the fun as you work together to create a hotel paradise that everyone will love!

Targeted at kids, this exciting cleaning adventure promotes teamwork, responsibility, and imaginative play. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, there's always a mess to tackle and fun to be had. So grab your cleaning tools, and let s bring back the sparkle to Crazy Holiday Hotel!

How to play free Sweet Baby Hotel Cleanup game online

To play the Sweet Baby Hotel Cleanup game, use your mouse to navigate through various hotel areas. Click on messy spots to clean them up, and use the tools provided to get rid of slime monsters. Decorate each room and explore different hotel facilities by tapping on the icons available on your screen!
