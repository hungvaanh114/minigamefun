Airplane Fight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Airplane Fight is an exciting online game that you can enjoy at no cost. In this thrilling adventure, a fleet of sinister aircraft has invaded the sky, plotting to launch an aerial assault. Taking on the role of a skilled pilot, it's your duty to defend your country and uphold its honor. Prepare for an action-packed experience as you soar into the skies with your trusty plane!

Navigate your aircraft through dangerous aerial environments, skillfully avoiding enemy fire while strategically targeting the hostile planes. The objective is clear: you must destroy every enemy aircraft that threatens your territory. With intuitive controls at your disposal, you'll be able to steer your plane with precision, aiming with accuracy to take down your adversaries. Keep your finger on the trigger as you shoot continuously to eliminate the incoming threats.

The game combines heart-pounding excitement with a competitive spirit, allowing you to test your piloting skills against the evil invaders. Challenge yourself to improve with each playthrough, as you earn points and accolades for your heroic efforts. Get ready to immerse yourself in an adrenaline-fueled battle in the skies, where every second counts, and only the bravest pilots emerge victorious!

How to play free Airplane Fight game online

To start playing Airplane Fight, use the arrow keys or WASD for navigation to guide your plane. Aim with your mouse, or use the keyboard controls to target enemy aircraft. Press the spacebar to fire continuously at your foes. Stay alert, and avoid incoming fire while racking up points for each enemy plane you shoot down!
