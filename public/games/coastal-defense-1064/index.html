Coastal Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Coastal Defense invites you to take command in a high-stakes battle to safeguard your coastline from enemy forces. As waves of soldiers approach both from the sea and the air, it is your duty to strategize and deploy a variety of powerful weapons to thwart their invasion. Utilize state-of-the-art artillery and a range of defensive units to create an impenetrable barrier against relentless attacks.

Your seashore is under siege, and every second counts! Adapt your tactics dynamically as enemies come at you from different directions, planning your moves carefully to protect vital areas. Upgrade your defenses between waves, ensuring your artillery is ready to confront the increasing challenges. From riflemen to tanks, and air defense systems to coastal turrets, you hold an arsenal of resources at your disposal to ensure victory. Test your reflexes and decision-making skills as you tactically deploy your troops to maximize their effectiveness against oncoming threats.

In Coastal Defense, you re not merely a participant; you are the last line of defense for the inhabitants of the coastline. Rally your troops, command your artillery, and get ready to fight off the marauding enemies intent on breaching your shores. Engage in epic battles that will put your strategic prowess to the test, and show the world that your coastline will not be conquered!

How to play free Coastal defense game online

To play Coastal Defense, use your mouse to aim and click to fire your weapons at incoming enemies. Strategically build and upgrade your defenses as you progress through each wave. Keep an eye on your resources and make quick decisions to ensure your shoreline remains secure. Good luck!
