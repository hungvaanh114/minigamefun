How to play free Infinity Cubes 2048 game online

To play Infinity Cubes 2048, simply drag cubes with the same number together to merge them into a higher value cube. Use swipes in different directions to move the cubes across the playing field. Keep merging and strategizing to achieve the largest cube possible before running out of moves!
