Limax.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Limax.io is an entertaining multiplayer io game that invites you to indulge in its whimsical world filled with slugs. In this vibrant and quirky environment, your mission is to grow increasingly larger by consuming food orbs scattered throughout the arena. As you evolve from a tiny slug into a colossal and formidable creature, you will find yourself dashing around in a bid to eliminate other players. The gameplay is both thrilling and satisfying, as you strive to become the biggest limax on the leaderboard.

Starting off as a small slug, your journey begins by devouring food orbs that are readily available. The more you consume, the plumper you become, allowing you to speed up and outmaneuver your opponents. To defeat other players, you must accelerate and strategically force them to collide with the trail that you leave behind, effectively causing their demise.

As you continue to grow, you will not only increase your size but also your speed, making it easier for you to hunt down food orbs and other players alike. The visual appeal of Limax.io, combined with its engaging mechanics, makes it a delightful experience for players of all ages. Your ultimate quest is to ascend the ranks and claim your spot at the top of the leaderboard, showcasing your dominance in the realm of slugs.

How to play free Limax.io game online

To play Limax.io, navigate your slug using your mouse for movement, and click to dash for speed. Eat the glowing orbs to grow larger and outrun opponents. To eliminate rivals, dash into them or force them into your trail, ensuring you stay agile and watch out for enemies!


Instructions

Mouse to move Left click to dash
