Baby Hazel Sports Day

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel and her friends for an exciting Annual Sports Day at preschool! This fun-filled event is packed with a variety of competitive games and activities that promise to keep you on your toes. Your mission is to assist Baby Hazel as she practices essential sports skills before the big day. With your help, she will get ready to be the star of the event!

Delight in the spirited competition as you participate in classic games such as the Spoon and Egg race, where balance and speed are the keys to victory. Try your hand at Frisbee Throw, testing both precision and aim as you throw the frisbee as far as possible. Each game is designed to be engaging and enjoyable, allowing you to cheer for the kids while witnessing their playful antics.

As teams form and everyone gets into the competitive spirit, you ll have the chance to help Baby Hazel and her friends overcome various challenges. Will your team emerge victorious and take home the trophy? With so many games and activities to enjoy, it s a day filled with laughter and camaraderie. Let the sportsmanship shine through as you cheer on your favorite teams and partake in the delightful atmosphere of Baby Hazel's Sports Day!

How to play free Baby Hazel Sports Day game online

To play the Baby Hazel Sports Day game, navigate through the various fun challenges by clicking on the screen to guide Baby Hazel. Complete each game by following the instructions given, like balancing the egg on a spoon or throwing the frisbee. Collect stars and cheer for Baby Hazel to win the competition!
