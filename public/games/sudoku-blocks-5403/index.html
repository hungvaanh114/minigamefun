How to play free Sudoku Blocks game online

To play Sudoku Blocks, start by examining the grid where some blocks will be pre-filled with numbers. Your objective is to fill in the remaining empty blocks while ensuring that each row, column, and designated square contains the numbers 1 through 9 without repetition. Click on empty blocks to input your chosen numbers based on logic and deductions. Enjoy and have fun playing!
