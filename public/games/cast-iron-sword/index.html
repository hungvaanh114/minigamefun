Cast Iron Sword

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the soothing world of the Cast Iron Sword, a leisure game designed to melt away the stresses of everyday life. In this engaging experience, you embark on a journey to create the perfect sword by utilizing various grindstones. Each grindstone has a unique shape, allowing you to mold and polish your sword prototype into a magnificent weapon.

The gameplay revolves around the strategic use of a magical stone. As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter specific trajectories that guide you in eliminating unnecessary material from your sword. Precision is key, as each movement must align with the designated path to ensure success. Straying from the prescribed trajectory can lead to failure, adding an exciting layer of challenge and focus to your crafting experience.

Not only does the Cast Iron Sword provide a relaxing escape, but it also encourages players to sharpen their concentration and honing their skills as swordsmiths. The satisfaction of refining your sword to perfection keeps players engaged and motivated as they advance through the game. Whether you're a casual gamer seeking to unwind or someone looking to cultivate new skills, this game offers a perfect blend of creativity and challenge.

How to play free Cast Iron Sword game online

To play Cast Iron Sword, simply enter the game and select a level. Use the grindstones to polish your sword by following the on-screen trajectory with your magic stone. Make sure to eliminate the excess material precisely as directed. If you stray off course, retry to properly shape your sword without fail!


Instructions

Press your magic stone according to the track given in each level and let it eliminate the redundant materials so that the sword takes its proper shape
