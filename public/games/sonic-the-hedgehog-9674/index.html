Sonic the Hedgehog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Sonic the Hedgehog, you take on the role of the iconic blue hedgehog, Sonic, as you race through vibrant levels filled with exciting adventures. With your trusty sidekick, Tails, by your side, your mission is to stop the nefarious Dr. Robotnik from carrying out his evil plans. This classic arcade game, originally released on the SEGA Genesis in 1991, introduces players to an engaging storyline where animals have been kidnapped for Robotnik's twisted experiments.

Your journey will see you traversing through various landscapes, collecting golden rings while dodging traps and defeating menacing foes. As you dash, jump, and spin your way through dozens of unique stages, you'll encounter new challenges that test your skills and reflexes. Teamwork with Tails adds an extra layer of fun, as you can strategically coordinate moves to conquer obstacles and defeat enemies.

The game s colorful graphics and fast-paced gameplay have made it a beloved title for gaming enthusiasts. Sonic s incredible speed and agility set the foundation for thrilling arcade action. Whether you re a seasoned player or new to the franchise, Sonic the Hedgehog offers an exhilarating experience filled with nostalgia and excitement.
