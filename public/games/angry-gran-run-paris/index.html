Angry Gran Run: Paris

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the exhilarating adventure of Angry Gran Run: Paris, where you dash through the enchanting streets of Paris alongside a spirited granny on the run from her asylum. This feisty old lady may not look like it, but she possesses incredible speed that keeps you engaged as you navigate through the City of Lights.

As you sprint down bustling alleyways adorned with delightful Parisian themes, you'll encounter a variety of obstacles inspired by the French culture. From crooked cafes to outdoor boulevards, prepare to duck and dive to avoid quirky challenges as you guide Granny to freedom. The game also showcases a delightful roster of new romantic and humorous characters, perfect for the Valentine s Day season, adding an extra layer of fun and whimsy to your escapade.

Explore the beautifully rendered graphics as you hop over hurdles and take on exciting missions while collecting coins and power-ups to enhance your gameplay. Players can experience the thrill of outrunning the gendarmes and dodging mustache-twirling characters while enjoying the charming background of Paris. With wonderful animations and a lively soundtrack, this game promises endless entertainment as you embark on a wild chase with Grandma!

How to play free Angry Gran Run: Paris game online

To play Angry Gran Run: Paris, simply use the arrow keys or swipe your touchscreen to control Granny's movements. Tap or swipe to jump, slide, or turn as you navigate through various obstacles and collect coins along the way. Power-ups will help you gain speed, making it easier to escape from pursuing enemies!
