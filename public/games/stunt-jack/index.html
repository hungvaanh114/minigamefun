Stunt Jack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you prepared to become the next legendary Stunt Fly Pumpkin? In Stunt Jack, you'll need unwavering courage and impeccable precision to navigate through a series of challenging levels. As you progress, the difficulty ramps up significantly, requiring keen reflexes and a strategic approach. Be prepared to tackle levels multiple times, as achieving mission objectives can be quite demanding!

Your primary task is to collect all the stars scattered throughout each level. To do this, you'll need to expertly maneuver your pumpkin through various hoops while avoiding crashes. But that's not all; watch your magic bar closely! At times, you'll require extra energy to complete a level, which means you must search for potions to boost your power.

Are you ready to soar through the skies, little witch? Embrace the thrill and excitement of Stunt Jack as you embark on an unforgettable adventure filled with obstacles and enchanting challenges. With every star you collect, you'll get closer to mastering the ultimate flying skills!

How to play free Stunt Jack game online

To play Stunt Jack, use your keyboard or touchscreen controls to navigate your pumpkin through each level. Aim to collect all the stars while flying through hoops. Keep an eye on your magic bar and gather potions to refill it. The time to soar and become a stunt flying expert is now!
