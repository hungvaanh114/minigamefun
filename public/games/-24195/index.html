Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of being a true transporter in the Army Truck Car Transport Game, the standout transporter game of the year! Step into the shoes of a military transporter and navigate a world filled with powerful army tanks, cargo airplanes, and military vehicles. If you've ever dreamed of becoming the ultimate army vehicle transporter, this game is tailored just for you. Dive into the action with the US Army Car Transport Cruise Ship Simulator 2020, equipped with the best features of army transport gaming.

This engaging transporter game presents two distinct modes that challenge your skills: Cruise Ship and Airplane Transport. In the cruise ship mode, your mission begins by loading military tanks and army cars onto an 18-wheeler truck. Your journey then leads you to a cruise port where you must park the cargo truck and unload the military vehicles. Once the unloading is complete, it's time to drive the army tanks and cars onto the cruise ship, taking on the role of a proficient driver as you navigate from one city to another. Remember, time is of the essence as you transport these essential army vehicles!

In the Airplane Transport mode of the US Army Car Transport Cruise Ship Simulator 2019, start your heavy trailer engine and load it with army tanks and military cars. Carefully drive your loaded truck to the nearest airport, park it, and prepare for the next exciting task: becoming a pilot! Experience the challenges of flying a cargo plane as you transport your military load to a new city, ensuring a safe landing on the airport runway. Immerse yourself in this captivating blend of truck and flight simulator gameplay, and establish yourself as the top transport simulator and legendary driver of 2020!

How to play free Army Truck Car Transport Game online

To play the Army Truck Car Transport Game, simply start by selecting your transport mode either the Cruise Ship or Airplane Transport. Once in-game, follow the on-screen instructions to load the military vehicles onto your truck. Drive carefully to your designated destination, completing tasks that involve parking and unloading before transitioning to piloting a cargo plane. Enjoy the thrilling transport experience!
