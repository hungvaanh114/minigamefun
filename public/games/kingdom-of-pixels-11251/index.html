Kingdom of Pixels immerses players in an engaging 2D MOBA platformer experience that harkens back to classic pixel art aesthetics. Echoing elements found in renowned games like League of Legends and DOTA 2, this title invites you to select from a diverse array of heroes, each offering unique abilities and playstyles. The ultimate objective is to vanquish the enemy's crystal, leveraging tactical prowess and strategic map control.

As you delve into battles, you'll earn Gold by dispatching foe minions and enemy heroes. This currency allows you to upgrade items and enhance your character's capabilities. The combination of gaining Experience points through combat rewards also enables your hero to level up, thereby increasing their strength. Using environmental elements like Runes and Brushes becomes essential as you master the art of outmaneuvering opponents and seizing victory.

The game, currently in its Pre-Alpha phase, boasts several match formats, including 1 v 1 ranked matches for a solo showdown and 2 v 2 ranked matches that emphasize collaboration and strategy. A Practice Match system is also available, letting you sharpen your skills and experiment with various item builds in a solo setting. Average match duration is around 15 minutes perfect for quick bouts of action!

Before the match begins, utilize the 45-second preparation time wisely. Position yourself strategically near the Gold Rune to maximize your income. Remember to purchase items strategically, saving some Gold for a Health Potion to sustain your presence in the lane. Buff Runes appear every minute after the match commences; those boosts can shift the balance of battle in your favor, especially during cooperative matches.

How to play free Kingdom of Pixels game online

To play Kingdom of Pixels, choose your hero and buy items during the preparation phase in your spawn area. Focus on destroying enemy towers to empower your minions, enabling them to serve as your primary method to damage the enemy crystal. Always keep an eye on Gold Runes and Buff Runes to gain an advantage!
