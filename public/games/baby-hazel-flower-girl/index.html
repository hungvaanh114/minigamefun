Baby Hazel Flower Girl

Get ready for an exciting adventure with Baby Hazel in "Baby Hazel Flower Girl"! Aunt Lisa's wedding is just around the corner, and Baby Hazel has been given the delightful honor of being the Flower Girl. Thrilled at the prospect, she sets out with her mom to find the perfect outfit for this special occasion. Every little detail matters as Baby Hazel wants to look absolutely stunning walking down the aisle.

Your task is to help Baby Hazel select the ideal dress, shoes, and accessories that will make her stand out. With so many options, you ll have to guide her in picking colors and styles that reflect her personality while ensuring she feels like a princess. But the fun doesn't stop there!

Next up is the makeover! Baby Hazel is ready for a transformation, so apply her makeup with care to enhance her natural beauty. Choose from various lipstick shades, blush colors, and eye makeup designs to make her shine radiantly on her aunt's big day. Once her makeup is perfectly finished, it's time to dress her in her stunning Flower Girl outfit!

With creativity and style, you can make Baby Hazel the most beautiful girl at the wedding. Show off your fashion sense and attention to detail as you prepare her for this unforgettable moment in "Baby Hazel Flower Girl".

How to play free Baby Hazel Flower Girl game online

To play the Baby Hazel Flower Girl game online, simply navigate through the different shopping and makeover sections. Use your mouse to interact with various clothing and accessory items. Click to dress Baby Hazel and apply her makeup, making sure to complete each task before moving on to the next. Enjoy the creative process!
