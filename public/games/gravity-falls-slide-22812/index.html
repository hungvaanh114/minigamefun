Gravity Falls Slide

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Gravity Falls, a seemingly ordinary town teeming with secrets and mysteries waiting to be uncovered. At first glance, it may appear to be a dull location, but beneath its facade lies an extraordinary and whimsical realm. The twins, Dipper and Mabel Pines, arrive in this captivating town for what they believe will be a tedious summer vacation. Little do they know that their lives are about to take a thrilling turn.

They have come to stay with their great-uncle Stan, the proprietor of the Wonder Cabin souvenir shop, a place that serves as the epicenter for their upcoming escapades. During their time in Gravity Falls, the twins befriend local characters like Zus and Wendy, who join them in a series of hair-raising adventures filled with the supernatural and the absurd. Together, they unravel the town's riddles, explore hidden mysteries, and face off against bizarre creatures, turning their once mundane holiday into an exhilarating journey.

Gravity Falls is not just about fun and games; it is a place where friendships are forged, and life lessons are learned amidst strange encounters and peculiar happenings. As they delve deeper into the secrets of Gravity Falls, Dipper and Mabel embrace their boldness and curiosity, ultimately transforming their summer into a magnificent tale filled with excitement and discovery.

How to play free Gravity Falls Slide game online

To play the Gravity Falls Slide game, simply click on the puzzle pieces on the screen to move them into the correct position. Your objective is to rearrange the pieces to form a complete image of Dipper and Mabel's exciting adventures. Use your mouse or finger (on mobile devices) to drag the pieces and solve the puzzle before time runs out!
