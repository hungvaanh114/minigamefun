Killer Touch

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to put your reflexes to the test in Killer Touch! This exhilarating game immerses you in a high-octane aerial battle where enemy planes viciously invade the skies. With nimble fingers and quick thinking, players must tap the screen to eliminate these foes before they can reach your position. Each enemy plane that comes flying toward you increases the tension and excitement, creating an electrifying experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat.

As the action intensifies, you'll need to maintain razor-sharp focus. Just one hit from an enemy plane spells game over, so every tap must count. Do you have the skills to defend the skies with your lightning-fast reflexes? The challenge lies in your ability to anticipate the enemy's movements and react swiftly, ensuring that not a single plane slips through your defenses. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Killer Touch promises hours of entertainment as you sharpen your shooting skills.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a newcomer looking for some fun, this game offers an addictive mix of challenge and excitement. Test your limits and prove that your touch can be deadly as you soar through the skies, shooting down waves of enemy planes in a race against time. Are you ready to emerge victorious and become the ultimate sky defender?

How to play free Killer Touch game online

To start playing Killer Touch, simply tap on the screen to fire at incoming enemy planes. Stay alert and react quickly to their movements. If an enemy plane reaches your position, you ll lose the game, so aim to shoot them down as efficiently as possible to achieve a high score. Enjoy!
