Police Auto Rickshaw Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of law enforcement in Police Auto Rickshaw Drive, an exhilarating game where you take on the role of a US police officer in a fast-paced chase against criminals. Get behind the wheel of a powerful police tuk tuk rickshaw and embark on a heart-pounding mission to track down gangsters trying to flee the city. In this nonstop action-packed game, your objective is straightforward: ensure that no wrongdoer escapes the boundaries of your town.

Jump into this highly engaging driving simulator where the excitement never ceases. Your duties as a police cop involve chasing down suspects, making daring arrests, and using your skills to shoot at grand city thefts. Navigate through bustling streets and employ your driving prowess to outmaneuver the criminals while adhering to city laws. Each chase presents unique challenges that test your reflexes and strategy, making every moment intense and exhilarating.

With stunning graphics and realistic gameplay, Police Auto Rickshaw Drive immerses players in a vibrant city filled with danger and excitement. As you race through traffic and navigate tight corners, remember that timing is crucial. The criminals rely on every opportunity they can find, but with quick thinking and agile driving, it s up to you to bring them to justice. Are you ready to enforce the law from behind the wheel of your turbo-charged police auto rickshaw?

How to play free Police Auto Rickshaw Drive game online

To play the game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to drive your police auto rickshaw. Steer to chase criminals, apply brakes to avoid collisions, and press the action button to arrest or shoot at fleeing thieves. Keep an eye on your map to track their locations and don t let them escape!
