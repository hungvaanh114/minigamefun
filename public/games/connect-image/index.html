Connect Image

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the Connect Image game, players encounter a fun and engaging puzzle experience that challenges their spatial awareness and problem-solving skills. The gameplay begins with various picture pieces located at the bottom of the screen, while a shadowed outline of the complete image waits at the center. The objective is to reconstruct the picture by accurately placing the puzzle pieces into their corresponding spots outlined by the shadow.

To dive into the action, simply select a piece from the bottom row. Once chosen, drag the piece towards the shadow and drop it in the area that matches the shape and color of that part of the picture. If the piece fits correctly, it will snap into place, indicating a successful placement. If it doesn't fit, feel free to try again with different pieces. This continues until you've successfully assembled the entire image. What sets this game apart from traditional puzzles is that each piece is part of the final image rather than just a random shape, providing a unique twist on a classic genre.

The game boasts a total of 60 enthralling and challenging levels, each designed to test your skills further and keep you engaged. Upon completion of a level, the next one loads automatically, showcasing a new picture to reconstruct and new challenges to overcome, ensuring that players remain entertained throughout their gameplay.

How to play free Connect image game online

To play the Connect Image game online, simply start the level and select a puzzle piece from the bottom. Drag it into the shadow and drop it in the appropriate spot. If it fits, it will lock into place; keep going until the entire image is complete! Enjoy the fun!
