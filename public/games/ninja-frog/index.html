Ninja Frog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ninja Frog is an engaging platform game that evokes nostalgia, reminiscent of the classic Super Mario. In this exciting adventure, players step into the webbed feet of a courageous ninja frog, navigating through a vibrant world filled with obstacles and challenges. The main objective is to control your agile frog as it leaps from platform to platform, moving swiftly and gracefully.

As you progress, you'll encounter numerous boxes that can be smashed for rewards, including delicious strawberries that serve as points. One of the key features of Ninja Frog is using your jumping abilities to defeat enemies. By tapping on foes lurking above, you can effectively dispatch them and clear your path. However, it s essential to stay alert and avoid harmful spikes, spinning blades, and various other hazards that can impede your journey.

The game boasts five uniquely designed and captivating levels, each presenting different challenges that test your reflexes and strategy. With a variety of environments and obstacles to navigate, players will find themselves immersed in a whimsical yet thrilling adventure. Challenge yourself to execute long-distance jumps and discover the hidden secrets within each level. Embark on this extraordinary journey with Ninja Frog and have a blast as you uncover the fun that awaits!

How to play free Ninja Frog game online

To play Ninja Frog, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your frog character. Tap the jump button to leap over obstacles and enemies. Smash the boxes to collect strawberries, and tap on enemies from above to defeat them. Keep an eye out for spikes and blades to ensure a safe journey as you complete the levels!
