Word Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Word Challenge is an exciting and engaging game that revolves around the fun of anagrams, appealing to both seasoned word game enthusiasts and those looking to expand their English vocabulary. This innovative game promises to enhance the popularity of the genre by offering unique challenges and a dynamic gameplay experience.

As you dive into this captivating world, you will find letters appearing at the bottom of a circular field. Your task is to skillfully combine these letters to form meaningful words. Successfully created words will be automatically moved to the designated free cells at the top of the screen. With each new level, the challenges become increasingly difficult, testing your linguistic skills and creativity.

Throughout your journey, you ll have the opportunity to earn points for every word you form, making it not only a test of knowledge but also a competition with yourself to improve your score. Should you find yourself stuck, fear not! The game offers helpful hints, represented by a lit light bulb icon in the lower left corner of the screen. Use these clues wisely to overcome obstacles and advance through the levels.

How to play free Word Challenge game online

To play Word Challenge, start by joining an online platform that hosts the game. Once in, letters will appear at the bottom of your screen. Drag or click on the letters to form words and make sure to place them in the cells at the top. Use the hint button when you need assistance!
