Minigolf

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dive into the exciting world of online Minigolf, a delightful game you can enjoy on any device, whether it's a computer, tablet, or smartphone. With 19 uniquely designed levels awaiting your skills, you have the opportunity to challenge yourself and refine your technique. The objective is clear: complete each hole while adhering to the minimum number of strokes to achieve a higher score.

Playing is effortless! Use your mouse for precise aiming and shooting on computers, or simply tap your finger on the screen for a seamless experience on touch devices. The intuitive controls ensure that players of all skill levels can jump right in and enjoy the fun. Each time you finish a level, your progress is saved, so if you need to take a break or leave the game, you can effortlessly pick up where you left off by loading your last game.

As you navigate through the levels, don t forget to push yourself hitting the fewest strokes possible not only aids in mastering the game but also boosts your status on the global leaderboard. Once you've completed the game, you can add your score to represent your skills among players worldwide. Challenge your friends or see where you stand against others, making every hole-in-one even more rewarding!

How to play free Minigolf game online

To play the Minigolf game, simply select your level and aim your shot using your mouse or touch screen. Adjust the power of your swing based on the distance to the hole. Once you re ready, release to take your shot. Complete all holes while keeping your strokes to a minimum, and enjoy tracking your progress!
