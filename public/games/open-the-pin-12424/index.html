Open the Pin

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the intriguing world of Open the Pin, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your logic and strategy skills. In this game, colorful balls are trapped inside a transparent container, held back by a series of gold pins. Your objective is to remove these pins in a specific order to allow the balls to fill the container completely.

As you navigate through the levels, you'll encounter various challenges that test your problem-solving abilities. Each level presents a unique arrangement of colored and gray balls. The gray balls serve a special purpose; by mixing them, you can transform them into vibrant colors, aiding in your mission to fill the container. The further you progress, the more complex the puzzles become, featuring additional elements that will keep you engaged and entertained.

The satisfaction of successfully completing a level lies in achieving a full container. Each time you remove the rods, you must strategize to ensure that no colored ball gets left behind. If the container is not filled to the brim, the level will remain unsolved. Prepare to immerse yourself in a series of increasingly challenging tasks that promise to delight and stimulate your mind. Experience the thrill of accomplishment as you master the art of pin removal and unlock new stages in Open the Pin.

How to play free Open the Pin game online

To play Open the Pin, start by selecting a level. Click on the gold pins to remove them one by one, ensuring the colored balls can roll into the container without obstruction. Be mindful of the gray balls and strategically mix them if necessary to maximize your chances of filling the container. Aim for 100% completion to advance to the next challenge!
