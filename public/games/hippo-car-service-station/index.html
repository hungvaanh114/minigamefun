Hippo Car Service Station

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of the Hippo Car Service Station, where your adorable little hippo is on a mission to help the car owners in her town. In this delightful game, you are greeted by a charming atmosphere as various animals arrive at Hippo's new service station to get their vehicles repaired and serviced. Each animal brings its own unique story and trouble, making every visit at the station a new adventure!

In today's special event, Uncle Hippo rolls into the station, looking for assistance with his muddy car. With you as the Giraffe shop assistant, your task is to help him get his car back in tip-top shape! Start by grabbing a water gun to wash away the mud that has accumulated on his vehicle. Once it shines bright and clean, it s time to dive into a comprehensive examination of the car to identify its issues.

As you inspect Uncle Hippo's car, you will find three main problems that need your attention: parts that require replacement, trash that must be cleared out, and a flat tire that needs fixing. Engaging in these fun challenges will not only enhance your problem-solving skills but also provide lots of entertaining moments. Are you ready to get your hands dirty and show off your mechanical expertise? Let s jump into the game and find out how to resolve Uncle Hippo's car issues!

How to play free Hippo Car Service Station game online

To play the game, simply click on the various tools and objects around the service station. Use the water gun to clean the car, identify the issues by clicking on problem areas, and follow the on-screen instructions to replace parts, clean up garbage, and fix the flat tire. Enjoy and have fun fixing cars!
