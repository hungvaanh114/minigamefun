Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pole Vault Jump 3D offers an exhilarating experience that captures the thrill of competitive pole vaulting. In this engaging sport simulation, players take on the role of skilled athletes who must master the intricate techniques required to launch themselves high into the air using a flexible pole. As competitors race toward the bar, they must time their run and pole placement perfectly, demonstrating both speed and agility. This dynamic game brings excitement not only through its high-stakes jumps but also through the realistic graphics and physics that create an immersive atmosphere.

The objective is to clear the set height by skillfully using the pole to propel oneself over the bar. With each successful jump, players can feel the rush of satisfaction as they progress to higher levels. The game requires concentration and precise control, as the slightest miscalculation can lead to disappointing results. The more you play, the better you can understand the mechanics, leading to impressive performances and high scores.

Pole Vault Jump 3D also allows for personalization and upgrade options, letting players enhance their equipment and skills as they advance. Whether you re a seasoned pro or a newcomer to the sport, the challenge of mastering the art of pole vaulting is always appealing.

How to play free Pole vault 3D game online

To play Pole Vault Jump 3D online, start by selecting your athlete and customizing their appearance if the option is available. When you're ready, click 'Start' to begin the game. Use the on-screen controls to run, plant your pole, and jump. Timing and angle are crucial for clearing the height successfully!
