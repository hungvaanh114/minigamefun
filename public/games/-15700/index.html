Welcome to Cookie Blast, a delightful and classic match-3 puzzle game that immerses you in a colorful world of candies! With its stunning HD graphics and spectacular visual effects, each level is a treat for your eyes and your brain. Get ready to embark on an exciting journey where you'll match vibrant candies to clear the board and score big points. The addictive gameplay will keep you engaged as you strategize to swap, match, and blast your way through various challenges.

Cookie Blast brings an array of fun-colored candies that add a whimsical touch to your gaming experience. Each level presents unique obstacles, enhancing the challenge and excitement. Your goal is to unlock all the levels and prove your puzzle prowess by achieving high scores and completing challenging objectives. With every move, you'll find yourself captivated by the satisfying combinations and dynamic animations that the game offers.

As you progress through Cookie Blast, you'll discover new mechanics and power-ups that will aid you in your quest to become a winner. Whether you're playing casually or aiming for the top of the leaderboard, this game caters to all skill levels and promises hours of enjoyment. So, gather your candies, and let s see how far you can go!

How to play free Cookie Blast game online

To play Cookie Blast, simply swap two adjacent candies to create a line of three or more of the same type. Matches will disappear, allowing new candies to fall into place. Keep matching to achieve your level's objectives and earn special power-ups for bigger blasts!
