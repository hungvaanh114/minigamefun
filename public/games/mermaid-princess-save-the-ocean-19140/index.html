Mermaid Princess Save the Ocean

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the enchanting world of Mermaid Princess Save The Ocean, you join Princess Ariel on an urgent quest to restore beauty to her underwater garden. Marine pollution has reached alarming levels, and as Ariel approaches her beloved sanctuary, she is met with a disheartening sight: the once vibrant garden is now suffocated by litter. This sad discovery dampens her spirits, but she is determined to make a difference.

As a player, you ll embark on an adventure alongside Ariel, collecting trash that has spoiled the marine landscape. Your primary mission is to help Ariel clean the ocean and her cherished garden, turning what was once an unhealthy environment back into a vibrant underwater paradise. With each piece of rubbish you gather, you ll not only uplift Ariel s mood but also contribute to the well-being of the ocean's ecosystem. As you tidy up, keep an eye out for treasures hidden among the debris which can be used to further enhance the garden.

Once the clean-up is complete, it s time for a delightful transformation! Help Ariel choose a new beautiful dress that reflects her royal essence and spirit of environmental activism. With your style choices, you can personalize her look as she prepares to celebrate the revitalization of her home. Join her for whimsical play and magical moments in her newfound oasis!

How to Play Free Mermaid Princess Save The Ocean Game Online

To play the game, simply use your mouse to click and drag the trash items into designated bins. Keep track of the timer as you clear each area. After cleaning, navigate to the dress-up section to customize Ariel's look. Enjoy exploring the vibrant ocean garden!
