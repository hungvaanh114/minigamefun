3D Funny Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of shooting in a whimsical universe with 3D Funny Shooter, the fantastic free-to-play online shooter game that promises non-stop fun! Prepare yourself for an exhilarating adventure where you ll be plunged into a colorful, engaging world brimming with excitement and challenges. Every moment in this game is designed to keep you entertained as you navigate through its visually stunning environments.

Your mission in 3D Funny Shooter is straightforward but packed with adrenaline: hunt down the sparkling crystals scattered across various levels! As you blast your way through obstacles and enemies, gathering these crystals will unlock the doors that lead to the mysterious temple. The unique blend of humor and action in the game makes it an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Whether you're playing solo or competing against friends, you ll find endless enjoyment and replayability in every session.

As you dive deeper into the game, expect to encounter a plethora of challenges that test your shooting skills and reflexes. With each level, the difficulty escalates, keeping you on your toes and engaged. Don't miss out on the fun; team up with friends for cooperative gameplay or take on opponents in exhilarating matches. The vibrant graphics and playful sound effects add to the game's charm, making it a delightful experience from start to finish!

How to play free 3D Funny Shooter game online

To begin playing, simply use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot at the crystals scattered throughout each level. Move your character using the WASD keys to navigate through the vibrant environments. Collect all the crystals to unlock the door leading to the temple and complete the level!
