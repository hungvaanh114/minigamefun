Fashion Girl Spa Day

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the fabulous world of Fashion Girl Spa Day! Step into the shoes of a salon manager and create a serene oasis for your clients. In this engaging game, you are in charge of helping girls unwind and revitalize themselves with a variety of beauty treatments. From squeezing out pesky acne to plucking excess eyebrows, every detail counts in this delightful spa experience.

At your salon, you will have the opportunity to cater to the unique needs of each customer. Pamper them with leg shaving, hair dyeing, and other luxurious services that will leave them feeling rejuvenated and beautiful. As you take on various tasks, you ll encounter different challenges that will test your skills in providing the ultimate spa experience. Your customers will leave the salon not just looking fabulous but feeling fantastic too!

The game is designed with colorful graphics and easy-to-use controls, ensuring an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Immerse yourself in the world of fashion and beauty, as you design your own unique spa routine for each girl. Get ready to unleash your creativity and transform the virtual salon into a trendy hotspot where girls come to indulge and relax.

How to play free Fashion Girl Spa Day game online

To start playing Fashion Girl Spa Day, simply select your client and choose from a variety of beauty treatments available in the menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to perform each procedure, from skin care to makeup application, and personalize the experience to match your client s preferences. Enjoy the process and watch as your spa evolves into a popular retreat!
