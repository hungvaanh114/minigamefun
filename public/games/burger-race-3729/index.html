Burger Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the delicious world of Burger Race, where your culinary skills are put to the test! In this engaging game, your mission is to select a variety of ingredients to create mouth-watering hamburgers. As you expertly assemble each burger, you earn money that will help you build a bridge to advance to the next thrilling stage.

Customization is a key feature of Burger Race. Players have the exciting opportunity to personalize their characters with over 30 vibrant colors. Dive into the colorful spectrum and express your unique style! As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new colors and other exciting features that allow you to enhance your character even further.

The more burgers you create and sell, the more coins you accumulate, paving the way for your success in this race against time and ingredients. The challenge lies not just in making the perfect burger, but also in strategizing how to reach the next level. How quickly can you build that bridge with the money you've earned?

With its fun mechanics, vibrant graphics, and engaging gameplay, Burger Race provides endless entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you're a casual gamer or a burger-building enthusiast, this game promises an exhilarating experience filled with culinary creativity and competition.

How to play free Burger Race game online

To play Burger Race, simply start by selecting your ingredients to assemble delicious hamburgers. Complete orders efficiently to earn coins. Use your earnings to build bridges and unlock more colors for your character as you move through the stages. Have fun and race against the clock!


Instructions

Move character using WASD or drag mouse to desired direction
