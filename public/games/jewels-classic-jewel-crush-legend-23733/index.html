How to play free Jewels Classic - Jewel Crush Legend game online

To play Jewels Classic - Jewel Crush Legend, simply swipe your finger to match three or more jewels of the same type. This will cause them to crush and disappear, earning you points. Keep an eye on the level objectives and try to complete them before running out of moves!
