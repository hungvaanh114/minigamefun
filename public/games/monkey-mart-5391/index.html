Monkey Mart

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Monkey Mart is an exciting and colorful game from TinyDobbins where you step into the shoes of an adorable monkey entrepreneur. Your mission is to successfully run your very own supermarket. Start by planting various fruits that will attract customers to your store. You'll need to keep the shelves stocked with delicious bananas, fresh corn, and a diverse range of products to meet your shoppers' needs.

As customers flock to your supermarket, you must ensure their satisfaction by managing the cashier desk efficiently. Every customer interaction is an opportunity to enhance their shopping experience, which is crucial for retaining loyal clients. With each completed sale, you'll accumulate resources that allow you to unlock new upgrades and expand your market operations.

As you progress through the game, you can enhance your monkey character's abilities, explore new workstations, and even bring in additional workers to improve efficiency. By strategically managing your resources and staff, you can create a thriving market that attracts a steady stream of customers. The idle management aspect of Monkey Mart means you can make progress even when you're not actively playing, making it a perfect fit for gamers of all ages.

How to play free Monkey Mart game online

To start playing Monkey Mart, visit the game platform of your choice and select the game. Use the mouse or touchscreen to plant fruits, stock shelves, and manage the checkout line. Pay attention to customer requests and keep your store running smoothly by making upgrades as you earn in-game currency.
