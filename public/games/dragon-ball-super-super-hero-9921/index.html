Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero is an exhilarating addition to the expansive Dragon Ball franchise, intricately intertwined with the popular trading card arcade game, Dragon Ball Heroes. Launched on November 11, 2010, in Japan, this game has captivated fans with its engaging gameplay and beloved characters. Notably, in 2016, Super Dragon Ball Heroes emerged, featuring significant updates that enhanced user experience through improved graphics and increased accessibility to various characters.

This action-packed RPG immerses players in stunning 3D visuals and animations, allowing them to experience a compelling storyline featuring a brand-new character designed by the legendary Akira Toriyama: the enigmatic Saiyan, Shallot. Players have the unique opportunity to join Shallot and a plethora of familiar Dragon Ball characters on a quest to restore his lost memories and ultimately save the world from impending doom. As players embark on this journey, they will encounter a range of challenges, fierce battles, and strategic gameplay that keeps them engaged and entertained.

With its captivating narrative and beautifully crafted visuals, Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero resonates with both long-time devotees of the series and newcomers alike. Whether you're strategizing in battles or relishing the rich lore, this game continues the legacy of Dragon Ball with freshness and excitement, inviting players to uncover the secrets of Shallot's past while reliving some of the most iconic moments from the franchise.

How to play free Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero game online

To play Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero online for free, start by selecting your character and entering the battle arena. Use the directional controls to move and the action buttons to perform attacks and special moves. Defeat your opponents to progress through levels and unlock new abilities for an enhanced gaming experience!
