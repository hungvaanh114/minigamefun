Sweet Baby Clean House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This is a cleaning game for girls. If you usually like to clean your room, our game is designed for you. From bedrooms and living rooms to kitchens and bathrooms, there are many different spaces to clean. Children will become familiar with different cleaning tools and learn about the beauty and differences between a clean room compared to a messy one.
