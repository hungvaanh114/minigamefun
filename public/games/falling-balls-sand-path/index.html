Falling Balls: Sand Path

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the fascinating world of the game, where balls and sand become part of an exciting adventure! Your task is to guide the balls through sand levels, destroying obstacles in their path. Use logic, ingenuity and precise calculation to clear the way, avoiding traps and overcoming difficult obstacles. Game features: -Many unique levels - from easy to real challenges. -Interactive physics - sand reacts to your every action. -Various obstacles - traps, moving platforms, blocks and much more. -Exciting gameplay - plan your actions to guide the balls to the finish line.

Instructions

Destroy sand Tap or swipe the screen to remove sand and create a path for the balls Avoid traps Carefully avoid spikes pits moving blocks and other obstacles Use physics Correctly calculate the trajectory of the falling balls
