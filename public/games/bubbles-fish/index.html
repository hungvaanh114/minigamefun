Bubbles Fish

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Bubbles Fish! Prepare yourself for an engaging bubble shooter adventure that will put your skills to the test. In this exciting game, your objective is to aim and release bubbles to form groups of three or more matching colors. With each successfully matched set, you'll clear bubbles from the screen and earn points.

As you dive deeper into the game, you'll need to strategize carefully to ensure you collect enough bubbles to meet the required quota. The challenge increases as you play, as you'll have to navigate the ever-encroaching borders of the screen that threaten to end your game. Don t let those bubbles reach the edge while you compete for the highest score possible! So grab your bubble shooter and immerse yourself in the beautiful underwater scenes filled with colorful bubbles and charming marine life. Good luck, and remember to have a blast!

How to play free Bubbles Fish game online

Playing Bubbles Fish is simple! Use your mouse or finger to aim at the bubbles on the screen. Tap or click to shoot a bubble from your launcher. Try to match three or more bubbles of the same color to pop them and score points. Keep track of your available shots and avoid letting bubbles touch the screen s edge!
