Yeti Sensation

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the fun-filled adventure of Yeti Sensation, where an embarrassed Yeti is on a quest to find his new home! As the mischievous Bigfoot roams about, he has left our furry friend in quite the predicament. Your mission? Guide the Yeti through a treacherous landscape filled with sly snowmen, hazardous traps, and various other obstacles that threaten his journey.

Put your reflexes to the test as you navigate the icy terrain, dodging enemies and pitfalls while collecting valuable items along the way. Discover the excitement of shopping in the supermarket, where you can purchase essential boosts that will help the Yeti gain speed and enhance his agility. With each level, the challenges will increase, and you ll need your wits about you to plan the best route to keep the Yeti on the move and far from danger.

With vibrant visuals, engaging gameplay, and quirky characters, Yeti Sensation offers hours of entertainment. Will you be able to help the Yeti escape the clutches of the snowmen and survive the wild environment? Lace up your snow boots and get ready for a whimsical race to safety!

How to play free Yeti Sensation game online

To play the game, simply use the arrow keys or swipe on your screen to maneuver the Yeti through obstacles. Jump over traps, slide under dangers, and collect power-ups as you run. Keep an eye out for the supermarket to purchase boosts that will help you escape further. Enjoy the race!
