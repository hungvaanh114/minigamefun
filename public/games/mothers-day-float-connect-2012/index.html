Mothers Day Float Connect

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the joy of celebrating Mother's Day with the engaging game, Mothers Day Float Connect. In this delightful adventure, you are tasked with linking pairs of matching Mother s Day items through a carefully crafted path. The catch? Your connection can only contain a maximum of two 90-degree angles, which means strategic thinking and planning are key to success.

Your goal is to clear the board by eliminating all pairs of identical Mother s Day items scattered throughout the beautifully designed levels. However, watch out for the dynamic tiles that float in various directions, including up, down, left, right, center, or even split. This adds an extra layer of challenge, requiring you to adapt your tactics as you progress through the game. With 27 challenging levels to conquer, you ll find plenty of opportunities to test your skills and quick thinking.

To earn extra rewards, make sure to complete each level before time runs out. Not only will you enjoy the excitement of connecting the items, but you will also be rewarded for your speed and efficiency. So gather your concentration and get ready to celebrate the essence of Mother's Day in this unique and colorful puzzle game!

How to play free Mothers Day Float Connect game online

To play Mothers Day Float Connect, start by selecting two identical items on the board. Ensure your connecting path does not exceed two 90-degree angles. Watch out for floating tiles that shift positions. Clear the board before the timer expires to earn bonus points and advance through the challenging levels!
