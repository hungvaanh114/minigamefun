Marie Prepares Treat

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Princess Marie has a passion for cooking, and with the holiday season just around the corner, she s set out to create a delightful surprise for her boyfriend. Dive into the culinary world with Marie as she takes you on an exciting journey of making sumptuous handmade chocolates. Get ready to explore the rich variety of chocolates: from decadent dark and creamy white to velvety milk chocolate. Personalize your chocolate creations by adding an assortment of nuts and exciting culinary decorations like colorful sprinkles, fresh fruit, and vibrant lollipops!

But the fun doesn t stop at chocolates! Next on the agenda is baking delicious cupcakes. Join Marie as she bakes these sweet treats to perfection, then lets your creativity shine by decorating them with luscious cream, fruity toppings, and delightful sprinkles. It's all about making these festive cupcakes a feast for the eyes as well as the taste buds!

Now that the sweets are ready, it s time for Marie to select the perfect festive dress that complements her culinary masterpiece. A refreshing cocktail will complete the holiday preparation, making it an unforgettable celebration. Join Marie in the kitchen and turn this festive season into a magical, delicious affair!

How to play free Marie Prepares Treat game online

In the game, follow the on-screen instructions to help Princess Marie prepare her holiday treats. Start by selecting the type of chocolate and ingredients for the chocolates, then move on to baking and decorating cupcakes. Use the mouse to interact and create a surprise that will impress her boyfriend!
