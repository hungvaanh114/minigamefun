Yoga Skill 3D is a relaxing yet engaging game that invites players to dive into the serene world of yoga. In this unique experience, you are tasked with guiding a young girl as she attempts to navigate through a challenging diamond wall. The objective is to help her assume the perfect poses to successfully pass through various gaps without getting stuck.

Your role is crucial, as you ll need to tap on specific red points on her body to adjust her stance. These adjustments are essential, as they determine whether she fits through the wall. Above her, a drawing on the top screen serves as a visual guide, showing you precisely what shape she needs to take for success. Once you feel the pose is perfect, you simply need to tap a button to confirm it. Your goal is to skillfully maneuver through the wall, avoiding any mishaps along the way.
