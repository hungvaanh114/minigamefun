How to play free Bubbles Shooter Squirrel game online

To play Bubbles Shooter Squirrel, simply aim your bubble shooter at the cluster of bubbles above. Click or tap to shoot and match three or more bubbles of the same color to pop them. Keep aiming and shooting until you free all the squirrels trapped within the bubbles!
