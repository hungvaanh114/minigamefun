How to play free Line Puzzle: Pipe Art game online

To play Line Puzzle: Pipe Art, simply tap on a pipe to rotate it. Connect all pipes of the same color to form a single line while ensuring that no two pipes intersect. Complete all levels by creating beautiful designs before the timer runs out, ensuring a fun-filled adventure awaits!
