Welcome to Factory Inc, an exhilarating arcade game where you step into the role of a factory operator. Your mission is simple yet thrilling: eliminate unqualified products using a powerful hydro press. As you tap, the machine comes alive, crushing the faulty items with satisfying force. This action not only keeps your factory running smoothly but also serves as a unique and enjoyable way to relieve stress throughout your day.

However, don't let your guard down! The factory is not without its hazards. You must remain alert and avoid dangerous elements such as bombs and modulator tubes that can emerge unexpectedly during the gameplay. Staying focused is key to maximizing your score and ensuring that your factory continues to produce quality products. With its engaging mechanics and addictive gameplay, Factory Inc keeps you on your toes, providing both fun and a great way to pass the time.

Whether you are looking to unwind or challenge your reflexes, Factory Inc delivers an enjoyable experience that will keep you coming back for more. So, gear up and get ready to crush your way through the factory, make the most out of your operational skills, and become a master factory operator!

How to play free Factory Inc game online

To play Factory Inc, simply tap on the screen to activate the hydro press and crush the unqualified products. Avoid obstacles like bombs and modulator tubes to maintain your streak. Keep your tapping rhythm steady and see how far you can go without making a mistake!
