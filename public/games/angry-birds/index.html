Angry Birds

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to unleash your competitive spirit in Angry Birds! In this thrilling game mode, the Star Cup, you'll face off against individual opponents as you take on the challenge of mastering your slingshot skills. Show off your prowess by outsmarting your friends and climbing the ranks on global leaderboards, all while having a blast with your favorite feathered friends.

As you embark on this exciting journey, prepare to strategize and plan each shot to topple the dastardly pigs. Every level is packed with obstacles and puzzles that require precise aiming, so hone your skills and unleash the full potential of your bird against your rivals. The more skilled you become, the higher you'll soar in the rankings, claiming your spot as the ultimate Angry Birds player in the world.

Rally your friends and engage in fierce competitions as you all vie for the top position. Display your achievements and bask in the glory of victory as you share your progress with others in this vibrant online community. Challenge yourself to break records and smash through tough levels to demonstrate your mastery over the art of Angry Birds. Are you ready to launch your way to the top?

How to play free Angry Birds game online

To play Angry Birds online, simply select your bird, aim the slingshot by dragging back to adjust power and angle, then release to launch your bird at the pigs. Your objective is to knock them down using limited bird types. Keep an eye on your remaining birds and strategize for maximum destruction!
