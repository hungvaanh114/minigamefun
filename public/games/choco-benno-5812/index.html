Choco Benno

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Choco Benno, a charming 2D platformer that promises a fun-filled adventure! In this game, players take on the role of the adorable Choco Benno, whose main quest is to gather all the glasses of chocolate milkshake scattered throughout the levels. Each glass you collect brings you one step closer to reaching the coveted Chocolate exit door that leads to the next level.

As you traverse through the vibrant and engaging environments, you'll encounter various challenges. Beware of the cunning chocolate eater monsters that lurk around, ready to thwart your progress! The levels are intricately designed with obstacles like sharp spikes and enormous blades that will test your skills and reflexes. With a total of 8 captivating levels, the game ramps up in difficulty as you advance, ensuring that you stay on your toes!

Choco Benno is not only about gathering treats but also about exploring strategically while avoiding dangers. The combination of delightful graphics and engaging gameplay makes this platformer a must-try for fans of the genre. Perfect for players of all ages, Choco Benno invites you to dive into a captivating journey filled with sweet surprises!

How to play free Choco Benno game online

To play Choco Benno, use the arrow keys to navigate and jump around the platforms. Collect all the chocolate milkshake glasses in each level while dodging monsters and hazards. Reach the exit door to complete the level, and brace yourself for even tougher challenges ahead!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
