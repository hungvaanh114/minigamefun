Euro 2020 ( 2021 )

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the excitement of Euro 2020 like never before in this thrilling free kick edition, where you can select from a pool of ten top-tier football teams. Tested against some of the best competitors in Europe, you'll guide your chosen squad as they aim for championship success. With realistic graphics and engaging gameplay, you can immerse yourself in the intensity of penalty shootouts and showcase your skills on the pitch. Whether you favor the technical prowess of Germany, the strategic finesse of France, or the captivating flair of Italy, there's a team for every fan.

As you progress through the levels, the stakes rise, and you'll face increasingly challenging situations. Score goals against formidable opponents by executing perfect free kicks, and watch as your team's morale and confidence soar. The joy of scoring gives you a sense of achievement, while every miss builds a tension that mirrors real-life matches. The game combines fast-paced action with strategic decision-making, ensuring that you keep coming back for more. Establish your legacy in European soccer by leading your squad all the way to raising the trophy in glory!

How to play free Euro 2020 (2021) game online

To play the Euro 2020 free kick edition online, simply select your preferred team from the main menu. Use your mouse or keyboard to aim your shots and adjust power. Timing is key, so watch for the goalie's movement and take your free kick when you feel ready. Enjoy the thrill of the game!
