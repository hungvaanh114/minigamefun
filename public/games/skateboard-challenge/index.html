Skateboard Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Skateboard Challenge" is an exhilarating extreme sports game that promises endless fun and excitement! In this vibrant world of skateboarding, your primary goal is to gather all the letters needed to spell "S-K-A-T-E" and "C-O-M-B-O". Each level presents unique challenges, and you'll need to strategize to achieve the required target score, which escalates with every level you conquer.

As you navigate through the game, you can execute a range of extreme maneuvers that showcase your skating skills. Utilizing rails creatively allows you to combine tricks, boosting your score multiplier significantly. This not only enhances your points for each combination you perform but also adds an element of strategy as you plan your moves. The more daring your maneuvers, the higher your score will climb!

Collecting coins is another essential aspect of the game, as these coins enable you to unlock a variety of characters. Each character comes with distinct styles, adding diversity to your gameplay. Challenge your friends to beat your scores and show off your tricks, making Skateboard Challenge a perfect game for competitive play. Get ready to have a blast while demonstrating your skating prowess!

How to play free Skateboard Challenge game online

To play "Skateboard Challenge", use the arrow keys to control your skateboarder and perform tricks. Collect letters to spell "SKATE" and "COMBO," while aiming for the target score in each level. Execute combos on rails for bonus points and gather coins to unlock new characters. Enjoy the ride!
