Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Fruits Blocks Collapse, a delightful puzzle game where you embark on a fruity adventure! Your main objective is to collect fruit slices by tapping or clicking on them in groups. The more slices you gather at once, the more points you earn. Form larger groups to rack up your score, but beware! Picking a single slice will cost you 200 points. Keep an eye on the Projection box, which shows the value of each collection.

As you play, aim to collect at least two slices in a turn to score points. If you're skilled enough to gather more than seven slices simultaneously, you will unlock powerful bonuses such as Bombs, Arrows, or Magic Slices. These special items can help you clear fruits faster and achieve your targets more efficiently.

Each level presents a new challenge where you'll need to reach or exceed the target score to progress. Clearing all the fruit slices from the board will take you to the next exciting stage, filled with fresh challenges and more fruity fun! With its colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, Fruits Blocks Collapse promises an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Gather your fruit, boost your score, and enjoy the journey in this thrilling puzzle game!

How to play free Fruits Blocks Collapse game online

To play the game, simply click or tap on groups of two or more adjacent fruit slices to collect them. Watch the Projection box to gauge your score. Aim for larger groups to access power-ups and clear the board to move through the levels. Enjoy the fruity fun and challenge your skills!
