How to play free Solitaire TriPeaks Garden game online

To play Solitaire TriPeaks Garden, simply select a card from the board that is one rank higher or lower than the card in your stack. Continue clearing cards to earn points and grow your flower collection. Utilize power-ups wisely to tackle tricky levels and enjoy the soothing gameplay!
