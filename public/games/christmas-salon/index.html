Christmas Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of the Christmas Salon, a delightful game that lets you unleash your creativity this winter season! Help adorable animals and a charming Santa Claus whip up stylish looks just in time for the holidays. From designing eye-catching hairstyles and fluffing up furry coats to crafting an impeccable beard for Santa, the joy of creation knows no bounds!

In the Christmas Salon, you ll find a plethora of fun activities waiting for you. Dress up the cutest of critters in fashionable winter apparel and prepare a delectable array of cookies and frozen ice cream that will tantalize taste buds. Your artistic flair will shine as you decorate a beautiful Christmas tree, selecting the perfect ornaments and lights to make it sparkle. With each task, you ll contribute to the holiday spirit and bring joy to the furry friends and Santa alike!

Gear up for a winter makeover adventure filled with joy, creativity, and festive cheer that you can enjoy with friends and family. Christmas Salon promises hours of fun, whether you re a budding stylist or simply looking to enjoy the festive season through play. Add a touch of magic to this holiday season by diving into this interactive experience!

How to play free Christmas Salon game online

To dive into the action in the Christmas Salon game, start by selecting your character, whether it's Santa or one of the adorable animals. Use your mouse to navigate through the various design options, including hair, fur, and outfits. Simply click on your desired items and drag them onto your character. Enjoy the festive makeover!
