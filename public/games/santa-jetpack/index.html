SANTA JETPACK

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of flying with Santa in Santa Jetpack, a fun and festive adventure that puts a unique twist on holiday cheer! Picture this: Santa, dressed in your business suit and equipped with a cutting-edge jetpack prototype, takes to the skies in a race against time. Soar high above the runway while collecting exciting bonuses that will help you on your journey. But watch out! The skies are filled with pesky obstacles that can hinder your flight. Navigate through these challenges and see how far you can go!

As you float through the air, you ll need to demonstrate your agility and quick reflexes. The more bonuses you collect, the better your score will be, adding a competitive edge to this joyous jaunt. It s not just a game; it s a test of skill and strategy! Perfect for players of all ages, Santa Jetpack offers a delightful experience that combines festive elements with exhilarating gameplay. Fly high and spread holiday joy as you guide Santa on his airborne escapade!

How to play free SANTA JETPACK game online

To play Santa Jetpack, simply use the arrow keys or touch controls to navigate Santa through the skies. Collect bonuses to increase your score while avoiding various obstacles to keep your jetpack flying! The goal is to fly as far as you can without crashing. Happy flying!
