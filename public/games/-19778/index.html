Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Robot Mania, where chirpy machines have seized control of your living space! In this action-packed game, your mission is clear: eradicate the pesky robots that have intruded into your home. Armed with a range of futuristic weaponry, you ll need to strategize and utilize your skills to reclaim your territory from these metallic marauders. Navigate through each room, making tactical decisions that will lead you closer to your ultimate goal of destroying all invading robots.

As you progress, you'll encounter different types of robots, each with unique abilities and strengths. From nimble hover bots to heavy-duty enforcers, adaptability is key. Collect power-ups and upgrades hidden throughout the house to enhance your arsenal, ensuring that you re always prepared for the next wave of robot attackers. The immersive graphics and captivating sound design will make you feel like you re truly battling for your home s freedom!

At its heart, Robot Mania combines fast-paced gaming with strategic planning, ensuring that you re always on your toes. With an engaging storyline and increasingly challenging levels, you ll find yourself lost in this robotic mayhem, eager to advance to the next challenge. Are you ready to take back your home and show these robots who's boss? Jump into the fray and destroy them before it s too late!

How to play free Robot Mania game online

To play Robot Mania, simply use your mouse or keyboard to navigate through the rooms. Aim with the cursor, click to shoot, and utilize power-ups as they appear. Keep an eye on your health meter, and be prepared to dodge attacks as you eliminate the robotic foes to reclaim your home!
