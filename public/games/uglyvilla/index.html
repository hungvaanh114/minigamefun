UglyVilla

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to UglyVilla, a whimsical world inhabited by charmingly unattractive toys! In this peculiar abode, the quirky little figures find themselves in a bit of a predicament. The toys urgently need to be placed in the box, and it s up to you to assist them on their wacky adventure. Among the colorful cast of characters are the green and red ugly toys, who must grab the elusive key to unlock their way to safety. Your mission is clear: help these delightful misfits reach the box before time runs out!

The journey through UglyVilla is filled with challenges and obstacles that will test your agility and quick thinking. As you navigate through this vibrant landscape, don t forget to collect the precious buttons scattered throughout the environment. These buttons are essential for the toys' survival and serve as points to enhance your performance throughout the game. But remember, this is no ordinary task while collecting buttons, you must remain focused on guiding the ugly toys to their rightful destination!

With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, UglyVilla promises an exciting experience that will keep you on your toes. Can you successfully lead the ugly toys to the box while amassing a collection of buttons? Dive into this unique adventure and see if you have what it takes to rescue the toys in need!

How to play free UglyVilla game online

To play UglyVilla, simply use your mouse or keyboard to navigate the green and red ugly toys through the environment. Collect buttons along the way while steering them towards the box. Be mindful of obstacles and keep an eye on the timer. Good luck!
