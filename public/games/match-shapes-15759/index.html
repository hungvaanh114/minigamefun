Match Shapes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Match Shapes, an exciting and brain-teasing puzzle game where your objective is to slide blocks of various shapes to find their matches. The game challenges players to engage their strategic thinking skills as they maneuver the shape blocks across the board. Each move counts, so you'll need to plan carefully and reduce unnecessary slides to maximize your score. When you successfully match two blocks of the same shape, both will be eliminated, rewarding you with 100 points!

However, be cautious! If you slide a block more than once before making a match, you will incur a penalty of 10 points for each additional slide. This incentivizes players to think critically and act efficiently, promoting a challenge that will keep them engaged for hours. With 24 meticulously designed levels, each boasting its own unique challenges, players will find plenty of opportunities to hone their skills and outsmart the game.

Are you ready to tackle the addictive gameplay of Match Shapes? Test your abilities, sharpen your mind, and complete all levels to claim victory. The colorful blocks and intuitive mechanics create a visually appealing and user-friendly experience, perfect for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages. Let's dive into the world of Match Shapes and see how quickly you can eliminate all the blocks!

How to play free Match Shapes game online

To play Match Shapes, simply click and drag the blocks to slide them across the grid. Aim to align blocks with identical shapes and eliminate them. Plan your moves wisely to avoid unnecessary slides and maximize your score. Keep an eye on the points as you progress through each level and enjoy the challenge!
