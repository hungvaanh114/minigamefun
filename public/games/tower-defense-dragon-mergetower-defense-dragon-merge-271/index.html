Tower Defense: Dragon Merge invites you to engage in an exhilarating battle where strategy meets chaos, ensuring you savor hours of unique and gripping gameplay. In this thrilling adventure, you'll need to devise clever tactics to protect your fortress from relentless waves of invading forces. The fusion of dragons adds an innovative twist, allowing you to create powerful allies that can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Prepare your defenses by visiting the in-game shop, where you can spin for valuable PowerUps. As you progress and level up, your arsenal expands, granting you access to various tools and upgrades that enhance your combat capabilities. Should the enemy onslaught become overwhelming, take advantage of the mighty cannon feature by mashing the button to unleash a barrage of firepower and hopefully fend off the attackers.

The key to survival lies in reinforcing your castle walls and fortifying your defenses. Continual upgrades are essential for maintaining a fighting chance against increasingly formidable foes. Establish a robust strategy that balances offense and defense to thwart the invaders and secure your domain. Remember, every decision counts in this intense battlefield as you create a legendary defense against the chaos.

How to play free Tower Defense: Dragon Merge game online

To play Tower Defense: Dragon Merge, utilize your mouse or touchscreen to control your defenses. Set up towers strategically to fight against incoming waves of enemies. Access the shop to purchase power-ups and upgrade your defenses. Focus on merging dragons for enhanced capabilities and manage resources effectively to secure victory.
