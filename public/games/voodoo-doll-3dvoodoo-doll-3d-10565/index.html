Voodoo Doll 3D is an engaging clicker game that offers a uniquely twisted experience where you take out your frustrations by torturing a virtual doll. Immerse yourself in a colorful environment where you can unleash a barrage of punches, stabs, and burns on this unfortunate doll. Each action counts as you aim to build a higher score, unlocking various weapons and tools along the way. The game features an array of creative torture methods such as fire and ice, adding an inventive flair to the gameplay.

As you start your journey with Voodoo Doll 3D, your primary objective is to navigate through different levels of torment using your mouse or touchscreen. With every successful attack, you ll earn points that help you progress in the game. The more you hit the doll, the more weapons you can unlock to amplify the fun and chaos. A range of outfits for the doll rewards you for your persistence, so be sure to keep pushing your score higher to enjoy all the game has to offer.

Whether you're looking to release some steam or just enjoy a quirky gaming experience, Voodoo Doll 3D is sure to captivate your attention. It's bright visuals and addictive clicker mechanics keep players returning for just one more session of doll torment. Good luck as you embark on this peculiar adventure!

How to play free Voodoo Doll 3D game online

To play Voodoo Doll 3D, click on the doll to strike it, using various weapons you unlock. Each click increases your score, allowing you to unleash more tools like fire and freeze. Continuously hit the doll to earn points and unlock new outfits for a more entertaining experience.
