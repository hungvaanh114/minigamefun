Instructions:

In QUICK SCRAP, you take on the role of an intrepid scrap collector navigating the remnants of an abandoned mine in outer space. As you delve into the depths of this eerie environment, you'll encounter an array of challenges, primarily the alien inhabitants that now call this place home. Your objective is clear: gather as much scrap as possible while evading these extraterrestrial threats. The mine's four descending levels each present their own unique obstacles and opportunities for resource collection.

As you explore the abandoned mine, you'll need to be quick on your feet and strategic in your movements. The aliens are not just mindless creatures; they are intelligent and will try to thwart your plans to scavenge. With every level below, the challenge intensifies, requiring careful planning and swift reactions to navigate your way through perilous environments filled with valuable scraps waiting to be collected. Only by avoiding capture and maximizing your haul can you finally reach the exit portal, marking your successful escape from this treacherous space mining adventure.

QUICK SCRAP offers an engaging experience for players who enjoy exploration and strategy. Gather resources, avoid dangers, and prove your skills as the ultimate space scrap collector. Get ready for an exhilarating ride through the cosmos!

How to play free QUICK SCRAP game online

To play QUICK SCRAP, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character through the mine. Collect scrap items by moving over them while making sure to steer clear of the aliens patrolling the levels. Keep an eye on the exit portal to know where you re headed and strategize your route to maximize your scrap collection.
