Chernobyl Zombie Hell

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the aftermath of the Chernobyl disaster on April 26, 1986, a ravaged wasteland emerges, wrought by the catastrophic explosion at the reactor. This desolate region is now a haunting ground, plagued by mutated creatures that have fallen victim to the unrelenting radioactivity. You are thrust into the role of a cleaner, tasked with the perilous job of venturing into the heart of this cursed territory to eliminate the lurking horrors.

Every week, teams of cleaners are deployed to the area to fend off the monstrous remnants of nature, ensuring that the sounds of chaos remain suppressed. Equipped with an arsenal of weapons and tools at your disposal, your mission begins at the local hospital, where the shadows of the past cling to the walls. As you navigate through the eerie streets of Chernobyl, a sinister atmosphere envelopes you, with zombies, mutated dogs, and ravenous rats waiting to spring forth from the darkness.

Your journey will take you deeper into the gritty underbelly of the reactor, winding down through the treacherous tunnels where danger abounds. With stunning high-quality graphics and meticulously balanced gameplay, every encounter is a reminder of the lurking terror that resides within these contaminated grounds. Prepare yourself for spine-chilling visuals and intense combat as you strive to clear the area of its grotesque inhabitants, ensuring a safer environment for future cleansers.

How to play free Chernobyl Zombie Hell game online

To play Chernobyl Zombie Hell, begin by selecting your character and loadout, then enter the game. Use the WASD keys for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot at enemies. Collect weapons and resources as you progress, and stay alert for ambushes from the undead lurking in the shadows.
