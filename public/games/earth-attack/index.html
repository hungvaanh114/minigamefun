Earth Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The fate of the Earth hangs in the balance as a coalition of malevolent planets invades the Solar System, threatening destruction with each passing moment. However, our planet is not going down without a fight! In a thrilling twist of events, the Earth retaliates, supported by the Moon in its bold campaign to defend their home. As players, you ll have the opportunity to arm yourselves with an array of powerful weapons, evolving them as you progress to enhance their capabilities in this fierce battle for survival.

Earth Attack is an exhilarating gaming experience, offering a journey through 10 action-packed levels filled with an assortment of enemies just waiting to be defeated. Each level presents new challenges that will test your skills and strategy as you navigate through detailed environments, complete with stunning effects, immersive sounds, and dynamic animations. Along the way, you can upgrade your speed and weaponry, making every fight more intense and engaging.

Gear up for a heart-pounding adventure where quick reflexes and clever tactics will be essential for outsmarting foes and ultimately destroying the invading planets. The exciting storyline culminates with an exhilarating conclusion that will leave players on the edge of their seats, eager for more. Join the battle and prove that Earth can defend itself!

How to play free Earth Attack game online

To play the Earth Attack game, simply use the arrow keys to navigate your character through the galaxy. Press the spacebar to fire your weapons at incoming enemies. Collect power-ups to evolve your abilities, boost your speed, and upgrade your armaments as you progress through each challenging level. Good luck!
