Bubble Shooter Classic Pop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Bubble Shooter - Classic Pop, an addictive bubble-shooting game! Use your precision and quick thinking to match bubbles and clear the board. This game combines reflection and puzzle-solving, making it suitable for all ages. Drag to aim and release to shoot! Match three or more bubbles of the same color to pop them. Master the art of bouncing bubbles off walls to reach tricky spots. Every clever shot brings immense satisfaction!
