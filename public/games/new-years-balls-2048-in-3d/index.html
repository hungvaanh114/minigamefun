New Years Balls 2048 in 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

A game with a New Years atmosphere! Compete with friends and colleagues! In the game you have a game zone, where balls fall and connect if they have the same color! Connect as many balls as possible to get the biggest one, collect points! Become the first on the leaderboard! Play and relax!

Instructions

1 At first it will be easy to connect the balls but over time there will be more and more of them Once you connect the biggest ball in this game it will disappear and give you the most points
