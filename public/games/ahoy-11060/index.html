Ahoy!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ahoy! Gear up and set sail on an adventurous journey where the vast ocean beckons you to construct the grandest raft ever seen! Dive into the spirit of exploration as you gather resources from the sea and islands to build a sturdy vessel. With just a few essentials under your belt, your crafting skills will be put to the test while you aim to create a mighty floating fortress that can brave the elements.

Your voyage begins with nothing but a small raft and your determination. As you paddle through the shimmering waters, you ll come across various materials floating by. Collect wood, ropes, and other essential items to expand your floating home. Decorate your raft with innovative designs and ensure that it reflects your unique flair while also bolstering its durability against unpredictable storms and waves.

Engage with other players, trade resources, and explore the mystery-filled islands that are scattered across the game world. Each island presents new challenges and treasures waiting to be uncovered. Teamwork can be the key to survival, as you gather forces with friends to conquer obstacles and enhance your raiding skills. Remember, in the heart of the ocean, every sailor's dream is to rise above the waves and claim their place as the ultimate raft builder!

How to play free Ahoy! game online

To start your journey in Ahoy!, use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate your raft. Collect materials by clicking on them, and then access your crafting menu to design and upgrade your raft. Interact with other players for trading and explore islands to uncover valuable resources!
