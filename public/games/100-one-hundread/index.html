If you have an affinity for numbers, a passion for alchemy, and a knack for dealing with relatively harmless creatures, then 100: One Hundred is the ideal game for you! Step into your private tower, where you will gather your cauldron, vials, and flasks to mix together some truly bizarre brews.

In this puzzle-based adventure, you'll encounter an array of adorable little creatures, each marked with a percentage. Your goal is to group these creatures strategically to reconstruct 100% cubes. The mechanics are simple yet engaging slide the different creatures around, both horizontally and vertically, to combine their percentages. As you successfully create a 100% cube, it will freeze in place, marking your achievement and preventing any further moves.

As you navigate through the game's sixty levels, you'll find challenges becoming increasingly intricate. Your task is to assemble all the cubes to advance through each stage while testing your critical thinking skills. Gather the small creatures, concoct thrilling potions, and establish your dominance as a master alchemist. Can you solve every puzzle and claim the title of the ultimate alchemist?

How to play free 100: One Hundread game online

To play the game, use your mouse or finger to drag the creatures into position. Combine them to create 100% cubes by aligning their percentages. Once a cube reaches 100%, it will freeze, so plan your moves carefully to clear all the levels.
