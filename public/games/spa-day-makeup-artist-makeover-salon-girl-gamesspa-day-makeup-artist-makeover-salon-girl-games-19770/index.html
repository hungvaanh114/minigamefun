Hey girls! Step into the world of relaxation and beauty with Spa Day Makeup Artist: Makeover Salon Girl Games! This captivating game invites you to run your very own beauty salon, where your creativity and care will help rejuvenate your clients. With six unique services at your fingertips, you can customize each experience to meet the specific needs of every girl who walks through your doors.

Your journey begins with a soothing facial SPA treatment. Use a variety of tools to wash your client s face, shape her eyebrows, and clear away any blemishes. Once her complexion is fresh and glowing, you can move on to create a nourishing DIY hair mask. Utilize realistic hair spa tools to revitalize her tresses, ensuring she looks fabulous from head to toe.

Don't forget about accessories! Choose from an array of stylish options that will perfectly compliment her overall look. After pampering her visage and mane, switch gears and provide a relaxing back SPA massage. Wax her back for a smooth finish and place massage stones to melt away her worries. Your clients will leave feeling renewed and stress-free, showcasing the ultimate spa experience that only you can provide!

How to play free Spa Day Makeup Artist: Makeover Salon Girl Games game online

To play the game, simply click on the various tools and options available in the salon interface. Follow the on-screen instructions to provide each service - from facials to massages. Use your mouse or finger to interact with the objects, ensuring your client receives the ultimate spa treatment. Enjoy creating beautiful makeovers!
