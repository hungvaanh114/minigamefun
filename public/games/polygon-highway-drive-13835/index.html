Polygon Highway Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Polygon Highway Drive invites players to immerse themselves in an exhilarating racing experience where the goal is to navigate through bustling traffic, dodge trains, and collect a diverse array of vehicles. This action-packed game emphasizes fun and adventure as players gather coins, unlock reward boxes, and build impressive collections to acquire new vehicles and other exciting prizes!

As you speed down the highway, pushing your vehicle to the limits, your primary objective is to score big points and become the ultimate champion. Prepare for a thrilling crash time! In the event of an accident, take control of your vehicle and smash into passing cars for bonus points. The game showcases stunning voxel art visuals, enhancing the overall gaming experience.

Polygon Highway Drive offers four distinct worlds for players to explore, boasting an extensive garage that features 55 unique vehicles to choose from, including taxis, tanks, UFOs, police cars, army 4x4s, dragsters, monsters, and even motorcycles and boats. The game introduces a variety of challenges, including 11 vehicle collections and three engaging game modes, perfect for players of all ages. With map themes ranging from deserts and snowy landscapes to lush greenery and water settings, excitement awaits at every turn!

How to play free Polygon Highway Drive game online

To start playing Polygon Highway Drive, simply choose your vehicle and select a game mode. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car through the traffic, collecting coins while avoiding obstacles. Aim for high scores by executing daring maneuvers and completing missions as you race towards victory!
