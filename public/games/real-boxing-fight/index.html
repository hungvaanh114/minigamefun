Real Boxing Fight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Real Boxing Fight, an exciting two-player fighting game that invites you to test your boxing skills against either a computer opponent or a friend. With its dynamic gameplay and realistic graphics, this game immerses you into the ring where every punch counts.

Real Boxing Fight offers players three distinct difficulty levels: easy, normal, and hard. This allows both beginners and advanced players to find an appropriate challenge. As you step into the shoes of a boxer, your mission is simple yet thrilling: defeat your opponent to claim victory in the match. Employ your strategy, dodge incoming strikes, and unleash a flurry of punches to secure your win. Each match is packed with intensity and excitement, keeping you on your toes!

Whether you're looking for a quick match against the AI or want to engage in an intense showdown with a friend, Real Boxing Fight has you covered. The intuitive controls and responsive gameplay ensure that both players can enjoy a seamless experience. So gear up, get ready, and remember that every fighter has their own unique style embrace yours and climb the ranks!

How to play free Real Boxing Fight game online

To play Real Boxing Fight, simply choose your mode: Player vs. PC or Player vs. Player. Use the arrow keys to move around the ring and the designated punch buttons to attack. Dodge and weave to avoid your opponent's hits while trying to land your own. Aim for the knockout and enjoy the thrill of the fight!


Instructions

Use the keyboard to play this game Player 1 A S D W to walk B N M for an attack space bar for defense Player 2 arrow keys to walk 1 2 3 for an attack 0 for defense
