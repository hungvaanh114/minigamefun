Real Rickshaw Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get behind the wheel of an auto rickshaw and experience the thrill of the streets in Real Rickshaw Drive. This unique rickshaw driving game takes you on a fun-filled adventure, allowing you to explore the city while managing your own transportation service. Whether you're navigating busy traffic or picking up passengers, the excitement is never-ending!

Step into the shoes of a rickshaw driver and embark on a journey to provide pick-and-drop services to various passengers. With realistic driving mechanics and stunning graphics, this auto rickshaw simulator offers a captivating experience that all fans of tuk-tuk games will enjoy. You'll need to pick up passengers from designated locations and drop them off at their desired destinations, all while adhering to traffic rules and avoiding obstacles along the way.

The game s immersive environment is designed to keep your adrenaline pumping as you tackle varying weather conditions and road scenarios. Keep your rickshaw in tip-top shape and earn money for every successful ride to upgrade your vehicle and enhance your driving skills. It's not just a game; it's a test of your driving prowess!

How to play free Real Rickshaw Drive game online

To play the Real Rickshaw Drive game online, simply use the on-screen controls to steer your rickshaw and accelerate towards your passenger's location. Once you reach the pick-up point, stop and let the passenger in. Then, follow the map to drop them off safely, avoiding obstacles and managing your speed. Enjoy your drive!
