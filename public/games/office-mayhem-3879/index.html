Office Mayhem

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Office Mayhem is an exhilarating online game that places you in the role of a soldier tasked with dismantling a corrupt office environment. Clad in combat attire, your objective is clear: eliminate the unscrupulous office workers who are spreading chaos and disorder within the workplace. Throughout your journey, you will traverse multiple levels, each packed with unique challenges and obstacles that test your skills and strategy.

Your path will be fraught with locked doors, prying security cameras, and various environmental hazards that you must cleverly navigate. Each level presents a new set of enemies, all equipped with their own weaponry and tactics to thwart your mission. As you progress, you'll need to employ stealth, speed, and cunning to outsmart your adversaries and successfully complete your objectives.

In Office Mayhem, every corner could hide a threat, and each decision could mean the difference between success and failure. Mastering movement and learning to utilize the environment to your advantage will be key to defeating those office troublemakers and restoring order. Are you ready to answer the call and bring peace back to the chaotic office? Dive into the fray and show them what a soldier can do! With engaging gameplay and a humorous take on office life, this game promises hours of entertainment.

How to play free Office Mayhem game online

To play Office Mayhem, use the arrow keys or W/A/S/D to move your soldier around the office. Aim with your mouse and click to take out corrupted workers. Keep an eye out for obstacles and security cameras; use your skills and stealth to effectively navigate through levels, ensuring you accomplish your mission without getting caught.


Instructions

WASD - Move LMB - Shoot RMB - Aim Mouse Wheel - Change weapon R - Reload Space - Jump
