Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to My Mini Car Service, the ultimate game designed for automotive aficionados! Dive into the thrilling world of car maintenance where you will take on the role of a garage owner. Your mission is to manage and nurture your very own car repair shop. From diagnosing issues in engines to applying stunning paint jobs, the game offers a plethora of activities for you to enjoy.

In My Mini Car Service, every vehicle that rolls into your garage comes with its own set of problems and customization requests. You ll need to put your skills to the test as you repair engines, fix brakes, and upgrade components. The game provides a wide range of modification options that allow you to tailor each car to reflect your unique style. Enhance performances, add sporty features, and ensure that every vehicle leaves your garage in top-notch condition.

As you progress, you ll earn resources that enable you to expand your garage and unlock new tools and parts. The more you work on cars, the better your reputation will become, attracting a larger clientele eager for your expertise. With a user-friendly interface and engaging gameplay, My Mini Car Service promises a truly immersive experience for anyone who dreams of managing a successful auto shop.

How to play free My Mini Car Service game online

To start playing My Mini Car Service, simply select a vehicle from the waiting list. Follow the on-screen prompts to diagnose mechanical issues, then choose the appropriate tools and parts for repair. Remember to customize the vehicle with paint and modifications before sending it out to satisfy your customers!
