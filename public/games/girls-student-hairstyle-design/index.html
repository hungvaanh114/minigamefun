Girls Student Hairstyle Design

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rebecca has just unveiled her brand new hair salon, and excitement is in the air! As a talented stylist, she is ready to assist her friends Iris, Adella, and Sofia with trendy hairstyles that will make a statement at school. With her creativity and expertise, Rebecca plans to design unique looks for each of them. The salon is filled with colorful hair dyes, stylish accessories, and an array of tools that are just waiting to be used.

Each friend has their own style and preferences, so Rebecca is eager to hear their ideas and incorporate them into personalized hairstyles. Iris might lean towards a sleek, modern cut, while Adella could prefer something playful and whimsical, like braids or colorful streaks. Sofia, on the other hand, might be interested in a classic look with a fresh twist. Together, they will navigate through the different styling options as Rebecca helps them select the perfect hairstyle that reflects their unique personalities.

As they experiment with various hairstyles, Rebecca will provide guidance and tips on how to maintain their looks throughout the school day. With a cheerful atmosphere and lots of laughter, this hair salon visit promises to be a memorable experience for the girls. They can t wait to show off their fabulous new styles when they return to school, feeling confident and trendy!

How to play free Girls Student Hairstyle Design game online

To play the Girls Student Hairstyle Design game, simply start by selecting one of the girls to style. Use the various tools available in the salon to cut, dye, and accessorize their hair. Customize each hairstyle according to your preference, making sure to experiment with different looks before finalizing the perfect design!
