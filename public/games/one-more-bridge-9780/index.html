One More Bridge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to One More Bridge, the ultimate test of timing and precision! In this thrilling game, you are tasked with constructing bridges to allow your character to cross various gaps. With each tap, you determine the length of the bridge, making split-second decisions to ensure your success.

However, be cautious! A late tap will send your character tumbling into the abyss below. Similarly, crafting a bridge that is either too short or too long will lead to the same unfortunate fate. The challenge lies in your ability to gauge the right timing and measure accurately, making every tap count. How many bridges can you build consecutively before you take a dive? Aim for the highest score and challenge your friends while enjoying the captivating gameplay.

With its simple yet engaging mechanics, One More Bridge is designed for players of all skill levels. As you progress, you ll feel the adrenaline rush as the speed and complexity increase, providing you with endless entertainment. Test your reflexes and see how far you can go without falling in this addictive game!

How to play free One More Bridge game online

To play One More Bridge, simply tap the screen at the precise moment to construct a bridge of the right length. Pay attention to the gap you are crossing if the bridge is too short or too long, you will fall. Stay focused and keep tapping to build bridges in succession!
