Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

How to Slice is an engaging and casual puzzle game that challenges players to use their cutting skills to fill deep pits with stone blocks. With a simple yet captivating gameplay mechanic, you will use your cursor to draw a cutting line across the stone above the pit. By clicking and dragging from the left side of the screen to the right, you create a cutting action that splinters the stone into manageable pieces that fall into the pit below.

The objective of the game is to clear the screen by strategically slicing through the stones in such a way that the falling blocks create a smooth and even surface to fill the deep void. Each level introduces new challenges and requires players to think critically about their cutting angles and the shapes of the stones. The satisfaction of watching the blocks fall into place is both rewarding and addictive, drawing you into a world of thoughtful strategy and relaxing gameplay.

As the game progresses, you will encounter various obstacles and scenarios that will test your puzzle-solving skills. The aesthetic of the game is both minimalistic and visually appealing, making it suitable for players of all ages. With its easy-to-learn mechanics and progressively challenging levels, How to Slice guarantees hours of entertainment while keeping your mind sharp.

How to play free How to Slice game online

To play How to Slice, start by clicking on the screen to initiate the cutting line. Drag your cursor across the stone and release it to make the cut. Watch as the blocks fall into the pit, aiming to create a level surface. Challenge yourself with each level by finding the best cutting strategy!
