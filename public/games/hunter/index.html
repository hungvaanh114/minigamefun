Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Hunter, a captivating 3D game designed to elevate your hunting skills. This exceptional simulator provides an opportunity for players to hone their shooting accuracy through engaging and dynamic gameplay. With a focus on precision, players will have the chance to utilize a sniper rifle to take aim at a variety of moving mechanical targets.

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter different scenarios that challenge your reflexes and shooting prowess. Each target presents a unique challenge, allowing you to practice under various conditions and improve your overall expertise. The realistic graphics and immersive sound effects bring the thrill of hunting right to your fingertips, making every shot feel impactful and rewarding.

Choose your sniper, calibrate your aim, and prepare to take down each target that comes your way. Earn points based on your accuracy and speed, and strive to achieve the highest scores. Competing against yourself, you can continuously improve your techniques, preparing for the excitement of the real hunt. Hunter combines enjoyment and skill development, making it the perfect training ground for any aspiring marksman.

How to play free Hunter game online

To play the Hunter game online, simply select your sniper and get ready at the shooting range. Use your mouse to aim at the moving targets and click to shoot. Focus on your timing and accuracy as you progress through various levels, trying to beat your high score!


Instructions

Hold left mouse button or right mouse button to aim release to shoot
