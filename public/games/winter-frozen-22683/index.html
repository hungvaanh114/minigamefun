Winter Frozen

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the mesmerizing world of Winter Frozen, where the enchanting beauty of ice crystals takes center stage. The game presents you with a spectacular array of ice pieces, each uniquely shaped and sparkling like precious diamonds under the winter sun. Your objective is to collect a designated number of these striking blocks to successfully complete each level.

Engage your strategic thinking as you maneuver through the frozen landscape. To remove the sparkling ice figures from the board, align three or more identical blocks in a row or column. As you progress, the combination of colors and shapes make for a visually stunning experience that adds to the thrill of the game. With each match you make, you will unlock new challenges and levels, keeping the excitement flowing. Gather your wits and showcase your matching skills as you challenge yourself in this delightful winter adventure.

Winter Frozen invites players of all ages to experience endless fun and relaxation while immersed in its beautiful icy realm. With each level you conquer, you ll feel a sense of achievement as you master the art of matching and strategizing against the test of icy time. So, gather your focus, and prepare to embark on this sparkling adventure! Can you collect all the ice blocks and become the master of Winter Frozen?

How to play free Winter Frozen game online

To play Winter Frozen, simply match three or more identical ice blocks by clicking or tapping on them. Align the blocks either horizontally or vertically to clear them from the board. Keep an eye on the required number of blocks to collect for each level, and strategize your moves wisely to maximize your score!
