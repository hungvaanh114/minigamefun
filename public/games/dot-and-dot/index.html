Dot and Dot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Dot and Dot is a colorful puzzle game where players connect matching colored dots on a grid. The goal is to create paths between all dot pairs without crossing other paths. Features include hints, double-click to automatically complete the last line with a smooth animation, and the ability to remove lines with right-clicks. As you progress through 300 increasingly challenging levels, your logical thinking and spatial reasoning skills are put to the test.
