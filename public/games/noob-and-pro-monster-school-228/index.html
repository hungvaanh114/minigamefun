Noob and Pro Monster School

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Noob and Pro Monster School, where you must navigate through a perilous school teeming with terrifying monsters! In this action-packed adventure, you'll find yourself pursued by one of two fearsome creatures: a relentless green monster or a sinister black monster. Your main goal? To evade these ravenous beasts at all costs!

As you sprint through the chaotic halls of the school, remember that your survival hinges on one critical rule: never, ever look back. Focus on your path, and be swift in your escape! Along the way, you ll encounter sparkling diamonds that you can collect to boost your score and unlock exciting features. The ultimate objective is to reach the glowing portal at the end of each level your gateway to safety.

Team up with Noob and Pro to enhance your adventure! Utilize their unique skills and strategies to outsmart the school monsters and complete the challenging levels. With each successful escape, you ll face more intricate obstacles and tougher monsters, testing your reflexes and decision-making skills. Can you outrun the monsters and gather every diamond on your exhilarating journey? Dive into this heart-pounding escapade and find out!

How to play free Noob and Pro Monster School game online

In the game, control your character using intuitive on-screen buttons to run and dodge obstacles. Collect diamonds as you race toward the portal, all while keeping a close eye on the approaching monsters. Stay alert and navigate through various levels, aiming to reach the exit without getting caught!
