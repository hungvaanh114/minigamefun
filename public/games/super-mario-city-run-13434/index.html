Super Mario City Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Super Mario City Run, an exhilarating running adventure that will test your reflexes and skills! In this lively game, you will guide our beloved Mario as he dashes through the bustling streets of the city. Experience a whirlwind of excitement as you tap or click your way to victories, leaping over obstacles and collecting shimmering coins along the way.

The streets are filled with challenges, and it s up to you to navigate through them. Avoid treacherous pitfalls and other hazards while maintaining your speed. Each coin you collect boosts your score, making your journey even more thrilling. The more coins you gather, the higher your score climbs, so stay focused and keep moving forward! Can you outscore your friends and claim the title of the highest runner in the city?

With its engaging mechanics and captivating visuals, Super Mario City Run is perfect for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the adventure, the simple controls allow you to immerse yourself in the fun instantly. Get ready to embark on a fast-paced journey filled with excitement, skill, and endless possibilities!

How to play free Super Mario City Run game online

Playing Super Mario City Run is easy and fun! Once you're in the game, simply tap or click the screen to make Mario run. Tap again to jump over obstacles. Collect coins by running into them, and strive to evade any pitfalls in your path. Try to beat your high score each time you play!
