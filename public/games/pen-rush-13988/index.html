Pen Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pen Rush is an engaging arcade game that brings a unique twist to your gaming experience. Imagine a vibrant world where your sole mission is to guide a pen as it races across a tabletop. Players will find themselves sliding their fingers to maneuver the pen, which glides effortlessly on the surface. Your goal is to collect additional pens scattered throughout the area to boost your score and enhance your gameplay.

However, the challenge lies in avoiding obstacles that may hinder your progress. As you dart across the table, be wary of books, pen containers, and various other items that can interfere with your run. Staying agile and attentive is essential, as one wrong move could mean the difference between a high score and a frustrating end. The anticipation builds as you reach the finish line, where all the extra pens you ve gathered will contribute to a special bonus inside a virtual book, rewarding your efforts and skill.

With its addictive mechanics and colorful graphics, Pen Rush is sure to keep players entertained for hours. Whether you're a casual gamer or a seasoned pro, this game offers a delightful challenge that will test your reflexes and precision. Are you ready to embark on this fast-paced pen adventure? Take up the challenge and see how far you can go!

How to play free Pen Rush game online

To play Pen Rush, simply slide your finger on the screen to control the pen's movement as it runs across the table. Collect as many pens as possible while avoiding obstacles like books and containers. Make it to the end to see the total number of pens you've gathered!
