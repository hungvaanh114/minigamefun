HORSE UP

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of HORSE UP, the highly addictive game where your skills as a caretaker and rider are put to the ultimate test! In this vibrant and dynamic racetrack environment, you ll race against time as you merge horses of similar levels to boost their abilities. The more you merge, the stronger your horses become, unlocking new breeds and improving your stable along the way.

Your journey in HORSE UP is not just about speed; it s also about strategy. Each horse brings unique traits to the track, and your job is to enhance their performance through merging. As you upgrade your horses, you will gain access to a variety of powerful steeds, each with its own distinctive skills that can help you dominate the competitions. The goal is to develop an unstoppable team by strategically merging horses and managing your resources effectively.

As you advance in the game, your stable will expand, and you ll find yourself immersed in the excitement of horse racing. Customize your horses and stable to match your style while competing against other players globally. With each race, you'll feel the adrenaline rush and the satisfaction of seeing your hard work pay off as your horses gallop towards victory!

How to play free HORSE UP game online

To play HORSE UP, simply tap on horses of the same level to merge them. Watch as they evolve into stronger breeds! Upgrade your stable by earning points, and race against others to see who has the best team. Keep an eye on your resources, as they are key to your success!
