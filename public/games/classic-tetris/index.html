How to play free Classic Tetris game online

To play Classic Tetris online, simply use the arrow keys to move and rotate the falling Tetriminos. Aim to fit them together at the bottom of the screen to create complete lines. Clearing lines will score you points and keep the blocks from stacking too high. Enjoy the challenge!
