Mahjong Chains

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Mahjong Chains is an engaging take on the classic connect game, where players embark on a captivating journey to find and connect identical mahjong stones. The fundamental objective of the game is to remove pairs of identical tiles from the board by connecting them with no more than three straight lines. This unique connecting mechanism adds an interesting layer of strategic thinking and planning as you navigate through each level.

Players can choose between two distinct modes: normal and relaxed. The normal mode presents a challenge with a timer, pushing you to make decisions quickly, while the relaxed mode is perfect for those who prefer a more leisurely experience. In relaxed mode, there are no time constraints, allowing you to take your time and strategize. Both modes feature countless levels of gameplay, ensuring that you will never run out of fun, engaging puzzles to solve, making Mahjong Chains a delightful choice for casual gamers and hardcore puzzle enthusiasts alike.

The colorful and beautifully designed mahjong stones add to the visual appeal of the game, while the sound effects enhance the overall gaming experience. Whether you are a seasoned mahjong player or a newcomer to the genre, Mahjong Chains promises hours of entertainment as you challenge yourself to find pairs and clear the board. Dive into this captivating world of tiles and see how far your skills can take you!

How to play free Mahjong Chains game online

To play Mahjong Chains online, start by selecting a game mode normal or relaxed. Your goal is to identify and connect identical stones by drawing a line with no more than three straight angles. Click on the pairs to remove them from the board, and continue until all tiles are cleared.
