Greedy Frog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Greedy Frog, an engaging leisure game that promises fun and challenges for players of all ages. The game's mechanics are simple yet captivating, as you take control of a hungry frog with the primary objective of stretching its tongue to catch sparkling diamonds scattered throughout the screen. Each diamond collected increases your score, making each successful tongue flick an exhilarating moment.

As you navigate through the colorful environment, be prepared for various obstacles that will appear along your path. These obstructions require quick reflexes and strategic planning to avoid, as coming into contact with them costs you a precious life. The balance of precision and risk adds a layer of excitement to the game, making it both challenging and enjoyable.

Greedy Frog is designed to keep you entertained for hours, whether you are looking for a casual gaming experience or aiming to set a new high score. The vibrant graphics and delightful sound effects enhance the overall experience, immersing you in a whimsical world. Do not miss out on this incredibly engaging game; give it a try and see how many diamonds you can catch while skillfully dodging obstacles!

How to play free Greedy Frog game online

To play the Greedy Frog game, simply use your mouse or finger (if on a mobile device) to click or tap to control the length of the frog's tongue. Aim for the diamonds while avoiding any obstacles in your way. Collect as many diamonds as you can before losing all your lives!
