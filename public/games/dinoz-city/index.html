DinoZ City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The thrilling adventure of DinoZ continues in the exciting sequel, DinoZ City. This time, the dinosaurs have taken control of the city, holding hostages captive, including invaluable scientists and police officers. As a brave player, your mission is to rescue these hostages and restore peace to the city while navigating through dinosaur-infested areas.

Equipped with powerful armored vehicles, you will have the means to combat the intimidating dinosaurs who roam the streets. Choose to embark on this action-packed journey either solo, immersing yourself in the gripping narrative, or team up with a friend in the thrilling co-play "2 Player" mode. Work together to devise tactics that will help you save the hostages while minimizing risk.

As you traverse the city, stay alert for cunning traps set by the intelligent dinosaurs. Each level presents unique challenges, including cleverly devised barriers and aggressive dinosaur foes that are determined to stop you. To succeed, you must strategize your movements, utilize your vehicle's firepower effectively, and outsmart each dinosaur you encounter. Don't forget to prepare for epic boss battles these incredibly powerful dinosaurs serve as the ultimate test of your skills!

How to play free DinoZ City game online

To play DinoZ City online, simply visit the game s website or platform where it's hosted. Use the arrow keys to navigate your armored vehicle and the action button to eliminate hostile dinosaurs. Coordinate with your friend in "2 Player" mode for cooperative strategies, and always be on the lookout for traps!
