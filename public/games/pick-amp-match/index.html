Pick Amp; Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pick & Match, an engaging game designed to test and sharpen your memory skills! In this entertaining challenge, you will rely on your natural ability to recall and match pairs of cards featuring various animals. The objective is to reveal matching animal pairs while racing against a time limit to succeed.

As you navigate through the game, each level presents a unique layout of cards that will challenge your recollection and strategic thinking. You must open pairs of cards, and if you manage to find all matching pairs before the timer runs out, you will emerge victorious! However, if the time expires without completing the task, the game concludes, adding an element of excitement and urgency to your experience.

For every level you complete, you will earn rewards in the form of coins. These coins can be utilized to purchase helpful features within the game, enhancing your gameplay experience and providing you with an advantage in subsequent levels. The delightful graphics and charming sounds will keep you entertained as you strive to improve your scores and unlock new levels. So, get ready to embark on a captivating adventure filled with adorable animals and fun challenges in Pick & Match!

How to play free Pick & Match game online

To play Pick & Match, simply start by clicking on the cards to reveal the animals. Remember the positions of the cards as you strive to find matching pairs. Keep an eye on the timer at the top of the screen; success depends on matching all the pairs before time runs out!
