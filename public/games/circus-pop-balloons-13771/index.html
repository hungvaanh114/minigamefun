To play "Circus Pop Balloons," simply select your desired game mode from the main menu. Use your mouse or touch controls to aim and pop balloons as they float across the screen. Aim for clusters of balloons for higher scores and keep an eye out for special bonuses that appear randomly. Enjoy the vibrant circus atmosphere as you compete for the best score!
