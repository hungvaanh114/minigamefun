Pride Mahjong

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pride Mahjong, an engaging and delightful twist on the classic Mahjong game that brings together vibrant visuals and exciting gameplay. In this captivating tile-matching game, your goal is to locate and match three identical food tiles to eliminate them from the board. The unique layout allows you to place seven tiles at a time, adding an extra layer of strategy as you plan your moves.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter various tiles featuring colorful and appetizing dishes, creating a feast for your eyes while challenging your matching skills. The fun doesn't stop there! Pride Mahjong also incorporates three special types of bonuses that can assist you in easing your gaming experience. These bonuses can provide valuable advantages, making it easier to clear tiles and overcome obstacles, ensuring that both beginners and seasoned players enjoy the challenge.

Pride Mahjong offers a fresh take on the traditional gameplay, filled with dynamic challenges and engaging visuals. Each level is designed to test your observation and strategic thinking, keeping you entertained for hours. Dive into this whimsical world of culinary delights and experience the joy of matching tiles while aiming for high scores!

How to play free Pride Mahjong game online

To play Pride Mahjong, start by identifying three identical food tiles. Click on them to select and destroy them, clearing space on the board. Utilize the bonus features wisely when the tiles become challenging to match. Keep an eye on the tiles available to ensure you can strategize your next moves effectively!


Instructions

Match three of the same tiles Click on a tile to move it to the collection spots at the bottom If you have collected three of the same tiles there they will be removed
