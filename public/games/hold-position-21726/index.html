Instructions:

In the gripping strategy game, Hold Position, tensions rise as a neighboring country plots an attack to claim a portion of your nation's border. As a commander, you hold the key to your country's fate. Your mission is clear: rally your troops, devise a tactical plan, and repel the impending invasion. With strategic foresight and effective management, you must strengthen your army and upgrade your weapons and defenses to ensure victory.

As you step into the battle, you'll face a series of challenging scenarios that will test your strategic thinking and resource allocation. Coordinate your forces, fortify your positions, and create a stronghold to withstand the enemy's advances. Utilize a variety of upgrade options to enhance your capabilities, making your units more formidable on the battlefield. The enemy will not relent, and their determination to seize your territory will intensify, compelling you to stay one step ahead.

Mastering the art of warfare is essential; choose the right mix of troops to counteract your foe's strengths and exploit their weaknesses. With every successful maneuver, you'll gain experience, unlocking even more powerful upgrades for your soldiers and defenses. The fate of your nation rests on your shoulders, so prepare yourself for an epic clash where strategy and quick thinking will determine the outcome.

How to play free Hold Position game online

To start playing Hold Position, use your mouse to navigate through the game's interface. Click on units to deploy them strategically along the borders. Upgrade your troops and defenses by accessing the upgrade menu with a simple click. Monitor incoming enemy forces and adapt your tactics to repel them effectively.
