Stack Colour

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Stack Colour, a captivating game that challenges your color recognition and strategic thinking skills. As you navigate through a dynamic landscape, your main objective is to collect squares of the same color. Each square you gather not only adds to your collection but also significantly increases your rewards! The more squares of a single color you stack up, the richer your rewards will be, allowing you to enhance your gameplay experience.

Throughout your journey, it's essential to remain vigilant and pay close attention to the colors of the squares around you. Timing and precision are key elements in this colorful adventure. As you deftly maneuver through each level, you'll encounter an array of colors that can either aid your quest or hinder your progress. Make strategic decisions about which squares to target and aim for larger combinations that yield higher rewards. With each successful collection, you ll rise through the ranks, unlocking new levels and challenges that test your skills and reflexes.

Whether you're playing for fun or aiming for high scores, Stack Colour offers endless enjoyment as you strive to beat your own records and those of other players. So, let the colors guide you on this exciting journey can you become the ultimate color collector?

How to play free Stack Colour game online

To play Stack Colour, simply use your mouse or finger to move around and collect squares of the same color. Focus on gathering as many squares as possible to boost your score. Be mindful of the colors you collect, as different combinations yield varying rewards!
