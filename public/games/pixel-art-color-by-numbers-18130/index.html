Pixel Art - Color by Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the joy of creativity with Pixel Art - Color by Numbers, a delightful game that invites players of all ages to unleash their artistic side. Simply follow the numbers corresponding to each color, and watch as your blank canvas transforms into a stunning pixelated masterpiece. No artistic skills are necessary this game is designed for everyone!

This app offers a vast collection of free pixel images that are perfect for both adults and children. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day or seeking a fun activity to share with friends and family, Pixel Art provides a perfect escape. With its easy-to-use interface, you'll find yourself captivated by the colorful creativity that awaits you.

As you dive into the world of digital painting, you'll encounter intricate designs ranging from simple patterns to complex images. The satisfaction of completing each piece and seeing your pixel art come to life is incredibly rewarding. Join thousands of other art lovers and indulge in painting and coloring now your next favorite pixel artwork is just a click away!

How to play free Pixel Art - Color by Numbers game online

To play Pixel Art, simply select an image from the gallery. You will see a grid filled with numbers; each number corresponds to a specific color. Tap on the numbered sections and fill them in according to the indicated colors. Keep coloring until your picture is completed, and enjoy your pixel creation!


Instructions

Left click to draw a pixel hold 2 seconds and drag to draw multiple pixels
