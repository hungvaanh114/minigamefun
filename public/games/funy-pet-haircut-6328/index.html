Funy Pet Haircut

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Funny Pet Haircut, a delightful simulation game that takes you into the bustling environment of a pet salon! Here, you ll have the chance to be the proud owner of a grooming business, tasked with making all your adorable furry friends look their absolute best.

Your charming customers will arrive in less-than-desirable conditions, so be ready to roll up your sleeves and get to work! Start by giving your pets a thorough wash to remove any dirt and grime. Once they re squeaky clean, it s time to dry them off and prepare for the ultimate makeover. Each pet will require a unique touch, and you ll have a variety of grooming tools at your disposal.

Unleash your creativity as you brush, shampoo, and de-flea your loyal clients. With an array of colors, hairstyles, and makeup options to choose from, the possibilities are endless! Want a pink pomeranian with sparkly bows? Or perhaps a dapper dachshund with a slick hairstyle? Your salon will quickly become the talk of the town as you transform each pet into a magnificent masterpiece.

Give your imagination free rein and adorn your furry clients with fabulous accessories to complete their new look. Try Funny Pet Haircut today and watch as your grooming talents shine!

How to play free Funny Pet Haircut game online

To play Funny Pet Haircut, simply click on the various buttons to select grooming tools and options. Wash and dry the pets first, then choose hairstyles and colors. Use your mouse to drag and drop accessories onto each pet, and complete the makeover to earn points and unlock new features!
