How to play free Go Kart Boost Playground game online

Once you're in the game, use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your go-kart. Press the boost button (usually the spacebar) to gain extra speed. Explore the race track or stunt arena, performing tricks and speeding around corners to maximize your fun. Enjoy!
