Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the festive world of Christmas Mahjong 2019 where holiday cheer meets classic puzzle-solving fun! In this enchanting game, your objective is to match and eliminate pairs of Christmas-themed Mahjong tiles from the board. Each tile features delightful seasonal imagery, making your gameplay experience both visually appealing and entertaining. To successfully remove tiles, you must ensure they're 'free,' meaning they aren t blocked by other tiles and have at least one side (left or right) unobstructed.

Prepare yourself for an exciting challenge across 25 unique levels! As you progress, the difficulty will increase, requiring you to think critically and strategize your moves. Each level offers fresh challenges, so you can enjoy the game as many times as you wish, with different layouts and combinations waiting to be discovered. Aim to clear the tiles in the least amount of time possible to improve your skills and score higher!

The delightful atmosphere and engaging gameplay make Christmas Mahjong 2019 a perfect way to celebrate the holiday season while enhancing your cognitive abilities. Whether you're a seasoned Mahjong player or a newcomer, this game is designed to bring joy and fun to players of all ages. Gather your friends and family, cheer each other on, and see who can complete the levels fastest!

How to play free Christmas Mahjong 2019 game online

To play Christmas Mahjong 2019, click on any free tile that matches another to remove them from the board. Remember, a tile is considered free only if it's not covered by another tile and has at least one unobstructed side. Create pairs strategically to clear the board within the time limit!
