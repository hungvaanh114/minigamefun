It Can Happen: Visitors

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In "It Can Happen: Visitors," you've just awakened from a deep slumber to a peculiar sight: your home is now teeming with tiny green beings! These playful visitors have taken over your space, and it s up to you to uncover the mystery behind their sudden appearance. As you navigate through your house, you'll encounter various challenges and clues that will guide you in your quest to restore normalcy.

Explore each room thoroughly, as hidden objects and quirky puzzles await your attention. The little green people may hold the key to understanding why they chose your home. Pay close attention to their behaviors and the items they interact with, as these can lead you to important discoveries that can help you either communicate with them or find a way to convince them to leave. As the storyline unfolds, you'll uncover a whimsical narrative filled with laughs and surprises.

The vibrant graphics and engaging sound design enhance the experience, immersing you in a world that is both strange and enchanting. Interaction with the environment is vital, so be prepared to think creatively and outside the box. Whether it's using household items in unexpected ways or piecing together their clues, your adventure will be intellectually stimulating and fun. How will you restore peace to your home, and what will you learn from these unexpected guests? Join the adventure and find out!

How to play free It Can Happen: Visitors game online

To play "It Can Happen: Visitors," use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with objects around your house. Click on items to collect them and solve puzzles by dragging and dropping objects in the correct places. Pay close attention to the behavior of the little green people to learn more about their presence!
