Step into a chilling alternative history with Slenderman History: WWII Faceless Horror. The game plunges players into the dark depths of World War II, where the notorious Nazis have not only committed horrific atrocities but also delved into the arcane and the forbidden. They have experimented on their own soldiers in a desperate bid to create the ultimate super-soldier. It is within this nefarious backdrop that they have summoned an otherworldly being through the use of powerful ancient artifacts a being recognized by many as the embodiment of terror: Slenderman.

Your objective is to infiltrate a heavily guarded Nazi base, uncover its secrets, and locate a concealed bunker. Within this hidden chamber lies a treasure trove of classified documents sealed inside two briefcases, which detail the grotesque experiments conducted by the dark regime. As you navigate the eerie corridors and face the lurking shadows, strategy and stealth will be your best allies. Armed with nothing but your wits and courage, you must evade the horrors that await, including Slenderman himself.

This thrilling game is brought to life with stunning 3D graphics that enhance the immersive experience. The intuitive controls make it accessible for players of all skill levels. With multiple levels to conquer, an engaging WWII narrative, spine-chilling jumpscares, and disturbing sound design, Slenderman History: WWII Faceless Horror promises to keep players on the edge of their seats.

How to play free Slenderman History: WWII Faceless Horror game online

Once you're in the game, use the WASD keys to move around and the mouse to look around. Navigate the environment by interacting with objects to uncover clues. Stay alert for enemy soldiers and Slenderman, as they can appear at any moment. Collect the briefcases while avoiding detection!
