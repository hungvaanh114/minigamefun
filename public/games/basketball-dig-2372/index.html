Basketball Dig

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the excitement of Basketball Dig, where you're invited to dig through sandy terrain to score incredible slam dunks! In this engaging game, players will find themselves completely immersed as they master the art of digging while aiming to score by launching the basketball into the hoop. With the challenge of attaining 3 stars on each level, you can unlock various rewards and purchase unique skins that enhance your gameplay experience.

Basketball Dig features captivating and addictive gameplay, boasting innovative features such as teleports, bombs, crates, and stones that will keep you on your toes. Each new level presents fresh challenges that encourage logical thinking and unleash your creativity as you devise strategies to succeed. Thanks to its realistic physics engine, the game provides an authentic feel as you dig through sand and unleash the basketball into the hoop. The reward system motivates you to push your limits, while the shop offers an array of unique skins to customize your gameplay further. With a total of 50 diverse and attractive levels, there's no shortage of fun waiting for you!

How to play free Basketball Dig game online

To play Basketball Dig online, simply dig into the sand with your finger or mouse while avoiding obstacles. Guide the basketball through the sandy terrain and aim for the hoop. Collect stars and bonuses to enhance your score, and remember to use power-ups effectively to overcome challenges!
