Jelly King

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Jelly King, a delightful adventure brought to you by the creative minds behind the iconic Candy Crush Saga! Dive into the world of sweet treats and vibrant colors as you immerse yourself in unique challenges and exciting game modes filled with bubbly purple soda. Join Kimmy on her quest to locate Tiffi by swapping and matching your way through enchanting new dimensions of gameplay.

Experience the thrill of this mouth-watering puzzle journey either solo or with your friends, competing for the highest scores! With monthly seasonal updates packed with fresh quests and engaging gameplay, there's always something new to discover. Complete quests to advance through the Season Pass while earning valuable rewards and helpful boosters to aid you in your adventure.

Showcase your competitive spirit in the Episode Race, racing against players to finish levels swiftly and progress faster. Or collaborate with others in the 4 in a Row mode, where teamwork leads to scrumptious rewards! You'll encounter over 6000 match-3 Sodalicious levels, stunning graphics, and a changing environment as you play.

Enjoy exciting game modes that involve unique gameplay mechanics, like releasing trapped Candy Bears by switching bottles and matching candies. With an array of unique candies and mouth-watering combinations, you'll be hooked on the puzzle-solving fun! Whether it's making Swedish Fish by matching four candies or the all-new Coloring Candy with seven, there are endless possibilities.

How to play free Jelly King game online

To play Jelly King online, simply use your mouse to swap adjacent candies and create matches of three or more. Complete the levels by satisfying the objectives set for each stage, such as collecting certain candies or clearing obstacles. Don't forget to utilize boosters and power-ups to help you on your journey!
