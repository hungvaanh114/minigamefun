Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Stay Away from Machine, a captivating relaxation vehicle survival game that features dynamic 3D stickman models and an array of customizable vehicles. As you navigate through various terrains, your key objective is to survive and creatively interact with other players. With unique weapons at your disposal, you can strategically attack competitors on the platform, adding an exhilarating twist to the gameplay.

In Stay Away from Machine, it's crucial to maintain a close proximity to your surroundings, even while evading potential damage from adversaries. This element of strategy enhances the gaming experience, yielding endless possibilities for combat and exploration. With its engaging graphics and intuitive controls, this action-packed game guarantees hours of fun as you challenge yourself and others while striving for victory.

The combination of relaxation and adrenaline-inducing action will keep you on your toes, ensuring that every session is as thrilling as the last. Prepare for an unforgettable journey in Stay Away from Machine as you engage in epic battles and showcase your skills on the platform.

How to play free Stay Away from Machine game online

To play Stay Away from Machine, start by selecting your stickman character and customizing your vehicle. Use the on-screen controls to move around and dodge attacks while engaging with other players. Aim and shoot your weapon to knock out opponents. Stay close to the action to maximize your chances of survival!
