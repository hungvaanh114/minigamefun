Train Dispatcher in Mall

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Train Dispatcher is a fast-paced puzzle game where trains and tunnels are marked with country flags. Your task is to switch the railroad tracks in time to send each train to the correct tunnel. Test your reflexes and strategic thinking as the railway network gets more complex!
