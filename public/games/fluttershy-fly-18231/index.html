Fluttershy Fly

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure with Fluttershy Fly, an engaging side-scroller game that puts you in control of Fluttershy, the beloved character from the magical world of My Little Pony. As you soar through vibrant landscapes, your main goal is to navigate as far as possible without colliding with various obstacles and pesky enemies lurking along your path. Your journey is fraught with challenges, but with skill and quick reflexes, you can master the skies.

During your flight, keep an eye out for shimmering coins scattered throughout the levels. Collecting these coins not only boosts your score but may also grant you valuable health, allowing you to endure longer against the hazards you encounter. With a maximum of three health points, every decision you make is critical. Whether you're dodging towering trees, navigating through narrow gaps, or avoiding hostile creatures, every moment counts in this fast-paced adventure.

To take flight, simply press the SPACEBAR or click the left button on your mouse. This will make Fluttershy ascend gracefully into the air, ready to conquer the obstacles ahead. Should you prefer an immersive experience, you can press F12 to switch to full-screen mode and truly feel the excitement of fluttering through the heart of Equestria.

How to play free Fluttershy Fly game online

To play Fluttershy Fly, simply start the game and use the SPACEBAR or LEFT MOUSE CLICK to begin your flight. Navigate through the air while avoiding enemies and obstacles. Collect coins for points and health to stay in the game as you aim for the highest score possible!
