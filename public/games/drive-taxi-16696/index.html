Drive Taxi

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the driver's seat and take on the exciting role of a taxi driver in Drive Taxi! Your primary goal is to navigate through the bustling streets, picking up passengers who are in need of a ride. As you race against the clock, it's essential to maintain your focus to ensure that no customer is left behind. Time is of the essence in this fast-paced game, so rush to your target locations while considering the safety of your passengers.

In Drive Taxi, the thrill comes from facing the challenges of urban driving. You will encounter various obstacles along the way, including other vehicles, pedestrians, and unpredictable traffic patterns. It's crucial to maneuver swiftly and strategically to avoid collisions with cars on the road. Each passenger you pick up adds to your score, so stay alert and drive with precision to deliver them to their destinations smoothly.

The game features stunning graphics that immerse you in a realistic city environment, enhancing the overall experience. With each successful drop-off, you unlock new levels and increase your earning potential, making for a rewarding gameplay experience. Whether it's the thrill of speed or the challenge of weaving through traffic, Drive Taxi promises to keep you entertained as you master the art of taxi driving!

How to play free Drive Taxi game online

To play Drive Taxi, simply use your keyboard to control the vehicle arrow keys for movement and acceleration. As you drive through the city, look for icons representing passengers and stop to pick them up. After loading passengers, follow the route shown on the map to deliver them safely to their destinations. Keep an eye out for traffic to avoid accidents!
