Pumpkin Stick

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a thrilling adventure with Pumpkin Stick! This exciting game challenges you to stretch a stick to navigate between platforms successfully. Your goal is to balance on the edges of these platforms while avoiding falling into the abyss below. With each new level, the platforms become increasingly spaced apart, testing your skills and timing. Are you agile enough to master this ninja-inspired mechanics? The further you go, the more chances you have to unlock special rewards and enhance your abilities.

As you play, you'll experience a delightful mix of strategy and dexterity. Each stretch of the stick requires careful consideration too short, and you'll plummet down; too long, and you risk misjudging your landing. Gather your wits and embody the spirit of a ninja as you strive to reach new heights! Collect bonuses along the way and aim for a high score. Challenge your friends and see who can conquer this precarious journey. How far can you go? Only the most skilled players will reach the ultimate platform!

How to play free Pumpkin Stick game online

To play the Pumpkin Stick game, simply click and hold the screen to extend your stick. Release the mouse button or lift your finger to drop the stick when you think it's the right length. Carefully aim to land on the platforms without falling. Keep stretching and landing as you progress through the levels!
