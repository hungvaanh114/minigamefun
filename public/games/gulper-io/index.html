Gulper.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Gulper.io is an exciting multiplayer snake game that draws inspiration from the popular Slither.io. In this vibrant and engaging game, you take charge of a small, glowing worm and your main objective is to grow larger while eliminating other players on the map. To increase your size, simply gobble up the luminous dots that are scattered throughout the game arena. The challenge lies in navigating swiftly while constantly scanning the surroundings for potential threats, as other worms will be eagerly attempting to outmaneuver and trap you.

As you master the game, you can employ tactics to destroy your opponents by ensnaring them within your body. A well-timed maneuver could lead to strategically positioning yourself, making it difficult for others to escape. The thrill of competition and the constant rush of adrenaline make Gulper.io an addictive pastime. The more you focus on consuming the glowing dots, the larger your worm will become, increasing your chances of dominating the playing field. So, can you rise to the challenge and become the largest glowing worm?

How to play free gulper.io game online

To play Gulper.io, use your mouse to navigate your worm around the map. Move towards the glowing dots to absorb them and increase your size. To trap enemies, circle them swiftly, creating a barrier with your body. Stay alert and avoid crashing into larger worms to survive as long as possible!
