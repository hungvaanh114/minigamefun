Hungry Shapes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exciting world of Hungry Shapes, your mission is to satisfy the appetites of various geometric figures by skillfully dropping the right shapes onto them. Each of the 'hungry shapes' has a specific geometry they crave, allowing for different colors but requiring the same overall form. If you drop an incompatible shape, you will face the consequences and lose a life. The challenge ramps up as you advance through the game, introducing multiple hungry shapes, position swaps, and even increasing the speed at which new shapes fall.

As you progress, you'll encounter an array of mesmerizing colored shapes while trying to match them to their hungry counterparts. The satisfaction of successfully pairing shapes is compounded by the thrill of a ticking timer, pushing you to react quickly and think strategically. Ensure you plan ahead, because with more shapes appearing on your screen, the difficulty escalates rapidly. With each level, the challenges become more complex, making every play session dynamic and engaging.

How to play free Hungry Shapes game online

To play the Hungry Shapes game, simply drag and drop the falling shapes onto their corresponding hungry shapes. Remember, they must match in form, even if the colors differ. Keep an eye on the pace as it increases, and be ready for swaps! Stack those shapes wisely to maintain your lives!
