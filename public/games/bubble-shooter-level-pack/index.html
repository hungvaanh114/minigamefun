Bubble Shooter Level Pack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the captivating world of Bubble Shooter Level Pack, a timeless bubble shooting adventure that promises to keep you entertained for hours. In this classic game, your objective is simple yet challenging: shoot bubbles and eliminate them by matching three or more of the same color. With vivid graphics and engaging gameplay, each level offers a unique challenge that tests your skills and strategy.

Throughout the game, you'll encounter a variety of colorful bubbles appearing on the screen. You need to carefully plan your shots to ensure successful matches, as you can only shoot one bubble from your selection of three available at any time. To choose the bubble you want to fire, simply tap on the bubble located on the left side of the aiming arrow. Timing and accuracy are crucial in this game, as any miss will cost you points. If you shoot a bubble that fails to form a match of three or more similar bubbles, a deduction of 200 points will occur, so precision is key.

With a total of 48 levels to conquer, each new stage introduces greater difficulty and requires quick thinking and strategic shooting. The satisfaction of clearing each stage fuels your progress in the game, motivating you to achieve a perfect score. Will you master all levels and emerge victorious? Jump into the vibrant bubble-filled fun of the Bubble Shooter Level Pack and challenge yourself!

How to play free Bubble Shooter Level Pack game online

To play the game, simply launch it in your browser, select your preferred bubble from the left sidebar, and aim using the arrow. Tap to shoot your bubble towards the cluster, aiming for a match. Repeat this process until you clear all bubbles from the screen to complete each level.
