The epic third installment of the acclaimed top-down 2D tank shooter, Awesome Tanks 3, has officially launched, presenting players with a host of exciting new features. The game introduces stunning visuals, enhanced physics, and user-friendly twin-thumb controls that promise an immersive experience as you navigate through intense combat scenarios. Prepare for war!

In Awesome Tanks 3, players will engage in thrilling battles as they annihilate monstrous enemies and demolish rival tanks. Explore various hidden areas throughout the game, each filled with surprises and challenges that await your discovery. Additionally, the Level Editor lets you unleash your creativity by designing and sharing custom levels with the community, adding a personal touch to your gaming experience.

As you progress, collecting money is crucial. These resources enable you to purchase advanced weaponry and upgrade your tank's stats, giving you the edge needed to dominate the battlefield. With its engaging gameplay and expansive content, Awesome Tanks 3 guarantees countless hours of entertainment for fans of action-packed shooters.

How to play free Awesome Tanks 3 Game online

To play Awesome Tanks 3 online, simply enter the game and use the on-screen controls to maneuver your tank. Utilize your twin-thumb controls to move and shoot simultaneously. Engage enemies by navigating strategically, aim carefully, and don t forget to collect coins to enhance your tank's capabilities as you progress through challenging levels!
