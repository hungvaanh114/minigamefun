Dig This 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Dig It!" is an engaging casual game that challenges players to demonstrate their ingenuity and problem-solving skills through a series of intriguing logic puzzles. In this immersive experience, you will navigate through countless captivating levels, each designed to test your creativity as you dig tunnels in the soil.

Your main objective is to successfully guide all balls into their respective holes by manipulating the landscape. Using your finger (or mouse), you'll carve out paths in the earth, ensuring that each ball reaches its designated spot without colliding or falling off. With each level presenting unique challenges, players must think outside the box and strategize their digging approach to complete the tasks efficiently.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter various obstacles and puzzles that will keep you engaged and entertained. The vibrant graphics and intuitive mechanics make "Dig It!" not only fun but also visually appealing. Whether you're a casual gamer or a puzzle enthusiast, this game has something to offer everyone as you find innovative ways to orchestrate success with each level you tackle.

How to play free Dig This 3D game online

To play "Dig This 3D," simply use your mouse or finger to create tunnels in the ground. Tap or swipe to dig, guiding the balls toward their respective holes while avoiding obstacles. Plan your moves wisely to solve each level's unique puzzles and enjoy the satisfying mechanics of creating your path to victory!
