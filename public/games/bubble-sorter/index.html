Bubble Sorter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Bubble Sorter, an engaging puzzle sorting game that challenges your organizational skills and dexterity! Your objective is to sort colorful bubble balls, carefully placing them into test tubes according to their hues. Each tube is designated for a specific color, and to succeed in the game, you need to ensure that you only place bubbles of the same color within each tube.

As you progress through various levels, the challenge increases, requiring strategic thinking and planning. Players must utilize their problem-solving abilities to determine the best order for sorting the bubbles. Thankfully, there s an extra tube available for you; this offers an added layer of flexibility as it allows you to maneuver the bubbles to achieve the desired color arrangement without getting stuck. Your success in sorting all the bubbles correctly will lead you to the next exciting level filled with new challenges.

With its vibrant graphics and smooth gameplay mechanics, Bubble Sorter promises an enjoyable experience for puzzle fans of all ages. As you embark on this colorful sorting adventure, enjoy the satisfaction and sense of accomplishment that comes with each sorted tube. Get ready to mix, match, and master the art of bubble sorting!

How to play free Bubble Sorter game online

To begin playing Bubble Sorter, simply click on a bubble to lift it, then select another tube to drop it into. Ensure that the target tube is either empty or contains bubbles of the same color. Use the extra tube wisely to help rearrange the bubbles effectively and achieve color harmony in each tube to complete the level.
