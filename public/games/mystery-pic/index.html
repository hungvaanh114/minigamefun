Mystery Pic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Mystery Pic, the intriguing puzzle game designed to challenge your observational skills! Your main objective is to decipher the identity of the blurry image displayed on your screen. Due to a humorous narrative involving a head injury, your view is obscured, adding an extra layer of excitement and difficulty to the game.

You will navigate through 150 captivating levels filled with mind-boggling images that will put your guessing abilities to the test. Each image presents a unique challenge, inviting you to think outside the box as you attempt to figure out what you are seeing. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay create a mesmerizing experience that keeps you immersed for hours.

Should you find yourself in a tight spot, don t fret! Mystery Pic offers an in-game currency system where you can use your earned coins to purchase helpful hints. These hints can assist in revealing a crucial part of the image or providing a clue, ensuring that you ll never be stuck for too long. Get ready for a journey filled with fun and mind-bending challenges!

How to play free Mystery Pic game online

To play Mystery Pic, begin by selecting a level. You will see a blurred image before you; your task is to identify it. Type in your guesses below the image. If you are unsure, use your coins to buy hints for extra help. Keep playing and unlock new levels!
