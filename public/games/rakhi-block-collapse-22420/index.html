Rakhi Block Collapse

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the charm of the Rakshabandhan festival with "Rakhi Block Collapse," a delightful online puzzle game celebrating this traditional Indian holiday. During this auspicious occasion, sisters tie a protective thread known as RAKHI around their brothers' wrists, symbolizing love and affection. In this engaging game, players must click or tap adjacent blocks of the same color to collect them. The more blocks you group together, the more points you earn, making this puzzle not only fun but also competitive.

Begin your journey by targeting single blocks for 200 points, but aim for larger groups to maximize your score and unlock exciting power-ups. Each level presents a new layout of blocks, challenging your strategy as you strive to collect them all. Completing levels will test your skills and reflexes while immersing you in the festive spirit of Rakshabandhan. The colorful graphics and cheerful sounds enhance the gameplay experience, making it perfect for players of all ages. So get ready to enjoy the vibrant world of "Rakhi Block Collapse" and celebrate the bonds of brotherhood in this entertaining format!

How to play free Rakhi Block Collapse game online

To play Rakhi Block Collapse, simply click or tap on adjacent blocks of the same color to collect them. Aim for larger groups to earn more points and activate power-ups. Complete all the blocks in a level before progressing to the next one. Have fun and strategize to achieve the highest score!
