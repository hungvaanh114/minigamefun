Bricks Breaker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the ultimate relaxation with Bricks Breaker, the most exciting bricks breaker game of the year! In this captivating game, you ll be challenged to touch and shoot balls strategically to demolish all the bricks on the screen. Your primary objective is to eliminate every last brick to successfully complete each level.

To maximize your score, you need to find the perfect angle for your shot. The bricks will begin to crumble once their score diminishes to zero. Keep a close eye on the descending bricks, for if they reach the bottom of the screen, it's game over! With over 3,000 levels available, the challenges are endless, offering countless hours of fun and satisfaction. Plan your moves wisely, and crush as many bricks as you can in each round!

Whether you re a casual player or a seasoned pro, Bricks Breaker is designed to engage you like never before. The graphics are sleek, the mechanics are smooth, and each level introduces unique arrangements of bricks that will test your aiming and strategic skills. Put your concentration to the test as you break through colorful bricks and watch them shatter into dust. This addictive gameplay will have you coming back for more. Get ready for an exhilarating journey filled with vibrant visuals, mesmerizing sound effects, and major brick-breaking action!

How to play free Bricks Breaker game online

To play Bricks Breaker, simply tap on the screen to launch balls toward the bricks above. Aim your shots to ensure they collide with the bricks at the optimal angle. Keep breaking the bricks until all bricks are cleared before they reach the bottom of the screen. Good luck!
