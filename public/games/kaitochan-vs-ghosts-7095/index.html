Kaitochan vs Ghosts

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a spine-chilling adventure in Kaitochan vs Ghosts, a captivating 2D Halloween-themed platformer that will keep you on your toes! In this spooky realm, your mission is to collect vibrant yellow balls while skillfully dodging an array of perilous obstacles. From mischievous pumpkin foes to sweltering flames, treacherous spikes, and menacing flying ghosts, every corner is filled with challenges that will test your agility and focus.

As you navigate through each of the eight increasingly challenging levels, your objective remains clear: reach the Exit door to advance to the next stage. However, the Exit will only unlock once you've successfully gathered all the yellow balls scattered throughout the level. Get ready to strategize and hone your jumping and dodging skills, as every level presents a whole new set of intricacies and traps designed to thwart your progress.

Whether you're playing solo or competing for a high score with friends, Kaitochan vs Ghosts will immerse you in a delightful mix of suspense and excitement, perfect for lovers of Halloween and platform games alike. Explore various landscapes, outrun the ghosts, and embark on this thrilling quest filled with spooky surprises at every turn!

How to play free Kaitochan vs Ghosts game online

To play Kaitochan vs Ghosts, use the arrow keys to maneuver your character. Jump over obstacles and collect all the yellow balls in each level. Carefully time your moves to avoid the pumpkin enemies and flying ghosts. Once you gather all the yellow balls, head to the Exit door to advance to the next level!

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
