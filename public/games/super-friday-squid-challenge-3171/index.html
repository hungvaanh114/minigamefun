Super Friday Squid Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Boyfriend and Girlfriend in a thrilling new adventure in Super Friday Squid Challenge! After losing all their money in a gambling spree, this daring couple is forced to participate in the notorious Squid Games. Your mission is to help them navigate through an array of intense musical challenges spread across various weeks.

As they dance their way through perilous levels, you'll face a set of demanding songs where timing and precision are crucial. Beware, as the stakes are high, and failing to keep up with the rhythm could result in dire consequences. With each completed level, they edge closer to reclaiming the money they lost a staggering prize of 38 million USD awaits the winner!

In Super Friday Squid Challenge, survival isn t just about avoiding failure; it s about mastering the dance moves. Hundreds of contestants are vying for the prize, but only those who can flawlessly hit every arrow without missing a beat will earn their chance at victory.

The game includes a total of 12 challenging levels, each designed to test your skills and reflexes while ensuring an exhilarating gaming experience. Prepare yourself for a blend of music and competition as you guide Boyfriend and Girlfriend through this crazy game filled with unpredictable twists and turns!

How to play free Super Friday Squid Challenge game online

To play Super Friday Squid Challenge, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to match the rhythms of the songs. Press the keys as arrows align with the markings on the screen. Keep an eye on the timing and stay focused to prevent missing any notes. Survive all 12 levels to win the grand prize!
