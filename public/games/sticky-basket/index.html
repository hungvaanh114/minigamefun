Sticky Basket

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Sticky Basket, a thrilling shooting game that puts your precision and aim to the test! In this engaging experience, you ll point your finger, take a deep breath, and shoot for the basket as you progress through an array of exciting levels. Each level offers a fresh challenge that encourages you to improve your shot accuracy and distance. Show off your skills as you strive to sink those baskets from ever-increasing distances, bringing you closer to your personal bests.

If you prefer a more leisurely pace, Sticky Basket has you covered with its relaxing training mode. Here, you can take your time to practice your shots, honing your technique by shooting freely from different ranges without the pressure of a timer or score. This mode allows you to increase your confidence and shooting skills while enjoying the satisfying feel of making perfect baskets. With each successful shot, you'll watch your abilities grow, preparing you for the challenges that lie ahead.

How to play free Sticky Basket game online

To play Sticky Basket, simply use your finger to aim at the hoop. Drag to adjust the angle and power of your shot, then release to see if you can make that basket! Keep track of your progress as you attempt longer distances, and switch to training mode anytime to practice your shots!
