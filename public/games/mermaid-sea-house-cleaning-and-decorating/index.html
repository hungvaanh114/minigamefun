Mermaid Sea House Cleaning and Decorating

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Mermaid Sea House Cleaning and Decorating, where you assist the lovely mermaid princess Zoe in revamping her stunning underwater palace! While her palace is already a sight to behold, Zoe feels it's time for a refreshing change. Today, she is determined to tidy up her beautiful abode and infuse it with new and delightful decorations.

As you dive into this magical adventure, get ready to help Zoe clean every inch of her palace. You'll need to gather up seaweed, remove unwanted debris, and organize her precious treasures. After sprucing up her rooms, step into the role of an interior designer by selecting fabulous decor items that reflect Zoe's unique style. From dazzling chandeliers made of seashells to vibrant coral furniture, the options are limitless!

But wait, Zoe can't start her decorating spree without a brand-new outfit! Help her transform into the mermaid of her dreams by choosing beautiful tails, flowing hair, and sparkling accessories. With your creative touch, Zoe's underwater palace will not only be clean but also radiate charm and elegance, making it a true underwater paradise.

So what are you waiting for? Join Zoe in this delightful cleaning and decorating escapade and unleash your imagination as you bring her vision to life!

How to play free Mermaid Sea House Cleaning And Decorating game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse to interact with the cleaning tools and decoration items. Click on the objects in Zoe's palace to clean them up and then browse through the decorative items to create a vibrant and stylish environment. Don t forget to customize Zoe s outfit to match her newly decorated home!
