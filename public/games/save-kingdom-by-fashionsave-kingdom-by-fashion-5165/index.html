In a whimsical world where style reigns supreme, a curious witch stumbled upon a mysterious magic ball. Little did she know that her curiosity would lead to chaos; upon breaking the ball, an enchanting but mischievous spell escaped, declaring, "Make all kingdoms that are not fashionable frozen!" As a result, Sofia s vibrant kingdom transformed into a glacial wasteland, leaving her as the sole survivor with her unique ice magic.

Determined to restore her beloved kingdom, Sofia embarks on an adventurous journey filled with fashion challenges. To save her realm, she must navigate through intricate levels, completing various fashion-themed tasks to collect three precious fashion crystals. Each crystal can only be acquired from one of three charming princes, each residing in a distinctly styled kingdom. As Sofia interacts with these princes, she must showcase her fashion sense, creativity, and problem-solving skills to impress and earn their crystals.

Join Sofia in her quest, where style meets survival! Will you help her defeat the witch's spell and bring color back to her kingdom? Get ready for a fashionable journey that challenges not just your style but also your strategic abilities. Collect outfits, unlock magical accessories, and use your unique ice powers to navigate through this enchanting yet frozen world!

How to play free Save Kingdom By Fashion game online

To play the game, navigate by clicking on different fashion challenges while collecting items along the way. Dress Sofia in various outfits by selecting clothes and accessories. Complete each challenge to earn the fashion crystals from the princes. Keep an eye on your time and rewards to maximize your score!
