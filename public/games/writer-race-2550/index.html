Writer Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Writer Race an exhilarating adventure in wordplay! In this captivating game, you ll embody the role of a lightning-fast scribe as you navigate through vibrant literary landscapes. Your challenge? Transform the narrative into something remarkable! With a limit of just 80 words, unleash your creativity to craft a story that entertains, intrigues, or surprises.

But time is of the essence! With a ticking clock, you must think quickly to produce the most engaging tale. Whether you're competing against friends or AI opponents, Writer Race will test your storytelling skills and speed. Will you be able to construct a narrative that stands out and earns you the title of a literary champion? This game promises to be a fun and thrilling way to enhance your writing prowess while engaging in friendly competition.

Get ready to embark on this journey towards literary greatness, where every second counts and every word matters. Put your skills to the test, and see who can conjure up the most compelling story in record time. Writer Race is not only a game; it's your gateway to becoming a master storyteller!

For more information and to start your adventure, check out the official website.

How to play free Writer Race game online

To begin your game of Writer Race, simply select whether you want to challenge friends or play against AI opponents. Once the game starts, read the prompt provided and begin writing your story using the 80-word limit. Strategically fasten your storytelling process as the timer counts down, and showcase your creativity before time runs out!
