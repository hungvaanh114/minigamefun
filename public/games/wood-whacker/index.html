Wood Whacker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Wood Whacker, an exhilarating game that challenges your lumberjack abilities as you chop wood and evade branches in a vibrant forest setting! While the mechanics may seem straightforward, truly mastering the game will prove to be quite a challenge. You'll find yourself quickly drawn in as you navigate through two dynamic environments and work to unlock a collection of 10 unique characters.

Get ready to grab your trusty axe and dash through the woods, chopping down trees as swiftly as possible! The excitement of Wood Whacker never fades, thanks to its engaging one-touch control system which allows for easy gameplay. With addictive mechanics reminiscent of classic arcade games, you'll find yourself wanting to return for more. Best of all, it's completely free to play! For more information and updates, visit the developer's website at https://www.giugames.com/.

How to play free Wood Whacker game online

To play Wood Whacker, simply tap the screen to chop wood as you progress through the forest. Be mindful of branches and obstacles that may obstruct your path. Aim to collect bonuses and unlock new characters to enhance your gaming experience. Enjoy the thrill of chopping and try to set a high score!
