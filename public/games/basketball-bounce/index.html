How to play free Basketball Bounce game online

To play Basketball Bounce, simply use your mouse or tap the screen to close the brick cage whenever the ball approaches. Keep a close eye on the bouncing ball to ensure it doesn t escape from the screen. Aim for quick reflexes and enjoy the thrilling gameplay!
