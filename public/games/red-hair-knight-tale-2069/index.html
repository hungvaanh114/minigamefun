Red Hair Knight Tale

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling journey in Red Hair Knight Tale, an exhilarating 2D medieval adventure that combines action and strategy. In this captivating swordman game, players are thrust into a world filled with formidable monsters and mystical landscapes. Your ultimate objective is to conquer each level by vanquishing these beasts and racing to the brown flag, which signifies your triumph.

Along the way, keep an eye out for gleaming gold coins! These treasures, while not essential for your progression, contribute to your total score and can be discovered in chests or dropped by defeated foes. The vibrant graphics and fluid animations immerse you in a fantasy realm where quick reflexes and strategic movement are crucial for survival. Prepare to face diverse enemies, each with unique abilities and challenges to test your skills.

To navigate the perilous terrain, you'll need to master the art of jumping. Utilize double jumps to reach higher platforms and uncover hidden areas filled with rewarding items. The game demands agility, as you must skillfully maneuver past impending threats while striving to complete each level. Be wary of the lurking monsters that aim to thwart your progress time your movements wisely to outrun them and secure your victory!

How to play free Red Hair Knight Tale game online

To play Red Hair Knight Tale, use the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Press the jump key to perform jumps and double jumps for higher platforms. Slash through enemies with your sword and collect coins along the way. Be quick to reach the brown flag before the monsters catch you!


Instructions

On PC AD or Left and Right Arrows move Space jump J attack On Mobile Tap buttons on the screen
