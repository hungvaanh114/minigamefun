Hammer Flight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Hammer Flight, where you engage in intense combat using various hammers. Your objective is to deliver the most effective strikes while minimizing damage to your barrel. Precision is essential with every swing as you face down formidable foes in exciting multiplayer arenas.

Gear up for exhilarating battles in Hammer Flight. With hourly and daily bonuses at your disposal, you can swiftly advance by accumulating the wealth you earn during confrontations. To climb the ranks within the competitive hierarchy, it is crucial to emerge victorious in every skirmish. Every match is a test of skill, where you ll strive to outmaneuver and outpace your opponents.

Enhance your warrior s abilities with a selection of 16 distinctive weapons, each offering unique mechanics that can shift the dynamics of battle. Additionally, there are 7 different barrels to choose from, allowing you to customize your gameplay experience. Strengthening your character with upgrades can provide a vital edge, although it s important to note that not all upgrades are guaranteed to succeed. Prepare yourself for fierce encounters as you refine your skills and choose your strategy wisely!

How to play free Hammer Flight game online

To play Hammer Flight, simply join an online match and select your hammer. Use the controls to aim and swing at your opponents while guarding your barrel. Focus on executing powerful hits and dodging enemy attacks to secure your victory. Remember to check for bonuses and upgrades throughout the game to enhance your performance!
