Nend.io

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Nend.io, a unique life simulation game where your choices shape your destiny! Here, you can either climb the social ladder by getting a job, or find yourself scavenging through trash. You can invest in a home or settle for a makeshift sleeping area. As you navigate this vibrant yet challenging world, don t forget the importance of legacy; purchasing a worthy successor from the nursery is critical, as time is not on your side!

In Nend.io, your journey to financial success involves various avenues. You can opt to work in a variety of jobs from a computer operator to a laboratory technician, or even a CEO in an office. Alternatively, you might take on cleaning duties, picking up litter or detritus, always on the lookout for stray cash on the ground. The crux of your income comes from investing in buildings; the more they are frequented by other players, the greater your earnings will be.

As you progress, upgrading your character is essential. Improve your skills such as your computer and broom skills, negotiation tactics, strength, speed, bag capacity, and research abilities to maximize your income, work efficiency, and lifestyle. Your character's well-being is also crucial; manage your mood, food, sleep, and bathroom needs wisely. Enjoy leisure activities like dancing, shopping for clothes, and indulging in snacks, but don t forget to keep your living situation secure by either renting a bed or sleeping in a squatting area.

How to play free Nend.io game online

Once in the game, start by choosing your occupation. Focus on completing jobs and cleaning tasks to earn money. Invest your earnings in properties to boost your income. Keep your character s needs met eat, sleep, and have fun to maintain optimal performance. Remember, managing your skills effectively will maximize your potential!
