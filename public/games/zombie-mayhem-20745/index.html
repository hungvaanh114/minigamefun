Zombie Mayhem

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Zombie Mayhem, where chaos reigns in a city overrun by the undead! You emerge as the sole survivor in a once-thriving town now filled with zombies lurking in every corner. With your heart racing and adrenaline pumping, you must summon all your survival instincts to fend off these grotesque creatures and protect your life.

As the last beacon of hope, you are tasked with exterminating waves of zombies that threaten your very existence. Armed with an array of weapons and the knowledge of the terrain, you will need to strategize your attacks and make quick decisions. Every challenge will push your limits as you navigate through desolate streets, abandoned buildings, and treacherous alleys, hunting down these ghoulish foes. It s a constant battle between life and death, where only the swift and smart will survive.

In addition to battling hordes of zombies, you ll have the opportunity to uncover hidden resources, collect power-ups, and upgrade your weaponry. Each encounter becomes more intense as waves of zombies become increasingly relentless. The more zombies you dispatch, the more skilled you become, enabling you to face greater threats in your quest for survival.

Are you ready to take on the challenge and prove your worth in Zombie Mayhem? The fate of the town relies on your strength and cunning. Gear up, aim true, and prepare to eliminate the undead menace stalking your streets!

How to play free Zombie Mayhem game online

To play Zombie Mayhem, use the arrow keys to move your character and the mouse to aim and shoot at the approaching zombies. Collect power-ups and ammunition for better weapons as you explore the city. Survive each wave by strategically eliminating zombies and avoid getting cornered!
