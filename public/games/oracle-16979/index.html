Oracle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the captivating world of Oracle, where you join forces with remarkable heroes: Harold Hammer, Diana Driver, Wanda Wrench, and Samuel Saw. As a player, you will embark on a thrilling quest to navigate and conquer dungeons filled with challenging puzzles and menacing foes. Each hero brings unique abilities to the table, offering you the chance to strategize and devise clever solutions to overcome obstacles. Engage your mind and tap into your inner Oracle as you lead your heroes to victory!

In this exciting strategy puzzle game, all of the heroes move in sync, making every decision critical to your success. You'll need to think several steps ahead, predicting enemy movements and positioning your heroes effectively to thwart attacks. The intricately designed dungeons are fraught with cunning traps and foes that will test your strategic acumen, so stay sharp and adapt as you progress. Your ultimate goal is to clear the puzzles and emerge as the true Oracle!

Oracle not only challenges your intellect but also immerses you in a captivating fantasy setting with stunning visuals and enchanting sound design. Collect rewards and unlock new levels as you delve deeper into the dungeon, all while collaborating with your heroes to unravel the mysteries that lie ahead. Can you outsmart the dungeon and prove yourself worthy of the Oracle title? The adventure awaits!

How to play free Oracle game online

To play Oracle online, begin by selecting your heroes and familiarizing yourself with their abilities. As you enter the dungeon, observe the layout and enemy positions. Click on your heroes to move them strategically, keeping in mind that they all move together. Solve puzzles and defeat enemies to progress through the levels!
