Fruit Slicer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Fruit Slicer, where swift reflexes and sharp focus are your best allies! In this engaging game, you'll find yourself amidst a flurry of colorful fruits appearing on your screen. Your primary task is to slice these fruits with precision and agility: the more you slice, the higher your score. However, beware if any of the fruits slip past you and fall off the screen, you will lose a life. Accumulate three lost lives and the game comes to an end!

But that s not all! Stay on guard for the sneaky bombs that can appear among the fruits. These explosive hazards will also take away a life if you slice them, so be careful. The thrilling challenge of Fruit Slicer keeps you on your toes as you try to balance slicing fruits and avoiding bombs!

The game features three distinct modes: ARCADE for fast-paced fun, ZEN for a more relaxed experience, and FENETIC for when you want to test your limits. Gather your friends and challenge each other to see who can achieve the highest score. Who will emerge as the ultimate Fruit Slicer champion?

How to play free Fruit Slicer game online

To play Fruit Slicer, simply use your mouse or finger (on touchscreen devices) to swipe across the screen and slice the flying fruits. Aim to cut as many fruits as you can while dodging bombs. Keep an eye on your life count to avoid losing the game!


Instructions

Mobile drag your finger across the screen to cut the fruits PC hold down the left mouse button and drag the arrow across the screen to cut the fruits
