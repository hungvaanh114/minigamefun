Subway Surfers Singapore 2022

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The beloved world tour of Subway Surfers makes its exciting return to Singapore in 2022! Get ready to immerse yourself in the vibrant streets of this bustling city, filled with lively shops and cozy cafes. Glide through the urban landscape, dodging obstacles as you race between towering skyscrapers and along the stunning Orchard Road.

Your adventure wouldn t be complete without a trip to the iconic Merlion Park, where you can witness the spectacular sight of the famous lion statue. Experience the colorful charm of Chinatown, with its alluring markets and delectable food stalls waiting to be explored. Don t miss the chance to admire the breathtaking Bay East Garden, a paradise adorned with tranquil ponds teeming with ivy and lilies.

As you navigate the lively Clark Quay promenade, you ll enjoy some of the best views in the city, making your journey even more exhilarating. "Subway Surfers: Singapore" is a classic endless runner game where your reflexes will be put to the test. Avoid trains, trams, and various obstacles as you strive to run as far as possible in this endless pursuit of adrenaline and fun. Good luck out there!

How to play free subway surfers singapore 2022 game online

To play Subway Surfers Singapore 2022, simply swipe up to jump, swipe down to roll, and swipe left or right to change lanes. Your goal is to dodge trains and obstacles while collecting coins and power-ups to enhance your run. Stay alert and enjoy the immersive environment as you aim for a high score!
