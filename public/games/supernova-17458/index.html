Supernova

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Supernova is an exhilarating skill-based game that tests your agility and reflexes as you navigate through a vibrant universe in your spacecraft. Each level offers a unique challenge set against a backdrop of stunning visuals, immersing players in a colorful array of procedurally generated environments. As you fly your ship, you'll encounter a variety of objects and obstacles that you must skillfully avoid to advance through the game. With each successful dodge, your speed steadily increases, elevating the difficulty and excitement with every passing moment.

The game also features an impressive selection of vehicles for players to unlock, each with its own unique characteristics, allowing for different strategies to master the increasingly complex stages. With its captivating graphics and adrenaline-pumping gameplay, Supernova keeps players engaged as they aim to beat their previous scores and establish an unbreakable streak. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to skill games, Supernova promises an entertaining experience filled with challenges and bursts of color.

Prepare for the ultimate test of precision and endurance as you take on the fast-paced action of Supernova. The thrill of dodging obstacles will keep you coming back for more, as you strive to improve your skills and conquer the ever-changing landscape of this mesmerizing game.

How to play free Supernova game online

To play Supernova, simply use your mouse or arrow keys to maneuver your ship through the colorful stages. Avoid collisions with oncoming obstacles while keeping an eye on your increasing speed. The objective is to navigate past as many objects as possible to achieve a high score!
