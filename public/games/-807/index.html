How to play free Slinky Sort Puzzle game online

To play Slinky Sort Puzzle, simply tap a slinky to select it, then choose another empty space or a matching color stack to move it. The goal is to group all the colors together within the designated slots. Plan your moves wisely to avoid any blockages as you progress to higher levels!
