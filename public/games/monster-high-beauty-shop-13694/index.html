Monster High Beauty Shop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Monster High Beauty Shop, where your favorite ghoulish characters are eager to unleash their creativity and transform their clients into fabulous fashionistas! In this vibrant salon, the stylish Monster High girls are ready to take your hair, makeup, and outfit to the next level. Each character has a unique style, and it s your role to help them shine by offering advice and ensuring each client leaves the shop looking stunning.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter a diverse array of customers, each with distinct beauty needs. Whether it s a spooky updo, glamorous makeup, or a stylish outfit, let your imagination run wild! Help the Monster High girls design a variety of looks by mixing and matching colors, accessories, and hairstyles. With their creative flair combined with your fashion expertise, you ll make every customer feel like a true star.

The more customers you assist, the more experience you gain, allowing you to unlock exclusive styles and beauty items. Enjoy this fun-filled day of fashion alongside beloved characters like Draculaura, Cleo de Nile, and Frankie Stein. Engage in delightful customization and watch how your styling choices impact their confidence and happiness. Get ready to indulge in this magical beauty adventure!

How to play free Monster High Beauty Shop game online

To play the Monster High Beauty Shop game, simply click on the character you want to style. Then, navigate through the various beauty tools available, such as hair products, makeup palettes, and clothing options. Select your desired styles and mix different elements to create stunning looks for each customer. Have fun experimenting!
