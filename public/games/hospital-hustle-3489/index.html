Hospital Hustle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hospital Hustle is an engaging management simulation game that immerses you in the hectic world of hospital care. As the head of this bustling medical facility, your primary responsibility is to ensure that every patient receives the care they need. This involves accepting patients, diagnosing their conditions, and utilizing different medical machines to cure them effectively. The challenge lies not only in managing patient flow but also in ensuring you have enough resources to keep your machines operational.

To run your hospital smoothly, you can hire various staff members, including doctors, nurses, and receptionists. Each role is essential, and you can enhance their skills and capacities through upgrades, ultimately improving the level of care your patients receive. Keeping your staff energized and efficient will contribute greatly to the happiness of your patients, which is tracked by a happiness bar displayed at the top of the screen. Your goal is to maintain this bar at maximum levels, reflecting your success in managing the hospital.

With colorful graphics and fast-paced gameplay, Hospital Hustle offers a delightful challenge for those who enjoy strategy and time management. As you progress, you'll face new challenges and scenarios that test your decision-making skills, making for an exciting gaming experience.

How to play free Hospital Hustle game online

To play Hospital Hustle, begin by accepting incoming patients and assessing their needs. Use your resources wisely to operate various medical machines and treat patients accordingly. Hire staff members to enhance your hospital's efficiency, and continually upgrade their skills to keep the happiness bar filled. Manage your time and resources effectively to build a successful hospital!


Instructions

Player 1 WASD or Arrow Keys For movement Player 2 Arrow Keys For Movement
