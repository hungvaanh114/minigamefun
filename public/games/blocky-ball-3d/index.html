Blocky Ball 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Blocky Ball 3D is an engaging puzzle adventure that challenges players to navigate a colorful world filled with obstacles and brain teasers. In this 3D experience, your main objective is to guide a rolling ball to its exit point. To do this, you will be required to strategically move wooden disc blocks to create the optimal path. Timing is essential, as you must complete the levels within a specified duration.

The game features a plethora of levels over 200 in total ensuring endless fun and mental stimulation. As you progress through the stages, you will encounter various challenges and puzzles that will test your critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Collecting stars scattered throughout the levels not only increases your score but also provides valuable hints to help you navigate the tricky paths.

However, some marble discs present in specific sections are fixed and cannot be moved, adding an extra layer of complexity to your planning. Each stage demands not just speed but also strategy, as you will need to analyze the best moves, maximize your efficiency, and successfully guide the ball to safety while collecting stars along the way.

How to play free Blocky Ball 3D game online

To play Blocky Ball 3D, simply click and drag the wooden blocks to construct a path for the ball. Observe the layout, identify the fixed discs, and strategize your moves carefully to reach the exit within the time limit. Gather stars as you progress for hints and score boosts!
