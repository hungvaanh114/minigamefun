Paint Tomato

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Paint Tomato is a vibrant grid puzzle game where you match tomato colors to adjacent ones. Use strategic moves to swiftly mirror neighboring tomatoes. The urgency intensifies with a ticking bomb, pushing your quick-thinking skills. Plan carefully to transform the grid within the countdown.
