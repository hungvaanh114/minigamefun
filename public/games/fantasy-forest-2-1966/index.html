Fantasy Forest 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Fantasy Forest 2, an exciting HTML5 match logic game that challenges your puzzle-solving skills. With an impressive array of 100 unique levels, this game offers an engaging experience that will keep players entertained for hours on end. Your objective is to collect a specific quantity of three different types of blocks within each level.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter increasing difficulty, requiring you to think strategically about your moves. Be mindful of the rules: you can leave no more than one extra block behind after completing your objective. The game features an in-game store, which serves as a valuable resource, allowing you to purchase power-ups and helpful items that will aid you in conquering more challenging puzzles.

Each level is beautifully designed, bringing the fantasy theme to life as you match blocks and solve intricate arrangements. The vibrant graphics and captivating sound effects enhance your playing experience, inviting you deeper into the mystical forest. So, gear up for a journey filled with puzzles and enjoy the magic of Fantasy Forest 2!

How to play free Fantasy Forest 2 game online

To play Fantasy Forest 2, start by selecting a level from your menu. Match blocks of the same type by clicking or tapping on them to combine and collect them. Ensure that your total matches the goal for that level while leaving no more than one block over, and utilize the store to acquire helpful upgrades as needed!
