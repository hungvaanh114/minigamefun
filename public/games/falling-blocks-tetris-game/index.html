Falling Blocks - Tetris Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Falling Blocks Tetris Game" is an engaging and nostalgic puzzle game that takes inspiration from the legendary TETRIS. In this game, players must skillfully maneuver falling blocks as they cascade down the screen. The primary objective is to align and complete as many horizontal lines as possible, clearing them to score points and prevent the blocks from stacking too high.

As the game progresses, the speed and frequency of the falling blocks increase, adding a layer of excitement and challenge. Players must think quickly and strategically decide where to place each block to create full rows. The bright colors and simple mechanics make it accessible for players of all ages, while the competitive aspect keeps you coming back for more.

Experience endless fun, whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the genre! Challenge your friends or strive for a personal best score. How to play free Falling Blocks - Tetris Game game online

To play "Falling Blocks Tetris Game," use the arrow keys to move and rotate the falling blocks. The down arrow speeds up the descent, while the left and right arrows shift the blocks horizontally. Once a horizontal line is completed, it will disappear, giving you valuable points and more space to continue the game!
