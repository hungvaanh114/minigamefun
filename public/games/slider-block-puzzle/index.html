How to play free Slider Block Puzzle game online

To begin playing Slider Block Puzzle, simply swipe the blocks on the grid to move them. When two blocks with the same number come into contact, they will merge into a single block with a higher number. Your goal is to continue merging blocks until you reach the highest value possible. Enjoy the challenge and aim for the top!


Instructions

- Slide the same numbers in any of the eight directions down left right or diagonally to merge them - Get higher numbers when you merge multiple same numbers together - Keep merging numbers to get the highest number possible
