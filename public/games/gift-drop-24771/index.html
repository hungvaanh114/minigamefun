Gift Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Help Santa make his grand escape from the dark, foreboding forest in the thrilling puzzle game, Gift Drop! In this engaging experience, you ll tackle 60 meticulously designed levels filled with fun and challenging physics-based puzzles. Your mission is to clear away all boxes, except for the grass, which serves as Santa s route to freedom.

To complete each level, simply click or tap on the various boxes or blocks to eliminate them from the playing field. Each decision counts strategically using the remaining blocks will be key to guiding the precious gift drop safely onto the grass. As you progress, the complexities increase, making it essential to think creatively and solve puzzles efficiently. Will you be able to lead Santa out of the prison and into the cheerful world beyond?

Gift Drop combines enjoyable gameplay mechanics with captivating visuals, ensuring hours of fun for players of all ages. Challenge your puzzle-solving skills while enjoying the festive spirit that surrounds the beloved character of Santa Claus. It s time to put your strategy to the test and help Santa reclaim his freedom in this delightful physics puzzle adventure!

How to play free Gift Drop game online

To play Gift Drop, begin by clicking or tapping on the boxes to destroy them strategically. Make sure to clear a path for the gift drop to land safely on the grass. Use the remaining blocks creatively to solve puzzles in each of the 60 challenging levels!
