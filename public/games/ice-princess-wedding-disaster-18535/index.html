Ice Princess Wedding Disaster

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Elsa and Jack in the enchanting adventure of their wedding day in "Ice Princess Wedding Disaster." Elsa has been dreaming about her magical wedding for months, anticipating a day filled with love and joy. However, Jack, in his playful spirit, decided to add an unexpected surprise to their cake a balloon! This light-hearted prank quickly turns disastrous when Elsa cuts into the wedding cake, resulting in an explosion of icing and a mess that ruins both her beautiful makeup and elegant wedding dress.

Now, it's up to you to assist Elsa as she navigates through this wedding catastrophe. She needs your help to freshen up and choose a stunning new wedding dress that will make her shine on her special day. Explore the whimsical bridal boutique filled with gorgeous outfits, accessories, and beauty products that will ensure Elsa looks absolutely breathtaking, despite the mishap.

Your mission is to rescue Elsa s dream wedding and help her step down the aisle with confidence. With your creativity and stylish instincts, transform her look and restore the glamour of her celebration. Every detail matters! Choose the perfect dress, add charming accessories, and refresh her makeup to turn this disaster into a memorable moment. Can you help Elsa save her wedding?

How to play free Ice Princess Wedding Disaster game online

To play the game, start by selecting the different dress-up options available for Elsa. Use your mouse or finger to choose her new wedding gown, hairstyle, and makeup. Once you have styled Elsa to perfection, help her navigate through various mini-challenges that will involve cleaning up the mess and preparing for the ceremony. Make decisions quickly to ensure her big day goes off without a hitch!
