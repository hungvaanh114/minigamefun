Plane War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Plane War, an exhilarating shooting action game that puts you directly in the midst of intense aerial combat. In this high-octane adventure, you will engage in fierce dogfights against enemy planes as you navigate hostile skies. Your mission is to obliterate as many adversaries as possible while advancing deep into enemy territory.

Throughout your journey, you'll have the opportunity to collect an array of powerful weapons, fuel resources, and life enhancements to keep your aircraft in optimal fighting condition. Each successful mission will reward you with the tools necessary to upgrade your performance and increase your combat effectiveness. However, be on high alert for incoming enemy planes and their relentless bullets, as the battlefield is filled with danger and surprises.

Pilot your aircraft with precision and speed, strategically dodging enemy fire while positioning yourself to launch your own attacks. The further you venture into enemy turf, the greater the challenge will become. Make each shot count and utilize the power-ups you collect to ensure your survival in this relentless aerial war. Are you ready to take to the skies and prove your dominance in the tumultuous world of Plane War?

How to play free Plane War game online

To play the Plane War game online, use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D to control your plane's movements. Aim with your mouse to target enemy planes, and click to shoot. Collect pickups for weapons and health, and avoid incoming enemy fire to survive and advance deeper into enemy territory.
