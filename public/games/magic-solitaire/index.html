How to play free Magic Solitaire game online

To play Magic Solitaire online, simply stack cards in descending order while alternating colors. You can move cards between piles, and the objective is to clear all cards from the table by creating four foundation piles in ascending order. Use your fairy helper's suggestions for optimal moves!
