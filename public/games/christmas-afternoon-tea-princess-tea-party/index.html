Join Sofia and Zoe in the enchanting world of Christmas Afternoon Tea - Princess Tea Party! Dive into the game to discover an array of tempting recipes and seasonal delicacies that will get your festive spirit soaring.

In the game, you'll start by selecting ingredients and tools for each dessert. Follow guided recipes to prepare your treats, then set the table by choosing beautiful dishes and decorations. Once everything is ready, enjoy a delightful tea time with Sofia and Zoe, savoring the lovely atmosphere you've created!
