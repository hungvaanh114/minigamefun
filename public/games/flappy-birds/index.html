Flappy Birds

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Flappy Birds is a captivating and surprisingly addictive mobile game that challenges players with its simple yet frustratingly difficult mechanics. The core concept revolves around navigating a small bird through a series of pipes, which serve as obstacles. The game s simplicity is what makes it so accessible to all ages; players need only tap their device s screen to make the bird flap its wings and soar upward. With each tap, players must time their movements perfectly to avoid crashing into the pipes lining each side of the game s screen. The objective is clear: fly as far as possible without hitting any walls.

The charm of Flappy Birds lies in its minimalistic design and straightforward gameplay. As you guide the bird through the increasingly challenging gaps in the pipes, a score tracker keeps count of your successful flights. Every pipe you successfully pass increases your score, leading to an exhilarating rush as you try to beat your previous record. However, beware; just a single miscalculation can lead to a quick and infuriating demise! The game's simplistic graphics and easy-to-understand controls draw players into its world, while its high level of difficulty keeps them coming back for more, attempting to master the flying technique required for success.

How to play free Flappy Birds game online

To play Flappy Birds online, simply access the game on your web browser. Once the game starts, tap anywhere on your screen to make the bird flap upwards. Keep tapping rhythmically to navigate through the gaps between the pipes without crashing into them. Aim for the highest score by traveling as far as possible while avoiding obstacles!
