Poppy Strike 4

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Poppy Strike 4 is an exhilarating horror shooter game that plunges players into a chilling world set within a haunted toy factory. As you navigate through the eerie corridors and dimly lit rooms, your ultimate goal is to find the exit on each level while facing terrifying adversaries. The game s main antagonists, the Huggy Wuggy dolls, lurk around every corner, ready to pounce on unwary players. Armed with your trusty weaponry, you must aim true and shoot them down before they get too close. The suspense builds with every level you conquer, as the factory reveals darker secrets and increasingly formidable foes.

This game deftly balances heart-pounding action with strategic gameplay. Each level presents new challenges, including traps and puzzles, that further elevate the tension. Players must utilize their shooting skills to fend off the Huggy Wuggy creatures while also being vigilant about their surroundings. With remarkable graphics and engaging sound design, Poppy Strike 4 immerses players in a nightmarish yet thrilling environment that keeps them on the edge of their seat. Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled adventure as you delve deeper into the factory and confront the haunting whispers of its past.

How to play free Poppy Strike 4 game online

Once you start playing Poppy Strike 4, use your mouse to aim and click to shoot. Move your character using the WASD keys, and explore each level carefully while keeping an eye out for hidden exits. Collect power-ups to enhance your shooting abilities and stay alert for enemy movements!
