Head Volleyball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to spike, serve, and score in the exhilarating world of Head Volleyball! This unique take on volleyball condenses the action into an engaging head sports game where your main goal is to outscore your opponents. Taking a simplified approach, the game is accessible and fun for players of all ages.

Before you jump into action, you have the opportunity to choose and customize your character, giving you the chance to express your individual style on the court. Tailor your character s appearance and adjust other gameplay features such as the playground setting, atmospheric conditions, AI difficulty level, and match duration. All these options enhance your playing experience, ensuring every match feels fresh and exciting.

Control your character with just four buttons! Use two keys to maneuver around the court, while another allows you to leap into the air to intercept the ball. The last button lets you unleash your kicking abilities, striking the ball with either your foot or head to make those crucial plays against your opponents. As you practice and refine your skills, you'll soon be racking up scores and dominating the matches!

How to play free Head Volleyball game online

To play Head Volleyball, use the arrow keys to move your character across the court. Press the jump key to elevate and intercept incoming balls, while the kick button allows you to strike the ball towards your opponent's side. Aim for strategic shots to secure your victory!
