999

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of 999, you are entrusted with the critical responsibility of delivering your patient to the hospital promptly. Every second counts in this exhilarating challenge as you navigate through traffic-filled streets, ensuring that your passenger reaches safety. To enhance your journey, be on the lookout for various power-ups that can give you a much-needed boost and increase your speed. However, you must remain vigilant; the road is fraught with obstacles and other distracted drivers who can impede your progress.

As you maneuver through the busy streets, you will encounter numerous challenges that test your reflexes and decision-making skills. Each successful delivery brings you one step closer to mastering the art of swift and safe driving. However, if you collide with other vehicles or fail to dodge the numerous hazards scattered across the road, you risk sustaining damage that could end your missions abruptly. Balancing speed with caution is key to ensuring a successful delivery.

Engage in this captivating experience where strategic driving and quick thinking are essential. Will you be the hero your patient needs and arrive at the hospital before it s too late? Join the race and find out if you have what it takes to conquer the roads in 999.

How to play free 999 game online

To play the game, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your vehicle. Collect power-ups to gain speed boosts and navigate around obstacles while avoiding collisions with other vehicles. Keep an eye on the timer to ensure you reach the hospital in time!


Instructions

Accelerate with upward arrow key or space bar other arrow keys to move left right and backwards Tablet or smartphone - upper or lower left side of the screen to move forwards backwards and centre left far right side of the screen to steer
