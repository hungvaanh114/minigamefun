Bakery Protection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bakery Protection is an exhilarating online video game that immerses players into a chaotic world plagued by relentless hordes of ravenous zombies. These undead creatures have a singular goal: to feast on the scrumptious confections crafted in your bakery! As the player, you take on the heroic role of the last remaining baker, charged with the mission of defending your beloved bakery from these voracious attackers.

Set against a grim post-apocalyptic backdrop, where a viral outbreak has turned most of the populace into mindless zombies, your resourcefulness and strategy will be put to the ultimate test. Each wave of grotesque enemies poses a new threat, demanding that you sharpen your skills and implement clever tactics. Utilize a diverse arsenal of weapons and defensive measures to thwart the endless advance of zombies and protect your precious baked goods.

As you navigate through various levels, you will face an intriguing array of challenges and unique strains of zombies, from nimble runners to formidable bosses that require unparalleled prowess to defeat. The game features captivating gameplay and thrilling mechanics that combine to create a satisfyingly addictive experience. Get ready to construct formidable defenses, set ingenious traps, and unleash devastating attacks to keep those flesh-eating foes at bay. Will you rise to the occasion and safeguard your bakery from the imminent apocalypse? Your adventure awaits!

How to play free Bakery Protection game online

To play Bakery Protection, simply start the game and familiarize yourself with the controls. Use the mouse to aim and select your defenses, while strategically placing traps and utilizing weapons to fend off the incoming waves of zombies. Remember to upgrade your defenses to enhance your chances of survival!


Instructions

WASD - Move LMB - Shoot RMB - Aim Mouse Wheel - Change weapon R - Reload Space - Jump G - Grenade
