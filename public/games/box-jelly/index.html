Box Jelly

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Box Jelly, where your mission is to guide adorable jellyfish to safety! In this exciting puzzle adventure, you'll encounter a variety of cute jellyfish, each with its unique color and traits. Your objective is to navigate them to the designated rescue area while managing their distinct behaviors. Some jellyfish may require a gentle push to advance, while others may surprise you by swimming backward!

As you maneuver through each level, you'll need to strategize and think critically to overcome obstacles and direct the jellyfish effectively. Time is of the essence! The quicker you solve each puzzle, the more chances you have to collect coveted 3 gold starfish for your impressive performance. Get ready to dive into an ocean of challenges and fun as you aim for a perfect score!

How to play free Box Jelly game online

To play Box Jelly, use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the jellyfish. Push or guide them toward the rescue area while considering their individual behaviors. Keep an eye on the timer to enhance your chances of earning the gold starfish as you successfully complete each level.
