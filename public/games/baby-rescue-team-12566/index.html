Baby Rescue Team

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the adventure as a volunteer in the heartwarming game, Baby Rescue Team, where your mission is to rescue injured animals stranded in the forest. Team up with a cute little panda as you navigate through challenging terrains to save 6 different species, including a majestic tiger, a laid-back sloth, a striped zebra, and even playful penguins. Your journey begins by identifying the locations of these injured creatures.

Once you ve spotted them, hop into your rescue truck and drive to their habitats to bring them back to the safety of the rescue station. Each animal comes with its own unique set of ailments that you'll need to examine closely. As you assess their conditions, you ll perform operations tailored to their specific needs, ensuring they receive the best care possible.

Post-surgery, it's time to pamper the healed pets! Feed them their favorite treats, ensuring they regain their strength and vitality. You'll continue your mission by re-examining each animal, confirming they are fit for release back into their natural habitats. After giving them the care they deserve, your responsibilities don t end there you ll also clean their quarters at the rescue station to maintain a nurturing environment.

Are you ready to make a difference and enjoy endless fun? Step into the world of Baby Rescue Team and experience the joy of animal rescue!

How to play free Baby Rescue Team game online

To play Baby Rescue Team, use your mouse or touchscreen to drive the truck to the injured animals' locations. Follow on-screen prompts to examine, operate, and care for the animals. After treatments, interact with the animals to feed them and ensure they are ready for release. Don't forget to maintain cleanliness in the rescue station!
