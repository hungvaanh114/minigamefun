Flappy Family

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the world of Flappy Family! Here, you ll embark on an exhilarating adventure where you flap your tiny wings and navigate through a vibrant yet challenging Flappy World. The goal is simple: steer clear of the notorious pipes that obstruct your path while soaring through beautiful landscapes. With its simple controls, Flappy Family is accessible for players of all ages, making it a perfect game for family fun.

One of the standout features of Flappy Family is the exhilarating multiplayer mode, allowing you to compete against friends on the same device. Gather your loved ones and see who can fly the longest while dodging those pesky pipes. But that s not all! As you hone your skills, you ll have the opportunity to unlock up to 7 unique characters, each bringing their own flair and style to your flapping journey. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, the competition is sure to be fierce as everyone vies for the highest score.

Flappy Family brings joy and challenge together, as while it is easy to learn, mastering it will put your reflexes and concentration to the test. The vibrant visuals and fun gameplay create an engaging experience that will keep you coming back for more. So, get ready to flap, dodge, and soar through the skies in this delightful adventure!

How to play free Flappy Family game online

To play Flappy Family, simply tap on your screen to make your character flap its wings. Keep tapping to maintain altitude while navigating through the pipes. Your objective is to fly as far as you can without colliding with any obstacles. Collect points and unlock new characters as you progress!

The faster you tap the higher you go Each tap represents a wing flap and higher flight Once you stop you drop towards the ground
