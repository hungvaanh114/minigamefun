Touba

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Touba is an engaging 2D platformer that puts you in control of a vibrant yellow bird navigating through a colorful and challenging world. Your main objective is to collect bowls of grains scattered across the levels while skillfully dodging hostile enemy birds and hazardous spikes. Each stage presents unique obstacles that increase in difficulty, ensuring an exciting experience as you progress through the game.

With a total of eight levels, Touba offers a mix of stunning graphics and fun gameplay mechanics. As you glide your way through lush landscapes and perilous platforms, you'll need to be quick and strategic to reach your destination the bird's nest at the end of each level. Mastering the game's mechanics while avoiding opponents will keep you on your toes, making each victory all the more rewarding as you unlock the next challenge.

The game's charming visuals and intuitive controls provide an enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Whether you're new to platformers or a seasoned veteran, Touba invites you to experience its thrilling levels filled with exciting twists and turns. Will you gather all the bowls of grains and soar your way to victory? Jump into Touba and find out!

How to play free Touba game online

To play Touba online, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your bird character. Navigate through the levels by jumping over spikes and avoiding enemy birds. Collect all the bowls of grains before reaching the nest to complete each stage and progress to the next. Have fun and good luck!
