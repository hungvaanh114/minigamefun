Connect the Insects

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Connect The Insects is a captivating puzzle game that draws inspiration from the well-loved Chinese tile removal game, Mahjong. This engaging game challenges players to clear the board by eliminating all the tiles present. To achieve this, players must connect identical tiles, but with a twist: the connections cannot exceed two turns. This rule introduces an added layer of strategy as you meticulously plan each move to optimize your chances of clearing the board.

Featuring 21 exciting and increasingly difficult levels, Connect The Insects offers a perfect blend of challenge and enjoyment. As you progress through the game, you'll need to think critically and quickly to eliminate tiles before time runs out. Completing a level within the allotted time not only gives you a sense of accomplishment but also rewards you with bonus points, enhancing your overall score and gameplay experience.

The beautifully designed graphics and vibrant insect-themed tiles add an eye-catching aspect to the game, making it visually appealing for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle solver or just looking for a fun way to pass the time, Connect The Insects guarantees a captivating experience that promises to keep you engaged level after level. So, gear up for this exciting adventure, strategize your moves wisely, and see how far you can go in this intricate game of connection!

How to play free Connect The Insects game online

To play Connect The Insects, simply click on two identical tiles to connect them. Remember, each connection can only have a maximum of two turns. Keep an eye on the timer, and aim to complete each level as quickly as possible to earn extra bonuses!
