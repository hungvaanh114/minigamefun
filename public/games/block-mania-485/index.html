How to play free Block Mania game online

To play Block Mania, simply drag and drop blocks into the designated areas on the grid. Arrange the blocks strategically to form complete lines, which will then disappear for points. Keep an eye on the upcoming blocks and plan your moves to maximize your score with each turn. Enjoy the challenge!
