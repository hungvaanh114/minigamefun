Among Us Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Among Us Match 3, an engaging and entertaining match-three puzzle game that immerses you in the whimsical universe of your favorite crewmates! Your goal is simple: align blocks of similar colors in a row of three or more to earn points and aim for the highest score possible. As you navigate through various colorful levels, you ll encounter increasingly challenging puzzles that test your strategic thinking and quick reflexes.

However, there's a catch! You must keep a close eye on the scale on the left side of the screen. If it drops below a certain point, the game will come to an abrupt end. So, while you strategize to create longer combos and maximize your score, you also need to be vigilant to ensure the scale stays balanced. Dive into the captivating world of Among Us Match 3 and enjoy hours of fun as you play against the clock and challenge yourself with every round!

How to play free Among Us Match 3 game online

To get started with Among Us Match 3, simply click to swap adjacent blocks and create matches of three or more. Keep an eye on the scale as you play, and aim to create chain reactions for bonus points. The more matches you make, the faster you ll gather points, so stay sharp and have fun!
