PRINCESS COLLEGE BEAUTY CONTEST

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of Princess College, where glamour and rivalry intertwine in the exciting new game, PRINCESS COLLEGE BEAUTY CONTEST! Join the beloved characters Jasmine Belle and Rapunzel as they embark on a fashionable quest that transforms friendship into spirited competition. In this interactive game, players are tasked with helping these princesses prepare for a highly-anticipated beauty contest.

Each princess has her own unique style and personality, so it s your job to create an extraordinary look tailored just for them. Dive into an expansive wardrobe filled with stunning dresses, stylish shoes, and dazzling jewelry. But the fun doesn't stop there! You also have the opportunity to experiment with makeup, allowing you to enhance each character's beauty in creative ways. Whether you prefer a classic look or wish to break the mold with something bold and trendy, the choice is entirely yours.

As your princesses showcase their best selves on the runway, keep an eye on their rivalry - will the bonds of friendship withstand the pressure of competition? Get creative and help each princess shine in her own right as they vie for the coveted prize. The path to victory is filled with excitement, fashion choices, and a delightful dose of fun!

How to play free PRINCESS COLLEGE BEAUTY CONTEST game online

When you enter PRINCESS COLLEGE BEAUTY CONTEST, navigate through the menu to select your preferred princess. Use the available styling tools to pick outfits, accessories, and makeup. Click and drag to dress your princess, experimenting with different combinations until you achieve the perfect look. Once done, watch them dazzle in the contest!
