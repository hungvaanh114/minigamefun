Draw to Crush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In this game, eggs have appeared that you will have to fight with and break. In front of you on the screen you will see a platform on which the egg will be located. Above it you will see a special field. Using the mouse you can draw any object on it. When you finish your actions, this item will fall directly onto the egg. If your calculations are correct, then you will break it and thus destroy it. After this you will move to the next level of the game.

Instructions

Mouse click or tap to play
