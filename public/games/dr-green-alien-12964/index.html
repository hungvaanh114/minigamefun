Dr Green Alien

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey with Dr. Green Alien, a captivating space adventure featuring a charming yet resilient alien doctor dedicated to cleaning up toxic waste! In this vibrant universe, players join forces with Dr. Green as he sets off to collect hazardous barrels that litter the cosmic landscape. With his radiant green hue and out-of-this-world personality, he s on a mission to ensure the galaxy remains free from pollution.

Your objective is simple yet challenging: navigate the treacherous terrain and strategically gather the toxic waste barrels. However, beware! These barrels are not only harmful but also pose a significant risk to your journey. Each barrel collected adds to your score, but you must be cunning in your approach. Gather as many barrels as you can, keeping an eye on the toxic levels as you make your way to the finish line. Time is of the essence, so quick reflexes and sharp decision-making are essential to avoid the dangers that lie ahead.

Join Dr. Green Alien in this thrilling adventure where your skills will be tested. Can you outsmart the obstacles and collect all the barrels, proving that even alien doctors can be heroes? With stunning graphics and engaging gameplay, you'll be mesmerized by the vibrant world as you work hand-in-hand with Dr. Green. Step up to the challenge and make a significant contribution to the galaxy's health!

How to play free Dr Green Alien game online

To play, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate through the levels. Collect barrels while avoiding toxic hazards. Keep an eye on your score and try to reach the finish line while gathering as many barrels as possible for the highest points. Good luck!
