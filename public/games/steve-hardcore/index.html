Steve Hardcore

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to dive into the thrilling world of Steve Hardcore, where every moment counts! Step into the shoes of Steve, who embarks on a perilous journey filled with vibrant green monsters lurking at every turn. Your mission is to collect all the scattered obsidian pieces to unlock the secrets hidden deep within the game.

As you race against time and enemies, you must gather each obsidian piece and make your way towards the ominous black hole. Once you've collected all the pieces, your next goal is to safely traverse through the black hole and find the portal nestled away in the cave. However, this isn't just a leisurely stroll; you're playing in hardcore mode!

In hardcore mode, you have only three lives. Every encounter could be your last, so tread carefully. If you happen to perish more than three times, it means starting from square one, as all progress will be wiped clean. The stakes are high, and every decision you make can lead you closer to victory or back to the starting point.

How to play free Steve Hardcore game online

To play the game, use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate Steve through the environment. Collect obsidian pieces while avoiding or defeating green monsters. Stay alert, as the black hole will appear once you've gathered all pieces. Proceed through the black hole and follow the path leading to the portal remember, conserving your three lives is crucial for progress!
