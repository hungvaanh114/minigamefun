Ice Scream: Horror Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ice Scream: Horror Escape is a thrilling and spine-chilling adventure puzzle game that immerses players in a world filled with dread and excitement. One fateful morning, you witness your best friend being captured by a mysterious figure and taken away in an ice cream truck. Now, it s up to you to embark on a daring rescue mission. Not only do you have to save your friend, but you must also rescue other children who are in peril, trapped within the sinister confines of the ice cream truck.

As you navigate through the game, you ll encounter a series of perplexing puzzles and chilling scenarios. The ice cream truck driver is not just a threat; he is clever and relentless, so you must use your wits to stay one step ahead of him. This adventure demands a combination of strategy, stealth, and sharp observation skills. Explore the eerie surroundings, look for clues, and gather necessary items that will aid you in your quest for freedom.

With each passing moment, the tension escalates, and the need for quick thinking becomes utmost. Are you brave enough to face the horrors of the ice cream truck and outsmart the villain? The fate of your friends depends on your ability to solve the puzzles and avoid the clutches of the evil ice cream man. With chilling graphics and suspenseful gameplay, Ice Scream: Horror Escape offers a heart-pounding experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat!

How to play free Ice Scream: Horror Escape game online

To play Ice Scream: Horror Escape, simply use your mouse or touchpad to navigate through the environment. Interact with objects to collect items, solve puzzles, and unlock new areas. Stay alert and avoid the ice cream truck driver by moving stealthily. Your goal is to rescue the trapped kids and escape unharmed!


Instructions

1 Use WASD or the joystick to move 2 Press the button to interact with the scene 3 Escape from the Ice Cream Trucker and save your friend
