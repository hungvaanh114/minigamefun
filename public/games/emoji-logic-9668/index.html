Emoji Logic

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Emoji Logic, an engaging and imaginative puzzle game where your emotional intelligence and creative thinking are put to the test. In this game, you'll embark on a colorful journey through a variety of playful scenarios where your goal is to connect pairs of emojis representing different emotions or ideas. Each level presents a unique challenge that encourages you to think outside the box and find the hidden connections between diverse emoji pairs.

The gameplay is simple yet captivating. You'll see a selection of emojis organized in distinct columns. Your task is to explore these columns, tapping on or dragging connections between emojis that you believe share a relationship or association. With each successful connection, you ll draw a line, visually representing the bond between the two emotions. As you progress through the levels, the puzzles become increasingly intricate, testing your logical reasoning and creativity. Are you ready to flex those mental muscles and uncover the meanings behind the emojis?

Although it may appear straightforward, connecting the right pairs is more challenging than it seems. Each level requires careful consideration and a bit of strategy; sometimes, you may find that the connections are not what you initially thought. Are you ready to immerse yourself in this delightful world of emotions and logic?

How to play free Emoji Logic game online

To play Emoji Logic, simply tap on an emoji from one column, followed by tapping another from a different column to connect them with a line. You can also drag to draw a line between emojis. Aim to pair all emojis correctly to complete each level. Good luck!
