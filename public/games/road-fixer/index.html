Road Fixer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Road Fixer is an engaging puzzle game that invites players into a world of strategic challenges. Your main objective is to clear various obstacles to pave the way for a car, guiding it successfully to the end of the road. Each level offers thrilling experiences that keep players on their toes, demanding both critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

The game features a plethora of diverse levels, each introducing a unique blend of obstacles and puzzles. With numerous levels to conquer, players can look forward to an exciting mix of challenges that will test their ingenuity. Whether it's navigating around roadblocks, removing debris, or figuring out the best route for the car, Road Fixer ensures that boredom is never part of the journey.

Players will encounter a wide variety of obstacles that stand in their way, each requiring different strategies to overcome. The combination of challenging puzzles and engaging gameplay mechanics keeps players coming back for more. Moreover, the stunning graphics enhance the overall gaming experience, making every journey visually captivating. Immerse yourself in this delightful adventure as you help the car reach its destination while enjoying the beautiful scenery.

How to play free Road Fixer game online

To play the Road Fixer game, simply click on the obstacles to clear them from the path. Use your mouse or finger to drag and arrange elements strategically, making sure to create a clear route for the car. Once the road is clear, tap the "Go" button to set the car in motion. Enjoy solving each level s challenges!


Instructions

Use mouse or tap to move controls for fix obstacles - Levers and obstacles are connected by color - The orange twister needs to be twisted left or right - The blue button needs to be pressed and held - The green stick needs to be moved left or right
