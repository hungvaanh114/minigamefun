Welcome to Rainbow Friends Run, where suspense meets strategy as you navigate the haunting grounds of Spooky Park. Step into the shoes of a nameless child who has been kidnapped during a school trip. You will encounter the notorious Red Monster, who serves as your guide in this captivating mobile survival adventure that is part of the acclaimed Rainbow Game series.

In Survivor In Rainbow Monster, your objective is clear: survive five harrowing nights in an abandoned amusement park that holds a chilling atmosphere and the looming presence of the rainbow monster. Experience the thrill of exploring the eerie park while evading the monstrous creature that stalks you. Your every move must be calculated as you search for clues and tools that might assist in your escape from this strange, lifeless environment.

This game is uniquely designed to immerse players in a world where they must rely on their instincts to stay alive. As you traverse through deserted rides and dark corners, the suspense builds with each passing hour. With the combination of enigmatic puzzles, immersive graphics, and a haunting soundtrack, players will find themselves captivated by the challenges presented within.

Are you ready to face your fears and outsmart the rainbow monster? Download Rainbow Friends Run now and prepare for a thrilling experience that tests your survival skills and resolve!

How to play free Rainbow Friends Run game online

To play Rainbow Friends Run, simply navigate your character using on-screen controls. Move through the park, collecting items and avoiding the rainbow monster. Keep an eye on the time, as survival depends on successfully completing tasks before night falls. Use collected items wisely to aid in your escape!
