Noob Prison Escape Obby

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Obby in an exhilarating and daring mission to rescue his brother, Bacon, from the clutches of prison in the thrilling adventure of Noob Prison Escape Obby. With the help of his trusty friend Noob, who cleverly organizes a helicopter for the escape, Obby is determined to break into the heavily fortified prison and save his brother.

Inside the prison, Obby embarks on a search mission to locate three elusive iron bars. These essential components will allow him to craft a key crucial for unlocking Bacon s cell. As players navigate through intricate puzzles and avoid numerous obstacles, they can feel the adrenaline rush building with each step closer to freedom. Once Obby successfully gathers the iron bars and constructs the key, the real challenge begins.

With the custom-made key in hand, Obby unlocks the cell, freeing Bacon. But the duo faces a new challenge as the ever-watchful officer, Steve, patrols the area. Stealth becomes vital; players must be cautious and strategic to avoid detection. If Steve catches them in the act, their escape will be thwarted, leading to an instant game over.

Can Obby and Bacon outsmart the police and remain undetected long enough to reach the helicopter? As they dash towards freedom, Noob awaits them in the helicopter, ready to whisk them away to safety. This exhilarating journey tests courage, strategy, and quick thinking, promising endless fun and challenges.

How to play free Noob Prison Escape Obby game online

To play the Noob Prison Escape Obby, navigate your character using your keyboard. Explore the prison to find three iron bars and keep an eye out for Steve as you search. Once you have the bars, use them to craft a key and unlock the cell. Move stealthily to avoid being caught, and dash to the helicopter waiting for your escape!


Instructions

You can move by pressing the WASD keys Collect the 3 iron bars and make a key Use the key to unlock the prison iron bars Be careful of obstacles and avoid the police officer
