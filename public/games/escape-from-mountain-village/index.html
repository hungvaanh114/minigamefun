Escape From Mountain Village

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Escape from Mountain Village is an engaging point-and-click adventure game developed by 8B Games/Games2Mad. Set in a quaint village nestled within a mountain range, players find themselves trapped in a mysterious forest house just as dusk falls. With the gate secured tightly, it soon becomes clear that the only way out is to explore the surroundings and solve various intriguing challenges.

Throughout your journey, you'll encounter hidden objects that are cleverly concealed within the environment, alongside a series of clues and intricate number puzzles. Each item you uncover will help piece together the mystery of your imprisonment. The ambiance of the game immerses you in a world where every corner hides surprises, turning your escape into a thrilling adventure.

Your ultimate goal is to navigate through this enchanting yet foreboding wilderness. Carefully examine your surroundings and utilize the tools and hints you discover along the way to orchestrate your escape. Remember, patience and keen observation are key as you uncover the secrets of the forest that will lead you to freedom. Good luck as you embark on this captivating journey, and have fun unraveling the mysteries that await you!

How to play free Escape from Mountain Village game online

To play Escape from Mountain Village, simply use your mouse to interact with various objects within the game. Click on items to collect them or to solve puzzles. Pay attention to clues that will guide you toward your escape route. Take your time, as each hidden object and riddle is essential for finding your way out!
