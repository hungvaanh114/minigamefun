PJ Masks Pick Up Power Up?PJ Masks Pick Up Power Up

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Have you ever dreamed of becoming a superhero? Now's your chance with the delightful PJ Masks Pick Up Power Up game! Join Catboy, Owlette, and Gekko as they embark on an exciting new adventure. Before they can set out on their mission, they need your help to boost their strength and gather crucial power-ups! Can you assist the PJ Masks in locating these vital enhancements while also tidying up the surroundings?

Your role in this fun-filled adventure is to examine a heap of various items and sort them into two distinct categories. This unique gameplay not only allows you to acquire beneficial power-ups but also encourages cleaning up the environment. Sounds like a blast, right?

The game features user-friendly controls that make it easy for anyone to join the fun. All you need is your mouse; simply click on an item to lift it, then drag and drop it into the appropriate container. Dive into the action by selecting one of the three characters: Gekko, Catboy, or Owlette. Once you re ready, the sorting begins! You'll decide between the green bin for garbage and the brown wooden box designated for power-ups.

But beware! The challenge is only complete when there are no items left on the screen. The pile boasts an assortment of things, including empty cans, rocks, and sports balls. Sort these items correctly to fill the progress bar and earn bonus points. Incorrect selections will reset your progress, so attention to detail is key. There s no time limit, so take your time just don t let distractions lead to mistakes!

How to play free PJ Masks Pick Up Power Up game online

To play the PJ Masks Pick Up Power Up game, simply click on the items you want to sort with your mouse. After selecting an item, drag it to either the green garbage bin or the brown box for power-ups. Continue sorting until all items are correctly placed to clear the screen!
