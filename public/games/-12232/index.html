American Trash Truck?American Trash Truck

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the exhilarating challenge of keeping your city clean in "American Trash Truck." This immersive game offers players the chance to take the wheel of four different garbage trucks, all rendered in stunning HD graphics. You'll explore a vast open world where your primary goal is to gather trash and restore the city's cleanliness while navigating various weather conditions, including night-time and rainy scenarios.

The game features engaging garbage collection missions that will test your driving skills and time management. As you drive through the city, the residents will rely on you to eliminate unpleasant odors and debris that have cluttered their neighborhoods. Utilizing the truck's two sirens, you can alert other vehicles during your missions, allowing you to maneuver efficiently through the urban landscape.

Collecting trash is not just about maintaining the city's hygiene; it also comes with rewards! Completing missions earns you money, which can be spent on purchasing new garbage trucks or on upgrading your current vehicle for better performance. The controls are simple, allowing you to operate your truck seamlessly with arrow keys or a steering wheel interface. The game's open-world element lets you step out of your vehicle freely, exploring on foot or taking a ride in other cars when you want a change of pace.

The best part? "American Trash Truck" is completely free to play offline! Dive into this engaging experience, and if you enjoy it, feel free to leave comments for more offline games. Enjoy the game!

How to play free American Trash Truck game online

To navigate the game, use the arrow keys or your steering wheel to drive the garbage truck. Follow the mission markers on the map to locate trash and perform collections. Activate your sirens to clear the way when necessary and collect trash to complete missions and earn money for upgrades!
