Block It!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Block It! is an engaging and captivating block puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and spatial awareness. In this game, players must skillfully select and place sets of blocks onto a designated board. Your primary goal is to arrange these blocks in such a way that they create continuous lines either horizontally or vertically consisting of 10 blocks.

When you successfully complete a line, those blocks will vanish, clearing space on the board and allowing for more strategic placements. The thrill of Block It! lies in the scoring system; the more blocks you clear in a single move, the higher your score will soar. This makes planning each move essential, as managing the space on the board and efficiently using the available blocks can lead to impressive point totals.

However, be cautious not to allow the board to become overcrowded with blocks! The game continues until you can no longer fit the available block sets onto the board. Your quick thinking and ability to anticipate future moves are crucial to mastering Block It! and achieving a high score.

How to play free Block It! game online

To play Block It!, simply drag and drop the available block sets onto the game board. Look for opportunities to form lines of 10 blocks to clear them. Continue placing blocks strategically to avoid clutter until no more blocks can fit. Enjoy the game and aim for the highest score!
