Black Hole

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Black Hole is an engaging puzzle game that combines intelligence and entertainment in a captivating outer space setting. Perfect for players of all ages, this game invites you to embark on an interstellar journey where fun meets challenge. The objective is simple yet intriguing: guide a star, on the brink of explosion, into a black hole by ensuring it is oriented correctly. With 30 levels of increasing difficulty, players will find themselves deeply immersed in this cosmic adventure.

The game is designed not just as a pastime, but also as a mental workout that sharpens your problem-solving skills while keeping the experience enjoyable. Each level presents unique obstacles and puzzles that require strategic thinking, making it a great choice for those looking to stimulate their minds while having fun. Along the way, players can collect valuable information provided by in-game advertisers, enhancing their gaming experience.

Whether you re a seasoned gamer or new to the genre, Black Hole offers an irresistible combination of strategy, excitement, and the thrill of outer space exploration. Dive into the universe and see if you have what it takes to save the star before it s too late!

How to play free Black Hole game online

To play the Black Hole game online, start by using your mouse or finger to rotate the orientation of the black hole. Once aligned, release the star to send it hurtling towards the black hole. Ensure you adjust the angle correctly to prevent the star from exploding. Enjoy each level as you solve the puzzles!
