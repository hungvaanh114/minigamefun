Prepare yourself for an exhilarating experience in Dead City: Zombie Shooter, one of the top first-person sniper shooting games available. Immerse yourself in the chaotic realm of the Last Hope, where the world has plunged into an apocalypse. The wasteland is laden with dangers, including hordes of zombies, crafty raiders, and other vicious adversaries that threaten your existence.

Your mission is clear: survive and protect the remaining survivors scattered across this perilous terrain. With immersive graphics and a gripping storyline, Dead City: Zombie Shooter promises to keep you on the edge of your seat as you fight for survival against relentless foes and uncover the mysteries hidden in the wasteland.
