Gumball Class Spirits

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Gumball Class Spirits, an engaging online game you can enjoy for free at superkidgames.com. Join Gumball on a hilarious adventure packed with supernatural twists as he grapples with the tumultuous emotions of jealousy. The storyline follows Gumball s trials after his friend Leslie begins spending time with Penny, leading Gumball to a fit of jealousy that manifests into a spirit determined to create chaos within the school.

As you embark on this thrilling quest, you will assist Gumball in capturing the pesky spirit of Jealousy before it has a chance to unleash its malign influence on classmates. Your primary weapon in this endeavor is magical dust, which you will use to trap the spirit within special boundaries made from salt. These salt barriers are crucial in subduing the malevolent force and preventing it from infecting everyone with its malicious desires.

The game draws inspiration from the episode "The Flower," infusing it with extra gameplay challenges that keep you on your toes. As Gumball rushes through classrooms and hallways, your objective will be to confront and round up evil specters, returning order to the chaos that jealousy has wrought. Each level promises exciting encounters and clever puzzles, all designed to keep you entertained as you strive to restore harmony in Gumball's world.

How to play free Gumball Class Spirits game online

To play Gumball Class Spirits, use the arrow keys to navigate Gumball around the school. Collect magical dust while avoiding spectral enemies. To trap the jealousy spirit, draw salt lines using the mouse to create containment barriers. Ensure to round up all spirits before time runs out!
