Flappy Dove

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Flappy Dove is an exciting game that offers a delightful twist for fans of the classic Flappy Bird. In this engaging adventure, players must guide a dove through an array of obstacles by tapping the screen. The gameplay is easy to grasp, making it suitable for players of all ages. Your goal? Keep the dove airborne while navigating a challenging series of barriers all while trying to achieve the highest score possible.

The controls are straightforward: for PC users, simply utilize the mouse to keep the dove flying. For those on smartphones or tablets, tap the screen to ensure your dove stays in the air. The rules are simple yet addictive, providing an exhilarating experience that encourages players to improve their skills and beat their own scores.

What makes Flappy Dove even more appealing is its accessibility. Whether you re on a computer, tablet, or smartphone, you can dive into the action directly in your browser no downloads required! This ease of access means you can enjoy the game anytime, anywhere. So, gather your friends and challenge each other to see who can get the furthest!

How to play free Flappy Dove game online

To play Flappy Dove, simply click or tap on the screen to make the dove flap its wings. Your objective is to maneuver through the gaps between the obstacles without touching them. The more gaps you pass through, the more points you earn. Strive to beat your high score and enjoy the endless fun!
