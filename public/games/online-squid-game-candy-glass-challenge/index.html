Online Squid Game - Candy Glass Challenge

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of online Squid Game - Candy Glass Challenge! In this high-stakes game, only the bravest and fastest players dare to compete in a series of challenging tasks designed for those in dire financial situations. Test your limits as you race against time to complete each daunting challenge!

Players face a variety of tasks, each with its own time limit. Failure to complete a task results in elimination from the game, and players who break the rules will also be knocked out. Engage in harrowing challenges like the classic "Green Light Red Light," where you must stop at the signal and move only during the green light phases, or risk elimination. The stakes are high; once you're out, you can never return!

Another adrenaline-pumping task is the Candy Challenge, where you'll sculpt a candy into specific shapes using a pin. Be careful! If the candy breaks in the process, you're gone. The final test introduces the Tempered Glass task, where players must carefully choose their steps on a treacherous bridge made of fragile glass. Jump on the wrong piece, and you ll plunge into elimination. Maintain your composure as you navigate this perilous course!

The game's challenges are designed to test not only your skill but also your strategic thinking and speed. Stay focused, complete the tasks within the given timeframe, and you may emerge victorious, claiming the coveted prize pack! Participate in the most dangerous tasks and prove that you are the ultimate Squid Game champion!

How to play free Online Squid Game - Candy Glass Challenge game online

To play, simply follow the game's on-screen instructions. Use your controls to move and stop at red lights, timing your actions to avoid elimination. For the Candy Challenge, click to shape the candy, and during the Tempered Glass task, jump carefully on the strong glass to stay in the game!
