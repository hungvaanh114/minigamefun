Ghost Patrol

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the enchanting world of Ghost Patrol, you take on the role of a mischievous spirit whose primary thrill lies in stealthily scaring unsuspecting museum visitors. As a ghost, your main goal is to outwit the guests and guards by sneaking behind them to deliver frights, all while avoiding detection. Your ghostly abilities become compromised if anyone lays eyes on you, so precision and strategy are key components of the game.

Utilize your tactical skills to navigate through the intricate museum layout, taking advantage of shadows and hiding spots to position yourself for the perfect scare. But beware; being in someone s line of sight can cause you to vanish, so you must remain vigilant and aware of your surroundings at all times. Use your agility to maneuver smoothly between exhibits while keeping an eye out for potential prey. Visitors and guards alike can be your targets, but eliminating them is a delicate process that requires stealth and cleverness.

A unique gameplay mechanic allows you to "hide in armor," letting you blend in seamlessly with the museum s artifacts. This skill is crucial when high-alert guards are on patrol, as it provides you with a temporary safe haven to plot your next scare. The thrill of evading capture and orchestrating the perfect fright underpins every moment of Ghost Patrol, making it an exciting test of your ghostly prowess.

How to play free Ghost Patrol game online

To dive into the exciting gameplay, simply navigate your ghost character using directional keys or your mouse. Evade guards while aiming to frighten visitors. Use the hide in armor feature to disguise yourself and plot strategic moves. Stay light on your feet to maximize your scaring potential!
