Draw Weapon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Draw Weapon, where your destiny lies in the balance as you control your character using the mouse. Navigate through perilous environments while avoiding a bottomless abyss that threatens to consume you. Armed with razor-sharp blades, you can not only slash through formidable opponents but also obliterate sturdy concrete columns effortlessly!

As you advance through each level, the game offers the exhilarating chance to change both the shape and type of your melee weapons. You ll find yourself on a small field where you need to draw your weapon with precision and creativity. Choose wisely between two distinct types of arms: the powerful long two-handed swords for close combat or the agile short throwing knives that will dart gracefully around you.

Your journey will pit you against relentless hordes of enemies wielding an array of bizarre weapons, from chairs and guitars to axes and shurikens. Even more menacing foes may utilize cannons and bazookas that fire homing missiles! Put your swordsmanship skills to the test as you expertly slice through the air, deflecting even bullets aimed at you!

Don t miss the exhilarating boss battles that await you, each demanding your utmost skill and strategy to triumph. Prepare for an electrifying adventure filled with intense action and relentless challenges in Draw Weapon!

How to play free Draw Weapon game online

To play Draw Weapon, use your mouse to control your character s movement and draw your selected weapon on the field. Choose either the two-handed sword for powerful strikes or the throwing knives for agility. Navigate obstacles, defeat enemies, and reach the finish line while showcasing your impressive combat skills!
