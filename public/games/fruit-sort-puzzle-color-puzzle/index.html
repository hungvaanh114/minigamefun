How to play free Fruit Sort Puzzle - Color Puzzle game online

To play Fruit Sort Puzzle, simply drag and drop the fruits on the screen. Match three or more of the same type to combine them into a higher-level fruit. Be cautious of the fork s limits, and strategize to avoid overloading it. Work efficiently to reach your goal of creating hawthorn candy canes!
