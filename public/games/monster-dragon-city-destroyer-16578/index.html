Monster Dragon City Destroyer

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exciting world of Monster Dragon City Destroyer, the citizens have lost sight of your devastating powers, the very abilities that made you a legendary figure in the realm of dragon city games. It s time for a triumphant return as you unleash chaos upon the unsuspecting city! With your immense strength and flight capabilities, you will engage in a fierce aerial assault, reminding everyone of your exceptional prowess.

Prepare to soar through the skies and initiate an onslaught of destruction in this thrilling flying attack game. Navigate your ferocious dragon over the sprawling metropolis, targeting buildings, vehicles, and the frightened inhabitants below. The graphics are stunning, immersing you in a vibrant urban landscape that is ripe for destruction. Each level offers unique challenges and obstacles, demanding strategic maneuvering and a keen eye.

Your journey through the city will not only be about wreaking havoc but also about regaining your status as the ultimate dragon. Collect power-ups and bonuses that enhance your abilities, allowing you to unleash even more ferocious attacks. Whether you're breathing fire or crashing down onto structures, every move will echo your legend as a force of nature. Engage in epic battles against other dragons and take down anyone who dares to challenge your might!

How to play free Monster Dragon City Destroyer game online

To play Monster Dragon City Destroyer, simply use your keyboard to navigate your dragon through the city. Press the designated keys to take flight, swoop down, and unleash fire breath on structures. Watch out for enemy attacks and collect power-ups to boost your strength and speed. Enjoy the thrilling destructive gameplay!


Instructions

Use WASD or arrow keys to move the dragon Left mouse click - fire
