Eliminate Corona

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In a world plagued by the relentless attack of the Covid-19 virus, it is your mission to safeguard the people and eradicate the virus once and for all. "Eliminate Corona" challenges players to step into the role of a hero, fighting against the pandemic that has disrupted lives globally. As you navigate through 20 exciting levels, you'll face various obstacles and tasks that require sharp thinking and quick reflexes to overcome.

Each level presents unique challenges related to the spread of the virus, including protecting communities, distributing vaccines, and thwarting the virus's attempts to infect more individuals. You will use strategic planning to deploy resources effectively and ensure everyone remains safe. With stimulating gameplay and engaging graphics, "Eliminate Corona" not only entertains but also educates players about the significance of community health and safety.

As you progress through the levels, the difficulty will increase, demanding better coordination and quick strategic thinking to defeat the virus. Collect power-ups and bonuses along the way to enhance your abilities and increase your chances of success. Your ultimate goal is to conquer the virus and restore safety to the world. Will you rise to the challenge and become the hero that the world needs? Gear up and get ready to eliminate the threat!

How to play free Eliminate Corona game online

To play "Eliminate Corona," simply use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through the levels. Click or tap to perform actions such as targeting the virus, deploying health resources, and interacting with characters in the game. Pay close attention to the instructions provided at the start of each level to succeed in your mission!
