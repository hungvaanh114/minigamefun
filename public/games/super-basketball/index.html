Super Basketball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Super Basketball" is an engaging and highly addictive game that revolves around realistic physics mechanics. In this game, you will tap and slide the basketball to control its angle and power before shooting it towards the hoop. The well-designed graphics enhance your gaming experience, making each shot even more thrilling.

This endless arcade game presents players with a challenge as you aim for the hoop to progress through levels. Each finished level brings increasing difficulty, with the hoop exhibiting four different random movements. Players have just three attempts to sink the ball in the basket for each level will you rise to the challenge and conquer the leaderboard?

With its addictive gameplay and fantastic ball physics, "Super Basketball" offers an exhilarating shooting experience. Enjoy cool graphics and impressive sound effects that elevate your enjoyment best of all, it's completely free! With an easy zoom camera effect and simple one-touch controls, both young and old players can enjoy hours of fun.

Whether you re a casual gamer or a shooting expert, this game ensures you'll have a blast while trying to break your own records. Get ready for a great time, as Super Basketball is easy to start but hard to put down!

How to play free Super Basketball game online

To play the game, simply touch the screen to select the basketball. Drag to adjust the power and angle of your shot. Once you're satisfied with the positioning, release your finger to shoot the ball towards the hoop. Remember, you only have three shots per level to achieve your best score!
