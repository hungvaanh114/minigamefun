How to play free Halloween Match 3 game online

To play Halloween Match 3, simply tap on two adjacent colored pets to switch their positions. Aim to create a line of three or more matching pets to clear them from the board and eliminate dirty cells. Stay alert and plan your moves wisely to beat the clock and complete each level!
