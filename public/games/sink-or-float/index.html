Sink or Float

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a fascinating journey into the world of physics with Sink or Float. This game is designed to challenge a youngsters understanding of buoyancy and mass in an entertaining way. The player is presented with various objects and their task is to predict whether these objects will sink or float when dropped into water. Each correct answer earns the player points, making the game engaging and educational.

Instructions
