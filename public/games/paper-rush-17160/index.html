Paper Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Discover the enchanting world of Paper Rush, a delightful one-button game that brings you into a beautifully hand-drawn universe. In this engaging platformer, your primary task is to navigate through various levels filled with spikes and platforms. Your objective is to reach the elusive black hole, which signifies the completion of each stage.

As you journey through the game, you will encounter numerous obstacles designed to challenge your agility and timing. The visual appeal of the hand-drawn graphics enhances the overall experience, making each level not only a test of skill but also a feast for the eyes. Collecting stars as you progress adds an extra layer of challenge, as players strive to complete each level perfectly. Can you dodge the spikes and jump your way to success while collecting all the stars?

With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Paper Rush is perfect for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just looking for some casual fun, this game promises to keep you entertained for hours. Immerse yourself in the charming aesthetics and see how far you can go while perfecting your skills. Good luck on your journey through Paper Rush!

How to play free Paper Rush game online

To play Paper Rush, simply tap or click to make your character jump. Timing is crucial, as you will need to avoid spikes and land on platforms to reach the black hole at the end of each level. Remember to collect all the stars for a perfect finish!


Instructions

Use the left mouse button to jump Click twice to double jump
