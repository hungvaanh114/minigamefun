Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating showdown in Collect Balloons. This isn t just any battle; it's a race that emphasizes speed over firepower. Team up with your friends in this thrilling competition and get ready to grab as many balloons as you can before your opponent does. Race against the clock as you dash around the vibrant landscape, hunting for colorful balloons scattered everywhere.

Each player will join either the red or blue team, and the goal is simple: be the first to collect 20 balloons. As you jump and maneuver your way through the lively environment, you'll need to show your agility and strategy in catching as many balloons as possible. Once you've gathered your treasure, it's time to deliver! Head to the balloon machine and deposit your colorful finds. The faster you can collect and deposit your balloons, the closer you'll be to victory. Only one team can claim the title, so stay focused and keep racing!

With its bright graphics and fast-paced gameplay, Collect Balloons is not just about speed, it's about outsmarting your opponent. Can you take home the crown? Gather your friends, and may the best player win!

How to play free Collect Balloons game online

To play Collect Balloons, use the arrow keys to move your character around the map. Jump using the space bar to catch balloons in mid-air. Collect 20 balloons before your opponent and bring them to the designated balloon machine to score points. Work together with your teammates and dodge obstacles to secure your victory!
