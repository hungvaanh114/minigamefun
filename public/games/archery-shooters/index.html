Archery Shooters

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of bow and arrow in Archery Master 3D, the premier archery simulation game now available on mobile. This game offers an unmatched level of realism, pushing the boundaries with its breathtaking 3D visuals, fluid animations, and user-friendly controls. Get ready to immerse yourself in an engaging archery environment where every shot counts.

In Archery Master, players shoot arrows at distance targets to accumulate coins. These coins can be used to purchase new bows, arrows, and various upgrades, enhancing your archery experience. You ll need to stay sharp and focused as you face off against fierce competition from Olympic Archery champions. Drawing back your bow, taking a deep breath, and releasing your arrow can lead you to the coveted bull's eye. Do you have what it takes to outshine others and become the ultimate archer?

The game offers a variety of features to keep you engaged, including:

  • Four beautifully designed locations: Pine Forest, Archery Field, Deadly Desert, and Rain Forest.
  • Smooth animations paired with realistic 3D graphics.
  • A variety of over 20 intricately designed archery gear.
  • More than 100 captivating levels in normal mode.
  • An online mode to challenge real players from around the globe in 1-on-1 competitions.

How to play free Archery Shooters game online

To play Archery Master 3D, launch the game and select your preferred mode. Use your finger to aim at the target, adjusting for distance and wind. Tap the screen to shoot when you re poised, aiming for the bull's eye. Compete against real players online to test your skills!
