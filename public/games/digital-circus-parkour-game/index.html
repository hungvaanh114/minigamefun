How to play free Digital Circus: Parkour Game online

To play Digital Circus: Parkour Game, simply use your keyboard to control Pomni s movements. Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate and jump over obstacles. Timing your jumps and movements accurately is crucial for progressing through each unique level. Can you lead Pomni to freedom?
