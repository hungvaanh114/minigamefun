Running Santa

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Santa Claus on his festive adventure in the game Running Santa, where the jolly old man embarks on an exciting quest to collect gifts for children around the world. Your mission is to assist Santa in navigating through various challenging levels while ensuring he reaches the finish line safely. As Santa dashes through snowy landscapes, you'll need to help him jump over obstacles, dodge rocks, and avoid other dangers that could end his merry mission.

Utilize trampolines scattered along the path to achieve higher jumps and access hidden gifts in tricky areas. As you guide Santa through the colorful platforms, make sure to collect all the presents on your way to maximize your score and spread holiday cheer. Each level presents unique challenges, so be quick and strategic in your decisions. With vibrant visuals and engaging gameplay, Running Santa promises fun for players of all ages. Dive into this holiday adventure, conquer all levels, and enjoy the thrill of helping Santa in his great gift-giving journey!

How to play free Running Santa game online

To play Running Santa, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to guide Santa. Press the jump key to leap over obstacles and bounce on trampolines, allowing for higher jumps. Keep an eye out for gifts to collect as you dash through each level. Time your jumps carefully to ensure Santa's safe passage. Enjoy the game!
