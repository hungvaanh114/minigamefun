Cinderella Banquet Hand Spa

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare for an enchanting experience with Cinderella Banquet Hand Spa! In this whimsical game, you join Cinderella just before a grand ball organized by the prince. Eager to attend and impress, she seeks the aid of her magical fairy godmother. While the godmother creates stunning outfits and magnificent carriages with her powers, Cinderella faces an unexpected issue her hands have become rough and unattractive from all the hard work she's been doing. Unfortunately, the fairy godmother's magic can t magically beautify her hands. This is where your creativity comes into play!

Your mission is to assist Cinderella in transforming her hands from drab to fab! Dive into an interactive spa experience, where you engage in various beauty treatments designed to pamper Cinderella's hands. You will have access to a wide array of spa tools and products, including scrubs, moisturizers, and nail polish. The goal is to carefully follow the steps to clean, nourish, and beautify Cinderella's hands in preparation for the ball.

As you progress through the game, you will enjoy soothing music and vibrant graphics that enhance the atmosphere. Unlock new treatments and products that will add a touch of magic to Cinderella's hand spa experience. Your skills will play a crucial role in ensuring that she is ready to capture the prince's heart at the ball!

How to play free Cinderella Banquet Hand Spa game online

In the game, follow the on-screen instructions to utilize different spa tools and products. Start by cleansing Cinderella s hands, then apply scrubs, moisturizers, and nail polish. Complete all steps carefully to make her hands look stunning before the ball!
