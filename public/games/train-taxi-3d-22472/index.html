Train Taxi 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Train Taxi 3D, your primary objective is to navigate through various levels while picking up passengers and reaching your final destination. As you gather more passengers, your train will expand in size, providing a visual spectacle as you journey onward. Each level presents unique challenges and captivating obstacles that require skills and strategy to overcome.

As you progress, you will earn gold coins, which can be used to unlock an array of exciting train models. This feature encourages players to explore and complete levels diligently. To expedite your earnings, take advantage of special bonus levels, where your goal is to collect as many coins as possible within a limited timeframe.

With a total of 9 distinct taxi trains available for unlocking, you can customize your experience and tailor the game to your preferences. The controls are designed to be smooth and intuitive, ensuring that players enjoy a seamless gaming experience while maneuvering through levels, collecting passengers, and avoiding traps. Prepare for a delightful combination of strategy and fun as you embark on this thrilling journey!

How to play free Train Taxi 3D game online

To play Train Taxi 3D, simply use your mouse or touchpad to steer your train and pick up passengers along the route. Avoid obstacles and traps while trying to reach the finish line. Navigate through the levels to collect coins, upgrade your train, and unlock unique models.
