Running Dragon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Running Dragon, where you take on the thrilling role of a dragon soaring through the skies. Your ultimate goal is to outrun a monstrous fireball that speeds towards you at a staggering 330 kilometers per hour. Time is of the essence as you navigate your way through an endless landscape filled with enticing coins and precious diamonds. Each shiny collectible you gather will boost your speed, propelling you further away from the fiery threat looming behind.

As you race through this exciting, ever-changing environment, you'll encounter various challenges like spinning saws that pose a threat to your speed and momentum. Avoiding these hazardous obstacles requires quick reflexes and strategic planning. If you fail to sidestep them, your speed will decrease, making it easier for the fireball to catch up to you. The thrill of trying to maintain your fast pace while collecting treasures adds an engaging layer to this already action-packed game.

With easy-to-learn mechanics and increasingly difficult levels, Running Dragon promises an immersive and fun experience. As you progress, you'll face new challenges that keep the adrenaline pumping, requiring you to stay on your toes. So gather your courage, spread your wings, and prepare for an unforgettable run that will test your skills and reflexes. Good luck out there!

How to play free Running Dragon game online

To play Running Dragon, simply use your arrow keys to control the dragon's movements. Collect coins and diamonds to increase your speed and avoid saws to maintain momentum. Stay alert and dodge the obstacles while racing against the fireball to achieve a high score!
