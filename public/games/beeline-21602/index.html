Beeline

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the whimsical world of Beeline, where your main objective is to navigate through enchanting landscapes filled with delicate flowers. As you soar above the vibrant blooms, your mission is to 'hatch' these small flowers and transform them into magnificent, larger floral wonders. The thrill of the game lies not just in collecting flowers but also in the heart-pounding challenge of dodging pesky flies that hide in unexpected corners. These annoying insects can ruin your beautiful flower-hatching adventure, so stay alert!

The game boasts a visually captivating environment, drawing players into its charm with colorful graphics and an engaging soundtrack. Each level presents new challenges as you guide your way through clusters of flowers, making split-second decisions to avoid the lurking flies. With every successful hatch, the satisfaction of growing your floral collection grows, driving you to achieve higher scores and unlock new levels.

Beeline is designed for an enjoyable gaming experience, balancing the joys of exploration with the exhilaration of avoiding obstacles. Whether you re a casual gamer or a passionate flower enthusiast, this game offers a delightful escape where skill and strategy converge. So spread your wings and dive into the adventure as you cultivate beautiful flowers while mastering the challenges that come your way.

How to play free Beeline game online

To start playing Beeline, use the arrow keys or touch controls to navigate your character through the flower-filled landscape. Collect flowers by flying over them, but steer clear of the flies that appear in tricky spots. Your goal is to hatch as many flowers as possible before returning to base!
