The Haunted Mansion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the eerie world of The Haunted Mansion, where your quick thinking is put to the ultimate test! The story unfolds as a young man finds himself regretting his decision to venture out, as he is relentlessly pursued by a horde of menacing zombies. With time running out, he takes refuge in one of the numerous rooms within the mansion, but safety is a fleeting illusion. The floor beneath him creaks ominously, hinting that the undead are itching to reach him.

Your mission in The Haunted Mansion is clear: fortify the floor using crates before making your next move to another room. The game continuously escalates in difficulty, presenting you with even more zombies and increasingly intricate puzzles at each new level. Not only will you have to think fast, but you ll also need to strategize effectively to ensure our protagonist remains out of reach from those grasping hands.

The Haunted Mansion offers a thrilling experience for players of all ages. It s a perfect blend of action, adventure, and brain-teasing challenges that invites family and friends to join in the fun. While it carries an air of spooky excitement, it's designed to ensure everyone can participate and enjoy the thrill of outsmarting the undead.

How to play free The Haunted Mansion game online

To play The Haunted Mansion, use your mouse or keyboard to interact with objects in the room. Quickly drag crates to barricade the floor before the zombies break through. Work your way through the rooms, solving puzzles to keep progressing. Stay on your toes as each level brings more undead challenges!
