How to play free Ladybug Temple Run game online

To play Ladybug Temple Run, use the arrow keys or swipe gestures to guide Ladybug through the temple. Jump over obstacles, slide under barriers, and turn corners to keep running. Collect coins and power-ups to boost your performance while avoiding hazards. Focus on your reflexes to achieve the highest score!
