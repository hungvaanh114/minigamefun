How to play free Extreme Supercar: Stunt Drive game online

Jump into the exhilarating world of Extreme Supercar: Stunt Drive by using the arrow keys to accelerate, steer, and brake. Try to complete the challenge while earning points for each successful stunt and enjoy the breathtaking gameplay!
