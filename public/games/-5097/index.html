Sling Tomb 2D?Sling Tomb 2D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the captivating world of Sling Tomb 2D, team up with adorable little pets as you embark on a thrilling journey to ascend the towering tomb. This engaging game challenges players to slingshot their furry companions upwards while deftly avoiding a plethora of obstacles and treacherous traps that lurk in the shadows. Your mission is clear: rise to the top and escape the ever-encroaching water that threatens your life!

As you soar through the tomb, collect shiny coins and sparkling diamonds that are scattered throughout the levels. These precious collectibles are vital for unlocking new pets, each with their unique abilities, allowing you to climb higher and set impressive records. The more you play, the more opportunities you have to challenge your friends by sharing your score and competing for the top spot on the leaderboard.

Sling Tomb 2D not only provides an entertaining arcade experience but also introduces power-ups that can change the tide of your adventure. Utilize these boosts wisely as you navigate the intricate paths of the tomb. Will you help your pets overcome the challenges and race against the water, or will you succumb to the traps that await? Join the adventure and discover who will emerge as the ultimate tomb climber!

How to play free Sling Tomb 2D game online

To play Sling Tomb 2D, use your mouse or finger to aim and release your pet, launching them upward. Tap or click to sling your pet and watch them bounce off surfaces, gaining height. Collect coins and power-ups while avoiding hazards, and be mindful of the rising water below.
