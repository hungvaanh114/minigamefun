How to play free Juicy Match game online

To play Juicy Match online, simply swap adjacent fruits to create matches of three or more. Aim for larger combos to earn bomb bonuses. Use these to clear challenging obstacles and maximize your score. Keep an eye on the moves left and strive to collect stars before time runs out!
