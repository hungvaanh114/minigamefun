Fashion Celebrity Dress Up Game 1

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tina is feeling the pressure in the world of fashion as her ranking has recently suffered a significant decline. This situation has left her feeling anxious and uncertain about her future in the industry. However, her friend Kim provides a glimmer of hope by suggesting that if Tina participates in the upcoming Bikini Contest organized by Celebrity Magazine, she has the chance to secure a spot on the cover of their next issue. Although apprehensive, Tina decides to seize this opportunity and prove her fashion prowess.

Join Tina on her journey to reclaim her status by helping her select the perfect outfit for the contest! Your keen sense of style will be instrumental in choosing the ideal bikini, accessories, and hairstyle that showcases her best features. This is not just about looking good; it s about making an impression that can change the course of her career. Will your fashion expertise lead her to victory? Step into the world of glamour and make Tina shine like the star she is meant to be!

How to play free Fashion Celebrity Dress Up Game 1 game online

To play the Fashion Celebrity Dress Up Game 1, start by selecting various outfits, including bikinis and accessories, from the menu. Use your mouse to click and drag items onto Tina, mixing and matching until you find the perfect look for the contest. Once you're satisfied with her appearance, hit the 'Done' button to see how she looks and prepare her for the competition!
