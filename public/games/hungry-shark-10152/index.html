Hungry Shark

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Hungry Shark, you step into the fascinating underwater world as a voracious shark ready to embark on an exciting hunting expedition. The ocean is your playground, where you must feast on everything in your path to survive. From small fish to larger, daunting creatures, your hunger knows no bounds as you navigate through vibrant coral reefs and open waters.

As you swim through the depths, you will encounter a variety of obstacles and challenges. Stay alert, as predators lurk around every corner, and hazards such as jellyfish and mines can end your feeding frenzy in an instant. With each meal, you grow larger and more powerful, unlocking new abilities that allow you to hunt down even bigger prey.

Collect coins and rewards as you munch your way through the ocean, which you can use to upgrade your shark or unlock new species. Feel the thrill of prowling through the depths, avoiding threats while chasing after tasty oceanic treats. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay will keep you hooked as you strive to become the ultimate predator of the sea!

How to play free Hungry Shark game online

To enjoy the Hungry Shark experience, simply select your shark and dive into the ocean. Use your mouse or touch controls to navigate. Click or tap to swim and bite at fish or objects. Your goal is to eat and survive while avoiding dangers. Keep an eye on your hunger meter to stay alive!
