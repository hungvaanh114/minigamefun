Kick The Dummy FN is an entertaining anti-stress game that allows players to unleash their frustrations in a lighthearted and enjoyable way. Whether you re looking to blow off steam after a long day or simply enjoy some playful antics, Kick The Dummy FN is designed to keep you entertained and smiling.
