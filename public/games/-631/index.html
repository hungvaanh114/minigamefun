Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pet Fall, an exciting puzzle game that puts your strategic thinking to the test! In this vibrant world, you'll have the opportunity to move adorable animals across the playing field. The objective is simple: arrange and collect complete horizontal lines of animals to make them vanish and earn points!

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter some unique challenges. Certain animals may be frozen in ice, requiring you to release them twice to set them free. Others might be trapped in cages, immobilizing them until you can figure out how to unlock their potential. The key to advancing through each level is not only to collect the animals but also to utilize various bonuses effectively to overcome these obstacles and score even higher!

With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Pet Fall presents a delightful experience that appeals to players of all ages. As you progress, the challenges increase, inviting you to hone your skills and become a master of this captivating puzzle adventure. Explore the nuances of each animal's plight and devise clever strategies to clear the board. Are you ready to rescue the trapped pets and conquer the levels ahead?

How to play free Pet Fall game online

To play Pet Fall, simply drag the animals from side to side to create complete horizontal lines. Collect animals to make them disappear and earn points. Keep an eye out for frozen or caged animals, and use bonuses wisely to navigate through levels. Have fun rescuing all the pets!
