Spaces Solitaire

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Spaces Solitaire is a captivating card game that challenges your strategic thinking as you organize a full deck of cards. The game is set on a card table where all cards are dealt into four separate rows. Each row features a single empty space, creating an intriguing puzzle that you need to solve. Your main objective is to rearrange the cards, creating an ascending sequence for each row, ensuring each row consists of one suit only.

The challenge intensifies as you navigate the rules that govern card movement. You can move a card into an empty space only if the card adjacent to the empty space is of the same suit and has a lower face value. This mechanic requires players to think several moves ahead and strategize their card placements to complete the game efficiently. Whether you are a seasoned Solitaire player or new to the genre, Spaces Solitaire offers a unique and engaging experience that will put your problem-solving skills to the test.

With an intuitive interface and sleek design, Spaces Solitaire is perfect for players looking to challenge themselves and unwind at the same time. The game s pace allows for both quick sessions and longer gameplay, making it suitable for any moment of your day. Dive into the world of Spaces Solitaire, where every move counts and strategic planning leads to victory!

How to play free Spaces Solitaire game online

To play Spaces Solitaire online, start by selecting the empty space next to a card of the same suit and lower value. Move the card to the empty space. Continue rearranging the cards until all rows are in ascending order with a matching suit. Keep strategizing to make the best moves!


Instructions

Use spaces to arrange all cards in suit and in sequence from 2 to King You can move a card to a space or empty spot if it is the same color and 1 higher in value than the card to the left of the space
