Bubble Animal Saga

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Bubble Animal Saga, where traditional bubble shooting games are transformed into a delightful adventure featuring charming pet faces and playful farmyard pals! In this colorful experience, the mundane multi-colored orbs have been replaced with an array of adorable creatures. Picture your favorite furry friends, including puppies, playful piglets, chirping cockerels, and yes, even those mischievous angry birds! These whimsical characters pop out at you as round bubbles waiting to be matched and dropped.

Your mission in Bubble Animal Saga is simple yet exhilarating: aim, shoot, and match! Use your bubble shooter to fire at clusters of pets and farm animals. The challenge lies in strategically compounding three or more of the same faces to create an explosive chain reaction that sends these delightful characters tumbling down. The more you pop, the greater your score, and the more fun you ll have unlocking new challenges throughout the game.

As you progress through various levels filled with vibrant backdrops and whimsical graphics, you'll encounter unique power-ups that can boost your shooting capabilities. Take your time to aim accurately; precision is key to clearing the board and progressing in this addictive bubble shooter saga! Dive into the excitement today and experience a bubble-popping party like no other.

How to play free Bubble Animal Saga game online

To play Bubble Animal Saga, simply aim your bubble shooter at the cluster of animal faces. Click to shoot and try to form groups of three or more identical bubbles to make them pop. Keep shooting to clear the board and advance to the next level!
