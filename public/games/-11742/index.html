Destruction Day?Destruction Day

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing "Destruction Day," a thrilling survival game that puts your reflexes to the test in a captivating 2D environment. As a player, your mission is to navigate through a chaotic world where meteors and various hazards rain down upon you. Your survival skills will be challenged as you dodge debris and avoid obstacles to stay alive. Each level progresses in intensity, increasing the frequency and difficulty of the incoming threats. This simple yet engaging game is perfect for anyone looking to experience adrenaline-pumping gameplay.

Developed using Unity, this marks my first venture into the world of game development. "Destruction Day" presents players with intuitive controls and straightforward mechanics, making it accessible for both new and seasoned gamers alike. With vibrant graphics and immersive sound effects, you'll find yourself engrossed in the challenge of surviving the relentless onslaught of meteors.

To complement the thrilling gameplay, I've included a powerful soundtrack titled "Strong," produced by Alex-Productions. You can listen to it on YouTube. This music is freely available under the Creative Commons CC BY 3.0 license, ensuring that players not only enjoy the game but also appreciate the dynamic audio experience.

How to play free Destruction Day game online

To play "Destruction Day," simply control your character using the arrow keys or WASD to move around the screen. Dodge the falling meteors and obstacles while collecting power-ups that may appear. Stay vigilant, and try to survive for as long as possible while aiming for high scores!
