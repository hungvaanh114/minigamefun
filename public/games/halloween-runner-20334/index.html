Halloween Runner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the thrilling adventure of Halloween Runner, where you embark on a spooky mission to assist a friendly witch in gathering pumpkins scattered across a haunted landscape! As you navigate through eerie forests and abandoned graveyards, stay alert menacing bats, ominous tombstones, and creepy spiders lurk in the shadows, ready to thwart your quest. Your agility is key; you'll need to jump over obstacles and duck beneath treacherous barriers to keep your witch safe from harm. Collect as many pumpkins as possible to help her return home safely. The enchanting graphics and spine-chilling soundtrack will transport you to a world filled with Halloween magic and excitement!

Every level presents new challenges as you race against time. The more pumpkins you collect, the stronger your abilities become to defeat the ghostly enemies and navigate the hazardous terrain. Will you be able to help the witch complete her mission while mastering the art of dodging and leaping? Put your reflexes to the test and show your skills in this lively Halloween adventure!

How to play free Halloween Runner game online

To play Halloween Runner, use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to jump and duck as you run through the haunted landscape. Collect pumpkins while avoiding bats, tombstones, and spiders. Stay agile and responsive to keep your witch safe and ensure she makes it home with as many pumpkins as possible!
