Crossy Chicken

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Crossy Chicken is an engaging arcade game developed by SquareLemurs that draws inspiration from the timeless classic, Frogger. In this fast-paced adventure, players take control of a plucky chicken on a treacherous journey across bustling roads and dangerous rivers. The objective is simple: navigate through chaotic traffic, leap across floating logs, and dodge oncoming trains while collecting as many coins as possible.

As you embark on your quest, you'll encounter various hazards that require quick reflexes and strategic thinking. Each level presents a new challenge, pushing you to stay on your toes to avoid being squished by vehicles or splashed away in the water. The intensity ramps up the longer you play, so remember: if you stand still for too long, you might end up as dinner!

Along the way, each coin you collect contributes to unlocking an exciting array of new characters. From quirky farm animals to fantastical creatures, these unlockable options add a fun twist to your gaming experience, ensuring you always have something new to strive for as you hop your way to victory.

How to play free Crossy Chicken game online

To play Crossy Chicken, use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to move your chicken forward or sideways. Your goal is to cross roads, rivers, and train tracks while dodging obstacles. Collect coins and try to reach as far as possible without being hit. Good luck!
