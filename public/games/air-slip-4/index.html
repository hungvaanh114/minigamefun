Air Slip

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Air Slip, an exhilarating game that challenges your reflexes and precision! In this unique gaming experience, you will find yourself controlling a beam that spins and rotates. A vibrant green ball rests on this beam, and your task is to ensure it deftly moves to catch incoming green cubes while skillfully dodging the purple ones.

As cubes rain down from above, you must remain alert and focused. The green cubes represent your goals, and each time you successfully collect one, you earn points. However, the purple cubes are perilous; touching even a single one will cost you a life. You start the game with three lives, and your adventure ends when all three are lost. This high-stakes environment keeps you on the edge of your seat as you strive to achieve the highest score possible.

Test your coordination and speed as you navigate through increasingly challenging levels. Air Slip is not just a game of skill; it also encourages you to improve your strategic thinking as you decide when to collect cubes and when to maneuver away from danger.
