Burger King

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the delightful world of the Cooking Burger Maker Restaurant, where culinary creativity meets fun! In this engaging cooking game, you ll embark on an exciting adventure to become a master chef by whipping up delicious burgers. Tailored for both girls and boys, this game invites all food enthusiasts to showcase their cooking skills in a vibrant virtual kitchen.

Your journey begins as you fire up the grill and choose from a variety of fresh ingredients to create mouth-watering burgers. From juicy patties to crispy lettuce, you have the freedom to customize each burger to your liking. Get imaginative with toppings such as cheese, tomatoes, pickles, and sauces the possibilities are endless! As you progress, you will face different challenges where you must prepare orders under a time constraint, making your kitchen adventures even more exhilarating.

With incredible graphics and easy-to-use controls, the Cooking Burger Maker Restaurant is accessible to everyone. Not only will you learn about the different elements of cooking, but you'll also develop your time management skills as you serve up burgers to hungry customers. As you unlock new recipes and levels, the excitement never ends. So what are you waiting for? Let your culinary fantasies unfold in this fantastic burger-making experience!

How to play free Burger king game online

To play the Burger King game online, click on the "Start" button when the game loads. Follow the on-screen instructions to prepare and assemble burgers. Use your mouse to select ingredients, and drag them onto the burger. Once complete, serve the burger to your virtual customers before time runs out!
