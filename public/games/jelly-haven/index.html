Jelly Haven

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Jelly Haven, an exhilarating action-arcade experience set in the vastness of space! Immerse yourself in the whimsical journey of a bubbly jellyfish as it traverses a fantastical alien planet, on a relentless quest to collect dazzling gems. But beware! The path is fraught with peril, as you must dodge an array of treacherous obstacles, such as rogue meteors and menacing rockets that threaten your survival.

The gameplay of Jelly Haven is designed to be fast-paced and engaging. With each shiny gem your jellyfish collects, you'll accumulate points that can be used to upgrade your abilities in the store. As you enhance your skills, the challenge escalates, demanding quick reflexes and strategic moves to keep your jellyfish safe while pursuing your gem-hunting adventure. The excitement doesn t stop there; the game is available in a whopping nine languages, including English, Russian, French, and more, making it accessible for players around the globe.

Featuring vibrant graphics and fun sound effects, Jelly Haven offers an addictive blend of action and strategy that keeps players coming back for more. Whether you're new to arcade games or a seasoned pro, this delightful jellyfish adventure is sure to provide hours of enjoyment as you navigate through the cosmic chaos in search of those precious gems!

How to play free Jelly Haven game online

To play Jelly Haven, use your mouse or arrow keys to control the jellyfish. Guide it through the score-laden levels, collecting gems while skillfully avoiding meteors and other hazards. Don't forget to visit the store to purchase upgrades that will enhance your jellyfish's survival abilities and speed up your gem-collecting journey!
