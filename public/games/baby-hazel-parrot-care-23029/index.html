Baby Hazel Parrot Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel as she embarks on an exciting journey of love and care in the delightful game "Baby Hazel Parrot Care." Baby Hazel is thrilled to announce that she's welcomed a new feathered friend into her home a vibrant parrot named Charlie. Filled with affection, she dives headfirst into catering to Charlie's needs, serving him his favorite treats and showering him with love. However, her mischievous pet dog, Bruno, can t help but act possessive and aggressive towards the new addition, leading to a petty squabble that leaves the parrot with a hurt wing.

Take part in this heartwarming adventure where you ll assist Baby Hazel in nursing her beloved pet back to health. Explore different fun activities as you gather supplies, apply soothing treatments, and ensure Charlie receives the utmost care. Watch as Baby Hazel learns valuable lessons about responsibility and compassion towards animals. Will she be able to heal Charlie and mend their little family? Experience the joy of ensuring that all ends happily, and witness the sweet bond that develops between Baby Hazel and her feathered friend!

How to play free Baby Hazel Parrot Care game online

To play the free Baby Hazel Parrot Care game online, navigate through the game using your mouse. Click on various items to collect necessary items for caring for Charlie. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete tasks like feeding, healing injuries, and interacting with both Baby Hazel and her pets. Enjoy the delightful animation and engaging gameplay!
