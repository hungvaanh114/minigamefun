How to play free Candy Blast Match3 game online

To play Candy Blast Match3, simply swap adjacent candies to create matches of three or more in a row, either horizontally or vertically. Special power-ups can be earned by making larger matches, and these can help clear difficult levels. Keep an eye on your moves, as each level has a limited number of attempts!
