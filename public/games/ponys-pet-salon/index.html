Ponys Pet Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Ponys Pet Salon, where every little girl can immerse herself in a delightful pony grooming experience! In this charming game, you'll have the opportunity to dress up your adorable pony and give it the most fabulous mane styles that will dazzle onlookers. With a multitude of dazzling accessories and colors at your fingertips, you can transform your pony into a true beauty!

Take on the role of a talented stylist as you pamper your pony with a variety of grooming tools. Start by washing your pony to get its coat squeaky clean, then move on to styling its mane and tail. Choose from an extensive selection of hairstyles that will suit your pony s personality perfectly. It s not just about looks; you ll also need to ensure that your pony feels loved and special with fun toys and treats!

Once your stunning pony is all dressed up, don't forget to accessorize! Add colorful bows, sparkly ribbons, and dazzling jewels to give your pony that extra touch of glamour. The more creative you get, the more unique your pony will be! Show off your stylist skills and share your creation with friends. This pet care game promises endless fun as you explore unique styles and unleash your imagination!

How to play free Ponys Pet Salon game online

To start playing Ponys Pet Salon, select your favorite pony and click on the various grooming tools available. Use the wash station, choose hairstyles, and accessorize your pony to create the perfect look. Don t forget to save your favorite designs to showcase later!


Instructions

Press and hold the left mouse button to play the game
