Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the bustling world of the Doner Kebab: Salad Tomatoes Onions game, where you take control of your very own food establishment! Here, you ll step behind the counter and immerse yourself in the art of assembling delicious kebabs. The challenge lies in your ability to prepare meals quickly while catering to your customers' unique requests. In this captivating arcade-style game, you'll need to think fast and act faster to keep your patrons satisfied.

Your objective is to create as many mouth-watering kebabs as possible while managing ingredients like fresh salad, ripe tomatoes, and crisp onions. By perfecting your assembly skills and understanding the complexities of customer preferences, you ll become a maestro in the kitchen. The fast-paced environment will keep you on your toes as you juggle orders, ensuring that every dish you serve looks and tastes great.

As the clock ticks down, every second counts! Be prepared for fierce competition and exhilarating gameplay that will sharpen your reflexes and enhance your culinary creativity. Doner Kebab: Salad Tomatoes Onions offers you exciting levels that gradually test your skills and speed, all while delivering a fun-filled dining experience. Are you ready to rise to the occasion and be the kebab king?

How to play free Doner Kebab: Salad Tomatoes Onions game online

To play Doner Kebab: Salad Tomatoes Onions, start by selecting your ingredients from the menu when a customer places an order. Use your mouse or touch controls to drag and drop the components like salad, tomatoes, and onions onto the kebab. Complete the orders quickly to earn points and progress through the levels for more challenges!
