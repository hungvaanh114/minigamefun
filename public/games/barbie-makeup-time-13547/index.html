Barbie Makeup Time

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to unleash your creativity with Barbie Makeup Time! It s an exciting opportunity for you to step into the glamorous world of Barbie, where you're in charge of her transformation for an important fashion shoot. This interactive game allows you to play stylist as Barbie gets prepped for an unforgettable evening in the spotlight.

Begin Barbie's beauty regimen by selecting the perfect makeup shades and styles for her. Explore a wide array of lipsticks, blushes, and eye shadows to find the most dazzling combination that shows off Barbie's unique personality. Whether she s in the mood for something bold and bright or soft and subtle, the choice is yours!

Once her makeup is flawlessly applied, it s time to dive into the fun of fashion! Choose from an impressive selection of outfits that range from chic dresses to trendy casual attire. Don t forget about accessories; stilettos and stunning jewelry can add that extra sparkle to Barbie's overall look.

Finally, assist Barbie in choosing the perfect hairstyle to complete her transformation. With creative styling tools at your disposal, you can opt for flowing locks, elegant updos, or fun and funky styles. With every choice you make, you'll see Barbie transform into a stunning star ready for her big shoot!

How to play free Barbie Makeup Time game online

To play Barbie Makeup Time, simply start the game and follow the prompts. Use your mouse or touchpad to pick various makeup styles, outfits, and accessories. Drag and drop items onto Barbie to try them on and achieve the perfect look. Make her ready for her exciting fashion shoot!
