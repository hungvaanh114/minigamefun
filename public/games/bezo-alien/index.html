Bezo Alien

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling universe of Bezo Alien, a captivating 2D Sci-Fi platformer that challenges your agility and strategy. In this vibrant world, you assume the role of an intrepid alien tasked with collecting shimmering blue cubes scattered across intricate landscapes. Beware, as you navigate through perilous enemy aliens, rotating saw blades, treacherous spikes, and menacing flying UFOs that aim to thwart your progress.

The game features a total of eight immersive levels, each designed to push your skills to the limit. As you advance through the levels, the complexity and difficulty intensify, presenting you with new obstacles and enemies to outsmart. Your primary goal is to cleverly maneuver through these hazards, gather as many blue cubes as possible, and ultimately reach the mystical loop hole that transports you to the next challenging stage.

With its vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Bezo Alien offers a delightful experience for fans of platformers and Sci-Fi adventures alike. As you dive deeper into the alien realms, you'll encounter various gameplay mechanics that can enhance your abilities, making each level an exciting test of reflexes and dexterity. Get ready to embrace the adventure, collect those cubes, and complete your quest!

How to play free Bezo Alien game online

To play Bezo Alien, use the arrow keys or your keyboard s WASD keys to navigate through the levels. Jump over hazards, collect the blue cubes, and avoid enemies as you make your way to the loop hole. Time your movements wisely to progress through each increasingly difficult level!
