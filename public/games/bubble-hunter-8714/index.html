Bubble Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Bubble Hunter, an exciting bubble shooter game that invites you on a thrilling journey through the jungle! In this captivating adventure, you play as a little boy on a quest to uncover ancient treasures. By visiting iyigames.com, you will embark on this fun-filled journey filled with challenges and excitement.

Your mission is to match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to eliminate them from the board, paving the way to unlock valuable artifacts from the past. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay promise to keep you entertained for hours. With over 1000 stages to conquer, Bubble Hunter offers endless adventures without needing an internet connection.

Use your mouse or fingers to aim and shoot at the colorful bubbles floating in the sky. Each successful burst not only clears the board but also builds your treasure trove. Experience thrilling moments as you activate bubble shooter boosters with consecutive lucky shots, making it easier to navigate through challenging stages and achieve remarkable bubble blasts!

How to play free Bubble Hunter game online

To play Bubble Hunter, simply use your mouse to aim at the bubbles above and click to shoot. Make sure to match three or more bubbles of the same color to clear them. Keep shooting to unlock treasures and activate boosters for extra power! Enjoy your bubble-popping adventure!
