How to play free Easy Solitaire game online

To play Easy Solitaire online, start by selecting your preferred deck and shuffling the cards. Your goal is to arrange the cards into four piles, one for each suit. Click on available cards to move them; suggested moves will be highlighted for your convenience. Aim to clear the tableau by stacking cards in descending order by suit.
