Paint Tiles Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Paint Tiles Puzzle, where creativity meets logic in a colorful puzzle adventure! This is a relaxing puzzle game that combines drawing and puzzle-solving elements. Each level presents a tile grid and your task is to fill the empty spaces with the right colors. Tap the brush to paint the same colors. Each level features different patterns and color combinations, testing both your artistic skills and logical thinking. As you advance, the puzzles become more complex, keeping you engaged and challenged.
