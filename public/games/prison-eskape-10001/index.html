Prison Eskape is an engaging physics-based puzzle game that boasts 60 exciting levels of brain-teasing challenges! Your mission is simple: guide the imprisoned character to safety by strategically eliminating all the boxes, except for the grassy area where the escape is targeted. Each level presents a unique layout, requiring clever thinking and precise timing to achieve a successful escape.

To play, interact with the blocks by clicking or tapping on them to destroy them. The clever use of remaining blocks is crucial as you must think critically about how they can help facilitate the prisoner s movement towards freedom. As you progress through the levels, you will encounter increasingly complex puzzles that will test your creativity and problem-solving skills. If you happen to make a mistake, don't worry! You can easily reset each level and try a different strategy.
