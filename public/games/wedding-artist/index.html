Wedding Artist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to dive into the enchanting world of Wedding Artist! With just a month left until her wedding day, Jennifer is feeling the heat of wedding preparations. Her to-do list is overflowing with essential tasks that need to be tackled for her dream celebration. This is where you come in! Step into the shoes of her personal wedding planner and help transform her vision into reality.

In this engaging game, you ll have three stylish girls to choose from, each ready to lend a helping hand to Jennifer. Select one to take the lead in designing the perfect wedding, paying attention to every detail, from the dress to the decor. Will you choose the elegant bridal gown or the trendy decorations? Your choices will define the essence of this beautiful day!

As you navigate through the game, explore various options and let your creativity shine. Tailor the wedding theme, pick the ideal color palette, and coordinate with accessories that reflect both Jennifer's personality and her fiance's preferences. The goal is to create a magical wedding atmosphere that will leave everyone in awe and make the day unforgettable.

How to play free Wedding Artist game online

To play the Wedding Artist game, select your favorite bride from the three available characters. Use the interactive tools provided to customize her dress, hairstyle, and makeup. Then, explore options for decorations and choose the perfect elements to complete the wedding setup. Click on the items to apply changes and watch as Jennifer s dream wedding comes to life on the screen!
