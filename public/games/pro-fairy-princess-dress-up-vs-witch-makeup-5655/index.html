Pro Fairy Princess Dress Up vs Witch Makeup

Attention all young fashionistas and magical beings! The highly anticipated beauty contest at the School of Magic has reached its thrilling final round. In this exciting showdown, two rival teams one of radiant fairies and the other of captivating witches are ready to battle it out. The stakes are high, as the winning girl not only secures the title but also gets a chance to go on a date with the boy of her dreams!

The atmosphere is buzzing with energy as both the fairies and witches exhibit their unique styles and extraordinary magic to impress the judges. The confident fairy, known for her impeccable sense of fashion, faces off against the innovative witch, whose hypermagical skills are simply out of this world. The competition has never been fiercer, and tensions rise with every passing moment.

As both teams prepare, the pressure mounts. Do you have a favorite? Will you be rooting for the enchanting fairy or the spellbinding witch? Now's your chance to step in! Grab your makeup brushes and dress the contestants to shine bright. Transform them with a myriad of styles and spells to help your chosen one claim victory. Ensure that she dazzles the judges and captures the heart of her crush!

How to play free Pro Fairy Princess Dress Up VS Witch Makeup game online

To play the game, select your character a fairy or a witch and start by choosing from an array of enchanting hairstyles and outfits. Use various makeup options to enhance her magical look, and don t forget to accessorize! Once you're done, showcase your creation to see if she can win the hearts of the judges!
