Tiny Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tiny Ball is an incredibly addictive shooting game that will test your precision and strategic thinking. Players must aim and fire tiny balls to hit a star located at various distances and angles across the playing field. The challenge lies not only in reaching the star but also in doing so with the least number of shots possible. This unique twist adds a layer of strategy, as every shot counts towards your overall star rating, making it essential to gauge your power and angle accurately before firing.

As you progress through the levels, the complexity increases, requiring sharper aim and more thoughtful planning. The visually appealing graphics and smooth gameplay enhance the overall experience, keeping you engaged for hours on end. Each successful hit rewards you with points and unlocks new levels, urging you to improve your skills and aim for higher ratings. Tiny Ball's combination of simplicity and complexity makes it a must-try for anyone who enjoys challenging shooting games.

With a variety of levels waiting to be explored, Tiny Ball invites you to indulge in some lighthearted competitive fun. Challenge yourself and your friends to see who can achieve the highest star rating, and share your accomplishments on social media to showcase your sharp shooting skills. Are you ready to take on the challenge and become the ultimate Tiny Ball champion?

How to play free Tiny Ball game online

To play Tiny Ball, simply aim at the star using your pointer, adjusting the angle and power by dragging your finger or mouse. Once you feel ready, release to shoot. Try to hit the star in as few shots as possible to earn a better rating. Good luck!
