Whiff Whaff

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to continue enjoying the timeless and traditional game. In this game, you will encounter features that resemble other whiff-whaff games you have played. For example, the ball will accelerate when it hits the platform. You need to be careful with the position of the platform as the speed increases. If you do not catch the ball, your opponent will earn a point.
