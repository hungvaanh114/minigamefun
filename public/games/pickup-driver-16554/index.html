Pickup Driver

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of Pickup Driver, immerse yourself in the thrill of driving as you navigate a stunning mountainous landscape. Take control of your robust pickup truck and tackle a series of challenging parking missions that will test your skill and precision. Maneuver through tight spaces, avoid obstacles, and showcase your exceptional driving abilities as you complete each mission successfully.

If challenges are not on your agenda, you have the freedom to explore the breathtaking surroundings at your own pace. The picturesque mountain area offers a beautifully designed environment filled with winding roads, scenic viewpoints, and diverse terrains that make every drive an adventure. Whether you're perfecting your parking skills or just enjoying a leisurely drive through nature, Pickup Driver provides an engaging experience that keeps you entertained.

As you progress, unlock new vehicles and upgrades to enhance your pickup's performance, allowing for a more enjoyable driving experience. With intuitive controls and realistic driving physics, you'll quickly find yourself immersed in this fun and challenging game. Take on the role of a skilled pickup driver and immerse yourself in a world where adventure awaits at every turn.

How to play free Pickup Driver game online

To start playing Pickup Driver, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control your truck's movements. Accelerate, brake, and steer through the colorful mountain landscape while following the on-screen instructions for parking missions. Immerse yourself in this gripping driving experience and complete each objective to advance through the game.


Instructions

- WASD or arrow keys to drive the pickup - C to change camera - Space bar to use handbrake
