How to play free Evil Invader game online

To play Evil Invader, navigate through the game using your mouse and keyboard. Use the WASD keys to move your character and click to shoot at incoming enemies. Collect power-ups and weapons as they appear to enhance your abilities and improve your chances of survival. Stay alert and dodge enemy attacks to keep your health intact!
