Cat Clicker MLG

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Cat Clicker MLG merges two beloved gaming elements: the exhilarating world of clicker games and the vibrant MLG (Major League Gaming) culture. In this remastered version of Cat Clicker MLG, players embark on a delightful journey by clicking on an adorable kitty to accumulate gold, which can then be utilized for various upgrades and enhancements. With a plethora of options at your fingertips, you can customize your gaming experience with 9 unique accessories, 12 innovative buildings, 12 powerful skills, and 12 strategic upgrades.

The game features an intricate 2-layered prestige system that offers deeper engagement, complemented by a prestige shop filled with extensive upgrades, including the newly added autobuy feature. As you progress, collect mew orbs to enhance both your income and your MLGness, bringing an extra layer of excitement to gameplay. Additionally, players can cultivate diverse crops on their virtual farm, allowing them to sell produce for emeralds. These emeralds can be spent in the exclusive emerald shop for further upgrades and enhancements.

With a vast array of content and a defined endgame set at the 20th ascension, Cat Clicker MLG promises hours of fun and nostalgia for both seasoned players and newcomers alike. Don t forget to strive for all achievements, as they offer significant boosts to your overall income, making every click count!

How to play free Cat Clicker MLG game online

To play Cat Clicker MLG, simply start by clicking on the kitty to accumulate gold. Use this gold to purchase upgrades in the shop, access different buildings, and enhance your skills. Cultivate crops for emeralds and invest them in exciting upgrades to maximize your gameplay experience. Enjoy the fun!
