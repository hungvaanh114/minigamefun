Introducing "Zombies Golf Grenades," an engaging mini-golf style game set against the backdrop of a post-apocalyptic city overrun by the undead. A catastrophic virus has turned the population into relentless, silly zombies, and as you indulge in your favorite pastime of mini-golf, a sudden zombie attack ensues. What was once a bustling metropolis is now a desolate wasteland filled with the undead. Armed only with a mini-golf stick and a handful of golf balls, your mission is clear: survive the onslaught of rising dead.

In this unique gaming experience, you ll step into the shoes of a zombie hunter, navigating the dangers of Zombieland armed with your trusty golf equipment. Your objective is to aim, swing, and eliminate as many silly zombies as possible while simultaneously racking up points by smashing through various items scattered throughout the dilapidated cityscape. The game fuses elements of zombie survival, mini-golf, and shooter mechanics into a delightful and light-hearted adventure.

In "Zombies Golf Grenades," every swing matters as you take on waves of undead foes attempting to breach your sanctuary the mini-golf course. Forget traditional weapons; your golf club and balls are your only line of defense. As you navigate through this quirky zombie fighting scenario, focus on your accuracy to clear the area of these goofy foes and ensure you can play your game in peace. With its casual yet thrilling gameplay, this title offers a fresh twist on the zombie genre that is both entertaining and humorous.

How to play free Zombies Golf Grenades game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse or finger to aim your golf shot at the approaching zombies. Adjust the power and direction by dragging your cursor before releasing. Aim for accuracy to hit multiple zombies or objects for bonus points. Survive each level by eliminating the undead before they reach you!
