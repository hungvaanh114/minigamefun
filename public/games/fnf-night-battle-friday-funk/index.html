Get ready to rock the stage in FNF Night Battle - Friday Funk! This exciting twist on the classic Friday Night Funkin game lets you take center stage in an electrifying musical showdown. Armed with your rhythm skills, you ll be challenged with unique Music Tap gameplay that requires precision and timing.

As the Boyfriend, your mission is to rescue your beloved Girlfriend from the hands of your musical rivals. Engage in thrilling rap battles where tapping circles on your screen is your key to success. Drag circles along fixed paths to keep the beats flowing and showcase your talent in front of an enthusiastic audience.

But that's not all! The game features various difficulty modes, including Hard and Sick, that will test your skills to the limit. How to play free FNF Night Battle - Friday Funk game online

To play the game, use your touchscreen to tap the circles as they appear. Be quick and accurate to hit each note in rhythm with the music. Additionally, drag the circles along their defined paths for bonus points. Pay attention to the beats to ensure you keep the flow and impress the crowd!
