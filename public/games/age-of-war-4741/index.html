Age of War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Age of War is a thrilling base defense strategy game that immerses you in the heart of an ongoing battle. Here, you must continuously generate units to safeguard your base while launching offensives to obliterate your foes. As you manage your resources wisely and build your army, you will navigate through various eras of civilization, each offering its own unique set of technologies and strategies to enhance your combat capabilities. The stakes are high, and every decision counts as you aim to dominate your enemies and protect what is yours.

Throughout your journey, you will earn experience points (XP) as you defeat enemies and successfully defend your base. This XP is vital, for it allows your civilization to evolve through the ages, transitioning from primitive forces to advanced weaponry and units. As you progress, you'll gain access to new advancements that will dramatically improve your defensive and offensive strategies. Mastering these technologies will be key to outsmarting your opponents as they too will grow stronger and more cunning with each passing age.

In Age of War, engaging in fast-paced battles and tactically deploying your forces adds layers of excitement and challenge. With each new era, you'll feel the thrill of evolution, balancing your army's strength while ensuring your base is fortified against incoming threats. Prepare for an epic showdown where strategy and skill come together, leading your civilization to ultimate victory.

How to play free Age Of War game online

To play Age of War online, start by generating units to defend your base and attack enemy fortifications. Click on the designated icons to create units, and strategically select upgrades as you gain XP. Keep an eye on the battlefield and adjust your strategy as new technologies become available with each evolution.
