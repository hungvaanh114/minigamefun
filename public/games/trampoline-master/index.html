Trampoline Master

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Trampoline Master, where creativity takes flight! In this exciting game, you embark on a mission to transform the monotonous exterior of your city s multistory buildings. Armed with colorful wallpaper designs, you'll bounce your way from one skyscraper to another, turning those dull walls into stunning masterpieces.

As you leap from trampoline to trampoline, you ll have the chance to showcase your artistic flair. The thrill lies not just in the jump, but in the precision of your placement, making sure that each piece of wallpaper aligns perfectly. With each successful application, you ll see the cityscape morph into a vibrant canvas that captivates both locals and tourists alike. The challenge increases with each level, introducing tougher building heights and faster bounces, ensuring that your skills as a trampoline artist will be put to the test!

Immerse yourself in this colorful journey where every wall transformed is a step towards becoming the ultimate Trampoline Master. Take on unique challenges that keep the gameplay fresh and engaging, while unlocking new wallpaper designs to expand your creative repertoire. Are you ready to elevate your city and leave your mark through art and skill?

How to play free Trampoline Master game online

In Trampoline Master, navigate through the colorful cityscape using the trampolines scattered throughout each level. Jump from trampoline to building, timing your leaps perfectly to stick the wallpaper on the walls. Align your aim to ensure a flawless application, and move through levels to unlock exciting new designs!
