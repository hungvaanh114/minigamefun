Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tom And Jerry Match 3 is an exciting and entertaining puzzle game that brings the beloved characters Tom and Jerry into action. This vibrant match-three game challenges players to strategically align blocks of the same color in groups of three or more. The objective is simple: create large combinations to rack up the highest score possible. As you progress through the levels, the difficulty increases, requiring sharper skills and quicker thinking. Players must keep a close eye on the scale on the left side of the screen, as it presents a countdown that, if it drops too low, will signal the end of the game. The charm and humor of Tom and Jerry make every playthrough enjoyable, ensuring that fans of the franchise will have a blast.

Throughout the game, players can also unlock power-ups and special blocks that can help clear larger areas of the board, making it easier to achieve impressive combos. Each level features unique challenges that keep the gameplay fresh and engaging. The colorful graphics and catchy soundtrack add to the overall fun, making Tom And Jerry Match 3 a must-try for both casual gamers and hardcore fans alike.

How to play free Tom And Jerry Match 3 game online

To play Tom And Jerry Match 3, simply tap on the blocks you want to exchange to form a line of three or more matching colors. As you align the blocks, they will disappear, and new blocks will fall into place. Remember to watch the scale on the left; if it reaches the bottom, the game ends. Enjoy the challenge and have fun!
