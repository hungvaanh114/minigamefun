Dragon vs Mage

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Dragon vs Mage, an action-packed and free online game where agility and quick thinking are key to survival. In this thrilling escapade, you ll take on the role of a brave Mage tasked with evading the clutches of an evil dragon. The stakes are high as you navigate treacherous terrains, dodging obstacles and challenging the limits of your skills.

Your mission is simple yet engaging: help the Mage flee from the relentless pursuit of the dragon while collecting valuable points scattered throughout the world. As you dash through the vibrant landscapes, be prepared to face various challenges, including bottlenecking spots that can impede your progress. Utilize quick reflexes and timing to overcome these obstacles and maintain your lead against the fearsome foe.

Destroy everything in your path to gain bonus points, and watch out for hidden treasures that can provide extra boosts to your score. The more you run, the more opportunities you ll have to showcase your prowess and outsmart the dragon. With each level presenting new trials, the excitement never wanes, ensuring a fun and immersive gameplay experience. Good luck!

How to play free Dragon vs Mage game online

Once you're in the game, use arrow keys or on-screen buttons to control the Mage's movements. Jump to avoid obstacles and initiate power-ups by clicking specific icons. Remember to collect points while smashing through barriers and keep an eye on the dragon s movements to strategize your escape!
