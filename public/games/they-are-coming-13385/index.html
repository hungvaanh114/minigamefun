They Are Coming

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready to test your reflexes and make quick decisions while being relentlessly pursued? In They Are Coming, you step into a thrilling world where you are relentlessly chased by frenzied red stickman armies. This engaging 3D arcade game immerses you in a heart-pounding experience that challenges your survival instincts. Navigate through intricate platform tracks filled with obstacles as you skillfully maneuver to avoid being caught.

Your primary goal is to gather an arsenal of weapons, including various firearms, which will enable you to fend off your pursuers. The gameplay involves quick thinking as you collect guns and dodge obstacles at every level. You'll face increasing challenges as the relentless stickman hordes close in on you, making your quest for survival all the more exhilarating. But don t worry; triumph lies just beyond the finish line!

Once you successfully cross the finish line, you ll gain access to powerful weaponry like the machine cannon, allowing you to turn the tables on your aggressive foes. Unleash your cannon and obliterate the chasing armies, ensuring that they never have a chance to catch you. Remember, in They Are Coming, staying ahead is your only option so keep moving and don t let the enemies close in!

How to play free They Are Coming game online

To play They Are Coming, start by controlling your character using the arrow keys or 'WASD' to navigate through the platform tracks. Collect weapons by running over them and avoid obstacles by jumping or sidestepping. Keep an eye on your pursuing enemies and aim to reach the finish line to access your powerful cannon and eliminate the threats! Enjoy this high-paced chase and survival adventure!
