Miss Hollywood - Vacation Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an unforgettable journey with Miss Hollywood in the delightful game, Miss Hollywood - Vacation Game. Having won an amazing vacation to the stunning Paradise Cove, Miss Hollywood and her adorable pet companion are ready to dive into an array of fun activities. Choose your favorite dog and get started on exciting beach adventures.

At Paradise Cove, there are endless possibilities for enjoyment. You can unleash your creativity by constructing and coloring a magnificent sandcastle, ensuring it reflects your unique style. But that's not all! Get ready to whip up some scrumptious ice cream and refreshing juice to share with friends under the sun. If you re feeling competitive, challenge yourself to get the golf ball neatly into the hole, adding some excitement to your beach day.

However, not everything is perfect in paradise. The hotel is in disarray, and it's up to you and your furry companions to bring it back to its former glory. Participate in various island adventure challenges to earn fabulous rewards that can be used to transform the resort into a stunning vacation hotspot once again. The more you play, the more you help!

How to play free Miss Hollywood - Vacation Game game online

To play Miss Hollywood - Vacation Game, choose your favorite pet dog when you enter the game. Engage in various activities like building sandcastles, making ice creams, and playing golf. Compete in mini-challenges to collect prizes that will help restore the hotel to its luxurious state.
