Stickman Collect Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stickman Collect Run is an exciting parkour adventure where you dash, jump, and slide through a colorful world. Control a stickman as he runs through a vibrant, action-packed platform. Your goal is to keep running, collect as many other colored people as you can, and arrive at the finish line.
