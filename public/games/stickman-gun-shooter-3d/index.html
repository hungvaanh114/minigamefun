Stickman Gun Shooter 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Warning: Stickman Gun Shooter 3D is not for the faint-hearted! Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-pumping experience where you take on the role of a fierce stickman warrior! Your mission is clear: eliminate enemy stickmen before they get a chance to take you down first. How long will your survival skills last in this chaotic battlefield of bullets and blood?

This exceptional 3D shooter game boasts everything you could possibly want from a gaming experience. Immerse yourself in a fully 3D environment, where beautifully rendered stickman characters engage in fierce combat. The game doesn't shy away from graphic content expect plenty of blood splatter and gruesome effects that heighten the stakes as you eliminate your enemies.

Enjoy ragdoll physics that create satisfying death animations, making every kill feel impactful and rewarding. With an arsenal that includes a shotgun, machine gun, MP5, M16, AK-47, and Uzi, you ll have plenty of options to choose from to ensure your enemies think twice before they challenge you. Get ready for hours of thrilling action packed with relentless shooting and a lot of humorous death scenes!

To top it all off, the game features an exhilarating soundtrack, with tracks like "Global Mechanization" by dem0lecule, which keeps the intensity alive. In Stickman Gun Shooter 3D, you are the predator, and every enemy stickman should dread your approach!

How to play free Stickman Gun Shooter 3D game online

Once you're in the game, maneuver your stickman character using the directional controls. Aim your weapon using the crosshair and click to shoot at the enemy stickmen. Stay alert, move strategically, and utilize different weapons to maximize your combat efficiency. Remember, the goal is to survive as long as possible!
