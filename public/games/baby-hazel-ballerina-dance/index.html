Baby Hazel Ballerina Dance

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel on an exciting and cheerful morning filled with delightful surprises! It all begins when Baby Hazel discovers some intriguing gifts, sparking her curiosity and joy. As she enjoys a refreshing shower, her mom presents her with fun tasks that, when completed successfully, reward her with adorable ballerina toys. These gifts amplify Hazel's excitement, especially since she s about to embark on a new journey to learn the enchanting art of ballet dancing.

However, the prospect of stepping into her dance class brings a wave of nervousness to our little star. The thought of performing in front of others is a little overwhelming for Hazel, but with the right encouragement and support, she can overcome her fears. As you play, your mission will be to comfort Hazel and help her gain confidence as she follows her teacher's instructions step by step. Can you offer her the reassurance she needs to dance like the little ballerina she aspires to be?

Monitor Hazel s progress through various mini-games designed to nurture her ballet skills while keeping her spirits high. Engage in interactive activities that help her practice the essential ballerina moves. With your guidance, each task completed will bring Hazel closer to becoming a confident dancer, full of grace and joy.

To play the free Baby Hazel Ballerina Dance game online, simply use your mouse to navigate through the different tasks.
