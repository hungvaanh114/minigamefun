Trapped in Hell: Murder House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the chilling world of Trapped In Hell: Murder House, where your fate hangs by a thread. Seven days loom ahead, and with each ticking second, your chance of escape slips further away. The atmosphere is thick with dread as you find yourself ensnared in the nightmarish grip of Hell. Your ultimate mission is to get out before you're condemned to eternal suffering.

As you navigate through the eerie corridors of the Murder House, beware of the malevolent presence lurking in the shadows. The Evil Knight, the relentless Keeper of this infernal domain, is always on the lookout for intruders. His disturbing ability to hear even the faintest sound makes stealth your ally. You must tread carefully, as any noise could give away your position and seal your fate.

To break free from this nightmarish prison, your quest involves scavenging the dark halls for crucial items necessary to unlock the door to the portal. Time is of the essence, and each item you gather brings you one step closer to freedom. You ll need to think strategically and act quickly to ensure that you can escape before you are forever trapped in the clutches of darkness.

How to play free Trapped In Hell: Murder House game online

To play the game, use the arrows or WASD keys to move your character through the House. Collect items that may help open locked doors or create distractions to divert the Knight. Stay quiet and use your surroundings wisely to avoid detection. Good luck!
