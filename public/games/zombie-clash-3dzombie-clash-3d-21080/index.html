Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This Halloween, immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Zombie Clash 3D, where you join a fearless group of Harlequins on a mission to reclaim an abandoned circus from a horde of evil Zombies. Equip yourself with powerful weaponry, including a deadly double-barrel shotgun and a unique crossbow that fires explosive arrows, as you navigate through this eerie landscape. Your primary objective is to lead your team to victory while defending against relentless Zombie attacks, who are also eager to join the Halloween festivities.

Throughout your adventure, you'll need to strategize and showcase your skills. One key element involves sneaking the Scarecrow dummy from the sinister Mad Circus and successfully delivering it to the enemy base. This clever tactic will grant your team the upper hand in the battle for survival. As you progress, take advantage of opportunities to upgrade your weapons and unlock dazzling golden skins that set your character apart.

Engage in intense firefights with hordes of Zombies, aiming to rack up the highest scores possible. In Zombie Clash 3D, every shot counts, and precision is vital in overcoming the undead opposition. With each session, your skills will sharpen, paving the way for a dominance that will have your name etched in the record books this Halloween! Are you ready to take on the challenge and emerge victorious?

How to play free Zombie Clash 3D game online

To play Zombie Clash 3D, navigate your character using the WASD keys or arrow keys, aiming with your mouse. Switch weapons with the number keys and shoot using the left mouse button. Look out for power-ups and complete objectives such as delivering the Scarecrow dummy to the enemy base for bonus points. Good luck!
