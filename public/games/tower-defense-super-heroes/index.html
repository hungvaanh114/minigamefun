Tower Defense : Super Heroes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Tower Defense: Super Heroes is an exhilarating arcade game that immerses players in a thrilling defense strategy. In this game, an endless wave of superheroes, representing various powers and abilities, traverses a designated path that leads directly to your health reserve. Protecting this reserve is crucial, as each superhero that manages to reach it will deplete your precious health points.

Your only line of defense consists of a small yet formidable team of super-villains, each equipped with unique traits and skills. Strategically position your villains along the path to maximize their effectiveness. Your goal is to thwart incoming superheroes, eliminating as many as possible before they can breach your defenses and inflict damage on your health. This clash between good and evil not only tests your tactical skills but also offers a vibrant gaming experience filled with action and strategy.

As you progress through levels, the gameplay intensifies, introducing a variety of superheroes with different powers and speed. Utilize your super-villains' strengths wisely and consider their placement carefully to create the most effective defense against the relentless tide of superheroes. Can you construct a powerful fortress and defend your health points against this formidable superhero onslaught?

How to play free Tower Defense: Super Heroes game online

To play Tower Defense: Super Heroes, start by selecting your super-villains. Once in the game, click on the designated spots on the path to place your heroes strategically. Monitor the incoming superheroes and adjust your defenses as necessary to prevent them from reaching your health reserve. Keep your health points intact!
