Home Rush Draw to Home

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Home Rush: Draw to Home is a calming yet engaging rescue game that invites players to embark on an adventure to reunite friends with their families. In this unique game, you're tasked with sketching a path that leads from the house to where your buddies are located. Your ultimate goal is to ensure that their parents can successfully pick them up. Throughout your journey, you will encounter a variety of challenges that gradually increase in complexity, testing your problem-solving abilities at every turn.

The game features an array of obstacles, including treacherous deep holes and fearsome animals. Players must navigate around the likes of the Blue Monster, Grimace, BanBam, and many other formidable creatures. Each level presents new difficulties, urging you to think creatively and strategically as you draw your escape routes. With each successful rescue, you'll gain experience and improve your skills, making you even more adept at overcoming the game's various challenges. Home Rush: Draw to Home not only offers excitement but also provides a peaceful gaming experience, perfect for relaxing after a long day.

How to play free Home Rush Draw to Home game online

To play Home Rush: Draw to Home, simply tap and hold to draw a path from the house to your friends' locations. Avoid obstacles like deep holes and fierce animals while ensuring a safe route for the parents to arrive. Complete each level to advance through the game, testing your skills with increasingly difficult challenges.


Instructions

Draw a straight path from the parents house to the buddies Avoid letting two people collide with each other Be careful of aggressive dogs holes or other dangerous obstacles Collect coins while rescuing the buddies Ensure everyone returns home safely
