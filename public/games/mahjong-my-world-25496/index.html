Mahjong My World

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a world tour of the largest and most famous cities on the planet. The first stop is Paris and you should thoroughly explore the city, there is a lot to see here, only in the game Mahjong My World the inspection will involve solving a mahjong puzzle on each of the ninety six levels. The task is to collect two identical tiles and remove them from the field. The tiles must be free on both the top and sides, at least on three sides. Quickly remove the pairs you find and get coins to later spend on the necessary auxiliary bonuses in Mahjong My World.
