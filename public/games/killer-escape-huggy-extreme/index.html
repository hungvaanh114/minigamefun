Killer Escape Huggy Extreme

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This game is extremely more difficult and filled with challenges compared to the previous one. You need to become Huggy and catch all your clones. Catch all the clones and hunt them down, but remember, some of them must be thrown out of the maze. No one should see you, and you must not get caught while hunting someone. Be very careful, the clones are everywhere and extremely dangerous. Some doors can only be opened by you. Open the doors and hunt the clones.

Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move Press the Space key to kill someone Playable on both mobile and PC Collect energy boxes extend the mission time
