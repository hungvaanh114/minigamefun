Horrible Lovely Manicure Halloween 2019

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Princess Elsa as she embarks on a magical transformation for Halloween in the exciting game "Horrible Lovely Manicure Halloween 2019." This year, Elsa has chosen to become a mesmerizing witch, and she wants to make her Halloween celebration unforgettable. With your help, she can work on her enchanting look down to the very last detail.

Start the adventure by creating a festive makeup for the princess. Utilize classic Halloween colors like black, orange, and red to give her a spellbinding appearance. Once Elsa s face is flawlessly made up, shift your focus to her hands. Begin by treating her nails with a file to smooth out any rough edges, and apply a soothing cream to eliminate irritation. This step is vital to prepare her nails for a stunning manicure.

Next, let your creativity shine as you decorate Elsa's nails. Choose from a variety of nail polishes, textures, and decorations to craft a unique design that reflects the spirit of Halloween. From spooky patterns to dazzling designs, the possibilities are endless! Finally, complete Elsa s transformation by selecting the perfect witch costume. Browse through an array of fabulous hats and stylish belts that will leave her friends in awe.

How to play free Horrible Lovely Manicure Halloween 2019 game online

To play this exciting game, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to select options and navigate through the various tasks. Begin by applying makeup, then move on to manicure activities and finish by picking out the perfect costume for Elsa. Enjoy creating a magical Halloween look!
