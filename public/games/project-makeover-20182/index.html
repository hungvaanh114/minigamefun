Project Makeover

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Project Makeover, where you get the chance to unleash your creativity and sense of style in a thrilling fashion challenge! Dive into the glamorous world of model styling, where your mission is to prepare a stunning model for an upcoming fashion shoot. You ll need to carefully select an ensemble that showcases current fashion trends, pick the perfect hairstyle, and choose an eye-catching photo background that complements your model s look.

As you craft the ultimate fashion statement, you ll also have the opportunity to personalize the photo with various decorative elements. Once you ve finalized your creation, it will be put to the test in an exhilarating voting round against three other competitors. The key to success lies in making strategic choices that will impress the judges and earn you the top spot in the contest. Gather your fashion knowledge, sharpen your aesthetic vision, and get ready to dazzle!

Project Makeover not only lets you express your style but also challenges you to stay ahead of your opponents. Each decision you make will have a direct impact on the results, so choose wisely and aim for the win! Will you rise to the occasion and become the ultimate style icon of Project Makeover?

How to play free Project Makeover game online

To play Project Makeover, simply select a model, then customize her look by choosing outfits, hairstyles, and backgrounds. Once you're satisfied with your choices, decorate the photo. Finally, submit your design and watch as your creation faces off against others in a voting competition! Aim to impress to claim victory!
