Tailor Kids

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting realm of fashion with Tailor Kids, where your creativity and design skills will shine. In this exhilarating dressmaking salon, you have the chance to explore your passion for style and colors, transforming ordinary into extraordinary. Your customers will come in seeking unique makeovers, and it's your job to cater to their wishes while showcasing your talent for fashion design.

From selecting the finest fabrics to creating eye-catching patterns, the possibilities are endless. As you engage with each customer, you'll have the opportunity to customize their outfits with an array of accessories and embellishments. The joy of giving them a fresh look and enhancing their confidence will be rewarding as you build your reputation as a talented tailor.

In Tailor Kids, you'll not only work on creating stylish clothing but also learn valuable skills such as attention to detail and time management. With every successful makeover, you'll unlock new materials and design tools, allowing you to expand your creativity further. Whether it's a fashionable dress for a party or a casual outfit for everyday wear, each design you create will make a mark in the world of fashion.

How to play free Tailor Kids game online

To start playing Tailor Kids, select your first client from the salon. Follow the on-screen instructions to choose fabrics, design patterns, and assemble the outfits. Use your mouse to drag and drop items onto the dress form, and don't forget to accessorize! Experiment with styles to impress your customers and advance through the levels!
