Enter the exciting world of Pumpkinoid, a captivating arcade game that combines elements of classic breakout and thrilling pinball action. With its intuitive one-touch gameplay, players of all ages can easily dive into the fun. Your task is straightforward: bounce the ball off your paddle and destroy the pumpkin-shaped blocks scattered throughout the screen.

As you progress through the game, you can earn up to three medals based on your score, encouraging you to improve your skills and maximize your points with each round. To add to the experience, Pumpkinoid offers six unique paddles that you can unlock as you accumulate higher scores, providing even more variety and excitement as you challenge yourself to beat your previous records.

Each time you start a new game, you'll be greeted by one of seven vibrant backgrounds, ensuring that every playthrough feels fresh and engaging. From whimsical themes to spooky autumn vibes, the visuals are designed to enhance your gaming experience and keep you immersed in the fun. So gather your friends and see who can achieve the highest score in this delightful arcade adventure!

How to play free Pumpkinoid game online

To play Pumpkinoid, simply tap or click to launch the ball. Use your paddle to bounce the ball and break the pumpkin blocks. Keep an eye on your score as you aim for medals and unlock new paddles with higher achievements. Enjoy the fun and beat your best score!
