Killer City

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Killer City, an electrifying sequel to the popular Stick City! In this intense arcade game, your main objective is to navigate through perilous streets while avoiding ruthless killers and traffic. The stakes are high as you attempt to gather as much cash as you can; every dollar counts in this dangerous environment! The city is fraught with challenges, so sharpen your reflexes and stay alert!

In Killer City, you will encounter various enemies, including aggressive killers who are relentless in their pursuit. Aside from these threats, the city is bustling with vehicles that can also end your adventure in an instant. Your agility and quick thinking will be your greatest assets as you dodge and weave through the chaos. Luckily, you ll find a baseball bat along the way, providing a means of defense against any hostile threats. Use it wisely to fend off your pursuers and create space to escape!

The game combines adrenaline-pumping action with strategic elements, as you ll need to prioritize cash collection while ensuring your survival. With engaging graphics and immersive sound effects, Killer City guarantees an exhilarating gaming experience as you leap over obstacles and maneuver through tight spots. Keep your score high while your heart races, making every second count in this urban battleground!

How to play free Killer City game online

To play Killer City, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your character. Navigate avoided killers and vehicles while collecting cash. If you encounter any aggressors, utilize the baseball bat to defend yourself. Your goal is to survive as long as possible and build up your score!
