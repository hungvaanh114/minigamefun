Jelly World - 3D Running Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating adventure in Jelly World - a captivating 3D running game that promises to deliver a unique blend of fun and challenge! In this vibrant world, you ll take control of a gel-like character that bounces and jumps through a series of intricate platforms. The objective is to navigate your jelly character effectively, soaring into the air as you aim to hit the next platform without falling. The thrill of flying awaits as you master the art of timing and movement!

As you guide your jelly up and down, you'll need to enhance your skills in order to make those successful landings. The sky is filled with sparkling diamonds just waiting to be collected; grab them while you leap from platform to platform! In addition to the diamonds scattered throughout the air, you can touch small items that offer additional rewards, making your gaming experience even more exhilarating and satisfying. The challenges will escalate, and with each level, you will find yourself more immersed in the delightful world of Jelly World. Be prepared, as the game is designed to keep you hooked, providing an addictive experience that will have you coming back for more!

How to play free Jelly World - 3D Running Game online

To play Jelly World online, simply use your mouse or finger to control the jelly character. Tap or click to make your jelly jump, and adjust your movement vertically to hit the right platform. Collect diamonds and additional items as you master the jumps and navigate through the increasingly challenging levels!
