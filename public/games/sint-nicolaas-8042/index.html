Sint Nicolaas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sint Nicolaas is an engaging puzzle/platform game inspired by the cherished Dutch holiday tradition of Sinterklaas. Players are tasked with reuniting Sint, who has been robbed of his precious sack filled with presents and gingerbread cookies. Throughout the various levels, you must gather the scattered gifts and treats, racing against time to deliver them to the correct chimneys before dawn breaks.

The adventure unfolds with Sint navigating over rooftops, each chimney marked with a specific color indicating where to place the corresponding present. The challenge lies in Sint's ability to only drop the first gift into the chimney, requiring strategic thinking to ensure that presents are collected and delivered in the proper order. Keep an eye on the clock at the bottom of the screen, as you strive to complete each level promptly. Swift completion may even unlock exciting bonus levels!

Sint's journey isn't without its hazards; players must exercise caution to avoid dangerous falls, navigate slippery surfaces, and dodge sharp TV antennas. Be wary of mischievous birds that may try to snatch away precious gifts or cookies. While Sint can leap onto these birds, earning extra points may be better achieved by leaving them unharmed. Don t forget to collect all the chocolate letters spelling 'S I N T' as they grant you an extra life, making your quest even more thrilling!

How to play free Sint Nicolaas game online

To play Sint Nicolaas, use your keyboard's arrow keys to navigate Sint across the rooftops. Jump with the space bar to avoid obstacles and collect gifts. Follow the color-coded chimneys to deliver presents. Keep an eye on the timer and gather extra lives by collecting chocolate letters along the way!

Arrow keys or W A S D and or Space to jump
