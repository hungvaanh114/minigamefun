The Squid Sniper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the world of traditional Korean games and embrace the role of a sniper in The Squid Sniper. In this thrilling and action-packed game, you'll need to rely on your sharpshooting abilities to pick off players who fail to follow the rules during the infamous green light-red light game. Your mission is to detect and take down players as they move, ensuring you only target those who misstep. Each correct shot earns you points, while hitting the wrong player will cost you valuable score. Aim for the highest score to flaunt your skills and compete against friends, making every round an exciting challenge!

The Squid Sniper also features a unique weapon available in the popular game SharkBite. Priced at 1,250 shark teeth, this sniper offers impressive versatility but requires a fair amount of time to earn. Although its firing speed is slower compared to the Ray Gun, its long-range capabilities make it an excellent choice for distanced targets. The Squid Sniper itself is visually striking, adorned in yellow with a gray barrel and wrapped with a pink tentacle, embodying its aquatic theme. Its shots release a spinning squid, creating a whimsical yet deadly effect. Utilize the expanded mode by pressing Z for those moments when accuracy is crucial!

FEATURES:

  • Numerous challenging locations and scenarios
  • Action-packed shooting game
  • Game store with a wide range of guns to choose from
  • Eye-catching 3D graphics and amazing sound effects

How to play free The Squid Sniper game online

To play The Squid Sniper, simply enter the game and familiarize yourself with the controls. During the green light-red light sequence, focus your aim and take out only the players that are moving while the light is red. Be strategic with your shots to maximize your score and proceed through the levels!
