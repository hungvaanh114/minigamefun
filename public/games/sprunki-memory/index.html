Sprunki Memory

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Sprunki Memory offers you to simultaneously train and test your visual memory. Choose a mode: single, passing levels and playing for two. They are essentially identical in that you must quickly find pairs of identical pictures and remove them from the playing field. An interesting version of the game for two, where you will compete with a real opponent to see who can remove all the cards from the field the fastest.
