Hana Bot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hana Bot is an engaging 2D sci-fi platformer that invites players to step into the metallic shoes of a clever robot. In this thrilling adventure, your primary goal is to navigate through an intricate maze filled with challenges as you collect all the test tubes scattered across the level. However, it's not as simple as it sounds. You'll have to stay vigilant and avoid a myriad of dangers, including hostile enemy bots, menacing flying robots, sharp giant blades, and treacherous spikes that threaten your progress.

The game features a total of 8 meticulously designed levels, each presenting unique obstacles and increasing difficulty as you advance. As you dive deeper into the world of Hana Bot, you'll find that quick reflexes and strategic thinking are essential to successfully reach the Exit door and progress to the subsequent stage. With colorful visuals and dynamic gameplay mechanics, Hana Bot promises hours of excitement and challenge for platformer enthusiasts.

Immerse yourself in this thrilling sci-fi landscape and test your skills as you maneuver through intricate environments filled with both puzzles and peril. Can you master each level, collect all the test tubes, and outsmart your robotic foes? Only the most skilled players will be able to conquer Hana Bot and emerge victorious!

How to play free Hana Bot game online

To play Hana Bot, use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move your bot around the level. Jump over obstacles and collect test tubes while steering clear of enemy bots and traps. Your main objective is to gather all test tubes and reach the Exit door to advance to the next level before time runs out!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the player s movement Use W or Up arrow key twice for double jump
