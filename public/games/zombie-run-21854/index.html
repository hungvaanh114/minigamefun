Zombie Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Zombie Run is an exhilarating HTML5 endless runner game that thrusts players into a post-apocalyptic world teeming with hordes of relentless zombies. As the protagonist, your mission is to sprint, jump, and dodge obstacles in an attempt to escape the merciless undead. With vivid graphics and captivating sound effects, this game immerses you in a thrilling atmosphere filled with tension and adrenaline.

As you navigate through increasingly challenging environments, you ll encounter various hurdles that will test your reflexes and decision-making skills. Collect coins scattered throughout the landscape to unlock upgrades and power-ups, allowing you to enhance your character s abilities and improve your chances of survival. The more you play, the more addictive Zombie Run becomes, as each run presents new surprises and obstacles to overcome.

Zombie Run is designed for players of all ages, offering a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to jump right in. With simple controls and fast-paced gameplay, you will find yourself hooked on the action as you race through streets, alleyways, and abandoned buildings while evading the clutches of the living dead. Get ready to put your skills to the test and see how long you can survive in this relentless pursuit of escape.

How to play free Zombie Run game online

To play Zombie Run, use the arrow keys to control your character s movements. The up arrow will make you jump over obstacles, while the down arrow allows you to slide under dangers. Collect coins as you run to unlock upgrades and aim for the highest score possible!
