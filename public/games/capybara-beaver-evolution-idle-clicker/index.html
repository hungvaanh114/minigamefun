Step into the whimsical world of Capybara-Beaver Evolution: Idle Clicker, where adorable capybaras and industrious beavers come together in a delightful and engaging idle clicker experience. The core gameplay of Capybara-Beaver Evolution: Idle Clicker revolves around tapping and accumulating resources.
