Streets of Anarchy: Fists of War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Streets Of Anarchy: Fists Of War, where the urban jungle is rife with corruption and lawlessness. The dark alleys and bustling city blocks are overrun by criminals who sow fear in the hearts of the innocent. In this action-packed adventure, you take on the mantle of a fearless hero, determined to cleanse the streets of vile villains and restore peace and order to your city.

Your journey begins as you navigate through chaotic environments filled with peril and mayhem. Equip yourself with a range of powerful moves and upgrades, allowing you to take on various criminal masterminds. Mastering the art of combat and employing strategic abilities will be crucial in your quest to defeat enemies that lurk in every corner. With each successful encounter, you will gain the confidence to confront even the most dangerous foes.

As you progress, the challenge intensifies, unveiling intricate levels packed with riveting storylines and formidable adversaries. Utilize your skills defensively and offensively to outsmart and overpower your enemies. Band together with fellow players or venture solo to experience the thrill of the fight and the satisfaction of victory. Remember, each defeated villain brings your city one step closer to peace. Good luck, brave crime fighter, the streets are counting on you!

How to play free Streets Of Anarchy: Fists Of War game online

To play Streets Of Anarchy: Fists Of War, use the WASD keys to navigate your character through the city. Engage in combat with the spacebar to punch and utilize special moves with the number keys. Collect power-ups and defeat enemies to level up your skills and restore justice to the streets!


Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move spacebar to jump Z or X to Attack or C to Defend
