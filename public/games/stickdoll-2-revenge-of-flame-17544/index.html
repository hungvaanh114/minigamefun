Stickdoll 2 : Revenge of Flame

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stickdoll 2: Revenge Of Flame is an exhilarating sequel to the acclaimed Stickdoll: God Of Archery. This installment plunges players into the heart of the Fire Kingdom, where ambition has ignited a fierce quest for supremacy. Flame, the protagonist, embarks on a mission to collect five powerful elemental stones that will enable him to assert his dominance over the realms. With the knowledge that merging the main elemental stone with others documented in the dark magic book grants him invincibility, Flame is determined to harness this power.

However, treachery lurks around every corner. In a shocking twist, Gargle betrays Flame by stealing the main elemental stone, thus sparking a relentless chase for the remaining five stones. This betrayal sets the stage for an epic adventure filled with obstacles and formidable foes. Each episode features guardians from various kingdoms tasked with protecting the elemental stones, all while Flame navigates through challenging platforms that test his skills and resolve.

Players will find themselves immersed in a rich storyline, engaging visuals, and an assortment of unique challenges. The goal is clear: reclaim the stolen stone and unite the elemental forces to restore balance to the Fire Kingdom. Get ready for an adventure packed with thrills, excitement, and strategic gameplay as Flame faces off against powerful adversaries in his quest for vengeance.

How to play free Stickdoll 2: Revenge Of Flame game online

To play Stickdoll 2: Revenge Of Flame, use your arrow keys to navigate and jump across platforms. Collect elemental stones as you defeat the guardians. Utilize special abilities gained from combining stones, and strategize to outsmart enemies. The goal is to reclaim the stolen elemental stone and conquer challenges as you progress through each episode.
