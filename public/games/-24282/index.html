Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Extreme Golf takes the traditional sport of golf to exhilarating new heights in this thrilling online game. Players are challenged to expertly navigate through a range of unique and exciting courses, each designed to test their skill and precision. Your goal is simple: control your character as they tee off with a golf club and aim to sink the ball into the hole with as few strokes as possible. However, be mindful landing a ball in the hole grants you a point, but shooting the ball out of bounds will cost you one of your precious golf balls!

The game allows for fast-paced action and encourages you to improve your strategy and accuracy with each attempt. With engaging graphics and smooth mechanics, Extreme Golf provides an immersive experience that keeps players coming back for more. As you progress, you ll face increasingly challenging obstacles, making every shot count as you seek to build your score. Can you rise to the challenge and achieve the highest score possible? The pressure is on!

Add to the excitement of the game by competing against friends or players worldwide. Share your scores and aim to beat theirs, allowing for endless fun and friendly competition. Put your golfing skills to the test and experience the thrill of Extreme Golf today!

How to play free Extreme Golf game online

To play Extreme Golf, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to aim your shot. Adjust the power of your swing by dragging to set the desired force. Release to hit the ball and watch it soar towards the hole. Keep practicing to improve your skills and score!
