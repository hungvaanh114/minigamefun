Read the Color

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exciting visual challenge with Read The Color! In this engaging game, you ll need to read the color displayed inside a circle and quickly tap the button that matches it. With just 20 seconds on the clock, every moment counts as you strive to score higher!

The game's concept hinges on the intriguing nuances of color perception. Colors, including red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple, are characterized through various categories. This vibrant display of color is derived from the activation of photoreceptor cells due to electromagnetic radiation, creating a fascinating sensory experience. Crafted by Trezegames and Kutty Company, this game will put your reaction time to the test!

As you engage with Read The Color, you'll need to stay sharp and avoid confusion. The challenge intensifies as similar hues may distract you from the true color you're supposed to identify. Can you keep your focus and achieve the highest score within the limited time? It s a fun and addictive way to explore how our brains perceive color and challenges our cognitive abilities!

How to play free Read The Color game online

To play Read The Color, simply start the game and wait for the color to appear in the circle. Quickly identify the name of the color and tap the corresponding button that matches it. Remember, you only have 20 seconds, so think fast and tap accurately to maximize your score!
