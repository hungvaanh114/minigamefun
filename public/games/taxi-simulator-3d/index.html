Taxi Simulator 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Taxi Simulator 3D, an immersive taxi driver simulation game that lets you take the driver's seat and experience the rush of city driving. With ten unique levels to navigate, you'll have the opportunity to pick up passengers and transport them to their specified destinations. Each level is designed with increasing challenges, keeping you engaged and on your toes as you race against the clock.

As a taxi driver, your main objective is to complete transport missions meticulously and on schedule. You will encounter various road conditions and will need to manage your vehicle's speed and direction effectively to ensure a smooth ride for your passengers. The game provides realistic driving mechanics, allowing you to feel the thrill of maneuvering through a bustling cityscape. Be prepared for unforeseen obstacles like traffic jams, pedestrians, and unexpected turns as you work to earn a high score and unlock new levels!

Taxi Simulator 3D offers extensive customization options for your taxi, giving you the freedom to select the vehicle that best suits your driving style. With stunning graphics and immersive sound effects, you will feel like you're truly behind the wheel. Complete each mission to gain experience and improve your skills as a professional taxi driver!

How to play free Taxi Simulator 3D game online

To play Taxi Simulator 3D, simply use the arrow keys or touch controls to navigate your taxi through the city. Follow the on-screen prompts to pick up passengers and drop them off at their destinations. Make sure to manage your time wisely to complete each mission successfully!
