Beauty Makeup Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the enchanting world of the Beauty Makeup Salon, where a beautiful princess awakens from a long slumber under a wicked witch's curse. After many years in her castle, she has been rescued by a brave prince who bravely battled the fierce dragon that guarded her. In celebration of her awakening, the kingdom is hosting a magnificent banquet, and the princess is eager to look her very best for the prince who saved her.

The princess is excited to explore her expansive fitting room, brimming with an array of stunning outfits, fancy shoes, and exquisite jewelry. With so many beautiful options at her disposal, she seeks your help in selecting the perfect makeup and costume to make her the star of the evening. Choose sparkling dresses, stylish shoes, and elegant accessories to craft a look that will captivate the hearts of all guests and showcase her beauty.

Unleash your creativity as you mix and match various makeup styles, from soft and natural to bold and dramatic. Don t forget to add the finishing touches with charming hairstyles and dazzling jewelry every detail counts! Help the princess prepare for her grand entrance so that she can dazzle not just her prince, but the entire kingdom as well.

How to play free Beauty Makeup Salon game online

To play the Beauty Makeup Salon game, start by selecting makeup options from the menu. Use your mouse to apply makeup, choose outfits, and access accessories. Click on the items you want, and drag them onto the princess to see how she looks. Experiment with various combinations to create the ultimate beautiful princess for the banquet!
