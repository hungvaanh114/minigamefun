Xmas Mahjong

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the festive spirit with Xmas Mahjong, a delightful HTML5 game that combines traditional mahjong mechanics with a charming Christmas theme. Your goal is to connect all the Christmas-themed tiles in order to clear the board. Engage your mind and challenge yourself to discover how far you can progress through the various levels. Can you reach the maximum level?

As you navigate through the colorful tiles adorned with images of Christmas trees, ornaments, snowflakes, and more, the objective is to match corresponding tiles and eliminate them from the board. With a user-friendly interface, Xmas Mahjong is suitable for players of all ages, making it an ideal game for family gatherings during the festive season. Each play session offers a refreshing challenge, so you can return again and again to enjoy the snowy puzzles.

Should you find yourself stuck or in need of a nudge in the right direction, the game conveniently provides Hint and Shuffle buttons. Use the Hint button for a helpful suggestion on which tiles to connect next, or click Shuffle to rearrange the remaining tiles on the board when you're faced with limited options. Enjoy the merry gameplay as you strive to complete each level while basking in the holiday cheer.

How to play free Xmas Mahjong game online

To play Xmas Mahjong, start by selecting two matching tiles that are free on at least one side. Once paired, the tiles will disappear, and your task is to continue this process until all tiles have been removed from the board. Use the Hint and Shuffle buttons when needed to assist in your gameplay!
