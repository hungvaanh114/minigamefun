Battle City Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Battle City Online, a nostalgic journey back to your childhood where you can command your very own tank across 35 unique levels! Each map presents a variety of terrains and obstacles, providing a challenging gaming experience. Navigate through exciting landscapes and defend the city against enemy tanks with impressive AI. Each tank is equipped for a different type of battle, and you will need to strategize to utilize their strengths effectively.

Engage in multiplayer modes where you can enjoy local gameplay with friends or prepare for the thrilling online battles that are coming soon. The game also allows you to unleash your creativity by designing custom maps that you can share and challenge your friends with. While battling, be mindful of the obstacle-laden maps that vary significantly and require quick thinking to master.

As you progress, you can take advantage of the in-game shop where players can buy or sell rewards and their uniquely designed maps. Anticipate a coin system in the future for betting and tournaments, which promises to add an extra layer of excitement. The developers are also working on joystick support for the map editor, ensuring an intuitive design experience. With different AI difficulty settings available, players can start at easy levels and gradually work their way up to the hard mode for intense challenges.

How to play free Battle City Online game online

To play Battle City Online, simply enter the game lobby and choose a gameplay mode. You can select between single-player or multiplayer options. Use the directional keys to control your tank and shoot using the designated attack key. Collect power-ups for enhanced abilities and work with teammates to strategize and protect your city!
