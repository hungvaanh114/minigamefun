Smurfs

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Smurfs, also known as Les Schtroumpfs in French and De Smurfen in Dutch, are endearing characters originating from a Belgian comic franchise. These small, blue, humanoid beings reside in whimsical mushroom-shaped dwellings nestled within a lush forest. The franchise first emerged in 1958 under the creativity of Belgian comic artist Peyo, the pseudonym of Pierre Culliford. Over the decades, the Smurfs have become iconic, with a diverse cast of over 100 characters, each named after distinctive traits they embody.

From the mischievous Jokey Smurf, who delights in pulling pranks on his friends, to the beloved Smurfette, the series' first female Smurf, every character brings a unique charm to this colorful world. Their characteristic Phrygian caps serve as a symbol of liberty, drawing from historical significance in modern culture. The Smurfs adventures often revolve around their efforts to thwart the evil wizard Gargamel, who constantly seeks to capture them, providing a delightful mix of humor, friendship, and action.

Throughout the years, the Smurfs have transcended their comic origins, expanding into animated series, films, merchandise, and video games, solidifying their place in popular culture and the hearts of fans worldwide.
