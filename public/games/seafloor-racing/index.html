To play Seafloor Racing, use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle. Accelerate with the up arrow or W, and brake using the down arrow or S. Stay alert as you race, avoiding obstacles while maintaining your speed to secure a win! Enjoy the thrill of racing below the sea!
