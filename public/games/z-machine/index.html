Z Machine

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the electrifying realm of the Z-Machine, where an exhilarating arcade environment comes alive with vibrant graphics and intense action. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, you are tasked with taking down endless hordes of the undead. Armed with a formidable tank, you ll navigate through chaotic streets filled with zombies, all while powering your way through this thrilling adventure.

Your primary objective is to eliminate zombies while collecting cash to boost your arsenal. Every defeat not only brings you rewards but also threats from more resilient creatures emerging from the shadows. After gathering the funds, your secondary mission is to transport them back to your warehouse, completing your objectives and ensuring your survival.

As you progress, you ll have the opportunity to upgrade your tank. Increasing your vehicle's capabilities is essential for enhancing both combat effectiveness and defensive attributes. Choose from various upgrades to tailor your tank according to your playstyle, ensuring you can handle the escalating wave of challenges. The more zombies you eradicate, the more money you make, allowing you to fully realize the tank's potential!

How to play free Z Machine game online

To play Z Machine, simply use the arrow keys or WASD to maneuver your tank. Aim your weapon with the mouse and click to shoot. Collect cash from defeated zombies and return it to your warehouse to score points. Don t forget to upgrade your tank for improved performance!
