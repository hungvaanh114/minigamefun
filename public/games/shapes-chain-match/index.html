Shapes Chain Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Shapes Chain Match, an engaging puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning and strategic thinking. With its vibrant visuals and intuitive gameplay, you ll find yourself connecting similar shapes in a race against time. Utilize either your touchscreen or mouse to bring together shapes of the same type. Your goal is simple: align three or more identical shapes horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to change their colors to blue.

As you link the shapes, watch as the blocks transform, lighting up in brilliant shades as you complete each successful connection. Your task is to continue linking shapes until every block on the grid has turned blue. This will signify that you ve completed the level! With 24 levels to conquer, each stage becomes progressively challenging, testing your skills and determination. Strategize your moves wisely, as you ll need to think ahead to master the puzzles presented in each level.

Shapes Chain Match is not just about speed; it s about precision and critical thinking. Will you rise to the challenge and become a Shapes Chain Master? Jump in, and see how quickly you can turn all the blocks blue!

How to play free Shapes Chain Match game online

To play the game, simply select a shape by tapping or clicking on it. Then, tap or click on adjacent shapes of the same type to connect them. Remember, you need to create groups of three or more identical shapes to change their color. Strategically make your moves to complete each level by turning all blocks blue!
