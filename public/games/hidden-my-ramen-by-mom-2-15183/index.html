Hidden My Ramen by Mom 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exciting game "Hidden My Ramen by Mom 2," players embark on a delightful quest filled with mystery and intrigue. The primary objective is simple yet engaging: locate your hidden ramen, which your mom has cleverly concealed once again. You find yourself in a whimsical world where the love for ramen drives your every action. However, there's a twist your mom is on the lookout, and she won t let you indulge in your favorite dish that easily.

This puzzle adventure requires your wits and strategic thinking. As you navigate through various levels, you'll encounter a range of clever obstacles and traps designed to thwart your efforts. The game encourages players to interact with the environment by utilizing different props and tools at their disposal to outsmart their mom. As you search for the prized ramen, you ll also need to cook it once you've found the ingredients, adding another layer of complexity to your quest.

With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, "Hidden My Ramen by Mom 2" brings a sense of fun and challenge that keeps players on their toes. Each level presents unique puzzles to solve and rewards for your culinary adventure. Get ready to dive into a world where a love for ramen sparks thrilling escapades. Can you outsmart your mom and enjoy your ramen feast? Your exciting adventure awaits!

How to play free Hidden My Ramen by Mom 2 game online

To play "Hidden My Ramen by Mom 2," use your keyboard or touchscreen to navigate your character through the house. Search for clues and items that will help you locate the hidden ramen while avoiding traps and your mom's watchful eye. Use collected props wisely to outsmart obstacles and complete your mission!
