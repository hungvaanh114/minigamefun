Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Taylor in an exciting bakery adventure as she prepares to treat her best friend Jessica for her upcoming birthday! Jessica adores dolls and has a sweet tooth for lemon cakes, making it the perfect opportunity for Taylor to combine both passions into one delightful creation. In the Baby Taylor Doll Cake Design - Bakery Game, you'll help Taylor design and bake a stunning doll cake that is sure to impress Jessica.

As you dive into this fun bakery challenge, you'll be tasked with gathering ingredients, mixing, and baking the cake to perfection. With your creative flair, you will also have the chance to decorate the cake, adding beautiful designs and decorations that capture Jessica's love for dolls. This delightful game blends culinary skills with artistic flair, making it a wholesome experience for players of all ages.

Can you rise to the challenge and help Taylor create the most beautiful doll cake ever? Stand by her side to make this birthday unforgettable for Jessica. Put on your baker's apron and prepare for a journey filled with flavor, creativity, and friendship!

How to play free Baby Taylor Doll Cake Design - Bakery Game online

To start playing the Baby Taylor Doll Cake Design - Bakery Game, follow the on-screen instructions to gather the necessary ingredients. Then mix, bake, and cool the cake. Once baked, unleash your creativity by selecting different decorations and designs to make the cake truly special for Jessica's birthday!
