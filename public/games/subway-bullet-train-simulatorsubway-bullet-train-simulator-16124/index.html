Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in Subway Bullet Train Simulator, where the pulse-pounding excitement of being a train driver awaits you. This expertly crafted train racing simulation allows you to step into the shoes of a professional train operator, commanding some of the most sought-after 3D commercial trains available. Experience the thrill of the tracks as you navigate the bustling metro systems of a vibrant city, testing your driving skills to the limit.

In this city metro train simulator, you will face the challenges of operating a long vehicle through tight corners and sharp curves. Take on the role of a US locomotive engineer, steering a high-speed bullet train through cleverly designed underground tunnels. Your mission is to control the speed of the 3D railroad cars while maintaining precision on the metro railway tracks. Slow down carefully when approaching tricky turns to avoid accidents as your aim is to become the ultimate metro train driver the city has ever seen.

As you settle into the driver's seat, master the controls and indicators within the train's cabin. Feel the adrenaline surge as you accelerate through the rails at lightning-fast speeds, knowing that the express is wholly under your command. Be vigilant and stop the train before entering any danger zones while picking up passengers at designated stops. The ability to change camera angles offers a more enjoyable and personalized driving experience. Prepare yourself for an immersive adventure filled with realistic subway train simulations, sure to keep your excitement levels sky-high!

How to play free Subway Bullet Train Simulator game online

To play Subway Bullet Train Simulator, simply launch the game from your browser. Use directional controls to navigate your train on the tracks. Adjust your speed and manage passenger pickups, all while avoiding obstacles. Remember to monitor the signals and stop at stations to successfully complete each level!
