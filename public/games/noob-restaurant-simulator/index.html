Noob Restaurant Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Noob Restaurant Simulator, where your culinary adventure begins! Step into the shoes of Noob as you navigate the challenges of running a bustling restaurant. Customers flock to your establishment, eager to savor the delightful dishes you prepare. Your mission is simple yet engaging: serve your patrons delicious meals like mouthwatering soup, savory kebabs, and delectable cake to keep their hunger at bay and their spirits high.

As you take orders, it s essential to prioritize customer satisfaction to maintain your restaurant s reputation. Each happy customer boosts your restaurant score, while any disgruntled diners could spell disaster for your establishment. The game cleverly combines time management and cooking skills, making it both challenging and enjoyable. Don't forget to close the shop at the end of the day to recharge for the next round and head to the market for supplies to enhance your culinary repertoire!

Noob Restaurant Simulator invites you to immerse yourself in a lighthearted environment filled with food, fun, and excitement. With every dish you serve, you'll unlock new recipes and opportunities to expand your culinary empire. Are you ready to rise to the challenge and become the most popular restaurant in town?

How to play free Noob Restaurant Simulator game online

To play Noob Restaurant Simulator, start by choosing a dish from the menu to prepare. Follow the prompts to cook the meal, serve it to your customers, and collect feedback. Manage your time wisely, and ensure to restock supplies from the market when needed. Keep your customers happy for a successful dining experience!


Instructions

Check the stock by pressing the Tab button Use WASD Keys to play Cook by pressing the E button
