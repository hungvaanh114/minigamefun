Lord of the Knights

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the epic world of Lord of the Knights, where valor and strategy are put to the ultimate test. As a noble lord adorned in shining armor, your primary objective is to protect your castle and ensure the safety of your subjects. The tranquility of your city is threatened by an ominous army of skeleton warriors, and only you can stand against this formidable foe. With a powerful crossbow in hand and fortifications at your disposal, you are tasked with defending your realm.

Equipped with essential gear and the fierce determination of a true leader, you stand ready to confront the impending danger. As the skeletal horde advances, your skills as a marksman will be vital. Take aim, unleash a flurry of bolts, and eliminate the skeletal threat before they breach your defenses. But be vigilant every shot counts, and timing is crucial in this exhilarating shooting adventure.

In Lord of the Knights, you'll also have the opportunity to command your own army. Strategically position your troops and strengthen your defenses to bolster your chances of survival. Your heroic efforts can inspire your subjects, rallying them to stand with you against the menacing undead. The fate of your castle lies in your hands, so prepare for an exhilarating battle for your honor and kingdom.

How to play free Lord of the Knights game online

To play Lord of the Knights, begin by selecting your character and familiarizing yourself with the controls. Aim your crossbow with precision and fire at the oncoming skeletons. Use the keyboard to navigate and activate special abilities to repel the invaders. Strategically place your soldiers around the castle for an effective defense.
