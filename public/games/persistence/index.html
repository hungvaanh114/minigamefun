Persistence

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the game Persistence, you will step into the shoes of the final survivor of the Nenju tribe. As darkness envelops your homeland, it has been ravaged by a horde of terrifying monsters. Your primary objective is to guide this lone survivor to safety in Eiju. But beware, the journey will demand an incredible amount of "Persistence" as you navigate the numerous challenges and obstacles that lie ahead.

What sets Persistence apart is its innovative approach to storytelling. The game cleverly breaks the fourth wall, allowing players to gain insights through the protagonist's dialogues. These lines might offer valuable hints to assist you in your quest or they could lead you astray! As you progress, remember to utilize the arrow keys to cycle through various game options and hit that return key to make your selection. It s an adventure that invites you to push forward and save the last glimmer of hope for the Nenju tribe.
