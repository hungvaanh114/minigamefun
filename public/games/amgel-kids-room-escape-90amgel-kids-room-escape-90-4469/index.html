AmgelEscape presents the thrilling adventure of Amgel Kids Room Escape 90, a captivating point-and-click escape game that tests your problem-solving skills and sharpens your attention to detail. In this vibrant and colorful kids' room, you find yourself mysteriously locked in, facing the challenge of unlocking your way out. As you navigate through the charming yet perplexing environment, every corner holds clues and secrets waiting to be discovered.
