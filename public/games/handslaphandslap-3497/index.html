Hands is an engaging multiplayer game designed for two players, where quick reflexes and strategy come into play. Choose your preferred arm and get ready to face off against your opponent. The game is divided into two roles: the red side, which takes on the role of the attacker, and the blue side, tasked with dodging incoming strikes. Each round is an exciting test of skill and agility as players attempt to outmaneuver each other.

The objective is straightforward be the first to score 10 points to claim victory. The red player earns a single point for each successful strike against their opponent. If you re on the blue side and successfully dodge the attack, you get to switch roles, allowing both players the chance to showcase their strengths and strategies. The dynamic nature of the game ensures that every round is filled with anticipation and excitement, making Handslap a thrilling experience for both players regardless of their skill levels.

Hands provides a fast-paced environment where participants must think on their feet and react quickly. Each round s fast tempo keeps players engaged and eager for the next challenge. Whether you re playing casually with friends or in a more competitive setting, the satisfaction of executing a successful dodge or a well-timed strike adds to the overall fun. Join your friends, and let the wits and reflexes determine the true champion in this exhilarating game.

How to play free Handslap game online

To get started with Handslap, first, decide who will be the red attacker and who will be the blue dodger. Once the game begins, the red player will attempt to land strikes while the blue player skillfully dodges. After each successful dodge, players will switch roles until someone reaches 10 points!
