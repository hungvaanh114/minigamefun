Draw Around

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Unleash your inner artist with Draw Around! Dive into a colorful world where your creativity takes center stage. In this engaging game, you ll encounter a variety of images that invite you to bring them to life through drawing and coloring. It s a unique opportunity to relax and express your artistic flair at your own pace. Whether you see yourself as a Picasso or simply someone who enjoys doodling, this game is made for you.

As you embark on your artistic journey, you ll need to swipe your finger to paint lines that complete the patterns displayed on your screen. Each level presents a fresh and exciting design challenge, allowing you to create stunning works of art. Collect gems along the way to unlock an array of unique art supplies, including different pencils, pens, and brushes that can transform the way you approach each drawing.

With over 300 levels filled with delightful line painting fun, there s no shortage of creative opportunities to explore. So, gear up for a painting adventure that not only sharpens your skills but also provides hours of enjoyment and relaxation!

How to play free Draw Around game online

To play Draw Around, simply swipe your finger across the screen to draw lines and patterns. Aim to match the designs presented on-screen. As you progress, collect gems to unlock a variety of art supplies that enhance your drawing experience. Enjoy the challenge while creating beautiful artwork!
