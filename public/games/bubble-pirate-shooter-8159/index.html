Bubble Pirate Shooter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an adventurous journey with Bubble Pirate Shooter! Set sail in quest of untold riches on the mysterious lost island, where danger and excitement lurk around every corner. But beware! You re not the only treasure hunter navigating these treacherous waters. Rival pirates are hot on your tail, and they won t hesitate to send you to a watery grave if you let your guard down.

Arm yourself with your trusty cannon and prepare for a thrilling showdown! Your objective is clear: pop the bubbles on both port and starboard to create a path to victory. As you fire your cannon, aim strategically to sink enemy pirate ships before they get the chance to attack. Time is of the essence; every second counts, and the wrong move might lead you to face the ferocious fish and sharks that lurk beneath the surface.

With hundreds of colorful bubbles to pop and pirate ships to obliterate, you'll need sharp reflexes and cunning tactics to outmaneuver your adversaries. Immerse yourself in vibrant graphics and exhilarating sounds that bring the high seas to life. The sun shines brightly overhead, but treachery lurks just beneath the waves. Are you ready to get your feet wet and claim your pirate legacy?

Gather your courage and dive into this nautical adventure where only the sharpest and swiftest will emerge victorious. Will you be the captain that walks away with the treasure or will you find yourself lost at sea? Grab your cannon and let the bubble-popping begin!

How to play free Bubble Pirate Shooter game online

To play Bubble Pirate Shooter, use your mouse or touch controls to aim your cannon. Fire at the bubbles and enemy ships by clicking or tapping. Aim carefully to pop as many bubbles as possible while sinking rival pirate vessels. Stay alert to avoid incoming attacks!


Instructions

You have to combine 3 bubbles to pop them in this shooter game
