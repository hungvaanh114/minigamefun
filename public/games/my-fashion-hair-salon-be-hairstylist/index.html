My Fashion Hair Salon - Be Hairstylist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to "My Fashion Hair Salon - Be Hairstylist" where every girl s dream of looking fabulous comes to life! In the world of fashion, a stunning hairstyle can be the crowning glory that makes any girl shine. With your very own salon, you have the opportunity to pamper clients with the most exquisite and trendy hairstyles. Your talented skills can transform simple locks into beautiful styles!

Begin your hairstyling journey by offering a serene and comfortable hair treatment experience. Start by gently washing your clients' hair to ensure it feels fresh and rejuvenated. From there, whip up a nourishing homemade hair mask that will add shine and vitality to their tresses. Once the pampering is complete, it s time to unleash your creativity! Design delicate and chic hairstyles that embody the latest trends, ensuring each girl looks and feels stunning.

But the fun doesn t stop there! Elevate your clients look by selecting fashionable accessories like chic headwear, gorgeous necklaces, and sparkling earrings. Your styling choices will take their look to the next level, allowing them to express their unique fashion sense. Every appointment is an opportunity to let your creativity flow, and every satisfied client will leave your salon beaming with confidence!

How to play free My Fashion Hair Salon - Be Hairstylist game online

To play, simply navigate through the intuitive interface of the game. Start by selecting your clients and proceed to wash their hair. After applying a hair mask, use the styling tools available to create unique hairstyles. Finally, accessorize with headwear and jewelry to complete the look, and watch your clients leave with a smile!
