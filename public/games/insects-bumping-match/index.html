Insects Bumping Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Insects Bumping Match, a fun and engaging puzzle game where strategy meets skill! Your objective is to slide colorful ice cream blocks towards identical blocks to eliminate them and score points! Each successful elimination awards you with 100 points, encouraging you to think ahead and make calculated moves.

However, be careful! Every time you slide a block more than once, the points awarded will decrease by 10 for each additional move. This adds an exciting layer of challenge as you attempt to solve each puzzle with as few moves as possible. Can you maximize your score and clear the ice cream blocks with efficiency? The game features 24 uniquely designed and challenging levels, each progressively testing your problem-solving abilities and strategic thinking.

As you navigate through the levels, you'll face increasingly complex arrangements of blocks, requiring you to adapt your approach and think creatively. With its vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics, Insects Bumping Match promises hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Get ready to slide, eliminate, and rack up points in this delightful puzzle adventure!

How to play free Insects Bumping Match game online

To play Insects Bumping Match, simply click and drag the ice cream blocks to slide them. Align identical blocks to eliminate them, aiming to clear the board using the fewest moves possible. Keep an eye on your score, and think strategically to increase your points!
