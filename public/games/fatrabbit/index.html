FatRabbit

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the whimsical world of FatRabbit, you can leave behind the pressure of dieting and embrace a delightful adventure filled with delicious treats! Your objective is to help your adorable rabbit indulge in an array of sweets, cakes, and cookies raining down on you from above. As you navigate this sugary buffet, every bite counts as your fluffy friend gets fatter with each tasty morsel!

Unlock exciting gifts that not only reward you with shiny coins but also provide practical bonuses, enhancing your gameplay experience. These bonuses will help you maneuver through the game's obstacles more effectively. But be careful! There are traps scattered throughout the candy-coated environment that your bunny must avoid. Keep an eye out and time your hops perfectly to steer clear of danger.

As you progress, explore new horizons and gather even more coins to unlock special features. The charming aesthetic of FatRabbit and its engaging gameplay will keep you immersed in this delightful escapade, as you work to grow the cutest, chubbiest bunny in the game. So why wait? Dive into this enchanting universe and indulge in the sweetest journey with your very own FatRabbit!

How to play free FatRabbit game online

To play FatRabbit, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your bunny left or right. Collect falling sweets while avoiding traps. Tap to jump over obstacles and use any unlocked bonuses for smoother navigation. Enjoy the game and have fun fattening up your adorable rabbit!


Instructions

UP - Jump 2x UP - Double jump Left - Move left Right - Move right E - Reduce speed of fruits and cakes R - Reduce overweight
