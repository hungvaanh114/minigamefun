Treasure Quest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Treasure Quest, where you join Prince Arthur on his noble quest to unearth hidden treasures! With a gameplay experience that flows seamlessly, you will help Arthur fulfill his father's wishes of collecting their family treasures. The allure of various colorful treasures awaits you as you navigate this captivating world.

The game features an assortment of colorful treasures, each represented by matching bowls. Your task is simple yet challenging: pull the correct pin to release the treasure into its corresponding bowl. However, precision is key! If you select the wrong pin, the treasure will be misplaced, and the game will start over. This engaging mechanic keeps you on your toes as you attempt to match treasures to their rightful bowls.

As you progress through the game, your goal remains clear: collect as many treasures as possible by skillfully pulling pins corresponding to the bowl color. The more treasures you collect, the greater your rewards will be. Treasure Quest combines strategy and fun, making it a delightful experience for treasure-hunting enthusiasts!

How to play free Treasure Quest game online

To play Treasure Quest, begin by selecting the right pin that matches the color of the bowl below. Pull the pin to release the treasure, ensuring it lands in the correct bowl. If you make a mistake, just start over and try again. Good luck on your treasure-hunting adventure!
