Survivor Rainbow Monster

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Survivor Rainbow Monster, the mobile adaptation of the thrilling Rainbow Game! As the ominous Red Monster, I will guide you through this harrowing experience. Once a carefree child, you find yourself trapped after a school trip to the eerie Spooky Park. Your playtime has warped into a survival game against unimaginable odds.

The storyline unfolds as you, an unnamed child, must endure five terrifying nights in a desolate amusement park where you are not alone. The only other entity present is the menacing Rainbow Monster that hunts you relentlessly. Each night presents a unique set of tasks and challenges designed to test your wits and courage. The challenges span various scenarios, demanding quick thinking and stealth to avoid detection. Your heart will race as you navigate the dark corners of the park, searching for items like the elusive Bl on Night 1 while constantly evading your colorful pursuer. The anticipation and suspense will keep you on the edge of your seat!

With each passing night, the complexity of the game escalates as the Rainbow Monster becomes increasingly aggressive. You must gather essential resources and solve puzzles to unlock the secrets of Spooky Park and ultimately escape the grip of the Rainbow Monster. Are you ready to face your fears and conquer this nightmarish ordeal?

How to play free Survivor Rainbow Monster game online

To play Survivor Rainbow Monster, navigate through the dark park using your controls to move and hide from the Rainbow Monster. Complete your nightly tasks by locating specific items and solving puzzles while remaining undetected. Utilize strategic hiding spots and listen for audio cues to avoid encountering the monster.
