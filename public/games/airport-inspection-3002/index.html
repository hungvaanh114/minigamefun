Airport Inspection

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Airport Inspection, where attention to detail and diligence are your greatest allies! As an airport security officer, your mission is to ensure the safety and security of every passenger boarding the flight. The inspection process comprises three critical stages: passport control, body check, and backpack check.

During passport control, you must diligently verify that each passenger's name, age, and occupation match the details provided on their documents. It is essential to be vigilant and watch for inconsistencies or mismatches that could indicate an imposter. Additionally, in the body check phase, you will examine each individual for any prohibited items. This includes ensuring that no dangerous objects such as knives, animals, or even dead bodies are concealed on their person.

The final step of the inspection involves a thorough backpack check. Here, you'll scrutinize every luggage piece for banned items. Your keen eye for detail is crucial in spotting anything suspicious that could pose a threat to flight safety. Remember, your sharp instincts can make all the difference in preventing potential dangers and maintaining a secure travel environment.

How to play free Airport Inspection game online

To play Airport Inspection, simply log in to the game and start your role as a security officer. Follow the on-screen instructions to perform checks on passengers by verifying their documents, examining their bodies and personal belongings for any suspicious items. Stay alert and trust your instincts to identify any potential threats!
