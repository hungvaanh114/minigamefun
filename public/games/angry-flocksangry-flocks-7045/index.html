Welcome to the vibrant world of Angry Flocks, where you'll embark on an exhilarating journey to save the Earth from an alien invasion! Assemble your flock and prepare to unleash your slingshot skills against the extraterrestrial foes that threaten your homeland. With each level presenting unique challenges, you'll need both strategy and precision to progress through this captivating universe.

In Angry Flocks, harness the power of special abilities that take your aim to the next level! Each little hero you control possesses unique strengths, allowing you to adapt your gameplay as you tackle various alien fortifications. Your mission is not just to defeat the aliens but to obliterate their shelters, clearing the path to victory. Get ready for epic boss battles that will test your mastery of the slingshot, as you unlock new power-ups to enhance your gameplay. With a total of 40 tricky levels, you'll be entertained for hours as you strategize and execute your gameplay plans effectively!

Join forces with fellow players, experience thrilling fantasy elements, and enjoy the colorful graphics and engaging sound effects that make Angry Flocks a must-play. Are you prepared for the challenge? Gather your flock and dive into this action-packed adventure!

How to play free Angry Flocks game online

To play Angry Flocks, simply use your mouse or finger to aim and launch your heroes at the alien structures. Adjust your angle for optimal power as you release your slingshot. Aim for the critical weak points of the shelters to maximize damage, and utilize special abilities at the right moment to clear levels efficiently. Good luck!
