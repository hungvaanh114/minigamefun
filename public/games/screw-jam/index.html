How to play free Screw Jam game online

To play Screw Jam, simply touch and drag the screws on the screen to untwist them. You can rotate the pieces in any direction to find the right configuration. Once all the screws are released, you ll advance to the next level. Enjoy the satisfying feeling as you solve each puzzle!
