Birds Connect Deluxe

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Birds Connect Deluxe is an engaging and classic puzzle game that promises to challenge your strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. The main objective is to clear the game board by eliminating all the tiles present. You can achieve this by connecting identical tiles to each other, which creates a satisfying sense of accomplishment upon successfully matching them. However, be mindful of the connection rules; you are allowed to make no more than two turns for each connection, adding an intriguing level of strategy to the gameplay.

The game features 15 distinct and challenging levels, ensuring a progressive increase in difficulty that keeps players engaged and eager to advance further. Each level introduces different tile arrangements and layouts, enhancing your problem-solving abilities as you devise the best approach to eliminate all tiles from the board. Plus, completing a level ahead of the designated time grants you time bonuses, rewarding your swift decision-making and efficiency.

Birds Connect Deluxe is perfect for puzzle enthusiasts looking for a delightful way to pass the time. Its charming visuals and addictive gameplay make it accessible to players of all ages, whether you are a seasoned gamer or someone new to the world of puzzles. Dive into the delightful world of Birds Connect Deluxe and test your skills as you connect, eliminate, and conquer those pesky tiles!

How to play free Birds Connect Deluxe game online

To play Birds Connect Deluxe, start by identifying two identical tiles on the board. Click on one tile, then trace a path to the other, making sure to only use two turns to connect them. Keep eliminating tiles until the board is clear, and aim for the best time to earn bonuses!
