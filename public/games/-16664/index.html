Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the festive joy of winter with Flappy Snowball Xmas, a delightful game that adds a merry twist to the classic flappy genre. Dive into a world of swirling snowflakes and cheerful snowmen as you embark on a challenging journey to guide your snowball through a series of festive obstacles. With each tap or click, your cute snowball bounces upward, testing your timing and agility. The goal is to navigate through the gaps without colliding with the barriers, all while collecting points to earn bonus rewards.

Show off your skills by performing perfect dunks, which not only add to your score but also unlock more adorable snowman characters to play with. The excitement grows as you strive for higher scores, paving the way for a challenging yet ultra-fun gaming experience. Merry Christmas fills the air as you enjoy this engaging gameplay that encourages replayability. Will you rise to the challenge and become a Flappy Snowball master? The festive atmosphere and enchanting graphics create a truly joyful gaming environment, promising hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Happy playing!

How to play free Flappy Snowball Xmas game online

To navigate the Flappy Snowball, simply click or tap your screen to make the snowball bounce upwards. Aim to dodge the holiday-themed obstacles while trying to land perfect dunks. Collect points and progressively unlock various snowman characters by achieving high scores. Enjoy the cheerful winter wonderland!
