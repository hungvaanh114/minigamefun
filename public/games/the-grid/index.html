The Grid

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The Grid is a fast paced puzzle game where the players are required to repeat the pattern shown in the Grid. The players have only a few seconds to repeat the correct pattern in order to advance further. The challenges become more and more difficult as you progress and some may need to change your perspective as well. Try to score as much as you can before the time runs out and then try again to beat your high score or be at the top of the leaderboard. The grid is also mobile friendly which only requires click / tap controls.
