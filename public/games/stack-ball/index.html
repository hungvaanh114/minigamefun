Stack Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating gaming experience with Stack Ball, the next installment in the addictive Helix series! In this fast-paced game, your primary objective is to guide a bouncing ball through a vibrant tower composed of colorful tiles while avoiding dangerous black ones. As the ball falls, you need to swiftly tap to break through the layers, allowing gravity to propel your ball down as fast as possible. The further you descend, the more thrilling the experience becomes!

Stack Ball challenges your reflexes and concentration as you navigate through intricate levels filled with unexpected obstacles. The rhythm of the gameplay, combined with catchy sound effects and captivating graphics, makes each session enjoyable and encourages players to return for more. With every slide and bounce, you'll find yourself enmeshed in a whirlwind of colors and exhilarating motion. Just keep an eye on those black tiles, as hitting them will cost you your progress. The key to mastering Stack Ball lies in timing your taps and ensuring the ball continues its descent unhindered, all while soaking in the dynamic atmosphere of the game.

Want to know how to play this exciting game? It s simple! Tap the screen to let your ball break through the stacks, while making sure it doesn't land on the black tiles. The goal is to reach the bottom of the tower as fast as possible while staying clear of hazards. As you advance, the levels become more challenging, but the satisfying feeling of breaking those blocks keeps you hooked!

How to play free Stack Ball game online

To play Stack Ball, simply tap the screen to make your ball bounce and break through the colorful layers of the tower. Avoid touching the black tiles, as they will end your game. Keep tapping to build momentum and race toward the bottom!
