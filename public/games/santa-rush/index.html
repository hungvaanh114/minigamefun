Santa Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a festive adventure in Santa Rush, where the holiday spirit meets thrilling gameplay! Christmas has arrived, and Santa is on a mission to deliver gifts at lightning speed. However, the journey is fraught with challenges as you guide Santa through a snow-covered landscape. Your primary objective? Help him sprint as far as possible, navigating the treacherous terrain without letting him slip on the icy patches!

As you dash forward, you'll encounter candy-filled sugar sticks that can boost your score. Collect these delicious treats to increase your points and showcase your skills. Keep an eye out for large gaps in the ground that require you to perform in-air jumps using springs scattered along the path. Mastering the timing of your jumps will be key to ensuring Santa soars over obstacles and maintains his rapid pace.

However, the adventure is not without its dangers. Watch out for icy spikes that lurk along the way, as contact with them could bring Santa s exciting journey to a swift end. With each level, not only will the challenges intensify, but the rewards will also grow greater. Can you help Santa navigate this winter wonderland while securing a high score? Gear up for an exhilarating ride filled with festive fun!

How to play free Santa Rush game online

To play Santa Rush, use the arrow keys or swipe your finger on mobile devices to control Santa's movements. Jump over obstacles by pressing the jump key, and collect sugar sticks for extra points. Stay alert to avoid icy spikes and keep Santa moving swiftly down the snowy path!
