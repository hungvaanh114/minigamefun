How to play free Chocolate Tetris Game online

To play the Chocolate Tetris Game, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard. The left and right arrows will move chocolate blocks sideways, while the down arrow will speed them up. Rotate the blocks using the up arrow to fit them perfectly and complete rows. Enjoy the sweet fun!
