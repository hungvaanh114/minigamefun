In the engaging puzzle game Collect the Ball, players are tasked with maneuvering a white ball into a designated container located at the bottom of the screen. The challenge lies in the various obstacles represented by different shapes positioned between the ball and its target. Each level presents a unique arrangement of these figures, requiring strategic thinking and planning to overcome them.

To successfully guide the ball to its resting place, players must utilize the black pieces available at their disposal. These movable objects can be adjusted vertically or rotated, providing an essential tool for solving the puzzles. By double-clicking on the screen or pressing the mouse button twice, you can manipulate these shapes to create pathways for the ball. It s crucial to think critically and visualize multiple possibilities before settling on the optimal configuration.

Once you have arranged the pieces to your satisfaction, simply press the Start button located in the upper left corner of the screen to set the ball in motion. The game's dynamic nature will keep you engaged as you tackle progressively challenging levels, each inviting you to enhance your problem-solving skills while enjoying a visually stimulating experience.

How to play free Collect the Ball game online

To play Collect the Ball, simply navigate through the levels by moving the black shapes around with your mouse or by tapping on the screen. Adjust their positions and orientations to create a clear path for the ball. After positioning the objects wisely, click the Start button to initiate the ball's journey into the container!
