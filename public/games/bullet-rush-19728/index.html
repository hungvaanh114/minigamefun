Bullet Rush

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Bullet Rush, where you find yourself on a perilous island overshadowed by a catastrophic outbreak. A deadly virus has mutated the local wildlife and turned them into fearsome green monsters, posing an extreme threat to your survival. With your primary objective being to eliminate these creatures, you ll need to navigate through treacherous territories, gathering your courage and firepower to combat the overwhelming numbers you ll face.

Your mission is clear: clear the island of these monstrous foes and reach the designated extraction point where a rescue plane awaits. But beware; the island is teeming with dangers at every corner. Utilize a variety of weapons at your disposal, and as you progress, level up and upgrade your arsenal to take on even more formidable enemies. The intense gameplay, requiring skill and strategy, will keep you on the edge of your seat as you strive to survive against the relentless hordes.

As you continue your journey through the islands, each one presents its unique challenges, and no two experiences are the same. With each successful mission, you will unlock new levels and features, enhancing your gameplay and immersing you even deeper into the action-packed world of Bullet Rush.

How to play free Bullet Rush game online

To enjoy the Bullet Rush game online, start by using your mouse or touchpad to aim and shoot at the green monsters. Move your character with the WASD keys for agile navigation. Collect in-game items and upgrade your weapons as you progress towards the extraction point. Stay alert and enjoy the action!
