Shelf Shift Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Test your strategy and speed in Shelf Shift Match! Shift items on the shelves to create perfect matches of three, clearing the way to reveal hidden shadowed items. But beware&amp;mdash;unwise moves can block your shelves, leaving no space to shift and leading to game over! If youre stuck, use the shuffle button to mix things up and find new matches.
