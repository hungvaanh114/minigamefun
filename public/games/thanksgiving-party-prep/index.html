Thanksgiving Party Prep

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to assist Emily in her Thanksgiving dinner preparations in the engaging game, Thanksgiving Party Prep! Emily is eager to impress her guests with a spectacular feast, and she needs your help to make it happen. Your journey begins as you help Emily choose a stunning festive outfit that will make her the star of the celebration.

After selecting the perfect attire, it's time to enhance her look with a tasteful autumn-inspired makeup palette and a stylish new hairstyle. Once Emily is dressed to perfection, you'll transition into her charming kitchen, where the real fun begins! Your culinary skills will be put to the test as you prepare a mouthwatering turkey and a delectable pumpkin pie, essential treats for any Thanksgiving gathering.

But the excitement doesn't stop there! With the cooking completed, it's time to showcase your decorating talents. Assist Emily in setting up an inviting table for her guests, ensuring every detail is just right to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Immerse yourself in the joy of creating the ultimate Thanksgiving dinner as you help Emily make unforgettable memories with her loved ones.

How to play free Thanksgiving Party Prep game online

To play Thanksgiving Party Prep, use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate through various activities. Begin by selecting fashionable outfits and applying makeup, then follow the prompts to cook the turkey and pie. Finally, drag and drop tableware to set the perfect dining scene for Emily s guests!


Instructions

Use your mouse to play the game on a desktop and tap to play on mobile devices
