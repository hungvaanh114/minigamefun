How to play free Drift Cup Racing game online

To play Drift Cup Racing, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate your car. The up arrow accelerates while the left and right arrows help you steer. Make sure to time your drifts perfectly around corners by letting off the accelerator at the right moment to maintain speed and control.
