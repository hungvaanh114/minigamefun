Knee Surgery Simulator

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the innovative game "Knee Surgery Simulator," players step into the role of a skilled surgeon tasked with alleviating Sarah's debilitating pain caused by Knee Osteoarthritis. After exhausting all possibilities with medication and other treatments, Sarah s only hope rests on undergoing a critical knee joint replacement surgery. This realistic simulation immerses you in the operating room, where your expertise will determine the outcome of this medical endeavor.

Your mission is to delicately and expertly replace Sarah's damaged knee joint, eliminating the bone-on-bone friction that has caused her immense suffering. As you navigate through each step of the surgical process, you must make precise incisions, carefully manage the anatomy of the knee, and employ advanced medical tools. Each decision impacts the success rate of the procedure, so a focus on accuracy and technique is essential. Successfully completing the surgery will lead to Sarah's recovery.

Once the operation is a success, the game allows you to express your creativity by dressing Sarah in festive attire to celebrate her journey to recovery. Choose from a variety of outfits and accessories, marking a fresh start for Sarah's life free from pain. This engaging combination of medical simulation and fashion makes "Knee Surgery Simulator" a unique gaming experience that promotes empathy while enhancing your surgical skills.

How to play free Knee Surgery Simulator game online

To play the "Knee Surgery Simulator" online, simply navigate to the game interface, where you will see a step-by-step guide to conduct the surgery. Follow on-screen prompts to select tools, make incisions, and complete each surgical task. Be meticulous, and you will help Sarah recover successfully!
