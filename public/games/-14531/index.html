Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Introducing Soccer Basketball, an exhilarating arcade game that seamlessly blends the excitement of soccer with the finesse of basketball. Players can tap and slide the soccer ball, controlling its angle and power as they aim for the hoop. The game incorporates stunning graphics and realistic physics effects, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in each shot you take. Your objective? Score as many points as possible by shooting the soccer ball into the hoop, all while tackling increasingly challenging levels.

As you progress, the game's difficulty ramps up, introducing four random movements for the hoop, making each shot a new challenge. You only have three shots available to complete each level, so precision is essential. Can you conquer your way through the ranks and establish the best record on the local leaderboard? With simple yet engaging gameplay, Soccer Basketball promises endless fun and competition!

Key features include addictive gameplay that keeps you coming back for more, great ball physics, impressive graphics, and immersive sound effects. Best of all, it s completely free to download and play! The zoom camera effect enhances your experience, and with just a single touch to control, players of all ages can enjoy it easily. Prepare for hours of entertainment while enhancing your shooting skills in this unique blend of two beloved sports!

How to play free Soccer Basketball game online

To start playing Soccer Basketball, simply touch the screen to initiate the action. Drag the soccer ball to adjust power and angle before releasing it to shoot. Take care to time your shots well, as you have only three attempts per level. Stay focused and aim for the best scores!
