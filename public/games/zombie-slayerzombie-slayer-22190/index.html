Welcome to Zombie Slayer, an intense survival game that plunges you into a world overrun by the undead. Your mission? To survive while completing a variety of challenging tasks that will test your skills and strategy. Whether you re tasked with safeguarding a hostage from relentless attackers or defending a critical line of defense, every level presents a new challenge. With a total of 100 diverse levels, you will continually find yourself on the edge of your seat, navigating through hordes of zombies and overcoming obstacles that threaten your survival.

In Zombie Slayer, upgrading your arsenal is crucial for success. As you progress through the game, you'll have the opportunity to improve your weapons, ensuring that you're always equipped to deal with increasingly difficult adversaries. Make smart choices and craft a strategy that suits your playstyle, whether you prefer stealth or explosive action. In desperate situations, don t hesitate to unleash grenades or call in airstrikes to clear the undead menace, allowing you a moment of respite.

Survival is not just a goal; it s an adventure filled with adrenaline and unexpected twists. Gear up, stay alert, and become the ultimate Zombie Slayer as you take on the undead challenge that awaits you.

How to play free Zombie Slayer game online

To play Zombie Slayer online, simply select your mission and prepare for action. Use the directional controls to move your character, and aim with your mouse for precision shooting. Don t forget to upgrade your weapons and utilize grenades and airstrikes when needed. Complete each mission to progress through the levels!
