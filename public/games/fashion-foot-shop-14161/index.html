Fashion Foot Shop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fashion Foot Shop, the ultimate makeover game that lets you step into the shoes of a trendsetting foot care expert! In this engaging game, you run your very own foot shop, where you cater to a variety of customers with unique issues concerning their feet. From tackling long and unkempt nails to addressing dirty nails and minor wounds, there s never a dull moment in your stylish beauty salon.

But don't forget, you are more than just a manicurist! As a talented nail artist, you have the opportunity to showcase your creativity by designing stunning nail styles that make your clients feel fabulous. Each customer comes with different tastes and personalities, allowing you to utilize your fashion potential and craft nail art that matches their individual characteristics. Dive into a world where your artistic flair meets foot care, and transform your customers feet into true works of art!

Let your imagination run wild as you experiment with colors, patterns, and designs that best represent your clients unique styles. Fashion Foot Shop is not just about aesthetics; it s about providing a complete pampering experience, ensuring that your clients leave your shop feeling confident and satisfied. So, put on your apron and get ready to make a lasting impression with your nail designs in this exciting and addictive game!

How to play free Fashion Foot Shop game online

To play Fashion Foot Shop, begin by selecting a customer waiting in line. Assess their foot issues and decide on the necessary treatments. Use your mouse to interact with the tools, clean their feet, trim nails, and apply fabulous nail designs that suit their style. Enjoy creating a variety of stunning nail art!
