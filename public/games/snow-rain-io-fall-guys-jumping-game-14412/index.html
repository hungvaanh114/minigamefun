How to play free Snow Rain.io Fall Guys Jumping Game game online

To start playing, use the on-screen arrows to move your snowman left or right, performing double jumps to evade snowballs raining down from above. Aim to land on sturdy platforms, collect coins, and keep track of your score. Avoid falling into the icy waters and stay alive as long as possible for the highest score!
