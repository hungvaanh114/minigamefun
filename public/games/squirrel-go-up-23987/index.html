Instructions:

Jump into the thrilling world of Squirrel Go Up, where you take control of an adorable squirrel on a daring upward adventure! As you embark on this delightful journey, simply use your mouse or press the enter key to start the game. The objective is clear: guide your cute squirrel through a maze of platforms while avoiding a variety of obstacles that threaten to knock you down.

Utilizing your keyboard, maneuver your furry friend as it climbs higher and higher, deftly jumping from platform to platform. Stay alert! Rocks will come tumbling down, and it's essential to dodge them to keep your game alive. As you ascend, you'll encounter scattered hazelnuts that are waiting to be collected. Gather these tasty treats to earn more points and boost your score, adding an extra layer of excitement to the gameplay. With its simple controls and charming graphics, Squirrel Go Up is easy to master but offers unlimited fun, making it the perfect choice for gamers of all ages.

Challenge your reflexes, enjoy the whimsical landscape, and see how high you can go! Whether you're looking for a quick distraction or a way to pass the time, this addicting game guarantees hours of entertainment. So what are you waiting for? Jump in and start your adventure today!

How to play free Squirrel Go Up game online

To play Squirrel Go Up, use the arrow keys or the "W," "A," "S," and "D" keys on your keyboard to move the squirrel. Jump on platforms while avoiding falling rocks. Collect hazelnuts for extra points, and keep climbing as high as you can!
