Poppy Granny Playtime

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Poppy Granny Playtime Horror immerses players in a chilling atmosphere where the primary goal is to survive through investigation and puzzle-solving. Set in diverse locations, the initial setting is a shopping mall that falls victim to a sudden and sinister event. You must unravel the mystery of what transpired and find a way to escape, but the exits are locked tight.

Your only companion in this daunting adventure is the teddy bear named Poppy, who guides you through the eerie environment. To earn his trust and gain access to vital paths, you'll need to complete his task: locating three small bears hidden throughout the mall's various areas. However, a significant challenge lies ahead since one of these bears is secured within a warehouse, accessible only with a special key.

As you navigate through the haunting corridors, you'll encounter various puzzles and challenges. Each bear you find brings you closer to unlocking the mysteries of the shopping mall and ultimately, your escape. The game s tension escalates as you explore, uncover secrets, and figure out the best strategies to stay alive while working against the clock. Are you sharp enough to solve each puzzle and evade any lurking dangers in your pursuit of freedom?

How to play free Poppy Granny Playtime game online

Once you're in the game, navigate through the mall using your keyboard to explore. Interact with objects by clicking and search for clues that will help you unlock doors and solve puzzles. Keep an eye out for the small bears, and remember to communicate with Poppy for hints as you strive to escape!
