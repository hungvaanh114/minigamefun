Welcome to Brain Twister, an engaging and challenging puzzle game that puts your problem-solving skills to the test! The objective is simple yet captivating: you need to determine the precise arrangement of colored balls in a given sequence. As you dive into the game, you'll find an array of colorful balls and empty slots, ready for you to make your strategic moves.

To play, simply drag the balls into the vacant spaces provided. Remember, each ball can only be used once in a particular sequence, so make sure to plan your moves carefully! Once you have filled the line with your colors, you will receive feedback in the form of colored balls. A Grey ball indicates that you have a color that is correct but placed in the wrong position. Conversely, a Black ball signifies that you have chosen the correct color and positioned it accurately. Use these clues wisely to refine your strategy and unlock the correct sequence!
