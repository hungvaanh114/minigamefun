Car Blocks

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Car Blocks, an exhilarating racing experience set in a vibrant blocky universe! Jump behind the wheel of your powerful muscle car and navigate through a bustling city filled with traffic while you race against the clock. Challenge yourself in race mode, where survival is key; speed through the streets while dodging other vehicles and obstacles like trains, police cars, and road blockades. Your goal is to rack up the highest score and cover the maximum distance without crashing!

But that's not all are you ready to unleash your destructive side? Switch to demolition mode, where you'll have just two minutes to wreak havoc by smashing as many cars as possible. Chain your crashes together to create explosive combos and maximize your points! This mode is perfect for players who love high-octane action and want to see their wrecks go up in flames.

If you're looking for a more laid-back experience, the city mode invites you to explore at your leisure. Drive through a sprawling metropolis filled with charming streets, an airport buzzing with activity, and plenty of hidden features waiting to be discovered. Hit the exploding barrels and trigger special events that will change the mood of the city, from transforming the time of day to triggering a nightclub opening or watch buildings burst into chaos!

With each game mode offering unique challenges and a ton of fun, Car Blocks promises endless entertainment and dynamic action for all racing enthusiasts.

How to play free Car Blocks game online

To play Car Blocks, choose your preferred game mode from the main menu. Use the on-screen controls to navigate your vehicle through the vibrant streets, avoiding collisions. In race mode, speed is crucial, while in demolition mode, focus on crashing into other cars. In city mode, simply enjoy driving and exploring!
