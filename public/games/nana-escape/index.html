Nana Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Nana Escape is an engaging point-and-click escape game meticulously crafted by 8BGames. In this delightful adventure, you get to step into a heartwarming scenario where you visit your beloved nana s home for a weekend of fun and bonding. However, this joyful occasion takes an unfortunate turn when you discover that your nana is trapped inside her room. Your mission is to help her escape by utilizing your keen observational skills and puzzle-solving abilities.

Your journey begins as you explore each corner of Nana's charming home, with an emphasis on finding hidden objects that hold the key to your nana s freedom. As you delve deeper into the game, you ll encounter intriguing clues and riddles that need to be solved. This interactive format keeps you engaged and challenges your logic and creativity, making it a fun experience for players of all ages. The vibrant graphics and intuitive controls add to the overall enjoyment, creating an immersive environment that feels like a true escape room experience.

With a series of captivating levels laden with mysteries waiting to be unraveled, every decision counts in making your way towards freeing your nana. Will you be able to find all the hidden objects and solve the puzzles in time? Put your wits to the test and embark on this adventurous quest filled with love, excitement, and a touch of nostalgia. Good luck, and most importantly, have fun!

How to play free Nana Escape game online

To play Nana Escape, simply use your mouse to navigate through the room. Click on various objects to collect hidden items or clues. When you find a useful object, drag it to the area where it can be used to solve puzzles and help free your nana. Remember, think critically and enjoy the adventure!
