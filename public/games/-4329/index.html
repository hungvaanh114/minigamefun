Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the role of a skilled special agent in Helicopter Assassin, where your mission is to eliminate traitors who pose a threat to national security. As you soar through the skies in your advanced helicopter, you must carefully navigate the terrain and engage your targets with precision shooting. Utilize your aerial advantage to scout out enemy hideouts and assess the best approach to take out your foes.

In Helicopter Assassin, each successful mission brings you closer to unlocking new weapons and upgrades, enhancing your combat capabilities. The adrenaline rush of airborne warfare awaits as you take down multiple targets, avoiding counter-attacks and ensuring your own survival. Every mission presents unique challenges, from urban landscapes to hostile territories, ensuring that no two experiences are the same.

Your earnings from each mission can be strategically spent on a wide range of weaponry, enabling you to tailor your arsenal for maximum efficiency. Upgrade your helicopter and weaponry to become the ultimate assassin. The more missions you take on, the more ambitious your objectives become. Prepare to tackle tougher adversaries as you advance through the ranks and establish your reputation as a legendary assassin.

Experience the thrill of tactical helicopter combat in Helicopter Assassin, where skill, strategy, and sharp reflexes are your greatest allies. Will you rise to the challenge and eradicate the enemies of peace, or will you fall victim to the chaos of the battlefield? The choice is yours!

How to play free Helicopter Assassin game online

To play Helicopter Assassin, navigate your helicopter using the on-screen controls. Aim your weapon with precision and shoot down targets as they appear. Stay aware of your surroundings to avoid enemy fire, and complete each mission objective for rewards. Upgrade your arsenal to become more powerful with each gameplay session!
