Climb Birdy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey in Climb Birdy, a fast-paced arcade game designed to challenge your reflexes and agility. Your main goal is to swipe your bird upwards while carefully navigating through a treacherous landscape filled with spikes. Quick swipes will not only elevate your bird faster but also enhance your overall flying experience, giving you the agility needed to dodge obstacles.

As you soar through the skies, be on the lookout for insects scattered throughout the environment. Consuming these insects will boost your energy, vital for the challenges that lie ahead. Along the way, you ll gather stars and collect valuable gems that can unlock an array of amazing new characters. Each bird you release brings with it a unique flying style, unlocking endless fun and strategic gameplay.

The game introduces different sizes of birds, each offering a distinct advantage. Larger birds can effortlessly snatch up bugs and stars, while smaller ones cleverly navigate through narrow gaps to avoid spikes. The mechanics also reward you for taking longer and careful laps; a well-planned flight will improve your score and allow you to pilot your bird with finesse. Additionally, investing in premium birds will enhance your stamina and extend your flying range, providing an edge in your quest.

How to play free Climb Birdy game online

To play Climb Birdy, simply use your finger or mouse to swipe up and guide your bird upwards. Avoid spikes and collect insects for energy, while also gathering stars to unlock new characters. The faster you swipe, the quicker your bird ascends, so stay alert and react swiftly!
