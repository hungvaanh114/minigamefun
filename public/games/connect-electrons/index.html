How to play free Connect Electrons game online

To play Connect Electrons, start by clicking on the available electrons to select them. Your goal is to drag and connect these electrons in a straight line without any interruptions. Be mindful of the available paths, and strategize your moves to ensure that all electrons are linked together efficiently. Enjoy the challenge and aim for the highest score!
