Experience the timeless joy of Petits chevaux (small horses), a delightful board game that can accommodate 1 to 4 players. Rediscover the nostalgic charm of your childhood as you navigate your little horses around the board, now easily accessible on your mobile or tablet. This classic game stands out not just for its simplicity, but for its enduring appeal among board game enthusiasts. Whether you enjoy playing solo against the challenging artificial intelligence or with friends, Petits chevaux guarantees hours of entertainment and friendly competition.

Your goal? To be the first to maneuver all your horses into the central home area. The vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics will have you engrossed in this delightful race, as you strategize your moves and outsmart your opponents. Engage in this beloved childhood classic, where every turn can lead to excitement and unexpected twists!

Join your friends or family members, set your strategies, and see who will emerge victorious by placing all their horses in the coveted central box first! This easily accessible format allows you to play anytime, anywhere, making it the perfect pastime for enthusiasts of all ages.

How to play free Petits chevaux: small horses game online

To start playing the Petits chevaux game online, simply select the number of players and choose your horse color. Roll the virtual dice to determine your moves, and strategically advance your horses toward the center of the board. Utilize special squares and watch out for competitors to reach victory!
