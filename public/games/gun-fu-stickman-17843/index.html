Gun Fu Stickman

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the thrilling world of Gun Fu Stickman, the official online sequel from the popular Gun Fu series. In this adrenaline-pumping game, you take on the role of the iconic Stickman, equipped with a pistol in each hand. Your mission is to navigate through waves of relentless enemies, showcasing your shooting skills and strategic agility as you aim to dominate the battlefield. With its fast-paced action and engaging gameplay, Gun Fu Stickman promises to keep you on the edge of your seat!

As you blast through hordes of foes, you'll encounter various challenges that test your reflexes and precision. Each successful elimination boosts your score, allowing you to unlock new weapons and special power-ups. The game features vibrant graphics and fluid animations, creating an immersive experience that captivates players from start to finish. Join the battle and prove your worth in this fun shooting game that has captivated fans around the globe.

Whether you are a novice or a seasoned player, Gun Fu Stickman offers a perfect blend of challenge and excitement, making it a must-play for anyone who enjoys action-packed shooting games. Aim carefully, move swiftly, and watch as your Stickman becomes a legend on the battlefield!

How to play free Gun Fu Stickman game online

To play Gun Fu Stickman, use the mouse or touchscreen to aim and shoot at your enemies. Move your character using the keyboard or on-screen controls. Keep an eye on your ammo and reload as needed. Survive wave after wave of attackers while trying to achieve the highest score possible!
