City Car Stunt 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

City Car Stunt 3 elevates the thrill of driving simulation to a whole new level with its enhanced physics engine and stunningly realistic cars. This third installment in the City Car Stunt series invites players to tackle six unique routes, each presenting its own challenges and time constraints. As you race through the dynamic courses, you'll unlock new cars, which significantly enhance your performance in subsequent levels, allowing for a more exciting gameplay experience.

The clock is ticking, and strategic racing is vital to securing the fastest vehicle available. Whether it's navigating through intricate routes or mastering tricky maneuvers, your driving skills will be tested like never before. The game offers a visually captivating environment that is sure to immerse any car enthusiast. The redesigned expansive "Free Driving" map is a highlight, where players can indulge in various recreational activities, such as darts, soccer, and bowling using their cars creatively!

In free driving mode, the pressure of the timer is lifted. This allows players to unleash their creativity and perform jaw-dropping stunts using ramps at their leisure, providing an exhilarating sense of freedom to explore the game world without limits. Get ready for an unforgettable driving adventure where skill and speed combine in City Car Stunt 3!

How to play City Car Stunt 3 game online

To play City Car Stunt 3 online, simply choose your preferred game mode from the main menu. Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate your car and the spacebar for stunts. Complete missions by racing through designated routes and unlock new vehicles to enhance your gameplay. Enjoy the freedom of the "Free Driving" map for unlimited fun!
