Number Arrange

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Engaging in puzzle-solving activities such as Number Arrange offers numerous advantages, notably enhancing the brain's production of dopamine. This neurochemical not only fosters a sense of pleasure but also positively influences cognitive functions, including learning, memory retention, concentration, and overall motivation. In Number Arrange, players are challenged to organize numerical boxes by swapping them until they reach the desired order. Each puzzle offers a refreshing challenge that keeps players actively engaged in the dynamic world of puzzles.

Number Arrange presents an array of features designed to enhance the gameplay experience. It includes elements such as a display timer and move count, allowing players to track their progress actively. The game also offers customizable difficulty levels, enabling users to recreate the board or shuffle the boxes at their discretion. Its user-friendly interface makes it accessible for players of all backgrounds, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the challenge of numerical arrangement.

Newcomers to Number Arrange will find the game accommodating, as it allows players to customize their difficulty settings. The game offers a variety of levels to fit different skill sets, including:

  • Easy - (3x3) grid
  • Normal - (4x4) grid
  • Hard - (5x5) grid
  • Extreme - (6x6) grid
  • Insane - (7x7) grid

How to play free Number Arrange game online

To play Number Arrange, simply select your preferred difficulty level once in the game. Use your mouse or finger to click on the boxes you wish to swap, aiming to arrange all the numbers in sequential order. Keep an eye on the timer and move count for a fun challenge!
