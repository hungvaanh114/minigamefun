Noise Clicker

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Noise Clicker is a highly engaging idle clicker game that challenges you to amass as much noise as possible, which serves as your in-game currency. By clicking on the screen, you readily generate income that fuels your journey toward becoming a noise-making tycoon. With each click, you accumulate currency which can be used to unlock a myriad of thrilling upgrades, innovative tools, and items that will help automate your earnings.

As you delve deeper into the game, you'll discover that you have the opportunity to invest in various enhancements. These upgrades allow you to generate passive income, meaning your earnings continue to grow even when you re not actively clicking. This mechanic not only adds layers of strategy to the gameplay but also ensures that whether you prefer an active clicking approach or a more relaxed, idle strategy, Noise Clicker offers a satisfying experience for every type of player. Your objective is clear: if you wish to build the ultimate roaring empire and ascend the leaderboard, you will need to harness your clicking prowess and make strategic decisions on your upgrades.

So, are you ready to immerse yourself in the world of Noise Clicker? It s time to test your skills and see how far you can go!

How to play free Noise Clicker game online

To start playing Noise Clicker, simply click on the screen to generate noise, which acts as your currency. Use your earnings to purchase upgrades and tools to automate your income. Keep clicking to maximize profits and compete with other players on the leaderboard!
