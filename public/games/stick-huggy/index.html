Stick Huggy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you a fan of exhilarating runner stickman parkour games? If so, then "Stick Huggy" is the game for you! In this action-packed adventure, you'll assist our nimble stickman character, Huggy, as he takes on a challenging course filled with a variety of obstacles. Your goal is to help him navigate through each level by jumping over barriers and sliding under obstructions to keep the momentum going. The faster you play, the higher your score will soar!

In "Stick Huggy," speed and precision are your best allies. As you guide Huggy through each stage, you'll encounter a range of challenges that will test your reflexes and gaming skills. The colorful graphics and fluid animations create an immersive experience that keeps players engaged for hours on end. Compete against yourself or see how you stack up against friends as you strive to achieve the highest score possible!

Utilizing the keyboard controls is simple: just press the W key to jump and the S key to slide under obstacles. With each successful jump and slide, you'll move closer to mastering the course. So don t hesitate dive into the action and show off your parkour prowess!
