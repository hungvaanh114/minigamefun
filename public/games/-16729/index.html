Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for a thrilling adventure in Zombie Massacre, where the undead rise up to wreak havoc in the wild west! As a brave gunslinger, it s up to you to defend innocent townsfolk from hordes of relentless zombies that threaten to turn the frontier into a graveyard. Arm yourself with sharpshooters and revolvers, and take a stand against these ghastly creatures. It's not just about survival; it s about teamwork. Offer assistance to the townspeople, and their gratitude will come in the form of support and resources!

To outsmart the various types of zombies, you ll need to master your arsenal. Upgrade your firearms with powerful enhancements to increase damage and accuracy, ensuring you re always ready for an onslaught. You can also recruit companions and escorts who will aid you in battle, providing additional firepower and strategy. Don t forget to stock up on essential supplies like medikits, ammunition, and adrenaline shots to keep yourself in fighting shape. The relentless waves of zombies are endless, each with unique abilities, so adaptability is key to your survival.

In Zombie Massacre, the action never stops. The question remains: how long can you withstand the zombie onslaught? As you navigate through the hostile landscape, employ tactics and sharpen your skills to become the ultimate protector of the wild west!

How to play free Zombie Massacre game online

To play Zombie Massacre, use your mouse to aim and shoot at incoming zombies. Upgrade your weapons and purchase items from the in-game store using resources collected during gameplay. Work together with hired companions to fend off the undead and complete missions for bonuses!
