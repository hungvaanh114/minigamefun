Horror Hospital

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chilling realm of Horror Hospital, an adrenaline-fueled FPS survival game that will push both your mental fortitude and tactical prowess to their limits. Set against the backdrop of a nightmarishly desolate hospital, players must navigate through shadowy corridors and creepy rooms inhabited by menacing clowns. These ghoulish adversaries come at you in relentless waves, prompting you to think quickly and act decisively.

Your primary objective is survival. Equip yourself with an impressive array of weapons that can be purchased throughout the game, enhancing your firepower and improving your chances against the malevolent clowns that lurk in every corner. Each defeated enemy earns you points, pushing you closer to higher scores and ultimate bragging rights on the leaderboards. The thrill of the hunt is ever-present as you strive to fend off these terrifying foes and prove yourself as the top player.

With stunning graphics and immersive sound design, Horror Hospital offers an intense gaming experience that will keep you at the edge of your seat. As the tension rises with every wave, will you be able to hold your ground and become the ultimate champion in this district of dread? Step into the heart of Horror Hospital and find out if you have what it takes to survive the terrifying encounters that await you!

How to play free Horror Hospital game online

To play the Horror Hospital game, simply start your session and navigate your character using the controls. Purchase weapons with accumulated points, keep an eye on your surroundings to avoid ambushes, and shoot the approaching clowns to survive each wave. Strategize your movements for maximum efficiency!
