How to play free Tripeaks Solitaire: Kings and Queens game online

To play Tripeaks Solitaire: Kings and Queens, begin by selecting a card from the tableau that is one higher or one lower than the card on the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to clear all cards from the peaks. Utilize wildcards strategically and keep an eye out for magic cards to help you along the way!
