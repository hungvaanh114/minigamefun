Draw Climbing

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Draw Climbing is an engaging puzzle game that challenges your creativity and problem-solving skills. In this game, your primary objective is to sketch a cube that ascends a vertical path filled with obstacles and traps. As you navigate through the levels, careful planning and precise drawing are essential to keep your cube moving upwards without encountering spikes and falling off the ledges.

Your journey in Draw Climbing isn't just about reaching the top; it's also about collecting as many coins as you can along the way. These coins serve as rewards for your thoughtful drawings and adventurous climbs. The game features various levels, each designed to test your skills and push your limits. You'll need to think strategically about how to draw the cube's path, ensuring it can overcome challenges while maximizing your coin collection.

As you progress through the game, the levels will become increasingly complex, introducing new dangers and requiring even more creative solutions. The challenge lies not only in drawing but also in timing and spatial awareness to ensure the cube doesn't collide with any spikes or stumble during its ascent. Immerse yourself in this addictive puzzle experience and see how far you can climb!

How to play free Draw Climbing game online

To begin playing Draw Climbing, simply use your mouse or touch screen to draw a path for the cube. Ensure the drawing is solid and connects with the necessary surfaces to keep the cube ascending. Avoid spikes and gaps while collecting coins. The more coins you gather, the higher your score!
