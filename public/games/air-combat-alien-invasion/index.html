Air Combat. Alien Invasion

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In a not-so-distant future, the Earth faces an unprecedented crisis as an alien invasion overwhelms humanity. With the defenses shattered and some nations turning against their own, a faction of traitorous individuals has allied with the extraterrestrial foes, aiming to eliminate the last remnants of human resistance. Amidst this chaos, a courageous organization rises, determined to fight back and reclaim our planet.

Your mission begins with a daring assault on an enemy airbase controlled by the collaborators. Armed with advanced aircraft, your team embarks on perilous aerial maneuvers, traversing hostile skies filled with enemy ships. With every flight, you hunt down alien forces, focusing on dismantling their powerful cruisers to thwart the emergence of numerous alien carriers threatening to overwhelm the last bastion of human hope.

As you engage in heart-pounding combat, strategy and precision become your best allies. Players must master their flying skills, manage resources wisely, and employ tactical strikes to ensure victory. The stakes are high, and the fate of humanity depends on your ability to confront this alien menace boldly.

How to play free Air Combat: Alien Invasion game online

To begin playing, simply navigate to the game's main page. Use the arrow keys or joystick to control your aircraft, avoiding enemy fire while aiming your weapons at incoming ships. Complete missions, earn upgrades, and collaborate with teammates to efficiently bring down your alien adversaries.
