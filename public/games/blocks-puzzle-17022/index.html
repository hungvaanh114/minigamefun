Experience the excitement of Blocks Puzzle, also known as 1010, a captivating HTML5 puzzle game that guarantees hours of fun! In this engaging game, your main objective is to strategically drag and drop various block shapes onto a grid board. As you place blocks, aim to create complete vertical or horizontal lines. The challenge lies in planning your moves wisely. You'll need to think ahead and anticipate the best spots for your blocks to avoid running out of space. How to play free Blocks Puzzle game online

To play Blocks Puzzle, simply drag the blocks that are presented to you onto the grid. Position them thoughtfully to create complete rows or columns.
