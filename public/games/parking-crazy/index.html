Parking Crazy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This is a casual game that combines light - hearted fun with parking challenges, allowing players to enjoy the unique pleasure of parking during their fragmented time. The game graphics adopt a fresh and cute 3D cartoon style, with bright and lively colors. The gameplay is simple and easy to pick up, yet full of depth. Players only need to click and slide the screen to control the vehicle to move forward, backward, and turn. The level design is ingenious, with the difficulty increasing gradually. In the novice levels, the venue is open and the parking spaces are spacious, helping players quickly master the operations.
