Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Boxes Fright Night, where delightful color Boxes embark on a thrilling quest to retrieve all the lost pumpkins in a Halloween Puzzle Adventure! How to play free Boxes Fright Night game online

To play Boxes Fright Night, use your mouse or touch controls to navigate the Boxes. Click or tap to make your character move, jump, and rotate.
