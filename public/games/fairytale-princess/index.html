Fairytale Princess

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the enchanting world of "Fairytale Princess," where you will meet the iconic character of Sleeping Beauty, a princess with a beautiful story. Cursed from the moment she was born, she will fall into a deep slumber for a hundred years when she turns sixteen. However, with the help of the good fairies, she is brought back to life, looking more stylish and elegant than ever before.

This game offers a delightful opportunity for you to unleash your creativity and fashion sense. As you take on the role of a stylist for Sleeping Beauty, you'll have the chance to pick from an array of stunning outfits, accessories, and hairstyles. From royal gowns adorned with sparkling gems to playful and trendy outfits, your choices will transform the princess into a fashion icon of her own fairytale world.

As you navigate through this fun and interactive game, you'll discover that there s more than just dressing up the princess. You'll have various accessories to choose from, such as tiaras, shoes, and enchanting jewelry. Experiment with different combinations until you find the perfect look that reflects the magical essence of Sleeping Beauty.

How to play free Fairytale Princess game online

To play the "Fairytale Princess" game, simply click on the start button to enter the dressing room. Use your mouse to drag and drop clothing items onto Sleeping Beauty. Browse through the selections of dresses, shoes, and accessories, then click to apply your chosen looks. Don t forget to save your favorite styles!
