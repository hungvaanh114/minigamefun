Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling adventure of Duo House Escape! Join our heroes, Steve and Herobrine, as they navigate through a mysterious and challenging house filled with hidden secrets. Your mission is clear: help them find a way out by solving puzzles and collecting essential items scattered throughout the house.

To successfully escape, you must first locate a diamond and a key that are crucial for unlocking the magic chest. The chest is your final destination once you open it, a portal will magically appear, allowing for a swift escape from the confines of the house. As you embark on this adventure, be prepared to explore multiple rooms, decipher clues, and interact with various objects to aid your friends on their quest.

Work together with Steve and Herobrine to complete their tasks, as teamwork is vital in overcoming the challenges that await. The clock is ticking, so make sure to be quick and efficient in your search. Can you help them unravel the mysteries of the house and jump into the portal for a successful escape?

How to play free Duo House Escape game online

Once you're in the game, use your mouse or keyboard controls to navigate through the house. Click on objects to interact with them and collect necessary items. Keep an eye on your surroundings for hidden clues that will guide you to the diamond and key. Once you gather the required items, head to the chest to open it and activate the portal.
