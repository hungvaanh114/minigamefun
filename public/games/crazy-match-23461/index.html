Crazy Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spooktacular time with Crazy Match, the thrilling memory game designed to celebrate the essence of Halloween! Dive into a world of ghoulish fun as you navigate through 8 challenging levels featuring a total of 52 unique cards. Each card possesses its own delightful Halloween-themed design that will ignite your festive spirit.

As you embark on this exciting adventure, you ll start with $100 in virtual currency. But beware! Every time you mistakenly flip over the wrong card, a penalty of $10 will be deducted from your total. However, with keen observation and a little luck, discovering the correct cards will earn you a rewarding $100 for each success. Your goal is to unlock all the right cards and cleverly complete the game without running out of funds.

Crazy Match is not just about winning; it's about having fun and honing your memory skills. Explore the enchanting Halloween atmosphere, challenge yourself through various levels, and enjoy the thrill of unlocking new cards. Can you master the game while cherishing the witching season? The challenge awaits!

How to play free Crazy Match game online

To play Crazy Match, simply choose any two cards to flip over and reveal the images. If they match, you'll earn $100! If they don t, you ll lose $10. Keep flipping until you find all matching pairs while managing your money wisely. Happy matching!
