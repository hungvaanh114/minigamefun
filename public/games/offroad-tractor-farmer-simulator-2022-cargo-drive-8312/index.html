Offroad Tractor Farmer Simulator 2022: Cargo Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating experience with Offroad Tractor Farmer Simulator 2022: Cargo Drive. This immersive farming game places you in the shoes of a dedicated farmer tasked with transporting cargo to various local farms using a powerful tractor. As a modern farmer, you ll face the exhilarating challenge of navigating rugged off-road tracks that test your driving skills to the limit.

In this simulator, you'll discover that the heavy-duty tractor, with its zigzag wheels, makes traversing muddy paths more manageable. However, be cautious on steep hills, as slow and steady driving is essential to prevent accidents that could jeopardize your transport missions. Your goal is to master the art of transporting cargo while dealing with a variety of challenging terrains.

This farming simulation is specially designed for tractor enthusiasts eager to take on the role of a cargo driver. In addition to transporting goods, you will also engage in essential farming tasks like harvesting, ploughing, and sowing crops across picturesque countryside settings. Become an expert heavy-duty tractor driver and navigate through hills and mountains as you build your agricultural career.

The game features realistic farming machinery, including modern tractors and multiple attachments, to enhance your gaming experience. With offline play available, you can manage parking at store garages and tackle impossible transport missions set in expansive, open-world environments. Prepare to conquer the steep trails and narrow paths while balancing your load on the powerful tractor.

How to play free Offroad Tractor Farmer Simulator 2022: Cargo Drive game online

To play the game, simply drive your tractor using the on-screen controls. Attach the trailer to your tractor before loading it with cargo. Follow the marked paths and navigate through off-road challenges, ensuring to balance your speed and avoid obstacles to successfully deliver your cargo to the farms.
