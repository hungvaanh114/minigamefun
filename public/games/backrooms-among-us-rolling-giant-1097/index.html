Backrooms Among Us Rolling Giant

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the spine-chilling world of Backrooms Among Us Rolling Giant, players find themselves trapped in a nightmarish maze filled with haunting figures and relentless foes. Your primary objective is to evade the relentless Impostor from the popular franchise, Among Us, while managing to navigate through the treacherous environment. But that s not all! Granny, who seems to wander aimlessly, adds an unexpected twist haunting your every move, making your escape even more challenging. It s a nail-biting race against time!

To succeed in your escape, players must collect various phones scattered throughout the Backrooms. This frantic scavenging is critical because once you've gathered enough phones, you can make a crucial call to a friend for assistance. However, be forewarned: the clock is ticking! You must act quickly before your time runs out, as the dangers lurking in the shadows will stop at nothing to prevent your escape. Every corner could bring a new threat or a hidden phone, so be vigilant!

The atmosphere is thick with tension as you weave through various rooms, each filled with unique challenges and eeriness. The design of the Backrooms adds a layer of true horror, keeping players on edge and immersed in a thrilling, heart-pounding adventure.

How to play free Backrooms Among Us Rolling Giant game online

To play Backrooms Among Us Rolling Giant, simply enter the game and start exploring. Use the arrow keys to navigate through the maze, while gathering phones. Keep an eye on the timer and avoid the Impostor and Granny at all costs. When you collect enough phones, find a safe spot to make that crucial call!
