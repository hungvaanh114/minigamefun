Idle Restaurant Tale

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This is a restaurant management simulation game, start as a street food bar, hire new staff and upgrade machine to earn more money, then open a bigger restaurant! Feature: - Simple and easy to start - Strategically upgrade your restaurants to maximize revenue - Various restaurants and food waiting for you to unlock.There are 11 cities and more than 100 different levels. - There are many game adventure events.

Instructions

Game control is very simple just tap the machines and foods to complete the game levels
