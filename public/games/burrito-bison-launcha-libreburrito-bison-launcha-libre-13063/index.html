Burrito Bison is back with a bang, and this time, he s not going it alone! In the thrilling new installment, "Burrito Bison Launcha Libre," players can join forces with their beloved hero and his quirky friends, Pineapple Spank and El Pollo. Together, they take on a barrage of gummy invaders in a vibrant, action-packed world. With over 15 million plays on Kongregate.com, this game has captured the hearts of fans around the globe, making it more popular than ever!

This exciting adventure kicks off as you launch Burrito Bison into the air, using his incredible strength to smash through gummy foes. Players will experience a whirlwind of bouncing, flying, and power-ups as they navigate through colorful landscapes filled with candy-coated obstacles. Your mission? To defeat the gummy enemies that threaten to ruin the day and to gather delicious cake along the way. Think you can handle the mayhem?

As you progress through the game, you ll unlock new abilities and upgrade your power to help Burrito Bison and his friends soar higher and smash harder! The game features stunning visuals and hilarious antics, making every launch a fresh and thrilling challenge. Join in on the fun and see if you have what it takes to conquer the gummy menace!

How to play free Burrito Bison Launcha Libre game online

To play 'Burrito Bison Launcha Libre', simply use your mouse to aim and launch Burrito Bison. As he soars through the air, click to activate special abilities and boosters. Bounce off gummies and collect cakes while avoiding obstacles to score points and unlock new upgrades. Enjoy the adventure!
