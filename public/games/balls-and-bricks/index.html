How to play free Balls and Bricks game online

To play Balls and Bricks, simply aim your projectile at the bricks to destroy them. Tap or click to launch balls, adjusting your angle for precision. Each brick has a number indicating how many hits it takes to break. Score points by clearing levels as you progress through the game!
