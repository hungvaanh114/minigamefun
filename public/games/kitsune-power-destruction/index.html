Kitsune Power Destruction

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare to embark on an exhilarating adventure in Kitsune Power Destruction, where you take on the role of a furious Kitsune that has just descended into a mystical forest. With your sights set on wreaking havoc, your only mission is to obliterate the ancient pillars that support the revered temples scattered throughout the landscape. Each pillar holds deep-seated power, and by breaking them down, you ll unleash a wave of chaos in this enchanted domain.

As you navigate through the forest, be on high alert! The temple protectors are determined to thwart your plans at all costs. They are clever and concealed among the pillars, armed with powerful weapons to defend their sacred grounds. Don't underestimate their prowess; they are not just ordinary guardians but formidable opponents, prepared to clash with you at any moment. Strategy and quick reflexes are crucial as you strive to demolish the pillars while evading and countering the protectors attacks.

Gather your courage and unleash the full extent of your Kitsune powers as you break down the defenses that stand in your way. Will you be able to outsmart the protectors and fulfill your destructive destiny, or will you fall prey to their traps? Step into the wild and let the chaos reign in Kitsune Power Destruction!

How to play free Kitsune power destruction game online

To play Kitsune Power Destruction, use your mouse or touch controls to move the Kitsune character across the screen. Click or tap on the pillars to aim and unleash your destructive powers. Keep an eye out for hidden protectors and dodge their attacks while you take down each pillar. Stay alert and have fun!


Instructions

Move from left to right to destroy the pillars
