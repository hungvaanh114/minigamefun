How to play free Box Colour Fill Game game online

To play the Box Colour Fill Game, simply select a color and tap on the boxes you want to fill. Your objective is to fill the entire board with the chosen color by connecting adjacent boxes of the same color. Plan your moves wisely and strategize to clear each level successfully!
