Save the Ghost

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a thrilling adventure in Save The Ghost, where your agility and cunning are put to the ultimate test! You are tasked with the exhilarating job of defending your ghostly friends from relentless ghost hunters. With stealth as your greatest ally, you will need to navigate through various challenging levels filled with traps and surveillance cameras that can give your position away in an instant. The objective is clear: collect the lost ghosts scattered throughout each level while skillfully outmaneuvering the hunters on your tail.

As you dart through the shadows, you ll encounter various obstacles, including deceptive traps designed to ensnare any ghost that crosses their path. Be on high alert, as the ghost hunters are equipped with tools to capture and contain you. Your mission is not just about survival it's also about strategy. Use your sneaky skills to push the ghost hunters into traps instead, creating a spine-chilling spectacle that will drive them away. Each level brings new challenges and tighter security, compelling you to think outside the box and act swiftly to secure victory for your ghostly companions.

In this captivating game, every move counts. The more snags you succeed in evading while inventorying your ghostly pals, the more exhilarated you will feel. Get ready to immerse yourself in the hauntingly fun world of Save The Ghost, where every second is crucial, and every retreat counts toward victory!

How to play free Save The Ghost game online

To play Save The Ghost, use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your ghost character. Your goal is to navigate through levels, collect other ghosts, and avoid hunter traps and cameras. Strategically push ghost hunters into traps to eliminate threats and advance to the next level!
