Pet Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Pet Salon, a vibrant and enchanting world where the delightful ponies Flora, Honey, and their friends are eagerly waiting for your creative touch! Step into this magical realm where you can showcase your grooming talents and transform these adorable ponies into breathtaking beauties for the big event. The event is not just a celebration; it s a moment for Flora, Honey, and their pals to shine, and they need your help to look their utmost best!

From hairstyling to fashionable outfits, the Pet Salon offers you an array of grooming tools and accessories to play with. Will you give Flora a fabulous new hairstyle or perhaps dress Honey in a stunning outfit? The choice is yours! Gather your imagination and creativity as you mix and match hairstyles, colors, and accessories to create unique looks that reflect the personality of each pony. With a delightful array of customization options at your fingertips, you are bound to unleash your inner stylist.

Ready for some pony fun? Join Flora, Honey, and their wonderful friends in this interactive experience. Each step will bring you closer to preparing them for the grand event, as well as providing hours of wholesome entertainment. Whether it s styling their mane or accessorizing them for the occasion, every moment in Pet Salon is filled with joy and creativity. Get your scissors, combs, and brushes ready adventure awaits in the Pet Salon!

How to play free Pet Salon game online

To play the Pet Salon game, simply click on the icons to choose different hairstyles, colors, and outfits for the ponies. Use your mouse to drag and drop accessories and make adjustments to each pony's look. Enjoy the creative process and have fun styling the ponies for their big event!
