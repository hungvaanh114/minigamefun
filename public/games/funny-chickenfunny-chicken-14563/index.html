Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the entertaining game Funny Chicken, you will embark on a delightful adventure on a lively farm. The scene unfolds with a charming chicken wandering across the yard, while a farmer busily tosses grains onto the ground. Your objective is simple yet engaging: keep a keen eye on the screen as the chicken moves at a steady pace, searching for food.

The gameplay revolves around timing and precision. As the chicken trots back and forth, it will occasionally stop over a grain that the farmer has scattered. Your task is to ensure that you click on the screen with your mouse the moment the chicken hovers over a grain. This quick action prompts the chicken to pause and peck at the delicious morsel, earning you points with each successful catch. The more grains you help the chicken find, the higher your score will soar!

Get ready to immerse yourself in this fun-filled farming escapade and test your reflexes in a game that combines simplicity with addictive gameplay. With its charming graphics and upbeat atmosphere, Funny Chicken guarantees hours of entertainment for players of all ages. So gather your wits, sharpen your reactions, and help the hungry chicken fill its beak with sweet grains!

How to play free Funny Chicken game online

To play Funny Chicken, simply load the game on your device. Watch the farmer toss grains onto the ground and observe the chicken's movement. When the chicken stops over a grain, click with your mouse to make it peck. Each successful click scores you points. Enjoy the farm adventure!
