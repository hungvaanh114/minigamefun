Bear Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bear Escape is an engaging point-and-click escape game crafted by 8BGames and Games2Mad. In this captivating adventure, players are transported to a mysterious location, where they encounter an adorable bear that is unfortunately trapped. Your mission is to aid the bear in its quest for freedom by uncovering hidden objects and solving intriguing puzzles.

As you navigate through the game, you ll come across various items that are cleverly concealed within the environment. Each hidden object you discover will provide vital clues that help you piece together the mystery of the bear's confinement. The game challenges your observational skills and problem-solving abilities, making every moment thrilling as you work to free the bear. Are you ready to embark on this adventurous journey? Good luck, and most importantly, have fun!

How to play free Bear Escape game online

To play Bear Escape, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the environment. Click on the items that pique your interest to collect them or unveil clues. Explore every corner of the screen to find hidden objects, solve puzzles, and ultimately release the bear from captivity. Enjoy the challenge and save the day!
