HEX-a-MONG

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to HEX-A-MONG, an exhilarating space adventure where camaraderie meets challenge! Join the daring crew aboard a spaceship as they host an exciting tournament designed to test your agility and endurance. Are you ready to take on the challenge and prove your skills against gravity and time?

In this high-octane game, you will race across a unique three-storey platform composed of hexagonal panels. The catch? As you dash, each hexagon you step onto will vanish within seconds! Maintain your momentum, or risk plummeting into the abyss when you stop or slow down. But don't worry; you have a safety net: you are allowed to fall twice before the game ends. However, make it past that, and the stakes soar falling a third time means a quick exit from the tournament.

Whether you decide to tackle the challenge solo or invite a friend to join you in the exhilarating 2-player mode, HEX-A-MONG promises endless fun and thrilling competition. The key to victory is constant movement and strategic planning, as you navigate this dynamic arena where every second counts.

How to play free HEX-A-MONG game online

To play HEX-A-MONG, use your keyboard's arrow keys or a game controller to race across the hexagonal panels. Keep moving at all costs to prevent falling! Time your steps wisely and prepare for the panels to disappear as you dodge and weave across the platform. Compete for the highest score, and enjoy the excitement!


Instructions

Controls Player 1 Move WASD keys Jump Q Player 2 Move ARROW keys Jump Space
