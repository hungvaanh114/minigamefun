Baby Taylor Black Friday Storekeeper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This is a shopping game for girls. Its Black Friday. Taylor is planning to go to the mall to experience being a salesgirl for a day. Its a nice day. After breakfast, Mom will take Taylor to the mall and wish her good luck. The first thing you need to do as a salesperson is to organize the goods. You need to help Taylor put the clothes in the warehouse on the shelves according to their categories.

Instructions
