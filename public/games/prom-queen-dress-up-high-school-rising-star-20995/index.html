Prom Queen Dress Up - High School Rising Star

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to immerse yourself in the glamorous world of high school fashion with "Prom Queen Dress Up - High School Rising Star." As graduation approaches, six stylish young ladies are gearing up for an unforgettable prom night. This is not just a regular party; it's the chance to make lasting memories and showcase their unique styles. But the question on everyone s mind is, What to wear?

In this game, you will take on the role of a fashion designer, wielding your skills to curate the perfect outfits for each of the ambitious students. With a plethora of dresses, accessories, hairstyles, and makeup options at your disposal, it s your mission to help these girls shine on the big night. Each choice you make could lead one of them to be crowned the prom queen! Will you craft a timeless look, or will you push the boundaries with daring fashion statements?

Unleash your creativity as you mix and match different elements to create breathtaking ensembles that reflect the personalities of each young woman. With your impeccable sense of style, the pressure is on to ensure they not only feel their best but also turn heads as they make their grand entrance. The fate of the prom queen rests in your hands, so make every decision count!

How to play free Prom Queen Dress Up - High School Rising Star game online

To play the game, simply start by selecting one of the six fashionable girls. Browse through the various clothing and accessory options displayed on the screen. Choose the perfect dress, style the hair, and apply makeup to complete each look. Experiment and finalize your designs to see which girl becomes the ultimate prom queen!
