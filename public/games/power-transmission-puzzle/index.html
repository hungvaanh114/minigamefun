Power Transmission Puzzle

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Power Transmission Puzzle is an engaging puzzle game that invites players to dive into the world of electrical connections. Your objective is simple yet stimulating: link the light bulb to its power source in order to illuminate it. As you progress through the game, each level presents unique challenges that require strategic thinking and creativity.

To succeed in Power Transmission Puzzle, players can interact with various components by tapping on wires or light bulbs. This action allows you to rotate the pieces and position them correctly, ensuring a seamless flow of electricity. The game is designed to test your problem-solving skills as you navigate different configurations and obstacles that grow more complex with each advancement.

With a total of 100 levels to conquer, Power Transmission Puzzle guarantees hours of entertainment. As you work your way through each challenge, you can explore delightful shops that offer an array of adorable themes and objects to enhance your gameplay experience. Unlocking these items adds a personal touch to your journey, allowing you to customize the visual aesthetics of your game.

How to play free Power Transmission Puzzle game online

To play Power Transmission Puzzle, start by tapping on the light bulb and power wires to examine their orientation. Rotate pieces strategically to create a continuous connection from the power source to the light bulb. Complete each level by successfully illuminating the bulb as quickly as possible!


Instructions

Tap on a wire or a light bulb to rotate it to a proper orientation
