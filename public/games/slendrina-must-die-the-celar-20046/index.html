Slendrina Must Die: The Celar

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Slendrina Must Die: The Celar, the terrifying tale continues as you find yourself trapped in a dark and sinister cellar after uncovering the chilling truth behind Slendrina. Having collected all eight pieces of the mysterious painting, your fate takes a grim turn when Slenderman knocks you unconscious, leading you straight into a place that resembles a bunker more than a cellar. It's a nightmare from which you must escape.

Your task is straightforward yet perilous: locate eight hidden books scattered throughout the treacherous environment to secure your escape. Each book you find brings you one step closer to freedom, but be warned the cellar is not empty. You are not alone. Deadly creatures and relentless threats lurk in the shadows, ready to pounce at any moment.

To aid you in your desperate mission, you have access to four powerful weapons: a knife for close combat, a gun for precise shots, a shotgun for dealing with multiple foes, and the rapid-fire MP5 to fend off waves of enemies. Choose your arsenal wisely and use it strategically to survive the horrors that await you.

The game features stunning 3D graphics that enhance the immersive atmosphere of dread and suspense, pulling you deeper into the chilling world of Slendrina. Will you be able to conquer your fears, unravel the secrets of this nightmarish place, and escape the grips of Slendrina? The challenge awaits!

How to play free Slendrina Must Die: The Celar game online

To play the game, navigate through the eerie cellar using the arrow keys to move and look around. Use your mouse to aim and click to fire your weapons. Search meticulously for the eight hidden books while staying alert for threats, and collect weapons to defend yourself. Your survival depends on your skill and strategy!


Instructions

W A S D to walk around Mouse to Look around Left Mouse Button to Fire Right Mouse Button to Aim Mouse Wheel To Change Weapons G for Grenades R to Reload F to Pickup Items Left Shift to Run Left CTRL to Crouch X to Prone V to Melee Space to Jum
