Stack City Online

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Stack City Online, an exciting merge game that invites you to become a city architect! In this vibrant virtual world, you have the opportunity to construct your unique city using various elements such as stacked scaffolding, shops, and buildings. Every strategic decision you make contributes to building a bustling metropolis where every structure plays a role in generating income for you.

Your journey begins by dragging scaffolding onto the grid to create tall buildings. Each building enhances your income and provides you with the resources needed to expand your urban landscape further. As you progress, you'll find different shades and colors of blocks that determine how you can stack and merge them. Make sure to match colors when building to create towering structures and maximize your potential earnings!

In Stack City Online, freedom and creativity come together, allowing you to design your dream city. Explore new territories and acquire additional scaffolding as you grow your city into a sprawling success. The more you construct, the more money these buildings generate, paving the way to unlimited possibilities and expansion. Can you rise to the challenge and create your magnificent Stack City?

How to play free stack city online game online

To begin playing Stack City Online, start by selecting scaffolding and dragging it onto the grid. Match color-coded blocks to stack and merge them into taller buildings. Continue stacking and expanding your city, generating income as you go. Use your earnings to explore new lands and enhance your construction options!


Instructions

Select and move scaffolding - click and drag the left mouse button or finger
