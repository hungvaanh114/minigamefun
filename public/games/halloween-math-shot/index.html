Halloween Math Shot

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a spooky math adventure in Halloween Math Shot! This engaging game invites you to join the excitement of Halloween night, where you will face off against creepy bats flying in the moonlit sky. Each bat is adorned with numbers on its wings, alongside a crucial comparison sign that you must match using your trusty pumpkin.

Your objective is straightforward yet thrilling: shoot the bats using the pumpkin that contains the correct comparison sign. Whether it's greater than, less than, or equal to, be ready to make quick decisions under pressure. Each level presents a new challenge where you must shoot down a set number of bats before the timer runs out. The fun lies not only in the action but also in sharpening your math skills as you navigate through increasingly complex levels.

With engaging graphics and a delightful Halloween theme, this game offers both entertainment and educational value. As you advance, you'll encounter various fun obstacles and power-ups that can aid you in your quest to maintain your score. Plus, the festive music adds a unique charm to the experience, making every level a treat. Whether you re a math whiz or just looking to enjoy a ghoulishly good time, Halloween Math Shot promises to captivate your attention and enhance your mathematical abilities.

How to play free Halloween Math Shot game online

To play the game, aim your pumpkin at the bats and observe the numbers and comparison signs displayed on their wings. Select the appropriate comparison sign that corresponds to the target numbers provided at each level. Shoot the bats before the timer expires to progress and demonstrate your math skills!


Instructions

Use Z or X buttons on keyboard or touch arrow buttons on the screen to move Left or Right
