Angry Crush 2.1

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating journey in Angry Crush 2.1, a fresh installment of the beloved puzzle adventure series! In this thrilling game, the iconic birds find themselves trapped within vibrant balloons, and you can be sure that the mischievous pigs are the masterminds behind this chaos. Your mission is to tap on matching balloons to burst them into pieces, ultimately liberating the feathered friends! With over 1000 expertly crafted levels, your balloon-busting skills will be put to the test as you strategize your way to outsmart those pesky pigs.

The game is designed to challenge your problem-solving abilities, allowing you to discover ingenious ways to tackle puzzles, achieve high scores, and earn the coveted three-star ratings. To keep the excitement rolling, don't forget to grab your rewards daily through the Daily Quest feature. If you've already completed these tasks, engage in the weekly events or hunt for this month's unique puzzle pieces. Feeling competitive? Climb the ranks on the global leaderboard and see how you stack up against other players worldwide. With an ever-expanding selection of levels, the adventure never ends!

How to play free Angry Crush 2.1 game online

To play Angry Crush 2.1, simply tap on groups of matching balloons to burst them and free the birds. Each level presents a unique puzzle challenge, so think strategically to maximize your moves. Keep an eye out for boosts and daily rewards to help you along the way!
