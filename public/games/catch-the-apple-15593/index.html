Catch the Apple

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a delightful adventure in the captivating physics-based puzzle game, Catch The Apple. Your mission is to help a charming little hedgehog navigate through an array of challenging levels filled with obstacles and puzzles. Each stage presents you with the exciting opportunity to collect delicious apples while tackling the unique challenges it lays before you.

As you progress through the game, gathering apples won't be the only objective. Amassing stars is equally essential, as they play a crucial role in unlocking new levels and boosting your overall score. The game is designed to put your perception and problem-solving skills to the test, providing an engaging experience that keeps you on your toes.

Throughout your journey, you'll have access to a variety of helpful items and equipment. From balloons and fans to tubes and destructible crates, each item can be used strategically to achieve your goals. Additionally, friendly animals scattered throughout the levels can lend a helping hand, making the gameplay even more dynamic and enjoyable. Embrace your creativity as you manipulate these objects to collect every last apple and star!

How to play free Catch The Apple game online

To play Catch The Apple, simply use your mouse or finger (for touchscreen devices) to interact with the elements on the screen. Drag items into position, tap to activate tools like fans or balloons, and guide the hedgehog through obstacles to collect apples and stars. Aim for a high score!
