Fit #039;em All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fit'em All, the ultimate photo restoration adventure! In this engaging game, you'll step into the role of a skilled photo technician at a bustling restoration photo shop. Your mission? To help clients revive their treasured memories that have been damaged by unfortunate accidents. From torn edges to misplaced fragments, it s up to you to restore these beautiful photos to their former glory.

As you embark on this journey, you'll encounter a variety of challenging images and pieces that need to be reassembled. Carefully examine each fragment, and use your keen eye to move them into the correct position. Successfully completing each photo not only brings joy to your clients but also rewards you with currency to enhance and decorate your store. With each successful restoration, witness your establishment transform into a vibrant gallery of creativity and color!

Fit'em All isn't just about fixing photos; it's also about managing and upgrading your storefront. Use the earnings you collect from satisfied customers to purchase new decorations, equipment, and other enhancements. The more beautiful your shop becomes, the more clients will be drawn to your talent. Get ready to immerse yourself in a world where your puzzle-solving skills translate into beautiful memories restored, and artistic creations come to life!

How to play free Fit'em All game online

To play Fit'em All, simply click and drag the photo fragments into their correct positions on the main screen. Pay close attention to the existing image as you arrange each piece. Once the photo is complete, you'll earn points to upgrade and decorate your photo shop!
