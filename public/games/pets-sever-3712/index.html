Pets Sever

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pets Sever is an engaging puzzle game that is sure to capture your attention from the very first level. The main objective of the game revolves around drawing a protective box around your beloved pet, shielding it from relentless bee attacks. With every passing level, the challenges become increasingly intricate, putting your problem-solving skills to the test.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter various environments and puzzle types that will require you to think creatively and strategize effectively. The charming graphics and delightful animations make each level an enjoyable experience, while the escalating difficulty ensures that players remain engaged and challenged. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle expert or a casual player, Pets Sever offers a delightful blend of fun and mental stimulation.

The game employs straightforward mechanics that enable players to easily grasp the concept, while incorporating enough complexity to keep you coming back for more. As you navigate through the levels, you'll need to consider the placement of your protective box and anticipate the movements of the bees to keep your pet safe. With its unique gameplay and addictive nature, Pets Sever is a delightful way to practice your logic and strategy skills.

How to play free Pets Sever game online

To play Pets Sever online, simply navigate to the game s website. Use your mouse or finger to draw a box around your pet on the screen to create a safe zone. Avoid letting bees enter the area while ensuring your drawing is effective. Complete each puzzle before time runs out to advance through the levels and keep unlocking new challenges!
