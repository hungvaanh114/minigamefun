Cannon Man

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Cannon Man, a thrilling game that tests your timing and precision! In this explosive escapade, your mission is to strategically release a cannon bomb at just the right moment to hit a moving target, another cannon, which dances around the screen unpredictably. The excitement builds as you aim carefully and fire your cannon, hoping to connect with the target.

As you successfully strike the opposite cannon, you are rewarded with in-game currency, adding a layer of competition and motivation to your gameplay. Use the money you collect to upgrade your cannons or unlock exciting new features! Each level presents a new set of challenges, as the speed and randomness of the opposing cannon ramp up, making your task increasingly difficult yet more thrilling. How far can you go? Will you master the art of cannon fire?

Boasting vibrant graphics and engaging sound effects, Cannon Man offers a fun and immersive gaming experience for players of all ages. So gather your skills, and prepare to unleash some cannon blast action!

How to play free Cannon Man game online

To play Cannon Man, simply use your mouse or finger to aim at the moving cannon across the screen. Release the bomb at the optimal time to hit your target. Remember to collect the money after each successful hit to improve your cannon or progress through levels. Aim carefully and enjoy the explosive fun!


Instructions

Tap the screen or left mouse to release the cannon man
