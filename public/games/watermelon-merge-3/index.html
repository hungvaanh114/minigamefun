How to play free Watermelon Merge 3 game online

To play Watermelon Merge 3, begin by matching two identical fruits by tapping them together. This action will merge them into a larger fruit. Keep merging your fruits in the designated area until you manage to combine them into a full-sized watermelon. Strategically plan your moves to optimize your fruit combinations!
