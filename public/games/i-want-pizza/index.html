I Want Pizza

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exciting culinary adventure of I Want Pizza! In this engaging simulation game, players take on the role of a talented chef tasked with collecting and stacking dough to create delicious food items. Your primary goal is to prepare an array of mouth-watering dishes, including the ever-popular pizza, delectable donuts, and savory hotdogs. Each dish you make brings you closer to becoming the top chef in the culinary world!

As you progress through the game, you'll have the opportunity to expand your food preparation skills and explore a variety of recipes. Make sure to keep an eye on your customers appetites, as the more you deliver, the more recognition and wealth you will gain. Upgrading your food items not only makes them look more appealing but also boosts their earning potential, allowing you to rake in more profits!

A blend of strategy and creativity, I Want Pizza offers a fun way to develop your time management and cooking skills. With each level, you'll face new challenges that will test your speed and efficiency. As you master the art of food preparation, you'll inevitably unlock new recipes and upgrade your kitchen, helping you serve even more demanding customers.

How to play free I Want Pizza game online

To play I Want Pizza, use your mouse or touchscreen to collect dough and stack it efficiently. Select different food items from the menu to prepare based on customer orders. Time your actions well to maximize your efficiency, and remember to upgrade your dishes to increase your earnings!
