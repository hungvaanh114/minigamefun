Tower Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for an epic battle as a relentless horde of grotesque monsters charges toward your kingdom! In this thrilling Tower Defense game, you must strategize quickly and erect your fortifications in a race against time. As waves of monsters approach, it s up to you to ensure the safety of your realm by constructing various towers that unleash deadly arrows and boulders to fend off these vile creatures.

Each level presents increasing challenges, requiring you to think ahead and plan your defense meticulously. But mere arrows and stones may not suffice against these ferocious foes. Leverage elemental powers to gain the upper hand! You can utilize fire, ice, and lightning to enhance your towers and inflict devastating damage on multiple enemies at once. Choose your upgrades wisely and experiment with different combinations to create an unbreakable defense.

As you progress through the game, you will encounter new types of monsters, each with unique abilities that will test your strategic skills. Collect coins and resources with each defeated enemy to enhance your towers or unlock new ones. Will you rely on a fortified frontline or a well-placed artillery tower? The choice is yours, but remember, the fate of your kingdom hangs in the balance!

How to play free Tower Defense game online

Once you launch the game, start by selecting your towers and placing them strategically along the monsters' path. Monitor your resources and upgrade your defenses as you eliminate foes. Utilize elemental powers to enhance your attacks, and don t forget to keep an eye on upcoming waves to strategize your moves effectively!
