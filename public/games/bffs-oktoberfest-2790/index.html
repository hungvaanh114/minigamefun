BFFs Oktoberfest

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Emma and Kiara, two best friends, as they dive into a lively celebration during BFFs Oktoberfest! This festive event is brimming with the vibrant flavors of Bavarian cuisine, offering a delightful array of traditional dishes and a plethora of refreshing beers and beverages. Your mission? To help these stylish BFFs deck themselves out in stunning Oktoberfest maid outfits, ensuring they shine at this magical feast.

As you explore the world of BFFs Oktoberfest, you'll be able to customize and choose outfits that reflect the true spirit of the festival. Mix and match beautiful dresses, playful accessories, and fashionable hairstyles to create the perfect look for both Emma and Kiara. With each click, unleash your creativity and give them a truly unforgettable appearance, ready to embrace the festive spirit and savor the delicious Bavarian delights that await them!

Gather your friends and join in this exciting event at 7sgames.com. The BFFs are eager to enjoy not only the sumptuous food and drinks but also the wonderful company of enthusiastic festival-goers. Discover the joy of Oktoberfest as you indulge in this unique dress-up adventure, filled with laughter and friendship.

How to play free BFFs Oktoberfest game online

To play the BFFs Oktoberfest game, begin by selecting your favorite character, Emma or Kiara. Use your mouse or touchscreen to browse through a variety of beautiful outfits, accessories, and hairstyles. Simply drag and drop the elements onto the characters to create stylish Oktoberfest looks. Once you're satisfied, you can show off your fashionable BFFs and enjoy the festival atmosphere!
