Tank Soccer Battle 1 2 3 4 Player

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready for a tank battle with your friends? A tank battle with a soccer ball! Bring your friends and fight with tanks.Fill up your tanks&amp;rsquo; ammunition and blow up all your friends&amp;rsquo; tanks. While blowing up your friends&amp;rsquo; tanks, don&amp;rsquo;t forget to shoot the soccer ball. The player who gets the soccer ball to the goal wins. You need to score a goal to win. Instructions

Green Tank Use WASD to move and Q to fire Blue Tank Use Arrow Keys to move and P to fire Yellow Tank Use UHJK to move and M to fire Red Tank Use FCVB to move and N to fire
