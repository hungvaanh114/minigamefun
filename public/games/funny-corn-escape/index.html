Funny Corn Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Funny Corn Escape, a delightful point-and-click escape game crafted by 8BGames and Games2Mad. Set amidst a picturesque village surrounded by a lush forest, this game invites players to journey through a charming landscape where a unique twist awaits. You ll find yourself on a farm filled with vibrant corn crops. However, our funny little corn has found itself trapped in a house, and it's up to you to help it break free!

As you embark on this entertaining adventure, you'll need to exercise your keen observation skills. The game challenges you to search for hidden objects throughout the house, each cleverly integrated into the surroundings. Solving a series of interesting clues will be vital for your escape journey. Explore each nook and cranny, as the game's design ensures surprises lie in every corner! Your mission is to unravel the puzzles and assist the corn in finding its way back home. Good luck, and most importantly, have fun!

How to play free Funny Corn Escape game online

To play the Funny Corn Escape game, simply click on objects and items you find around the house. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with clues and collect hidden items. Examine each hint carefully to unlock new areas and solve puzzles. Your goal is to find the way to help the corn escape successfully!
