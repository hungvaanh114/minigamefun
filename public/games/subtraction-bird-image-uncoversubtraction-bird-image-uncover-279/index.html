Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of "Subtraction: Bird Image Uncover," a fun and educational game that seamlessly blends subtraction practice with an engaging visual challenge. Players will embark on a quest to uncover a hidden bird image that lies beneath a series of subtraction expression tiles. Each tile presents a numerical challenge, asking you to use your subtraction skills to unlock the secret within.

In this game, players are tasked with dragging and dropping the correct number bubbles onto the appropriate tiles that correspond to the subtraction expressions displayed. As you successfully solve each mathematical problem, the hidden bird image begins to emerge, piece by piece. The satisfaction of completing each subtraction not only enhances your numerical skills but also rewards you with the gradual revelation of a beautiful bird picture.

The goal is simple yet engaging: uncover the entire bird image by accurately solving all the math expressions presented on the screen. This interactive format encourages players to think critically and reinforces their subtraction skills in an enjoyable way. As you progress, the challenges may become increasingly complex, ensuring students of all levels remain engaged and motivated.

How to play free Subtraction: Bird Image Uncover game online

To play the game, simply click and drag the number bubbles to the corresponding subtraction expression tiles. Each time you solve a subtraction problem correctly, a portion of the bird image will be revealed. Keep working through the expressions until the entire image is uncovered. Enjoy learning while having fun!
