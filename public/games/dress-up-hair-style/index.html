Dress Up Hair Style

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of the beauty salon where creativity and style come together in the ultimate game of transformation! In "Dress Up Hair Style," you take on the role of an accomplished fashionista, running a bustling beauty shop that attracts numerous clients seeking your expertise. Your salon is the go-to destination for anyone wanting to elevate their look, and as the owner, you are the star of the show.

Each day brings a new wave of customers to your salon, all eager for a makeover. Time is of the essence, and there s no room for doubt as you dive into each appointment. Your clients have been waiting patiently for their turn, and they deserve to leave your salon feeling stunning. With an array of tools at your disposal scissors, hair dye, styling products, and accessories it's your mission to unleash your inner artist and create fabulous hairstyles. Transform each girl with bold and beautiful styles that reflect their personalities while ensuring they walk out with confidence and a smile.

As you progress through the game, you ll unlock new styles and tools, allowing you to take on even more challenging hairstyles. Keep your clients satisfied by paying attention to their preferences and needs. Good luck, and may your salon thrive as you make each girl more attractive than ever before!

How to play free Dress Up Hair Style game online

To play the "Dress Up Hair Style" game, simply select a customer waiting in your salon. Utilize various tools to cut, dye, and style their hair. Mix and match different accessories and outfits to complete their look. Pay attention to their requests to earn satisfaction points!
