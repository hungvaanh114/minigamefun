Embark on a fun-filled adventure with Mulan in Mulan's Magic Makeover, where you re given the opportunity to transform this beloved princess into a trendy city girl! Dive into the world of beauty and learn the delicate art of makeup application. With your help, Mulan will master the essentials of beauty, starting from the basics like foundation and concealer to setting powders that will give her that flawless finish.

As you progress, you ll focus on highlighting her features. Use rosy shades to accentuate Mulan s cheekbones and select the perfect lipstick to enhance her pout. Don't forget the finishing touches! Add false eyelashes and a vibrant eye shadow to make her eyes stand out. Experiment with different looks and unleash your creativity as you explore various color palettes and styles that reflect Mulan s personality.

For a mesmerizing change, Mulan can also try out contact lenses, allowing her to switch up her eye color whenever she desires. This feature adds an exciting twist to her transformation journey. Each choice you make helps in crafting a unique style that resonates with Mulan s inner fashionista. Get ready to play stylist and have fun with Mulan's makeover!

How to play free Mulan's Magic Makeover game online

To play Mulan's Magic Makeover, start by selecting the makeup tools from the available options. Click on each makeup item to apply it to Mulan s face, experimenting with different colors and styles. Follow the on-screen prompts to guide your makeover process. Have fun and be creative!
