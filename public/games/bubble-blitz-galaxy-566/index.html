Bubble Blitz Galaxy

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Bubble Blitz Galaxy! Get ready to embark on an exhilarating journey where your bubble-blasting skills will be put to the test. In this vibrant galaxy, aim and launch bubbles at matching colors to collect them before they drift too close to the edge. Your quick reflexes and strategic thinking will be your best allies as you navigate through this colorful universe.

Bubble Blitz Galaxy features two thrilling gameplay modes. In Arcade Mode, your goal is to survive for as long as possible while racking up an impressive score. Each bubble you match and collect adds to your points, but be careful not letting the bubbles reach the boundary is key to maintaining your game. With each level, the challenge intensifies, pushing your skills to the brink.

Alternatively, in Challenge Mode, you'll encounter a series of progressively difficult tasks where you must hit target scores within specific conditions. Tackle new obstacles and changing dynamics with each successive round, ensuring that your gameplay remains fresh and exciting. Both modes are designed to keep you engaged and entertained as you immerse yourself in this bubble-blasting escapade.

How to play free Bubble Blitz Galaxy game online

To play Bubble Blitz Galaxy, use your mouse or touchscreen to aim at the bubbles you want to shoot. Align with similar colors and release to blast them! Keep an eye on the limit to avoid letting bubbles touch the edge of the screen. Score points by collecting bubbles and completing challenges across both modes!
