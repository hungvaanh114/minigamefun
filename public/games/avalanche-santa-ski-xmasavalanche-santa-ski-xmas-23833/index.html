Get ready for an exhilarating holiday adventure in Avalanche - Santa Ski Xmas! As an avalanche thunders down the mountain, it s up to Santa to navigate the snowy slopes in a thrilling ski race. Race against time and the elements as you collect presents scattered along the way, all while trying to avoid obstacles that can slow you down. The festive graphics and cheerful soundtrack will immerse you in the spirit of Christmas as you glide through the winter wonderland.

But Santa can t do it alone! Head over to the shop to upgrade Santa's skiing abilities. With each successful run, earn points and upgrade perks such as speed, agility, and even special abilities to help you tackle tougher slopes. The further you ski, the more gifts you can gather, leading to massive rewards and unlocking hidden characters that will diversify your skiing experience.

Compete for the highest score and challenge your friends to beat your records! The game supports both English and Russian, making it accessible for players from various backgrounds. So, don your ski gear, embrace the festive vibe, and let the adventure begin! It s the perfect game to enjoy during the holiday season and an excellent way to spread some Christmas cheer. Remember to ski safely and collect all the gifts waiting for you. Play now to experience the joy! Merry Christmas and Happy New Year!

How to play free Avalanche - Santa Ski Xmas game online

To start playing Avalanche - Santa Ski Xmas, simply use the arrow keys or swipe on your device to guide Santa down the mountain. Collect gifts along the way while dodging obstacles. Keep an eye on the power-ups available in the shop to enhance your skiing performance!
