How to play free Car Transporter Ship Simulator game online

To play the Car Transporter Ship Simulator, simply navigate using your joystick or arrow keys to steer your ship. Load the cars onto your ferry by following on-screen prompts, and once you're loaded, set your course across the sea. Remember to manage your speed and maneuver around obstacles to prevent any damages!
