How to play free Stack The Gifts Xmas game online

To play Stack The Gifts Xmas, simply use your mouse or finger to tap or click when you want to release a gift onto the stack. Aim for the center of the stack for better balance and higher scores. Keep stacking as you earn points and try to reach new heights with your festive tower of gifts!
