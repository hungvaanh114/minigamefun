Foot Treatment

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the engaging game "Foot Treatment," players step into the role of a skilled podiatrist ready to alleviate the pain of distressed patients. Each character arrives with unique foot problems that range from sprained ankles to stubborn calluses, and it s your job to diagnose and treat their ailments using a variety of advanced medical tools. The urgency in their voices as they exclaim, "Hey Doctor, I hurt my feet and it is so painful!" sets the stage for an interactive medical experience.

Your patients trust you to provide the best care, and you have everything you need to deliver relief. Wheel out your top-notch medical equipment, apply soothing ointments, and bandage those tired feet back to health. Once you ve expertly treated the pain and discomfort, it s time to focus on nail care. Each patient has unique needs, and you must skillfully trim and polish their nails, helping them leave with a smile on their face and healthier-looking feet.

With every completed treatment, you build your reputation as the best foot doctor in town. Don't forget to pay close attention to detail, since even the smallest oversight could lead to a setback. The satisfaction of seeing your patients walk out pain-free will not only inspire you to treat each foot properly but also make the game incredibly rewarding.

How to play free Foot Treatment game online

To play the Foot Treatment game, simply click on the patient you wish to treat and follow the on-screen instructions. Use your mouse to select medical tools and perform the necessary treatments. Ensure to complete the nail care process to finish the treatment and earn trust points for your skillful care!
