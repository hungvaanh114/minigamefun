Coopter War

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Coopter War is an exhilarating and engaging combat strategy game that promises players a thrilling experience filled with adventurous missions and explosive confrontations. As a Commander, you will lead your troops into battle against a multitude of opponents online, confronting terrorism and conflict head-on. Prepare yourself for an intense challenge where strategy and skill are paramount. With your ability to recruit formidable troops and build an unstoppable army, you can vanquish villains and achieve remarkable victories.

Featuring four electrifying combat modes, including Campaign, Challenge, Endless Zombie, and Online PvP, Coopter War offers diverse gameplay to keep you entertained. You will traverse through ten spectacular battlegrounds, from dense forests to bustling factories, picturesque seaside locations, and beyond. The richness of the game's environment enhances the immersive experience, making each encounter unique.

With over 20 troop types and battle machines at your disposal, from the fierce assault force to the powerful M1A2 Tank, AH-64D Apache, and devastating cruise missiles, you have the arsenal needed to dominate your foes. Gather gold and earn PvP victory bonuses to upgrade your forces, enhancing your combat capabilities. Utilize your strategic skills to deploy troops effectively and execute precision airstrikes that will obliterate enemy lines. The user-friendly controls and action-packed gameplay ensure a seamless experience as you take command and lead your forces to victory.

How to play free Coopter War game online

To play Coopter War, simply choose your desired battle mode from the main menu. Recruit troops and deploy them strategically on the battlefield. Use your resources wisely to upgrade your units and launch powerful attacks against the enemy. Engage in thrilling PvP battles or challenge yourself with Campaign missions!
