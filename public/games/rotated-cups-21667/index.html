Rotated Cups

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rotated Cups is an engaging and skillful game that promises to challenge your precision and timing. The objective is simple yet captivating: you need to rotate a cup that holds a ball and guide it to drop directly into a designated bucket. This game not only tests your hand-eye coordination but also enhances your focus. As you maneuver the cup, you'll find yourself using either your mouse or your finger, depending on whether you're playing on a device or computer, to ensure the ball lands in the right spot.

With each level, the difficulty increases, introducing new obstacles and challenges that make the gameplay even more exciting. It's not just about dropping the ball; it's about strategy and skill, as you need to calculate the perfect angle and timing for a successful shot. The delightful graphics and smooth controls create an enjoyable gaming experience that keeps you coming back for more.

This game is perfect for players of all ages, as it combines fun and challenge in a simple yet addictive format. Whether you're looking to pass the time or aiming to improve your gaming skills, Rotated Cups will surely entertain you. Dive into this captivating game and see how quickly you can master the art of cup rotation and ball dropping. Have fun as you progress through various levels and aim for the highest score!

How to play free Rotated Cups game online

To play Rotated Cups, simply use your mouse or finger to rotate the cup that holds the ball. Your goal is to align the cup perfectly to drop the ball into the bucket. Pay attention to the angle and timing for the best results, and enjoy guiding the ball to the target!
