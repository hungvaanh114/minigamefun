Happy Easter Links

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enjoy a delightful Easter-themed challenge with the game Happy Easter Links! In this engaging puzzle game, your objective is to connect two identical Easter items with a pathway that is limited to no more than two 90-degree angles. The thrill lies in trying to think strategically as you work to clear the board by removing all pairs of matching Easter items.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter different levels of difficulty, with a total of 27 challenging stages to conquer. Be mindful of the gameplay's unique twist some tiles may float, requiring you to calculate your moves carefully. Tiles can drift to the bottom, top, left, right, center, or even split, adding an unpredictable element to your puzzle-solving journey.

Time is of the essence! Completing levels before time runs out not only secures your victory but can also earn you additional bonuses. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a casual gamer looking for some holiday fun, Happy Easter Links promises hours of engaging gameplay filled with festive charm.

How to play free Happy Easter Links game online

To play Happy Easter Links, simply select two identical Easter items to connect. Make sure your path does not exceed two 90-degree angles. Keep an eye on the timer and attempt to clear the board before time runs out to maximize your score and earn bonus rewards!
