Idle Store Cleaner

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the shoes of a diligent cleaner in Idle Store Cleaner, where your mission is to transform a messy mall into a pristine shopping paradise! In this exciting simulation game, you'll take on the task of tidying up by picking up litter, mopping the floors, and venturing into new areas to discover hidden messes that need your attention. As you undertake these responsibilities, you ll earn money which can be reinvested into your cleaning abilities and character upgrades.

Each day presents new challenges and opportunities as you explore additional parts of the mall, meeting various obstacles that require your skills. Improving your cleaning character will not only speed up your cleaning process but also significantly increase your earnings for every completed task. From shiny floors to immaculate storefronts, the more efficiently you clean, the more rewarding your experience will be. With each level you pass, watch as the mall transforms before your eyes, evolving from a cluttered space into a sought-after shopping destination.

Idle Store Cleaner is perfect for players looking for casual yet engaging gameplay where strategy meets relaxation. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to make a mess-free haven that every shopper would be happy to visit!

How to play free Idle Store Cleaner game online

To start playing Idle Store Cleaner, simply click on available trash and messes to clean them up. Use the virtual currency you earn to upgrade your cleaning tools and character. Explore different areas of the mall to increase your cleaning efficiency and income!
