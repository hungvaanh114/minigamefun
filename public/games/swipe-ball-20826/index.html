Swipe Ball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Swipe Ball, where your agility and reflexes are put to the test! In this captivating hypercasual game, you will navigate a vibrant landscape filled with sparkling diamonds and deadly bombs. Your primary goal is to collect as many diamonds as possible while skillfully dodging the bombs that threaten your progress. The gameplay is simple yet addictive, requiring quick reactions and strategic movements to outmaneuver the obstacles in your path.

As you dive into the game, you'll find that each level presents increasing challenges, making it crucial to remain focused and agile. The more diamonds you gather, the higher your score climbs, setting the stage for friendly competition with your friends. Engage in thrilling rounds of Swipe Ball, and see who can achieve the highest score! Challenge your friends to beat your personal best and enjoy a fun-filled experience that keeps you on your toes!

Swipe Ball is designed for players of all ages, offering an accessible yet exciting gameplay experience. The colorful graphics and engaging sound effects enhance the enjoyment, providing an ideal setting for endless fun. So, are you ready to swipe your way to victory? Jump into Swipe Ball and start your adventure today!

How to play free Swipe Ball game online

To play Swipe Ball, simply use your finger or mouse to swipe left or right to maneuver your ball around the screen. Collect diamonds by moving towards them while carefully avoiding the bombs. The game will track your score, so aim to gather as many diamonds as you can before your game ends!
