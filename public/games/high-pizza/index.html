High Pizza

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Bring your culinary dreams to life in High Pizza, a delightful casual game that promises hours of fun! In this enchanting 3D environment, you become a skilled pizza chef, tasked with serving mouthwatering pizzas to an array of eager guests. Your goal is to keep them happy and satisfied, but time is of the essence, so you must work quickly to avoid disappointed customers.

As you dive into the bustling kitchen, beware of the messy surroundings! The back kitchen is filled with broken pizzas and various obstacles that can slow you down or hinder your progress. You'll need to navigate through this chaos skillfully while assembling scrumptious pizzas according to your guests' requests. With each level you conquer, the challenges become more intense, enhancing the excitement of the gameplay.

High Pizza is not just about speed; it also requires you to hone your strategy and multitasking skills. As you continue to serve up tasty treats in increasing levels of difficulty, you ll unlock new recipes and upgrades for your pizzeria. The colorful 3D art style makes every moment enjoyable, immersing you in a whimsical world where every slice counts. Get ready, chef, because your pizza-making adventure awaits!

How to play free High Pizza game online

To play High Pizza, simply navigate through the kitchen using your mouse or touchpad. You'll need to select pizza ingredients quickly and assemble them according to customer orders. Avoid touching any broken pizzas or obstacles to keep your serving speed high. Serve the pizzas before time runs out to earn points and progress through the levels!
