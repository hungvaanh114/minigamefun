Doodle Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Doodle Jump is an enchanting and lively platforming game that challenges players to assist a charming doodle character in jumping to new heights on whimsical, hand-drawn platforms. With its endless gameplay, your primary goal is to reach the highest score possible by continuously leaping from one platform to another! Your character will jump automatically, so your main responsibility is to guide their left and right movements using the arrow keys, adjusting their jumping path to stay on the platforms.

As you venture upward, you'll encounter a variety of different platforms. Some will break upon impact, like the brown platforms, while others, like the green platforms, provide springs that grant you an extra boost! However, it s essential to keep an eye out for pesky bugs that may hinder your progress. The game s charming graphics and addictive mechanics will keep you entertained as you strive to soar higher and set new records. How high will you manage to go in the captivating world of Doodle Jump?

How to play free Doodle Jump game online

To play Doodle Jump online, simply start the game and use the left and right arrow keys to control your character's movement. Jump onto platforms to gain height and avoid enemies. Keep jumping and exploring different platforms to achieve the highest score!
