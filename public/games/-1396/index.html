Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the dazzling world of The Prism City Detectives, where vibrant colors burst to life in a quest to reclaim the vibrancy of a monochrome city! Join Luna, Ruby, Skye, Willow, Daisy, Violet, and Lumi, a group of spirited detectives determined to restore their home s lost hues. This whimsical adventure invites players to venture through enchanting realms filled with hidden objects, captivating dress-up challenges, and mind-bending puzzles.

In this immersive journey, each detective brings their unique abilities to help uncover the whereabouts of the elusive Rainbow Gem, a vital artifact required to revive the city s color. As you explore various levels, you ll face enchanting dress-up scenarios that allow you to customize your characters, making the experience even more personal and engaging. Work together with your friends or race against the clock as you collect clues and unveil secrets in your quest to restore unity among the citizens of Prism City.

The game features an array of visually stunning environments, each begging to be explored, full of hidden treasures waiting to be discovered. Utilize your sharp eye for detail to find objects cleverly concealed within each beautifully crafted scene. The storyline unfolds as you progress, ensuring that every moment spent in the game is full of intrigue and excitement.

How to play free The Prism City Detectives game online

To dive into The Prism City Detectives, start by selecting your favorite detective character. As you navigate through various vibrant levels, click on hidden objects to collect clues and complete challenges. Dress up your characters creatively for added flair while solving exciting puzzles that propel you closer to retrieving the Rainbow Gem!
