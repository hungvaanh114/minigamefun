Help Me

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

"Help Me" is an engaging point-and-click escape game crafted by 8B Games/Games2Mad. In this thrilling adventure, players follow the story of a girl trapped in her own room, desperately seeking a way out. With her mobile phone as a tool, she embarks on a quest to locate the person responsible for locking her in.

As you immerse yourself in this captivating escape experience, you'll encounter a series of intriguing puzzles and hidden objects scattered throughout the room. Each puzzle you solve brings the girl one step closer to freedom. Whether it s unlocking a mysterious box or piecing together clues from various objects, your problem-solving skills will be put to the test. This game not only challenges your intellect but also offers an exhilarating escape room experience that keeps you hooked from start to finish.

The game's colorful graphics and immersive sound design enhance the overall experience, making it feel even more engaging. "Help Me" provides players with the opportunity to think critically and creatively, ensuring that every moment spent in this room is filled with suspense and excitement. Good luck, and most importantly, have fun as you unravel the mystery behind the locked door!

How to play free Help Me game online

To play "Help Me," simply use your mouse or touchpad to point and click on various objects within the room. Interact with items to collect them, solve the hidden puzzles, and help the girl escape. Remember to explore every corner of the room and use your keen observation skills to find all the clues!
