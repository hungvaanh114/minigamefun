Hearts Blocks Collapse

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Hearts Blocks Collapse, an exciting and colorful puzzle game that will keep you entertained for hours! In this vibrant world filled with charming heart-shaped blocks, your objective is to click on and remove groups of hearts that share the same color. The challenge lies in strategically planning your moves to connect as many hearts as possible, as they must be aligned either horizontally or vertically to be eliminated.

As you progress through the levels, you will encounter increasingly complex puzzles that require quick thinking and foresight. Use the Projection box to your advantage, which showcases the points you ll earn with each heart removal. Keeping an eye on this information will help you maximize your score and ensure you reach your target points for each level.

Remember, even if you don t achieve the target score on your first attempt, you can always retry the level to improve your strategy! With each retry, you ll become more adept at spotting potential chain reactions and planning your moves effectively. The more hearts you clear, the more satisfying the gameplay becomes!

How to play free Hearts Blocks Collapse game online

To play Hearts Blocks Collapse, simply click on clusters of connected hearts of the same color. Aim to remove as many as you can in a single move. Keep track of the target points displayed in the Projection box and make strategic choices to achieve or surpass this score to advance to the next level!


Instructions

Remove hearts by clicking on hearts with at least one heart of the same color next to it horizontally or vertically
