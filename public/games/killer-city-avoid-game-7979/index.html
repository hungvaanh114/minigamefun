Killer City - Avoid Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Killer City - Avoid Game, players immerse themselves in a transformed urban landscape where danger lurks around every corner. Once a simple stickman, your protagonist awakens to find the streets he once navigated now shrouded in peril. The city, while visually unchanged, has morphed into a hazardous environment fraught with threats at every turn.

As you guide the hero through this deadly metropolis, you'll discover that even the simplest journey can become a treacherous endeavor. Automated vehicles speed by with no regard for safety, and shadowy figures lie in wait, ready to pounce from alleyways. Your goal is to collect as much money as possible, and the vibrant green packs strewn across the ground and hidden within buildings beckon you to pick them up. However, beware! Each step carries the risk of sudden attacks, and you must be quick on your feet.

Prepare yourself with a trusty bat, the only weapon you have to defend against potential aggressors. This vital tool can be the difference between life and death in Killer City. Additionally, crossing the busy streets requires your utmost attention; timing your moves is crucial to avoid becoming the next victim. Only the most agile and resourceful players can thrive in this ruthless environment, so gather your courage and show the city who's boss!

How to play free Killer City - Avoid Game game online

To play Killer City - Avoid Game, navigate your stickman using arrow keys to move around the city. Collect the green packs to accumulate money while avoiding vehicles and enemies. Use the bat with the action key to fend off attackers while remaining alert as you cross busy roads. Stay alive and gather as much cash as you can!
