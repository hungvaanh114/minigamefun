Steve and Alex

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Steve on an adventurous quest in the captivating love story of Steve and Alex! As Steve, your primary mission is to rescue Alex, who is trapped in a perplexing realm. You will need to gather scattered portal pieces to create the powerful Alexsin portal. Navigate through diverse landscapes and overcome various obstacles as you collect ten precious obsidians to complete your task.

But beware! The journey is fraught with danger. You'll have to stay sharp and avoid treacherous falls, as a single misstep can lead to your untimely demise. Additionally, steer clear of the volatile TNTs that litter the path; if you touch them, they will explode, sending you back to the start! Utilize your platforming skills and strategic thinking to dodge hazards and reach your goal safely.

As you traverse the vibrant world, uncover hidden treasures, and solve puzzles to enhance your chances of success. The excitement escalates as you make your way to the portal, ensuring that every moment is filled with thrilling challenges. Will you gather the required materials in time and save Alex? The fate of their love story lies in your hands!

How to play free Steve and Alex game online

To play the game, use the arrow keys or WASD to move Steve and navigate through obstacles. Collect obsidians by jumping on platforms and avoid falling or touching TNT. Keep an eye on your health, and remember to explore diligently to find all the necessary pieces to create the Alexsin portal!
