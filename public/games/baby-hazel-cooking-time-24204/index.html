Baby Hazel Cooking Time

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel in an exciting culinary adventure as she discovers her cooking talents! With her mom away from home and a growling tummy, Baby Hazel is eager to whip up some delicious snacks. Your mission is to assist her in this fun and delightful cooking escapade.

The first stop is the shopping mall, where you will help Baby Hazel gather all the necessary ingredients. Navigate through the aisles to find fresh produce and other essential cooking items, ensuring that everything needed for her tasty creations is in the shopping cart. Choose wisely, as each ingredient plays a vital role in making the perfect snacks!

Once back in the kitchen, it s time to roll up your sleeves and assist Hazel in crafting her favorite mini quiches and delightful fruit wands. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully to mix, bake, and present these mouthwatering treats. You'll get to experience the joy of cooking alongside Baby Hazel while also igniting your own culinary creativity.

Finally, after all that hard work, it s time to enjoy the fruits of your labor! Join Baby Hazel and her adorable pets at the dining table for a delightful meal, celebrating the delicious snacks you ve prepared together. Whether it s sharing laughter or savoring those tasty bites, this cooking time is bound to be memorable.

How to play free Baby Hazel Cooking Time game online

To start playing Baby Hazel Cooking Time, simply click to navigate through the game s interface. Follow the prompts to visit the mall, collect ingredients, and then enter the kitchen. Use your mouse to interact with different objects and complete tasks as you help Baby Hazel cook her favorite dishes.
