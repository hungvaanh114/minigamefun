Immerse yourself in the delightful world of Animals Collection! This engaging game challenges players to gather various adorable creatures scattered across the screen. Simply tap on any animal to initiate the game, and subsequently glide your mouse or fingertip over adjacent identical animals. You can connect them horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally. The goal is to select a minimum of three matching animals in one continuous motion.

Once you've formed a connection, release the mouse button to secure your match. As you progress, keep an eye out for bonus opportunities! When you collect every sixth animal, you'll trigger a special bonus. If you're able to gather more than seven animals in one go, you'll earn a time bonus to help you in your quest.

However, there s a catch! You need to collect the required animals within the allotted time frame. Failing to do so means you ll unfortunately lose the game. As you navigate through various levels and challenges, the excitement builds, and you'll find yourself striving to beat your high score while enjoying the vibrant animations of each animal.

How to play free Animals Collection game online

To dive into the game, start by tapping on any animal to begin. Once in the game, move your mouse or fingertip over the identical animals you wish to match. Ensure you connect at least three to score. Release the button to confirm your selection, and remember to collect the required animals within the time limit to succeed!
