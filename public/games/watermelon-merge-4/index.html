How to play free Watermelon Merge 4 game online

To play Watermelon Merge 4, start by tapping on two matching fruits on the board to merge them into a larger fruit. Continue this process to create progressively larger fruits, eventually forming a watermelon. Keep an eye on your available moves and try to strategize for optimal merges!
