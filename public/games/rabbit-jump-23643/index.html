Rabbit Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the delightful world of Rabbit Jump, players are invited to join an enthusiastic little bunny on his incredible journey to soar through the skies. With the help of a self-invented catapult, this adventurous bunny aims to reach astonishing heights. It's not just about jumping; it's about collecting treasures and utilizing power-ups along the way!

Your mission begins as you launch the bunny into the air, navigating through a vibrant environment filled with challenges and rewards. As you ascend to greater heights, be on the lookout for shimmering coins that will help you unlock exciting upgrades and abilities. Engage with various power-ups scattered throughout the skies to enhance your jumping potential, giving you the extra boost you need to reach new heights and break records.

From soaring through clouds to dodging obstacles, every jump is a chance to embrace the exhilaration of flight while aiming for the ultimate goal: jumping to infinity! Join this charming bunny in Rabbit Jump and explore fascinating landscapes all while enjoying colorful visuals and delightful sounds that will keep you immersed in this joyous adventure.

How to play free Rabbit Jump game online

Playing Rabbit Jump is simple and fun! Use your mouse or tap the screen to launch the bunny into the air. Collect coins by jumping through them, and gather power-ups to enhance your jumping abilities. The goal is to jump as high as possible while dodging obstacles. Enjoy the thrill of the jump!
