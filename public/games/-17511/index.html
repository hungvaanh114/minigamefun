Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Stunt Car Challenge 3, a captivating stunt racing game that invites you to race against the clock while performing jaw-dropping stunts. As you hurtle down the track, your objective is not only to finish quickly but also to execute flips and tricks that will help you gather coins scattered throughout the course. Mastering front and back flips can significantly increase your coin collection, unlocking exciting opportunities for you.

Your goal is to achieve a stellar 3-star rating by collecting every single coin available in each level. Accumulating stars will unlock the new area known as "Port," featuring numerous new challenges and levels that will test your driving skills and creativity. Throughout your exhilarating journey, you will discover a total of 15 exceptional vehicles that you can unlock. Each car comes with its own unique characteristics and potential for upgrades.

Customize your vehicles by applying a range of upgrades including enhancements to the engine, tires, and suspension. These modifications will elevate your car s performance, making it the fastest and most agile on the track. Don t forget to splurge on a stunning paint job that reflects your personal style, ensuring that your car stands out as you compete. Navigate through each exhilarating level, perfect your stunt techniques, and earn those coveted 3 stars to establish yourself as the ultimate champion in Stunt Car Challenge 3.

How to play free Stunt Car Challenge game online

To play Stunt Car Challenge 3, use the acceleration button to speed up and direct your car using the arrow keys. Perform stunts like flips by pressing the appropriate buttons mid-air. Collect coins by driving through them, and aim to complete levels quickly while gathering all coins for maximum stars!
