Poppy Infinity Trail

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the exhilarating world of Poppy Infinity Trail, a thrilling adventure game that intertwines the excitement of Poppy Playtime with cosmic challenges! In this engaging game, players must take on the role of Huggy Wuggy, a beloved character known for his charm and cunning. Your ultimate mission? To safeguard your planet from relentless falling asteroids!

As you navigate through a stunning cosmic landscape, you will face the daunting task of intercepting these celestial threats. Remember, your planet has a limited resilience; it can withstand only two direct hits before it succumbs to destruction. This adds an exhilarating layer of intensity as you race against the clock to protect your homeland! How many points can you rack up before it's game over?

With simple yet captivating gameplay mechanics, Poppy Infinity Trail is designed to keep players on the edge of their seats. Each asteroid represents a potential threat, and your agility will determine the fate of your planet. Strategic movements and quick reflexes will allow you to dodge or destroy the incoming asteroids while gathering points and power-ups scattered throughout the level.

Embark on this galactic adventure and find out how long you can sustain your points tally while preserving your planet's integrity. Can you master the skies and emerge as a hero in the face of adversity? Join the ranks of high scorers and challenge your friends to beat your best score!

How to play free Poppy Infinity Trail game online

To play Poppy Infinity Trail, simply use the arrow keys to navigate Huggy Wuggy. Move left and right to dodge falling asteroids while clicking to destroy them. Keep an eye on your planet's health, and aim to reach the highest score possible before it's too late!


Instructions

Keyboard left and right arrow keys or A and D
