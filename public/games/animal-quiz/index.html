Animal Quiz

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the exciting world of wildlife with the Animal Quiz! This interactive application boasts a collection of 100 captivating pictures of renowned mammals, 89 photos showcasing stunning birds, 19 images of daunting reptiles, 4 photographs of amphibians, 44 unique fish varieties, and 46 diverse arthropods from every corner of the globe. Whether you're a fan of wild animals or domestic pets, this game covers the entire animal kingdom, presenting a true zoo experience!

Challenge your knowledge as you attempt to guess the animals from the provided images. The game is thoughtfully designed with animals categorized into five distinct levels. You ll begin with mammals, featuring unique species such as the African rhinoceros and the Australian echidna. Can you tell the difference between a meerkat and a groundhog? Move on to the realm of birds where you ll encounter the small but iconic American robin alongside the enormous African ostrich, as well as the elegant flamingo and intriguing emu.

Further along, you'll dive into the intriguing world of reptiles and amphibians, testing your ability to identify creatures like the python and the majestic Galapagos tortoise. Switch gears as you explore the underwater realm filled with fish, ranging from fierce sharks to delicious salmon. Lastly, navigate the complex and fascinating domain of arthropods, where you ll decide if that creepy crawly is a mantis or a scorpion!

How to play free Animal Quiz game online

To start playing the Animal Quiz, simply choose a category from the main menu, and then view the images presented. Try to guess the animal based on the picture and select your answer from the options available. Test your skills across all levels and enjoy learning about the diverse creatures that inhabit our planet!
