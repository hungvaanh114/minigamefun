Grimace Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Grimace on an exciting adventure in Grimace Drop, a captivating physics-based puzzle game featuring 60 challenging levels! In this engaging experience, your primary objective is to aid Grimace in his escape from a forest prison. The game presents a series of boxes, and your task is to eliminate all of them, except the grassy area where Grimace must land safely.

To play, simply click or tap on the various boxes or blocks to destroy them. The challenge lies in using the remaining blocks wisely and creating the best path for Grimace to reach the grass. As you progress through each level, the puzzles become more intricate, requiring creativity and strategic thinking to devise solutions. Will you guide Grimace to safety and help him break free from his prison?

With vibrant graphics and enthralling gameplay mechanics, Grimace Drop promises hours of entertainment as you navigate through its cleverly designed puzzles. Each level offers a unique twist, providing a mix of excitement and difficulty that will keep you coming back for more. Put your skills to the test and see if you can help Grimace escape from the grasp of the forest prison!

How to play free Grimace Drop game online

In Grimace Drop, use your mouse or finger to click or tap on the boxes to destroy them strategically. Focus on clearing the way for Grimace to fall onto the grassy area. Assess the layout carefully and utilize the remaining blocks to create a safe path for his escape!
