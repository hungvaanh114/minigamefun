Santa Basket

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Santa Basket is an exhilarating sports game designed for players of all ages. This engaging and technical challenge puts your aiming skills to the test as you strive to score goals by launching Santa into a basket. The longer you stay in the game, the better your aiming ability will need to be, making for an exciting and competitive experience.

In this festive game, you must carefully calculate the optimal angle and force required to successfully shoot Santa through the hoop. Mastering these mechanics is key to achieving high scores and extending your gameplay. Whether you're playing for fun or aiming for a personal best, Santa Basket offers endless enjoyment and the thrill of competition, perfect for friends and family gatherings or solo play.

So gather around your device and get ready to take aim! With each shot, you'll hone your skills while enjoying the enchanting background and cheerful holiday theme, which adds to the game's overall charm. Challenge your friends and family to see who can achieve the best score, or take on the game solo and strive for greatness!

How to play free Santa Basket game online

To play Santa Basket, simply align the angle of your shot using the on-screen markers and adjust the power indicator to set the force of your throw. Release the button to shoot Santa into the basket. Aim for precision to keep playing and set new records!
