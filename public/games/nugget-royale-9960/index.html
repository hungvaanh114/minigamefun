Instructions:

Prepare for a wild ride in Nugget Royale, where 80 chicken players battle it out to be the last one standing on a precarious disk poised above a perilous grinder. Only the wiliest and fastest chickens can avoid being turned into a delicious chicken nugget! Players can utilize a variety of moves including walking, sprinting, jumping, and dashing as they navigate this frantic race for survival.

Ascend to victory by outmaneuvering your opponents while aiming for the top spot on the leaderboard. The champion, crowned as the ultimate nugget master, stands out with a prestigious crown that signifies their dominance over the flock. Along the way, players collect nuggets as currency to unlock a series of fun hats, ranging from quirky visor caps to playful ketchup lids and deep fry baskets. The goal is to collect all unique hats while showcasing your creativity!

Upon entering the game, players start out as the smallest chicken but quickly learn that devouring corn will help them grow larger, providing an edge in pushing other players off the disk. Strategic jumping, effective dashing, and clever positioning on the disk will pave the way to survival. Nugget Royale is not just about surviving; it's about having a blast while pushing others out of the way and remaining the last chicken out!

Nugget Royale can be enjoyed online, competed with up to 80 players, and can also be played locally with up to four friends on a single device. It s time to play at Nugget Royale and become the ultimate chicken champion!

How to play free Nugget Royale game online

Once you re in the game, use the W, A, S, D keys to move around. Jump by pressing the spacebar, and dash using the shift key to avoid enemy pushes. Your goal is to eat corn, grow larger, and push the other chickens off the disk while avoiding the grinder below. Good luck!
