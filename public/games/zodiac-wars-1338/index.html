Zodiac Wars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the cosmic battlefield of Zodiac Wars, where the stars align for an exhilarating adventure! In this thrilling arcade-style game, players find themselves aboard a powerful starship equipped with a formidable laser cannon. Your mission? To obliterate an array of blocks and intricate zodiac symbols that populate the screen, all while showcasing your reflexes and precision.

As you navigate through the vibrant and colorful levels, you'll encounter a plethora of challenges presented by the zodiac signs, each representing unique obstacles to overcome. Utilize your laser to strategically target and eliminate these blocks while dodging hazards and incoming attacks. With intuitive controls and dynamic gameplay, Zodiac Wars invites players of all ages to immerse themselves in an engaging battle among the stars.

Suit up and prepare for an interstellar showdown, where every move counts. Collect power-ups and bonuses along the way to enhance your starship's capabilities and increase your chances of achieving high scores. The faster and more accurately you clear the zodiac signs, the more rewarding your experience becomes. So, aim carefully, unleash your laser, and claim victory in this cosmic confrontation!

How to play the free Zodiac Wars game online

Once you're in the game, control your starship using the arrow keys or on-screen joystick. Use the laser to shoot at zodiac signs and blocks by pressing the space bar or tapping the fire button. Aim carefully, avoid obstacles, and try to clear the stage before time runs out!
