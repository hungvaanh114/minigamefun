Ninja Treasure Match 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ninja Treasure Match 3 is an exhilarating online puzzle game designed for treasure hunters and puzzle enthusiasts alike, and the best part? It s completely free! In this vibrant world filled with colorful gems, you'll take on the role of a nimble ninja whose goal is to gather precious treasures. The gameplay is simple yet captivating, making it accessible to players of all ages.

To embark on your quest, you need to connect three or more gems of the same color by drawing a line to eliminate them from the board. The satisfaction of creating beautiful combos is a rewarding experience, and if you manage to link five or more gems, you re in for a delightful bonus as well as additional time to keep playing! Each level presents a unique challenge, requiring strategy and quick thinking to maximize your score and advance to the next stage.

As you progress, the game introduces various obstacles and power-ups that will either aid or hinder your mission. You ll find that planning your moves carefully can lead to spectacular chain reactions, clearing the board and unearthing treasures galore. Ninja Treasure Match 3 promises hours of fun and excitement as you continue to unlock new levels while competing for high scores.

How to play free Ninja Treasure Match 3 game online

To play Ninja Treasure Match 3, simply click and drag your finger across the screen to connect gems of the same color. Aim to align three or more gems to eliminate them. Keep an eye out for special bonuses that appear when you match five or more, allowing you extra time to keep the treasure hunt going!
