Kick Grimace

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you a fan of physics-based puzzle games that challenge your brain? If so, "Kick Grimace" is the perfect game for you! In this exciting adventure, you ll be tasked with cleverly launching a red ball to hit the purple character known as Grimace. This isn t just any ordinary game; it combines strategy and skill by making you think critically before you take your shot.

Your goal is to strike Grimace with the red ball while aiming to collect 3 delicious Grimace Milk Shakes scattered throughout the various levels. Each level presents unique challenges and obstacles that will test your problem-solving skills, ensuring that you never run out of exciting gameplay. With countless amazing levels to explore, you'll find that each stage poses new puzzles that require innovative thinking and precision. Get ready to enjoy thrilling gameplay with vibrant graphics and engaging challenges!

As you dive into "Kick Grimace," the colorful visuals and delightful sounds will keep you entertained as you strategize your next move. Gather your wits and prepare for a brain-teasing journey that promises fun and excitement. The more level you master, the closer you get to becoming a true Grimace kicker!

How to play free Kick Grimace game online

To play the "Kick Grimace" game, simply tap on the screen to aim and adjust the power of your shot. Once ready, release to drop the red ball and try to hit Grimace. Remember to collect the milkshakes along the way for extra points!
