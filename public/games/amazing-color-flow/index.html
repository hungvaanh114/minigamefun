Amazing Color Flow

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Amazing Color Flow is an exhilarating arcade game that challenges players to fill an empty tank with vibrant colors. Your mission is to ensure that the water flows seamlessly in the correct direction. By strategically removing sticks placed in the way, you can guide the colored water to fill the tank while avoiding any cross-contamination of hues. The game offers an exciting mix of strategy and skill, requiring you to think critically about each move you make.

With an abundance of water available, it's up to you to manage the colors wisely. However, beware: mixing the colors can lead to disastrous results! As you progress through the game, you'll encounter 20 increasingly difficult levels, each designed to test your problem-solving abilities and reflexes. Adapt your strategies as you face new challenges, and aim to complete each level with the right combination of shades. Good luck!

How to play free Amazing Color Flow game online

To play Amazing Color Flow, start by selecting the level you want to tackle. Use your mouse to click and remove the sticks obstructing the water flow. Your goal is to guide the water to fill the tank without mixing different colors. Stay alert, and plan your moves carefully to ensure a successful fill!
