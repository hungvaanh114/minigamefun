Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the captivating realm of Castle Defense Online, an exciting tower defense experience that features a unique Origami art style. Your beloved castle is under siege by a horde of mysterious, terrifying monsters that threaten to invade your city. It s your duty to take command and devise effective strategies to thwart these menacing creatures. With each challenge presenting its own distinct enemy, you must analyze their weaknesses and adapt your tactics to ensure the safety of your kingdom.

In Castle Defense Online, you'll find that the key to survival is the ability to manipulate your soldiers' formations. Continuously altering their positions allows you to create effective defensive lines and outsmart the frightening beings coming your way. Gather your courage and lead your gallant troops as they fend off relentless waves of foes, fighting valiantly to the very end! Choose your soldiers wisely and observe their strengths and weaknesses as you tailor your defense to counteract each unique adversary.

As you progress through the game, you unlock new abilities and defenses, enhancing your military might. With a combination of strategic planning and swift execution, you will engage in epic battles that put your skills to the test. Can you save your city from destruction and emerge as the heroic protector? Charge into the fray and experience the thrill of Castle Defense Online!

How to play free Castle Defense Online game online

To play Castle Defense Online, start by selecting your soldiers and placing them strategically around your castle. Keep an eye on incoming monsters and adjust your formation as needed to exploit their weaknesses. Use special abilities wisely to maintain your defense, and don t hesitate to call for reinforcements when under attack!
