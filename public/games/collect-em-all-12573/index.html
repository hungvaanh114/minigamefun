Collect Em All

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to "Collect Em All," an engaging match-3 puzzle game that features vibrant 3D colorful balls! Your mission in this addictive game is to gather a specific number of designated balls within a limited number of moves at each level. As you progress, you'll encounter various challenges that require strategic thinking and quick decision-making.

Each level presents unique puzzles waiting to be solved, making it crucial to plan your moves wisely. If you find yourself struggling to complete a level, don't worry! You can earn additional moves by watching ads. However, it's highly recommended to conserve your limited moves to successfully clear all levels. This adds a layer of excitement and encourages players to strategize effectively to maximize their gameplay experience.

With its colorful graphics and dynamic gameplay, "Collect Em All" promises to keep you entertained for hours. Challenge yourself to think ahead and anticipate your moves while navigating through the delightful world filled with fascinating puzzles. Will you be able to collect them all and conquer each level? The adventure awaits!

How to play free Collect Em All game online

To start playing "Collect Em All," simply match three or more balls of the same color by swapping their positions. Aim to collect the required number of marked balls within the limited moves available. Use strategic moves to create combos and achieve your goals efficiently. Have fun collecting!


Instructions

Tap and hold to connect the balls with the same color
