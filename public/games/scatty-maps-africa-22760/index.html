Discover the wonders of Africa with Scatty Maps Africa, an engaging and educational game designed to enhance your knowledge of African geography. Suitable for players of all ages, this game features two distinct modes: Easy and Full Map. The Easy mode provides a simplified layout, making it perfect for younger players or those new to African geography. This mode tests the knowledge of more advanced players, pushing them to recall the finer details of Africa's geography.

How to play free Scatty Maps Africa game online

To play Scatty Maps Africa, start the game and choose your preferred mode, Easy or Full Map. Once you re in the game, use your mouse or touchscreen to drag and drop the country names onto their corresponding locations on the map.
