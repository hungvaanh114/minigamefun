Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Draw Legion, players are challenged to assemble a formidable army in the face of relentless invasion. Utilizing your creativity and strategic skills, you will draw and craft unique units to fend off enemies that threaten your kingdom. Each stroke of your pen can bring forth warriors of various abilities, allowing for countless combinations and tactics. The more you strengthen your army, the more capable you become of overcoming even the toughest adversaries.

As you progress through the game, you ll gather resources that enable you to enhance your troops and fortify your defenses. Choose wisely which units to deploy on the battlefield, as your decisions will greatly influence the outcome of each clash. Those who occupy the throne will need to showcase their leadership skills and tactical acumen to rise above their foes and establish peace in their realm.

Your journey in Draw Legion is filled with epic challenges, allowing players to immerse themselves in a captivating fantasy world where every choice matters. Become a legendary king by outsmarting your enemies and optimizing your army s strengths. With each victory, you ll carve your name in history, leaving a legacy that future generations will admire.

How to play free Draw Legion game online

To play Draw Legion, use your mouse or touch controls to draw military units directly on the battlefield. Position your drawn soldiers strategically to counter enemy waves. Tap to deploy and lead your army to victory by customizing each unit's formation and powers based on the challenges you face!
