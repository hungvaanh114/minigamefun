Super Blast

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Super Blast, where your precision and strategy will be put to the ultimate test! In this captivating game, your objective is to accurately land a mine within the designated safe zone using the fewest attempts possible. Each level presents a unique challenge, demanding sharp focus and clever use of angles to ensure your success. As you navigate through increasingly complex terrains, you will need to consider various factors that can impact your shots, including wind direction and obstacles.

Super Blast combines simplicity with a thrilling level of strategy, making it accessible to players of all ages. The colorful graphics and engaging sound effects enhance the experience, immersing you in this explosive adventure. As you progress, you will encounter exciting new levels that escalate in difficulty, keeping you on your toes. Your skills will be tested as you aim to beat your own high scores and compete against friends!

The satisfaction of perfectly landing the mine after careful calculations is unparalleled. Whether you're playing casually or aiming for the top of the leaderboard, Super Blast offers endless fun and excitement. Join in on the challenge today and see how many attempts it takes you to master each level!

How to play free Super Blast game online

To play Super Blast, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to set the angle and power of your shot. Aim for the safe area marked on the screen and tap to launch the mine. Keep track of how many attempts you take, and try to beat your best score with each level!
