CoronaVirus19

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of survival in the online game CoronaVirus19.fun, where you navigate a world turned upside down by the coronavirus outbreak. This browser-based game combines humor and strategy, allowing players to collect essential items like toilet paper while dodging the perils of infection.

In CoronaVirus19.fun, your mission is straightforward: avoid being infected while collecting valuable items. There are four distinct character roles to choose from, each with unique abilities. As a Healthy Person, you can roam the map, searching for disinfectors or bats. With a bat in hand, you can opt to become a Killer, eliminating infected players or seeking out the elusive doctor.

Your journey might take a dangerous turn if you become Infected with coronavirus. As an infected player, your speed is significantly reduced, but you can spread the virus by coughing and vomiting onto others. Alternatively, you can seek out a doctor to recover and rejoin the fight. Doctors are crucial players equipped with disinfectors to save those afflicted by the virus, making them highly sought after. Meanwhile, the Killer, armed with a bat, uses their strength to take out any infected players, adding a bit of chaotic fun to the gameplay.

As you navigate this wild world, managing your health is essential. Every action you take as an infected player costs health points, which can be replenished by collecting toilet paper. In this quirky and entertaining game, strategy, speed, and survival instincts will determine your success.

How to play free CoronaVirus19 game online

To play the free CoronaVirus19 game online, simply choose your character role and navigate through the map. Collect toilet paper to restore your health, use disinfectors as a doctor to help others, or wield a bat as a Killer to defeat the infected. Stay alert and strategize to survive!
