How to play free Forest Jump game online

To play Forest Jump, simply use the arrow keys or spacebar on your keyboard to make your character jump. Timing your jumps is crucial to avoid traps and obstacles. Aim for platforms to ascend higher and collect bonuses along the way. Good luck!
