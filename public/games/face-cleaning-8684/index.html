This is where you come in! Become the ultimate beauty guru and assist the lovely princess in overcoming her daunting acne woes. With your expertise, she will learn how to maintain clear skin and even how to apply makeup without looking messy. With each correct treatment, watch as her self-esteem grows, paving the way for her to embrace her beauty and royal responsibilities with confidence.

To play this enchanting face cleaning game, simply follow the on-screen instructions. Use tools for cleansing, applying masks, and treating acne.
