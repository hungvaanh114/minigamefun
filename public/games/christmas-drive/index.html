How to play free Christmas Drive game online

To play Christmas Drive, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen buttons to steer your car. Collect coins while avoiding obstacles on the road. Access the shop after each level to purchase new cars and upgrades. Keep driving and enjoy the festive challenge!
