Baby Hazel Birthday Surprise

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a delightful adventure in Baby Hazel Birthday Surprise! In this engaging game, Baby Hazel is in a cheerful spirit, busy showering love on her adorable pets, Katy the cat and Bunny the bunny. Just when she's having fun, she receives a reminder call on her mobile about her best friend Akito's birthday. To her surprise, she realizes she almost forgot about the special day!

Determined to make it unforgettable, Baby Hazel quickly devises a grand plan to throw Akito the ultimate surprise birthday party. To bring her vision to life, she enlists the help of her other friends. Together, they set out on a mission to prepare everything for the celebration.

Throughout the game, players will assist Baby Hazel and her friends in various fun activities to get ready for the big event. From selecting party decorations to finding the perfect gifts, your help is essential in ensuring that everything goes according to plan. As you assist them, remember to cater to their needs and keep the party preparations a secret to surprise Akito!

How to play free Baby Hazel Birthday Surprise game online

To play the Baby Hazel Birthday Surprise game, simply use your mouse to interact with the various elements on the screen. Follow the prompts to complete tasks like gathering supplies, decorating the party area, and helping Hazel s friends. Keep an eye on the needs of each character to make sure the surprise party is a success!
