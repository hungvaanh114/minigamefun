Idle Hero: Counter Terrorist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Idle Hero: Counter Terrorist, an engaging idle game that puts you in control of a squad of skilled soldiers tasked with defeating relentless enemies. Your mission is to tactically select your troops while managing your finances wisely to build a formidable force. As you embark on this thrilling adventure, you'll need to keep an eye on your resources and strategize effectively to succeed against foes that seem to have no end.

In this game, you ll face an endless loop of challenges, and the key to victory lies in choosing the right combination of soldiers, each equipped with unique abilities and strengths. Be strategic with your spending; running low on funds can jeopardize your chances of winning the ongoing battles. Whether you prefer heavy hitters or tactical sneak attacks, ensure your choices align with your overall strategy.

The gameplay is designed for continuous engagement, allowing players to progress even when they re away. Your soldiers will automatically fight enemies, but regular oversight is essential for managing upgrades and reinforcements. As you advance, you ll unlock new characters and enhance your team's capabilities, providing added layers to your approach against the progressively challenging adversaries.

How to play free Idle Hero: Counter Terrorist game online

To play Idle Hero: Counter Terrorist, start by selecting your soldiers from the available roster. Pay attention to their skill sets and cost. Use your resources wisely to engage in battles, which your troops will undertake automatically. Regular upgrades and reinforcements are vital to keep your momentum going. Strategize and adapt your team to defeat wave after wave of enemies!
