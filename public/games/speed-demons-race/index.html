Speed Demons Race

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to unleash your inner champion in Speed Demons Race, where colossal and fierce Monster Trucks take center stage! In this adrenaline-packed racing game, you'll have the opportunity to design and customize your very own Monster Truck. Challenge yourself against a lineup of formidable opponents as you tackle challenging racecourses filled with steep hills, treacherous slopes, and heart-pounding obstacles such as high metal jumps and towering containers. Only the most skilled drivers can navigate these courses successfully!

The thrill of racing doesn't stop there. In Speed Demons Race, you can perform jaw-dropping stunts to impress the audience and earn coins. Use your hard-earned coins to upgrade different aspects of your Monster Truck, enhancing its speed, power, and overall performance. The more tricks you perform and the faster you cross the finish line, the more accolades and rewards you will receive helping you rise to the top of the leaderboard.

Your goal is clear: outshine your rivals and prove you re the ultimate Monster Truck pilot. To achieve victory, you must master each track, refining your driving skills and strategy along the way. Race through dynamic environments and be prepared to break through obstacles that stand between you and the coveted first place!

How to play free Speed Demons Race game online

To start playing Speed Demons Race, choose your customized Monster Truck and select a track. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to accelerate, steer, and brake. Complete laps while performing stunts for bonus points, and aim to finish in first place to unlock upgrades and new trucks!


Instructions

The right arrow key or the pedal on the right on the screen is gas The left arrow key or the pedal on the right on the screen is the brake reverse gear The space bar or the acceleration button on the screen is turbo
