Solitaire World Tour

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

This game features an incredible 100 levels. Open 4 cards in a row to activate a combination and earn +1 extra card to your deck. Every flipped card has a 30% chance to generate a coin icon. If you open that card immediately, you win coins. Additionally, some cards start locked, adding a challenge to keep the game exciting and engaging.

Instructions

Mouse click or tap to play
