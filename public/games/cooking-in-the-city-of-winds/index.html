Cooking in the City of Winds

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting City of Winds, where the air is filled with the delightful aroma of a culinary festival that captivates every food lover. Today marks the beginning of an extraordinary event, where talented participants from all corners of the magical realm gather to showcase their culinary prowess and charm the local citizens with their exquisite creations.

As you immerse yourself in this festivity, you ll have the exciting opportunity to whip up delectable treats such as silky smooth pudding, refreshing tapioca bubble tea, and the renowned Hocus-Pocus marmalade dessert. This festival is a true paradise for those who crave sweet indulgences, as the vibrant displays and flavors will surely awaken your inner pastry chef.

However, remember that success in the City of Winds isn t solely about flavor. The visual presentation of your culinary masterpieces is equally important. Channel your creativity and cooking techniques to arrange your dishes with elegance and flair, ensuring they are as visually stunning as they are delicious. With each beautifully crafted dessert, you ll inch closer to claiming victory in this festive competition!

How to play free Cooking in the City of Winds game online

To play the game, simply select a dish to create from the festival menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to gather ingredients and mix them together. Once your dish is ready, use various decoration tools to enhance its appearance. Finally, present your culinary creation to earn points based on taste and aesthetics!


Instructions

Depending on the gaming device a computer mouse click or a simple touch on touch screens is used for control
