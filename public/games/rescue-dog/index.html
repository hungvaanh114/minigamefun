Rescue Dog

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rescue Dog is a fun and engaging puzzle game where your goal is to free the trapped dog from its cage. Each level presents a unique challenge, requiring you to carefully plan your moves to unlock the gate. Using the limited moves displayed on the screen, you must figure out the correct sequence to release the dog. As you progress, the puzzles become more complex, testing your logic and problem-solving skills. Can you rescue the dog in every level? Play now and put your puzzle-solving abilities to the test!
