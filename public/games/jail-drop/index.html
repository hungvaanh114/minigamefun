Jail Drop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Jail Drop, a thrilling physics-based puzzle game that offers 60 captivating levels designed to challenge your ingenuity and strategy! The objective is simple: remove all boxes, except for the grass, that are currently supporting the prisoner. This task requires a keen eye and thoughtful planning as you'll need to take advantage of gravity and physics to solve each level.

To eliminate boxes, simply click or tap on them to destroy them. However, beware removing the wrong block could jeopardize the prisoner s safety. You'll need to think creatively and figure out how to utilize the remaining blocks in your environment. Every move counts, and miscalculations can lead to a frustrating setback. Fortunately, the game allows you to reset each level if things don t go as planned, letting you strategize until you achieve success.

With its engaging gameplay and visually appealing graphics, Jail Drop promises hours of entertainment as you navigate through enticing puzzles. Each level presents a unique challenge that will entice both casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts alike. Experience the joy of solving mind-bending puzzles and see if you can get the prisoner safely to the grass!

How to play free Jail Drop game online

To play Jail Drop, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to click or tap on the boxes surrounding the prisoner to eliminate them. Your goal is to clear a path that allows the prisoner to safely fall onto the grass. If you make a mistake, don t hesitate to reset the level and try again!
