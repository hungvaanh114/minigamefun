My Factory

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the bustling world of My Factory, where daily production takes center stage! In this engaging simulation game, your primary task is to manufacture various products, such as light bulbs and lanterns. As you take charge of your very own factory, your first challenge is to understand the intricate processes involved in production. This includes learning about the required materials and the latest technologies that will help streamline your operations.

To thrive in My Factory, you must continuously expand your business. This means researching and developing new products while managing resources effectively. The game allows you to purchase additional trucks and vehicles, which are essential for enhancing your transport capabilities. Efficient logistics enable you to distribute your products to a wider market, thus improving your overall production efficiency and boosting your profitability.

As you progress, you will encounter various challenges and opportunities that will test your strategic thinking and business acumen. You will need to balance production output, manage your workforce, and ensure that you have adequate supplies at every stage. Thriving in this competitive environment requires quick decision-making and a keen eye for detail.

How to play free My factory game online

To start playing My Factory, simply log in to the game and familiarize yourself with the user interface. Begin by selecting the products you want to produce, gather the needed materials, and set up your production line. Manage your vehicles for transportation and keep an eye on orders to maximize your factory's efficiency!
