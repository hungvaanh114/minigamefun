Forest Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Forest Match, an exciting game where your puzzle-solving skills are put to the test! In this vibrant forest setting, you ll connect four or more identical objects to clear entire rows off the board. With each successful match, not only do items vanish but you also progress through various challenging levels.

Every level is packed with unique missions that you must complete in order to advance to the next stage of the game. As you delve deeper into the adventure, you ll encounter increasingly difficult challenges that will require your strategic thinking and quick reflexes. Don t forget to take advantage of daily bonuses, including the wheel of fortune and special missions that can bestow you with amazing rewards!

The thrill of Forest Match lies in collecting stars as you play. The more challenges you conquer, the more stars you can earn. Challenge yourself and your friends to see who can gather the most! With its enchanting graphics and engaging gameplay, Forest Match promises hours of fun. Are you ready to embark on this colorful journey and demonstrate your matching skills? Jump in and start matching today!

How to play free Forest Match game online

To play Forest Match, simply tap on the objects to connect four or more of the same type. Complete the level s missions by clearing the designated items from the board. Utilize daily bonuses and special rewards to enhance your gameplay experience for added fun!


Instructions

Connect three of the same objects in a row to make them disappear and collect points
