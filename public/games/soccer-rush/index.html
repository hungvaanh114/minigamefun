How to play free Soccer Rush game online

To play Soccer Rush, simply use your arrow keys to move your player around the field. Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and avoid being tackled by defenders. As you navigate the chaos, remember to collect power-ups that appear on the field for extra abilities. Aim for the goal to score!
