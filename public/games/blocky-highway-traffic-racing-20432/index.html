Blocky Highway: Traffic Racing

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of high-speed racing in Blocky Highway: Traffic Racing, where the excitement comes from dodging traffic, avoiding trains, and unleashing your inner racer. The game offers a vibrant atmosphere packed with gorgeous voxel art graphics, creating a colorful backdrop for your racing adventures.

As you zoom through the various worlds, you will collect coins and open prize boxes that unveil a plethora of new vehicles. With an impressive line-up of 55 different vehicles ranging from taxis to tanks, space shuttles, and even boats, every race will be a unique experience. The more you play, the more cars you can collect, striving to complete all 11 car collections.

In Blocky Highway, the adrenaline doesn t stop at just racing. You can also rattle a few traffic cars for extra points, especially during crash time! The game boasts multiple modes, including endless easy mode perfect for kids, alongside thrilling missions that keep the gameplay fresh and engaging. You can compare your scores and achievements with friends through the Game Services Leaderboards.

Explore four distinct worlds desert, snow, green, and water each packed with unique challenges. With its addictive gameplay and various achievements to unlock, you are guaranteed endless fun. Be sure to rate the game and provide feedback to help the developers at Dogbyte Games improve your racing experience.

How to play free Blocky Highway: Traffic Racing game online

To play Blocky Highway, simply use your touchscreen or keyboard to steer your car through traffic, avoiding obstacles while collecting coins. Tap or press to accelerate and brake when necessary. Keep an eye out for prize boxes and enjoy the thrill of crashing into other vehicles for bonus points!
