Donald Duck

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Donald Duck is a beloved cartoon character who made his debut in 1934, crafted by the creative minds at Walt Disney Productions. This charming anthropomorphic duck features a distinct white body, complemented by a vibrant yellow-orange bill, legs, and feet. Known for his sailor outfit, which typically consists of a sailor shirt, cap, and a bow tie, Donald is instantly recognizable around the world.

What truly sets Donald apart is his unique, semi-intelligible speech that perfectly matches his mischievous and easily provoked personality. His amusing antics and emotional outbursts make him a fan favorite among audiences of all ages. Together with his close companion, Mickey Mouse, Donald holds a special place in Disney lore, earning recognition as one of the most iconic characters in animation history. In 2002, his popularity was solidified when he appeared on TV Guide's list of the 50 greatest cartoon characters of all time.

Donald Duck's prowess in entertainment is further emphasized by his extensive filmography, making him the most featured character in Disney films. Additionally, he has achieved unparalleled success as a comic book character, being the most published one outside of the superhero genre. Over the decades, Donald has continued to win the hearts of fans, remaining a vital part of the Disney universe and pop culture.
