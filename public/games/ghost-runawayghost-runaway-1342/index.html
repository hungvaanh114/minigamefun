Welcome to the thrilling world of Ghost Runaway! In this exhilarating game, you take control of a spirited specter navigating a haunted runway. Your goal is to assist the ghost in running as fast as possible while avoiding various obstacles that appear along the path. Sounds exciting, right?

However, beware! A menacing fireball looms just behind the ghost, ready to scorch it to ashes if you linger too long. The pressure is on as you must employ your quick reflexes and strategic thinking to keep the ghost safe. The stakes are high, and timing is crucial; any hesitation could lead to a fiery end!

In your adventure, you'll encounter several power-ups scattered throughout the runway. These magical boosts are essential for increasing your speed and giving you the upper hand against the relentless fireball. Collect as many power-ups as you can to maximize the ghost s chances of survival. The faster you go, the better your chances of reaching the next stage safely.

Join the fun and immerse yourself in this spooky yet thrilling escapade. Can you guide the ghost to safety and avoid the fiery doom? Prove your skills as you race against time and unleash your inner ghost runner!

How to play free Ghost Runaway game online

To play Ghost Runaway, simply navigate your ghost character using the arrow keys or swipe gestures, depending on your device. As you move, collect power-ups to enhance your speed and avoid obstacles in your path. Keep an eye on the fireball chasing you; the faster you run, the safer the ghost will be!
