Among Us Hidden Stars

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an entertaining journey with Among Us Hidden Stars, a captivating free online skill and hidden object game. In this exhilarating experience, players are challenged to uncover hidden stars cleverly tucked away within various images. Each level presents a total of 10 stars to discover, inviting you to sharpen your observational skills and pay close attention to detail.

There are six immersive levels to explore, each more challenging than the last. As time is of the essence, you'll need to act swiftly to locate all hidden objects before the clock runs out. However, be cautious! Clicking on the wrong area multiple times will penalize you by deducting an additional 5 seconds from your remaining time. This adds an extra layer of excitement and urgency to the gameplay, keeping players engaged and eager to improve their scores.

With vibrant graphics and intuitive mechanics, Among Us Hidden Stars not only hones your cognitive abilities but also offers a delightful way to unwind and have fun. So, gather your friends and family, and see who can find the most stars with the least amount of time. Get ready to jump in and explore this hidden star adventure!

How to play free Among Us Hidden Stars game online

To play Among Us Hidden Stars, simply start the game and look at the images presented on your screen. Search for the 10 hidden stars within the time limit. Click on the stars when you find them, but avoid random clicks to prevent time deductions. Stay focused and enjoy the challenge!
