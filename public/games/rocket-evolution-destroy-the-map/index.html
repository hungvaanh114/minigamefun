Rocket Evolution Destroy the Map!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Rocket evolution - destroy the map! &amp;mdash; a casual timekiller game where your goal is to destroy the map as much as possible. The gameplay itself is an upgrade of the projectile (rocket). That is, while you are falling, controlling the rocket, you can collect various bonuses. What bonuses are there in the game: &amp;bull; +1 and -1 to the size of the rocket &amp;bull; destruction of the projectile (the main rocket gets only -5 to the size) &amp;bull; +1 rocket (be careful, this works as a buff and debuff) &amp;bull; +1 to the speed of the rocket.

Instructions

Controls on PC arrows or AD On mobile using the on-screen buttons After the first rocket reaches the ground a button appears in the form of a save icon You need to click it to save the progress and go to the match completion window
