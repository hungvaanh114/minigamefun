Mario Card Match

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Mario Card Match is a delightful and engaging online game that brings together the beloved characters from the iconic Mario franchise. Players dive into a vivid world where they can enjoy fun-filled match-making gameplay featuring Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach, and other familiar faces. Gamers are tasked with matching cards that bear illustrations of these beloved characters, combining memory skills and strategy to achieve victory.

The Mario series, created by the legendary Shigeru Miyamoto and owned by Nintendo, is the cornerstone of video gaming culture. Since its inception, Mario has evolved from a simple arcade character into a global icon, starring in over 200 games. Residing in the whimsical Mushroom Kingdom, Mario continues to embark on thrilling adventures, often alongside his trusty brother Luigi, to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of the mischief-maker Bowser. With an astounding sales record of more than 210 million units worldwide, Mario is recognized as the best-selling video game franchise of all time.

Apart from the beloved platform games, Mario has ventured into a variety of genres. The exhilarating Mario Kart series has captivated racing enthusiasts, while the Mario Tennis and Golf series offer exciting sports challenges. Role-playing gems like Super Mario RPG and the innovative Paper Mario series take players on narrative adventures, while educational titles such as Mario Is Missing! impart fun learning experiences. Mario s expansive influence has permeated various mediums, including television, films, comics, and merchandise, proving his enduring legacy in entertainment. Since 1990, the charismatic character has been brought to life by the iconic voice of Charles Martinet.

How to play free Mario Card Match game online

To play Mario Card Match online, simply click on the start button to begin. The cards will be laid out face-down; your task is to flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs. Keep track of the cards you've revealed, and use your memory to make successful matches before time runs out!
