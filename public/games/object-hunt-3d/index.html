Object Hunt 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for the ultimate game of hide and seek in Object Hunt 3D! In this thrilling experience, players must cleverly disguise themselves as various props scattered throughout the environment. Your goal is to find the perfect hiding spot, blending in seamlessly to avoid detection. If you fail to conceal yourself well, you ll risk being spotted and eliminated by the hunters!

On the other side of this intense game, players can take on the role of the catcher. As a seeker, your mission is to uncover the disguised players and eliminate them before time runs out. Use your keen observational skills and quick reflexes to identify who is a prop and who isn t. This dual gameplay ensures that both hiders and seekers will experience heart-pounding moments filled with excitement and strategy.

Object Hunt 3D is not just about stealth and speed; it s also a test of wit and cunning. Will you choose to silently wait for the right moment to strike, or will you bravely chase after your foes? The choice is yours, and every round promises new challenges and surprises. Gather your friends and enter this enchanting world where agility and ingenuity define the path to victory!

How to play free Object Hunt 3D game online

To play Object Hunt 3D, choose whether you want to hide as props or seek as the catcher. Use the movement keys to navigate your character, select a prop to disguise yourself, and find an optimal hiding spot. If you re the catcher, explore the area carefully and click to tag the hidden players before time runs out!
