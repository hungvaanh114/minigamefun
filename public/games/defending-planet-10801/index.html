How to play free Defending Planet game online

To play "Space Shooter: Defending the Planet" online, simply choose your spacecraft and start the game. Use the arrow keys or joystick to navigate your ship, and the spacebar to shoot at incoming enemies. Collect power-ups to enhance your weapons and ensure your survival as you protect your planet from hostile forces.
