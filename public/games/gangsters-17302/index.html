Gangsters

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Gangsters is an exhilarating pixelated shooting experience that immerses players in the gritty underworld of gang warfare. As a player, you take the reins of a powerful gang determined to protect its territory from opposing factions. With retro-styled graphics that charm nostalgic gamers, the action is anything but dull, featuring intense and blood-soaked gameplay.

Arm yourself with a diverse arsenal of weapons, ranging from pistols to powerful machine guns, to obliterate rival gangs that threaten your domain. The physics-based gameplay adds an extra layer of realism, making every move and shot feel impactful. You have the unique challenge of managing all three members of your gang, coordinating their actions to dominate the streets.

Getting into the action is straightforward: use the 'W' key to advance your squad, while the 'E' key allows you to swing into action, raising your members' arms to unleash a barrage of gunfire or throw punches. Strategy and precise control are essential as you strive to overcome enemy gangsters while asserting your authority as the true mafia boss.

How to play free Gangsters game online

To play Gangsters online, navigate to the game's main menu after loading. Use the 'W' key to move your gang forward and the 'E' key to attack. Coordinate team movements, selecting the appropriate weapons for each encounter, and outsmart rival gangs to claim your territory. Enjoy the pixelated chaos!


Instructions

To jump player one use W and player two use I To use the weapon player one use E and player two use O
