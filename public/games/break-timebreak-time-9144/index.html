Break Time! Experience the thrilling chaos of a satirical corporate beat 'em up where destruction reigns supreme. Dive headfirst into the office dungeon and navigate your way through a labyrinth of cubicles, water coolers, and conference rooms filled with robotic drones intent on stopping your rebellion. With a fully destructible environment, unleash your fury on the corporate aesthetic around you, turning mundane office supplies into weapons of mass hilarity.

In this alpha version of our project, players will take on the role of an overworked employee fed up with the corporate grind. Your mission? To dismantle the oppressive corporate empire from the inside out. Battle against ridiculous drones programmed to keep you in line, and ultimately confront the overlord representing the soulless corporation. The further you progress, the crazier the challenges become, complete with outrageous power-ups and hidden secrets scattered around the office lands.

With humor as its core and a vibrant art style to match, Break Time! offers a refreshing take on workplace injustices, allowing players to vent their frustrations in an exhilarating gaming experience. Gather your friends and join the fun as you strategize together to clear levels filled with comedic mayhem and action-packed scenarios. Are you ready to break free from the clutches of corporate control and secure your place as the ultimate office hero?

How to play free Break Time game online

To play Break Time, simply use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press the space bar to attack and interact with items in the environment. Collect power-ups and combo moves as you defeat drones, and aim to reach the overlord by navigating through challenges. Happy breaking!
