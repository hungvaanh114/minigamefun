Jewels Legend

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Jewels Legend, developed by LinkDesks Inc., offers an exhilarating and captivating match-3 adventure that promises to keep players hooked with its vibrant and dazzling jewelry effects. Step into a world adorned with stunning gems and embark on a thrilling journey through meticulously crafted puzzles! Whether you're at home or on the go, this jewel-themed game allows for versatile play, ensuring endless hours of entertainment.

As you navigate through challenging levels, your mission is to match three or more jewels of the same type to clear them from the board and earn points. With each completed challenge, you unlock stunning castles and advance through the rich storyline of this charming Jewel Saga. The game features a plethora of colorful, well-designed puzzles that cater to both casual gamers looking for a soothing experience and those seeking more intense gameplay.

Become the ultimate Jewel Star by mastering various strategies and utilizing special power-ups that assist in overcoming particularly difficult levels. The delightful graphics are accompanied by engaging sound effects, immersing you in a treasure-filled world where every level presents new challenges and opportunities for rewards. Inflate your skill and beat your high scores as you collect shiny jewels and explore enchanting locations.

How to play free Jewels Legend game online

To play Jewels Legend, simply swap adjacent jewels to form lines of three or more matching gems. Try to complete objectives within a given number of moves or time. Combine special gems for powerful moves and aim to score the highest possible points before advancing to the next level!


Instructions

Match 3 or more identical jewels in a single move to make a match and watch as it magically disappear Drag click or tap a jewel to move it vertically or horizontally in the direction to complete a match To earn the most points fill up the color meter
