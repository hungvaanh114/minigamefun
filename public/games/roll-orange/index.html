Roll Orange

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling adventure of Roll Orange, players will take on the role of a courageous little orange who finds himself precariously perched atop a stack of boxes and platforms. Stricken by a fear of heights, this quirky protagonist wants to make his way safely to the ground, and it's your task to assist him on this daring descent.

Your journey begins as you navigate through various levels, where you'll need to strategically remove boxes and platforms that impede the orange's path. However, as you dive deeper into the game, you must remain vigilant, as dangerous hazards like bombs and cacti threaten your progress. Touching these perilous obstacles will cost you a life; and with only five lives at your disposal, each move counts! If you happen to exhaust all your lives, the game will come to a dramatic end, challenging your skills and strategies.

As you advance through the levels, you have the opportunity to score points and earn extra lives, allowing you to continue your adventure. The exhilarating challenge of Roll Orange lies in the balance of speed and caution will you take risks to reach the ground faster, or will you play it safe and eliminate dangers first? Test your resolve and help this frightened orange find his way down! The exciting journey awaits you!

How to play free Roll Orange game online

To play Roll Orange, simply click on the boxes and platforms to remove them strategically. Use your mouse or arrow keys to guide the orange downward, avoiding traps like bombs and cacti. Keep an eye on your life count and aim to collect points for additional lives!
