Moto Pizza

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Moto Pizza, an exhilarating game that fuses speed and strategy with the delightful world of pizza delivery! In this fast-paced adventure, you take on the role of a nimble pizza delivery driver, tasked with the important mission of satisfying hungry customers who eagerly await their orders. Your objective is clear: ride your pizzeria cargo bike and make swift deliveries to ensure every pizza reaches its destination on time.

As you navigate through the vibrant streets, keep an eye out for the designated parking zones highlighted on your route. Expertly maneuver your bike to these locations, as proper parking is essential for a successful delivery. Once parked, it's time to jump into action! You'll need to hop off your bike and guide the pizza delivery character to the customer's door, ensuring the pizza is handed over promptly. The thrill of racing against the clock adds an exciting layer to every level, making each delivery a test of both skill and speed.

With various challenging levels and increasingly complex routes, Moto Pizza promises endless fun as you strive to beat your best times and unlock new bikes and upgrades. Get ready to experience the rush of being the best pizza delivery driver in town, while also honing your navigation and time management skills!

How to play free Moto Pizza game online

To play Moto Pizza, simply use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your bike through the city streets. Park in the highlighted areas to deliver pizzas efficiently. Once parked, switch to the delivery character by moving towards the customer s door, making sure to reach them before time runs out!
