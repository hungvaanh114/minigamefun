Idle Farm

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Idle Farm is an engaging casual idle farming simulation game that invites players to escape the hustle and bustle of urban life. Here, you can cultivate your very own picturesque farm where the possibilities are as abundant as your crops. Discover and unlock a multitude of plants, vegetables, fruits, and animals to cultivate, each contributing to your overall wealth, even during your offline hours.

As you expand your farming empire, you'll have the chance to sell your harvests and organic products, generating profits that allow you to enhance your farm further. With the earnings from your crops, you can invest in a variety of new seeds, including some that are truly magical! This journey to becoming the world's most prosperous farmer starts with every seed you plant and every decision you make. Remember the wise saying: when life hands you lemons, don t just make lemonade; plant those seeds and watch them flourish!

How to play free Idle Farm game online

To play Idle Farm, simply tap on the crops to harvest them and earn coins. Use your earnings to purchase new seeds and expand your farm. Set up your farm strategically, and let your crops grow even when you are not actively playing. Don t forget to check back regularly to sell your produce and buy more exciting items!
