Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chilling world of Nina The Killer: Go To Sleep My Prince, where darkness and tragedy converge in a tale of revenge and survival. Meet Nina, a young woman whose identity transformed from an ordinary Jeff The Killer fangirl into a notorious killer, fueled by the haunting memories of her past. After enduring relentless bullying alongside her brother throughout her school years, Nina's sanity shattered following a tragic incident. In a fit of rage, she took matters into her own hands and eliminated her tormentors, marking the beginning of her transformation. Her once normal skin now bears the ghostly pallor of a monstrous persona, as she bleaches her face to embody her vengeance.

This game presents two gripping story levels that allow players to experience the chilling narrative from different perspectives. You can choose to play as Poisonguy, a determined hunter on a mission to track down the elusive Nina, navigating a dark and treacherous environment filled with suspense and danger. Alternatively, step into the shoes of Nina The Killer herself. After escaping from the asylum, you'll need to utilize your cunning and agility to avoid capture while unleashing chaos in your quest for freedom. Every choice you make plays a crucial role in determining the game's outcome.

How to play free Nina The Killer: Go To Sleep My Prince game online

To play the game, simply enter the online platform where Nina The Killer is hosted. Choose your character either Poisonguy or Nina then navigate through the eerie settings using your keyboard or controller. If you are Nina, your aim is to evade capture by finding hiding spots and evading your pursuer. If you re Poisonguy, strategize and search for clues to locate Nina before time runs out. Utilize the environment to your advantage and keep an eye out for useful items!
