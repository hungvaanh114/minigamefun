Ninja Block

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the world of Ninja Block, where a lone ninja faces off against a series of seemingly impossible challenges. Navigate through a variety of exhilarating modes, including Classic, Survive, Escape, Storm, Outcast, Rampage, Deadfall, and Shuttle. New modes are on the horizon, promising even more thrilling gameplay!

In this unique game, you'll discover that there are no casualties it's all about testing your reflexes and brainpower. Armed with only one brain, two eyes, and ten fingers, can you rise to the occasion? Tasked with guiding a stickman through a hazardous environment is just the beginning. The real challenge arises when you're challenged to control anywhere from 2 to 36 stickmen simultaneously!

Ninja Block is more than just a game; it s a brain-teaser that pits your coordination and speed against relentless obstacles. As you progress, you will find yourself dodging and leaping over various objects, all while racing against the clock. The ultimate question is: can you survive for just 10 seconds? You'll need quick thinking and precise movements to outpace your challenges.

How to play free Ninja Block game online

In Ninja Block, use your mouse or keyboard to control the stickmen. Your mission is to jump over obstacles and avoid pitfalls. Pay attention to the rhythm of the game, and be prepared for rapid movements. The more stickmen you can manage without crashing, the better your score!
