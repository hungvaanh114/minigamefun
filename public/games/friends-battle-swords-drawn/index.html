Friends Battle Swords Drawn

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling third episode of the Friends Battle series, prepare for an intense showdown in "Friends Battle: Swords Drawn." As the battle begins and swords are unsheathed, players must be ready for action. Gather your courage and sharpen your blades, for only the fiercest warriors will triumph.

Team up with your trusty companion as you embark on a quest to capture the flag. The goal is simple but challenging: whoever secures the flag the most times and holds it for 20 seconds is crowned the victor. However, it's not just about taking the flag; you must also protect your own. Strategically set up your flag while simultaneously attempting to reach your opponent's location.

In this fast-paced game, using your sword effectively is crucial. Engage in epic battles where you must best your friend in combat to seize their flag. Be prepared to dodge, weave, and strike, as each clash will test your reflexes and strategic thinking. Can you outsmart and outmaneuver your adversary to emerge as the ultimate flag-capturing champion? Compete fiercely and rise above the rest in this thrilling adventure!

How to play free Friends Battle Swords Drawn game online

To play this game, start by choosing your character and joining a session. Use your mouse or keyboard controls to move around the battlefield. When you see the enemy's flag, engage in combat to defeat your opponent and capture their flag. Remember to defend your flag while keeping an eye on the timer!


Instructions

The red player moves using the WASD keys The blue player moves using the arrow keys Stay at the flag for 20 seconds You can press S and DOWN ARROW to kill the enemy
