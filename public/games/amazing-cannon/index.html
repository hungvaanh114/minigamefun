Amazing Cannon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling game of Amazing Cannon, players engage with an innovative cannon that launches vibrant balls into the air. However, the primary focus isn't simply on firing the colorful projectiles but rather on skillfully directing them to fill a designated container situated at a distance from your cannon. The challenge lies in the fact that simply shooting won't guarantee accuracy; players must carefully manipulate various devices positioned on the field to alter the trajectory of the balls.

The game offers an abundance of shells, intentionally designed to exceed the container's requirement for filling. This excess ensures that even if a few balls veer off course, players still have a viable chance to achieve their goal. The ultimate objective is to reset the value displayed on the container in Amazing Cannon successfully, leading to the completion of each level. Each stage presents its unique set of challenges, requiring strategy and precision to master the art of aiming, bouncing, and redirecting the balls effectively. Can you master the art of ball trajectory and conquer all the levels?

How to play free Amazing Cannon game online

To start playing Amazing Cannon, simply position your cannon and aim towards the target container. Use the devices on the field to adjust the balls' directions after firing. Keep shooting until the container's value is reset, ensuring you account for any potential misses in your strategy!
