1010 Christmas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the festive spirit with 1010 Christmas, an engaging puzzle game designed to challenge your strategic thinking. Your objective is simple yet captivating: collect all the Stars scattered across the board. To do so, you'll need to strategically fill entire rows or columns containing these enticing Stars.

To engage with the gameplay, you will utilize the block sets available on the left panel. Carefully select and drop these blocks onto the board to create complete lines. Each time you successfully fill a row or column, the Stars that reside in that line will be collected, bringing you closer to your goal. However, keep in mind that space is limited! You can continue to place blocks as long as there s room to maneuver. The game ends when there are no available placements for the blocks.

As you immerse yourself in this delightful Christmas-themed game, you'll enjoy the festive aesthetic while honing your puzzle-solving skills. The more Stars you collect, the higher your score becomes, allowing you to climb the leaderboard and compete with friends and family. It s a perfect blend of fun and challenge to celebrate the holiday season through captivating gameplay.

How to play free 1010 Christmas game online

To play 1010 Christmas, simply select block sets from the left panel and drop them onto the board. Aim to fill rows or columns containing Stars. Keep placing blocks until the board fills up, and try to collect all the Stars before you run out of space!
