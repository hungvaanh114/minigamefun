Maya

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Maya, a colorful and captivating bubble shooter game that combines strategy and delightful artistry. This game stands out with its charming visual style, making each level a feast for the eyes as you navigate through vibrant landscapes filled with bubbles of various colors.

In Maya, you will use the directional keys to move the emitter left and right, allowing you to aim precisely where you want to launch your bubbles. The space bar is your launch mechanism. As you fire bubbles at the clusters above, your goal is to connect three or more bubbles of the same color. When successful, these bubbles will magically burst, disappearing from the screen and scoring you points.

The satisfying sound of bubbles popping and the joyous visual effects create an engaging experience that keeps players returning for more. Each level presents new challenges and obstacles, requiring you to think critically about your moves to maximize your score and progress through the game. Whether you're playing for a quick break or a longer session, Maya offers an enjoyable and relaxing way to test your skills.

How to play free Maya game online

To play Maya, use the left and right arrow keys to adjust the bubble emitter's position. Press the space bar to shoot the bubble towards the clusters. Aim to hit three or more bubbles of the same color to eliminate them and earn points. Challenge yourself to complete each level!
