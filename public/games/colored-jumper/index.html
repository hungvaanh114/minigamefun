Colored Jumper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Prepare yourself for the exhilarating challenge of Colored Jumper, where sharp reflexes and quick thinking are essential! In this dynamic game, your main task is to manipulate a bouncing ball that drops onto various platforms below. You'll need to click on the screen to initiate the ball's descent, while a moving array of platforms shifts beneath it, each waiting to catch the ball. The catch? The platforms must match the color of the ball; otherwise, your game will come to a halt as the ball plummets into the abyss.

As you progress, you'll find yourself able to switch the colors of the platforms by engaging with the three colored levers positioned at the bottom of the screen. By clicking on these round keys, you can change the hues of the platforms in real-time. This feature adds an engaging twist, as you'll need to think strategically about when to alter the colors to ensure the ball continues to bounce safely. The goal is to help your ball navigate through a colorful world and maximize your points along the way. Each successful jump rewards you with points, and your highest score will be recorded within the game, allowing you to compete with your past performances.

How to play free Colored Jumper game online

To play Colored Jumper, simply click on the screen to drop the ball. Watch closely as it bounces off the colored platforms below. Use the three colored levers to change platform colors when necessary, ensuring that the ball always lands on matching colors to keep the game alive and continue scoring!
