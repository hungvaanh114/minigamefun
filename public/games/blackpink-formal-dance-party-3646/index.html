Blackpink Formal Dance Party

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to join the glamour and excitement of the Blackpink Formal Dance Party! The fabulous members of BLACKPINK Jennie, Lisa, Jisoo, and Rose are all set to celebrate their high school graduation. This is a special night filled with beautiful memories, and you have the ultimate chance to make it unforgettable by helping each member look their absolute best for their senior night.

In this interactive game, you ll step into the high-energy world of K-pop fashion and style. Your mission is to select elegant and stylish dresses for each member, featuring light and flowing skirts that capture modern trends infused with a touch of classic elegance. Think of dreamy colors, sparkling embellishments, and fashion-forward designs that will make them shine on the big night.

But that's not all! The fun doesn t stop at choosing outfits. You will also need to pamper the girls by styling their hair and applying the perfect makeup that complements their exquisite dresses. Mix and match hairstyles and choose vibrant makeup shades that capture the essence of each member's personality. It's your creative touch that will transform them into stunning prom queens!

Join the celebration and showcase your fashion skills with the Blackpink Formal Dance Party, available at dressupwho.com. Have a blast as you assist BLACKPINK in making magical memories on this monumental night!

How to play free Blackpink Formal Dance Party game online

To play the Blackpink Formal Dance Party, simply enter the game interface where you'll find each BLACKPINK member waiting for your styling expertise. Click on the dress icons to browse through a variety of outfits and accessories, and drag your selections onto each member to dress them up. Once you're satisfied with their looks, choose hair and makeup styles for a complete transformation!
