Celebrity Foodie Style

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Celebrity Foodie Style, where culinary delights meet high-fashion! Step into the shoes of fashionable celebrities who are equally passionate about their favorite foods. These glamorous girls are on a quest to explore the latest trends in both cuisine and style, and they need your help to create the perfect foodie fashion statements.

In this thrilling fashion adventure, you will have the opportunity to browse an exquisite wardrobe filled with chic outfits designed to pair perfectly with a variety of luscious dishes. Your mission is to select a unique costume for each celebrity, reflecting not only their personal taste but also the flavors they represent. From casual brunch looks to elegant dinner attire, flexibility and creativity are key as you match their outfits with the food trends of the moment.

Don t forget to accessorize! Accessories can make or break an outfit, so ensure each girl is styled to perfection with stunning jewelry, trendy bags, and elegant shoes. Once dressed, prepare them to flaunt their amazing looks on a virtual runway or at gourmet events, showcasing the perfect blend of food and fashion.

How to play free Celebrity Foodie Style game online

To play the Celebrity Foodie Style game, simply select a celebrity girl and dive into her wardrobe. Browse the outfits and food styles, then drag and drop clothing options onto her. Don t forget to choose complementary accessories to complete each look! Once you're satisfied, showcase their stunning foodie fashion on the runway.
