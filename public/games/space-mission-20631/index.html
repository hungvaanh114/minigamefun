Space Mission

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exhilarating journey through the infinite reaches of space with Space Mission! This captivating game melds the charm of retro graphics with the cutting-edge capabilities of HTML5 technology. Whether you choose to embark on a solo quest or team up with a friend, Space Mission promises thrilling gameplay across multiple modes. Select from the engaging Campaign mode, challenge the CPU, or go head-to-head with another player in the Player vs Player mode.

While battling against the computer, you have the option to customize your environment by selecting either a SPACE setting or a challenging Maze Map. Add more excitement to your gameplay by choosing between the Survival or Death Match game modes, each offering unique challenges to test your skills and strategy. Keep your spaceship safe from the relentless Space Trap guns while collecting an array of power-ups and upgraded weaponry to enhance your capabilities. Don't forget to keep an eye on the mini-map located in the game bar, which will help you navigate your surroundings efficiently.

How to play free Space Mission game online

To dive into Space Mission, simply select your desired game mode at the start. Utilize the arrow keys or WASD controls for movement, and deploy your weapons with the designated keys. Pay attention to your surroundings indicated on the mini-map, dodge traps, and collect power-ups to boost your performance. Enjoy the adventure!
