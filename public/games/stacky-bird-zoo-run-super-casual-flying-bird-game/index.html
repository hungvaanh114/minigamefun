Stacky Bird Zoo Run: Super Casual Flying Bird Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Stacky Bird Zoo Run, the ultimate casual flying game where your feathered friend embarks on an adventurous journey through vibrant landscapes! In this exciting game, your goal is to build a towering stack of eggs to glide over obstacles while dodging cunning monsters and sharp spikes. Each level presents a new set of challenges, inviting you to create long chains of eggs and fly gracefully toward whimsical birdhouses.

As you traverse the colorful levels, aim for three perfect landings to activate Shooty Mode, propelling you into higher challenges. Master the art of stacking the right number of eggs to ensure a smooth flight and reach the finish line as the top bird on the leaderboard. With each successful run, collect coins that allow you to unlock a medley of charming characters like chickens, cats, and pigs, so you can find the perfect companion for your airborne escapades!

Stacky Bird Zoo Run is easy to pick up yet challenging to master, providing hours of delightful gameplay. Explore new lands, gather treasures, and climb to the top by competing for daily, weekly, or all-time high scores. Enjoy limited-time live events and mini-games, adding an extra layer of excitement to your flights. Don t miss the chance to discover secret characters and cozy birdhouses for your collection!

How to play free Stacky Bird Zoo Run: Super casual flying bird game online

To play Stacky Bird Zoo Run, simply tap the screen to make your bird fly and swipe to stack eggs. Keep an eye on obstacles, and release your eggs at just the right moment to float over them. Collect coins as you soar through levels, and remember to aim for those perfect landings to unlock special modes!
