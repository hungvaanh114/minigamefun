Duo Apple Monsters

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Duo Apple Monsters invites players into a delightful and vibrant adventure as they embark on a quest with their favorite fruity heroes Red Apple and Green Apple. Nestled in the enchanting realm of Appleton, this game offers a rich backdrop filled with whimsical landscapes and perilous obstacles that await brave adventurers. Tasked with collecting apples and restoring harmony to their beloved land, the duo must utilize their unique abilities to navigate the various challenges.

As the story unfolds, players are introduced to the menacing evil sorcerer, Morula, who threatens to engulf Appleton in an eternal darkness. With 13 thrilling levels, players will traverse through lush orchards, treacherous terrains, and fantastical locales as Red and Green Apple band together to thwart Morula's sinister plans. Each level presents a fresh array of challenges that not only test players' skills but also reward them with the satisfaction of triumph as they gather apples and unlock new paths.

With captivating graphics and engaging gameplay, Duo Apple Monsters combines the thrill of adventure with strategic thinking. Players will experience a journey filled with merriment, teamwork, and heroism as they help Red and Green Apple take back control of their world, culminating in an epic showdown against Morula to restore peace and color to Appleton.

How to play free Duo Apple Monsters game online

To play Duo Apple Monsters, simply use the arrow keys to move Red and Green Apple through the levels. Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and collect apples scattered throughout each level. Remember to work together switch between the two characters to solve puzzles and overcome challenges!


Instructions

The green male character moves with the arrow keys while the character in cherry color is moved with the WASD keys to collect apples
