Connect Vehicles

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Connect Vehicles is a captivating puzzle game that draws inspiration from the beloved Chinese tile removal game, Mahjong. The objective is simple yet addictive: eliminate all the tiles that are displayed on the board. To do this, players must connect identical tiles, working strategically to make sure that every connection is formed using no more than two turns.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter 15 challenging levels that test your puzzle-solving skills and quick thinking. Each level presents a unique configuration of tiles, requiring you to think critically about your moves and plan ahead to clear the board effectively. Complete a level within the designated time limit to earn extra bonuses, adding to the challenge and excitement.

With its engaging gameplay and satisfying mechanics, Connect Vehicles is sure to keep you entertained as you strive to eliminate all the tiles and reach higher levels. The blend of strategy and time management ensures that every decision counts, making it a delightful experience for players of all ages.

How to play free Connect Vehicles game online

To play Connect Vehicles, start by selecting a tile on the board. Look for another identical tile that is accessible within two turns. Click on the first tile, then the second to make the connection and eliminate them. Keep an eye on the timer to complete each level successfully for extra rewards!
