Corona Lines

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Corona Lines, where your mission is to conquer a menacing virus that continues to expand its reach. Your objective is to rotate the core and uncover chains of thorns that share the same color. Quickly destroy these chains before the virus successfully engulfs the entire Corona. Strategy and quick thinking are essential, as you ll need to explore adjacent cells in every direction to identify potential matches.

As the clock ticks, the pressure mounts. Each move is time-restricted, pushing you to think fast to thwart the virus from spreading uncontrollably. With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Corona Lines presents a thrilling challenge that combines action and puzzle-solving skills. Clear out as many chains as possible to prevent the virus from dominating and claim victory with your quick reflexes. Get ready to engage in an electrifying battle against time and strategy!

How to play free Corona Lines game online

To dive into the action of Corona Lines, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to rotate the core and line up matching thorn chains. Explore each cell around you to find adjacent colors and click to destroy them. Stay alert, and make swift decisions to keep the virus at bay!
