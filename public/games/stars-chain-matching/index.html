Stars Chain Matching

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on a vibrant journey with Stars Chain Matching, an engaging puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and quick reflexes. In this exciting game, your goal is to connect matching star blocks using either your touchscreen device or your mouse. Each star block has a specific color, and it s your task to create groups of three or more identical blocks.

To successfully connect the stars, you can link them horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. When you successfully form a group of three or more adjacent stars, their color changes to blue, signaling your success in the match. The captivating graphics and smooth gameplay provide an enjoyable experience as you aim to turn all the blocks blue across various levels.

Stars Chain Matching features a total of 24 levels, each designed to test your puzzle-solving abilities. As you progress, the challenge increases, presenting you with more complex arrangements of stars that require keen observation and planning to conquer. With each completed level, you gain a sense of accomplishment while honing your skills. The immersive sound effects and vibrant visuals keep you engaged as you strive to complete the game and become a champion of star connections!

How to play free Stars Chain Matching game online

To play Stars Chain Matching, simply use your touchscreen or mouse to tap and connect adjacent stars of the same color. Aim to create groups of three or more to turn them blue. Your goal is to make all star blocks blue within each level to progress through the game!
