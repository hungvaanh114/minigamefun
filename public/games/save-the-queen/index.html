Save the Queen

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of adventure with Save The Queen, one of the most beloved rescue puzzle games globally. In this captivating game, you step into the shoes of Olaf, an intrepid hero on a mission to rescue a princess held captive in a perilous situation. Your journey is not only about courage but also about solving intricate puzzles and navigating through treacherous terrain.

As you progress through the game, each level presents unique challenges that require strategic thinking and creativity. By clicking on the screen, you can pull out the iron bar that will automatically advance your quest. However, you must remain on high alert! The path is fraught with dangers, including menacing beasts that lurk in every corner. Use your cunning to devise mechanisms that will eliminate these threats and ensure your rescue plan stays on track. Every decision could mean the difference between success and failure, so think carefully!

In essence, Save The Queen is not just about rescuing the princess; it s about using your wits to navigate a series of cleverly designed challenges while keeping your protagonist safe. This exciting combination of action and problem-solving will keep you engaged and entertained for hours on end!

How to play free Save The Queen game online

To play Save The Queen, simply click on the screen to control Olaf's actions. Pull out the iron bar to move forward, and be sure to strategize when encountering beasts. Use the mechanisms wisely to defeat enemies and ensure the princess's safe rescue!
