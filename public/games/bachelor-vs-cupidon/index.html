Bachelor vs Cupidon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Bachelor VS Cupidon, where love is in the air, but not necessarily for our carefree bachelor! Embracing his single life, our protagonist thinks he has it all figured out until Cupid enters the scene, armed with arrows of love and a mission to pair him up. Are you ready to dive into an epic showdown between the bachelor and the mischievous little Cupids? Your support for the bachelor is crucial as he battles against these flying love-struck adversaries!

In this action-packed game, you ll take on the role of the bachelor, skillfully dodging Cupid's love arrows while launching socks at those pesky Cupids. Use the A and D keys on your keyboard to move left and right across the screen, avoiding any incoming projectiles. Your mouse will serve as your weapon, allowing you to aim and shoot at those mischievous Cupids trying to ruin your single status. With each successful hit, watch as refreshing beers rain down from the sky these are your lifelines in this love-infested arena!

Your ultimate challenge is to survive the onslaught of cupids and avoid the shackles of marriage for as long as possible. Keep an eye out for those falling beers to replenish your lives, as getting hit by arrows means losing them. Are you up for this hilarious adventure? Test your skills and see if you can outlast Cupid and remain happily single!

How to play free Bachelor VS Cupidon game online

Once you enter the game, use the A and D keys to navigate left and right. Aim your mouse to target the Cupids and click to throw socks at them. Collect falling beers for extra lives while evading Cupid's arrows. Stay alive and fend off love for as long as you can!
