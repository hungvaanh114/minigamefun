Modern Sudoku

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Modern Sudoku offers a fresh twist on the classic puzzle game with a sleek design and 20 unique boxes instead of the traditional 9x9 grid. Enjoy a vibrant gradient background and modern input fields as you complete the puzzle by filling in the missing numbers. This fast-paced, visually engaging game encourages players to test their logic and problem-solving skills. Featuring a win screen for successful completion and a draw screen for retrying, Modern Sudoku keeps you hooked with its challenging gameplay. Perfect for both casual players and Sudoku enthusiasts, Modern Sudoku delivers a modernized, fun puzzle experience for all. Play Now!

Instructions

Click or tap on any empty box to select it Enter a number between 1 and 9 by typing on the keyboard or selecting it from the on-screen keypad if available Once all the boxes are filled the game will automatically check if the puzzle is correct
