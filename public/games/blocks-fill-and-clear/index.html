Blocks: Fill and Clear

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the world of strategic puzzles with our exciting game! Fill the board with random blocks, collect lines and clear the space to score as many points as possible. Inspired by the classic Tetris, the game offers unique modes: Classic: test yourself in endless mode. Timed: manage to score maximum points in a limited time. With bombs: defuse the bomb blocks before they explode. With unique shapes: try to make lines of non-standard shapes. Mixed: a challenge for the most experienced players!

Instructions

Fill the field with blocks You are given random blocks that need to be placed on the playing field Drag them with the mouse or your finger Collect lines Place blocks to fill horizontal or vertical lines
