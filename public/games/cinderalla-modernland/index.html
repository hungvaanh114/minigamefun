Cinderalla Modernland

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Cinderella Modernland, a delightful makeover game where you can help our beloved princess rediscover her enchanting beauty! In this whimsical adventure, Cinderella faces an unexpected challenge when her magical carriage unexpectedly turns back into a pumpkin, leaving her a little distressed and slightly injured. Your mission is to assist her in recovering and looking fabulous once again!

Begin by tending to Cinderella's wounds: use your magical skills to cleanse and heal her cuts. With the flick of your unicorn wand, you can also transform her torn dress back into its elegant form. Once Cinderella is all patched up, take her to a relaxing spa session where she can rejuvenate. Pamper her with facials, soothing baths, and delightful beauty treatments to lift her spirits and prepare her for the adventures that lie ahead.

After her spa day, it s time for the real fun! Explore a variety of stunning outfits and accessories to dress Cinderella in modern styles that reflect her unique personality. Match her outfits with glamorous hairstyles and chic shoes, ensuring she looks stunning for her royal engagements. The goal is to help Cinderella shine like the princess she is, blending modern fashion with her timeless charm!

How to play free Cinderalla Modernland game online

To play Cinderella Modernland, simply use your mouse to navigate through the game features. Start by cleaning Cinderella's wounds, then wield the unicorn wand to fix her dress. Afterward, guide her through the spa treatments and select fabulous outfits for her. Click and drag to style her until she looks perfect!
