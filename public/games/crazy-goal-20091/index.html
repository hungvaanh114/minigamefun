Crazy Goal

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Crazy Goal, an exhilarating and fast-paced soccer game where your shooting skills are put to the ultimate test! In this game, your mission is simple: shoot the ball and score goals at each stage. To make progress, you need to score five goals before advancing to the next level. The challenge escalates as you must shoot the ball from increasingly distant points, making each goal more hard-earned than the last.

As you navigate through the game, you'll encounter dynamic opponents who will attempt to block your shots. Not only do you need to focus on scoring goals, but you also have the opportunity to take down these rivals for extra points. Keep an eye out for the goalkeeper; hitting them can grant you additional rewards, but be strategic to ensure you dodge the moving adversaries! Crazy Goal is easy to pick up and play, but mastering the art of shooting accurately from various distances will take practice.

Utilizing a unique swipe mechanism, players can predict the ball's trajectory, providing an intuitive way to control their shots. As you compete against others, aim to reach the farthest point possible to secure your victory. With each level, the excitement and challenges grow are you up for the task of becoming the ultimate Crazy Goal champion?

How to play free Crazy Goal game online

To play Crazy Goal, simply swipe your finger on the screen to aim and shoot the ball towards the goal. Keep an eye on your opponents and the goalkeeper while making your shots. Score five goals to advance to the next level, and use strategic shots to take down rival players for additional points!
