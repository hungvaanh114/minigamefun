Baby Hazel Puppy Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you a puppy enthusiast? If so, then you re sure to adore playing Baby Hazel Puppy Care! In this delightful game, you will join Baby Hazel as she showers her cuddly puppy, Bruno, with love and attention. Today is a special day as Hazel has decided to pamper her pet with a scrumptious treat and create a cozy kennel for him.

Your adventure begins with helping Hazel bathe Bruno. It's time for some bubbly fun! After that, you will get the chance to serve Bruno his favorite food, ensuring that he feels cherished and satisfied. But the day doesn t stop there! Hazel has a trip planned to the pet shop where you will assist her in picking out all the necessary supplies to build a fantastic doghouse.

Once you have gathered everything, it s time to unleash your creativity. You will help Hazel construct and decorate a delightful home for Bruno. With your guidance, Hazel will transform the supplies into a beautiful kennel that Bruno will love. This engaging game will not only entertain you with its adorable visuals but will also teach you the joys of caring for pets.

How to play free Baby Hazel Puppy Care game online

To play the Baby Hazel Puppy Care game, simply use your mouse to navigate through various tasks. Click on the objects needed for each activity, such as bathing, feeding, and building. Follow on-screen prompts to complete each step and help Baby Hazel make the day special for Bruno!
