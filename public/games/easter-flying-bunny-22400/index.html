Get ready for a delightful adventure with our latest kids game, Easter Flying Bunny! This vibrant and engaging game invites players to assist the adorable Easter bunny as it takes to the skies to collect colorful Easter eggs. Bounce through whimsical landscapes, avoid pesky obstacles, and gather as many eggs as you can to achieve the highest score possible. Whether you re a seasoned gamer or new to the scene, the controls are simple and the graphics are bright, making it perfect for children of all ages. Have a blast while you hop into this fun-filled Easter celebration!

In Easter Flying Bunny, the excitement doesn t stop at merely collecting eggs. You'll have the chance to unlock various power-ups and boosters that can enhance your flying capabilities. Make strategic decisions about when to use these aids to maximize your score. The more eggs you collect, the more fun you ll have as you race against the clock. Challenge yourself and your friends to see who can achieve the highest score and be crowned the ultimate egg collector!

How to play free Easter Flying Bunny game online

To play Easter Flying Bunny, simply use your mouse or arrow keys to steer the bunny as it flies through the air. Tap or click to make the bunny jump higher and collect as many Easter eggs as you can. Avoid obstacles, and aim for power-ups to boost your score!
