Ricosan

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Ricosan is an enchanting 2D platformer that invites players into a delightful world filled with charming graphics and engaging gameplay. In this whimsical adventure, your primary goal is to collect all the pineapples scattered throughout the levels while skillfully dodging various hazards such as pesky enemies, perilous spikes, and menacing ground saws. Each level presents a fresh challenge, designed to test your agility and strategic thinking as you navigate through the vibrant environments.

The game features a total of 8 thoughtfully crafted levels, and as you progress, the difficulty ramps up, ensuring that you remain engaged and challenged as you strive to collect every pineapple and reach the Exit door. The colorful visuals and cute character designs make Ricosan not only a test of skill but also a visual treat that captivates players of all ages.

Your journey through Ricosan will be filled with surprises and obstacles, requiring you to master the controls and develop quick reflexes. Jump, run, and explore intricate pathways while keeping an eye out for hidden secrets and collectibles. With each level completed, you ll find a greater sense of accomplishment that will keep you coming back for more pineapples!

How to play free Ricosan game online

To play Ricosan, simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate your character through the levels. Press the jump key to leap over enemies and obstacles, and collect all the pineapples you encounter. Reach the Exit door in each level to advance to the next one. Enjoy the challenge!
