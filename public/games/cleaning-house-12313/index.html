Cleaning House

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Cleaning House is an adorable and engaging cleaning simulation game that redefines the way you perceive chores. Immerse yourself in an enjoyable experience where tidying up becomes a source of fun rather than a mundane task. This game is perfect for anyone who enjoys organizing items and sprucing up spaces.

Begin your cleaning journey in the bedroom, where your first task is to declutter the closet. Sort through clothing items and put them in their designated places to create a serene space. You'll also need to clean the windows for a sparkling view and pick up any stray garbage lying on the ground. With each accomplishment, you'll feel a sense of satisfaction as you transform the room.

Once you've completed the bedroom, you can easily transition to other parts of the house. Whether you're in the mood to tidy up the kitchen or refresh the living room, just slide the page up or down to navigate to your favorite area. Each room presents new challenges and opportunities to put your cleaning skills to the test, ensuring that the fun never stops!

Curious to dive deeper into the delightful mechanics of Cleaning House? Experience the joy of cleaning like never before and see how satisfying it can be.

How to play free Cleaning House game online

To play Cleaning House, simply use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with items in the rooms. Drag and drop clothes to their proper places, swipe to clean windows, and pick up trash to tidy the space. Explore each room by sliding your screen up or down to switch between different areas of the house.
