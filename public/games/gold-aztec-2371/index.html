Gold Aztec

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Gold Aztec where your puzzle-solving skills will be put to the ultimate test. Set against the backdrop of an ancient stone door adorned with a grid of 16 interlocking blocks, the game invites you to align colorful crystals and rings in a challenge of strategy and foresight.

Each block is decorated with vibrant crystals and matching rings, forming a symphony of colors that beckons for organization. The thrilling twist? Each colored crystal must find its match within the corresponding color of the ring. With a delicate balance at play, the number of crystals per color is perfectly mirrored by the rings, adding layers of complexity to your task.

To navigate this visual conundrum, simply tap the center section of any four blocks. In a dynamic twist, the selected crystals will spin clockwise, shifting their positions in a seamless flow. Your goal is to cleverly manipulate these shifts until every crystal is perfectly aligned within its color-coded ring. As the clock ticks, think strategically about each maneuver to avoid unraveling your well-laid plans. Patience and careful consideration of each move will lead you toward victory in this enchanting journey through the Aztec realm.

How to play free Gold Aztec game online

To play Gold Aztec, click on the center of any four blocks to rotate the crystals clockwise. Your goal is to match each crystal with its corresponding colored ring. Keep clicking and strategizing until all crystals are in their rightful places. Enjoy the challenge and have fun!


Instructions
