Drunken Duel 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for the wild antics of Drunken Duel 2, the highly anticipated sequel that brings a whole new level of fun and mayhem! This time, our lovable stickman characters have taken their crazy battles to new heights literally as they face off on rooftops. With an exciting array of new challenges, players must navigate precarious platforms while engaging in hilarious drunken duels. The game introduces an arsenal of unique weapons, each requiring different skills that will test your reflexes and strategy in the heat of battle.

As you delve into the chaotic world of Drunken Duel 2, you'll discover that the stakes are higher than ever. Not only can you challenge your opponent from rooftop to rooftop, but you also have the chance to take down a hovering helicopter! Unleash your aim to shoot it and send it crashing toward your rival, adding even more chaos to the fight. The objective is straightforward yet exhilarating: knock your opponent off the roof a total of five times to claim victory. The fun never stops as you engage in frantic, laughter-inducing showdowns with friends or AI characters.

Gather your skills, keep your balance, and let the drunken mayhem unfold in Drunken Duel 2, where every match is filled with unpredictable moments and endless entertainment!

How to play free Drunken Duel 2 game online

To play Drunken Duel 2, use the arrow keys to move your character left and right. Press the spacebar to jump and aim your weapon. Strike your opponent with your weapon to knock them off the rooftop while avoiding their attacks. The first player to send their opponent tumbling off five times wins!
