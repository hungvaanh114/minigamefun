Flappy Helicopter 2 Player

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you ready for an exhilarating two-player helicopter showdown? In Flappy Helicopter 2 Player, the stakes are high as you battle against your friend to see who can dominate the skies. The objective is simple: detonate as many helicopters as possible to outscore your opponent. Engage in thrilling aerial combat, where each explosion counts towards your victory!

The game is not just about your shooting skills; it requires precision in flying as well. Players need to be cautious, as flying too high can lead to crashing either into the ground or the sky, resulting in your helicopter exploding. Each player has a limit: explode 10 times, and you are the loser of this fierce duel. Will you be able to navigate the challenges and defeat your friend, or will you be the one to face defeat? It's time to find out!

Challenge your friend to an epic duel and see who has the skills to reign supreme in the helicopter battle. Gather your reflexes, prepare your strategies, and take to the skies in a game where only the most agile and tactical players will survive. This highly competitive game promises endless fun and a test of your flying and shooting capabilities!

How to play free Flappy Helicopter 2 Player game online

To get started, choose your helicopter and the player controls before the match begins. Use the designated keys to maneuver your helicopter through obstacles, while firing to shoot down your opponent's helicopters. Keep an eye on your count of explosions avoid crashing, as precision is key to securing your victory!


Instructions

To use the red helicopter press the W key To use the blue helicopter press the Up Arrow Key The first to reach 10 points wins
