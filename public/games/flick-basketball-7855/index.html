Flick Basketball

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of Flick Basketball, an engaging and fast-paced game that puts your reflexes to the test! In this exciting arcade-style challenge, basketballs will rain down from above, and it's your duty to catch each one. The more you catch, the higher your score. However, be cautious: every missed basketball costs you a heart, and you only have five hearts to start with. Losing all your hearts means game over, so stay sharp and keep your eyes on the prize!

With each level, the intensity ramps up, and the basketballs fall faster and in greater quantities. Flick and swipe your finger or cursor to catch the balls with precision. Your agility and timing will be crucial as you try to maintain your score while dodging those unexpected misses. Whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive player, Flick Basketball offers a fun challenge for everyone. The vibrant graphics and realistic sound effects enhance the immersive atmosphere, making it even more addictive. So gather your concentration, and see how many basketballs you can catch before your hearts run out!

How to play free Flick Basketball game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse or finger to tap on the falling basketballs to catch them. Aim to catch as many as you can while avoiding misses. Keep an eye on your heart count, and try to maximize your score with quick reflexes and precise movements. Enjoy the game!
