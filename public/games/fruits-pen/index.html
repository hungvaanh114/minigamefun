Fruits Pen

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the vibrant world of Fruits Pen, a thrilling game that encapsulates the essence of fun and precision! In this exciting arcade-style game, your objective is to skillfully toss a pen into a variety of fruits, such as pineapples and apples. The challenge lies in accurately hitting the perfect center to maximize your score! Each successful hit brings a surge of joy, especially when you manage to hit the center two times in succession. The delightful animations and cheerful sound effects add to the overall enjoyment of the game, making every throw feel rewarding.

The simplicity of the mechanics makes Fruits Pen incredibly accessible. However, don t let that fool you; true mastery requires practice and a steady hand. Invite your friends to join in on the fun and engage in friendly competition to determine who will emerge as the ultimate Fruits Pen master. With a captivating premise and charming graphics, this game is bound to keep you entertained for hours as you strive to perfect your throwing skills.

Whether you're looking for a casual gaming experience or a competitive challenge, Fruits Pen delivers excitement and joy in equal measure. So pick up your pen, aim carefully, and let the fruity fun begin!

How to play free Fruits Pen game online

To play the game, you will need to aim your pen towards the selected fruit. Click or tap the screen to throw the pen. Target the center for maximum points, and try to achieve consecutive hits. Compete against your friends to see who can score the highest and prove their pen-throwing prowess!
