GMTK-Game-Jam

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

GMTK-Game-Jam presents an imaginative and humorous experience through the unique puzzle game "Horse Divorce." This short yet captivating title invites players into a surreal world where two halves of a horse symbolically represent the complexities of love and relationships. As players take control of these two disjointed halves, they embark on a quirky adventure filled with challenges and obstacles that test their cooperation and understanding of each other.

The gameplay focuses on collaboration, as players must navigate their elongated horse through various levels filled with puzzling scenarios. Each puzzle requires players to think critically about how to reconcile the differences between the two horse halves, emphasizing themes of love, breakup, and unity. The whimsical art style and playful sound design further enhance the surreal atmosphere, making the gameplay not only a test of wit but a delightful experience for both casual and seasoned players alike.

Horse Divorce cleverly uses its mechanics to symbolize the dynamics of relationships, presenting a refreshing twist on typical puzzle games. As players manage the contrasting movements and capabilities of each half, they are challenged to work harmoniously to overcome the hurdles that lie ahead, leading to a deeper understanding of empathy and cooperation. The short format keeps the gameplay engaging and ensures that every moment is filled with quirky encounters and playful surprises.

How to play free GMTK-Game-Jam game online

To play Horse Divorce, use the arrow keys to control one half of the horse and the WASD keys for the other. Work together to move past obstacles and solve puzzles, ensuring both halves coordinate their movements to succeed in navigating through the surreal landscape.
