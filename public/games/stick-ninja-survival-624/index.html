Stick Ninja Survival

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Stick Ninja Survival immerses players in an exhilarating rogue-like arcade experience set in a haunting 2D world filled with stickman characters and formidable monsters. As the ominous Red Sun rises and the sky shrouds in a blood-red moon, terror spreads across the land, forcing people to flee from menacing creatures that lurk in the shadows.

In this dynamic game, you take on the role of a fearless stick ninja, navigating through a chaotic environment where dodging enemy attacks and evading their relentless bullets is essential for survival. One of the standout features of Stick Ninja Survival is its automatic attack mechanism, allowing you to focus solely on avoiding danger and strategizing your next move. As you battle through waves of monsters, you will collect valuable gem resources that can be used to enhance your character s abilities, skills, and talents, empowering you to face increasingly challenging foes.

Each playthrough offers a mix of excitement and unpredictability, making Stick Ninja Survival an addictive option for fans of the arcade genre. Master your dodging and learn the intricacies of your character's powers to become a legendary stick ninja. Gather gems, upgrade strategically, and show the monsters who s the boss in this thrilling survival quest!

How to play free Stick Ninja Survival game online

To begin playing Stick Ninja Survival, simply use the arrow keys to navigate your stick character through the game screen. Focus on dodging incoming attacks and obstacles while enjoying the automatic fighting mechanism that takes care of your offensive moves. Collect gem resources along the way to upgrade your skills and enhance your gameplay experience!
