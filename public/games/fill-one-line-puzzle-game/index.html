How to play free Fill One Line Puzzle game online

To play Fill One Line Puzzle, simply select an empty space and click to fill it. Continue connecting spaces with a single line, ensuring that no previous lines are crossed or overlapped. Use your logic to figure out how to fill each gap without breaking the path until you solve the level!
