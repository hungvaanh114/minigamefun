Angry Heroes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Are you nostalgic for the frantic fun of angry birds? Look no further! The thrilling escapade of Angry Heroes is back, inviting you to engage in spectacular slingshot action. Your mission is to topple your adversaries concealed within a towering fortress by launching furious birds at them. With an impressive collection of 20 distinct chapters, each escalation presents increased difficulty that will certainly keep you on your toes.

In this exciting game, strategy and calculation are crucial elements of your gameplay. As you navigate through the challenging levels, each bird represents a chance to analyze how many enemy monsters you can bring down in a single shot. Set your angry bird in the slingshot and prepare to unleash your wrath. Pull back, aim carefully, and once you've locked onto your target, release and watch as your fiery avian projectile takes flight!

Every section promises to freeze time in an exhilarating battle against your foes as you unleash your fury. We re confident that you ll breeze through the levels while enjoying every moment. Don't miss out on the colorful chaos as you engage with these endearing, yet fierce, birds. Click now to dive into an adventure brimming with laughter and excitement let's ignite the fun together!

How to play free Angry Heroes game online

To play Angry Heroes, simply aim your slingshot using the on-screen controls. Select your bird and pull it back to adjust your shot's angle and power. Release the bird to launch it toward your enemies! Keep track of your score and see how many foes you can defeat with each throw. Enjoy the adventure!
