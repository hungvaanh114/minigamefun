Subway Surfers World Tour: Berlin 2021 marks the thrilling 123rd edition in the beloved Subway Surfers series, showcasing the vibrant and dynamic city of Berlin as its captivating backdrop. Players will dive into a world filled with colorful graffiti, iconic landmarks, and energetic music as they skate through the bustling subway lines. The Berlin edition offers new challenges, characters, and exciting missions that keep the gameplay fresh and engaging.

As surfers, players must evade capture from the determined inspector and his mischievous dog, all while dodging trains and collecting coins. This edition also introduces special characters and hoverboards that celebrate the culture and spirit of Berlin, making it an even more exhilarating adventure.
