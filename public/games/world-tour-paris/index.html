World Tour Paris

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for an exhilarating adventure as you plunge into the vibrant world of Subway Surfers World Tour: Paris! This exciting update invites players to explore the enchanting architecture and stunning beaches that Paris has to offer. As you dash through the colorful streets, you'll be mesmerized by the city's unique landmarks and the lively atmosphere that surrounds them.

Join forces with Sofia, a captivating character who is set to take your gaming experience to new heights. Unlock her dazzling Tango outfit to add flair to your character and boost your style as you navigate the bustling urban landscape. The thrill of the chase awaits, so hop on the cool Roto board and breeze through the streets, evading obstacles and collecting coins along the way.

But that s not all! Keep your eyes peeled for shiny horseshoes scattered throughout the city as they hold the key to winning exciting Weekly Hunt prizes. These collectibles will enhance your gameplay and provide opportunities to earn fantastic rewards that will enrich your Subway Surfers experience. Are you ready to conquer the streets of Paris and claim your prizes?

How to play free World Tour Paris game online

To play the World Tour Paris game, simply swipe your finger to navigate your character. Avoid obstacles by moving left and right, and leap over hurdles by tapping the screen. Collect coins and shiny horseshoes as you run along the tracks, aiming to score the highest points while evading the pursuing train. Enjoy the adventure!
