Girl Beauty Salon

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the enchanting world of the Girl Beauty Salon, join three lovely friends Diana, Flora, and May who are gearing up for an exciting party tonight! They have decided to visit a fabulous beauty salon first to prep themselves and truly shine at the event. Each girl has a unique makeover plan to get a fresh and stunning look.

Diana is focused on revitalizing her hands with a fabulous nail art makeover, aiming for a look that will steal the spotlight. May, on the other hand, wants to pamper her legs and make them look fabulous and smooth, ready to showcase with confidence. Meanwhile, Flora dreams of achieving smooth and glowing back skin that will make her radiant from every angle. As you help these princesses prepare, you'll have the opportunity to select the most fashionable outfits to complete their transformations.

Your choices will determine how pretty and charming they appear at the party, ensuring they turn heads as they arrive. Dive into this fun-filled experience of creativity and style, and make sure to have a blast giving these girls the makeovers they desire!

How to play free Girl Beauty Salon game online

To play the Girl Beauty Salon game, simply start by selecting your favorite princess. Follow the prompts to apply different beauty treatments to their hands, legs, and back. After that, choose fashionable clothing and accessories to complete their look. Enjoy the transformation process and get them ready for the party!
