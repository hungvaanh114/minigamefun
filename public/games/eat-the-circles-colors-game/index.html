How to play free Eat the Circles: Colors Game online

To play the game, use your mouse or finger to guide your circle around the screen. Consume the small dots to grow larger, while carefully dodging the bigger dots and stars, which can hinder your progress. Keep moving and strategizing to achieve the biggest circle possible!
