White House Escape

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

White House Escape is an engaging point-and-click escape game crafted by the talented developers at 8B Games. In this thrilling adventure, players find themselves trapped inside the prestigious and enigmatic White House. Your mission is to search the surroundings for helpful items and intriguing clues that unveil various symbols. These elements will aid you in solving intricate puzzles that will ultimately lead to your escape from this iconic residence.

As you navigate through the intricacies of the game, you ll need to be observant and resourceful. Each room holds secrets and hints that are vital to your quest for freedom. Use your wit to combine items creatively, unlock doors, and uncover all that the White House has to offer. The blend of realistic design and thought-provoking challenges will keep players entertained as they unravel the mysteries hidden within these historical walls. Good luck, and enjoy the journey to escape!

How to play free White House Escape game online

To play White House Escape, navigate your mouse to explore different areas of the room. Click on objects to interact with them, gathering useful items and clues. Use the inventory feature to combine items or solve puzzles. Keep your eyes peeled for hidden objects that could help you unlock new rooms and ultimately find your way out!
