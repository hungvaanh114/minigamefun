MonkeyMart Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Monkey Mart is an entertaining management game that invites you to dive into the bustling world of grocery shopping alongside adorable monkeys. These playful creatures are in dire need of a mini market to cater to their shopping needs, and it's up to you to make that happen! Begin your journey by cultivating the beloved fruit of the monkeys bananas. These ripe, delicious treats will become the star attraction of your store.

As the game progresses, you'll need to gather the ripened bananas and arrange them beautifully on your counter to entice your hungry customers. But that s not all! To take your store to the next level, raise chickens with the bananas you have grown. Collect their eggs and display them for sale alongside your bananas. With multiple food items up for grabs, you'll also have the opportunity to sell banana puree, canned goods, flour, bread, and milk. Each sale brings in coins that you can reinvest to expand your store and enhance the shopping experience for your monkey clientele.

Get ready for the ultimate shopping adventure where you ll experience the joys of managing your own mini grocery store. With delightful graphics and engaging gameplay, Monkey Mart offers endless entertainment as you navigate the busy aisles and serve your furry friends. Are you ready to become the best grocery manager in the jungle? Join in on the fun and open your mini mart today!

How to play free MonkeyMart Game online

To start playing MonkeyMart, simply use the mouse to interact with the game objects. Grow bananas by tapping on the fruit trees, collect and organize items like eggs and canned foods, and manage your inventory effectively to serve your monkey customers. Enjoy providing them with their favorite snacks!
