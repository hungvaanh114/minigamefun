Battalion Commander 1917?Battalion Commander 1917

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the shoes of a courageous soldier during World War I in Battalion Commander 1917. Faced with daunting orders, you're tasked with executing a high-stakes mission that involves traversing dangerous enemy territory to take out a colossal enemy war machine. The fate of 1,600 of your fellow soldiers rests in your hands.

As you navigate through treacherous lands, your goal is not only to destroy the massive enemy machine but also to liberate comrades who have been captured. By rescuing these brave souls, you amplify your forces, turning your mission into a more formidable operation. Every successful venture allows you to collect gold, which can be utilized for acquiring powerful upgrades, such as a heavy tank that enhances your combat capabilities.

Prepare yourself for intense action and strategic planning as you embark on this perilous journey. How you manage your resources and team will determine the outcome of your mission and the lives of your comrades. Embrace this thrilling challenge and prove your mettle as a soldier in the tumultuous times of World War I.

How to play free Battalion Commander 1917 game online

To start playing Battalion Commander 1917, navigate your soldier using the arrow keys or WASD for movement. Engage enemies using your space bar or designated attack button. Collect gold and free prisoners as you progress through levels, using the acquired resources to purchase upgrades for your missions. Good luck!
