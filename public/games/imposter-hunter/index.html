Imposter Hunter

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the world of Imposter Hunter, where danger lurks within the ranks of astronauts. With everyone hidden behind their suits, you must exercise caution as you navigate the battlefield. Your mission is clear: uncover the imposters that threaten your journey. Only the strongest will survive in this thrilling arena.

As you engage in intense battles, your primary objective is to seek out and eliminate the imposters before they can jeopardize your voyage to Jupiter. Every decision counts, and each interaction is a matter of life and death. To become the last astronaut standing, you must rely on your instincts and skills to defeat these cunning foes.

In Imposter Hunter, operations are straightforward. Simply drag your finger to move your astronaut and release to launch an attack on imposters within striking distance. As you progress, you'll be able to unlock unique skins and weapons to customize your character, enhancing your chances of survival.

The game offers an exciting variety of modes, including the challenging Zombie Crowd City, where you can upgrade your skills for enhanced fighting capabilities. In Battle Arena survival mode, you'll face dangerous imposters that test your mettle as you unlock new zones filled with challenges. Are you ready to prove your worth?

How to play free Imposter Hunter game online

To play Imposter Hunter, simply drag your cursor or finger on the screen to maneuver your astronaut. Release to attack with precision against the imposters lurking nearby. Keep an eye on your surroundings, upgrade your skills, and unlock new zones to enhance your gameplay experience. Survive and conquer!
