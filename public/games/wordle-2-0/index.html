Wordle 2.0

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Wordle 2.0 is a word-based puzzle browser game that acts as an enhanced spin-off of the original NYT-Wordle game. It allows players to play in multiple languages, with word lengths from 3 to 5 letters, and with optional hints to help them make better guesses. Perfect for all players, from beginners to experts, Wordle 2.0 is going to be your ultimate challenge for vocabulary!
