Sweet Shapes

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful universe of Sweet Shapes, where vibrant candies cascade from the heavens! In this enchanting game, your main goal is to match and pop two or more shapes of identical color to conquer each level. With every sweet pop, you'll find yourself immersed in a world of colorful candies, offering a visually stimulating challenge.

As you journey through various levels, you'll encounter adorable pets that have become trapped amidst the sugary shapes. It's your mission to lead these pets to safety by clearing the sweets above them. Each level is uniquely crafted to present obstacles that will test your matching skills and strategic thinking.

Prepare to explore a collection of intricate challenges that progressively increase in difficulty. Experience the thrill of completing each stage, unlocking new features, and discovering hidden surprises as you advance. Whether you're a casual player or a matching game enthusiast, Sweet Shapes promises hours of engaging fun as you work your way to rescue all the pets and collect astonishing rewards!

How to play free Sweet Shapes game online

To play Sweet Shapes, simply tap on two or more adjacent candies of the same color to pop them. Aim to clear paths to rescue the trapped pets below. Strategically plan your moves to create bigger combos and achieve higher scores. Enjoy the sweet adventure!
