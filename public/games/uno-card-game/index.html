UNO Card Game

UNO Friends is an exhilarating twist on the timeless classic UNO card game, renowned for its simplicity and engaging gameplay. This vibrant and colorful card game is perfect for gatherings or casual play with friends and family, making it an ideal choice for players of all ages. The rules are straightforward, allowing newcomers to quickly get in on the action, yet there s enough strategic depth to keep seasoned players on their toes.

The primary objective of UNO Friends is to be the first player to reach 500 points, which you score by being the first to get rid of all your cards in each round. Players take turns matching a card from their hand with the top card on the deck, whether by number or color. Special action cards introduce exciting twists, such as skipping opponents' turns, reversing play direction, or forcing everyone to draw more cards. This dynamic gameplay ensures that no two games are alike, making it thrilling and unpredictable.

How to play free UNO Card Game game online

To play UNO online, simply join a game room or create your own with friends. Each player is dealt seven cards, and the first player chooses a card to match from their hand. Continue playing by following the color or number of the top card in the discard pile until someone runs out of cards!
