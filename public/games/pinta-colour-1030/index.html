Pinta Colour

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Pinta Colour is an engaging digital coloring app that allows users to hand-color a variety of unique illustrations on their mobile devices, tablets, or computers. With a diverse collection of drawings featuring dogs, cats, birds, vehicles, numbers, and more, this app promises endless hours of creativity and fun.

Users can customize their coloring experience by selecting the thickness of the pen and exploring a vibrant color palette through an intuitive color picker. This feature lets you choose your favorite shades with ease, making every artwork uniquely yours. The innovative magnifying glass tool enables you to zoom in on intricate parts of the drawings, ensuring you can color even the smallest details. Once you've made your artistic choices, simply click the magnifying glass again to return to the original view.

When you're satisfied with your masterpiece, Pinta Colour allows you to download your artwork effortlessly. With just a click of the down arrow, you can save your creation directly to your device, preserving your hard-earned work for future enjoyment. Whether you re looking to unwind or explore your artistic side, Pinta Colour is a delightful way to unleash your creativity.

How to play free Pinta Colour game online

To play Pinta Colour, simply select a drawing from the available options. Once you've chosen your image, adjust the pen thickness and pick your colors. Use the magnifying glass to zoom in on details and start coloring. Once you're finished, download your artwork by clicking the down arrow.
