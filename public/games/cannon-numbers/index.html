Cannon Numbers

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Cannon Numbers, an exhilarating ball-shooting game that puts your skills to the test! In this engaging idle game, your task is to swipe the cannon expertly and prevent bouncing balls from touching it. As you blast through the waves of colorful balls, you'll have the opportunity to upgrade your cannon, aiming for new high scores and taking your gameplay to the next level. Become the ultimate ball blast master as you enhance your skills and earn excitement with every shot.

The captivating visuals and delightful sound effects combined with addictive gameplay create a soothing experience, perfect for unwinding after a hectic day. Cannon Numbers transports you into a world of fun and relaxation, allowing you to forget the stresses of daily life. Dive into the action, and you'll find yourself lost in the gameplay, aiming to achieve a higher score and become a true ball blaster!

As you progress in the game, you can unlock various bonuses that will further assist you in your quest to blast balls and enhance your cannon. Don't let the balls touch your cannon; keep your reflexes sharp and master the art of aiming. Whether you are a casual player or a competitive gamer, Cannon Numbers will keep you entertained for hours!

How to play free Cannon Numbers game online

To play Cannon Numbers, simply swipe the cannon to aim and shoot at the approaching balls. Your goal is to prevent them from touching your cannon while maximizing your score. Upgrade your cannon by collecting bonuses and aim for the highest score possible, all while enjoying the soothing visuals and effects!
