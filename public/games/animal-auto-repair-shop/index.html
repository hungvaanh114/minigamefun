Animal Auto Repair Shop

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Animal Auto Repair Shop, where you take on the role of the finest mechanic in town! In this whimsical auto repair shop, you will encounter a variety of animal customers, each with their own unique vehicle troubles. Your mission is to diagnose and fix the broken parts of their cars, ensuring they leave with a smile and a properly functioning vehicle.

Today, you have three frantic customers eagerly waiting for your expert assistance. Each animal is filled with anxiety, as their cars have failed them at the most inconvenient moments. Your job is to assess their problems and apply your superior mechanical prowess to restore their vehicles to pristine condition. From changing tires to fixing engines, no challenge is too great for you in this delightful repair shop.

Once the repairs are complete, you can take your skills a step further by cleaning and redecorating the cars. A thorough wash and a stylish makeover can significantly increase your customers' satisfaction. As you impress your clients with your detail-oriented approach, your shop's reputation will soar, inviting even more customers to rely on your remarkable services.

How to play free Animal Auto Repair Shop game online

To play the Animal Auto Repair Shop game, simply navigate through the various tools and parts available in your workshop. Use your mouse to select and interact with items, and follow the on-screen prompts to complete each repair. Don't forget to clean and customize the cars as well to keep your customers happy!
