Stickman Attack

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the chaotic world of Stickman Attack, where you find yourself in a battle against relentless waves of stickman enemies. These stick figures charge at you, eager to exploit your first mistake and put an end to your reign as the chosen stick hero. With no clear reason behind your selection, all you can do is unleash your inner martial artist and fend off these comical foes with an array of kung fu moves.

While you may question why these stickmen continue to rush towards you, they seem oblivious to the pain awaiting them as you aim your punches at their most vulnerable spots. Your objective is to tap the white circle on your screen to obliterate these annoying adversaries and collect the souls they drop upon defeat. These souls serve as your currency, allowing you to upgrade your arsenal and equip yourself with various weapons to enhance your combat abilities.

As you progress through the game, prepare yourself for increasingly difficult waves filled with more stickman enemies. Each round tests your reflexes and strategic planning, pushing you to discover the best techniques to survive the onslaught. By demonstrating your skills in combat, you may find yourself as the ultimate stickman champion, proving that even the simplest of heroes can overcome overwhelming odds.

How to play free Stickman Attack game online

To play Stickman Attack, simply start the game and tap the white circle whenever enemies appear. Each tap will strike down a stickman, earning you souls. Use the souls you collect to purchase new weapons and abilities, enhancing your gameplay and increasing your chances of survival against the stickman hordes.
