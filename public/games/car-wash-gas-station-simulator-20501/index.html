Step into the exciting world of the Car Wash & Gas Station Simulator, where you get to manage three essential tasks: driving, refueling vehicles, and washing and parking cars in designated spots. This immersive game delivers an exceptional driving and parking experience, packed with numerous challenging missions that will put your skills to the test.

Enjoy fluid controls and carefully crafted gameplay that ensures smooth navigation through each task. The vibrant and beautiful environment enhances your gaming experience, making every session a delight. Whether you re maneuvering through tight spaces or trying to master the art of fuel refilling, you will find yourself engrossed in the captivating mechanics of this simulator.

This simplicity allows both seasoned gamers and newcomers to swiftly grasp the gameplay and jump straight into the action. With a variety of cars and scenarios, each mission keeps you engaged and challenged. Are you ready to take on the role of a car wash and gas station expert?

How to play free Car Wash & Gas Station Simulator game online

To start playing, use the WASD or arrow keys for driving your vehicle. Keep an eye on the map for designated washing and fuel refilling stations. When you've reached these locations, use the mouse to begin the washing process or to refuel the car. Complete each task to advance through the game!
