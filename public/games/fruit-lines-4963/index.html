Fruit Lines

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fruit Lines, a vibrant and engaging puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and foresight. Your objective is to move the colorful items around the board to create a horizontal or vertical line consisting of 5 identical pieces. It's simple yet exciting! To move an item, just tap on it, followed by tapping on the blank tile where you want it to go. Watch as the item travels to its new home, but remember, there must be an uninterrupted path for the move to be successful.

Every time you attempt a move that doesn t result in a match, three new items will populate the board. This mechanic adds a delicious layer of complexity and urgency keep an eye on the board as too many mismatched items can lead to a cluttered playing field. If the board fills completely, it s game over! Strategically planning each move will help you avoid this situation and keep the fun rolling.

With its easy-to-learn mechanics yet challenging gameplay, Fruit Lines is perfect for players of all ages who love a good brain teaser. Can you keep the board manageable while striving for those sweet combos? Dive in, sharpen your skills, and see how many points you can rack up in this juicy puzzle delight!

How to play free Fruit Lines game online

To enjoy Fruit Lines, simply tap the item you wish to move and then tap on the blank space where you want it to go. Ensure there s a clear path for the item to travel. Remember, if your move doesn t form a line of five, three new items will appear on the board. Keep playing until you can no longer make a move!
