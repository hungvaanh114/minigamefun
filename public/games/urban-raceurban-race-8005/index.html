Get ready to hit the streets in Urban Race, a thrilling driving adventure that tests your skills and reflexes! As you take control of your car, you'll need to navigate through a bustling urban environment filled with various obstacles. Avoid collisions with cars, street signs, and other hazards as you race down the pavement. The longer you survive, the more cash you can accumulate, allowing you to upgrade your experience!

In Urban Race, not only will you be dodging obstacles, but you'll also need to steer clear of the police. Their sirens will ring loud as you make your escape, adding to the excitement and urgency of the game. Every distance you cover means more coins collected, which can be used to enhance your fleet of vehicles and select faster, better cars for your next adventure.

But beware! Your fuel is limited, and running out of gas will lead to a game-over scenario. Make sure to keep an eye on your fuel gauge, planning your strategies carefully to ensure you keep racing for as long as possible. With each successful run, you ll unlock new challenges and opportunities for thrilling gameplay.

How to play free Urban Race game online

To play Urban Race, simply use your keyboard's arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car. Navigate through the streets, dodging obstacles and police cars while collecting coins. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge, and make strategic moves to gather cash for unlocking new vehicles. Enjoy the race!
