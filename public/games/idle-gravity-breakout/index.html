IDLE: Gravity Breakout

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to IDLE: Gravity Breakout, a captivating blend of idle mechanics and strategic gameplay where your primary objective is to obliterate the brown balls that serve as the source of gravity in the game. With each click, you can directly destroy these gravity centers, unleashing a satisfying chain of effects. But don t stop there expand your arsenal by purchasing a variety of different colored balls that will join the fight on your behalf. Each one possesses unique designs and abilities that enhance your overall destruction power.

As you progress, merging your balls becomes a key strategy to amplify their strength. Combine two or more of the same type to create a more powerful version that can deal greater damage to the pesky brown gravity balls. You ll feel a heightened sense of achievement as your enemies are eliminated more efficiently, paving the way for you to earn resources for upgrades.

Upgrades are essential in IDLE: Gravity Breakout. Invest in them wisely to equip your balls with new abilities that can significantly change the dynamics of your gameplay. This system allows for deep strategic decisions as you balance between clicking to destroy and letting your balls fight automatically, all while you watch the chaos unfold.

How to play free IDLE: Gravity Breakout game online

To play IDLE: Gravity Breakout, start by clicking on the brown balls to eliminate them. Collect resources and use them to purchase additional balls that will autonomously attack. Merge your existing balls to enhance their strength and buy upgrades to unlock new abilities. Continuously strategize to optimize your destruction efforts!


Instructions

- Click the brown enemies to deal damage - Earn money to purchase new balls to fight by your side - Earn gems to help you via upgrading your balls
