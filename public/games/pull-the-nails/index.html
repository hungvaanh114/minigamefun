Pull the Nails

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into a realm of captivating visuals and inventive challenges with the game "Pull the Nails." This unique gaming experience puts your problem-solving skills to the test as you navigate through an array of complex levels designed to engage both your mind and reflexes. In this competitive setup, it's not just about personal achievement; you can also invite friends to join the fun and see who can master the art of pin removal more effectively. The mechanics are simple yet deeply satisfying, encouraging players to think creatively as they interact with the unconventional laws of physics.

Your primary goal is to guide various balls into a designated pipe by strategically removing pins that could either obstruct or facilitate their path. Each level presents its own set of challenges, and the action ramps up in difficulty as you progress, putting your abilities to their ultimate test. As you pull the pins, you ll need to be mindful of gravitational forces and anticipate how each move impacts the trajectory of the balls. Are you innovative enough to figure out the optimal way to solve each puzzle? Or will the challenging designs have you scratching your head? Dive in and find out!

How to play free Pull the nails game online

To play Pull the Nails, start by clicking on the pins you see on the screen. Remove them in the right sequence to ensure all balls make their way into the pipe. Use your intellect and timing to navigate through each level successfully. Keep track of how the balls react when the pins are pulled; that s key to mastering the game!
