Quantities

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Imagine a delightful game designed specifically for preschoolers that transforms the learning of numbers and fundamental math skills into a fun experience. Introducing Kids Numbers and Math! This engaging game allows children to enhance their numerical abilities while collecting puzzle pieces for exciting Bug Collection Puzzles.

In its paid version, parents can customize the number ranges, enabling their children to work with numbers as high as 20. This flexibility speeds up the learning process and keeps children motivated. Did you know we also provide a comprehensive reading curriculum? It includes Kids ABC Letters, Kids ABC Trains, Kids ABC Phonics, and Kids Reading. Your child will thoroughly enjoy the immersive experience offered by Kids Numbers and Math, all while you can relax, satisfied that your child is not just having fun but also acquiring valuable skills.

How to play free Quantities game online

To start playing the game, simply select a number range appropriate for your child's skill level. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete various math-related activities. As your child solves problems, they will collect puzzle pieces that can be used to build their Bug Collection Puzzles. Enjoy learning along with fun challenges!
