Embark on an exhilarating journey in Hungry Squirrel, the action-packed game where you play as a charmingly hungry squirrel! This thrilling game is available for free on your computer or any device, allowing you to dive into an adventure anytime, anywhere. As a player, you'll navigate through vibrant landscapes, frantically collecting nuts while maneuvering through the trees.

In Hungry Squirrel, speed is your ally. The faster you move, the more nuts you can gather, which ultimately boosts your score. Keep a keen eye on the map and leap and glide through the treetops. But beware flock of troublesome birds are lurking, and they won't make your journey easy. Stay sharp, as obstacles will appear, testing your reflexes and agility. The further you progress, the quicker your squirrel becomes, turning this arcade game into a delightful race for survival!

As winter fades and spring arrives, the squirrels are ready for adventure. Grab your nuts, avoid the pesky yetis, and strive for the highest score possible. Open your picnic basket and prepare for an action-packed quest that promises endless fun!

How to play free Hungry Squirrel game online

To play Hungry Squirrel, use the arrow keys to navigate your squirrel around the screen. Press the space bar to jump and glide through obstacles while collecting nuts. Keep moving fast to increase your speed, and aim for high scores while avoiding birds and other challenges!
