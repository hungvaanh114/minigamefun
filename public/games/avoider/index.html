AVOIDER

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to AVOIDER, a thrilling and engaging arcade experience designed to test your reflexes and strategy! In this action-packed game, your main objective is to dodge an array of falling objects, including boxes, balls, and even menacing monsters. As you navigate through the chaos, you'll need to stay vigilant and make quick decisions to avoid getting hit. The challenge escalates with the addition of lasers, which can catch you off guard if you re not paying attention!

Your journey will be filled with sparkling gems scattered throughout the game. Collecting these gems not only boosts your score but can also unlock exciting features and power-ups, making your gameplay even more dynamic. Utilize your jumping ability to escape dangerous obstacles that come crashing down from both sides and directly overhead. Each level becomes progressively more difficult, keeping you on your toes and encouraging you to improve your performance. Try to survive for as long as possible while maximizing your gem collection!

How to play free AVOIDER game online

To play AVOIDER, use the arrow keys or touchscreen controls to move left and right. Jump over obstacles by pressing the jump button or the spacebar. Your goal is to avoid all falling objects while collecting gems for points. Stay alert and react quickly to navigate through the chaos!
