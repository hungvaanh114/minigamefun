Witch Flight

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling adventure of Witch Flight, take on the role of a daring witch soaring through the darkened sky on her trusty broomstick. Set against the enchanting backdrop of the night sky shimmering above the vast ocean, players will encounter an array of whimsical yet hazardous obstacles. As you navigate your flight, be prepared to dodge mischievous ghosts, buoyant flying pumpkins, and an assortment of other surprises that lurk in the shadows.

The objective is simple yet challenging: avoid crashing into any obstacles as you glide through the air. Each moment in Witch Flight is filled with excitement, as your reflexes will be put to the ultimate test. The longer you fly without encountering an obstacle, the more exhilarating the experience becomes. Will you manage to keep the witch airborne, soaring gracefully over the ocean, or will you succumb to the perils of the nighttime sky?

This endless game offers players an opportunity to immerse themselves in a captivating world where skillful navigation is the key to achieving high scores. With vibrant visuals and enchanting soundscapes, Witch Flight promises to keep you engaged and entertained for hours on end.

How to play free Witch Flight game online

To play Witch Flight, simply use your mouse or touch controls to steer the witch's broom up or down as she flies through the night sky. Tap or click to make her ascend, and release to descend. Remember to avoid all obstacles to prevent crashing into the ocean!
