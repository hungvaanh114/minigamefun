Welcome to "BasketMan: Joker In City," a thrilling adventure where you take on the role of a heroic character tasked with defeating the infamous Joker. Armed with your trusty bat, you will face off against various enemies across a total of five action-packed levels. Each level presents unique challenges, culminating in an intense boss battle in level five.

As you progress through the game, you ll encounter increasingly difficult adversaries designed to test your skills and reflexes. Your goal is to navigate through the chaotic cityscape, using your bat to outmaneuver and defeat the foes that stand in your way. However, the ultimate test arises in level six. Here, only the most seasoned players can prevail as you return to face the same challenges as level five, but without the protective shield that assists you in dodging attacks. You ll need to rely solely on your quick thinking and agility to dodge enemy strikes.

In addition to defeating enemies, you will need to collect three special black Batman items scattered throughout the game. These collectibles will boost your abilities and help you in your quest against the Joker. The stakes are high, and only the bravest players can conquer this urban battleground and emerge victoriously!

How to play free BasketMan: Joker In City game online

To play BasketMan: Joker In City, simply use your arrow keys to move your character around and the spacebar to swing your bat. Dodge incoming attacks and collect black Batman items as you progress through the levels. Defeat enemies and prepare for the challenging boss in level five, and remember, level six is for legends only!
