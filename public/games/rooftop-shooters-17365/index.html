Rooftop Shooters is an exhilarating action-packed shooting game where your skills and precision are put to the ultimate test. The objective is simple yet exhilarating: knock your opponent off the rooftop using a variety of weapons. Designed to keep players on their toes, this game provides an adrenaline rush as you dodge bullets and strategize your attack from above. With vibrant graphics and fast-paced gameplay, Rooftop Shooters promises to keep you entertained for hours.

Experience the thrill of competition in three exciting game modes. How to play free Rooftop Shooters game online

To play Rooftop Shooters, simply choose your game mode online multiplayer, local two-player, or practice. Use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D to move your character around the rooftop. Aim with your mouse and click to fire your weapon at your opponent. The goal is to push them off the edge while dodging incoming fire!
