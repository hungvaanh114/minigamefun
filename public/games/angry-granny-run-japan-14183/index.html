Angry Granny Run: Japan

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join the zany adventure of Angry Granny Run: Japan, the latest installment in the popular Angry Gran Run series of 3D running games! In this thrilling episode, our feisty grandmother finds herself in Japan, ready to unleash her wild side in a quest for chaos and excitement.

Travel through vibrant streets and stunning landscapes as you guide Granny on her mission to wreak havoc. Encounter various characters and obstacles such as punks and iconic Japanese landmarks. Use swift reflexes to jump over traditional elements like torii gates and dodge the numerous obstacles that lay in her path. The more coins you collect, the happier Granny will be, fueling her enthusiasm for future escapades!

Your primary goal is to survive as long as possible while navigating through bustling cityscapes and playful challenges. The faster you run and the more daring your moves, the more thrilling the experience becomes. With each run, you ll be challenged to improve your skills and scores, adding an extra layer of excitement and competition. Are you ready to take on the challenge and see how far Granny can go?

How to play free Angry Granny Run: Japan game online

To play Angry Granny Run: Japan, simply use the on-screen controls to navigate Granny through the bustling streets. Tap to jump over obstacles and swipe to change directions. Collect coins and power-ups along the way to boost your score. Try to outrun your competitors and make Granny as happy as possible!
