Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Sweet Home Cleanup: The Princess House Cleanup Girls Game! Immerse yourself in a delightful world where you take on the thrilling role of a cleanup champion. In this vibrant and exciting game, you'll join an enchanting crew of princesses dedicated to turning their lovely residence into a spotless paradise. Tackle a variety of cleaning challenges that would test your organizing skills while transforming each room into a sparkling beauty.

As you navigate through the levels, engage in fun mini-games that require you to search for hidden items, pick up messes, and make essential repairs. From the grand living room filled with charming decor to the kitchen brimming with magical cooking tools, every corner of the princess house is full of surprises. These interactive adventures not only scale up the entertainment but also encourage creative problem-solving skills as you strive to restore the household to its former glory.

Utilize your unique sense of style as you rearrange furniture, select decorations, and personalize spaces to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Don't forget to earn points and unlock exciting rewards as you progress. Gather your friends and family to see who can achieve the highest score and become the ultimate cleanup champion!

How to play free Sweet Home Cleaning: game online

To play Sweet Home Cleaning, simply select the level you want to start with. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with objects as you clean and organize each room. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete tasks such as finding hidden items and placing furniture. Enjoy transforming the house into a beautifully tidy home!
