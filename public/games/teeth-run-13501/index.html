Teeth Run

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of Teeth Run, a whimsical game designed to bring a smile to your face while you take on the role of a toothbrush-wielding superhero! In this entertaining experience, you ll dash over the tiny teeth of adorable little characters, all while ensuring they maintain a sparkling grin. The gameplay is filled with laughter as you race through vibrant environments and tackle the whimsical task of brushing their teeth.

Do you enjoy keeping your teeth clean? If so, you'll love the unique options available to you in Teeth Run. You ll have the choice between two unconventional cleaning agents traditional toothpaste or something laughably unexpected, like manure! This light-hearted twist on oral hygiene adds a comedic flair to the game, making each round an adventure of ridiculous fun. The vibrant graphics and silly sound effects will keep you entertained as you perfect your brushing technique and navigate through various toothy challenges.

Whether you re a fan of silly games or just looking for a fun way to practice your brushing skills, Teeth Run offers endless entertainment. Team up with friends and see who can brush the most teeth while having a blast! Prepare yourself for laughs and memorable moments in this charming dental escapade.

How to play free Teeth Run game online

To play Teeth Run, simply use your arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your toothbrush character. Aim to brush the teeth of the little men while choosing between toothpaste or manure. Collect points for every tooth you clean, but be careful to avoid obstacles!
