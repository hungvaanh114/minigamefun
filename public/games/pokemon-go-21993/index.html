POKEMON GO

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join a vibrant community of Trainers from all corners of the globe as you embark on an exciting journey to discover Pokemon in your surroundings. Pokemon GO has taken the world by storm, racking up over 1 billion downloads, and receiving accolades such as Best Mobile Game from the Game Developers Choice Awards and Best App of the Year from TechCrunch.

Step outside and immerse yourself in the magical world of Pokemon! Explore your local area to uncover a diverse range of Pokemon waiting to be caught. The thrill of discovery drives you to catch them all and complete your Pokedex! Accompany your Buddy Pokemon on your adventures to strengthen their abilities and earn valuable rewards along the way.

Take on formidable Gym battles to showcase your skills, and join forces with fellow Trainers in exhilarating Raid Battles to capture powerful Pokemon together! With every step, you re not just playing a game; you're engaging in real-life adventures that integrate seamlessly with the world around you. Are you ready for the challenge? It's time to get moving! Let s GO!

Note: Pokemon GO is a free-to-play app with optional in-game purchases. It is designed specifically for smartphones, requiring devices with 2GB RAM or more and Android Version 4.4 7.0+. GPS functionality is essential for gameplay, and a stable network connection is recommended for precise location tracking. Compatibility may vary, so check PokemonGO.com for the latest updates.

How to play free POKEMON GO game online

To start playing in Pokemon GO, download the app and create your trainer profile. Once in the game, simply walk around your area to find Pokemon. Tap on a Pokemon to enter catching mode, and use Poke Balls to capture them. Complete challenges, participate in battles, and enjoy your adventure!
