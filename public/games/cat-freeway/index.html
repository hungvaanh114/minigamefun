Cat Freeway

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Cute Overload game challenges your reaction skills. The main road is filled with adorable cats moving back and forth, while the side roads are lined with cats waiting for their turn to cross. Your goal is to click at the right moment to let the waiting cats into the main road without causing any collisions. Help all the cats safely cross and youll advance to the next level. With cute graphics and simple gameplay, Cat Freeway is the perfect game to play anywhere and anytime. Are you up for the challenge? Download Cat Freeway now and let the feline fun begin!
