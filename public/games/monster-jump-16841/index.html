Monster Jump

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Monster Jump, where your skills in timing and strategy will be put to the ultimate test! In this fun and engaging game, players tap, click, and hold to regulate their monster's jump power. The excitement ramps up with each leap as you navigate through a vibrant landscape filled with challenges and goodies.

Your main objective? To leap as far as you can while collecting adorable mini-monsters along the way. These mini-monsters serve a critical role in reviving your main monster should it face extinction. Thoroughly explore the game environment and make sure to dodge the dangerous rockets that come hurtling down from above they could spell doom for your valiant creature!

As you jump, keep an eye out for delicious candies scattered throughout the level. Eating these candies will give you the much-needed power-ups to enhance your performance, allowing for longer and higher jumps. With each jump, you'll want to beat your own distance record because the further you go, the more achievements and bragging rights you'll earn!

So, how far do you think you can jump? Put your skills to the test and embark on this exciting adventure in Monster Jump. It's time to play and discover the limits of your jumping abilities. Play now!

How to play free Monster Jump game online

To play the Monster Jump game, simply tap or click on the screen to start jumping. Hold your finger down to control how high and far your monster jumps. Keep an eye out for mini-monsters to revive your character, and avoid the falling rockets while grabbing candies to boost your power!
