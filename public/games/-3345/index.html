Hollywood Fashion Pets?Hollywood Fashion Pets

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the glamorous world of Hollywood Fashion Pets and unleash your inner pet stylist! In this captivating game, you will encounter five adorable stray pets in dire need of some love and care. These poor creatures are hungry, thirsty, and have fur that has seen better days. Your mission is to rescue them and transform their lives, giving them the star treatment they so rightfully deserve.

Let s roll up our sleeves and begin our journey! Start by giving each pet a soothing bath to wash away the dirt and grime. As you lather and rinse, watch as their fur transforms from scruffy to sleek and shiny. Once they are squeaky clean, it s time to treat them to some delicious food. Watch their eyes light up with joy as you fill their bowls with tasty meals, nourishing them back to health.

After they've enjoyed their feast, it s grooming time! Select from an array of stylish accessories and grooming tools to give each pet a fabulous makeover. With your creative flair, you can mix and match colors and styles to make each animal look like a Hollywood celebrity. Your goal is to ensure every pet shines bright like a star, ready for the spotlight and a loving forever home.

How to play free Hollywood Fashion Pets game online

To play the Hollywood Fashion Pets game online, start by selecting a pet from the available options. Use your mouse to navigate through different grooming and feeding options. Complete each task bathing, feeding, and styling by clicking and dragging the necessary items. Have fun turning these strays into fabulous pets!
