Count and Match Christmas

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready for a festive and engaging experience with Count And Match Christmas, a delightful game designed especially for kids and anyone looking to sharpen their counting skills while enjoying the holiday spirit! The gameplay is refreshingly simple and intuitive, allowing players to drag and drop numbered tiles into corresponding match boxes with ease.

In the game, you will encounter two distinct levels of difficulty. For the first 20 levels, players match 4 numbers to 4 boxes, providing a gentle introduction to the fundamentals of counting. Once you ve mastered that, the challenge increases as players are tasked to match 5 numbers with 5 boxes in the subsequent stages. No need to worry, though this is a stress-free game where mistakes won t cost you a thing!

The soothing environment coupled with a charming Christmas theme makes it suitable for players of all ages. The bright graphics and playful characters create an enchanting atmosphere that keeps players engaged for hours. Enjoy the delightful Christmas sounds and music while challenging your matching skills through all 40 levels available. Whether you re playing solo or with friends, rest assured you won't be rushing against the clock; it s all about having fun in a festive setting!

So gather around with family and friends, and dive into the holiday spirit with Count And Match Christmas. Have fun counting, matching, and celebrating this joyful season! Wishing you a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year!

How to play free Count And Match Christmas game online

To play the game, simply tap on a number to select it, then drag it towards the corresponding match box. Align the number with the box s image and release your finger to drop it in. Your goal is to fill all the boxes with the right numbers to progress to the next set of challenges!
