Burguer Farm

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the whimsical world of Burger Farm, a delightful and exhilarating game that challenges your agility and reflexes. In this vibrant arcade-style atmosphere, players are tasked with catching as many delicious burgers as they can as they tumble from the sky. However, the journey is not entirely straightforward; you must skillfully navigate around various advertisements that can obstruct your path to victory. The key to glory lies in your timing and precision as you reach for those coveted burgers.

As you immerse yourself in this fun and addictive experience, watch as your burger count climbs, aiming to achieve the highest score possible. Every session brings new challenges, testing your eye-hand coordination and quick thinking. Compete against your friends or set personal records, all while basking in the joy of burger collecting. With its simple mechanics and engaging gameplay, Burger Farm is perfect for anyone looking to indulge in a light-hearted gaming escape.

Do you have what it takes to emerge as the ultimate burger collector? Jump in and put your skills to the test in this exciting race against time! The charming graphics and upbeat music make every moment enjoyable, ensuring that you never want to stop playing. Log in, collect burgers, dodge those pesky ads, and see how high you can score!

How to play free Burger Farm game online

To play Burger Farm, simply use the arrow keys or swipe on your touchscreen to move your character left or right. Catch the falling burgers while avoiding any ads that might be in your way. Keep an eye on the score counter to track your progress as you collect more burgers!
