Army Cargo Drive

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of military logistics in Army Cargo Drive, a captivating 3D driving simulation that challenges your truck driving expertise. In this game, you will select one of the specially designed army trucks and embark on a mission to deliver crucial cargo to various military bases. Your primary objective is to ensure the cargo reaches its destination safely and on time while navigating through various challenging terrains and obstacles.

As you cruise through the game, you'll encounter ten distinct levels, each with its own set of challenges and hurdles to overcome. From narrow mountain pathways to rugged battlefields, your driving skills will truly be put to the test. Careful driving is key avoid tipping your load or dropping it along the way, as failing to do so will result in needing to restart the level. The game features three powerful trucks, each unique in handling and appearance, allowing you to choose the one that suits your driving style best.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to driving simulators, Army Cargo Drive offers an engaging experience that combines strategic planning with real-time driving skills. Ready to take the wheel and conquer the military driving challenges ahead?

How to play free Army Cargo Drive game online

To play Army Cargo Drive, select your preferred truck and begin your delivery mission. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate your truck through the levels. Steer carefully to avoid obstacles and ensure the cargo remains secured. Complete each level by reaching the military base on time!
