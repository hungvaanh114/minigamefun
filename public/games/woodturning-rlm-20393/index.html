Woodturning?

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the fascinating world of woodturning with the ultimate simulator that lets you unleash your creativity while completing customer orders. In this immersive experience, you will transform raw blocks of wood into stunning works of art, honing your skills as a virtual woodturner. Each job comes with specific requirements, challenging you to achieve the perfect shape and finish.

Your woodturning adventure begins with selecting a piece of wood and utilizing a variety of tools to meticulously cut and carve it into the desired form. Every detail matters as you polish and paint your creation, giving it that professional touch. Add unique stencils to personalize your projects and make them truly one-of-a-kind. As you successfully fulfill each order, your reputation will grow, leading to more complex tasks and higher-paying commissions.

Earn virtual money through your hard work, which can be reinvested to enhance your lathe room. Upgrade your equipment, purchase new tools, and unlock exciting features to expand your woodturning capabilities. The satisfaction of seeing your craftsmanship come to life is rewarding, making each project a unique journey.

How to play free Woodturning? game online

To play the Woodturning game online, start by launching the game and selecting an order from the customer list. Use the on-screen tools to cut, carve, and shape the wood according to the specifications. Once completed, polish and paint your creation to make it stand out. Finally, submit your work to earn money and unlock new upgrades!
