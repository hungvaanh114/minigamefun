Rescue Plan - Flight Control

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Rescue Plan - Flight Control, where you step into the shoes of an air traffic controller tasked with restoring order at a bustling airport! The airport is currently experiencing chaos, and it s your mission to ensure that the planes land safely without colliding into one another. Your keen sense of strategy and quick reflexes will be put to the test as you navigate through this thrilling simulation.

Your responsibility is to manage multiple incoming flights, directing them to the tarmac with precision and skill. You'll guide each aircraft, monitoring their positions while simultaneously avoiding mishaps. Touch and tap on the planes to redirect their paths and maintain spacing as they make their descent. With countless aircraft vying for a successful landing, the challenge intensifies as you progress through increasingly complex levels.

Enjoy the satisfying feeling of accomplishment as you successfully land aircraft, all while enjoying the vibrant graphics and immersive sounds of the airport environment. The excitement is palpable as you rush to avoid collisions and manage the air traffic like a seasoned pro. Whether you're a casual gamer or a dedicated enthusiast, Rescue Plan - Flight Control promises endless hours of fun while deftly honing your strategizing and multitasking abilities.

How to play free Rescue Plan - Flight Control game online

To play Rescue Plan - Flight Control, use your mouse or touch screen to interact with each aircraft. Tap on incoming planes to modify their landing paths and ensure they reach the tarmac safely. Keep track of multiple planes to prevent any crashes and successfully land all flights. Happy controlling!
