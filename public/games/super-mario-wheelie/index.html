Super Mario Wheelie

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the exhilarating world of Super Mario Wheelie, a free online game that invites you to rev up your motorcycle and embark on an unforgettable adventure! Join the fearless Super Mario as he takes to the streets, executing daring stunts and navigating through challenging levels. With every twist and turn, the excitement intensifies, and your skills will be put to the ultimate test!

Your main objective in Super Mario Wheelie is to perform thrilling wheelie stunts while racing towards the finish line. Balance is of utmost importance, as you'll need to maintain control of your motorcycle to clear each level successfully. Click on the screen to keep Mario moving forward, but remember, it s all about timing! To execute and sustain a wheelie, you must click constantly keep that front wheel in the air to rack up points and impress your friends. However, be cautious; if the front wheel touches the ground, it's game over!

As you ride, you'll also have the opportunity to collect valuable coins scattered throughout the course. These coins not only add to your score but also enhance the overall gaming experience by providing challenges along the way. So, gear up and help Mario showcase his impressive skills as you tackle each level, unveiling new obstacles and surprises at every corner.

How to play free Super Mario Wheelie game online

To begin playing Super Mario Wheelie, simply click on the screen to start your adventure. Your motorcycle will automatically accelerate forward. Continuously click to perform a wheelie and keep the front wheel off the ground. Collect coins and complete each level while aiming for the highest score!
