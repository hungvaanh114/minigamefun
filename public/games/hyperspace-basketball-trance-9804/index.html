Hyperspace Basketball Trance

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Hyperspace Basketball Trance, where your skills in tossing planets into a celestial hoop will be put to the test! In this captivating game, players aim to throw planets into a target hoop while dodging treacherous asteroids that threaten to interrupt their scoring spree. As you watch the planets zoom through space, collect shining stars and radiant diamonds that enhance your progress.

As you embark on this cosmic journey, be mindful of the disappearing and escaping hoops that add an extra layer of challenge. Gather enough stars to advance through successive levels, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting. You can also use your diamonds to purchase powerful shields that obliterate any asteroids in your path and to enlarge your hoop, making it significantly easier to make those distant throws. Don't forget to invest your diamonds in new and improved planets to spice up your throwing experience!

Featuring easy controls and dynamic effects of explosions and hits, Hyperspace Basketball Trance offers an engaging gameplay experience across 105 levels of increasing difficulty. With a total of 10 unique planets to discover and toss, players can challenge themselves with timed rounds of 45, 60, and 75 seconds. Prepare for an exciting journey with a variety of asteroids, an intuitive throwing mechanic, visually appealing graphics, and delightful sound effects. This game promises fun for players of all ages!

How to play free Hyperspace Basketball Trance game online

To play Hyperspace Basketball Trance, use your mouse or touch controls to aim at the hoop and adjust the power of your throw by pulling back and releasing. Collect stars and diamonds as you successfully toss planets and avoid asteroids. Keep an eye on the timer if you're in challenge mode, and collect enough resources to upgrade your abilities!
