Shoot Up!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Shoot Up! is an exhilarating soccer game that puts your penalty shooting skills to the ultimate test. The goal is simple: score as many goals as possible while facing your friends in a competitive environment. With thrilling graphics and engaging gameplay, this game is perfect for soccer enthusiasts who want to show off their scoring prowess. However, with only three misses allowed, each shot counts, making it essential to focus and time your penalties wisely.

In Shoot Up!, you ll experience the rush of taking penalty shots, aiming to outdo your opponents and secure your title as the best penalty taker in the world. Whether you re playing solo or against friends, the challenge of scoring under pressure will keep you on the edge of your seat. The more goals you rack up, the higher your chances of climbing to the top of the leaderboard and proving your skills. Enjoy the thrill of competition and see who can achieve the highest score!

Gather your friends and get ready for some intense battles on the soccer field, where every kick can lead you to glory. Will you emerge victorious as the ultimate penalty shooting champion? Dive into the action of Shoot Up! to find out!

How to play free Shoot Up! game online

To play Shoot Up!, position your player on the field and aim using your mouse. Click to shoot the ball into the net. Remember, you have only three missed attempts, so make each shot count! Compete against your friends and keep track of your scores to see who rises to the challenge.
