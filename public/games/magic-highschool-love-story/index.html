Magic Highschool Love Story

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the enchanting world of Magic Highschool Love Story! In this captivating game, you dive into the life of Hailey, the rebellious daughter of the Wicked Witch. With her mother s cool demeanor and allure, Hailey is set to take on the first day at the elite Heirloom High School, which was established by the legendary Snow Queen to nurture gifted individuals in the art of magic.

As Hailey steps into this prestigious institution, she seeks your help to transform into the ultimate magical queen. Your role is to assist her in creating the perfect look starting with her makeup. The game features an array of stunning and edgy dark makeup options. Unleash your creativity as you decorate her captivating eyes and bold lips, making her stand out among her peers.

Next, explore a wardrobe filled with fashionable, evil-style outfits that truly reflect Hailey s unique personality. Choose the most stylish attire to cement her status as the coolest girl at school. Don t forget to fix her enchanting wings, which add to her magical charm. As you guide her through the makeover process, you will experience a world filled with magic, style, and exciting adventures!

How to play free Magic Highschool Love Story game online

To play the game, simply use your mouse or touch controls to interact with different features. Start by choosing makeup options, then select Hailey's outfits and accessories. Make sure to customize every detail to create the perfect look as she embarks on her magical journey! Have fun!
