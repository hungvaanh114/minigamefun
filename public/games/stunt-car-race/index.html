How to play free Stunt Car Race game online

In Stunt Car Race, use your keyboard's arrow keys to control your vehicle s movement. Accelerate and steer through the obstacles while collecting gold coins. Pay close attention to balance on ramps and utilize checkpoints to avoid starting over. When you need a speed boost, press the designated nitro key for a burst of speed!
