Hide Heist

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Hide &amp;amp; Heist is a single-player game where player control two characters &amp;ndash; the smart and the strong. The smart excels at open locks and hacking systems, while the strong excels at eliminating guards and breaking through obstacles. Players must strategically utilize both characters unique abilities to sneak into secure corporate offices and banks, execute the heist, and escape undetected. The gameplay combines actions, strategy, stealth, and quick thinking, offering an engaging and challenging experience.
