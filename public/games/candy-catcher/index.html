Candy Catcher

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Candy Catcher, a delightful and addictive game that will challenge your reflexes while satisfying your sweet tooth! In this whimsical world filled with colorful candies, your task is simple yet engaging catch as many falling candies as you can to build your virtual chest. The more candies you capture, the more upgrades and unlocks you can achieve, revealing an array of new, delectable treats to add to your collection.

As you progress through the game, each level introduces exciting new challenges that will test your agility. The candies come in various shapes and sizes, each with its own point value and unique features. Some candies may even grant you special abilities, such as slowing down time or multiplying your points, so stay alert and aim for the most rewarding sweets! With vibrant graphics and an enchanting soundtrack, Candy Catcher offers an immersive experience that will keep you hooked for hours.

Compete with friends and players around the world for high scores on the leaderboard, and don t forget to share your achievements on social media. Whether you're a casual gamer looking for some fun or a competitive spirit aiming to top the charts, Candy Catcher promises endless enjoyment. So grab your virtual basket and prepare for a sweet adventure!

How to play free Candy Catcher game online

To play Candy Catcher, simply use your mouse or touch screen to move your basket left and right. Catch the falling candies as they drop from the top of the screen. Try to avoid catching non-candy items, as they may deduct points. Catch enough candies to upgrade your chest and unlock even more sweet surprises!

Mouse - To move the chest and to interact with the menu
