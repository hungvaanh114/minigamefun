Whack the Mole!

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Whack The Mole!, the exciting and fast-paced game where quick reflexes and keen eyesight are essential! In this lively challenge, your goal is to swing your hammer to hit as many pesky moles as you can before the timer ticks down to zero. Each mole you whack not only brings a satisfying crunch but also contributes to your score, helping you climb to the top of the leaderboard.

As you progress through the levels, the moles will pop up at a faster rate, testing your hand-eye coordination and ability to concentrate under pressure. You will have the chance to collect the Super Hammer Power-up, which temporarily doubles your points, pushing your score even higher! Keep an eye out for this power-up, as it can make all the difference in achieving the highest score. With each successful hit, your excitement will escalate, making Whack The Mole! an adrenaline-pumping experience for players of all ages.

Challenge your friends to beat your high score and see who can whack the most moles in this competitive game. Whether you're trying to pass the time or looking for a serious challenge, Whack The Mole! is guaranteed to keep you entertained! Are you ready to experience the thrill of the chase? Grab your hammer and let s start whacking!

How to play free Whack The Mole! game online

To play Whack The Mole!, simply click on the moles that pop up from their holes as quickly as you can. Each successful hit scores points, and be sure to grab the Super Hammer Power-up when it appears to double your points temporarily. Keep your eye on the timer and aim for the highest score possible!
