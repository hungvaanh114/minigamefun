Solitaire Crime Stories

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the intriguing world of Solitaire Crime Stories, where each card you play brings you closer to solving captivating mysteries. This delightful Solitaire game immerses you in a rich narrative that unfolds in the quaint town of Springdale. With its charming yet enigmatic atmosphere, Springdale appears to be an idyllic small American town, but beneath its serene surface lies a web of secrets waiting to be uncovered.

As you guide the story through the perspective of Lana Whitt, a tenacious young journalist, you'll encounter an array of colorful characters, each harboring their own hidden truths. From puzzling murders to long-buried family secrets, every level of Solitaire offers a unique challenge that tests your skills and logic. Navigate through the intricacies of the game, solving card puzzles to reveal clues that drive the investigative narrative forward.

Stunning locations in Springdale serve as backdrops to the riveting stories that invite players to explore various facets of the town s lore. Immerse yourself in this thrilling blend of gameplay and storytelling, where unraveling a mystery is just a card flip away. With each successful completion of a level, you ll delve deeper into the dark history of Springdale, making every moment spent playing an enticing adventure.

How to play free Solitaire Crime Stories game online

To play Solitaire Crime Stories, simply arrange the cards in descending order and alternate colors. Complete each level by clearing the tableau and collecting stars that reveal clues. Keep an eye out for special cards that may assist you in unraveling the secrets of Springdale. Enjoy the captivating storyline as you progress!
