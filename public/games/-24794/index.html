Basketball Street?Basketball Street

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Basketball Street, the ultimate challenge for basketball enthusiasts eager to showcase their shooting skills! In this fast-paced game, you ll have the opportunity to select your favorite player from a choice of three unique athletes and put your free throw prowess to the test. With a total of one minute on the clock, you will attempt to score as many baskets as possible by taking 25 shots.

Strategically plan your shots as you aim for the basketball hoop. Keep an eye out for striped balls, which are not your ordinary basketballs; they offer you two points per basket, doubling your scoring potential! This rewarding twist encourages you to focus more on these special shots, enhancing your overall gameplay experience as you aim for a high score.

Basketball Street is designed to be an exhilarating experience that combines skill, speed, and strategy. Whether you are a seasoned player or just picking up the controller for the first time, the straightforward mechanics allow everyone to enjoy the excitement of the game. Compete against yourself or challenge your friends to beat your top score. The street is your arena, and it s time to shine!

How to play free Basketball Street game online

To play Basketball Street, select your favorite player and get ready to shoot! You have one minute to complete 25 shots. Click or tap to aim and shoot the ball into the basket. Focus on hitting the striped balls for double points and maximize your score!
