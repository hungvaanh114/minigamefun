How to play free Flick Superhero game online

To play Flick Superhero, simply tap and drag to adjust the angle of your flick. Release to send your superhero ball soaring towards the basket. Keep an eye on the speed and power of your shot, as these will affect your score. Aim wisely and see how many baskets you can make!
