How to play free Fluffy Rush game online

To play Fluffy Rush, simply use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to move your character. Jump over obstacles and collect bonus items by pressing the space bar. Remember to explore secret areas to maximize your score and challenge yourself to finish each level in record time!
