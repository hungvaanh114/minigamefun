Super Market : Shopping Games

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the delightful world of the Kids Supermarket game, where fun meets education! Join Mommy, Daddy, Hippo, and her little brother as they embark on a shopping adventure filled with laughter and learning. Mommy has prepared a shopping list of essential items, and it s up to you to help Hippo collect every product on it!

As you navigate through the vibrant supermarket, keep a close eye on the top of the screen for the list of goods you need to find. Each aisle is stocked with a wide range of familiar products, from fresh fruits and vegetables to clothes and garden tools. When you spot an item from the list, simply drag it into the trolley. But be careful! Daddy and Hippo's little brother might be tempted to add some extra goodies that aren t on the list!

After gathering all the items, accompany Hippo and her family to the checkout counter to pay for the goods. Once all transactions are complete, you'll reunite with Mommy, who will check your shopping haul against her list. Make sure to stick to the list collect all five stars to make Mommy proud of your shopping skills!

This game is not just about fun; it s also designed to teach kids valuable lessons like counting, recognizing different products, and understanding the importance of following a budget. With colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, the Supermarket game guarantees hours of enjoyment while enriching your child s learning experience. Get ready to dive into this exciting shopping adventure!

How to play free super market : shopping games game online

To play the Kids Supermarket game, simply start by selecting a character. Follow the shopping list displayed at the top of the screen as you explore each aisle. Drag the correct items into your trolley, all while avoiding sneaky additions by Daddy and little brother. Once your trolley is full, head to the cashier to complete your shopping!
