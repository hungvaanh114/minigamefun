Heart Box - Physics Puzzles Game

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the challenging world of Heart Box, a captivating physics puzzles game where your primary goal is to navigate a clever robot through a series of intriguing experimental rooms engineered by the eccentric Professor Rat. Each puzzle presents a unique challenge that requires you to creatively apply the properties of various physical objects to successfully rescue the robot from its charging station.

Throughout the game, you will encounter an array of 20 different interactive items, such as test-cubes, bowling balls, and dynamic saws, each offering distinct functionality. Use spring trampolines to launch the robot, or maneuver movable platforms to create pathways. The game also features exciting mechanics like conveyor belts and pneumatic tubes, along with hazards like dynamite and lasers that demand careful navigation. With each level, the puzzles become increasingly complex, encouraging players to think strategically and experiment with different approaches to find solutions.

With its engaging gameplay and diverse mechanics, Heart Box not only offers endless fun and challenge but also helps sharpen your problem-solving skills. Are you ready to take on the task and free the robot from Professor Rat's twisted experiments? Dive into Heart Box and discover how physics can be both fun and educational!

How to play free Heart Box - physics puzzles game online

To play Heart Box, use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the physical objects in each level. Drag and drop items, activate switches, and carefully plan your moves to guide the robot to the exit. Complete each puzzle by releasing the robot from its charger, utilizing the properties of different objects effectively!
