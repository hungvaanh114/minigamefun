Dog Hide N Seek

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an adventurous hide-and-seek game in Dog Hide N Seek! Join a group of playful kids and their furry friend as they hide and seek in a beautifully illustrated forest. Quick reflexes and strategic hiding spots are essential to avoid being seen by the dog. Can you outsmart the dog and emerge as the ultimate hide-and-seek champion?

Instructions

Start the Game Tap the play button to begin your hide-and-seek adventure Move Quickly Tap the screen to make your character walk faster Hide Smartly Avoid being seen by the dog by stopping when the dog is looking in your direction Win the Challenge
