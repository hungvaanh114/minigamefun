Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Embark on an exciting adventure with Tom & Jerry Run, an exhilarating endless runner game that promises hours of fun! Inspired by classics like Subway Surfers, this game invites players to join Jerry as he zips through the vibrant streets of Subway City. With the iconic duo of Tom and Jerry at the forefront, you'll encounter a delightful blend of action and humor.

In Tom & Jerry Run, speed is essential. As you guide Jerry, you'll need to dodge various obstacles, including barriers and other challenges, while collecting sparkling gold coins scattered throughout the environment. The game's colorful graphics and dynamic settings make every run feel fresh and engaging. Similar to Talking Tom Gold Run, players can unlock upgrades and power-ups that enhance Jerry s abilities, making for an even more thrilling experience.

As you race through the subway, keep an eye out for special items that can help you along the way. Whether it's a speed boost or a magnet for gathering coins, these power-ups will aid you in your quest to achieve the highest score possible. Join forces with Jerry and experience the action-packed joyride in this fun-filled game!

How to play free Tom & Jerry Run game online

To play Tom & Jerry Run, simply tap to jump over obstacles and swipe to change lanes. Collect gold coins as you dash through Subway City, and be on the lookout for power-ups for added benefits. The objective is to run as far as you can while avoiding various challenges that come your way!
