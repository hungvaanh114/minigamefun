City Defender

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of City Defender, you take on the role of a heavily armored tank tasked with protecting your beloved city from relentless invaders. As the air becomes filled with the ominous sound of falling bombs, your primary objective is to neutralize these threats before they reach the ground and cause destruction. Your tank is equipped with powerful artillery that enables you to shoot down the bombs in mid-air.

The game features various bombs that drop at different speeds, adding an extra layer of challenge to your defense mission. You'll need to act swiftly and strategically, prioritizing the faster bombs to ensure they are eliminated before they can inflict damage. Each bomb that successfully hits the ground will cost you a life, and running out of lives will ultimately lead to a game over, intensifying the urgency of your task.

As you navigate through the relentless waves of enemies, you'll find that the fate of your city lies within your hands. Mastering the controls and developing your shooting skills will be crucial to achieving high scores and completing levels. Every shot counts, so stay vigilant and ready as you embark on this exhilarating journey to protect your city from impending doom.

How to play free City Defender game online

To start playing City Defender, use the arrow keys to move your tank left or right. Aim your cannon with your mouse, and click to fire at the incoming bombs. Remember to prioritize faster bombs and keep an eye on your lives; if you lose them all, it s game over!
