Knife Storm

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of precision and skill with Knife Storm, a captivating game that invites you to wield a variety of unique knives across three distinct game modes. Whether you prefer to go solo or challenge a friend in a two-player format, there's an option for everyone. Each mode offers a fresh twist on knife-throwing mechanics that will test your dexterity and reaction time.

In the "Classic" mode, your objective is to successfully throw all of your knives at a rotating target filled with fruits and other enticing objects. However, you must be careful to ensure that your knives do not collide with one another as they hurtle towards the target! Precision is key as you strive to reach high scores.

The "Shoot" mode elevates the excitement by having you aim at flying fruits while dodging bombs that can end your game in an instant. Time is of the essence, adding an additional layer of challenge as you race against the clock while trying to break records.

For those who have a knack for slicing, the "Ninja" mode challenges players to slice through airborne fruits using your mouse. The key to maximizing your score lies in creating combo points through skillful and synchronous slicing. But beware of the bombs; hitting one will result in an abrupt game over! Knife Storm combines captivating gameplay with an exhilarating atmosphere, making it an adventure not to be missed.

How to play free Knife Storm game online

To play Knife Storm, simply select your preferred game mode and start throwing knives or slicing fruits. Use your mouse to aim accurately and execute your throws or slices. Keep an eye on the targets and avoid any bombs while trying to achieve the best score possible. Good luck!
