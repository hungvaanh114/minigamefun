Slash Dunk is an engaging basketball puzzle game that combines the thrill of sports with clever problem-solving. In this unique gaming experience, players are challenged to strategically cut threads in order to guide the basketball towards the hoop. As you navigate through a series of intricate levels, each featuring complex thread arrangements, your ability to think critically and act with precision will truly be put to the test.

With a finite number of cuts available in each level, players must carefully consider their moves to ensure the ball reaches the goal successfully. Each level presents distinct challenges, where the arrangement of threads can create both hurdles and opportunities. The vibrant graphics, combined with captivating sounds, enhance the gameplay and immerse players into a world where creativity meets strategy.
