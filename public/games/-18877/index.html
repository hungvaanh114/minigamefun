Welcome to Granny: Chapter Two! In this spine-chilling sequel, you find yourself once again trapped in a house, but this time, it's not just Granny who keeps you confined; it's also Grandpa! To escape, you must tread carefully and maintain silence, as Granny s keen sense of hearing will alert her to any noise you make. Although Grandpa may not hear as well, his strength is formidable, and he will pose a serious threat if you cross his path. You ve got five days to devise your escape strategy. Good luck!

The backstory is haunting, with rumors circulating about a maniacal old woman who preys on children at night. Known for her ruthless nature, this Granny is said to be far more terrifying than any slasher or malevolent clown. When a brave girl named Dorothy becomes a victim of her sinister grasp, it's up to you to help her escape without being caught. Prepare yourself for an exhilarating experience as you navigate through Granny's creepy lair!

Gameplay offers multiple modes to suit your preference. In Escape Mode, teams of Granny and Dorothy must either flee the house or catch the escapees. Infection Mode ramps up the stakes as you go solo, switching roles as you capture or evade. Meanwhile, Story Mode allows you to choose a specific role with unique abilities to tackle Granny together with teammates. With a variety of character classes, such as Bomber, Thrower, or Healer, there's a strategic edge that makes your escape efforts more exciting!

Get ready for breathtaking survival horror in an environment filled with unsettling sounds and relentless threats. Collaborate with your friends, customize your character, and show off your skills to conquer the game and achieve new high scores!

How to play free Granny Kill 2 game online

To play Granny Kill 2 online, join the game and choose your role as either Granny or one of the escapees, Dorothy. Gather essential items, locate hidden clues, and communicate with your teammates to devise a successful escape plan. Be stealthy to avoid capture, and remember to help or hinder as needed!
