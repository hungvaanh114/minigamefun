Experience the thrilling combination of two beloved franchises with Angry Birds Star Wars Coloring, a delightful online coloring game for children! This exciting game features eight unique images inspired by the iconic characters of the Angry Birds universe and the legendary Star Wars saga. Your mission is to bring these illustrations to life by selecting from an impressive palette of 23 vibrant colors. The quicker you color, the higher your score, adding an exciting competitive element to your creativity!

As you dive into this colorful adventure, you'll have the opportunity to unleash your imagination and artistic skills. Whether your favorite character is a Force-wielding bird or a dastardly pig disguised as a Sith, each picture provides a blank canvas for you to express yourself. Once you've completed your masterpiece, you can easily save the colored images to share your creations with friends and family. Angry Birds Star Wars Coloring is not just a game; it s a chance for kids to explore their creativity while having fun!

How to play free Angry Birds Star Wars Coloring game online

To start playing, simply select one of the eight images available. Use your mouse to choose a color from the palette, then click on the areas of the picture you wish to fill. Try to color as quickly as possible to achieve a high score! Don't forget to save your artwork at the end!
