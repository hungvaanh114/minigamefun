Among Akero Bots 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Among Akero Bots 2, an exhilarating 2D platformer that takes you on a thrilling adventure through a vibrant sci-fi universe. Step into the shoes of an Akero Bot, tasked with the mission of collecting enchanting red magical gems scattered throughout a series of challenging levels. As you navigate through the world, you'll encounter a multitude of formidable enemies, including menacing flying robots and other hostile bots, all determined to thwart your progress.

Your journey is fraught with peril as you must skillfully avoid a barrage of bullets, rotating lasers, and treacherous spikes lurking around every corner. Each level is uniquely designed to test your reflexes and strategic thinking, with increasing difficulty that keeps the excitement alive as you progress. With eight immersive levels to conquer, every challenge you overcome brings a sense of accomplishment that propels you further into this captivating game.

This sequel to the original Among Akero Bots series builds on the foundation of its predecessor, offering players enhanced gameplay mechanics and rich visuals that draw you deeper into the Akero Bot universe. Will you be able to collect all the gems and reach the Exit door in each level to advance to the next? The adventure awaits!

How to play free Among Akero Bots 2 game online

To play Among Akero Bots 2, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your Akero Bot, jump to avoid obstacles, and navigate through the levels. Collect all the red magical gems while dodging enemy bots and traps. Reach the Exit door to progress to the next level and keep your skills sharp!
