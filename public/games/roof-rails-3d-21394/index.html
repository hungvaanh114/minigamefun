How to play free Roof Rails 3D game online

To play Roof Rails 3D, use the left and right arrow keys or swipe left and right to move your character and avoid traps. Collect as many diamonds as possible while completing your missions within the time limit. Stay alert and keep your speed to overcome obstacles and reach the finish line!
