Draw Race 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Unleash your creativity in Draw Race 3D, a thrilling game that combines art and racing in an innovative way. Here, you have the unique opportunity to express your drawing skills and imagine what kind of car you would like to create. Your artistic vision will take the form of the vehicles that race on the track. The better your drawing, the faster and more agile your car becomes!

As you doodle your dream machine, don t forget to pay attention to which switches will allow your vehicle to pass through. It's not just about artistic flair; strategic choices play a pivotal role in your success on the racing circuit. You have the ability to reshape your car at any point in the game, so you can adapt to challenges on the go. This feature adds a layer of excitement and encourages players to experiment with various designs to find the perfect racing machine.

Whether you're a skilled artist or just looking to have some fun, Draw Race 3D provides an enjoyable experience filled with surprises. Gather your friends or enjoy some solo time, and step into this vibrant world where every race is uniquely yours!

How to play free Draw Race 3D game online

To play Draw Race 3D, start by sketching a car with your mouse or touchscreen. Ensure your design is functional by considering its shape and balance. Once drawn, you can set it in motion by hitting the race button. Watch your creation speed through the track as you engage various switches to maximize its performance!
