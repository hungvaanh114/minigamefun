Zombie Idle Defense

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to face a relentless horde of the undead in Zombie Idle Defense! This thrilling idle defense game is designed to test your strategic prowess and quick reflexes as you fend off countless waves of zombies. As the ultimate line of defense, you must take swift action to build and fortify your stronghold while upgrading your arsenal to combat the increasingly challenging zombie onslaught.

In Zombie Idle Defense, your primary objective is to construct a robust defense system. Begin by placing your automatic turrets strategically around your base to maximize their efficiency. Upgrade your weapons frequently to ensure they remain effective against tougher zombie variants. As the waves progress, the undead will come at you with increasing ferocity, so staying ahead in weapon enhancements and tactical placements is crucial for survival.

In addition to upgrading your weapons, you can also set your turrets ablaze to unleash devastating firepower on the approaching zombies. The combination of automated defenses and your strategic planning will be vital as you strive to protect your base from destruction and keep the zombies at bay. Are you ready to dive into this thrilling challenge and defend against the terrifying zombie apocalypse?

How to play free Zombie Idle Defense game online

To play Zombie Idle Defense, simply click on your screen to place turrets and upgrade your weapons as you generate resources automatically. Keep an eye on the zombie waves and strategically enhance your defenses to withstand each attack. Use your available resources wisely to outlast the undead!
