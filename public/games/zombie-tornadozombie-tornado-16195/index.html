Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the thrilling world of Zombie Tornado, the relentless conflict between mankind and the undead reaches unprecedented levels. Although many brave souls have succumbed to the onslaught of zombies, you stand out as one of the fortunate few who have survived the chaos. However, don't celebrate just yet; danger lurks around every corner, and survival hinges on your quick thinking and daring maneuvers.

Your mission is clear: resist the relentless hordes of the walking dead while simultaneously rescuing as many survivors as you can. With each passing moment, the situation grows increasingly dire, making every decision you make crucial to your survival. You ll need to strategize your movements carefully, avoiding gruesome confrontations while keeping a keen eye out for fellow humans in distress who need your help.

As you traverse the post-apocalyptic landscape, be prepared for unexpected challenges and fierce zombie encounters. Arm yourself with an array of weapons and develop your skills to improve your chances against the undead. The stakes couldn't be higher; not only is your life on the line, but so are the lives of those you aim to save. Step into the heart of this nail-biting adventure, rally your instincts, and join in the fight against the undead. Every survivor you rescue and every encounter you bravely face could be the difference between life and death.

How to play free Zombie Tornado game online

To play Zombie Tornado, use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Click or tap to attack zombies as they approach. Your objective is to navigate through the game s levels, rescuing survivors while avoiding zombie attacks. Gather weapons and power-ups to enhance your abilities and ensure your survival.
