Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the exciting puzzle game, White or Black, perfect for kids and puzzle enthusiasts alike! In this engaging game, you will encounter two vertical beams one white on the left and one black on the right. Balls of varying colors will cascade down from the center, and your task is to strategically interact with the beams based on the color of the ball that lands on the target between them.

The objective is simple yet captivating: when a white ball reaches the target, you must promptly tap the white beam to deflect it, earning points for your accuracy. Conversely, if a black ball arrives, you need to hit the black beam to score. The faster your reactions, the higher your score! However, be mindful selecting the wrong beam will end your game, adding to the challenge and excitement. This game not only tests your reflexes but also sharpens your decision-making skills in a fun and interactive manner.

As you progress, the pace at which the balls fall may increase, making it essential to keep your focus sharp. Compete against your own high score or challenge friends to see who can achieve the highest points. White or Black combines simplicity with an exhilarating gameplay experience, making it a must-try for players of all ages. Dive into this colorful world and put your quick-thinking abilities to the test!

How to play free White or Black game online

To play White or Black, simply start the game and wait for the balls to drop. Observe the color of the ball as it approaches the target, and tap the corresponding white or black beam based on the ball's color. Aim to tap accurately for maximum points, but be careful, as pressing the incorrect beam will end your game!
