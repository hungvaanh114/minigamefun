How to play free Neon Painter game online

To play Neon Painter, simply swipe your finger across the screen to guide the neon water towards the hidden shapes. Make sure to fill in the outlines completely to reveal the hidden objects. Keep an eye on your score and complete each level as efficiently as possible to unlock the next challenges!
