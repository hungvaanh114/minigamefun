Stupid Chicken

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Stupid Chicken, a delightful and engaging game where the objective is simple yet entertaining: feed the chicken to collect eggs. As a playful child farmer, your task is to click on the screen to encourage your chicken to peck at the food you provide. The more effectively you feed her, the greater your chances of receiving eggs in return!

But the fun doesn t stop there! As you guide your chicken, don't forget about the insects buzzing around. These tasty little snacks are a bonus for your feathered friend, providing her with additional nourishment that enhances her egg-laying prowess. The gameplay is straightforward, making it ideal for players of all ages who are looking for a lighthearted experience.

As your chicken feasts on the provided food and delicious insects, she will gradually gather enough energy to lay eggs. Your goal is to collect as many eggs as possible within the time limit. Once the game concludes, you can trade your hard-earned eggs for extra time, allowing you to continue your fun and progress further in this delightful adventure.

Embark on this whimsical journey and see how many eggs you can collect while enjoying the charming graphics and engaging gameplay of Stupid Chicken!

How to play free Stupid Chicken game online

To play Stupid Chicken, simply click on the screen to feed your chicken. Watch her peck at the food and munch on nearby insects to build her energy. The more she eats, the more eggs she'll lay. Aim to collect as many eggs as you can before the timer runs out, and exchange them for additional playtime!
