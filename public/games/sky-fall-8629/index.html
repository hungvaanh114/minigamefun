Sky Fall

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Sky Fall, an exhilarating endless adventure that challenges players to navigate through a thrilling sky filled with obstacles. This captivating game takes you on a magical journey where you'll need to steer clear of a variety of hindrances such as helicopters, birds, and balloon bombs that add an extra layer of excitement. With each descent, the challenge escalates, making this game both fun and addictive.

At the heart of Sky Fall is the joy of mastering your parachute control as you strive to achieve the highest score possible. The rules are straightforward and user-friendly, ensuring that players of all ages can quickly grasp the concept and dive right into the action. Simply swipe on the screen to maneuver your character through the exhilarating sky, avoiding obstacles along the way.

Keep your eyes peeled for tokens scattered throughout your journey. Collecting these tokens is vital as they allow you to reach your cash-out limit and climb the leaderboard. Your primary goal is to score as high as you can, making every dive an exciting challenge. With stunning visuals and engaging gameplay, Sky Fall promises to keep you entertained for hours.

How to play free Sky Fall game online

To play Sky Fall, simply start the game and swipe your finger across the screen to move your character left or right. As you descend, avoid all obstacles like helicopters and birds. Don t forget to collect tokens for scoring points and reaching your cash-out limit!
