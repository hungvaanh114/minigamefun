NoobCraft Totem

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In the exhilarating world of NoobCraft Totem, players embark on an epic quest to collect the elusive immortality totems scattered throughout a festive yet perilous winter landscape. As you navigate through this enchanting forest, the goal is clear: gather every totem to unlock a mystical portal that leads to your next challenging adventure.

However, the journey is not without its dangers. On this chilly winter day, you must be wary of snowmen and polar bears lurking around, ready to impede your progress. Quick reflexes will be your best friend as you dodge these formidable foes, who are ever ready to pounce. Fear not! Armed with a trusty sword, you have the option to throw it at these creatures and defend yourself when necessary.

As you venture deeper into the woods, don't miss the opportunity to collect gold scattered in the environment. It s crucial to gather both the gold and the totems to fully power up and activate the magical portal. Once you ve amassed enough totems and treasure, make your way to the portal and step through to discover what challenges lie ahead.

How to play free NoobCraft Totem game online

To play the game, navigate using the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press the space bar to throw your sword at enemies, while collecting totems and gold along the way. Make sure to reach the portal after gathering enough items to unlock new adventures. Enjoy your journey!
