Santa Roll

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to join Santa in a delightful adventure with Santa Roll! This festive game invites players to assist jolly old Saint Nick in gathering all the candy scattered throughout the screen. With a simple touch or press, you can guide Santa left or right, propelling him toward the mysterious North Pole hole! Each level presents a unique challenge, as you navigate through various obstacles and collect yummy treats along the way. The playful graphics and cheerful sound effects make this a captivating experience for players of all ages.

As you control the crazy and lovable Santa, you ll find that each candy collected brings you one step closer to festive victory! The game is designed to keep you engaged, with a variety of levels that increase in difficulty and creativity. Whether you re a casual player looking for some holiday fun or a gamer aiming for high scores, Santa Roll offers a joyful escape into the spirit of Christmas. So, gather your friends and family for some fun-filled gameplay and see who can help Santa collect the most candy!

How to play free Santa Roll game online

To play Santa Roll, simply tap or click on the left or right side of the screen to move Santa in the corresponding direction. Your goal is to collect all the candies on the screen while avoiding obstacles. Keep guiding Santa until he reaches the North Pole hole safely to complete each level!
