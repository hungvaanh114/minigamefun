Shoot Penalty

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to experience the thrill of striking a penalty shot in this exciting football game! The stakes are high as you step up to the penalty spot, with the goalkeeper poised to thwart your shot and a defending player ready to interfere. Every detail is crucial as you prepare for your attempt to score. The ball is stationary, just waiting for your decisive kick.

To execute your kick, you need to carefully choose where in the goal you want to aim. Take a moment to observe the goalkeeper s position and any defenders who might obstruct your path to glory. With a simple drag of your mouse, you can determine the direction and strength of your kick. Will you choose to go low and to the left, or perhaps attempt a daring shot to the upper corner? The choice is yours. Remember, your goal is to outsmart the goalkeeper and hit the back of the net, all while avoiding any defenders that may be closing in on the action.

With every shot, the excitement builds as you hope your calculated effort will succeed. Each successful penalty not only boosts your confidence but strengthens your desire to keep playing. This game will put your precision and decision-making skills to the test, giving you the ultimate football experience!

How to play free Shoot Penalty game online

To start playing, simply select your target in the goal where you want to shoot. Then, drag your mouse to define the angle and power of your kick. As you release, watch the ball's trajectory closely to see if it makes it past the goalkeeper and into the net!


Instructions

drag your mouse in the direction of the kick and hope that the ball will go right into the net without hitting the goalkeeper or a running player
