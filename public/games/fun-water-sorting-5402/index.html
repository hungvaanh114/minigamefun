Fun Water Sorting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Fun Water Sorting, an engaging and colorful puzzle game where your mission is to organize liquids based on their hues. The challenge begins as various tubes filled with vibrant liquids appear on your screen. Your objective is to mix and sort these liquids until each tube contains only a single color.

Utilize your logic and problem-solving skills to figure out how to rearrange the liquids efficiently. With each move, consider the combinations and sequences required to achieve the desired results. If you find yourself stumped, don t worry; the game offers useful hints and the ability to undo your last action. This feature makes it easier to learn from mistakes and experiment with different strategies.

Fun Water Sorting isn t just a solo experience. Invite your friends to join in the fun and see who can conquer the puzzle the fastest. You can easily share your progress, achievements, and times, fostering friendly competition and adding an extra layer of excitement to the game. Are you ready to put your sorting skills to the test?

How to play free Fun Water Sorting game online

To play Fun Water Sorting, simply drag and drop the liquids into the tubes. Each tube can hold a limited amount of liquid, so think wisely about your moves. Aim to sort the colors so that every tube contains one uniform color. Use hints for assistance or undo previous moves if needed.


Instructions

To play click on a bubble to select it and then click on a tube to drop it You can also tap the screen or use the left mouse click to select and move the bubble
