Baby Hazel Skin Care

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Winter is a season that children eagerly anticipate, as it offers a magical opportunity to frolic in the snow. Baby Hazel, like many kids, delights in engaging in outdoor activities amidst the winter wonderland. However, the cold weather can take a toll on her delicate skin, causing it to lose its softness and become dry. As a caring friend, your role is to assist Baby Hazel in maintaining her skin s health and hydration.

Your adventure begins by helping Baby Hazel take a soothing warm water bath, an essential step to cleanse and refresh her skin. After her bath, it s important to apply a gentle body lotion to lock in moisture and keep her skin soft and supple. Don t forget about her lips! Apply a nourishing lip balm to shield them from becoming chapped in the cold air. Alongside skincare, ensure Baby Hazel is fueled with nutritious meals and plenty of warm water to keep her energized during her winter playtime.

With your help, Baby Hazel can enjoy every moment of winter play while staying comfortable and protected from the harsh elements. Dive into this delightful game and experience the joy of taking care of Baby Hazel as you experience the beauty of winter together!

How to play free Baby Hazel Skin Care game online

To play the Baby Hazel Skin Care game, start by following the interactive prompts on the screen. Use your mouse to guide Baby Hazel through her winter skincare routine by clicking on the items such as the bath and lotions. Complete each task to ensure she is cozy and ready for snowy adventures!
