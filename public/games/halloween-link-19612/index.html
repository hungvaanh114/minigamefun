Halloween Link

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Halloween Link is an engaging block elimination game that captures the spirit of Halloween with its vibrant themes and captivating gameplay. Players are tasked with clearing the game board by matching two identical blocks. However, there s a twist the two blocks must be connected by a line that can twist a maximum of two times. This mechanic introduces a strategical element that requires players to think critically about their moves and plan their sequences in order to eliminate blocks effectively.

With 21 challenging levels to conquer, players will find themselves immersed in a delightful experience where quick thinking and spatial insight are key to success. Each level becomes progressively more intricate, adding layers of difficulty that will test your skills and enhance your enjoyment of the game. The ultimate objective is to eliminate all the cards from the board, a feat that demands focus and precision. Halloween Link not only offers a fun way to celebrate the holiday but also provides an exciting opportunity for players to challenge themselves.

Whether you re a fan of puzzle games or just looking for a festive way to pass the time, Halloween Link appeals to all ages and creates an enjoyable gaming atmosphere that can be played alone or compete with friends to see who can achieve the highest scores.

How to play free Halloween Link game online

To play Halloween Link, simply tap on two matching blocks that are connected by a line with a maximum of two turns. Continue eliminating blocks to clear the board, advancing through all 21 levels by completing each one and eliminating all cards. Strategize your moves wisely!


Instructions

Control Mouse or Touch Pad Find the similar blocks which can be connected with a line with maximum 2 turns Now click or tap both items continuously
