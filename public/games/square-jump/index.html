How to play free Square Jump game online

Playing Square Jump is intuitive and engaging. Use the space bar or tap the screen to jump your square character over obstacles. Keep an eye on the spikes below and time your jumps wisely to avoid any deadly encounters. Collect bonuses to boost your score as you run and jump your way to victory!
