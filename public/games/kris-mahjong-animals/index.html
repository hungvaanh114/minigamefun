Kris Mahjong Animals

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

In Kris Mahjong Animals, immerse yourself in a delightful world of vibrant animal-themed tiles designed to challenge your concentration and strategic thinking. This captivating game features a variety of adorable creatures, from playful cats to majestic lions, each beautifully illustrated on the tiles that you must connect. As you navigate through the different levels, you'll need to spot matching pairs of animals to clear them from the board and score points. The fast-paced nature of the game means you ll be racing against the clock, making every move count!

Your objective is simple yet engaging: find and match the identical animal tiles before time runs out. Each connection not only helps you accumulate points but also unlocks the vibrant visuals and delightful sounds that make this game so unique. With every level, the challenge intensifies, calling for sharp focus and quick reflexes. How many points can you rack up in this whimsical adventure before the timer ticks to zero? The clock is ticking, so sharpen your skills and see how far your attention can take you!

How to play free Kris Mahjong Animals game online

To play Kris Mahjong Animals, simply click on two identical animal tiles to link them together. Make sure the connecting line between the tiles does not exceed two right angles. Your goal is to clear the board within the allocated time limit. Good luck!
