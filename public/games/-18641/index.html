Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to the thrilling world of Bubble Space, an addictive bubble shooter game that promises endless entertainment! With its simple mechanics and captivating gameplay, you ll find yourself completely absorbed once you take your first shot. Your mission is clear: defeat all enemies while working to rescue a stranded spaceship!

Bubble Space offers a vibrant universe filled with colorful bubbles and challenging foes. Each level is thoughtfully designed to keep you engaged as you strategize your moves. You can enhance your gameplay using special items that will assist you in your quest. Collect a [Heart] to restore your health, grab a [Helmet] for increased stamina, or use a [Gun] to boost your attack power. Additionally, the [Pair of Bubbles] skill allows you to expand your spare bubble capacity, ensuring you have enough ammunition to overcome even the toughest obstacles!

With each successful elimination of enemy bubbles, you will move closer to freeing the captive spaceship, making the experience more rewarding. The colorful graphics, along with engaging sound effects, create a captivating atmosphere that keeps players coming back for more.

How to play free Bubble Space game online

To play Bubble Space, simply aim your bubble launcher at the clusters of bubbles. Click to shoot and match three or more same-colored bubbles to eliminate them. Use your special items strategically for maximum effect and try to clear the level before running out of bubbles. Enjoy your journey through space!
