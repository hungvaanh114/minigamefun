Subway Surfers Havana 2021

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Immerse yourself in the vibrant streets of Havana in the 2021 edition of Subway Surfers! In this exhilarating endless runner, you find yourself in a colorful world filled with the charm of Cuban culture. As you dash through the city's subway stations, your primary goal is to evade the ever-persistent grumpy inspector and his loyal dog who are hot on your heels.

Your journey is fraught with challenges, as you must skillfully navigate through a plethora of obstacles such as trains, trams, and various items scattered across the tracks. To aid your escape, collect shining coins along the way, which not only serve as points but can also be used to unlock exciting bonuses and new characters. Each character brings something unique to the game, enhancing your speed and agility as you break records and reach new high scores.

With each level, the game becomes more challenging, demanding quick reflexes and strategic planning. Are you up for the challenge of outrunning the inspector and exploring the beautifully designed landscapes of Havana? Good luck as you embark on this thrilling adventure in Subway Surfers!

How to play free Subway Surfers Havana 2021 game online

To play Subway Surfers Havana 2021, simply swipe left, right, up, or down to maneuver your character. Jump over trains, slide under obstacles, and switch tracks as you collect coins. Be quick on your feet to outrun the inspector, and use power-ups wisely for a smoother escape!
