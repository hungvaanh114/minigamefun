Barbie Make Up Time

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the glamorous world of Barbie with "Barbie Make Up Time"! In this enchanting evening scenario, Barbie is all set for a date with her charming boyfriend, Ken. The atmosphere is filled with excitement, and it s your moment to shine as you assist Barbie in transforming into the perfect evening princess. Get ready to dive into a delightful experience where you will help Barbie pick out the most stunning dress and create an eye-catching makeup look.

As you enter the game, you ll be greeted by a vibrant interface displaying a variety of options for Barbie's evening attire and makeup. Your creativity will be put to the test as you navigate through numerous menus filled with colorful outfits, stylish accessories, and trendy makeup choices. Whether it s a glamorous evening gown or a chic cocktail dress, the decision lies in your hands! Explore different shades of lipstick, eye shadow, and blush to find the perfect combination that complements Barbie's beauty and personality.

The fun doesn't stop at just choosing a dress and makeup! You can mix and match various styles and colors until you create the ultimate Barbie look that Ken won't be able to resist. Once you re satisfied with your creation, you can admire your handiwork and watch as Barbie gets ready for her big night out.

How to play free Barbie Make Up Time game online

To start playing "Barbie Make Up Time", simply select from the easy-to-navigate menus on the screen. Click on dress options to explore different styles, and then choose your preferred makeup items. Use your mouse or touch controls to drag and drop items onto Barbie, putting together a fabulous look for her date night with Ken!
