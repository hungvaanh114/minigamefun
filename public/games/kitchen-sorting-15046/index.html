Kitchen Sorting

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Kitchen Sorting offers a delightful twist on the beloved mechanics of ball and water sorting games. Immerse yourself in your cozy kitchen as you engage in a unique sorting adventure. Your mission is to organize various cooking ingredients into different glass jars, utilizing your problem-solving skills and strategic thinking.

As you sort the items, the vibrant colors and appealing visuals of the ingredients will keep you captivated. Each jar presents a challenge, requiring you to determine the best order for sorting to maximize your efficiency. Once your ingredients are neatly organized, you can take the next step and pour them into a pot, where you ll be able to craft delicious meals. This game not only entertains but also sharpens your cognitive abilities as you navigate through increasingly complex levels.

The satisfaction of seeing everything in its place, along with the joy of cooking, creates a harmonious gameplay experience. Whether you're a casual gamer or a dedicated enthusiast, Kitchen Sorting offers endless fun and creativity right in the heart of your virtual kitchen.

How to play free Kitchen Sorting game online

To play Kitchen Sorting, simply touch or click the ingredients to move them into the appropriate jars. Your goal is to group similar items together without exceeding the limits of each jar. Once sorted, drag the ingredients into the pot to cook. Stay organized and strategize your moves to complete each level efficiently!


Instructions

Tap jar to pick one or more ingredients of the same kind from the top Tap another jar to drop them in You can drop them only on top of the same kind of ingredients or into an empty jar You can use swipe from source jar to destination one instead of tap
