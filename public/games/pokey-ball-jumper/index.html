Pokey Ball Jumper

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

The excitement never stops in the captivating world of Pokey Ball Jumper, an engaging hypercasual game crafted by the talented team at MaskedGames. Players are challenged to propel a ball to reach towering heights in pursuit of glittering treasures. Your objective? Stretch the ball as far as possible, then release it to launch it upwards.

Control is key as you must tap the screen to securely anchor the ball using a needle. However, beware this needle will not penetrate solid walls, demanding precision in your timing and strategy. Each obstacle presents a unique challenge, requiring patience and skill to navigate through the game s vibrant and dynamic levels.

With a total of 50 diverse levels to conquer, Pokey Ball Jumper ensures that the fun remains endless. Each stage introduces new trials, keeping players on their toes as they continue their quest for high scores and valuable rewards. Get ready to embrace the thrill of the jump, and dive into the excitement that awaits!

How to play free Pokey Ball Jumper game online

To play Pokey Ball Jumper, start by stretching the ball to the desired length. Release it at the right moment to launch it skyward. Tap the screen to insert the needle into the wall to keep the ball from falling. Aim to time your actions perfectly to overcome obstacles and reach the treasure!
