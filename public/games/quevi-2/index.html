Quevi 2

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Quevi 2 is an engaging 2D platformer that immerses players in a vibrant sci-fi world filled with challenges and excitement. As you embark on this thrilling adventure, your primary objective is to collect all the metallic spheres scattered throughout each level while deftly avoiding a range of menacing obstacles. From enemy robots that patrol the areas to cunning flying robots that soar above, every moment requires your utmost attention.

Your path is fraught with dangers, including relentless bullets, spinning lasers, and massive blades that can end your journey in an instant. Strategically maneuver through these threats as you push towards the Exit door at the end of each level. Successfully reaching the door transports you to new challenges, as Quevi 2 features a total of eight levels, each more difficult than the last, ensuring that the gameplay remains both stimulating and rewarding.

This sequel builds upon the foundations of the original Quevi series, enhancing the gameplay with dynamic elements and increasingly complex level designs. Discover new strategies and platforming skills as you navigate this futuristic landscape and aim for the highest scores. Get ready to dive into Quevi 2, where each level evolves the gameplay and tests your reflexes.

How to play free Quevi 2 game online

To play Quevi 2, use the arrow keys to move your character left or right. Jump using the space bar to avoid enemies and navigate platforms. Collect all the metal balls while dodging bullets, lasers, and blades. Reach the Exit door to complete each level and advance to the next challenge!


Instructions

Use WASD or Arrow keys for the players movement and Use W or up arrow key twice for double jump
