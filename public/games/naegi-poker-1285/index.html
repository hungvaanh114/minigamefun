How to play free Naegi Poker game online

Once you're in Naegi Poker, simply choose your preferred table type, 9-Max or 6-Max. After selecting your avatar, you ll be seated at a table where you can place your bets, raise, call, or fold based on the cards you receive. Stay sharp and use your poker face to outwit your opponents! Enjoy the game!
