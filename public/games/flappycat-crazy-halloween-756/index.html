How to play free Flappy Cat: Crazy Halloween game online

To play Flappy Cat: Crazy Halloween, simply tap the screen to make Flappy Cat flap his wings and rise or fall. Navigate through the spooky towers by timing your taps carefully to avoid obstacles. The goal is to collect points while keeping Flappy Cat airborne and aiming for the highest score possible!
