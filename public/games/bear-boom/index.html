Bear Boom

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Welcome to Bear Boom, the ultimate free match-3 puzzle game that will tantalize your taste buds! Dive into a vibrant world of adorable jellies, where your objective is to link and collect these delightful treats to complete each level. If you crave an exciting and flavorful gaming experience, Bear Boom is just the right dose of sweetness for you.

In Bear Boom, matching 3 or more adjacent jellies is the key to earning points and fulfilling your level goals. The game rewards strategic thinking, as connecting 7 or more jellies not only boosts your score but also creates powerful, explosive special jellies that can help clear the board in a spectacular fashion. With a variety of exciting levels to conquer, each presenting its own unique challenges, you'll need to plan your moves wisely to succeed.

The charming graphics and delightful sound effects will keep you engaged as you navigate through this extraordinarily scrumptious quest. Use your limited moves strategically to achieve level targets and crank up your gaming experience by creating powerful power-ups that can turn the tide of your puzzle-solving adventures. Whether you're looking to relax or challenge yourself, Bear Boom delivers a satisfying blend of fun, strategy, and pure entertainment.

How to play free Bear Boom game online

To play Bear Boom, simply swipe or tap to select and match 3 or more adjacent jellies. Aim to create matches that lead to special jellies for powerful effects! Keep an eye on your limited moves and plan your strategy to accomplish level goals and earn exciting rewards. Happy gaming!
