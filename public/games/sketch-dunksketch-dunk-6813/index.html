Step onto the virtual court with Sketch Dunk, an exciting and vibrant basketball game that challenges your shooting skills and precision! How to play free Sketch Dunk game online

To play Sketch Dunk, simply hold the basketball above your character, and adjust your aim and strength by timing your taps correctly. Release to shoot the ball towards the hoop!
