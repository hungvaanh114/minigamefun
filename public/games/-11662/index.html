Welcome to the enchanting world of Magic Pet Salon - Magic Makeover! Step into the whimsical pet salon owned by Luna, the little witch, where magic and creativity blend seamlessly to create stunning transformations for her adorable clients. Every day, Luna welcomes a variety of charming pets who are eager for a magical makeover, and she needs your assistance to ensure they leave looking fabulous!

In the Magic Pet Salon, there's never a dull moment as you engage in an array of delightful activities! You can become a skilled groomer, wielding your magic brush to tidy up furry coats, or try your hand at being a designer, creating stylish looks that will make the pets stand out in the crowd. From extravagant hairdos to chic nail designs, the possibilities are virtually endless! As you work your magic, you ll discover new and exciting ways to pamper each unique pet.

The lively and colorful environment of the Magic Pet Salon is designed to captivate players of all ages. With each new customer that walks through the door, you'll experience the joy of helping these special pets shine like never before. So gather your magical tools and get ready to unleash your creativity. The salon is bustling with activity, and each pet makeover is a delightful adventure waiting to unfold!

How to play free Magic Pet Salon - Magic Makeover game online

To play the game, simply follow the on-screen instructions as you select your pet client. Use your mouse or touch controls to groom, dress, and accessorize each pet. Experiment with different styles and magic effects to create the perfect look. Don t forget to save your favorite transformations!
