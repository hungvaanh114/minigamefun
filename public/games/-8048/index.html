Baby Panda Chinese Holidays?Baby Panda Chinese Holidays

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the delightful adventure of Baby Panda Chinese Holidays, a beloved cooking game where you join Baby Panda in celebrating traditional Chinese holidays. Step into the vibrant world and discover the joyous activities that take place during these festive times.

In this engaging game, you ll have the opportunity to learn how to prepare delicious moon cakes, a staple during the Mid-Autumn Festival. Follow the easy-to-understand instructions to mix, roll, and shape your ingredients into these delectable treats. Additionally, you will dive into the cultural significance of Zongzi, learning the art of making this sticky rice dumpling wrapped in bamboo leaves, perfect for the Dragon Boat Festival.

Your adventure doesn t stop at cooking! Participate in an exhilarating dragon boat competition against your furry friend, the little dog. Navigate the waters, race to the finish line, and see who emerges victorious! Also, embrace the artistic side of Chinese culture by experiencing the intricate process of paper-making. Create beautiful sheets to use in your crafts and learn about the rich history behind this ancient practice.

No matter your age or skill level, Baby Panda Chinese Holidays promises endless fun and learning, ensuring that you will have a fantastic time celebrating Chinese traditions with Baby Panda!

How to play free Baby Panda Chinese Holidays game online

To play, simply click on the game link and enter the colorful world of Baby Panda. Follow the on-screen prompts to cook moon cakes and Zongzi. Participate in the dragon boat race by controlling your boat with directional keys. Enjoy various mini-games to learn and have fun!
