Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Enter the heart-pounding world of Five Nights at Horror Games, where your courage is put to the ultimate test! As a vigilant security guard in the ominous Haggy Lee Mansion Hospital, you must remain alert. Unsettling noises and uncanny creatures dwell in the shadows, constantly keeping you on edge. Your main task is to closely monitor two formidable foes: Huggy Lee and his malevolent counterpart, Granny Grandmother. Use the surveillance cameras wisely, but beware electricity is a limited resource, and every decision counts as you strive to keep these haunting entities at bay.

Immerse yourself in the intricacies of this labyrinthine hospital, where every corner hides potential horrors. Feel the heartbeat of adrenaline rush through your veins as you navigate this chilling environment, trying to survive five harrowing nights against the forces of darkness. Will you make it till dawn, or will the horrifying residents of Haggy Lee Mansion claim another victim? Prepare yourself for a thrilling experience filled with suspense, strategy, and unexpected twists!

How to play free Five Nights at Horror Games game online

To play Five Nights at Horror Games, use your mouse to navigate the surveillance cameras placed throughout the hospital. Monitor Huggy Lee and Granny Grandmother's movements to prevent them from reaching you. Conserve your electricity by limiting camera usage, and keep a watchful eye as you manage your defenses against their terrifying advances throughout the night.
