Super Frog is a delightful platform game that brings back the nostalgia of classic retro gaming, reminiscent of the beloved Super Mario series. Immerse yourself in a vibrant world filled with challenging levels, where you can take control of our heroic amphibious protagonist. Your goal is to traverse through 8 magnificent levels, each packed with unique obstacles and colorful landscapes that will keep you engaged for hours.

As you navigate through the game, you'll have to jump on the heads of various enemies to defeat them. Be quick and precise with your jumps, as letting these foes touch you will result in losing valuable lives. The game not only tests your reflexes but also encourages players to collect fruits scattered throughout each level. For every 30 fruits you gather, you will be rewarded with a regained life, allowing you to progress further in your quest!

The levels are designed to ramp up in difficulty, presenting you with new enemies and challenging platforms as you advance. The charming graphics and catchy soundtrack enhance the overall experience, making Super Frog not just a game, but a delightful adventure that returns players to their childhood gaming days.

How to play free Super Frog game online

To play Super Frog, use the arrow keys to move left and right. Press the spacebar to make your character jump over obstacles and onto enemies. Collect fruits to regain lives, and navigate through each level without losing all your health. Have fun and conquer all the challenges!
