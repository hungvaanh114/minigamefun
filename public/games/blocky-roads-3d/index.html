Blocky Roads 3D

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Get ready to rev your engines in Blocky Roads 3D, an exhilarating physics-based racing game that lets you explore breathtaking terrains including lush green hills, icy mountain peaks, and vast desert dunes! After a tornado wreaks havoc on your farm, scattering its pieces across the globe, it's time to embark on an exciting adventure to collect the lost items and restore your beloved farm to its original charm.

In this engaging 3D racer, you'll face off against the clock to prove your driving prowess in a vibrant, blocky mining universe. Choose from nine unique vehicles, or tap into your creativity by building your very own car, block by block. Once you conquer the first three tracks, you ll unlock the innovative car editor that lets you customize and paint your vehicle to stand out on the racecourse.

With a total of twelve thrilling tracks to master, plus four additional challenge courses to really test your skills, there s no shortage of excitement in Blocky Roads 3D. The game features an assortment of vehicles, including trucks, bikes, monster trucks, and tanks, each providing a distinct racing experience. With stunning voxel graphics and engaging gameplay, kids and adults alike will find joy racing through its interactive world.

Keep up with the latest updates and news from Dogbyte Games, the creators of popular titles such as Offroad Legends and Dead Venture, as you dive into this action-packed racing adventure!

How to play free Blocky Roads 3D game online

To play Blocky Roads 3D, simply choose your vehicle and select a track. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate through the courses, collect farm parts, and compete against your best times. Make sure to explore opportunities to unlock the car editor after completing the initial tracks for added customization!
