Phone Case DIY 4

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Phone Case DIY 4 is a captivating and trendy art game that allows you to unleash your creativity while designing the perfect phone case. Upon starting the game, you ll have the opportunity to select your preferred phone case design from a variety of options. Once you ve made your selection, you ll apply a layer of glue to your blank canvas, ensuring it's evenly spread to prepare for customization.

Next, it s time to get colorful! You will have access to an extensive palette featuring dozens of vibrant shades. Whether your style leans towards a rustic pastoral theme, a bold dark approach, or a cute girly aesthetic, there s a perfect paint waiting just for you. After painting your case to your heart's content, use a hairdryer to dry the colors and get your creation ready for the next step.

The fun doesn't stop there! Head over to the sticker section where you can choose from a delightful assortment of fruit, animal, cartoon, and vegetable stickers. Personalizing your phone case has never been more exciting. To top off your masterpiece, embellish your design with a delicate phone chain and your choice of sparkling rhinestones that reflect your unique personality.

How to play free Phone Case DIY 4 game online

To play Phone Case DIY 4, start by selecting a phone case from the options available. Then, apply glue and choose your favorite paint color from the selection. After drying your case, browse through the sticker options and add your favorites. Finally, enhance your design with a phone chain and rhinestones for a complete look!
