Moto Maniac 3

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Experience the thrill of extreme biking in Moto Maniac 3, a captivating tilty bike game designed to push your trial skills to their limits. Set against a backdrop of lush forests, this game immerses you in an exhilarating competition where you'll navigate through intricate trails, climbing over fallen branches and rocky terrains.

Your primary objective is to conquer diverse obstacles in record time, showcasing your agility and reflexes as you balance your bike through challenging courses. However, there's a twist! An angry bear, disturbed by the noise of your roaring engine, is hot on your heels. Not only do you have to focus on speeding through the course, but you must also be mindful of evading the ferocious creature chasing you!

Each level presents unique challenges, requiring quick thinking and precise movements. The forest setting is not only visually stunning but strategically designed to keep you on your toes as you race against time and the bear. Every jump and swerve you execute propels you closer to the finish line, but beware one wrong move could lead to being caught, making the game even more exhilarating!

How to play free Moto Maniac 3 game online

To play Moto Maniac 3, use the arrow keys or tilt controls to balance your bike as you accelerate through the forest trails. Navigate obstacles, avoid falling off your bike, and keep pace to escape the bear chasing you. The faster you finish each level, the better your score!


Instructions

Up or W Down or S - Drive forward backward Left or A Right or D - Tilt left right P - pause
