Sugar Factory

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Step into the whimsical world of Sugar Factory, where your sweet tooth and strategy come together in an exciting coin pusher X merger adventure! In this delightful game, you will drop an array of cookies and scrumptious desserts into a colorful sugar pit, aiming to amass an incredible collection of sugary treats. The more delicious items you can push into the pit, the greater the rewards you will reap!

As you engage in this tasty quest, you will encounter special power-up tokens designed to unleash spectacular combos that elevate your rewards to new heights. Fill the sugar pit to the brim, and watch as bonus sugar cubes shower down, providing an extra surge to your score. Each sugary treat you collect contributes to your overall success, turning this game into a sweet competition among friends!

Are you ready to challenge your friends? Track your high scores and see who can gather the most treats in this sugary showdown. With charming graphics and addictive gameplay, Sugar Factory is sure to become your new favorite way to enjoy some light-hearted strategy and fun.

How to play free Sugar Factory game online

To play Sugar Factory, simply start dropping cookies and desserts into the sugar pit using your mouse or touchscreen. Keep an eye out for power-up tokens; use them wisely to create powerful combos. Aim to fill the pit completely for bonus rewards and strive to beat your friends' scores!
