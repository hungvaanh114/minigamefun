Baby Hazel Summer Fun

Hướng dẫn cách chơi / Instructions:

Join Baby Hazel in her exciting summer adventure as she navigates the sunny season filled with fun and laughter! The scorching summer heat can lead to discomfort, and Baby Hazel is feeling the effects of the blazing sun. It's essential to protect her delicate skin from harmful rays, which is where you come in! Your mission is to help Hazel prepare for her day out by applying sunscreen and talc to ensure her skin stays safe and healthy.

Get ready to assist Baby Hazel as she picks up all the necessary essentials before she steps outside. This includes gathering her favorite sun hat, sunglasses, and beach toys. Making sure she s equipped with these items will allow her to enjoy her summer day to the fullest while remaining safeguarded against the sun s intensity. With your guidance, Hazel will not only have a wonderfully fun time but also learn the importance of taking care of herself under the sun.

As you embark on this bright adventure with Baby Hazel, you'll encounter various mini-games and challenges that will make your summer fun even more exciting. Whether you're applying lotion, packing gear, or playing outside, there s never a dull moment when it comes to summer with Baby Hazel!

How to play free Baby Hazel Summer Fun game online

To play the game, use your mouse to interact with various items around Baby Hazel. Start by applying sunscreen and talc to her body, ensuring she s well-protected. Follow the on-screen instructions to help her gather her essential summer gear, and enjoy playing fun mini-games as you make the most of her sunny adventures!
