Are you ready to master the art of trading? Trading Master 3D - Fidget Pop invites you into an engaging world where negotiation and strategy go hand in hand. This captivating trading business simulation arcade game features an array of vibrant 3D items laid out on a dynamic table, waiting for your savvy trading skills. How to play free Trading Master 3D - Fidget Pop game online

To play Trading Master 3D - Fidget Pop, begin by selecting items from the table to negotiate with your opponent. Aim for the green and yellow zones on the left side gauge, which indicates a favorable deal. Use your trading instincts and strategies to finalize each transaction, and successfully complete the levels in order to progress!
