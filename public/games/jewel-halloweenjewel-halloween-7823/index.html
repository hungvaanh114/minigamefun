How to play free Jewel Halloween game online

To start playing Jewel Halloween, simply match three or more similar colored jewels to clear them from the board. Use your mouse or touchpad to swap adjacent jewels to create the strongest combinations. Progress through levels by completing objectives while keeping an eye out for hidden treasures and spooky surprises!
